I see I have several improvements to make if I want mine to be as good, if not better.

The three options that my calculator offers that this other site does not are:
1) You can specify an entire territory as your starting or ending point
2) You do not have to specify any starting or ending point, and then you will find the most profitable trades possible between any bases
3) You can specify how much money you have to invest (especially if you do not have a lot), and maximize your profits based on that. (This is useful if you have less than about 15000 credits).
