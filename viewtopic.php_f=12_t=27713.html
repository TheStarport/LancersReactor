Attention all Matrix lovers

I found something that some of you might be interested in. There is a Mod Team which is creating a Total Conversion of Max Payne into a Matrix game. Many people played Enter the Matrix, but most didn't like it and wondered why it wasn't more like Max Payne when it comes to graphics and gameplay. This Mod Team thought the same thing and are now working on a Mod called The Matrix Revolutions Mod.

The Matrix Revolutions Mod has cool features like:

-Fully Controlable Flying
-Kung Fu moves and other moves from the Matrix Series
-About 250 Animations
-Playable characters from the the movies, each having special moves (8 skins to play with, like Neo, Morpheus, the Twins etc.)
-About 20+ new maps (From the Matrix 1 , 2 , 3 )
-Gun Kata (from the movie Equilibrium...excellent movie btw )
-Unlocking Feature which never has been done before
-New music made by there own music artist
-20 new weapons, 6 melee weapons (each having different attacks)
and a lot of other stuff which will take pages to write.
