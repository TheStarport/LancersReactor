For me, it has to be the Eagle all the way. I've fought guys in Sabres and Titans and they turn really slow. Once you get on their tail its all over because you can out turn them. The difference in the Hull doesn't make a noticeable difference at all. If you think about it, the 2700 point hull advantage that the titan has over the Eagle doesn't mean anything. If you're sheilds go down, you're in big trouble. 2700 points isn't going to save anybody because guns can do that in 1 second easy. If you like to duel people, go with the Eagle. The only thing that is "bad" about the eagle is that yes, its hull is weaker than the other 2. So this means you can't go flying around in radiation as long as the others can.
