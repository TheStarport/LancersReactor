now you've actually got a copy of SL, I suppose you ought to know a few things. It can be buggy, due to completely screwed dynmic sound. You must turn off 3D sound in the game options and don't set EAX or it will crash at the most annoying intervals. Some of the movies can be b*ggers for loading, so make sure you have plenty of RAM and a defragged hdd. You don't need a stonking fast system to play it, but any obvious bottlenecks will be very noticeable on SL. And the MS Sidewinder joystick works beautifully with it. Welcome, you're a REAL Lancer now!

do the training first, SL combat is a knack you have to learn, it's not as easy as FL at all. Practice with your missiles especially Jackhammers as you never carry enough, so make each one count.
