Lt Rook

Mon May 12, 2003 9:15 pm by Lt Rook

Well, I have used all the freighters extensively. Even tried the mule out for several hours. Now I am not saying by any means I am the most knowlegable freighter pilot. These are just my opinons. It really depends on several things as to which one is the best. The general consences is the Dromedary or the Humpback. If you prefer to manuver and fight your enemies straight on, the Dromedary is by far better than the Humpback. But if you prefer to set your destination on a way point, and go to turret mode, go for the Humpback. In addition to the lower shields, armor and guns, the Dromedary has a weak spot (attacking from low and behind) where you can only fire 2 guns on an enemy.

Now I have been jumped by level 10 guys before and even in the Humpback I was quickly destroyed. But I wonder how well a single humpback in turret mode could stand up to a single fighter (real player) around the same level. Let's say a Banshee. I think in the right hands, the Humpback could hold its own under similar conditions. There should be a freighter that can stand up to the top level fighters in the game.

One setup I have been wanting to try is a formation of 6 or so freighters. Pick up Niobium in Tau 37 and hull it Dreden via the boarder worlds route. With the lead formation freighter controlling the flight and everyone else on turrets, it would be interesting to see if we could make it. It would be like the WWII bomber formations over Germany.
