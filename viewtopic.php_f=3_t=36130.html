NukeIt

Wed Nov 17, 2004 11:59 am by NukeIt

I occasionally, just for kicks, take a tour of Sirius using a Liberty Dreadnought...judging by how difficult it is to maneuver those things through asteroid fields and such, it must be a real chore to keep up with the SP environments!

For docking with battleships, I'm pretty sure there is no mooring point (not sure whether that particular mod has mooring enabled)...if all it requires of you is a standard landing approach, position yourself, properly oriented(toward the stern for Liberty Bships), as close as you possibly can to the docking bay. Try this OUT of SP several times until you find the correct position. Once you are correctly positioned, hit the dock button. Since your ship will never fit into that small docking bay, you must hit esc. to skip the docking cutscene. Launch should be no problem, but occasionally you will hang up on launch, so save the game before you launch again.
