Thu Nov 20, 2003 4:21 am by Chips

Well - optimal class depends on power supply, and nothing more. Choose what class you want to go up to with weapons, then choose a power unit to suit it. If you like, you can take a spectrum of power useage by weapons, then average out that power, and on your maximum mount ratings, you should have about 10 seconds of firepower. Then work out what would give you 30 seconds (average energy drain) and then take that and look it up. As you can see, that is extensive, so i just think of a ship similar, and use that......
