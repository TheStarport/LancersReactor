Hobbes78

Wed Aug 04, 2004 9:12 am by Hobbes78

hello all,

well i am now in the process of building a website for Freelancer Model Repository. It will include the ability for you to upload your model files (in zip format) directly from the website. There will also be a 'Contributors' page that will display a list of who has submitted models. This Will also act as a permanent links page as well.

If you guys have any suggestions as to what type of features you would like to see in it feel free to share them.

---

The idea of this site will be that anyone who submits thier models to it will be giving permission for modders of FL to use these models in thier mods. The ppl downloading the models will still have to give credit to the creators of the models.

Samurai Studios will kick off the repository by putting in several models of our own, our way of giving a little back to the FL community.

Also (maybe) being added to this site will be a Texture section, where ppl can submit textures they would like to share with the FL community.

I will post a news item to TLR when the site is completed.


Roland "Hobbes" Orr
Founder, Samurai Studios
