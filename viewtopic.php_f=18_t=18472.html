Hooray!!!!!!!!!!!

A fellow spirit!!

I'm dying for some Elite & Frontier ships to appear in Freelancer, and maybe a new system, called Lave, with a certain symmetrical station in orbit...

I think an Elite mod would fit very well with the rest of FL - people can obviosly add new systems and ships to the game, so the more notorious systems (such as Lave, Reorte, Riedquat and maybe Beta Hydri, Achenar and Gateway from Frontier and First Encounters) could be added along with all the ships.

The classic ships from Elite have very simple geometrical designs, and 3ds models (untextured) are actually available for download at Ian Bell's website. For all you clever modders out there it shouldn't be much of a hassle to stitch together an Elite ship mod. I'd try to do it myself if I had the time.

The ships in Frontier and First Encounters are more complex, but I think models have been done by fans in the Frontier community (check out http://galnet.doosh.net/ )
I have not found any available for download yet, though.

By the way;
Frontier and First Encounters are shareware nowadays, available at the Elite Club ( http://www.eliteclub.co.uk/ ). To run FFE on windows you'll need jjffe from http://www.jaj22.demon.co.uk/index.html.

Sure, the graphics sucked even when the game was released in 1994, and combat is just annoying, but flying over planets can still be spectacular. And it's frigging HUGE!!! Thousands of systems, lots of uncharted space to explore...

I hope someone takes the time to make an Elite mod. Until then, Ill make do with whatever FL has to offer...

And a little FFE nostalgia besides...
