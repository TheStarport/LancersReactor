Since the whole game is centered around the mouse, we should talk about it, don't you think?

I use the Logitech Dual Optical. Great mouse. And before that I used a Logitech iFeel Optical (2 buttons w/scroll, not the Mouseman). My next purchase will probably be the MX500, unless a newer one comes out by Christmas.

Also, if anyone uses the MX 700, please tell us about it! I'm all for wireless mice being the next big thing in gaming, and only testimonials will get the ball rolling.
