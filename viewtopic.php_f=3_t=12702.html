Keelhauling--Corsair Style

For those that don't know what Keelhauling is....

It's a punishment used back in the old sailing ship days...by crusty old sea dogs...They would tie a rope around you and drag you along the keel (bottom of the ship)

I just read a rumor that Corsairs have a version of this:

Put you in a Vac-Suit, attach a tow cable to you, and fly thru a jump hole.
