Gestoords Audio Pack version 3

Hello peeps,

Humbly I want to present my Audio pack v3...

The audio pack is an optional addon that can be used with any mod as long as it uses the standard FL music tracks.

This mod is client side only and doesnt need to be installed serverside since the audio tracks are retrieved from the players PC.
Singleplayer should work to but it hasnt been tested yet.

If you are running a mod just activate the audio pack on top of it.

What does this pack (v1.1) do:

It replaces all battle & space music tracks of the vanilla systems (except the unknowns)
The new audio tracks give Freelancer a fresh feeling...
This pack contains mostly electronic music tracks.

Version 2 changes:

The landing scene music tracks are replaced with new ones.
Thanks to Black Eagle the mod has been reduce in size...

Version 3 changes:

Added new danger music tracks

For futur release some theme packs are a possibility ( percussion tracks / guitar theme / symphonic theme / ...)
I am also considering a Audio fun pack which which will replace some of the character audio to some fun stuff ("halt you are carrying illegal goodies !! Stop or get toasted !!!" / "get in here !!! your request to dock is taking to long"
