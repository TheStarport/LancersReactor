go to sigma-13 (the GMG home system one) and go to sector 5c then work your way up. you'll come across 4 or 5 OINF (Old Imperial Navy Fighter) wrecks that carry the following weapons:

> OINF in sector 5C carries 2x JADE, some cruise disruptors, and drone mines
> OINF in sector 5C carries 2x DIAMONDBACK some cruise disruptors, and drone mines
> OINF in sector 4C carries 2x ONYX some cruise disruptors, and drone mines
> OINF in sector 4C carries 2x BLOODSTONE some cruise disruptors, and drone mines
> OINF in sector 3C carries 2x IRON HAMMER some cruise disruptors, and drone mines
> Kassel in sector 3C carries 2x SILVER FIRE some cruise disruptors, and drone mines

None of the above carry any commodities, but the weapons will surely make up for that! Hope this is better than the Mongoose wreck, as it was for me! Each weapon does on average 1000 odd hull damage and 500 odd shield damage.
