I'm afraid that a lot of what you are talking about, with regeneration because of equipment and commodities, would not be possible without .exe editing or using an outside program to manage this stuff. (As I:TW uses.) Especially when you are talking about regeneration due to other units or players in system, is just not what the FL engine was made for, and the only possible way to implement this would be to build an outside program to monitor player locations and adjust statuses at regular intervals.

However, the bases, reps, and encounters are totally possible and in cases fairly easy if you use a graphical program liks FLE for aspects of it. (Like placing new bases and encounters, which can be a real pain to hand code into the INIs.)
