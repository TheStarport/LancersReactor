Chips

Thu Oct 16, 2003 3:02 am by Chips

Already done, have four docking rings on one of my planets - which in turn lands you at four different bases on the planet - namely

North - Bretonian
South - Rheinland
East - Kusari
West - Liberty

At each base you can buy the guns for the house faction

At other bases in systems you can buy other houses guns, but they cost more than they do in their homeland - same with ships too.

In fact - its the same with nearly everything - you cannot buy ammo for high class missles anywhere - you have to find them at bases, nano bots and bats cost more out of house systems than inside them - with gamma and alpha costing 280 each. (independant worlds and so on cost 220, borders cost 240)

Ships from Bretonia in Kusari cost 50% more than in Bretonia, same for everything else - there are new ships as well

Also - there are 20 or so new commodities - 5 of which are contraband.
You do require reps to buy everything - ships, guns, commodities etc, and also detroit munitions sells every gun in the game and shields etc, only problem is that the higher the class, the more they cost, so corsair high level guns cost 2.5 times more than normal ones.
