For the Class 5 slots: Outcast Pyros Type 2 (particle)
For the Class 6 slots: Corsair Angelito Mk II (neutron)

Both guns are 4.0 refire, 600 range, 600 speed and hit hard for their class. Efficiency is quite decent, around 1 pts energy/5 pt hull damage each. They're also available in or very close to Kusari space, I guess saying more about that would be spoilage.

Gung_Ho

Sat Jun 28, 2003 5:09 am by Gung_Ho

It depends what you are wanting to do with it, I'm thinking.

If you plan on dueling other light fighters, then I'd go with 2 Advanced Tarantulas, one Borroco (shield Killer) and one Tarantula. I'd probably choose this loadout for fighting the AI as well.

For dueling VHF's (its not good odds, but it's possible) I'd go with 2x Borroco and 2X Adv Tarantula. You'd need to consistently beat that shield regen rate, and this loaout will make that a possibility.
