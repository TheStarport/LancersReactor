Wed Jul 09, 2003 9:15 pm by The Dark

I've done some pirating in a Y-Wing. 325 cargo space is enough for high-profit items (like diamonds from Omega-whichever to New Tokyo), and the ability to mount six Class-10 guns and a pair of turrets makes the Y-Wing a great commerce raider. I've got a pair of Diamondbacks and a pair of Thor's Hammer for guns, two Cannonball missiles launchers, and two turrets whose type I don't recall (I believe they're class 8 with a refire rate of 4.00). My college roommate and I tend to do pirating once we've got decent ships in MP, because missions get boring after a while, since they're all one of three types: blow everything up, blow up a base, or blow up a ship and tractor something in. With piracy, you have to sneak through systems and scan ships to find decent cargo. It's more of a challenge, and much more interesting.
