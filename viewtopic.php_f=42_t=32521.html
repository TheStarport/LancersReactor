Texas Tradelane Crash-To-Desktop

I think I've discovered the problem...
Or, at least, a possible problem.
It affects versions 2.3, 2.4, and 2.5.

In the Data\Equipment\Misc_Equip.ini file:

There several power units:
nickname = ge_fighter_power01
nickname = ge_fighter2_power01
nickname = ge_fighter4_power01
nickname = ge_fighter5_power01
nickname = ge_fighter6_power01
nickname = bw_fighter_power01
nickname = bw_elite_power01
nickname = bw_elite2_power01

All of them are missing the following 2 lines:
DA_archetype = equipment\models\hardware\li_fusion_reactor.3db
material_library = equipment\models\hardware.mat

Add these lines after the "Mass =" line.

These omissions cause a non-recoverable error that adds, and adds, until it finally causes a CTD.
Which basically means...
The more battles you're in, and the more ships you destroy, the higher your chances of a CTD.
I'm not exactly sure how many, because it depends on how many Generators become salvage.

And for some, strange, reason...
The Texas TL, and the Buffalo base, are highly sensitive to these sorta things.

Watch your 6!
