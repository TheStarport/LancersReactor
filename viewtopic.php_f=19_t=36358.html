There is also my server's mod, called Boston SuperMod. If you do not install the client-side opt-in mod then you'll still experience the client-side part of the mod. It is designed to specifically enhance the FL experience yet leave the PvP part virtually untouched. NPCs are stronger and are no longer relatively easy pushovers. There are many details all of which can be read on my forum here.

We stress balanced gameplay in the SuperMod and are also working it into our future mod, the Procyon mod. Without the balance then it becomes a game of hacks where only a few ship/equipment combinations work to the exclusion of all else.
