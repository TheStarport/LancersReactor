Shadows Mod (sorta update)

I mentioned my mod about i'd say 8 or so months ago, but since then i have made some DRASTIC changes but nothing that has not been done already Either way the story is kinda as follows...(still a work in progress )

""The shadows of eminent war have appeared over sirius, The galaxy is clouded with deciet and peril, House governments have joined forces to cleanse the systems of the "pirate threat". The Pirate factions delacred total war upon the house governments. With this delcaration, an new order was formed the Outworld alliance, you the son of the late Edison Trent must decide to join forces with the governments or the outworld alliance and discover the truth behind your fathers death.""

it takes place i'd say 22 years after the events of freelancer, Trent is killed while on a trading mission through Liberty space (hint hint hint). So the player takes off as the sone of trent, depending on the "side" you choose either the "Government Coalition" or the "Outworld Alliance" you decide on how to learn of your fathers death.

The story is not really a "scripted" story like SP. Rather it is a woven web of "information" from bar patrons and other NPC's you run across in the huge galaxy that is being created.
