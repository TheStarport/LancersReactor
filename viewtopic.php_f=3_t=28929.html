Fri Mar 05, 2004 4:19 am by Midaus

So far the best FL experience, in my opinion, is offered by the Real Space 2.2 Mod by Louva-Deus. At least for the SP missions. The changes made are logical. Better lighting and shading, bright green and red lights on the trade lanes, beefed up NPC's with better equipment, differing thruster speeds for light n heavy fighters.

I played the evolutions mod and it's real good too. I don't think it works well for the SP campaign though. It's very, very difficult to get a new ship after the StarViper you start with. The viper is a real fun ship to fly but you'll not find a replacement at any of the old haunts anytime soon. I'll not say where to find the couger, hunting for a replacement is part of the fun. Still, evolutions does some great stuff with their mod and I'll bet it's KA on multiplayer. The NPC's are a real challenge and there's oodles of new ships out there. Check it out.
