I'll say this again--the locking the system thing is about keeping battles in certain divisions. I push forces into a system, they meet up with your forces, a few minutes of quick shots and explosions and then its over. No time to send reinforcements. I'm actually fine with it--it makes you think exactly how ready would you be in any given system for an attack--and therefore strengthens your defence and strategy.

What's more of a problem for me--this is better addressed in #4--is the FA FD CA CD. Why would fighters not have a specific attack and defence?

I think the new stats should be: Attack, Defense, and Versatility. Attack=how much damage ship X inflicts. Defense=How powerful an attack it takes for the ship to succumb. Versatility: How easily (in percentage) X can dodge an attack.

E.G: Kusari LF: Attack 4, Defense 3, Versatility 50%
