An quick word on prevention. If you are in cruise, always make sure that your scanner is set for ships that way you can spot the bastages as they close on you. As soon as you see them closing on you, switch to mouse flight and just turn on a 45 degree angle from them. The angle and speed will make it almost impossible to intercept. Wave bye bye as they drop off your scanners.

Also, if you kwow that they are near while in cruise listen for the shot or the incoming missle and keep a finger on the C button. drop two counter measuares in quick succession. {Hey two c meausures are alot cheaper the batts, nanos, hull, ECT}. For some reason two just work better especially againts the dreaded hornet missle.

If you do get disrupted the power slide maneuver that runs through this thread works best of all. However, if you are near a tradelane. Jinking across space and performing a combat dock will get you out of trouble faster then trying to go into cruise. One thing that can help you out is to activate your countermeasure dropper in your weapons list in the lower right corner. If its active it will automatically drop countermeasures when needed. Disrupters can still get through if the enemy is RIGHT behind you though. Also make sure to unselect it when you go to fight otherwise you'll drop a countermeasure every time you fire. This works best whenever you are already at cruise and you just want to flip the "ignore enemies" switch.
