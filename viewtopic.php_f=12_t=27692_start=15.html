Yeah Mustang, the charecter models suck. Thankfully once you finish the campaign, or decide not to play it (the campaign is like 10 percent of the game) you never have to see him again!

Let me give you a few little tips: Hunt pirates (shoot until they eject then order it to dock at a shipyard and sell) or trade Energy in the wall until you have just enough money to buy a factory and ship(s) to support it. You wont be raking in any credits until you get a factory up. Your first factory should be a soler power plant in Kingdoms End or Lucky Planets or somewhere like that.
