The Coveted lvl38

Just a thought, but a valid one I think nonetheless:

Why is attaining lvl38 so easy in Freelancer? Am I the only one here who thinks that Dig. Anvil should reconsider the criteria (ie money count) that allows the player (especially in MP) to gain lvl38 status?

My mind wanders over to the early days of Diablo 2 Classic where there was little by way of cheating and duping (or in FL terms, mods and hacks). getting to lvl99 back in those days was a coveted thing...very few people had it, and everyone wanted to be there. FL hardcore players can safely attain lvl38 in only a few hours these days, and even the average player who pays attention can reach that level in 6-8 hours easily.

I just wondered if I was alone on this one, as it would be nice for high level characters to actually mean something.
