ATTN: nVidia video card owners

Download the new Detonator 43.00 drivers. I have a GF4 Ti 4400, and they markedly increased the image quality (especially the background space stuff). It's not just me, though; I know of at least 3 other people that have noticed a similar improvement.
Also, enable Digital Vibrance (move from "off" to "low". That makes everything, both the 2D desktop and 3D games, look a lot more colorful. To find that option, do the following:
-right-click on desktop, and click properties
-click the settings tab, and click the advanced button
-click the GeForce tab, and click the nView list item on the left
-click the "Device Settings" drop-down button, and click "Color Correction"
-Drag the Digital Vibrance slider from "off" to "low" or "medium", and watch your desktop background instantly and noticeably look better

Have fun

[edit Here's the download link: http://www.majorgeeks.com/article.php?sid=3294&cat=28
I also found out that they aren't beta; they're actually official drivers from the PNY site, that aren't on the nVidia site for some reason
[/edit
