*** ALL Rebalance Mod FANS *** READ : Massive Addon/Fix for

Hey all you Rebalance fans, this is Ro9ue, a fellow FL-addict and semi-experienced modder. Though not many people know, I helped a lot on making 3.5 (not bragging, just giving background). ArchKaine and I made the "Aten Add-on" for Rebalance 3.4 (( available here )), and we implemented many of those things in 3.5. However, (not to bash anyone here, just stating the straight up truth , many of the kickass changes and additions were chopped way down, removed, or limited in 3.5. And not only that, but I agree whole-heartedly with what's been flying around lately - all the complaints about the simply retarded engine-kill problem, bonus ships, etc - so I want to let all you guys know about something we're cooking up.

ArchKaine and I are currently working on an "Aten II Add-on" mod for Rebal 3.5. We are going to go back and fix things, add some new things, and try to make Rebal 3.5 the mod it was meant to be: the best one out there to date (like it was during our closed beta testing).

Please post here things you would like fixed. We'll do our best to whip up a nice addon patch to hopefully restore some of Rebalance's previous shine.

And one more thing of note: I am a high school student (currently "studying" as I type ), so I cannot mod 24/7 like I could in the summer. ArchKaine's internet is prone to occasional whacky cut-outs. So while it might seem like this is hopeless :p it really isn't. We just have to work our FL modding into busy schedules, but it IS a major focus for us. So this addon won't pop out in the next week, but it should be ready to go in the next 3 weeks or so.

This is a small (but growing) list of currently fixed problems and added things:
- Engine kill problem fixed for all ships (big w00t, eh? );
- Raptor's cockpit fixed (Predator and Phoenix in progress);
- Tweaks in loadouts for elite ships (difficulty 15-19, just for added kicks);
- The Wrath VHF for The Order (Orillion's personal fighter; deadliest and most advanced fighter designed in Sirius to date. Consequently very rare and high-value piece o' equip )

So please post any ideas, requests, or questions in here, and we'll all try to build Rebal back up to its former whoopaz level that MD intended

Just dropping a note to say how much I and Ro9ue appreciate your input, I'm still hacking away at the Wrath, which has now diversified into two seperate versions, and I've also done a few fun things to the Nightshade MkII (brought it up to spec), they'll be available in the addon, once and IF I can get my internet back to a working status.

For those who are wondering... the Wrath, in its two incarnations are going to fly a bit like the NS2, but it won't be as maneuverable, since it's a heavier fighter. Also, it's going to have FULL hyperdrive capability, not some half-assed mockup. I'm having texturing trouble right now, partly because my Milkshape went belly-up (crappy software, I swear). The textures won't export properly to ms3d format from 3dsmax, though I have the max2ms3d plugin for 3dsmax 6. I may have to totally retexture and try again, I dunno. I hope not though, took a long time to get it looking that good.

Well, a few new things in tohoku that I've been playing with... Planet Atum has a ring, and it doesn't cause crashes .... Also, it's got a partially functional docking ring, I haven't gone in and done missions or zones, since those are more Ro9ue's areas of expertise. My specialty is ship design You can purchase the NS2, and the Sobek there, and I've left room for the Wrath when it's completed. That will probably be one of only two places in the game that you're going to be able to purchase the Wrath... The other one being... You guessed it... The Battleship Osiris, there'll be one version at each location, though I haven't decided which to put where... My instinct says to put the Shadow Wrath on Atum, and the Orillion's Wrath on BS Osiris, sound ok to you all? If you're wondering what the differences are between those two variants of the Wrath, you'll just have to wait and see for yourself

Oh, btw, all the modifications that I have made so far to my game files have yet to require me to restart the campaign, I hate that too... the only thing that slightly worries me is when Ro9ue and I go and incorporate our seperate changes... Ro9ue and I will do everything we can to maintain compatibility with rebalance 3.5, while updating things and fixing others.
