The Vulture 2 (class 5) is 2nd in hull damage effeciency: 6.192 (161 damage at 26 energy [3 shots/sec). Maximum hull damage belongs to Diamondbacks and Cerebrus (which have identical stats).
Heck - you can even break it down into damage against types of shields... you just have to match your loadout with your style of fighting. If you're a sucky shot and keep firing, you don't want a Cerebrus because it will drain your battery at 2 shots/second. Sunblast A (class 6) gives you 8 shots/second with 79 hull damage/shot at 19 energy drain. That's a possible 658.07 hulldamage/second draining 158.27 energy... and 8 chances a second to hit. The Nomad Blaster gives you 4 shots/second with a max possible 2540 hull damage/second - but zero energy drain...
