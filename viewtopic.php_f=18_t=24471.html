Phezzan

Sun Sep 21, 2003 1:57 pm by Phezzan

If you want to hunt around you will find that the CRUISING_SPEED
is a universal constant that can be changed in constants.ini

No matter what speed you set it to - the dial will show 300
your actual speed will not be shown while the Cruise engines are engaged.

There is _no_way_ to make different engines / ships have different cruise speeds.

watch out for asteriods.
