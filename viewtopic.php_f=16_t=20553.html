Gung_Ho

Tue Jul 01, 2003 4:15 pm by Gung_Ho

I haven't used standard engines in months...

I remapped my increase/decrease speed (W and S) to afterburners/reverse. those two, and strafe will not break you out of engine kill mode. I mapped Tab as my engine kill button. Everytime I leave a base, come out of cruise, or whatever, I kill my engines, and start flying on afterburner (just a quick puff here and there will keep you at 200 speed indefinately). I only use the decrease speed button occasionally (i remapped it to X) and even then only when I want to come to an absolute stop of 0 speed.

In combat vs players, it makes you harder to hit, due to the sudden erratic changes of direction that are typical of flight in EK mode. However, I find the AI actually hits you a little easier. Nonetheless, I still use EK mode vs the AI, if only for the practice. It's not like they hurt you much anyway.

EDIT: Also, using EK mode when in cruise will keep you going at 300 speed for a period of time. It's a great way to get away from AI, as disrupters can't break you out of 300 speed, when you're only drifting..
