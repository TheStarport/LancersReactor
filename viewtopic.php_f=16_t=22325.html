Hi everybody !

In reading all the messages in this forum, i had an idea about the order.

Do you know Isaac Asimoov, the writter of Foundation, Second Foundation, Foundation and Empire, The robots, etc... There are very good SciFi stories. In these stories, there is a protector of mankind (like the order say to be), and this protector is a robot called Daniels.
But nobody know it. This is a secret.

When i look at the Sirius sector, what can i see ? Just agressive humans in fight. Fight for business, for control, for money, etc... Mankind seem to be an expert in destruction. And, all humans in Sirius sector are just settlers from our solar system. Why are they in Sirius ? Because there was a war between humans for planet Earth (this is what the intro say). And the intro say too, "We will never forget" !

So my point is : Human is unable to protect himself from himself. The only protector must be an non-human. A robot, or a robot race (programmed for it) for example.

I have read in a message that when you have no allies and every faction is your enemy, planet Gammu (robots, and a look similar to Toledo Order planet) become unlocked.

So what is happening when everybody is your friend, when the indicator is maximum green for every faction ? Maybe Monkey planet become unlocked ? I will try it. I dont have any real idea about monkeys for now, except : monkey imitate human !
