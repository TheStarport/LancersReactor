bakedpotato

Wed Dec 08, 2004 2:03 pm by bakedpotato

Just wondering but when you say play Multiplayer games alone...
Do you mean can you play Mutliplayer on your computer by yourself...
If that's your question then the answer is yes you can but I do believe that you need a network card or integrated on the motherboard in your computer for this to work.
All my comps have network cards installed so the reason I believe that you need it is Finalday tried to run flserver.exe but could not because he didn't have a network card installed...

Start flserver.exe which comes on the Freelancer disk and installs when you install Freelancer...you may have noticed that when you installed Freelancer.
The Freelancer Mod Manager can start Flserver.exe for you or make a shortcut to Flserver.exe on your desktop and start it from there before you start the game.

The default location for flserver.exe is:
C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer\EXE
in other words it is in the exe folder a sub folder of the Freelancer directory (folder)
