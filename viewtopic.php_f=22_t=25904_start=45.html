Roleplaying Rules:


Roleplaying or not is a choice every player is entitled to make for himself when he creates his character. If you choose to roleplay you need to create a tagged character.

Tagged Characters

Create an occupation or a clan tagged character.

The following or any other occupation tags can be used as well as any clan tag decided upon by your clan. If you are using some other occupation tag report it to the admin so it can be added here.

All occupation tags will be sorted into one of the following tag categories.

Law Abiding Tags (examples)
[TR = Trader
[MN = Miner
[SS = Secret Service Agent
[PA = Police Agent
[BM = Businessman
[PT = Politician

Inconclusive Tags (examples)
[BH = Bounty Hunter
[SF = Soldier of Fortune
[MR = Mercenary
[CS = Crime Syndicate Member

Criminal Tags (examples)
[SC = Small Criminal
[SM = Smuggler
[PR = Pirate
[SK = Serial Killer
[AS = Assassin

A clan tag is determined by the clan.

All clans are reqired to declare themselves as a clan belonging to one of the above tag categories. They must also state an occupation of the clan. Multiple occupations allowed. Maximum 2.

Characters wearing Criminal or Inconclusive tags aren’t allowed to engage anyone under level 20 inside the original FL version systems. Expanded systems are an adventure. Enter at your own risk regardless of your level.

All Characters wearing Law abiding tags can engage anyone with a Criminal tag any time anywhere regardless of their level. Except in New York.

All characters wearing inconclusive tags can be engaged by all other taged characters if provocation is given.

All allowable engagement is subject to player interaction. If someone submits it isn’t advisable to kill them. Set your loot or fines at a reasonable level or you will never get paid. We suggest maximum 300k for fighters and 800k for freighters.

All Characters wearing Law-abiding tags are asked to be of assistance to all new characters wearing Law-abiding tags and all non-tagged new characters.

Characters wearing Criminal tags can help new characters wearing Criminal tags if they wish to do so.

No one helps new characters with Inconclusive tags. Everyone is suspicious of them.


Tag-less Freelancer Characters

Tag-less Freelancers can engage in RP activities only if they team up with one or more tagged characters. When doing so they accept the full RP consequences of such actions.

Single tag-less Freelancers when not in a group with tagged characters can only do missions, trade, mine or duel with others if they both agree. Untagged players could also join groups with traders so they can act as escorts for a fee.

Tag-less Freelancers can ask Law-abiding tagged characters for backup anytime

Tag-less Freelancers, regrdless of their level, with any contraband in their holds are considered smugglers and can be engaged by Law abiding tagged characters.

Contraband items are as follows:
Cardinmine
Artefacts
Slaves
Child slaves
Assins
Refugees
Absynth
Tobacco.


General to all of the above

All tagged characters activity has to be in the spirit of the occupation they have chosen. For example if the smuggler is killed by a [PA tagged character, the [PA character then should also shoot and destroy the contraband.

Ignorance or disregard of the above rules will result in warnings and or fines.

The admin's decision is final.
