Hey RedFox

What's really IMPORTANT, is to start with a fresh, clean load of FreeLancer. If I want to save my current version I rename it to something else, Freelancer.xxxx. Then do a reload of the game. If you have been playing on line, DO NOT uninstall, it will change your MP ID. If you don't have the room on your hard drive, just delete all of the files in the freelancer directory.

Then Load the SDK 1.3. I havn't had any trouble with the non advanced 1.4 patch.

Then activate your mod. You may find that ModManager still says that the mod is still active. If so under tools, run the restore backup option. That will clear the field and you're good to go.

If you did want to save your current game, don't forget to move your saved games to a secure place. You'll find them under my docunents/my games/freelancer/accts/singleplayer.

I hope this helped.

BTW, some of the newer mods don't play well under WIN 98. I had to switch to XP
