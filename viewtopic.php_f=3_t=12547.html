Good PVP Config

After my experience in PVP'ing on several servers i have ultimately concluded this to be the best PVP configuration.

Level 10 Ship: Eagle
Level 10 Guns: 3 Nomad Blasters
Level 9 Guns: 2 Tizona Del Cids
Level 10/9 Missle: Cannonball Missle (can be found on Crete)
Level 10 Shields: Adv HF Brigantine
Level 10 Cargo: HEHE J/K
Cruise Disruptor: w/e will do
Mines: Ripper is a must

Ok heres the most advanced PVP setup ive ever used. Why?
Contrary to popular belief the real damage inducers are the level 9 Tizona Del Cids and the Cannonball Missle. The Nomad Guns are their for the ride as well, but they dont do the 1.2k Shield damage of the Del Cid or the 4.5k Hull damage of the Cannonball, they sorta do both, which is why they are a MUST. Ok most of you are prolly like, WTF? At this point. Let me brag then explain.

I have taken out another fully decked out Level 10 Eagle and Sabre AND Titan in under 8 seconds with this setup. Tizonas doing 1.2k Shield damage each, Del Cids and Nomad guns take out the shields in about 3-4 rounds. Once thats done with all it takes is a shot or two and ONE CANNONBALL, YES THATS RIGHT ONE, and BOOM GAME OVER. Thing is for cannonball u gotta get close to em, which usually isnt a problem in most duels ive participated in. Once their shields are down, (TY Del Cids), just get up close and throw a cannonball, it almost never misses close up, hasnt for me at least. With this setup u can own most anyone.
Best thing is all the guns are in synch with the same 600 M/S AND 4.00 Refire Rate, making ur aim elite
