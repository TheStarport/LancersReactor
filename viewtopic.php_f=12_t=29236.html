Flatspace (Indie space game)

http://www.lostinflatspace.com/

I got a chance to try this game out for an hour or so last night, and it's really cool. You can choose different careers, like trader, bounty hunter, pirate, policeman. You can use the standard rules and galaxy map, or you can randomize the map, change the number of stations and asteroids, and so on. It reminds me a good mix between the graphical prettiness and mouse control of Freelancer, with the depth and longevity of Escape Velocity Nova. Definately worth checking out if you're into this sort of thing
