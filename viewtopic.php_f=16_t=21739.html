I must disagree with Lutz here - you cannot utilize the advantages of a heavy fighter unless you have full use of both hands. The left hand needs to switch weapon groups and/or fire missiles/torps, and the bad manoeuvrability of the HF means they cannot fight well unless you use afterburners and X (brake and reverse) liberally in order to track enemies and also to dodge enemy fire. You can fly a light fighter pretty well just with the mouse alone (movement + guns), especially the Drake. Afterburners are always a must but a mouse with an extra button can help there.

For the Hovis race this does not matter since you only need to activate the cruise engines once and the rest is just steering with the mouse.
