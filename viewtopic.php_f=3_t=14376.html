Thu Apr 17, 2003 7:29 pm by Sudden Death

The (Civillian) Advanced Debilitator level 8 pulse turret is about as good as it gets for sheild killing. It's got 700m range, 700m/s speed, 4 refire rate, 795 sheild damage, 23 hull damage, and 47 power used. Theres also a Corsair version called the Rapier Turret. The numbers could be wrong, though, because the strategy guide I'm reading from is full of errors.
