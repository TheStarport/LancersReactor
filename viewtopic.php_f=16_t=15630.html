Lone_wolf

Thu May 01, 2003 8:50 pm by Lone_wolf

Paksman,

The reason why Mactan fired on you, or and Hacker turns hostile when you're rep with them is neutral, is due to three reasons:

1) You fire on one of their ships/bases enough to piss them off.

2) You refuse to surrender cargo.

3) You fly near an Outcast. Rogues, Hackers, and Outcasts have strong ties. If you're neutral with two of them (Rogues and Hackers), but still hostile with Hackers, the Rogues and Hackers will turn hostile whenever an Outcast ship comes within range.

This also happens with the Bundshuh and Unioners - if you're neutral with one but hostile with the other, both will turn hostile when you encounter them at the same time.

The jumpholes in Magellan and Mactan base is regularly patroled and visited by the Outcasts, so chances are you'll face an angry reception unless you fix your rep with the Outcasts, too.
