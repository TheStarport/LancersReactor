Patriot is easier than the Dagger is, IMHO, being more maneuverable. Since there isn't too much armor difference(1300-1800...not that much when higher level weapons are shooting you), but the ship is much more agile. Same with the Starflier, only that is somewhat more difficult due to its lack of anything resembling firepower.

That Falcon of yours should be more than plenty to tackle 200k missions. Just remember to let your sheilds recharge on their own when you can; they come back up at around 20% to start, so usng shield batts to recharge from that point is a lot less costly than from a fully downed shield. Just go completely evasive any time you lose shields.
