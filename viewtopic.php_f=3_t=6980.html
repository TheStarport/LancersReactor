To me it does have a radar and I use it a lot! The box on the bottom left side of your cockpit shows a list of the items your ship's radar has detected, such as rogues will be red letters and it will show you the distance they are from you. White lettered items are things you can tactor into your cargo hold. And the arrows will show you the direction the item is from your ship. What esle would you want a radar to show you? It really works better than the old radars you saw in Privateer and WC games. The interface in Freelancer was confusing for me at first..but the more I play it...the more it makes sense and it does work extremely well. DA done a fantastic job with the interface!
