How to delete the Intro screens

If you look in your freelancer.ini file you'll spot this:

[Startup
;movie_file = movies\MGS_Logo_Final.wmv;
;movie_file = movies\DA_Logo_Final.wmv;
;movie_file = movies\FL_Intro.wmv;

Deleting that whole section, or making it into a comment (Thanks SuPaFrEaK) will just load up to the selection screen.

SuPaFrEaK

Sat Mar 15, 2003 12:41 am by SuPaFrEaK

This doesnt affect multiplayer in any way... And instead of deleting the section, just comment it out by placing ;;; in front of each line. That way, if you want to restore the vids later, you can just delete the semi-colons

For example:

[Startup
;;;movie_file = movies\MGS_Logo_Final.wmv
;;;movie_file = movies\DA_Logo_Final.wmv
;;;movie_file = movies\FL_Intro.wmv
