Newsletter:

The new "Soldier" class of escort fighters is complete. These fighters are based loosely on the old Liberty Defender design, but have been upgraded to match the statistics of a Very Heavy Fighter.

The Origin entered New Columbia this morning, and performed the first test use of the Genesis Ray on the system's second planet.

Bridge, ATS Origin, Near Planet Fairfax, New Columbia System.

The engineers looked over their consoles, going through the motions they had been training for.

"Cartography analysis, complete. Uploading map and co-ordinates to computer."

"Jump calibration stabilized."

"Transport links are secured and stable."

"Signal from Eden Station, loud and clear."

"Atmospheric conditions optimal. Generator set to compensate for nutrient cycles."

"Eden reports materials match specs. We're ready to go."

The captain turned to the communications relay, displaying the face of Samuel Regin. He smiled. "All systems go. Awating your orders, sir."

"Fire the Genesis Ray."

The control officer threw the switch.

Space, Outside Eden Station, Near Planet Cambridge, Cambridge System

The Planetform techs waited silently on their cargo cruisers, watching the ring above them. When the blue light of a Jump Gate appeared in the center, they moved as one.

Twenty-five cruisers immediately activated a link to the Gate, sending their materials through, ready to be harvested on the other side.

Bride, ATS Origin, Near Planet Fairfax, New Columbia System

Blue lights shot out from around the bridge, shimmering across the planet like waves. Where they passed, they left behind the telltale green and blue of a terrestrial world. The waves moved, slowly surrounding the planet until the entire sphere was engulfed in a kaleidoscope of light.

The light faded. Before them, where once had been a bleak and desolate rock, there now lay a green, fertile world.

"Objective accomplished, sir."
