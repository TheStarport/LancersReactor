You'll probably have to start FL from the beginning (if single player campaign) for the mod to work right. I installed this mod fresh by (like it said to do) unistall and DELETE the FL folder, re-install, then activate mod in mod manager. 100% stable is this mod I can't tell you how happy I am finally able to play a modded FL without crashing. I liked a lot of the things in other mods and wish there was more system mods in TNG but the no crashing is a breath of fresh air. Good job to the modders on this one. BTW, I started this mod and activated the open SP add-on and was concerned before I began playing that it would take too long to acquire enough in a fast enough time to really enjoy it. Little did I know that the loot in this mod can be very profitable. I highly recommend it. Although seeing Trent in a suit (trader costume mod) was a little ackward.
