Balefire

Wed Apr 09, 2003 3:46 am by Balefire

It's a matter of taste which freighter you use, I guess; each seems to have its fans. I've tried them all, though, and wouldn't consider changing from a Dromedary to another freighter. It has the largest hold space and is more maeuverable than the others, looks better--IMO, of course--and can put up a better fight if necessary, although it can't take as much punishment as the Humpback. I didn't find the Drone significantly more maneuverable than the Dromedary, and the extra hold space makes for a more favorable tradeoff, IMO. I don't recall how the two compare firepower-wise, but I went back to the Dromedary after first flying it, then the other freighters.

I think it really comes down to how you prefer playing. I'm playing an aggressive trader/explorer with the Dromedary, and I think it's better fitted for that role than the other freighters are. If I were willing to go into cruise and run every time I were accosted, or use the turrets while beating a "strategic retreat", I might prefer either the Humpback or the Drone; I personally find the Drone ugly, though, and think the Humpback has better survivability in that style of play. Since I'd rather respond to "drop your cargo" demands with gunfire, and only retreat from really overwhelming odds, I much prefer the Dromedary. YMMV.

It's certainly easier to acquire the Drone, reputation-wise. You might want to try the Drone out but keep your reputation good with the Lane Hackers, so you can trade the Drone in on the Dromedary again, just in case.
