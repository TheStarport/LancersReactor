parabolix

Sun Jul 02, 2006 5:23 am by parabolix

Harmony, we realize starting off in Epsilon is difficult...But here are some tips...

1.) Buy a Patriot. - It costs about $200 to upgrade if you keep the equipment you had and dont buy the equipment that comes with the Patriot. The Patriot is also faster and has better weapons.

2.) Loot wrecks. - While it may be dangerous, there is a good amount of wrecks right around where you start.

3.) Change those weapons. - Don't keep those Justices. You can find some good guns close to Manhattan at Rochester Base or Trenton. Don't buy guns that are too powerful though, or your power supply won't support them easily.
