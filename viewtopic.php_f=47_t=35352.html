Hunter 2 (exploring Freelancers underworld):

Chapter 1:

Freedom, it was gone at the turn of the 11th century, Rhienland Waged war on Kusari, Bretonia attack Liberty, Liberty allied with Rhienland. After 20 years of war, few were left to fight and so the war just ended. The strongest formed clans and took over the Government, in 1067 AE A(after earth) the houses were rebuilt but government policy was changed. But a brave group of ‘super humans’ better called the Elite of the Elite. Enhanced beyond any human they were a sub-race of humanity. They attacked new Berlin system, with massive warships, and took the System. But the surviving navy of Rhienland destroyed the jump-gates and collapsed the jump-holes. But in a odd coincidence a shipment of gasses and explosives crashed into each-other and a ‘off’ jump-hole was born. People went through but never came back. They the hunters realized that using a set amount of chemicals they could make the jump-hole go to any system they wanted….

Speed up about 30 years 1109 AE and if you go to New Berlin and enter a bar that’s were you will find me. Who am I? A hunter, in fact I am captain of a 8 man ship, proud father of two 9 year old kids already enrolled in the finest schools. Who am I? I am one of those who was on the legendary raid to New York, Liberty. I am a hero among my people, when ever some one sees my ship ‘Dawn’ they flee for there lives. My name? Herrian Watts Third House Line of Arion Watts. Some may know that name, some scoff and say it’s a myth. The hero who saved our land from certain death, a boy who started fighting at 14 and won the greatest war at age 16. But the greatest Thing I stole was his personality. After the war Arion recorded his personality into a hologram of him. It has a memory bank so it knows who we are, no one can figure out how he did it, but we think it was a piece of alien technology.

But I have something a bit less then that, but to others a bit more then that. I have his ship, I never let it leave the hangar. Oh did I mention our families space station, that’s right we own a small space station that’s were Arion’s fighter is. My kids love it there, something about 0 gravity fighting that they love. My family is one of the greatest houses in the Hunter empire, we have great money and some of the greatest warriors. I was even offered to be a captain of a 2000 crew warship but I said no, I prefer a few men you can rely on. At 14 my sons will enter the army and will server aboard the Dawn. The following will be my exploits in the following war we are about to wage
