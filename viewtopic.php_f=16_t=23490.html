Well, that is what it looked like, but I did not know total sales. Guess I'll have to ask around.

I know Wing Commander Prophecy sold 700k units -- but Origin had decided to drop all of the projects for multiplayer on-line stuff and was dropping the Wing Commander line. The game designers basically put the expansion pack (#1 of two) on the web for free as their last act out of the door and Origin eventually packaged it in as Prophecy Gold. I suspect Prophecy would have sold more if the plot cut scenes had all been done, rather than dropped at one third of the plot.

Since then, the general market wisdom is that space sims are dead. Starlancer did nothing to counterdict that, but Starlancer had its own problems.

Freelancer is a strong game, but ...


It needs an autopilot (or equivilent) -- I know, the game designers wanted you to enjoy their world and appreciate their design work and ideas, but ...

It needs an esc by-pass for the cut-scenes, including the jump gates, etc.

Ideally, a sequel, addition/scenario pack would add both.

It also wouldn't hurt if the weapons and equipment, were a bit more differentiated, more like Privateer.

I'll have to give it more thought, but if they wanted a game that was more accessible ...
