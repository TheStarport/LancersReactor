Death Penalty: Too Low?

Does anybody else believe the death penalty is too low? At the moment, an increasingly large number of cheap "Get rich quick" strategies or faction-buildling tricks involve suiciding at some point. Any ideas on how to increase it? I was thinking it would be nice if, on death, players were fined cash for the value of their ship.
