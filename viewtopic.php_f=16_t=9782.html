curath

Sat Mar 15, 2003 2:31 am by curath

During the SP missions just about everyone is at least neutral to you.

During 'free' play neutral pirates may attack you, depending on how big a wad of money you're waving in front of their noses (meaning: value of hauled cargo. Empty cargobay = no hostility).

In MP I managed to get into an Outcast base when I had done a few bounty hunter-killing jobs for the Corsairs (nobody likes bountyhunters, though all pirates like them dead) and the Outcasts faction bar was a bit under 5 blocks on the negative side.

Of course they'll refuse to sell almost anything, but it's easy to get a bribe in for the faction who's base you're in. If no bribee around, exit base and re-enter to shuffle the bar attendees.
