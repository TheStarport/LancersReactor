Team Speak server is running! I am now hosting a Team Speak server, so if you want to use it, connect to this IP: 65.19.75.30:8767 . For those of you who are new to Team Speak, follow these instructions carefully.

Under File, select Connect.


Fill in the fields that are marked. The information that is in the screenshot you see is already the correct one's. Hit the connect button.


You will hear a voice saying "Link engaged", this means you were able to successfully connect to the server. Now under the menu Self, select Register With Server.


Fill in a username, and a password (twice) and click OK. You are now ready to use the server. Don't forget to configure your Team Speak so you can use it correctly while playing. Have fun!
