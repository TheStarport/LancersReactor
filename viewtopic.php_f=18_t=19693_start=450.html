I am affraid that i don't know what their ships are called in the files. Howver - it is in the goods.ini file. In here you can find ship "packages" and so on. When you find the correct one you will see somethign like this (this is an example from the normal game)

[Good
nickname = bfr_package
category = ship
hull = bfr_hull
addon = ge_bfr_engine_01, internal, 1
addon = br_freighter_power01, internal, 1
addon = ge_s_scanner_01, internal, 1
addon = ge_s_tractor_01, internal, 1
addon = shield01_mark03_fr, HpShield01, 1
addon = LargeWhiteSpecial, HpHeadlight, 1
addon = SlowSmallYellow, HpRunningLight01, 1
addon = SlowSmallYellow, HpRunningLight02, 1
addon = SlowSmallYellow, HpRunningLight03, 1
addon = SlowSmallYellow, HpRunningLight04, 1
addon = SlowSmallYellow, HpRunningLight05, 1
addon = SlowSmallYellow, HpRunningLight06, 1
addon = SlowSmallYellow, HpRunningLight07, 1
addon = SlowSmallYellow, HpRunningLight08, 1
addon = contrail01, HpContrail01, 1
addon = contrail01, HpContrail02, 1
addon = DockingLightRedSmall, HpDockLight01, 1
addon = DockingLightRedSmall, HpDockLight02, 1

Of course yours would say TIE or something. Anyways - these don't come with guns, but if they do, they will have the lines of
addon = li_gun01_mark02, HpWeapon01, 1

And things like that. You can change the nickname to another gun instead. of course - i hear that TNG have encryption that means you cannot even find the info in their files - and no, i have no idea how they did it.....and i don't care either.
