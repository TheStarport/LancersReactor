those planet names were test names, and so were some vessels. Avalon was changed to New London, but i'd never heard of the Bismarck. Battleship Westfalen in Hamburg however, is the last Rheinlander Battleship, and it's of Bismarck Class.

The names were used at the E3 previews, as well as the starlancer-like cockpit HUD design. Of course, media was released during the E3 periods that TLR collected and hosted.

The systems on the mainmenu are inaccesible without modifying, and still they have basically no environment except for what you see. Ships moving through these intro screens are created via .thn (thorne) files, and require complex modding to create.
