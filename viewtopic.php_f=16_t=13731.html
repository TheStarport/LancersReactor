Nightsilver

Sun Apr 13, 2003 4:20 pm by Nightsilver

The loadout for MP changes based on what you're doing...for instance, I've never seen a guy in a Titan manage to take out an Eagle if the Eagle manages to start circling around the Titan...you simply can't turn fast enough in the Titan to keep up with the Eagle, and if you break the loop, the Eagle will just follow you.

Anyways, my suggested loadout for PVP is:
Eagle:
4x Nomad Energy Cannons/Blasters (they do about the same damage per second - cannons do 10 more)
1x Salamanca II (can be replaced with a second Tizona if you choose)
1x Tizona del Cid
1x Salamanca Turret (not necessary, since it fires backward on the Eagle)
Ripper Minelayer
Hornet Cruise Disruptor (you can use a Sunslayer, but you'll probably never hit anything..)
Adv. Countermeasure Dropper
Cl10 Adv. Brigandine HF Shield
Adv. Thruster

If you're going against computer fighters, then I suggest a bit of a change. Remove the Salamanca II and put in a Cannonball Missile. Computers seem too stupid to drop flares, and Missiles are helpful when you come against one of those enemies you just can't seem to hit.
