Blaster Universe

Blaster Universe is the project of a young talented friend which always wanted to create his own mod. So Blaster (you might know him from the lancerradio project) came to me asking for help. That was the birth of the Blaster Universe mod. Blasters ideas how his mod should look like are sometimes unconventional but still very interesting. In the end he created (with a bit advice and buxfixing from my side) a nice mod which has a pretty good potential.
Unfortunatly Blaster wasnt able to finish his mod so there wasnt any development on it for about one year.
Now Blaster is back and a new concept has been created to make this mod bigger, better, simply incredible.

Blaster Universe Story:

Liberty, at war with the Outcasts, it was predicted that this would happen. The Outcasts were appearing in Liberty space, anonymously. No one knew how, but Liberty Intelligence spotted a jumpgate in Bering. A complete new Area of sirius that was a secret home to the Outcasts. So many systems hidden throughout the Universe. Liberty were amazed, and to this day fight for the systems and for victory.
