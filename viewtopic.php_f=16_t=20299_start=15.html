It is strange that they made the Krakens 700/700 speed/range. The other particle guns of the Outcasts have 600/600 while the tachyon kit has 700/700; so you have to make a choice between the better damage/efficiency of the particle guns or the better targetting characteristics of the tachyon guns. IOW, a classic tradeoff.

The Krakens combine the best of both so why would anyone want to buy the Wyrms? The particle guns have a penalty against molecular shields but their higher damage compensates that to some degree and if you have shield busters the point becomes moot anyway. In fact, if the Krakens were 600/600 they would match the Tizonas perfectly.
