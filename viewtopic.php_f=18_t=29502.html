Umm, okay, I've never posted here before, so forgive me if I sound ..... unproffesional. Anyway ........

In the Freelancer Spoiler forum, I recently started up a thread about the "perfect" base for Freelancers. I said that maybe you , as modders, would be interested in people's preferances, so maybe you could put some of the bases mentioned in future mods. The link to the page :

The Perfect Base Thread - By Neo_Kuja

Some quotations while you wait for it to load :

Darkstone - Tekagi's Arch, a short distance above Malta, with maximum security, an average population, open to all. Great architecture and cardamine on the doorstep


Bounty Hunter Jackson - Hmm... a large, heavily-armed 'battlestation' newly built in one of the Unknown systems would be fun. I'll say a Bounty Hunter base because I'm biased :p -- and I think they need a base close to the enemy, but hidden from them. Many Bounty Hunter patrols too -- see how well the AI Bounty Hunters do at engaging the Nomads. Obviously, it'd have to have biodomes because it's not likely to get much in the way of freight, so it'd be growing its own food. I'd put it in the empty Unknown system, just so there's something... there.


Cowami - I would say a Liberty battleship hijacked by Outcasts then completely rearmed with Outcast turrets and defended by heavily armed Outcast Sabres.


Enjoy

NOTE : I did not "promise" you would make the bases, but said I would only repost it here to show you modders peoples prefered bases.
