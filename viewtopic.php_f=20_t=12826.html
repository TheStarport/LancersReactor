- I was going thru a mine field hunting for a ship wreck that was hinted to be in the area. I saw 5 mines and waht looked to be a ship in the middle of them. I moved in carefully and had one explosion as I entered. I fired at the wreck andno effect??? As i went to fire again my ship explodes! I was like WTF!

- I had the Titan Very Heavy Fighter with 2 sets of the Lvl 10 "salvaged" guns on it. They do 1000 damage each, so I fire them all it is 4000 damage!!! I shot a low level ship with like 1000 points it will fly for like 20 meters and then explode after I shot it. It is freaky cause some are still shooting at you after you have supposedly 'killed" them.

What I figure is that like in the Mech Warrior 3 game that Microsoft made they have screwed up the damage system in multiplayer.
