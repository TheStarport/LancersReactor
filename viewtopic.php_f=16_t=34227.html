Megistos

Thu Aug 26, 2004 1:24 am by Megistos

The best missions are on Malta and Crete because that's where the hardest enemies are. It makes sense:

Leaving aside the whole Rheinland>Kusari>Bretonia>Liberty hierarchy, the further you go outside of colonised space, the tougher the pirates become, because you're closer to their big bases. In addition, there are more hazards in the Edge Worlds than in the Houses, so you need a tougher ship to survive those. (Think Omega 11 and 41). So if the pirates have better ships, it follows that the factions warring with them in the edge worlds (GMG and BHG) have good ships as well to compete with them. Thus the missions are harder and have higher payouts. Think about it - blowing up 6 Pirahnas is a lot easier than taking down 6 Hammerheads.
