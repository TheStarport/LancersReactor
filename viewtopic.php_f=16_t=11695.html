Yeah, the jumphole to Dresden in Frankfurt has a ring around it like a planetary ring... which makes finding it a lot easier, because otherwise it'd be just sitting out there in open space. But, that doesn't look like a black hole to me...

The "thing" in Omega-3 is easily overlooked because it's in an ice field that obscures your vision. It's in the middle of a clear area inside the field; and I've seen this hole-in-the-clouds effect in other places, but the weird object that is in the center of it is unique... at least I've never seen it anywhere else. You get radiation at point blank range to this anomaly (as I like to call it), and it isn't a selectable object, so I don't think it's an alternate form of jumphole. If you look at the Graham Ice Field on the map with the mineable area highlight turned on, you'll see a round area that's darker than the rest of the cloud, indicating the mining there isn't as good; but it's an easy way to scope-in on this open area so you don't get lost in the fog.
