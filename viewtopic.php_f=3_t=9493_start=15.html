Thu Mar 13, 2003 3:36 am by Bullethead

FL is like most RPGs in that you start out low-level and become high-level, with better gear and all that. As player capabilities increase, the baddies' capabilities have to increase as well to keep challenging the player. So the game world has to have the full spectrum of places that range from easy to hard, which the player is expected to move through in order.

This is why Liberty ships and gear are weak compared to other places. The SP game starts in Libery, so that's the "newbie" area with the weak stuff.
