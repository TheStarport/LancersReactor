Crash-Drive

Thu Mar 27, 2003 8:23 pm by Crash-Drive

Don't fire a full salvo at wrecks, as you might damage, if not destroy, some of the stuff coming off of it -- just using one gun blast is sufficient. However, if you see the guns popping off in flames, that means the wreck has already been looted. In an MP game, someone else got there first. In SP, well, you must have looted it and forgot about it. Anyway, the stuff will respawn after awhile (some people say a week, but I'm not so sure about that), or when the server is restarted in an MP game. The Blue Blaze are pretty good, but the Wild Fire are even better; good luck!
