Because its not an expansion, its a mod.

You aren't expanding the game at all, just changing a few things about it - or modifying it

Expansion not only sounds offical, it is what games companies call their expansions for games. It would confuse people and more - just like no mod is called "Freelancer 2: blah blah" - because its not really an official sequel - its someones mod.

Like it has been said before - Microsoft is being tolerant of us modders, letting us mod the game without ANY interference at all into this. They are aware of what we are up to, and as long as we don't step over the bounds/take the piss - then they are more than happy for us to continue. However, they might see someone advertising their mod as an expansion as taking the piss. I am not saying that they would, but they MIGHT. For the sake of a name, is it really worth it? Not just for own egos sake, but thinking of everyone elses enjoyment too?

There is one last detail. 95% of mods like this never even make a alpha stage, let alone beta stage. People have all sorts of misconceptions about modding, and let it get to their heads. Once they find out that their volunteers don't have the patience or the inclination to spend a few hundred hours of their lives modding a game for free to then only have others complain about it - it then falls back to just one person. Trying to keep yourself motivated for several hundred to several THOUSAND hours of work, especially when it doens't work time after time - often leads to abandonment. More projects have been abandoned - including those that were near "beta stage" than I can remember.

Now if this is a mod announcement, then i'll move it to the correct forum. If its going to go ahead - good luck and all the best. However, if the replies are going to be pissy along the lines of "why not - we can call it what we want", then i can safely say that i retract my statements about good luck, as i hope you fail miserably. Simply put, if attitude of "I am not going to do what you ask as you cannot tell me what to do" applies, and therefore goes against some kind advice.......then it will be the last advice I give. I just hope that all advice given during your lives isn't going to be cast aside like that!!!
