Erm, could you be more specific about that?

Here's how to get a ship ready to sell, in a nutshell:

1. The ship's Hull and Package must be listed in goods.ini

2. You must list the ship in the Marketships.ini file, and put it on sale at a Base that has a ShipDealer, such as LI01_02_Base (that'd be Pittsburgh). For example... a mod that I and Mandinga are going to release sometime soon ... has a ship called the R9SD in it. Here are the entries for LI01_02_Base:

[BaseGood
base = Li01_02_base
marketgood = r9sd_package, 1, -1, 1, 1, 0, 1, 1
marketgood = lfr_package, 1, -1, 1, 1, 0, 1, 1

There's more to putting a ship on sale than just that, of course- the Powerplant, Engine and other details must all be complete. But hey... that's what all of the Tutorials are for.
