Puke Barwalker

Wed Jul 23, 2003 4:54 am by Puke Barwalker

Weapons are small and don't take up much space. But no sense carrying any you don't need. Save the more powerful weapons for when you can buy a ship to use them and sell of the rest. (e.g. When you recover Level 10 weapons from a wreck and are flying a ship that won't fit more than Level 6, hang on to the guns!)

Now missiles and mines do have a maximum storage space, unlike guns and launchers. You might find there are drone mines or wasp missiles that you cannot tractor in, since you run out of space for them.
