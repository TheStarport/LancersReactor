Fishyboy

Mon Apr 28, 2003 10:25 pm by Fishyboy

Two good, legal, trade routes that I like are the diamond run and the niobium run. Buy diamons from the Bautzen station in Dresden (about $320 per unit) and sell them on Planet Leeds, Leeds (for $1275), then buy niobium from the Holman Outpost in Tau 31 ($290?) and sell on Leipzig Station in Dresden ($1600). This return run can net over half a million credits in the bigger freighters, but it is a long trip.
Also, some of the outer systems pay up to $25,000 for the random missions, but the tend to be quite hard.

Tom.
