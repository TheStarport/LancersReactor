Rob Schaefer

Aliases: Lots; those in the know refer to him by his callsign "Codename"
Age: 37
Faction: Freelancer
Appearance: Nondescript. Neither particularly tall, nor short. Average build. Brown hair kept perhaps slightly longer than average. Brown eyes. Sometimes has facial hair, sometimes doesn't. Essentially, the sort of person you forget the second after you see him.
Occupation: Freelance Bounty Hunter/Enforcer/Spy specializing in covert operations
Ship: Variable. Between his considerable monetary wealth and the myriad favours owed, Rob is able to acquire the ship he thinks best suits his job. He tends to prefer the Kishiro manufactured Civilian line of ships and is most often seen in a Falcon. (Short answer, Falcon, though it might change.)

Bio
Not much is known about the man who calls himself Rob Schaefer. He is most comfortable working in secrecy, and guards his most intimate secrets jealously. To him secrecy is freedom; freedom to act as he will, freedom to achieve his objectives on his schedule and freedom to only take jobs he deems worthy. This love of secrecy and privacy effectively prevented Rob from being a real military man, so he chose to go the freelance route instead. Beginning small, as all freelancers do, he quickly gained a reputation as one of the finest covert operators in Sirius. He does not take just any job though. He has a strong aversion to any faction that does not conform to his sense of right and wrong.

A bit long, I know. Apologies.
