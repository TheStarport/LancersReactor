force-feedback technology basically allows the controls to shake with explosions on the screen.
The whole idea is that if an explosion happens on the left side of your ship (for example) and you try to turn that way, there will be a resistance felt through the joystick simulating the force of the explosion.
Not the best explanation but it gives the general idea. As for how good....the general consenus from those I know with them reckon it makes that sense of "living in the game" one step nearer
Hope that helps.
