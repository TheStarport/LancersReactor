Circumcisor

Wed Jun 18, 2003 11:45 pm by Circumcisor

Your assumption is correct and here's the evidence.

In the .ini's, each gun weapon has a line for weapon type (eg "weapon_type = W_Photon01".

This is used in weaponmoddb.ini to specify damage vs shields

[WeaponType
nickname = W_Photon01
shield_mod = S_Graviton01, 0.800000
shield_mod = S_Graviton02, 0.800000
shield_mod = S_Graviton03, 0.800000
shield_mod = S_Molecular01, 1.000000
shield_mod = S_Molecular02, 1.000000
shield_mod = S_Molecular03, 1.000000
shield_mod = S_Positron01, 1.200000
shield_mod = S_Positron02, 1.200000
shield_mod = S_Positron03, 1.200000

... meaning photon gun types do 120% damage to positron shields but only 80% damage to graviton (01, 02, 03 being LF / HF /FR )

The nomad guns don't have a line for weapon type - so they must do 100% damage to each shield type.

Fill your class 10 weapon slots with Nomad weapons. They use zero energy & deliver more firepower than any other weapon available.
Nomad cannons deliver 2566/second, and the Nomad blasters deliver 2540/second but at a faster rate of fire. As with all weapons other than pulse, their shield stripping ability is half that output.
In your Class 9 positions, use a pair of Kraken 2 (9) which deliver 2344/second each. If not available to you yet , then Wyrm 2 (9) at 1925/second or Salalmanca 2 (9) at 1956/second are fine.
Do not overload with Tizona del Cid's as they are virtually useless once they have stripped the shields. Good to have one, but one only.
You can bypass the Tizona by using your turret weapon - if it fires forward - as a Rapier (8) turret which gives 3232 shield damage /second.

If you dont have a forward facing turret - then do have 1 x Tizona. Your turret should then be a Kraken (8) or a Skyblast A (8)
