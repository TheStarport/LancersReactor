Rules on 24/7 Proxima Dedicated

As long as people follow the rules on Proxima Dedicated, anybody is welcome.

They are :
a) No modding, hacking or cheating = Instant Ban reason !

b) No PK'ing in the New York system = Overhear warning from Server Deputy, and you get kicked, come back and repeat, you get banned.

c) No dockcamping that last over 30 minutes.

Anything that threatens the stability of the server, will also get you kicked.
Sorry guys, if you have pings in the 500-600's you threaten the stability of the server for all the other players. So upgrade those 28K8 and 33K6 modems to at least a 56K and hope your POTS lines are good enough for at least a 44K connection, if you want to go on Proxima Dedicated without the risk of getting kicked.

Dockcamping IS a part of the game.. Choose to pay the toll, or try to run, your choice to make. But excessive dockcamping will be frowned upon. 30 minutes is the limit.

That's the rules on Proxima Dedicated. Adhere to them, and we will all have fun. Break them and suffer the consequences.

B. Regards
