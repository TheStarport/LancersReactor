Myo

Thu Oct 28, 2004 5:00 pm by Myo

Yeah, the idea is that Mother Earth united with Sky producing the Titans. The final couple produced were Cronus and Rhea. Cronus helped Mother Earth in taking revenge on his father Uranus (Sky), after castrating him, Cronus took his place and threw his brothers the Hundred Handed ones and the Cyclopses into Tartarus. Cronus married his sister Rhea and they went on to produce the Gods, Demeter, Hestia, Hera, Hades/Pluto, Poseidon and Zeus. Cronus devoured them at birth to prevent a prophecy by his parents that he would lose power to one of his offspring. However, after a long time of this, Rhea realised she was pregnant again (with Zeus), she fled to Crete to protect him. She left the baby in the care of the Oceanid Metis, giving Cronus a stone wrapped in clothes which he thought was the child.
When Zeus grew up he forced Cronus to swallow a poison and he regurgitated the children, the children joined forces, liberated the Cyclopses and the Hundred-Handed ones, and declared war on Cronus and his brothers (although some of the Titans did not support Cronus and assisted the Gods).
After the Battle (which the gods won), Cronus and the Titans were punished, many of them (including Cronus) were thrown into Tartarus with the Hundred-Handed ones to guard them.

(information c/o 'Greek Mythology' by Sofia A. Souli)
