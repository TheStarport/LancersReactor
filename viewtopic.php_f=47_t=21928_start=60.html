The flight group gathered around the cruiser, the Vengance. New armour plating stood out a mile away, clean and shining against the old matt black of the original plates. Space dust and debris would quickly discolour it though, it was unavoidable on a ship that size. Punishment on board a cruiser was often deck cleaning, and they didn’t mean the deck you walked on inside…
The idea of the second lighter armour was a good one should they run into the other Defender-xs if it was fully armed. Whilst it wouldn’t stop the initial blast at all it would remarkable help reduce a spread of the molten metal. The fact that it was partially mesh, thus reducing surface area would greatly assist. In theory it was similar to a twin hull, and a less dense armour meant that it would take longer for the particle beam to have as good as an effect. Lutz was praying that the second Defender-xs wasn’t armed though. He still only had 10 batts, and he’d need them all, because if his shields droped it'd be game over...
Drifting into formation he fell in behind the Vengance, Terranova at his side, Michael just in front. The idea was that all the fighters at the front of the battle group, Wings Echo and Alpha, would engage any fighters in the area, leaving Lutz, Terranova and Michael to fly straight through to attack the main doors of the base. The flight group flanking the Vengance, Delta Wing, would distract the base turrets and Arrigoni was stationed directly below the cruiser, flying upside down so that the hull of The Camel and the hull of the Vengance protected each other partially. That Dromedary of Arrigonis had a lot of turrets that would be handy, and although he’d not spotted it Lutz was sure there was a torpedo launcher on it somewhere. He’d spotted Arrigoni eyeing up a couple of torps on Hunter base, a bit of an oddity for someone supposedly without a torpedo launcher.
The formation moved swiftly from trade lane to trade lane, the flanking fighters pushing forward, as the Vengance barely fit. On his scanners he could see the odd ship outside of the trade lane. All flashed up red, but the trade lane was never disrupted. No wise man disrupts a flight group with a cruiser like that in it.
New Harlem was suddenly looming before them, the docking ring open and ships were flying out both ways. It must be a busy time of year thought Lutz to himself.
O’Connor: “OK, this is where we ride all the way in. Cruise engines only, keep in formation. I’m plotting a way point on your neural nets. The base is located on the “dark side” of New Harlem. We’ll be cruising round the planet and go forward. There’s a low density asteroid field there, nothing much, but it’ll cause the Vengance and The Camel a few probs. If you see an asteroid heading our way please don’t be shy with the guns. I'll be joining the fray when we arrive. O’Connor out.”
The ships regained formation, the cruise engines of almost a dozen ships fired up and the battle fleet moved out. Lutz gazed out across the void to his left, seeing the odd ship that was Terranova’s. He liked that man. He didn’t trust him. Hell knows, he trusted virtually no-one. But he had style and charm. He would be an interesting partner that’s for sure. The asteroids were clear now, coming up fast. As the main ship of the fleet the Vengance dropped her a engines, and all the other ships did like wise, all falling in perfect synch with the Liberty cruiser. Ahead was a huge asteroid that had been partially terraformed into a base. It loomed before them and fighters were pouring out of it. The two wings of ships at the front of the Vengance streamed ahead, the wing at the side tore off to by pass the main group and aimed for the base.
Terranova: “OK guys! Lutz, Michael… lets roll!”
Michaels Falcon blasted along side the two of them, swinging to the left of the Liberty cruiser, whilst Terranova’s ship went above, and Lutz channelled his Defender-xs below, flying between the Vengance and The Camel. As they flew past the front the rejoined, Michael in the middle, getting ready to knock on the front door so to speak. All around them explosions were rife. According to the current results 4 pirate ships had already been taken down, and none of the “allies” ships were down at all. But there was a lot, and a large group had cruised in way over to starboard. Delta fighter wing had made it to the base and had started the assault. Two of the main cannons were damaged when Delta 1 shouted over the comms:
Delta 1: “The main hanger has got a shield over it! A big one as well! We’ll see what we can do.”
Terranova: “Damn it! How’s Michaels Falcon gonna get through that? He needs to smash down the doors!”
Delta 1: “We think we can do it, but it’s designed to be mobile, so it’s a lot stronger than you’d think! I’m not sure it can be taken down!”

Lutz: “Not if he’s suggesting it’s as powerful as a planetside shield. However if we overload the generator, hit it as hard as possible from as many sides as possible we’re in with a chance.”
Terranova: “This is Terranova, I’m taking command of Delta wing. All fighters break off, split off and pick your own side of the shield. All ships fire on my mark. Hit it with everything you have.”
Fighters flew in a complex myriad of shapes as they broke off, and spun back around to face the shield. Behind them the Vengance and the other fighters were giving and receiving hell. It was all out war.Many acts of bravery went unseen. An Alpha ship was badly damaged, limping home to dock with the Vegance. A missile sreamed after it and he was out of flares.
