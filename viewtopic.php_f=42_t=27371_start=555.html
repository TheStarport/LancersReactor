Bob McDob:
That links says that, "This page is not available."
BTW-"You've got mail!"

pSYCHO:
If you've got a version of the mod...
Simply open the "Shiparch.ini" file...
Goto the "Nickname = Ge_Fighter" section...
and change the:
DA_archetype = ships\geminiwc\?????.cmp
material_library = ships\geminiwc\????.mat
to:
DA_archetype = ships\geminiwc\mtalon.cmp
material_library = ships\geminiwc\mtalon.mat
(or whatever ship you want to check.)

Just make sure that the *.cmp & *.mat files are in the:
data\ships\geminiwc folder.

ge_fighter is the Starflier in FL, the Epee in The Reckoning, and the MTalon in Privateer's Privateer.

That's all I do to check for visibility.
To check hardpoints, just add more.
IE:
If it says:
hp_type = hp_gun_special_1, HpWeapon01
and you want to check for 4 hardpoints, change it to:
hp_type = hp_gun_special_1, HpWeapon01, HpWeapon02, HpWeapon03, HpWeapon04
