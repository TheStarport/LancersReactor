First of all, welcome to TLR

While you are playing Freelancer, simply press the 'Print Screen' button which normally is located at the top of your keyboard, a bit to the right. That way you can make a screenshot everytime you press it. Normally the screenshots you have made will automatically be stored in My Documents\My Pictures\Freelancershots. Of course there are also many software programs on the market you can use to make screenshots, such as Fraps.

Hope this helps.
