Today the largest construction effort in the history of DSE was completed. The Battleships Lionheart, Judicator and Reaper launched from Baltimore Shipyard today. The Lionheart, the new flagship of the DSE Navy, is commanded by Admiral Kershaw. The Judicator is commanded by Vice Admiral Kenshin, a former Kusari navy officer who joined long before the BHG wiped out the Kusari Navy, and the Reaper is commanded by Flag Captain Dominic. The ships have been placed in the newly formed Justice Fleet , along with the Cruisers Revenge, Excalibur and Adventurer .

DSE NAVY ABANDONS EXPLORATION ATTEMPTS

Today a spokesperson from DSE Naval Command on Planet Pittsburgh stated that there would be no more exploration attempts for a long time. In his statement, he said, 'We have already lost a lot of brave adventurers searching for new systems, and we have greater need for men like them now, in this time of crisis. Therefore, we shall be postponing all exploration missions indefinitely.
