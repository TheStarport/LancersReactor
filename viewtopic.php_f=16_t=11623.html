Ether Dragon

Tue Mar 25, 2003 4:15 pm by Ether Dragon

I like nearly any ship over those three. The only VHF I fly is the Hammerhead. Given the overwhelming firepower of the VHF's, however, I like playing in the lighter craft. I have one character in a Falcon, and another in a Legionaire.

I've yet to fight a VHF with a light fighter, but I've taken on as many as four Stilettos or Centurions at one time, giving me the impression that I could at least hold my own against a single VHF. The light fighters' maneuverability goes a long way towards protecting you - at least from the AI.

Stats are not my only concern when buying a ship, either. I've tried nearly every ship in the game, but I only stick with the ships that look good to me. The only ones that I consider worthy are the Hawk series, Pirhana series, Crusader, Legionaire, Dagger, and any freighter. I have reservations about the Hawk series. It's very aesthetic, but seems somehow generic. I feel like a sell out anytime I fly one. The Corsair ships are a mixed bag. I enjoy the Legionaire as long as I'm looking at it from behind. Anytime I see the ungainly cockpit that got slapped onto such a graceful frame, I cringe. The heavier versions lose their appeal with the excessive "finnage."

Ark-Builder

Wed Mar 26, 2003 3:11 am by Ark-Builder

I did all my border and edge world exploring in a Banshee (Rheinland Light Fighter).

I really like that ship. The Hawk is a bit more attractive, but a Banshee equipped with a Class 7 Graviton shield and GMG guns (2 Sunblast A's, 2 Sunblast B's ) can do just about anything you need done.

I have completed several 200K+ fighter missions in a Banshee with that loadout, the largest one was 235K I think. It's maneuverable, and it has just enough offensive and defensive capability to handle the high level missions. It's hard as hell to get through those 3 wave missions with 6 VHF's per wave, but I've never been killed on a fighter mission, and it's a lot more satisfying to take them out in an LF than a VHF.

In my opinion, anything more powerful/effective than that is unnecessary in Freelancer.
