Actually it is your router, I spent hours on the phone with MS Tech Support before I finally worked out the issue.

Turn on Port Triggering (under the Port Forwarding options). Set it up to forward ports 2300-2400 outside to 2300-2400 inside. It will make some rules that will cause FL to start working. At least that's what finally fixed it for me. And I worked on a solution for a week.

Good Luck,
Feel free to e-mail me if you want an exact setup.
Andon

From the client side, you shouldn't have to change a thing on that Linksys. I run the same router/gateway and there were no specific tweaks for Fl multiplayer.

I am running a server however, and for that, I had to forward UDP ports 2300-2400 to the server's IP *AND* open up the DirectPlay ports as well.

You might try upgrading the firmware on your BEFSR41 as well. Easy to do and takes less than 5 mins.
