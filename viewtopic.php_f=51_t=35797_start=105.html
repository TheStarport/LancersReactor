Do the save to disk method, presuming you have Windows XP, when you are downloading, open another window on Lancers reactor. Continue downloading. When it seems t freeze, use the 2nd window to reload lancers page. If it won't, click on the (x) and close the download. leave the pages open, and keep trying to go to the download again. When you can, click to download again, and it will pick up where it left off. Lancers, from time to time, hangs up,a nd has to rest, that it when downloads are lost. It helps if you have either DSL or cable to get the downloads faster, before TLR can lock up.
