Payment For Bretonia Mining And Metals Sent Away

Today President Hawk Of Prey personally oversaw the deployment of the convoy shipping the payment for Bretonia Mining And Metals. This was following a deal made by the two corporations for a Small Gas/Metal Miner to be constructed for use by Deep Space Engineering in the newly pacified Sigma 13 system, following the invitation by the Bounty Hunters Guild for all lawful groups to come and harvest it's rich gas deposits.


Deep Space Engineering President Hawk of Prey Leaving For Rheinland Peace Summit

Today President Hawk Of Prey announced that he would be leaving for the Helgoland Peace Summit. In an exclusive interview, he stated, 'I will be leaving for this summit to secure good relations with House Rheinland. That is the only official reason, and I am confident we shall obtain this simple goal.' However, even though the President appears quite confident that the trip should go smoothly, a large number of DSE Security Personnel have been preparing for the trip as well.


New Manta Class Gunboat 'Undertaker' Opened For Weekly Tours

Today Admiral Kershaw of the DSE Navy announced to a press conference that the newly constructed Manta Class Gunboat, the Undertaker, would be opened for 2 months of weekly tours. Admiral Kershaw's statement to the conference was, 'I remember as a little boy seeing a Liberty Cruiser on patrol nearby the transport I was on and thinking to myself, "I wonder what it's like inside one of those?" Now, citizens of Sirius can come aboard the Undertaker and see for them selves just what it's like to be in a Gunboat. And as an added bonus, the tourists can even fly and control the ships, under the watchful eye of our best instructors.' The Undertaker will be docked at Baltimore Shipyard and an Armoured Personnel Carrier will be repurposed for transporting tourists to the ship during the tour period.
