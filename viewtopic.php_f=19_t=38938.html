Hmmm, never considered a server with a bandwidth limit but then I have both my servers on two ADSL lines with no limits.

You could work it backwards. 50 GB in 30 days, 1.667 GB a day, 69 MB an hour, 1.157 MB a minute and 19.2 kB a second. That equals to nearly 192 kbps. Since it has to be two directions and for a FL server the uplink is about the same as the downlink, that that is 96 kbps one way. IMO that's about a 10-12 player amount. Thus you could run a 10-12 player server around the clock.

Unless you had lots of friends playing at all times, I doubt you'll reach this limit of 50 GB a month. If you ran something else though...
