I finally figured it out!!

And it is something that hasn't been mentioned in any other thread, so I figured I better post the answer.. (I spent at least 20 hours re-installing drivers, the game, DirectX, the whole Operating System... I would save others this pain)

What was actually going on was that there was too much memory in my system.

Win9x can't handle more than 512M. It actually does a weird thing and starts using up its own memory to handle its memory. Or something.....

So, I followed the instructions given in a MS technote (http://support.microsoft.com/default.as ... -us;253912) and then I retried the game, and I haven't had a single problem with it since.

Well, not with running it at least. There was one or two times where I died... Was that a problem....?

Hope this helps *someone* out there.....

Nemir
