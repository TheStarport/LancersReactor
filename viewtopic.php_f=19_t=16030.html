When you die in MP...Whatever you have mounted on your craft is not lost. Anything not mounted, such as cargo or extra weapons, is lost.

Fly around and kill each other is one thing you can do. You can also trade, explore, be a pirate, be a bounty hunter...Lots of things, lots of options.

The "Cheap Shot" thing is something we call PK (Player Kill). On some servers, Elite, --Argonus-- (mine) and some others, you cannot PK in certain areas...Well..You aren't allowed to...You can do it...but it's against the rules. That is something we have to police ourselves for now.


Hope that answers your questions.
