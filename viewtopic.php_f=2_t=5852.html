This is a disgrace.

I am sorry that my first post here is a rant, but I really have to vent.

So, I finally got to the last mission of the game. I had encountered a few crashing bugs along the way, with the game crashing to Windows for no apparent reason, but I shrugged it off and moved on, because the game was just that much fun.

After an hour or so of attempting the last mission, I finally complete the final object - only for the game to crash, giving me some error about a module trying to load. Now, I'm sorry, but if you design a game that crashes randomly during missions, including the VERY END of the LAST MISSION, then the least you can do is release a PATCH. Sure, Digital Anvil has better things to do, but the game has been out 3 years - 3 YEARS, and no patch. I ALONE, if I knew the code, could've patched the game in that amount of time.

A game that has been out for 3 years without a patch would USUALLY be a good sign, but apparently in this case it's just a sign of laziness. I love computer games, more than I ever will love console games, but this is the sort of thing that makes me never want to play computer games again.

I just hope Freelancer fares better in the bugs department.
