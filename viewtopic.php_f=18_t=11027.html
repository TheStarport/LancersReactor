Norfleet

Sat Mar 22, 2003 3:34 am by Norfleet

As far as I can tell, it's a flat 10% chance that a Nomie, when killed, drops a gun he is carrying, as defined in lootprops.ini. The number of times you kill them doesn't seem to affect this. However, if you're killing them inside of a highly irradiated system, such as the pink Unknown, they could be burning up from the radiation before you can scoop them up. Also, if you're raining death on them with GMG SBBs, which fire quickly, do a fair chunk of damage, and are considered be the best L9 guns in the game, the hail of bullets you're unleashing could be hitting some of your intended loot. Try firing in a more controlled pattern, and being quicker on the tractor beam.
