The Titan is in the Very Heavy Fighter class. It costs around $750,000, requires that you be level 30, and can be bought either at Crete (Omicron Gamma, Corsairs) or at Leon Base (Omega-41, Corsairs). You can jump to Omega-41 from several adjoining Omega systems with jump holes, just watch the high radiation. As for Omicron Gamma, don't set foot there if the Corsairs don't like you.
