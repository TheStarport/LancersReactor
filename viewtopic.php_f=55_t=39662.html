If you are despair at last, try this.

Get as close as you can to those 3 ships, hit them with your ship at the speed of 1-20 m/s. That leads to a series of tiny but continuous collisions. Yes, both of you would get damaged, but your shield is regeneratable and theirs aren't. Thus it is just a matter of time. My friend encounterd this problem like you, and he "solved" the 3 ships in this way.

The game won't realize that you are attacking a neutral unit of you are not firing. It worths a try.
