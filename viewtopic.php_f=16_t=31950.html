I've had this game for a couple of months now and I tend to find new thigns every day. A couple weeks ago I found Planet Hades and Planet Cerberus in sigma 17 (or was it 19). I still haven't been to the bottom Unknown system, that's one place I need to go. I'm aiming with my single player character to fully map the universe.
