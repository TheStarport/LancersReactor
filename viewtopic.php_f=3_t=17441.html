Actually, to change the cruse speed you have to edit the constants.ini

Get BiniQDU (in the downloads section) and decode and save the ini. Then under the [EngineEquipConsts header, add the line CRUISING_SPEED = **** at the bottom. Save and fly. Just a word of advice though, BACK UP THE INI!!!

Also, i have found that 1000 works well for me as the only things i can't just cruise through are Explosive Gas, Mines, or the Badlands. Those places you might have to watch you ship and toggle your cruise engines on or off before you splatter yourself al over the solar system.
