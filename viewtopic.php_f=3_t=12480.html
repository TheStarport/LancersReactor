Which ship was you favourite in Privateer2?

While playing Freelancer my thoughts went back to the time when I was playing Privateer 2. So, which fighter was your favourite back then, and why did you love that one?

For me, it was the Faldari Mk.II. It had "only" 4 weapon mounts, but with two Craven Mk.V-lasers, it wasn't such a lack. And it was quite small, not much thicker than a Heretic. And, it had two gaps in its wings, and while the enemys were firing at me, most of the shots went through those gaps.

I didn't like those flying "bricks" (can't remember the names though), they were not very manoeuverable and every salve fired at you was a hit, guaranteed!

Hail thee, my metal-brethren[!

bobk

Mon Apr 07, 2003 9:10 pm by bobk

FreeLancer does have some good qualities, and I'm enjoying it. However, P2 has some things which I've never seen in another flight sim. The first and foremost, in my mind, is the dynamically changing universe. It was interesting to read the bulletin boards and see what was going on here or what was happening there and how the prices of commodities were affected by those planetary events.

Another thing that I liked about P2 is the AI built into the wingmen. As most of you know, the "dedication" of the wingmen was influenced, to a large degree, by how much you were paying them. I never paid a lot for a wingman, and used them mostly as distractions or cannon fodder. One time, I hired one, only to have him bug out on me in the middle of a battle that had suddenly turned ugly. So, I waited, and I waited, and eventually found him, again, on another planet. I eagerly hired him, then waited until we cleared the area of enemy ships. Then, I TARGETED THE SLIMY LITTLE SCUM BAG and took my time killing him!!! Boy, those Roberts brothers sure knew how to build a space sim.
