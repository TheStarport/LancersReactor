T-hawk

Sun Jul 20, 2003 2:08 am by T-hawk

You can get the Dromedary in single player as soon as you are sufficient level.

The last few missions are a lot of fun in a Drom. Believe it or not, that's actually the ship with the biggest firepower (excluding torpedoes) that you can take on the last few missions. With the turrets, it can fire forward with seven hardpoints (one of the turret slots can't), one more than the Dragon/Barracuda/Anubis. And the freighter shields have around 20% more capacity than the HF shields as well.

One trick to getting the Drom is to go to the other base in the system where Mactan is, and accept any mission. That will make the pirate factions around Mactan temporarily neutral, since you don't get attacked while you're on a bar mission, and you can go land on Mactan.
