Nogana Naishi

Sun Mar 09, 2003 11:19 pm by Nogana Naishi

All your weapons (except for missles/torpedoes) are fired by default with the RMB unless you de-selected them. While in space, look in the bottom-right window (press Backspace to open/close it if it isn't there) and see if all your regular weapons are highlighted. If not, click on their names to select/deselct them... Note that you shouldn't select any missles like this, since you'll basically be wasting a missle every time you shoot.
