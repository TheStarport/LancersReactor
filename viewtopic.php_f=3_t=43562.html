Bejaymac

Sun May 14, 2006 11:42 am by Bejaymac

It's a glitch (sort of), the autosave can have corrupt data from a bad save and this interferes with the leveling settings. Your next level requirements are being reached and recognized but the corrupt data means that the trigger to increase you rank is missed, and once you miss one you miss the rest. The only remedy is to delete the autosave and use the last solid save point to try it all again.
