First of all welcome to TLR !!

To answer your question:

1. You can ambush the ones which the Bounty Hunters have a struggle with (like the Navy and Police forces etc.). That will make you score points with the Bounty Hunters.

2. You can also bribe certain people, so they can put in a good word with the Bounty Hunters for you

Here are some places where you could bribe people to get you on the good side of the Bounty Hunters Guild:

Ames Research Station/Kepler - D4 (Zoners)
Atka Research Station/Sigma 17 - D3 (Cryer Pharmaceuticals)
Freeport 1/Omega 3 - C4 (Zoners)
Freeport 5/Omega 41 - F5 (Zoners)
Freeport 6/Tau-29 - D6 (Zoners)
Freeport 10/Tau-37 - F4 (Zoners)
Java Station/Tau-23 - E6 (IMG)
Kurile/Sigma 17 - F5 (Samura Industries)
Luxury Liner Hawaii/Sigma 19 - E5 (Orbital Spa and Cruise)
Rügen Station/Omega 3 - F6 (Daumann)

When you´re at a base or planet, go to its bar. When you´re there, hover over the people, so you can see what actions are possible with them (like hearing a rumor etc.) When you hover over someone and you see a $ sign, this means it´s someone who you could bribe. Usually you need a lot of cash though !
