Not sure how to post pictures, so I'll try to describe the area again first.

As stated before, it is in grid point 7D towards (but not completely at) the upper right. If you have the "*New York -> Magellan" warp gate text on your map (you would have had to found that first), it is immediately above the text and touching the top straight part of the "k" when the map is in the zoomed out position, and as stated above if you look really closely at your zoomed out map, it's in a small, completely black circle there (but that's a bit hard to see).

If you zoom in the map to this same area, you should see the Ithica Research Station in the extreme top left of one of the squares (again if you found it, and it's unmarked - you have to put your cursor over it on the map to see the name, though it's right by a trade lane so it's not hard to spot). Go right two more squares and look around the mid-to-bottom left. Hope this helps!

Also, cool that it has a function. I look forward to finding out more about it in the single player game.
