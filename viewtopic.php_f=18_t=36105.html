Tue Nov 16, 2004 4:57 pm by Argh

@Mobius:

What I'm doing with Warriors of the Sky is pretty radical, but it's not reshaping the game engine. I wish that it was possible to do what you're asking (along with a lot of very arcane things that I won't get into, but they'd be very useful for modders), but short of M$ suddenly getting a clue and either providing the community the source code (like they did for Allegience... which I doubt will ever happen) or providing a development SDK so that people can code things from scratch (again... not going to happen), there are a ton of things that are going to remain impossible, sadly.

I really think that M$ has shot itself in the foot over and over again with regards to making its games moddable in general, and with this one in particular... sales would've probably been double what they were if the game wasn't purposefully "closed" and obfuscated so that making new content is a royal pain (today's tools make it easier, but in the beginning, it was amazing that people made anything new whatsoever, and I'm glad they kept hacking away).

I should mention that Mobius isn't a sock puppet or acquaintence of mine, so I'm glad he observed what I'm doing with the Alpha...
