Tygrys

Mon Jul 14, 2003 1:33 pm by Tygrys

Game is prepared to use existing systems in SP story, so you must at least preserve half of the systems in the new one. Map size is 16x16, so you have 256 possible locations, of which about 50 are used by the game. Making totally new universe is a very hard job, since some rules are hardcoded in the engine and in dll's. The only thought is to pack existing systems into one corner or even in one point (it works, however looks stupid). You may also make them not visible on the map and force the user jump to the new systems from NY at the very beginning. Some of this ideas were used in Freelancer Massive Addon to not make sector map unreadable.

IMHO, This is not as simple as renaming the DATA folder.

Tygrys
