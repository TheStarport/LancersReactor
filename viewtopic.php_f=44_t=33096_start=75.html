Argh

Sun Sep 04, 2005 9:47 pm by Argh

Blade, you need to make a spheremap. And I sort've doubt that anybody has made a spheremap for the DS that doesn't include the DS's geometry- that funny dish-shape and the "ring" around the equator.

What you need to do is make a proper, low-poly model of the DS... use a spheremap for the main model, a planar projection for the dish, and a cylindrical map for the ring. See my Pretty Planet Project and How to Skin links on this Forum for more help with this.

The Serenity from the former TV show "Firefly" by Joss Whedon. Serenity is a Firefly-class transport ship with twin engines and a Standard Radeon Accelerator core for high speed travel between planets. Granted, you can't exactly simulate a Radeon Accelerator in Freelancer, but the twin engine setup is perfect for it. The ship itself isn't terribly large. I would say only about twice as big as a Dromedary. Cargo carrying would be pretty good, considering its a Cargo ship by nature.
The only weapon it should have is a single gun turret on the top (as portrayed in the movie "Serenity" even tho the actual ship never had any weapons.

It would still be an awesome looking ship to fly around in, and it doesn't seem too hard to model. Here's an image for those who don't know what it looks like.
