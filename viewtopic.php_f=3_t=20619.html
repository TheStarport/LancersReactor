Mining isn't very hard (though it is also not very profitable). First, you've got to find a suitible location. The best way to do this is to look on your map. Once you've arrived at that location, you put your ships engines to neutral, and stand still. If you're in a good location, you should see little rocks flying around. Shoot those and they will break. In most cases they will break and leave nothing, but sometimes they drop items such as scrap metal, water, diamonds, etc (depending on where it is that you're mining). And that's it. Kepp breaking rocks and picking up the cargo 'til your cargo hold is full, and then go off to sell the material. Like I said, this isn't exactly the most profitable means of gaining money, but it is an option.
