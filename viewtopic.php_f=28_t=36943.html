Freelancer: Exodus (Warning: Large images)

Freelancer: Exodus

Freelancer: Exodus (Exo) will be an unofficial expansion for Freelancer.
It will, among many things, feature a player driven universe with which you can interact and player-corporation owned stations all over the new universe, named Cerberus sector.

But we will not only create new features for the game. Among this, every ship, weapon, faction, station, solar system and the whole universe map, will be new. Nothing old will be left in the game. Except for the memories of the old Sirius sector.

Would you like to join this modification for Freelancer, please send us an email to freelancer_fan@hotmail.com or joxf@hotmail.com.

Features for the game:

Sector features

- Circa 40 new systems, designed from scratch.
- A redrawn map for the universe.
- The ability to choose among joining five expanding empires, or go your own way.
- Player teamwork is the key. Work together to get through the sector.

System features

- Reworked solar systems
- Trade Lanes have been removed in the major part of the game. Use the engines.
- The systems have expanded, there’s now much more to risk when you travel alone.
- Planets, aren’t they too small? All planets are now made huge.
- Stations will play a major role, since the most planets aren’t even dockable.

Station features

- Buy stations and then upgrade them.
- Make profit out of your stations. A mining station can make astronomic profits if you know how to.
- Sell ships aboard your station to make even more money.
- More profit can be made. Commodites, bars, and weapon dealers all makes some extra credits for the station owner.

Player features

- Player driven universe. Interact with the politics, either by giving a helping hand or making raids on your enemies property.
- Start a corporation, and begin to produce your own ships, custom made or mass-produced.

Other features

- An online Internet site with the latest politic news, updates on various new corporations and forums where you can discuss the most recent ingame news.
- The stations you own will be registered into your personal “Neural Net” on the website. Here you can keep track of the corporations you are a member or leader of, your stations and more.

Are there any other features you see fit? Give us a shot at the emails written above the features list. Thanks.

Fri Dec 31, 2004 3:47 am by Orillion.

@Argh:

- Buy stations and then upgrade them.

I guess many people want to know.
First of all, you must own an active account on our homepage, which isn't online yet so don't ask for any URLs.
Then you simply go to a "Station Buying" menu, and fill in some forms. This will be the data for your base. You will select sort of station, loacation and faction-friendlyness, among others. Press submit.
Now we will control location, that you have the credits needed. If you have, you will receive an email confirming this; if not you will get an email saying what is not right, maybe there is a base at the location, there may not be enough money in your viritual neural net account.

If everything is right, a station with your preferences is in the game, on the map and in our website account. You can now upgrade it using a similar method.

- Make profit out of your stations. A mining station can make astronomic profits if you know how to.

If you have a station with the ability "Mining", then you can earn up to eight hundred thousand credits per update/patch.
The preference is that the station have the ability "Mining" online and that it's at least three kilometres from a asteroid/debris field.

- Sell ships aboard your station to make even more money.

Selling ships is easy. Just go to the "Upgrade" menu in your website account, and then upgrade your station with a ship dealer.
You can now choose among every ship in the game to be sold at the ship locations of your base, but this comes at a cost. To sell the Starflier, for example, you need at least one and a half million credits to spare, and a place for the ship. But this is easily earned back.

You get a basic income of 10 % every time we have an update. Use the "Ship" Menu you should have on the website account, to increase this income with at a maximum of 25 % per update. This costs a small fee, though.

- More profit can be made. Commodites, bars, and weapon dealers all makes some extra credits for the station owner.

Apart from the ship income, why not upgrade with the three other locations available on a station. You get 6000 extra credits for every such upgrade every update/patch.

---

Easy, wasn't it ? There will be a tutorial explaining this better on the website.

(Everything above MAY and CAN change, when we update or change our website.)
