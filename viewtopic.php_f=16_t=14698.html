The game can''t end like that!

Wow, I just finished the main campaign in the solo game. It was truly awesome. Hard enough to be a challenge and easy enough to be an enjoyable game. The story is well driven for a space flying game. And the cinematics, even if they could look better, are well done ; and being made with the game engine they add a lot to the immersion.

But what an abrupt end!

- I thought Juni would marry Trent. Hey, why not ? They lived a lot of adventures together. Even if they don't openly admit it, they seem to both attract each other. Couldn't she at least give him a kiss, instead of a wink and a smile ?
- I thought Trent would become rich. But, no. Back to freelancing. Not because adventure is so addicting that he can't help to go back for more. But because he needs to live and he's as poor as when he arrived at Liberty (the ship apart). I would be him, i would use my celebrity to make money : earning millions for making show on TV, selling my biography or derivative products...
- I thought Trent would become famous. But no. Nobody knows him. The Liberty bartender reminded he had seen Trent's face on the news when he was just an anonymous from Freeport 7. Despite he saved the world, nobody in any space station tell him "hey, aren't you Trent ? I saw you on TV. May I have an autograph ?". No, they still say "Are you new here ?". Nobody knows him.
- I thought Trent could sometimes meet his friends (King for instance) again to remember the good old time. Even if they don't give any more missions, they could just say "Hello Trent, how are you ?". Trent can't meet them, they disappeared or they don't want to see him... "Thanks Trent, you were helpful when the world was in danger, but from now on everything is fine and we don't need you anymore!".

Ok, he saved the world. But no wife, no friends, no money, no celebrity. Just a crappy meddal to hang in his ship. This guy looks like some war veterans : true hero during the war, they sometimes become true loosers when the conflict is over... Shame the main campaign ends like that.
