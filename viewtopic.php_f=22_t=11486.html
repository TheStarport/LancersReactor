Freeport 7

Welcome to Freeport7!

This is a Freelancer server hosted on a 1.5Mbps ADSL connection on an Intel P4 2.4GHz with 512DDR PC2100 CL2.5 RAM.

The server's name is:" Freeport 7".

If the server is not in the internet server list, one of two things could have happened:
a) The server crashed. We will restart it ASAP.
b) The server's internet connection went dead. It could come back up in a few minutes or it could take a couple of hours.


Please use this discussion thread to talk about anything regarding Freeport 7.


SERVER DESCRIPTION:
Max player setting of 48.
Server is open 24/7
Only 3 rules to follow:
- No Cheating. No PVP in New York. No base/wreck camping.


WARNING TO ALL RULE BREAKERS!:
We will not tolerate cheaters, PVP in New York or campers. Anyone caught breaking the rules or benefitting directly/indirectly from a rule breaker, will have their account banned from the server without warning. Email us at freeport7server@hotmail.com if you suspect someone of breaking the rules. State the nick of the player and the facts that support your suspicion.

Do not be intimidated by cheaters and do not accept obscene amounts of cash from someone if you suspect he is cheating. Also do not accept NPC weapons/items such as Nomad Lasers and Class 10 Graviton shields. If they kill you for not accepting their *cheat* cash/items, your loss will be repaid by us and cheating players will be promptly banned if found guilty of cheating.

We will handle rule breakers on a case by case basis. Severity of the punishment (character deletion or account ban) will depend on the severity of their actions.

You may appeal your ban by emailing us at freeport7server@hotmail.com. Please provide proof or witnesses of your innocence.
