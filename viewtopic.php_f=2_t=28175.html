I never used to find the nova cannon useful. But after I started playing SL again quite recently, I've found it's useful for some things. For example, some Coalition cruisers have quite 'delicate' subsystems. If you have to take out, say, the shield generator, then you can charge the nova cannon while you're on the way, get close, and then unleash it. It's only taken more than one shot to disable such a subsystem on a full-blown carrier. Which also mean's it's useful for quickly taking down Berijev subsystems, especially if you're out of missiles.
