Yah, it is a tiny bit too short, but it did its job to entice you into getting the game.

You can fly the majority if ships you see. Not the large capital ships tho, only the single fighter/freighter ships. So even tho you cant fly those really long cargo ships, you can still purchase some freighters which have cargo hold sizes of around 80 units, enuff to make a couple ten thousand bucks in a run.

You can't truely "join" a faction but you can align your reputation so taht they like you the most. And in multiplayer, your name will sport a tag showing other ppl which faction you are liked by most. The more a faction likes you, the more equipment you can buy and the more higher paying missions you can get.
