New Ship "Sachmet" - a dedication to the film &quo

Hi
I woke up this morning ... standing in the kitchen and watching my coffeemachine in hope of my first cup of coffee. At this moment i rember the request for the ship from the movie "Last Starfighter".
And i remember myself standing in front of the first arcademachines and how much money i spend there (ps: great time, wasnt it?)
And of course i remember the film and all what it remains to me! With Tron this movie makes me interesed in make my own 3D-Graphics.

Ok ok ... enough of this old stuff ... as a matter of fact i made this "new" ship named "SACHMET" (Sachmet was the egyptian Warlord with the lionhead ... a female god). Also another ship for the Order

Here a few thoughs about this ship an the possible stats:
1. I think this one should be a class 10 Fighter with 8 weaponhardpoints and of course class 10 shield ... BUT!!! ...
2. weak armor (i suggest 4000 hitpoints ... maybe only 3000) ... so if you've been hit an your shield is down you should have a BIG problem!
3. it should be very nervous in handling an aiming and very quick with selfturns to simulate the Death Blossom of the original ship.

This ship should'nt be a "Blockade-Runner" or a "i-stay-at-my-target-and-ignore-all-the-enemies-behind-me-ship" ... i like to see it more like a ship which require a high skill in flying it. Highlevel-Weapons combined with weak armor and extrem fast moves!

Please forgive me my school-english ... i hope you understand what i like to say! And here it is:

http://mos.00server.com/Sachmet.jpg

Greetings & good hunting ... MOS

ps: Dont forget ... this is NOT the original ship ... its more a dedication to the film and the original ship!!!
