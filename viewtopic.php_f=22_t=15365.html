Factions:
each player chooses a faction. each faction has ship restrictions, ally restrictions and player killing restrictions.

Ships
all ships can be found here: http://www.lancersreactor.com/t/flships/default.asp
a player must be in a legal ship for his or her faction by level 5
anubis: the anubis is now available on our server at planet pittsburg for 90,260 credits. This ship is considered liberty navy.

Allies:
these are the alliances need to be in character for your choosen faction. all alliances must be at least 0 nuetral by level 20.

Player Killing:
No Player Killing in Liberty space. each faction has PK restrictions.

ex: if i choose corsairs i would be [CORpneumonic81. i owudl be unable to kill any other player w/ a [COR in front of there name.

Navy: [NAVY Navy Units are the primary defence against invasion by a hostile force. if alienas or a hostile foe were encountered. the navy would be the faction to deal with it.

ships: Britonia, kusari, liberty, rhineland, Bounty Hunter fighters. No Frieghters
allies: all police, navy and shipping
pk: cannot kill any police, navy or free trader

Police: [POL Police enforce the law in each system defending trade lanse and jump gates.

ships: Britonia, kusari, liberty, rhineland, and civilian fighters. No Frieghters
allies: all police, navy, shipping, mining, bounty hunters
pk: cannot kill any police, navy, bounty hunter

Free Traders: [TRAD Free Traders are the merchants of the galaxy. stalwartly hauling merchandise to the most dangerouse parts of the galaxy.

ships: all freighters and civilian fighters
allies: none
pk: cannot kill any police or navy

Freelancers: [FREE Freelancers are a little bit of everything. peopel who want to be able to occationally haul some loot, or be hired to deal w/ a local problem. this gives freelancer pilots a wide variety of ships to choose from, but unfortuntly makes them less popular w/ the criminal elements.

ships: Britonia, kusari, liberty, rhineland, civilian, and border world fighters and frieghters
allies:all police, navy, mining, shipping and bounty hunter
pk: cannot kill any police, navy or bounty hunter

Bounty Hunters: [BH the police forces hired guns. when there is a criminal u just cant catch, call the bounty hunters!

ships: bounty hunter, civilian and pirate.
allies: police
pk: cannot kill any police

Mercenaries: [MERC Mercenaries have been around in many forms for thousands of years. mercs are the hired guns of the navy often times. hired to hunt down larger elements and military targets. sometiems hired to bolster a navy formation. but also hired by civilan traffic for escort duty and so on.

ships: border world, civilain, corsair, pirate, and bounty hunter.
allies: navy
pk: cannot kill navy

Corsairs: [COR Corsiars are probably the largest criminal organization in the siruis sector. soem of there ships are the most feared. naturally this makes them an enemy of any law abiding faction. drug and artifact smuggling is common place among the corsairs

ships: corsair, pirate, and civilian.
allies: corsairs
pk: cannot kill corsairs

Outcasts: [OUT Outcasts are the second largest criminal organization, and have allied them selves w/ many of the local hoods. drug trafficing is a staple for these thugs.

ships: border world, pirate, and civilian.
allies: outcasts, lane hackers, liberty rouges, GC
pk: cannot kill outcasts

Pirates: [PIR Pirates are just that. if your on a trip, watch your loot. pirates will hack the trade lanes and ambush a lonly free trader. better hire a merc for escort duty!

ships: border world, pirate, civilian. no dromedary.
allies: none
pk: can kill anyone
