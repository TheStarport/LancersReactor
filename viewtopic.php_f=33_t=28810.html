Thanx guys, this is my first TIE model, more SW ones will come, all done from the enhanced XWA models now available at Darksaber OPt Station. I got permission from Tony himself to use these in FL. I know it supposed to have solar panels, but means overalying(or overlapping, i dunno...) some textures and i could not do that with the current model. But my skills will improve that's for sure. Hopeffuly i'll be able to add some cocpits into the fray, perhaps for the rebel ships as well...
This one is gonna be a heavy fighter with 4 guns/4turrets/2 torp launchers and 3300 armor. Also dual thrusters for speedier getwaways....
Gameplay will be more stable, at least in EFA. I tracked down that error that was preventing players for buying commodities such as niobium at Earth.And even more tactical as i intend to make enemies tougher. 4 new types of armor will be available instead of the old common upgrades: Durasteel Armor, Titanium Armor, Strontium Armor(yep i know it sounds weird...)) and Depleted Uranium Armor. These will offer increasily better protection against explosive weaponry such as mines and missiles, and not only the players but the Npc's will be able to use them. Their normal properties of hull HP enhacement remain intact.
