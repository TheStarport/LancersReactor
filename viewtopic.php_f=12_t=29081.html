Freelancer on one cd

This may have been talked about before, but I couldn't find a topic specifically addressing it.
There is a very good reason why FL is on one cd, and why it could be on 2 or 3 or 4, and be the exact same. Think of other games on multiple cds. A few I know of are Descent 3, Nightfire, and Diablo 2. What do these games have in common? most of their cutscenes are high res movies, and they also have several movies in them as well. This takes a LOT of space. Having CG script takes a lot less space. And that is exactly what FL does. It's only movies are the intro ones. All the cutscenes are CG script, which is small.
I may be dead wrong, but I know that for only a few movies in Diablo 2, they take up a whole CD. When FL was originally planned for multiple CDs they may have planned on making the cutscenes movies instead, which would take alot of space.
What I'm saying is that Maybe they didn't cut a whole lot out of the game, but instead just used CG cutscenes instead of movies. That would save a whole lot of space, with all the cutscenes in FL, probably several CDs worth.
Like I said, I may be wrong, But I think I have a good point.
