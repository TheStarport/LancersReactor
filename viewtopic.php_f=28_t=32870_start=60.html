Hey, Zero117. Please allow me to give some words on this mod as a defender

Your are right about the tough start in this world. But if you have a liitle more patience, you will find the real interest of it. Easy money in the original game can make one level up fast, but it will also make the game boring fast. Once you get a Titan, you will find the rest of money useless. There ARE some very expensive weapons such as sunslayer. But there is no need to use them any more when you have enough to buy them, cos you can use titan to blow down most fleets with nomad guns. Actually, most people stop playing the game soon after they get Titan. On the contrary, in this world owning the best ship is only the first step to enter the ultimate battlefield. Even with that you can still find many challenging enemies. Money is still badly needed there cos you must use some huge-damage-weapon like torpedos in large amounts.

So here is my advice if you would still like to try this mod for one more time. First, avoid fighting unless your ship is far more stronger than your enemies. That is to say, avoid taking job with difficulty level near you own level. Then you will ask how to earn money? I suggest you play the story mode. Enemies in story mission are much easier to defeat and when you finish the story you will already have quite a deal of money to start trading among systems. If you like to play openly, then I suggest you may try to collect some wreck, that will make you rich quickly as well as give you some powerful equipment. If you must fight one fraction in order to get well with another one, dont fight their wings directly. Fighting their depot will meet your need in an easier and faster way.

As to the questions in your first post, you can find Adv. CSV at Kabukicho Depot in New Tokyo, and Starblazer in planet new London( or Leeds I'm not sure). The cruise speed IS 450 mph in this world, though it appears to be 300 mph, you can feel the difference. However, this will make the race game with Hovis in story a little tougher

I realy recommend this mod if you are an experienced lancer who are seeking new challenge. Although this mod does no add too much to the original game, the life style, as you have already seen, is very different in this new world.

Black Eagle

Wed Sep 08, 2004 8:48 am by Black Eagle

Hi Zero117!

At first thanks for playing the mod and for posting here.

To the money. If you play the story again, you will get a higher reward for the first mission (4000 Cr. and in the next version this will be 6000 Cr.). Of course I understand that you don't want to play the whole story again. So I will submit a new version of the OpenSP mod specially for The Monkeys. There you will also get 6000 Cr. to start.
And there is one other thing. In the badlands in the New York system, there is a wreck with 20 cardamine. If you fly to this wreck and then sell the cardamine on Manhattan, you'll get 30,000 Cr.. This is enough to start with.
I increased the prices in the game, because it was to easy to earn money. In the original game it was possible to start with OpenSP and buy a Titan after 10 hours.
Try to earn some money (or find the wreck I mentioned above) and then buy a freighter. There are many profitable traderuns, so that you will soon be able to buy ships and equipment of the Monkeys, the Robots or the Nomads.

The Starblazer is for example on Planet New London and the Adv. CSV is buyable on Kabukicho Depot in New Tokyo system.

I'm not sure about the SP Money mod. Try if it works and if you encounter problems, deactivate the mod and play without it. But you shouldn't activate it if FLMM says that it is incompatible to my mod.
For the Speed mod. The mod should work with mine, but maybe the Robot Thruster will no longer be the best Thruster in the game, because the Speedmod also changes the original thrusters. As the Robot Thruster is not in the original FL, it will not be changed. If you do not use the Speedmod, there is no way to go faster than 450.

The Thrusters drain so much, because in the original game you could use your thrusters to flee from your enemies. Because of this, the cruise-disruptors were useless.
I made all thrusters faster, but also increased the energy drain. So you can use the thrusters in a fight to have some time for your shields to reload or to avoid being hit by the enemy.
But if you want to flee you have to start your cruise engines and you also need countermeasures to avoid to be hit by a cruise-disruptor.

And again, you normally start with 4000 Cr. (6000 in the next version). But the OpenSP mod takes all your money away. So please wait for my own version of OpenSP. I will submit it to lancersreactor during this week.

I hope you still like my mod. Thanks again!


Sorry Powerdoll!

I forgot to thank you for your answer on Zeros post. You really understood what I wanted to achieve with my mod. It is a mod for advanced players and sometimes it can be really tough.

Thanks!
