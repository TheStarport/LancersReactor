Sherlog

Thu May 29, 2003 10:09 am by Sherlog

The "Destroy Large Space Station" jobs can be over in seconds if you are not required to take out the fighters (read the fine print), so you can do 2 or 3 of them in the time it takes to do one 'normal' mission. Of course, there won't be any loot and so the assassination/tractoring missions may be a bit more lucrative overall.

The highest reward I got was $240000, from the Corsairs, for removing Red Hessians from Omega-41. The missions in that system tend to be very lucrative in general because you can make $100000 or more in loot, in addition to the mission reward. I think they can be more lucrative (though less challenging) than the kill-Bounty-Hunters missions that you can get on the home worlds of the Corsairs or the Outcasts.
