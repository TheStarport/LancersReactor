If you're really sad, like me, or just bored after level 38, you can do a list of all the guns and work out damage per second (ie refire rate x damage) and compare it to energy use per second (i refire rate x energy use)

Top damage per second for a lvl 9 gun is the Kraken Type 2 at 2,344 with 4.8 damage per energy

Skyblast B is second with 2,033 at 4 damage per energy

Best efficiency is the Salamanca Mk II with 5.04 damage per energy (1,956 damage per second)
