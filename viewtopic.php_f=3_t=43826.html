Andkat

Wed Jun 21, 2006 11:05 am by Andkat

I prefer Evolutions 1.30. It's essentially what FL was intended to be: it includes multitudes of novel ship components (capacitators, sensors, power supplies and boosters, engine variants, etc.), in conjunction with new ship variants (many corporations now have unique vessels and all houses have some form of VHF, and most existing lines now have powerful SHFs added to them), capitol ships and actual transports may now be purchased (and capital ship encounters and missions have been added), story systems tend to actually contain dockable bases and things to purchase, new factions, systems, and commodities have been added, and most ships have had a general rebalance (all fighters now have woefully low cargo spaces, but often have modified armour, manueverability etc.). Overall, it enhances FL without utterly changing the focus of the game or the backstory.
