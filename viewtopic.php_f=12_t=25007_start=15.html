ICQ stands for I Seek You and is a tool to chat with other people using the same program. You can download it from their site www.icq.com

With MSN people mean a tool they provide, namely MSN Messenger, which is pretty much the same thing as ICQ. Check it out here. If you have a .Net account (such as hotmail), than you can chat with other people who also have MSN Messenger. Just add their .Net email address and you can see when they are online (if they are signed in on Messenger) and than you can chat with them. You can also see if you have any new messages send to your hotmail address. Really convenient. In my opinion, Messenger is way more popular then ICQ, so I should go for that.

Hope this helps
