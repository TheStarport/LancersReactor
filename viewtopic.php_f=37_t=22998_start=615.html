To all.....

Time for an update...

Epsilon 2.7 is about to enter beta testing, the mod is almost complete and will include:

additional factions, both good and bad. In addition to the existing factions, new AI enemies and factions have been added to several more systems. Liberty, Kusari, Rheinland, Borderworlds, New Systems..

Nazo (Kusari)
Hussars (Rheinland)
Exiles (Borderworlds)
Elementals (Liberty)
E.D.I. - Epsilon Defence Industies - (they fly the Nemesis - and will likely cross your path soon enough)
Atlanteans (Rheinland)

of course we left all your "old friends" in place.

Ships.
Rangers now have their own ship (Warlord), Hackers have a ship now...Exiles have another ship....Nazo....Hussars....Junkers have a new one....Liberty and Bretonia Police.. Some mobile surprises have been added that do go off with a BANG.....

10 new ships should be added - 8 of them buyable.

Mantis - (Alliance fighter from the intro clip). Heavy Assault Fighter - Junkers
Howler - Light Fighter - Liberty Police
Mercury - Light Fighter - E.D.I.
Tsunami - Medium Fighter - Heavily armed - Kusari Nazo
Firesprite - Light Medium Fighter - Elementals & Bretonian Police
Mirage - Medium Fighter - high performance - Borderworld Exiles
Gorath_Hekkan - Light Fighter - fast, slippery and very nasty - Bundschuh and Atlanteans
Goldfeuer - medium fighter - Hussars
Spike MK1 and MK2 - undisclosed AI and NPC at the moment.

The ships have now got custom almost everything, thrusters ,engines, shields, scanners, weapons. plus a host of new weapons and equipment has been added.

The systems have added depth plus been improved and we have had some help on one system in particular from Buck Danny.

For Mp some special features have been added for server operators including special admin equipment available only to them plus a place you can send players who require "punishment"

Be back with more soon..
