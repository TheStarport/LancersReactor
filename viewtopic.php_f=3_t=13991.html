Sapient6

Mon Apr 14, 2003 3:43 pm by Sapient6

First look in your nav map and hit the mining button (the icon is something like a pickaxe and shovel). You'll see minable areas marked in gray. Fly there. Switch to a single weapon so you'r not firing everything you've got (otherwise your shots may destroy the ore). You'll notice some of the faster moving objects get a spinning white reticle when you point at them. Shoot these, some of them will release ore which you can then tractor in and sell.

Note: you'll get these little destroyable rocks/debris in areas not marked for mining, but so far I've never gotten ore from them outside a mining area.
