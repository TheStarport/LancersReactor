Sherlog

Fri Jun 20, 2003 1:17 am by Sherlog

Wouldn't it have been better to post this topic in the Spoiler Forum? Specific/detailed answers would be spoilers and so we can't post them here ...

Here's my vote for the best fighters that are not maximum class VHF:

Patriot with Graviton shield, Xeno guns and cheap homing mines

With this ship you can go anywhere in the civilized systems and the borderworlds between them and fly bar missions up to about difficulty 25 without trouble. The only thing it has to fear there is swarms (>= 6) of class 6 light fighters like the Hawk if they have decent weapons, but heavier ships (Dragon, Stiletto ...) are no trouble because they not manoeuverable enough to hit you except by accident and smaller ships don't have enough firepower to be a threat. With really powerful mines you can venture further out and tangle with bigger fish (Falcon, Hammerhead etc.) in small numbers, just for kicks ... but be prepared to die if you fumble or your luck runs out.

Drake with Graviton shield, Xeno guns and cheap homing mines

With this ship you own all of Sirius except for the home systems of the Corsairs and the Outcasts where the enemies are too tough to take on more than two at a time without trouble (I mean NPC ships, of course ). Best ship for making money fast as a fighter, but limited cargo capacity (loot!). You can even fly it through most of the SP campaign if you plan accordingly; the guns must be looted anyway so you don't have to wait until you have high enough level to be allowed to buy high-class weapons. This is something I plan to do real soon ... The last couple of times I played the campaign with very light ships like Dagger and Patriot (Starflier not finished yet) and consequently it was quite difficult at times. With the Drake it will be the opposite because it is more powerful than the game designers planned for.
