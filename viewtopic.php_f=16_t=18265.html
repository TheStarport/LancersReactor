Sherlog

Tue Jun 03, 2003 12:43 am by Sherlog

The Vultures have an efficiency of 6.00; they are the most energy-efficient weapons in the whole Sirius sector and as such they can make certain parts of the final SP campaign missions much easier. Compared to the Death's Hand guns (nice sounds, no?) they can do almost twice the damage to the target before the powerplant of the Anubis runs dry.

However, there are other guns easily available at that point in the campaign that also have decent efficiency, like the Reaper Mk II (Order) with 5.00 or the Rheinland Flamecurse Mk I with 4.80. The Bundschuh Luger is also nice for equipping the Anubis; efficiency is only 4.00 but it does 50% more damage than the Death's Hand and has very good speed/range (700/700). The GMG Sunblasts are also a very good choice for pulverizing Nomads.

Pretty much everything you can buy at that point in time is good enough to do the job. The only really important question is: what do you want to have fun?
