Which would you choose?

Here's a tough question I posed to some of my friends. They all agreed this is difficult decision to make, but one that can reveal a bit about a person. Just for fun, of course:

Would you rather have $1,000,000 or be given a spot on the next space shuttle and take a trip to the moon?

It's obvious what the benefits of a million bucks are, but it would never buy you a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to actually travel to the moon; to be able to point up at and tell your friends "I've been there."

So, which would you choose?
