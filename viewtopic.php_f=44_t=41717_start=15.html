Bejaymac

Thu Aug 23, 2007 2:15 pm by Bejaymac

Okay folks here's a little freebie for everyone.

It's the Sai from Starlancer, the CMP is multiple component, there are damage 3DBs and best of all it has a close fitting custom SUR that takes weapon and collision damage. The files were created in Milkshape using Colin's plug-ins and HardCMP to orientate and add a couple of hardpoints, and yes that does mean the SUR was built using the SUR exporter, the one most people think is useless (look in the mirror to see what's useless).

The mod is just a small OSP that I use to test ships with, and as a means for you to try the ship to see how it works. The shiparch entry in the script is a copy and paste job, starflier IDs and barracuda handling, the collision groups and simples you will need to keep (you can change the nicknames) as well as the mass of the ship (the SUR is set to 150).

Keep an eye out for other starlancer ships as I'm working on some of the others in between other projects.
