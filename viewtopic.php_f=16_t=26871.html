T-hawk

Tue Dec 16, 2003 6:34 am by T-hawk

Both platforms run on timers. (The "ship destroyed" comms are timed events; they don't actually depend on ships taking damage and being destroyed.) The trick is to go first for the platform closer to you as you approach the shipyard. The two platforms run on different timers; the nearer one starts and expires significantly sooner.

As for weapons, Xeno Adv.Scorpion guns are the highest damage-dealing and most energy-efficient guns you can put on a Patriot. Definitely get these if you're trying to beat the entire campaign in the ship. Before accepting the Transport mission, you can go to Colorado system and kill Xenos until they drop some of these guns, or kill Navy ships until the Xenos like you enough to let you land on a base and buy the guns.
