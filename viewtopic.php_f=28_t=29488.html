Starting faction/ship modpack for MP

I'll be going to work on an update to my Freelancer Starting Mod Pack during spring break. For those who don't know what it's about, here's a quick description:


These Multiplayer Mods will change the starting ship, faction, and position for newly created characters. Tired of starting out as Liberty Navy? Then this mod pack is for you! These mods are designed to change the monotony of starting out the same way. You can choose to start as any of the more common factions. Police or Criminal, Trader or Raider. Great for clan servers too, that wish to specialize as a particular faction. Current factions included are:
Liberty Police
Bretonia Police
Kusari Police
Rheinland Police
Outcasts
Corsairs
Liberty Rogues
Lane Hackers (two versions)
Xenos
Mollys
Bounty hunters
Zoners

Also thrown in is the Juni defender and the Order faction, starting out with the Anubis fighter.


I'd like to know what factions I should add to the next update. If there's enough interest, I'll try making a mod for every faction.
