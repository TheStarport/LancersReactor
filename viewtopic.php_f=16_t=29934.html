Theoretically, you can pretend to be a Liberty Rogue. Grab a Bloodhound or Wolfhound, arm up with Liberty Rogue lasers, head out their with your molecular shield and either defend their convoys or hijack transports near tradelanes. Then head back to Motezuma or Buffalo with your loot. You know you've made it when a Bounty Hunter comes along and puts a Sunslayer up your ion drive :p

The Order vanish from the game once the campaign is over. They have no base, no patrols, and don't appear in the reputation window either. Unless you replay the Order part of the campaign, you can't join or pretend to join The Order.
