Fri Jan 27, 2006 10:17 pm by Doc

You can find a BLUEBLAZE (I think) just a few clicks from the New Berlin jumphole in Sigma-13. Its located in sector C5. There's several Old Imperial Navy Fighter wrecks there. Personally you're better off getting a couple of Skyblasts (Lvl 9) from Gas Miner Naha. You can also get a *Good* level 9 turret of the same name there.
Remember - ALL the codename weapons have a refire rate of 2 (two)/second and gobble LOTS of energy. You're much better off finding level 9 weapons with refire rates of 5/second or better.
If you're really desperate then go to the Nomad world jumphole in the Outcast system; pop through and kill a few Nomads and get their weapons - they don't use any energy. I also think the game gets obnoxious at that point - its just no longer fair or fun when you can't run out of 'bullets' or when you can't die.
As far as shields - your best one is on Crete or tripoli shipyard. So far as I know, there's no level 10 shield in the game. Its advertised but I think the game developers were under pressure to 'get the game out' and just didn't finish a few things.
You always find what you're looking for.
