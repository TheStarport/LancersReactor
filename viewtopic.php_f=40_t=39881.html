Calling All Freeworlds Mod Developers and Testers

If you are a Freeworlds Mod Developer or one of those who has been an aged old veteran tester, then please sign up to the board at the link below this post and PM myself on that board with your details and what part of Mod Development you have been involved in. I don't want to hear 'I would like to help in this bit.' I am only interrested in those who have either created stuff for this mod already or have been assigned tasks by myself or ZaccaR to complete for the mod. If you do not fall into one of those two catogories, you are not a member yet.

Testers: Testing went all over the place before. I only want those signing up to be a tester if you have already activaly been a Tester on the Test Server for this mod (not neccessarily the current Test Version, but as long as you have done some testing on 1.66). By this, it means having spent at least several hours in game and having made contributions to the Mod Testers forums in the previous locations it was stored.

This is the new Independant Freeworlds Mod Development Team Forum. It is not tied to any particular server, it is purely intended for Mod Developers and Testers only to sort the next version of the test mod out. Non-Testers / Developers can still sign up there and you will have access then to the questions board and the Bar as well as being able to view the art of the mod section and the Freeworlds News section. However, pay attention to the rules, there is one warning only and that is NOW. Any Flame or Spam or any unnice posts on there and you're gone. No second chances. Myself and ZaccaR are the admins and you know how zero tolerant we can be sometimes. So there is your warning.

Finally and in big bold capitals:

DO NOT PM ME ASKING TO BECOME A MOD DEVELOPER OR A TESTER. THIS IS FOR EXISTING TESTERS OR DEVELOPERS ONLY.

Just so we are clear.
