Masaki_Ryu

Wed Aug 17, 2005 11:30 am by Masaki_Ryu

why would i redo all that crap?


Because universe.ini holds the info for all the bases and planets, and it's trying to load the bases and planets in the systems you deleted.
To fix it, just figure out every base in every system you've deleted and put them on a list, then find one of those base charts so you can find them in the universe.ini, then simply delete all those entrys.

Hope it helps.
