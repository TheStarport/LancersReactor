The red circle you added was probably an ambient population zone for jumpgate traffic. To add a jumpgate/jumphole, you need to do the following:

1) Double click on the previously existing system you want the jumpgate/hole in
2) Right click on the spot where you want the jumpgate/hole.
3) Select jumphole or jumpgate.
4) When bounced back to the navigation window, double click on the new system and repeat steps 2 & 3.

Be warned, this faeture is a little buggy and the editor may crashe when you attempt to add jumpgates/holes. Keep trying, eventually it will work, but you may need to reboot your computer to get it to go.
