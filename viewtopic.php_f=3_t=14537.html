Sun Apr 20, 2003 3:22 pm by redeye

Like Flak said. I never bothered about the types although loads of people do. Especially after you have finished SP and properly affiliate yourself with a particular faction, you can pick your shield and weapons to best match the strengths and weaknesses of your main enemy. Unfortunately there isnt too much choice at the top end of the shield market and the choice between gun types always seems to come down to max damage and or projectile speed.

If a gun is 'most effective' against a particular shield type it will do +20% damage and the opposite for least effective. Therefore even if you are only fighting one faction a random weapon with 20% more punch will be just as good as a matched weapon plus it will mangle any other enemies 20% more.

I found in the lower SP missions that the sheild type did make a difference, as I saved a game at a point and tried a Molecular sheild with a higher rating than my Graviton and promptly got my butt kicked when attacked by a horde of ships!

At the higher levels it makes less of a difference other than the max rateing you can mount on your ship.

As far as weapons go try getting the max hull damage you can mount. Sometimes I just don't think it makes much of a difference on the guns with mostly sheild damage as I always shoot a missle at them to take those down before we get to it.

I try to mix the weapon types Lazers, proton cannons, etc to have a complimentary load out. I personally like having a missle slot as it does do more damage than a gun even though you have to buy them. DO NOT shoot them at the enemy unless you are coming head on, Have a great side angle shot, or they have a very slow ship and you are right up their tail pipe or you are guaranteed to miss!

Just some observations from the edge of space!
