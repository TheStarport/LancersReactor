Dunno if anyone posted this already, but get one of the Gas Miners Guild Skyblast Turrets. They've got an 8.33 fire rate and do huge amounts of damage, but require a lot of energy. However, if you're putting it on the Eagle, which has one backwards-shooting turret, then it's no problem. You can't fire your primary weapons backwards in turret mode, so it's a great idea to put a Skyblast A or Skyblast B turret there which you can use to fend off enemies while entering a trade lane, docking, anytime that you would be vulnerable to attack from behind and cannot maneuver.

Just my two cents, that's all.
