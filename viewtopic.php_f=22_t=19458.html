Dreglor you mentioned 8000+ accounts. Not bad, but from one server admin to another, out of those 8000+ accounts, how many are "ghost" accounts? The reason I ask is ghost accounts will bog down a server too in my experience.

What are ghost accounts you ask? The ones with no names directly below the MP ID you see within the console. Edit/Accounts and look for the MP ID lines that are all by themselves. Delete those and your server will be a little bit more stable. Ghost accounts are created when someone logs onto your server and doesn't choose a name or deletes all their olds ones.

That a nice tweak for all servers.
