Ijuin

Wed Aug 27, 2003 2:20 pm by Ijuin

The Anibus can outgun any of the ships that you are able to buy before you finish the campaign, so it is to your advantage to buy one unless you prefer to have a more nimble fighter such as the Drake (or if you like a challenge and want to use one of the lower-level ships--I won in a Dagger recently--very cool ^_-). In addition, it carries 70 cargo, which is more than any non-freighter except the VHF ships that you can't buy till you reach level 30+, and it defends itself better than any freighter. However, it does indeed become obsolete once you can purchase a VHF or a Falcon/Valkyrie/Stiletto.

Thus, the Anibus is in essence a niche fighter--it helps you a lot during the final campaign missions and immediately after you finish the campaign, but it becomes obsolete once you can get the really good stuff.
