1) - nope

2) - Change all AI (npc) docking to use something other than berths - like Jumps etc. This will mean that you will then change all docking rings/dock entries to this as well - apart from upon your custom ship. The custom carrier could retain a berth method of docking. This will mean no-AI can dock with the ship at all (as they will be unable to use the docking sphere) as any NPCS docking with a players station caused crashing issues. Next up is the fact that you still have issues with players leaving a server with people docked upon it. That may not be avoided - unless the dock actually docks you with a real base, meaning their ship is a 'docking ring' so to speak (ie - the ship is the method of docking to a base, and not the actual base itself). You may not launch or you will crash at that point.


Will it work? Never tested it, not even interested to be honest. I don't think its going to add anything in gameplay over the 'wow' factor - which always dies damned fast! Anyone is welcome to try it though.

3) Yeah, if it has no matt files, then it will be invisible - kinda like what you have a custom ship that you cannot see etc. You can select it, just cannot see it.
