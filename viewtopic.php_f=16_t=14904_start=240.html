robocop

Fri Apr 21, 2006 3:13 pm by robocop

The class 10 weapons are only available from lootable wrecks. They are the ones listed in capital letters in the lootable wrecks list. Be advised that while they have huge damage potential, they require enormous amounts of power and generally have a refire rate of only 2 shots per second. Not very good for fast moving targets.

If you are over level 34 you can kill Nomads in Gamma, Alpha, and the Unknowns and tractor in Nomad Blasters and Nomad Cannons. Those are nice because they have a higher refire rate (5.33 for Blasters and 4 for Cannons). They do less damage per shot than the CODEWEPS but similar damage per second. The best thing about them is that they don't use energy.
