I find that the easiest trade route is this:
Buy Diamonds on planet Cambridge.
Sell them on planet Leeds.
Buy Optronics on planet Leeds.
Sell them on planet Cambridge.

This trade route involves four system jumps there and back and only 2 dockings, and yields 745$ per cargo space. In a dromedary, that's 204 875$ going there and back once! As added bonus, the highest level enemy you're liable to encounter on this one will level 6, so any player could do this run in his sleep.
