Puke Barwalker

Wed Jul 23, 2003 3:49 am by Puke Barwalker

No one way jump holes that I know of. Now when you go through a jump hole from one system, remember that it's not going to have the same name on the other side. Say you go from Dresden to Frankfurt. On the Dresden side, it says Frankfurt jump hole. On the Frankfurt side, it says Dresden jump hole. But the jump hole should be there. (I think there are 1 or 2 jump holes which simply don't ever show up on the map, even after you've used them. But they are in remote areas you probably won't get until after the game missions are over.)

To see details better, move the cursor over a spot on the map and click the magnifying glass. That should zoom in on it. But jump holes, wrecks, even stations won't show up on a map until you have been in visual range of them at least once.

Maybe you can't see the jump holes because you haven't zoomed in on them.
