Thu May 29, 2003 5:23 am by Kasdia

I wouldn't. You'd turn your back for a second, and your brand new Eagle would be missing, along with all your credits.

Though I'd love to cruise around in the Bebop...

As for the TC, that doesn't really fall into the violation thing as much. Fan made Models and art are one thing, but the actual official music being put into Mp3 and distributed to the public in a Mod would be pretty bad.

Not to mention HUGE.

The TC itself would be difficult too, as while there are lots of ships in the Bebop universe (well, solar system), they're not well informed about. Though seeing the Swordfish II et.al. would be nifty.

That's not to stop you converting those music files over yourself on your own PC, though. Just convert the Mp3s into Wave files, then save those waves into either a 16bit or 8bit, Stereo, Unsigned wave file format, at 22050khz or 11025khz, I think.

Then just rename the file to which music track you want to write over, and voila!
