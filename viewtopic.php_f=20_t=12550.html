*FIX* Text Problems

Heh BOW before me, I have figured out how to fix it. Here is a step by step tutorial just as I did it to fix my friend's copy.

1. Go to your Freelancer\EXE\ folder

2. Look for a file named, "flconfigdatabase.txt" and open it.

3. Scroll down until you see the name of your manufacturer.

4. Now look for your GFX card's model number.

5. Make a new line under it

6. Hit TAB then paste this: TextureOffset

7. Scroll down to the bottom of the model number list and you should see some words like this:

UnsupportedHardware
BrokenAlphaTest
ForceSquareTextures
break

8. Delete the ForceSquareTextures line.

9. Save the file then close it.

10. Run Freelancer.

NOTE: If you can't get it to work post here and I will help you. If it DOES work for you PLEASE post here with your model number and a copy/paste of the edited lines.

This has been tested with the Intel 810 GFX card and it worked no problem.
