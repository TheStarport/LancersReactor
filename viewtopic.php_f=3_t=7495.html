Anyway,if You basically want to enable all the ships in the demo that were suppose to be flyable download this.


In order for it to work RENAME THE FILE to market_ships.ini & place it in your C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer Trial\DATA\EQUIPMENT folder in which it will replace the current Market ships.ini file.


After that youll see that the DEFENDER & Startracker are now available since they are now LEVEL 1 vehicles but they still have the same attributes as they would a normal level 2 or 4 vehicle pertaining to upgradeablility.Just make sure you have enough money or net worth to buy either.
