I have found a way to make the small asteroids highly profitable:

\SOLAR\ASTEROIDS\name_of_asteroid_field.ini
(I used the ew04_napo_nomad_asteroids.ini in Omicron Theta)

[LootableZone
...
asteroid_loot_count = 1, 1
dynamic_loot_count = 1, 1
asteroid_loot_difficulty = 1
dynamic_loot_difficulty = 1

Changing values for the asteroid_loot_count made no difference, even though the "dynamic" asteroids were fun to hit jackpot with every shot.
