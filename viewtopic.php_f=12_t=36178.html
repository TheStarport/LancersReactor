Actually kimk you're on the right track,in a way we men should be girls . Lemme explain *Puts on professor hat*

If you guys know your biology we know that females have XX chromosomes and males have the XY chromosomes. Simple stuff. Then, if you get more technical you look at how after conception males and females develope.

In females X chromosomes creates the Mullerian system, which ends up to later on to become female internal sex organs (Gonads)

In males, the Y chromosome carries the SRY gene. This gene activates the anti-Mullerian hormone. This inhibits the development of the Mullerian system, and gives way to the male system called the Wolffian system.

Since males need to have the SRY gene and anti-Mullerian hormone to develop, whereas females, put simply, do not need any outside hormones or genes, females are known as the "Default race."

This is also why birth defects such as babies without the second chromosome (another X or a Y) end up looking female only without internal sex organs.

A bit longwinded I know, but I'm a biology/psych major so all of this stuff is overflowing in my brain
