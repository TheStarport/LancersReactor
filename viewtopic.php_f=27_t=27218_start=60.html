Note that FMA doesn't like waypoints that cross or end up at Manhattan. Test this by plotting best path to the planet... you will immediately crash to desktop.

You must buy enough commodities and land on Manhattan while at Level 2 WITHOUT plotting a waypoint there (ie. by pilot's eye. But comeon! If you can't identify Manhattan when in the New York System and fly there with your toenails while blindfolded, you don't deserve your freelancer license.)
Now, at Manhattan, you must sell the commodity so as to achieve Level 3 WITHOUT LEAVING MANHATTAN. Juni's mission should trigger, and you SHOULD theoretically be able to meet her at the Bar without any crashes.

Fri Jun 17, 2005 9:18 pm by LeFire

My theory was correct... I successfully did the Level 3 mission and am now Level 4 without crashing.

Thus:
To avoid the Freelancer Massive AddOn 1.1 crash
1.) Buy enough commodities from a planet other than Manhattan... enough so that you level up to 3 when the goods are sold in Manhattan.
2.) Fly to Manhattan WITHOUT using waypoints.
3.) Sell the goods and level up.
4.) Go to the Manhattan bar and accept the mission Juni gives you WITHOUT LEAVING MANHATTAN.
5.) Carry on with the game.

The crash might come back again in future missions that automatically give you a Manhattan waypoint. Will post a solution when I get there.
