Starter Trade Route

The first thing you have to do is bind your cruise control to the tilde key. This way you will have only one key to press for quick escapes because you will definitely need it. Best Ship for the trade is the Rhino Freighter. Money for this endeavor can be gotten by locating the derilects in the New York System. Do not sell the turrents because they can be used on the Rhino Freighter. When you get the Cardamine make sure that you make a bee line to Planet Manhatten.

New York System locate the jump holes to Colorado and Texas.
Texas System locate the jump hole to California.
When you enter the new systems you will be ambushed by the various factions.
Use the tilde key as soon as you finish the jump to minimize the damage.
You will be able to make the most money by flying this trade route after the Ashcroft mission.

Planet Manhatten, New York Consumer Goods Denver, Colorado
Denver, Colorado H-fuel Beaumont Base, Texas
Beaumont Base, Texas Engine Components Los Angeles, California
Los Angeles, California Polymers Houston, Texas
Houston, Texas Ship Hull Balitmore Shipyard, New York

Repeat as Necessary
