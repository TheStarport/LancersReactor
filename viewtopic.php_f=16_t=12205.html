Best mining Sites....Depends what you're looking for.

For Quick Time turnaround, mine Silverton in Colorado, and sell in Manhattan.

For more profit (and alot more time spent travelling from the mine to your selling destination)

Mine Diamonds in Dresden or Omega-11, Sell them in Leeds or New Tokyo
Mine Artifacts in Omecron Gamma, Sell them in Manhattan (most of the Capital Planets...eg: New London, New Berlin etc) have reasonable prices, but Manhattan's highest on my list (at least, for now)

At least, that's the best I've found so far.

It takes less time, and less risk (assuming your mining in a Freighter....it takes much less time, but there's a lot less profit if you do it in a fighter) to buy from one of the stations close to the mining zone.
