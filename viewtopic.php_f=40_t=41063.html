The Dreadnaught can dock at special dreadnaught docking rings at Kuat, Coruscant and Bastion (situated behind the planets). We have changed this for 1.66 however, so that several stations have capital ship docking moors and planets have skyhooks where capital ships can dock at (especially considering that there is ~50 capital ships in 1.66, including the star destroyer and several varients of the mon cal destroyers).

As to the type of background you are asking, try searching this site here for something called the Freeworlds 1.65 visual enhancement patch. I believe you will find this will produce the effect you are after with the 1.65 version (I released it about a month ago). We have already included this in 1.66 as an option upon mod loading and the tests have shown it to be working fine.
