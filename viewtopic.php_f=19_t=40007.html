The difference between Singleplayer and Multiplayer can be huge.
It depends on how you look at it.
If you enjoy playing by yourself against NPC's using the same AI all the time except for some MOD's that crank up the NPC's skills and some that also let the NPC's use Nano bots and Shield batteries.

Multiplayer is what you make of it. Some characters enjoy playing against real pilots thank think on their own. Also some of the online servers have communitys that you can join and meet some new people and even make new friends and some even have websites, teamspeak and or ventrillo servers, IRC, etc..

Teamspeak and Ventrillo are overlooked.
They offer free voice communications, think of a cell phone where you can talk to anyone in the world that has the software and you're on the same server.
Great for talking to people that are playing the same game you are or even talking to your girlfriend or grandmother but no long distance changes as long as you have a computer and internet connection, which you're paying for already so why not use teamspeak or ventrillo and talk for free...

I wish I had more time to play, any games for that matter.
Well that's my opinion anyway.
Although "when I was younger I could remember anything...
Weather it was true or not"...
