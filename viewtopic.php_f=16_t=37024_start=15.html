Something I found out completely by accident that I've checked for on here: it's a very very short route and can be done legitimately.

Start on planet leeds. There's a ship dealer on Planet Harris in the adjacent Tau-31 system that will sell you a Humpback, so get it. If not that, a Dromedary if you can. Buy all the pharmaceuticals you can, then launch, take the Tau-31 jumpgate and sell them at the Holman Outpost. Buy a cargohold-full of Niobium and head back to leeds, and sell it at Stokes Mining station. Each run takes me 9 minutes 10 seconds in a humpback and about 9 minutes in a clylesdale.

Assuming you have a humpback:
Pharmaceuticals - 239x250= 59,750
- 400x250= 100,00
Profit - 40,250
Niobium - 320x250= 80,000
- 640x250= 160,000
Profit - 80,000
Total Spent: 139,750
Total Income: 260,000
Total Profit: 120,250

Considering you can make half this in a clylesdale mid-storyline, it's not too shabby. With a dromedary it's a very decent way of making money as an ended-story freelancer. Enjoy!

Oh and I never used the new tokyo-new berlin route because my game errors - I can never take off from tokyo because the game crashes windows. I just have to avoid it and/or delete my savegame.
