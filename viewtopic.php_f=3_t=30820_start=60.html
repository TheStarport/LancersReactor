Seeing as this is an ideas thread...
To create a game that was more organic and lifelike, i.e. one where the planets actually move, and orbit the sun (even if the change is negligible over the course of the game, so as to promote realism), perhaps you could replace the current tradelanes with trade beacons.
In practice, the trade beacon would function almost exactly like the tradelane, but it would allow for them to move through the system without screwing up travel. The idea for a lane beacon is that it warps the space around it, allowing properly equipped ships to enter the accelerated state that allows faster travel between points. With this method though, the lane could curve around obstacles, instead of having them all go in a straight line. Also, if you only had to be in the vicinity of the beacon instead of going through it, it might make docking a little easier.

Okay I bought Freelancer when it first came out and finished in in the first month...but I've been thinking about it lately...maybe I grow homesick for the Sirius sector again or something.

Anyway, foresure Freelancer 2 needs to be made...and maybe an expansion or 2 for the original before FL2 comes out.

Add...
1) 3rd person...walk around planets, espionage missions against government buildings, ambush outside the bar, kidnap missions with hostage begging to be released, hand to hand combat, pick pocketing in the market area, bounty hunt for guy on a pleasure cruiser while he's in the pool, hit the Casino, take a vacation to a resort planet for a week (all work and no play...lol), explore the dark side of a small moon. I'd also add external space combat in space suits (like in the Intro movie of Freelancer) like a space marine.

2)True ship damage...guns shot up, out of missiles, engine won't fire...etc. Add to this the above space combat in 3rd person, you could be dead in the water, so what do you do...you put on your space suit, grab your portable rail gun and head to the airlock and start defending the small piece of scrap metal you are floating on before you go up in a ball of fire!

3) In atmosphere ship combat...fly across the surface of the planet, attack ground vehicles, buildings, other ships, compete in races (like star wars pod racing) Land in underwater cities.

4) More races, more cities on the planets...800 years have passed since your civilization got here so there should be a much larger population...ability to interact with the general populace too...

5) A universe based on physics...every action has a reaction and an outcome...blow up a star and see what happens (large scale example but you get what I mean...) Planets should move (tracked by onboard computer), new mining sites pop up, new bases/settlements built, space pollution, contruction projects at the ship yard actually get finished...political system in houses, elections, death of the king/queen, etc. Keep the game evolving!!!

6)Create your own customizable character, name, hair colour, cloths, gender, age, maybe species...and starting location.

Well that's enough for now...fingers are crossed for FL2!!
