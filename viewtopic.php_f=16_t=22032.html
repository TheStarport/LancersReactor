It's simple. Make sure you have a light fighter, then hold your fingers over shift+w. When you click the button to start the race, press the keys immediately to enter cruise mode. Take the inside corners of each ring, but avoid crashing into them. When you're attacked, drop Countermeasures if you feel like you need to draw their missile fire away. You'll always win if you follow these rules, and cruise all the way!
