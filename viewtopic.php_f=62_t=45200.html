Openlancer Websites

Openlancer Project Homepage - http://www.openlancer.com: Has news and general information, the development forums and wiki were moved to OpenGameForge.org.

Open Game Forge - http://www.opengameforge.org: New forge site, free hosting for all free open source games and some FL mods if they dual release original assets under an open license.

Source Forge - http://sourceforge.net/projects/openlancer
OL source code on CVS.

IRC: irc.dal.net:6667 #openlancer
General chat, also used by the OL committee for discussing minor issues.
