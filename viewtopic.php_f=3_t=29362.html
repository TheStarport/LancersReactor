CW2.33 Vorlon Dreadnaught: Simply too powerful

I just loaded up the clanwars 2.33. There is a ship in there called the Vorlon Dreadnaught. I'm all for having powerful ships, but when compaired to the rest of the galaxy this thing is rediculous. Has four thrusters and afterburns at a cool 870. There are seven lvl 10 gun/missle mounts, 7 turrets, 2 Torps and holds two shields. I loaded it with 7 Boes missle launchers firing at 8.33/tps and 10 nomad lazers. since you can mount whatever you want just about wherever you want. With Borg shields it's got 110,000 shield points.

This would be awesome if there was anything in the galaxy that could make you break a sweat. I started on another sever today and withing wo hours of starting out with my starflyer I had a dreadnaught loaded for bear and a nice 40 million in the bank. -Do you see a problem here?

If the idea was to create a Mod that is harder, has more places and offers an environment for PVP wouldn't it be better to have ships that might actually be vulnerable to some form of attack. Making planets jump out infront of the thing doesn't count...

There's some cool guys on the servers, I really enjoyed meeting them but I might have to go back to evolutions or Real Space 2.2 for a challenging game. The fact is with me in that ship you can't hit me with more than a stray shot or two. Never enough to worry about.
