The USB (United Systems of Browclopsia) is a union of clans that has agreed to recognize the laws and territories of the member clans.

In addition, Federal laws may be created and are empowered by the members of the USB. These federal laws encompass all territories that the members agree to be Part of the USB territories or Federal territories. All clan territories are by default federal territories. This means that each member clan may assist in enforcing USB laws in any territory that is considered federal territory. These enforcement activities are not invasive as they are helping with the common agreed apon law of the Union. When, how, and by whom this the federal laws are enforced are up for negotiation. The member clans will enforce federal laws in their own territories and will receive assistance only if they are unable to apply adequate force. If a law is disagreeable, then it will not be a Federal USB law.

The USB is a team of clans. We are united to help eachother. If one member gets attacked, then the other members should help. Or we are united to help in role-playing wars possibly in civil wars over new territories.

Basically, the main laws so far are is to respect eachother territories and laws while in those territories. And Mutual assistance, which means that a member clan may ask for assistance in a conflict and assistance should be given. It in implied that each clan has right of passage through all other member territories.

Additional laws will be created by the members. If AE decides to become a member, then they will be able to take part and help mold the USB into something that is beneficial to them as well. If AE decides to remain hostile, then they may suffer when other clans decide to join and they are eventually out numbered.

SD as far as I know is allies and united with GOV. SD and GOV are the only members of the USB as of right now. AE seems to be very hostile to eveyone except for the AE leader pinkie/invincible.
