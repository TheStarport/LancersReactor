Nevermind, here was the reason for anyone else who may encounter it. If you have Ahead Nero installed (CD/DVD burning software), then one of its video codecs triggers that warning due to faulty checking in some versions of copy protection (freaking idiots). Goto "%SystemDrive%\Program Files\Common Files\Ahead" and rename the folder "DSFilter" to something like "_DSFilter" and things will run fine then, however Nero dies if you try to record DVD's and such, so remember to change it back for that.

Why this does not trigger in normal Freelancer I have no clue, but the idiots who chose this copy protection deserve to hang by their testicles (or other equally painful part). Enjoy your day.
