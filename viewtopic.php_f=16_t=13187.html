Actually, it doesn't really require any sort of higher ship, because the mines kill you instantly no matter your hull or shields. But there's a way through them. If you look at the jump gate, you can see the direction in which it opens. If you keep travelling around the mine field so that you're "right in front of" the jump gate, as in, directly in front of the part that opens, then there's a huge space in the mine field for you to go through. I think this opening is somewhere around the bottom left corner of the minefield. If you look on your map of Zone 21, at the bottom left, there is a small brown circle, and then below it, depicting the "corner" of the mine field, a small brown circle with part of it missing, the entrance is somewhere between those two. But Annylina is right. In MP, there's really no reason to go there. But it's a pretty creepy feeling while driving through the mine field, nonetheless.
