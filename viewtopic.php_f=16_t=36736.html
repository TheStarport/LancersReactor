A Strange Weapons Situation

First of all, I want you to know that there are some minor spoilers included in my question. For those of you who played the game for any length of time, these spoilers aren't really spoilers but rather a common knowledge.

My question has to do with obtaining Nomad Energy Blasters.
I am NOT looking for Nomad Energy Cannons.
I am looking for Nomad Energy Blasters and Nomad Energy Blasters only.
My question has NEVER BEEN asked before (other than myself asking this question in the "wrong" "non-spoiler" forum. Hence the need to re-ask this question in the proper channel.

******************************My Situation ********************************
After completing the single player campaign and decided to explore the rest of the universe a little more. Particularly, I am interested in searching for Nomad Energy Blasters, which requires zero energy to use. I found the location of nomad fighters in zone 4F in the Omicron Alpha system by accident while I was looting those Outcast Fighter wrecks nearby the unknown wormhole. After killing Nomad Fighters in the area for a while, I noticed that they were dropping Nomad Energy Blasters and Cannons. After collecting a number of these, I flew to the nearest friendly planet and sold some of them, keeping only the ones needed for equipping my ship (which happens to be a VHF Sabre). Alas, I goofed and I sold all but 3 Nomad Blasters and 1 Nomad Cannon, mistaking the cannon for the blaster! So I flew back to zone 4F in Omicron Alpha system and started hunting Nomad Fighters again.

Now, this is where the problem arises. Before, I was obtaining a nomad weapon from about one out of every 4 or 5 nomad fighters destroyed. But after revisiting the zone 4F, and after having killed well over 100 nomad ships, I found ZERO nomad weapons.

What am I doing wrong? Why did it work before but not work again the second time? Any helpful assistance would be greatly appreciated. Thank you in advance.

Puzzled and Frustrated,

nickster
