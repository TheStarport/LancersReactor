One of the most impressive looking NPC incidents I've seen was in Dublin, around Battleship Essex.

Myself and a few squad-mates were heading to the Hood via Essex when we got chased by a unit of around 6 Mollys. We didn't bother to fight them and carried on toward the Essex, when another group of Mollys joined the chase.

When we reached the Essex, as well as the usual Armed Forces fighter welcome, the battleship opened up with one of it's primary weapons, something that I've never seen before in the game. A massive, white, corkscrew-effect blast narrowly missed frying one of the Bloodhounds.

OK, not technically a an NPC dogfight, but it made me think twice about messing with Battleships in the future...
