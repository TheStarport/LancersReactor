I've figured out how to get the game to work in a single player game. First start a game with no mods activated. You may have to do a tools/restore backups as I did. Then make sure your option to automove saved games is dechecked. If you have it checked, uncheck it. Start a new game with no mods running. Complete mission one and go land on Manhattan. Save your game. Exit the game. Bring FLMM up and then activate the WTS mod. Now, Start the game and load your saved game and it should work. However, after getting the mod to work I'd like to take this opportunity to report several bugs. 1. I found the first hidden jumphole to one of the new systems but when I emerged from the other side the game crashed. It does it every time. 2. this concerns the third mission when I'm to go with walker to willard research station when it's attacked. Once there I see all kinds of NPC ships there but the gunboats are 3 to 4 kilometers out and when I try to reach them to destroy them the game anounces that I'm about to leave the mission area. I tried several times to do this and when I follow then and destroy them I am presented with a load screen saying I've fled the mission. I kept at it and finally, after about the fifth time flying the mission, I caught and destroyed them then went back and finished killing off the bombers and other NPC ships but then I sit for 45 minutes before I decided to restart the mission again. I finally figured out how to get around this. Go for the Gunboats as soon as possible. Carry missles as these will be useful in catching and destroying them quickly. Then go back and shoot down the bombers first then shoot all but one of the remaining baddies. Then shoot the remaining baddies but do not destroy it. Let the good NPC ships finish killing it off and the game will procede. These are the only bugs I've to report at this time. I got board with it as I could not access any of the new systems so I went back and reloaded the Firebase mod and am enjoying the new silencer ship mod..really kewl ship..

Later and good luck out there.



Mr. P
