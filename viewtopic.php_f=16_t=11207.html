How to win?
Theories vary, but a good try seems to be to NOT activate your cruise engines (then your opponent wont do it as well) and fly the course that way. Or simply try it several times - often enough the enemy racer AI makes some stupid mistake and falls back a considerable step.

As for "wchich ship" - it doesn't matter since by default, all ships are equally fast in Freelancer, no matter if its a fighter or freighter - the only thing is that some of the more 'bulky' ships handle a bit worse (turn/pitch/yaw).
