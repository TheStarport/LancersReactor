warzog

Wed Jul 21, 2004 11:17 am by warzog

A couple of problems I've found...
Some may have been reported before, but here they are:
1) Entries get repeated every time you re-edit a file.
(Usually the last entry made gets repeated-IE 2 identical lines.)
2) Adding a new base messes up the Market_Ships.ini file.
(Any previous new bases get an extra oe_package line, and after a while, you get lines of numbers, without a package type.)
3) Robots used for the "Space Costume" entry, for bases is messed up.
The entry should be: space_costume = , robot_body_e, but is often space_costume = 0, robot_body_e.
The "0" causes an error that occassionally causes a CTD.
(And it doesn't matter which robot, A-E.)
4) Having problems with Nebulae.
Setting a "Fog" color, causes CTD's.
Haven't been able to get one to work without crashing.
5) Bases in the Universe.ini file messed up.
FLE places bases at the bottom of the file, as an append would.
Some bases don't work unless they are placed Before the System entries.
6) Adding to, or Editing, Zones is messed up.
Adding to, or Editing, an existing Zone doesn't show all of the current settings.
So, upon editing, all of the old settings are reset.
7) Editing infocards is messed up.
If you try to edit an old infocard, it simply makes a new one, instead of allowing you to change the old one.
Changing, or Creating names.
If a name is being used by FL, FLE points to that name, and doesn't create an entry in the dll.
Particularly annoying when, as I did, I named a jumphole Manchester, but I got Manchester Jump Hole, which I didn't want.
So, modifying the name is impossible, have to create a new entry.
9) Exclusion zones.
Sometimes linking exclusion zones to asteroid fields works, sometimes it doesn't.
Haven't figured out what causes that.
10) There are some particles/items that don't exist, or don't work, tracking them down, to eliminate CTD's is difficult, shouldn't they be removed from FLE?
11) Combination POP zones would be nice.
In FL there are several "single_base_law, jumpgate" and "single_base_unlaw, wormhole" types of pop zones.
12) POP zone comments/names.
There is an entry for naming the Zone, but it doesn't show up in the System's INI file.
Makes editing the "single_base_law, jumpgate" and other entries hard to do.
13) Zone/Field comments.
While the name's for fields do show up, any comments do not.
Overlapping, similarly named fields, become confusing without the comments.

Now that I've said all of that, I'd like to say that I love the program, and that I realize that adding, or changing these things may be nigh on to impossible, but if they can be, it would be very helpful.
Thanks for all the work you've done on a great modding tool!

Watch your 6!
