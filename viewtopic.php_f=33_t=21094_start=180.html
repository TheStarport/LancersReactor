Ways to get money in Rebalance Mod.

Do missions with Weapon Platforms in it. They drop Deep Scanners worth 12k each! Can be sold anywhere.

Treasure Looting for Cardamine, since you wont be using all the weapons from the ship wrecks, sell them for extra dough..

If you are starting a new game in SP? I suggest you go loot Cardamine from London, Leeds and NY and California. They are all close and only a JHole away, if you know the ones that were added in Rebalance.

Freighting/Trading is also another way to earn money. Or you can hijack other enemy ships holding precious cargo. Just hang out at the jumpholes near NY-Texas for Cardamine and Artifacts. And the NY JH in Texas for Artifacts. You can even wait at the Magellan-Cortez Jh for Engine Components to be sold at Magellan for 1.3k each.
