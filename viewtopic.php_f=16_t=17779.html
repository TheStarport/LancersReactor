Spiro

Tue May 27, 2003 1:40 pm by Spiro

The best way to align yourself criminally early on is to shoot Bounty Hunters or Xenos in NY until the Rogues or Junkers are neutral, then start taking missions for them. Junkers won't improve your rep with the Rogues much, but you'll become friends with them, and their missions are against BH and Xenos, which is good. Once you're good with the Rogues, progress to Lane Hackers. If you go an all fighting no trading way, go to Mactan (Magellan) then to the Islay Base (Gaians, Magellan), through the Tau systems, then the GC and Dragons in Kusari (no Farmers Alliance of Hogosha, they'll improve yopur rep a bit with the Police or Samura), then into Rheinland for Bundschuh, Unioners and Hessians, then onwards into the Omegas and Omicrons. If you trade early on you can skip Bretonia, they're boring. But it's best to go through all four because you'll be taking missions that are in your ship's capabilities. Going straight from Liberty to Kusari will get you dead quick because of the big jump in mission difficulty. Liberty -> Rheinland is suicide unless you have a high-level escort with lots of cash, but then it would be boring.
