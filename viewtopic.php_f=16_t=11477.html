Crash-Drive

Mon Mar 24, 2003 6:42 am by Crash-Drive

You can go into the clouds of explosive gas without getting wasted, just slow down and be careful. There is often stuff to find in the central area of such clouds. But, it's much safer to do with the class-10 shield, of course. It can be funny to hang out inside one of those clouds (or minefields) and listen to the radio chatter of the AI pilots as they cruise around in there, getting themselves messed up... they're really bad with mines. Anyway, I know there is one of those clouds next the the Sigma-17 jumphole in Sigma-13, but the jumphole isn't inside it -- just go around it.

If you want to go to Omicron Alpha from Omicron Theta, that jumphole is weird. The map won't display a label on it, and your sensors call it an "unknown object" when it's in range. It's in the upper right part of the system; I don't remember the exact coords.
