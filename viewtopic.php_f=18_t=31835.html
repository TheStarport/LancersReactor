Wasabe

Tue Jun 01, 2004 9:03 am by Wasabe

Well, the EASIEST way is to change all your encounters in patrol paths. So, instead of:

encounter = patrolp_assault, 8, 1.00000

change them to:

encounter = area_assault, 8, 1.00000

That will eliminate the crashes and is the easiest way to do it. However, that does have drawabacks - the npcs will not take down trade lanes, etc. If you really want to have proper patrol paths, prepare for a LOT more work. You will need to create paths that connect to a jump hole or a base, need to use the correct encounters for the correct type of path and faction, and some other little details.

But I would suggest that for now, you go with eliminating any of the patrolp_assault, tradep_trade, etc. Change those to area_assault and area_trade, etc and your crashes will go away. =)
