Hi all,
I've been busy modding the ini files and have found a few things; probably nothing that you guys don't know already. I haven't been able to recreate that 'negative worth' trick no matter what I do (I've tried making certain comodoties negative in worth and many other permitations but it doesn't seem to work for me). One neat trick is to alter the ships available at Manhattan. Since the ship dealer has 3 stands here, you can view three ships (other bases only seem to have one stand) at a time by changing the lines found here in market_ships.ini, for example:

[BaseGood
base = Li01_01_base
marketgood = oe_package, 0, -1, 1, 1, 0, 1, 1
marketgood = lf_package, 0, -1, 1, 0, 1, 1, 1
marketgood = le_package, 0, -1, 1, 0, 1, 1, 1

(first digit seems to be your required level, second is friendliness and third needs to be 1 if the ship is to be made available. Not sure about the other digits yet).
gives you the two liberty ships and the kick-arse Anubus from the order; A snip at just over 1,000 credits! Just mess around with these and place whichever three ships you want to look at. Some ships aren't available in the demo but you'll be supprised how many are; lots of freighters are available, including the nice looking Bretonion one. The Kusari ships look a bit daft with their dragon wings, I think the urge to place 'culture markers' on the house ships got a little out of hand there. A wwII mitsubishi zero colouration with a nice big red hinomaru and maybe some neat Japanese house emblems would have done the trick in a more stylish manner. It's a shame other interesting cultures aren't represented in the game too but I suppose that's because they were incinerated in starlancer.
I'm very interested in any news on the UTF file format; I'd like to write a filter for my 3d modelling software if I get my hands on it (did the same for homeworld and numerous other games).

Tim.
