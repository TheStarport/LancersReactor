Barragers are the best 8.33 Photon guns up to level 6. Works good on pesky lil Xenos once their shields are down. Try throwing at least 3 Mark 3 Barragers (Level 6) onto a Dromedary, but watch your power bar. You can get them at Kreuzberg Depot in New Berlin-check the patrol routes to find it.
