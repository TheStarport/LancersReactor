Fl multi ship or just one.

Hi guys, I keep popping in from time to time to see the news, don't get much time to look in on you lot for long though.

As an Elite fan, I love the freedom of deciding your own destiny and not having to worry if this is the last misson in the game. Then I found Terminus and... What a surprise, I can have several ships going at once, selecting the one that suits the current mission without selling up and starting over. No more struggling with cargo in a small fighter or getting into strike missions with a big and heavy cargo ship. It would have topped it nicely by allowing you to employ pilots to fly one ship while you flew the other. You could then have your own ship escorting you on trading missions or pilot the fighter yourself to escort your own cargo. Since I never found an upper limit to the number of ships I could have, a rich person could create their own fleet and take over the area.

I just read Mad Dogs review and FL sounds good. However, there's just one question I'd like to ask.

Are we stuck with just one ship or can we berth others so we can have a fighter and a trading ship going at the same time? Or is it back to the days of Elite where you have to decide if your ship can handle any given mission?

Cheers guys.
