Radeon slowdowns?

From DA:

During our internal testing we've seen very poor text rendering performance with the latest drivers for various Radeon video cards (i.e. 7000, 7500, 8500 & 9700). Freelancer will lag when various menus are accessed. This is an external problem and future versions of ATI’s catalyst drivers will hopefully resolve this issue. There is a known workaround. You have to adjust the hardware acceleration slider. This slider can be located on the display properties troubleshooting tab. The following steps may be followed:

1. Access the control panel.
2. Choose the Display Properties icon.
3. Select the Settings tab.
4. Click on the Advance button.
5. Locate the Performance tab and you can easily locate the Hardware acceleration slider.
6. Lower this slider by one notch.

Hope that helps...
