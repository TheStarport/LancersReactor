http://pc.gamespy.com/pc/fallout-3/529744p1.html

I never really realized what the deal was with Fallout in and on itself, but I did play (and still have) Fallout 2, and found it to be one of the most in-depth games i ever played, I only played a little bit of the first town and wandered around killing things and exploring (experiencing the random sequences too, the most memorable being the one where a gang of 3 huge, chaingun-handed mutants, shoots an old man for disobeying them).
