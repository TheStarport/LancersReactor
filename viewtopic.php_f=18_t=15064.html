Ut

Thu Apr 24, 2003 4:23 pm by Ut

Now, was this a request, or a demand? Because people don't usually take too kindly to demands. You want twenty systems? And I take it, you want EVERYONE to make 20 systems... Have you any idea how long it takes to make a system? First you have to decide the size of the system. The you have to figure out how many planets you want, how many stations you want, if you want tradelanes or not, which systems it should link to, and finally what kind of system it should be (ice, sulfer, stone, scrap, whatever). Then you have to decide where to put the planets and stations, what kind of planets and stations they'll be, who runs them, what they'll sell, and how much things will cost there. After that, you have to make the ROOMS for each of these bases; if you want custom planets, you also have to model a 3d landing pad, skin it, and place all the hardpoints and write all the code to make it work properly. Then you have to populate these bases, and come up original names for them all. After this, the system background must be chosen, the nebulae and debris fields must be made, with exclusion zones as well. You have to figure out if you want the system to be radioactive, or just a nebula or two, or none of the above. You have to place wrecks, decide what will spawn from them, create encounters, sound bites, music, and ships. AND, you want it all right now, again and again and again, and for free. A project like that would take MONTHS. Even with more than one person working on it, no one's made a complete system yet. Everything is still WIP, because it takes a !@#$ long time to put everything into place and troubleshoot it.

So, if you want twenty new, original systems per week, complete with new backgrond art and music, as well as new ships, pilots, factions, and whatever else, I suggest you either ask VERY VERY nicely, or become VERY VERY good and VERY VERY fast at doing it yourself.
