I whipped this together:

Freelancer 2 Mod:

After the Nomad invasion (no clue how long):

Rhieland government has colapsed. The criminals (Cosairs and Outcasts)
and the GMG have take these systems for themselves. Liberty has most of the
corporations under it's wing. Britonea is mostly the same; but has expanded
into old Rhieland systems. The Kusari have become xenophobic after
the Nomad invasion.


Houses:

Rhieland:
- old governemnt collapsed
- outer sectors near Omegas/Omicrons have been taken over by other factions
(GMG, Cosairs, Outcasts)
- new government: not sure what its going to be called yet; "elected" dictator
who has support from all ex-Rhieland companies and from military/police; famous
general who rose to power and took over after the fall of the government
- still a powerful military might

Kusari/GMG:
- Kusari/GMG are allies
- hold complete control of H-fuel supplies
- jumpgates into Kusari/GMG space have been locked down/guarded by
military
- the research station (I forget the name) has discovered some new
technology ("Uh Oh"

Liberty:
- since Rhieland's fall has gotten stronger fighters and more
powerful weapons ("salvaging" basically stealing...)
- economic power of the Siruis sector; cheap prices; except for H-fuel, which
is exptreamly expensive
- Liberty has cleared the systems between them and Britonia of criminals (they
think...)

Britonea:
- has very close ties with Liberty

Others:

The Cosairs:
- have a 1 or 2 allies
- have an power influence over governments
- control many more systems
- slowly becoming less "criminal" (ie: less raiding); more "country-like"
- this refers to the point made above:
- have more classes of ships
- have "resource" bases; outposts where mining happens
- about 1/4 the size (area controled/poly power) of an actual
house
- still willing to kill Outcasts

Outcasts:
- have allied with most other criminal factions in the southern section of
Sirius (no Xenos)
- are now the more feared/larger criminal organization
- are being over-run and pushed out of the Omicron systems (bye bye Malta);
retreating/setting up in the Tau systems (other side of the sector)


So....these are some of my brief ideas...what do you think? I'm not sure
what we will have the player do...I don't think anyone has managed to create
"story" missions...but...we shall see.

Also, I will not engage in illegal activities (ie: hacking the code, copying
copyrighted stuff, not giving credit {I consider illegal } and other fairly
obvious stuff) so if this project is moving towards the fact that it will not
be possible to make without hacking the actualy code we will not finish it.

I am currently trying to get in contact with "New Universe Mod"'s creators to
see if we may mutually benefit from ideas flying back and forth. I am also
trying to see what they are implimenting so I am not just copying from them...

That is all,
_Saber_
