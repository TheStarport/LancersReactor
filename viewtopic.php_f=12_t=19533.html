1. Upload your sig image to a host.
2. Place the exact link to your image in the Signature box in the Your Profile link.
3. Enclose it with the img tags. You can see the proper formatting here.

Some general guidelines.
Try to be courteous with your sig. Try to only use it (select and unselect "Check here to include your profile signature." below replies when you need to) once per thread, and make sure it isn't too large. A lot of people have broadband but some still have dial-up, plus large sigs tend to clutter threads. Thanks
