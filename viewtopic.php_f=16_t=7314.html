Sat Mar 01, 2003 5:13 am by sabaphish

I had alot of trouble with this one too, but I finished it with the Rhino:

3 Missile (or 2, I forget how many gun slots the Rhino has. one was a seeker, the other two the Javelin or something. Variance is just to be able to sustain a regular firing of missles)
3 RoF=2.0 turrets (since guns only fit into 3.5-ish categories anyways)
2 EMP, RoF=8.33 turrets (the 3.5th gun)
1 mine.

Emp, fire missles continuously, mine when he/she/it ("sheit" for short) is ~200/300 and strafe away so that stupid mine doesnt decide to tag you . If target not dead, then just let him fly away (don't chase w/afterburners), and just mine/turretview the other guys off your tail. Rinse and repeat, heal shields when they empty.

Needless to say, this is really expensive .

Optional: shoot Juni for being so damn useless.
