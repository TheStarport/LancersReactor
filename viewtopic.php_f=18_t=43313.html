Fri Apr 21, 2006 1:12 am by Chips

Freeworlds changed a lot of the feel of Freelancer's hud.

If, for example, you open UTF editor - then navigate to data\interface\hud - then open a file like contact_loot.3db , click on the little + icons to open the trees up.
Find texture library, click on it's sub node - which is the name.tga, and then onto the MIP0 node.#

Click "export", and literally you will be saving the icon as a tga image - so type in "name.tga" - the name of it's node remember?

I would expect you can edit the image (not tried this part admittedly) and then import it back as a tga - named exactly the same. There may be more to this, never really thought it was worth changing any of the icons because they.... well, fit so damned well anyways!
