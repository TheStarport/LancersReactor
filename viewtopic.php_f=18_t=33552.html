CRUISE_DISRUPT_TIME is the time your engine stays disrupted after being hit by a cruise disruptor. The constant you want is in the section for each individual engine (in engine_equip.ini), change "cruise_charge_time" to a different value.

Mephistopheles

Sun Sep 26, 2004 9:47 pm by Mephistopheles

Download the Freelancer misc database. It shows you what nickname belongs to which ship. The engine for the bw_fighter (=dagger), for example, is then named bw_f_engine01 or something like that, not so hard to figure.

EDIT: Oh, and this automatically affects NPCs if you don't change their loadouts (i.e. dont give them other engines but the default ones).
