What do you mean?
There are several ways of making money: You can mine, trade or do missions.

Missions are more profittable because you can also sell the loot you gain when you kill a fighter (tractor it with Default key B ). But you need to be a good fighter because if you survive with heavy damage the profit is low.

Trading is a good and fairly save way of making money. You need to have some money to get started ofcourse, but once you can buy a full hold of valuable stuff for a low price, you can make money quite easily. Just be carefull not to die, because you loose all your cargo and thus the money you spend on it.

Mining is a low-cost operation. You mine the comodities (Coper, Water, Oxigen, etc...) by shooting the small floating astroids and tractor in (B ) the loot. Where ever you sell it you will gain a certain amount of profit. Depending on the comodity and the station/planet you sell it, the profits can be very high. (Up to 2000 credits!)

All this can be done in a fighter aswel as in a frieghter. Naturally for missions you're better of in a fighter (like the Crusader) and for trading/mining you're better of in a freighter.

Since you are still in the storyline, I sugets you stay with the fighter, because switching to a new ship costs a lot of money and the Crusader has actually a nice cargohold to make a few bucks.

When Juni calls you again to do a mission with her and you think you need more money, you can just ignore her and do missions or trade some more until you thinks you're rich enough. Juni waits forever (Man she's desperate isn't she ).

Well, hope this helped you. Have fun and stick around.
