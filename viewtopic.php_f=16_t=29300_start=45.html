Sun Mar 21, 2004 8:06 pm by NukeIt

I got bored of the higher end of the ship spectrum. It made too many systems utterly...not challenging to hunt in. 1 hit kills, no matter how easy the money is, aren't all that exciting, and only fighting in the border worlds all the time would get a little dull after a while, so I went the lighter route, and now things are a bit more interesting. Two loadouts are useful, one for killing shielded enemies, and the other for Nomads. The latter crams just about every bit of armor killing firepower and rapid refire goodness that can be crammed into a class 3 LF(preferrably without risking an irreplaceable Ripper mine launcher, instead using the next best thing). I die just about every other time I try it, but it's some quality entertainment when it comes off clean with a few Nomad guns to drag home and sell.

BTW, it IS possible to get through the heavily irradiated systems (even 11 and 41 in the most deadly sections, if you keep the visit short) in a Starflier or Patriot, it just takes good timing with the nanobots, and knowledge of how fast you can reach the nearest safe port. Fighting in those areas isn't the brightest of notions.
