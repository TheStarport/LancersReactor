I have had that happened to me a few times, best solution I found was to run Norton's Win Doctor. Do everything and fix everything it tells you to. Then uninstall FLMM from your system. Make sure the directory has been deleted. Go into your registry and delete everything relating to FLMM and then reboot your system.

After that Defrag the harddrive, reinstall FLMM and it now works until the next time.
