God damn i hate IE 5. I just hit back to check something then it wipes my post.... grrr.... anyway heres a shorter version of it :

Usually i head north from liberty. Not picking a good/bad path as such, keeping my rep fairly static by taking missions for the good guys but also blowing the hell out of any of their cargo ships i see for loot

By the time you get to kusari you should have enough cash to get started in your chosen career. I think it makes a good starting point cause liberty and bretonia are pretty crappy with regards to guns/ships/equip to buy whereas rhineland is good but has some enemies that can be problematic early on. Personally i would buy a hawk/drake and get my ass to a pirate base, hoist the jolly roger and start lane hacking You can just as easily become a cop/scavenger or trader from this point tho, hopefully the journey from libery should have given you a clue as to which you would prefer.
