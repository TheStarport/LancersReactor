Sun Jul 27, 2003 3:17 pm by Saiyan

It was my character Azrakai, but Wiz deleted the post because I hadn't signed up at that time. Heres his profile.
The man...

My Character is a mercanary called Azrakai San . Is of Kusari origin and about 6'1. Has long Spiked black hair and a large scar across his left eye. Dresses like a combination between Blade and Neo. Is a master at Martial Arts specialising in his clans custom Saiya-Kwon. Prefered weapons are a pair of high powered blaster pistols (FR-34's) which are banned through out Sirus due to their destructive nature. Also uses a variety of throwing daggers and a long Katana sword which is kept in a holster on his back. Has also been known to use a Naginata in certain circumstances.

His Ship...

The name of his ship is Yusei-Gachi (which when translated means - Winner through superiority) a name which suits it well. The Yusei Gachi was once a standard AP-8050 Hammerhead which has been heavily customised. Instead of it flying the way a standard Hammerhead would fly it actually flies on it side (the standard Hammerhead flies standing vertical, while Azrakais ship flies horizontally). A good way of demonstrating this is go to the Hammerhead picture in the pilotable ships guide and rotate it once anti-clockwise). While this may seem unorthadox it allows the Yusei-Gachi to have increased handling and in the hands of a pilot as skilled as Azrakai can be compared to the Dragon in terms of agility. It is armed with a pair of Reaver Mk II's (which were 'confiscated from a Junker in the Texas system) a pair of Tizona Del Cid's (which were taken from a Corsair who offended the Azrakai's father) and a pair of Skyblast A's as well as a Skyblast B turret (which were all obtained using a contact in the GMG). Azrakai has installed a Sunslayer Torpedo launcher and removing the original Mine placement has replaced it with a rear firing Cannonball missle launcher (to deter those who would wish to follow the Yusei-Gachi. Should his mission be to capture a target Azrakai keeps a Paralyzer missle launcher in his hold which when mounted replaces one of the Reaver Mk II's. Six Statis chambers have been outfitted in the cargo hold at the cost of 15 storage units. The Yusei-Gachi incorporates a level 8 graviton shield purchased through a contact on Malta (Azrakai's got quite a few contacts out there).
The Yusei Gachi is one of the very best ships that the Saiyan fleet has to offer, used only for their most dangerous missions. This is pleases Azrakai as it allows him to use his ship to its fullest potential, but if he were to fail using a ship of this caliber, the shame to his honor and family ultimate.

The Saiyans...

His mercanary clan is called "The Saiyans" and they operate out of a Base not to disimilar to "The Arch". They are they elite in the sense that they never fail to complete a mission and rarely ever lose a soldier in battle. If a Saiyan is struck down then their body and ship are both destroyed so that their souls can wonder the stars freely. They work as unoffical special forces team for large corporations and occasionally the houses and will never betray a deal. But be warned, if a Saiyan is double crossed, the whole wrath of their clan will be brought to bear on those who dared to defy them.

Azrakai is the son of a Saiyan Noble and posesses the extraordinary reflexes, speed, agility and strength of his kin (it is unknown if genetic engineering is repsonsible for this but it would explain a few things) his fathers name is Harako San and is one of the greatest Saiyan warriors in the clans history. This fact pushes Azrakai to excel himself so that he doesn't have to live in his fathers shadow and can earn his place in the Saiyan's Pride. A hall where the greatest Saiyan warriors are honored.

Azrakai is cool and calculated but on occasion has a tendancy to let his rage get the better of him a slaughther anyone that gets in his way. This trait is not unique to Azrakai though, it has been recorded through out the ages and greatly increases a warriors fighting ability (possibly a by product of genetic engineering)

Allignment: To those his masters wish him to serve.
