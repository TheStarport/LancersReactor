Nickless V2

Mon Nov 22, 2004 4:01 pm by Nickless V2

Untill you finish the storyline completely, there's no point in becoming good with pirates.
After the storyline, (you'll notice. You'll be dropped back on Manhattan again and don't get shot at by the Liberty Police etc.) You just have to shoot everything that's green. Police, Navy and corporations are the targets of choice. Bounty hunters are enemy of all pirates, so they are good targets aswell.
Best is to either kill some transports or destroy some depots. Switch to "loot" in you scanner hud, lower left part and then click the little box-icon, it will show you the depots, if they are around.
After you get a neutral reputation, try doing some missions for teh pirates.
A bribe is always handy, but if you rep is realy bad, it'll cost you around 100k credits.

Good luck
