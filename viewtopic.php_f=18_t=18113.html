First off thanks people!

As is said in my last post, i now have a green window!You have to change the Dc-value of cv_glass.The numbers u see if u use the UTF-Editor's EDIT-function are color-values, the following ones are from the Eagle:

0.654902 - red
0.529412 - yellow
0.317647 - blue
0.000000 - green

1 seems to be the upper limit and means 100% of this color is used.Now you can change the window color very easily!
