If startign a new character

1. hit the wrecks in New York, flint especially
2. Raid the depots in New york that are near the Detriot trade lane - that also gives you Safe rep for lane hackers so you can then trade later.
3. hit the wrecks in Cali/cortez/shikoku

By this time you should have enough to buy a Dromedary, get it and then Do a single run from Manhattan to Auni in Hokkiado with Lux Goods and return to Rapongi in Tokyo with Cardamine, repeat Lux Goods - Cardamine from Rapongi to Hokkiado. 5-7 mins flight time each way with no-one hassling you expect for the odd lane hack or human pirates.

Once you have made 2mil head off for you ship of choice, this takes on average 2hours maybe 3 if no wrecks are full.
