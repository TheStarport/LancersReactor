Wed Jul 16, 2003 6:21 am by Shadowrunner

this did it for me:
In weapon_equip.ini

nickname=fc_or_gun01_mark03
ids_name=263222
ids_info=264222
;DA_archetype=equipment\models\weapons\li_plasma_blaster.cmp <-- old
;material_library=equipment\models\li_equip.mat <-- old
DA_archetype=equipment\models\weapons\rh_gamma_beamer.cmp
material_library=equipment\models\rh_equip.mat
...
...
The thing to remember here is to copy both the 'DA_archetype=' and 'material_library=' lines together.

Hope it helps...
