Try shooting down a few dozen Bounty Hunters. Killing Bounty Hunters improves your reputation with everybody. After killing four or five dozen of them (if you also avoid killing anybody else), everybody will be neutral to you (meaning that nobody will shoot you on sight--except for the Bounty Hunters of course). I don't know if there's an upper limit to how much it can improve your reputation, but if there isn't, then a couple hundred Bounty Hunter kills will make everybody green with you.
