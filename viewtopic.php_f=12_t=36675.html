Okay. I'm going to introduce you all to a little thing I like to call a DICTIONARY. It works like this:

1. You discover a word you have never encountered before
2. You decide you want to know what it means
3. You go to the dictionary and look it up. Beside the word you will find a definition that should help you understand what the word means. The process of looking up a word in a dictionary varies. In paper dictionaries the words are alphabetized and you have to flip through the pages until you come across the page with the word in it. For example, the word facetious would be found somewhere in between the words "fabulous" and "factoid". The link I gave makes the job much easier. Smply type (or even copy and paste) the word into the field and hit Search. The definition it gave for facetious was "Playfully jocular; humorous: facetious remarks ." That sounds about right to me.
