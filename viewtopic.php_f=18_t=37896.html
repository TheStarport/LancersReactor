Anton

Wed Feb 23, 2005 7:26 pm by Anton

Those numbers are crcs. FL uses them extensively for name lookups... There is currently no real way of reversing a crc but you can make them with crc calc available from the downloads section.

Explanation :
Manhatten = li01_01 = 0x0BCD355B (197997907)

the number after is a flag to tell the nav map what to display...
1 = flown past
31 = flown past & some info
41 = landed and all info
