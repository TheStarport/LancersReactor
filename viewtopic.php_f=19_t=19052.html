Archangel ---- Omega-41, D4
Bloodstone ----- Sigma 13, C3/C4

Cerberus ----- Omicron Alpha, F4

Diamondback ----- Sigma 13, C3/C4
Golden Blade ----- Omega-11, E6
Guardian ----- Omicron Alpha, F4
Iron Hammer ----- Sigma 13, C3/C4

Nomad Energy Blaster ----- Omicron Alpha, F4
Nomad Energy Cannon ----- Omicron Gamma, F2
Onyx ----- Sigma 13, C5

Thor’s Hammer ----- Omicron Theta, E6
Wild Fire ----- Omicron Beta, E5
