Reven

Sat Aug 02, 2003 9:30 pm by Reven

Oh, regarding hardpoints...
The answer is yes and no.

No, I'm not going to implement hardpoints in a way that's compatible with HCl's exporter. HCl did some great work, but the exporter has bugs (it inverts the Z axis), it doesn't support mesh groups or LODs, and it doesn't handle the material library.

What we need is a new exporter, and that is what I plan on doing. I wrote the importer with reusable UTF and VMESH classes just so this could be done. With a working importer and exporter with source code, I can then proceed to make a bunch of tool plugins for adding/editing hardpoints, mesh groups, and so forth.
