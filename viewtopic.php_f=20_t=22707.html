Ah, I actually fixed my V8 HU pretty well. I don't get any crashes except one a couple hours into playing. I actaully made some ground and finished the single player. I'll try to explain exactly how I did it but I may forget something. Basically it's unistall everything and re-install. I unistalled the mod (and deleted it), unistalled freelancer, unistalled my Cat 3.6 for my 9700, unistalled Fraunhoefer codec, and Creative MP3 codec (probably was the problem with 320kps support), booted up with a start up disk (winME), re-installed windows, re-installed all DX up to 9b, installed WMA 8 & 9 codecs, installed Cat 3.4's, installed Freelancer, then installed the mod fresh, and used my previous saved games. I also have limited my settings to 2x samples AA, and 4:1 AF in quality mode all around. Go to 4x sampleing and you get crashes. Basically everything has been pretty good since. My System.ini does show a L3Codec whatever listed but apparently it's not too much of a problem. I hope this might help. I didn't want to do too many registry fixes myself and was trying to find a simple more conventional way to fix this. Still a little buggy though. I also have EAX enabled and 3D sound turn on. I've found setting the settings higher for my system runs the game better.
