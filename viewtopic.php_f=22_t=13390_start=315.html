Nomads are clearly the most powerful guns in the game, but they might not fit into a particular players style regarding speed, range, refire and single-shot damage. This leaves the issue a matter of opinion. I prefer to have a high refire myself, and the combo of 2 blasters/2 cannons gives roughly 6 shots a second (first set is simultaneous, followed by 2 cannon and 3 blaster shots - non-simultaneous). I've been toying with the idea of finding a ship to equip with weapons bearing the same speeds but with differing refires of 3.03, 4, 5.88, and 8.33 for a grand total of ~17-18 (unsynced) shots in a second. Call it a little project. The damage wouldn't be great, but it might be an interesting combo on a class 5-7 ship.

As for the role-playing idea it is hardly dead. It has just undergone some birthing pains. That it sprung up suddenly in the first place indicates a niche for that sort of play. For the most part, it died just as suddenly due to an overly ambitious implementation policy. I predict it will be back, as the current vacuum once again requires it's existance. Eventually it will fall into balance, as overly ambitous clans fail, and less-ambitious implementations take on greater meaning and grow. We have only seen the first sputtering of it's existance, and i guarantee there is more to come.
