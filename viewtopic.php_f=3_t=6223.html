Got my Sneak Preview Demo Today

Well my fellow TLR's. I finally got the demo. I loaded it and began to play.
Now most know that I am a hardcore joystick fan, currently with the
Thrustmaster Cougar sticks, I love these sticks. Within 15 minutes of playing
Freelancer I forgot about the joystick issue. I was very VERY, skeptical about
this mouse keyboard control scheme, but all I can say is, is that it really does
work very well. I can see why now that Tachyon and some of the others defended the control scheme so much. Boscoe my twin bro you were right. I LOVE this game. By the way my version has cockpit struts in it. I'm flying a Liberty Defender right now and the struts are very well done. I have only been playing right now for about 4 hours but all I can say is WOW.... . For those of you who were wondering what landing on Planets and Bases where like, well every landing is different, when you get a different ship the landing is also different, it was for me anyway when I landed on Manhattan with my new shiny Defender.

I am still learning some of the keyboard controls and can't wait to try them out.
I have stopped playing for now so I could post about the Demo. Got one hell of a headache just took some aspirin but I'm going back for more Freelancing later tonight. For all those waiting for this game you will not be disappointed. The graphics are stunning and the game play is fun. Bargib thanks for having the Contest and thanks to DA and Microsoft. This is one happy camper.
