Fire_Starter

Wed Jun 11, 2003 10:09 pm by Fire_Starter

Best "trade" route would have to be the Rochester -> Manhattan run. Actually, it's more of a pirating run. Wait outside Rochester (about 900m or so where they can't hit you, and they're gonna want to after you do this a few times) for either a Rogue Mule or a Junker CSV. As soon as they're in range, scan them. If they have Cardamine or Artifacts, pop em, tractor in the cargo, and head for Manhattan. If you fly over the north pole you won't get scanned by LSF patrols (well, I've never been scanned....yet). I can make 105,000 per run in my Eagle with a hold full of Cardamine or 89,740 with a hold full of artifacts. Not a bad haul for a few minutes work. Only drawback is sometimes you have to wait quite a while. I've made runs where I was able to bag a CSV full of Cardamine before even reaching Rochester, and I've also sat outside using Rogues as target practice for an hour and not one good transport has come along. You can find Diamonds, Niobuim, and Gold coming in and out of Rochester, but the price you get on Manhattan really isn't worth the trouble.
