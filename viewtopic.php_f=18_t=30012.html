Is it for SP or MP? If SP, then you could make it for sale only on 1 base and level 10 limits the type of ship. Change the dropable/salvage
Sample of wep data/equipment/weapons equip/

turn_rate = 90
lootable = true ;change this to false so no one can recover it
LODranges = 0, 20, 40, 80, 100

And that will narrow down the field of who can get it. Hope this helps

Sat Apr 03, 2004 5:53 pm by Chips

Make it a level 10 turret class, and then mod one ship (your ship) to mount the turret as well. If your the server admin, you can edit your character to give you the ship - which doesn't sell anywhere - meaning NO-ONE can mod it in at all. After that, only your ship that no-one else can get can mount the gun. The reason? No ship in FL has class 10 turrets, and therefore the 1.1 patch will kick everyone else for mounting it if they tried to............even if they mod it in.
