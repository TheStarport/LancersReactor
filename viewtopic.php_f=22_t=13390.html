Brown maybe you can do me a favor. Under a decent load, around 10 - 16 people, can you please take a look at your task manager under the Performance tab and tell me if the server load is being evenly distributed over the two processors?

The reason i ask is because alot of "game server"s are written without consideration for multiple processors. Windows 2000 and XP can only do so much to spread an application over the two processors but if you've ever read up on dual processor motherboards being benchmarked you'll notice that if an application is not written to take advantage of two processors, you will see only a marginal increase in speed, sometimes you will even see a decrease in speed because of the data overhead.

I'm just doing some research to see if it is worth making a dual processor server, thank you in advance.
