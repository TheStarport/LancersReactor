This has nothing to do with textures. It looks more like a few fonts in your \windows\fonts\ folder have become corrupted, or don't exist at all.

Put the Freelancer CD-ROM into your drive. Locate its "FONTS" folder. Copy the three files out of this FONTS folder into the C:\WINDOWS\FONTS\ directory, overwriting existing files if asked to. Restart Freelancer. Now the text should be correctly sized.
