Well, Whasp, I took the idea for the Avarice guns from Star Wars' ion cannons. The idea of the Avarice guns, and the ion cannons in general, is that they can help in taking down a shield but do no hull damage. Once the shields are gone, the ion bolts will take down a ships system by shorting out or overloading them. This allows for capture of target craft, or target elimination without blood shed.

The idea of the Stormblade was to absolutely take out a ship's shields in one hit, or in the case of cap ships a couple of shots. After the shields go down the Stormblades is utterly useless. It worth is quick shield failures so missiles, regular guns, and Fireblades have full effect on a target.

The Avarices' bolts aren't that effective alone. It take a lot of them. That's why there gatling guns. Even as gats it takes a few second to strip a shield.

On Malys's character, I think it would work well, if she did reveal to the group what she really was, at least right away. It'd be dramatic irony and logic. If you were one the last of an ancient and unknown race, you would great people, "Welcome, Quigley-san. I am Malystryx, a member of secret alien race you have no knowledge of. Would you like some sake?" Only, as her background states, the GC and Blood Dragons know about her true form. They would take such a secret to their graves. Even Quigley who is very close to the GC would not be told because he isn't a "true" member.
