{SOLN} Recruiting members

If you're looking to join a great clan on a very challenging server then the SOLN is for you! The {SOLN} is an LSF clan devoted to the protection of Liberty and we are looking for honorable pilots with good role play skills. If you're interested in joining visit http://www.sccommand.com/phpBB2/index.php There you can download the mod for HA1 (the primary server we play on) and catch one of us on {HA1}, post in the open server chat area stating you would like to join, or visit {HA2}Grimaxe (no mod required) and find us online there. You'll find that not only is the Hunter's Association servers a great (and addicting) place to play, but also that the {SOLN} is one of the top clans there whose membership forms a tight nit group that always has fun and helps eachother out.
