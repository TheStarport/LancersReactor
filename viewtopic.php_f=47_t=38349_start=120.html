The pirate threat must be stopped now or they will soon become too strong and we will both risk major casualties. We have declared neutrality earlier, but we understand the danger that awaits us.


@ All:
DSE setting prizes for technologies

From now, factions are allowed to officially ask for building Jump Gates, Trade Lanes and Bases from the Deep Space Engineering. This offer is opened to all lawful factions throughout Sirius Sector.

Jump Gates
Base price of a Jump Gate (in fact 2 Gates, 1 in each system) together with Trade Lane connection of both Jump Gates to bases of your choice, is varying between 500-700 Credits, depending mainly on the distance between chosen systems. You can lower this price by:
- providing us the resources needed to construct Jump Gates - 50 units of Metal in total, will lower the resulting price by 50 Credits
- providing fighter escort on at least one side of the Jump Gate will lower the cost by 25 Credits for each side you will defend. Acceptable fighter escort is at least 30 fighter wings of your choice, with a Cruiser or Gunboat counting as 5 fighter wings, and a Battleship counting as 10 wings.
So in total you can lower the price up (down?) to 400-600 Credits.
The construction of a Jump Gate link will be finished in 2 standard months from its start. After that, both Jump Gates will be given fully to your control and we take no responsibility for their protection or further usage.

Space Stations
Base prices for Space Stations are as follows:
Small Space Station - 150 Credits
Large Space Station - 300 Credits
Capital Ship Shipyards - 200 Credits
You can lower the prices similary to Jump Gates:
- by providing the needed resources you can lower the price of a Space Station by 1 Credit per Commodity Unit provided. Note: You can either provide all of needed commodity, or none. Partial deals are not accepted. However, you can provide one required commodity and pay for the other.
- by providing fighter escort on the Station construction site will lower the cost by 25 Credits. Acceptable fighter escort is at least 30 fighter wings of your choice, with a Cruiser or Gunboat counting as 5 fighter wings, and a Battleship counting as 10 wings.

All prices are subject to change.
