MegaStar

Tue Jun 24, 2003 5:39 am by MegaStar

The Alaska Jump Gate is located within the Zone 21 minefield east of Norfolk Shipyard and the Battleship Missouri. If you fly up to the minefield you should be able to see a jump gate in the distance. You cannot fly directly to gate through the minefield because there is no way of avoiding the mines and each of them is strong enough to kill you in one explosion. There is though a small tunnel through the minified. To find it follow theses instructions. Select the jump gate and choose the schematic view by clicking on the icon of the ship that appears to be in a box with only corners. It is located next to the highlighted lines that look like text on the top of the left panel of your head up display. Use this in combination with you main view to position yourself so that you are lined up directly with the entrance of the jump gate. Activate your cruise engine and fly strait toward the it. Make slight adjustment to your course once you can differentiate the tunnel from the rest of the minefield.
