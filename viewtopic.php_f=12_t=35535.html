Interestingly, daddy-long-legs or "harvestmen" are NOT insects but also NOT
truly spiders.

Insects have three-segment bodies, true spiders have two-segment ones and
d-l-ls one.

Although a cousin of true spiders, being an arthropod, a d-l-l is in an order all its own, the phalangida. True spiders are in the order arachnida.
