Hmm, well, in that case, maybe you should just find the entry instead of asking for it. If your having trouble, let me once again refer you to Subxeros Miscillaneous Database. In future you can also look at the goods.ini loadout entry for the engine, where you also get powerplants and more. Search goods.ini file for a pirate ship, and there you find the engine nickname once again.
Don't know the nickname of the ships package though?
