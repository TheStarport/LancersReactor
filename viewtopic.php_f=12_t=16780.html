This is an actual news article:

US mental group seeks Klingon interpreter

A mental health organisation in the US state of Oregon needs an interpreter who can speak Klingon. The language created for Star Trek is one of about 55 needed by the office that treats mental health patients in metropolitan Multnomah County. Multnomah County research has found many people consider Klingon to be a complete language. Although created for works of fiction, Klingon was designed to have a consistent grammar, syntax and vocabulary.
"There are some cases where we've had mental health patients where this was all they would speak," said county official, Franna Hathaway. The officials say they're obligated to respond with a Klingon-English interpreter, putting the language on a par with less common tongues like Dari and Tongan.
