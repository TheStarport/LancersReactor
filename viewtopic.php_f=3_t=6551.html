Post your DEMO experiences here.

First off: WOW DA, this demo is amazing! The universe has so much flavor and variety, I just can't stop playing, you've made my day!

Anyways, this demo has some amazing experiences that I thought alot of us would love to share. What's amazing is how DYNAMIC the universe is, It's not static or dead, It's bristling full of life with Freighters that actually have destinations and a Star Patrol that actually... Patrols!

After the story missions, I decided to do some sight seeing on my own, so I quickly departed from New Pitsburgh, and set a course toward The Military Training Facility. Upon departing from my first set of trade lanes, I come upon a world unto it self, there are a few stations set, and traffic that has a purpose, but I also noticed a wing of Rogues as well. Seeing the opportunity to help out the police I quickly set course to engage those scoundrels.

The fire fight was intense, and I foolishly did not take the time to upgrade my ship before departing New Pitsburg. In the end though, we became the victor, although my hull was a flaming wreck and I had lost a wing. Let me tell you, there's nothing like limping home and being EXTRA careful about what paths you take - in order to avoid any pirates. The tension is astounding.

Again - awesome game DA. This is gonna be a classic.
