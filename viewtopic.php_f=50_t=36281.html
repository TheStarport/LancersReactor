Actually...
The best way to find out...

Look at the Br01.ini file.

It's got a bunch of them:
[zone
nickname = Zone_Br01_Southampton_debris_field
(I deleted the zone's data)
Music = zone_field_debris

[zone
nickname = Zone_Br01_Devon_rock_asteroid_field
(I deleted the zone's data)
Music = zone_field_asteroid_rock

To change the system's music, edit:
[Music
space = music_br_space
danger = music_br_danger
battle = music_br_battle

Very simple, actually!

Watch your 6!
