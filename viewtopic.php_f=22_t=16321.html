I haven't played FL for a week or 2 but last time I remember, your worth/rank is calculated based on the following

1. ship you own
2. cargo you carry
3. weapons/equip you have
4. cash on hand

Your rank can jump while docking, because I believe the commodity you carry is based on the default price. You can observe this phenonom yourself. Go to Montezuma and buy Cardomine. You'll see your rank drop. Sell it in Manhattan, your rank goes up.

Your rank often drops after you purchase a better ship + equipment because the price you pay to buy weapon/armor isn't the same as you can sell. I believe they use the sell price to calculate your worth.

You can experiment with these yourself. I have a storage character that still has starflier but at level 21. He has nothing but level 9 & 10 weapons that I get from fighting or salvage.

On elite, I lost my a couple levels before the random explosion bug is "fixed". I had a dromedary and it hurts even more.
