LIBERTY TAKES OVER NEIGHBORING SYSTEMS:
Today Liberty Navy High Command announced a plan to take over and annex independent systems
neighboring Liberty Space. Because Cortez has already been overtaken, and Kepler under Alliance
control, remaining systems to be overtaken are Bering, Hudson, Magellan and Galileo.
Liberty Navy ships have moved in to these systems and Liberty has declared these systems to be
"under protection" by liberty military. The Navy High Command has justified these actions by
saying that it will attempt to create aditional security around Liberty core systems.

NEW SYSTEM DISCOVERED.
Today Liberty Navy exploration teams discovered yet another new system. The new system is located
between Colorado and Tau-29, and the Barrier extends to the system, covering about 25% of the system
with ice asteroids. The system was first spotted by long range space telescope Discoverer 850, located
on the orbit of planet Denver. After discovery of the system, an exploration team was sent to the system.
Explorations are continuing in the system. So far five planets have been found in the system, two of them
deemed suitable for colonization. One of the remaining planets is athmosphereless, rocky and hot planet
unfit for any life, and the other two are of gaseous form. The system has been decided to be named Alabama
system and the planets Atlanta, New Orleans, Savannah, Jackson and Montgomery.

LIBERTY NAVY DECLARES SELF-SUFFICIENCE IN JUMPGATE TECHNOLOGY.
Due to recent technological advancements, Liberty Navy is now self-sufficient when it comes to
system-to-system travelling. The Navy is no longer dependent of Ageira and Jump Gate technology, because
it has developed a new system that will make Jump Gates obsolete. Navy research scientists have developed
a jump drive system which creates an artificial jump hole for a short time, enabling a ship or an group of
ships to travel long distances in few seconds. The advantage of jump drives compared to the jump gates is
that with jump drives ships are not dependent on permanent structures such as the jump gates. Howewer, the
technology requires precise computer technology to avoid mishaps such as the infamous Dallas Incident. All
more detailed information regarding Jump Drive technology is TOP SECRET.
LIBERTY NAVY RESEARCH COMMAND. SECRET REPORT(OOC for you guys)
TO:All fleet commanders and the supreme commander of Liberty Navy.
In case of Jump Drive technology falling to the wrong hands, the Navy Research Command has developed a
jump hole destabilizer device as a backup measure. The system disturbs jump holes making them unstable
and unable to be used for travelling, while not destroying the jump holes. With the technology jump
holes can be "locked up" and opened later. This technology can be applied also to artificial jump holes
created by jump drives.
