Strider - oTO

Tue Sep 09, 2003 2:38 pm by Strider - oTO

The mod has been having problems with custom loadouts on "mission critical" enemies in the single player campaign. After a lot of debugging, i found that the game was crashing in many places because of the prisoners (Pirate ships were supposed to drop prisoners) Anyways, if you go to the loadout.ini file, search for the word prisoner, you should find something like goods prisoners = 1 in several entries. Delete this line whereever you see it. Thats what fixed it for me....
