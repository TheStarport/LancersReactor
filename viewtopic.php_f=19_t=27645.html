The TNG 1.7 mod, played on the Norwegian Heaven Server is, I think, as good as modded Freelancer gets. There are rarely less than 40 people on the server, a good clan following, races, etc.

Another good ‘up-and-coming’ mod is the Evolutions mod by Chips, a site regular here on TLR.

Both mods are available for download from this site, and you need the latest beta release of Freelancer Mod Manager for Evolutions, that is also in the download section of this site.
