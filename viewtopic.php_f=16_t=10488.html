Easy Rhino

Sat Mar 22, 2003 10:06 am by Easy Rhino

Old thread, but I was actually able to "circle-strafe" the generators with some success.

Get in really close. Fly along the side of the big rock in circles. Aim at just the edge of the rock and blast at it with your guns. Apply side thrusters to keep you moving to the outside of your orbit.

This way you basically drive around in circles. It keeps you moving which makes you a little bit harder to shoot, but it also keeps you close to the generator to shield you from weapons.
