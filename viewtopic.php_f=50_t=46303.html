For speed of engines look in the engine_equip.ini to adjust engine speed look for max_force = 72000 and divide 72000 by 600 to get the speed of that engine. If you want say an engine that has a top speed of 150 then you will need to times 150 by 600 = 90000 so max_force = 90000 which is equal to 150 engine speed. Now if you want to take longer or shorter time to get to cruise speed look for cruise_charge_time = 5 the 5 is 5 seconds just change that to how many seconds you what be it 0-6 seconds.

Now if you want to change the cruise speed you will need to add this CRUISING_SPEED = the speed you want? To the Constants.ini file under the [EngineEquipConsts section
