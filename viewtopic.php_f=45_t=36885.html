TRD trader clan recruiting on Solaris Evolutions server

The TRD trader clan is recruiting on the {HA5} Solaris EVO server. Solaris is running the Evolutions 1.28 mod with the 1.02 patch integrated. Solaris recently joined the Hunters Association community of servers. One of the admins from the Hunters Association is designing an add-on mod for Solaris. We will soon have Star Wars and other ships in addition to those found in the Evolutions mod. Each clan will also get its own custom designed system as part of the add-on Solaris mod. Meanwhile, we're playing Evolutions 1.28 with the 1.02 patch integrated. Any characters created now should be playable when the add-on mod is completed (so you won't have to start over).

Members of the TRD clan should have at least two clan characters: a trader and a fighter. If you join TRD and see me in-game, I can give you enough money to buy and equip a large train and the fighter of your choice (probably the Federal version of the Ravenclaw). If you want to buy a capital ship (Rheinland battleship is the best), you'll have to earn the money, but having a large train will make that relatively easy (if you can avoid members of the Prowlers terrorist clan and the three pirate clans that belong to the SSA). If you need an escort and you don't see any other TRD online, ask a member of the LIA for help, since they're a police clan allied with TRD.

Here are the links to the Solaris website, Solaris forums and TRD status forum. You'll have to join TRD to get access to the TRD clan discussion forum.

How to join TRD (or contact us to discuss doing so):
1) Create a civilian character on the {HA5} Solaris EVO server;

2) Talk to the TRD leader ({TRD}eliminator) or co-leader (me, a.k.a. Juan_Arquero, {TRD}_Psycho, {TRD}_Traficante_Pepe and {TRD}_Juan_Williams) in-game or send us a private message on the Solaris forums -- you'll have to register and send the message to "{TRD}eliminator;{TRD}_Psycho" -- to get permission to create TRD clan characters;

3) Register on the Solaris forums;

4) Read the Solaris server rules.

Note that steps 3 and 4 can be done earlier.

Important Note: TLR forums won't let me show the correct TRD clan tag which uses standard brackets. Check the Solaris forums to see correct usage.
