House fighters.

Yes, another "Best Ship" thread. Kind of.

I got to thinking about this while deciding on what ship I should go for next on one of my MP characters, and was deciding between the Banshee and the Valkyrie. I got the Banshee, mostly because I was tired of doing missions in Rheinland with a Defender and wanted something better, NOW.

But what do you prefer in terms of the House LFs over th House HFs? When you're fooling around in Kusari, do you get a Dragon or Drake? When you're starting a character, Patriot or Defender? When you have to choose the lesser of two evils, Cavalier or Crusader?

I've been mostly an LF player since I bought the game, but I tried a Defender for the first time in a long time for a few days and got kinda attached to it. In Bretonia, I always take the Crusader, in Kusari it's a mix of Drake and Dragon, and I don't fly either Rheinland ship much, but when I do it's pretty even either way.

And NO, I don't care about your Eagle or Centurion or Pirhana or Dagger. This is for House ships.

NukeIt

Wed Sep 29, 2004 6:26 am by NukeIt

Not that my preference is any mystery, but I always go with the house LF. I could probably get used to the house HFs, but they lose too much maneuverability for too small of an armor upgrade. At the extreme of this, the Patriot has 1300 armor opposed to the Defender's 1600. Sure, the Defender carries more weapons, but with as little armor as that you really need all the agility you can get. And, as opposed to the pirate/borderworld/corsair/civilian ships, the house HFs only get a power class advantage of 1- and their cargo bays aren't all that impressive, either.

The Dragon and the Valkerie are still fine ships in themselves, but I'd prefer to take a Drake or Banshee given the choice.
