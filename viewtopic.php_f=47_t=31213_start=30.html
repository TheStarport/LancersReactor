Thanks for continuing the story mate.
Although, just nitpicking here, but my plan for the story was that Antonio hates Trent, King and Juni because they killed his brother who was an Order pilot during the attack.
He was then broken out of prison by his Corsair buddies (the Order has largely disbanded after the Story), escorts Core Zaire to New London, where they part ways, Core gets some work as a Molly (fresh from prison, he decides to join the er... darker side), ends up in Dublin, where he meets Trent again and races them at the track (Trent runs it now, after beating Hovis in a race where Hovis bet everything he owned). They become friends, but are forced to leave Dublin when Hovis shows up with a few heavily armed friends.... and that's all I'd came up with.

You see, this is why you should write your own story, instead of continuing mine.
I had a plan for this story, and I don't like it when people don't follow it.
You should start your own fan fic, then you could invent a storyline all of your own, instead of simply finishing mine. This is why I started one in the first place, to write something unique. I didn't want to follow the pattern of other fan fics, ie. Trent and Juni fight Nomads. You could do that too, explore areas that no-one else has, write new and interesting. Something that's your's .
No matter how well you write this fan fic, it'll always be mine, recycled by you.
If you start your own, you can create your own universe, your own characters, which is what writing is really about.

Just my 2 Cents, anyways.
