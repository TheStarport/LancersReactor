Mon Jun 09, 2003 3:27 am by Darkened

Note to all that posted here: I would like to say that there is no single ship that is the best. Of the 3 ships that can mount class 10 weapons EACH has its own advantages. The Sabre has the best firepower because its turret can fire foward while the Titan is the best tank, and as a result flies like one. And the Eagle (my preference) is the most agile of the 3 and the theory behid that is, whats the point of having more firepower (Sabre) when u can hit it? And so what if you have an advantage in armor class 10 weapons tend to rip apart armor even that of the Titan and the ~3k advantage over the Eagle can easily be dealt with. That is why I, and others prefer the Eagle.

Though I'm not saying you should hop on the bandwagon I still think you should try all 3 if you can.

Courage isn't the ability to fly head on into the jaws of death with an arrogant smile. Its the ability to run away from a couple of Xenos and face the laughter.
