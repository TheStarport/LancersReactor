Best cockpit view?

Admittedly, most cockpit views in Freelancer suck. But I played through the SP game again and this time traded my Anubis for a Rheinland Falcon. (Much as I liked the Anubis, it got shot to pieces when I was jump hole hunting in the Omegas. Fought off about half a dozen Red Hessians to get through a hole, only to find more than that waiting on the other side. I was lucky to make it back alive with warnings of "hull breach imminent" and no nanobots or shield batteries left. I lost my best guns and missile launcher too -- along with the wing they were attached to. Try fighting without guns sometime.)

Anyway, I clicked on cockpit view in the Falcon just to see what it was like. It's great! The ship's hull is almost completely out of sight, no wobbly structure that make you feel like you're in a cardboard box etc. My L7 Eliminators crackle like Gandalf's wand, and the incoming laser fire screeches across my shields.

Anyone else found any cockpit views worth flying in?
