Jevryn Markes

Fri Jul 25, 2003 3:41 pm by Jevryn Markes

The Loki is a short-ranged, limited point defense fighter. With no jumpr-drive capability nor astral navigation systems, the fighter cannot deploy into deep space and is instead consigned to defend close to its ward, be it space stations or a mothership. The swiveling arm is a docking umbilical designed to attach the fighter to the outer hulls of ships, allowing the fighter to be carried without taking up precious space on a hangar deck. Cheap, slow, but agile, the Loki's purely intended for mass-produced defense-- a flying defensive turret, asi it may.
