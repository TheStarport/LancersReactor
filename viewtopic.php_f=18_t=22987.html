Answer to the first question:
Weapon damage only reads the server side vaules. You can not have super damage weapon in a MP game if the server admin didn't mod the weapon file.
The guy bring down your shield in 1 shot might have more guns than you think. If a player edit his ship and carry 20 extra guns, others wont' able to see them or tell when he is firing. You may get shoot once by over 30 tizonia combined, though you can never tell.
Answer to the second question:
Same as the weapon, the shield vaules reads the server side vaules. But interesting, you can mod your shield capacity down to 0, and you can shoot players who mounted that shield without any trouble, you will damage his hul since your computer read he has no shield HP. But, you can not mod the shield capacity up, or increase the regeneration rate in the MP.
