Kurgan

Tue Jun 03, 2003 3:18 am by Kurgan

From the picture that can be an awesome ship. I agree with just wing mounting the weapons, that should look good. As far as textures go, I build my ships in pieces. Wings, Body, Fins, etc. that way I can texture each piece. If you have duplicate pieces, only make one then use the duplicate tool and then vertex mirror either left<->right or top<->bottom. If you do this the the parts are equal size and if the original was textured then the duplicate will be also. Then when the textures are mapped all you have to do is regroup the parts into 1 mesh and it is totally skinned. That saves time and makes it easier to get the details the way you want.
