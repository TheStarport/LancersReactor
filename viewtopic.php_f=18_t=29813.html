kaegogi

Mon Mar 29, 2004 12:48 pm by kaegogi

I might be reading you wrong but it seems like you're not interested in the shiparch.ini. Are you trying to open a .cmp file for a specific ship? SDK only allows you to see .ini's decompiled.
From what limited knowledge I've picked up here is what the different files are:
.cmp = the ship model
.mat = the textures that go on the model
.ini = tells the game what your model does.
If you're trying to look at a .cmp you'll need milkshape with a importer plugin. If you have your own models and you want to put them in the game you can't just copy them into an existing .cmp. If that's what you want to do I second Schizm. Go back through Drizzt's tutorial from start to finish and do what he says step by step.
If I did get you wrong, no offence intended, just trying to help.
