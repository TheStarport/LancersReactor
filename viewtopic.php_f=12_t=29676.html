We Made It!

Wed Mar 24, 2004 3:00 am by We Made It!

I think a lot of the criticism levelled at Arnie is unfair. He's never pretended to be a brilliant or versatile actor, but he does very well in his chosen genres, action and comedy. Because his films are big-budget blockbusters for the mass audience, there's never going to be any real tasking of anyone's acting abilities, but he carries these parts off so well that you have to admit that he must have some acting ability; which dosn't go amiss in his political career as well.

Some of his films have stunk and he wasn't up to much in T3 either, but imho that's more to do with poor scripts and lacklustre direction. At his best, Arn's fun to watch and can pull off an exciting afternoon's entertainment. Look on his films as just that, entertainment, and nothing else.

My personal fav is Last Action Hero, which I found rather amusingly self-deprecating for Arnie and with more than a few wry insights into Hollywood
