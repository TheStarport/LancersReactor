Wilde

Sun Dec 12, 2004 6:20 am by Wilde

By all means the Hood is a base. It's outdated and ancient...

The Hogosha are a small faction. They only operate inside Kusari. By comparison the Junkers operate in every part of Sirius. Large Factions: GMG, IMG, Zoners, Corsairs, Outcasts, Junkers. That much I know. There might be some others that jacob will consider adding (hint hint Hessians...)

The commodities are: Food (F), Water (W or H20), Oxygen (O), H-Fuel (H-F or HF), and Metals (M). Basic abrreviations.

The difference between pirates and smugglers is (correct me if I'm wrong) that smugglers merely are involved in lucrative trade, whereas pirates will kill off anything in sight--civilians, police, and commercial targets.

Take this as a general rule for large bases: If its a bloody huge asteroid with docking ports and gun turrets, take it for granted that it's a large station (only pirates, revolutionaries, and smugglers have this kind of base). Anything with "Fort" or "Base" in its name is large. If it sells any ships whatsoever, or has a long range of commodities it sells, its probably large.
