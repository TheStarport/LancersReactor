The best route by far I've found involves buying Luxury Goods in Manhatten for around 120. Take that to Planet Cambridge and sell for 800 (about 150k profit in a hump back). Buy diamonds there (dont remember the exact price) and take them to Planet New London. Sell the diamonds (about 50k profit in a hump back), Buy Luxury Foods at New London, and take it back to Planet Manhatten (about 250k profit).

The whole run in a humpback will get you a bit over 400k, in pure profit.

The journey is about 60 waypoints total. It will take you about fifteen minutes to make a round trip. If you are in a humpback with decent gun turrets, nothing can touch you. If you are in a Clydesdale or Rhino, be careful about the Manchester -> New London jump gate. There are frequently lots of Corsairs waiting there.

Oh, this only works in MP. As by the time you get to the Braetonia systems in SP, Liberty hates you. =

Oh, for people who want the full system names... starting from Manhatten:

New York System -> California System -> Cortez System -> Manchester System ->
New London System -> Cambridge System -> New London System -> Manchester System -> Cortez System -> California System -> New York System.

It looks alot longer than it really is. About 15 minutes round trip. Be careful on the way back from London to Manhatten, the lux goods go bad fairly quickly. In a humpback I lose 10-15 out of 250.
