Hi Anubis-Dk!

First of all I like to welcome you to The Lancers Reactor! I hope you'll enjoy your stay with us

The storyline of Freelancer only lets you see a portion of what the entire universe has to offer. The storyline doesn't let you see all the systems, all the bases, all the planets etc. There are still lots of systems for you to explore, which you problaby haven't seen yet by just playing the storyline. Here are a few things you can do after the storyline ends:

- Earn more money by finding profitable traderoutes
- Buy all the ships and weapons available in the game
- Get your killscore up
- Explore all the systems, planets, bases, hidden wrecks etc.
- Play random missions
- Choose the Multiplayer option to play with your friends or with others online
and much more...

If you've done all that, try out one of the many Mods that have been created for Freelancer. They offer lots of things, like new ships, different gameplay, new systems, all kinds of tweaks for the original game, new weapons etc. etc.
