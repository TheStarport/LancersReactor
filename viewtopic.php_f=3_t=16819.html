The missiles in the lower slot are def a nice way to avoid the drain problems.

Trouble is missiles are a bit crap (except early on) and the cost bucks every time you fire. Bucks (especially in MP) are good to hold on to. The other problem is that the lower the level weapon the higher the efficiency - in general. You are usually sacrificing some efficiency when going for the higehst weapons.

Personally i take a couple of mid rangers with 1 or 2 top range depending on the number of mounts.

Its all about when your power cuts out. If you're a really good pilot you can probably afford to have all top class weapons cause you will be able to waste your foes before the juice runs out. I'm not that good tho and i hate having to fly around waiting for the damn lasers to recharge while dodging fire.
