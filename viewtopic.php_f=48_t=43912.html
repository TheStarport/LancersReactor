SWAT_OP-R8R

Fri Jun 30, 2006 5:00 am by SWAT_OP-R8R

"StarLancer - The Sol War" will be released in about 2-3 weeks

What is it?
Basicly it is a new game based on a public game engine telling a story that plays after the original Starlancer story.
The game is a space shooter like Starlancer itself. It will have joystick support (including force feedback), 3d sound, a screen resolution up to 2048x1536, a physics engine, dynamic light effects, reflections and tons of other features.
The game will have a singleplayer story campaign with 24 missions (just like in the original SL) and 10 simulator missions. You will get a mission briefing before every fight does start and hopefully you will enjoy the fights together with your wingmen.

Story?
well the story will start right after the story of the original SL did. The Yamato is going to be repaired and the 45th Flying Tigers will be thrown back into the fight against the Coalition.
I wont tell you how the story will develop or end... i just can say that this game is just the first part of X games which will show what happened during the 100 years of war against the coalition to close the missing gap between SL and FL.
