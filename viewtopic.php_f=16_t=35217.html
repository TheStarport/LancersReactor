Nice little spoiler

I discovered something very interesting not too long ago. If you have a server and change your ship to a Nomad fighter, go to Omega-41 with the Neutron Star. Fly directly toward the star, you will not blow up, cause I have done this myself. Eventually you will fly straight into the star, but here's the kicker. Instead of burning up like with most stars, you will bounce right off it. I guess they didn't need a burn zone cause typically the radiation will eat up the hull of most fighters, even the Titan. For some reason though, the Nomad fighter will not die before you bounce off the dead star. Interesting, isn't it?
