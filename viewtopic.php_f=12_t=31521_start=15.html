works fine for me, never had a problem with it. Have you contacted EA or Creative Assembly? On the face of it you should have no trouble playing this game, and it is well worth playing. I love it and I know Fear Factor virtually lives in it.

techy stuff..

check in your BIOS that you don't have shadowing enabled for your vidcard or anything else. Are these onboard graphics? get a real video card, nothing flash, and disable the onboard rubbish. Make sure your AGP aperture is set to 128mb as well. If vga palette snoop is enabled, disable it. Unless the video adapter is actually faulty, what's happening chum is that your adapter is addressing areas of memory that are being used/allocated to something else, you need to free up resources so that the adapter gets first call, if you will.

if you get stuck you know where i am. Believe me Shogun is well worth the effort!
