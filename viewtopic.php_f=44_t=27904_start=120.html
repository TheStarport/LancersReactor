Drizzt4.0

Thu Mar 25, 2004 4:45 am by Drizzt4.0

no not really... what you are doing is selecting the face/ or faces where you want the hardpoint to be and clicking duplicate selection from the edit menu OR regroup from the group menu... copying the hard point face allows you to move it around while regrouping that hardpoint face will completely detach the face from the mesh(so if you want to be able to move the hardpoint- duplicate dont regroup.) Or yes you could create a new face. but i really dont think thats necessary.

good luck,
