If you're saying Trent's looks change from time to time then yeah, in the SP campaign sometimes he wears glasses and in some other cutscenes he doesn't. You can run the mp_costume.bat batch file, it also affects Trent in SP. He looks like an old guy in the Navy costume btw (!).

Okay, now I've been looking at a couple forums, I noticed not many mods advertise what exactly they're doing lol. I mean yeah lists of ships and guns and stuff, that's great, but I think a list of features would be more helpful, like "docking scenes removed", "made engines buyable", etc.

Btw I read some comments on other threads, it's weird ppl saying Rebalanced is too hard, imo it gets it just right. I suspect those people were expecting to jump into the border worlds early on and buy the "best" items. The Rebalance mod gets the 'balance' just right... makes the LFs and HFs also worthy of checking out instead of always just buying VHFs and Capships. In terms of difficulty, it looks like most of the stuff is spot-on already (although the Earth systems could use a little tweaking, see Rebalance forums re: complaints of too-lucrative trade routes available early).

If people expect to jump into Rebalance and cruise around in Sabres/Titans/whatever from the start, then they should look elsewhere... Rebalance gives a nice challenge. I like that, and judging from the number of posts here and on their boards, plenty of other people do too. Those whiners can always go to PerfOptions.ini and SET DIFFICULTY SCALE = 0.00 :p
