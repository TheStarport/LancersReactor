Thu May 08, 2003 8:07 am by D3ViL

U can only buy the Eagle at Freeport 9. Freeport 9 is in the Omicron Theta system.

New Berlin -> Dresden (Jump Gate)
Dresden -> Omega-11 (C4, Asteroid Field)
Omega-11 -> Omega-41 (C5, Asteroid Field)
Omega-41 ->Omicron Theta (F4, Above Right of Freeport 5)
Omicron Theta -> Freeport 9 (E4-E5, Right betwen those two)

The Ship will cost you $558,550, so get your money done first. The people that owns the base are Zoners, so make sure you are Neutral or Friendly with them.
