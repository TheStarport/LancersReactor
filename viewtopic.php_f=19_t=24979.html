moby's right about the starflyer. Been out to Theta myself in one.

As for speed hackers, they are far tougher to catch. A screenshot tells you nothing. FLSO shows normal thrusters and engine installed. This is one of those times server police are invaluable. Before the ARs came to my server, I would first notice the server load was high then had to watch for them in the game or in FLSO's update list (it shows player location). Like running from Cortez to NY through California (including both jump gates) in 1.5 mins. Even then that's not 100% verification so I had to get ahead of them in the game and see that the hacker first appears on my display at only 1k away. At normal cruise speed (300) players first appear 4-5k away.

I also notice if someone is leveled at high twenties or low thirties but has played less than an hour. Of course that isn't direct evidence of a cheat because they could have got the money several different ways, hence the need to go in the game and see what you can see for yourself.

Even though you can't prove a speed cheat through some evidence (like a screenshot), as X-Treme mentioned you can report him. Enough reports (maybe even one depending on the sysadmin and your respectability with him) can be enough that he will be tracked down and closely watched.
