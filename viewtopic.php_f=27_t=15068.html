*HSDS*VINDICATOR: could you explain that problem more? Did you e-mail me about it? I can't help you figure out your problem if you never tell me about it

All of you having problems with Freelancer crashing at startup: yes, that is caused by saved games containing systems or ships that don't exist. FLMM comes with 2 menu options ("Tools\Delete Autosave" and "Options\Disable Autosave" ) that help you to avoid that problem. If it still crashes, then that is probably because you manually made a saved game with a mod loaded. You'll have to delete that saved game (or activate that mod again) before Freelancer will start.
