Tue Jul 29, 2003 6:27 pm by Sherlog

In this special case - neutral base turns hostile because hostile allies are present - you could exploit the 'mission neutrality' bug that turns hostile passersby neutral while you are in a mission. Book a mission on Freeport 4 (IMG), preferrably against Outcasts in The Barrier. Then go fetch your Drom from Mactan and do as you please.

Note: the bug affects only ships, not bases. And if you get near a hostile base then the temporarily-neutralized enemies will turn hostile again if they are allies (or owners) of the base.
