ReVeNaNt, I think what you have done is commendable, I really do. Just be careful with it. I have visions of people's horror stories when they've overclocked CPU's and Video cards and found out the hard way it can and does fry some very expensive pieces of electronics. Take it slowly, we're in no rush!

Modding certainly isn't an exact science, it's full of experiementation, great rewards and sometimes confuzing results. Most of all it's fun as heck to be creative! But typical (and safe) experimetation has always been the tried and true method of set incremental increases. These steps are done slowly, tested, retested and results confirmed before moving to the next level. There is bound to be a point of deminishing return on performace verses stability.

I'm no expert in the field of modding, far from it, but my words above have some merit and I wish you well, just be careful. Nothing on this earth is unbreakable! Keep up the good work ReVeNaNt, just be careful!
