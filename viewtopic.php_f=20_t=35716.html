Are you saying the clients actually crash? Or is it they get kicked from the server instead? Should be the latter.

First, you cannot turn off the cheat detection in flserver (not any way I know of). There is LESS cheat detection WITHOUT the FL patch v1.1 though. You could use a patchless setup but that isn't as good because of the enhanced cheat detection, plugging a memory leak, more stability, and a few other things you'll be missing.

As for your problem, it's with the FL MP saved game files! Something has been placed into your character files that flserver does not like. A reinstalled flserver does nothing for your characters. I suggest you start over with new characters and delete the old ones.
