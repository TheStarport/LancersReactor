@DukeD - RPG is the way to go! Many servers (including mine) have RPG rules. Although RPG seems opposite of the name of the game Freelancer, it is done to force player interaction. For example any player carrying an illicite commodity (usually cardemine and artifacts) must be interdicted by Military and Police RPGers. It's part of the rules and thus has to be done. If you are a Pirate RPGer you are usually allowed to attack anyone and in return everyone else EXPECTS you to attack them! Can't feel bad about someone that expects to be attacked because he choose the RPG class of his own. If you are not a Pirate RPGer then consider another RPG class that is more suited to your taste. Except for minor details most RPG servers follow this format.

Since RPG is not for everyone my server is opt-in. This means if you wish to do RPG you must follow the rules and have an appropriately named character. Details can be found in rules through the link in my sig below.
