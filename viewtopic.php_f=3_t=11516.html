dmwnz

Tue Mar 25, 2003 7:04 am by dmwnz

The only restriction is that you need to do key single player missions to advance in level and that without advancing in level you won't live long in some of the more dangerous bits of space because you won't be able to buy the right ships/gear.

But you can delcine missions or just not respond to Juni's summons and it won't affect your status in the single player game. She'll just wait for you.

If you really wnat to bypass the single player missions just fire up the multiplayer server app that comes with the game and play on your own multiplayer server. You can do this quite happily without ever letting anyone else into the game.
