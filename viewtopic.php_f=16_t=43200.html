Sprague and lots of enemy? Where the hell is that Sprague place agin? Is that where they are blocking your jump gate as you lead out from Bretonia? No! Damned if I recall? Oh yeh, I think I have it. Bottom of the board, in the middle? Yeh? If so I'm not so sure you should be attacking the Cruisers. Keep back and go for the fighters. You have good support in the mission iirc. Also obey commands, when they tell you to dock or split do it.

Regarding ships, there is a ship available when Tobias gives you the creds, you can even buy it cheaper on New London, again if memory serves me right. It should last you a while if you can live with its god awful aestethics. I could not so kept the glorious Dagger.

Warp disruptors are good for pirates who want to stop prey from fleeing but you are more defensive atm. Maybe later, or even more so, maybe online PvP depending on your clan.

Maxed out weps drain the power waaayyy to quick. Maybe a better spread would work, some heavy some quick energy weps, some missiles for finishing off all with a lower class which would be more effective in long running dog fights?
