bakedpotato

Tue Jul 03, 2007 6:15 am by bakedpotato

The Quickfix will not change or add to any other files except for the Freelancer files.
So the answer to your question would be no, it will not harm your computer in any way.

Also if you have used any Mod's or change Mod's you will probably have to delete your Freelancer folder then reinstall Freelancer and then install the Quickfix.
And move or delete any saved games because they will cause problems too.

The Freelancer folder is located at
C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer

Your saved game files are located at
My Documents\My Games\Freelancer
Both the Singleplayer and Multiplayer folders.
