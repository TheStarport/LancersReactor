Recusant

Mon May 19, 2003 4:33 pm by Recusant

They scan you for illegal cargo (cardamine and artifacts), if they find any they'll ask you to drop it (from what I can tell they'll leave you alone if you do). I don't worry about it tho, I arrive at a planet from the poles and then head towards the docking ring so that I'm not even near the police till the last minute. If they do catch you, i would try and dock before they open fire. If they do open fire as you go in, either buy a new ship on the planet to escape or (if you want to be boring) save the game and reload, they'll cease to be there waiting for you.
That saved my butt once, I was in a dromedary near Tripoli shipyard when a corsair in a Titan told me to drop my loot of 275 gold ("hell no", so I docked with Tripoli just as i came under fire. I saved, sold my goods and went out to face the music.
1 badly-equipped dromedary vs. 20 titans and Tripoli shipyard doesn't last long
But when I reloaded everything was hunky-dory!!

OK, here's the skinny:

Selling cardamine will not affect your rep, even if you DO get caught with it. The cops will attack you, but leave them alone. What DOES affect your rep is if you FIRE at the cops once they start shooting. JUST RUN AWAY. If you attack the cops, docking rings have a tendency to go hostile. Along with the rest of the systems. Once you get 3 or 4 traderings away, AND you didn't attack cops, all is forgiven, and no rep damage.
