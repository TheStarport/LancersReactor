Dev

Sun Oct 29, 2006 2:22 am by Dev

Counter to its name, explosion_resistance is a damage multiplier. If you set it to 0.1, then 10% of the damage from the explosion will be dealt to the piece of equipment. If you set it to 100, presumably 100 times the normal damage would be dealt.
