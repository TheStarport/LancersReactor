tha_rami

Hi. I've got some questions about evochron.
Hey, I'd be glad to answer some. Keep in mind the creator of these games is very open to questions and feedback and will be happy to take your wishes into account if they are feasible - go to www.evochronrenegades.com and click on Forums.

I've downloaded the demo, and now have something to ask:
I've read the game is modable, so,
can the missile range be increased(say, to 8-10k)?
No, I think not. Could be available on request.

Velocity and range of guns, can it be modified somehow?
Their name can be modded, I'm not fully aware of range and velocity changes. I think they could be available on request.

What are the difference between SP and MP(it's said they ALMOST don't differ)?
You cannot hire fleet members during MP. Your savegame is shared between SP and MP. That's about all as far as I'm aware - I've noticed no other differences.

What is the actual speed of your ship? It states "1020", but i guess this means 102m/s? And thruster gives you >400m/s, not 4k?
1020 units. Check out the demo to see what speeds you're moving at relative to any nearby planet. Someone figured out the actual speed on the forums, you could ask there.

After I bought an engine, which characteristics were 2 times greater, than the one I sold, actual speed was "1060", ?
Take into account further modifications to your ship. The heavier your ship, contents and cargo, the stronger your engine needs to be.

So, what's the maximum speed, and can it be modified?
>4K units

So what can be moded in the game in general? And if something can't be moded, will the developer agree to implement some features or help implement some features?


A few words about capships, their destruction, stations destruction?
First one: yes, definitely - even for certain contracts. You can also take contracts to spy on them and they will resist heavily. Capships are floating safety beacons or dangers and definitely tough preys unless with a big fleet or 3+ person group in MP when new.

About looting, only cargo or guns too?
Cargo. You can loot guns, missiles and special weapons ect. from hidden cannisters.

Destructible components of ships, is it there?
Yes, engine NAVigation systems, ENGine components and WEApons systems.

This, though I am not impressed with missiles and guns range, seems to be a very good(if not say more) game anyway, why such a lack of attention here?
Because we've had a lack of questions. LR seems to be unwilling to promote much of Evochrons updates and this resulted in a lack of posting on these forums. Therefore I'd like to point you to www.starwraith.com/forums/index.php for further questions!

Glad you like the game, hope to hear more from you on our forums.
