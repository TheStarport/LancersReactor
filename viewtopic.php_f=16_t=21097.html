I guess, people dislike the Bounty Hunters because the game sets them hostile for no apparent reason (I am not talking about the one time where a reason is given) and at that point you have only two choices.

First, let them shoot at you with impunity instead of vaporizing them like any other ships that attack you, until you have slain enough criminals to appease the Bounty Hunters.

Second, fight back when you get attacked. As a consequence, your rep with law enforcement (house police, armed forces) will get worse in a hurry because the BH are thick with them.

There are similar considerations with the Corsairs - they are the only source for pulse guns of certain classes and so it is a bad idea to shoot back when attacked. This can get ticklish in mine fields or clouds with volatile gas pockets because in these environments it is much more dangerous to run away than to simply destroy the attackers.
