Concerning the mouse: you can either hold down the left mouse button or select continuous mouse flight without having to hold the button by pressing the space bar once. In both cases you drag/move the pointer to some point on the screen and your ship will try to allign itself to that spot. In other words: the farther you move the pointer from the center of the screen the harder is the turn of your ship into the given direction. A click on the right mouse button fires the weapons. It pretty much resembles Joystick flight with one difference: your crosshair isn't fixed to the center of the screen because your weapons are on moveable turrets.

I think i got it right, if not... well.
