I was figuring that if I made tons of money before engagin more of the story missions, then by the time I was doign the missions again and finishing them, I'd go straight to the next level after finishing the mission... meaning, if you finish mission 3 of the story, and are level 5 (I think?), and it says your next level reqiirement is to make more money, what I THOUGHT I could do was make that money in advance. You can't. No matter how much money you have, you always have to make a little more to gain the next level.

Interesting thing, though - your net worth includes the value of your weapons (which will have the same value regardless of where you sell them), ship, and cash, but NOT your cargo, which has different values in different locations. So, if you want to go up another level right after finishing a story mission, just see to it that you've got your cargo hold full of something that'll be very valuable on the station where you're ending the mission. Land, the mission ends, you go up a level, go sell your cargo, go up another level.
