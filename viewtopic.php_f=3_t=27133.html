Well, about the mission thing, I'm sorry, nobody can change that. But you can try and do high level missions in a light fighter. very amusing and forces you to outfit your ship with the best stuff around. It also improves your fighting skills a bit.

And for all the other entertainment, I direct you to the Multiplayer section of the game. Try out a few fairly crowded servers and meet new people. That's realy way better compared to singleplayer. But that's your choice.

You can also try out some mods wich make the game a tad harder (NPC's shields regenerate and such) and add new guns/ships/systems to the Freelancer Universe. I can sugest The Next Generation (TNG), Hostile Universe (HU), Rebalance, Freeworlds and a nice little mod called Level 2 wich can be found at www.frost-works.com

Also, Chips made a mod called Evelutions, wich can be found on the mainpage of this site.

See you online someday and have fun in the mean time
