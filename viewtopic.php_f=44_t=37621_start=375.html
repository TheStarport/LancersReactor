Argh

Sat Dec 10, 2005 3:33 pm by Argh

For a civilian version, I think that it'd look good in a pale green- like a lime color, with some gently-applied dirt, scratches, etc to keep it from looking too neat and tidy. It basically looks like a fun little sports-car, with waaaay too much engine for too little body, so it should feel sporty. Me being me, I'd probably end up putting racing stripes on it

As a military craft, on the other hand, it could look really nice with an over-under job- dark color on top, lighter on the bottom. Which might be a pain- grouping this is going to be tricky. I'd suggest a deep navy blue, with proper detailing to make it not feel totally flat. To emphasize its functional nature, if a military craft, I'd suggest some detailing on the sides of the engines to give them that "WWII fighter" sort've feel- some small vents with oil smears trailing down the engine cowling would really make it look like a serious piece of work. People always liked the Patriot, I think, because although it's a kind've silly design in some respects (the "feathers" are pretty un-functional-looking) it has an overall feel that suggests that its performance will be nice and sporty- which fits its in-game role really well. I think DA did a very good job of taking that design and making it feel believeable in its specific role.
