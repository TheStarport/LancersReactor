Chips

Tue Feb 10, 2004 2:45 pm by Chips

I know its late to put this point in, and it echoes what the third post said (i think).

Play storyline.

play storyline again.

Go online to a busy server and play.

Join a clan

Play more and more online until you know all weapons/ships/NPC's/bases/systems etc etc.

Once you have done that, then start with mods. UNTIL that day, don't touch the things. Seriously - you should be able to get around 2 months of pure enjoyment out of FL by playing up to 3hours a day without any trouble. Only after that time, then try mods - and start small for christ sake - like one ship or something.

The recommendation to "try this mod" and then mentioned a mod of considerable size is the one sure way to kill your game in under 2 weeks.

When you get bored of playing online, play the storyline again - i musta played it about 15 times already
