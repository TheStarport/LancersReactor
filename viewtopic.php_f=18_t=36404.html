Argh

Mon Nov 29, 2004 7:54 pm by Argh

Uh... well...

For those of us who're mod devs.... it's not a big deal. I have zero attachment to any save files, because I know that they're going to get trashed... today, tomorrow, next Tuesday... it doesn't matter. I have no idea how many Air Pirates I've shot down while playing Warriors of the Sky- and it really doesn't matter, much- if the mod works, and it's cool, what other reward do I need?

Getting attached to savefiles is a two-edged sword- when savefiles get corrupted/damaged during the modding process, they're dead. So if you have a savefile that's precious... go find it and manually back it up somewhere, if you're a modder. Or just get over it- after all, the savefile's just data, and if it's an SP save, you can just edit up a new one...

If it's a MP file... back it up. Or just live with the consequences if you make a mistake. I guess I just don't get attached to my MP "characters" like some people do, but I can see where you're coming from. Still, when you're a modder, these things will happen , and you need to be ready... and do what must be done.
