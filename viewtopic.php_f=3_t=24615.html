Freelancer Prequel / Retraction Pack

No sequel BS here. This is partly serious and partly making fun of all the Sequel/expansion pack threads. This is the place to say what shouldn't have been in the game if anything.

Personally for a retraction pack I'd like to go back to a joystick (That is the only time it will be said, okay.)

I'd also like to retract from CG movies to Real Actors.

For a prequel, I wish there was a better understanding of the beginning politics of the Sirius system. How each faction staked out their domain.

A place of origin for a lead character with real homey details (for better or for worse) would be good. What I mean by that is if it was a good home it would be hard to leave, if it was a bad home Trent couldn't wait to leave or whoever the character.

It would also be nice to be in ships that still weren't perfected and had some minor and major faults. And there wouldn't be heavy classes yet for anyone except the military.

Nanobots and shield batteries should be retracted in my mind.

Watching the construction of tradelanes would be neat. But I personally don't like the whole trade lane system. I'd just want to have the jumps closer to each other.

Personally I would also like the radiation zones retracted. If a place is that bad for a ship there wouldn't even be a jump to it.

Again, this is a prequel and retraction pack thread, not a "would be neat to have thread."
