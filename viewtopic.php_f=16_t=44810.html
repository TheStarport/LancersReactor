Holy Mackerel! It works!

I reinstalled the game, got up my total worth to 1.5mil (just to be on the safe side), and went at Mission 7 with a Crusader armed thusly...

2x Pyros Type 1
3x Pyros Type 2
Catapult
Starkiller

...and I looted myself a Level 7 shield from one of the Razor Wing fighters during the "Destroy the Rheinland Fleet" phase of the mission (after leaving Shinkaku).

Note that Razor Wing is odd in that they seem to regenerate their shields faster than normal NPCs. Maybe they have different shields -- that might help explain why you can loot shields in this case, but not in other cases.

Edit: By no means does the shield drop every time. You'll probably have to try quite a number of times, maybe 10, maybe as much as 20. My superstitous theory (probably totally baloney) is that your chances improve if you make a highly damaging killshot (hence the suggested loadout -- though twin Catapults is probably even better).
