Diivox

Fri Jun 13, 2003 11:35 pm by Diivox

I find all the VHFs to be balanced. People who scream about the eagle being the best havent fought a pilot capable of an engine kill or even an elementary knowledge of how to use turrets. I see people all the time justifying the sabre as the best due to forward facing turret, but if you apply yourself for one good night of Freelancer, that pretty much becomes irrelevent, because its not hard to use a rear facing turret effectively.

So I guess it comes as no surprise my favorite is the Titan. it is the Heavy Tank of Sirius, I just love all that extra armor, because I explore more than anything else and its niec to have more buffer when you are in radiation. also against the AI all it takes is some class 9+ weapons and your turn rate is irrelevent. they are easily beaten.

the titan only closely edges out the sabre for me, but thats due to my absolute adoration of the Outcasts (always the first to green, best illegal cargo, generally safe and non confrontational runs from Omicron Alpha to Liberty, and the fact that if I was Trent, I would willfully get addicted to Cardimine for the extended life span, the narcotic aspect being a nice side effect =D). the Saber is a no brainer ship and is great fun to go hunting Corsair Titans in. and for the record, i always buy it from Omicron Alpha, because the journey there is alot of fun and quite beautiful, while vogtland is rather bland.

non VHFs I go with the Dagger. simply the most logical of all the ship designs in freelancer and definitely the most beautiful. not to mention the fact that a dagger with class 2 xeno scorpions (dont even need the turret) is overpowered for Liberty (yet can be bought in starting system) is evenly matched for Bretonia, and can still survive in Kusari assuming a level 4 shield and some flight ability. no other ship gets that kind of "bang for buck". too bad the Chugoku system is so incredibly useful, because I tend to have to "thank" the blood dragons for the use of their system by buying the hawk each time i stop by.

Dromedary is cool because it is the only ship that actually has use after you can afford to rig it with beyond-spec stuff. it has the largest cargo hold of any ship in the game, is relatively difficult to get a hold of - that whole sense of accomplishment thing for making everyone im magellan your friend - and is the only ship you tend to see online other than Eagles...

and finally, the Hammerhead recieves honorable mention. it is quite satisfying to blow up the class 10 VHFs with only class 9 guns, and a loadout of Skyblasts is just downright scary. I beleive it to be the equal of the eagle, and flown by the right person can hold its own versus a saber, and against AI/most players rips Titans to shreds.

I play this game excessively, and dont get out much, sorry for the novel =P

(6 times through the game, and now flown every single ship)
