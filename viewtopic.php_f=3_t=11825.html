Thu Mar 27, 2003 6:38 am by guptasa1

I did figure this one out. Most of the music (assuming the default install) is in the C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer\DATA\AUDIO\MUSIC directory. BUT...for the music in scenes that you say isn't there (the walking out scene), that's because they must record the music and the voices together for that. It can be found in the C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer\DATA\AUDIO\MIXES folder instead! Careful in there though - all the major cutscenes seem to be done that way, so if you haven't played the game and pic the wrong one you could hear something you didn't want to.

BTW, the tarmat music you meant is in the file mix_s010x.wav in that directory (along with dialogue).
