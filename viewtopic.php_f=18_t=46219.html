Thu Aug 02, 2007 8:20 am by Ogu

@flybyu: You could put one of those docking beacons at the center of the planet and give it the planet's name (same infocard numbers). Just increase the solar radius and docking radius of the object in solararch.ini so that they are larger than the radius of the planet and it's death/burn zone. Now, you probably won't be able to click on the planet directly to dock, you will likely have to click the name of the docking beacon from the contact/target list.

Now, when you undock, you won't want to be inside the planet. Throw a hardpoint outside the planet's atmosphere/burn/kill zone, and set it as the undocking hp.

I've been meaning to mess around with this for a while. It would be nice if there was an easy way to do this.
