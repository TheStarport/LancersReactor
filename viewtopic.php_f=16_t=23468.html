Personally, I preferred the Alien organisms from Glasgow to Cambridge ($600 profit per unit, minus minor losses during the trip), diamonds from Cambridge to Leeds ($396 prifit per unit), and then a short trip from Leeds back to Glasgow (I take consumer goods there... only $23 profit per unit, but I might as well take somehting as long as I'm going over there...)

This totals up to $1,019 profit per unit of space you have for every cycle of the trip. In a Drone, which has 175 units of cargo space, that's $178,325 profit per trip (minus about $10,000 if you took long enough to lose 5 alien organisms on that leg of the cycle). I like it because there's not a heck of a lot of resistance, it is at most a 17 waypoint jaunt (Glasgow to Cambridge), and it doesn't take much time per trip.
