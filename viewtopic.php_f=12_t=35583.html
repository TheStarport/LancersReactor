that's not q as simple as it sounds. as the MX440 is still being asembled by oems it has been made DirectX9.0+ compatible by some, although it belongs to the DX8.1 generation at best. Nividia drivers should allow you to play most DX9+ games with a reasonably late and well-made card, although you will almost certainly ahve to turn a lot of stuff off. Your real issue is memory. your bios AGP aperture should be set to 64mb and your AGP multiplier to 2x/4x, no higher, and you need a minimum of 512mb RAM in the system to allow any respectable performance out of that card.
