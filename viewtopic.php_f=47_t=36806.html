Chapter 2: A New Threat (apparently the prolog was chapter one)

Trent loves his new cloaked ship. So he decided to go off to Omicron Gamma to chill with the Corsairs. Trent lands on Crete, sips on some Sidewinder Fang. He feels at home with his newfound friends. So he goes and starts talking to this Corsair chick.

TRENT: Hi, my name is Trent.

CHICK: Hi, my name is Gabriella.

TRENT: Well, Gabriella, I have lost my number. Can I have yours?

CHICK: OMFG, get away from me you Liberty Pansy.

TRENT: WTF? Whats your problem?

CHICK: Well, I just got back from a patrol. We were attacked over by a new jumphole we discovered.

TRENT: Nomads?

CHICK: No, but what I saw made my blood chill.

TRENT: What?

CHICK: While I was dogfighting, I saw letters on the side of the ship.

TRENT: What did they say?

CHICK: COALITION.....

Trent thought to himself, Coalition? Was this some sort of prank? Some sick headed Outcast or something? He laughed to himself. Coalition forces, here in Sirius. Ha. They are all stuck on that rock, Earth. However, curiosity got the best of him. He payed the Corsair chick like 10.000 credits or something, and headed off to the coordinates she gave him.

Trent flew for what seemed like hours. Then, he heard his Neural Net beep telling him he was getting close to the jumphole. Rubbing his tired eyes, he took the ship out of cruise. What he saw will remain burned in his mind forever.

A large fleet of ships, complete with a couple battleships and the works, were all rallying together. Trent started to jump back into cruise, afraid of them seeing him, but remembered he had the cloak. So he slowly slid closer to the odd looking ships. His neural net showed them as UNKNOWN. The ships looked nothing like anything in Sirius. As he edged closer, he began to see markings on the side of the ships. Letters. Letters that spelled out a word he knew couldn't be true. COALITION.

However, this was nothing compared to what he saw next. Behind the COALITION battleship,he thought he saw a few ships, bluish in color. Glass like. He gasped. NOMADS!

In his panic, he accidentally pushes the UNCLOAK button on his console. His ship decloaks. Trent, just watches in awe as the ships begin to converge on his ship. Oh no! In a last second decision, he punches the thrusters. The Nomad and Coalition ships begin to fire upon Trents ship.

TRENT: Faster, baby, faster!

The ships shields were nearly down. So he heads to the unknown jumphole, and enters the unknown.

Chapter 3: El Diablo

Trent breathes a sigh of relief. He made it. He escaped through the jumphole. New question began to arise, as he wonders what will happen to him once he gets to the other side of the jumphole. More Coalition forces? Nomads? A new galaxy? Trent grows pale with fear as he approaches the end of the jumphole.

WHOOSH. The familiar white and blue of hyperspace dissolves to the black and white of space.

TRENT: Where am I?

The space around him is tinted with green, much like the Omicron systems. His ship is illuminated by the twin suns, embraced in a cataclysmic struggle, throwing off yellow light everywhere. The nav computer struggles to identify the system, but only reads "UNKNOWN".

All of a sudden, the tranquility of space was shattered by the sound of his computer...

"WARNING : INCOMING TORPEDO"

"WARNING : INCOMING TORPEDO"

Trent immediately snaps out of his trance. He hits the thrusters, and struggles to pull back the joystick. The force of his manuever has him planted in the back of his seat, sweat dripping off of his brow. Pulling over 9 G's, he watches as the torpedo sails past him, missing him by mere meters. He could feel the ambient heat from the torpedo as it sailed by, and he killed the engine leaving only his thrusters on for better manuevering.

"THAT WAS NO TORPEDO I'VE EVER SEEN!"

After he whispers this, he freezes as he notices the torpedo changing direction.

TRENT : HOLY JEHOSAPHAT!

The torpedo moved at speeds matching a missile. It streaked back towards his ship. As a reflex, he hits the thrusters, trying to outrun it. However, the torpedo quickly closed in on him. So Trent, out of ideas, drops his last countermeasure. The torpedo detonates mere meters away. His ship is shuddered as the torpedo ignites, knocking down his shields with the shrapnel, and then the shock wave hit his ship. Lights flickered on and off on his HUD, and he noticed he was loosing control of the ship. His once nimble fighter now handled like a Humpback freighter.

Trent hits the cruise engines, and thats when he sees his attacker. A most unusual ship, much like the dagger, but bigger. And it was much faster than he was.

The unknown ship fires what appears to be a cruise disruptor, and Trent his knocked violently forward as the ship dissengages cruise. This makes Trent very angry.

TRENT : "You wanna play it soft, I'll play it soft. You wanna play it hard, I'll play it hard"

Trent begins to use evasive manuevers, trying to fend off his attacker. The ship behind him isn't as agile as him, so Trent fakes left, then pulls hard right. The ship, thrown off, streaks past. Trent pulls a quick loop, and situates himself behind his attacker. He fires his captured Nomad Cannons at the ship, watching them bounce off the shields.

Then the ship hits the brakes, and turns away. Trent, too late, streaks past him, and sees the letters "COALITION" printed on the side. Trent and the Coalition fighter then go head to head. They head towards each other in a life or death game of chicken.

"WARNING : INCOMING TORPEDO"

Trent quickly slides left at the last second, watching his attackers torpedo sail past, and Trent lets a Sunslayer do its business.

WHOOOSH ---------------BOOOM!

The Coalition ship is instantly nuked. However, the close proximity of the explosion blew his shields clean off, leaving him exposed. And then he remembered the torpedos have a very deadly homing mechanism. He punches the thrusters.......

"WARNING : INCOMING TORPEDO!"

Quickly, as his ship hurtles through space at nearly 200 kilometers a second, he spins to face backwards, and fires on the torpedo.

BOOOOOOM - His ship, unshielded, feels the effects of the torpedo's antimatter properties. His ship is shredded up, as pieces of hull fly off in all directions.

He was defenseless. His cloak didnt work. His thrusters were gone. His cruise was disabled. He could only limp away at 80 k/s. His cloak was destroyed. Shields were damaged. Half his weapons rendered unoperational. He could only drift......

"WARNING : LIFE SUPPORT DAMAGED"

That was when he really began to realise, he'd experience asphixiation in less than five minutes. Already he could feel the cold of space trickling in, biting through his flight suit. He could only wait.

Then, all of a sudden, he realised he wasnt alone.

Trent's blood turns to ice, as the coalition fleet de-cloaks around him.

One ship stood out in particular. The one that was many times the size of any battleship. The one that registered as a Juggernaut class ship, named the El Diablo.

Trent can only watch in awe as the Coalition fleet closes in on him.

TRENT : Oh no............
