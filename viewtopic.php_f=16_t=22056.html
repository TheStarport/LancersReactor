Nukenin

Mon Jul 28, 2003 7:45 am by Nukenin

Actually the Skyblast B is the most damaging class 9 turret over one second--it does 2,032.52 hull, while the Salamanca Turret only does 1,956 hull and the Buckshot Turret only does 1,564 hull. Not sure if there are any other class 9 turrets.

Since on the Hammerhead, Titan, or Eagle you are rarely firing the turret in conjunction with your full guns, the energy drain of the Skyblast B is inconsequential. For maximal efficiency (perhaps only really important on the Sabre when the turret is doubling as a seventh forward-firing gun) the Salamanca is probably the way to go, it only burns 388 for a full second of firing while the Skyblast B burns 508.13 and the Buckshot burns 488.
