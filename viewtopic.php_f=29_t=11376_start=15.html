Okay I just finished it :

DLL Numbers
-----------
The DLL numbers don´t say the name of the resource DLL without the number
that´s explained in freelancer.ini, for example, in the freelancer.ini,
under the Resources, the NameResources.dll is on the third place, that
means it has got number 3.
Is is also possible in the freelancer.ini to put your own resource DLLs in
the file to get a better overview. The DLL numbers will become logically
6,7,8, etc. The standard numbers in freelancer.ini are :
1 InfoCards.dll
2 MiscText.dll
3 NameResources.dll
4 EquipResources.dll
5 OfferBribeResources.dll
6 MiscTextInfo2.dll

For further help/questions/suggestions you can post something at freelancerfan.de
or send a personal message to Skyshooter
