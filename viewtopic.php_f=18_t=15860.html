First you need to read my System tutorial in the tutorial forum. That will give you a grasp as to how systems can be edited. This is the most important part as the system appears to set the level of the attackers.

Then you need add the ships to new systems to the AI factions available ship list. This means editing a lot of files. ITs a big job. Very big. Adding ships is almost like adding your own from scratch. Almost.

So the second thing you have to learn is how to add ships.

Once you have learn systems and ships the rest should become clear to you.
I could explain it now but youd just be back in a day asking about either systems or ships. So its better you learn both of those first then ask the questions.

Giskard
