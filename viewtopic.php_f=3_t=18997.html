If you've finished the story missions, then this is normal (well, it happened to me both times). What heppens is, instead of the number showing the amount of credits you have to have to get to the next level, it shows the amount of credits you have to earn to get to the next level. It's the same thing really, just a different way of showing it; it confused me for a long time too. You'll keep going up levels (until 38 at least) normally.

My game also periodically changed my next level requirement to "Mission", after I'd completed the story - but changed back after I earned some credits. Odd.
