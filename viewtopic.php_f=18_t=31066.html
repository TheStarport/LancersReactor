baene

Tue May 04, 2004 2:56 am by baene

I noticed that my Milkshape sometimes crashes on exports if the whole model isn't selected for some reason, so go to "EDIT" then "Select All" and then try to export.

As for ship not being visible, size can do that, also MAKE SURE that you are NOT using 32 bit textures!!! Freelancer will not display 32 bit textures which can also cause an invisible ship. Open up Photoshop or whatever program you use for picture editing and re-save (or just check and make sure) that your textures are 24 bit or lower.

Good luck...

Baene
