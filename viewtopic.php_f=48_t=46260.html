OMEGA Freelancer Mod

The OMEGA Freelancer mod is a new Freelancer mod being made due to the Worlds Unveiled mod becoming very inactive.



812 AS, 10 years after the Nomad War. Sirius has expanded into many new systems, pirate activity has increased, and new factions are emerging. Everything was perfect once the War ended, until...


*Imcomming Transmission From Explorer 7142B*

We've encountered an unkown alien race in an unknown system! Were trapped please help us! Our hull is at 15 %! We can't hold out mu... ch... long... errr....

*Transmission Ended*


The new alien race was a very bad thing, as, the next day a group of Nomads were discovered. They were not all gone. Sirius is now falling apart, and it is up to you to decide the future of Sirius.

Here is what it will have:

MANY new systems.
MANY new bases.
A few new factions.
New weapons.
Flyable capital ships.
And much more.
