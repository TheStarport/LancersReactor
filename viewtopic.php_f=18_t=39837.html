Argh

Tue Jul 12, 2005 2:06 am by Argh

These are all nice, but what you should keep in mind is that "mechs" will have to have "legs" that are either obviously "locked" into a static pose, or have jets in place of legs, or legs with rockets on the soles of the "feet". You're definately not going to see fully-animated mecha in this game engine, unless somebody really unlocks the animation formats and also gives mod designers the ability to tie animation states to either user input or variable states... in short, don't hold your breath. What I will get around to building at some point soonish will be pretty kewl, but don't expect it to be a Gundam that has a big plasma sword, k?
