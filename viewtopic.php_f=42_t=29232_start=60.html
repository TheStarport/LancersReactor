0sprey

Sun Mar 14, 2004 11:05 pm by 0sprey

Hi Guys,

Not to stick my nose into your business or anything, but I noticed a post by Sir S:

As an aside, is it possible to limit the number of commodities you can buy when they're available? Privateer did that and it would make it harder to make a profit trading in the game.


One thing you can do is alter the amount of space (volume) a given unit of choice commodities takes up in the cargo hold. In the select_equip.ini just alter the volume:

<pre><font size=1 face=Courier>[Commodity
nickname = commodity_tradelane_parts
ids_name = 261645
ids_info = 65903
units_per_container = 18
pod_appearance = cargopod_blue
loot_appearance = lootcrate_blue
decay_per_second = 0
volume = 1 <------- alter this value
hit_pts = 250 </font></pre>

I've been doing that with my mod and by simply changing the value to 2, you effectively "halve" the amount of that particular commodity any player can carry in their cargo holds.

If you've already explored this option, forgive my interruption.
