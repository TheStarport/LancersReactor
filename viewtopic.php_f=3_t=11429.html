I agree, I'm on the last single player mission which I expect to finish tonight. I think I've got about 30 hours into the singleplayer game and I feel that it was money well spent. I'm anxious to finish the single player game and begin exploring some more of the game. The last few missions I kind of rushed through leaving much to be discovered.

My final impressions on the game is that it's extremely approachable for newcomers to the genre and quite enjoyable as a quick fix for a verteran guy like myself. It's a tad short in comparison to the other games before it but overall the production value is top notch and it's a great ride while it lasts.

It feels much like Privateer lite in many ways and it has left me with a thirst for a deeper game like X2 or maybe EVE Online. So that gives me something to look forward to while I poke around the FreeLancer Universe.
