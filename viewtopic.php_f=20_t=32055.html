I'm gonna go ahead and try a previous display driver and see what happens. Ill post to let all know. Kinda sucks to to have to crash FL everytime your rdy to exit. Oh and for those of you that cant get your start bar to pop up, it does indeed pop up in the background, you just cant see it. Just do this, 1: hit the windows key, 2: scroll up 2 times, 3: hit enter, 4: hit enter again. This will activate the switch users option for windows, and take you to the user screen. Once you log back in, you will still see freelancer frozen, just go ahead and hit the windows key, and now it should let you see the start bar, therefor allowing you to right click freelancer and hitting close. If you do all of these steps and you still cant get the start bar, at step 4 press the right arrow key first then hit enter. This will log you out and close all programs. I hope that helps you guys that are having to reboot. Ill let ya know what happens with the previous vid drivers.
