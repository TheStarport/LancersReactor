Tue Jun 07, 2005 4:00 am by zazie

Running XP SP2 ?
I have a similar setup and experience more CTDs when launching with new comp with XP SP2.
But:
Most of such crashes are caused by a corrupt file of your game or a corrupt savegame. This can happen with modded and unmodded games.
Backup your savegames, reinstall FL (the way you are told before installing one of the major mods) and try your savegames (if autosave does not work take a earlier save).

If this does not help you may try to run FL in Win2000-compatibility-mode (right-click FL-icon/properties/compatibility)
