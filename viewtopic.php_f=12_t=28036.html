Tue Feb 03, 2004 3:03 pm by Esquilax

I too, have a Live! card. It does the job, but does not support EAX 2 and 3. Version 3.0 is the latest incarnation, and offers great 3D sound. One option for you if you are into music composition is "Hoontech". Their cards aren't too expensive, and they are professional quality. There are some Hercules carda around, but they are not as good as Creative cards. The Audigy 2 is probably the way to go, but I'd avoid the Audigy 2 ZS. It has support for 7.1 surround, but who has (or needs) that many speakers?
