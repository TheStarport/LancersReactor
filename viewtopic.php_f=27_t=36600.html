IDS? will show up when the game does not fine the name/infocards the numbers in the inis are referencing to. That can be caused by a bad installation, a screwed FLMM installation (unlikely) and many other circumstances, like more than mod activated might overwrite a dll containing infocards etc.

When everything they made names etc. for gives IDS?, it is obviously no the mod's fault (if they do not state that there were no infocards done yet), because no one would release a mod without doing infocards and without telling you that they would be done later.
