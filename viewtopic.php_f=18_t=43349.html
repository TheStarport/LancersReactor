Ilerien -

Your question is very very very vague and virtually unanswerable my friend...

Chips' suggestion is an excellent one to help you with your mods.

I will also suggest that you use his Single Player mod for debugging before you try FLServer.

For this problem, you have not given us much information to help you.

Your question is saying "I made a system and it's telling me it can't find people in it, what is wrong?"

Well I will try anyhow...

Your system definition is probably defining incorrect body names for the characters at one or more of the bases you have made. You have to use body parts and hands and heads that are in the original game files, my friend.

When you make a new system, you should define your bases and people and planets and suns and asteroids and nebulas and ships etc... by using existing parts. If you want to invent your own new ones then you must also construct every part you use too. That is not easy.

So - for your new system that does not work... if you put the characters in it yourself, you should be able to find the places you defined characters that look like "elite" or similar.

Go and find the original DATA\MISSIONS\mbases.ini and one of the good system ini files like DATA\UNIVERSE\SYSTEMS\LI01\Li01.ini - print them both out and look at them on paper and compare them carefully with your ones.

Then if you have specific problems you can't see easily, try asking a better and more precise question again please, BUT YOU HAVE to post your system ini file and the part of the mbases.ini that describes your system if you want to help us to try to help you accurately.

Be very careful when you define your systems and everything, most problems are caused by bad spelling so when the game tries to translate them, they do not match with existing parts that the game knows about. So printing out your system definition files on paper is a good way to help you see problems.

I'm trying to help you understand what you have to do and how to ask. Many friends here will reply very well to precise questions. But very few will reply to vague ones like this one.

Good luck pal.
