Dark Shadow,

You should be able to add more than one faction to the game; I have 11 now and I read someone had over 15!

Adding factions is a delicate thing; some errors can trigger desktop crashes when you've just started up the game, even before you get to the menu. Errors that cause a dtc are most probably somewhere in initialworld.ini or empathy.ini.

A nasty type of error (from my experience), that is not so visible at first sight (because, on debugging, you usually look for typo's or impossible values or missing data), is where you've made a faction like two groups that hate each other -- there is a loyality conflict and the game apparently can't handle that. When implementing a faction, it's best to make it neutral towards all the rest, and leave the reputation and empathy stuff for later. It's also recommended (I fear Í sound like a schoolmaster now) to backup your mod before adding a new faction.
