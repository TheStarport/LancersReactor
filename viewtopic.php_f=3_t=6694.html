Gryf

Mon Feb 24, 2003 10:02 pm by Gryf

If you want to get on the Liberty Rogues good side do the following:

Go to Detroit and kill Xenos... Kill a lot of Xenos.. Lots and lots.

Then go to Rochester (Junker base) and take missions against Xenos, build up faction with the Junkers. Prety soon the LIberty Rogues will be neutral with you and you will be able to land at Buffalo base in the bad lands.

Then you kill a few bounty hunter or navy guys to build your faction with the Liberty Rogues. BE CAREFUL! you kill too many and Buffalo is the only place in New York you will be able to land.

Then when you get on good enough terms with the Liberty Rogues you can take missions from their bases increasing your faction even more.

Good luck!
