The first time I ever used a torpedo. It was around mission 6 or 7 in the SP campaign, and I had traded in my Javelin missile launcher which wasn't helping much anymore for a Starkiller launcher.

The first enemy ship I faced after that, we face head on, start shooting, I hit the torpedo button... <font size="+3">BOOM!</font>

Torpedoes have been my favorite weapon ever since. (I've taken out three Bounty Hunter Hammerheads in one "jousting" pass with Tizonas and Sunslayers. Shoot-shoot-BOOM, shoot-shoot-BOOM, shoot-shoot-BOOM.)


Other "wow" moments were seeing the triple-sun Sigma system, the red-giant Omega system and limping over to Solarius station out of nanobots, seeing the pirate war system in Omega-5.

Oh, and another great moment was when I was entering New Tokyo system between missions in the SP campaign. While heading for the trade lane I scanned some adjacent ships to have something to do. One was full of Gold. I'd never pirated before and wanted to see how it works. Started shooting the transport, and the instant it exploded I hit B and jumped into the trade lane. Didn't even see what I'd picked up until I landed on New Tokyo, but it was 30 units of gold and I was suddenly $40,000 richer.

Seanus

Thu Jun 12, 2003 11:48 am by Seanus

Started a clan recently.
Honourable venture.
Not the bullying type.
Small tolls to pass through Omic Gamma. Get you honour guard and protection. You know the sort of thing.

One non noob refused to pay. I was charging him I think $50 and he was L25. I know this is nothing, but it's fun right.

Anyway. The guy docked with home planet, whilst i'm trying to explain that he couldn't do this. Clearly he was well up for it, taunting me left right and centre. Calling me and my clan everything under the sun. I politely informed him that failure to pay would be a BAD idea, but he wouldn't have any of it. I'd docked to repair, and by the time i'd left to rekindle our little tet-a-tet, he'd left the system heading north.

I began the chase which, if you've ever been a bounty hunter is real hard. You know which system they're in, but you don't know where they're going. This for me is the real challenge, and therefore the real fun part. I'll try and cut this short. I chased him through dresden and the other Rhineland systems, made some bad calls,getting futher and further behind.

suddenly I realised he ws heading back to NY.

This is bad, as the server rules allow sanctuary to all outside manhatten. I must profess I was shaking with adrenaline. You must realise that this fella is taunting all the way. I knew the odds were stacked against me. BIG TIME. all the guy had to do was stop somewhere anonymous or dock, and i'd never know where he was.

But he kept moving on system by system. getting closer to NY.

Finally he hit NY, i calculated I was about 30 secs behind him when he went thru JG. but that's a lot of time to make up.

I hit NY, knew the quickest route to Manhatten and sped thru tradlanes towards Westpoint. Upon reaching the academy, i knew i'd lost my mark. This next trade lane journey was unstoppable, and the end was his sanctuary.

I turned to leave. Really pissed, but buzzing from the chase. There he was, sitting under the acamdemy. In all the excitement I hadn't even checked my immediate vicinity.

Spaced him. It was miraculous. Even the Mark was impressed with the Tenacity shown.
