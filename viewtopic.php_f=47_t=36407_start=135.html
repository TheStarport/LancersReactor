Gaia News

War Brewing?
There has been rumors abound about a war between the Corsairs and Rhineland, even after peace has been made. It seems that the Outcasts are the problem, because the Corsairs want the sleeper ship Hispania, but if they attack the Outcasts, Rhineland will attack them.

Hispania Wanted!
The Corsairs have declared that they demand the sleeper ship Hispania from the Outcasts. Why the heck they would want it is unknown, but the Outcasts seem to deny the Corsairs the ship just to annoy them. More news will be announced as it becomes available.

Jamaica Ready!
Planet Jamaica terraforming has been completed. Although some people seem to think Gaians are against all terraforming, High Commander Roc has clarified that we are against it by other factions who would pollute the planet, but we can trust ourselves to keep the planet clean. The planet is living up to it's name, being a tropical paradise with many crystal clear lakes and other bodies of water, along with palm trees and perfect weather. The planet is named after a tropical island on Earth in the Carribean, which the system the planet is in is named after.

Perth Captured!
Perth Station, a small Planetform base in charge of watching over the Terraforming efforts on Planet Ayr, has been captured by the Gaians and is now our base. High Commander Roc has declared that the base may still be used to terraform Planet Ayr, but the Gaians will do it, so it doesn't become polluted.

A New Power?
The reformed Hispania is becoming more and more powerful. With the Corsairs, Zoners, Blood Dragons and Samura Industies, money and commoditeis are being made by the freighterload, and they have claimed to have the second best economy, only being beaten by Bretonia. Will this new power become even stronger than the mighty Bretonia? Only time will tell, but the Gaians clearly want Bretonia to stay the greatest power, as it is our best ally.

Rhinland Tohoku?
Rhineland has claimed the system of Tohoku has Rhineland space. This will definately not go well well with Kusari, as Tohoku is owned by Kusari. More news on this will become availible as the story develops
