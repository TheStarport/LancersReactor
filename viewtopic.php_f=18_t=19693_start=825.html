parabolix

Mon Nov 29, 2004 2:53 pm by parabolix

Hi wizard

To change the music you will need to go into your Music folder. It is (if you installed Freelancer into the default directory) "C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer\DATA\AUDIO\MUSIC" You will then need to find the music you want to replace. The name of the Manhattan Bar music is music_bar_li01.wav. You then just rename your song to music_bar_li01 and paste it in the folder, overwriting the previous file. Make sure the file is in wave format. You can download Wavepad here. Wavepad lets you easily change audio files into many formats.
