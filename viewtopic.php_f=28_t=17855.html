Here's my first wish list. It's based on the ships in that old space trading classic: Privateer.

The Tarsus: The basic freighter, with four weapon slots, a small-medium sized cargo hold and not much else really...

The Orion: A small heavy fighter with very large shields and armour, a decent cargo hold as well as a rear turret, although turns rather slowly and only has 3 weapon slots

The Galaxy: A massive frieghter with 4 weapon slots, and 2 turrets, one at the top, one at the bottom (though I think we could change this to four turrets for Freelancer, unless that's too much...), and a huge cargo capacity, but being a freighter is the slowest in maneuverability and is only slightly faster than a Tarsus.

The Centurion: Very Heavy Fighter that is very fast and very maneuverable (although big), has 6 weapon slots and a rear turret. Only weakness is that it is slightly under-armoured, as well as having limited cargo...


I also thought up another wish list as well. How about ships based on Blizzard's Starcraft? I doubt we could have Zerg units (although that would be very novel, and kudos to anyone who manages to attempt it), but surely the Terran and Protoss Units could have a go...

Light Fighters:

Wraith (Terran)
Scout (Protoss)
(Possibly) Interceptor (Those little ships manufactured in Protoss carriers)

Heavy Fighters:

Valkyrie (Terran)
Corsair (Protoss)

Frieghters:

Terran Dropship
Protoss Shuttle

Battleships:

Battlecruiser (Terran)
Protoss Carrier

What do you think?
