Compared to how well the game looks, it is very conservative on the PC Requirements. MP really requires a good pipe to the Net, but if you consider that a 32MB TNT2 graphics card (a card close to 4 years old!) and a PC with a 450 MHz P3 CPU ran this game well enough, most of you guys should be homefree.
