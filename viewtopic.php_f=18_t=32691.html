The ship.ini file, find the ship you want. This is just a sample -
HP_tractor_source=HpTractor_Source
shield_link=l_elite_shield01, HpMount, HpShield01, HpShield02, HpEngine01
hp_type = hp_gun_special_10, HpWeapon01, HpWeapon02, HpWeapon03, HpWeapon04, HpWeapon05, HpWeapon06 ;this is how many you have
hp_type = hp_gun_special_9, HpWeapon01, HpWeapon02, HpWeapon03, HpWeapon04, HpWeapon05, HpWeapon06 ;the first Item , 9 in this case, tells the level of gun alowed. 10 , means level 10 guns.
