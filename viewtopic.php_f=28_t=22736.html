Australis Mod

Hey everyone,

I've been working on a mod for about 1 month now, which has been named the Australis Mod. The Australis mod is my first ever big mod and is set to include...

- 4 Systems with new news, loot, wrecks, bases, etc.
- 7 new ships
- Several New Commodities (including illegal)
- Purchasable Battleships
- Nomad Ships
- Some hidden secret for you to find out

By the name you can guess that the mod is based around Australis (Australia). Australis, Oceanus, New Zealand and Rimworld are the new systems. The six new ships are: The Monaro (HF, in the USA it's known as the Pontiac GTO I think), Commodore SS (LF), Astra (Civ), XR-8 (HF), Slipstream XJ-376 (Custom), StormUte (Freighter), Focus (civ). New commodities include Beer, Slaves, Pirate Software, and more (not all have been finalised). New equipment will be available but that will be for you to find.

Please tell me what you think of it. So far Alpha 1.0 is out but features very little but a basic system. Alpha 1.2 should be out soon, adding battleships, nomads, semi-warp drives and much more!

http://australis.vze.com
