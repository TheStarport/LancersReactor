I think the TNG mod was the one that was a bit clever. If you opened up stuff like constants.ini and altered the cruise speed, it would make no difference. To solve the problem, open up "Freelancer.ini" in the EXE folder, and look at what files are assigned to what. The section you're looking for starts like this: (in the TNG Freelancer.ini)

asteroids = solar\asteroidarch.ini
equipment = equipment\light_equip.ini
equipment = equipment\select_equip.ini
equipment = Solar\dockable\asteroid_base.cmp
equipment = Equipment\models\commodities\nn_icons\special.3db

Notice that the last two equipment files are not ini files, and don't look like anything to do with equipment. Nonetheless, open them up with Notepad and you'll have what you want. In your specific case, the ship file is:

characters\Bodies\new_male_body.dfm

but bear in mind that I have an old version of TNG, it may be different.
