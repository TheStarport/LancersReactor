I don't like bribing organisations, especially criminals/pirates. It is costly and wrecks your rep with other factions. The biggest problem with bribes, however, is that the rep change you get is completely OTT. For instance, I was hostile to the corsairs and wanted a certain (ahem) shield from them. I didn't want to bribe them as this would have cost me 150K, I barely had enough for the shield as it was. So I found mutual enemies to destroy. I hung a round the sigma-13 jump hole in the new berlin system and tore a bunch of red hessians to shreds. It took a while and a lot of dead hessians, but slowly the corsairs became neutral to me. I could then buy the shield. Wsnt that a lovely story?

Now, my problem is that the red hessians were the only organisation that made any difference to my rep with the corsairs. I now want to explore outcast systems, but cannot find any mutual enemies that make much difference to my outcast rep when i destroy them. I amgenrally on good terms with police, millitary, corporations etc, and on bad terms with criminals. Who should I kill to make the outcasts like me and where can i find my targets?
