I think that fan site is misleading, but it isn't actually incorrect. See, if you're in a freighter, and you join formation with a friendly NPC freighter, you will follow that ship through combat (evasive maneuvers, docking with trade lane, etc). That would allow you to fully concentrate on firing your turrets. However, you cannot follow an ENEMY while you fire at them.

And TwoSheds--I believe that the "neural net helps you aim" comment refers to the little aiming cross that shows you how to properly lead enemy ships. Coming from a background of WWII sims, I can tell you that that little aiming cross is a big deal in itself. Makes things almost too easy...
