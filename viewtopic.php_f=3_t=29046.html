Visconti

Tue Mar 02, 2004 9:19 pm by Visconti

Do you already have it? Well there're varying reviews about it, but most of them are positive.

Graphics: Very good. 1 Year old and I still admire many of the locales.

Story: Excellent! I like it so much, it keeps you glued to your seat trying to get past the next set of missions to see what will be. Nevertheless the ending leaves something to be desired :S I don't say that it's bad, but it didn't quite tie up all the loose ends.

Voice Acting: Is also very good, very convincing. However! Dialogs get repeatious. Too few of them that you hear over and over again. But as far as the main campaign goes it's very well done.

Characters! Man I love them. This game managed to create characters that're very convincing. You grew to care and liked them a lot. It's unfortunate you can't get to see them that much.

Game Play: Dog fighting pretty fun. Whenever I feel frustrated I'd load my eagle up with sunslayer and shoot the first thing that comes my way
