Your english is fine, I understood what you meant with no problem. They're just very different games. It's been quite a while since we focused on Starlancer here, but we used to edit just about everything in that game. At one point I had a fleet of Homer Simpsons shooting lasers out his mouth and tachyon cannons out his "ehem" derriere. I had the entire enemy fleet's ships set to a maximum speed of 1, and so they would just sit there with their engines smoking while Homer went over and farted Tachyon beams until all the bad guys were space dust. But it's true, with Starlancer you're a member of the military, and have people you need to answer directly to. Freelancer was designed for you to have the choice of whatever faction you want to ally with (after the missions, of course). They're both great games, and yes, both quite different with the exception that they both take place mostly in space.
