Thanks for your replies.
Here's what I've been able to piece together:
*Ahem*
When you hit F8 your character's rep shows up and -every- faction/company is listed along with an "attitude barometer". If you click on a faction/company you get a short paragraph describing what they do or their general disposition with respect to the rest of Sirius. There's a total of 48 of them.
From there you can hit the 'print screen' button.
Then Ctrl-Alt-Del.
Freelancer will 'minimize' and give you the system status/restart window as well.
Open your favorite image editor.
Click 'New' and/or 'paste'.
The image in FL will paste to a new pic file that you can edit/copy, etc.
Next: Click on the FL button in the system tray at the bottom of your screen.
FL will come back up and you can 'print screen' the next faction/company or whatever you want to copy.

I use ACDSee which will open its own new folder and put your image into the folder automatically. *Cool*
Then I cropped the images (200+k/image) down to just the description. Now I have all 48 companies/factions that I can reference any time without going in and out of the game.
Hope that helps.
