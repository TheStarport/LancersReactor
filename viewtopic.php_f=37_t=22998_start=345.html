Harrier, Parabolix, and Glock 36

The Epsilon Ships Mod Version 2.5 has some of the best ships I have ever seen in FL mods. The mod is also very stable; I visited most of the stations and not once did I CTD. That’s about all I can say that is nice.

1.The Ceres - It is 50/ 50 on docking with any thing. It spins around all the zoner’s bases. It likes to crash into planets when docking and as far as trade lanes it never docks automatically. The level 6 guns and shields make this 600 cargo space ship like my ex-wife useless.
2.The Nemesis - Has some of the same issues as the Ceres for docking just not as often go figure. A question, why is this a cargo space of 200 when it handles like a battleship and the Ceres is a 600 cargo space and it handles like a light fighter?
3.The Wraith VHF – A great ship with Lv 10 Silver Sword guns which drain power
in a second and a cargo hole of 40 what the hell is that all about.
4.That brings me to the next point the guns, Steel Rain, Green Terror, Retribution and Silver Sword these are all great guns but the refire rate is so high that the ships power supply is drained in seconds. The Venom" Beam Cannon is the only gun I found I could use with a refire rate of 4. Please don’t get me wrong I like fast refire rate but when the power is gone bad langue just doesn’t cut it.
5.The other ships are fun but lack the weapons, shield level and cargo space like the Taipan HF, I was already at level 10 guns when I found it so I didn’t even try it.
Please don’t take offence I think that these ships are great and I hope they find there why into other mods along with there weapons, mods which have power supplies that can actually handle the power drain and shields that don’t have a level (Hint).
