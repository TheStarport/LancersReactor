Actually you have stumbled upon the ingenious system for placing docking rings on planets. Due to the planets gravitational influence on the docking ring structure, the docking ring cannot be constructed near the intended plante. So a docking ring will be constructed somewhere along the planets orbit, but at a speed just slightly slower than the planet is orbiting. After construction is complete it is just a matter of time for the intended planet to catch up with the ring. The rings you have stumbled across are just such rings. These rings will be installed on their intended planets, most likely just after you stop playing FL. Hope this clears thing up, hehe. Have the drugs worn off yet?
