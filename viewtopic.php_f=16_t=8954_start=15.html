For a time I thought my ancient reflexes would stump me too, but i cracked it on the 6th or so attempt.

The trick is, as others have said, be hitting your cruise drive and burners the instant the count-down ends. Also, look ahead to the next ring and cut inside the turn of your opponent, like F1 drivers over-tking on the inside.

At the end it gets tricky, with a steep turn for one ring and his mates shooting at you if you're winning but finishing it was a great rush, sending me to work happy.

Alot of people (me included) thought or think F2 and Shift -w do the same thing. For all intents and purposes they do (in mouse free flight). However, your ability to fly your ship is very different. With F2 (goto function) in mouse free flight you are constantly fighting the autopilot (i.e. drifting, drastic heading changes, and altitude changes). With shift - W you will find your ship is almost too easy to manouver through the rings.
I must admit that I personally spent about 20 trys to do the race. 19 with F2 and one with Shift - W. I even tried to formation with dexter to get through the damn hoops (didn't even come close to working). So this is to all who were smart and used Shift - W on the first try: we are not stupid or crummy pilots, we just do things the hard way.
