The plot thickens...
Proud Rheinland, on its knees, begging for allies.
(I'm gonna start doing this every now and then. Just a catch-up on recent events, from a neutral perspective.)

The Colony News!

Is War on the Horizon? : Today, Rheinland Chancellor Wilde was reported to have gotten very shocked to hear several pieces of news. First, Bretonia declined his generous non-agression pact offer. Then, his long time ally, gunblader of Samura Industries, left him, and joined the Corsairs. With tensions the way they are after the Rheinland/Kusari conflict, a large war could be brewing.

House attacks on neutral factions continues : Recently, there has been a lot of talk about the hush-hush events in house and borderworld space regarding all of the four Great Houses attacking pirate groups and corporations. All the houses have begun a brutal campaign to forcefully invade and take over all bases in their space. Only very powerful corporations and pirate groups, such as the Outcasts, Corsairs, and the IMG, have managed to escape these attacks. More to come, as this story unfolds...

Liberty President still unsure : Liberty President John Sisko has refused to give any word in reply to an offer by House Rheinland to a non-agression pact. Similar to the Bretonian offer, Rheinland is offering huge amounts of resources and money to the house to remain neutral in the war that Rheinland thinks is brewing. Sisko's failure to immediatly respond means that the long-time ally of Bretonia could be preparing to attack Rheinland, or, he might just be taking time to consider remaining neutral. As always, the Colony News will be the first to tell you of any response by Sisko to Wilde.

Will the BHG Fight? : One question that is being asked by government and military officials all around Sirius is as to whether the BHG will get involved in any upcoming conflicts. The long-standing criminal fighting guild has been recently angered by attempts by Liberty to invade its borders, and questions are abound as to whether Guildmaster Richardson will come out to support his Bretonian allies if they go to war, or if he chooses another path. Whatever his choice, it could be cruicial to the scheme of things, since the BHG has some of the best pilots in Sirius, and, with the addition of the new Freedom Shipworks, is currently working on a naval force to compliment its fighter fleet.

This has been an edition of the Colony News Service!
Over and out from our headquarters in rainy New London!
