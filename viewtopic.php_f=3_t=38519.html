Preacher0815

Wed Apr 06, 2005 12:49 am by Preacher0815

There is a way out, but it's more a bug exploit than anything else. Move your ship so its front has contact with one of the walls of your prison. Then hit F1 -> select new character and chose the one you were just playing. During the Loading screen, hit your aftterburner several times quickly, or keep it pressed all the time, until shortly after the game has finished loading. You should now be flying through the walls of the station. If you don't, just try again. This is very tricky and might take a few runs, but it works.

This method works on online servers, however I'm not sure if it does using OpenSP or while playing the storyline.
