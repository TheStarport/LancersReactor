FriendlyFire

Wed Aug 30, 2006 3:11 pm by FriendlyFire

Well most of the mods are what I would call "ship packs", but just upgraded to have weapons, equipment and systems. But you're right burzelmann, they all come down to the same.

Though I have to say some stand out from the pack. Freeworlds is a great example, featuring a nice plot (Star Wars, heh), and linked and realistic things together. There are many others which I just dont remember here (I haven't played that many, so I can't tell) which might have a similar concept. Total Conversions usually feature better plots with tightly linked ships/equipment/weapons, since they base off of an universe, they must have a plot in order to define what exactly is behind the mod, what is driving it. Tides of War is another upcoming Star Wars mod. And of course I must plug my own work in by adding Freelancer: Combat Evolved to the list, which is an upcoming Halo mod. I think I heard of a Battlestar Galactica mod too, but I can't tell for sure. There's also Warriors of the Sky, which is one hell of a Total Conversion, being that you now fly planes. And well... I can't come up with any other right now, but there are plenty.
