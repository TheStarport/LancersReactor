guptasa1

Sun Mar 16, 2003 11:49 pm by guptasa1

No rush from my experience. There are only a few limitations:

*SLIGHT SPOILER WARNING*

1. You won't be able to buy certain weapons/equipment/ships until you level up, and that does require you do some storyline missions. If you're okay with not buying ships/weapons beyond your level, you can still make as much money as you want.

2. Juni will wait. For the second story mission I met her and declined her, and she waited for quite a while I explored/made money/upgraded myself. I assume (gonna try it this time) she'll also wait if you simply don't go back until you're ready as well, but I don't know for sure (I always make a separate save just in case).

3. You'll be restricted where you can go. Following the storyline opens up new jump gates and such for you, and you can't access these until you do some story missions. However, again, you seem to be able to play as much as you like in the areas you do have access to.

I'm taking my time, exploring, reading up on everything, and just having a good time (played for many hours and have only gotten past mission 2 in the storyline). If that's what you want to do, go for it - you won't regret it.

Hope this helps!
