Did anyone hear about this game. I saw the preview of it on gamespy and It seemed to me a great Freelancer like game. awdougherty

Mon Oct 27, 2003 5:45 pm by awdougherty

I don't mind the discussion of another game on this site if it introduces space sim fans to another possibility (we can't play Freelancer forever). But that being said, X2 has some really good points and bad points going for it. It's on my must buy list because I enjoyed its previous versions so much.

It has a fantastic trading model and fantastic graphics. But you can't land on planets, it wouldn't surprise me if the story comes across as weak, and the graphics are bringing even top end systems to their knees, so many people might not experience the breath taking visuals of the screenshots.

I wish X managed to incorporate some of Freelancer's interface into it (Freelancer seriously raised the bar in this department in my opinion), and the X games have always had somewhat crappy dogfighting. Hopefully that was rectified. One last thing, X games play slowly. This can often be a deal breaker for some people.

X2 should be a great addition to any space simmer's library. Sometimes the previews make it seem like we're about to behold the ultimate space sim. If you've never played an X game, it may be worth it to check out the demo before buying.
