Dev

Thu Apr 19, 2007 7:24 pm by Dev

I actually considered this for my mod at one point, though eventually I decided it wasn't what I wanted. If you're going to make the armor buyable, you might want to make a bunch of dummy shield generators and put hardpoints for them on your ship's hull. The shield generators would have .surs that more or less match the hull of the ship where they are mounted. This makes it easy to upgrade armor, since it's just buying/selling of shield generators.

However, there are some big drawbacks to customizable armor. Since your ship is not a big cube, the armor plating over different ship parts will have different shapes (so, different .surs). This obviously means that you will need multiple shield generators types per ship type. If they can be bought/sold, you will want to avoid having players mount shield generators in the wrong spots, which requires multiple shield classes per ship, over and above having no overlaps between ships. In other words, you can put plate on just a few ships before you run the risk of people mismounting armor.

If you opt to avoid customizable armor, you can avoid the above problems and just make the armor plates breakable parts of the ship itself (like fins or whatever). It's 100% doable, just kind of a pain to fit plates to the hull. Also, if you have a complex hull and/or if you have multiple plates per surface, you will probably need more than 18 parts to handle the hull + armor, which will take some creativity with utf edit (the splice can handle any number of .surs, but the .cmp exporter for MS3D caps at 18).
