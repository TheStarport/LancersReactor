Romulus

Wed Nov 10, 2004 6:44 am by Romulus

The Evolutions Mod is a flat out blast. Multitudes of new systems and factions and ships. All ships are original Mods and you can customize them with ooodles of new equipment, ie, shield regenerators, powerboosters, different types of thrusters, Hull Armor...etc. etc.. Plus there are varieties of ships of the same model. For instance you might prefer the RavenClaw with better armor or the one that has better manueverability. Some of the evolutions servers have fairly vibrant clan communities. I'd suggest HALs_2003_Evos server. Great Ping, very stable and Clan friendly. New Clans get a starter package depending on the number of players they bring to the server, ie, 30 million for 5 and 10 million per each after that.

Plus if you'd like to test your PvP skills... heh. there are some fantastic pilots on HALs.
