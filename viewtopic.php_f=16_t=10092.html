UnjustEnd

Sat Mar 15, 2003 7:22 pm by UnjustEnd

You make docking sound so complicated. Just target the thing, get your cruise on if its over 2 k away (if you are close you might as well just afterburner up to it) Aim for the part of the tradelane that is green, once you are basically point blank hit your dock button. I suggest practicing till you instantaneously go into it. If nothing else its something to amuse yourself with while you are still just trading. WIth the jump gates, as long as they aren't active or the pattern is full, you can basically go about 2 meters into the forward hole before you hit dock and you''ll go into the cinematic instantly. Another trick that sometimes works is if the pattern is full at a jump gate, just select the people ahead of you in line nad hit the formation key. This will let you dock with them instead of waiting for after. Warning on that though its been known to cause some problems, like missing the gate completely, turning off your dock, forcing you to make a second approatch.

Oh you can also quick dock with the planet rings. I've got it down to where if you select the rigns then the instant you get past the claws and inside the ring hit dock you go in without waiting for the arms to even open. Might practice this one if you aren't confident, running into planets isn't fun. Even if the pattern is full, its usually better to go ahead and wait till you are inside the rings to hit dock, ti'll stop you right there, and as soon as the other person "enters" them you'll start up the docking cinematic and make it in.
