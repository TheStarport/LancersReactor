Joe , I don't have the complete list of what you need. However, weapon, ships, and commodities can have their nicknames used instead of hash numbers in the *.fl files. The nicknames are available in the *.ini-s; you might want to use bini.exe available here on the download section to convert them into plain text.

For example, ships information can be obtained from shiparch.ini . You can search FL data directory for this particular file. Weapon can be found in weapon_equip.ini and commodities are in goods.ini .

Anyway, previously Sherlog stated that he (she?) has a database for all the nicknames and their 'English' equivalent. Perhaps he's the one who can help you complete your own database.

Wed Sep 03, 2003 9:56 am by JohnSteve

The Anubis has a better sibling! It has no official name and only referred to as The Order Ship .
Although considered as a light fighter, it is more powerful than Eagle, Sabre, or even Titan in some aspects:

1. All of its hardpoints can mount class 10 weapon
2. Cargo capacity is 600 units; even the Dromedary has only 275 units max.
3. Unlimited storage for nanobots and shield batteries
4. Two hardpoints for torpedoes / disruptors

Try it out. Its nickname is or_elite_msn01 . Yes, they are the ones that blew up the Donau in the storyline (1st mission).
It can also outrun Hovis on the race, right from the starting line.

To use it, edit the ship_archetype line as follows
<pre><font size=1 face=Courier>ship_archetype = or_elite_msn01 </font></pre> and equip the ship with weapon, shield, thruster, lights, and cargo accordingly.
