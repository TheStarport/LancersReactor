In the "my games"folder there's a file called "prefoptions.ini",open it with a text editor(notepad),find"DIFFICULTY_SCALE"and change it from 1.00 to either 1.25,1.50,1.75 or 2.00 then "save as".

How about the Equalizer Mk V Mod by Warzog
Mk V is the largest in the Equalizer series and incorporates Xerx's OpenSP v1.1 mod, so it's purely Open Singleplayer (no storyline) and for Multiplayer games.

Also check out The Equalizer Mod Mk IV
This version is for Singleplayer games only.
The options don't work in Multiplayer games. If you want this Mod for Multiplayer games talk to Warzog.

But what this Mod adds is an option screen when you activate the Mod in the Freelancer Mod Manager.
Options...
Choice of 34 different Fully-Armed Starting Ships. Juni and Trent are in some of the cinematic scenes.
Choice of 6 AI's - Difficult, Hard, Medium, Easy, Beginner and Deathwish.
Choice of High or Low Pay Mission Pay.
Also the Extra Armor, NPC shields, & Infinite Power have been removed from NPC ship loadouts and a few typos have been corrected in several files changed from The Equalizer Mk III MOD.
New "Super Dagger" on Pittsburgh.
Hovis Race Bypass.
Increased Speeds are based on the Speed Mod edited by IGx89 Made from the Cruise speed mod for Freelancer v1.0 by Daekar and the Ion Thrusters mod v1.0 by Migl.
