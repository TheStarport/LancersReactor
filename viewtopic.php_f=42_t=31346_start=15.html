Omega 3 is now Telar.
Cambridge is now J900, which is just south of Telar.
(Both of which are in the Fariss Quadrant.)

(Which is on the other side of Gemini in the Potter Quadrant.)

I've edited Mission 05 so that you need to go to Junction (New London) before heading to New Constantinople (Leeds.)

(Otherwise I won't know if it's the mission, or the Sector, that's causing problems.)

Personal note:
I've gotta go outa town for a day or two.

Update:
With all of the changes to both new, and old systems, it takes about 2-6 hours to completely check everything in each system.
The most common problem is jumpgate/hole orientation.
(Getting them so you're facing the right way when you come out of a jump.)
The second most common problem was my desire to create some unusual systems.
There are several suns, and dust particles, that are rare, or never seen in FL, but are in the coding.
Turns out...
Some of them are only half there...
And trying to use them causes CTD's, and other things.
The third most common problem is exclusion zones.
(Keep needing to add, or change them.)
In systems where I've added new gates/holes, I need to add Exclusion Zones so that asteroids aren't smack in the middle of them, preventing a jump.
Same goes for the planets, stations, and various Tradelanes in the new systems.
Other times, I simply need to rename the zones to correspond to their new Gemini names.
(zones with the wrong names will cause a CTD!)
The last most common error is due to my cut-n-paste gate/hole creation.
Oft times, I've forgotten to rename part, or all ,of the new gate/hole.
(Creates a really neat, never-ending, jump.)
(or a CTD)
