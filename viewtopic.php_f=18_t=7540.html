Client-side cheating! Uh oh!

Okay, this REALLY shocked me. I put up a dedicated on one of my machines, and ran a client on another machine. Decompressed one of the ini files on the client and modified it so that the ship's costs are drastically low. Logged into my dedicated from the client and guess what? THE SHIP'S PRICES WERE AS I TWEAKED THEM ON THE CLIENT!!! WTF?! Went ahead and bought the ship at the price. Tried a different client on a different machine with a clean ini set. Ship's prices were normal (since it had no tweaked ini's).

Anyways, long story short, the server does not check for INI consistency, which means Client-side cheating is FULLY possible, and quite easy. Shame on DA for letting this happen!
