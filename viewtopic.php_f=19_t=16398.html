There are many things to consider when creating a new squad, but ultimately it all comes down to having fun. If a group of people want to make a new squad, why shouldn't they? In the end, it means more people playing FL for longer, which can only be a good thing. There are many FL servers out there, and still not enough clans to fill them.

That's not to say that people shouldn't try to get into an existing squad if they feel that's the best way to go, but FL can only benefit from a varied and vibrant squad community, rather than one dominated by only a handful of squads. It makes sense.
