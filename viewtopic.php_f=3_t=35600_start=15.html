As Tawalkana mentioned, try ELITE -- the "mother" of all games of this type. You can find several adaptions on line, if you Google for it. (There was one with fairly decent graphics, but one of the game creators made the site take it off line.)

ELITE is about 20 years old now and is best for nostalgia for us older guys. But you might like to try it just to see how much of an influence it has on games even now. e.g. Starting with a basic ship and making money to equip it -- by bounty hunting, mining, trading or pirating; an evil alien enemy; hyperspacing to different systems; salvaging cargo in space; even a few missions to fly. And remember that it was designed to use just 32 kilobyte of memory!
