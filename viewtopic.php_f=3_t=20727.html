Hello mate.

If the missions are crashing, report it in detail at Respawn in the forums there. Otherwise that will never be fixed. No bug report, no fix.

http://www.respawn.co.uk

As for tips, i have some. Version 7 contains several ways to make a lot of money very quickly at reasonable risk level.

1) Shoot down bigger ships than you and run for it after grabbing their cargo. Some weapons are worth 6k a piece in Newmars alone. More in other systems.

2) The wanted man feature can earn you a lot of cash just by dropping the guy at a pirate base if you have access to one. Even (lots more) more if you them to malta or the corsiers base.

3) Most if not all Research stations sell Secret research papers that can be sold at a pretty large profit if you can get them to base that wants them without being scanned by pirates or the police.

4) Basic elements such as water, oxygen and food are all found on planets and sold at stations. Do this and you will always make a profit from them. These are localised commodities now. They are the same in every system so noobs can get started trading.

5) There are new wrecks no too far away from the planet newmars. Though they are well hide. You can make a packed off of those.

6) Prisoners and passengers are also easy money, depending on where you take them.

7) Theres a noob ship at Farscape in Newmars that can carry a single level 8 gun and 2 level 4 guns. Its the fastest thing in the game and only costs 20k. With its primary weapon you can take out almost anything. However its armour is weak but it can be fitted with great shields.

Finally, Your choice of shields is vital. The old modifier of 30% extra damage of 30% less damage depending on what weapon was used against what shield was increased to 50% in Version 2 of HU. In Version 7 i further developed the shields so they had very special properties that made them useful in other situations. Figure out the system and you will be able to go head to heard with a level 10 fighter and win. Get it wrong and you wont scratch some shields.

Giskard
