well this is how you enable the debug mode or at least how i did

in freelancer.ini in the exe folder stick a ';' in front of

DLL = MiscTextInfo2.dll ;Contains MP UI elements as well as other misc. text

then load it up you will see lots of the misc text missing and when u lauch into space you will see clearly debug mode in front of your name.
maybe this will work for all of the std dlls, I didnt check.
the commands like warp and teleport that are in keymap.ini dont seem to work tho'. Maybe someone out there will get it going.
