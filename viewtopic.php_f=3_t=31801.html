A strange experience while playing Total War

I was playing total war, and I got a viper. (Patriot) And a few guns to match. Then I see in the chat box:

"All LSF friendly units please report to sector 6D"

Strange. Normally people don't go all RPG. Anyways, that player was named Zypher. He turned out to be quite a decent leader. I was in a group with 3 other people. We were going to try a....Lvl 21 mission! (Dramatic music) In Total War, that is pretty much suicide. And indeed it was. Yet I heard another "battle cry" from zypher: FOR TRIGGERHAPPY!! (That's my online name.) They slaughtered 'em. Strange how people can range from people like zypher to idiots who attack players coming out of manhatten. Then I got to manhatten, where they were going back to sell thier loot and repair their ships. I ended up getting a good 60K just for dying in a mission. A tip to all Total War players: Do suicide missions with higher-level players. It really pays off
