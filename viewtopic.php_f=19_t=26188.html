Tue Nov 18, 2003 6:59 am by Captain Tylor

There's already a mod that creates a deathmatch system for pvp insanity. Look it up cause it's in the downloads section, and really adds lots to the type of server you're asking about.

Problem with an exclusive pvp server, if that's all you can do there, what else do you have to look forward to? Also what if there's no other players around or they're not interested in fighting you? Being a Freelancer means having options, and I'd rather have other distractions around. Maybe some choice encounters that I can test myself against would be fun. A few nomad battleships maybe, or about 10 nomad fighters with the strongest lasers equipped.

Plus there's always a balancing issue. If a player knows how to get 1mil in an hour, they'll do it, buy their fighter and weapons of choice, then lay to waste on everyone else. When a battle starts, will there be a ref present? Will the server's web page post who the best fighter is? And let's not forget about the occasional cheater that rears their ugly head.

On my server, I host pvp contests in the system that has the planet of the apes. no loot from the asteroids worth the trouble, nomads are made friendly, and ALL accounts are backed up and them moved to another folder so that I can choose the ships the players will end up using. Since they're all on planet primus, they can't purchase weapons or shields, so that more or less forces them to duel on equal terms with each other. I always try to give a variety of weapons for their blasting amusement, usually two main types of guns and the two shield types that are most affected by them. Almost makes it a game of chess. Do I equip these guns that're good against graviton, or against molecular shields? That's my best solution for a pvp server with server side mods. As for my shameless advertisement, the next contest on my server will be pvp dueling.
