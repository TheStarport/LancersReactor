Ro9ue

Mon Mar 08, 2004 4:46 pm by Ro9ue

Try getting the Upgrade Powerplants and/or Upgrade Engines. The Upgrade Engines are supposed to use 0 (no) energy, and the Upgrade Powerplants have significantly increased capacities.
This powerdrain is common when you mount a powerplant with insufficient capacity (or more oftenly, one w/slow thrust and power regeneration rates) on a larger ship (i.e. putting a light fighter or Small Block into a heavy fighter/VHF/ freighter).
Doing so will use weapon energy, and it does stink to disengage Cruise to have 1/4 or 1/3 of your weapon energy.
So try out the Upgrade equipment; it may be a little expensive, but all of it is worth every credit!
