[MC]Menzoberranzan

Thu Mar 04, 2004 4:52 am by [MC]Menzoberranzan

A cheat would be any mods or hacks that gives u an UNFAIR advantage compared to other players. E.g. Advanced Beam Mod, Cardamine Spawning Mod, Cloak Mod, Speed Mods, Weapon/Ship Mods, that sorta stuff.

Modding is different. Mod stands for Modification. A modification can also exist as a Graphics mod, E.g. I use a blue engine effect than the usual yellow eagle engine. That doesn't give any advantage to me other than liking the way my eagle looks . Another mod would be music mods. I modded my Omicron Music to play as the SP Nomad Encounter music. Very nice. No unfair advantage there. Modding weapon visuals E.g. The Tizona Del Cid, u can't see it, I modded mine to be visible as a sort of blue ball. Fits in perfect

Therefore I prefer the term "Cheat" regarding players who mod for their own advantage. "Mod" would mean to me, enhancing gaming experience in a non harmful way.
