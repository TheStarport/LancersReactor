I came up with a rather interesting theoretical proposition about that.
As result, I wrote a short story about the origins of The Order.
Maybe a little far fetched, but hey...it's an idea..
================================
Origins of The Order.
In the 23rd Century...
Originally, When the nomad mother ship blasted the Star in the Sol System, General Atikus Rockford survived the resultant shockwave on the far side of Pluto. Shortly afterwards, he left for Sirius to warn the colonies about the new threat. However, during that time; a remnant of the CSF was outside the Sol System on reserve patrol when the star was blasted out of existence. They returned to find what was left of the Sol System and most fell victim to the nomad collective. Those who were assimilated by the nomads left the Sol System and headed for the unknown Edgeworlds of Sirius, slowly changing into something hybrid between nomad and human.
However; there was a few who managed to escape assimilation and they found a clue left behind by General Rockford, then followed him to Sirius by a different path just ahead of the nomads, out of their reach.
After his arrival in Sirius, General Rockford was met with skepticism and disbelief by the colonists regarding the news of the Solar System and the nomads. He was soon dismissed as a lunatic, stripped of his rank and soon swept away into guarded seclusion to prevent causing mass hysteria among the colonists.
Now a civilian, Aticus Rockford languished in his confinement for two years until the handful of Coalition remnants arrived in Sirius. Once they located Aticus Rockford, they broke him out and soon found refuge among the ranks of the criminal element. Apparently, it was the Liberty Rogues who listened and believed the fantastic story about their original home system being wiped out.
Rockford was then restored to his former rank of General and through
consensus of his peers established a new organization in secrecy that would take advantage of the knowledge they possessed.
This secret organization was named, The Order.
Over the years they amassed knowledge and information about the nomads
and followed the trail of the assimilated coalition to the edgeworlds.
It was in the edgeworlds where they founded Toledo in Omicron Minor and
established their primary base of operations there in the heart of the nomad systems.

That was over 800 years ago.

During the last nomad conflict, Marchall Rockford, a decendant of Atikus Rockford, led a force of The Order to defeat the nomads at Dyson City and
restore peace to the Houses of Sirius.
His Flagship was a Liberty Dreadnought prototype that he and his crew
comandeered from the Liberty Navy secret shipyard in the Alaska System
accessed only through Zone 21 in the New York System.
That ship was christened, the Osiris.
It was during that time they also rescued the Liberty President, Jacobi from certain compromise by Chancellor Neihman who was already a host for the nomads.
You know Marchall Rockford by the name, Gasper Orillion.
================

(The story continues on with other information regarding what transpired
during the 20 years since the battle at Dyson City)
Story By: Rankor

This "theory" story stitches the past and present together and lends credence
behind the creation of The Order. It also goes on the basis of the original
pre-release video instead of the actual release with the CD.
