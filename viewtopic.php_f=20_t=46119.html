Wed Jul 25, 2007 3:26 am by Ogu

You could try deleting your old savegames from the My Games/Freelancer/Singleplayer folder.

If that doesn't work, delete your Freelancer folder in Program Files/Microsoft Games and reinstall FL. You won't lose any of your MP characters as long as you do not choose the "uninstall" option when you pop in the CD.

Edit: Also, you may want to disable your antivirus software and make sure that FL is allowed to access the Internet. Though I haven't heard of people having problems with Avast (it's usually Norton, ZoneAlarm or McAfee causing issues in my experience).

The only other thing you can do is post the last 10 lines of your FLSpew file so we can see what the errors are.
