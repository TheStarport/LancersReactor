A better, or at least shorter route is the following.

New Berlin - Buy Diamonds at 396/unit.

New Tokyo - Sell Diamonds at 1650/unit, net profit 1254/unit, Buy Niobium at 512/unit.

The Ring, New Berlin - Sell Niobium @ 1440/unit, net profit 928/unit.

New Berlin - Buy Diamonds again, and repeat the cycle. It's 6 jumps round trip, if you can use the Sigma 13/New Berlin jump hole. 1 more if you can't, I believe. You make a net profit of 2182/unit round trip. Or 600k in a Drom.
