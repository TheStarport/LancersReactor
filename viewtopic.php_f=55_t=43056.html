Puke Barwalker

Sat Mar 25, 2006 7:52 am by Puke Barwalker

This is really a question the moderators don't like to see, since too often it ends up with people flaming each other.

But actually the "best" VHF depends on how you like to fight. The Titan has the heaviest armour and the most shield bats. On the negative side, it is almost a gunboat rather than a fighter. It handles sluggishly compared to the other VHFs. Still, it can't be beaten for strength if you just like to overpower your enemies.

The Eagle handles much better and packs a powerful arsenal. To balance that, it has lighter armour and fewer bats.

The Sabre is much like the Eagle in handling, but carries an extra gun.

The Hammerhead is a Level 9 VHF, not a Level 10. But it does handle nicely and is strong enough to take on Level 10 VHFs. Would have been nice if it could use those Level 10 guns though.

My personal preference is for the Eagle, though the Sabre is only slightly behind that. Nevertheless, I don't mind flying the Titan. One thing I like better about the Eagle the cabin view. Sitting in a Titan is a bit like looking out of a bird cage.

If you like a challenge, stick with a Hammerhead and Level 7 shields. That gives you a real test. Instead of being simply the biggest baddest freelancer in the neighbourhood, you have to use skill. Go up against half a dozen Outcasts in Sabres in a Hammerhead and see how good you are. Takes some skill to beat them, but it's a fun match up.

So again, what is the best VHF? Who knows? Everyone will give you a different answer.
