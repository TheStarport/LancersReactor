Thorkild

Wed May 14, 2003 10:59 pm by Thorkild

I believe it was at this point (or slightly later on) that I discovered the wonder of leaving the engines disengaged and tapping the thruster. Even with a cheap thruster, you should be able to stay above 90% and keep your speed around 200, even through a turn (which can be very tight if you tap the thruster repeatedly).

It threw my ability to shoot accurately for a mission or two, but when I got used to it, AI pilots couldn't touch me anymore. If you keep your speed up, you should be safe in most engagements, until you're up against eight to ten opponents.

If you're already doing this, than go back a bit, make some more money before you accept a campaign mission, and buy the Bretonian HF.
