*FLSDK Announcement* -General Update-

We are looking for skilled texturers to texture new ship models for inclusion into Freelancer. If you are intrested, make a post on the resume forum at the FLSDK website with a couple small samples of your work that I can look at. The work on ships is suspended until I can find a texturer.

The Visual Systems Editor is coming along, but still in pre-alpha stages. It is already looking *very* nice, and this is something that you should not be disappointed with.

As always you can contact me at FLSDKTeam@hotmail.com, or Private Msg me on the FLSDK forums.

Much thanks to ElectricBrain for posting us on the front page of TLR, since the announcement we have had a 457% increase in site traffic.

I also must apologize for the lack of tutorials at this time, I had to take them down due to trouble with the posting method. I will try to restore them tomorrow morning as early as possible. (Pacific Standard Time)

Thank you for your continued interest in us, as without our fans and the communities support, this would be impossible.

Thank you.
