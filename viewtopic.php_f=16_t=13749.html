Even if you're neural with a faction, if in their presence you're fired on by someone with whom they're allied, the previously neutral faction will become--temporarily--hostile, in support of their friends.

In your case, you might want to try to become neutral with the other Rogue allies who frequent that system. Hackers, for example.

Or leave the area and come back, hoping that you won't be attacked by any Rogue allies before you can dock at the Rogue base. Good luck!

It's better to burn out than to fade away

Cham

Sat Apr 12, 2003 5:07 am by Cham

Your problem is that you are hostile with the Lane Hackers. The Liberty Rogues are allies of the Lane Hackers and thus are alerted to attack you regardless of reputation when your presence is detected. Your only chance to dock at the base is to sneak in when there are no Lane Hacker ships around. Which probably will rarely occur since the Rogues and Hackers are such good allies. You can try it though. Once you have confirmation from docking personnel after pressing F3 you will be guided in no matter if the station switches to red in the process. All you will need to do then is survive while in the docking process.
