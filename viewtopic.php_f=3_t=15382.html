Hypothetical: If there was an Expansion Pack...

Okay, once you get to Level 38 it's easy to say you have probably come into contact with a Titan/Sabre/Eagle fighter and have the highest level weapons available. If there was an expansion pack, wouldn't it be safe to say it follows the Single Player campaign? Do you think that DA would add in extra content to the original game? What I mean by, is that an expansion would either add new ships (likely) or change them so that all the House ships are on par with the Border Worlds and Corsair fighters. If they go for the first option, would you expect to see Patriot, Rhino and Defender Mk2s or maybe Justice Mk 5 weapons? This would be practically the only way to balance the expansion pack. It makes sense to have the House ships weaker than the Corsair and Border World fighters because you have access to them early on in the game. Only after the SP campaign are you able to acquire a Titan/Eagle/Sabre. So.. Did I make any sense at all?
