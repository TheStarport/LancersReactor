Thu Jul 31, 2003 5:06 am by RadiologistRebooted

It involves editing the Freelancer data .ini files. Search for PerfOptions, then open the file. At this point, I can go two ways, I can tell you and then destroy all of the anticipation that Freelancer Jockey has built up, or let you search the PerfOption files. Don't worry about the function to hack, just scroll down the PerfOptions file and look for something that might change the DIFFICULTY of the game. In theory, if you change the number on the SCALE, you can make it harder or easier. The higher the number, the more difficult it is. If you make it lower... then you become invincible.
