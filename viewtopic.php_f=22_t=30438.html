Have you checked out all coincidences from all crashes? If you are using FLSO you could see if a certain player's name is always logged on during the crashes. Have you looked at the Event Viewer? Windoze OS's have a program called Event Viewer and under Application it is possible to view reasons (however obtuse they may be) for flserver to crash. Was any other untoward activity happening at the time that seems to always happen during a crash? Maybe lots of players logged in, lots of players in one system, firewall messages about lots of ping or other attempts, too much traffic for the amount of players online, too much CPU utilization for the amount of players online, etc. What kind of crash is it? Is it something FLSO recovers from or is it a lockup?

I ask because I have on average one Event ID 1000 crash a day and a lockup a week but have never found any definitive explaination. The crash can be recovered from by FLSO but not the lockup. I have yet to see any mention by anyone on these issues but fortunately they are not enough of an annoyance to rise above my player's level of tolerance. Yet.
