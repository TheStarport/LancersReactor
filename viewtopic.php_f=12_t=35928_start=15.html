Bandersnatch

Mon Nov 08, 2004 11:18 am by Bandersnatch

but it's not really possible to separate the practicality of time travel from the morality of doing it, is it? If you could travel to the past, anything you do would in some way upset the timeline, as Star Trek demonstrates q often in its time travel plots. So how could you travel in time without affecting history even in the smallest way? same works for the future, you go to the future, get some useful info, act on it in your "native" time, and thus alter the future probably in ways you couldn't begin to predict.

as it stands time travel is a practical and logical possibilty. if it were possible it would be immensely dangerous and a terrible weapon. I can understand the fascination, almost everybody has a part of the past they hark back to and would like to know more about, and of course the idea of putting "right" things that went wrong is very appealing. but just think about it and you wouldn't, would you?

it would be dangerous for the time traveller too. Let's say for the sake of argument that you could make a short leap back to say just before 9/11. what would you do? warn everyone in the WTC that there's going to be an attack? you'd be in a police station in a flash. go to the Federal Government? same thing except a few days later you'd be in Guantanamo Bay on suspicion of being involved. Maybe you could get on the planes and try to stop the hijackers yourself? how would you get past security? what would you fight them with?

and this is the other side to the time travel coin, what do you do when you get there? how do you stop yourself from standing out? how do you get by in what is in effect a foreign land? can you remember the details of that period other than the attacks?

I once read a short story in which time travellers had become the hoboes and vagabonds of the future, as they could only travel to the past and could only go naked (like in Terminator) and were mostly made up of the disaffected who didn't fit in their own time and wanted to try out their favourite past. They ussually ended up in abject poverty, mad, or locked up and maybe executed as witches/whatever.

time travel - not a good idea, leave it in the realm of science-fiction.
