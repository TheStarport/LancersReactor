Mon Dec 11, 2006 6:48 am by Mancer

Hi there!

I use 3dsmax but i do a small trick with exporting/importing.

For importing into max i read the cmp in milkshape and save it as VRML. Simle model fromat and does the job very well. After it i import this VRML into max and save it as max.

When exporting i got an MD3 plugin for max. The finished and textured model is exported as MD3 (quake3 engine) format. The textures are lost but the texture mapping stays here. The md3 is then imported to milkshape and save it to ms3d.
I do this since i make models and no great flaws happened.

You could figure out something like me for your maya. Use another format like ido and forget the ms plugins. They are only useless crap.
