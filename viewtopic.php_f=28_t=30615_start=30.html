Simple Mod Version 1a and 3 made by Glock36
The Zip file Simple1a3a.zip contains two folders:
1a and 3
Individual installation instructions are contained in each folder.
Thanks to everyone who sent feedback and suggestions. Version 1a and 3 are the result. Graphics bugs were corrected in Version 1 for those who preferred Version 1 to Version 2
HERE IS WHAT IT DOES:
On Planet Manhattan in the New York System:
You will see a "showcase" of ALL mountable weapons and equipment available in the game. Planet Manhattan is where you end up after finishing the missions. You must have the appropriate ship and be at the appropriate level in order to purchase these items. After finishing the missions, you will not be at the required level but this "showcase" will allow you to see what's out there.
On Planet Crete in the Omicron Gamma System and on Planet Malta in the Omicron Alpha system:
You will be able to purchase all mountable weapons of Class 8 or greater and ammo for all weapons regardless of class. Included are the Nomad weapons, all the Special weapons, all class 10 shields, and even a couple of weapons the designers declared but forgot to use! Again, you must have the appropriate ship and be at the appropriate level in order to purchase these items. I selected these bases because they are the most difficult to get to.
In Version 1a:
The only difference between Version 1 and Version 1a is that graphics problems with Nomad weapons have been corrected. All weapons now display properly when mounted on a ship. No changes have been made to any weapons.
In Version 3:
Graphics problems with Nomad weapons have been corrected. Some Nomad weapons have been re-tweaked again for performance. The Nomad Cruise Disruptor is available and working now. The Cannonball missile has been tweaked for performance. Firing Graphics have been changed to reflect re-tweaking of weapons. You can really cheat now if you just enjoy blowing things up!
UPDATE: The zip file has been updated now and the mod should now work correctly!
