Allow me to answer the question as to why Origin is no longer making any WC games and a possible candidate for why Roberts left Origin and will not return. The reason is that EA is having Origin focus all of it's attention on the Ultima Online brand of MMORPGs. EA has decided that since Origin released UO that the future of Origin lay in it's UO franchise, and thus needed to focus their attention on expanding and maintaining the level of quality said franchise deserves. Once they had a stable user base they will continually get an income from the monthly fee that their users pay to play UO. Because of this, Origin has not made a single new game outside of their Ultima Online franchise. Similarly, Janes-EA has not made any new flight sims since their release of F/A-18. This is a true shame, because the industry lost 2 game companies that made truly high quality games (Longbow series, Wing Commander/Privateer series, and the true Ultima series to name a few). It's just how the industry works. A fast growing company is making quality products and the larger company that has been around for several years decides it want's part of the action. So, the larger company buys the smaller and changes the smaller company's focus on something the larger company thinks is more important. That's just how it is...it's sad, but unfortunately, very true.
