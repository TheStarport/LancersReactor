Argh

Sun Feb 27, 2005 5:52 pm by Argh

Honestly speaking;... it needs to be developed into something resembling... something... before we can give an opinion.

Right now, it looks like some sort've bizarre alien tuning fork, imho.

And I have no idea where you're heading with this concept. There isn't anything all that sexy about the simple shapes you're using- you basically have 3 boxes with angled ends all plowing into another box that's not quite square.

Soooo... basically... I have 2 pieces of critique:

1. It's not a very sexy basic shape, and the fact that the 3 boxes don't even flow into the rear square with some sort've attempt at streamlining or with some greebles (small details) that suggest that this thing is never in an atmosphere (which would make its blockiness excusable)... says that this has a long way to go. About the only good thing about the model thus far is that you're modeling it efficiently- it's very low-poly. But that's not much of a complement- it's like telling a girl that she's ugly, but wears practical shoes.

2. Not to be too mean about it, but an experienced modeler can kick out something like this is a minute- this doesn't look like Mona Lisa- more like a cruddy sketch you'd put on the back of a napkin.

I know that you're just getting going, but if you're expecting a lot've feedback over something that simple... you either don't realize how simple that thing is for those've us with experience... or you needed to be told. Come back again when it's a lot closer to DONE.
