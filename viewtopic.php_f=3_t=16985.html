Light, Heavy, or Very Heavy?

When I first played the game, once I got my hands on a heavy fighter, I never went back to the LF. Then I got the the Very Heavy fighters, which seemed to be the the final choice in the character development of everyone. But now I have played through SP again using only Light fighters. I have actually noticed I was easier with the advanced LFs. This may contradict all you believe, but I think I have come to my opinion on this. I like the light fighters!!

LF- The best against a single fighter or a small group, for your superior manuverablity can keep you on his six and him off of yours. Against larger groups, though, they get torn appart. A good light fighter with an excellent pilot can beat anyone one-on-one.

VHF- With excellent longevity and weapons, it's the last word in fighting against hordes of enemies due to it's ability to shirk off most blows and still deliver a devastating attack to its foe. But lack of manuverablity can hamper it from chasing down the smaller single light fighters and gives it trouble with fleeing itself. Good for those long trips into enemy territory.

HF- The compromise between the two. Average firepower, average armor, average manuverabilty, but deadly in the right hands. All the features of the VHF without the sluggish movement. Some HFs are extremely powerful (Centurion, Valkyre). Good for any job.

What about all of you? Tell me what you like!

Sherlog

Sat May 17, 2003 1:48 pm by Sherlog

If you compare ships of the same class then light fighters win hands down in every category except cargo space.

Heavy fighters may have up to 50% more fire power (plus a better powerplant) but because they are not agile enough they get hit much more often than light fighters, which means HF need better shields than LF for the same job, one or two classes higher. And the bad turn rate means it takes an HF longer to dispatch a non-stationary target, despite the better fire power.

An exception to this is the Piranha which is supposed to be a light fighter but _moves_ like heavy one; consequently it eats much more enemy fire than the Patriot or Cavalier. Actually, the Piranha is neither fish nor fowl. It is too sluggish to be a light fighter, too weak to be a heavy one, and too small to be a freighter.

The only reason VHF appear to be good against hordes of enemies is that you get VHF up to class 10 but there are no LF beyond the class 6 Drake or the class 7 Banshee.
