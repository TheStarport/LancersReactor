zazie

Tue Sep 13, 2005 10:49 pm by zazie

If you are playing with different mods - and if you think about to continue later one day with that or that mod - then it is a good idea to make a copy of your save-games in a different folder.
FLMM does have a routine to save the save-games from different mods. But I have seen several examples where FLMM (or the mod itself ??) could not reestablish the old save games when the same mod was activated again.

If you dont want to copy all your save-games then just take the last two (and NOT the auto-saves !)

All the above is SP-related. In MP FL-Server identifies your character and the last save for each different mod.
