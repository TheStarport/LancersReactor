Some odd things about rings...

I noticed some odd things that i'd simply call "bugs"

There are several planets with descriptions mentioning "magnificient ring system" or something like it but that don't have any rings whatsoever.

There are also two planetless rings floating in space. One of them (in Rheinland) has a jumphole in it's center which would explain the forming of the rings (gravity well) and it seems to be intended. The other one, however, is next to the Funchu prison in Kusari and is supposed to have a planetoid in it's center. The problem is: the planetoid is invisible and has a size of zero. You can target it, fly as close as 0 meters to it and you can even bump into it, but it's not there.

Can anyone confirm these observations/bugs?
