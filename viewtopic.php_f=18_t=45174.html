New Open SP Mod

I am working on a new Open SP mod and here are the features. Would this be intersting to anyone and used if I release it to the community?

Anyway here it is.

Open Single Player Mod for Freelancer v1.0 - v1.1

Choose between 11 Factions
There will be one question, and that is
What starting faction would you like to belong to?
Then after you choose you will simply be told to Pick your faction here also! 2 more times.
What this Open SP mod does is
1. Lands you on one base of your faction.
2. Sets you to the same rep as the faction you choose.
3. Gives you a starting ship (Light Fighter) of that faction.
Starting money is 2500

Thanks to Xerx for the Original Open SP design
Thanks to TLR and there Great Forums for on Editing, and the many Downloadable tools.
Thanks to everyone who shared there knowledge at the TLR Forums.


Any helpful suggestions or comments please post here.
