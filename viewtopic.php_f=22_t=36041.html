Fri Nov 12, 2004 11:11 am by Romulus

The Evolutions mod is fantasticly balanced and challenging. You can download either the SP version or Multi-play version. The latest version is Evolutions 1.28patched. It has a multitude of new ships, also Battleships and Trains and freighters, and even offers different versions of the same ships, mostly in the fighter classes. It offers a fantastic array of new equipment and weapons and even new equipment mounts so there's many ways to 'customize' your ship to suit your style of play. The AI's are much more skilled and are a challenge. Plus they have nice drops but it's not 'overblown' as you say.

I must admit the mod is fantastic for PvP but if your not into that as it sounds like a skilled pilot can avoid that. Many of the factions missions are revamped and pay quite well, especially for the Uber missions, but trade is also a new experience with a wide variety of new commodities and systems. Trade can also be quite dangerous since the AI's are more powerful. Reputation makes a huge difference in what you can trade and where you can go safely. I've found it makes escorting a viable trade.

There are even wrecks with special equiment and weapons that are one time loot only for your profile and some equipment is only available if looted off of certain AI's.

Well, this was a mouthful, but the mod is alot to chew on. A fantastic job by Chips and the Evo's crew. I'd suggest HALs_2003_EVOS server if you want to play online.
