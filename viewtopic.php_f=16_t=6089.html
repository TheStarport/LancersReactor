Sat Feb 08, 2003 9:50 pm by Stinger

Hehe

If in distress in Kusari space, the best thing to do is find a way OUT! Generally speaking the jump holes are your best bet. Careful most bounty hunters and pirates frequent those areas so be on your toes. You have basically three paths to consider. The best choice at this point is to head south toward Shik oku and then into either Kelper or Galileo systems. Keep going back towards Liberty space for some serious credit building and upgrading your ship (or what's left of it). Once you build yourself up, get some bribes, and align yourself with some of those baddies. This is afford you the chance to upgrade some of those really nice Borderworld goodies as you continue to work south into Rheinland space and the next time you meet up with your "friend" and his buddies, fart in the general direction to bait them into a fight. Revenge is best served cold. The other two options are to run to the Tau systems to the West and then south through Leeds. Do not go North from the Tau systesm, death awaits you. This will get you into Britonia space and the realitve safety of a lot of merchants. Get in good with them and pile up the credits. Rinse and repeat the meeting with your buddy and you're good to go. The last choice is to head East directly into the eadge worlds of the Sigma's. This is a dog eat dog world out there but excellent for making some very profitable short cargo runs. Careful, the Corsairs and Outcast roam the areas and don't like the very factions you need to buddy up with to increase you wealth. The 4th choice could be the best, but it is the most risky by far. Head North into the Chugoku system and hope like hell you're on good terms with the Blood dragons. They have excellent weapons for the medium powerful pilot, but they are a cagey group that tend to be on drugs most of the time. Unpredictable to say the least. Any choice will do Fear Factor, but pick a direction and stick to it. Jump ho's are your friends!

Stinger out.
