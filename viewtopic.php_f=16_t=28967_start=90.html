It's easy to defeat Hovis (I watched my wife do it).

After she enters the Dublin system, she switches to cockpit mode and makes a beeline to the Hood. She saves her game the minute she's in the Hood's docking bay. She goes through the storyline, takes on Hovis and moves to the starting gate (still in cockpit view). On go, she hits the afterburners, goes into cruise mode and gets a good 300 meter head start. I swear, the way she holds the course is the same way she holds the Interstate in her SUV. She never misses a gate, doesn't bump Hovis and wins every time on the first try.

And she used a Clydesdale!
