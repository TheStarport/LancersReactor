Argh

Mon Mar 14, 2005 6:11 pm by Argh

Not to crush your parade, Godzilla style... but based on your previous posts... I have a feeling that you mainly need to calm down... slow down... and take the time to read about how to get things done .

In one of your previous posts, you were told by another modder that FLE/FLSE is not an automatic tool for making mods , and requires that you understand the fundamentals of INI coding before using. Instead of taking that to heart, scaling back your plans, and starting with small experiments (like, say, moving the position of objects in the NY System around) to help yourself learn how to edit Systems... you're now asking somebody else to clean up your mess, and you won't even explain what your "great ideas" are.

Asking somebody to fix something you've broken, when you cannot even explain what's broken... is not a good way to get help. There are literally hundreds of pages of documentation around here about INI coding- READ THEM. I've helped others fix various things, from models that they couldn't turn into CMPs to coding problems... but I wouldn't touch your project with a ten-foot cattle prod, because thus far... I see no reason why it wouldn't be a waste of my time.

Modding isn't easy- if it was, everybody would be doing it. Building anything more complicated than a single System is quite tough, and involves sitting down and hand-editing a lot of INI files. It's not something that you can just whip out in an hour.

There aren't any easy ways out, and quite honestly... if you want it done right, you're going to need to learn how to do it yourself. Maybe I'm wrong here- maybe somebody will take pity on you and take a chance on your unknown and probably very buggy code... but I doubt it
