Tobmaster

Tue Mar 25, 2003 9:07 pm by Tobmaster

That is absolutely Possible, if I get what you are saying. I have already made some decent mods of this purpose. I have made five levels of engines with cruise charge time and energy consumption differences as well as speed differences. to go with these I have made seven level of powerplants each with a higher recharge rate and thruster rate to go with my own thruster I tweeked. With all these modifications I can start with a heavy fighter capable of lvl 10 weapons and isn't any better than the startracker for power and be able to customize it to your desire. All the engines and powerplants are buyable and equipable. I am sure other Modders have already done this since it isn't all that hard. Adding the infocards were a pain though. I just used the thruster icon for the engine and the sheld battery icon for the powerplant since I can't add/change icons. For the most part these ideas have been discussed in other threads, but they tend to be all over the place.
