A list with all mods in it. Players must be abled to vote on each title. One vote per Mod per Logged in User.

In that way players can choose the best mod suitable for them.

I like to give you my idea of the best system for it:

Vote 1 = -2 (Horrible Mod)
Vote 2 = -1 (Bad Mod)
Vote 3 = 0 (Average Mod)
Vote 4 = +1 (Good Mod)
Vote 5 = +2 (Superb Mod)

The list should be like this:
1. Name of Mod.
2. Link to mod on the site.
3. Average vote.
-Count all votes together and divide by the ammount of votes and you got the -average vote (-2 to +2).
4. Also place a column for each vote (1-5) with the ammount of users for each vote.
5. Make a percentage list.
-votes 1 and 2 are negative, 4 and 5 positive, forget vote 3.

Example:
Mod1: 1000 are positive 750 negative
Mod2: 200 are positive 100 negative

Wich is the best mod? Mod 2 of course. 2/3 (66,6%) are happy with it.

So in listing you get this: (values are fictive and are not calculated out in percentage, etc.)

NAME / LINK / AVERAGE / VOTE1 / VOTE2 / VOTE3 / VOTE4 / VOTE5 / %GOOD
