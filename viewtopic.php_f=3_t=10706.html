What''s the greatest odds you''ve won against?

I quickly dismissed the light fighters from the end game when I left New York system for the first time. Of course, I was flying a Rhino at the time. My reasoning was simple, "if my Rhino, with it's shields, armor, and numerous guns, was having so much trouble surviving, there was no way a light fighter was going to last."

Since then, I've had to rethink that theory. I happened upon a Legionaire for sale at some base, and decided to try it out. The first time I was ambushed, so far from New York, I ducked, dodged, and managed to escape from a huge number of bad guys. It wasn't long before I got comfortable with my ships ability to evade. I was traveling routes populated by heavy fighters and the occasional VHF, without worry.

I've since switched to a Hawk light fighter, purely for the extra 10 cargo units it can carry. The Hawk also has slightly better weaponry available to it; and for the first time, I didn't just evade and run, I stayed and fought - four bloodhounds and a wolfhound against a lone me. I hardly took a scratch as I took out one enemy after another. I could've probably avoided more damage if I was faster with my countermeasures.

The enemy wasn't the most dangerous I've ever faced; I've fought against VHF's with my own VHF's, for instance. This was the first time, however, where I fought such great odds when I wasn't sure what my ship was capable of in combat (aside from the odd time when I just gave up the battle as lost and did a suicide charge.)
