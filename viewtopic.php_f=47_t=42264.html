Time for Neuromancer''s 2005 Fan Fiction Awards!!!

Seeing as how we have reached the end of another year, I thought it might be appropriate to honor some of the great fan fiction written during 2005.

It seems to me that there wasn't as much written this year as there was last year, but what came out this year was nothing to spit at. Great storylines, memorable villains---it's all here.

I've decided on several categories for the awards, listed below:

Best Villain
Best storyline
Best fan fiction "WTF?" moment

and the coup de gras:

Best overall fan fiction

Feel free to nominate fan fiction on this thread. Be sure to name the fan fiction, author, and category you are nominating for in your post. I'll tally them up and post the results at the beginning of the year. If you're a writer, feel free to nominate yourself---nothing wrong with a little self-promotion.

Oh, one other thing---If you'd like to add another category to the list, feel free to do so.

See you on the red carpet!
