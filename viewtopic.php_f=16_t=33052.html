Wiz914

Sun Jul 18, 2004 6:49 pm by Wiz914

The Junkers are the ones you want to rely on to get your rep fixed with the Rogues. The Junkers are a bit like the Zoners in that they give free access to their bases to most other factions. So go to Rochester or Beaumont and bribe some Rogues. Alternatively, you can wait for after you reach Mactan Base in the SP campaign. At that point, the Lane Hackers become neutral with you. So you can run missions for them. Since they are allied with the Outcasts and the Rogues, you'll gain reputation with them too. Doing missions for the Hackers is way cheaper than a bribe (in early game...).
