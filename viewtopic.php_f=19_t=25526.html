The Pirate Code of Conduct
Players role playing as pirates must follow The Pirate Code of Conduct.

Role
Only pirates are allowed to engage in activities such as mugging/tolling and taxing.

Tag
Pirates must use the tag [Pirate , [X or (X)
You may use a clan tag in addition to the pirate tag.

Pirate Activity Areas
Pirate activity is allowed anywhere apart from the following areas:

New York
New Berlin
New Tokyo
New London
Omicron Theta

Taxing
Pirates may not tax any player under level 20, this will be punished by a 500k fine collected by Admins or [Hunter. If an admin collects the fine the money will goto the player taxed, if a [Hunter collects the money it will be split 50/50 with the player taxed.

The maximum tax allowed to be collected is 100k or 70 cargo.

Pirates may kill any hunter regardless of level aslong as it is in a pvp zone.
