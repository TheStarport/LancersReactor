Acuttaly this isn't as daunting as one might think, you simply need to make one jump hole *the one that works* like you would normal, but the non working jumphole just needs to be a nodock type of object. No docks are found in a few places, for example the dreadnaught you see 'docked' to norfolk is a good example of a nodock. You will need to make a new solararch of a jumphole that is nodock and this will mean you you will need a new cmp file *not hard just make a copy of a standard jumphole's cmp and mat files and just give it a new name* add the new entry and add you nodock jump hole with the appropiate nick. It should work, I havn't tested it *a 'one way jumphole theroy' but I have made a nodock jumphole, so i don't see why it would. Hope that helps.
