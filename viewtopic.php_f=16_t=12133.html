There's a whole bunch of Outcast fighter wrecks to search there, and only one has the Cerberus guns; but several of them have Ripper mine droppers and Wyrm Type 2 guns. The way I make sure I've checked all of them is to avoid getting too close to the jumphole they're clustered around, because when you're near there your sensors have reduced efficiency, and then select each wreck in turn from the sensor list and look at it's direction indicator to see if I've already damaged it. This works pretty well for ensuring you got them all, but the constant radiation and packs of Nomad fighters that show up make it a bit of a pain. Good luck!
