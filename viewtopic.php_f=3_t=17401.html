The sequence of events during that portion of the mission is as follows:
- you engage the Rhineland fleet
- as soon as all of the Valkyries are destroyed you are told to make a run for the New Berlin jumphole
- Cobo and Lord Hakkera make their speeches during the flight to the jumphole
- when both of them are done speaking you are given the objective to dock with the jumphole, and the jumphole starts working

As I said in my earlier post, the jumphole will not be activated unless all Rhineland fighters are destroyed and both Cobo and Lord Hakkera complete their speeches. The jumphole will remain locked if you retreat from battle before you are told to do so, and it will also remain locked if Cobo or Lord Hakkera cannot make their speeches because they are dead (or because your sound system does not work properly).
