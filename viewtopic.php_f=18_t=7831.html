Capture the Base Mod

Hello, we are starting work on a Base control type mod for freelancer, our intent is to create smaller 'systems' that contain central controllable base points and planets - the concept of the game is to 'take control' of these areas by either touching some form of flag or switch. Upon controlling an area all turrets, battleships etc. will be 'friendly' to your team and 'enemies' to the other and so you can see how this works.

[another option here is for each side to have ONE battleship that must be escorted and 'defended' for 5 mins in the area to control that area

Very similar to Tribes Capture and Hold or king of the hill type games.. and that is the point.

We also hope to have purchaseable and placeable weapons like Weapons Platforms and turrets etc.

We also would like to have pilotable battleships and capital ships, but these would be for specific purposes like in-field repairs and ammo drops etc.

Anyway, post more ideas/interest here!
