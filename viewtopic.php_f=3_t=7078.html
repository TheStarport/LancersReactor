Only one Major complaint...

Hail All,

Okay, so my will-power broke and I started playing the demo.

I have to say that I am actually pleased with the game, and will be even more pleased when the 4th arrives and my pre-order shows up on my doorstep.

However....you KNEW this was coming...

There is ONE (1) thing that is REALLY irritating me, and to 99.999% of other humans this will sound very trivial...

They forced usage of the F1 key to open the maingame-menu, instead of the Escape key.
Yes, this is VERY minor to most people, but come on, even an idiot knows you hit "Esc" to open the main menu of a game & thereby pause the game!
What really ticks me off though is that they don't even allow us to re-map that key.

Plus, it just doesn't really make sense.
The default layout of F2, F3, & F4, are "Go To", "Dock", & "Formation" respectively...it only MAKES SENSE that F1 would be "Free Flight" to match the HUD Display top center of the screen as all the other "F" keeys seem to do!

As I said, trivial perhaps.
But, when I have to pause the game real quick to run to the door, kick the dog, or shoot out at the neighbor's kids, and I hit the "Esc" key out of habit only to come back to find the "Load Savegame" screen...well, it gets old. LMAO!



Other than that one small pet peeve I am pretty much happy with the game.
Yeah, there are some other small issues that I dislike, but I will still enjoy it, and it is still worth the price....to me at least.



Anyway, just needed to vent that.


Fly Well,

Pyrate Dredd
