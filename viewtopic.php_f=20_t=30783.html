Esquilax

Mon Apr 26, 2004 1:54 pm by Esquilax

Alright, I was content to let Final handle this, but I guess I'll have to step in . As Final suggested, the problem is most likely due to the sound and video going out of sync due to bottlenecks. Most commonly, a slow graphics card will cause this problem, but it can also be caused by a number of factors. In order to increase performance and eliminate as many bugs a possible, I suggest the following; download and install the latest drivers for your graphics card, make sure that you have the latest version of DirectX (9.0b), and defrag your hard disk. If the problem still evidences itself then, as Final suggested, try turning down all of the detail levels in FL, and/or play at a lower resolution so that your graphics card doesn't have to work as hard.

There! Try the above, and let me know how it goes.
