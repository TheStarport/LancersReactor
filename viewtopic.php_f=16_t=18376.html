redeye

Mon Jun 02, 2003 4:23 pm by redeye

Online MP is a dangerous game to be playing. Check out some of the posts on the MP forum, those guys are serious. Wars, truces, rules of engagement - there might not be as sweet missions as SP but theres usually a good plot

If you're gonna play LAN co-op tho get one of the latest mods. Go for one with capital ships - FLRebalance, Reynen's, Hostile Univese, Babylon 5 etc. Means you can fly a posse with someone going big guns (gunship/cruiser etc) and other folk flying fighter backup. Cruising round the borderworlds in a formations like that, looking for cap. ship fleets to waste is much more satisfying than alot of SP missions.
