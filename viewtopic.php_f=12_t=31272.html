What kind of music is it anyway? Don't try doing this w/ rock/metal music, you'll end up disappointed with the results.
You can try finding the right frequency out via trial-and-error method, ie trying it out in winamp using it's built-in equilizer. Just cut the freq's out by turning the bands all the way down one by one. When you notice that the voice has faded or is very quiet you found what you were looking for so work from there on. Fine tuning takes time, but no pain - no gain.
As for the programs you can use, well, you'll have to ask someone else... I use Sound Forge for all my sound-related exibitions, but I think it has a 3 digit price, and the first digit is >= 2
