rkcohs

Thu Mar 27, 2003 7:23 pm by rkcohs

imagine a Startracker with a forward canard (wing). Looks much cooler than any of the other civ LF's.

As far as I can tell, it's not implemented anywhere (maybe on planet traffic scenes), but the model is present in the game folders.

The ship would be a great addition in a mod IMHO. Give liberty space a nice advanced LF.

If anyone wants to see what it looks like, here's a quick & Dirty method.

use BINI to convert 'data\ships\shiparch.ini', rename the original files extension (I just add .bak to the filename). Remove the '.txt' from the new file.

Open the new file in Notepad, do a Find for "ge_fighter2".

Under "ge_fighter2", you should see:
DA_archetype = ships\civilian\cv_startracker\cv_startracker.cmp

Change that to:
DA_archetype = ships\civilian\cv_starblazer\cv_starblazer.cmp

Basically just changing 'tracker' to 'blazer'.

Save, load up the game, and go git you a StarTracker. Voila.
