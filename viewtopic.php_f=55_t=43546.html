Missions appear at random. Since you failed to complete your mission, it is gone and you got paid nothing. Also, your rep with whoever gave you the mission probably suffered a bit too.

When you take a mission, you do the mission -- nothing else. You can't stop for a pizza or a sidewinder fang just because you feel like it. In effect, you abandoned your mission. That mission may reappear eventually, but don't count on it. (Maybe another freelancer did it!)
