Whilst you can play HU and replay the single player campaign, I do recommend people play the campaign to the end before installing HU because Hu increases the difficulty a bit.

As for NEW campaigns.

The last thing I wanted was a second campiagn that ends when you finish the last mission. Ive taken a much more open ended approach to the same campaign idea. So the missions are infinately repeatable and available on and off line.

Dont expect HU to lead you by the hand through all the missions. Nor should you expect HU to hand all the missions to you on a plate. You have to explore to find the missions clues and take it from there. Like the original games Outcast leaders missing son news item on Malta. A 6 million reward for his return.

You wont see that news item in new york and you should not expect all Hostile universe plot lines to start and end in Newyork either.

Explore, conquer, trade!

The typical player who will enjoy Hostile Universe V8 are the ones who like a challenge and know how to explore and find the goodies ive hidden all over the universe. Bars are invaluable for this, as is checking the info cards on anything you find.

BTW, the players you will meet in one bar that you may have played against online are not actually the real players, they just submitted their names and stuff they wanted to say to the players and i added them as new characters in Hostile Universe. Just thought id get that out of the way before anybody asks how i got real player to player bar chats going, I didnt.

However it does tell you the sort of attention to detail I given HU and in what areas HU differs from normal mods. You see these areas are linked to the new features so simply reading the plots, clues and news will actually introduce you to Most of the new stuff eventually. Providing you dont dismiss what is said to you as just bar talk. Coz it is not. Well some of it is

Giskard
