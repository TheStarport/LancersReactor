Sat Apr 05, 2003 1:26 am by Silver71880

You actually can get friendly to most of the groups out there after you finished the first mission. Except the Xenos. But since they don't have a base inside the New York System, it's quite easy to pick them as black sheep, because everyone hates them. Just hang around Detroit Munitions and kill a lot of Xenos. Your reputation with the Junkers starts as 4 blocks red, but still neutral. The rogues & outcasts are 6 or 7 blocks red and hostile. Just keep on killing Xenos' fighters till the Junkers show only 2 blocks red.

If that happens, go to Rochester and take on jobs in the bar. Just take the ones where you have to kill Xenos dudes. Don't choose any Bounty Hunter missions. Your rep with the Junkers will go up. Just continue to do jobs for the Junkers killing Xenos ships and your rep with the Outcasts & Rogues will go up as well.

Eventually, your rep with the Rogues will be 1 or 2 blocks Green. Still Neutral, but they are already in the green. If you look on the job board again in the bar, you'll see that you sometimes have an opportunity to do mission for them as well. Only do them when they tell you to go after the Xenos. If you play a couple of missions for them, they will be green as well, the outcasts will be green and if you even continue your job as Xenos Butcher, the Bounty Hunters Guild will like you as well. Same as the Police & Navy. (but they were already Green in the first place)

And all without any bribe whatsover.

I have to admit that it is a rather strange sight when you see for example rogues and the police fighing against eachother. They both are marked as friendly units then.. But what's the big deal anyway. Everyone likes you so Peace dudes!! ..

Except for those Xenos toads of course.....
