a fairly long trip at 30 min each way average, but you can do it in a defender by yourself:

Buy Boron (~155) at Newark Station in Manhattan, New York
Then take it to
Strikers Mining Outpost in the Leeds system sell the boron (for ~860) and buy Berilyium (~300) and transport it back to Newark Station and sell it for (~960)
one round trip nets about 60k in a defender.

I find its usually an easier trip if you go through the Magellan system instead of the Cortez system (too many damn trade lane disruptions in that system, at least for me)
