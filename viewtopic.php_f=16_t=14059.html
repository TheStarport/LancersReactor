You can acquire the c10 guns from wrecks at any level. I took the time when I was given "freelancing time" while at Kyushu during the SP game to go to Tau-37 and loot the Griffin wreck with it's Dark Blossom guns... instant level-up. BTW, the wrecks do respawn their stuff after awhile... not sure how long; but, attempting to loot them (even when you get nothing) resets the timer, so if you keep trying without waiting long enough, you'll never get the stuff.
