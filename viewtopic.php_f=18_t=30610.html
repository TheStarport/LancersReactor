Yeah, FLE has alot of bugs. I advise doing that by hand, it isn't really hard.

Just open the news.ini in ...\DATA\MISSIONS.

Then you do this:

[NewsItem (remember to close the brackets)
rank = base_0_rank, mission_end - 1
icon = system - 2
logo = news_newlondon - 3
category = 56122 - 4
headline = 56122 - 5
text = 56123 - 6
audio - 7
base = li01_01_base - 8

1. This is the RANK that you want. base_0_rank, mission_end is basically freetime, after the missions, or when you have any SP Mod activated.

2. The ICON that you want it to have. Check (i.) for more ICON Names.

3. The LOGO that you want it to have. Its the big picture when you've clicked on the news. Check (ii.) for more LOGO Names.

4. The Category of the News (basically the headline). *

5. The Headline of the News (Most people keep it the same). *

6. The TEXT that you want to have, or the actual News Report. *

7. Just keep this the same

8. On which bases the news should be on. Check (iii.) for more base names.

* All Infocards are from the RESOURCE.DLL. Its also the reason why it won't work with FLE. FLE can't save into the RESOURCE.DLL
