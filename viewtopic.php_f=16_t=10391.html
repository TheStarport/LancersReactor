Brother Grimm

Tue Mar 18, 2003 10:34 pm by Brother Grimm

Baracuda seems to be a bit more nimble (might just be my imagination) and it holds 45 cargo to Dragon's 40. Otherwise they are identical. There's not any real reason you couldn't get thru Mission 8 with a Hawk (some of the missions AFTER that could be more of a problem, however).

The Starkiller Torp will be the only one you CAN have until after the SP missions (During the story missions, you land on the one base you can buy the SunSlayer, but you won't be high enough level to get it). It's better than nothing. If you didn't do any damage with the torp, odds are you didn't hit the platform with one. A little known fact about torps and missles is that they will NOT track stationary objects (weapons platforms, battleships, bases, etc.) You must be lined up perfectly to hit them from any distance (use the GO TO command to get lined up, drop out of cruise and fire the torp or missle).

Missles at this level in the game are a waste of money....just get some better guns. Personally, I think missles are like throwing money at someone to blow them up, but that's just my opinion.
