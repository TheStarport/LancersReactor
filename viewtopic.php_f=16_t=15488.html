I've fooled around with all the weapons and also the Titan and the Sabre. Here's what I have found works best for me: I used the Titan for a long time, until I read someone say that it drove like a truck. It does. So I traded it in on a Sabre. The Sabre can be fitted with the same Adv Brigantine shield as the Titan plus it has the added advantage of mounting the turret horizontally so that it can fire forward, thus givining you in effect one more gun than the Titan has. And it is much more manuverable as well. I use 4 nomad blasters and two Tizona Del Cid's plus one Rapier Turret. That combo knocks down shields pretty fast and the fact that the nomad blasters don't use any energy allows me to shoot constantly if I need to. Also, the strategy guide says that the aiming retucule averages the firing rates to aim, so if all your weapon firing rates are the same, you get an absolute aim point. All the above weapons are the same rate. Some of those big energy hungry class 10's fire at 2.00! IF you combine that with somthing that fires at 8.33 you have an aiming mess. All mine fire at 4.00. Hope this helps you
