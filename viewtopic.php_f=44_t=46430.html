Just a minor tip for those who wish to make these icons, it's a pleasure to see your own work in the game.

The tutorial is super. But it's best not to copy or imitate the same original FL background.

I use a light blue-tinged single-tone background and almost every icon is nice and sharp and clear.

But you have to do the slog of deleting the original background and make it transparent, this takes about 5-15 minutes to do.

No worries Gibbon, no offence taken.

Sure, it's a matter of personal choice.

The reason for my comment is that in the mod I had to fix (many hours of despair, let me say!!), trying to emulate the original background, the new ship icons were very unclear against it.

And it does take a bit of skill to get it right, even after many tries, when I tried to make them clearer using as close to the original background as I could get, it turned out to be a bad background to use when the new ships have a variety of new colours that the original ships don't have.

So I gave up on that and played with different background schemes, and the coloured ships stood out best with a much simpler gradiented lighter backdrop.

But it really is a matter of personal choice, of course. And apart from that, like everyone else, I like to think of my way as better - for me!
