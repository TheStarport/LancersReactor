Humpback Challenge

Hi folks,

I just wanted to share a little bit of my current Freelancer project, playing the campaign in a Humpback.

By far the most difficult mission is the Hovis race. At first I thought it was impossible, but after a *lot* of practice and a *lot* of frustration, I finally got the hang of it, as you can see:



It is by far the most difficult challenge in the game, and obviously the game wasn't designed for it to be even possible. Even now that I have beat Hovis a few times (4 to be precise), the race is still really, really frustrating: although certainly you have to fly flawlessly, even then your success is mostly determined by luck.

While I wouldn't recommend this challenge for fun and enjoyment, I thought I might mention for the sake of trivia that it is indeed possible to beat Hovis (and the game) in a Humpback.

I might write a little mini-walkthrough for the occasion. Stay tuned!

-breslin

breslin

Thu Oct 26, 2006 6:59 am by breslin

I've heard people getting stuck on the Hovis race, but I didn't on the first time through the SP campaign.

I would not say personally that the Hovis race is difficult in general, but certainly winning the race flying a Humpback is extremely difficult indeed, probably the most difficult challenge imaginable in the game. Significantly more difficult than beating the whole game in a Rhino (rather more difficult than the Starflier campaign), or hunting Nomads in the Unknown systems flying a Starflier.

The Humpback is the least maneuverable ship in the game (others being at least *twice* as maneuverable, some being *four times* as maneuverable). It being possible to win the race flying a Humpback, it's clearly possible to win with the other ships.

Beating Hovis in a Dromedary took me a few tries. That's a fun challenge too, and one you can take without modding the game.

breslin

Fri Oct 27, 2006 7:36 am by breslin

dudeofthed, you might want to double-check your mods. Maybe you didn't set the right maneuverability or turn radius, got the speed wrong or something. In any case, I believe you would find that winning with a Humpback is *extremely* difficult, and has a whole lot to do with luck. I would say on average it's *possible* to win 1 in 10 times, if you fly perfectly, and to fly perfectly takes an enormous amount of practice; the necessary maneuvering techniques are not obvious.

It's not a "win once lose once" kind of thing, but a "lose 30 times, finally get the hang of it, lose another 10, even if you fly perfectly, almost win once but make an extremely frustrating error, lose another 20 times, finally win OMG!!" kind of situation.

And I had that kind of trouble ater flying the Humpback continuously for several campaign missions. That another might just hop in and win the race first or second try seems vanishingly unlikely. I'm not saying you can do it, but I am saying you definitely can't do it without a whole lot of patience and practice.
