Epsilon Mod - Comments and Feedback **New Thread**

Hi all,

This is the new thread for the Epsilon Mod. The old one reached almost 750 comments (thanks to all the people who contributed and made it one of the largest threads in TLR history ). We feel that it's necessary to start a new thread, so I will lock the old thread and this will be the new place to leave general comments/questions for Epsilon. I will also link between threads so if you need to get to either one you can. The old thread also contains the URL's to download the mod. I will post them here too. Please, if you enjoy our mod (or don't ) and would like to see things changed or added, leave us a note! We always listen to the player's comments, and try to make the mod as near perfect as we can, so we appreciate any comments you have. Don't worry if you have a negative comment, we aren't that mean.

The latest version of Epsilon is 3.11. You can download it here or by clicking on this image
