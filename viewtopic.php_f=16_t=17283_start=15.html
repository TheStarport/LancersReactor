Just out of scientific curiosity i did an experiment to find the turn and pitch rates.

What i did was get each ship, put it into a full turn and time how long it took to do 10 full rotations. (3600 degrees). Then i repeated it for pitch.
The lower the time taken the better the turn and pitch rate.
i used the sun as the point of refrence to time it.

You know what? both the sabre and titan got:
turn = 50sec :- Turn rate = 72 deg/sec
pitch = 59sec :- Pitch rate = 61 deg/sec
give or take a second for inherent recording innacuracy
The eagle was alot better with:
Turn = 38 sec :- Turn rate = 95 deg/sec
Pitch = 47 sec :- Pitch rate = 76 deg/sec

I allways thought the sabre felt a bit quicker than the titan, but numbers dont lie. (least not mine LOL)

So now you know
