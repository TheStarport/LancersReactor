PCHolt

Wed Feb 26, 2003 4:05 pm by PCHolt

What I think is the best comprimise is the heavy fighter. Most (all?) have at least one turret mount, and several weapon mounts. They also have great armor, good manuverablity, and a modest amount of cargo space.

I may bounce back and forth between a light and heavy fighter, but I don't think I'll ever bother with a freighter unless I find a real need for the extra cargo space. Freighters are also grossly underpowered. The heavy fighters have the power to handle firing multiple guns repeatedly or a long period of time.

One thing I don't see many people talking about is how they use their guns in combat. I've found that by mounting two higher class guns that are more effective against shields, and then a couple that do more damage to hulls, you can switch between the two sets, and never worry about draining you gun energy down to nothing. Personally, I don't really care for missiles at this point, because they cost to much, and don't hit their target as often as you like them too.
