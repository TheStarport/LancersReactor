Actually that's quite a weak defense. Cheaters who just do their own thing and never bother others? Doesn't exist of course. Now why is that?

This is multiplayer. If a person just wanted to mod, he'd do it on his own server. When people want to cheat vs other players, they do it in multiplayer.

The following actions of a cheater ruin the experience for other players:
1: Killing others with uber weapons (obviously)
2: Being unkillable by others (Why? Because you are ruining their PK/pirating experience! Pirating comes with risks, yes, but there should be a possibility of killing the other player, otherwise you might as well turn PvP off)
3: Cheating to get money (Why? Because you flood the game economy, forcing other players to pay more for items traded/purchased)
4: using a mod that lags other players and servers.
5: ANYTHING that makes the game easier for you personally (why? Because it cheapens the accomplishments of the other players)

If you can find some cheats that don't fit into those 5, I might be inclined to believe the crap about keeping to yourself. But since I highly doubt any cheat exists outside of those 5 criteria, these statements about harmless cheating are largely self-justification and posturing.
