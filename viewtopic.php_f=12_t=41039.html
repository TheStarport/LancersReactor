Orillion.

Tue Sep 27, 2005 9:33 pm by Orillion.

@Freighter Fighter: Nearly all of the staff members of this project have seen your work. Would you be interested, you are welcome aboard.

@Mike G: The 'main' benefit would, of course, be an unlimited space for imagery. We will give a few artists a chance to have their own part of a art-site, but there will not be nearly as much members as at for example DeviantART.
By sumbitting their best works, they show us exactly what they can do. The art sumbitted will never be used without the artists' permission. If you do not want to 'risk' it, do not send in your art.
We don't do anything with the art - except evaluating and choosing what we consider to be the best few artists - until they are a part of the site. Then they can freely submit art onto the site where it will apear in their gallery.

@Wilde: Sexual art will be allowed, to some extent. Though, sexualy related art will also have to be submitted to a staff member first, who can evaluate if it's sexual art or pornography.
The only types of art we will never allow are racially discriminating and, as before stated, pornography. In some cases the staff may have to delete a specific submission. We will always try to state the reason for why this was done.

We will try to welcome as many as possible into the project and in certain cases the staff members will vote.

I hope this answers your questions. If you have any more concerns, please, don't hesitate. Ask in this thread or at the submission e-mail: zobani@gmail.com
