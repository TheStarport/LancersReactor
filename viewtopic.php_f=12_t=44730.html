Features and still more coming.

GAMEPLAY

• You can easily recruit pilots that are in friendly status with your civilization, while pilots that are not, will attack you
• Player can recruit and command up to 3 wingmen to his fleet, which can be ordered individually to attack, form-up, and other various commands
• An open ended, beautiful and innovative 3D space environment dressed with endless missions, stars, planets, moons, asteroids, comets, black holes, star bases, flying cities is open for exploration, trade, crafting, diplomacy, fight and filled with an infinite number of action adventures
• choose your role randomly or follow the storyline
• before adventuring begins, players will be able to develop a concept of the role they would like to play in the game and choose between trader, spy, soldier, scientist, headhunter, doctor, pirate, policeman or simply an adventurer
• You will be able to explore the galaxy as member of one, among 10 civilizations, located on different starting location across the universe
• Trade goods, destroy ships and buildings, explore unknown anomalies and ancient ruins, and visit orbital station and distant places, travel with jump gates
• make your way through known and unknown star systems in the Milky Way galaxy
• 56 large systems so far and maybe more to come as this is still a work in progress
• discover a lot of special equipment including damage boosters or mining tools
• As you progress through the game each ship can be modified in your own creative style: speed, target system, jammer, shield, armor, maneuverability, weaponry, afterburner, cargo space and weight
• Integrated in-game diplomacy system will determine whether you in war or at peace with another civilization are
• Trade with military, narcotics, technology, tobacco, medicine, materials, industry, consumer, computer, chemicals and luxury goods
• loads of galactic press will help you combine facts making your funds go sky high or down under; by investing you can make more money or lose everything
• 10 civilizations available
• Since AI plays a crucial part in Space Force 2, it will create a thrilling playing experience throughout the game
• Non-stop action with loads of star vessels within beautiful constellations makes Space Force 2 intriguing and exiting space game
• Ships neural core computer system – Harmonia will guide you through, help you win situations and make your coffee
• rank advancement over
• The dynamic universe
• build a steady reputation and be well known throughout the galaxy
• ID cards will help you infiltrate, and communicate with your enemy

AI

• NPC is aware when he is locked
• NPC knows when we are on his tail
• NPC calls for assistance through radio if he is in danger
• NPC can respond to calls of other NPC’s
• NPC knows when a missile is fired upon him
• NPC is aware of surrounding objects (buildings, meteors, spaceships… and also of his locked target ship and chooses the best maneuver
• NPC's advance through skills, just like your character
• NPC knows when he has disadvantage in battle and when it's time to flee
• NPC's awareness in diplomacy makes him act accordingly to rules
• NPC will attack us if our cargo is valuable to him
• NPC recognizes if we fire upon him, and he communicates with other ships to convince them to attack us
• Some races are more intelligent than others, and for example are aware of the sun direction so they can use it to flee
• Every NPC will make enough room for him to make the attack
• During a battle NPC's communicate with each other in order to rearrange forces, so there will be maximum two NPC's on one target, and they will not be on each other's way
• Every NPC has his own personality, and can make his own decisions despite his current orders. His decision also depends on pilot’s race, for example, collective pilots are violent and inhuman, which makes them hard to back down and easy to attack you without cause. Pirates can attack if you carry valuable cargo, but they will easily flee if situation is not favorable for them.
ENGINE
• High performance real-time 3D rendering using DirectX 9.0c
• True 64 bit HDR rendering if card supports it, HDR fresnel, HDR specular, HDR bloom and glow, star effect, tone mapping (adaptive exposure), HDR antialiasing, blue shift
• Real time environment lightning
• Cool post processing effect like full screen motion blur, black and white rendering, blur, screen filtering, and screen space refraction
• Pixel doubling rendering for slow graphics cards
• 3 different rendering engines SM 1.4, 2.0, 3.0 so a wide list of graphics cards is supported
• Advance particle system with collision including effects like billboards, flares, lens flares, smoke, fire, sparks, debris, beams, all particles can be rendered in refraction buffer and make effect like glass, smoke, heat effect, shock wave and much more
• Flare per pixel occlusion
