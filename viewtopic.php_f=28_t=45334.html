Thanks, ColdWarKid

New Character Mod

There are 64 new characters in the mod allowing you to start with any faction or as a civilian from almost any planet.

The new character starts with a basic ship and loadout for that faction at the nearest factions' base to Planet Manhattan, Planet New London, Planet New Tokyo or Planet New Berlin.

The CSV has been made pilotable for the Junkers and Hogosha.

Faction reputations have been modified to align them with info card descriptions.

Civilian locations and your chosen factions' bases are visible on the nav map.
