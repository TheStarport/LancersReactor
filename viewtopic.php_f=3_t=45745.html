I actually saw a name for it somewhere before, but honestly don't remember what it was called exactly. When I "recreated" the model I gave it the designation of:
A (attack) fighter
10165 (date built 10/16/2005)
E (Escort) sleeper ship escort
Loadout, 2 guns only. (wanted to keep it close to the original design, however this one also has a "hidden" cruise disruptor & internal thruster)

I had given it the code name, "Wraith" because it seemed to "fit" .

I really have no idea what they are named or designated, but I've been working on recreating those two models. They're acutally very gorgeous ships, but for the life of me I don't know why DA didn't include them in the game itself. So, I set out to rectify that situation.
You know, after all the times I played the game, I never did find any of those ships until I recreated them. There was a brief moment of hope when I ran across some "alliance" ships..but they only turned out to be the "Farmers Alliance" faction of Kusari who were flying Star Flyers, Star Trackers and Hawks.
I was so dissappointed.
So, I think that is when I began to learn moddeling.
The 88WASP was my first attempt at recreating the Wraith and that was only going on my faulty memory, until I found a way to view and "pause" the movie so I could get closer perspectives of the actual ship.
After all of that, the concept of the Domkavash was left wide open for imagination and interpretation since they're only mentioned in passing during the story line.
My particular versions had to be somewhere "outside the box" of the usual freelancer type ships, more advanced looking and somewhat based on the coloring of the Active Artifact map crystal.
Has anyone ever seen some of the "concept" drawings? OMG! One ship was a Britoninan LF and HF, then there was this Nomad "Time Gate" . UNREAL!
