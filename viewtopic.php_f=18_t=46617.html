First don't go screwing around with somebody elses mod, always make sure you have their permission first, especially where ships are concerned as 90% of them don't belong to the mod maker, and shipwrights are VERY touchy about people messing with their creations.

Your {CollisionGroup}'s/damage 3DBs are probably what's wrong.

Cost is assigned in the same way as the ship hull, depending on how many hitpoints need to be replaced will determine the cost, I'm pretty sure there is a multiplier assigned to the ship class as well, in my experience a 100 hitpoints {CollisionGroup} costs more to repair on a VHF than on a LF.
