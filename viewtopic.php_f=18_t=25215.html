I think you are wrong on FMA and me.
Thirst thing: FMA is a compilation of mods, not a mod by itself. It was compiled the same time you announced TNG, and is based on simmilar material. And FMA was first in the public. So no clone from TNG FMA is an addon to the single player game, expanding the universe to explore, not for fighting or MP.
