Tungsten (Wolfram)
Discovered: 1783.
Symbol: W
Atomic Number: 74 (group VI b)
Atomic Mass: 183.85
Electronegativity: 1.7
Atomic Radius: 0.137 nm (nanometers)
1st Ionization Energy: 0.78 MJ (MegaJoules)
Electron Config.:5d4 (1s2 2s2 2p6 3s2 3p6 4s2 4p6 3d10 5s2 5p6 4d10 3f8 6s2 6p6 5d4)
Tungstem is a lightweight metal, light grey in color with caracteristical metalic shine. It reacts with strong acids, and it forms weak hydroxides.
Tungsten is mostly used in lightbulbs and in lightweight steel production. It's a mediocre conductor of el. current, and a very good heat conductor.
