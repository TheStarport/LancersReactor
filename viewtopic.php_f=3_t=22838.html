Tue Aug 12, 2003 5:33 pm by NukeIt

You don't need to beat on bounty hunters to get in good with the big bad pirate factions. Heck, you don't even need to make any new enemies.

The first step is in Manhattan, where you park yourself mid-trade lane and start blasting transports. Make sure no single faction goes below midlevel neutral, but keep killing transports in general. The Lane Hackers should be the first to lose their hostile status.

Then you go to Freeport 6 in Tau-29, where you can bribe the Outcasts with all that cash made off of blasting transports. This pushes the Hacker rep up more, too.

After that, head to Freeport 9 in Omicron Theta, where you can bribe the Corsairs. This will push the Outcasts neutral again, but nothing will go hostile if you play your cards right.

Now go to Bruchsal base, in the Frankfurt system. Bribe the Red Hessians. THis will push the Corsair rep neutral, but should leave the Outcast and Hacker ratings alone.

Through all that, the bounty hunters don't go hostile. There shouldn't be any hostile corporations or houses, either. Now you have pretty much free range through the Omicrons, Taus, Sigmas, and Omegas.
