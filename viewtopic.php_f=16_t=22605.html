Sherlog

Thu Aug 07, 2003 3:57 pm by Sherlog

I have looked into the .ini files: those two battleships (BS Texas and BS Arizona) are '10%-invulnerable' until the cutscene with Walker begins. That is, they stop taking damage when their hitpoints are down to 10%. No wonder I got nowhere with Drillers/Starkillers.

There are several other places in the campaign where things like this were done, even to stuff that you are supposed to destroy - weapon platforms during mission 1, BS Unity + cruisers during mission 4, power generators during mission 12 ...

The BS Omaha and the 3 cruisers are okay, though. Unlike many other battleships in the campaign, the Omaha is a real ship and not a mission solar.
