**MOD KIT** Freelancer EXTRA: Every single ship in Freelance

I'm making a mod with every single ship in freelancer for sale, upgradeable engines, power plants, scanners and tractors.

It still VERY EARLY AND NOT FULLY TESTED.

To use it, you have to know .ini files (for now...). I made a test system with a copy of Manhattan in it, called mod01_01. Look in market_ships.ini, there are lots of lines for you to remove the ';' that disables them. Remember, 3 ships per base maximum. All ships are at LO LO prices and fully loaded, for them to be easy to try out.

Highlights:

27 new ships!
Starblazer
Juni's defender
Blood Dragon
Mining ship - nothing else like it!
Liners! (no, they are NOT both using the same model!)
Kusari capital ships - Destroyer particularly
Transports, Trains and CSVs, oh my!

read the readme.txt for details
best used with FLMM
you NEED the "Ghost docking ring" mod to use the capital ships! (comes with FLMM)

you can download it at THIS PAGE

I am looking for any CONSTRUCTIVE feedback. Please don't repeat what's in the Known Issues of the readme.
