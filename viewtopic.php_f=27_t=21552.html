Tue Jul 22, 2003 1:37 pm by Tygrys

There is no reason to put one big mod on another. They may change the same thing in the game in the different ways, so no sense of doing this. Other problem is the DLL merging. Every mod has its own idea and merging them will make no good.
From the technical point of view only the scripted mods with no DLLs can be installed over another one. From the big ones only FMA (Masive Addon) is scripted, but uses own DLL, so no way here. You can use some small mods like 'Friendly Nomads' or 'Not so Easy Money' with other mods. But in general, no mod merging is possible without compiling them to one. FMA is such a compilation.
