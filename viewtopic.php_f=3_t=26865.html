Mon Dec 15, 2003 5:35 pm by Sh|ft

Ah, it took me a while to figure this out, spending hundreds of thousands of credits equipping weapons. There is a wierd box in the Upper Left corner of the weapon. An open grey means it is not equipped and cannot be equipped. A closed grey means it is equipped and cannot be unequipped (usually shows up while in space) an open blue box means it is not equipped and a blue box means it is equipped. Clicking on an open blue box will close the box and equip the weapon. Clicking on a closed blue box will open the box and un-equip the weapon.

Hope this helped.
Viper
