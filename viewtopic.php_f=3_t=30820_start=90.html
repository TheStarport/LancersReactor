Here is my dream space game. I hope that someday someone would develop it whether that is FL2 or another game.

3D space (not domed 2d circles)

be able to establish a home base
be able to build defense turrets, towers, etc ( and upgrade them)
be able to have multiple bases
buy multiple ships
tow empty ship to base
stow locker at home bases (ammunition, fuel, batteries)
repair droid aboard ship
repair droids at bases ( repair or scrap salvage items)
make repair droids upgradeable
factions (create new or join) with NPC factions that rise and fall in populations (as they make kills their population increases and visa versa)

Negotitations with factions (protection money, truces, cooperative goals, smuggling, etc)

Only be able to fly ships from the faction you belong to.

Alien artifacts.

No story mode ( all dynamic and continuous events, catastrophes, wars, plagues, etc)

Newtonion physics in space then be able to open airfoils at a reduced speed and drop into a planets atmosphere with gravity, drag, and normal plane handling.
(Would love to see a squadron of enemy fighters break through a planets atmosphere in pursuit or chase a bounty hunter into a planets atmosphere and find his home base to capture)

Mission boards should run missions whether or not someone took the job. Want to be able to snoop on a hostile base or corporation mission board, see what actions are being planned and intercept. (Would like to enter a hostile planets atmosphere, land within so far from a city and tap into their communications: even leave a spy probe to continue getting data until it was found and destroyed).

Have a way to gain experience points like a RPG game. LevelUp and use points to increase your reflexes (ship responds faster), intelligence ( could be used to increase snooping range, repair speed, language knowledge of different factions, etc), charm ( better prices). As you level up the AI should level up and all AI pilots get better skills with better ships, weapons, etc. If a story line is included then at the end of the story line, (even if a maximum level has been reached) the AI pilots should continue to level increase, quantity increase, etc. until at an insane level.
Make difficulty settings based on starting AI pilot levels only (e.g. easy-AI pilots one level below you, normal-AI pilots even with you, hard- AI pilots one level above you, real hard- AI pilots two levels above you: really, real hard- three levels, insane- pick a level above three)

Mostly, I would like a game that is different with each restart. Planets, jumpholes, faction positions, etc. all new and having to explore the new restart for my intelligence gathering.

I'm not much on MP play because of slow internet connection and not enough time to play for several hours at a time but for the MP players if there were game settings for winning (e.g. last one standing, highest level after 10 days, top 3 dogfight duel w/winner take all, etc) with game announced resets, I believe the MP game would keep growing and continually attract new players.

Have bank account (individual or faction)
Comm frequencies that can be set by each faction to communicate with each other.

AND if I get shot down, I loose my ship, cargo, money that I'm carrying, etc and restart with a basic starter ship ( bank account money, base inventories, etc would remain for me to use unless that base is captured by someone else).

This is my dream space game. I wish someone would develop it.
