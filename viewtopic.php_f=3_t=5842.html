mustang

Wed Jan 29, 2003 1:14 pm by mustang

Well the full background goes something like this.

In the original concept of Freelancer, the Nomads showed up towards the end of the Coalition\Alliance war in a cloaked Dreadnaught and decided the human race was not fit to live and took out the entire solar system with a single blast at the sun (the ensuing expolsion was pretty impressive in the FMV by the way). The sleeper ships still (obviously) managed to escape and get to the sirius system.

About a year or so ago, DA decided to knock the Nomads down a peg or two. I mean how exactly do you fight an enemy with the ability to take out Solar system with one blast from a vessel that can sneak into the solar system undetected.

After that piece of news came out pretty well much nothing has been heard about the Nomads since. As you can read in the interviews with Jorg and Phil the Nomads are in the storyline somewhere. However what part they play exactly is unknown to all, except DA, Beta Testers (whoever they are) and the PR players.

Who knows guys you may win a copy of the sneak preview and be able to find out for yourselves pretty soon.
