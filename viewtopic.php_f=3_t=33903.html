VroomZo

Thu Sep 02, 2004 5:07 pm by VroomZo

It's a pain being a pirate.

Buy a Freighter (Dromedary is best) and purchase some decent guns and shields for it. Go to any trade lane, and shoot it untill the trade lane is disrupted. Wait for a transport to come.(don't attack freighters) When a transport arrives, use your guns to shoot its cargo pods. Eventually, the cargo pods will explode and reveal maybe 150 or so of a commodity. Keep on doing that for all of the cargo pods. However, avoid destroying the transport. Destroying the transports will get you loot, however, your reputation with the faction will be worse... If you keep on shooting the cargo pods, the ships will turn hostile, but your reputation will not be affected.

Online, you can pirate other players by either asking for their cargo, or just asking for their money. If they refuse, you can always kill them . It's not very nice, and usually, from my experience players will refuse.
