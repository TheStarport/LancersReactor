Sat May 06, 2006 4:11 am by Bejaymac

@ Xeno & Locutus, poly count is critical in Freelancer, to many and you loose frames (fps) and servers suffer serious LAG. Try and keep ships to less than 10,000 polys for player only ships and 5,000 for NPC flown ships.

Most of the STAR TREK models you find on other sites are done for rendering purposes (pictures) or CGI animations and are highly detailed. This makes them useless for games without major reworking of the mesh.
