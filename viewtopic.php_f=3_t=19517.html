Sherlog

Tue Jun 17, 2003 1:13 am by Sherlog

There are no such restrictions in free play - just start FLServer for yourself (there is a shortcut in the in the Windows Start menu) or connect to one of the servers on the Internet.

In the campaign you have to do story missions in order to level up but you need level only for ships, shields and high-level ammo (missiles, mines). Two of the best ships in the game require only level 10 - Dromedary, Drake - and these ships are good enough to go all the way to level 38. There are no level restrictions on what you can loot and so you can get much better guns than you could buy, by shooting the right enemies or looting the right wrecks.

The guns of the outlaws tend to be a lot better than the stuff that you can buy in ordinary shops and so you can get really powerful guns a lot earlier than the game wants you to have them. The Xeno guns are particularly good - I beat the game with their class 3 Adv. Scorpion (they fall off Startrackers in Colorado) on a Patriot, and a Drake equipped with Tarantulas easily owns all of Sirius except for the homeworlds of the Corsairs and Outcasts.
