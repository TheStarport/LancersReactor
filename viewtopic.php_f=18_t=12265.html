Check your added entries and make sure that everything is defined, and that you haven't made a typo.

Shiparch.ini for your mk2 ship and goods.ini for the hull and package files

Anything else custom that you added (weapons or equipment.).

The tiniest typo can crash everything.
I know from experience when I accidently overwrote one single _ when defining something, so that I had something for sale that didn't have a corresponding definition.

This is one of the main reasons I only clone new ships, I leave the original stuff alone so I always have a working example to look back to.
