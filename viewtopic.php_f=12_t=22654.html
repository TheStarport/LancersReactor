The Force.net has a hilarious thread going on about connecting Star Wars Episode III to the Original Trilogy.

Topic: Misguided attempts to connect to the OT (humor)

It is very important for Episode III to provide a smooth transition from the PT to the OT. The visuals, the cinematography, the design... everything has to flow correctly.

But there are some things from the OT I just don't think we need to link back to.

• While Anakin gets cybernetic implants to become Vader, Yoda is digitally blasted apart and put back together with rubber to become a Muppet

• Clonetroopers convert to cheaper stormtrooper technology; Anakin turns to the dark side out of frustration of being made to shave the fins off 1,200,000 clonetrooper helmets

• Chewbacca given a midi-chlorian test, just for the hell of it

• As speculated by many, Obi-Wan takes his name from Ben Quardinaros the Podracer; their meeting takes a mere half-hour of plot time to arrange and is an utter delight to all six fans of Quardinaros

(Private Ackbar undergoes a Trap Classification and Detection training session)
• Fellow Private Tarkin cuts class that day, misses Danger Assessment Test. Instead, smokes cigarettes under the bleachers at Corsucant Stadium with cadet Needa.

• Yoda graduates from the Coruscant Culinary Institute with honors. Final Thesis: Making Tasty Stew With Ingredients From A Slimy Mudhole. "Crank it up a notch, you must."

• Anakin/Vader's wardrobe session with Coruscant clothier: "Sea Green? Too trendy. Mauve? Taupe? Tan? Too sandy. Ah hell, just make it black!"

• Obi wan will arrive with Luke to Tatooine. Once there he will realize his mistake:
"Oh sshi.. the girl, I left the girl!!!!.

• Jar jar: I´m your father
Chewie: Nooooooooooooo

• In order to resonate deeply with ROTJ, Palpatine will give Vader his new helmet and say, "See, it's a hat."

• Greedo will get into a speeder accident and will break his arm in the process, causing several seizures in his arm to occur whenever he holds a blaster. So that will explain why Greedo shoots first..and misaims really badly.

• Tarkin's 1 hour liposuction surgery scene deleted due to his ongoing malpractice suit. "I said take me down to 175 pounds, NOT 125!!! I look like freakin' Skeletor!"

The Clone Wars - I hope they show some of this. I'll be pretty disappointed if the movie starts with Anakin and Obi Wan down in the Jedi Temple's locker room saying, "boy that was a tough war, I'm glad we won."

Anakin and Padme have to make Luke and Leia, but Anakin can't know he has any kids because he doesn't learn about this until EP4. Maybe Anakin will learn she's expecting but he won't know she's having twins because he doesn't know Leia is his daughter until EP6.

Anakin must turn evil

Count Dooku goes away somehow - I'm hoping Anakin takes care of him. Remember how Luke beats Anakin and is about to kill him and the Emperor wants him to do it because it will push him over to the dark side. My instinct is that Anakin will get Dooku in the same situation and then he'll kill him and take his place as Palpatine's apprentice.

Anakin and Obi Wan must fight - the outcome will be Anakin falling into hot lava or something that makes it necesary for him to spend the rest of his life in an iron lung.

All the Jedi except for Ben and Yoda get killed - my instinct is that Palpatine will have some scheme to finish off a bunch of Jedi at the same time. It would be cool if we got to see Anakin clobber them all one at a time but that could hardly fit in the movie

So far the only hint about the movie I've heard is when Samuel L Jackson told Jay Leno he was filming his parts and that he didn't 'go out like a punk'. It would be cool if Han Solo and Chewbacca were in the film somewhere.

It would also be cool if there were a space battle where Porkins was heroic. He gets a bad rap because he dies so fast in EP4. It's not really his fault he was a victim of poor maintanence.
