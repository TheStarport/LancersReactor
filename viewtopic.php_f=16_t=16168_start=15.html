Sat May 10, 2003 6:38 pm by Airborne

I find a "bi-directional" trade route which gives you a good profit and you don't need to make friendly to any criminal.

Buy Gate/Lane Parts from Pueblo Station in Colorado to Tau-31 Gate Construction Site in Tau-31. Buy-in price is $140 and sell-out price is $1400, at a profit of $1260 per unit. On the return trip, buy Beryllium at $300 and sell them at $1020, at a profit of $720 per unit. Total profit is $1980 per unit. If you flying a Humpback, the total profit will be 495000!

The most improtant thing is... The trip between these two bases is really short! It's only required 4 system jumps between Colorado and Tau-31 if you find all the required jump holes. However, if you are hated by the Outcasts and don't want a hard fight, don't use the jump hole from Kyushu to Tau-31 because you will pass the Outcasts' Cali base in Tau-31. Jump through Tau-29 as the alternate route, and the Tau-31 GCS is just a few steps away from the Tau-31 -> Tau 29 Gate. So 1 more system jump doesn't matter at all.

I evaluated some of the round-trip trade lanes recommended on this forum

Here is the (interim) result, which I will update by editing this posting:

Note:
1. No offense meant to all who recommended their prefered trade lane - if I comment negatively on the route, it's just because of the measurement taken.
2. My only enemies are the Bounty Hunters, so if you have trouble with other factions, some of the trade lanes may not be feasible for you.
3. The measurement runs have been done in a Dromedary freighter (a big thank you to Gnavpot for pointing out the benefits of that ship to me !), but should be valid for most other vessels, too.
4. I prefer not to trade in Cardamine, therefore those routes are not included (yet). Still, it seems those are the most profitable short-range routes.

The statistics shown consist of:
- The trade route (self-explanatory),
- the profit per unit (based on a complete round-trip. Multiply this with the cargo capacity of your vessel, eg x250 for a Humpback, or x275 for a Dromedary)
- the tour time (full round trip, including trade lane disruption, queuing at gates, buying, selling, mapping, but not natural breaks for the player . These times may vary from mission to mission.)
- the profit per unit/ minute: This is the objective measure of a tour's quality

If I don't mention a destination's system, use F6 and the Base List to locate that system.

And here we go:

Tour 1:
[Recommended by Pinger, modified by Dunham
[Comment: Avoid, avoid, avoid

Buy engine components at Honshu
Sell at Mactan
Buy gold
Sell at Manhattan
Buy Luxury Consumer Goods
Sell at Honshu

Profit: 2340$ / unit
Tour time: 60 min
Profit/unit: 39$/min

Tour 2:
[Modified tour 1
[Comment: Barely better

Buy engine components at Honshu
Sell at Denver
Buy Luxury Consumer Goods
Sell at Honshu

Profit: 1432$ / unit
Tour time: 33 min
Profit/unit: 43$/min

Tour 3:
[My old favourite
[Comment: Not too bad

Buy Niobium at New Tokyo
Sell at The Ring (New Berlin system)
Shuttle empty to New Berlin
Buy Diamonds
Sell at New Tokyo

Profit: 2182$ / unit
Tour time: 24 min
Profit/unit: 92$/min

Tour 4:
[Recommended by Airborne
[Comment: A waste of time

There are two routes for this mission - one north via Kyushu (passing harmless minefield and using jumpholöe to Shikogu), one south via Leeds (using Magellan jump hole). Both take approximately the same time.

Buy Beryllium at Tau-31 Gate Construction Site (system Tau-23)
Sell at Pueblo Base (Colorado)
Buy Gate lane parts
Sell at Tau-31 Gate Construction Site

Profit: 1980$ / unit
Tour time: 46 min

Tour 5:
[Recommended by hairfarmer
[Comment: Pretty good, although illegal

Sell at Yanagi Depot (Sigma-13)
Buy Cardamine
Sell at New Berlin

Profit: 1138$ / unit
Tour time: 5 min
Profit/unit: 227$/min

Interim summary: You either trade in Cardamine or you go back to Crete, Malta, etc., and kill Bounty hunters for money. Trade doesn't pay (enogh).

Ok, this is a round trip so you can start it at any point closer to ypu, here is what I do:

1)Planet Manhattan, New York

2) Planet Baden Baden, Stuttgart
Sell: Luxury Consumer Goods - $1,260
Profit: $1,080 per unit
Buy: Water - $15

3) Solarius Station, Omega 11
Sell: Water - $195
Profit: $180 per unit
Buy: Diamonds - $219

4) Planet New Tokio, New Tokio
Sell: Diamonds - $1,650
Profit: $1,431 per unit
Buy: Luxury Consumer Goods - $810

5) Planet Honshu, Honshu
Sell: Luxury Consumer Goods - $990
Profit: $180 per unit
Buy: Engine Components - $150

6) Planet Cambridge, Cambridge
Sell: Engine Components - $1,275
Profit: $1,125 per unit
Buy: Luxury Food - $160

7) Planet Denver, Colorado
Sell: Luxury Food - $1,360
Profit: $1,200 per unit (you loose 12-16 units on the way, the food rotts and won't smell pretty in your ship )
Buy: H-Fuel - $269

Planet Manhatan, New York
Sell: H-Fuel - $360
Profit: $91 per unit

Total Profit (per complete round trip, disregarding the Luxury Food loss): $5,287

I run this cargo with the Hammerhead (cargo 70), it takes me 30-50 minutes, and I gain $350,000 per trip (including the Luxury Food loss)

*notes: if you are friendly to Hessians (wich I am not) you can buy the Diamonds $21 cheaper form Freital Base, Omega 11 or Ronnenburg Base, Omeg 5
if you are friendly to Hackers (also I am not) you can sell the Engine Components to a better price at Mactan Base, Magellan (+$149)
this route is not based on maximum/minimum prices, but on continuosly cargo run (no trip with ypur cargo hold empty).
it's also fun as you get to meet/team/fight almost every faction in the game on the way
