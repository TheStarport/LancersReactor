As you note, this has no limits on nanobats and bots, also has a vast 600 hold size, and also has a cockpit cam and turret cam - so its identical to the player one, except it can mount better stuff, and has no limits on things. Many ships in shiparch.ini are like this - they have no limits as players weren't supposed to fly them - ergo, it didn't need limits on it

You have to make sure you give them the correct one

Also - abotu being kicked, depends upon what you give them. Typically - if its equipemtn and stuff that cannot be bought normally, then they will be kicked, if its things that are mounted too high (ie class 10 guns on class 3 mount) then it will kick them. You cannot just do what you like with all the anti cheat stuff running
