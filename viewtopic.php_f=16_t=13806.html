Balefire

Sun Apr 13, 2003 8:45 am by Balefire

If you enjoy trading, you might see about getting your reputation to at least neutral with the Lane Hackers, and buy the Dromedary freighter from them. Not as well armored as the Humpback, but much more maneuverable (do a search for discussions of the ship), and with more cargo space. Then make a lot of money trading and doing a lot of exploring along the way, stopping at every base you can, to gather info and enhance your income possibilities. Keep an eye on each system's patrol routes to find hidden jump holes, bases, etc.

With enough money to buy the ship and gear you need, and to make any bribes you must, you can then try to adjust your rep with *both* corsairs and outcasts, and then, when you're tired of trading, you can try out all three of the VH fighters and see which you prefer...they each have pluses and minuses.

You might also consider trying your hand at mining while you have reasonable cargo space; the fighters have only enough for carrying the stuff you loot from destroyed enemy ships, IMO.

Once you switch to a fighter, you can continue exploring the more dangerous area, look for wrecks to loot, take lucrative missions, etc. It depends on what you find fun to do, of course. Almost nobody will hate you for slaughtering Xenos, slaying bounty hunters makes you popular with criminals but can make your life a little irritating...they're everyhere.

I'm actually playing two parrallel games, SP but post-story: one as a trader/explorer with the Dromedary, and one as a more...ah...martial freelancer with a VHF. I choose the game to load depending on my mood. <g>

Nightsilver

Mon Apr 14, 2003 3:32 am by Nightsilver

The Corsairs have 4 bases that I know of:

Cadiz Base, in Omega 5 (bottom left corner of D-5). This is the least advanced of the 4.
Leon Base, in Omega 41 (Top-center of D-6). This is about equal with Tripoli as far as technology.
Tripoli Shipyard, Omicron Gamma (Top-rightish D-7). About even with Leon for tech.
Planet Crete, Omicron Gamma (Bottom-Right E-5). Corsair Home planet - has most of the best purchasable equipment in the game. Anything these guys don't have can probably be bought at Tripoli or Leon.

To get to Omega 5, there are a few options.
In Omega 3, there's a jumphole at the intersection of D-7 and E-7.
In Cambridge, there's a hole at F-6.
In Omega 7, there's a hole at C-6.
In Omega 11, there's a hole at C-4 (This is right next to a Hessian base, so I discommend taking this one, since Hessians probably hate you if Corsairs like you).

To get to Omega 41, there's more options...
In Omega 5, jumphole at the intersection of D-6 and E-5.
In Omega 11, jumphole at bottom-left C-6.

To get to Omicron Gamma, there's only one option (coming from the Bretonia side, anyways...there is another way in, but you won't care).
In Omega 41, there's a jumphole at the top of E-5.

Good luck!
