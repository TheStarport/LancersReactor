Pirate Liberty, Open Roleplay

There were no ships for hundreds of klicks, at the very least. This was good; very good indeed. A Liberty patrol would be more than enough to cause the entire plan to fail, as this was the most vunerable part of the mission. A flight through open space, between two debris fields. The gap was hundreds, possibly thousands of kilometers wide. This meant that the sensory cover of the debris fields, which most pirates relied on, was unavailable. But Aserval was willing to take the risk.

The mule cruised through space. By now it was apparent that this trip from Alcatraz to Buffalo was going to take a long, long time. Aserval put the ship on autopilot and entered his cargo hold. He opened up one of the crates and took out a can, popping the top and inhaling the contents. Cardamine. It was in such high demand that even where you could get it cheap, it was still over one-thousand credits a box. Even so, there were places where it was in even higher demand, so it was more than worth it. Besides, it made people feel good to have some. Aserval enjoyed it himself, so he made it a note to have a sample of the cargo on every trip.

The proximity alert came off. Aserval swore and ran to the cockpit to see a patrol of the LPI. He broke cruise speed and prepared to engage.

Chocolate Stiletto

Wed Dec 20, 2006 7:58 pm by Chocolate Stiletto

Jimmy Ferguson desperately mashed at the controls, as his Stiletto was being ripped apart by enemy fire. He was a Lane Hacker, and his wing was destroyed by a Liberty Navy patrol. He had to act quickly and activate his cruise engines.

"It's over for you, Hacker! Time for you to die!" The militant launched a missile at Jimmy. Fortunately for him, the missle smashed against an asteroid.

Jimmy used his nanobots and shield batteries. He cut his engines, spun around, and used most of his ship's power to shoot his Hellflurry tachyon cannons. They destroyed the shield of a Defender and damaged its hull. A tachyon bolt hit the cockpit and disabled the ship. The other 3 Defenders flew around in circles, throwing plasma and laser particles at him.

One of the Navy ships ordered the rest of them to shoot their torpedoes at Jimmy. They launched. These weren't the ordinary torpedoes used on other ships- they moved very fast. They hit Jimmy's ship.

"Crap!" Jimmy's weapons system went offline. The bottom wing of his ship was blown off, but he was able to tractor in the weapons. He dropped a lot of countermeasures and pressed the "Activate Cruise Engines" button. His cruise engines began charging.

"Quick! Launch CD!", screamed a militant. One of the Defenders launched a cruise disruptor, but it hit a countermeasure. Jimmy escaped the fight.

"Oh, God damn it! He's escaping! Navy Command will be very upset about this.", said the leader.
