*Cut and Paste from other thread*

I've got a relatively decent, super short run.

Buy a humpback/dromedary.

Buy engine components in Freiburg Station, Stuttgart. ($375)
Travel 1 trade lane west to Stuttgart>Omega-7 Jumpgate
Jump to Omega-7
Travel 1 more tradelane west from the Omega-7>Stuttgart Jumpgate
Dock at Freistadt Station.
Sell the engine components. ($825)
Buy Silver.
Go back to Freiburg and sell the silver.
Rinse, and repeat.

$112,500 profit (PROFIT, clear in the hand) for a two tradelane, one jump trade route. I made a cool "gorilla gorilla's"(1,000,000) in less than 1/2 an hour.

Oh, beware of Red Hessians, and Corsairs. In MP, I found that if I couldn't get armed escorts (by that I mean other players online...) to come with me, the best way to survive trade lane disruptions was to perform a circle in front of the damaged tradelane with afterburners on until it was repaired, then re-dock. Dogfighting in a freighter ain't exactly my forte.
