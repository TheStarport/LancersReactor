FanFic: Life of a Corsair

Hey new to Lancers, and Fanfics, but hope you enjoy....

Chapter 1: Birth

Sitting at the bar on Planet Crete are two close friends Cadeus, a small dark haired intelliegent youngster, and Casseus a battle scarred pilot of the Corsairs.

"You have served the Corsairs for 13 years Casseus. And still all you do is smuggle artifacts from Crete to Manhatten!" said Cadeus

"What's your point?" said Casseus

"You've brought more money to the Corsairs then the Corsairs own family has!" exclaimed Cadeus

"Cad, it's my duty... I trade, I kill, I gain." Said Casseus calmly. "My family has served this House for years."

"Exactly! Don't you think you deserve a little.... recognition?" Cadeus lowered his voice for the last bit.

"Well...." An alarm sounds just as Casseus started his sentence. "Sounds like the Outcasts are back." Laughed Casseus.

"Crap I didn't even finish my drink!" Cadeus complains as they run out to the landing deck. Casseus boards his dented and marred Titan fires up the engine and sets off. "When are you getting a better ship Cass?" said Cadeus with a smirk

"This ole' gal can still fight with the best of em! Too bad the same can't be said for your Sabre! Haha" laughed Casseus. The two friends among many other battle ready Corsairs flew straight into the Outcast cloud.
