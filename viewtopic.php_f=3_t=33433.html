Submit your Fan Fiction to TLR

Hi everyone,

Just wanted to let you all know there is a newly created Fan Fiction Forum for you to read excellent FL stories, comment on and to add some yourself of course.
There is also the recently added Fan Fiction page on TLR. I already have a few stories on there (some are from the TLR archives) and I have about 10 to 15 more to put up there, so expect to read some more good stories soon.

If you want to submit Fan Fiction to TLR, please do so and I'll see if I can put them up there on the Fan Fic page. But keep in mind I do screen all the submitted stories for suitable content and based on that I make a decision, so not all stories might make it to the page. You'll be notified by e-mail about the definitive decision.

FL fan fiction can be send to me at eraser@lancersreactor.com

Happy writing!
