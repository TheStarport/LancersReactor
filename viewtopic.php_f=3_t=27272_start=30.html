I think some more non-linear action would be good. Those who own Deus Ex/Deus Ex 2 know what I mean. An example from Deus Ex:

(747 - Orders: Kill Lebedev)

Kill Lebedev, Leave Agent Nevarre : Manderly's happy when you get back to HQ, Your're are given all of your op bonus (money).

Kill Agent Nevarre/ Leave Lebedev : Manderly's peeved when you get back, minimum op-bonus, get more ammo at the armoury.

Kill both : Manderly is seriously peeved with you, no op bonus. No new weapons

Kill neither :Manderly's still peeved, but you get free medkits, new weapons, ammo, and armour at the armoury for "humane reaction"

I think this could be incorperated into Freelancer's storyline, with Juni giving you a *bonus* for destroying ships you only have to. Maybe the storyline could have secondary objectives ( For example, get to the prision in Alaska, but disable Weapon Posts guarding the Alaska jump gate). And yes I know this isn't for Freelancer 2, but if the original changed enough, it could feel like a sequal.
