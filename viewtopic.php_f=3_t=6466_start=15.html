I'll stick to what i think can be done, perhaps even for FL1 either by the community or by DA in a patch/add-on...

1) More mission types!!!! Can't stress that enough. Here's a couple of ideas
- Escort
- Cargo Delivery (requiring you to fly a freighter)
- Courier services... Get from point A to B at the least amount of time, without being blasted out of the sky. Requiring you fly something small and manuverable.
- Recon, go in fast and silent and come out with the goods on the system or pirate base or what have you.

2) Hiring wingman or wingmen
3) Hiring a freighter while you escort it (like FL2 had)
4) The ability to specify user made missions in MP. Think this was mentioned here a couple of times.

Some other things it would be nice to see but IMO are unlikely:

Forget about dynamic pricing for commodities...how bout a demand drivven economy? But i think that in itself would require a couple of years of coding.

User added systems. I know there are people out there with the know how and means to texture and create new systems.

A more modable enviorment. Mods add ALOT to the logetivity of games. Sometimes outlasting the original. Even small things like custom paint jobs and decals add alot.

Don't know if this has ever been mentioned...ATC? (Air Traffic Controler)
I think that near stations and the final dicking rings near planets someone has to be monitoring traffic. So an ATC channel would be kewl.


I've seen things you people wouldn't believe. Attack ships on fire off the shoulder of Orion. I watched C-beams glitter in the darkness at Tan Hauser Gate. All those moments will be lost in time like tears in rain. Time to die.
