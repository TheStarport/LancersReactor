One of the best times to fly MP is on saturdays generally between 7pm and 11pm GMT. This is the time of the BIG MP as it is known, which includes races and PvP competitions.

Evochron does have a small but dedicated community, and there are regular fliers generally during the times above throughout the week. Anyone can host a game, but VerbsEAserver (the first 24/7 dedicated server) is usually the place to fly. One of the best ways to ensure that others are flying is to post in the MP announcements thread to the evochron forum (www.starwraith.com/forum), and (ICQ is generally also used to arrange games with other pilots.

It's hoped that TLR will help in MP becoming the fantastic MP experience it has the potential for, we look forward to seeing you fly

There is also a MP league which I have started which can be found at www.beautifulhandmadethings.co.uk/sw3d , which includes a pilot league a Clan league and a who's flying now feature.

These can be incorporated into another site and it's also possible to have data inputed from other servers.....
