Welcome!

Keep in mind the SDK is much much more than a base for modding. It is a prerequisite for most large or TC mods these days, such as Rebalance, Evolutions, etc. Its a good thing to have installed even if you aren't running a modded game, as it repairs several glitches in the original FL design, and results in more stable, enjoyable gameplay.
