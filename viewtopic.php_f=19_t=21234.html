First a lil about NEO:
NEO is a (somewhat) small clan, we have roughly 15 members. Though all of our members are pretty far above average when it comes to PvP skill, our main focus is on trust, and preventing any sort of disunity within the clan. That is to say, if someone is god at PvP, they won't get in because of that alone. Only after showing that they are capable of being trusted, getting along with all of our current members, and are able to work as a group will someone be considered.

Now on to the ugly:
NEO is a PK clan. People don't like us, and I don't blame them for it. We more often than not kill about every person we meet. We "request" small (200k-300k) tolls from people passing through certain systems while we are online. We are also hostile with about every clan on the Elite server. The reports that we dockcamp people nonestop are highly exagerated. The reports that our members cheat are nothing but lies.

Now on to the not so ugly:
Despite all the asinine things we do, we are not totally evil (Not all of us, anyway ). NEO members actively attack known cheaters, gather evidence of their activities and lobby for them to be banned. People who are on good standing with us are also protected from hostiles, and are given aid when they need it.

You can find the (work in progress) NEO site at http://neo.thenewfrontier.net/ . Feel free to visit our forums to get an idea of how we act n' stuff.
