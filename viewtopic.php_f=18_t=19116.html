hans olo

Wed Jun 11, 2003 10:39 pm by hans olo

Open ..\\DATA\FX\jumpeffect.ini

and scroll to

[JumpGateEffect
nickname = jump_effect_bretonia

There you see:

jump_out_time = 1.500000
jump_out_tunnel_time = 7
jump_in_tunnel_time = 3
jump_in_time = 1

which you can al set to a lower value.
