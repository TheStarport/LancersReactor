Boscoe

It's pretty much dependant on your reputation, and which system you're in. You may find yourself allied with the Corsairs, who hate the Red Hessions. In some systems, you'll get Hessions firing at you almost the whole time. In others, you won't see any and you'll go about your merry way. I've found that handling one's reputation is an integral part of exploration, and adds to the fun of the game.
