Fly to a no-pvp zone and trade, switch user, accept, switch user, accept. done.

2 things...

First of all.. why jettison your crap into space? Why not just park both characters 20k above a planet.. then...

Initiate Trade request.

Windows Key - Windows Key - L - S - Select other login - Alt-tab
(this sequence takes me about 1.5 seconds)

Accept Trade Request.

Now you can swap your goods and money however you like without danger.

Now secondly. This is certainly meta-gaming... but it is not cheating unless the server forbids multiple accounts such as Excelsia does. Being able to manage your guns and money between characters without bugging other players with it is absolutely invaluable. On Excelsia I am unable to do this, and I can't believe what a major hassle it is to get my guns and goods swapped onto the right ships.

It is only cheating if it gives you an advantage over other players. As even a player with a single login can manage to swap stuff by themselves, then I don't see the major advantage. True it is nicer this way, but so long as the second login is just used as a bank then there is no unfair advantage. Anyone who says otherwise needs to unclench their ass cheeks a bit before their heads explode.
