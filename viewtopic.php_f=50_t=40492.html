Well, maybe i SHOULD have included a help file >< ok, turn off all mods, and click the export to new button. Write freelancer - vanilla or something. Now activate a mod, and run freelancer. Once it gets the the Main Menu you can quit. Now click Export to new and type a name, most logical being the name of the mod. Now you will have 2 folders selectable from the drop down list. Freelancer - Vanilla and your other mod. Just choose one and click on it. Do this for all the mods that you will be using, just turn it on, play, and then export to new. Sorry if its not the most efficient of programs, but i made it in 4 hours.
