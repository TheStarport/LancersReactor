PCHolt

Wed Feb 26, 2003 6:03 pm by PCHolt

People are failing to remeber that the Liberty Rogues are but one group of Pirates in Freelancer. In the full version, you can travel to other systems where you are not known, and work on building your reputation with the pirate factions. Do some smuggeling, do a few missions for some of the pirates allies, or spend the big bucks and up your reputation with bribes. Eventually to pirates hatered toward you will decrease, but then local police, and other groups will begin to hate you.

Being a pirate will obviously mean limiting the number bases you can visit, and places you can travel safely.
