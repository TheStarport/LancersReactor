I have broadband, I have my own server, my own modem, no networked PC's, latest update for FL, and a virus checker and a firewall.

the latest update for FL may be the culprit , it is mainly a server patch and not a client patch , you are better off removing it (which basically means re-installing it from scratch)

Firewall , does it allow access for FL ? simplest way to check is to turn it off , see if you connect , if you do , it is the fire wall , disconnect , configure it and start it up again

Virus checker has no impact unless you run a lowend system then you are better off turning it of while you are playing to reduce overhead on your system

Also make sure you have the latest drivers for your video card , sound card and install Directx 9.0b (mentioned somewhere already)
