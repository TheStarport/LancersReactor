hp_type = hp_gun_special_6 (this is the weapon class), HpWeapon01, HpWeapon02, HpWeapon03 (these are the hardpoints capable of fitting that class)

hp_type = hp_gun_special_3, HpWeapon01 (=hp1 can use class3 gun)
hp_type = hp_gun_special_2, HpWeapon01 (=hp1 can use class2 gun)
hp_type = hp_gun_special_1, HpWeapon01 (=hp1 can use class1 gun)
hope this solves your edit-nooby-questions :p
