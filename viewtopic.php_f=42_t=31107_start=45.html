Hi all. Couple things I've noticed playing the mod.
1) Picked up the Gladius. Looks good, but the starboard (right) wingtip laser is invisible from the top hemisphere of the craft. Once you move the camera to the ventral side the laser appears. No effect to the game itself, just a minor eyesore.

2) I think this might be a potentially problematic exploit. The ships with triple after burn have an enormous advantage on smuggling and escape. If the pilot engages afterburn, then toggles auto-slide (default z key) they can maintain close to max velocity with only minimal bursts from the after burner, due to a fairly slow velocity bleed. If you get comfortable enough with zipping around at 800+ kps (super suicide afterburners installed on a gladius), you can actually control the ship well enough with controlled bursts to zip through the densest asteroid fields. I wouldn't try that without level 7 shields though . I think the leech missile may have just become obsolete. Also handy in big number furballs. Maybe a faster deceleration in autoslide could balance it out, I don't know.
