Walks with the Snails

Wed Apr 02, 2003 4:35 am by Walks with the Snails

I've found a really easy way to make money is to rotate between the capitals. Get Luxury Consumer Goods at Manhattan and sell them in New Berlin. Buy Diamonds there and sell them in New Tokyo. Buy Engine Parts there and sell them in New London. Then buy Luxury Foods there and sell them in Manhattan. Just repeat the process and you'll be rolling in cash. The other nice thing is, the routes tend to be well-patrolled, so it's fairly low risk. It's all legal, too, but you'll get around $1000 per good per route, except for the Manhattan-New Berlin one. The most problem I have is with Outcasts on the New Tokyo-New London route, but if you're neutral with them it's a breeze.
