Crash-Drive

Thu Apr 03, 2003 11:39 pm by Crash-Drive

Once I can put it all together, I always use:

2x Salamanca Mk II (or 2x Tizona del Cid)
4x Nomad Blasters (or 4x Wild Fire if I feel like going non-Nomad)
Salamanca Turret
Hornet Cruise Disruptor
Ripper Mine (very expensive ammo, use wisely)

Adv. Brigandine Shield
Adv. Thruster
Adv. Countermeasures

In the SP game, it's likely you'll already have the best thruster and c/m dropper after completing the story... no reason not to. In a PvP multiplayer game, I swap the Salamanca guns for Tizona del Cid guns (shield shredders). I like the combo of Salamanca (or Tizona) with Nomad Blasters for matched projectile speed and refire rate... although the matched refire rate is mostly an aesthetic preference. I gave up on using missiles... maybe I just don't understand the right way to use them, but they were mostly just money down the drain for me. Same with torpedoes, although I did use starkillers to help take out shield generators in the SP game as quickly as possible, but I abandoned them afterward.

I used a Titan for a long time, but I got to where I could handle 5-6 waves of Nomads in the unknown systems before having to leave to replenish bots & bats. Then I switched to an Eagle, and the Nomads became so much easier. Using your thruster in pulses and chasing them with erratic weaving (the Eagle excels at this) really throws off the aim of the ones you aren't locked onto. Yes, I was bored that day.

Heh, once you know basically where the wrecks are with the good stuff, it's fun to do a "surgical strike" into hostile areas to get in, grab the stuff, and get the heck out, before you're really "ready" for those kinda places.
