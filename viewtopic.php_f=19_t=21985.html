Yes, you have to start a new character on each server you play on unless you can convince the server admin to accept your user file from another server. I understand that that is *very* rarely done though (correct me if I'm wrong guys).

The good news is that starting out fresh can be fun. It really grew on me after a few times. I'd rather work my way up from the bottom on my own than accept a million credits from someone for nothing. Just me though. Enjoy it!

I play on 24/7 Seattle Based which seems to be a very stable and lag-free server. Just be sure to read the rules carefully before starting a character paying close attention to the nickname rules (no # characters allowed). And most of all, don't forget to turn off *all* mods before you enter the server. Being caught with a mod on even accidentally is a no-no that can and will get you banned.

See you online. Have fun!
