Bob McDob

Sun Mar 30, 2003 3:43 am by Bob McDob

Hmm ... well, if you people really want to do this, it begs the question - how much do you want to do?

Changing the ship stats is simple enough, and we're making good progress with cracking the model format. The problem here then is, do we want to replace ships or clone them? Do we do only the fighters, or the capships and transports as well, which will mean roughly 60% more work?

How many games do we want to cover? Privateer is simple, only four ships ... WCI-III is a lot harder ... then there's every WC game including Privateer II, which I am in no way touching.

What kind of stats should the ships have? (FL's balancing terrifies me. NEVER AGAIN)

Do we want to change the music? (Simple enough) ...

What about editing factions to make them resemble WC ones more? (Since Wing Commander only has two main factions, this should be relatively simple, as long as we're focusing on changing the names and maybe relations, nothing more)

Change/add commodoties? (Not sure how hard this is ...)

Change/add systems? (*NO*)

Change/add weapons? (I really don't see the point of it)

That's basically my take on it. For the moment there's not much we can do, except maybe tweak the music and fiddle around with editing to get a feel for it.
