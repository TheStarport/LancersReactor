^^^What he said^^^

By far the best places are the two home systems of the Corsairs and Outcasts. I've received more bounty in those two places than any other.

Another thing to do though for quite a lot of cash, though, is to join up patrols with fellow Bounty Hunters in the Omega-11 system. In my AP-class Hammerhead, I've scored millions from the various level-7 weapons the enemies leave when they die an explosive death -- and as they mostly fly Stilletos (Red Hessians), they're pretty easy to deal with even with overwhelming odds. Hence, Omega-11 system specifically. And they drop enough Nanobots for you to stay out in the radiation for quite a while. So long as you stick near enough to Solarius Station to get repairs to your subsystems periodically, you should be fine.
In MP, it's very lucrative to take bounties from other players, BTW.
