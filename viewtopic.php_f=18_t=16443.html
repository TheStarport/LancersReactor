If your playing multiplayer, heres the reason.

Client tells to server it activates tractor beam. Client displays graphic of tractorbeam picking things up. Server adds the item to clients inventory. If clients inventory is full, nothing happens. So your client thinks that your ship stores more than the server thinks it stores. The result, and Endless tractor beam from your ship to the loot until you go out of range (but only you can see it).
