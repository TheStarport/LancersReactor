Yogimus

Sun Jun 01, 2003 12:39 am by Yogimus

Do the new ships overwrite old ships, and if so, or even if not, is it possible to install the mod without the new ships? Also, do NPCs get the new ships as well?

------------ The ships do NOT over write existing ones, they apperar in the shopsjust like the others. (sometimes they take the place of another ship, but you can buy that other ship at another base) NPC-s DO get new ships. And they KNOW how to use them.-----------------

Does the mod cause problems with saved games and such, would I have to restart the SP? Also, does it cause problems with MP?

-----------------Yes, restarting the single player campaign is HIGHLY recommended. First, there is a lot of old savegame related bugs, second, it's like playing a whooole different game.------------------------

Do ships go at differant speeds now even without engine upgrades?"

---------Yes. And they have UNIQUE handling characteristics as well. It will actually MATTER what ship you have in your crosshairs, because you will need to develop new strategies to combat them.--------------

Don't forget, you can also change the difficulty with included .bat files. If a particular mission is too hard now (escape from Manhattan comes to mind), switch to lvl. 1 difficulty temporarily, and ratchet it up again afterwards. Better loot comes with increased difficulty, you can only loot weapons and cargo at lvl.1, but you can loot shields/engines/scanners at lvl. 2.
