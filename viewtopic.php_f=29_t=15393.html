Sorry Keith, just got used to people point to tutorials that dont cover the required information. Getting a bit sick of it to be honest. And yes, i was thinking about writing my own tutorial on the subject but first i want to get version 2 of my own mod out of the door.

docking:

Docks usually contain those scripts. See the solar folder for the stations inis. You dont need to go into the other folders, the one you want is directly in solar, i just forget its name. Its one of 3 in there.

Ship docking usually works without the need to tell your ships how to dock other than the can use berth/moors commands. So it could be an error in your shiparch.ini.

Eg the "can use" lines are missing or wrong.
Do a search for Berths in the editing forum and you will uncover lots of info about this.
