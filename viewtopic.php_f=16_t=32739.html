Leveling up in SP depends on two factors: Success with the storyline missions, and ones net worth. For example, you need to get $5,000 to advance to the next level. So you do a trade run and you net $30,000. Not only will you level up, but if you made enough money to meet the next level requirement, you may move up multiple levels. I have noticed this several times when I have played through the single player game.
