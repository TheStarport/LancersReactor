Provided that you have the Upgrade 2.1 installed/activated :p, Troy is accessible from Califonia, Texas, and the Minbari Empire systems.
In TX, go to the Bering JG @ bottom right of NavMap (at Houston, go on the right 'leg' of the Y formed by the diverging Tradelanes), and there'll be a JG to Troy at that end.
In CA, look about 8 to 10 km "below" (or south on NavMap) from CA Minor and Willard. It's a little below the system's eliptic plane, too - not exactly 'flat' or level with the rest of hte system.
And in Minbari, go pretty much straight "up" from the Planet in there. I mean UP this time, not north on NavMap, but literally above the planet. I can't remember how far exactly it is, but it's a good 15 to 30 km, i think.
