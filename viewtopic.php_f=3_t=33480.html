Prologue

The year was 313 AS. Kasuri and Liberty who had been good allies since arriving in Sirius began to have minor disputes in Kepler and Galileo. Kasuri had blamed all of the recent convoy attacks on Liberty's inability to control the Rouge problem, Kasuri on the other hand, only had 1 Major criminal organization, the Golden Crysathiums (sorry for my spelling, you know who I'm talking about.) The Blood Dragons had not been formed yet, and the Lane Hackers were still on the side of Kasuri. Nearly 25 convoys were destroyed every 3 hours passing through the Galileo system, the Liberty Rouge problem was out of control. But it was not necessarily Liberty's inability to get the rouges under control, it was just laziness. Liberty's precedent Georgia Brush had been a corrupted drunk all of her life, even as a kid. LNC (Liberty Navy Cruiser) Lake Superior neared planet New Tokyo, caring Georgia Brush to attend a meeting with the leader of Kasuri escorted by several battleships. But there was a GC ambush, and 3 of the battleships had been infiltrated by GC undercover agents. They began to fire the front cannons at the Lake Superior, destroying it in a matter of seconds. The Precedent of Liberty was now dead. Liberty blamed Kasuri for assassinating the precedent, but Kasuri denied it. With almost one hundred battleships, and two hundred cruisers, Liberty assaulted planet New Tokyo, killing the Emperor. Kasuri declared War on Liberty, while a new criminal organization, known only as The Resistance, somehow assassinated all of the leaders, and the mayors of all the Independent Worlds. Rienland blamed the Independent worlds, Britonia blamed Rienland, and the Independent Worlds blamed Liberty and Kasuri. After some minor setups by The Resistance, all of Sirius was now at war.

Now is when you can post your parts, please remember to follow the rules. And comments on the stories are welcome. But only on the stories, nothing like "YOU SUCK," and "YOUR STORY SUCKS" Keep the commets clean.
