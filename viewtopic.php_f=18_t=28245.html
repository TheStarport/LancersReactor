The IONCROSS Freelancer Character Editor will let you edit:
ship - including changing the ship...
power plant
engine
shield
forward hard points - what weapons you have mounted...
turrets
equipment
lights
cargo
location
factions
AND money
**** EDIT CAREFULLY AND BACK UP YOUR FILES! ****

Also capable of editing:
ship
power plant
engine

forward hard points
turrets
equipment

location
factions
AND money
**** EDIT CAREFULLY AND BACK UP YOUR FILES! ****

bakedpotato

Thu Feb 12, 2004 7:12 pm by bakedpotato

You can still buy that custom ship and although without adding the Game Data files from the Mod to the IONCROSS folder you can still edit the equipment on that custom ship like the money, guns, shield, thruster, engine, power plant , cargo, running lights, location, factions reputation and what not but the custom ship itself will not show up in the ship option box. Using the IONCROSS Freelancer Character Editor mk.V by JoeBoomz for Singleplayer saved game files ((non autosave only)) and the IONCROSS Freelancer Server Operator mk.III.1 by JoeBoomz for Multiplayer saved games.
The IONCROSS programs are so good that they find, load and parse the saved games for you so you don't have to search for them...Nice Touch Joe...
Just click on the save game name in Singleplayer and go to town and click on the character name for your Multiplayer saved game.
