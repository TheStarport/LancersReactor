I'm going to muddy the waters even more on this one...

Even assuming you take turns making one direct shot at each other, the existence of the shields prevents predicting who would "win": shield regeneration will totally skew the punches traded.

If you're talking about shots against bare armor, 1 for 1, on ships with equal armor, then it's a matter of multiplying SINGLE SHOT damage by number of guns. Your loadout was weaker than his if you used energy blasters; if they were nomad cannon, yours was stronger. Ignoring the minor armor damage done by a Tiz, the armor hits would break down like this:

5 Kraken 2's => 5*586.8 = 2934 per volley
4 nomad blasters => 4*635 = 2540/volley
4 nomad cannon => 4*847.6 =3390.4
3 blasters, 1 cannon => 3*635 + 847.6 = 2752.6

So if you had 3 blasters and 1 cannon or 4 blasters, your volleys were weaker than his.

If you normally get involved in 1-hit tradeoffs like that, though, go for a "no shields" deal and switch to high shot energy guns for the competition. If you have 4 class 10 slots and 2 max class 9 slots, try 4 Cerberus/Diamondback guns (1173 dmg/shot) and 2 Firekuss MkIIs (978 dmg/shot). At 6650.4 dmg per volley, you'll be in the lead fast.
