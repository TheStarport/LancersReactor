Wrecks with loot show up on your navmap as a red X and have a short discription on what they were carrying and how they got there. They can also be detected by your onboard scanner, click on the "Everything" icon in your scanerhud. Its the most right of the five (Important, Ships, fixed objects, Loot, Everything)
The ones you mean are probably the heavily damaged storage depots, usually in astroidfields, you can't destroy them.
You can however, distroy the ones that are not damaged. They also show up as loot in your scannerhud, but those are usualy near stations, thus pissing off the various factions when you try to shoot them.
Also abanded stations like Ichata Researchstation are indistructable and not realy interresting.

The spoilerforum has some threads about salvegable wrecks.
