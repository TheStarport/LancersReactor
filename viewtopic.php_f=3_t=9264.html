Never get your ship blown up at a gate again!

I found a really good way to make sure you never get shot all up while trying to use a jump gate.

Target the gate right in the middle, but dont use the dock. Wait until you are almost flying through the middle of the gate, then dock. This way you can keep your cruise on all the way to the gate. You can do the same for gates that lead to other systems. Cruise right up to the point where it opens, and then dock, it should almost immediately go to a cut screen. The same also applies to jumpholes, you get really close before docking. With a little work you can do the same at stations, but you have to know which bay it is that opens.

Here's my strategy:

(1) When in range, hit dock so your ship aligns with the correct ring.

(2) Then hit 'free flight' (not mouse flight) and cruise/burn to the center of the ring.

(3) Hit dock, and you should do it almost immediately.

With jump gates, you can sometimes get around the queue by forming with a ship that's already going through. Doesn't work all the time, though, and can cause you to take even more time to realign yourself.
