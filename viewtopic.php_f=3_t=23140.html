Improving a rep and not making enemies is a delicate thing. Your best bet is to first have a detailed look at who is enemies with the faction you're trying to get in with....then examine their allies/enemies list....try and find an enemy that has no allies, there are quite a few out there, so by blowing the crap out of them you can only affect one reputation.

Its a delicate thing, I'm not entirely sure whether its possible to be entirely neutral with everyone, but no doubt someone will prove me wrong. Thats the beauty of the game, its all trial and error.

The easy alternative is to swing from good to evil whenever it takes your fancy....you only live once right?!
