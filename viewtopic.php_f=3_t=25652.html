Freelancer isn't dead, but it's downside is the lack of an instant action mode in multiplayer. There is no hop in and start shooting here.

It takes hours to work your characters up to the point where they are more than just potential victims, the first 4 or 5 times doing this is interesting, there is a lot to explore in the Freelancer galaxy, but after repeating this theme for the 10th, 15th, or 20th time (a process that must be repeated on each new server), it becomes downright monotonous.

I've often suggested for those wishing to start a server for Freelancer veterans, to set the starting credits to 1or 2 million, so players can go immediately for a decent ship and get down to the business of shooting each other, which is what multiplayer is all about.
