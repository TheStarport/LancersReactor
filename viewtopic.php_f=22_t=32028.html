Global Player Stats - Remove Rank Sort?

Folks - as many of you know, my Global Player Stats * lists the top 1,000 players sorted by both Kills and by Rank. I am seriously considering removing the Rank sort as it has become pretty meaningless. Some servers give you lots of cash when you join, others have "admin" characters with high ranks, and some have mods that change the value of items. Therefore it is not fair to compare the ranks of players on one server against those on another. To get on the top 1000 by Rank, you've got to be at least rank 65, and that's after most cheats are crudely filtered out.

I feel that listing the players sorted by their Kills is a much better indicator of how good they actually are, and players with low ranks can do just as well as those with high ranks. By removing the sort by Rank, then the Global Stats will be simpler and more efficient.

Please let me know your thoughts on this...

Cryo.
