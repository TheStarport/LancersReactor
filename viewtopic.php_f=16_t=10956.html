Sorry to disappoint you, but there are no lvl 10 freighters. The highest one is lvl 7, which is the Rheinland Humpback (holds 250 cargo space). It's a lvl 7 freighter that uses lvl 10 equipment. The Dromedary is sold at the Mactan Base in Magellan, and it'll only set you back 54Gs.
