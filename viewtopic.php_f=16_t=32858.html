Darkov

Sat Jul 10, 2004 6:36 pm by Darkov

This may be the one you are talking about. I use it and it's quite...rewarding. I got it off the Mercenary Navy Freelancer clan website, thanks guys. This is a direct copy and paste from their site. Just thought i'd point it out to you.

Fly to the Rheinland nation and to the New Berlin system. When you get there, land on planet New Berlin and purchase the Humpback freighter. This shoud set you back around 170,000 credits. Now that you have a great freighter, you need a great route to run it on:

Buy Diamonds At New Berlin>>>>>Sell At Planet New Tokyo>>>>>Sell Niobium At The Ring

This is the most profitable route in the game that has been discovered to date. Although it can be dangerous and diamonds are an attractive targets to the pirates of sigma 13 this will earn you more money than you can spend. (get your corsair rep up in sigma 13 - you'll see why later!)

If you run this route properly you should make around 800,000 credits per journey. Each journey should take no more that 10 - 15 minutes. Keep trading like this until you have the money you need to get yourself a very heavy fighter. Realistically you need around 5-10 million credits if you dont want to trade ever again.

The Mercenary Navy seem pretty smart. Although i've never met one of their members, i owe them a lot of thanks. This set me up to be a made man in multiplayer. Cheers guys!

1. The route below is definitely on the longer side ... and also exposes you to nearly every pirate faction in the sector. If you can make the journey, however, you'll find the price to be well worth it.

Planet New Berlin: Engine Components [225c -> Freital Base [1.050c
Freital Base: Diamonds [198c -> Planet New Tokyo [1.650c
Planet New Tokyo: Luxury Consumer Goods [810c -> Planet Honshu [990c
Planet Honshu: Engine Components [150c -> Arranmore Base [1.500c
Arranmore Base: Gold [271c -> Planet New Berlin [1.189c

Total Profit: 1.340.625c

2. Here's the other route that I picked up on. It's also on the longer side ... and will cost some money to make repairs from radiation damage in Omega-11.

Solarius Station: Diamonds [219c -> Planet New Tokyo [1.650c
Planet New Tokyo: Niobium [512c -> The Ring [1.440c
Planet New Berlin: Mining Machinery [120c -> Solarius Station [499c

Like someone else mentioned in this thread, you can also buy diamonds at New Berlin. If you download the mod for Soulforge's Train (modified by Chips), you'll definitely find the cargo space allows for a sizeable profit in no time at all.

But as a friend of mine once wrote: "But beware of the Corsairs and Outcasts [in Sigma-13, they are merciless and efficient killers ... The interference is high, so don't on seeing the next Ring on your contact list."
