|Shodan|

Wed Jul 23, 2003 6:15 am by |Shodan|

Fix your rep with the Corsairs. Goto Omicron Theta, dock at Freeport 9 and then talk to the bartender. He offer rep fixes for the Corsairs and others. If you don't get the Corsair bribe, launch back to space and dock again. The repfix is random. If the reputattion-bar is completely red you could have to pay upto 150K to fix your rep with the Corsair.

When you've fixed your rep, goto Omicron Gamma - Tripoly Base and buy the Del Cids at 120K each.
