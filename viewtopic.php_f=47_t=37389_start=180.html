Codename

Tue Jul 19, 2005 11:24 am by Codename

I'm not going to say that it's too dramatic (though I think it's the most dramatic development in the game to date - at least, in terms of the amount of wholesale destruction), but there is a problem. The destruction of Dublin conflicts with the BAF mission involving Nick Warren, DSQ's new character. The trap that his forces were going to spring was meant to catch - if I understand correctly - Mollies pursuing some transports coming from Dublin. If I were you I'd get in touch with DSQ and, at the very least, work out the timeline of events so there's no confusion.

As far as the actual destruction goes, well, I do have some questions. First, who did it? Second, why? Third, how? Fourth, where did they go afterwards (and how)? The characters don't need to know any of this but, for a development of this magnitude, it really would be nice of you if you'd let your fellow authors/players in on your plans for this whole thing.

The biggest concern I have is that if there's a faction able to wield enough destructive force to swiftly and secretly destroy an entire system, then the Sirians are up against a foe they likely cannot defeat. And the game will suck majorly if everyone dies.

Let us know where you plan on taking this and we'll see what everyone has to say.
