Black_Eagle...

I encountered that same problem a long time ago... and guess what... when you want your turret rotate 360 degrees you just add the value of 6.283185 from your min and max... Now if you want to limit the rotation of your turret like 5 degrees to right and 210 degrees to the left, this will be an issue to fl and your 210 degrees to the left will work up to 180 degrees only. Tried many methods and fl will still give 180 degrees rotation.

Still a thought... i tried to limit the y axis (gun up/down) and this too cannot be limited. i mean what ever your value is your gun can still point up 45 degrees...
