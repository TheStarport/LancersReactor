Lev Arris

Sun Feb 23, 2003 5:26 pm by Lev Arris

Cruise (outside the tradelane of course) to a ring that is far away from a base (15k should be sufficient). FIre a couple of shots at the desired tradelane ring. They have a blue shield indicator, if it's down to zero the ring will flash for second which means that it is disabled. Now you have to wait a couple of seconds until someone drops out of the lane. If you are lucky it will be a transporter or freighter with a weak escort. If you are unlucky however, it may be a very pissed patrol of Libery Police or Navy fighters. One more thing: when you get to attack a freighter/transport destroy it as fast as possible, grab the cargo and get the hell outta there! It doesn't take long until the police or at least some more tradelane users will arrive... and after a tradelane attack they ALL will hate you and try to kill you. So keep your cruise drive ready.
