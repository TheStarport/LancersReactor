A great trade for newbies in Bretonia

If you are like me, lvl 8-9 and with only mission 3 completed which brought you to Bretonia, you will appreciate how hard it is to start here and figure out how to make some quick cash.
I bought a freighter figuring i will spend quite a while achieving next cut off point for mission 4 and i will probably have lots of time to explore and trade to achieve that mark of 56000 worth.
After many poor earnings and long unprofitable trips i discovered that you can pick up Niobium at 320/unit and get twice as much for it on Stokes station. It is a short trip, low risk and perhaps only some Gaians to bother you in Tau 31 system.
Beside that, i found some planetform depots among the ice debris in Tau 31 but i haven't had the time to blow it up and see what i can pick up.

Hope some of you that struggle with money and are at a low level, can put this info to good use.

BTW i had 14 thou worth of Niobium in my Clydesdale cargohold, and sold it for a cool 28 thou on Stokes (just so you know the details of how much i actually earned )...I'm so proud of my self he he he
