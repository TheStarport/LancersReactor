Just for a bit more information on the million credit mission:
The terminators come at you in waves of 12-15 ships
There are 2-4 waves
The only backup you will ever receive is what you bring with you.
All of the Terminators fly Eagles with 6 Krakken II's, hornets, rippers, and a lvl 10 gravitron shield.
Thier shooting ability has been made to be as accurate as the game allows as has thier flying, attacking, evading tatics.
They each carry 30 bots and 30 bats and will usually use them faster than any human can
This was designed so that 3-4 grouped, advanced players (with missles) would be able to complete the mission although frequently 2 will die and have to respawn and rejoin the fight.

We have also made missions available against the singleplayer nomads with occasional backup from the Order. These missions can be taken from Planet Toledo in Omicron Minor and are geared for the level 5-7 (max shield mount) ships.

Keep a lookout for a short move that will highlight the Terminators verocity and other Boston Supermod points of interest coming to the front page of TLR in the coming weeks.

Boston Freelancer server is alive and well but now I have another server! Yes, I have been convinced to run the Epsilon mod. Look for Boston Freelancer (Epsilon mod) server after you install the Epsilon mod found here on TLR.

This mod is not for the faint of heart but certainly makes you work for the fun! Come down and try one of our two servers!

And for the sysadmins? I've managed to get two FL servers on the same LAN to work through a dual Internet gateway. I thought no more than one FL server could be on a LAN at a time but I guess I was wrong. I did loosely bind each server to one exclusive ADSL line and have different port numbers. Still no conflicts for the clients from either the Internet or the LAN side!
