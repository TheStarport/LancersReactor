I post this because I found the reason for the error and maybe someone else might have the same or similar problem in the future.

I had a jump hole incorrectly mapped/set up.
I entered the hole in MP and couldnt get out, F1 didnt work so I had to ctrl alt del to exit the game.
After this, when I tried to play in MP the server crashed with the above error. I guess thats why I could play in SP with no problem.

To resolve the problem, I uninstalled and deleted all the fl files/folders and re-installed FL, the mod now works in MP, just had to fix the jump hole issue.
