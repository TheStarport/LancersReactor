Wing Commander:Vega Scourge Project

Ok, Heres the gist of the story:

Vega Sector right after WC1 ends, before SM:1. A new ship the Eagle's Talon, takes over as sector command ship as the Tiger's Claw moves to help the Fireekans. Lately concern has been growing in the sector over a new faction, the Vega Scourge that has been threatening galactic commerce. Little is known about the new enemy, except that they are deadly and strike relentlessly. A perfect opportunity for a freelancer to make big bucks on either side of the struggle.

What we have planned:

New, Custom models for WC1 ships
New Bases and Custom Rooms
Vega Sector Universe
New Faction and Costumes
WC1 Music and SFX
WC1 Weapons

Heres whats Done:

Hornet Light Fighter: 75% Complete (See website for pics)
Able System, Douglas Quad: Layout Planned, Beggining Design Phase
Weapons: Have SFX, working on custom effects and custom meshes.

What we Need help with:

System Design
Custom Costumes and Factions
Custom Base Rooms

This one looks to be very exciting. We're hoping to bring a very WC experience to FL. We'll keep everyone informed on the progress.
