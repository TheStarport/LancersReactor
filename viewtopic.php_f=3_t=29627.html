Midaus

Fri Mar 26, 2004 1:56 am by Midaus

If you were disappointed because it just wasnt' challenging enough, your not alone. Don't give up on FL just yet. Some of these guys doing the mods have added things that were supposed to be there in the first place or added their own enhancements. For singleplayer, the RealSpace 2.2 mod was great. It makes some minor changes to the game that make it a much more challenging and rewarding experience. And The lighting changes and Better computer pilots were most welcome. For multiplay the Evolutions 1.27 mod has been Faaaaantaaastic! It is a true challenge in single player and Multiplay. It adds so many new ships / items / dimensions to the game I can't list them all. One of the aspects I appreciate is it's balance. It doesn't start you off with all the money you can spend, or Uber equipment so powerful theirs no challenge. It is hard to raise the money for what you need or want at least a good 80-90 percent through the climb. If your not into trading, keeping yourself going, supplied, and moving upward with a fighter is extremely difficult. It's just awesome. To top it off the online player community is great. I am eagerly awaiting Evolutions 1.28.

Honestly the game will be worth playing with the right mods.
