TheSilentCartographer

Sun Mar 02, 2003 6:52 pm by TheSilentCartographer

The one thing I would not reccomend is attacking a transport right outside a base. The base and all of the nearby police will open fire on you (so you cannot dock and sell the cargo). The single exception I've had to this was when a Gateway shipping convoy was taking fire from the Liberty Navy.

When attacking Transports / Heavy Transports, you can shoot the commodities off the transports. This yields more cargo then killing the transport outright.


What I've found to be a good tactic is to have one player in a fighter, one in a freighter. The fighter blows up the supply depots / cargo from transport. The frieghter tractors it all in and sells it at the station (since its not seen as hostile). The fighter evades the return fire and flees, meeting up with the freighter as soon as the cargo has been sold. the fighter can tractor cargo as well, transfer it to the freighter a small distance away, allowing the freighter to sell that too.
