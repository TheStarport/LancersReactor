To all concerned - I believe that the majority of problems with SL are caused by having the "3D Sound" checkbox enabled. If you are still having problems and you have not selected this option, then there are only two things that I can suggest. One is to try a No-CD patch (I'm telling you where; TLR rules, Sorry!), the other is to play around with the "Compatibility Settings" in Windows XP. Apart from that, make sure that you have the latest drivers for all of your hardware, and an up-to-date version of Directx (9.0b).

This sort of problems occur with older games (particularly on XP). Good Luck!
