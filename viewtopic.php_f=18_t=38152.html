@BIGMAG:

Yes, we get that notice, but only if the executable outputs debugging information.

I have determined that the errors are most likely sound-related- see the "sound hang" thread. These are the ones that tend to crash so suddenly that the game doesn't post debugging information.

So, for your mod... comment out all of your Voices lines in FactionProps.ini and in your new Systems... and see what happens.

Doing so in my mod has resulted in error-free operation.

buck danny

Mon Mar 14, 2005 2:25 pm by buck danny

I would like to add this. Chips is correct, crashes inflicted by encounters never generates a flspew output. But some other errors will do the same thing.
I found that the fact that the sound hangs does not imply that the error that is causing the crash is sound related. The sound present at time of crash is simply continued by your soundcard and it has not yet been told to shut up untill FL releases your computer. Than the soundcard will also be released and the sound will stop.
Sometime ago watercoolerwarrior made a very valueable suggestion. Use filemon(sysinternals.com) to monitor FL, maybe in that way you would get a clue.
