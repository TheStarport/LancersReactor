Freelancer manual!

Im sure all of you are wondering what the manual will be like. Well, since the NDA has been lifted, we can tell ya all about it.

Well, the version of the manual im looking at right now is in PDF, cuz it came with the latest build of Freelancer we got for testing. The manual is cleverly disguised as a declassified "Basic Doctrine of the Liberty Security Force." The backgrounds to the manuals are textured with a basic map of the Sirius System along with some metallic hexagons scattered and in bunches producing a border around the content. The general color of it is a grayish green. Looks very metallic and cool. Not lame at all.

Tobias (you'll know who he is when u play) is the one who found this "rare" booklet and he gives it to you, Trent, as a guide book. He puts in many sticker notes which are just tips for playing the game. The manual is full of text describing all the icons in the game and their function -- standard stuff.

No, there isnt any background info like some of you had hoped for but there is a short letter from Tobias near the start of the manual.

There are screenshots in every other page and Tachyon even makes a special appearance in page 25. The screenshot was taken from the big stress test we had a couple weeks ago. And Tach's msg to the world: "lol" Hhahahaha!

In total, there are 41 pages in the manual, 43 if you count the front and back covers. Oh yah, and on the back cover, theres a quick list of some essential keys you'll need to know.

Oh, and no, TLR didnt get a mention Nor did any of the beta testers. Oh well, not a big prob at all.

AWESOME MANUAL, DA! DAMN! This pdf is eating up memory. 64 megs just for Adobe Reader. Yeeesh
