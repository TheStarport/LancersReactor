Sorry, the only way to get a lvl8 graviton shield is to buy a "Titan" where it is equipped in as a default. I'm not sure if a Sabre has one too, but at least I can definitively tell you that it isn't sold anywhere.

The max. shields being sold are:
c10 Positron - Planet Crete, Omicron Gamma (or other Corsair Stations there)
c09 Molecular - Freeport9, Omicron Theta
c07 Graviton - Gas Miner Naha, Sigma 13

there are also other places where you can buy some of those, but as a general rule the c10 positron shield is _only_ sold by pirates, so if you're a nice guy... better go buy some rep hack before going there.

There are no c9/c10 gravitons and no c10 molecular shield in the game, only the NPCs have those and you can't loot them for it.
