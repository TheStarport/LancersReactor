I still enjoy the game in multiplayer mode. But i agree with you. It's been a long time waiting (5 years?) to find out that two weeks is enough to get tired of it.
Well, not tired yet, but almost there.

My hope is that maybe, by design, this game can be easily expanded and new missions and stuff added. Because the game has an awesome potential. The engine is the very best i've seen. It seems like they had not enough time to fill it up with content.

I am surprised by the fact that there isn't any mission out of the story line. Something like a guy in a bar that has a special cargo to transport...something like privateer was.

In fact, the story line has a really boring design. It is completely game driven. You, as player, just do not trigger anything with your actions.

For instance, Juni could tell you that there is a planet called X somewhere where
you can find a girl named Y who has the answer to some questions, and then leave it up to the player to explore and find that planet, instead of take you there as a guided trip hitting F2 once and again to go to one and another waypoint and missing the real thing, that is exploring the unknown & making wealth.

Besides these details, this game still can be the best, if someone can make the
players feel like the universe is really unlimited.

I see Christ Roberts concept spread all over the game, and a great job from Digital Anvil, but someone did cut it down to fit the funds available. Many features are there, unfinished...what is the point of the 'Hail' action? And the target subsystem feature? they are nice, but should be usefull too. And so many other stuff...(radar, flight comp software like in privateer, missile time lock, cargo missions)... I hope they finish it with one or more patches.
