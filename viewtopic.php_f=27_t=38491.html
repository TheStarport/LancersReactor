You can unload some of your redundant missiles, coutermeasures etc. Part of the hold is for shields and some weapons.

The tradelanes are removed from some of the systems. You can use the 'in system' jumpholes in some systems, e.g. New York, to get around.

Hi again;

Yes, the cargo hold will contain weaps, turrets, shields etc. After selling off your redundant missiles etc., I think that your hold may then return to 'normal' space allocation. However, should you come across something on base or as loot that starts with NPC.........., be sure to grab just one as more than that may fill your cargo hold and you won't be able to clear it unless you edit the next Save (fl) file or possibly. jettison the excess.

Should you be at a base, planet or station, you will not be able to target a waypoint-to a jumphole, station etc. When you are in 'Space' you can open the map, magnify it and target stations et al. I tried it before writing this and it worked-for me, anyways.

I hope this helps.
