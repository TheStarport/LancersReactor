Storage Character Bug!!!
Note that one of the possible causes is large deposits. Not just the amount that you have put in one storage character. It is possible that if you put smaller deposits in your character at one time, then you could have 500 items. Maybe there is no limit, just a limit on the deposit size. Every time I had this problem, it was during very large deposits.
Another person had the bug at 80 items or so, but I know that 165 items works, so maybe it is just the deposit size and not the balance. Please put your experience under the Storage Character topic so we can accumulate more ideas in one place. We may be able to narrow it down if more people participate in that thread.
