Argh

Tue May 24, 2005 9:31 am by Argh

Well, if we're not talking about the Voices (the dialogue of the characters), then it's pretty easy. Most of the sounds in FL are listed as WAV, but they're really encoded using the Frauenhofer MP3 codec. You can replace sounds with WAVs @ 22khz, 16bit, and they'll play just fine. As for re-encoding in Frauenhofer... there are some issues with that that haven't been resolved yet- basically the FL MP3s use some strange nulls to start/end the sounds.
