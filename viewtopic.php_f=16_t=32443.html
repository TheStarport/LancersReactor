NukeIt

Fri Jun 25, 2004 2:47 pm by NukeIt

Rheinland battleships are the easiest type to eliminate. Look around the ship's nose for an enclosed area midway up the bow. Fly in the side, and you will be protected from no only the battleship's guns, but very nearly everything else as well. Then you can chew away at the battleship all you want. You can even use this area as cover after the battleship has been destroyed, since the wreckage remains for a while after the ship has broken up.

If you really can't get the rest of the ships, hide in that little tunnel until your allies have mopped up everything else. Most of the time, they're invinceable anyway.
