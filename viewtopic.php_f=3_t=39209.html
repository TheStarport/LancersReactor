PrivateerFan

Tue May 31, 2005 7:38 pm by PrivateerFan

The easy way to get on an enemy's good side Coldblade is to kill people they don't like. Just use the F8 key to view your status and find the Junkers in the factions list at left. Click on their name and you'll see a description of the group and a list of their allies and enemies. Find the enemies nearest you and start hunting them down. Sooner or later the allegiances will shift. Probably sooner.
