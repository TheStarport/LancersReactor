***Warning Demo Spoiler***

The Liberty Defender can be found on the Battleship Missouri, which is next to the Norfolk shipyard.
The Pirate Bloodhound can be found at Rochester base (Junkers base) in the Jersey Debirs field, go to the main patrol junctions on your map.
The Borderworlds Dagger can be found at Buffalo base (Rouges base) in the Badlands, once again look for the patrol junctions.

If your mortal enemies with any of these groups your going to have to work long and hard getting them back in your favour, otherwise you won't be able to land at thier base. Still I've weighed them all up and i'd say the defender is better than the bloodhound and about on par with the dagger, I wouldn't kill yourself trying to get either ship.
