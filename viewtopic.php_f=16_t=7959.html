Most lucrative options?

So far, the best way to make money I've found is to go to Hamburg (Planet Hamburg) and pick up a load of diamonds for about C400 or so, then fly to New Tokyo (there's a jump hole from Hamburg to Sigma 13 that cuts out the extra 5-6 waypoints from Frankfurt) and then sell them for a whopping C1650.

My Order Heavy Fighter can carry 70 cargo units and I make about C87,500 off of every run.

I decided to buy a freighter in New Hamburg so I could carry 275 units which should yield about C343,750!!! A couple runs like that and I'll have enough money for a long time. Then I can get a nice Reihnland heavy fighter and smack around some bad guys in true Freelancer fashion.
