Dev

Wed Mar 09, 2005 1:16 pm by Dev

While I was building the alpha for Tides of War I found that the game would crash if I tried to fire more than 32 weapons simultaneously. I don't know if there is a restriction to the number of weapons that you can carry on your ship at once, but since there is a limit on how many can shoot it really doesn't matter too much. Now, you can sort of get around this by making the weapons multi-barreled, as the restriction is strictly based on the number of weapons, not the number of projectiles; however, you will of course lose the weapon firing sound if you have more than one barrel. Granted, you can attach a sound effect to the projectiles themselves, but this really isn't a substitute for having the guns making the sounds.
