SideshowBob

Wed Aug 06, 2003 10:03 pm by SideshowBob

There are 100's of servers to choose from but not all are 24/7. Personally I would pick a 24/7 so that you know it will be there when ever you go on line.

I play 56k and you sometimes get a bit of lag, ships will suddenly jump from point to another but it is fairly uncommon and most of the time you can live with it.

Player killing is actually not all that common at all, I know when you look up and down these boards there is constant discussion about it, but in reality you hardly ever meet a Pker. Most servers either ban or discourage Pking in at least New York, some all Manhattan and even some all of liberty, so you normally get a chance to find you feet in safety.

Do not play with your mod, some servers can pick up that mods are being used and you can banned for life almost instantly with no chance to explain yourself or anything. Further more most players will hunt modders down relentlessly which also makes it a bad idea.

Depending on how good a pilot you think you are, I would be tempted to stay away from Calgary, Seattle, Elite servers as they are the most popular and also where all the 'clans' hang out. The whole clan thing attracts PvP fights either because of inter clan rivalry or else hot head players that want to fight a particular clan for some reason. You can become a pawn in the middle of this, as Pkers might target a newbie just to get the attention of an anti Pker clan etc etc.

Also you are only really in danger if you are carrying cargo, as that and unattached guns are all you loose if you die.
