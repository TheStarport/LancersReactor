Chips opened his eyes, but quickly closed them again as a bright glare assulted his eyes. His head felt as if it would explode at any moment. He opened his eyes again, more careful this time.

Slowly all his senses started to return and he realised that he was tied to a chair. Vague memories floated through his head. He remembered something about being in a bar and talking to a beautiful girl. Other than that, he didn't have a clue.

He slowly turned his head from side to side, trying to see what was going on. He realised that he wasn't alone, to his left a row of chars, most occupied stood.

"Ah, finally you decided to join the land of the living"

He tried to focus on the face of the man next to him. The man seemed vaguely familiar. He shook his head, trying to get rid of the dizzyness and then looked at the man again. He was shocked to realise that the man was someone he knew from TLR.
Not just anyone at that, it was Eraser!
Chips, now dumbfounded looked at the man sitting next to Eraser, it was Parabolix. As he studied each of the men, he realised that he was looking at TLR's moderators. He turned his attention back to Eraser.

"What's going on?"

Eraser shook his head.

"We don't know. It looks like someone decided to kidnap all TLR's moderators, no idea why though"

Chips looked through the row of seats, again noticing the open chairs.

"Well, it seems like we're getting some more company sometime, lets see, there are 18 chairs and only 14 are filled. Thats weird, there are 19 people on TLR's staff, isn't there E?"

Eraser slowly nodded his head; "You're right there, Chips, Fear Factor, Harrier, HCl, Bigman and sw are the only ones who are not here yet."

"Pah, sw is probably unfindable, I bet she's busy feeding her pet lion or something"

Chips was not surprised to hear Tawakalna making such a comment, they were all used to it by that time. looked around in the room the were held in, it was bare except for the chairs in which they were tied up. He was still pondering that when a voice boomed accross the room.

"Welcome, staff of TLR, I am sorry that the rest couldn't join us, but at leaast they won't bother us either."

They all strained to look behind them where the voice was coming from. When the man stepped into full view, there were gasps. They could not see his face, but there was no mistake about his identity. He wore a black outfit and a mask in the from of the head of a rabbit.
