Is the race ticking you off too???? *spoiler*

Good ol' Dexter win all the time against you too?

Well, I'm not as good at this game as some of you (probably too old and slow) and couldn't beat Dexter.

WELLLLLLL, I decided I didn't like the ship he was using....

1. Get the INI decompresser.
2. MAKE COPIES OF YOUR FILES!
3. Uncompress the npcships.ini file under DATA\MISSIONS\M06
4. See Dexter as the first entry? It looks like this:

nickname = MSN06_Dexter
loadout = MSN06_Dexter
level = d6
ship_archetype = bw_fighter
pilot = MSN06_Dexter
state_graph = FIGHTER
npc_class = lawful, FIGHTER

Hrmmm, some kind of fighter....we don't want that, too easy for him to win! Lets give him something slightly larger....like a BATTLESHIP!

5. Change the entries above to the following:
nickname = MSN06_Dexter
loadout = li_n_li_dreadnought
level = d6
ship_archetype = li_dreadnought
pilot = MSN06_Dexter
state_graph = CRUISER
npc_class = lawful, battleship, d25

6. Now RACE! LOL! Dexter can't even fit through the hoops!!!! LOL! Take your time. But I found there seems to be a time limit so don't take too much time! Dexter might fly around a bit, but he can't fit through the hoops so he will lose!


Sorry, I just hate annoying missions like this.
