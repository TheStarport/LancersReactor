sorry - mission created solars are things made for missions. The statements that look like this:
Li01_01_base are reffering to base files - which is what lets you go inside an object when you dock. In fact - you cannot dock with an object unless it has that feature just there - a base file, as it refers to it in the ini fils as:
dock_with = Li01_01_base
base = Li01_01_base


So what the above post was showing, was a stack of bases that are made solely for the purpose of the story - like the research station willard, the base on planet sprague. However, mods can recreate bases again. I have made sparague dockable with a base, albeit its not the one in the story due to clashes with the story - but the dockring is round the other side. You can in fact have more than one base at a planet, as my mod shows where 1 planet has four docking rings. Go through the north dock - its bretonain base, go through the east - its kusari, west - liberty and 'south' is the rheinlanders - each with their stuff to sell.......kinda neat huh?!

As for is it possible to recreate the planets - no planets are lost in the story - and also, if you link jumpholes or jumpgates - or just open them back up again, you can in fact get to the systems used in the story. I went to the last one expecting nothing to be there and got raped by nomads instead, so they are already funcioning systems (not just activated for the storyline).

Hope that is a little more enlightening, as i posted just before bed last night! . The smell of early mornings and strong coffee are great!

Any more questions about what is possible - ask away - if you want to know how to do something - come to my thread in the modding forum for tutorials, how to's and general helping out with mods. I also have a thread in the tutorial forum for people who experience crashes when modding. Its a kinda troubleshooting for those with problems, but no-one to answer them!
