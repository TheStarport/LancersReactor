What were those Rogues THINKING?

Okay, so I'm cruising along a trade lane in California when I get the all-familiar "Trade lane disrupted" message. It's a bunch of Liberty Rogues, but they don't attack me because I'm neutral to them. I hail one, and I'm told that they are on their way to Alcatraz. Since I've never been there, I decide to follow them back to base.

After a few minutes, I hear one of those guys say, "I'll give you one chance -- drop the cargo!" (or something). I didn't get the expected dialog box, so I knew they were talking to someone else, but looking at my scanner, I didn't find any other ships nearby.

Hearing no response, the Rogues start attacking...a trade lane. That's right, folks -- those Cardamine-crazed loonies ordered a TRADE LANE to drop its cargo, and when it did not respond, they decided to blast away.

The moral: Just Say No to Cardamine.
