Chips

Wed May 05, 2004 8:22 am by Chips

Class engines like weapons - no you canont do this (i wish we could), but selling is simple. use same method as for selling a thruster - so make sure you have your engine_equip or misc_equip.ini entry (which is where my engines are) and your corresponding goods.ini file (ie - misc_goods.ini file), Then copy the name for your engine (nickname of goods entry) and paste it OVER the nickname of a thruster for sale in the market_misc.ini file instead.

Do same for anything else you wish to sell......
