Quixotal:
I completely forgot about this...
In the "Data\Equipment" folder is a zip file.
If you open it, you will find a printable Commodities Map!
On the map are listed Minable Commodities in each System.
Along with what type of bases are in each system.
Any place with an "=P" beside it, indicates a Planet.

As to Tungsten, there are 10 systems where it can be mined.
And there are dozens of places to sell it at a higher than standard price.
But you must remember this:
Mineable Minerals are only needed by Refineries, so avoid any other place.
Also, the farther you get from a minable system, the higher the price.
And...
As a minable resource, which can accidentally be mined during combat, I had to keep the price low.
