WOW! Very impressed with you mod! Great work!

Now for some feedback. I realize this is beta, so nothing is complete. I'm impressed with the work done so far!
I like the planetary rotation! It threw me off at first, but it definately adds a good feel to the environment! One time though, I went on a mission, came back to manhattan, and the rotation had stopped.
The ships are great! All those little talons flying around are great! Just like in the game.
The details you've put into this is awesome. The cargo from privateer, to the weapons and equipment. Nearly all of it is there! Great attention to detail!
The music is classic! It's great! However, as someone else I think pointed out, it may get a little repetitive without variety. Maybe you could import music from other wing commander games?
When I boarded the battleship near norfolk, I took a look at the ships for sale. The stilletto's textures looked like they were pretty screwed up. It seemed like the mapping was off.
I bought the Arrow, but when ever I try to take the tradelane to the texas gate, I get a CTD. The arrow also seems too sluggish for a light fighter. I remember in WC3 of how it could turn on a dime. Right now, it seems like it has the turning of a hellcat. Not too bad, but not too great either.
With the "shields failed" and "shields restored" thing, I think that the shields could stand to be down a little longer. If I remember right, in WC3, if your shields got knocked out on one section, only one bar of shields would recharge for a while, and any hits to that section would most likely result in bleedthough damage to the armor until the shields were able to recover.

That's all I can think of at any rate. I've only played for about half hour, so if I missed something, that's why. Anyways, back to Privateer, FL style! Looks like I'll have to replace the Arrow. GL with the mod, and again, great job so far!
