RunOrDie

Sun Mar 16, 2003 2:45 am by RunOrDie

If you're having a lot of difficulty piloting the ship, try using a light fighter (freighter is harder). Hit cruise as soon as the race starts, and follow him through the loops. Use mouse-flight mode, and not the F2 autopilot.

All you need to do is keep up with him. He screws up towards the end of the race, so even if he's more than a ring in front you can still pass him.

Some people have big difficulties with the mouse-flight mode. In certain cases it's because the sensitivity is simply too high/low.

If you're not having problems shooting bad guys but can't control the ship properly, remember that, just like you have to POINT at the bad guy you want to shoot down, you have to POINT at the place you want to go.

You see a gate? Keep your mouse pointed on it and your ship will go there. If you try to lead the gate with your mouse, you'll overshoot the target.

Hope this helps.
