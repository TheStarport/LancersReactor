Brother Grimm

Thu Feb 27, 2003 10:06 pm by Brother Grimm

You guys should keep in mind that WANTING to fly around and do as you please is not the same as being ABLE to do so. The SP missions take you on a specific progression of difficulty that still exists in the MP world so you should keep that in mind when flying into an MP game.

What I'm saying is that flying your Patriot into a Edgewold system or the Rhienland border systems will make you a dead Freelancer very quickly.

BTW, the progression is LIBERTY -> BRETONIA -> KUSARI -> RHEINLAND. The border systems between each have varied risks (moving from colony to colony can be a challenge at early levels) and the edgeworlds will kick your butt.

A good indicator of whether or not you should be strolling about the galaxy at your current location are the mission levels in the bar. They should roughly equal your own LVL.
