UPDATE: Well, I picked up the Z-680 speakers, and set them up. Essentially, I have the rear left on the piano near the doorway, and the rear right on the shelf over the fireplace. The other three are arranged to the left, centre, and right of the monitor respectively. The setup seems to work very well, but I have to increase the rear speaker volume by about 30% over the front three, as that way I get a balanced sound. The sound quality is excellent! When I pick up a nice 16x DVD-Rom drive, I'll try LOTR in 5.1 Dolby DTS Surround. Should be good *drool* .
