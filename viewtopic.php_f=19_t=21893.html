New version of LancerGuard - v1.03

The new version of LancerGuard is out. LancerGuard is a small, server-side only anti-hack mod (players don't need to download anything).

LancerGuard blocks/nullifies the following cheats:
- Cloak
- Invincible Shield
- Invincible Hull
- Nomad Lasers
- Nomad Torpedoes
- Nomad Turrets
- Nomad Cruise Disruptor

NOTE - LancerGuard does NOT effect Nomad Cannons/Blasters at all. These will be dropped as normal by Nomads in the 2 Unkown systems.

Updates for v1.03
- People now can't pick up nomad lasers or cloaks. People docked with them are unable to launch. They cant un-equip them, sell them, or otherwise remove them. They're stuck on the planet. Anyone using a cheat item effectively renders their account useless.
