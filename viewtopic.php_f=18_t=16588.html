Here is part of the BW04.ini, omega 11 (decompressed with BINI)

This is the background radiation that is felt throughout the system:
[zone
nickname = Zone_Bw04_system_radiation
pos = -124, 0, 388
shape = SPHERE
size = 40000
damage = 15
sort = 99.500000

See the line that say's damage = 15, this controls how much damage the player ship takes (im not sure how you tell it that its radiation damage)

Here is the part that controls the highly radioactive part near the sun:

[zone
nickname = Zone_Bw04_Abstand
ids_name = 261309
pos = -243, 0, 513
rotate = 15, 0, 0
shape = SPHERE
size = 21000
damage = 220
ids_info = 66001
spacedust = leedsdust
spacedust_maxparticles = 125
sort = 99

notice how the damge is much higher

If you don't know the zone name, you can always look it up in Freelancer Explorer.

Hope this helps
