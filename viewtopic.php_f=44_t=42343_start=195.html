rgriffin

Sun Aug 19, 2007 9:26 am by rgriffin

Thanks Cold_Void, that means alot coming from you.

Yeah, I ended up fixing the texture on the side of the wings. And made some other changes to the model, to include making the wings look a bit edgier and modifying the engines abit in the attempt to to capture the rugged Corsair theme.

It was alot of fun doing this model, never before have I used milkshape to texture. After toying around with it, I've found its immensely easier than doing it in Maya. The only downside is its not as flexible and I can't be as precise with it as I can in Maya due to the fact I can't manipulate a duplicate of the geometry in Maya then transfer the UVs for a perfectly seamless and non-warped texture across the whole body of the ship.

Nevertheless, it does the job good enough and is hell of alot easier
