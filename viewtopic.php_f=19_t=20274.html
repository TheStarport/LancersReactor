Sorry to hear your misfortune, and backing up your MP account number is a must in freelancer, but for all you guys asking on occasions for the server admin to supply your account number: THEY DONT HAVE IT.
Account id's are generated from your id combined with a server id, Thats as simple a i can put it in laymens terms.
as a precaution against this here is what i suggest
1) right down or print your mp account number
2)once you have your freelancer installed with your new/old account number, export the registary entry that provides your account number.
3) when you have to install freelancer again just double click the the exported .reg file.
Voila.
the reg key is found here: HKEY_CURRENT_USER/Software/Microsoft/Microsoft Games/Freelancer/1.0
Highlight the "InstallKey" and click file and then export, name it whatever you wish and save it to floppy or a cd whichever you have.
When you need to reinstall Freelancer and want your old account just insert the floppy or wherever you saved the file, and double click it, then click ok.
You will find all is as it should be.
But asking server admins is a waste of their time and yours.
