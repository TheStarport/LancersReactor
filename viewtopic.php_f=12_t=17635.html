OK, lets start:
I like hard-core stuff... home-made Brandy like Plum or Appricot Brandy (40-52%), cocktails also come in handy (B-52, Tequilla Sunrise)...
I even made my own trademark cocktail: the Death's Hand.
You take a wide 0.5 l glass, and put the edge in crystal sugar, so it sticks.
Then you put a little ice in (2-3 cubes), pour 0.25 l of red Martini (dark vermouth), add 0.1 l of Rum (real, not the artificial flavour one), and finally add 0.1 l of Vodka (Gorbatchov or Troika, needs to be made from potato).
You stir it with two cross strokes, and enjoy. Best served cold.
Once I had three glasses of it in 15 mins... Thats how it got its name.
Also various flavoured whiskys like Ammaretto Whisky (almond) are nice...
Wine could be a proper substitute when nothing else is around...
Beer comes in a weak last. (I'm gonna burn for this one...)
Cheers!
