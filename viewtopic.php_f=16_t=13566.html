If you have finished the missions than you must have the Order Heavy fighter and some good weapons. That ship can easily get you back to the more lucrative places. Head back to Reinland and find a nice freighter or better fighter there.

Or you could simply start hacking away at enemies of the Lane Hackers to get on their good side so they'll let you land on their base to purchase the Dromedary.

But most importantly have fun exploring the rest of the universe. There's many cool places and things to buy that you haven't seen yet.

The missions in Liberty space will always pay squat. But the missions in Reinland and the outer worlds can become quite profitable. Basically, the further away from Liberty space you go the more powerful the bad guys and the more profitable the trade runs and missions become.
