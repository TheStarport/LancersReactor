Here's a little thing that I used to do: take missions at new berlin or any house/corporate base in Frankfurt. They will usually have 1 or more missions that have you killing Red Hessians. Now, because you are doing missions for the gov't or a corporation the Corsairs won't like that too much (let's give it a value...let's say --). However, because you are killing multiple hessians the corsairs will like that (let's say that get's a ++++++). Add those up and you still come out with a positive faction rating. To improve your standing with any faction through killing other factions, you need to take into account the empathy value not whether the factions are allies. To get better with the Corsairs, the following are the factions with empathy value lower than -.2 you need to aim for, ordered by their empathy value (the lower the more the Corsairs hate them). I also denoted in parentheses which other factions you will piss off by killing that faction. Naturally, the faction you killed you will piss off the most (empathy +1).


Bretonia Armed Forces (BAF) -.4
(BP +.35, BMM +.25, Bowex +.25, Gateway +.25, Planetform +.25, ...)

Bretonia Police (BP) -.4
(BAF +.35, BMM +.25, Bowex +.25, Planetform +.25, Gateway +.2, ...)

Bowex -.35
(BMM +.35, BAF +.25, BP +.25, ...)

Hessians -.35
(LWB +.25, Dragons +2, ...)

IC -.3
(Ageira +.25, LPI +.25, LSF +.25, Navy +.25, Universal +.2, ...)

IMG -.3
(Gateway +.25, ...)

Republican -.35
(ALG +.25, Daumann +.25, Kruger +.25, Military +.25, RP +.25, ...)

GMG -.25
(Kishiro +.35, Samura +.25, ...)

Kishiro -.25
(GMG +.35, KP +.2, Naval Forces +.2, ...)

Samura -.25
(KP +.35, Naval Forces +.3, GMG +.25, ...)


the others are:

ALG -.2
BMM -.2
Gateway -.2
Bounty Hunters -.15
Daumann -.15
Outcasts -.15
Cryer -.1
K. Police -.1
DSE -.05
Mollys -.05
Universal -.05
