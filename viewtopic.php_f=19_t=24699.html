The Battle with a God.

-----------Boston Freelancer Server, Cambridge System------------
Today I decided I didn't like AR's handling of the server pirates. So I declared war on AR, and hunted down two members in the Omega Systems. [RNEarendil (also admin of the server), as a true ally, came to help them.

He caught up with me in Cambridge. The battle was intense. I ran through all my shield bats and nanobots very quickly, but I held on. Earendil later told me he ran out of 'bats and nearly ran out nanos. The battle lasted a good 10 minutes or so. IT WAS THE MOST FUN I'VE EVER HAD. The battle went back and forth back and forth back and forth. At one point I almost had him...brought him down to somewhere around 5% or 10% hull health, but he repaired and came back strong. Eventually, he wore me down and I was sent back to Cadiz Base in Omega 5 in a flash. Earendil is the most challenging and the most skilled player I have fought to date. Consequently, I worship him as a God of PVP. See ya'll around.
