I would try deleting all your saved single and multi player game files first. A full reinstall may still be required but deleteing the saved games fixes a lot of errors.

(Assuming Win XP of some flavor here)
Delete
My Documents\My Games\Freelancer

The entire directory and everything in it, then try running your game again.
