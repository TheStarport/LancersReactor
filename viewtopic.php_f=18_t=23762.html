That is the adv champ gravitron players shield, they are tough, but not difficult to kill without artillery!!
To rid the game of that particular shield, go to your st_equip.ini and scroll down until you see that shield - player one though........
From there, comment out the lines of the shield like so
instead of:

Blah blha blha
DA_archetype = blah blah blah
blah blha
lsdkjlfdjk

Make it look like (notice blahs and random text are cause i can't be arsed to find the proper file entry!! HOwever, the DA_arch part is correct!)

Balh ahlahlh
;DA_archetype =
Blah blah
blha blah

This will leave anything carrying that shield without a shield!
So, you will need to make sure other NPC's don't have that shield mounted, or theey will be flying without shields!
