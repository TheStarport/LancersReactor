I assume your talking about escorting the convoy through the bouys.
It took me a few tries. Finally I just flew a little out of the way to the left when I was approaching the fight area (but before the ships appeared.) Be careful if you get too far off they won't hit the mine until you get closer.
After that, I just consentrated on the ships in the outskirts. After you take out a few then you can fly in and not repeatedly get wiped out as quickly.
Have lots of shield generators and nanbots repairs dudes.
If your shields are going out just run away (as Monty Python would say) and let them regenerate a little then get back in there.
Once you use this approach just a few times, you will suddenly get the win. Just takes a little practice.
GET AWAY from the big ships and take out the little fighters on the outskirts first.
Hope it helps

Wed Mar 24, 2004 10:15 am by Megistos

The convoy escort part isn't too hard, it might need a little practice but you'll get there. If you mean that you're having trouble with the bit where you defend Willard Station, make sure you stock up on Shield Batteries and Nanobots on Cali. Minor when you dock there. In the battle area, try to take out a couple of the gunboats, they're a pain. If you stick behind their engines, they can't hit you at all and you can just blast them. Remember that you can use the station itself as cover if you need time to let your shields regen
