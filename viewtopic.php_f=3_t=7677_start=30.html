Ether Dragon

Wed Mar 12, 2003 6:25 pm by Ether Dragon

Hmm. Haven't played the single player game aside from the first mission on the demo. I seem to recall that he drank at the bar after arriving on Planet Manhatten. He ordered the first round, the bartender recognized him from the news, and gave him a second for free.

As for me, I like the guy; mostly for technical reasons. I was amazed at the character animation for all of the models. They did a great job of showing expression on the characters' faces. Trent even has a bit of a swagger when he walks into the bar. I wince at some of his lines, like when he walks up to someone and says, "whatchya got?" He doesn't sound cool, he sounds like someone trying to play cool, and failing miserably. For the most part, however, all the voice acting in the bar scenes, including Trent's, is good.

What I really think is deplorable is the lack of accents in the game. I have flashbacks to Kevin Costner's performance in "Robin Hood: Prince of Thieves" everytime I go to Bretonia. I cringe when I hear some guy with a flat American accent using British slang. It's the same with all of the other nationalities represented as well.

Oh yeah, one other pet peeve is walking into a bar and seeing half a dozen people, each sitting alone at a seperate table, simply staring straight ahead, with their hands in their laps. At least they're all exhibiting correct posture...
