Sat Jan 01, 2005 1:48 am by Arania

Originally, the Japanese game 'Zero Wing', a popular scrolling shooter game is the origin. The phrase originated from a direct literal translation of the words.
'All your base are belong to us'
Since then, games and hackers alike, taking the phrase as a synonyn to 'T0T41 0WN4G3' and others.
this flash originated during that period, many people photoshopping 'All your base are belong to us' into pictures and arranging them in a slideshow. The 'Anal Exams' picture is a widespread favourite, even when unmodified.
Over time, use of the phrase has dissipated, and it is now hardly ever sighted online.
