Fri Jun 23, 2006 6:35 pm by ww2jacob

No, I think he means the Just for Fun Mod 3.0.

You can now choose to save Brandon Rowlett, Dr. Van Pelt, Walker, and 2 of Walker's crew. Just tractor in their life pods. You can drop them anywhere for a nice remuneration but the best price for Brandon is Machtan, the Hacker Base in Magellan. The best price for Van Pelt is Cambridge research station. Their life pods do not combine with other life pods or each other so they have a separate listing in the cargo bay. They do not take up cargo space either.
