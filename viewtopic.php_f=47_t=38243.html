Adventures in TNG Sirus

This is a story that involves players on the TNG server I ran. At least, it started with that intention. It bears the potential of becoming something very different.

Any questions posted in this thread, will be answered in the Off Story thread.

I took several liberties adding elements, to explain different portions of game play. One, being the Ship to Ship transporter. If a pilot was able to transport away from his ship before it was blown to smithereens, that would partially explain the ability to respawn. it would also cover being able to change between characters and ships. Now, I know it does not COMPLETELY explain these things, but that is where I am taking license.

You will notice that I don't focus on every detail. Nor do I start every sentence of dialog off with "he said" or "she said" or "Hawk said" or "Prey said"... it might appear confusing at times trying to determine just who says what... but read carefully and there will always be indicators... even if it is simply that people take turns speaking. But I do focus heavily on dialog.

I also write from a very visual standpoint. Each serial section is written in a fashion as to make it appear to be a scene in a movie. Just as movies have long scenes and short scenes, where there are long and short gaps in time between scenes, as well as details omitted between scenes, so is the format I follow with this story.

So enjoy, and feel free to comment in the Off Story Thread.

This first part, is entitled
To the Rescue, and Beyond!
