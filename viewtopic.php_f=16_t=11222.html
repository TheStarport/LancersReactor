Mon Mar 24, 2003 12:29 am by Bullethead

Here are some good runs I found in Bretonia space with Freelancer Cargo Computer. It has a cool feature that allows you to sort by profit per cargo unit, so do that and rule out all the drug running and such to bases you're not friendly with, and take the best thing that's available after that.

It takes money to make money. Depending on how much money you've got, different routes are the most profitable for you. The more money you can invest in buying stuff, the better the runs that become available.

When you get to Leeds in Bretonia, buy a Clydesdale. Then load up on Light Arms and go to Durham Outpost. This is only 1 tradelane jump away, you only get lane-hacked like 1 out of 4-5 trips, and a full load nets you nearly $8k. Fly back to Leeds empty and do it several times until you have about $120K. This is enough cash to really make money.

Because this trip is so short and safe, you can make more money in less time this way than you can making multi-stop runs. For instance, I started out with a complex circle route hitting places in Manchester, New London, and Leeds, that netted about $65k, but it took way longer than doing the Leeds-Durham run 8 times, plus you had to fight nearly every inch of the way and the money spent doing that ate into profits.

Anyway, once you have a bit over $120K, then you go back to Planet Leeds and buy a full load of Optronics. Take them to Planet Cambridge and net about $42k. At Planet Cambridge, load up on Diamonds and go back to Planet Leeds for another $50k or so. IOW, just flying back and forth between Planet Leeds and Planet Cambridge gets you a bit over $90k per round trip.

This route is 13 waypoints but is pretty safe. It goes Planet Leeds - Jump Gate - Stokes - Planet Dover - Jump Gate - Planet Cambridge. I only get lane-hacked about 1 out of 3-4 trips, always between Stokes and Dover. There is usually a fight going on at Stokes when I pass by but they ignore me and even if I get cruise-disrupted I can still afterburn to the tradelane without any ill effects. Give it a try.

I've heard there's a jump hole between Leeds and Cambridge, which would make the trip shorter, but I've yet to find it.

Covered in many other threads but here it is again. Trade in general will yeild more for distance. The problem is time. A trader wants the best yield per unit of time. As time is money. There are 2 fast routes that make high yields, both are in part illegal trades. You will need to be on neutral or better terms with some of the less savory types. Also if you plan on trading get the Dromedary freighter. It is the best in the game. The humpback looks tougher on paper but the thing handles like a whale on the beach to the Dromedarys whale in the ocean. OK here are the 2 quick routes.

In Liberty
Mactan base --> New york gate --> Texas jump hole --> Beaumont Base and back

Cardimine from mactan to Beaumont, Engine components from Beaumont to Mactan. Yield is 1139 per cargo unit per round trip. The law will never give you a problem.

In Reinland
Planet New Berlin --> sigma 13 Jump hole --> Yanagi Base and back

Engine components from New Berlin to Yanagi and Cardimine from Yanagi to New Berlin. The yield is 1138 per cargo unit per round trip. Use the trade lane to get to and from the jump hole less than 3k from it. The law may want to check your cargo in New Berlin but you should have pleanty of time to get on planet before they go hostile on you. This is the faster of the 2 routes but the bad guys are tougher if they don't like you.

I have done both and to make cash fast I like the Berlin run best, the badlands are apply named traveling through it is just not fun.

If you want to stay strickly legit you will want to do some variation of the New Berlin --> Sigma 13 --> Chugoku --> Honshu --> New Tokyo and back to The Ring (base next to New Berlin) with Diamonds and Niobium. Takes much longer but yields 2182 per cargo unit per round trip. If you go to Dresdan system you can get a yield of 2408 per cargo unit per round trip at the expense of more time.
