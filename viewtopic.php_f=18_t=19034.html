It's not too hard...First you need to use Reynen's compilation post to find all the base IDs...
PLANET IDs

PLANET IDs

I hope those links work

OK, you'll also need an .ini decompressor, BiniQDU should do the job
Here we go...

1. Use your .ini decompressor to open up the file called market_misc.ini
2. Use Reynen's Planet ID's post to find the planet you are looking for (they are alphabetical)
3. Now open up the weapon_equip.ini file with Bini
4. This may take awhile to open, dont worry, its just alot of words loading up...
5. Find the gun ou are looking for...
6. The guns are organized by Mk and which faction they are produced by
7. Like a justice would be under an Li listing because its made by Liberty
8. Once you find the gun you are looking for, look under the munitions tab.
9. Copy the nickname.
10. Now go back to market_misc.ini
11. Find the base you want to add the weapon to. Remember using Reynens Planet IDs post to do this.
12. Oops, OK, copy the first line under the Base.
13. It should be something like MarketGood = Ligun01mark01, 0, 10, 250, blah blah blah
14. Paste it and make it the first line.
15. Now go back to weapon_equip.ini and copy the nickname of the gun you want to make buyable.
16. Back to market_misc.ini
17. Copy the text over the gun you copied
18. You should be copying over the cloned line
19. Only replace the words...You know...Ligun01mark01 or whatever
20. Dont touch the "MarketGood=" text or any of the numbers...Unless the first number is high...You may want it to be 0 as it determines the level you have to be to buy the gun...
21. After this the gun will be buyable at whatever base.
22. You can also edit prices of guns by opening up weapon_good.ini
23. To do this, just find the gun you copied and change the price from its original setting to something like 5...
24. Good Luck and Have Fun!!!

P.S. - If you want something done if you don't understand this you can just email me what you want done...
