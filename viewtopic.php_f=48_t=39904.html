Chips

Fri Jul 15, 2005 4:28 am by Chips

To get people interested, you usually have to be able to specify some of what you wish to accomplish.........as well as giving them faith in your abilities (ie showing your working upon it). Making large mods takes alot of time, and trying to get strangers to get on board and dedicated, without much information to lure them, is not easy.

Try listing changes you are aiming for, showing images of what you have accomplished. If you have no ideas/no knowledge of modding, then you are going to find it exceptionally tough to motivate, lead and persuade people to join/work.
