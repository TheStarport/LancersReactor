Fri Mar 28, 2003 8:05 am by PurpleGerbil

Brake - X - Wil make you turn quicker
Turbo - Tab - Will make you hard to hit for a second or so
So if you are getting harrassed in combat - Turbo - Brake - Turn - Turbo - Brake - Turn - Turbo - Brake - Turn until you have confused the hell out of them.

At the start of combat, when a group of approaching ships gets to weapons range I normally Brake and shoot at the closest. If your weapons load is half decent you should kill at least one of them before they pass you. If they are a lot harder than you (and start trashing your shields), do the reverse. Turbo up to them, then Brake and Turn and get them up the bum.
