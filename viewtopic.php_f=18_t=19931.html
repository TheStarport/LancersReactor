The Rag model in FF8 was hi poly because it was a CGI MODEL, remember thsoe words, CGI MODEL. CGI Models are always over the 20K range, atleats everyone Ive seen so far. Since the CGI is a static recording there are no calculations as to how to render it, since the comp already knows where it will be for every second of the clip. The player version can be 1/4th poly count and be a accurate and faithful representation. Milkshape can handle the Rag, its just down to who will try it? Unfortunately my Milkshape skills are very lacking or I would gladly give it a try (I absolutely love the Rag's assault on the tower, blasting through the wall )

Ship Stats (From Squaresoft themselves)
Weight:
2,850 tons (without propellent, charge nor occupents)
3,134 tons (with one third propellent, full charge, and full occupents)
3,450 tons (with full propellent, full charge, and full occupents)

Length:
108 meters

Width:
77 meters

Height:
54 meters (landing), 65 meters (flying - the 2 rear engines expand kinda like X-wing S Foils)

Engines:
Two major (lower), Two Minor (upper)

Occupents:
A crew of 4. A navagator, a wireless operator, a cannoneer, and a cox. Adding to this, the ship is able to carry about ten more occupents.

Weapon:
609 mm charged particle cannon (the big bit under the head)
152 mm multibarreled laser x2 (just behind and to either side of the head)

Maximum Speed:
11.8 km/s (The second cosmic velocity, which can launch a rocket into space.)

Description:
It's body is red and you can see the letters ESTHAR on it's body. Ragnarok was made more than 20 years ago, and it is still one of the strongest flying ships. It was reportedly made secretly in an unknown country. There are at least 2 other flying ships of the same kind with different markings.
