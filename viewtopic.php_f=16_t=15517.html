StormCrow

Tue Apr 29, 2003 9:52 pm by StormCrow

The most effective tactic for beating Hovis is simply to know the turning circle of the craft you are using. As ships all travel the same speed in cruise mode, anticipating the amount by which you have to turn to reach the next gate, anticipating the turn and starting it before you go through the previous gate is all you need to do here.

Also, although hovis gives you a secod chance, its easier to beat him first time around, because the race doesent start until you hit accept and you can be ready with shift-W straight away. Second time around hovis usually beats you to getting into cruise mode.

I actually didnt know that hovis screwed up at the end, but by using this tactic it took me only five attempts (including second chances) to get in front of him and be BEATING him at the three quarters mark in my DROMEDARY boderworld freighter (didnt want to sell it). Just goes to show that if you keep you head this thing ain't so hard.
