In the USA, the Fox network showed the "Firefly" episodes out of order (the pilot was the last to air, after the decision to cancel). Three episodes were never broadcast, but the excellent DVD set has them all.

Firefly was very cleverly written. My favorite show was "Out of Gas," but it's hard to pick a favorite. The writers specialize in genre tweaking, and they like to combine aspects of westerns and sci-fi in unpredictable ways. Because the show was cancelled so quickly, much of the story remains to be told.

Producer Joss Whedon is working on a Firefly feature film. For more info, visit: http://www.fireflyfans.net/
