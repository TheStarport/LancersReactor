Huffer

Wed May 21, 2003 4:39 am by Huffer

Ok, I have the game, bought it the day after I saw the Matrix: Reloaded.

Graphics: Very good during the in-game scenes where you control the character, but some of the cinematics rendered with the game engine looks off, almost as if they were out of focus. Mixed in with the game are sections of video clips, some from the movie, some from the game. These look great and really add to the story and the feel of the game.

Gameplay: Very very fun, lots of cool moves and stunts to do, a few annoying spots, such as trying to snipe SWAT units who are shooting your allies. No really good puzzles to solve, but then it isn't really that type of game.

Story: If you liked the movie, then the game will do a lot for you, it fills in a lot of background, and gives information on several side-stories/plots that may be explored more in the third movie.

The only bad thing I found about this game is that it was waaaay too short, it only took me 4 days to beat it with one of the characters. However, the hacking section adds to the game and seems like it will increase it's longevity quite a bit.

Overall, I would definitely recommend getting the game, it was the best one I've gotten lately.

Sorry for writing a novel here, but I hope it helps with your decision.
