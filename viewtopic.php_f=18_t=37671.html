You can do design a ship to some extent. I`ve added some custom shipyards to my mod.
For example, you can buy an eagle in 3 configurations:
1 gunsx7, no turret, shield mounted onto old turret HP
2 gunsx6, turret on old shield HP, shield mounted onto old turret HP
3 gunsx6, no turret, additional class 8 shield mounted onto old turretHP
Then, you can also buy the powerplant to fit, scanners, tractors and even the engine. With the engine, you can buy either,
1 Basic, as standard
2 Long range, takes up less volume
3 Superiority, better max force and lower cruise charging time (more volume)
4 Intercepter, Best max force and lowest cruise charging time (most volume)
Then, you can either buy these engines in either Bretonian, Liberty, Kusari, Pirate, Bounty hunter, Rhineland.. style, so you choose your own engine effects..
