That is a kick ass idea!

Wonder if Braben would get touchy about his ships being nicked tho? Probably not, all the elite games are available for d/l from his site.
Other probs are number of gun mounts available - i dont remember more than front, rear and turret (poss left+right too?) Rebalance MOD might make that easier 'cause AFAIK the missiles mount in torpedo slot so no need to put extra gun mounts. Definitely a thargoid ship! The Boa or Anaconda for freighters? A sweet one would be the ship from Elite itself, all black with just white lines defining it. Guns would spoil the look tho but this could possible be worked around.
