Which ship are you flying? I got through with a Dragon. The most important things to buy before the mission are: a good shield, the max amount of nanobots and shield batteries, and also a torpedo launcher would be a good addition. All you really need to do is shoot off your torps, fly away, then turn around and shoot off some more torps. If you run out of ammo, just repeat the process firing your guns instead. The best advice I can give, however, is to keep moving. It's never a good idea to sit still, even for a moment.
