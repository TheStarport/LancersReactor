Crash-Drive

Thu Mar 27, 2003 8:47 pm by Crash-Drive

By "hits harder", I guess you're referring to damage output, or damage x refire. Of the c9 guns, the Kraken 2 has the highest damage output; and, in fact, higher damage output with better efficiency than most c10 guns, except for the Nomad guns, of course. So, 6x Kraken 2 guns on a VHF can be better than a loadout including non-Nomad c10 guns; unless you're going up against ships with shields that have an advantage against particle weapons. The c9 turret with the highest damage output is the Skyblast Turret B, which you can get on Gas Miner Naha in Sigma-13, if you are friendly with the GMG.
