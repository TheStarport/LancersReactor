Thu Mar 20, 2003 9:02 pm by cslim

Your systems reboots because of a system error or BSOD (Blue Screen of Screen). Newer WIndowses (lol) auto-reboot at such errors. You can change that somewhere in the My Computer > Properties, i don''t know the exact location. Just uncheck the box that say automatically reboot at system error. Now you get, I think, the blue screen which states the error code etc etc.
