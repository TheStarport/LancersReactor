A tip, if I may, for when I can't find a base or other hidden location:

Pull up your map, then switch into the "patrol route" view. If you've spent some time flying around in the system, and you're neutral with the Red Hessians, then you should see areas where 2/3 of the patrol routes for neutral parties converge. In my experience, these are usually in the areas of hidden bases or jump holes.
