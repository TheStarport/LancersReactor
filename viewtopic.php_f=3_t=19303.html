Peking Duck, since you are level 20 you have beaten the game, and after that your rank is determined from net worth alone; there are no mission requirements. The game sometimes says that the next level requirement is 'mission' but that seems to be a glitch and it has no consequences anyway.

dm2021lab, when the game says "Next Level Requirement: Mission" during the campaign then this does not refer to freelance missions; it refers to story missions and normally you are given these missions by NPCs (Juni in most cases). Hit the 'N' key to see where you have to go to get your next mission. I guess you went AWOL after mission 9; in that case Juni will be waiting for you in the Kyoto bar.

After the end of the game your rank (level) is determined by your net worth and it can go down as well as up. Traders can fluctuate 5 to 10 levels easily, but even fighters can go down several levels in one go when they purchase expensive equipment. And if you use consumables (ammo, nanos/bots) instead of collecting & selling them then it is easy to lose lots of money fast, especially hs higher-level missiles, mines and torpedoes are very expensive.
