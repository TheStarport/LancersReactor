Zoner Conspiracy Theory

Ok, I've been playing this game for quite awhile now, and was always under the assumption that the Zoners were neutral to everyone. But, as I was playing, I did notice my rep with the Zoners would change, even by a little bit. Well, I've figured it out: It's the Bounty Hunters! The Zoners have some kind of "secret" arrangement with the BHG. Have you noticed Omicron Theta? How the Bounty Hunters are SOOOOO thick there, that if you're hostile to them, landing at Freeport 9 can be a real headache. And the Zoner bartender at Freeport 9 offers a bribe for the Bounty Hunters! What it is, see, is that the Zoners pretend to be neutral, have all these criminals come to their stations like nothing's wrong, and then the damn Bounty Hunters pay them off for their information so they can hunt them down! At least, that what it seems like to me...
