Ut

Thu Apr 17, 2003 4:53 pm by Ut

Cruise speed is actually handled in constants.ini. It's hidden, however, so you have to add the lines yourself. For the cruise speed itself, the line is "CRUSING_SPEED" under the engine section. If you want to go above 990m/s, though, you have to add "ANOM_LIMITS_MAX_VELOCITY" under the physical constants section.

Thrusters are handled under the st_equip.ini, and all other engine properities are handled under engine_equip.ini.
