I only died once or twice in the single player campaign, although I had to reload a time or two after forgetting to resupply. If the arch is your chief difficulty, practice using your strafe controls to fly laterally, and keep the force field between you and the enemy cap ship while you destroy the shield generators more or less from below. Remember to pack a torpedo launcher and plenty of ammunition, and enable it when (and only when) you are fighting stationary targets like these generators.

Best of luck.

If you can handle a little risk (and a little outcast l0vin'), there's a trade route I've found that's relatively safe except for Sigma 13.

Buy Diamonds in the New Berlin system (I forget which planet/station sells them cheapest, but I bought from New Berlin itself just for convenience), sell them on New Tokyo, buy Niobium and sell it on The Ring, in orbit of New Berlin. With 250 holds I made like $580,000 round trip. You should be able to make around $700,000 per trip. That'll finance a lot of shield batteries/repairs.
