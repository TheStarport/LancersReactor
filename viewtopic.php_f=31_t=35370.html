Hi vitaljuices. Nobody will flame you for a good well described question here.
It's a known issue, Reynen's working on it. I've found a workaround which is to NOT fight the Rheinlanders, simply stay put when you are out of the trade lane. Going toward the enemy draws them near Glasgow Station, which is where the problem is. Their beam weapons will wipe out the Rheinlanders in seconds. Tobias needs to show up for the mission to continue, and if the cap ship is down, then he never shows. He needs to arrive to "save the day" and then when all Rheinlanders are destroyed, Tobias will inform you to go through the gate.
Sorry if this is a bit of a spoiler, but I wanted to explain the workaround in as much detail as possible.
Boscoe
