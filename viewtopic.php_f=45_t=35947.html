Shogunate Forces of Kusari - Asgard RPG Server



The enemies of the People of Kusari are numerous. Some are obvious, some are not. The People of Kusari need you to help protect them, and here is your opportunity to do so. We are the Shogunate Forces of Kusari clan, representing the People of Kusari on the Asgard Server and we are recruiting. In order to join, you must meet the following criteria:

1.) You must have a Pilot History that shows you are a citizen of Kusari. This must state clearly that you "reside" in any of the sectors of Kusari. This can be accomplished by registering on the Asgard Forum. The next step is to create a pilot history in the Pilot History forum. The final step is to apply to the faction in the usergroup and to PM our leader, Korg, with a link to your pilot history.

2.) You must be willing to participate in the role play, contribute on the server and the forum, and be willing to interact with other players.

3.) You must be willing to follow the standards that the SF-K/-P/-C set in place.

4.) You must download the Asgard RPG Mod, Version 2.0 and 2.1. Directions on how to activate 2.1 are included in the download.

We have just acquired the title to own the Black Dragon Very Heavy Fighter. This one-of-a-kind ship is a creation of Master Shipwright Baene, and is sure to be able to handle any threat posed to it. In addition to this ship, we will soon have our own faction specific weapons that will make other ships run and hide when you log on.

There are three different ways in which you can participate, but be careful how you choose. You are limited to just two of the departments. We have a military (SF-K ), a trade corporation (SF-C ), and a police faction (SF-P ). However you decide, you future is up to you in Kusari. Promotions and awards are earned based on demonstrated in-game and on-forum role play, not on favoritism.

Interested? Then take the next step. Come to Asgard and sign up now.
