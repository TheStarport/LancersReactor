Sherlog

Fri Aug 29, 2003 11:03 am by Sherlog

The advantage of strong armour and high nano capacity is way overrated. Even the class 5/6 ships (especially Drake and Anubis) have enough nano capacity and armour to fly missions in a system like Omega-41 or Omega-11, and the class 6ers are tough enough to raid all wrecks in these systems in a single pass (Crusader has to fly two passes in Omega-11). If you have a class 8 ship or higher then you don't have to worry about radiation anywhere, unless you want to fly scanning patterns in high rad for an hour (in this case a class 9 or class 10 ship is recommended).

If you want to fly missions in a high-rad systems consider using a class 6/7 ship. Radiation repair cost will be at least one order of magnitude lower than for a class 10er (just a few thousand instead of up to $50k or more).

Of course, the Titan does last a few minutes longer in high rad than a Sabre or Eagle, so a Titan Liver could hide in a high-rad zone and hope that the Eagle/Sabre player would go away.
