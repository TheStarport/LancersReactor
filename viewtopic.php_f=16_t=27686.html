There is an extremely easy way to find the hole in the minefield. Goto Battleship Missouri (or whatever one is in New York) and start heading counterclockwise around the mine field. Every now and then look back at the Alaska gate and check your position compared to it. When you are directly in front of the gate (a few dozen Kilometers away also), you are either in front of the hole, or there is no hole.
