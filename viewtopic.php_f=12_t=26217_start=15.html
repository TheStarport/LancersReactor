It can get pretty expensive to get a private game of poker in one of the big casinos. But some of the less big, the "locals" casinos usually have some free poker rooms you can buy out for a few hours.

Ok, now about the nightlife, I MUST recommend a party tuesdays and fridays at a hotel called The Palms, it has a party called "Skin" happens to be one of the best in vegas, its like a open party area type thing with a huge pool and a lasershow and all kinds of stuff, drinks very cheap there aswell, sometimes free (depending on time or special events)

As for the more strip bars I can list them in two categories, so here goes.

Topless Clubs: Centerfold Lounge (SPECIALIZES in bachelor parties ) then you have Cheetah's, then one of the best in vegas called Jaguars (known for a special Elite Room, admission? 10 grand heh ), and a definite favorite is also "Play It Again, Sam" which has very cheap drinks and buffets and stuff

then theres nude clubs, which you probably dont wanna hear about (lets say most of them are seedy)

and ill just suggest a few also good spots (none-nude places)

Also at the palms hotel (the one with the party) theres a bar all the way at the top called the "Ghost Bar" which offers also open style place, but the catch is that the floor is mostly glass its a helluva a view. In the same hotel theres also a club called "Rain In The Desert" and its quite nice, nothing outstandingly special but nice anyway. And finally theres one of the most popular nightplaces in vegas which is a club called Baby's in the Hard Rock hotel and casino, its very hard to find, its behind a unmarked door down a stairway into the very depths of the ground but it has a HUGE aquarium, cheap drinks, and its always just a lot of fun, PLENTY of people there (its one of the most popular in vegas)

PS: This is the best time of the year to visit (winter) during the days its still very hot but winds can blow in from the surrounding mountains and at night its like a little slice of heaven vegas at night in the winter is the most awesome thing, cool breeze very nice. You dont need sunblock (except maybe at night what with all the lights and all) by the way, dont get caught gambling too much, the house wins 90% of the time (and the loosest slots are usually the ones you pay less for anyway, like 5-10 cents a pop)
