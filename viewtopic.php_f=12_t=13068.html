Fear Factor

Sun Apr 06, 2003 11:22 am by Fear Factor

The picture you want to post has to be online. Go the page that contains the pic. When you see the pic, right-click on it and select Properties. You'll see a string of URL for that pic. Copy the whole thing and paste it to your TLR post.

Example: I want to post this pic:

http://dvdmedia.ign.com/dvd/image/osiris_02.jpg

That's the url.

now add the obligatory {img=url} so that it would look like this:

{img=http://dvdmedia.ign.com/dvd/image/osiris_02.jpg}

Remember change the "{" and "}" into "[" and ""

When you have changed them into proper brackets, the image will appear on your post:
