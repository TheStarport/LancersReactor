First off: Fighting Corsairs is just WRONG !!

Just kiddin...

Corsairs use angelito's and salamanca's, wich are both neutron based weapons.
Neutron based weapons work best against molecular shields and worst against positron shields..

So get a positron shield! Level 9 Brigandine is the one you want.

But if you really must choose between lv8 graviton and lv9 molocular:

lv8 graviton = 5890 shieldstrenght (adv. Protector)
lv9 molecular = 6050 shieldstrenght (Barrier)

Now.. their weapons work 20% better against the lv9 mol shields then they would against graviton shields so:

molecular: 120 % damage with corsair weapons
graviton: 100% damage with corsair weapons
positron: 80% damage with corsair weapons

6050 = 120%(damage 100% + 20% extra)

So.. minus that 20% = 5042 is what your molecular shieldstrenght really is against corsair weapons

So I thing you'd better use the graviton lv8 ones as they give you:
6050 - 5042 = 1008 more shieldstrenght against corsair weapons...

Note: Corsairs also use missiles.. Off course this I can't include in the calculation.
