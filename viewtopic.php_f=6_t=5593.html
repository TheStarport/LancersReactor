i had a lot of trouble with this one too. What i found was use a coyote with one jackhammer and the rest screamers. blast the shield generator on krasnaya with the jackhammer, take out some turrets and then a couple seconds after you hear that torps were launched from the krasnaya, burn in the direction of the planet(neptune?). Anyway, the torp bombers jump in and if you got it right, one of them will be within 100k of you and you can kill it easily. After that you can go for another bomber(but it'll usually have shot at least 1 torp before it dies) or just go torp hunting. when the reliant tells you to take out the turrets on the krasnaya, just kill them in the order that they show up in on the targeting screen. The rest of the mission is pretty easy, and after all that work, the krasnaya makes one satifying explosion

I found that by burning towards the rear of the reliant straight away where the krasnaya will appear you can get to the krasnaya pretty quickly. Then have some fun with the shield generator followed by turrets, engines and any pesky fighters that get annoying. If you are having too much trouble with the torps get a few locking missiles (I forget the name as I'm updating some stuff at the mo!!) and fire one per torp. don't wait to see if they will hit just press T for the next torp. and work your way through. This culled from experience with the mission set at Medium Difficulty--

I had played up to the point that I was able to run the mission in a Mirage, sporting that big honkin' space-gun in her nose. There are three torp boats making the attack, and they let off the fish singly. That means that at any one volley, you have three torps inbound on the Reliant. Press 'T-T-T' to cycle through the three bombers and lock onto the first torp. Get the crosshairs in on the lead box of the torp, and set up a shot. At Medium Diff, I'm able to kill a torpedo with a single shot from the Mirage's neutron particle cannon. Down goes one torp, target the second and shoot. Go for number three. Now, the three boats let off the second volley. Lather, rinse, repeat. Wait for the third volley, then deal with the fourth volley. Four waves of three torps each, and you're done with the torpedo attack. That's when it's high time to turn the Coalition capital ship into a floating target.
