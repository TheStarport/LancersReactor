T-hawk

Sat Jun 21, 2003 6:41 am by T-hawk

Steering and aiming are the same thing when using mouse flight. In the menu for control configuration, you can see that you can configure keys for up/down/left/right turning (steering), which let you turn your ship independently of the mouse pointer.

There is indeed a control setting to toggle auto-leveling. I mapped it to L on my keyboard, and usually turn it off when I enter a fight and back on once I'm done.
