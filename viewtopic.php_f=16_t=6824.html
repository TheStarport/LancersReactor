Tue Feb 25, 2003 3:34 am by Stinger

My favorite by far due to relative "shortness" of the trip and the loads of money you make:

Take luxery consumer goods from planet Manhattan in the NY system to planet Baden Baden in the Stuttardt system. Pick up Diamonds from either Freisberg station (Stuttgart still) or the LWB base Darmstadt (again Suttgart) which is off the beaten path and cheaper than Freisberg. Take the Diaomonds back to planet Manhattan..rinse repeat. In a Rhino it's about 80k-120k on each trip, but in a Humpback (also bought in Stuttgart) the rate of return is right aorund 300K each way. This is a 5 system trip and can be done on average of 20 minutes per trip. Keep in mind there are Unionists, Xenos, Lane Hackers, Red Husseins, Bloodhounds, Outcasts, Bruchends (sp?) and any number of other factions you might not be on good terms with lying in wait at just about every trade lane. Running cargo puts a big red target on you and everyone can see it from many clicks away.

Ok, Edited with the base names :

By favorite trade route so far is taking pharmaceuticals from Shinjuku Station in New Tokyo (or Aomori Station in Honshu its closer), selling at Ruiz base in Omicron Beta, profit about 250k. Then I buy Alien Organisms at Ruiz and sell them at Shinjuku, profit about 300k. This is not bad for only a few systems away. Also, it lets you swipe the lvl 10 guns while your in Omicron Beta, for another possible 100k or so. Note that the Ruiz base is in the Border Worlds and you will have to be nuetral to the Outcasts. When doing this run, be as quick as possible; I have had lots of pirates TRY to demand I drop my cargo, but by the time I would have had to answer, they are too far away and I get away without a faction hit.
