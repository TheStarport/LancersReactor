Wed May 14, 2003 3:17 pm by Haenlomal

Actually, we have already figured out why the Paralyzer missile launchers don't drop from the wrecks. The technical details can be found in this thread (and by following the links found in it). But to summarize the problem:

The basic issue was that the hardpoint used by the Paralyzer missile launcher was located on the wings of the wrecks in question, but the wrecks have had their wings removed. So, no wing = no more hardpoint = no more launcher. More generally speaking, if a piece of equipment is defined on a hardpoint that is located on a wing (or other such similar extension) of the wreck, you can almost always kiss it good-bye. The only exception that I can think of (off the top of my head) is the Liberty Defender wrecks, because those wrecks are defined with one of their wings still intact.

-- The Haen.
