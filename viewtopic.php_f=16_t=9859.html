Yes, if you want to go above level 18, you have to finish the storyline, or download the patch here, or play muliplayer.

But you can take off and do your own thing at many different places, as mentioned in other posts, and after you and Juni are on the run, pretty early in the story, ALL the systems are open to you, no restrictions except what you can handle at level 10ish. I've traveled all through Kusari space in a well equipt dagger and done my own thing for 30 hours, ignoring the SP storyline. I could complete the storyline, and buy a super-duper-fighter and then cruise around Kusari space later, but thats not what I want to do, I want to explore now and I can do that while ignoring the SP campaign.

If your only goal is to rush up to level 30 and get the biggest, baddest ship, then you need to complete the single player campaign since its "in your way". If you want to be a pirate, trader, bounty hunter, sherriff, explorer, there are a couple of places you can do that without completing the SP campaign to do so, you just wont be able to do them (yet) in an uber-ship.
