Argh

Wed Dec 01, 2004 8:42 am by Argh

It's possible to develop a new storyline... it's just been a low priority for modders. We've been busy with the fundamentals- building working content, better MP game-balance, and the like. And this is likely to continue to be the case- building a new storyline that didn't suck would have to involve a lot of cutscenes, and without the tools that DA used (or equivalents) it'd be awfully hard... doing animations by manually specifying joint rotations and vertex positions, for example, is a royal pain.

So, it's not likely that we'll ever see a SP experience that's new. MP, on the other hand... even if it's just "MP on my computer only"... is something else again There are literally hundreds of mods- some good, some OK, and a few that are awful in between. Most of them are at least interesting, and add a lot to the game's longevity on our HDs
