Time for a new post

Wowsa, been long time since I've been here, but for those who haven't met me, I was one of the Freelancer beta testers, and part of this site when we just dreamed of Freelancer, and talked of Starlancer. I was one of the very luck few to be flown to Austin, TX, to go meet the creators of Freelancer. I spent many hours sitting next to Phil and Jorg, in their studio, trash talking and multiplayering months before the game came out. Then, we all went out for a Texas Barbeque.

Ok, I'm only mentioning this because I haven't been here for quite a while, and it seems the forum activity is dead. This is a truly active game, and in this incredible modding community, we can keep the talk going. I just myself reloaded the game after a long time away. I also loaded a few mods I played shortly afterward. This game has life, and so does this site and these forums. I hope there are enough of you out there to respond to this.

Let's get this activitiy moving again. OK,so the game is 4 years old. It's still a great game. Worth discussing.

Let's hear from you all?/????
