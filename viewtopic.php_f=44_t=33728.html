An easier way to hardpoint a ship

I have worked with 3dsmax for years and I know quite a few people here use it as well. Personally I find Milkshape to be terribly cludgy and a pain in the patoot to use. But it works well enough for exporting to CMP especially with the newest plugins. Anyway I started out hardpointing my ships in Milkshape and found it to be a real struggle as Milkshapes viewports and cloning system aren't half as useful as 3dsmax's.
Anyway this all brings me to my point.
Using the plugin from this site> http://www.mds.mdh.se/~elt01mcg
You can export directly from 3dsmax to Milkshape, preserving all of the scene elements in the process.
This means you can do everything from modelling to texturing to hardpointing in 3dsmax where it is much easier and then export it all to a .ms3d file.
Also this makes it simple to create yourself a hardpoint template which can be imported into you models and reused again and again.

Anyway I hope this all helps some people.

Crymson
