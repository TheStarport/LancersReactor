Dude if it says "cannot locate CD-Rom" you need to get a game refurbisher (at gamestop, funcoland, software etc.) to clean it, and if it still dont locate, you need to buy ANOTHER FREELANCER. I am on my 3rd freelancer myself for the same reason. I went through the same problems, and i found out that it was the CD AND THE LENS that reads the cds, i took a Q-tip and some alchohol, took out the cd drive, then dipped the Q-Tip in the alchohol and wiped the Q-tip on the lens. I then took a very soft fabric, (DO NOT USE TOILET PAPER), to wipe it off, and now it works perfectly. Believe me i had the same exact problems with the game. Im glad i could help.
