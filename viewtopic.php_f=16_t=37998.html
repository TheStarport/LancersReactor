if u downloaded the trial version u will see how the war started.

The alliance and coalition were going to sign a treaty called the 'Balma Treaty'.
This would bring peace over the two factions ever growing intentions.

Story:
The alliance has been under attack for several weeks by pirates. The capitol ship ANS Yamato launches a fighter wing called the Ronin to finnish patrolling the to make sure that there are no pirates following them.

The wing finds a derelict convoy that has been destroyed pirates. With the pirates still in the area, Ronin wing engages and destroys all of the pirates, ecxept for one that manages to jump out.

Ronin follows pirate and encouters 3 coalition cruisers. They now know that the coalition is attacking the alliance with pirate ships to disguise themselves and that the Treaty is a setup. Ronin wing quickly leaves the area to an early warning station, in order to warn the Yamato and alliance fleet.

When they arrive at the early warning station, it is already under attack by "pirate" forces. Two coalition torpedo bombers jump in and fire a large salvo of torpedos at the early warning station. Ronin manages to destroy the torpedos right before they hit the station. Then they destroy the remaining coalition forces and return to the Yamato.

When they get back to the Yamato, it is already under attack. They move to destroy coalition fighters. Next another wing of alliance fighters jump in and help Ronin destroy the coalition fighters. After that, the 3 coalition cruisers from before jump in and start firing at the Yamato. The Yamato fires it's own torpedos back at one of the cruisers. Ronin manages to destroy the torpedos, while one of the cruisers gets hit and blows up. Again the Yamato fires a second salvo of torpedoes and kills another cruiser. Finnaly the Yamato fires its last salvo and destroys the last cruiser.

Then as if the battle seems over, a deadly class1 coalition carrier jumps in a fires more torpedoes at the Yamato. Once again the Ronin wing destroys the torpedos before they can hit the Yamato. With the Yamato out of torpedoes and the carrier about to launch more tordpedos; a new capitol ship allined with the alliance jumps in and fires torpedos at it. The carrier explodes in a huge explosion.

The new ship reports that the bulk of the alliance front line ships have been destroyed.

The alliance fighters land on the Yamato and the battle is over... for now.
