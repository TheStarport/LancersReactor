Make millions in minutes w/o trading/killing

There is a big bug in the game that I found. There are several shipwrecks in the game that will give you loot every time you go back to them. You just need to leave the system (go to a different system or dock with a planet/base), then go back and it will be reset. Three such ships are the Pathfinder, the Hosho Maru, and one of the Escorts of the Hosho Maru in the Omnicron Beta system. If you loot all three of those ships and sell at Ruiz base, you will make well over 200,000 credits for each trip and you don't have to kill anything or take long trade routes to do so. You also don't need to trade in your sleek fighter for a bulky freighter either. Enjoy all you soon to be level 38's
