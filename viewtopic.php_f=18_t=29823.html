Look in misc_equip.ini All the scanners are listed, change "Range = " to a higher number.

[Scanner
nickname = ge_s_scanner_01
ids_name = 263750
ids_info = 264750
volume = 0.000000
mass = 10
range = 2500 <------------- EDIT THIS
cargo_scan_range = 2000
lootable = false
