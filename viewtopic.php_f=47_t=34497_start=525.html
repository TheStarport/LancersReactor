In the time since the war began the junkers have been quietly flying below the radar of most of the major factions, maintaining a tenouous neutrality. Realizing that the proximity of their primary bases to major house capitals was rapidly becoming more dangerous than it was worth, they began an ambitious plan. They constructed huge mobile bases by recycling scrap metal and parts from the extensive debris fields scattered across the Sirrus sector. They also salvaged engine parts and capital ship shield technology from recent battle sites. Once their mobile bases were ready they quitely abandoned their asteroid bases in New York, New London, and Hamburg and dissappeard. They have not been heard from or seen since. Until now.
