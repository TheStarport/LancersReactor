Fri May 09, 2003 8:38 am by D3ViL

To open the pirts on a DSL modem or Router, u need to connect to it.

To connect:
open a IExplorer window, type in : 192.168.0.1 or 192.168.1.1 or what the router now got for IP-adress. Then u wil be promted to give a username and password. If nobody has changed it the user and pass will be:
User: Admin
Pass: Admin (sometimes its just blank)

Opening Ports:
What firewall/router/modem u got? (It depends on what u got)
Mostly u will need to look for a place called Virtual Server/IP or something like UDP ports. There u will have to type in the ports u want to open, 2300-2400, and your internal IP-adress.

To see your Interal IP-adress:
Go to "Start", Run and then type ipconfig. this usually brings up a command line, thus the old DOS-Promts. U wil se a IP-adress field, a Subnetmask fild and a default gateway field. the IP u are looknig for is in the IP-adress field. Usually something like 192.168.0.5 (just an example)

Then u should be able to play.
Check this for more info We have got this isue alot and most pople post it in the Technical forum.
