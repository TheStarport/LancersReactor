Mon May 24, 2004 3:55 am by cjabates

1) pull up windows explorer, click on tools->folder options
2) click on the "file types" tab
3) highlight .3db in the list and click the 'delete' button

This will erase the registry entry telling WMP to open those files.

Right click on the 3db file. Select "properties" and then see that just under its name is "opens with" - click on the CHANGE button, and select the UTF editor from there. That will associate it with UTF editor instead forever more
