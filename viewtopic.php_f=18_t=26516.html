Free lancer''s lost it''s mojo for me... What''s ganna bring

It's been a while since I played freelancer for fun. I have played it a few times this month, but that was all for mod testing. Whenever I do play freelancer (with a shiny new mod) I quickly loose intrest. This game was a blast the first time I played it, but ever scince I beat SP, That feeling that compelled me to keep going and reach new heights, well, left. Flying a dimond - filled freighter over and over again lost it's excitment. Dogfighting insane odds became so easy it wasn't enjoyable anymore. In short, the game was really boring.

When raymend's mega mod came out, that kept me playing for about a week. All the mods now are fun, but I can't play them with enjoyment for more than 10 minutes, then I'm interested in some other game. New ships are cool to dink around with, but that feeling of haven'tIdonethisbefore always comes back. Playing multi-player is horrible, because when I'm not dealing with cheaters I'm busy with the lag that totaly kills the fun from multiplayer. I'm on 640k DSL too, and I don't feel like shelling out +$200 a month for a T1 line.

Fokes, freelancer was fun for the time be-ing, but it's lossing its fun factor really fast. Any suggestions about what I could do to increase the fun factor?
