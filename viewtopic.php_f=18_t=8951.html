Well, assuming you opened up shiparch.ini, there are a few things you need to know in order to tell what ship is what. First, you need to look at the actual nichname of the ship... if its li_something, then you know your looking at a Liberty ship. Then if you got that down, then look at things such as number of guns it can hold, and also the CLASS.

For example, if you are looking at the ship nicknamed "li_fighter" and you see it has 4 lvl 3 gun slots, then you know your looking at the Patriot, because its the only Liberty ship that holds exactly 4 guns and is a fighter. I hope this makes sense too.
