Together the three of them headed to the command center. It had long been dug out after the AT-AT walkers had nearly brought it down around their ears before. It even seemed a little warmer inside to. In the command center, a number of droids were busy with tasks of keeping the systems working at the peak of perfection.

A young robed woman was at the scanners, checking readings and sending reports out. “Have a seat gentlemen” she spoke, pointing to a couple of chairs near by. She had not even looked their way, yet seemed to know they were here and why. As she turned in her chair to face them, her robe was opened and showed she was wearing a light saber. So, Han thought, other Jedi survived the destruction too.

“Yes, Captain Solo, a few of the Jedi dd survive, though we are less than half te numbers we were.” she answered the unspoken question. “ My name is Athena, and I am a Jedi, trained by Master Skywalker himself.”

“More like a Jedi Master, if my sources are right” Locutus spoke, a wisp of a smile on his face. He always wanted to get one up on the Jedi. Athena gave a deferential nod in acknowledgment.

“I was here when the Remnant destroyed the other Jedi.” she began, then she reached for a file and handed it to Han and continued, “the woman in the picture was the one responsible for Luke and Lea’s death, but, I have not ben successful in locating her. The folder held the picture of Carla, with another man in the far back ground.

“Yes, I know, she’s my daughter, Carla” Han hesitated, emotion growing, “But, thanks to Darkstone, she is a Jedi again and I have reunited with her.” He struggled and finally got control. “She was on the darkside, but, Darkstone was able to rescue her and bring her back.”

“You had a daughter?” Athena asked, incredulous. “My information was that your daughter died when she was a baby, but this woman, are you sure she is her?”

Han nodded his head and locutus picked up the conversation, “She was trained in the darkside, and her anger at her parents seeming betrayal of her fuel the move to the darkside.” pausing, he continued the report of all that was now known. He explained the fleet commands, the training of an apprentice and he defeat at the hands of Darkstone. At the mention of an apprentice, Athena looked shocked, and clicked a couple of buttons bringing an image on the screen. It was of Neo_Kuja and another man, though he was hidden in the shadows a little.

Locutus had not seen any pictures of the man, but from the report, he fit the description. And he said as much. “The younger man in the picture, is or was a student of the other man, I had been researching both when they disappeared two years ago” Athena reported.

The story was taking an eery turn now, one Han was not looking forward hearing about. But, for the sake of Carla, he would need to find out who had been training her and why.

The ramifications were to terrible for that to be true.

True or not, he may be the threat behind it all now, But, I believe that Carla was not the only one being trained to be a sith” Athena spoke, her voice and tone, grave.
