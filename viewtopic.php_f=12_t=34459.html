Safaris = baie lekker!

Just got back from my South African adventure. It was fantastic. I saw so many different animals, and (almost) all in their natural habitats. The highlight of the trip was at the Tshukudu private game park. They have a tame cheetah there named Savannah. She lives in the wild and hunts but is comfortable around humans so the rangers will take you to her and let you pet her and give her water. If any of you out there have serious amounts of money to burn I highly recommend you go to SA and experience that for yourselves.
