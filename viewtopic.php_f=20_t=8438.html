Thanks! I turned down my hardware acceleration levels to emulation in the Direct Sound window and the game runs fine now. I really can't tell any difference in the audio quality.
Moondog: to disable Direct Sound, open DirectX Diagnostic, click on the sound icon, and turn the hardware acceleration level all the way to the left. The window should say that there are no Direct Sound features enabled. This worked for me. I hope this issue is fixed in a future patch.

Changing the level of sound acceleration won't make a difference in the sound quality per se, but if there are a lot of sounds being played at once you may notice a slight delay, for instance a ship's weapons could fire and you hear the sound one or two seconds later.
