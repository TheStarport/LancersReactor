StClair

Sun Jun 20, 2004 6:32 pm by StClair

I'm not a huge mod user, personally. Too many mods are just about super weapons and/or ships, or adding the author's favorite ship from some other science-fiction series (these often look terribly out of place, though many of the models are excellent), or new systems where you can buy the best gear and travel all around the map instantly... it all seems kind of cheap to me.

My three favorite mods, then, are:

Privateer Mod (in progress) - as an old WC/Priv fan, I'm really looking forward to this TC. It'll be a little funny driving a Centurion through trade lanes, but I trust the mod team to get the feel right.

Real Space Lite - I don't care for the changes found in the full mod, so I'm glad there's a version that just changes the look of the game to something a little less garish. The colors are still there, but it looks more like (fantasy) space.

Civilian House Scheme Mod - This does wonders to improve the look of the Hawk-Falcon-Eagle series (my favorites), as well as the faithful old Starflier et al. I use the Liberty skin, which looks better than the default civilian texture but is still neutral-looking enough for general use, IMO.

Honorable mention to the Open SP mod, which is basic but useful.
