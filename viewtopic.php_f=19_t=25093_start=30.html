Yes exactly. Missiles are just a tactical option. VS some players you will find them forcing you to eat alot of your own missiles, and it forces you to change your patterns, or else dicontinue use strategically for that fight. This is one of the reasons pure missile duelers are a risky proposition. Once a person gets your number, in terms of timing, they can see you finished very quickly. This also goes for mine-spammers, who get owned by CD spammers in short order.

When I see a pure missile ship I sit up and take note. I adjust my flying patterns, and remain wary. But I also have a feeling of confidence that I'll win as long as I don't get careless.

It's the guys with a balanced loadout that scare me, because either they copied it from someone else, or they are used to winning, and selected a balanced loadout to give them tactical options so that they maintain their win-possibilities in any type of match.
