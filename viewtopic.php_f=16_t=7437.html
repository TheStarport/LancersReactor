I disagree about the torpedoes, I use them frequently in combat to great effect, sunslayers kill every ship in the game besides Titans in one hit if their shields are down. Torpedoes are only slow if you launch them at slow speeds, if you launch a torpedo while moving at 199 m/s, it will be much faster than one you launched at 79 m/s. Also, try launching them when you're close to a ship but not so close that the blast radius will damage you too, because sunslayers do some nasty stuff to shields.
