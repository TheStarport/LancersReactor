Teabold - I hope you have better luck with that MX700 than I did. I originally got it as a replacement for my Microsoft Intellimouse Optical that I've had for about two years now, and was pretty disappointed. Don't get me wrong, it's a decent mouse, but my Microsoft mouse is far better for gaming. The batteries tend to run out pretty fast after a couple hours of hard gaming, and it's not terribly precise. I had a lot of trouble sniping with it in games like GTA 3 and any of the Unreal games, don't know how important that is in FL but it's something to consider.

Ali_Bubba: My Intellimouse has a total of 7 mappable buttons ( mouse scroll up, middle mouse, which is basically just pressing down on the mouse scroll, mouse scroll down, one side button on each side, and of course the left and right mice buttons ). I guess that's your "mouse stick" . You can probably pick up one of these for about 20-30 bucks now.
