Decent Vertigo-1 Ship Mod?

I searched the forums and didn't find any info on this so I decided to go ahead and post it...

What about the Descent ship for FL? I am a big fan of the Descent series (and Descent: FS1&2) and was wondering of anyone could make this ship for Freelancer. I'm talking about the ship used in Descent 1 and 2, not the one from Descent 3. I can't use 3D studio/Lightwave 3D worth a darn or I'd try to make it myself. I figure the ship would have the following specs:

Gun Mounts: 4 lvl 10 (That thing could have some big guns when upgraded)
-Two on each wing

Missle Mounts: 4
-Two on each rear wing
-One Missle Harpoint and One Torpedo Hardpoint per wing (The torpedoes could double as Mega Missiles

And all the other standard FL equipment mounts.
Don't forget the 2 headlights in front!

Of course the guns/missile hardpoints don't have to be lvl 10's, but i figured what the heck. They might as well be so you could mount something equal to the Fusion Cannon and Mega Missiles. Out of all the games I've played, this ship has always been my favorite design. Simple, yet lethal. Anyone interested in this also?

I also want to give a big Way to Go to TFK-Killermatrix and Hunterkiller for the Star Trek: Voyager ship mod. Very Nice!!

Thanks
