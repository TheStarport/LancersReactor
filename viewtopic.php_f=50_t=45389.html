New Mod

Im working on a new mod that updates ships and weapons in Freelancer. I may be a 2nd Lieutenant, but I dont usually come on Lancers Reactor all that often. My mod does alot of things and can really help people who have trouble in the campaign beginning flying a Starflier, as the starflier now has 5 weapon hardpoints that can fire backwards and 2 turret hardpoints that can fire in any direction!!!!

Here are the details:
Anubis is buyable from Planet Hamburg (in place of the Banshee).
Anubis can accomodate class 10 weapons.
All freghters must use mooring fixtures.
Juni's defender is added.
Pirhana now looks like a Legionnare when it explodes.
Anubis hitpoints are 10050.
Pirhana can now mount class 5 weapons and a class 6 turet.
Daggers explosion is instant.
Dagger can mount 3 mines and 2 thrusters. (1 points forward, I couldnt figure out the orientation.)
All VHFs can accomodate all class 10 weapons.
Order weapons are available on Battleship Missouri.
Champion and Adv Champion are available on Crete and Malta

If anyone really wants something in this mod, tell me and ill try and get it in.
And if someone could help me with the Daggers thruster pointing forward, ill be happy to fix it.
