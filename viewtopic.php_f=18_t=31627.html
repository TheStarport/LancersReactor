Mon May 24, 2004 9:35 am by baene

I have made a few ships with 8 revolute hardpoints, and they work fine. As someone above me said, there is most likely an error in either Shiparch.ini or goods.ini...go through the entries for your ship in both of those places. ALSO, Crabtree made a spectacular program for putting ships into FL. Do a search for a program called "Shipscripter". You can create the shiparch.ini and goods.ini entries for you ship in that program and either use the script it generates to put it in FL OR you can copy and paste the entries into shiparch.ini and goods.ini manually if you like, but his awesome program with keep your entries error-free.

The program is in the downloads section here...just do a search.
