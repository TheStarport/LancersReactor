Megistos

Sat Dec 11, 2004 1:24 am by Megistos

Best shield - Adv. Brigandine Positron (Class 10). Buy it on Malta or Crete.

Best guns - your choice. There's a wealth to choose from. In my experience, the Corsair Salamanca Mk II and the Outcast Wyrm Type 2 have proven good. That's if you're not talking Nomad weapons. Remember with the 8.33 weapons that although they're easy to hit with, they drain your energy very fast.

Bes turret - Since you're friendly with the Corsairs, I'll recommend the Salamanca turret.

Best missile - The Cannonball. By far. Don't be taken in by the Firestalker's "tracking" ability. A properly launched Cannonball will hit just about any target anyway.

Best Countermeasure - Adv. CM Dropper. There are only three in the game.
