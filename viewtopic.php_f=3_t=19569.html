Cynicism Inc.

Tue Jun 17, 2003 6:41 pm by Cynicism Inc.

I don't particularly care for class 10 fighters - not enough variety for me - and have found missiles to be a useful option in almost any lower-level craft. On Frostworks I have currently a Patriot, and mounting missiles on that boat provides a huge boost in firepower that any class three gun just can't match. When paired with shield killers, I've found the Patriot can be quite effective against all kinds of targets, barring, of course, ships carrying high class shields, which a class 3 shieldkiller won't even scratch. Then the missiles run out and my potential damage output is 0.

On Elite I've played for some time with a Centurion, and while missiles are not a good trade off for guns at that level, the torp launcher is certainly helpful for killing Eagles and other VHFs.

Make sure you have loads of cash before you try though.
