I have a dillemma.

I have a version of this mod with most of the ships incorporated, as well as appropriete weapon stat and equipment changes. The problem isn't that the textures aren't much to look at (though they are, because we don't have a proper texture artist) or that there aren't any IDS name changes so everything is incredibly confusing (because we don't have a proper VB editor either), or that the mod lacks any sort of helpful documentation. The problem is that my copy of Freelancer crapped out, and for the forseeable future I'm unable to do any sort of helpful work.

Oh, and it doesn't work with any mod managers either, as far as I can tell.

And while I never claimed this project had advanced beyond anything pre-alpha, I don't want to hoist a sub-quality product on what few fans we have left - yet if this project is going to stay alive, that's what I'm going to have to do. So I'm going to bundle up this thing, numerous warts and all, up to lancersreactor as a "pre-alpha release". Problems may be posted to a subsequent help thread that I'll do my best to answer. This is more than a bug hunt - it's about the future of the mod, and I hope the C&C from this belated release can eventually propel it to the top of the mod community.

For obvious reasons, I can't really ask anyone to download this, but to those who actually do persist with it, I thank you in advance.
