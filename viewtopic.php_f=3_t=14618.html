Sun Apr 20, 2003 7:02 pm by AFX

I been using Catapult missiles (just in reserves for when I really need them)

Since I have two launchers in my loadout it will waste two missiles when I miss.
The thing I found with missiles, is you have to pay attention to the turn rate in the weapon stats.

Some angles of attack are better then others. First pass of an attack they are coming straight at you.... good time for a missile. If you can get turned before they've completed their initial pass so that you can get a shot while they are facing the oposite direction... also a good time for a missile..

When being engaged by a large force .... (like when you decide to start attacking the wrong people right near a jump gate or trade lane)
They are a nice way to soften up all the ships that are basically just standing still while your dogfighting an individual ship.
Example would be when you are fighting a group with a drone frieghter or two present, the drones with basically sit motionless and just blaze away while your engaged with what ever fighter escort they have.... So it's easy to pop off a missile or two at those stationary targets while still engaged with your current target.

Just MHO.
