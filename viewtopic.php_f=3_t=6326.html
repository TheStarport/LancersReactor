There is no diffrence in top speeds. They are the same for all crafts in FL.

There is a diffrence in accelration depending on what craft you fly and the level of thrusters you have on that craft. So you can accelrate faster and reach that top speed faster. It also effects how tight you turn and excute other manuvers.

The better the thrusters you have the longer that they last...i.e. they use the thruster fuel more efficiently...this has been mentioned before in other topics but the speeds go as follows:

Normal Engines: 0 - 80
Thrusters: these put on an extra 120
Cruise Engines: 300

Trade Lanes: somewhere above 1000
