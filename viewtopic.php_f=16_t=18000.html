As for the answer:
You an find Nomad Guns on Nomads. Just shoot them, and they'll drop it. Must be lvl 33+ to do this.
You can find Nomads:
a) Near the Unknown Jumphole in Omicron Gamma (Corsair Graveyard)
b) Near the Unknown Jumphole in Omicron Alpha (Outcast Graveyard)
c) Inside the two Unknown systems (pass through any of the above j'holes)
Happy hunting!
