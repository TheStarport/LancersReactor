This is one of the main features of FL. Changing alliances. There are a number of ways to achieve this.
1. You will notice that different factions have different allies and enemies. Killing a lot of any factions enemies will increase your favour with that faction. Similarly killing faction allies will make enemies of them. This is a 'small steps' strategy and takes time.
2. Find a faction who is an allie of whomever you are trying to ingratiate yourself with and do missions for them. You rating with the other allied faction will improve over time a little.
3. If you are only just neutral and want to increase favour to the point at which they offer you stuff, one way is to join formation with a patrol of that faction, and kill whatever they kill.
4. Buy a bribe. Quickest, easiest, most expensive. There are bribe location spoilers somewhere on LR. I think.
