Myo

Mon Jun 07, 2004 7:05 am by Myo

Yeah, I've used a sunslayer a lot, and it really isn't much good for anything except very, very large slow moving or stationary targets.

Fortunately our server runs a battleship,cruiser and gunboats encounters mod, so it does actually have some use (lasers are still better against the battleships and cruisers, but its really satisfying to vapourise a gunboat with a single torpedo).

Get one if you've got a lot of cash spare and just want to have a good laugh blowing up transports and gunboats (if you've got an 'encounters' mod on), but for any serious work just get a cruise disruptor, they're far more useful.
