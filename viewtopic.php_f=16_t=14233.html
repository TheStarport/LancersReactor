easy early cash

to get cash easily in the early stages follow these instructions:-
0.raise enough cash to get a Rhino
1.Fill it full of water from Manhattan ($15)
2.Fly to Pittsburgh (two jumps)
3.Flog the water ($30-double your cash)
4.Spend all cash on Boron($120)
5.ly to manhattan
6.Flog off Boron($155)
7.start again
you should find that your reserves build up quite fast. Also helps if you take a mission at either end to add a little extra.
