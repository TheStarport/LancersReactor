if you dont like the way people mod their ships alter it to the way you want it !!!???

but to save you looking for the information i will post a quick how too!

to stop a ship having lots of guns look in the shiparch.ini, find the ship they added. in it there will be a few lines like
hp_type = hp_gun_special_XX, HpWeapon01, HpWeapon02, HpWeapon03 ,...... etc. and
hp_type = hp_turret_special_XX, HpTurret01(where XX is a numer between 01 and 10)

for no turret remove completely lines with HpTurretXX in them.

for the weapons remove all but one of the words HpWeaponXX (this will be a sort of trial and error until you find the weapon mount you want to keep) when you
have finished you should have up to 10 lines similar to
hp_type = hp_gun_special_XX, HpWeaponZZ (where XX is a number from 01 to 10 and ZZ is a number from 01 to 06)

this information is widely available on thes forums, and is easy to use.

this all means you can have a ship the way YOU want it.
