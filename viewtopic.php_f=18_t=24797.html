The almighty Vesuvias.

Yes, it's the Vesuvias, the big bad sporty carrier from Wing Commander fame.
(Original wcp image)

It's been a little side project I've been working on. Unfortunatly I've no more free time due to college and was offering the project up to anyone interested in completing this monstrocity.

This monster weighs in at 981 verts and 1273 polies.
I have the main model and the skins completed, all you have to do is put it together and possibly tweak the hardpoints.

Here are a few of the wip shots (in milkshape)

For reference, the official Vesuvius stats stand at..
Class Heavy Carrier
Length 1,600 meters
Mass 250,000 tonnes
Max Velocity 150 k/s
Standard Velocity 100 k/s
Max Y/P/R 6/6/6 deg/s
Guns Dual-Mount Anti-Matter Gun (14), Mass-Driver Turret (16), Dual-Mount Laser Turret (24), Torpedo Tube (2)
Shields Phase
Front/Rear/Right/Left Armor 4,000 cm
Core Strength 6,000 cm
Fighter Complement 400
Jump Capable Yes

24 turrets, and with its design,it should be able to fire all of them, top and bottom at the same time at any given target. Tired of those pansy freelancer capships? This beast will give you a run for your money.
