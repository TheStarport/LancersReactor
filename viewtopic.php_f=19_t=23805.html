Hauling cargo can be much more profitable than missions, if you know what routes to do. With almost no equipment (I've done it with literally only a shield), certain trade runs can net several hundred thousand round trip, even with a Rhino. That's much more than any mission you'll be able to get that the average pilot can handle in Liberty.

I'm a mercenary online. I won't be on until this weekend, and possibly not even then, depending on my schedule, but send out a broadcast in-game and see if anyone will fly escort. You could make the pay even 40-50k and end up with a profit if you make the route right. Depending on the route, I charge between 25k-75k for protection...most of that payed pending successful completion of the trade run.
