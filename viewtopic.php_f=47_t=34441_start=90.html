In terms of going back through what i do, i have to say not that often. I believe that by keeping what i write as simple as possible, it will help younger readers to understand the story. There are often parts that i do like to fill in, but i find that building characters up over time gives perhaps a more "feel" to them. For example Halap in my first story, he came about halfway through and i spent much of the latter part of the story trying to persuade the reader that Halap, while he had lied to The Order, was in fact a great being who wanted nothing more than the survival of his friends. Chapter 7 - Continued

Trent, Juni and King ran as fast as they could to the docking bay

“What’s going on?” Shouted Sergio. The loud cracks on the station making it hard to hear anything but explosions.

“The base is under attack. Are you ready to leave!?” replied Trent shouting equally as loud.

“No, my brothers ship is not ready!”

“We can’t wait any longer. This place is about to fall apart!!” screamed Juni.

“But…” said Sergio

“No, we have to go! Now!”

Sergio signalled for his brother to follow him to his ship. Trent, Juni and king locked themselves up in theirs.

“This is going to be close!” Shouted Trent as the 5 ships flew out of Freeport 10’s docking bay back into open space.

The explosion came shortly after followed by an eerie calm. The 5 ships came about and looked at the ruins of Freeport 10.

The debris and dust was all that remained. No ships were being read in the area.

“Juni, are you picking up any weapons readings?” Trent asked.

“No. Nothing. Not a single trace” replied Juni. “Who would do something like this”

“Maybe you should stay away from all Freeport’s from now on Trent. There won’t be anything left if you keep visiting them” said King trying to break the tension.

“Very funny King. Lets get moving. I don’t think we are going to want another close encounter”
