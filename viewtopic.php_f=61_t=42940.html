warzog

Sun Mar 19, 2006 7:28 pm by warzog

You might try changing:
population_additive = false
to
population_additive = true
They may be working, but have left before you got there, or ran away from you, depending on the AI.
Also...
Increase the density.
1 of those 3 is you!
And with a maximum battle size of 4, you'll probably not have a battle, depending on the pilot's AI.
Some pilot's AI's have them turn tail if you outgun them.
I'd increase the density to at least 5, battle size is okay.
Alternately, decrease the battle size to 1 or 2, and leave the density alone.
