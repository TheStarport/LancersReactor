Cargo Movers Inc.

I'm thinking of setting up a service on Elite, kinda in the same sense as the RES, however, this service will be aimed mostly at merchants and traders looking to make a fairly bit more profit on their cargo runs.

For a 20% commission on whatever load is carried on our freighters, CMI will ship additional commodities alongside your ship to anywhere in the sector, with a virtually guaranteed delivery (Neutral rep with all but 3 insignificant groups ) adding a substantial profit to every trade run you make.

Example: You are shifting 250 Lux Consumer goods from Manhatten to Cambridge. For the purchase cost of an additional 275 Lux consumer goods + 20% commission on arrival at destination, you are shifting an additional 225 units of lux consumer goods. This equates to roughtly 141,750 more credits for you after this rather basic cargo run.

So, what do people think? I may expand it to include storage space for anyone who may be doing mining missions.
