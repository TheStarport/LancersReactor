That's not a bug, that's how it's supposed to be. The argument is basically "if you were neutral with EVERYONE, then you could do anything you wanted without any resistance (except other players, that is)...hence making it FAR too easy to progress.

If you start completely neutral in a server, it simply means that they've made changes. Besides, trying to be friendly with criminals is more like an extra challenge to gain access to the more powerful ships in the game. Some may disagree, but it certainly explains why criminals have the best ships (like the Saber).
