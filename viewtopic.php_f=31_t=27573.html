Suggestions for Improved/Unique Missions @ Bases

One thing that I have not seen playing this and other mods is alternative or improved missions in the bars. I look forward to any possible improvements in this area or even a mission editor available in the future. Missions, I think should be a bit more unique and could even be designed to be added by server admins in multiplayer to provide more interaction with the universe and other players. Below are some suggestions that I would like to see, what would you guys like???

- Long distance passenger transports (Set with possible ambushes along route etc)

- Assassinations that you must Hunt your prey rather than be given a location (Could say Joe Bloggs trading somewhere in Nexus, destroy his ship etc)

- Missions could yield unique one off weapons or upgrades or open up new secret wreak location or even new system wormholes that cant be found anywhere else. These missions should be hard to find and require the right reputation or a minimum missions completed at a location.

- Server Administrators could add their own unique missions as their universe evolves (Simular to what is currently being done a bit) but with more frequency and more variety.

- Missions that could interact with other Players in game (If player is always killing others then there is put a bounty on his/her head at regularly visisted bases)

I know some of these are big ideas but I don't want to underestimate the creativity of MOD'ers out there.

WHAT WOULD YOU LIKE TO SEE ADDED?????
