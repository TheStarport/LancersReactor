Fear, to help you out, this is properly a gaming question so it should be in the Freelancer forum.

To help Fighter freighter, both sigmas have a jumpgate in them that will lead to a more civilized system. Sigma 17, which has Kurile and Atka, has a jumpgate in the north center of the nav map that leads to sigma 19.

Sigma 19, contains the Hawaii and Gas Miner Ogashawa , has a jump gate in the middle west of the nav map that leads to Kusari space (Honshu?). That will certainly get you to Sigma 13. In Honshu, you can take the tradelanes south to the jumpgate to Sigma 13.
