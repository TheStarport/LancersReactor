Hi all. I'm now playing the vanilla open game (story finished, just exploring the galaxy) and I was asking me a question: Do the torpedoes have any utility? I mean, in the story maybe you will need them, as there are some battleships which has to be destroyed, but in the open game?
The torpedoes are designed to beat capital ships. All right, you can use them in a "kill the enemy base" mission, but it's a waste of money, as those bases are not too strong and with over four or five passes firing you lasers the base it's out.
