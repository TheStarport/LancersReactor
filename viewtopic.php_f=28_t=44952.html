Freelancer: Galactic Annihilation Mod!

Hi all. I am Haven. The administrator to the FLGA Mod Team, currently making the Freelancer: Galactic Annihilation Mod.

In basic, the mod's title is the only thing you need to know.

Galactic Annihilation

No, the nomads don't win, in fact the mod's RP isn't that far...

The mod sets the galaxy in 552 AFS. After the Founding of Sirius.

The Nomad war takes place what, 800 years?

Anyway, this mod sets the galaxy at war.
There are 3 sides to this war.

The Galactic Alliance consists of Liberty and Bretonia

Galactic Iron Cross consists of Rheinland and Kusari

The Border World Regimen consists of the Corsairs, House Italia, and several inner-house pirate groups.

There was also some minor debate amongst the team and it came to this.

A total reblance of everything in the entire game
