Go to Start->All Programs->Microsoft Games->Freelancer->Examine My Account ID
This will give you your secret number. But I'm not sure how to help after that. FL seems to only give one chance to enter/edit this number: the first time an account on a PC tries MP. I don't know how cyber-cafes do things but do they really allow you to install a game this size and play it? If they do, be sure to uninstall, because anyone else trying it out would probably go straight to your acct. I'd be careful. In any case, that Account ID number, and of course going to the right server, should hook you up w/ your MP chars from anywhere.
