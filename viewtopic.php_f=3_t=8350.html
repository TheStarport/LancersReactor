Norfleet

Sun Mar 09, 2003 11:42 am by Norfleet

Using the nanobots will reduce your net worth, but generally, the loss of a few $200 nanobots isn't going to drop you a level.

What's REALLY happening is that your ship is being shot up. This reduces its resale value, which correspondingly reduces your net worth. THAT is the primary factor which is reducing your NW. When you subsequently use nanos, they repair your ship....but using this as a repair method is more expensive than repairing in port, and doesn't fix all the damaged systems, just the hull, thus compounding your net-worth reduction.

This is why the effect is so much more noticeable with nanos than with, say, shield batteries.
