So it was that a not-so-young me purchased the very first Grand Theft Auto. I was so inspired by the term "wasted" which appeared on your screen whenever you'd died, that I transfered it onto the internet and gave it a little quirk, thereby becoming Mass Wastage. But of course, by then the day of the 1337 had come, and frankly Mass wastage was not 1337 enough. Therefore, it became MaSwastAge for a brief period of time when randomly capitolizing letters was all the rage, shortly before becoming siply maswastage. Finally, after many a month remaining as it was, the evolution was complete with the help of national pride, and maswastage became maswastage/BG, the BG standing for Bulgaria, and not some online clan as is often the case.
