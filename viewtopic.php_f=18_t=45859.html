you have to edit the initialworld.ini and erase the first locked_gate section stuff

If you do not make a player wipe at the same time, this will not affect playerfiles. You need to globally edit ALL chars so that they no longer carry the locked_gate lines.
The problem is: if there are 30 players with no locked_gate entries and a player with locked_gate entries joins the server, the entries will get added to all the other players as well.
(at least i have been told that it "works" that way)

I deleted all the "locked gate" entries below the "Group_File" header in the initialworld.ini file.

What I'm doing here is working on a major conversion mod for Multiplayer.
This means that new players will have to download the mod. This also means that all new players will have the same files as the server files.

What I did to be on the safe side was create a backup copy of the original initialworld.ini and tucked it away on my desktop. Then, I deleted all the "locked gate" entries below the "Group_File" header. Next, I activated my mod then started the server.
Once I got my hands on a little more decent ship (to get around the nomads) I went to the first jump gate that's normally locked tight: NY to Magellan..
It opened flawlessly and I went right into magellan without a hitch. Although, the lane hackers weren't happy to see me near their base.
Of every system that normally has a Story Line Locked Gate or Jump hole,
(and even some other holes such as Hokaido to Tohoku) all those holes were simply unlocked as though the story line didn't exist.
