Check the "Attenuation" level for your WAV.
Attenuation is effectively a volume changer. Negative values decrease the volume, 0 is normal volume and positive values boost the volume.

That said... -6 is about half volume. You might want to put it up to 0. I have a tutorial posted with instructions on how to convert sounds etc. so that they are in the correct format.
