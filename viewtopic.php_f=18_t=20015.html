Tue Jun 24, 2003 1:08 am by praryboy

The powerplant data is stored in DATA\EQUIPMENT\Misc_Equip.ini
but you have to find out which powerplant goes with your ship. most times it is just the nickname of your ship + _power01 (i.e. Dragon = ku_elite, ku_elite's powerplant is ku_elite_power01)

then you can edit the capacity, charge rate etc.

OR

you can find your ship package in DATA\EQUIPMENT\Goods.ini, which says which parts come with your ship (Lights, Contrails, Scanner, Engine, Tractor Beam, Powerplant, Shield), and give it a better powerplant, one from a better ship.

Open Goods.ini find bwf_package (its the DAGGER Package) and change the line that says
addon = bw_fighter_power01, internal, 1
to
addon = co_elite2_power01, internal, 1
which is the powerplant of the titan

OR

Open Misc_Equip.ini find
bw_fighter_power01 (the powerplant for the Dagger)
and change the capacity and charge_rate values to whatever you want
