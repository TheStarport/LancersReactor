Best Video game cutscene.

This is a topic dedicated to the cool cutscenes in between play in video games. Tell about your favorites, funniest, gorey-est, or just plain wierdest. Anyway, my favorite: In Metal Gear Solid: The twin snakes for GC, I have 2 in a tie for 1st. 1st one is in a cell, in which you meet a guy, who is chief of a company, condenamed DARPA. The DARPA chief suddenly dies of what appears to be a heart attack. Soon after, Snake hears someone getting thier butt kicked outside the cell. He goes out, and sees the gaurd on the floor, naked, and his gun missing. A woman appears behind him. "Don't move!" she says, pointing a FAMAS rifle at him. "You gonna shoot me, rookie?" Snake replies. "Careful, I'm no rookie!" Says the woman. "Liar! Your eyes wander, and there's no confidence in them- the eyes of a rookie. Is the first time you ever pointed a gun at a person? You're hands are shaking." Says Snake. He turns around and grabs the rifle, pointing it at her chest. She grabs the mussle, hits Snake in the shoulder, and ends up behind him, aiming the rifle at him. "You've never shot a person, have you? You haven't even turned the safety off, rookie!" Says snake. "Open that door." She commands. "Why?" Snake asks. "So we can get the hell outta here!" "Looks like we'll be a little delayed." After, there's a fight against a good 10 gaurds. After they're dead, the woman says: "Thanks for the help." And runs out of the door. "Wait!" He says, running after her. Then there's a highly exaggerated sequence showing a lovely view of her behind. (Go Konami!) Snake runs into the hall, and sees her calling up the elevator. The then fires, and misses. Then she fires in a circle, aiming at nothing in paticular. Snake runs backward, and does a one-handed cartwheel, dodging bullets all the way, running to the safty of a doorway. He spins from the doorway, aiming at the elevator as it goes down. Too late. Anyway, post your favorite here. I'll post the other one once I have time.
