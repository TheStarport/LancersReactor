Some of the places on the above list do not help with zoners

Here are some facts: Zoners and Xenos have some strange working relationship. If you kill enough Xenos enemies, like Outcasts or Liberty good guys [pick a side, you can dock at their bases even if you are slightly hostile to them. Pay the bribe for the Zoners at either Nome base in Kepler B5/5.7, or Quray base in Colorado (but you won't get both).
NOTE if you land on the base and the bartender will not offer you a bribe for Zoners, you must leave the base (not just the bar) re land and check again. Do this untill it is offered.
