Chips

Sun Apr 30, 2006 2:08 am by Chips

Remoev thruster mounts from the shiparch.ini, and just ensure that the goods.ini (ie ships loadout when bought) includes the thruster mounted on the thruster mount.

You can also remove the misc_goods.ini or st_goods.ini entries for the thrusters as well (whichever file they are in), and then remove the thrusters from sale at all bases, on equipment for all wrecks (loadouts.ini - search for secret i think it is) and finally from all loot drops from missions or just normal NPC's.
