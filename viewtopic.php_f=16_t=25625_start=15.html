If you download Victor's 1.3 mod from the downloads section, you will be able to buy a refitted Anubis (9 weapon mounts) in addition to the Liberty Cruiser and Liberty Dreadnaught at Norfolk shipyard. The rest is for you to discover

The Bounty Hunter fighters are generally underrated. While they are not well armoured, they do offer in agility what the Corsairs offer in their ability to take punishment. I might not put much money on a Piranha vs. a Legionnaire, but even so the Bounty Hunters should not be taken lightly.
