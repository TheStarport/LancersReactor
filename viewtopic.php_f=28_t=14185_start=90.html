Here's some answers

1.) The reason you crashed when you had fully loaded guns on the Dreadnaught was that a ship can have no more than 43 weapons on it, otherwise freelancer will crash, The only ship that HAS more than 43 weapon hardpoints is the Dreadnaught.

2.) Only way i know how to solve the Battleship cargo problem is : Prior to buying your battleship, buy 1 of some sort of cargo, like 1 oxygen. Then when you have your battle ship, sell it. You can now buy/sell cargo but it seems you cant tractor in commodities.

Hope that helps
