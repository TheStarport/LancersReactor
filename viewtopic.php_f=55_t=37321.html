Megistos

Mon Jan 24, 2005 12:43 pm by Megistos

If you think it's hopeless, start a new game. Then, make sure to loot the Flint in the New York system (check www.arbiter.de for location), and sell the Cardamine on Manhattan for an instant $30,000.

Anyway. There's an easy trick to evading cruise disruptors and getting away from pursuers. Kill your engines with the Z key, then use a brief burst of your thruster. You'll continue to move at thruster speed. Now, turn around to face your enemies. You'll still be travelling in the same direction (which is now backwards from your POV), but your enemies will scatter and you will gradually open up a gap between you and them. Once they disappear from your scope at 2.5K, just turn around and engage cruise. Job done.

Don't worry if you're having trouble - Freelancer fulfils the old cliché: easy to learn, tough to master. Experiment with using your thruster in short bursts in combat to reduce getting hit and thus reduce your spending on batteries.
