@ Dionisius - Do you stick to the original game's nickname convention? I mean, except for the St02c, St03b and FP7 systems (which do not show up on market listings anyway) all systems have a nickname composed of two letters and two ciphers, and all bases have a nickname composed of its system name + "_"+ two ciphers +"_base". E.g. Aa01_01_base, Li01_18_base, Br09_05_base and Xw01_02_base.

Maybe your problem is that you've used more exotic names, like Rh08_Konigsberg_base or whatever. I'm not 100% if this could cause a problem, but my mod was considerably messier until I adapted the orthodox nickname convention.

Black Eagle

Thu Mar 10, 2005 1:38 pm by Black Eagle

Hi!

At first, look in the post above mine, as this is a common problem!

Then one question:

I had this problem with one of my bases and none of the above fixes worked. Finally I solved it this way:

My base was similar to Tekagis arch and therefore had two parts. The Asteroid as first part and the Docking point as second point. I had given the same ids_name to both of these parts (of course it was the same as the strid_name) and could not get the trade route to appear.
As I deleted the ids_name from one part, it worked.

So be sure you have:
1. The strid_name the same as the ids_name
2. All bases named in the logical FL way (Rh01, Rh02, Ew01, etc.)
3. All bases over the systems in universe.ini and in a logical order (depending on the names)
4. If your base has two parts (like tekagis arch), only one part can have the ids_name

If you have all this, it should work.

Good luck!

Black Eagle
