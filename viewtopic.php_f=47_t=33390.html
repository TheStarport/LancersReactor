- -Prolouge- -


2 years have passed since the nomads were defeated, the Sirus Sector is returning to normal, and order has been restored. During all of the passing time, 7 old friends met up, and decided to go out for a drink in the Manhattan bar...




- -Chapter One: Old Frieds Unite- -

"So what'll be your pleasure?" the bartender cheerfully asked. "Well, I'm looking for somthing cheap for my friends back there, we just met up so I thought we should celebrate." Replied William Markson. "In that case, I got the thing your looking for, how bout 7 glasses of Liberty Ale?" "Sure why not." William picked up the drinks and carried them off to where his friends were sitting. He had been through alot in his life, his father was a trader, and his mother was a bounty hunter. He was left alone at the house for hours, even days at times. "I got the drinks." He said as he sat down "Liberty Ale? Is that all they have?" replied his stong headed friend Mark. "Thats all they have unless you want to pay an extra 10 credits." "Yea, point made." Mark had a problem with taking no for an answer, his parents were outcasts and they taught him to not take orders from anyone. After his parents died in a shootout with the corsairs, those were the only words he has lived by. "So, what has everyone been doing these past years?" asked Sarah Lindrey. "I've got a job at the commodities office." Said Patrick Harris. "Ah cmon Pat, you know that junk is boring." replied Mark "Well then what job do you got?" "I'm thinking about joining the L.S.F." "And shoot outcasts?" "No, stop battles like the ones my parents died in." The comment from Mark made everyone feel uneasy. After another round of drink everyone loosened up a little. "So, how bout Jack, where you been?" Jack Marlin was another of William's close friends, and he grew up in a great neighborhood, with rich parents. "Me, you know I went into hiding when those freaky nomad guys came" "Yea, kinda cowardly don't you think?" "Well at first we weren't going to leave Liberty, but My dad was nearly killed by one of those, and he told the family to go hide at Freeport 4." About an hour passed, when Jennifer Robinson got up and said "Well, it was fun seeing you again, how bout we meet up some other time, but make sure it's not a bar." "Why not?" Replied William "Casue I thnik Rick hads had enough." The group of six now relized where there friend Richard Anderson went. Rick was sitting at another table with a group of people trying to eat their meal. Rick was babbling when one of the people said "I don't know who you are but go away!" "You Can't tell me what to do! Your an ugly moron!" Rick replied "Excuse me, sorry bout everything, he's with us." William quickly said "You don't know me! And another thing, whats the deal with the collision alarm! I mean, we are in space, you just bounce away like a bird..." Richard trailed away till he fell asleep and slouched on the table. William pulled him off, and went out the door his friends following quickly. "You know, we really helped you back there. That guy was about to kill you." William quietly said to Rick, who was still unconscious. "Is he gonna be okay?" " Well, besides a massive headache in the morning, he should be fine." Suddenly Rick got up and yelled "Gun!" then he began to stumbly off. "He's just really drunk as I said" William explained. "Maybe he's not you little punk!" The group of people turned around to see a man in black clothes pointing a pistol at them. " Get up off the bench..All of you Now!" The group got up their hands raised. Jack looked out of the corner of his eyes and saw Rick coming back. Then Rick pulled out a gun and fired 4 shots, all missing the target, but it drew his attention away from the group. Mark jumped forward and knocked the gun out of the mans hand. Yet the man had a knife hidden in his sleeve. He cut a small wound into Mark, and was about to finish him when Richard got a lucky shot hitting the mans hand. "Ahh!" The man screamed and pulled out another pistol, now firing at Rick. A shot clipped Richard's leg, and he fell. Then out of the corner of the man's eye, he saw Jack holding a pulse shotgun. Jack pulled the trigger and the man blew back onto the pavement. "Where the hell did you get that?" William yelled "It doesn't matter, Liberty Police will be here any minute let's get outta here!" "Wait" Said patrick. "Look what fell out of his pocket." The group gathered around and saw a list with all their names and pictures on it. "What's going on, how did they get those pictures?" asked Sarah "I don't know, but we have to fi..." He was cut off by the loud noise of a Liberty Police officer yelling at them to give up. "Let's scramble!" Everyone left quickly. Richard pulled his head up, he was drunk, but he still could feel where he was shot. Suddenly Police surounded him guns drawn.

Here's a problem I have in any fanfic I try to write; overdependence on quotations. After a while, all those marks start to blur and you have trouble keeping your characters straight, especially with 7 main ones all chit chatting at once. Also, don't introduce facts arbitrarily. The backround about your first guy was well placed, with the intro bit about why he knew the bar well, but the rest of it felt forced. Wait until it needs to be divulged before revealing it. Just let the story tell itself, through action and movement, not necessarily through talking heads. Beyond that, the story seems to have a solid start, and I am eagerly anticipating where it's going next. --- VH16
