2 Million is doable in less than an hour of trading. And that's just one fairly random route I discovered during the story-line.

Since this is the spoiler forum I feel I can post the actual route (there are undoubtedly better routes - as I insinuated, I haven't spent any appreciable time trying to find the best routes):

Roppongi (New Tokyo) to Tau-31 Gate Site (Tau 23) w. Gate/Lane Parts:
$560 buy, $1400 sell ==> $840 profit
Tau-31 Gate Site (Tau 23) --> Narita Outpost (New Tokyo) w. Beryllium
$300 buy, $780 sell ==> $480 profit
TOTAL PROFITS: $1320 / cargo unit

Kusari space has a nice little 175 cargo unit freighter available. Using this, a round trip gathers well over $200,000 in profits.

Since the route (loop) takes about 10-12 minutes, that's well over a million credits an hour.

It does require some neutral or better faction standings to the criminal elements in the area (which just happens to be the case during the story-line).
