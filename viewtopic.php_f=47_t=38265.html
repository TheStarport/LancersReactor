Signups for Control Your Own Faction Part 5

Welcome to one and all!

The long-awaited CYOF 5 is here!

Here, you will have an oppertunity to sign up for and participate in the most popular Freelancer RPG ever to hit TLR!

There are three faction types that you may be.

House- (The 4 Great Houses)- To be a house, you must have obtained the rank of Ace in CYOF.

Major Faction- (BHG, GMG, IMG, BMM, Corsairs, Outcasts, Dragons, Hessians)- To be a Major Faction, you must have obtained the rank of Veteran in CYOF.

Minor Faction- (All others)- Any player may be a minor faction in CYOF.

As a review of the ranks:

Veteran- 1 FULL, official CYOF game.
Ace- 2 FULL, official CYOF games.
Admiral- 3 full, offical CYOF games.
President- 4 full, official CYOF games.
Master- 5 full, official CYOF games.

If you have never played CYOF, then you have the rank of Player.

I will post the full portion of the rules pertaining to signups in a day or two, and any questions regarding signups can be either posted or emailed to me at ww2jacob@gmail.com.

The deadline for signing up is next Monday, March 28th. That is when the game will begin.

Enjoy the game!

-ww2jacob
CYOF Rulemaster
