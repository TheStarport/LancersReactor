Coolbeano

Tue Mar 29, 2005 3:06 am by Coolbeano

Should be possible. Hopefully, no unmodded people should be able to get into the modified alaska because their alaska gate would be locked. If they did get in, then it would crash.


however, gate access is defined GLOBALLY, by the administrator. So it doesn't matter what the lockage info reads in the client, modded or unmodded, the admin sets their availability. So if one person can go trhough alaska, then so can everyone else.

a good alternative however, is to leave the original gate locked, but make another gate deep enough in Zone21 to not make any unmod people crash, with a different exclusion path.
The different exclusion path will allow people with the mod to fly through safely, and to another person w/out the mod, will simply see them flying through mines, unhurt. They can't fly however, and will be left behind, all the better so they don't crash near the gate.
This allows people with the mod to gain access to Alaska without disturbing anyone else.
