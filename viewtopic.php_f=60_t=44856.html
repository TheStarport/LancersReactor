Hello everyone. Nice to see the FL diehards won't die, just like me. Let's see, October 2006, that makes it 3 years 5 months my server has been up and running. As for my website that's almost 3 years it's been up. Seems like a long time.

I've seen a lot of people come and go. Some are stll around but most are either a shadow or gone. I also remember the fights we had on Elite both on the server and in Lancers Reactor. Those were the days!

Some of the people I remember:
Wan Solo, Stinger, Akataka, steel, Argonus, X-Treme, RN clan, NEO clan, Tasiin, hairy, Vortex, Neopolitan, Hellz, Captain Tyler, Frobozz, Wasabe, programexpert and dozens of others I haven't named or just plain couldn't remember.
