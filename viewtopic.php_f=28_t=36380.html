If you're going to rebalance weapons... I have some suggestions:

1. Remove about 70% of them. FL has dozens of totally-redundant weapon designs. This can be accomplished through hacking the loadouts.ini and market_misc.ini files- there's no reason to remove the current guns... just select the ones you want, and edit those two files so that the other ones just never show up in the game.

2. Look at the way FPS games do this. Good ones, like Counter-Strike, have almost every weapon balanced so that:

A. Even the weakest weapon can kill the strongest player.
B. Skill matters.
C. Skill > $$$
