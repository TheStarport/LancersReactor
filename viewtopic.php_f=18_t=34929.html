The word "lol" springs to ming. You cannot make a new gun, then go load it up on your client files and waltz onto a server and decimate everyone - it doesn't work like that. You also cannot make it have 100000000 damage cause either - as the damage line is taken from the servers side as well (ergo - making a class one do 10000000 damage and logging onto a server gets you.......nothing). There are plenty of weapons mods that let you cheat online already - so I am pretty darned certain nothing is going to happen from your idea.

However, no doubt some haters will post in the comments section if released to the public - but if its a brand new gun - then their moans are mute due to it not being used on servers.

By the way - this has been done, and you can still use thrusters. You cannot drain the thruster power - so they can still move anyway.

SO if its for SP - then you will have to:

Give NPC's proper power plants rather than the unlimted ones
Remove the thruster use from the pilots behaviour etc - OR remove them from the loadouts of the ships.
