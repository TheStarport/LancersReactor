I've tested out refire rate and actual damage done in realtime fights and looks like using high fire rates isn't better. It seems after testing with 2 differening setup ships that not every Shot counts as a hit if you just keep constantly firing against either using slower fire rates or bursts of fire.

So a simple test to show it, find a lone Rouge Hound. With Blasters and Skyblasts fire constantly at it a note the number of shots taken to kill it. Then find another and fire 2 - 3 volleys of fire only and you should kill it quicker. Further tested with Hessians and Corsairs to rule out shields affecting the results.
