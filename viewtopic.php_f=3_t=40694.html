Cold_Void

Mon Sep 05, 2005 2:59 pm by Cold_Void

yup....
1.assign keys to the ACTIVATE controls(ignore the ASSIGN controls,they do nothing)
2.launch to space
3.activate first group,select/deselect active weapons for that group in the gun list
4.activate next group,select/deselect
5.and so on

when you activate a weapons group it deactivates/activates weapons the same as you last had them when that group was active.

weapons groups are saved on a per-character basis,which means you can have different groups for all your ships
