Game Guide: Level 10 Weapons Locations/Stats

The old thread on class 10 weapons was becoming rather confusing and was somewhat incomplete regarding where to find certain weapons, so I took it upon myself to find them all and post their stats/location.

I have also calculated two statistics for each, which I will call 'power' and 'efficiency.' Power refers to damage over time, or what can kill your target the most quickly. It is calculated simply by multiplying hull damage (shield damage is half hull so it wouldn't affect anything to include it) and rate. This does rely on the assumption that rate is a linear value but that seemed a pretty safe bet based on what I was observing in the game for refire rates. Secondly, I calculated efficiency which is how much energy is sucked up per unit of power - it is calculated by dividing power by energy. In the case of the Nomad cannons and blasters this is, of course, infinite (you can't divide by zero). The Nomad cannons come out to #1 in power and efficiency, with the blasters not far behind. Of the non-Nomad weapons the most powerful were the Cerebus and Diamondbacks (which are the same weapon with different names), and the Prometheus edged out the Jade, Silverfire, and class 9 Wyrm IIs by a hair for efficiency.

It seems that the optimum loadout is Nomads and Wyrm IIs, although I did not calculate all class 9 energy weapons so I may be incorrect here.

If you find any errors in the following information, please let me know and I'll update.

Enjoy,
--Ryvar

The only level 9 weapon with the same projectile speed as the L10s is the Corsair Salamanca Mk II (9/489/244/600/600/4.00/97). Damage/sec = 1956. Damage/energy = 5.

The Outcast Wyrm II has a projectile speed of 700, which makes aiming harder when you're also using L10 weapons.

I'm quite fond of the GMG Skyblast B (9/244/122/600/750/8.33/61). d/s=2032 d/e=4. You can find them at Gas Miner Naha. It has the best d/s ratio of the L9 guns, and is fast both in projectile speed and refire rate. I've been using six of these, but I just went nomad hunting, and now I'm going to try a nomad/salamanca combo...
