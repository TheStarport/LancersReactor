I am currently working on a three part release of the starlancer models recently proveded so graciously by Hobbes.
The First part will be a vanilla "modders" ship pack with simple scripts ready to be modded and used by any mod maker.
The Second part will be a full blown "players" ship pack with all specifics laid out and ready to be installed by any player.
The Last part of the release will be a complete addon pack for Evolutions 1.28, of course that's assuming Chips gives it his blessing.

Also I would like to note that these ships will come with a few extra features. The CMP's will all have 3 shield HP's, 5 miscellaneous HP's, 3 Thruster HP's, and an abunbdance of gun turret and torpedo HP's This should make them all very modder friendly as they will have room for just about anything you want to set them up with. Oh and they will come with a handy graphic HP chart which will display where the HP's are located so there is no need to guess.

Anyway look for them later in the week.
