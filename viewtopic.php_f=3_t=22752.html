New Challenge/Occupation on FL!!!!

Ok, for all you pros out there, here is a new anti-outcast challenge/occupation.

1. Go to Tau - 37.
2. Dock at FP10 and get ready.
3. Fly to the Omicron Alpha Jump hole and dock.
4. Sit out side the jump hole when in Omicron Alpha.
5. Kill any of those drugys that fly by, you should get some big battles, the bounty hunters will help you.
6. When you done, tractor in Costumer Goods or Phamasuitals and go the Tau - 37.
7. Sell all your salvage at FP10 (sould get you 120000+ credits).
8. Repeat steps 2 - 8 until done.

If your feeling lucky then:

1. Fly to Matla and sit outside.
2. Kill any outcast that you see.
3. You sould get about 10+ Elite Sabre flying Outcasts on you.
4. Kill them all.
5. Do Steps 6 and 7 above. You will get even more credits.

This is a great and fun way to get credits. Only do it if you have a very heavy fighter and max capacity of weapons and repair stocks. Youll need it.

Post how meny outcasts youve taken on at once here and your story(no cheating/lying).
