ShadowFire789

Fri Feb 06, 2004 1:22 pm by ShadowFire789

Try the Game play Patch mod. It's great for people new to the game. A few tips for MP:

1. Don't expect to be fighting players in a kick-ass ship right away. It takes time.

2. Start trading. Trading pays off much better than missions. Do missions in your starflier until you have enough credits to get a freighter.

3. Join groups. That way you don't have to do missions alone, and you'll have someone to talk to on long trade runs.

4. Most importantly, HAVE FUN!

Happy freelancing!

kimk

Sat Feb 07, 2004 9:14 pm by kimk

IMHO the crusader looks too fat for my taste. eagles all teh way !! <no, not a best ship thread.

as for newbie tips, you should figure out all the controls and set it to your preference. i'd advice not sticking 'cruise mode' as shift+z or whatever it was. change it to one key. it'll save you heckuva frustration later (coughracecough)

then, when you're exploring, it'd be wise to open nav map, go to 'patrols' its the one with the loopy arrows. then see where many of the routes converge. at that point, there is a suprise for you. a base? a jumphole? empty space? no one knows but you usually won't be disappointed.

trading is good at the start as it brings more revenue than guns do. but later on in the game, the cargo hold doesn't really mean much when you can carry an unlimited amount of firearms in god knows where but brings so much more cash.

last but not least, find out which faction you hate most, then when you finish the campaign, kick their sorry butts
