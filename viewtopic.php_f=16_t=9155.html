Easter Egg, Planet of the Apes

Yo there,

I'll skip the the introduction as I'm a first time poster and get to the meaty stuff.

I don't think this has been posted yet, so here's what I ran across.
I was flying about systems, attempting to act as nothing more then a cartographer, when I ran across a jump hole in the Omicron Gamma system. It's surrounded with the hulks of Corsair fighters. If you head through you'll find a system named "Unkown" that's only got two planets. Careful, there are enemy spawns all through this system. One of these planets is labeled Planet Primus, and if you take the time and effort to land on it, you'll find that everyone on the planet is monkey headed. They're all listed as the faction "Monkey" and there are no goods or equipment for sale, but it's pretty freakin hilarious.

Hope you all can find and enjoy this,
