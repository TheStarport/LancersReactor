I started out using DOGA. It is an excellent tool for beginners and I would not be where I am now without it so chill Skystalker.

That said the parts used in DOGA have some glaring flaws that are exposed when you export them to other programs.

I would recommend getting Metasequoia which is an excellent free 3d modelling program that will further expand your abilities. You can also make custom parts for DOGA with it as well. I still use it to start my models befroe finishing them in 3DS Max.

Tightan Discreet makes 3ds Max NOT Milkshape. You'll find milkshape here
