T-hawk

Thu May 15, 2003 8:08 am by T-hawk

Do note that you can't always get a bribe in the listed locations. In New Berlin, I believe the person in the bar who offers you the bribe is a member of the Red Hessians himself; he won't talk to you (you'll see the empty speech bubble icon) if the Hessians hate you already.

To bribe a faction that's hostile to you, you need to get the bribe offered by a member of a third-party faction, that's ALLIED with the faction you want and also doesn't hate you. The easiest way to do that is find a base owned by that faction. For the Red Hessians, if you are friendly with the Corsairs, you can go to Cadiz base in Omega 5 and a Corsair member will eventually offer you the Red Hessian bribe.

You don't need to be friendly with Hessians to buy the Saber, just neutral.

Go find a Rheinland police supply depot, or any Rheinland trading house supply depot, and raid it. Or, shoot down some transport ships.

Don't kill them all, just kill or raid enough to affect the Hessians rep to neutral while still keeping the Rheinland police/military/trade houses in neutral.

Same will work against Bounty Hunters.
