This happens to me, usually because of Outcasts, let me explain:

Say, if you're neutral to Lane Hackers, but Outcasts hate you, and are KoS.

Because Lane Hackers are allied with Outcasts, if Outcasts decide to try to kill you outside Mactan, the Lane Hackers help, and become hostile in the immediate area. Therefore, you can't dock with Mactan.
If you go far enough away (15K, or something) from where you were attacked, the hostility disappears, and you can try again, and Hackers will once again be neutral again... unless there are more Outcasts around.

This can happen with any faction, in any system.

Easiest way to go about this is to do the mission where you flee from Bretonia and go to Kusari (7 or 8, I think). When you reach Cali, Outcasts are neutral to you, so you don't need to worry about them rallying the Hackers against you.
