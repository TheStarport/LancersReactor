Wed May 07, 2003 2:01 pm by NukeIt

The FL campaign may not have as many missions, but you can actually finish the SL campaign faster. The thing is that FL doesn't let you fail a mission and move on, while SL does. So in SL you can just launch, eject , and move on immediately for a good number of the missions(all you have to do is know what missions aren't critical). FL requires you to spend time in between many of the missions to build up your level, and your travel time between two points in space is MUCH longer.


In other words, the FL campaign is still a decent length, you're just not noticing it because there are fewer total missions.
