To solve the powerplant issue, first look at the nickname of the ship i.e. li_fighter. Then look for it in the goods.ini. The nickname of the are its in will tell you where the next related good is in the same file. The powerplant type is there. After that, open up the misc_equip and the look for the powerplant type specified in the goods.ini
