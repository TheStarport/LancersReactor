Open Omega 41's ini (bw04), and find this:

[zone
nickname = Zone_Bw04_Abstand
ids_name = 261309
pos = -243, 0, 513
rotate = 15, 0, 0
shape = SPHERE
size = 21000
damage = 220 <-- Radiation damage (per second I think)
ids_info = 66001
spacedust = leedsdust
spacedust_maxparticles = 125
sort = 99

This is the central asteroid belt, where the radiation is serious (it would chew a starflier in 5 seconds). Other parts of the system only have 5, and behind Schutz it's lower as well
