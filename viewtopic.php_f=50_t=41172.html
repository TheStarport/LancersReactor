The PTough.ini file in the Missions file controls what levels there are and how much your worth has to be to get to the level. Here's a entry from it

ptough_graph_pt = 11000 , 1


the part in bold is the worth needed and the italic part is the level you goto when you get your worth to the amount set in the bold part.
