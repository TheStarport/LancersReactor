Completely finished the Grendel Spawn Launcher. 99% sure it's 100% stable (whatever that translates to). For anyone not sure what it is... Think of FLMM. Great program, just great, but it's like a 4 in 1 printer/scanner/fax/copier. It's a vague program, designed to handle just about any medium, and that's great for anybody to just jump on and use. The fact of the matter though is that no 4in1 has all 4 components at the same quality as standalone pieces. If they were just as good, no one would have a $2,000 copier when they could have a $150 copier/printer/fax/and scanner. This launcher is custom tailored for my mod. I will be the first to admit that I looked at FLMM for many hours while thinking up the features this will have, so thanks to the maker(s) to all their hard work. This program, however, runs only with Grendel Spawn, and allows for automatic updates of commodity prices, trade routes, new systems... the list really goes forever. Anyway, just had to rant because Drzzt4.0 and I have probably spent 5 hours debugging that thing (which by the way, I hate Microsoft... they set all folders in windows with a flag of 16, then they didn't bother telling anyone that if the folder is compressed it has a value of 2064... Rediculous). Also, there's sight of a dedicated Grendel Spawn server just over the horizon now, so check back soon for great news.

Oh, one more thing. Everyone should know that I have made this into a SP/MP mod, meaning that you can play online and interact with people, or SP... but both versions have the dynamic content, so you all are urged to register at the forum if you want to play either way, you can have a say in what changes as time goes on, since no matter how you play you'll have to adapt to it.

I just keep thinking of more to say... Made two new weapons, the razorblade (short range, rast refire, extreme damage) and the sniper scope (huge range, 1/4 of the slowest refire rate to date, crazy amounts of damage, huge energy costs). This brings the total of 100% original weapons to 6, which isn't bad since I have the dynamics turned off for now.
