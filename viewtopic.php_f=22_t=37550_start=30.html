Thu Aug 18, 2005 9:28 pm by Earendil

@Nabishamash - Unless you have the hash codes and their meanings specifically made up for IFSO, forget using IFSO for editing. I don't have good enough files for my Epsilon mod server and I get IFSO crashing on average every week or so. There are some common mods built into IFSO but WTS World isn't one of them.

@Rower of death - Try FLAdmin. It's old but works. If you had a dedicated server Eagle would let you have FLAC but he is particular, with excellent reason I might add, who he gives it out to.

@Freelancer2108971 - You really should have broadband to host a server. In fact with servers that have mods that put a lot of moving objects on screen or have a fast pace (increased thruster speed for example), you'd be hard pressed to even use 56k to play (you'd then be a FL client). I think at 28.8k your FL client becomes very choppy even on the most reduced bandwidth server. As for uptime I have had one server up for 2 yrs 3 months and even that is no guarantee you'll get players on your server. If I were you I'd stick to single play (SP) until you get broadband.
