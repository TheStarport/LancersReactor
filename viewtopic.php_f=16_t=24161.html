If you look at the Freelancer intro, the narrator mentions a hundred-year war between two major powers (Alliance and Coalition). Well Starlancer is based on the start of this war -- you're a new recruit for the Alliance, fighting against the Coalition in a supposedly WW2 type story.

Well Freelancer is based on the premise of "build sleeper ships, go somewhere else and start over again". Rheinland is the name of one of those sleeper ships, and therefore the name of one of the houses. This of course has nothing to do with Starlancer because it's based like 100 years later (then another 800 years after that).
