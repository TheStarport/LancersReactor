WC/Privateer Conversion Mod for FL is back on!!!

Great news for Wing Commander and Privateer fans, the conversion mod Bob McDob started and was losing interest in, has been picked up by someone else to finish. Wing Commander The Reckoning Lives Again
Tolwyn called us up to deliver some happy news, the Freelancer mod The Reckoning is back in business! He will be utilizing models from the Wing Commander Saga Freespace 2 mod to continue the work that has already been done.
