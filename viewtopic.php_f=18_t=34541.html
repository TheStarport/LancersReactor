Point taken Chips but there are times when only the answer will do, irrespective of the fact some people can't be bothered to search the forums as that's why they're here. Sooo,

Goto Freelancer/DATA/initialworld.ini

Open that and put a ; in front of those gates u want open. The section u need is right at the top of the file when you open it, eg:

[locked_gates
;locked_gate = 2926089285

Do this for all the gates you need, do a player wipe on your server and off you go. Worth noting you can open the gates in single player this way as well
