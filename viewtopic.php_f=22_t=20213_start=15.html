Heres what I've found the patch does, through looking at the INI's:

It:
- Sets all nomad lasers to do a damage of 0.
- Sets ubershield strength and regeneration to 0.
- Sets armor_scale_invincible scale to 1.
- Adds cloaks to the weapons files.
- Adds cloaks as good to the weaons files.
- Sets nomads to use Nomad Cannons/Nomad Blaster guns instead of nomad lasers.

How that'd cause instability I dont know, as there are no real changes to any key files which would cause instability.

Everything else matches fine to my default files.

In fact, I'd go as far to say that this mod DOESN'T cause instability, as there really is nothing there to cause it!
