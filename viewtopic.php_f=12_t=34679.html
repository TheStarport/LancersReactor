Yes. The X800 comes in both PCI X and AGP flavors. But unless you can find another goof at Best Buy they cost as much as what your 6800 SHOULD have cost.

FYI, in the reviews of the latest and greatest cards from both makers, the comments have been that it isn't the PCI X that you are noticing. It is the latest and greatest GPUs from nVidia and ATI that are knocking your sox off.

Your friend should get pretty much the same result with an X800 AGP card. In your specific case, though, you got quite the excellent bargain so please don't take this comment as a negative about how you're now setup at all. Since your friend most likely will have to spend more money just for the card than you did for mobo and card, I thought I'd mention this to try to save him a buck or two.

Also, with the ATI X800, ultimate performance is much more dependent upon how fast and muscular the cpu also is. The slower/more feeble the cpu, the less of a bang you'll get out of the X800.
