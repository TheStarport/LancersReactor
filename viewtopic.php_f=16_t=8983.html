The victory sequence for that mission is pretty important to the plot. The mission isn't that hard - I failed once because of accidentally leaving mission area and won the first time when I did it properly. Just wait until you are close to the rock and that fight is going on, and fly under the rock. Fly up the hole in the middle, and use your strafing keys, guns and torpedos to destroy the "shield generators" on your target list. Once the shield goes down you only need to dock to win the mission.

This one took me a few tries. The key tactic is actually mentioned by Juni once you reach the "arch", so if you want to try again going on that do so....



otherwise 'spoiler" of sorts....















The arch platform has a hollow cylinder in the middle of it (Juni mentions that you should go under the base). If you go under and come up through the cylinder, the enemies will not follow you. Although you will get a message saying "taking radiation damage", there will be little actual damage (nothing a single nanobot cannot fix). From within the cylinder, you can bead onto one of the generators (3 or 4 total). Use torpedos and missiles and guns and charge straight for the generator on full afterburn. Once your within weapon range of the generators, the enemy fighters will come for you so do this quickly and then retread into the safety of the cylinder. Repeat this process approximately 2-3 times per generator and you should have no problems

btw Torpedos are very useful here
