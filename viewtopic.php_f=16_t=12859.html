The Nomad guns are not of a particular "type" (laser, photon, tachyon, neutron, etc.) In fact, none of the c10 guns has a specific type. This is easy to see by looking at the munitions entries for these weapons in the equipment .ini files. Therefore, it doesn't matter which kind of shield you use to counter these weapons, the damage will always be 100%, so just use the shield with the most capacity you can get.
