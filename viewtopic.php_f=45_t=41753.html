Shadows of Fury Clan [SOF

SOF plays on the Nima E-sports Evolutions Nightmare server.

Due to the unfortunate closure of NIMA server at this time and intill further notice SOF has moved to Solaris intill NIMA server reopens in spring of 2006.

SOF clan is governed by 3 Triad Members rather than just 1 Leader.

This method of governance more effective, as it doesn't delay decisions if the leader is away, and for major decisions, we have a vote where 2 out of 3 votes will decide the result of any major decision.

SOF have been on the Nima Server since February 2005 when we left the "Vanilla-ish (modded server)" server of Eagle Utopia.

The SOF clan currently claims and patrols Tau 31 system.

Allies of SOF are currently [Nemwar, [Wpx, [Legacy, [BORG, [OPG, [D-S and [ISF.

Enemies: Everyone else

SOF encourages roleplay on this server and strictly adheres to the server rules in all matters.


The SOF are a group of extremest pirates, who are fond of cardamine and absynthe. They like to run things their way. They have strong honour and codes, led by their charismatic triad, who likes to plague shipping lines and innocent travellers. Whilst most other pirates focus upon taxing transports, the SOF like to focus upon fighting, plundering, and looting of travellers and fighters without discretion.

When there are more than 1 SOF members on the server, they normally group up, and engage an enemy in formation.

Each member is a part of the family and will be helped that way. All together they make sure the clan advances in the game. SOF higher officers provide training on different elements of the game, as well as providing assistance to members in any way they can. This includes tours of systems, missions against NPC to improve character reputations, and fighting hunters and SOF enemies side by side.

The SOF clan is very active on the server, and boasts a fleet of Battleships and other supporting capital ships.

For further information, speak to a Triad member (Phreakish, Phame and DeaDCow) or one of our other members. Also visit our forums at s11.invisionfree.com/sofclan
