Thu Sep 30, 2004 12:57 pm by Balthazar Furious

(1) The main difference is of course that there is no storyline, you begin on Planet Manhattan with a starflier and go from there. You also have options to interact with other players which you wouldn't have. You aren't confined to NY until you're at a certain level, you can go where-ever you want except Single-Player story areas. Finally, your level has no impact on what you can and can't buy.

(2) No, a lot of people don't join a clan. Clans themselves aren't actually the ones you need to worry about. You will encounter players who are complete ******** and pick on new players, but they are few and far between and generally those people are frowned upon by the community they play in. Usually it's established players and/or cheaters who kill new players.

(3) Not sure what you mean by that. If by mod you mean downloadable mods, then no, most servers don't like you using mods. If by mod you mean kitting out your ship with good weapons and whatnot, then also no. You can play just as effectively with whatever ship and loadout you want. It would probably be easier with the best stuff, but where's the fun in that.

(4) Yes, everything from Single-Player will be there. But, you cannot use the jumpholes and jumpgates you used to get somewhere specifically for the story.

(5) Single-Player without the story filled with real people. Make of it what you wish.
