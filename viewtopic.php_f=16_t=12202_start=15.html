Cham

Tue Apr 15, 2003 6:02 am by Cham

I've gotten all my NECs/NEBs from Omicron Gamma as I am still hostile with the Outcasts (which makes Omicron Alpha a no-no). Initially, I was also hostile with the Corsairs. When it was time to finally get some Nomad weaponry, I had to improve my standing with the Corsairs first, without, however, becoming hostile with any other factions, especially not with the Red Hessians as I still wanted to be able to do the diamond trade. Hence I started doing missions for the Hogosha (Kabukichu Depot in NEW TOKYO). With every completed mission my rep with the Corsairs became a little better (the tradeoff being Hessians and GMG reps taking a slight hit). I kept repeating this until they had become decently neutral, while the Red Hessians were also still neutral. Not only did I make it possible for me to go to Omicron Gamma, I also made a lot of quick money in the process. The target ships in the Hogosha missions (GC + Dragons) drop plenty of equipment and cargo that can be sold for quite some cash. Mission waypoints are always near the Hogosha base and usually you pass by the Kyushu jumphole, where usually there will be more juicy targets with precious cargo.
