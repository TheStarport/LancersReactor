1. get a job doesn't mean what it says after the last mission. You can do what you want. Trade, do missions, or explore. You level up anyway.
2. Leveling up changes after the campaign for mysterious reasons only know to the creator (probably to keep the forums going) It now says how much MORE you need instead of how much you need in total.
So if it says Next level: $50.000 you need $50.000 more to level up.

In your case you needed $133,000 to level up and after a few missions you needed only $100,000.

As a newbie I've found the easiest way to level up is Sigma 13 & 19 wrecks. Tractor in all that lovely loot (level 10 weapons) and if you plan your route right you can go level 19 to level 28 in about 20-25 minutes. Check the wreck list though, it took me about 2 hours to go level 26, if I'd checked the wrecks list the 20-25 minutes would have been very realistic. On your way to Hokkaido pick up gate lane parts at Roppongi (opposite New Tokyo), dump them off at the gate lane construction site in Hokkaido, that will give you your first level (double money), then use the jump hole to the east of the Construction site to Sigma 13 and jump the next levels. Other people may give you better ways to rise but this is my newbie effort............................
