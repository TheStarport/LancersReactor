RavenDT

Tue Jun 03, 2003 8:22 pm by RavenDT

Wyrm II's are nice, but I prefer the Skyblast B's. They have high rate of fire and do very nice damage.
Tizona del Cid's are not for use with anything like an Eagle, Sabre, or Titan. If you outfit it with some nice guns, you don't need a Tizona to waste their shields. Tizonas are for use with ships that can't use level 10 weapons.

My favourite setup for Eagle/Sabre/Titan:
Skyblast B (9)
Skyblast B (9)
Nomad Energy Blaster (10)
Nomad Energy Blaster (10)
Nomad Energy Blaster (10)
Nomad Energy Blaster (10)
Sunslayer Torpedo / Hornet Cruise Disruptor (probably the CD)
Ripper Mine

Ripper mines are the strongest mines in the game (that I know of). Very nice!
Tizonas are not worth even one mounting point on any of these three ships.
Skyblasts have high rate of fire and very nice damage.
I prefer Nomad Energy Blasters to Nomad Energy cannons because they shoot faster by .97, but it doesn't really matter.
Sunslayer Torpedo is fun for killing transports and anything larger. There is a Starkiller Torpedo and Sunslayer Torpedo, but the Sunslayer (the one I use) has a range of 2700m, making it the best torpedo in the game imo

You'll never run out of energy with this setup.
