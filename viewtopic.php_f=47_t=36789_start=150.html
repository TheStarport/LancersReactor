NEWSFLASH
In an unprecedented move Chancellor Codename of Rheinland and Paul Kloppenborg, Leader of the Xenos have agreed to work together. This agreement came about after an intense bargaining session prompted by the Chancellor's offer of employment for the Xenos. It has been agreed by all parties that Mr. Kloppenberg will become CEO of Daumann Heavy Construction in addition to retaining his command of the Xenos. Since the Rheinland economy was devastated there has been a mass emigration from Rheinland but its infrastucture should prove more than capable of accepting the new workers. The former CEO of Daumann, Hans Lindemann, has agreed to retire in the interests of peace in Sirius.

When asked what prompted him to make this historic decision Mr. Kloppenborg replied, "He has given Liberty's Working Class work again."

For his part, the Rheinland Chancellor was simply extatic that he had a steadfast ally. "It is wonderful to know that there are still reasonable leaders out there. Mr. Kloppenborg has restored my faith in the greatness of humanity as a whole."

While some of the details of the switchover have yet to be finalised, it is generally expected that the change will be a smooth one.
