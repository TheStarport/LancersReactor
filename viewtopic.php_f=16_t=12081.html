I had a look through your list of trade routes, and thought I'd mention a few higher earning but more dangerous ones.

The top scorer: Hauling Alien Organisms from Ruiz Base (Omicron Beta) all the way back to California Minor. They are perishable, but they only cost $100 and sell for $2000, a massive $1900 profit a go. In a well armed Dromedary you would lose, an over the top guess of 25, during the trip. 275x100 = $27,500 cost, 250x2000 = $500,000 pay, profit of at least $472,500.

This one might not look like a good earner, but it pays both ways and could certainly help those Outcasts rule Sirius Cart Cardamine from Malta to Manhattan nets you $1200 profit each, and Light Arms from Detroit Munitions to Malta earns you $1260 profit each. Not perishable either, but you need to be on good terms with those pesky Outcasts. That'll be $150,000 at Freeport 5 in Tau 29.

And finally, if you want a shorter route you can try the recommended Diamonds route between Rheinland and Planet New Tokyo. Except don't get them from Berlin, Solarius Station (Daumann) in Omega 11 sells for only $219 vs. $396, and Freital Base (Hessian) also in Omega 11 sells at only $198. But then you've got to be willing to love those Rheinland pirates, is it worth it?

I hope that doesn't spoil finding the best trade runs for people, but none of those are exactly easy. Don't complain if you die in even a Dromedary doing them

Maybe I'm just impatient, but I hate making really long runs. It takes forever and you don't always have a good return trip. I feel I could probably make several shorter, less profitable runs in the same amount of time and make equal or more money.

I found a decently short trip to be running Alien Organisms from Glasgow Outpost in Leeds to Cambridge Research Station. That'll get you about $162,000 on that trip. Then run diamonds back from Planet Cambridge to Planet Leeds for $108,900. That totals $270,900 (in a Dromidary).

Only New London is between Leeds and Cambridge, so it's quick. And if you can get the Leeds to Cambridge jumphole to work, maybe even quicker. Either way, I bet I can easily make two runs of that in the time it takes someone to get from New Berlin to New Tokyo.
