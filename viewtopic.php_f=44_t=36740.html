There are to programs for editing CMP files in the download section.
1) HardCMP-to add, delete, or modify hardpoints. (And it helps you find a suitable SUR file for new models.)
2) Model Tool-to resize the CMP, and it's assigned SUR file.

Sorry, but I know of no other way, usless you can find a cage (or like) in Freelancer somewhere and re-position it in the centre of the gate... lots of work tho.....
