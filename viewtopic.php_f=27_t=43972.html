Ok, well are you talking about SP or LAN?


If your playing LAN and you change to a differnt mod and that char. was in the system from the other mod, your going to crash becuase the game cannot find that file, however if the system file is named the same thing as the other mod. (Like BR08.ini in this mod and the other mod has a system folder also named BR08.ini) You will show up in the other system in the spot that you were based from the other modded system. That is true for LAN, but SP I am not 100% sure.

Best thing to do is just to clear your files or if your so in love with your char, all ya need to do is to just move the savefiles onto your desktop, it isn't wise to use save game files from one mod and use them on a new mod. It causes to many bugs.

Opps' forgot to add, that also if the ship that your char is using is not in the new mod you add on, it will crash at start up also.
