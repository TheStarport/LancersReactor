Crash-Drive

Sat May 10, 2003 5:47 pm by Crash-Drive

You get the Wild Fire guns on the Pathfinder wreck in Omicron Beta, and all the wrecks in Omicron Beta have a glitch that make it so you can get their loot over and over. In SP, all you have to do is go out of the system and come back, or even just dock at Ruiz Base and go back out, and you can get their loot again and again. In MP, the server "remembers" the state of wrecks while it's up, but after a restart it will go by the flag in your player profile saying whether you have already tried to loot those wrecks, and the glitch comes back into effect because your profile never gets those flags set in it. This is also the case with the wrecks in Omega-7. A little mistake the devs made in the .ini files for those two systems. Lucky mistake, though, because the Wild Fire guns are the best of the c10 codename guns, I think, because they do just a little bit less damage than the Diamondback or Cerberus guns, but have the best energy efficiency of all of them.
