Are you running any, or have run any, mods at all?

Try a reinstall - check your graphics cards drivers are the most recent, check your Direct X installation is the most recent (or 9.0b will suffice!), remove all background software that maybe running un-necessarily, scan drives with Spybot and Adaware, use your virus scanner, check sound cards drivers are up to date, and reinstall the game!

Think that is nearly everything you should try out for starters
