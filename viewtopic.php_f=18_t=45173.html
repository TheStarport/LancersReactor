It's amazing what incredible things you can do with the current 10 levels of weapons and equipment.
Still, you can create new ones within those "guidlines"..and still have a form of class all to themselves.
As with all new creations and existing items, balance has always been the issue at hand. Wether it's with power or cost...or both.

@ Lancerlover;
I've seen variations of Nav Maps where the background had been changed.
That gave the overall looks something new to look at and give the impression you were actually somewhere else. Changing the appearance of existing systems also gives the game a new feel. So, adding new systems just expands it. There is a lot of open space to take up on the Nav Map.
Adding new systems that would show up on the map should be a piece of cake.
It's a matter of making sure the "Visit" to the new systems are set to "1" for them to show up on the map.
