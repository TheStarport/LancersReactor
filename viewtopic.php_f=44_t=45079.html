Dm Rankor's 88 Wasp(with some cleaned up geometry) - you can't see from the screenshot, but the glowing intakes are double-layers with two textures, one is a simple alpha texture with opacity and emissiveness, and the second (foreground) is a simple emissive.these two layers are moved diagonally by material animations (wink) so they appear to, well glow like plasma or something.

the nozzles around the outside of the engine are animated too(they give the illusion of rotation) the cockpit-glass shows how to use a dcdtocottwo property with an alpha'd texture to make a nice looking set of cockpit-struts
