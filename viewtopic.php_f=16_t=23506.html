Sherlog

Fri Aug 29, 2003 10:40 am by Sherlog

If you want the Sabre fast & easy then buy it from the Outcasts. Drive your Humpback to the Interspace Commerce depots near Planet New London, smash the two H-fuel depots (8 pods) and tractor their contents. Then smash one pod of the remaining depot and the Outcasts will stop shooting. Time taken: 30 seconds (plus travelling there, of course). If you trade well on the way to Malta then the multiplied loot profit will pay for the Sabre & more.

There are also Gateway depots near Kensington Shipping Platform (H-fuel, Mining Machinery and Consumer Goods IIRC), the Bounty Hunters have depots near Sheffield (Manchester) and Deshima (Shikoku), and there are several depots in the New York system (near Manhattan, near Baltimore Shipyard/Ft. Bush). All these depots have a good effect on the Outcasts and most have interesting loot.

Just make sure you don't smash too many depots of the same faction, because you don't want a company like Interspace or Gateway hostile. Destroying a depot pod lowers your rep with the owning faction by about one square (10%).
