Rosenrot

Are there any Rammstein fans out there? I'm pretty sure there are, maybe not registered with this site, but I'm sure that some people who are reading this like Rammstein. For those of you who don't know Rammstein, they're a German heavy metal band who've become very popular in the US. They've just released their new album, Rosenrot (in English it means Rose-red.) I don't speak German, and all of their music is in German, but they're still pretty damn good.

Rosenrot is a very good album IMHO, although almost everyone who's been with Rammstein for a while can agree, it's not their best. If you like Rammstein, or would like to get a taste of them, go to their English website rammstein.com. There you'll find six songs from Rosenrot that you can listen to for about a minute. Some of these are exceptional pieces of work IMO, such as Spring, Mann Gegen Mann, Wo Bist Du, Zerstoren, and Rosenrot. One last note, if you're a guy and you're not homosexual, DO NOT watch the video for Mann Gegen Man, you'll regret it.

Tracks:

Benzin (Gasoline/petrol)
Mann Gegen Mann (Man Against Man/ can also mean Man Gayfor Man, which is why I told you not to watch the video.)
Rosenrot (Rose-red)
Spring (Jump)
Wo Bist Du (Where Are You)
Stirb Nicht Vor Mir (Don't Die Before I Do)
Zerstoren (Destroying)
Hilf Mir (Help Me)
Te Quiero Pu**! (the only Spanish track on the album, means I Love You Bi***!)
Feuer Und Wasser (Fire And Water)
Ein Lied (A Song)

Please tell me what you guys think of Rammstein or their new album Rosenrot.
