#1: pick a nice one from this list: list im not 100% sure if i listed all flashes as well, but the tables are handy one way or another when building weapons.

#2: remove the const_effect line

#3: shieldbreakers are not possible. you cant bypass shields. experiments to simulate rad type damage for guns failed.

#4: custom sounds are very possible. check th audio directory, try to make connections to the weapon_equip.ini. search for the sound references in the audio folder. you'll find them, its pretty selfexplaining if you "hop" from reference to nicknames a couple of times. its also a good way to understand dependencies and how the ini structure works.
