Thu Nov 06, 2003 4:51 am by firebase

HardPoint 101
okay this are the hardpoints
first the fixed hardpoints. all hardpoints located below are refered to as its location in the ship.

Hp/Fixed/HpMount - the mounting is place below the center of the ship. i tells fl that it is the models bottom so as not the model to sink
Hp/Fixed/HpPilot - the pilot
Hp/Fixed/HpShield01 - The shield hardpoint
Hp/Fixed/HpEngine## - Ships engine exhaust location, ## means 01, 02... etc. that depends on your engine.
Hp/Fixed/HpThruster## - Ships thruster location, ## means 01, 02... etc
Hp/Fixed/HpRunningLight## - ships blinking lights
Hp/Fixed/HpDockLight## - ships red light when docking
Hp/Fixed/HpHeadLight## - Ships Headlight
Hp/Fixed/HpContrail## - Those thing that looks like smoke coming out of your wing when at high speed.
Hp/Fixed/HpCM## - counter measure hardpoint location
Hp/Fixed/HpMine## - Mine Dropper location

Hp/Revolute/HpWeapon## - main weapon location
Hp/Revolute/HpTurret## - turret location
Hp/Revolute/HpTorpedo## - Torpedo launcher/Cruise Disruptor location

i think that's all about the harpoint location
