If I understand correctly, you are having trouble with a mod and rather than email the author you decided to jump up on a soap-box and proclaim that all mods are lies and the whole modding thing is a huge conspiracy to dupe you into making your copy of Freelancer unplayable?

Interesting.

So it probably wouldn't do any good for anyone here to say the mod was working for them since they would obviously then be part of the consiracy and simply trying to dupe you further.

I would suggest the following to see if you can get it to work. First, install a fresh copy of Freelancer to ensure all of your files are correct. Second, extract the ship data files to the directories indicated (that would be under \data\ships\<path> with the last being particular to the mod). Finally, I would open the .XML file included in the mod file and make the changes to the .ini files manually. Don't forget to uncompress the .ini first (and make a backup).

If that doesn't result in happiness, I suggest a more polite request for specific assistance here.
