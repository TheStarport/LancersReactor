Advice of the Day

When you're flying a mission for the Bounty Hunters Guild, fighting against 8 Corsair Titans, and the BHG has sent 8 Hammerheads to help you... accidentally hitting one of the Bounty Hunters with a Sunslayer is a very bad idea.


Second bit of advice: When you launch to fly a mission, the fastest way to get your ship started is to hit the T key to highlight the mission waypoint then F2. Immediately automatically starts turning toward the target and charging the cruise engine. (If any other players are nearby, the T might highlight them instead.)
