LukeSkyfocker

Tue Apr 08, 2003 3:41 am by LukeSkyfocker

Here's my favorite trade route (I don't know if anybody has posted it here or not). I think its excellent especially since it's short. Only catch is that you need to be neutral to Corsairs.

First: Get to Cadiz Base in Omega 5. It's a Corsair base. Buy Artifacts for $135. Leave Cadiz and go to Planet New London (Omega 5 -> Cambridge Jump Hole -> Planet Cambridge Trade Lane -> New London Jump Gate. Then You go to Planet New London). Sell the Artifacts for $1,147, which is a profit of $1,012 per unit.

Then Buy Small Arms from Planet New London for $350. Go back to Cadiz the same way you got to New London. Sell the Small Arms at Cadiz for $840 which is a profit of $490 per unit. What is great about this route is that its really short. It takes two systems and there are only 2 Trade Lanes and about a 50K run between the Jump hole and Planet Cambridge. It takes 6 minutes give or take a few seconds to make it ONE WAY. So its 12 minutes total. If you have a Dromesdary, you can make some pretty quick cash. It holds 275 units of cargo. If you do the math, you make a profit of $413,050 round trip. Not so bad for only 12 minutes. If you break it down, you make $34,416.667 per minute of space flight. The only people that give me crap are Molly's, but I just fly past them.

The Mactan-Manhattan run is utterly ridiculous in SP, since the Magellan-NY jumpgate is opened. You can get a dromedary at level 10 (after first Bretonian mission). Load it up with cardamine, and head to NY. Since the Liberty guys are still after you, you can't sell at Manhattan. Instead, go to Rochester Base and sell it for a 100/unit less, then hack your rep with the Liberty Police. Bring back H-Fuel to Mactan. Then it's 800/unit profit for flying through 2 trade lanes, 1 jump gate, and roughly 20k of open space! (Though slightly less on the way back... feh. Also, keep in mind it's pretty tough balancing your rep so you can dock at Mactan AND Manhattan, but there's always Rochester Base for the less tactful)
