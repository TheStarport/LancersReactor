Sit tight folks, haven't forgotten you. The 'mod', as it is, is very messy. I want to clean it up and, if time permits, add some extras before releasing it to the hungry public.

And no, at the moment the Meandering Missouri isn't destructable - that will change though, given the information a few replies back!

Also I'm working on a 'global mission'. Details are sketchy, but basically I want to try adding a background super-plot to the FL universe, involving a cold war between Kusari, Rheinland and Bretonia - all sides using agents from the independant and edge worlds to suit their purposes.
