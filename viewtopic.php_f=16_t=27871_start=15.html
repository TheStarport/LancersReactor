Question 8: "Why do certain shipyards sell level 10 freighter shields when no freighter can equip them ? "

Unfortunatly, a lot of silly bugs were left in the game when it was rushed out by Micosoft. This shield problem is one of them. However some mods use level 10 freighter shields, so it has unintentionally helped the modding community with their work. Theres always light in the darkness

Next onto .....

************************************************************************************************
Question 9 : "Is there any way I can store my level 10 Weapons so that if i die, I won't lose them ?"

There is an old saying that is still in use today. " Use it or lose it", by equiping your weapons, if you die, they will be with you when you restart at a planet, only you will be without the cargo you were carrying at the time of your death. Of course, you won't try to die to test out this answer will you ?
