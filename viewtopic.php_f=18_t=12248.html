Xocyll

Sun Mar 30, 2003 4:12 am by Xocyll

Very easy to do.
Change the engine type installed.
The engines for "big" ships just get a small glow when you go into cruise, the smaller ships get the big "afterburner like" flames.

so change the "ge_tl_engine_01" to one of the fighter or elite engines.
I rather like the Blue flames of the Breton ships so I use
ge_bf_engine_01 .

Oh, and you might have noticed that the NPC ships have little trails from their engine flames (sort of like contrails?)

The engine definitions have two different trails, one for the default NPC ships and one with no trails for player ships.

You can find the definitions in engines_equip.ini and just copy the default trail over to the player_trail setting.

looks like this (Breton light fighter engine):
flame_effect=gf_br_smallengine01_fire
trail_effect=gf_br_smallengine01_trail
trail_effect_player=gf_br_smallengine01_playtrail
