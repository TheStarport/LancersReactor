WW2 Mod

Welcome to the WW2 thread. Here you can find out what's being prepared next for the WW2 Mod. If you'd like to post a comment please go ahead. If you have any suggestions or complaints I suggest you send me an email at bhg_necro@yahoo.com

**NEWS***
11 May 2004
-> The WW2 ship pack is no more! Hail to the WW2 Mod! That means we will no longer be releasing ship packs, but mods with new systems, weapons etc. Of course there will be a few beta versions of the mod released for you people to get a first glipse of the mod. Also we've now got some new members on the team!!


**INFO**
If anyone would like to help create WW2 planes and join Rayak and myself in our efforts then contact me. If you're a new modder then I'll help any way I can, but a basic understanding of how to make mods is required.

If you want to request a plane or any other ship then send me an email including 4 or more photos from different angles of the ship. Optionally you could also send the stats you'd like the ship to have. DON'T just send a ship name and expect me to go hunting for the info. That's not the way it works.
