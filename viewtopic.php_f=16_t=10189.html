You get to Omicron Alpha (Planet Malta) via Tau 37, but you had better be friendly with the Outcasts or you won't survive. You need a bribe to get in good with the Outcasts. You have to be level 30 in order to buy the Sabre. You can also buy the Sabre at Vogtland Base (secret Red Hussian base) in Dresden, but you will not survive or get there unless you are friendly with the Corsairs and Red Hessians. If you are friendly with the Corsairs, you are automatically friendly with the Red Hessians. I fought my way to Tau 23 and Tau 37 and got a bribe somewhere there -- can't remember where.
