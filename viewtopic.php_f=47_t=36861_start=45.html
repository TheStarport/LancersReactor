Unit Name: ‘Golden Strikers’
Faction: Kruger Minerals (Rheinland)
Symbol: A brown money sack with a scythe arching over it
Description:
The Golden Strikers are a rogue division of Kruger Minerals who deal with the protection of merchant vessels and the shipping of high-class ‘minerals’ throughout the Sirius Sector. They are primarily known for their merciless attitude towards bandits and pirates, which has gained them a high standing among all of the House Police forces. However, their over-zealous behavior cost the Golden Strikers dearly, with nearly three-fourths of their members dying during an assault on territory occupied by the Lane Hackers. Yet small groupings of survivors are still known to exist, and are in high demand as escorts for most of Sirius’ shipping companies.

Unit Base: None ( Nomadic Merchant ‘Fleet’ )
Unit Force:
The ‘Geschwin’ (Modified Armored Transport)
The ‘Chkeit’ (Train)
The ‘Schnell’ (Train)
4 Banshee Light Fighters (1 Wing)
4 Hawk Light Fighters (1 Wing)
8 Startracker Light Fighters (2 Wings)

Commander Name: Damien Regoba (Day-me-n Ray-go-bah)
Commander Description:
Damien Regoba has been a mercenary for nearly his entire life, with his experiences leading him into becoming quite the capable pilot and granting him a large amount of respect among the various corporations he worked for. One of these corporations, Kruger Minerals, eventually offered Damien a permanent job involving control over a portion of the Golden Strikers. Damien quickly accepted this offer, and began using his past influence to outfit his small fleet with the best equipment available. Soon enough, he had transformed his small unit into a formidable fleet under the command of his modified transport, the Geschwin. By the time of the Lane Hacker Incident, the Geschwin had been converted into the equivalent of a miniature battleship, with all of its storage areas being scrapped in order to allow for more weaponry and armor. The Golden Strikers, impressed by Damien’s flagship, immediately began trying to enlist his fleet in the battle against the Lane Hackers. Damien, however, saw no way for such a battle to be won, and refused to take part. Now he wanders as one of the only surviving Golden Strikers, continuing to serve as an escort for merchant vessels throughout Sirius.
