Far-fetched, I know, since Trinity could've bumped her head on her way to the plug-chair and has to spend time in sickbay and wouldn't have entered the Matrix. If so, then the Sentinels are also tasked with destroying the Nebuchadnezzar and kill Trinity in the real world. Either way, she had to 'die', unless Neo saved her. The Architect calculated everything.

Options and situations given to Neo:
1. Trinity enters Matrix (prompted by the death of Soren's team), and gets killed by agents. Neo must choose to save humanity (by returning to source) or Trinity (by not returning to source)
This scenario happened.

2. Trinity does not enter Matrix, but killed by Sentinels. This of course is less likely to happen (Nebuchadnezzar has not been found by Sentinels), but the Architect had to bluff. IF the Sentinels did find her, then the option goes like this: Neo must choose save humanity (by returning to source) or Trinity (by flying off to nearest Exit Point and unplug, then steers Nebuchadnezzar out harm's way, or warn them and charge the EMP blaster)
This didn't happen. Mainly because the Sentinels apparently failed to trace the Nebuchadnezzar. Architect was very lucky that his gamble worked (killing Soren).
