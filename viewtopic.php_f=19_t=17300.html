1.For every server you play in you have to have a different player, you can also have mulitple chars on one server
2. The games are usually small, but the main server which most ppl play on is Elite, run by lancersreactor.
3. Elite is full almost all of the time, im not sure about other servers. Elite holds about 30 ppl. Abut running into ppl, it depends on where you go, house capitals and edgeworld planets are usually crowded with a few ppl. Clan wars keep the game interesting.
4. If you die in multiplayer you may choose to respawn at the last base you docked in, also when you die, anything that is in your cargo hold will be lost, but money, mounted weapons, ship, and interior and exterior equipment will not be lost.
5. Everyone has there own ship, and besides, how fun would playing as a turret be? Anyway when you start yu have a starflier and 2000 credits
6. There are a few types of chat, one is system chat, which sends the message to everyone in your system, then theres group chat(look at rulebook for info on groups) which sends the message to everyone in your group, no matter where they are, and finally there is private chat, which sends a message to a particular person, no matter where they are.
I hope this helps
