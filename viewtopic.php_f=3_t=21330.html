Patriot is the better choice. Loaded right, the patriot can serve you through the entire campaign, while the defender's lack of maneuverability will eventually kill it. The Patriot may carry fewer guns, but it can and will dodge just about all of the enemy fire, so the only hull damage you might ever get is from radiation. Whenever I used a defender in SP, I wound up limping into Leeds with both wings shot off...I never got so much as a scratch in a Patriot.

Dagger is another fine ship, but if you're KOS to the Rougues, it's easier to go with the Patriot.
