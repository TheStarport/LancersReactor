Du7andal

Sun May 09, 2004 4:08 pm by Du7andal

Well, since this is the spoiler forum, I suppose we can tell you.

The green parts on the map are where the Omicron systems, They are the home to the Outcasts and the Corsairs. You can get to these systems but you have to find the right jumpholes and study where Outcast and Corsair patrols meet at in dust clouds, those are usually jump holes. To get to the Outcasts (they are on the upper side of the green, go to the borderworlds between Bretonia and Kusari, then explore until you find a jumphole that leads to "Omicron *something*". To get to the Corsairs, go the "southeast" portion of Rhineland and look for a Jumphole that goes to "Omicron *something*" (as you can see I haven't been there for awhile).

NOTE: Be sure you are either really friendly with the Outcasts/Corsairs (depends on where you are going) or the Bounty Hunters (whom frequent the systems). You probably want to be on the good side of the Outcasts/Corsairs because they sell you the awesome Sabre VHF (Outcasts) or the bolvine Titan VHF (Corsairs)
