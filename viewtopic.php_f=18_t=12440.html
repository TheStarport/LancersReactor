Some modifications to the game.

Hi, i have put together a package of modification that includes many ideas and tutorials that had been put on on this board before. Thanks all. With you guys help, this pack now features: (tested working in MP and SP). All the files altered/added are .ini files.

New Pilotable Ships
- Orisis Battleship, Large Train, Train, purchased at Planet Manhattan
- Bretonia Battleship, Bretonia Gunboat, purchased at Planet New London
- Reinland Cruiser, Reinland Gunboat, purchased at Planet New Berlin
- Kusari Battleship, Kusari Gunboat, purchased at planet New Tokyo

New Turrets (can be mounted on any ship with turret hardpoints)
- All Liberty Battleship Turrets and Prisonship Turrets
- All Bretonia Battleship Turrets and Gunboat Turrets
- All Reinland Battleship Turrets and Gunboat Turrets
- All Kursari Battleship Turrets and Gunboat Turrets

Light Fighter/Heavy Fighter/Freighter Mod
(I find my self like many of the light fighter design but because they become obsolete in the late games, i made the following mod to make them usable later on)
- All Ships now can equip up to level 10 weapons and level 10 shield (restriction on number of slots with level 10 still exists)
- All light fighters no longer use light fighter shields but can use heavy fighter elite shield
- Engine/Power are not altered


Encounters (Capital Ships) - occur at randomness predefined by FL
- All police forces and Navy now area defend, patrol and assult with battleship/cruisers with fighters in formation. (at random)
- Outcast now may attack/defend with Bretonia Battleships in formation
- Corsiars now may attack/defend with Reinland Battlehips in formation
- Blood Dragon now may attack/defend with Kusari Battleships in formation
- Bounty Hunter now may attack/defend with Liberty cruisers/gunboats in formation

NPC battleship/cruiser modifications
- Realistic Battleship Status: PC are still able to sink a Battleship without torpedos however strongly NOT recommended.
- Adds limited number of high dmg turrets to exisiting Battleships.
- HP increased
- NPC battleships still have no shields.

NPC train modification
- Incrased HP to make them last longer.

NPC shield Regan
- NPC Fighter/freighter shield regan.

If you like to see more added, please post your request here and i will try to have it met. I will try to upload this pack at end of the week and make it flmod manager compatitable.
