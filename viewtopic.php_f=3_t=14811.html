This has noting to do with Zonealarm IMO.

Just access 'Services' and look for 'Messenger'. (Services can be found in 'Adminitrative Tools' in the control panel.

Stop the service and change Startup Type to 'Disabled'.
From now on you'll never get this annoying popup messages again!
