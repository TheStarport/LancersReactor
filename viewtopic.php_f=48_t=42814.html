Chips

Tue Feb 28, 2006 12:05 am by Chips

Let me re-phrase, You have no chance.


There are tutorials where people have spent hours writing exactly how to acheive what you want to do... so you can do it yourself, all it takes is a little effort, and a little reading.

If you don't do it yourself, then it should answer exactly why no-one is wanting to help you out by doing it for you. If you lack the commitment to even attempt to learn on your own, then no-one else is going to commit themselves to it either.

Just not to be completely dissuasive, it's fairly easy to start learning to mod, and addictive once you do. The modding forums (and tutorials forums), and especially the modding for beginners forums, all contain quality threads on information and more about how to mod. They walk you through, the tools etc. As soon as you trip up or hit an issue, post in those forums for help (try searching, then create a new thread). Just please try first though, because people spend a lot of time trying to help solve problems, but if you don't make an effort in the first instance to attempt it yourself with the information provided, then it completely defeats the community objective.
