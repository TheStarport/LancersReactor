End of my Rope

I just maxed out on levels. I've been flying a Titan for about 8 hours now. I reached level 38 and can go no higher (not that levels matter after the SP missions are over). So I decided to become a cargo pilot. I buy a Humpback in Rheinland, max her out with a variety of turrets and seek out to make my fortuned. Well, I'm already worth 3 mil, but I figure anything will beat the monotony of flying ship kill, base kill, and bounty hunter missions. Besides, the only jobs that pay anything are against the Bounty Hunters in Omicron Gamma. There might have been better jobs flying for the Outcasts in Omicron Alpha or Beta, but I wasn't friendly enough with them to check it out. I made nice with every faction except for the Mollys, Lane Hackers, Rouges, Golden Chysanthimums, Unioners, Junkers, the aforementioned Outcast, Bundusch, and the Blood Dragons. Every one else is neutral of friendly (this really helps traveling the space lines in a Humpack when you're used to packing the punch of a Titan).

I guess what I'm asking for here is some help. Running around in a Humpack is very tedious, you wouldn't believe how many bases the computer AI could not dock me at. The ship was to immobile to maneuver itself properly to dock in tight quarters.

I'm open to all suggestions.

If you want to stick with your single player game, yet you don't want to spend the time visiting each space port in order to find out what they buy and sell, then I suggest you head for the Spoilers Forum. You'll find a few databases that tell you about the shortest and most profitable trade runs.

Aside from that, your other options are to join a multiplayer server, grab a mod, or both.

A few suggestions to continue the freelancing experience

1. Play through the game again to lvl 38 using only light fighters (not that hard)
2. Play through using only freighters (a little harder through the missions)
3. Play through using Starflier/Defender/Patriot (whichever you choose)
4. Play through as a pirate without using jump gates or trade lanes (holes only, missions excluded)
5. Play multiplayer
6. Mod your game to add new components (I created a light fighter and freighter to equal the Titan, and changed the net worth requirements so to get level 38 you need $100 million - I've only got to level 21 so far after 56 hours play time)
7. Gosub 1 (until Doom 3 comes out)

I recommend having a go at modding. Once you have found where to download Bini It's really straightforward and you can rebalance the game however you would have liked it to be, then play it again. Better than waiting for an expansion.
