On Cheating, Its General Prevelance, and Its Justification

First let me say that I've been playing Freelancer online now for about 4 weeks and I am impressed. Achieving "the best" seems to be the goal with the point of the game being the process of getting there rather than the actual endpoint. However I noticed that many players I had never seen before were level 38 despite the fact that I regullarly played on these servers.

Originally I thought they were superior players but I have been recently enlightened. I had a rather long discussion with three of the cheaters on a server and their entertainment sources, and their justification.

Firstly on open public servers it seems like anywhere between 1 and 5 cheaters will be playing at one time. Most of these are people who like to manufacture millions and hand them out and others are ones who like to kill everyone who comes out of Manhatten in a Nomad Battleship or an invulnerable eagle that goes 900 velocity. They seem to comprise about 60% of the cheaters on a given server.

The other 20-40% seem to be more the hugh heffner of cheaters. These are the kind one can discuss with and where any of my citations come from. First apparently one is not cheating if you have items that cannot be bought in the game, such as nomad lasers or level 10 gravitron shields. Also one is not cheating if you have a nomad battleship or the osirus. One is only "cheating" when a player is invulnerable or has unlimited ammunition. Getting impossible equipment is "modding" where as changing the statistics for certain weapons or shields is "hacking." I am told it is necessary to distinguish between these behaviors as only "cheating" should be punished whereas "modding" or "hacking" on a unmodified open server should be allowed.

I noticed these people before but generally ignored them as they didn't bother other players and generally were just fooling around. However recently the total percent of players using these techniques seems to have skyrocketed. I am bothered since it always seemed to me that the point of the game was to attain these "best" items through the game. Once you have them, the game is over, there is nothing more to get.

The main problem is there is no way to eliminate the problem on your average server.
1)The sysop is often absent or unreachable.
2)There is no votekick function.
3)There is no automated scanning for impossible items.

If anyone wants to add thoughts to this please do. I'm interested to know why people use the "modding," "hacking," and "cheating" devices in this game specifically.(As opposed to a FPS where the point of the game is to win, not to 'journey' as it were)
