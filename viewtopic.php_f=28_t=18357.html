Kurgan''s Shipyards Mod

This mod will be adding on the single player aspect of Freelancer, but could be used in the MP arena as well. It adds some other options to the available ships, without totally destroying the story line by giving you an "Uber" ship from the start. All the original ships will be available in their normal locations. Custom ships will be made available at stations that normally don't carry ships. Here is a screenshot from the early stages of development.


List of ships to be Included:
Z-95 Headhunter - StarWars
T-65 X-Wing - StarWars
Cylon Raider - Battle Star Galactica
Colonial Viper - Battle Star Galactica
UDL4 Cheyenne Dropship - Aliens
Fedex Galactic Freight - My Twisted mind
"Dark" Phoenix - Slightly modified G-Force

In the Next Release:
