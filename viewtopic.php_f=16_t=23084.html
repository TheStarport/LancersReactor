Diamond_smuggling

Now, I'm not sure if there are people out there, somewhere, that already know this.

This is my little Diamond smuggling route I stumbled upon, purely by accident. it is a one way route though, but it's very short, making it's money vs time ratio among the highest of them all...

ok Diamonds. cheapest at Cadiz in Omega5, but I think Ronneburg Base. will have to do, as the corsairs are a nasty bunch of artwork.

It is also the most expensive at new Tokyo, but for travel and time, I think Leeds will do. especially considering the 2 jump route I discovered....

so here goes.... as from Ronneburg going to Leeds.


straight forward...... fly to the Cambridge JH and jump in don't bother fighting, just run run run...


once through you will need to make your way up to the leeds jumphole, from
here on in, it should be plain sailing....


once through the Leeds JH in Cambridge, take particular note of the location of it, in the Leeds system, as it will not appear on any map. You will have to visually find it, on your way back, for the next run.


It is in the cloud, closest to the 4th gate in the Leeds>New London jumpgate tradelane. Take note of the New London jumphole over to the left. Where my ship is now, that is the location of the hidden JH.


then just fly on in to Leeds...



easy money......
