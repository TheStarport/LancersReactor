Longhorn System Requirements Leaked

An article over at Microsoft Watch states that the system requirements for Longhorn have been leaked. They are quite interesting, and I'll quote them below;
Microsoft is expected to recommend that the "average" Longhorn PC feature a;

-Dual-core CPU running at 4 to 6GHz
-A minimum of 2 gigs of RAM
-Up to a terabyte of storage
-A 1 Gbit, built-in, Ethernet-wired port
-An 802.11g wireless link
-And a graphics processor that runs three times faster than those on the market today.

The full article is available through this link.
