CTRL+(number) is how you assign groups. But there is a bug in it. After assigning groups, you must re-assign group 1 or it will be the same as the last group assigned.

Unfortunately, unless you have guns that are designed for sheild eliminating, there is no need for grouping.

There is also a distinct lack of Cyclic firing eliminates the need for grouping.

Haven't heard anyone mention the way I set my qroups..

First you bind keys for groups 1-6.. Group 1 is already assiagned all your guns. If you push the group 2 key it should be empty by default, with no guns assigned to it yet. Then you can just add the guns in manually by clicking them on your HUD.


I group mine as follows..

Group1 = All guns (Energy Bar Drops fast while spraying)
Group2 = Combo (Energy Bar goes up while spraying)
Group3 = Combo (Energy Bar stay in place while spaying)
Group4-Group6 = Vanity Combos. Just looks cool.
