Sell Anubis, buy Drake, and go to Omega-41 to earn up to $200k per mission (reward plus loot). Fastest way to get the class 10 ship of your choice 'at gunpoint', so to speak. If you think you're good you can use the Anubis instead of a fighter - it has much more room for looted commodities.

Or buy the Banshee and head to Omicron Alpha (lvl 18/19 Outcast Sabres) or Omicron Gamma (lvl 18/19 Corsair Titans). If they're neutral take missions from them, if they're hostile shoot them and get rich from loot. If you don't like it quite so hot try Tau-37 and Omicron Beta (lvl 15/16 Outcast Sabres) or Omicron Theta and Omega-41 (lvl 16/17 Corsair Titans). The Bounty Hunters fly Hammerheads of the appropriate levels in these systems.
