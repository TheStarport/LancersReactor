Crash-Drive

Wed Apr 23, 2003 10:58 pm by Crash-Drive

You may have noticed that the NPC ships are really dumb when it comes to mines. Even if you aren't setting off the mines around you, it's practically guaranteed that any NPC ships within your sensor range will be setting them off left and right -- and mines have a blast radius much larger than their trigger radius. When you're picking your way carefully through a minefield, and there are NPC ships blundering around, even as far as 1k away, you should watch your shield level closely... and be ready with the batteries.
