The race can be a pain in the posterior -- until you know how to beat it. There are other threads on it here, but in short:

1. Kick in cruise drive IMMEDIATELY. Get the jump on Hovis.
2. Don't try to beat him in a freighter! Use a fighter.
3. Aim for the corners of the gates of the course.
4. Make sure you know the course enough not to get lost.

Oddly enough, I could not beat Hovis no matter how hard I tried, until I finally figured out what to do. Now I can beat him any time in any kind of ship.
