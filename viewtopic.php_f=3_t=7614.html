aerundel

Sun Mar 02, 2003 11:01 pm by aerundel

There's no need to roll in space. Doesn't help you to turn in an airless environment, for one.

Docking automatically aligns you with the stations/gates, so unless you just don't LIKE being upside-down relative to the station, rolling isn't necessary. And turning off mouse flight will turn you over anyway.

Also, any dodging benefit from rolling was removed when they first put in the target leader, since it targets you on the middle of the very axis you're rolling on =T

And in most of the earlier games that people have praised over Freelancer for having rolling...well...their hitboxes were so big that rolling didn't even matter. So when they were deftly banking and rolling to avoid shots, it was all in their heads! =P

Rolling is the biggest gimmick of space games...
