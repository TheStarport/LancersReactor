Well the Anibus is superior to the heavy fighters that are available up to that point in the game--compared to the Defender, Crusader, and Dragon, it has a stronger powerplant, more cargo space and more armor, plus it mounts four level 6 guns, two level 5 guns, and a forward-firing turret (7 guns, w00t!). However, it turns at a much slower rate than light fighters (although it is still faster than the Titan and Sabre). Unless you prefer the Drake, which is the strongest of the light fighters available before you finish the single player campaign, I reccommend that you take the chance to buy the Anibus--it will serve you well up until you can afford to buy your higher-level ship of choice.
