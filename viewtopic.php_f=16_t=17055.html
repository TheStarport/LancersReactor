Sun May 18, 2003 1:25 am by redeye

@Razor

What mods are you using? The reason nomad weapons dont drop before a certain level is the same reason you cant get corsairs to drop TDC's when ur below level 30 (or whatever ) - in order for a weapon to drop you must satisfy 2 criteria :

1 ) You must have a hardpoint capable of mount that weapon.

2 ) You must be above the level required to buy that weapon.

What this boils down to is that if you cant buy a weapon at a dealer its never gonna drop for you. Nomad weapons are no exception, the only reason they appear different is 'cause you never see them for sale so you never see the 'You must be at least level XX to buy this...' bit. The only exception to this rule seems to be the other class 10's (diamondback, blueblaze etc) which anyone can loot. There was a 'lowest lvl to get a nomad gun' post ages ago but it got lost in the void.

Were you using an Opengate mod? Or one of the mods that removes the level requirements 4 buying ships etc?
