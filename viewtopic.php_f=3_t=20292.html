Sherlog

Fri Jun 27, 2003 10:50 pm by Sherlog

Have a look at the particle guns of the Outcasts and the Xenos. Highest damage, excellent efficiency, and the projectile speed ranges from acceptable (Outcasts) to very good (Xenos). Their overall fighting value is excellent and they make even the GMG guns look mediocre in comparison.

A light fighter equipped with these weapons can do more damage than heavy fighter equipped with mediocre stuff, despite having fewer gun hardpoints and a slightly weaker powerplant.

In fact, I rather take the class 5 versions of these guns - which can be looted - than the class 6 versions of any other guns, except perhaps GMG.
