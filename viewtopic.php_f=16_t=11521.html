HeymlicH

Mon Mar 24, 2003 4:23 pm by HeymlicH

1. purchase a rhino.
2. trade between Houston and LA. This is the most profitable trade route in liberty. I'm forgot what to trade there - you will find out. I think it was some "optical" thing and some "mechanical" on the way back ... But I remember it yields 30 k per run and a run takes <20 minutes.
3. once you have about 150 or 200 k buy the best ship I think I used the Dagger, but there are others ... and the best shield. Weapons don't matter, you will just have to run on your next mission... Don't waste too much time trading in liberty, there is a much more profitable trade route in bretonia.
4. In bretonia the best trade route is between leeds and cambridge (diamonds/engine components). freighters are available on these planets. At level 10 you can purchase a dromedary in mactan/magellan. The best fighter in bretonia is offered in dublin.

One more thing. Don't buy your next ship in bretonia too early. It resets the Bounty hunters to neutral. So shoot bounty hunters (in dublin) until corsairs and gaians are neutral, then purchase the next ship and get the bounty hunters neutral, too. That makes trading in bretonia much easier Of course, it takes >100 bounty hunters to kill ...

Oxymoron

Mon Mar 24, 2003 6:11 pm by Oxymoron

I don't think that your level would be high enough to buy a Barracuda - heck, I'm not even sure whether you can even enter the Cortez system yet.

Just stick to the Defender, buy a nice level 3 shield for it in California, as well as a few nice guns (Justice Mk III maybe), and be sure to be stocked up on shield batteries.
If you're having problems with missiles, just get yourself a countermeasure dropper, preferably the improved variant.
Other than that, just learn to fly better. Practice dodging enemy fire by using your afterburner, and to lead your targets.

The mission is easily possible with a well-equipped Defender.
