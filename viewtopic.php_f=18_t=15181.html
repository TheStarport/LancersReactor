Lol fixed most of ths issues now

The ships as big as i dare make it and still be able to dock everywhere. Got a slight texture problem on one side. EG one texture simply is not showing up but the same texture is ok on the other side of the ship. Weird.

Fixed the turret views too. Looks better now.

Also i figured out why ships sink into the deck.

The HpMount hardpart is considered to be the part of the ship that touches the ground. In most ships its in the center of the model so they sink into the deck. When i moved mine down to the very bottom of my model, my ship stopped sinking into the deck.

A lot of the AI only ships have this problem i noticed.
