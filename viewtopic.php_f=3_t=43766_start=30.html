The question of whether or not FL is "out of gas" probably shouldn't be simply answered with the impossible FL2. My new answer in the form of a question (to the modders, bless their souls) is: is it possible that all FL needs is something NEW?? Granted, current mods are great but almost all of them still revolve around Manhattan, Liberty, Bertonia, et cetera (yes, that is how you spell etc.) What FL needs is for modders to create a whole new bloody universe, scrap Liberty, the old ships/systems/factions. My best memories of playing FL are from whenever I found something NEW. Playing for the first time and discovering pirate bases and new ships and how everything fit toghether was amazing. What I'm talking about here isn't a "total conversion" mod but something new, cooked up by FL players, new ships, new weapons, new ship heirarchies (sp?), new factions, new systems. The best way of doing this would probably be to start small, say an 8 system universe with some ships and stuff (maybe just 1 or 2 systems would be better for a start). Start a new Forum Thread Post thingie to get input from TLR community and get it started, small at first, creating a new universe with back history, politics, commodities, techs, and locations isn't easy, I've tried (on paper). Borrowing ideas from other games may be neccesary (ex: X1,2,3, I-War2, Freespace 2, Tachyon, Hegemonia, Homeworld, Starwars, Startrek, Galciv, Wing commander, etc.) but outright copying stuff from them ie: ships, would ruin the entire point. I hope that with this post I have accomplised something other than fueling the useless debate over FL2, or Openlancer (good luck), but rather give a good realistic idea about something DOABLE. I wish you all well, may God bless.
