{Software} 3DB Exporter v1.0.0.1

Freelancer 3DB Exporter v1.0.0.1 for Milkshape3d made by Colin Sanby

This new plugin is designed to help 3D modelers, and mod makers, to create new single object type models like: icons, torpedoes, missiles, crates, signs, etc. People have asked for this and here it is! The package comes with a torpedo example, three icon examples, and a brief how-to. Questions? Comments?

- Supports simple objects: icons, torpedoes, missiles, crates, etc.

- Includes 4 example models
