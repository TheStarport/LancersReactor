J Dawg

Fri Sep 03, 2004 9:04 pm by J Dawg

This may come as a surprise to you, but there is NO ultimate configuration for any ship, especially the sabre. Your weapons loadout should reflect your personal style of combat, and flying, ie. whether for strike, raiding, or dogfights. The ultimate loadout is the one YOU pick. Some work better for some people and not so good for others. But, just so this isn't a total waste, here's my personal favorite:

2x Wrym Type 2 (or 1 and 1 Tizona del Cid)
4x Nomad Energy Cannon
Wrym Turret (or Salamanca Turret)
Hornet CD
Advance. Cm
Ripper Mine

Alternatively, the other school of thought (primarily) is to use fast-firing weapons, so many people will mount up to six GMG Skyblast B cannons, and a Skyblast B turret, or a combination of Skyblasts and Tizonas. Experiment around a bit. No one can give you an ultimate loadout. It's what you have to find for yourself.
