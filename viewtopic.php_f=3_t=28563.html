But things will happen? I won't just be floating around doing nothing?


I think it'll be kind of like playing alone on a multiplayer server. You could do things such as:

* perform randomly-generated missions for various factions
* ship cargo for profit
* mine
* explore the star systems (hint: look at patrol paths on F6 to find obvious places to go); salvage wrecks; find jump holes
* pillage cargo from transports

But the single player campaign is pretty interesting IMHO, so I would recommend playing it if you haven't already. After you play through the campaign, you can still play but then there's no more scripted storyline but you can do anything.
