Heya everyone, okay - my first releases are coming out right about nowish.

There are several, and surprisingly enough, although i have been posting here for a long time, i have never released anything for whatever reason. So, be kind

What is coming.

Well - the TEMP mod is being tested by people. IF you wish to have a peek, then email me (profile). It also has a bundle of other mods made for SP with it, that will:

Make the storyline easier!
Make the storyline harder, and impossibly hard!
Start you in SP story with your ship of choice
Start you in Open SP as your faction of choice, on one of their bases with a ship of your choice.
Start you in Open SP on any base, with any of the 60+ ships in the mod (that includes FL originals - i haven't stuffed that many ships in a mod!!!).

Other mods
My SP story mod.
This gives you a choice of 56 ships to start the storyline with in SP, also has options of 40 costumes, whether the AI regen or use nano bots and bats.


The Open SP version.
Right now, you can.........
Choose from 56 ships to start with (like in MP)
Choose from 40 systems to start in
Choose from 40 costumes to start with
Choose if AI have regen shields, use nano bots and bats


The new version of this will be out once i am sure its bug free, and you will be able to choose having capship encounters, either just at home planets - or at jumpgates to non-house systems.

Thanks - visit the other forums to give feedback, just thought i would give you all the heads up as my first releases are done

So - keep your eyes peeled!! LOL
