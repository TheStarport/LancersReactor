Um... my first guess would be that you have a Dc defined in your Material node. Get rid of it, and the problem may go away. The second problem may be that you've welded your model improperly, and what we're looking at is a bunch of mis-welded verts. To correct this problem, turn Auto-Group off in MS3D, unweld the model, save the model, and re-export it.
