Hengist

Tue Mar 30, 2004 4:04 am by Hengist

I forgot to say earlier that I also use the cockpit. The flashing gun effects are a lesser evil to me than the bright engine glow of 3rd person. Also I can see my targets better from the cockpit as the ship doesn't obscure my view of the target when they are in my lower quarter.
When I first started to play Freelancer, I used the default 3rd person view. When I changed to cockpit view, I was amazed at how much more detail I actually noticed. Instead of looking at the arse end of my ship all the time, in cockpit view I was suddenly aware of how great all the other ships looked.

However, the main reason I use the cockpit is for the immersion factor.
I want to believe that I'm sat in a powerful space ship (a joystick would have been even better lol).
The 3rd person/external view feels like I'm controlling some little kiddy toy space craft, it's just to arcady for me that way.

Different strokes for different folks I guess. As Voltaire once said (or something very similar)... I may disagree with you but I will defend your right to say it

Hengist.
