I haven't tried the other trade routes, but I found this as my first one when I got a Humpback:

Pick up <<Highly Perishable>> Luxury Food at Luxury Liner Shetland in Edinburgh ($320). Fly four jump gates away to New York/Manhattan: Edinburgh, Leeds, Magellan, New York. Sell at $1200. With any luck you should still have about 230 left. There, pick up Luxury Consumer Goods for $180, and fly back to Luxury Liner Shetland (sale price $990). You should clear over $409,000 round trip. I jump from level 20 to level 26 in one round trip run.

Tue Apr 01, 2003 3:48 am by Jaabaa

When starting off, this is my method of getting my cash reserves up as fast as I know how (using legal means)

When I start, I just kill a few liberty cops/navy until I am "just" neutral with the Rougues, after that it's time to start earning money.

BTW when you are neutral with Rougues, the Outcasts, Xenos & Hackers leave you alone too, so you can sell your shields/guns to fill up & max out the profit.

Seeing as you don't have a "load" of cash at the start, and are stuck in e starflier, I buy Boron on Pittsburgh (120), then go to Sheffield Station (Manchester via Magellan) and sell for 600.
Polymers - Sheffield Station -> LA (Buy a Rhino, forget about weapons)
Boron - LA -> Sheffield Station
Polymers - Sheffield Station -> Mactan Base (buy a Dromedary, forget about weapons) -> LA
Boron - LA -> Sheffield Station (** Buy weapons now, cause Corsairs/Mollys still don't like you yet)
Light Weapons - Sheffield Station -> New London
Luxury food - New London -> LA
etc, etc after that.

Inside of ~3 hours you should have enough to buy any ship you want.

Tue Apr 01, 2003 2:27 pm by Copperhead

Boron - Planet Los Angeles -> Sheffield Station

Buying at $167/- per unit
Selling at $600/- per unit
Rhino 80 cargo units
Initial Cargo Investment = $13,360/- (dun worry, this reserve builds up quickly)
Net Profit = $34,640/- per trip

I checked it out. Yup, this trade route is great for beginners. Easily accessible, fast turnaround time, low investment and good returns even with a Rhino. The pirates encountered in this trade route are Lane Hackers/Liberty Rouges (California) and Outcasts (Magellan).

If you do not wish to kill cops, a little Xenos blasting at Detroit Munitions Depot will fix the Liberty Rouges and Outcasts reputation to juz under attack on sight (which is good enuff to keep them off your back when using this trade route).

Thanks to Jaabaa for the hot tip!
