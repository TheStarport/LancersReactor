Mirage

Tue Jun 10, 2003 10:35 pm by Mirage

First of all, anyone who hasn't, do yourself a huge freaking favor by going (in any explorer window) to tools->folder options->view tab and UNcheck "hide file extensions for known file types".

Nexxxt, you should DELETE the original when you uncompress it. The uncompressed version works just as good. I am assuming you HAVE backed up your old files somewhere anyways--you should. Then when you rename the .ini.txt file to .ini, it will work just fine.

Oh, and btw, that's why it didn't work--FL only reads the .ini file, it does NOT read any .ini.txt files.
