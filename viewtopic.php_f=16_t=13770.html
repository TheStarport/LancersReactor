I took 3 trys to get the sheild generators.

I got dammaged and caught up with the fighters at first and then took damage from the battleship while trying to take out the gens. Needless to say I was in bad shape while I was trying to focus on the gens and was easily killed.

Then I ignored the fighters and approached the sheild from underneath (up the tunnel) and for some reason didn't get any flack from the fighters or the battleship. I didn't take out the battleship but just concentrated on firing guns and missles at the gens and dripping counter measures.

The first gen went down quick, I got more and more fire as I knocked out the other gens, but not near as much as the previous attempts comming straight in from up top.

Also, I really had to work at keeping in range but not sitting still by using the "A" "D" "x" and "z" buttons to control my distance. Up to now I haden't done much slow speed manuvering but it is really required for this mission. Too close and you bounce off of the sheild to far and you are out of range and closer to the battleship, sitting still you are taking fire.

I was glad to be done that one.

Mon Apr 14, 2003 5:07 pm by Zaratomb

Easiest way for me at least. After flying up through the middle fo the structure, you will see the underside of the battleship. Towards the rear of the battleship you will notice some nice fins/fans that project down past the ships hull. Sit right between these and start dumping ordinance into the battleship. The turrets cannot hit you here, and the low hanging fins protect you from incomming fire. After the battleship kabooms, the shield generators will be much, much easier.

Good Luck
