Dromedary MOD

I have a fancy for freighters, and the Drom caught my eye immediately. However, I was not able to obtain it easily because I was enemies with the Lane Hackers (the only ones who sell it) Plus, I was past the single player campaign and fighting tougher enemies, so the Drom's stats just wouldn't be able to cut it. So I decided to make a mod for the Drom. I should have it available in the next few days if you guys want it. here are some of the changes I have been making:


-Dromedary can be purchased at Planet California Minor (California System)
-Armor boosted to 8600 (I want this freighter to compete with titans and eagles)
-Weapons and shield max level is now 8
-Max nanobot/shield batt capacity boosted to 32
-Cargo capacity raised to 500 (a bit too much?)
-raised powerplant energy level to 4500

Any questions, comments, suggestions?
