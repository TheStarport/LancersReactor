Beta Testing

Has anyone else done beta-testing? I am doing my first ever beta test now, on Planetside. It is simultaneously one of the most fun and most frustrating things I have ever done. It is fun because the game is incredible, even in a paritially finished state, and it is frustrating because the game IS partially finished, and as such still has bugs, balance issues and the rest. Overall though, I'd say it is worth it, because I get to play the game for free and help the Devs find the problems with it. This has also made me feel sorry for the Devs, who have to listen to a whole bunch of people complaining about bugs in a beta. Imagine that, bugs in a beta-test.
What do you guys think about beta-testing?

A good experience, but not for those with short tempers or anybody who gets frustrated easily. I got into the closed beta for PS a few weeks before open began, and it was horrible.
