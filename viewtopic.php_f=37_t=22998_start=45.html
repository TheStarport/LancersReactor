Update:

The chaingun works just fine, it even has a name: Steel Rain
available as a turret, or playership model, It has been tested on a Kusari Drake against Nomads and Titans

Specs: level 6, damage: shield 180, hull 180, refire rate 100.00
Man the thing hits like a train but sucks energy like there is no tomorrow, (probably really level 12).
We calculated that if you could keep the energy up to it, if someone somehow fired 6000 shots in that minute then - it would deliver
Damage x rfr = 1,080,000 damage if they hit with all! That is enough to take out a cruiser..........
I cant claim all the credit, I only made the model in Ms based on a design by parabolix, I had help from wanderer and parabolix ..... but for some reason no-one seems interested......

Two other models are in millkshape: a twin gun dome type dedicated turret called Golden Lance for gunboats, transports, freighters, and a larger long range big gun turret called Black Harpoon for gunboats, the armoured transport, and capital ships like Nemesis.

Though I sometimes wonder why we bother......... not many responses to any of our posts - excepting those who have of course.... thanks to the people who have posted....Vorthas in particular, your idea's are noted and some of them at least have been acted on.

Regards to all,

Harrier.
