Sapient6

Tue Apr 01, 2003 12:48 am by Sapient6

I mostly stay away from the SP. Not that I don't like it, just that there are other aspects to the game I like better.

So most of my time is spent playing on a server on my local machine. When they're around I play with a couple friends who join me on the server and we mostly take missions and fly around shooting stuff. When they're not around I play with a different character and spend most of my time aimlessly wandering.

I think there are two real keys to my ability to sustain interest in the "lack of content" people complain about with FL.

1) I'm not in any rush. I take my time and I don't try to accomplish anything "as fast as I can". Yeah, you can potentially map every point in the entire game in short order, but not if you dawdle and take your time.

2) No spoilers. I think most of the people who complain about finding everything really quickly have actually achieved this through spoilers. Contrary to popular belief, not everything worth finding in the game is on a patrol path. So the only way you can find everything "in twenty hours" is via spoilers or having already found what you're "finding".
