Cold_Void

Mon Apr 25, 2005 1:09 pm by Cold_Void

this sounds like a driver or direct x problem to me / personally i've ran freelancer in low resolution on 550mhz KII's with 256mb of ram with the same card,so the problem shouldn't be there.I would suggest trying to change the resolution to something lower in your options and see if that doesn't help,freelancer may have over estimated your card's abilities and put it on a clunky resolution(and resolution is the #1 performance impactor) - if that doesn't do it it's time to reinstall your video driver,find and follow some good explicit instructions on how because geforce's have a nasty habit of not uninstalling properly(hint:its easier to change your adaptor in display settings before uninstalling the geforce's drivers)and if that didn't work you have either a direct x problem(unlikely but try reinstalling directx from the FL cd)or worse an XP problem - XP registry & driver problems is easiest to solve with a complete reinstall but short of that run all your utilities(norton windoctor etc,antivirus & malware scanners,defrag)

hope this helps,your computer should run FL fine(at a low resolution) is all I know for sure.
