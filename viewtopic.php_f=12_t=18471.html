My recommendation: get a new old-school computer. I'm talking Pentium 1 or a Pentium Pro and make sure it isn't faster than 200MHz. Then, load either Windows 95 or 98 (I'd go with 95 because it is the most friendly for a DOS environment). After that, either get/make a boot disk or write your own config.sys and autoexec.bat files. I've been annoyed that I cannot play many of my old yet still fun games on my new computer. So I'm putting together my old Pentium Pro 200 w/ voodoo2 card and putting Win95 (if I can find it) onto it. I'll finally be able to play Wing Commander 4, Privateer 1 AND 2, and Syndicate and Syndicate Wars. Huzzah!
