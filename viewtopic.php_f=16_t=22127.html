I've not flown the Valk yet, but I do enjoy the Hammerhead. I use boring single-weapon setups--with the Hammerhead I'm torn between GMG Skyblast A/B's and Salamanca Mk I/II's. I cannot say much about the Hammerhead because I have flown it so little but forget the Valkyrie. For only $50000 more you get the Falcon and it is so much better that it isn't pretty; the designers gave everything to the Falcon: better armour, better powerplant, better agility, bigger hold, more nanos/batts and nothing at all to the Valkyrie. And the Falcon is even easier to get ... Whoever is responsible for this should be flogged but that's how it is.

As regards loadout, my favourite is one class 8 Adv. Debilitator and the rest GMG guns. Perfectly matched projectile speed, perfect targetting characteristics (8.33 refire, 750 m/s projectile speed), decent damage, and since there is only one shield buster you can use caps lock for toggling it instead of having to use weapon groups. Another good loadout is 2 x Debilitator + 3 x Kraken but you have to use weapon groups and you have to remember that the projectile speed and range are not perfectly matched. And keep your Starkillers topped up since they have to deliver the knockout punch.

Sherlog

Tue Jul 29, 2003 8:01 am by Sherlog

Yes, the Sunblast B is a pretty decent gun but it does only 15% more damage than the class 6 Adv. Tarantula or Pyros Type 3 (872 vs. 759 hull dmg/s). Compare that to the class 8 Skyblast A (1325) or Kraken Type 1 (1526) ...

The Sunblast B is the best class 7 gun simply because there are no excellent guns available in that class. You can see that also by looking at the damage rate of the missiles or the shield busters. Excellent guns tend to have roughly the same damage rate as missiles, and shield buster guns do twice in shield damage what an excellent gun does in hull damage. This rule of thumb would put an excellent class 7 gun at about 1000 dmg/s (i.e. 1050 minus fudge factor). Another way to get that figure is to double the damage rate of the weakest gun. Of course, the lack of an excellent class 7 gun makes the missiles look more interesting than usual ...

That is also the reason why I like to put two Debilitators into the class 7 slots of the Valkyrie/Falcon and Krakens (or Skyblasts) into the class 8s. Using only one Debilitator and putting a hull gun into the other class 7 slot would increase hull damage output only marginally but using two Debilitators ensures that shields go away pronto and so it creates more opportunities for torping.
