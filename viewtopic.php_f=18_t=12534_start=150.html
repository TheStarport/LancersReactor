Sun Apr 20, 2003 9:06 pm by rapid

I haven't started modeling any rebel ship because gun rotation issue was not fixed. They look reall silly on some of the rebel ships when they are going all over the place.

Finally today, i got the gun fixed, they no longer move wildly! and i have put togehter a custom weapon model that is small enough to fit on the wing tip and underneath the cockpit ball of the TIEs. Still working on the chain linked laser effect though.

been pretty busy lately and will be wll for next couple of weeks, and aven't got time to fully test the new weapon yet, once its tested i will proceed and give all TIEs wing tip laser hardpoints for the new custom weapon.

Once i fully got the custom guns under control, rebels will join in soon.

Most of the TIEs now have two red running light at the back under the engine to simulate the TIE effect. And contrail have been removed on the TIEs, didn't seem right to have contrails coming from solar pannels.

lol, what can i say, spell check killed my spelling ability, i corrected it last night.

An additional seperate model pack will be release once all the models planned for this pack (ships and weapons) is completed and the small system is done. Hard to say when but i'll try to have it out soon.

sorry about the delay but trying to put together a more complete package this time, and it will be more fun for you guys and it will also help us get more information to develop better stuff for the next pack.

the falcon is not ready yet, its not up to scale (a bit small), going to have that corrected as well.

EDIT
There will only be a very limited number of new faction in the new system, and ISD will verly likely to be immbile at release. Unless, we get the .sur file and docking issue working perfectly by then. I doubt it though, they might take a while. The size of ISD is roughly 2-3 times as long as a rh battleship and 3 times as wide.

The biggest texture i used so far is 512 by 512, and thats on the ISD.

again, thanks for your patience.
