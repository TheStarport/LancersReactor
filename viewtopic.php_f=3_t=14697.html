In the end this is a personal issue. So personally i think frontier kicks freelancers ass any day of the week My main problem with FL is that, compared to frontier, it is too consoley. Very user friendly but at the expense of depth.

Things that should definitely be in FL from frontier :

- The combat radar.
- The Elite/Military ranking system.
- Ship/equipment flexibility i.e. ECMs etc, + your shields were determined by how many shield generators you bought. Any given hull could be theoretically used for trading or combat depending on how you kitted it out
- Risk factor with trading illegal goods i.e. when you go to sell it can be a police trap. Tho in frontier you never got scanned so FL has the edge there.
- Planets as 3D bodies, not decoration.
- Greater variety of missions.
- Loads more little things like transporting people for cash, mining planets, need to buy/scoop fuel, getting paid to dump toxic waste/rubbish, chance to double cash on rare good via bulletin board.......

Having said all that i do really enjoy FL and there are plenty of features it has the edge over frontier such as many more factions, more secrets and the existence of a storyline. Rumours of elite 4 persist but i think david braben has ruined his rep with First Encounters. Surely microsoft, when they were buying privateer, could have throw a few bucks brabens way? It will be a real shame if First Enc is the last incarnation of elite. Freelancer 2 : privateer 4 : elite 4 would be the game of all time
