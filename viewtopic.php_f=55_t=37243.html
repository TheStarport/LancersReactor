parabolix

Sun Jan 23, 2005 9:19 am by parabolix

You dont need to paste the screenshots into paint. Freelancer automatically saves them (in cursed bitmap ). Go to your "My Pictures" folder and look for Freelancer Shots, all the screenshots should be saved in their by default. I recommend emptying it every once in a while if you take alot of screenshots, as they are about 2.25 MB each, and I play under 1024x768 res. So they are even bigger files if you play under a higher resolution.
