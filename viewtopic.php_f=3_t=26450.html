Sh|ft

Thu Nov 27, 2003 7:05 am by Sh|ft

Click the "Switch to Target" button on your Radar List Thingamagic. This shows all the parts of the aircraft. You will notice that when you shoot it, parts start to change color, and will eventually be destroyed. Targetting a specific component will help you destroy it by moving the lead indicator (the cross that tells you where to shoot). So, parts being shot off the ship is NOT random, but what it drops IS completely random.
