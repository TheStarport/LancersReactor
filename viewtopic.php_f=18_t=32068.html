Helicopter-based ships

I'm wondering if anyone has looked at using a helicopter as a base mobel for a Freelancer fighter (sans-rotor blades, of course). The Apache seems to be perfect for strapping guns on, and would look pretty good. Heck, CorelDream 3D comes with a ready-made model of an Apache, so half the work's done already, so it would likely only need adapting and resizing for use as a fighter model.

Now, I'm not volunteering, 'cause I know next to nothing about making ships, and I don't have any of the software to do it (or the money to buy it either), but I'm simply putting the suggestion out that such examples would make an interesting addition to the vast numbers of fighters now avaiiable.

My suggested helicopters would be the Apache and canceled Comanche for light fighters (C'mon, where are you conna put cargo, really?) and things like the British Lynx and Russian Hind for Heavy/VHeavy, and perhaps the Blackhawk and HH-3E 'Jolly Green', as well as the bigger Russian models as freighters.

I'll shut up now, as I'm sure some of you modders out there are reading this with tears in your eyes... "Bloody idjut, tellin' us what to make next!"

When I originally posted this thread, I was hoping that someone had indeed created ships based on helicopter designs. One of the barriers is that helicopter fuselage are typically much more cylindrical, not to forget the typically rounded front and high level of curves used in their designs. The Apache would probably require far less polygons than, say, a Blackhawk, for example.

@ Sun Striker

Now THAT'S a gunship! That's the kind of thing I'm talking about!

As to 'why' make a heli-ship... why not? The beginning of this thread DOES mention not having rotors ('cause that would just be silly) but the basic designs of helicopters are basically sound, and when compared to the designs used in Freelancer, are quite similar.

Practically all of the ships possess large, forward-facing cockpit windows (as do helicopters). Many have destructible sections that carry weapons (As do most attack helicopters, not including the Comanche). Helicopters come in a wide variety of end-use configurations, from simple modes of transport (compare the Bell 500-series to the Starflyer), through weapons platforms (Any military fighters, but especially the heavies, compared to helicopters like the Apache and Hind), through to heavyweight troopcarriers and cargo shifters (like the Blackhawk and Skycrane).

I'm not saying that a helicopter in space would be good, because that would just look dumb, but their designs could be adapted to create new fighters and freighters. Heck, ship designers plagiarize sources both fictional and factual all the time, so how is looking at a particular field of avionics any different?

I'm happy to say that this only steels my intention to create one or more mods based on existing helicopter designs. However long that takes me will be another matter, but I intend to do so regardless.
