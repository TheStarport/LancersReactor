harrier

Tue May 16, 2006 9:46 pm by harrier

_[BlueScreen_RippeR,

Many thanks .. we work pretty hard at it..

Your questions:

1. Is it possible to increase the rank that players can reach?

Yes .. We can do this with a server side patch.. opening up levels to about 99

2. Is it possible for the Hermes and Nemesis to deny docking with stations and battleships?

Maybe, but I dont know how.. and I have never seen a solution to this problem.. I have gone on about this at length in another thread .. but there is AFIK no solution.. other than rescaling (which makes custom weapons redundant)and the necessary (logical) adjustment to cargo sizes.. which would compromise these ships..

For mine.. I guess players live with it and get smart about where the profits lie..as the capacity of these two ships make profits enormous even at lower margins.... and we continue to get the complaints from the instant millionairs...

OR,

We drastically modify or remove them from the game.. from the feedback we get it looks like small freighters that can 'dock with a rock' are the go.. profits will need (for some realism) to be adjusted accordingly...

The Hermes and the Nemesis are very useful powerful ships, but owning one requires some thought..

Which is best..?

3. Can you add some Battleship Encounters to some systems?

Battleship encounters will generally lag servers severely, however having said that.. we are planning on adding what could loosly be called a 'battlecruiser' to EDI... and may plan missions.. BUT DO NOT ATTEMPT THEM ALONE.. lol

The Preatorian - will be - in the true sense of the word.. OFFENSIVE!! its armament: multifire main and secondary guns, and multifire anti-ship defensive torpedo's will see to that..

Doing this requires a huge amount of work and at best you may see a single 'battlecruiser' with a 'cover' of offensive and defensive fighters, minefields and the like.. the rewards should be 'adequate'..
If you think on it a bit an encounter with a capitol ship and the defensive screen she could throw up .. should be the most dangerous hazardous mission there can be.. not just a pop-out - blow-the-opposition-to-oblivion-then-go-back-and-collect-a-fistful-of-cash-without-a-scratch-on-the-paintwork mission.. but something that players approach with due caution and teamwork..

4. Some new commodities would be great for the final release.

Possible but at this point unlikely..

We are working on some trade routes and the two files that we still have to do some work on is weapons and equipment on lots of bases plus some allocation for commodites,, Currently there are 'pocket profits' that can again pay dividends tho.. it also requires teamwork .. mainly mining.. one player buys a freighter another player(s) run fighters.. the fighters run the screen and take on the nasties (and mine in the lulls) the freighter mines and collects..., discarding what is not profitable. The hold full, it's back to base, divide the profits.. and its fun.. the only real problem is the life of the freighter captain can be boring... but there are some very high yield fields out there..

Harrier
