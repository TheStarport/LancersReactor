Hello all.

I've seen references to this problem, but I haven't seen a fix.
I've got a pair of new capital ships, and I have them set to 'use_med_moors' instead of 'can_use_berths' in the shiparch.ini. The problem is, neither of them actually USES the moors on the stations that have them. They start the docking sequence, then just sit there, with 'DOCKING' showing at the bottom of the screen- no cut scene, no movement, nothing. This happens both with stock bases (Ft. Bush) and with a custom base that has DockMountC defined as a med_moor (copied the entry type from Ft. Bush's ini entry to make sure I didn't screw that up).

Any suggestions will be appreciated.
