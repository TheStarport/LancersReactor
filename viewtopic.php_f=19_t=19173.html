These are actually good questions. Pinger summed it up really, but what i wanted to add is this.

You can obtain small plots via the missions. But with MP, it is my belief that the plot is generated by the players. Which means that each server is as good as the players that regularly participate.

I play on "** uk server **", where the administrator is all in favour of players adding depth to the whole experience by coming up with new ideas. We currently have clans on-line, although this is fairly new. Clans can be intimidating, but that depends on the guys that belong to it. They can also be good fun.

We also have bounty hunting, which for me is a great laugh.

By and large, we don't sit well with players who intimidate and bully others, but as i say, on some servers anything goes.

It's up to you. Give one a go. If you don't like it, vote with your feet (or mouse finger).
