Twinkee

Tue Jun 03, 2003 8:22 pm by Twinkee

to change the names of most things you need reshack or something similar to edit the "namesresources.dll" found in the exe folder

There are 2 ways forward

1. you use the editors search feature to find the name you want to change eg "justice"

2. you find the weapon / ship / station in the relevant ini files (either in the equiptment, the solars or the universe/systems/#name#) You find the one you want and look for the "ids_name = " subtract 196608 from that number then go find it in namesresources.dll

You can create your own names as well. Just add 196608 to the number in the dll and use it as an objects new "ids_name"

Don't forget to save your new dll file and transfer it into your exe folder (backups are your friend)
