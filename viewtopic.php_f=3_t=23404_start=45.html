HI again larry, here is what I did to solve the problem:
first off, I have a ton of money, and have manage through carefull choosing of missions and who to kill and who not to kill (ie run away from) only the xenos and mollys don't like me, so I went to all three bases and bought the SABRE, the EAGLE, and the TITAN. I made saves for each so I could come back and try them again. so this is what I found out
The TITAN and the SABRE have the EXACT SAME handling, and turn at the exact same speed. the only difference between the two of them is that the TITAN has more hull armor, and the SABRE has a turret that fires forward. THAT IS THE ONLY DIFFERENCE.
As for the EAGLE, it can turn faster then or just as fast as any ship in the game. (To test this out, I bought a two different light fights, the banshee and some other one, and it could handle and turn JUST AS FAST). Anyway, The EAGLE can do a 360 in just under 5 seconds, while the TITAN and SABRE can do a 360 in 6 seconds. Does sound like much but it is a huge difference. Also, the Eagle has less hull armor (not by much tho), and the Eagle's turret can't fire forward but can fire to both the left and right bottom corners of your screen (better angle then the titan's).

Personal preferance so far: eagle
