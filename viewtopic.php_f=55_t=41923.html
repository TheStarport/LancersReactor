Chips

Tue Nov 29, 2005 1:28 pm by Chips

Play vanilla! Play vanilla!

Whilst mods may indeed prolong gameplay, they ONLY prolong gameplay because you are used to the standard gameplay.

In reality, compared to the actual game, the mods do nothing apart from add a little "bling bling" factor. The game is what captures people - the storyline, the online communities (servers - MP is great, try it!)... only when you have got so sick of the normal game that you are about to consign it to the bin, then - and only then, should you start on mods.

I also wouldn't start on full blown mods either, start small and work your way up.

If you just start out with a full mod, you will loose 80% of the playability of the game.
