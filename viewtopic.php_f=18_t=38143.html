Lethal Dosage

To stop yourself dying when you fly into a planet, just find the planets death zone in the system.ini folder and remove all the damage (or make the zones smaller so that it doesnt go outside the planet model )

That way, you can fly right into the planet, without any damage done (apart from the very small amount gotten when hitting the planets .sur )
