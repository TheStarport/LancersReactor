Kurgan''s Shipyards Mod Release 1.0

This mod is designed for those of us who want a little variety added to the single player campaign. It adds 19 new shipdealers accessible only from the bars of various stations . These new dealers don't carry the standard ships, only those produced by Kurgan Shipyards.

10 ships available including:
Z95 Headhunter
X-Wing
UDL4 Cheyenne Dropship
Dark Phoenix
Gargoyle
