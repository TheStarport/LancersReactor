acerj

Mon Nov 10, 2003 4:30 pm by acerj

The sound fix worked for me with WinXP. Thanks a million!

The only tricky part came after I set the Fraunhofer codec to Priority 1. The check box for "Use this codec" WAS checked but when I OKed everything, the sound didn't work - in fact, there was no sound! When I went back to the Audio Codecs and double-checked the Fraunhofer codec, it had, for some reason, reset itself to "Do NOT use this codec." I re-checked the appropriate box and voila! Perfection!

Beware the mysterious checkbox demon! If the fix doesn't work for you the first time, check your Fraunhofer properties again! Cheers!
