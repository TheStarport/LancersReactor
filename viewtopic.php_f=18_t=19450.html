Starting Credits mod for servers

This is a simple way to make all players on your server start out with as many credits as you want...

In the file <freelancer dir>\exe\mpnewcharacter.fl

-replace-
money = %%MONEY%%
-with-
money = 500000

I'm sure everyone knows how to do this, but just in case
