Best Buy - Preorder FL soundtrack?

First time I've posted in a while, but it's interesting enough, I think. Wcnews is reporting that the FL soundtrack is available for preorder on Best Buy, with a release date of November 18th. You can find it here and I will keep looking for more info.

Update #1: Confirmed that this is real. This website has stated it's coming out on the 18th as well, and can also be preordered from Amazon as well. I also shot off an email to the James Hannigan site to dig out a little more info.

Update #2: A little info on who James Hannigan is.


James Hannigan, who composed the music for Conquest and is working on Brute Force, and as you correctly point out, we tapped for our E3 2001 trailer. (We asked him to compose something with the same "space wonderment" aspects as the "mystery" mission music in Conquest). By the way he also contributed to the music track on the 2002 trailer!!!

James is supplying us with the music for the single-player story - The Story Of Trent, if you will.


Taken from a 2002 dev update.

Update #3: Got a reply, James said he doesn't know what has been included on the CD yet.
