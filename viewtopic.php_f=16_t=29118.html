Wed Mar 10, 2004 8:58 pm by J Dawg

You just have to know how to use the geography to your advantage. I frequent Sigma 19 because it is one of the fastest passages into outcast territory. I had a horrible time with it at first not because of the criminal factions, but because of the GMG... guess they got tired of me slaughtering them in droves in Beta but in anycase there's usually someone to annoy you. I get flak from Samura too at that jump hole. In fact all of Kusari hates me. But that's beside the point. Anywhere around Gas Miner Ogashawa there are the handy little explosive gas pockets, which the AI is very inept at avoiding. Most of the time just fly into one and sit tight while the computers blow themselves to oblivion. As far as the area around hades... its a vicious place without allies, however if you're quick enough on your feet, or keyboard in this case, the anubis should be enough to get you through. After all, I financed my first read VHF by flying missions in Omega 41 in an anubis. Just use thruster/ engine kill to get away. most of the time the AI has bigger fish to fry and they will let you go after a short while. If this is too much just come at the planets from the northern side and take a far loop around. May be worth your time.
