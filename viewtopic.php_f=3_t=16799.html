Welcome to TLR!!
There isn't an option available to switch them off, they're there to highlight aggressive or friendly fighters in the area, it'll need modding to remove those, if its possible (sorry i'm no expert on the modding scene).
As for the storyline, you don't have to immediately go back to wherever your being sent to continue the story, you can leave it as long as you like. Also when Juni gives you your mission briefing you can often decline, she'll sound pissed off and will say she'll be waiting till your done, then you can go off and do your own thing.
You can avoid the storyline altogether by starting a local MP server and limit members to one, that way you get the entire universe to explore but no storyline at all.
