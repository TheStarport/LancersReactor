Chips

Wed Mar 03, 2004 3:17 pm by Chips

Its kinda tough to estimate a release date due to work. Hence at one point two weeks ago i thought i was about 2 weeks from finishing it. However, some crashes started, and after about 3 days of not finding the problem, i downloaded the last working version, lost 2 weeks of work before that, and started from there.

So that was a 2 week delay basically

Then you have things like - i mod stuff, think its fine, and then the beta team find out that i missed stuff, i didn't get it 100% correct and its causing a crash somewhere (or the funniest one is that i forget to put a file in when uploading for them to test.....which means that it crashes every time they try to load ) or a fix didn't work right/i forgot to finish it off properly. This means that right now i am working constantly on bugs and stuff, whilst they find them. Then i upload versions and they check them again whilst i fix more stuff.

The other thing is that Panther is still working on some ships. We have some going "spare" at the moment, so everyone is checking the ones added so far are balanced, sell in correct places, have good loadouts/correct equipment, and also that everything is cool (balanced).

This means that there are 4 stages left really....

1. Fixing all little bugs/adjustments (includes new bases and stuff)/problems until perfect through testing..

2. Balancing all ships up and ensuring that they are all correct and work well.

3. include the final batch of new ships, test/balance/position them.

4. Final beta testing stage including online with a dedicated server

okay - so there is alot to be done. I am only one coder, and have done more work then most other mods together . The mod contains over 1100 files. Now you might say rebal is 1300 or so - BUT that has 550 ships files, we have only got around 130 ships files. We have more ini files than any other mod. There is one HECK of alot of stuff in here, and we DON'T plonk stuff in......we strive to balance and explain everything

So my best guestimate would be at least 3 weeks left at the best.......but to be honest, i don't like giving out release dates - cause i can never keep them. Heck - this was going to be ready for a german games magazine - and that was way back in like January
