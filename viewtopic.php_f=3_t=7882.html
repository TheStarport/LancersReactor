Tue Mar 04, 2003 4:25 am by sellout

yeah, sorry for the abrupt post, I was just leaving to do some more testing. Its pretty simple how to do it.

go to your UserKeyMap file and add

[KeyCmd
nickname = USER_Z_ROTATE
key = 81

[KeyCmd
nickname = USER_Z_UNROTATE
key = 69

this assigns the roll to Q and E

and then in your keymap.ini file add this under IDR_COCKPIT

key = USER_Z_ROTATE
key = USER_Z_UNROTATE


The reason I didn't post right away is because for some reason your ship does rotate, but as soon as you switch to external cam, whether you have auto leveling enabled or not, your ship will auto rotate back.
