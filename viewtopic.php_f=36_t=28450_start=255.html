Toastrider

Tue May 18, 2004 9:53 am by Toastrider

Okay, been playing WTS 1.3 in SP...

Overall: Interesting. I like the models and ships, and it's nice to be able to go back through the Sirius sector and find new things The battlegroup convoys (battleships, cruisers, and fighters in formation) are a treat to see (although you might want to tweak their AI -- I saw a cruiser in one formation smack into a planet. Ouch).

Bugs I've seen:

Persistent 'vapor trails appear ahead of you when you stop' effect. Not a massive problem, but slightly peculiar.

Busted infocards. A lot of the new stuff added is missing infocards. All the new planets use an infocard from Bretonia (the gas giant that Kensington orbits), and the bases all have the Suffolk's infocard.

The Nomads that spawn in the new and unlocked systems (Z'ha'dum, Omicron Major) are using the older Nomad stats; that is, they're outfitted to fight Anubises, not Titans. Not sure if this is an actual bug or not.

Bribe offers at bases seem to be missing names sometimes (ex: 'You should know that , , and the Xenos will look down on our little transaction').

Odd mission offers; I got an offer on Crete to go fight the LSF. Very odd.

Voiceover quirk: 'Roger that, your request is granted; proceed one'. Jarring, but not nearly as weird as 'Freelancer alpha one-dash-one, this is (silence). Everything's a go for launch. Good flying!'.

--Toasty
