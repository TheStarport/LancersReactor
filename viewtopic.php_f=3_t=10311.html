Actually, you can hit any sub-system with your guns - however, you must obey 'line of sight' rules.

I've killed the thrusters on many a craft - but you HAVE to be careful. Targeting the componet is NOT enough, you must also line your guns up to Hit that area of the craft.
In other words - If you want to kill his thrusters - shoot him in the ass, where the thruster is located. If you can stay on his butt, you can knock out his thrusters without killing his ship. Pretty much leaving your enemy a sitting duck.

Killing thrusters would be something especially cruel, for Advanced Pilots, to do to newbies. Like a cat playing with it's food.
