The Esquilaxitavian Flag

Esquilax, Equine Leporidae, Scottish and Transylvanian noble descent (really), Rabbit-King of Esquilaxitavia.

Well, what can be said of Esquilaxitavia? Primarily it is a paradise, a safe harbour for rabbits, hares and other Lagomorpha from the depredations of the evil, rabbit-eating ruler of Tawakalnistan. Esquilaxitavia has been (unwillingly) at war with Tawakalnistan for... I don't know how long it has been now. Years I believe. Anyway, we fight defensively in order to protect the borders of Esquilaxitavia against rabbit-poachers and other threats, and our pacifist armies are lead by the famous Rabbit Chevalier, also known as the "Knights of the Libation" due to their habit of imbibing Esquilaxitavia's national beverage before combat (and after combat and indeed, most of the time).

Esquilaxitavia is a secular monarchy, and does not believe in religion, although its followers are free to believe in whatever they wish as long as it doesn't bother anyone. The main export is non-alcoholic Ginger Beer, also known as the "Holy Libation" for historical reasons, which possesses numerous mental and physical benefits to Leporidae (and human) health that are still not completely understood by scientists.

The ruler of Esquilaxitavia, the Rabbit-King, is an honourable and benevolent individual, and offers Ginger Beer and wisdom in order to provide people with an opportunity to walk the path that leads to inner peace and enlightenment. Due to this, he is often at odds with the aforementioned Mullah of Tawakalnistan, who lives in his oppressive, windy desert realm and has no purpose other than to spread his evil influence into Esquilaxitavia.
