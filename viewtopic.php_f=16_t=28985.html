Unless you use a mod, you can't get there after SP. There is however, at least one mod that will allow you to unlock all the gates without having to restart SP...I can't remember the name of it offhand, but it is somewhere in the downloads section.

An interesting note: although the factions that supposedly own the systems may be friendly with you, any bases in those systems that were hostile in SP remain hostile (presumably to keep you from docking with a base whose interior was only shown in a cutscene). You can dock with the Blood Dragon base in Tohoku. but there is nothing to do there. Also, the shield on Tekagi's arch goes back up after SP is over (which leads me to assume that the shields in the two Nomad systems go back up as well).
