Very simply... to get textures to "stick" correctly, you should UV-map your model. You *can* do this in MS3D, but its tools are very clunky. I'd suggest downloading UVMapper Classic (www.uvmapper.com), which is a freeware UV-mapping tool that's very easy to use. Simply give your objects some planar maps.

For tilemaps... the tools in MS3D are actually pretty good. The tiling tools are under Tools-->Tiling Texture Mapper. These will allow you to make planar or cubical tilemaps on your objects.

Just bear in mind... tilemaps look OK for certain things- "natural" areas, where you're generally going to go for something noisy, or mechanical areas where you want details, but have to cover a large surface area. Tilemaps generally look like crap if you cover an entire ship with them, though, and should be avoided when practical.
