Dyson Spheres are a theory that you can build a sphere around an entire star system to harness all of the star's energy.

However they are impractical at our stage of development. it would require more resources that we have. Even in our solar system, with the asteroid field between Mars and Juipter.

They have appeared in several books, and the only other visual representation of one, other then FL, I know of was, as previously mentioned, ST:TNG Episode "Relics"

The are major feats of technology and manufacturing. They encompass everything in a star system, and the population would live on the shere iteself.

Theorhetically...depending on the material used for the Sphere, you could technically make it so that the outter surface did not reflect light, so it might only show up on Thermal or Mass sensors...
