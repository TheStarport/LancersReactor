cavok7

Mon Mar 31, 2003 6:45 pm by cavok7

Using one gun is key, preferably your lowest level weapon or at least something that does min hull damage. Too much firepower destroys the rock and whatever was inside it.

I've mined every type of thing and, as mentioned, it helps a great deal to be at least neutral with whoever is in the area. Don't waste your time though with alien organizims. They decay rather quickly and by the time you get your second one, the first has expired.

I find that flying into a mining area, coming to a complete stop then switching to the mode that has your ship move with the mouse works great. You can spin in complete 360 degrees and just blast away with your one gun at whatever is floating about. The loot symbol will appear in your lower right hand side whenever you find something, then just tap the "B" key to tractor it in.
