Xca|iber

Tue May 30, 2006 3:13 pm by Xca|iber

Are you sure?
Your problem could be thus:

What you change in shiparch.ini only changes what is actually the max whatever. However, the game will still show the original stats, because stats are controlled by dlls. If you see a GENERATE... line in the ids_info thing for the ship, it is a little different.

To check whether this is your problem, buy max nanos at the dealer, and take off, if the number in your HUD is different than the number in the info, then you successfully altered the max, and need to change the in-game stats.

This is your actual reason right here.

Any player ship is actually started with a player_ prefix:

eg:
player_ge_fighter6 (or something like that) for the eagle.

It's best to match up the ship from the market_ships.ini and goods.ini files first.
