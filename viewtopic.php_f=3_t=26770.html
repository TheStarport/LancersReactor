corwin02

Thu Dec 11, 2003 1:52 pm by corwin02

1. main menu, or in game, go into the options
2. go to controls
3. look through the lists until you find "assign weapon control group #". (6).
for each of them, assign the keys ctrl and # (on the keypad, not the 1-0 above the keyboard).
4. go into your game (or return from the menu screen)
5. take off
6. now just press ctlr followed by the keypad (1-6) that you wish to assign your selected weapons to. you have the numbers 1-6 at your disposal, so you have 6 possible combinations per charachter. dont forget to re-assign if you change ships or gun layout. to select the weapons, press the keypad 1-6, as appropriate (dont forget to assign the weapon groups to be selected by pressing 1-6!!!)
