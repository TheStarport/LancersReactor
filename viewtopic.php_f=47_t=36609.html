-----------------------------------Chapter 1------------------------------------------------

Personal log of Rodreigo Sandres: 12 November 2957 HD
"Tensions are rising between the great powers, some even say there may be war but we just dismiss the rumurs as overeacting. But i cant be sure, the great powers are all vieing for control over the systems, sooner or later there will be war. All of us in the research department know that all the powers have enough weapons and ships to enialate whole civilisatons. Even we are working on a new device. I know im not supossed to write this but the world needs to know whos fault it was if our invention goes wrong.
We are currently building what you might call 'An Energy Gate' but far more complicated. As far as the tests have told us, this device can speed up matter to up to 10 times the speed of light! This could lead to mankind discovering new races, new technology and new medicines. But with light comes dark.The gate itself is incredubly unstable and it needs to be monitered constantly to make sure it doesnt malfunction, the effects of which may be catustrophic. If this gate were to even lose a bolt in its hull, then, quite possibly, the whole thing could blow up causing unmeasuarble amount of damage to further than we can see. The tests show it may not even be safe for humans but we all live in hope."
