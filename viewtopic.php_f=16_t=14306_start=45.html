Sun Apr 27, 2003 7:18 am by defaultuser

"While it does give a certain style to each faction, I still prefer a more varied designs."

On the other hand, it reflects how commercial lines are developed and sold. The continuity in basic design lines makes a brand unique.

For instance, if Toyota and Honda were each to make a new car, then show them off to test subjects, chances are every one of them who hasn't been living in a hole in the ground could pick out who made which car. It's not something you can describe easily in words, it's perception of the package as a whole.

Most manufacturered goods follow this pattern. It would feel absolutely unreal to me if there were this many ships to choose from, and every ship was completely different.

As for my favorite ship design, that would have to be the Corsair Legioniarre. Simple, yet extremely efficient space design, which means it's the smallest target in the entire game. It's so thin and short, not even the Starflier has a smaller targetable area. You have to fly over top or under it to even get a shot, no easy tail kills here.

It also has the power to handle maxed-out weapons, unlike some light fighters. So even though the weapon slots are 5/4, it's not a problem because you really can plug in a 5/4 combo without bleeding your batteries dry.
