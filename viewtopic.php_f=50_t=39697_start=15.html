Bejaymac

Thu Jul 07, 2005 9:09 am by Bejaymac

Adding ships and weapons is relativly simple depending on how good you are at editting ini files.

There are enough ship packs in the downloads to repopulate Freelancer a dozen times over and most have custom weapons as well. Just remember to get authors permission if you plan to offer your mod for download.

To add them there are two ways, you can use FLMM to do it,activate your mod then add the ship packs, then copy the files from the Freelancer directory.Two problems with this method 1)You can end up with a very big mod, 2)you can get several half filled FLMM dll's.

The second way is to copy the ship files into your mod and open the xml script file with notepad and copy and paste the entries into the correct files.
