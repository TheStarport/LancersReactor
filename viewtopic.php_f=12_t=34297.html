a handy trick if your stash is full up, is to carry your Horadric Cube around with you and use it as extra storage within storage, if you like. It won't help you with the gold, but you can carry more stuff, even if you're not doing any transmutations.

this won't work for online games over B-net. so don't try.

We Made It!

Sun Aug 29, 2004 7:53 am by We Made It!

what can't you do with the cube? there are thousands of "recipes" around on the web for Horadric cube thaumaturgy.

You certainly can upgrade gems, but it's expensive, you need X number of lesser gems to make a better gem (i think it's 3 but can't remember?) so by the time you've got to the top gems you've spent q a bit!

the bolts 'n' quivers thing is only any use if you're using bows that need em and you're picking up the other sort from the bad guys.

Wirt's leg in the Cube will get you to a secret level full of cows!

don't really know the best weapons for a druid, never played one.

Unique magical items cannot be imbued with extra powers, although by combining them on your person with other items, you can multiply their effect q handily. if you can get a matching set (rare, hard and time-consuming) of eg matching helmet, armour, sword, belt, boots, rings, bucklers etc, then that's supposed to be rather tasty (never done it)

you can have sockets added in Harrogath (?) by the smith as a reward, just as you can have an item made unique and magic as a reward. But it's a one shot, unless you use the LAN "cheat" I mentioned earlier. Generally I find skulls are best for sockets, as they increase your all round strength, defence, dexterity, stamina etc at the expense of the enemy's. Runes are wonderful but you can't always predict what they'll do, and it's too late once you've socketed them if you don't like them. Diamonds increase all round ability too but they don't leech life and mana like skulls do. Some unique items work better with the other gems but if you stick to skulls and diamonds in the main, you won't go far wrong.

Always carry a set of small and grand charms, a decent ring, and an amulet, they can make q a difference esp for defence. And a decent pair of boots that will allow you to conserve stamina. Dragonhide is great if you can find it. And a full complement of town portal scrolls, they are SO useful for getting out of nasty fights.

there are some excellent D2 community sites, i used this one a lot (I don't play D2 anymore, finished it on Nightmare and Hell last year)
