Ok hi guys I am here to give you a bit of insight into what we are going to do with FL well to start with we already have a mod in progress, It is not of huge size at the moment but upon release we hope to have around 6 new ships and 3 new systems. After the release of this mod we are regually going to release updates that will add newer stuff.

This mod consists of a Liberty vs Rheinland war and the nomads will also get involved but we think that we are going to restrict them to the border worlds, the 6 new ships that we are adding are 2 new VHF's one for Liberty and one for Bretonia because we feel that they do not have ships that are up to scratch with the other factions but we also hope to make the pirates harder so that they is a call for them.

The other 4 ships are torpedo bombers in this expansion there will be no ships in FL that are going to have a torpedo hardpoint so now we are going to have the torpedo bombers as the only source of getting a torpedo hardpoint. We also think that these will be needed because we are going to add mission that envolve destroying battleships.

We also hope to make the MP alot harder to complete usually it can take a person a couple of hours but in the end we hope to extend the MP so that it will take up to a couple of weeks to complete.

There will also be 3 new systems at least:
Warwick
Miami
Boston
But we will give out more information about these soon
