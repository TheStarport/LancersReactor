Ok. Lets take a look at that Anubis again. Actually, any of the ships will do from shiparch.ini.

hp_type = hp_gun_special_6 <- says this can have level 6 guns.
hp_type = hp_gun_special_6, HpWeapon01, HpWeapon02 <- says level 6 guns on weapon hardpoints 1 & 2.

So the Anubis listing (li_elite2) says it can have level 1-6 guns on weapon hardpoints 1-5.

Same thing with the shield, but only one hardpoint (level 1-6 shields on hpShield01)

Same thing with the torpedoes, level 1 & 2 torps on the singlular torp slot.
