WapCaplet

Wed Apr 16, 2003 4:38 pm by WapCaplet

If you die, you lose all cargo you are carrying (which includes whatever money you spent to buy that cargo). You keep all credits you have at the time of death (repeat, this does NOT include the cost of the lost cargo). You spawn back at the last base you visited.

So, if you have enough credits to buy your ship, then you are lucky. If you were counting on the sale of your cargo in order to afford your ship, then you are S.O.L.
