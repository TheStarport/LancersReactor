Actually, for those of us who still have the demo... you can enable many of the pilotable ships (that the demo has physical models for) by changing the market_ships.ini and the shiparch.ini

Some of the ships you can enable are Britonia's freighter, the Borderworld Fighter and Elite Fighter, All of the liberty ships, the Civilian Startracker, The Corsair Fighter and Elite Fighter, Kusari Freighter, Order Anubis, Juni's ship (Liberty Elite 2 Fighter, With some further modification to the ship database to make it a sellable item), and the Rheinland Fighter and Elite Fighter.

I've come to love the Rheinland Elite Fighter and the Order's Anubis. I had to make changes to a few other files to allow for some of the heavier weapons for those shipse to be bought at level 1, as well as adjusting the price of the weapons since you dont really get the random missions that pay you a lot more. I'll try and get these files on some public webspace for those of us who still have the demo and post a link

One thing to consider tho' if you have the demo... if you make the ships available at any given bases in the Li01_xx, make sure you dont put more than one ship available at Buffalo Base, Rochester Base, and the Battleship (Li01_12, Li01_13, and Li01_15) as this -can- cause the game to randomly crash.

Oh and if anyone has a lil' web space, I can send you the files easy enough too, along with the bini.exe (if you dont have it already
