Pyrotip

Fri Mar 21, 2003 10:02 am by Pyrotip

For those looking for the Ripper Mines read on. Someone mentioned an Outcast Fighter that has it as a lootable weapon. Thank you. I owe you big time. For those looking for that one fighter outta the 20 that must be there here is the easiest way to find that 1 fighter.

Head out to Omicron Alpha. If your in Omicron Theta there is a jumphole right above Planet Vespus, which is north of the Omicron Gamma jumphole. This jumphole will only show up on you system map as a jumphole, but it won't label it. When your north of the planet just zoom in on your system map, and you'll see it. Oh, and btw, it is also not in guide.
When you reach OA head north until you reach the jumphole labeled "Unknown Jumphole". Target it, and freeflight into the center of the jumphole. Now, face so that your ship is pointing to the right on the system map. Right infront of you should be 2 ships. The one you want is the one about 660-700 clicks away from you. Fire and loot boys cuz that's the one with the Ripper Mine launcher.

Be careful though. This place is laced with Outcasts (like, duh! it is their home system), and that Unknown Jumphole leads to a Nomad infested system, so they are around that hole too.

Happy looting!
