Crash-Drive

Tue Mar 25, 2003 9:01 pm by Crash-Drive

"Best" depends on how you define it. The Salamanca Mk II are high efficiency, and go well with c10 guns because the projectile speeds match, keeping the targetting system accurate. The Tizona del Cid are the best c9 pulse gun, but I find them to be a waste in SP. The Kraken 2 has good efficiency, and the highest damage output of the c9 guns. The Wyrm II has average efficiency, but does good damage. The Skyblast B, also average efficiency, good damage, and darn fun to hose things down with. But the Bounty Hunter c9 weapons have poor efficiency and poor damage compared to the rest, only go with them if you can't get the others.

Nope, you can't buy c10 weapons.
