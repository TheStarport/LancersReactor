Thu May 08, 2003 7:58 pm by magimix

Well like most people (one suspects) I got rid of the ship Juni gave me ASAP!. So from that point onwards it was:

Patriot - A great looking hsip that was fun to pilot.

Defender - I have to say I really liked the style of the Liberty ships, and this one was no mistake. However, I did not find it very nice to fly (I wasn't used to Heavy Fughter handling at the point)

Cavalier - The Bretonia ships are a bit odd looking, but this was a most fun ship to have. Reasonably tough at the time, and twitchy, responsive handling (okay, so I'm making likt this is Top Gear or something

Hawk - Reminded me a bit of a Klingon Bird of Prey. Othet than that, an unremarkable ship.

Anubis - A most excellent ship (again, loved the style). Kept this one through the end of the SP game, and didn't replace it until I got to L28.

Lastly, Centurion, Sabre, Titan (in that order).

I like my Titan! It may have the annoying 'feature' of a rear-facing turret, but it looks good, and it's is *bristling* the best darn guns in the game
