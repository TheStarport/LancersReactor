Common problem with lots of folks asking the same thing.

1. When you finish the game missions, for some reason the amount you need is MORE than what you have, not the total that you have. Who knows why?

2. Also, sometimes a bug comes up saying you need another mission. In this case, just take any mission or do some trading and it will eventually go away.

Have fun exploring. You have lots more to find -- systems, jump holes, planets, a few surprises. And when you find them all, try a mod or go to MP.
