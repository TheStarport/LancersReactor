You know, many might think this is a biased look at FL but someone should look at the use of wording here.

"Dynamic Universe" is in fact on the FL box. THe problem is MOST people seem to have a different understanding that what is the definition of "dynamic." Here's a simple definition:


dy·nam·ic: 1. An interactive system or process, especially one involving competing or conflicting forces: “the story of a malign dynamic between white prejudice and black autonomy” (Edmund S. Morgan). 2. A force, especially political, social, or psychological. 3. Characterized by continuous change, activity, or progress. Synonyms : activating, aggressive, ballsy, changing, charismatic, compelling, driving, effective, electric, energetic, energizing, enterprising, forceful, forcible, go-ahead, go-getter, go-getting, high-powered, hyped up, influential, intense, lively, lusty, magnetic, peppy, potent, powerful, productive, progressive, red-blooded, strenuous, vehement, vigorous, vital, vitalizing, zippy. Antonyms: apathetic, boring, dead, inactive, lifeless, static, unexciting, weak


I find this interesting because it makes sense that people who do not think FL has a dynamic universe ALSO find it boring. But that doesn't mean the FL universe itself is not dynamic folks. Couple that with the fact that on the same box that has the words "dynamic universe" it also clearly states a "coherent virtual economy" so the many people that try to combine those two phrases in the past (say bah, there's no dynamic trading involed all the prices stay the same) appear to be hedging the truth a bit to back up their opinions.

Freelancer is what it is and that is different for each individual. As strong as the arguements are saying FL doesn't have this or that, by the pure definition of the words (shown in this post) used to describe it it's not very difficult to see there actually is truth in advertising...just don't try to read between the lines folks and you won't be disappointed.
