"Thats crap! Why cant you blow one up and then get away from the owners. (like in GTA when not on a mission you can blow the crap out of the city) why cant you do that in fl?"


Actually, you can only kill people/blow up vehicles in the GTA series, but not actually destroy a building. You can shoot it with a rocket launcher and there will be a "burning" effect, but it will quickly go away, with the building being good as new afterwards.

Apparently, you can basically do the same in Freelancer - you can go on an interstellar killing spree, blowing up lots of weaker ships, but you'd better steer clear of your victims' territory after that.
And unlike the GTA series, you aren't even confined to being on the wrong or right side of the law. You can support the police by blowing up pirate craft, or earn the respect of pirates by plundering freighters and blasting police ships.
