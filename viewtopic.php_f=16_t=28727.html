"Best" level 10 guns depends on your style. Some are very powerful, but slow to fire. Others are not as potent, but fire more often.

As you've already been told, the guns are NOT for sale anywhere ... not even on basie that sell ships capable of using level 10 guns.

Some more advice: when you find 'em, hang onto those guns -- at least some of them. You can sell them right away for a lot of credits, but then you won't have them when you finally get a ship that can use them!

J Dawg

Wed Feb 25, 2004 6:55 pm by J Dawg

The standard class 10's are fun just for the enormous damage output, but the nomad weapons are your best bet. Just remember to have some of the class 10's from Sigma 13 (Diamondbacks, etc) or else the nomads will send you back to manhattan in a box. The nomad weapons have reduced damage output (slightly) but the advantage that they fire faster and use no energy more than makes up for it... 4 nomad energy cannons and 2 wyrm type 2's dish out so much damage it doesn't matter anyway. However some people believe that using nomad weapons takes all the challenge out of it, so its really up to you.
