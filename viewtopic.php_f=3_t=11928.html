1) Are they always found in the same place? What I mean is, if I start another SP game, will the same derelict ships be found in the same locations and contain the same loot?


Yes... same locations, same loot.

2) I am able to blow up some derelict ships, but not others. They all have a red damage bar that I am able to get down to 0, but sometimes the ship refuses to blow up at all. Explanation?


I don't know why some derelicts can be blown up. The destroyable ones seem to be part of larger groups of derelicts. Other than that it's normal that you can't blow 'em up. It's part of the game to work that way.

3) Do derelict ships "respawn" at all? What happens if I enter a MP game and find a bunch of derelict ships and take their stuff? Will other players lose out on that particular derelict ship? Would it then be possible to go around the universe, find ALL the derelict ships, shoot them, take the loot and thereby prevent any other players from getting anything from them?


I dunno how it's handled in MP (if at all, i want to play on Elite, but it's always full ) but i'd presume that every player get's his own stock of derelicts to make chances even. If that shouldn't be the case it would suck.

4) When searching for them, what is the range of your scanners? How wide of a search pattern should I set to be sure I am scanning all parts of any given debris field?


Derelicts always show up on the nav map if you come as close as 6km, the range of the targetting scanners depends on the enviroment though. But no matter how reduced the sensor efficiency is, the nav map isn't affected by it.
