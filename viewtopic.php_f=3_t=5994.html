Wed Feb 05, 2003 1:38 am by Tachyon

Yep, you can pirate! You can either destroy transports totally and collect the loot, or shoot off the canisters that are visible underneat the transport, if you like being merciful. There are also several variations of pirating, like shooting our trade lanes and so on. The traffic is dynamic, in that ships are coming from someplace and going someplace, and you can also find out that information if you like, so you can scan your cargo, see what they're taking there and perhaps copy them and make some money.
