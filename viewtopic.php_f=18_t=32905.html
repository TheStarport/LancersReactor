The voices are in "voices_base_male.ini" and "voices_base_female.ini", both in DATA/AUDIO. You'll have to do a bit of testing to get the right ones though, they're all named RVP___ but some of them are definitely the story voices.

The lines that say "voice = juni" etc. are the in-space voices, so can't be used with NPC's (Don't think so anyway).
