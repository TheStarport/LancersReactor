I've played through the SP game 5 times and I've only run into this bug once. So I don't know if this solution works always, but it worked the one time I was stuck.

What I did was mounted a missile launcher that fired the most expensive missiles availible at the time. I bought a bunch of missiles then went out and just fired them into space until my net worth dropped back below the requirement. Then I did a mission or two to meet the requirement a second time and I leveled up.

Since the bug hasn't occured a second time, I can't say for sure that this is a sure fire solution but I hope it helps!
