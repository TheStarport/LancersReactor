The FreePort 7 Mystery

I was flying around Sigma 17, from the Atka station to the Sigma17->Sigma19 jumpgate, when I stumbled across something strange - I later checked it and it appears that if you park by the jump gate and look toward the Medium Green sun, you'll see on it's right (assuming you're aligned with the system plain) the dwarf blue sun, and in between there is some strange box looking thingy.

When you get closer (it's around 6-7 klicks from the gate) it turns out to be the remains of a station - there is a blasted docking port along with other broken station parts. These parts don't add up to what appears to remain of the station in the opening scene, but it still got me wandering -

Are these the remains of FreePort 7?

Sat Feb 10, 2007 12:47 am by Rankor

There are acutally two Freeport 7 systems. The one you found with the remains of the station is one of them. That is Sigma 17.
The other is an exact replica but without the stations or other fields in it. (it has the 3 system suns identical to Sigma 17). This one is used by the game story engine to give your the start up movie when you begin a single player story line from the begining (which is THN script) . But this system is "hidden" over near the storyline edgeworld systems where you eventually come to Dyson City near the end of the single player story line.
Its actual desination is one of the Omicron Minor sub-systems on the nav map if you ever "expose" it. But when you look at the system.ini file, it is nicknamed "Freeport 7" system.
