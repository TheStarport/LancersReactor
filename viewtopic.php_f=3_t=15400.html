defaultuser

Sun Apr 27, 2003 9:48 pm by defaultuser

Since others have already posted spoilers in this thread, I'll post an extra suggestion or two:

* If you're going to make money carrying cargo, buy a Patriot immediately as soon as you start. If you re-use your shield, the Patriot only costs you 1800 of your 2k stake. Gives you 25 cargo capacity instead of 20.

Raid the hfuel tanks at Baltimore Shipyard, run like hell. You can sell it at Plane t Los Angeles for ~400 a pop.

Want a non-violent start? Go to Xenos space in Colorado and mine silver. Stay near the trade lanes, and every time a Xenos wing approaches, take the trade lane for about 10k. Exit trade lane, repeat. Take the Silver to Planet LA for 700 a pop.

LA is also very conveniently near Willard Research Station, the only place in Liberty where you can buy a class 3 Gravitron shield.
