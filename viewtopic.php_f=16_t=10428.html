Convoying

Cnovoying in MP for me is great fun when I used to be a trader. Whenever there was a treacherous system to cross and I needed protection, I would join the formation leader (I always try to find one with escorts) then hail the formation leader and find out where he is going, if hes going same place I am, its a awesome and fun trip. Especially if you get interrupted, your treated as a part of the group, the escorts break off and go at the bad guys while you and the other cargo ships use Turret-Mouse view to soften them up for the fighters. And the great thing is, it can reap great benefits in the border and edge worlds, when a lane is interrupted, if there is absolutely no way to get back in, they head to a nearby jumphole, effectively revealing the hole to, a hole you may have had to pay 30k and above to find the location of. So not only do you have great security when crossing bad systems, but you feel like your actually in a fleet, like your an actual part of the convoy. You can see some awesome battles this way aswell. And you even find bonuses along the way, and its very easy to find people going the same place you are by using the Hail command, so far, I have just been finding out more and more about this game, and every time I do it seems to become all the more fun and deep for me.
