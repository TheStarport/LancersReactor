Frobozz

Wed Sep 24, 2003 4:41 pm by Frobozz

There seems to be a LOT of factors:

1. The ISP. I dumped AOL and got Netzero and my ping went from 500 average to 300 just from that.

2. The location of the player Vs. the server. If not on the same continent expect extra lag.

3. The player's modem, settings, and phone line quality. Should see 49k connection at least.
.. (Hints: Try dialing other access #'s. There exists many freeware modem tweaking programs.)

4. Conditions on the server. Many people nearby in combat can cause lag, cheaters cause extra problems.

If all these issues are bad, yes the player can increase the server load, almost as much as a cloaker.
If they're NOT a problem (Good ISP, near server, tweaked settings/clean phone line, no cheaters on server)
then the 56k modem player will have NO more impact on the server load than any other player would.
