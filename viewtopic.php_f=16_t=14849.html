Tue Apr 22, 2003 3:25 pm by redeye

Yeah, it is sneaky the way they lure you into the last missions then give you no chance to resupply. Makes it a bit more interesting i suppose.

Usual advice :

Use your afterburners in short bursts
Use your side thrusters to weave about and give you a longer time to shoot in one pass or just hold them down.
Run away and weave about to regen your shields without using batts.
Turn sharply all the time and in combo with afterburners.
Remember to concentrate your fire on one gun/generator or whatever : Better to have 5/10 guys dead than 10 guys with half damage.

Personally i would ditch the missiles i think you're better off with guns, but plenty of people use missiles to good effect.
As for the torpedos i totally agree, they suck . Still be glad you've killed a few battleships, after the missions you never get another chance - that 0 beside BS killed can really get to u Good luck.
