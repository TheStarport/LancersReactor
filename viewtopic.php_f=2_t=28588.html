Pure

Sat Feb 21, 2004 5:35 am by Pure

Spectral shields. . . . the best reason to select a crusader for those missions against cap-ships, stations and kurgans. Activate them and flak just bounces off you - you're invulnerable for a few short seconds. Enough time to wipe a few turrets off the Ramases for example. In the booklet it says that they give you protection from ONE (the most common - however that is defined) type of energy weapon striking your craft at the point when you turn them on. In practice I only use them when on a straffing run against a capship or kurgan where they work beautifully.
