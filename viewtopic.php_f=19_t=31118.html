Tue May 04, 2004 2:43 pm by Chips

Depends upon how good at playing you are at the moment. If you can hold your own against NPC's without trouble - then go online

Some servers are VERY lively - which means that its a good place to play as you can interact with others. However, they also tend to attract "clans" who might not think twice of blowing your butt away for not handing over money/cargo....which can kill your character off if you are not used to making money.

There are many older servers that I honestly haven't checked out for a while, ones which are probabily quieter and fairly friendly. I used to play when i first started on the [OGZr Geezers In Space server - which was cool, slow and quiet - but with enough people that you got on with them okay. It was a good place to "learn" how to play the game in MP better, before heading on to more lively servers. Make a choice and try one of the many many servers, if its not what your after - leave it and choose another.
