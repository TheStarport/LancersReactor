After some experimenting I've discovered that the seesawing is caused by the ship handling, this means your settings are beyond what the primative AI can handle. The steering_torque, angular_drag & rotation_inertia are what's causeing it, try lowering/raising them to something similar to a vanilla ship of the same size.
