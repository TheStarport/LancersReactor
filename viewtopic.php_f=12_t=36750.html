sry to kill ur illusions, starcraft - just like any other RTS i know have NO "learning curve" in tactics.
the AI in all these games works with reactions based on actions.
sometimes you can see this very well.

attack an AI with an air unit - it will build anti-air defenses as a reaction.

what you prolly encountered are one of the many possible pre-programmed reaction patterns of starcraft.
these usually work with a bit randomness, some good premade decision-trees where you get the reaction for a specified action.
the AI isnt perfect - once you get to know it better - you can use it against himself.

there are also quite some situations that the AI cant handle very well.
mostly the special stuff in hte game - like surviving nukes, counter yamato guns, build a "good" defense against a rush an so on...
