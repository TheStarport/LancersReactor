That's because Freelancer uses Windows Registry... The savegames were located in the ...\My Documents\My Games\Freelancer, but the defauld 'My documents' folder in XP is %homedrive%\Docuiments And Settings\%username%\%username%'s Documents (where homedrive is the drive(partition) where you installed XP, and %username% is your logon username). so just find the old My Documents, and move the My Games\Freelancer to the 'new' My Documents
