Sh|ft

Mon Nov 17, 2003 9:30 pm by Sh|ft

<< Massive Spoilers >>

I don't know HOW you people have so many problems with this part of the game.

First of all, if you're having problems, maybe it's your ship. If you're good then you can do it with a freighter (I did it with a Dromedary), but if you're not so good, you might want to get a light fighter (ever think WHY the Cavalier was sold on Battleship Hood?). As soon as you can, hit the cruise engines. Unless you have lightning reflexes, you should be just behind Hovis (I got ahead once at the beginning. I'm so proud.). Keep to the inside of the rings and be careful not to hit them. Near the end, Hovis will mess up and slow down. Fly past him and finish the race.

Never, ever, attack Hovis or turn off your cruise engines. Cruise disruptors will not help you. Mines will not help you. Guns will not help you.

The Light Fighters (specifically the patriot and the drake) are very easy to win with. The Heavy fighters are a bit tougher and the freighters leave little room for error. If it is laggy, reduce your performance settings. If this doesn't help you, I don't know what will.
