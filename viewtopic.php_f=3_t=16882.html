How about obeying the general laws of physics.

Seeing as we are flying through a vacuum, why are the ships designed and controlled as if they are flying through an atmosphere? If we switch off our engines, we should continue to head in whatever direction we were flying until we counter the inertia.
So, theoretically, we could engage cruise engines, catch up to a slower ship, disable engine while still travelling at top speed, pivot the ship and do a strafing run down the side of the target. (Babylon 5 fans will be familiar with this tactic)
Also, being in a vacuum, we shouldnt be able to hear explosions, weapons impacting our shields, or other peoples engines.

Remember, a vacuum is as empty as Microsofts "got it right first time" pile.
