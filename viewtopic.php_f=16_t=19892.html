You cannot get artifacts and cardamine legally, they are contraband. They are sold at some outlaw bases and they tend to fall out of ships in pirate convoys if you shoot the right ones.

As regards the second question: you cannot send comm messages to NPC ships to tell them that you want their cargo. But the NPCs understand messages of the laser/plasma/tachyon kind, if you get the drift ...

Bases selling cardamine (in order of increasing price):

Planet Malta
Ruiz Base
Cali Base
Ainu Depot
Yanagi Depot
Trafalgar Base
Mactan Base
Montezuma Base
Alcatraz Depot
Kreuzberg Depot
Beaumont Base
Buffalo Base
Rochester Base

Bases selling artifacts:

Cadiz Base
Planet Crete
Leon Base
Freeport 9
Freeport 5
Yanagi Depot
Luxury Liner Hawaii
Kabuchiko Depot
Kreuzberg Depot
Trafalgar Base
Leiden Base
Padua Base
Rochester Base

You definitely want a utility like FL Datastorm or a decent trade calculator.
