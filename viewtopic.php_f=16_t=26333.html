VHF in one hour -- for you LF lovers

I know that this is kinda obvious but for those of you who love flying the dagger but hate spending hours or even days getting a VHF, I present to you:
____________________________________________________________________

How to get a Very Heavy Fighter in an hour -- For you Light Fighter lovers:

Step One:
Pick a Multiplayer server of your choice. I highly recommend Elite or Freeport 3. Create a New Character and join the server.

Step Two:
Sell all your useless shield batteries and nanobots. Buy a Justice Mk III if you wish, but it is not necessary. Now launch from Manhattan, and don't skip the transition scene. It's kinda fun to watch one in a while.

Step Three:
Here you are, outside of Manhattan. Switch to your Loot filter on your radar. Select the furthest Storage Depot and fly towards it. Some of you might already know what I'm planning =).

Step Four:
Destroy one of the Cargo Containers and tractor in the loot. Then Press F1, click Select New Character ---> Select the same character. I know some of you disapprove of this method, but it is highly effective. Sell your cargo at Newark Station.

Step Five:
Repeat steps two and three for the 3 remaining cargo containers.

Step Six:
Buy the Dagger Light Fighter at Buffalo Base. Do some high-level missions until you can afford to buy a hard missile loadout (and maybe a pulse turret).

Step Seven:
Head to the Omicron Systems and start doing 100-400K missions. I suggest going through Rheinland and taking the jump hole in Frankfurt. You should do missions until you have about 1 000 000 credits.

Step Eight:
After a few very LONG missions, you will now have enough money to buy a Sabre, Eagle or Titan. Congratulations, as this has probably taken a LOT longer than an hour, it is still a very fast way to get a Very Heavy Fighter.

And there you have it, your very own Very Heavy Fighter. Have fun. Kill Bounty Hunters. Kill Nomads. Heck, kill anything and everything that moves. But keep in mind that your reputation is probably badly damaged and in need of repair, so killing Outcasts and Corsairs would be the quickest way to remedy that. Have fun, and drive safe =).
