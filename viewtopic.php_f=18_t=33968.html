Bribes and their rep effects

Hi all,
I wanted to find out how the mechanics of a bribe affects your rep and did some searches here and couldnt find what I wanted so I did some research and now know , so if this has been posted before sorry, if not read on;

Reps run in a range from -1 to 1
-1 is totally hostile and 1 is totally friendly with 0 being totally neutral

You can see what your rep is by looking in the game and you can see 20 bars each full bar equals .10 (+/-) The middle line is 0 neutral

A rephack for a faction adds +0.60 to your current rep with that faction

FL then goes off to empathy.ini and looks up the section for the faction u just bought the rep for:

here you will see its list of how the faction relates to each rep.
FL now multiplies each line by .6 (your improvement) and adjust each faction by the answer .

so if I buy an Outcasts hack when I am completely neutral with them, I move up to +0.6 with the Outcasts and assuming I also was completely neutral with the liberty navy I move to -0.24 with them. (see below)

the -0.24 is calculated by multiplying the li_n_grp line for the Outcasts entry in empathy .ini by 0.6

the entry in empathy is
[RepChangeEffects
group = fc_ou_grp
event = object_destruction, -0.006000
event = random_mission_success, 0.140000
event = random_mission_failure, -0.045000
event = random_mission_abortion, -0.067500
empathy_rate = li_n_grp, -0.400000<-----------------------here

-.4 so (-.4*.6) =-.24

This is repeated for all factions entered

Just a slight correction: "A rephack for a faction adds +0.60 to your current rep with that faction"
Acutally, the rephack will raise your rep to the 0.60 mark no matter what your current rep. So if you are totally hostile -0.9, the rep hack will raise your rep to 0.6, which is a 1.5 difference. The rest is correct though. Multiply that 1.5 by whatever the empathy rate is to see how much the bribe will effect your rep with other factions.

*bump for mbase.ini entries

in mbase.ini the lines for a bribe normally look like this

bribe = br_m_grp, 10000, 16101 or
bribe = br_m_grp, 10000, 16100

the 10000 represents the base cost for a .1 improvement in your rep.
In wasabe's example where the bribe brings you from -.9 to .6 this is a movement of 1.5 so the bribe will cost you : 10000* (1.5*10) = 150,000
so for cheaper bribes lower the 10000 in mbases.ini

the 16100 and 16101 refers to an ids_name entry in resources.dll which displays the text for the bribe offer.
