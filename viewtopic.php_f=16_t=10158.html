Hi - had the same problem. The audio codecs are indeed the cause. For some reason, having no audio during the cutscene with the Rhineland Valkyries stopped them attacking. If I attacked them, gameover. I couldn't land anywhere, or use any trade lanes after the cutscene.

Just follow the readme.doc in the game folder. Basically it involves getting rid of Fraunhofer MP3 codecs (that were probably installed with some other dodgy software...), and reinstalling the proper Microsoft ones.
