Nightblade

Sat May 31, 2003 8:38 pm by Nightblade

I've got a few, but I'll pick my longest MP adventure. This had some trade route spoilers in it so don't read it if you don't want to know 'em!

This is kinda long, but it was a 2 hr journey...

A friend of mine (Shado) and I got together a convoy from Manhattan to make a really long run for Lux Goods, Diamonds, cardamine, etc... I didn't know the way, so I was just following him. Our group was:

1 Drome (me)
1 Humpy (Shado)
2 Defenders
1 Rhino
1 Starflier

To make things easier, he led one formation and I led the other, following him. We lost the Rhino right away... jumpgate collision issue, I think, but we made it all the way to Baden-Baden without losing anyone else!

Then we jumped into Omega-11, loaded up with water for Solarius Station. Poof! There went the Starflier. The rest of us ran like hell from the massive number of NPC pirates we'd collected. I think someone lost a wing before we got to Solarius, but we finally had our coveted diamonds! Next stop: New Tokyo, said Shado, going through the Sigma systems. We set out, each of us with 1 defender in formation. I still didn't know the way...

Well, everything was peachy until we hit New Berlin. I noticed several high-level players hangin' around the system, and one of them was chatting on the public channel about blowing up stuff. I told Shado I didn't like this, but we kept going. Sure enough, we come out of a trade land and the group is attacking a station right next to us and has knocked out the next lane we need. Shado hesistates, while I punch cruise. Poof! Dead Shado, dead defender #1. Ok, now out of 6... there's 2. And I don't know where we're going... so we run for the gate to Frankfurt.

Almost to the sigma-13 gate, our lane gets jacked by some pirates. No biggie, but just as it comes back up the same Eagle that toasted our buddies goes zipping by! So we hide in a nearby asteroid field while I try and figure out where to go now. A little research and I find the jumphole to Hamburg... better than nothing, so off we go.

Alas! On the other side of the hole, my buddy lags out and gets toasted by some pirates. So I eventually wander my way back to Manhattan (where I know its safe), the only survivor of our 6-ship get-rich-quick convoy.. 2 hours later.

"So, anyone want to go to New Tokyo with me?"

Gee... dead silence, big suprise, no?

-Nightblade
