ElectricBrain

Tue Feb 04, 2003 10:23 am by ElectricBrain

Just so you know, ive moved ur mouse topic to Offtopic.

You can control your turrets by going into Turret View, which basically switches you to a view where you can spin the camera around your ship. You can aim anywhere around your ship and fire your turrets in that direction. Typically, the turrets are mounted to the back of a ship, intended for targets behind you but there are exceptions -- some ships you buy have the turret mounted at the front of your ship.

There are no AI controlled turrets on your ship. Turret view is best for when you are in formation and when you jsut wanna take a gander at your surroundings.
