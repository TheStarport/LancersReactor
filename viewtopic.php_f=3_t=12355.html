Bribing the Bundschuh can be a pain - that list of bribes has the minor problem that it doesn't list what faction is offering the bribe. There may be someone on that base offering the bribe, but if you aren't at least neutral with them, no dice.

The easiest way to bribe the Bundschuh is to talk with the Zoner on Planet Hamburg, as that's the only non-criminal that will offer the bribe. If you're at least neutral with the Junkers, there's one on Gas Miner Naha, and Planet New Berlin, I believe.

If you have a lot of time on your hands, the Bundschuh are actually friendly with the GMG, so doing missions for the GMG helps all around. This is excruciatingly slow, however, and you're better off just bribing. If you insist on blasting someone, the Rheinland Military is the best, followe by the Rheinland Police. Blasting Rogues, Hackers, Hogosha, or Xenos works too, but the returns are abysmal.

You have to get back to dead neutral to buy what I'd assume you're looking for.
