Hmmm, you aren't editing the files inside the Freelancer folder directly are you?
The best thing to do is to download Freelancer SDK from EOA Players Consortium, install it on your desktop and edit those files. One you have finished editing them to what you want, right click on the folder and "Add to archive" (if using WinRAR) naming it YOURMOD.zip.flmod.

You will need to do a complete uninstall-reinstall of Freelancer and FLMM now.
