spacedrive

Wed May 28, 2003 2:25 am by spacedrive

A really good route I've found is between New Tokyo and Freeport6. You stay in the Northern areas of the universe map and the potential cargo thieves can be dispached easily with a loaded Humpback. You get consumer goods for $107 per unit at New Tokyo and sell them at Freeport6 for $390, then you get Niobium at FR6 for $287 and sell at New Tokyo for $512. In a Humpback you can net $127,000 in a round trip. For a brave person, try going to Vogtland base and getting diamonds for $285 and selling at New Tokyo for $1650. A run like that will net you $341,250 in a Humpback. You may not want to try it in a Dromedary, it's a tough route and you need some good weapons to get past some areas. If you aren't on neutral terms with the Red Hessians, don't bother trying since that base is controlled by them and they will likely lay some heavy smackdown on you before you even find the base.
