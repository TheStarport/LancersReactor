Since Freelancer uses a lot of nVidia, I would suggest going with something along that line.
If you ever look at your Freelancer Configuration Data Base (note pad) located in the Freelancer exe, I think you'll see a small list of compatable "hardware" along with a list of cards that have problems with it. (I still can't really make much sense of it)

At present, my laptop is running an ATI Radeon Express M200, and although Freelancer doesnt recognize it, (UNKNOWN) it still functions exceptionally well. The difference with this card is that it's running the graphics of Freelancer in "Wide Screen" (probably the reason I got a "Unknown graphics card" warning when I first cranked up Freelancer.

If your card is just now starting to go flakey...just consider replacing it if everything worked fine up until now.
My oldest PC came out around the time Freelancer was released, so it had no problem running the game. It has an intel graphics chip set on the board...and still runs the game without a hitch.
