Seal Universe, modders needed

Hey all.
I'm working on a mod that adds 4 new factions (Shadow Government, The Seals, The Hunters, The Hunter Elite and the Civilian Defense Force). The Seals are at war with the Hunters, but they are on the other side of sirius. So they need to cross the Neutral zone. Some systems have been completly wiped out, others have been damaged when the Seals and the Hunters broke trough.

All the new factions get new ships and there will also be new battleships. Also, most of the lvl 10 weapons changed to lvl 9 so that the lvl 10 weapons are really special. The battleships will be really strong and expensive, so you need a clan (and some torpedo bombers) to destroy them.
The new factions get new systems (exept for the CDF).
This mod will only work online, because if you join, you must choose between the Seals, Hunters or Civilian Defense Force. Example: If you choose to be Seal and your name is Tehn00b, you create a (new) account called: [Seal}Tehn00b.
If you are in a faction calld n00bs, this will be your name [Seal}[N00bs}Teh00b *
The admin will then place you on the Seal homeworld and will edit your rep(Seals very friendly, Shadow Government friendly, CDF neutral, Hunters hostile and Hunter Elite Very hostile).
*Your whole clan may only exist of faction.

Anyway, enough information now.

Here are the modders I need:
Someone who can add mission to bases.
Someone who can texture ships.
Maybe someone who can create ships (I can create basic ships).
Maybe a weapon creator.
Someone who can create factions.
Someone who can edit ships INI files.
someone who can add the ships to the game without crashing (didn't work for me )

My ships:
http://www.freewebs.com/sealuniverse/hauler.bmp
http://www.freewebs.com/sealuniverse/emp.bmp


!!! IMPORTANT NOTE !!!
This mod will be for our server only, and will be updated weekly or so, so that there will be new clan territory, progress in the Seals VS Hunters war (bases destroyed, new weapons completed, systems destroyed and so on) and more.

Any help would be welcome.


Greetz , Sealnova
