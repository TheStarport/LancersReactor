Jump Gate = Wormhole?

For those of you who have finished the campaign, you are familiar with the jump gate, which is not too unlike those of FreeLancer. Has anyone ever thought of these to be wormholes in spacetime? If so, how could a ship of such a small size ever create enough mass (or energy to convert into mass) to cause a collapse of spacetime on both ends . The fact is, it is impossible, with current knowledge, for a jump gate to function unless the process is initiated on both sides at the same time. Even if that does happen, the intense gravity would mean that it would take several, or even thousands, of years just to cross the wormhole, meaning that anything that goes in, will come out in a completely different (yet the exact same) existance than when they entered.

So what I am trying to say is, jump gates dont work the way they make you think they do.
