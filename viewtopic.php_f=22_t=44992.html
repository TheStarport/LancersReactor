@ w0dk4
I don't believe that Freelancer is, or should be an "Archade" game. It's still a space simulation game with some "archade" effects.
If you treat and mod Freelancer into an archade game...it will go the route like other archade games and loose its popularity. I've seen this too often with servers that have come and gone because it's gamers wanted an archade game and the mod developers gave them what they wanted.
The gameers got tired and left the servers.
It's like a disease that causes good servers to die.

Freelancer needs to evolve into a better space simulation game. This was the original intent of it's developers and all we need to do is unlock that potential.
I am afraid if we allow it to turn into just another archade game...even this site would go the "way of the buffalo".

You have to decide how you want it to evolve.
If you want just another "in your face" space shooter "archade" game..it's going to die and go the way of Atari "pong" like all the other archade games.

It's the realism with minimal archade attributes that is one of the major appealing factors of this game.
The developers were a pretty smart lot. They didn't make it too real and they didn't make it too "unreal".
