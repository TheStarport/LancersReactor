The files to edit are: Gate_Tunnel.ini, and JumpEffects.ini, in the \Data\FX folder.
You can change the color, duration, and speed, of jumps by editing the appropriate entries.
As to your accellerator idea, you'd need to have a custom model built, add it to the SolarArch.ini, make it Dockable, and add a loadout for it in the Solar\Loadouts.ini file.
