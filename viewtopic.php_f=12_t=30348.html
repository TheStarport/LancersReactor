Neo_Kuja was sitting in the bar on Pittsburg, when the door was flung open and two shots were fired. Neo swung around to see a man, holding a Heckler and Koch sub-machine gun in his hands. “O oh, this is trouble” he thought, automatically hiding his Colt 45.

“Everybody sit still and nobody will get hurt, you…” the man pointed at the bartender “get the head of the BP’s on the line, I want to talk to him.” “You..” he pointed at a beautiful blonde girl at the back of the bar, “come here”

She walked toward him reluctantly, when she got there, he grabbed her by the hair, plucked out a knife and slit her throat. He pointed at the bartender with the knife “Tell Aod (head of the BP’s) what you just saw, and then you tell him that Tawakalna want him to immediately release pSYCHO and fly him here.”

“Don’t flush the toilet… don’t flush the toilet” Neo heard the guy next to him muttering and was astonished by the words, this guy clearly was not normal. On his other side, he recognized EB the only cyborg in the FS. A plan began forming in his mind.

“You want to be a hero?” Neo talked softly, he didn’t want to upset the terrorist.
EB glanced at the stranger and then replied equally soft “I might consider it, as long as my circuits don’t get fried.”

“Just play along OK I know what I’m doing” with that words Neo stoop up, glad that he was wearing a jacket hiding his trusted colt.

Tawakalna saw the man standing “Didn’t I tell you to sit still!?”

“Sir I’ve got a bit of a problem, this is my brother, he is a cyborg, he has to be plugged into his computer every four hours, otherwise his implants will go haywire,” Neo looked very upset by the idea that his “brother” might go crazy “Sir he needs to be plugged in within the next five minutes please may we go to our room.”

“No sit down and shut up, your brother can die for what I care.” Tawakalne waved his H&K around menacingly.

“Nice try, I could have told you that he will flush it down the toilet” Zlo was in a very bad mood, and this stranger’s attempt at heroism just made it worse

“Don’t mind him, in about 5 mins just start going crazy OK” Neo didn’t bother himself by listening the weird guy sitting next to him.

“Easy for you to say, my reputation will be shot and they will want to remove my implants” EB was not in the mood for this.

“If I were you I’d do it, otherwise you may just die” Neo said threateningly.

“You come here,” Neo pointed to the girl sitting next to Zlo, his expression menacing.

“Don’t you dare touch her you dirty piece of toilet paper” Zlo jumped up and stood in front of sw.

Tawakalna sung his H&K in Zlo’s direction and pulled the trigger, who was hit in the stomach.


“Now start doing it now!” Neo’s voice was urgent.

“Fried circuits … fired circuits … burning circuits …agony … I need my computer! Give me my computer my circuits are frying, firing, burning….the nanobots will attack my brain! Stop them! STOP THEM! They’re hacking my brain! HELP! HELP!” EB stumbled around screaming.

Tawakalna swung around toward EB who moved to his side, it was just what Neo needed, he fired two shots, one hitting Tawakalna in his hand holding the H&K the other hitting him in the shoulder. He ran to where Tawakalna lay and grabbed the H&K and the bloodied knife “Game over tonto”
