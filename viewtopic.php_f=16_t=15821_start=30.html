When not in cruise mode, I fly exclusively on engine kill, using afterburners, reverse, and strafe for manuevering. This gives a constant 200 speed rating, while never dropping below 90% burner fuel.

Additionally, when i hear the missile warning while in cruise mode, i hit the engine kill key, and drift away at 300, safe from cruise missiles. This can cause a problem in asteroid fields, but even then you can often get enough space to safely re-trigger your cruise engines.

In the event I can't escape from those persistent homing missiles, I'll turn around (slowly) and head right towards the enemy while engaging cruise engines. This works for me probably 90% of the time vs. the AI. It would be a completely different story against a human player though.

I'll have to use those mines alot more. I never even considered it previously. Not sure why Thanx for the good tips!
