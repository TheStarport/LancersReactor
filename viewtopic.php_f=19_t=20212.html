LancerGuard Anti-Cheat Mod

LancerGuard is a small, server-side anti cheat mod that is designed to beat the most common online cheats/hacks. LancerGuard will bypass or nullify the following cheats completely:

Invincible Shield
Invincible Hull
Cloak
Nomad Lasers/Torps/Turrets

LancerGuard works completely server-side, clients do not have to install anything.

The mod is available for download here: http://www.wsheppard.pwp.blueyonder.co. ... rGuard.zip
