Incomplete SUN definitions: WARNING: ArchDB: Unable to find

Is this familiar? Do you hate it as much as I did when I was looking for relevant warnings in my mod changes but was swamped with these and other red herrings instead?

Grrrr....!!!

Microsoft / Digital Anvil did not complete the sun definitions in StarArch.ini.

Here is the completed file: http://downloads.rrjds.com/Fixed_stararch.zip

Put this together with my ENVMAPBASIC.MAT and ENVMAPGLASS.TXM fixed files from here for a complete solution.

You can then incorporate them into your own mod and/or other mods that are causing you these headaches, and you will have a clearer FLSpew / FLSpit log to debug your own problems and waste less time chasing red herrings!! Go a step further and add them into the .flmo files and you will have the fix every time you load those mods - unless those mods change these files, that is.
