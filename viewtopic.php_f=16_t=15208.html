Actually once you get a Drone/Dromedary, you can make boring and safe cash route in Kusari system. Simply buy Luxury goods from New Tokyo and sell them at Planet Kyushu for $190 profit each. Guaranteed NO combat since Blood Dragons and Golden C. is not hostile by default. On Kyushu, buy Niobium and sell those off at New Tokyo for $100 profit each. Considering it's such a safe and short jump, I think it's the one for you.

If you want more profit and low risk, I recommend New Tokyo to Colorado route.
Pick up Engine Components in Kusari System and sell it at $500 profit each and buy Luxury Consumers Goods and sell those in New Tokyo for $600 profit each.
You'll probably encounter lane hackers, xenos, liberty rogues on the way but they hardly pierce a Drone's Shield much.
