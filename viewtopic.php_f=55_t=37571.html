Striker44

Wed Feb 09, 2005 8:21 pm by Striker44

Out of the VHF class, the Eagle is probably the best. Basically, the Titan has the best armor but slowest turn rate, the Sabre has the best firepower, and the Eagle has the best agility (relatively speaking, for a VHF, lol). The turning advantage usually puts it over the top. The order Anubis barely qualifies as a VHF and is far inferior to any of those three.

That said, the best ship isn't necessarily a VHF. Plenty of pilots fly the super-agile Drake Light Fighter, and I personally like the challenge of flying something like a Banshee instead of my ship doing half the work for me. So it's really an open-ended question.
