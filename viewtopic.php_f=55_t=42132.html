Tue Dec 13, 2005 3:52 pm by H.Q. Victor

"Open Single player", is a mod. You use Freelancer Mod Manager to activate it, then start a new game. It starts after all story missions are complete, but you are level 1. You then can take off and run missions for any faction you want, go minning, or what ever you want. No Juni or King to bump into. The story line game, after you complete all missions, you can freelance as well, but Open SP makes it so you don't have to. Take off for the outer rim systems or where ever you want, all gates are open.
