Information on the Osiris

Now, Im not asking for any info on the Osiris but Im using this forum to express my ideas.

Battle Ship: Osiris
Primary Weapons: FM Bombardment Cannon x2
Secondary Weapons: Battle Ship Flak Cannons x4
Auxiliary Weapons: Missle Flak Launchers x2

The Battle Ship codenamed Osiris was originally created in the shipyards of the Alaska System, a system completly locked off from the rest of Sirius. The Osiris was the first prototype vessel of its nature to be created, utillising all the military secrets of Liberty mounted on one ship.

There are two theorys concerning the cloaking capabilities; either the sytem uses modified Nomad technology or the power systems needed to power it have been significantly miniturized to create the power requied. Nether the less the system has so far worked flawlessly and complete invisibility has been achived and maintained.

Also, this ship includes various other modifications such as: Hardened Hull panels, Improved Comms, Highly Efficent Power Sytems, Radiation Protection, Upgraded Secure Chanels, Larger Storage Capacity, Improved Repair Facilitys and other numerous modificaions to Ship Systems, Conditions and capabillites.

When Orillian descovered to Nomad threat he gathered his most trusted colleges and together persuaded the crew to form The Order. Then Orillian captured a number of secret Liberty fighters, commendered the Osiris and left Alaska for Omicron Minor where he and the Order created a base on planet Toledo.
Then the Osiris would contribute a major part in the Orders plans. The Osiris showed its use with the attack on Admiral Shultkzy with the cruiser carrying the Admiral destroyed and six Order fighters penetrating the Manhatten defense grid undetected until the cruiser was destroyed. Then Orillian used it a hidden base of operations to eliminate the Nomads anywere in the colonies but although it was effective the Osiris was reterned to Toledo were it would wait until they rediscovered the location of Trent and the artifact.

The Osiris is also credited with a number of cruiser kills, as its opponents crumble in the face of its supreme firepower. Thus the Osiris has proved itself as the ultimate in Battle Ship design, and remains in the hands of the Order, waiting of the time of reckoning when the Nomads return....

Current Location: Unknown, possible locations include: Toledo, Omicron Minor, Nomad Dyson Sphere, Omicron Major.
