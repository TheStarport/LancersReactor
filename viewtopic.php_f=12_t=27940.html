Griffon_26

Wed Jan 28, 2004 8:50 pm by Griffon_26

So you got past the mines?

You need to eject from your ship (Shift+E) and fly right up to it in your space suit so your nose is almost touching.
Once close enough you will get a movie...find out the rest for yourself
To get back into ship target it, get withing 30M of it and press Shift+E again.

Anyway, don't have your hopes in the plot being great, its short, and not exactly brilliant. The game doesn't focus on the story, and once the Khaak (threat) come from the story missions they stay the rest of the game! The games main focus is well really doing what you want, building an empire of Factories, having your own economy (requires alot of thinking), build your own massive fleet, go hunt khaak, or earn a living a different way by shooting pirates or other ships until they bail, so you can sell their ships. Alternatively you could get a Police licence so that every pirate you kill gives you money. Or you could just buy a destroyer and kill everything that moves in every sector

Anyway, don't hesitate to email me or post here any other questions you have.

Bob, full walkthrough for all SP missions here : http://www.the-commander.com/x2mainstorypart1.htm
Don't worry, it has them under each part so you only need to see the part your at.
