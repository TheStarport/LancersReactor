No its a not a problem.

Those are small mods people made and posted tutorials for mostly. Others like me are learning from them and using those ideas in our own mods which are much bigger.

The best known large mod is the B5 mod.

The little mods are all stepping stones to something better. Well worth trying individually but at the end of the day they are just small mods and not designed to work togather.

No two mods would work together if they used the same files anyway.

Though their is the chance that a 2 mods that used different files would work togather but thats a long shot. Still, I couldnt have got as far as i have without the train mod and tutorial or other mod tutorials those small mods spawned.
