How do you earn money mining?

I have done mining in a few asteroid fields, but most dispensed water, scrap metal and oxygen (how scientifically you can get oxygen is beyond me - suirely it would dissipate into space). I did mine copper (can't remember where).

However, I have only found two lucrative mining areas.

Dublin System: By Graves Station, shoot the rocks, you get Gold. Deliver it Alster Shipyard.

Sigma-17: In the massive cloud by the Unknown Jump Hole there are a load of rocks, and you shoot them, you get Alien Artefacts. Take them to Planet Manhaatan. Beware: Contraband alert!!!! (But you knew that anyway)

You can either use the Titan, Eagle or Sabre because of their shield cpabaility. Or, you can use a Dromedary or Humpback or Drone if you feel safe enough to maneuvre in asteroid fields.

The only mining that is genuinely profitable is by Graves Station, and selling the gold straight to graves station. If your ship is large enough to justify flying somewhere gold sells well you would be making more money by doing normal trading missions or piracy.

The only times you should mine for profit are:
When your starting out and cant afford a freighter.

You have a freighter, but cant afford cargo. (Due to dying and losing your cargo and having no spare cash, or due to bad planning)

You have a fighter, but cant afford the guns/shields to do missions. (Due to bad planning)

The thing about mining is all you need is a $500 gun/turret... thats it. Mining is a way to make the broke rich - not the rich richer.
