After doing everthing I could think of, and following most of the guides on this site multiple times, I stumbled on a fix.

1. Only attempt this if you have no other choice.

2. I updated my soundcard drivers (SB Live), got the latest DirectX(9b?), and then downloaded the K-Lite Codec pack 3.20.

http://doa2.host.sk/codecs/

3. I had given up, but I tried just for the heck of it, and there was sound. It was a magical moment.
