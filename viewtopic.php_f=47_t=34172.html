RPG-Omega

Another RPG by the creator of Liberty Vs Bretonia, Outcast 1-1 (me)

This RPG tells of Life in the Omega Systems. Systems, ships and factions allowed are:

Omega 3

Freeport 1-Zoners-Sells Hawk and Dromedary
Freeport 1-Bounty Hunters-Sells Pirhana
Douglas Station-BMM-Sells Hawk, Clysedale
Rugen Station-Daumann-Sells Hawk, Clysedale

Omega 5

Cadiz Base-Corsairs-Sells Centurion
Ronnenburg Base-Hessians-Sells Dagger

Omega 7

Freisadt Base-IMG-Sells Hawk, Falcon, Humpback
Freisadt Base-Bounty Hunters-Sells Barracuda
Elbich Mining Facility-Kruger-Sells Hawk
Breisenn Mining Facility-Daumann-Sells Falcon, Humpback

Omega 11

Solarius Station-Daumann-Sells Falcon
Freital Base-Hessians-Sells Stiletto

Omega 41

Leon Base-Corsairs-Sells Centurion and Mule
Freeport 5-Zoners-Sells Falcon and Dromedary

Zoner Ships: Hawk, Falcon, Dromedary
BMM, Daumann, Kruger, IMG Ships: Hawk, Falcon, Clysedale, Humpback
Hessian Ships: Dagger, Stiletto, Dromedary
Corsair Ships: Centurion, Legionaire, Enhanced Mule (equal stats to Dromedary)
Bounty Hunter Ships: Pirhana, Barracuda

RULES

1. Only 1 post per day. This is so people can keep up with the story.
2. You may only be a faction above.
3. You must start with a light fighter, you can buy a heavy fighter if you want.
4. For this RPG, stats of ships have been changed.
4a. Hawk=Dagger=Legionaire=Pirhana
4b. Dromedary=Mule
4c. Stiletto=Falcon=Centurion=Barracuda
5. Try and keep the number of Civilians, Hessians and Corsairs equal.
6. No moving into O-Gamma, O-Theta, Cambridge, Stuttgart or Dresden. Unless I say so (and i probably wont)
7. Post your RPG name, Faction, home base, ship and location at the top of each post.
e.g
Name: Fred Smith
Faction: Kruger
Base: Elbich
Ship: Hawk
Location: Elbich


This RPG is set in the Omega systems, as people dont often go there, and there are many things happening there.

NAME:Enrique Chavez
SHIP:Legionnaire
BASE:Cadiz
FACTION:Corsairs
LOCATION:Omega-7

Enrique was sitting on his fighter's cockpit. Three of his five wingmen were hiding behind the asteroids between Elbich facility and the jump gate to Stuttgart. Two Legionnaires appeared towing a badly damaged Humpback with their tractor beams.

Enrique:"Felipe, Jose, get that piece of junk into position and then get ready"
Felipe:"Roger"
Jose"Aye, skipper"

Enrique had made a genius plan. The Humpback's comm system was to send an automatic distress call to a Kruger silver convoy coming from Elbich facility. When the convoy would detach couple of it's escorts to help the ship, two of Enrique's wingmen would take them out. Rest of Enrique's wing would deal with the weakened convoy. The convoy was about to pass, Enrique and three Legionnaires hid behind asteroids near convoy's route. Enrique sent a signal to Humpback to start sending a distress call. The convoy was 10k away from Enrique. The Humpback started sending a signal.

Humpback:"This is Kruger Lambda 15, we have taken severe damage and need help immediately. Life support is failing, we've lost power to several decks...."
The transmission ended.
Convoy leader:"What the!Iota four and five, go and check it out"
Iota 4:"copy that Iota 1"
Iota 5:"understood"

Two Hawks split from the convoy and headed towards Humpback. The convoy was near Enrique's men. Now was time to act.

Enrique:"All ships!ENGAGE THE KRUGER SHIPS!OPEN FIRE"
The convoy was hailed with intense laser and missile fire from the four Legionnaires. At the same time, the two Hawks flew to humpback.
Iota four:"I'm going out in a spacesuit to check it out"
Iota 5:Okay be careful.
Iota 5 pilot opened cockpit hatch and climbed out in a spacesuit, he went to Humpback's hatch, opened it and went inside.
Iota 4:"This is a trap!There's no one in!Holy crap this ship is set to blow up!"
The Humpback exploded, killing iota 4 pilot, the two corsairs caught the surprised Iota 5 and quickly ended his life.

Meanwhile on the convoy:
Enrique and another Legionnaire started shooting the transport with missiles, after first salvo it exploded, spreading it's contents wide open. The two other Legionnaires took care of the surprised Hawk pilots. After the Kruger men were dead, Enrique ordered his men to tow in the silver:"okay let's bring it in, and get the hell outta here before more of them show up"
One of the pilots who had taken care of the lone Hawk contacted Enrique:
"It's all done here. Both of 'em are dead"
Enrique and his men activated their cruise engines and headed for Omega 5 jumphole. It was 10th such incident in last two months and Enrique was already gaining some fame. Now to Cadiz to celebrate the victory.
