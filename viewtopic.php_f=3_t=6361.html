4 ways of improving reputation with any given faction:
1. attack their enemies
2. ally with their friends
3. accept and successfully complete missions offered by a member of the faction
4. pay a bribe to have your reputation with the faction improved

If you land on a base that hates you but isn't actually hostile, the bartender is the only person who will talk to you and often has a bribe.
