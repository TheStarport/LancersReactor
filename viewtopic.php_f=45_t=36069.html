Lookng for a Few Good Pilots

As commander of the Bretonia Navy clan on the Topfreelancers server, I'm looking for a few good pilots to help us win the war against the Corsair Confederation.

We have a good role playing environment and our server mod provides you with modded ships equipped with serious weaponry.

We do it with a light touch -- come join the few, the proud, the over dressed . . .

We have Ventrillo so we can chatter away at each other while we fight. We've got folks from all over the globe on most times of day and night.

contact me at this email address:

raptor@topfreelancers.net

or post in the forum at www.topfreelancers.net and look for the BNav recruitment messages.

I will consider 5 to 7 players in an existing clan as a semi-autonomous subunit -- all BNav report to me, but I like to delegate a lot to subordinates.

So if you have a squadron, you can have a chance at a lot of fun right now and even more in the future.

The new version of the TFL Mod will be out soon. This new version will make life almost impossibly hard for anyone not in an official clan. Further, all official clans, like BNav, will get their own custom created new system guarded by a nearly invincible AI to keep all enemies out.

Ships, weapons, and goods are all to be redone in the new mod so that the economics of distribution will make cooperation among clans and conflicts as well inevitable.

Role play won't depend on voluntary compliance with a lot of silly rules. Role play will be made necessary by the very structure of the Mod.

Come help BNav in its unending war against the Corsair menace.
