Bargib,

Thanks for the solution. For anyone else interested in this "hack" just open up the PerfOptions.ini file with notepad located in My Documents\My Games\Freelancer. Scroll down to the part marked [Display and change the entry for size= to the res you want. If you've changed the res to 1280x1024, when you go back to options in FL the display will still be marked as 1280x960 even though its actually using 1280x1024. I took several screenshots with the two different resolutions and then opened them up in photoshop just to make sure. Have fun!

Later,
R2
