Onboard AC97 sound OR Soundblaster PCI 64
windows 2000 AND Windows XP

DirectX 9.0

I started out having full out lockups but after much Video driver tweaking I resolved the problem. It was related to my Video card being 'OC'd' by default.

My current problem:
Music is always off.
Full lockups with sound effects on. Let it sit 5 minutes and it is still locked up. I tried adjusting the sound acceleration from full,down to lowest hardware. I never tried running in the lowest (software emulation mode).
Sound effects off, I get short lockups, up to 30 seconds long, usually at the end or after dialogue finished. Sometimes I have then during a trade lane or jump sequence. After the lockup is over the game continues to play normally. The whole PC locks up, not just the game, alt-tab/ctrl-alt-del doesn't respond until the lockup finishes.

I recall having the same type of problems with Sound Effects in Starlancer, under a totally different hardware configuration. This is a newly built system and other games are stable after fixing video issues.

Anyone know a way to resolve this short of turning all sounds off in game? Does the game do captioning if you don't have dialogue sound turned on?
