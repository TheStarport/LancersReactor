Phase 1 of the Terra Empire Mod is now released and avalible for download!!! Follow the link bellow in this post to go get it and happy freelancing. (feel free to post comments/etc. here or on the offical Terra Empire Mod fourm on the site listed bellow)

(a Little Blurp about the mod)
Once I started playing freelancers moding ideas ran through my head. As i'm sure most all freelancer players can admit. As I started to learn about creating a mod for freelancer I decide first most I would create a mod that was more of a un-offical add-on. This add-on would have 3 new factions, Alpha Centauri Industries (ACI) a corpration backed by the Liberty Navy, Vega Technolgies (VT) a corperation backed by the third new faction, Terra Empire (TE) a militristc advanced human sub-culture. This is the basis for the add-on mod. I also decided that I wanted this mod to be multiplayer and single player freindly. Including adding beam weapons, moding ships and adding 'new' ones and so on. A reall add-on mod.

I decided as I started working on this mod the best way to work and release it would be in phases. This way I can work on one faction at a time, and there related systems and so on and still get it out for people to play without having to wait A LONG LONG TIME! There are a total of 3 phases.


For more information on the phases, screenshots and progress on this mod, please goto http://qsd.xpi.org.uk procede to the QFL sub-site, current/furture projects and then terra empire mod. If you have any questions that are not addressed in the info of the mod, please feel free to ask here or on the offical terra empire mod forum on the QSD site. Thanks and happy freelancing

I want to remind everyone that the fourms on the QSD website is still the preimer place, and offical forums for the Terra Empire Mod. However since I know many players/people who like the TE mod do not wish to register on QSD to access thoese forums, thats primarly what these forums or for. Use witch ever is more convenient to you, as I will be check both very reguarly. Thank you.
