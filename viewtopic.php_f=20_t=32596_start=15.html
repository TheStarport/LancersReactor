There are heaps of sites that deal with ATI cards (some of which I frequent), and almost as many that deal with nV cards. Unfortunately, I cannot reccommend any nV sites as I own an ATI card. If you would like me to explain something just ask, as long as you don't ask about how useful nv's "C for Graphics" is . In any case, the hardware accelreration settings are not in the display properties, but can be found by typing "dxdiag" in the "Run" dialog box, and clicking on the "Display" and "Sound" tabs. You'll see the options when you get there.
