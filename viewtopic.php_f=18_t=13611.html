"well, if you MUST know, I have heard a rumor..."

"Alright, you've got my attention..."

"Ok, here's what's going on..."

initialworld.ini describes relations between factions (ie Liberty Police and Liberty Navy). These are static.

mpnewcharacter.fl sets up initial relations between each faction and the player (ie "Trent" and Xenos). These are dynamic.

empathy.ini governs how a change in relation with one faction affects relations with others.

Finally, your present relational status is stored in character data (savegames) and can not be modified as of yet.
