Tigis

Thu Mar 06, 2003 1:33 am by Tigis

John is correct. It's a simple matter of entering the equipment dealer screen, clicking on the 'mounted' icon (looks like joined puzzle pieces)on the top left of the mounted gun graphic to unmount. Then scroll down the list to the item you want to mount, click on the 'unmounted' icon (puzzle peices apart) and the weapon is mounted. You'll hear a "Mounting" VO and effect too. Which is kinda neat

Also, since weapons don't seem to take up cargo space you can just leave your old ones unequipped until you're sure you want to sell. I often do this when trying out similar weapons. Not sure if the weapon space is unlimited, but it's definitely large.
