Okay, it's easy

For the first, your music have to be .waw format. If and whan it is, put the music file in the music folder aka:

<pre><font size=1 face=Courier>C/Program/Microsoft Games/Freelancer/DATA/Audio/Music </font></pre>

Now you want to change the music in Liberty Space, do the following thing:

1. Download the INI's Uncompressor.
2. Open the INI file li01.ini.
3. Search for the follwing line:

[Music
Space: music_li_space
Danger: music_li_danger
Battle: music_li_battle

4. Let's say your music is named 'My_kickass_music.waw'
5. Remove 'music_li_space' and add 'My_kickass_music' on the Space line.
6. That's it! Fly into Liberty space (preferably New York) and listen .

Hope it helpes!
