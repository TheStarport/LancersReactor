Mon Mar 24, 2003 11:18 am by Revenge

Learn the beauty that is "TAB"

Defender, lvl 2 shields, 2 magma hammers and 2 justice mk III's and a stunpulse turret.

Waiting for the cruiser to take down the battleship outside manhattan I took down 5-6 navy ships without using any nanos. At bedford I took down atleast 3 before the cruiser finished off the station, and I had to use my nanos once. In Magellan I took down atleast 2 or 3 bounties before the lane hackers showed up to assist.

Use tab to help you dodge and weave wildly, all the while keeping an eye on your target. Wait until he is making a run at you then turn as he gets real close, and hopefully you should end up right behind him. That is when you open fire, making sure to boost when he does so he wont get away from you.

Only fire when you are confident you will hit, so you can keep up a good bit of fire while chasing his tail up close. Basically the idea is that your target keeps running away, leading you away from his wingmen as you pummel him. Your target will also turn a lot, naturally causing you to dodge with him.

I tend to get hit more inbetween targets rather than when I am actually following one up close. It also allows you to use high drain weapons because you dont spend too much time firing wildly
