SLI is back!

I was just reading around, and SLI is back! A while ago, I told you about Alienware and that they were planning to include the ability to have two graphics cards working as one with their PCs, but now nV have decided to even the playing field and institute SLI with their 6800 series cards. Ah, SLI! I remember the good old days of the Voodoo2... that was the last we heard of SLI. Ah well. Oh, I imgaine that this post makes no sense to most of you so, instead of explaining it to you as I always do (and few people appreciate *sniff*), here is a link for y'all. There are many drawbacks to using SLI with nV cards, but the performance benefits would be astronomical. Still, even I'm not crazy enough to pay $1,500 AUD for two top-of-the-line cards!
