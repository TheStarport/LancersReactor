Sat Jul 12, 2003 7:30 pm by reynen

You only get house weapons off those ships associated with that factions.

All police drop their associated house weapons. You will get more of these being a pirate then being a good profession. Point being, a pirate has a hard life.
Certain other "house" corperations and pirate factions also drop their house weapns.

Ex: Liberty rogues drop the Liberty Cray gun

These are not easy to get. They are the BEST weapons in both damage, energy usage, and effects. They have excellent effects and are extremely deadly. They are also the rarest drops.

Hellfires and Starfurys also are special drops that are just slightly lower in terms of firepower of the house weapons.

Then there is the less rare drops

Hellfurry guns & turrets - 3x a sec, 2300 dmg
Starfury guns & turrets - 25x a sec, 2300 dmg
