I am Dutch as well...

I had the luck of getting a preview copy, and received a review copy last week because I am part of the crew of a large Dutch gamessite...

Really like the game... Story is very good, and especially the later missions are pretty challenging. Got through the main story in about 15 hours, so now it's time to explore the Sirius sector I guess

The main problem I have with Freelancer is that the non-story (Freelance) missions are not varried enough, and that you don't really have any in depth involvement with factions either (except for those in the main storyline). I would have liked it if they had included missions which fitted in with the factions character: peacekeeping/law-upholding missions for the Navy, Intel issions for the LSF, raids for the Corsairs and assassinations for the Bounty Hunters...
