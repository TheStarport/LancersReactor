black_lightning

Fri Apr 16, 2004 1:33 pm by black_lightning

the problem is generated by conflicting orientation data with the hardpoint. When this happens open up the cmp using the utf editor and manually set the orientation for it. Though this can be complicated depending on the angle you use and your math skills, so what you could also do is for your hardpoints triangle face in milkshape, to scale it as small as you can so it looks like a dot compared to the rest of the ship, then bring it to as close as the ships face you want it on without going through that face, this will cut back on faulty orientation when the cmp exporter does it.
