You ever have to sit there forever at a jumpgate and then it just lets you go after awhile of inactivity? Well that's what is happening to you. Other ships that have been waiting in cue to long are being given the chance to go So you are bumped back into cue. But don't worry. That is an uncommon occurrence when it comes to gate formation jumps.

Flying out of he jump is a more common occurrence. All NPC's do it if they are to far from the middle ship. But this does not always push you back in the cue for taking to long. It will usually fly out of the jumpgate, realign, then fly back in..

The reason it flies out like that. Your simply not close enough to the middle target entering the jumpgate. Solution.. Get closer to the middle ship before entering formation.

I've all but mastered this. But it still goes wrong from time to time nStuff. Solution..Start shooting everything in cue.
