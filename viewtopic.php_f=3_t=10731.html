Zen of guns for freighters

Okay, for those of us who like to drive trucks...

If you max out the weapons on a freighter, you will run out of power very quickly when firing weapons. Now, sometimes, I don't worry about this, and strap on big mammajamma guns, and let the power drain in one second.

But let's say that you want your gun power to last long in your freighter. That means you're going to have to skimp somewhere on the guns (usually with weaker guns). So the question, is, where do you skimp?

1 - Skimp on the turrets and get big forward guns. This way, if you're "dogfighting" with your freighter, you'll really be able to put the hurt on the enemy when you line them up. Also, since you've only got a couple forward guns, then you can really go to town with them.

2 - Skimp on the forward guns, and go with turrets. This could be good if you tend to stay on course and use turret view a lot. It could also be worth considerating since many of the turrets will face forward anyway. Downside is, so many turrets, you may still have power problems.

3 - Use missiles. I don't really use missiles, so I'm not comfortable with this option

Any thoughts?
