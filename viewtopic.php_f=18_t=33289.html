You get radio chatter in vanilla FL anyway...

If you want to increase the amount of chatter an easy way is to open Data/constants.ini and change the "CHATTER_MAX_DIST" line to a higher number, so you hear the chatter from a greater area.

Ro9ue

Mon Jul 26, 2004 5:17 am by Ro9ue

Yeah, check the constants.ini file for that. I think you can increase the maximum distance away from a base that you can hear it (it's called "wallah" in FL files). So, yes, do as Accushot says, change that value of any of these to get some different radio chatter:

[CommConsts
COMM_PLAYER_FAR_DIST=
COMM_PLAYER_FAR_DIST_ATTEN=
CHATTER_MAX_DIST=
CHATTER_MAX_DIST_ATTEN=
CHATTER_START_ATTEN=
COMM_CONFLICT_PRIORITY_CUTOFF=
WALLA_MAX_DIST=
WALLA_MAX_DIST_ATTEN=
WALLA_START_ATTEN=
WALLA_PRIORITY_CUTOFF=
