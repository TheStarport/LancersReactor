The homing rating is NOT worth the decrease in firepower. 90% of the time, you should be right on top of an enemy before even thinking about launching missiles, making the turn rate of the missile irrelevant. Just go with the greater firepower, in this case the cannonball.

As far as the intro goes, it IS a lot cooler...but anything about the Nomads or the fate of the solar system was dropped from the plot of the game, so anything past the sleeper ship part didn't happen in the actual storyline. It is cool to look at though.
