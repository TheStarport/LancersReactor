LVL 10 shields and Paralyzer missles

I think that Freelancer is a great game.

After many hours of playing both the multilpayer and single player game, and after discussing with numerous seasoned users on several FL servers, no one seems to have ever found:

Level 10 Advanced Barrier Shields

Level 10 Advanced Champion Shields

Paralyzer Missile LAUNCHERs

Now after spending $40.00usd of my hard earned money and after investing so much time AND I know I'm not the only person who has done the same, I have seriously wondered if Micro$soft, in their ru$h to get this tardy game out the door, forgot to include these three items.

I have searched high and low in Google newsgroups and and no one else seems to make mention or have found these items. I have been to Micro$oft's support site and there doesn't seem to be a definite way of calling or writing someone directly and asking them where these items are or why they are not there.

I am frustrated and feel I have been shafted by Micro$oft and Digital Anvil so far. If these items are somewhere in the game and they still haven't been found by the thousands upon thousands of consumers who have played this game and then my hat's off to their Development Team, good job, they have given us a semi-pleasing challenge. BUT, if these items are NOT there, and the multitudes of us are labouring over finding something that is NOT there, and then not only do the creators of this game should feel ashamed but they should also publically apologize to their customers. I have spent my time and money on using the internet to play the multiplayer, on the official guidebook and on the game and I do not feel satisfied.

Now my question:

Does anyone really know where they are?

Thank you in advance.

~signed by a fan with over 500 hours logged on one FL server~
