Deep Space Engineering Internal News Network
NEW SYSTEM DISCOVERED!!

In a suprise announcement, a very jubilant Admiral Kershaw of the DSE Navy addressed a crowd, saying, 'People of Deep Space Engineering, our constant attempts to find new systems has payed off!! We have discovered a new system, which contains not one, but two inhabitable planets, which have been claimed in the name of DSE! The first planet is a tropical paradise of a place, to be named Planet Lakewood, in honour of the search party commander. The second planet, a temperate wonderland, shall be named Planet Canberra, in rememberance of a nation on our ancient homeplanet of Earth. The system itself shall be named the Australia System.'

DSE GIVES CONSTRUCTION RIGHTS FOR THE AUSTRALIA SYSTEM

In a surprising turn of events, Deep Space Engineering has announced that the following organizations will have permission to build a Large Station each in the Australia system. In an official release, Admiral Kershaw announced, 'At the moment, House Liberty, the corporation of Daumann, House Bretonia, House Rheinland and the corporation of BMM have building rights in the Australia System. All other ships in the system will be deemed trespassing and ordered to leave, and the offending organization fined a hefty amount of 50 credits. Also, the Bounty Hunters Guild has permission to build any number of bases in the Australia System, primarily so they have a staging ground nearby Liberty to fight crime from.

DSE ADDS TO IT'S MEMBERS

Today, an amazing turn of events saw Planet California Minor and Ames Research Station throw off their old owners and join Deep Space Engineering. Admiral Kershaw immediately began checking the armouries and weapons of the stations. Looks like them military boys only think in terms of, "Will they attack us???"

COALITION WITH DAUMANN FINALISED

Today, in a side meeting at the Helgoland Summit, President Hawk of Prey finalised the coalition with Daumann Heavy Construction. In the message, he said that "this coalition will help to ensure our safety in this dangerous universe."

Deep Space Engineering Internal News Network
NEW SYSTEM COLONIZED!

Today the final transports touched down on Planet Lakewood and Planet Canberra, marking the completion of the planetary colonization after the recent discovery of the Australia System. The capital cities of both planets have already been constructed, and the Governor of the planets has vowed that the two planets would remain as pristine as the day they were discovered. To mark his words, the first mining station on the planet is using nonpollutant fuels and all waste is shipped into space and hurled into the sun via a primitive but effective catapult system.

PRESIDENT HAWK OF PREY DUE BACK TO PLANET PITTSBURGH SOON

With the Helgoland Summit drawing to a close, President Hawk of Prey is due to return withing the week. So far the summit has been a roaring success, with the finalisation of the DSE-Daumann Coalition marking the trip as a success within the first hours of the summit.

BATTLESHIP RELENTLESS IN SIGMA SYSTEMS

It was revealed today that the Battleship Relentless had arrived in the Sigma systems, escorting the Gas/Metal Miner Harvest. The official release to the press included a statement that "the Battleship Relentless has a secondary goal to defending the Harvest. Atka Research Station, an installation controlled by our long-time opponent Cryer, has opened in diplomatic talks with the goal of a change of leadership from Cryer to Deep Space Engineering."

DEEP SPACE ENGINEERING EXPANDING INTO OTHER REGIONS OF BUSINESS

Today DSE Chief Of Commerce Golding announced that Deep Space Engineering will be expanding into other regions of business. His official release was, 'With the securing of Atka Research Station, we gained a valuable item; the Synth Foods Biodome schematics. We now have the knowledge and the power to offer construction of the new and far more efficient DSE Biodomes, which were installed on the newly constructed outpost Victoria station, and have been a roaring success.
