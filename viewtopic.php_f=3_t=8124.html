docvego

Fri Mar 07, 2003 12:40 am by docvego

It's actually pretty easy to get a big chunk of the cargo from the Trains and Heavy Lifters.

I haven't seen this posted yet so here you go:

The Trick is to target their cargo holds (The big tanks or crates they are carrying). You can't target them on your ship computer though; you just need to be a good shot. I usually attack from above or below depending on the ship type so as much of the cargo tank is exposed as possible. You'll see this part of the ship take damage and explode, releasing more cargo then you'll probably be able to handle. Try to avoid actually blowing up the ship itself. If you're a good shot you should be take to blow all four cargo tanks without destroying the ship.

Good Luck & Happy Fishing!
