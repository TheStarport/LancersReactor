Sat Dec 03, 2005 5:14 pm by Griffon_26

Giants - Citizen Kabuto..

PC
1998
Err...action / strategy / base building first person space land-vehicle combat?

The game involved having the player as a sort of commander on the moon during a secret terratorial war between the US and Russia. From inside a sort of - well - space tank the player could issue orders to construct a base, defence towers, factories to produce more vehicles, power units etc, all from the first person. There were also mission objectives, from defend a base, to attacking the enemy or send out salvage vehicles to collect other enemies destroyed tanks. You could get out of your vehicle and get into others, or fight on foot in a space suit. This game is very underrated in my books, i just loved the unique style of combat...wd to anyone who gets it.
