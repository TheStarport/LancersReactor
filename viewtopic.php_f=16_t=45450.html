In an unpatched game, I have found that any wreck is destructible:

1. Fire at it from close range, reducing its hitpoints to half or a quarter.

2. Fly away about 1000 m. or right about when it dissappears from view.

3. Fly back at it and fire, making sure to hit it's hull. It should blow.

Beware pirates bearing gifts!
