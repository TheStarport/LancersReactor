A couple things...

So far, the textures posted (outside of a few of Jsncalif's thumbnailed pictures) have been quite small, so there aren't problems with size. However, in the future, if you have large textures, I ask that you, like in the show off your work thread, thumbnail or link to the pictures rather than just using the img code. Also, remember, you can also exchange unfinished models here for public use. I know you could always host them on the news page, but I think this is a more fun way, where we can get to know the people who are putting their work out to the public. Anyway, keep up the good work and hopefully we can get some more people contributing here
