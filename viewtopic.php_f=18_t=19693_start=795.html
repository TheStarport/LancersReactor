wizard_phoenix

Wed Nov 10, 2004 4:37 am by wizard_phoenix

vamp679:
I don't know much but about being friendly with pirates, u can change the friendliness of a faction with respect to the other in EMPATHY.INI.

Suppose U want to be friendly with the corsairs, open up empathy.ini and search for any faction u dont want to be friendly with or u aren't friendly with. eg mollys (or any other for that matter). You search for the section highlighting faction nickname of the mollys. You'll See underneath the main heading, the faction names of all the factions existant in this game with a no. in front of them. If you change that no to -1, the said faction will become the biggest enemy of mollys, if u change it to 1, the said faction and molly will become best friends.

So find the corsair faction there and change the value just after the faction name to -1. Your Done!!! Now if you kill a few mollys, Corsairs will become your best friends.

Thats all i know!!! There may be other ways which i am unaware of but this is all i can give u. I hope it helps!!!^--^
