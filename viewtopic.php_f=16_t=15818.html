Crash-Drive

Sat May 03, 2003 4:09 am by Crash-Drive

The anomalies: D7 in New York, C5 in Omega-3 - You see them during the course of the SP game, but it's at times when you're compelled to be following the story's missions and waypoints. The one in Omega-3 is more interesting to look at, but you can enter the corona of the one in New York without taking damage.

Nansei Research Complex, extreme lower right of C4 in Kyushu. Just because it's interesting and unique with it's large array of solar collectors (I guess that's what they are). Kusari does research on antimatter technology there, according to the info on the place, and it's ringed with weapon platforms to guard against the frequent attacks by Blood Dragons and GC. It's away from the trade lanes, with no real reason to ever go there, so it's probably not seen by all but the more thorough system explorers.
