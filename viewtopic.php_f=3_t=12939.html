All-In-Wonder 9700 + Freelancer + Thruview = Bliss!

I just got a All-In-Wonder 9700 Pro video card, and it's thre greatest gaming creation in the history of mankind. For those who don't know, the All-In-Wonder 9700 Pro is a normal Radeon 9700 Pro video card with a decent cable tuner/video adapter package jammed on it.

The TV component has some great capabilities. In addition to making the PC essentially a Tivo, it has a feature called Thruview. Thruview lets you run a TV window (or even a full screen) semi-transparent in the background. It blends your active image with the TV image so both are still intelligible. Great while your word processing, but I was hesitant to use it with anything 3D, didn't even think it would work.

Well my worries were unfounded since the TV and 3D components are integrated, as I most wonderfully discovered I can run it with Freelancer just fine. So up in that empty upper left corner of the hud I now always run a Thruview window with Freelancer. Sure takes the bore out of those long travel times. Heh, it's like my ship is equiped with cable TV!

The AIW also came with a RF remote control, which still works on the TV even when it's not the active app. So I can even still change channels and adjust the volume without having to exit Freelancer. Ah, it is bliss indeed.
