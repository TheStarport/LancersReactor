Uploaded two subtle but important (to me, at least) files.

ramscoops.ini does only one thing - but that thing is noticible whenever you hit shift-w. Instead of saying "CRUISE ENGINE CHARGING", the screen now reads "HYDROGEN RAMSCOOPS CLOSING", to reflect that element in Wing Commander.

Also, I felt the music in the bars was too tinny and soft - so off I went on music.ini, tweaking the attentuation to get better volume.

Incidently, I've been fiddling with the camera system and making new cockpit.inis - in turret view (which I use a lot) some of the ships have a rather jarring jump in distance. Some of the cockpits are a bit too low, too - the gunfire flash is overwheming.

Regarding balancing, I've been tweaking it a bit - I still feel the game is a bit too easy - and no, I haven't played any of the campaign missions. Actually, I've been doing free-form MP style - I saved a game immedietly after I finished the campaign in 1.06 and stripped everything out using IONCROSS. Once you get things like Lvl 3. shields and Isometal, it's nearly impossible to die unless you're really, really bad. Something to look at in the future, I suppose.
