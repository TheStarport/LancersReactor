Tue May 29, 2007 3:32 am by mh-dk

The easiest way to make custom starspheres is:

Find a starsphere you want to costumize and copy it.

Open it up with UTF editor and extract the textures (remember it must be extracted as tga files)

Then do whatever you want with them in photoshop or what you might use for this.

and import them back in the file (which you have renamed to what you want) with UTF editor.

Quick, simple and almost painless! Heheheh...

Hope this helps...
