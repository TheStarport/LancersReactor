I got the PC Elite when it was up on the website. After maybe 6 or 8 months, one of the creators of Elite spotted it and demanded it be taken down. (I think the other creator had said it was all right.) Thus the feud goes on.

I think a lot of the nostalgia for Elite is because it was so revolutionary! It was the first open ended space game. But when you get down to the story line, there wasn't much. Just trade, mine, bounty hunt, pirate, fight off enemy aliens -- buying equipment to upgrade and running up your rating until you eventually got to be elite. There were three missions, which really didn't amount to much: track down some bad guys and one time carry information through hordes of Thargoids who wanted to kill you.
