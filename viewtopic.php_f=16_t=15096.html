The ships you encounter in Manhattan are wimpy, and get progressively bigger/tougher as you go out from Manhattan towards the edge systems. That's more-or-less how the game tries to balance your early levels of development with the areas you're venturing into. The Xenos use Starfliers in Manhattan, some Startrackers in Colorado and Texas, and Hawks in Kepler, Bering, and Hudson. If they had a presence in the Edge/Border Worlds, you would probably find them using Falcons and Eagles.
