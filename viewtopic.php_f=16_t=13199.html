Liberty Navy (Navy) +.25

I know you asked for a place to bribe them, but I find that the
best way to re-align reputation is simply to help a faction's
friends and/or destroy their enemies. Unfortunately, the list
that shows up in-game is not quite accurate, so here's some
more accurate info straight from empathy.ini.

For Interspace Commerce, helping any of the House Police or
House Militaries will help you improve your reputation. You can
also try doing missions for any Liberty Corporations, except for
Spa and Cruise (IC doesn't seem to care one way or the other for
Spa and Cruise).

If you prefer destruction, destroying any criminal factions with
the exception of the Gaians and the Bundschuh will curry favour
with Interspace.

For Bretonian Police, helping out the Bretonian Armed Forces,
any corporations (except Ageira, Synth Foods, ALG, Daumann
and Kruger), and the Bounty Hunters' Guild will do wonders for
your rep.

For the more destructive path, killing Mollies, Gaians, Outcasts
Corsairs, Junkers, and Hackers will improve your reputation.

If you really still prefer to bribe, you should start looking at bases
of the factions mentioned in the less destructive path.

Click here for more information about faction reps and effects (plus
other useful information about factions)

-- The Haen.
