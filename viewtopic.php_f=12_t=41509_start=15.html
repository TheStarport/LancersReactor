I am running:

The min specs are:
Windows 98SE, ME, 2000, XP with 512Mb ram
Pentium IV 1.7Ghz, or Athlon Equivalent
128Mb 3D Direct X9 compatible GFX card, with Pixel Shader 1.3 support
2Gb HDD space (strange, my install is 3.94Gb!)
Mouse, Keyboard (joystick optional) and DVD rom. Soundcard.

Recommended specs are:
Windoze XP
1Gb of Ram
Pentium IV 2.4Ghz or Athlon Equiv.
256Mb 3D Direct X9 compatible with pixel shader 2.0 support
and then same HDD space/mouse keyboard etc.

I originally started with High Texture and High Shadow, but lowered it to Medium Texture, Low Shadow. I do not have anti aliasing at all (it wouldn't handle well enough), but the screenshots I showed you are on these settings of mine (except the first one, which was high/high and playing reasonably well). My game plays fine, it may have the odd framerate issue in Argon Prime, but it is very smooth the rest of the time. They do say if you have 256Mb it is better, and of course - if your system doesn't have 1Gb memory, then it maynot run quite as well (512Mb is the min recommended, but the more mem, the better it runs).
