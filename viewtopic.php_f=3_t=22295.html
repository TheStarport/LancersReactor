I thought of this while flying to Planet Manhattan

I was flying to planet manhattan while reading Trent's journal when I though of something. You know how in his journal Trent mentions that there are undiscovered planets in the border worlds. Well if there is ever (probably not) a freelancer add-on then you should be able to set up a base on a planet or an orbital space station. Here is how it would work:

Space Station: Outpost (2000 credits), Cargo yard (6000 credits), Shipyard (10000 credits), Freeport (25000 credits). After purchasing a space station you would hire mehanics, bartenders, and try to bring in some merchants. The cargo and ship yard could make you money.

Planet Base: Underground base (2500 credits), manufacturing plant (5000 credits), Science base (6500 credits), settlement (30000 credits). But to first settle on a planet you would have to scan if it would be okay to settle a base. What do you think?
