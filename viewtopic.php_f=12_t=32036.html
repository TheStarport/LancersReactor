Dead To Rights

Anyone played this game? I bought it for playstation 2 its one of those PS2 Greatest Hits thing. Its a great game, its mostly shooting but thats not a bad thing is it? It's made to be sort of like an action flick with all that slow motion and dodging and leaping and whatnot . I think the coolest part (for those who have played it) is the Disarm moves, their so great, I run around without any weapons disarming enemies. Its got a lot of style to it, especially those same moves in slow motion, like for example, my favorite shotgun disarm, has your guy leap over the enemy, grab his shotgun, twist his arm around his back (with him bending backwards), put the shotgun to the back of his head, lets a good second or so pass then blows him into the air, doing like 3-4 flips hehe. So if you guys want a good distraction and have a console outside of gamecube I highly recommend it.

PS: If this becomes a console vs console thing or anyone starts badmouthing the PS2 its deleted.
