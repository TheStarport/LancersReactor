Ein-7919

Sat Mar 22, 2003 12:39 am by Ein-7919

"The xxx Cid is a shield damaging gun. Not a lot of good at this level."

Well, Tizona's are probably what get me the most kills (particularly against the CPU). This is mainly because most of your guns do their highest lvl of damage against an unshielded ship. Now, 2 Tizona's are going to bring down most shields in 2 or 4 volleys (4800-9600). And, depending on the rest of your loadout, once the shields are down the ship will shortly be annihilated. Not to mention the psychological effect of the Tizona's in pvp. If you're in the heat of a dogfight and don't have time to scan your opponent then hearing 'shields failed' in the opening blasts usually ups my pulse. Anyways, their usefulness is entirely dependent on how ppl use them and whether or not they care about the brute armor damage value of their weapons. A weapon's value, like beauty, is all in the eye of the beholder. Cheers.
