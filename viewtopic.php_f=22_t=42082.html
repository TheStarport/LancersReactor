Deep Space 9 Server Mod

Deep Space 9 Server - www.ds9clan.co.uk

First of all I would like to introduce myself. My name is <DS9>PeaceKeeper and I am the Admin and owner of the Deep Space 9 Server. We have created a mod for our server with 35 ships in it. We do stress however, that all players read the rules first on our forums – we do enforce them, so please make sure you read them.

Our website is www.ds9clan.co.uk and from there you can follow the link to our forums to meet the other players on our server and to download the Mod. Please look for the flashing Red Alert symbol - thats where the mod can be found. We like to think we are a big family of players and the players on our server get treated with respect.

The ships we have in the patch are:

From Babylon 5:

DARK STAR, SHADOW CRUISER.

From Battlestar Galactica:

CYLON RAIDER, VIPER STARFIGHTER

From Final Fantasy:

RAGNAROK

From Star Trek:

CONDOR RAIDER, DELTA FLYER, FEDERATION FIGHTER, JEM'HADAR ATTACK SHIP, KLINGON BIRD OF PREY, ROMULAN WARBIRD, SURAK CLASS STARSHIP, U.S.S DEFIANT, U.S.S. PEREGRINE, U.S.S PROMETHEUS, U.S.S VOYAGER.

From Space 1999:

EAGLE TRANSPORTER ONE

From Star Wars:

B-WING STARFIGHTER, JEDI STARFIGHTER, MILLENIUM FALCON, NABOO ROYAL STARSHIP, RAVEN'S CLAW, SITH INFILTRATOR, SNOW SPEEDER, STAR WING GUNBOAT, T-WING, TIE DEFENDER, X-WING STARFIGHTER, Y-WING STARFIGHTER.

Other Custom Ships:

CHAOS HEAVY FIGHTER, DAGGER MKII, MINER SHIP, XT-17 PHANTOM II, SABRE MKII.

Tohoku is open to all players with Tekagi’s base now being dockable, the Battleship Osiris which is stationary, Planet Tohoku and the Deep Space 9 station. There are trade lanes to these bases which makes it easier to visit them.

All players undock in Omicron Minor where they are greeted by the Server Police which will be <DS9> members and /or <Sir> members. In new York there are other Server Police who help New Players get to know their way around, these are <Cadets> and <Squires>. They also enforce the rules as do the <DS9> and <Sir> members.

Our rules are simple, but effective:

No PvP in Omicron Minor or New York
No Mods or Cheats
No using bad language
No base camping
No using the Server Police tags
No dock killing
No F1’ing out of PvP

We do have a few more rules, they just cover minor details. All rules are enforced on the server as we do have young ones playing on there as well as parents.

New Clans who come on our server will receive 200 million credits to help them start up, plus we have a section on our forums with advice on how to start up your own clan and keep it going. Systems are rented by clans, who in turn can rent out bases to individual players who want their own base to reside in and pay rent monthly.

All Clans that are on our server are role play clans. If you do not wish to take part in the role play, please do not put any tags in front of your name.

There is a 3rd patch being made, hopefully it will be released for February next year. It will contain many more ships and more systems.

The link for Mod Manager can be found here: http://www.lancersreactor.com/t/downloa ... asp?id=927

We all look forward to seeing you on our server

Warmest Regards
<DS9>PeaceKeeper

Deep Space 9 v3.0

The DS9 server admin are very proud to announce v3.0 has landed.
Over the last year <DS9>Starfighter has been working very hard on v3.0, and boy does it show.

The Deep Space 9 server has been going for over 3 years now and is not 100% based on the star trek theme, we aim to cater for all types of players from stand alone freelancers, role players and even players who just want to be left alone to explore.
The server allows clans to buy systems for game credits and provides established clans wishing to start on ds9 with funds to help them settle in.
FL shell is running on the server and provides player pvp stats which all players can access in game.
We have active events like the fugitive hunt and wacky races which are free to enter and great fun.
All jump holes are unlocked and players start with the basic game full nav map.
New players will start in the delta flyer in omicron minor and have 50.000 credits.
Missions pay up to 2.9 mil and can be found in New York to help new players get a head start, you will also find high paid missions on most bases outside New York including sith and nomad missions.
During you time on the server you may wish to join the server police, we have an open selection for players that want to learn how to become server police and help them develop the skills needed to provide a cheat free server and safe server for all players of all ages.
This is done in 4 stages, Cadets, Squires, Sirs and then DS9, they each fly they own class of police ship. For more details please see the forums www.ds9clan.co.uk.

The mod was first tested back in Feb 2006 and now we are pleased to say it’s ready to go fully public.

V3.0

The mod includes ships from most Sc-Fi programs like Star Wars, Battle Star Galactica and Star Trek to name a few, most converted or custom built by <DS9>Starfighter and <DS9>Phoenix.
There is over 100 new ships added to the game and also includes new systems like Borg, shadow, sector 001, sith and a new dedicated pvp system (Top gun).
Lots of custom stations, jump gates and factions have also been added.
Tohoku has had a few changes with added trade lanes, planets/bases and the Deep Space 9 station.
Cruise and trade lane speeds have been increased.
We have added new trade goods and many new factions, some are very nasty!
Ship prices have been made more economical.
