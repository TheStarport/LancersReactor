Thu Jun 17, 2004 3:23 pm by VroomZo

There are only three fighters that support class 10 weapons. They are the eagle, sabre, and titan. The eagle is sold at Freeport 9 in Omicron Theta. The Sabre is sold at Planet Malta in Omicron Alpha and sold at Vogtland Base in the Dresden system. And the titan is sold at Planet Crete in Omicron Gamma.

Codenames are in wrecks. If you play multiplayer, other players might of looted the wrecks already. But the wrecks are usually intact right after a server reset. The Pathfinder wreck in Omicron Beta has WILD FIRE. And there's a wreck in Sigma-13 with DIAMONDBACK. And there's a wreck in Omicron Alpha with CERBERUS.
