Easy Rhino

Sun Mar 16, 2003 2:12 pm by Easy Rhino

Wow, convoying is actually fun!

I was trying to make ridiculous amounts of money in commodity trading, so I bought a Humpback. I was dismayed to learn how amazing unmaneuverable the Humpback is. Unlike some light freighters, it's not even feasible to try to dogfight in the humpy.

So, before heading through a particularly treacherous trade lane, I formed up with a Large Transport and three Startrackers/Starfliers flying escort. We got jumped by three bandits interrupting the trade lane. The three escorts peeled off to attack, while the transport (and me) kept going. And I got to finally use turret view effectively, and it turns out the enemy ships chasing you are really easy to blast with a turret. It was pretty freakin' fun, and I really got "into the feeling" of making a dangerous trade run.

However, during the fight, the transport surprisingly exploded, and as I was then breaking for the next trade lane, I noticed that one of the remaining escorts was *also* red to me along with the bandits. Then, when I landed, I noticed a "1" in the "Transports Killed" colum where there previously was a "0". I think I might have gotten a little carried away with the shooting. Oops.

Course, this just reaffirms my opinion that having convoy escort / cargo hauling random missions would be fun. Oh well, I still made af credits, so I can't complain *too* much.
