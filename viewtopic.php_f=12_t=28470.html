This is for XP but it should be reasonably similar for the other Windows OS's.

1. Go to control panel
2. Open regional and language options.
3. Under the Regional Options tab click on Customize
4. Click on the Time tab
5. In the time format field change the "H" to an "h"
6. Cick OK, then OK again and you're done.
