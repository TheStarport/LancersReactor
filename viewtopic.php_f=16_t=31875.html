Are you inside the Dyson Sphere? Or are you at the Great Barrier? The same happened to me, I run out of nanobots, batteries (and torpedoes) on the final levels.

Remember that losing your shield is not the end. Your hull can take an aweful lot of punishment. When your going after the Nomad Generators, do a strafing run on them, activate every weapons at your disposal, switch on that afterburner and swiss cheese that f*cker. Do 3-5 runs then break off. Kill one or two Nomad fighters and you should get some batteries which you can use to make you last a bit longer. then do the runs again. You only need to destroy 2 generators in the Dyson Sphere, if you're at the Great Barrier you'll need to take out all four.
Also, during the final levels Von Claussen is your wingman (and the only one not flying an Anubis) and as a result he will attack whatever you attack.

Hope that helps.

Merchant Trader

Thu Jun 03, 2004 9:06 am by Merchant Trader

Free tip: stick close to the generators that you're trying to take down. If you fly your ship along next to them, lock up you flight control with the spacebar, and then use your mouse to fire away, you'll find that you can take down the generators pretty quickly.

ALSO, mines will be attracted to the generators, so as you're making your pass, drop a few next to the generator.

I was able to take it down by buying the Anubis before departing from the Osiris on an earlier mission. Having six guns and a turret (all of them with high hull damages ... forget the shield damage, since the Nomads don't have shields) was a very quick way to even take down the battleships that you encounter earlier.
