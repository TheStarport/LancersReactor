The Trent Starting Ship Mods (all editions) Comments and Fee

This forum will contains any questions, feedbacks, or comments about the
Trent Starting Ship Mods (all editions)..

Start asking and talking, now...

Here are the Descriptions:

the Trent Starting Ships Mod - Light Fighters Edition made by Chris is a mod for the Single player campaigns in freelancer. It will change the Starflier into a Light Fighter. Choose between a Startracker, Hawk, Banshee, Cavalier, Patriot, Dagger, Bloodhound, Legionaire or Drake.
Installation instructions are on the download page.
To download the the Trent Starting Ships Mod - Light Fighters Edition click here.

the Trent Starting Ships Mod - Heavy Fighters Edition made by Chris is a mod for the Single player campaigns in freelancer. It will change the Starflier into a Heavy Fighter. Choose between a Centurion, Wolfhound, Dragon, Dragon (BD VERSION), Defender, Defender (JUNI’S DEFENDER), Crusader, Stiletto, Valkyrie, or Falcon.
Installation instructions are on the download page.
To download the the Trent Starting Ships Mod - Heavy Fighters Edition click here.

the Trent Starting Ships Mod - Very Heavy Fighters Edition made by Chris is a mod for the Single player campaigns in freelancer. It will change the Starflier into Very Heavy Fighter. Choose between a Titan, Sabre, Eagle, Hammerhead or Anubis.
Installation instructions are on the download page.
To download the the Trent Starting Ships Mod - Very Heavy Fighters Edition click here.

The Trent Starting Ships Mod - Freighters Edition made by Chris is a mod for the Single player campaigns in freelancer. It will change the Starflier into a Freighter. Choose between a Rhino, Mule,
Dromedary, Humpback, or Clysdale.
