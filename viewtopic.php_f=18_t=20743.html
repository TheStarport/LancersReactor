Hi,

Please, don't mess Freelancer Massive Addon with The Next Generation or Rebalance. Though simmilar in size, they are different. FMA is now version 1.1, it is not dedicated for SP story nor MP. Have some known bugs. FMA is the least invasive, it is an "addon", it adds new systems and ships in them but doesn't change the FL in any other aspect. TNG adds new stuff in original systems and doesn't change existing stuff, Rebalance changes everything that could be changed . The last two can work in MP.

For me the jump from Sicilia to NY works in and out, I can jump back to NY with no problem. Just find place where you entered the Sicilia from NY.

I'm preparing FMA 1.2, thanks for all reports you send here or to me.
