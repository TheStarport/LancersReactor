To change the size of Planets Download, Compilation of Utilities and other Goodies And open up Utf_edit.exe
Click on open file and browse to C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer\DATA\SOLAR and open up either the Planets folder or the SUNS folder. open up one of them, and expand the tree and click on radius. Make sure that float array is selected. And click edit. You should see 2 numbers
500.000000
0.000000
The one you want to edit is the top one. Then hit update and save the file.

To make an system bigger, you lower this number
NavMapScale = 0.850000
in Universe.ini in C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer\DATA\UNIVERSE
