Where to buy and what to buy? (updated, again)

Recently I posted a message in which I explained how to become rich in no
time (trading with Niobium and Diamonds). Now I have the complete list of all
planets and stations in Freelancer universe, so I give this list to all of
you who are interested in which commodity makes more money. Some tell that
it's best to trade with Cardamine, until today I thought it was Niobum and
Diamonds, but it's hard to determine which is the best way. It's not just the
type of commodity that matters, a variety of other factors like how much you
must travel from one location to another (buy-sell), how much waypoints are
in question and whether or not there are hostile targets, are important as
well. It's all up to you to choose which commodity will you trade with.

First line shows commodity name, second where to buy it at the lowest price,
trird where it's best to sell it (based on price), fourth what profit would
you make, and the last line shows the best path which you should go (based on
the number of systems you must go through, before you reach destination). If
there are more places to buy from or sell to, they are seperated with commas
and the system they are in is placed in brackets. If there is only one
location then planet or station is separated from it's system by comma
(sorry, I couldn't make it more complicated!). All of the commodities are
sorted by profitability, so you can see what is best and what really sucks!
All numbers are calculated based on 275 units of cargo space (Dromedary
ship). So, traders, don't wait and get into business!

I agree with you razerdoom, that the most important factor is time. I hate
traveling through 8 different systems even if the profit is $500,000. Not to
mention you have to go back where you started. I updated my first two posts, so
now you can see the best path for trading. Check it yourself and tell me what
you think.

Many areas in Freelancer are hostiles, so when traveling with a cargo ship and
no money you are forced to bypass some systems, and that extends your trip.
It's also hard to find a person who can fix your reputation with criminal
faction.

I didn't fully understand your question about 1k profit, but if you want a
profit of at least 1k per unit, you should trade with all commodities from
Beryllium and up (my first post).

About the second factor, look at my other thread "It's all about money, isn't
it?". In one way you're selling Niobum and on the way back you're selling
Diamonds. Both trades are on the same route and profit per unit is about 1k.

About the third factor, I really don't know what you're talking about
(sorry, English is not my native language)

I made a BIG research on commodities, and I still haven't seen one that can be
sold in the system you bought it, nor I think I will. For ALL commodities that
give you profit above 1k per unit, there are AT LEAST 3 systems between buying
and selling locations. You won't find any other commodity for which you will
have to travel through only 2 systems. Just read this whole thread and you'll
know what I'm talking about.

Happy Freelancing...

There is indeed exactly one trade route that gives $1k-plus per unit for only two system jumps.
