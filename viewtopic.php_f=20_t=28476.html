SOLUTION!

After much research, I have determined the following.

The crash and blue screen death caused by mission 3 as you fly towards the station is caused by audio, not video. I decreased the audio hardware acceleration on my laptop under Control pannel/sounds/sounds&audio devices. Then click Audio, then Audio playback, choose Advanced, then performance. I lowered the audio acceleration one notch.

No more problems. I was able to reset all video to high performance, and textyres. The audio seems to cause the crash becuase of the many channels and background features running during the bigger battles of the game.

Hope this helps!
