5."where can I get the Nomad guns?"
(you need to read the drek that comes up if you use search for this. Lots of ancient carping about how the plot was changed, etc.).

However, you need to decide if you are going to pay a rep bribe for the Outcats or the Corsairs. Easiest is to go to New London and wait for an Outcast Bribe. Then, make sure to stop on the bases on the way there, eventually you will be able to buy a level 10 shield, then on to the jump hole and Nomad hunting. If you want a Titan instead of an Eagle or Sabre, then bribe the Outcasts and kill two birds with one stone (level 10 shield and level 10 fighter) on the way to the Nomads.

10."can I get a c10 molecular or graviton shield?"

Omega-41 Leon Base, Omicron Alpha Planet Malta , Omicron Beta Ruiz Base , Omicron Gamma Planet Crete & Omicron Gamma Tripoli Shipyard -- the other level 10 shields listed in the game have a regeneration rate of 1 and you really don't want them -- not to mention, you can't get them.


11."is there a c10 turret?"

No, wait for an expansion.

12."How can I beat Hovis?" (Look here to find out how to skip the race )

Turn all graphics down to minimum settings, turn screen size up to the largest you can cope with, sell all your cargo, hit afterburners coming out of the gate as you shift to ShiftW (if you hit ShiftW immediately you slow down), watch the angles and you can be ahead out of the first gate. Regardless, he always screws up about halfway through, so at that point all you have to do is not miss a gate.

Diamonds from berlin to new tokyo, niobium from new tokyo to the ring in berlin system: 215,000 credits in my anubis (SP) in 15 min.
