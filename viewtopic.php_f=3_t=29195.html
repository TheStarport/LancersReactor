I think a Freelancer sequel is likely, and rumours tend to verify this suspicion, but nothing has been officially announced. But Digital Anvil must be working on somthing?

The reason we usally don't bring rumours on the frontpage are twofold:

a.) Freelancer was announced WAY to early, and thus nearly got in league with other games, where upon the fans wait for ages... (Somebody said Duke Nukem Forever?). If DA want to go silent for some time while building something, then I say let them work, and not have them have to jump to keep things down/announce stuff to early

b.) Rumours are just that ... rumours... and thus more often than not are totally false. We try to be pretty focused on what we know, and not speculate too much.

Rest assured that the great people at Digital Anvil are dropping by this site on a regular schedule... And when something is going to be told, we will be some of the first, if not the first, to tell!
