lol, OK I will give you guys the run down on this. First of you ride on a wakeboard. Its a little smaller then a snow board and a little fatter, and theres bindings on it. And you get pulled behind a boat. You then jump the wake of the boat, depending the size of the wake you can get real high in the air. You can do all sorts of tricks like a backflip and a 360.

And to the expensive part yes it can be if you make it... A good board runs about $400 good bindings about $300... Then for the boat my preference the 22 foot Tige, with taps, runs about $55,000... Then a good rope and handle about $150, gloves aboue $50 dollars... Ripping into a monster wake 20 feet in the air priceless

But I prefer to have everybody else buy the stuff and have them let me use it. Since they are at my cabin... So alls I have to put in is gas money about $1.40 a gallon
