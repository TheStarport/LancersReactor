For the bug where you see the movie but can't hear anything: There are some sound cards that don't work well with Freelancer. There are also some events that are triggered by the communications. If the sound card doesn't work well, the communication will never occur, and hence the next part of the mission will never be triggered.

For the one stuck at Kyoto...try this. Try buying expensive equipment, launching and dropping it, then buying more, repeat etc. Try to get BELOW the amount needed to trigger the mission, then make money again to get back above that amount. It might trigger the mission, I'm not sure, but maybe worth a try.
