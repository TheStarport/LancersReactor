Anime Loves/Hates

list some anime you like and some anime you hate, you can give reasons but you don't have to. if you don't like anime, don't post - or at least tell us why you don't like it(which animes have you seen/why did they annoy you)

Good Animes:
Trigun - a serious anime with moments of levity, but not too much (ex. Naruto)
Cowboy bebop - a fun, stylish cartoon with a cool universe and characters
Dominion Tank Police(movies) - an interesting plot that actually goes somewhere
Porco Rosso - a very good example of how imaginative anime can be, and a good movie
Samurai Champloo - its mostly good, but sometimes its got a stupid educational lecture by the narrator jammed in the middle
Perfect Hair Forever - just kidding/ listen to the hotdog!

BAD animes:
Inuyasha - every character has the same head shape,and the plot device(jewel shards) was lifted from a girls cartoon (sailormoon) - also the plot goes nowhere
DBZ - again, everybody has the same head and the plot goes nowhere
Scry-ed - honestly, i don't know WTF this thing is i just change the channel
Evangelion - i don't like giant japanese robots
Naruto - taken too seriously by fans, filled with constant silly-faces and martial arts fights

Undecideds:
Full Metal Alchemist - every time i start liking this show they do something to annoy me. Ed is definitely part of that
Lupin the Third - amazing how up to date pop culture references can make an old old old show like this watchable
Big O - sometimes intriguing, sometimes irritating, this is just a very typical anime imo

as you can probably tell, most of my exposure to anime is through the cartoon network
