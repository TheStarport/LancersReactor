Zone Alarm

Open your Zone alarm
Click on Program Control Tab (left side)
Click on Add Program
Navigate to your Freelancer's EXE folder
default is C:\Program Files\Microsoft games\Freelancer\EXE\
Click on Freelancer.exe and click ADD
Close Zone Alarm.

Remember to do the same for the FLServer.exe if you are running that!

Try asking your dad to allow you to permission Freelancer to access the net for MP usage?

As it is, disable the windows firewall if you have zonealarm, as their is NO BENEFIT from having the windows and zone alarm activated. Indeed, Windows doesn't even stop programs from accessing the internet, it only stops incoming traffic instead - which means it is effectively half a firewall, and completely useless.

I take it you do not have a router as well then? Also, once permissioned in Zone Alarm, I still had to manually click on "allow" for when connecting to the net with FL for the first time, as Zone Alarm was a bit naff in that respect.
