Then I read down a little farther about partying in a Rhino, and I remembered what my first concern was with all of these ships: living space. I'd definitely want comfort over looks. From that standpoint, I always felt that the Clydesdale or Drone looked the most roomy (if you look at the model rather than the stats.)
