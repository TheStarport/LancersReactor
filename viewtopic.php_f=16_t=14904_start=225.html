When a ship says max level weapons level 5 optimal level 3

Megistos

Tue Jul 13, 2004 10:58 am by Megistos

What it means is that the energy generator of that ship is best suited to that class of weapon. For example:

Imagine a fighter with 6 gun slots, and it generates 480 energy per second. Now imagine a series of guns, which occur in each of the 10 Classes. Class 1 uses 10 energy a second, Class 2 uses 20, etc etc

Now, the optimum class for this fighter would be 8. Why? 6 x 80 = 480, the energy output. If you use Class 10 weapons, you use 120 extra energy a second and will quickly run out. The numbers aren't often that convenient, but it's a good idea of how it works
