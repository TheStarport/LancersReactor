take out space stations or just simple weapons platforms, which are supported as usual by a large number of fighters. Finally

in answer to a couple of your questions:

PvP - player-killing is set on a server-by-server basis - some servers will have a little icon that shows that they allow player killing, other servers will not have the icon, which shows that it is not possible to kill other players

the variety in random missions is, i would have to say, the most disappointing aspect of both the SP and the MP game. there are really only the following types:

go and destroy this group of fighter
go and destroy this factions base
go and kill this guy and blow up what he is carrying
go and blow up this guys ship and bring back his escape pod

i would have liked to see a few more that werent so combat oriented - perhaps:

escort this convoy from here to here
take this object from here to here
ferry this passenger from here to here
go and locate an object hidden here (a lost passenger liner in an asteroid field for example)

it would just add that little bit more to the SP and MP experience

i dont mean to make FL sound bad - it isnt, and you will soon discover that it isn't
