Megistos

Fri Apr 30, 2004 10:12 am by Megistos

To make homing lasers, simply add this section in the [Munition entry of your gun

seeker = LOCK
time_to_lock = 0
seeker_range = 1000
seeker_fov_deg = 35
max_angular_velocity = 2.903330

Those values will give it the homing capacity of a missile, tweak "max_angular_velocity" to make it turn faster or slower
