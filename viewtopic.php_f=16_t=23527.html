Sherlog

Sat Aug 30, 2003 11:04 am by Sherlog

The resale value of the Nomad weapons is very low (about $4k) and they do not drop reliably unless you are rich. It seems that you need enough net worth to be level 32..34 in order to make the gods smile upon you. Note: it is the net worth that counts, not the level. Got Nomad gun drops during the campaign at level 13, when I went to fetch the Ripper mines from Omicron Alpha.

If you want to get rich from loot then kill Hessian Stilettos or Corsair Centurions until you have enough net worth that class 8 kit starts dropping reliably, and then switch to Hessian Sabres and low-level Corsair Titans (with class 8 Salamancas). Nice easy prey because they have weapons that do little damage (Hessians) or do not hit (Corsairs); quite unlike the Outcasts for example. When your net worth is back up to a level where class 9 kit drops then top up your Starkillers and start scouring hostile Omicron Alpha/Gamma. Fastest way to make money without trading or doing missions that might distort your rep sheet.
