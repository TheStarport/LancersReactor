Well ... a couple of things

I don't wanna sounds Newbish but I cant figure out Mines. When someone is behind me, I'll drop a Mine and turn around but the mine will still be following the guy and never hitting him. Thats my question. Now for a couple tips.

The first one: Not sure how helpful this is but ... When you're running from a guy and wanna dock at a planet but know the guy can shoot you while docking, hit your cruise engines real fast. When you have your Cruise Engines activated, fly towards the docking ring and hit dock. This will skip over the part where your ship has to get into perfect docking position so you dock much much faster.

The second one: Some of you probably know this already but ... Planet Crete is the best place to do missions. You can always get a $100,000+ mission there and every once in a while a $200,000+ mission. Add this to the fact that you always fight Bounty Hunters which always carry Pharmecuticals and H-Fuel, both of which fetch a fair price at Planet Crete, and you can salvage Class 9 guns from their ships which sell for $36,000+ , you can make a ton of money. On the best mission I had ($250,000 for the mission) I tihnk I ended up making a total of like almost $600,000!!

Now this is where everyone calls me a Newb and tells me they already knew this stuff LOL
