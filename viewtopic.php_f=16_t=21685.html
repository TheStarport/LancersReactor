Before reinventing the wheel have a look at some of the older threads and experiment with the runs mentioned there:
Best Trade Route?! (Trade cycle!)
Getting a class 10 ship in 2 hours and 49 minutes

Once you have a Dromedary and a couple hundred thousand in the bank the only interesting measure for a trade route is the absolute profit per unit per minute (ppum) balanced by the risk according to your rep sheet, since you have enough capital to trade in any commodity.

However, if you are low on cash then other trades can be more lucrative - those where you buy at very low price and sell at a high relative profit. Liberty is a good starting point for such 'multiplier' trades, especially Luxury Consumer Goods, Light Arms and Boron. And if you are extremely low on cash then you may have to trade a low-value commodity like food or water for a while.

For example, if you respawn with a Dromedary and $4 in the bank then you do not have enough seed capital to use any of the usual trade routes. But if you are somewhere in Liberty then you can go to Battleship Yukon (California) or Battleship Rio Grande (Colorado), sell three nanos or batts and fill your hold with toxic waste. Total outlay: $550. Ship this to Dortmund Station in New Berlin and sell @ $200, for a profit of $53950. For the return run to New York pick up ship hull panels right there at Dortmund. This leaves a cash reserve of $10000 and the profit is only $10000 less than other trades that would drain your cash completely (and $110000 is nothing to sneeze at anyway).

Back in New York you have enough seed capital to go on the Manhattan-Ainu route and enter the Niobium/Diamonds run from there or do the Manhattan/Montezuma circuit if your rep permits, and you have an extra $100000 in the bank as insurance against accidents.
