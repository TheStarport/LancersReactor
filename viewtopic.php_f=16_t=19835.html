Fast track to the Sabre ***SPOILER *** (maybe)

Hi All,

Sorry this turned into such a long post. (I had to share the moment

" I've been playing FL for a while now. I got tired of playing. Tired of flying around Primus wishing they would give me a mission or maybe Juni would show up
I found the best paying trade routes but hate flying a freighter :b
I hung out around Liberty hoping Orillion would make good on his job offer.
Nope. Lifes over. I gotta stop playing this game !

Wait ... Hmmm ... How fast can I get my favorite ship ? Oh ! hmmm ? Wow !!!

Hrs and Hrs of fun !

I made my first attempt , starting from newbie, going from ship upgrade to ship upgrade. Too long. Getting up to TAU-37 , making friends with outcasts, etc. Not again

Where else did I see the Sabre ??? Ahhhh ! I remember !

So I started planning the route. looking for wrecks. Checking my Rep. Counting the minutes it took from A to B... 7 Hrs.

I think it should have been 4 hrs. I died, took too long on that mission. Got lost

I kept re-running, cutting this, adding that. Finally ! 4 hrs 15 mins.

Wait !!! If I could just find one more wreck. Hmmm ... Yes ! 3 hrs 20 mins !!!

Ahh, Under 4 hrs. (a feeling of accomplishment floods in). But what about ... Hmmm...
Explore,Explore, time , time, Wrecks more wrecks. YES ! Wow, cool , no bad guys. 2 HRS 45 MINS !!!

Tweak, tweak, run,run,run. Now just the right missions ...

OMG ! 2 HRS 3 MINS !!! YES ! YES ! YES ! I'm the man ! I'm , I'm ...

Calm down. It's just a game... ok, I'm calm, but ... YES!!!! "

This is the most fun I've had in a while. The run I ended up with is very challenging. Lots of running, some good fights and a LOT of thinking. It just goes to show, just when you think the games is over, it starts all over again;-)

Have fun,
Sloan

PS... If you get it faster post it here ! (Cargo runs not included, void where prohibited

Good idea to keep the freelancer experience going a bit longer. Try some of the other exciting freelancer challenges (all posted in various threads before):

1 Finish single player using only light fighters (easy)
2 Finish single player using only freighters (medium hard)
3 Finish single player using only a Liberty ship of your choice (hard, but has been done)
4 Play as a pirate without using trade lanes or jump gates - holes only (not tried this one yet - sounds like it could be ok)
5 My personal favourite - finish single player using only 2 ships fom start to finish: I used Starflier --> Hawk for this. Gets tricky leaving Bretonia but not that hard really.
6 Feel free to add some more of your own.
