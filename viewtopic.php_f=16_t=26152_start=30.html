Bounty Hunter Jackson

Wed Mar 17, 2004 5:12 pm by Bounty Hunter Jackson

I'm (obviously) a Bounty Hunter's Hammerhead kinda guy. Her narrow profile and high degree of manoeuvrability over all the other very heavy fighters makes her the pick of the bunch, IMO. Granted, her energy banks aren't quite as full-to-the-brim as the class-10-capable ships, but if the Sabre can't get out from in front of you, it won't be long before class-10 weapons and shields are irrelevant . Of course, other people are going to tell you otherwise -- it comes down to you. Your fighting and flying style, and your personal weapons preferences, etc, will all come together to choose the best ship for you automatically. Tis all opinion. And you can keep telling yourself that when I tear your wings off and scoop up all your pricey weapons to sell later on . Seriously, though. Some folks prefer LFs, some folks prefer tanks, some folks prefer the inbetween... and some folks like freighters, who folks like me get paid to protect once in a while.

BTW, statistically (I take aft turrets into account simply because they are useful on a fighter that moves with all the grace of a brick -- the Centurion's armour pushes it ahead of the Hammerhead though, despite it's lack of any kind of turret) the best fighters are as follows:

Titan <-- Sabre <-- Eagle <-- Centurion <-- Hammerhead <-- Falcon <-- Valkyre

Obviously, that doesn't take into account manoeuvrability. Which can't really be quantified; you have to judge for yourself. For example, some say the Eagle handles excellently, but I don't think it beats a Hammerhead. Like I said, it's opinion. You have to fly them all, get a feel for them. Starting out though, you won't have much choice in the matter. You'll get a Starflier. Although so tiny and agile you can't hit it particularly easily, by the same token its weapons are so low powered they're of minimal use. From the Starflier I suggest you move up to a Patriot as soon as you can. I won't suggest any further, as to go through the ships you can get as you progress would be an even bigger spoiler than this entire thread is already
