The group number represents the number of a group. People with the same group number are in the same group. People with different group numbers are in different groups. People with no group number are not in a group.

I've never been asked for taxes...that's wierd.

PvP stands for Player versus Player. A lot of servers will have news saying no PvP in NY (New York) or no PvP in Liberty (New York, Colorado, Califonia and Texas). This means that PvP is not allowed in these systems. Some servers waive that rule if a duel is agreed to (by both players). If both players agree to duel then (if server rules allow) they may PvP in non-PvP systems.
