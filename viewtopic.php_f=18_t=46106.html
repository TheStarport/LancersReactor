Take a look in The TLR Texture and Model Exchange and you'll find a link to some of the bigger ships that I've managed to importer and fix (a lot of work went into some of them, bloody importer).

The importer can only import the VMS from the exporters CMPs and the 112.VMS from some of the original models. The 152.VMS & 212.VMS that are in a lot of the models just give you an explosion of vertices, they have slightly different coding that the importer just can't handle.

Here's hopeing the new Importer that Colin Sanby's working on will import them properly, but as it's still being tested and I'm not one of the testers I don't know how far on it is.
