J Dawg

Sun May 14, 2006 9:19 pm by J Dawg

1. The wrecks there, are just wrecks, and there are 25-30 of them to begin with. The only bases in Omega-5 belong to the Hessians and the Corsairs, who, as luck would have it are bitter enemies, which leads me to your next question.

2. The Hammen Hole is not a jump hole. In fact, it is little more than a no-mans land between the minefields of the respective sides, and is generally good for quite a bit of combat.

3. Whether any ship is 'good' enough to explore the Edge worlds (omicrons, etc), is entirely up to the way you fly. Many people, including myself go out there frequently in a lot less, but it often takes some skillful diplomatic or physical maneuvering. If you're speaking of the unknown systems (the monkey worlds, etc), the Hammerhead is good enough for exploring. And if you're going there, make sure thats all you do (i.e. Never drop out of cruise). No stock FL ship is strong enough to last too long in those high radiation, heavy combat environments. Strong pilots can hold out quite a while against the nomads there, but always have an escape route and plenty of batts/bots. It only takes one or two lucky hits from a Nomad to spread you and your ship across the galaxy.
