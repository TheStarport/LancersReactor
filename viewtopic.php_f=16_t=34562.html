It is not really all that diff. There are actually 3 wrecks in TX SYS. It seems most only know of 2. You only learn of 3rd from bar patron.

Use the Patrol Path display on MAP. This does nelp find holes BUT can also often help locate wrecks since many are near these. Set Nav Points on Map the head for one. Keep Map open & look for RED X as this is Wreck. Can go to next NAV w/o closing map by just hitting F2 after getting to 1st.

2 of them are close together N of CA Hole & W of Junk Base. The other is more of a challenge & will leave that pleasure to you.
