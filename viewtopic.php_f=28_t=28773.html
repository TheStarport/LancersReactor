go to the main page (here) and on the top right is a search engine box. Type in there "no intro movies" or something, and it should be there!

Alternatively, open up Freelancer.ini in the EXE folder of your Freelancer directory, scroll down a bit, and find the section [StartUp, there replace the following three lines:
movie = MGS_logo.wmv
movie = DA_logofinal.wmv
movie = fl_intro.wmv

with:
;movie = MGS_logo.wmv
;movie = DA_logofinal.wmv
;movie = fl_intro.wmv

them ';' (semi-colons) will 'comment out' them lines, basically getting rid of them!

problem solved...
