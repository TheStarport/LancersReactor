Wight Eagle

Fri Feb 04, 2005 12:04 pm by Wight Eagle

Strail, go to My Document\My Games\Freelancer\\Accts\SinglePlayer and look for a file called Datastorm.fl and delete that. Its what flds uses to save the map it generates so deleteing it will make flds scan the files again and show the errors.
As far as saving errors I dont know of any method other than copy and paste but for checking mod errors Id recomend FlScan its much faster and doesnt moan about npc only ships (such as capships in the stock game) having no hull entry.
