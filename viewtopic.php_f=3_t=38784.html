New game to play with FL

Ok, there are a few of us out here who "occasionally" have a beer or two while playing FL.

So by accident I developed a new drinking game to run during the SP story-line version, though I suspect some of you die-hards out there will use it everywhere. Though technically I suppose beer is not necessarily the preferred choice of some, I personally would not try or this with anything OTHER than beer (for obvious reasons).

It's called: DOCK & DRINK.

Rules -

[1 Everytime you hear the word "dock", you take a drink of beer (not the ENTIRE beer, and we know who YOU guys are...LOL).

[2 During long stretches between any docking maneuver, it is ok to take a drink based on each person's own personal drinking habits.

[3 If one spends waayyyyy too much time on a base switching between the bar, the equipment room, the commodity dealer, and the shipwright, I would highly recommend taking a drink between every above mentioned room.

Otherwise, it's game-on as usual.

That's it! Simple as pie...

Have fun and I will see you on the floor somewhere in Sirius!!!
