If you get around to adding new weapons, you might try adding some MACs (Magnetic Accelerator Cannon, from the book The Fall of Reach)
I always imagined them as a cross between a Gauss Rifle (from MechWarrior4) and a Warhammer (a heavy mass driver from Silver_Fire's Weapons Pack), kinda silvery/reddish cyclonic trail behind very high-velocity [maybe 1000 or 1500 m/s projectile. You could make them very damaging to hull, but not that great against shields, or maybe vise versa.
And if you get bored or else really motivated, you might consider adding an Epsilon Eridani system, including gas giant Threshold, its moon Basis, and a "Halo" station (this would be like the star system the the game takes place in)
But these are merely suggestions and ideas, in case you want to add more to your mod. I'm certainly not trying to force ya, as I'm no modder myself.
Good luck, and let us know about any updates/progress.
