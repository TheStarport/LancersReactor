Well, for the race part...
(I switch to cockpit view before I land on the Hood, before meeting Hovis)
I'd switch to a lighter ship for it's ability to turn tighter than a freighter. That Britonian Heavy Fighter for sale there works fine.
Somewhere along the race course, Hovis will make a mistake. Just keep your trajectory "inside" of Hovis. Always take the <span style="text-decoration: underline">shortest</span> path between gates. (Just make sure you are in Freeflight mode and make sure you go into cruise at the start of the race)

Now for Mactan and Willard Research Station...
What you're telling me is that you've got a bounty on your head.
This means all of Liberty (including their corporations and allies) have the "hots" for you.
Although the Lane Hackers might be friendly (or neutral) with you, their allies (The Outcasts) still don't like you. So when you get close to Mactan and there are Outcasts nearby, Mactan will side up with the Outcasts and shoot at you.
Your jouney will eventually take you to Kyushu which by that time, the Outcasts and Bounty Hunters will be neutral with you . But, you still have to beat Hovis to get to that point in the story (this is partly because you have to fight the "infected" Reinlanders to escape the Britonian Systems)
Between that time and before you meet Lt. Ozu, you'll be pretty free to jouney just about anywhere. (After you take a mission with Lt. Ozu of the Blood Dragons, you're reputation with just about all of Sirius will be crap..but that won't last forever)

Rankor

Thu Jul 26, 2007 1:32 pm by Rankor

The best you could do for your own little "hideaway" is sell three ships at most. In this case, your 3 favorite ships. You can also stock you little base with all those weapons that your ship can possibly mount. Which by the way, you still have to purchase unless they're just automatically switched over from one ship to the other when you make the purchase.
So, yes...this means you would have to "mod" your game.
I created my own little "Rankor Station" nicely hidden away in a large asteroid field. It sells my three favorite ships and all the equipment that can be mounted on them. As well, it sells and buys commodities at a fair profit margin. I've also added some humor in the rumors there.
The programs I used to do this are primarily:
FL SDK & FL Explorer
*both are the latest versions.

Note: Shooting Xenos will adjust your reputation with most of their enemies. However, the IMG and Zoners are not their enemies. So, if you shoot too many Xenos, I'm sorry to say that it will adversely affect your reputation with the Zoners and IMG.

To switch to "cockpit" view:
press: Cntrl V
