About getting Sunslayers without killing cops...I haven't tried that in particular yet, but experience in getting other gear suggests that if you kill sufficient Xenos, you can make pretty much everyone at least tolerate you, and probably--eventually--like you well enough to sell you pretty much anything.
"Sufficient" means an awful lot when it comes to befriending factions that already dislike you, though.
