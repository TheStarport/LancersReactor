@Blackhole,
You're right about "ourselves" being a threat. As you can see in Freelancer that the 5 colonial houses are either allied or at odds. For that matter there is much political subterfuge between lawful and unlawful factions. The part of the programming that handles the reputation slider is to say the least.....very interesting. For example, if a pilot whips up on say, the Corsairs exclusively... the Hogosha end up hating you worse than the corsairs even though you never fired a shot at the Hogosha.

Anyway, I've been attempting to keep up with the posts on this project. I understand that you're a young man of 15 years. I am very impressed and want to convey this encouragement to you. Keep it simple as possible and stick to your story line as close as possible. You're getting alot of fabulous and fantasic (some impossible) ideas thrown at you in a short time. It can get overwhelming but dont give up.
A famous aircraft designer of "SKUNKWORKS" once said, "...KISS...keep it simple and stupid". (We got the Stealth Bomber, U-2 and SR-71 out of it).

Keep your horizons broad, and your vision focused.

Just because alot of people want something in your project doesnt mean you have to include it. Like you said, if they can program...then lend a hand. Keep in mind there is way too many who really dont know what they're asking of you. That they are asking kids fancies and know nothing of the real meat of the machine that drives the game.

And yes, i like dodge's story as well (its what i used as a base for my intro). I threw in the nomads so that we could have a backup enemy. I personally am in favor of the colonies trying to destroy themselves from the inside-out. As for making him more than "A Guy", here is a proposed personality:

Name: Dan Julian Korban
Faction: Justician (Instead of liberty, we have Justicia)
Background: Dan was born on pittsburg and worked in the mines as a child. His father was a drunk, and he never had a mother. He grew up wishing to live this place and take to the skies, to space! Finally he was able to, 3 days before the destruction of the Donau. He was one of the rhino's that was flying past that day and ran away at full speed. After the events of those months he was starting to despise the Sirius Sector. A month later he found out his father had died.

Not really caring too much about his fathers death, he met a nice woman in an equipment shop (MUST EVERYONE MEET IN A BAR?!?! i say: NO! ) and they began to date. A few weeks later he saw the news about the colonization effort, and following his and his fiance's dreams, they signed up to start a new life, a peaceful life... Little did he know just how violant it was destined to be... *cue ominous music*

Hobbies: Although he is a trader at heart, he also enjoys a little dogfighting here and there. He usually writes in his journal during long patrols or trade runs to stave off the loneliness of space. He took lessons in playing the piano for a few months, but never really caught on.

Fears: He does not like pirates and would never want to become one. Too dangerous and his experiences as a trader with them have been anything but pleasent. He wants to be nice and safe within civilized terratory. (what he was thinking when he went on that ship, i do not know)

Habits: He gets paranoid when he's scared. Paranoid and suspicious of anyone.

Personality: He is a joking, mostly uncaring young man of the age of 26. He does not think of himself as the adventuring type, but deep down he has a raging longing for some kind of adventure. He usually keeps out of trouble and does not bid well to wrongdoers. He is not too outgoing, but he'll fit right in if you manage to get him into a party. He does not care too much for money, but he makes sure he has a decent amount.


Alright, thats just a sample, feel free to suggest revisions, additions, or completely rewrite him into another character.
