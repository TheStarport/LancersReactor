Oh, you mean modifications?! Ah - gotcha.

This type of thread has been made LOTS of times, and usually attracts the same names over and over again...with a bit of arguing and flaming for good measure.

What you should do is just go to the downloads section and peruse. You can order them by category - like ships, general, addons etc and search. The download amount may give an idea of which are good, but try them...since it sounds like you have time.

Why do i say that? Well, if you play the best mod ever....then your stuck. Where do you go when you get bored? Whats next? The answer is nothing - your done.

So if you explore a bit, and pick up a few ships and stuff mods - then try moving onto some of the bigger ones - it will all last ALOT longer!

Remember - your asking for peoples preferences, and whilst it may prove useful, you have to remember that there people who like Tofu and Sprouts in the world.......tastes differ vastly!!!
