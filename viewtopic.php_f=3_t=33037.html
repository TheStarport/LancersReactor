Well, aside from looking at the guide on this site...

If you want to find them on your own, consider that they are wrecks. So look for dangerous places that might wreck ships. Things like asteroid fields, nebulae with radiation, things like that. There are very few (if any) wrecks just lying about in open space. Remember to check your nav map periodically while searching. Wrecks appear there as a red cross

Sun Jul 18, 2004 2:36 am by Puke Barwalker

re: guns etc. Well, if all else fails try the manual.

As to wrecks, they are going to be in out of the way places -- or they would have been found by others. They will often be in places where wrecks are likely to happen: dangerous areas full of radiation, exploding gas clouds, mines etc. You need to check you nav map periodically, since wrecks will often be in areas where your sensors do not work very well. But if you pass within range of a wreck, it will show up on your nav map as a red X. And talk to people in bars. Several times I got rumours about a ship being wrecked some place, and once I was sold the location of a wreck.

I've forgotten how many wrecks there are, but most systems will have some. Go exploring. You'd be surprised how much there is to this game that most people miss. Just race through the missions with Juni and you are only getting maybe half of the fun! (Let her wait. She's not going anywhere without you.)
