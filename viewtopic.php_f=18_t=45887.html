Tue May 29, 2007 6:04 am by Ogu

Just make sure that you define any new encounters you place in your system at the top of your system INI or else you'll crash every time.

From memory, it's something like this:

[EncounterParameter
nickname = area_defend
file = missions/encounters/area_defend.ini

As richard said, play around with these lines (again, from my bad memory):

density = 12 <---bigger = more ships
repop_time = 10 <--- how long until more ships show up
max_battle_size = 8 <---self explanatory, so bigger = more ships
relief_time = 10 <---similar to above, but it's the length of time ships don't show up

If you set it too high and plan to run a server, you'll likely have a ton of lag.
