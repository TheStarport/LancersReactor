What this means that if you follow it, you can collect pirate pilots from their ships when you blow them up (the pilots 'eject' if you like..). If you then fly to bounty hunter bases - they sell for around 2k, otherwise they sell for about 50 credits.
Alternately - you can also sell them back to pirate bases for more money to 'liberate' their comrades (500 credits).
