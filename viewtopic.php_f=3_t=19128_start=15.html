Zaphael

Sun Jun 15, 2003 5:05 pm by Zaphael

Give me a Titan and I can give u a head on pass every moment you see me.

It doesn't maneuver well, agreed. But if you think of it in space context, you don't need to out manuever light fighters, just have to get all of your guns pointed at the light fighter and he's toast.

How? Most space sim pilots would know this maneuver.
1. Fly backwards at 100+MS.
2. The light fighter tails you, blast it.
3. Seeing the hail of firepower flying at him, he will opt to break his attack and burn, point your mouse at the direction he is head, hit the burners and follow.
4. He tries to out manuever u again, execute the slide and get your guns pointed at him again.

Rinse and repeat.

Missiles are trickier, but same theory. Include counter-measures after you hear the "incoming missile." Would be nice to retaliate with a missile yourself. Which is a bug actually. While executing a -200ms slide, the missile should actually fly backwards since its speed is actually only 90ms.
