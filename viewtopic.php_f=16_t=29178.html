My torps don''t miss

I noticed that when fighting against npc ship I can fire in such a way that my torps don't actually miss.

I'm smacking titans like flies with that tactic.

How to do it:
1) Get a good torp
2) Activate engine kill while running away from the enemy ship
3) Turn around the ship to face them
4) Fire thruster and rush them
5) They'll welcome you you just have to greet them by firing the torp

Voila 1 dead titan Could be more, if you're fighting smaller ships. As they stay in formation, sometimes you could take out 2-3 in one smack. Watch out that you don't fire too close, or that you're not directly in line of fire. I have been killed by my own torps more than any other sp mobs.
