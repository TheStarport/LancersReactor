If your playing SP, this is hands down the best trade route

Planet New Berlin, New Berlin system
Buy Engine Components @ 225
take jumphole to Sigma 13

Yanagi Depot, Sigma 13 system
Buy Cardamine @ 510
Go back go New Berlin
sell Cardimine for 1,349
Profit 839
Total profit per unit round trip 1,138
