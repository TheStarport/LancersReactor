If I had the time (which I don't) I would buy every ship, find a reference point in space, like a planet, and use a stopwatch to count how long it took to make 5 complete rotations. Then I could figure out the maneuverability of each ship in degrees per second, which is a stat they should have included in the game. Just going by weapons and armor ratings is a poor way to judge the effectiveness of a ship... for instance, take the equally armed and beter armored Titan against an Eagle one-on-one, and you will likely die unless you are a far superior pilot. From the in-game descriptions, however, the Titan seems superior. If someone HAD the time to test these all out and post the list of turn rates, they could likely reduce these repeated "best ship" postings... and win the people's ovation and fame forever...
