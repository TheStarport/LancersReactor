Coronos

Wed May 28, 2003 8:41 pm by Coronos

Chris03 you can increase the strength of the hull by opening the goods.ini file, finding the loadout package for the ship you want, and adding a line that reads as follows:

addon = armor_scale_##

There are many premade armor scales listed in the select_equip.ini file. I have been unsuccessfull in making new ones but the ones that are there seem to work good.

Hope this helps...
