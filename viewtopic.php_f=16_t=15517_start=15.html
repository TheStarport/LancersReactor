Ckaos2002

Mon May 05, 2003 4:37 pm by Ckaos2002

I just completed that mission last weekend. I had been struggling with this mission for a few days. I came onto this topic and read all the messages in order to help me figure out what I was doing wrong. (btw, the advice on the topic sure does help.. thanks) Here is what I concluded.
When I was stuggling, I was racing with a Kusari Drone freighter. I think my issue with this was that the turning radius or responsiveness of ships differ. In this case the resonsiveness or turning radius is fairly wide on a freighter. This makes this mission somewhat more difficult if you are racing in a freighter class ship. Don't dispair however, it is possible to beat Hovis in a freighter. I think it just takes longer to get accustomed to the freighter class wide turning radius.
In my case, I re-loaded a previously saved game and bought a Kusari drake light fighter. The results were that I failed on my first attempt as I bounced off one of the rings because I was trying to cut a corner TOO SHARP. On the offered re-race I beat Hovis hands down.
Sooooooo all this to say... :
If you are having trouble with this mission, you might have an easier time in a lighter/more responsive ship. Otherwise, keep practicing.
