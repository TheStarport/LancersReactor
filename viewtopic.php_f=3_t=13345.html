I can confirm that this was on G4. I saw it too. It stood out right away because there were very significant differences in the HUD and some of the graphics. The gunsight was larger and more like a long range targeting reticle (like sniper mode type). They also seemed a lot more mobile. The targeting around neighboring ships seemed cleaner somehow, lines weren't as thick and there was only 1 life bar, like the trade rings in the PC version. Or maybe the ships didn't have sheilds? But they were transports and stuff...

Also, there were a number of Turret type views zooming around the ships, especially through trade lanes. This was especially noticable because there was a nice warp-type effect around the ship, like it was blazing a trail through the trade lane. Yellow sparks around the front of the ship streaming off behind it.

I guess with the way the target reticle was moving about independently of the ship movement you'll probably control them with both sticks, left for ship movement and right for targeting, maybe? And shoot from the shoulder buttons with the face buttons used for countermeasures and sheild batteries/nanos?

The cut-scene and base stuff seemed the same as the PC, so maybe DA and M$ is only updating the flight graphics. Hopefully they'll patch the PC version with some of the same stuff. Looked almost better somehow, especially with the trade lane effect. I thought "Mine doesn't do that!"

Anyway, just thought I'd confirm the sighting.
