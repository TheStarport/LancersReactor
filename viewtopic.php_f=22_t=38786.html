Basically, he was starting a Nomad Clan and wanted to use my server as his home base for his clan. I said he couldn't do it because the server doesn't have the mod and since my server is running different mods, it couldn't be done anyway. The guy said that my server was not suitable and left. Now he wasn't mean or anything about it, but to me, it seems presumptious to ask a stranger to host your mod for his clan when he is very capable of starting his own server unless he has a slow machine or something.
