what is it you're looking for? name any good anime is a bit vague; what kind of things do you like?

if I had to recommend anything just for the sake of it, I'd say few people could go wrong with the collected works of Studio Ghibli, I don't see how anyone can dislike Laputa, Castle in the Sky, or Mononoké Hime, or Spirited Away.

if you're familiar with Ghost in the Shell, and liked it, try Patlabor: Mobile Police and Patlabor 2. Mecha but very deep and political.

Cyborgs, Battle Angel Alita or Spriggan (i fookin luv Spriggan, it's ace!)

if you like magic and legends, then Dark Myth is nice n meaty. for more sex n violence, try Tokyo Revelation or Wicked City, or silly Urotsokidoju(?)... oh I can't be bothered, I think it's cr*p but people still buy it. Legend of the Four Kings isn't bad.

for Mecha, then I'd suggest Detonator Orgun or any Macross, even Gundam Wing!

adventure series, Escaflowne* followed by Neon Genesis Evangelion followed by Martian Successor Nadesico followed by Blue Submarine No.6 followed by Record of Lodoss War

*luv Escaflowne too

Samurai and Ninjas, go for Ninja Scroll and Ninja Resurrection: Revenge of Jubei, oh and Ninjai (that's on the 'Net)

if you like being totally confused, try Serial Experiments Lain. Actually i was just bored, but it has a strong following amongst the young.. I think it's pretentious and directionless. Apparently if you don't "get" it, you're thick and won't even understand the Matrix. Boll*cks.

if you like watching utter piffle, then hey! buy yourself any Love Hina or Ah! My Goddess.. why was this bland mindless rubbish ever made, and who exactly buys it?

You might also like Wings of Honnemaise, it's an unusual film that's rather slow but with a great ending. The Cockpit is an unusual little number. Read or Die is quirkily entertaining too. Also get a look at 3X3 Eyes, Golgo-13:The Professional, Armageddon (not Bruce Willis) CyberCity Oedo, Shadow Skill, and Blue Sonnet

finally Animatrix, if you ain't seen it, you've got to. Brilliant.

there are certain others i could recommend to you but you're prob not old enough to watch those!

and please don't call it "japanimation" because it's a really crass and insulting term for what is in effect an art form. Nor is it "manga" because that's wrong, those are comics. it's "animé"
