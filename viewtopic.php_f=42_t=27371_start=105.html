I almost forgot...
In Wing Commander & Privateer there are 3 classes of fighters:
Light, Medium and Heavy.
In Freelancer there are 3 classes of fighters, too:
Light, Heavy, and Very Heavy.
To make conversion easier I'm using:
FL's Light=WC/P's Light
FL's Heavy=WC/P's Medium
FL's Very Heavy=WC/P's Heavy
Also, with the larger number of ships, and more stats available, I've managed to make a few formulas to convert Shields, Hit Points, Mass, Pitch/Roll/Yaw, gw/gj, etc.
So, I've got to redo the weapons, generators, shields, etc.
