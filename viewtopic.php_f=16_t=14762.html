Afterburns, like Zaratomb said and side thrusters. If you dont fancy using the side thrusters to weave around just hold one down, it definitely makes you harder to hit.
Instead of using shield batts. run away for a while turning sharply and using your after/side burners to avoid fire - meanwhile your shields should be regenerating. This way you can save your batts for really sticky situations.
Good luck.
