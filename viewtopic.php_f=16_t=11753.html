Trichord

Wed Mar 26, 2003 5:15 am by Trichord

I just bought a Humpback freighter, and traded goods until I had +5 million credits. Then I bought my titan, and am living happily off that money. I don't think you can expand your cargo, and there is a better way: missions in the border worlds. They pay huge amounts of cash (once I got offered 200,000 credits for one mission). Also, speeding up past 80 without the a thruster is easy. Use shift+w, then press z to cut the engines. You'll slowly decelerate, but you will still be moving in the same direction, and you can freely rotate and shoot things. However, if you do need to alter your course, you'll need to re-fire the engines while facing the proper direction.

If you're willing to modify your game files, you can expand the cargo capacity; check out the Editing Forum.

There's also at least one thread in here somewhere about a very lucrative trade route between New Berlin and New Tokyo.
Good luck, whatever you decide!
