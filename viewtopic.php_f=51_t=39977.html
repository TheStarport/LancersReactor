Chips

Fri Jul 29, 2005 1:20 am by Chips

That mod (starflier) is SP only and cannot be used in MP for any advantage or mischief!

As for the "mining" mod - its not available.

We try to provide mods that are fun to use or play with - in both single player and multiplayer. However, when a mod can be used with disasterous consequences in multiplayer which is "intended" for singleplayer, we will neither host or allow discussion of, that mod. Whilst we do our best to cater for all, we cannot jepordise the online game enjoyed by hundreds to simply host what is basically a "cheat" - offering no real gameplay difference.

There is no negotiation, no discussion, and no leeway given on this subject - mistakes can sometimes be made, but we rectify them asap in the instance that it does occur. Whilst some may grumble and complain about the lack of hosting of those mods, I am affraid that is our perogative. We are free to choose whatever mod we wish to host, and hundreds of online players and server admins alike thank us for our vigiliance against such damaging mods.
