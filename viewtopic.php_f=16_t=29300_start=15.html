NukeIt

Sat Mar 13, 2004 2:03 pm by NukeIt

Just about anything can give you a run for your money if the pilot knows what they're doing. There are only a few truly sucky ships in the game(one of them being the horrible Bloodhound, another being the even worse Startracker)...the rest either are agile enough to dodge almost every shot, or have enough firepower and armor that a shot or two will do any enemy in...or in some cases(Stiletto and most of the other mid-range HF's), a good mix of both power and agility.

I would say that Freighters actually have a better chance against an LF than a VHF does, simply because every freighter in the game has 100% coverage- there isn't a blind spot to exploit, and there are quite a few rapid fire/high projectile speed turrets available in the class 5/6/7 range. simply put, a freighter can keep a large portion of its firepower directed at an enemy no matter where it moves, which makes Freighters pretty damn good at tagging the game's smaller ships. It is also worth noting that a Freighter's shield is always slightly more powerful than the LF/HF shield of the same type. However, due to their lack of maneuverability and armor, Freighters don't make very good combat ships when they are faced with higher class opponents.
