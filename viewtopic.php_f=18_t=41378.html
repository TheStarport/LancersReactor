There's a couple of things, your dealers have the bounty hunter faction instead of the local base owning faction, i'm not sure if you intended that.
Also, your weights are a bit over, they should total 100 instead.
If you are adding vignettes, its best to add lots of them all over the place. Try to add a low density ambient pop to the entire system as it will help you to get the missions working. Try using different types of vignettes too, variations of unlaw-law, law-unlaw and unlaw-unlaw to suit the factions.
