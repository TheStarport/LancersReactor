Fixing the Mbases.ini file after corruption due to decoding

This is a brief 'how to' on this. It is the same for everyone if you open it with a biniQ application. Basically you know if its corrupted cos when you go for a mission on the jobs board at a base, it will say come back when on better terms, even though those guys LOVE your ass!! OKay - in Mbases.ini

This happens for EVERY instance in the file!!
Here is an example - its the first time you will see this okay;

[MBase
nickname = Li01_01_Base
local_faction = li_p_grp
diff = 1
msg_id_prefix = gcs_refer_base_Li01_01_Base

[MVendor
num_offers = 8, 12

[BaseFaction
faction = li_n_grp
weight = 13
offers_missions = mission_type = DestroyMission, 0.000000, 0.112387, 30
npc = li0101_lnavy_001_f
npc = li0101_lnavy_002_m
npc = li0101_lnavy_003_f

Notice the bold line? That is the line we are interested in:

It should look like this:

offers_missions = true
mission_type = DestroyMission, 0.000000, 0.112387, 30

but it doesn't. So - the true has been omitted by the decoding process. To fix the file you have to go back through the Whole of the file and put in =true again everywhere!! LOL
So every time you see this:
offers_missions = mission_type = DestroyMission, 0.000000, 0.112387, 30

You should be seeing this:
offers_missions = true
mission_type = DestroyMission, 0.000000, 0.112387, 30

Its a pain i know, but you gotta do it!! As a word of warning, don't get impatient. do it right, slowly scroll through checking the whole document - it will take about an hour or two hours to do! ONce done, SAVE IT AND MAKE A BACK UP!! That way you NEVER have to fix it again!!!

offers_missions = mission_type = DestroyMission, 0.000000, 0.112387, 30

offers_missions = true
