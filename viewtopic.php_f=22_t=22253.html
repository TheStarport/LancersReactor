Hey there. Sorry for the slow response, I just stumbled on this thread today...I don't browse the forums as often as maybe I should. Anyway, I'm the Admin of [OGzr}Geezers in Space. (and the other gameservers we run) I personally don't play FL much at all...I just don't have the time unfortunately. I put the server up at the request of some of my clanmates - you've probably seen them in the server - they may not use the [OGzr} tag in their names though, I'm not sure about that.

As far as the player limit on the server, it's now at 14 in order to limit the bandwidth it uses. It's become a bit of a victim of it's own popularity I'm afraid. When I first put it up, it was set for 20 players or more. That was fine at first, but as it became more and more popular the bandwidth charges got a bit out of hand and I had to cut it back. At 14 it's using almost all of the 30 Gigs/month that is included with the base charge to host the machine. Anything beyond that and I get charged extra. (per Gig transferred)

So...lately I've been thinking maybe I should try setting up some kind of a pay-to-play system. If you guys really like the server that much, would you be willing to pay say $1 a month for the privilege of using the server? The server would then be passworded, and only the players who pay would be given the password. (a different password every month of course) Just a thought...maybe it wouldn't fly.

Thanks for listening and for your kind words about the server.
