“This mission was so easy” not real sure why so many people think that is a good answer. Guess it just makes them feel better about themselves. Anyway…

The Ctrl-W to cruise is the answer. I tried and tried without it and once I used it 2 or 3 tries later I finally won. I was in the Barracuda, a heavy fighter. With a lighter one it was much easier.

Just Press after burn to start (tab) and hit ctrl-W as soon as you can. Try not to pass him until the very end. If you do he bumps you and it makes it more difficult not to miss a ring. The platforms will open up on you but just keep at it, they are not very good shots.

Even after figuring out how to do it still took my 45 year old, not playstation II honed reflexes a couple of tries.
