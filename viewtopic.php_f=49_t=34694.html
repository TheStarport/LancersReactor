FIRST BLOOD

August 29, 2003 0200GMT

USS Thorn SSDN 988 7th fleet Sea Wolves Multinational force, in the south Atlantic near the Falkland Islands.

“Captain on deck!!” The Chief of the boat announced as Commander (LCDR) Adui entered the control room.
“ As you were gentlemen. Officer of the deck, what is the status on that TR1700 sonar has been tracking off and on? I know we are on good terms with Argentina, but that sub was acting a bit strangely.” Having been on patrol for 3 weeks, and under orders to hold radio silence except to check in once weekly, Thorn had not yet been advised that only hours before Argentina declared the intent to defend their “sovereign right “ to the Falkland Islands with force. LCDR Adui, FC of the sub, had a bad feeling. With no evidence of wrong, he couldn’t justify a more combative stance.
“ Well sir, they had her for a good hour and a half, she went to the surface, ran diesels for a bit, and presumably transferred radio traffic. Then she dove and just vanished.”
“ Darn, electric boats are next to impossible to hear if they don’t want you too. Where’s the Flying Fish?” Thorns brother in arms, the USS Flying Fish of the 12th fleet, part 2 of the Bad Passives combat team designated K-12 was on patrol with them. However, the two crews had always been competitive. Ever since they began diving together during the two Boat commanders SOAC course months back. So in times of peace, and relative safety, they played cat and mouse to further the skills of the crews.
Last time we had solid track on her she was 25nm NE of us sir. But that was nearly an hour ago”
Adui let a grim chuckle, “ Knowing that crew of pirates they are sneaking in on us now to ping off our hull from 5kyds or less! Tell sonar to redouble their efforts to find her.”

“ Torpedo now bears 245 and closing sir!!”
Crap, thought Adui, where the hell is the Flying Fish; as if in answer. “ Con sonar I have a new contact bearing 045 closing us at flank, it’s the Fish and she’s fired a full spread! “ Darn fool is trying to pull the torpedo off us! Adui was thinking but wisely said nothing.
Con Sonar 4 tubes fired all units running normally. Torpedo bearing 248 is homing 2 second pings sir!”
“ Con ahead flank, diving officer surface now! FC another spread of CM please. Chief sound all hands brace for shock!”
The Thorn screamed ahead as the torpedo closed, the Flying fish racing toward them to try and help, and the enemy now had 8 MK 48 ADCAPS to run from.
!!!BOOOOOM!!!
Thorn rocked violently, as the inbound weapon hit a CM near to port. Already in an emergency surface blow, Thorn rocketed upward even as the reports of flooding in the engine room came in.
“ Damage control how bad are we hit? “ Adui nearly screamed over the groans and shudders the battered boat made as it rocketed out of the water momentarily then crashed back down throwing waves about.
The damage was serious but not fatal, Thorn lost her towed array sonar, and the flooding damaged some auxiliary equipment. But it was contained within 15 minutes. The engines would be down for about 2 hours his Engineer said. But diving would be risky, and below 200 feet they would probably flood again, worse.
“Con Radio Flying fish is requesting a status report” The speaker blared.
“What??” Adui was a bit confused; the Fish should be relentlessly pursuing the enemy. “ Get me a secure channel to them now please”
Moments later the all to familiar sound of LCDR Griphon, Flying fish’s CO was on the speaker by the captains chair. “ Tell me how bad are you off Terry?” Adui and Griphon had become more than teammates and often used first names.
“ Well Chris, we aint gonna be skimming the floor any time soon, but cheng (Chief engineer) says he can give me engines in about 2 hours, can you screen us that long in case that SOB comes back??”
“Obviously your sonar was hit then, shortly after you took the near miss the poor fool who shot at you took six of the 8 we sent his way. Not enough left of that boat to make a tin can from! The other 2 were mine, they plowed the bottom trying to follow the pieces down”

09-03-03 0800 hours Eastern.
Atlantic Fleet Head quarters, Norfolk Virginia.


Most of the senior staff officers of SW had already assembled in the conference room. “ Attention on deck!” commanded VADM Waltsinger as FADM Wardog entered the room.
“As you were please. Admiral Waltsinger, please begin.”
“ Aye sir.” He began, “ As you all know, Argentina has taken a far more aggressive stance than expected regarding the Falklands. Most of you are aware of the incident regarding the 7th Thorn, and the 12th Flying fish, while on observation patrol in the area.” He paused measuring the looks of the officers, all of whom were awaiting something new and ominous. “ What you don’t know, is that the reporting of the sinking of an Argentine sub was a bit incorrect. Yes, Thorn and Flying fish hit an Argentine owned submersible vessel. However. It was not one of there 3 combat submarines.” Pausing again VADM Waltsinger noted the surprised expressions surrounding the table. “ Apparently they purchased a civilian submarine a while back. It had been outfitted to emit sounds mimicking the TR 1700 that they use for combat patrol. This means that the attack on Thorn and her teammate was not only unprovoked; but it was also premeditated. A deliberate ruse, apparently designed to show the world that Argentina has a competent and lethal submarine force.”
There were several hushed comments and some groaning from the assembled officers.
“Gentle men, Argentina has issued a press statement stating that no force, even ours, is welcome in what they deem there sovereign waters. Any uninvited vessel will be subject to attack and or seizure by the Argentine naval forces. To emphasize this 2 civilian vessels, a car carrier and a box carrier, were sunk for quote violating the sovereign waters of Argentina.”
Waiting for the news to set in, the Admiral looked at each of the officers in the meeting.
“ We have sent the Carrier Enterprise, with a battle group from the 23rd fleet SWSD commanded by RDML Tarner to blockade the argentine coast in the waters off the Falklands until Argentina ceases these hostile actions. Attached to the group are the subs of K4 commanded by CAPT Uzo and RDML Sparks. In addition we have reluctantly agreed to let the 12th Flying Fish resume combat patrol wit the CBG.”
The FADM Stood up, “ Gentlemen, this is rapidly becoming a shooting war. Until further notice set DEFCON 2 and prepare for war. All SW vessels are authorized to take any reasonable measures in defense of themselves or civilian bystanders.
Any questions?”
CAPT Goucho, 7th FC lifted his hand. CAPT, go ahead..”
“ Sir. What is the status of the Thorn and her crew?”
“ Bad new is this sir, she took a harder hit than originally assessed. She can’t rotate the shaft without causing flooding. She’s currently in tow and protected by a couple of destroyers from the Atlantic fleet, as well as an Akula on loan from the soviets.”
Casualties sir?”
“Light, and no deaths. Those injuries that were sustained are recoverable. Amazingly her crew was able to stop the flooding in the main engine room. LCDR Adui has put several of his damage control crew in for medals.”
“Anything else?”
“Yes sir,” CINCPAC began, “ The Flying Fish has a capable crew sir, no one will deny that. However, Thorn is her team ship in the K fleet. Given LCDR Griphon rather aggressive style under normal conditions is it wise to let them go back into a combat zone?”
“ Way ahead of you, I have reserved the right to recall them. Besides, LCDR Griphon has a good crew, and he may be foolishly aggressive at times.. But he’s a damn fine sub driver. He will be ok.”…
