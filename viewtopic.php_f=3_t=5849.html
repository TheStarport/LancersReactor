Tachyon

Tue Jan 28, 2003 10:34 pm by Tachyon

Well it takes anywhere from five to ten minutes to reach a certain point of interest if there are no trade lanes available. Also, even in systems with trade lanes there are "hidden" things like pirate bases and jump holes to find, as well as other stuff. To help you, your long range sensors will detect and log "patrol routes" which is where ships have gone after a period of time. If you activate this filter on your nav map and look for junctions of routes, you will often find something interesting there.
