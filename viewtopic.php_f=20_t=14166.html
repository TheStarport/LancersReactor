The screen doesn't mess up, it just freezes on the screen that asks you if you want to exit, or the screen that says you've been disconnected, or whatever screen.

For you XP users, here's a much faster shortcut to kill the game and not put your PC to sleep:

1) Press CTRL + ALT + DEL
2) Press "fr" (without the quotes... this will select the freelancer.exe that is running... and you must type them quickly, otherwise you'll end up selecting whatever process begins with "r" *
3) Press ALT + E (this will bring up the End Process box)
4) Press Y
5) Your game will end and you will be returned to your normal life.

* if you somehow have another process running that begins with "fr" and you end up terminating it, try using "fre" or "free" or whatever it takes to make the Task Manager select freelancer.exe ... "fr" just works for me because it's the only thing running in the background that begins with "fr" ... it should work for 99% of others as well.

Now all of that is done blindly, but if you follow those directions exactly and correctly, you terminate the game without having to turn off your PC, put it in to sleep and wake it back, or whatever you do to get out of it.

I hope this helps.
