Just did some tests and searchs. I have two 'puters, one strictly for my server and the other for playing FL (a client) among other things . The FLspew.txt file was ONLY on the client system. It's contents look similar to the CONSOLE window in flserver. Lots of warnings, errors, messages about loading files, etc. On the server I do not even have that file, no where. So it must be a FL client message spew.

As for your problem, I have heard FL will not work correctly with any version of DirectX except v9.0b. In fact when I installed v9.0c I had lots of crashs, some were BSODs. But they are gone now when I changed the motherboard's memory setup from single to dual channel (nForce2 chipset). I have no clue why that would stop the issues in playing FL but that is the time they stopped. Go figure.

BTW I also have SP2 (and DirectX v9.0c) on both 'puters with WinXPPro firewalls disabled because I have a hardware firewall. I have had no problems on the server.
