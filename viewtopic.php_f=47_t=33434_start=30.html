Solar Pilot

Sun Aug 01, 2004 12:06 pm by Solar Pilot

Nathan Edwards/Patriot/Cortez Jump Hole, Magellan System/Liberty Police

The Cavalier of Chief Constable Alistair Grey of the Bretonia Police Authority and the Patriot of Captain Nathan Edwards of the Liberty Police had been speeding along side-by-side for a while now, en route to the Cortez Jump Hole. The two light fighters had made for the jump hole coordinates at best speed as soon as they'd cleared Freeport 4. On the way, Edwards had placed a call with Planet Curacao under the cover of a guest's family member. The operator on the comm channel had said what he'd expected: Orbital Spa and Cruise was evacuating all ships through the Cortez System, offloading them at Freeport 4 since it was a neutral site. They'd summoned whatever fighter support Orbital had available to them on Curacao, most of them Startrackers, a few Hawks. The fighters were supposed to escort about 20 Armored Transports through the jump hole. Edwards and Grey knew the fighters would be necessary as the convoy would be entering Magellan only two sectors away from Mactan Base, a Lane Hacker installation. He also knew Rogues patrolled this system sometimes too.

Taking a curving path to keep a safe distance away from Mactan Base, the two police fighters from opposite sides of the Barrier Ice Fields eventually came to a stop about a kilometer away from the Cortez Jump Hole.

"Well?" Grey asked. "Now what?"
"We wait," Edwards asked as he flicked a few switches. "Put your weapons in standby mode until the convoy arrives here. I get this nagging feeling Mactan's radar is going to light up with twenty-some odd Orbital signatures and a raiding party's going to come out. When that happens.... well, we'll just see how things turn out." And that didn't put Edwards' or Grey's mind at ease one bit.

Sure enough, about five minutes later, the first Armored Transports started arriving through the jump hole.
