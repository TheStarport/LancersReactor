As for today's events, it all started out when the evil NEOs believed me to be trapped in Malta, at Omicron Alpha.

However I quickly escaped to the complete bewilderment of my slow and sluggish enemies. My goal was to quickly find one of the amazing Ripper mine launchers and seek refuge in the safety of Free Port 9, which I easily reached in short time.

Alas the NEO fleet was hot on my tail. As I was leaving Free Port 9, I found myself alone facing a NEO task force and the trailing SW peons. Quickly dodging the enemy missiles and evading the laser blasts I managed to last as long as one can when faced against insurmountable odds.

But then, cruising right out the stars, the valiant pilot Alpha arrived!

While Alpha’s affiliation is unknown to me, it was obvious that we was angered by the unprovoked and tyrannical raid committed against him by NEO. So he charged headstrong into the NEO leader and utterly decimated him. The debris of Tasiin's ship was seen floating around Free Port 9 for quite some time.

While the leader's exact definition of "alt tabbing" eludes me at the moment, considering how the NEO leader was conversing just a few seconds before he engaged Alpha, Tasiin was sent back home licking his wounds while the news of his death reached every pilot in the Sirius sector.

This opened a marvelous window of opportunity. It became quite obvious to me that the lack of leadership caused confusion in the rest of the NEOs, whom unsurprisingly began to dissipate from the sector. This left the SW peons in solitude and completely demoralized. So once again, I launched, I charged, and I scored yet another victory!

The fighting raged on, and the attrition continued to increase for the NEO. Ultimately I found myself docked at Malta under a heavy blockade by one of the NEO pilots and the evil thugs who’ve flocked to the NEO banner. However, being very safe within the planet's atmosphere, I will wait for the proper time to launch, within the comfortable protection of the Malta bar.

To all my fellow pilots: Remember that the resistance is winning! Join us today and fight back for your own freedom!
