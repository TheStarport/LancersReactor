Recruitment Letter

We are to the -11th Fleet- Spartan Vanguard

http://www.svcommand.com

For you to have made it this far is a testament to yourself and your skill; at the Spartan Vanguard we only except the best of the best -- the elite.

You have taken the first step on a epic journey that will see you become a member of one the single-largest Star Trek gaming fleets. We are also one of the oldest, with a nine-year-history.

We don't beat our chests with propoganda and flame wars. We just fight when challenged. Our codes and ethics are strict and our morale caliber high. We expect recruits to hold their head high with Spartan pride and fear nothing.

The Vanguard has branched to many games, including new divisions for World of Warcraft, Joint Operations, Freelancer and Rome Total War.

We are active in Starfleet Command III, Armada 2, Elite Force, Elite Force 2 and Bridge Commander Division.

Regardless of what division you are joining, we encourage you to get involved with as many games as you wish, and will support you in anyway we can.

We are the brotherhood.

Just as the Federation in Star Trek have a General Order No. 1 (the Prime Directive) so do we in the Spartan Vanguard, and that order is have fun ... at the heart of this guild are the members -- a team, a family.

We are all gamers and enjoy nothing more then getting together in a game, playing together and dominating as a fleet. We are not 'power gamers', but a group of friends who lauhg and joke together on TeamSpeak and just generally have fun.

Whether you just want to play the game with the fleet and make friends, or want to get more involved with learning how to make mods or modeling, we will always do are best to help you. We operate servers for our games and have forums you will enjoy.

For your part all that we ask is that you read the regulations on our main Website and understand and follow them. And of course, have fun and enjoy the roleplay.

For recruiting contacts, write: SpartanVanguard@gmail.com

Interviews are held on TeamSpeak.

Not all applicants make it, but if you do, you will enter the halls of Spartan brotherhood that only a few have walked.

-salute-
