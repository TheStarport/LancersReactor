Imagine

Mon Aug 11, 2003 11:09 pm by Imagine

Harrier: your problem has super easy to fix
Under your ship entry in shiparch you will see LODranges followed by some distance values. When your ship is out of LODrange 0, fl looks for your LOD1 model, which you don't have, but fl's packaged ships all have. Change these numbers to 0, 1001, 1002, and so on so it disappears at range 1001.
