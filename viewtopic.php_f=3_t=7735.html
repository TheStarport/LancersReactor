Erin Roberts wasn't involved, as far as I know.

Chris Roberts was the original designer, but left after MS completed the acquisition of Digital Anvil. The overall concept for a space sim with mouse control, in a "dynamic" universe was Chris Roberts' idea.

"Elite 3" was called Frontier: First Encounters

David Braben and Ian Bell wrote the original Elite in the early 80s. David Braben wrote Frontier: Elite 2 and Frontier: First Encounters. Braben himself has said that development of Elite 4 is halted.
