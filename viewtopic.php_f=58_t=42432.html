AGP? The best cards for that slot spec are probably the following:

Radeon X800 series

Geforce 6800 series

In Canada, the Radeon X800 cards tend to cost about $350, and the Geforce 6600s, between $150-$200 while the 6800s tend to run about $250.

The X800 Platinum runs $700 Canadian-ish, and the 6800 Ultra costs almost $900, and those two are probably the highest you'll ever get in AGP

If you're spending money in US dollars, and are limited in your choice of graphics card, I would suggest looking into these from newegg:

Sapphire Radeon 9600 Pro - costs $79 US

Jetway Radeon 9600XT - costs $83 US
