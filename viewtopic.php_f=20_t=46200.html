It sounds like you installed the official Microsoft Freelancer patch version 1.1

The only way to get rid of the patch is to delete your Freelancer folder which is located at

do not uninstall Freelancer but instead delete the Freelancer folder...
and then reinstall Freelancer again and this time do not install the 1.1 patch.
Instead install the QuickFix patch QuickFix-v1.0c.exe

Ogu

Thu Aug 02, 2007 7:18 am by Ogu

Is it an equipment error (inconsistent ship setup) or is it a ship related cheat?

You might want to check the volume usage of your engine. You can either make the cruiser have a larger cargo hold (1000), or you can go to engine_equip.ini and make sure all the volumes are 0.

You could check the package for the ship (in goods.ini) and make sure that whatever it comes mounted with is placed on the right mounts (ie, make sure you don't have a turret mounted on a gun, or a Class 10 on a Class 9 slot, etc.
