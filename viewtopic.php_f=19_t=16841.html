Freelancer Ladder

Hey everyone,

I'm Nicodeme, or Nico, from the GamerHost Ladder.

GamerHost.com is a clan dedicated site offering services such as clan hosting, forums, server hosting, and we have a ladder with many games, including a new Freelancer ladder. In the Freelancer ladder (more of a marathon competition), clans will be able to compete against each other in the entire Freelancer map. The time of each match will be 3 to 5 hours, so be sure to have plenty of time and at least 5 to 7 players for each clan.

Since the GamerHost Ladder is new, any clans that sign up will have the opportunity to select what kind of games and ladders you want to compete in. As a new team we know your level of commitment and this is why we want to give you an opportunity to enter on the ground floor, and as an added incentive we are prepared to offer you an administration spot on one of our ladders and we can host your team in our community absolutely free of charge. With this hosting you will have your own forums at the GHL and we can offer you and your team prizes on the ladder such as free games and computer hardware.

Please fell free to stop by our web site and browse around. I am sure you will like what you see. http://gamerhost.com

I am looking forward to seeing you and feel free to ask any question that you want. Remember, we need your input to make this the very best gaming community on the net.

We also own the IPF http://www.iplayfair.org and we take a strong stand on cheating. We support anti-cheat programs and we would like to have your input on this. This is another benefit you get coming in on the ground floor to the GHL.


Hope to see you soon,
Nicodeme
