DiscoJason

Tue Feb 25, 2003 11:55 pm by DiscoJason

You buy launchers in the gun section of the equipment shop. I find that usually when you buy them, they give you a few missiles to go along with it. You then go to the missile, torpedo, mine, and countermeasure section to buy the type of missile that goes with that launcher. For instance, you would buy a Javelin launcher and then go buy Javelin missiles. These are the only two that match. You cannot buy a Javelin launcher and a Moonstalker missile. This goes for the mine launchers, countermeasure launchers, and torpedo launchers.

Also, notice there are normal missiles and disruptor missiles. Disruptor missiles are used for pulling ships out of cruising speed and normal missiles damage shields and hull. Some missels do more to shields and some do more to hulls.

When you shoot a missile, you should see it shoot out ahead of you unless you are doing the afterburner as you shoot it, in which case you would pass it up. I use Cruisers right now and it has a yellow flame shooting out of it and I see it shoot off in the ditance and can easily tell if it hit or missed.

Mines are not as easy and I tend to launch them with faith that they released. I basically use them if someone seems to be right ebhind me. Now, if you want to look behind yourself when you launch them, just hit the V key which gives you a view behind yourself. Hold that while launching the mine and you can verify that it is being dropped.
