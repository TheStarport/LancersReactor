Trade tip for making money early in the game:

Once you can get a Rhino and fill it, take Luxury Consumer Goods to the GC base (Atla?) in Hokkaido, bring cardamine back to Manhattan. If you do it early enough in the game, everyone in Kusari should be neutral or friendly with you (kill too many pirates and the all important GC start turning hostile). The only significant danger you face is from Lane Hackers in the border systems between Liberty and Kusari space. Upgrade to a Kusari freighter when money allows. And yes, go the long way around Manhattan by exiting the trade lane early to avoid some problems with the seemingly ever-present police outside the planet.

Thu Mar 03, 2005 7:29 pm by NukeIt

This thread might be a good candidate for one of those omnipotent moderator thumbtacks...*hint hint*

A few little tricks that can help you live longer:

* Use mines to disrupt tradelanes after entering them. If you are being pursued, your would-be killer now has to wait for the lane to cycle or fly to the next ring before he/she can enter the lane. To be really sneaky, disrupt the other end of the lane as well to slow them down twice as much.

* Engine kill is not just a handy way to check your six, it can be used as an effective combat tactic for an entire battle. Try leaving engine kill on and maneuvering around using just your thruster- you will be able to maintain a 200m/s top speed and fire in any direction at any time. You can also pull surprise turns in engine kill, and reverse course in much less time than it would take to turn all the way around at 80m/s (or at 200 using the thruster without engine kill).

* Wait as long as possible before using shield batteries or nanobots. Each will repair 300HP of shield or armor damage, but they will be partially wasted if you use them early (for example, hitting the battery key when your shield has only taken 100HP or so of damage). The longer you wait, the less nanobots and shield batteries you will waste on partial repair jobs.

* Mining/looting tip: When you are going out to loot wrecks or mine for valuable commodities, equip a low level weapon such as a Justice I or a basic Stunpulse, and use only that single weapon for that purpose. It only takes one hit from these weapons to pop an asteroid or a wreck, and there is less of a chance that you might accidentally fry your loot with your massive main weapons.
