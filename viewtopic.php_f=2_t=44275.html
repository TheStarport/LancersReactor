I'll toss in my one and a half cents here, even though you seem to have figured things out already.

As you probably know, persistance is the key. This is especially true if you're running on a newer system where SL isn't quite as stable and crashes frequently. Some of the later missions get especially difficult with these timed tasks, whether it is destroying targets or blocking a torpedo attack.

There's more than one way to skin a fish... while the guide on this site is excellent, choose a ship that works best for your personal style. I prefer the Coyote/Patriot series most of the time. Healthy mix of firepower and durability. As far as missile weapons go, I choose mostly a mix of Screamers (for Basilisks and turrets), and extra Afterburner fuel. I'm always using it, and always paranoid of running out. I will take other weapons, such as Hawk/Raptor pods, or Jackhammers only if required for the mission. Hawks/Raptors are useful for torpedos if you've got time...

Anyways, just some general advice. Though the forum may look quiet, people still watch this. Post and ye shall be answered!
