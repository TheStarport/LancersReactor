The lag can be due to your antivirus running while you're playing FL - I've noticed that excess choppiness can be cured by temporarily setting the antivirus off. In addition, it is good practice to defragment your hard drive. I have been recommended O&O Defrag and have used it - it works quite well and is faster than Microsoft's defragmenter.

Another reason for lag is out of your control; it's your ISP, and sometimes their connection may just bog down for whatever reason and there's little you can do about it (well, aside from binning your computer in frustration ).
