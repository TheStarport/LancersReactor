Wed Dec 24, 2003 5:35 am by Esquilax

Notebooks/laptops are great for mobility, however they are awful for gaming. They do not have the power necessary for GPU intensive games which are defined as pretty much any modern game these days. The "Centrino" laptops are not bad, they have low CPU power consumption, and may be worth considering. That is, if you swing that way .

A large USB Key is an excellent idea (or an external Netdisk). USB Keys can store a lot of information, they are very solid and redundant (just drop them in a pool and see! ), and they are quite cheap. Just remember; USB 2.0!!!. If it's not USB 2.0, it will transfer data slowly, and will cuase you no end of irritation.
