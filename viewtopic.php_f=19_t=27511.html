Mon Jan 12, 2004 2:53 am by Chips

I had 56k - and i was fine on most servers. However- go to the MP screen - and then click on PING tag. This will sort out servers due to their ping (low to high, if not - click again for reverse order), then pick one that looks lively without tons of lag. Ping of under 50 is excellent - but unlikely, ping around 200 is acceptable on a modem.


If you don't like the server- move on, no point staying somewhere you don't like - and i do advise you pick one with SOME poeple on it - as that IS the reason for playing in MP.

One last word of warning - pick a server that says it is 24/7 as well - nothing worse than logging on and finding your server aint there!. Check the news for rules and regs - and abide by them. Admins will ban if you break rules - and unlike the law - there is no court of appeal.

If the server has a website - check it out - it usually means that there is a community going there............which is BETTER than others - also - check websites for news about the server - in particular clans, as you might find one tolling you for access to certain systems - and when faced by an eagle with nomad loadout, and your in a freighter - well, its a little late by then!

Have fun - as MP is DEFINATLEY the best point about this game!!

Chips

p.s- DON'T CHECK OUT ANY MODS even if people tell you too - playing MP is great for MONTHS before moving onto new pastures. Its on hell of a big universe to explore
