I just completed the SP 2 days ago for the first time. The 1st time I played, I got a tad bit stuck trying to use a Bretonian Heavy Fighter. It could certainly dish out the abuse, but it took a pounding too, no matter how I tried to juke and dodge.

I restarted and tried it again, this time going the Light Fighter route. In the Light Fighters I found that dodging was much easier and that I rarely got hit. In fact, once I got the Kusari Drake I never used a shield battery again for the rest of the SP. I simply wasn't getting hit.

I don't know how true that would hold vs an experienced Human Player using engine kill maneuvers and flying backward and the like, though. I've seen some people say that nothing beats an "engine killed" Titan doing "spin moves."

Anyway, it was serviceable -- actually, more than serviceable -- in the SP.
