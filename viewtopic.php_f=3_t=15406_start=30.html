sandorski

Sun May 04, 2003 9:40 pm by sandorski

Haven't tried the Dromedary, but I don't find the Humpy as bad as people are stating. Sure, it does handle like a dog, I don't bother trying to manuever.

I've developed this strategy when doing a run in the Humpy:

1) Click on Waypoint or place you want to dock at
2) Point the Humpy in that direction
3) Goto Turret view and blast away
4) Use the "!" mode in your target box
5) Check distance to targeted Waypoint/Dock, once within 500ish M, press Dock key
6) Use Afterburners if you wish, I usually don't
7) Fire at attacker until they turn away or take shots at multiple targets, they'll peel off. Pay special attention to those firing at you

I haven't gone to the Outer Worlds in the Humpy, but can go anywhere else and survive any attack, so far. I use an All Guns config(no missiles) and one great thing with the Humpy is that you can Spray 'N Pray for longer than you'll likely ever need. Watch your targets though, last night I ended up fighting everyone cops, Rogues, DSE, BH, etc these guys don't know the meaning of Friendly Fire.
