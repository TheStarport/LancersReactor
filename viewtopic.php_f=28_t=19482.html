Star Wars: Empires

I'd just like to announce here that initial design and development work has begun on Star Wars: Empires, a total conversion for Freelancer... making the Sirius Sector into the Star Wars galaxy in every conceivable way!

Star Wars: Empires is a joint development project between Project 90 Software and the Frost Works Modding Group.

The goals for Star Wars: Empires are seperated into three phases:

Phase 1 - "A New Hope..."
This phase will change all existing Freelancer systems to be in-line with our vision of Star Wars, and will add all the ships seen in the original trilogy.

Phase 2 - "Echoes of the Past, Visions of the Future..."
This phase will add in all relevant prequel ships as well as ship types featured in games such as X-Wing Alliance. The system list will be expanded to include those found in the prequel trilogy, as well as popular expanded universe systems.

Phase 3 - "Expanding Universes..."
This phase will see the gradual addition, through regular patches, of new ships, stations, systems, and equipment based on what is found in the Expanded Universe materials... including the New Jedi Order and Prequel books. This phase will continue as long as there is new material to add...

The ultimate goal of Star Wars: Empires is to re-create the Star Wars galaxy in every conceivable way. From the traffic going to and from Bothawui, to the dangerous Kessel Run, to the secrets of the Unknown Regions... Star Wars: Empires will endeavour to show it all, and immerse you in the wonders of the Star Wars universe!

More information will be posted as our development progresses... look for a URL to our new website and forums as they go up!

Stalker
Co-Founder; Project 90 Software
