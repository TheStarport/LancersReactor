Alistair Grey/Cavalier/New York System/Bretonia Police Authority

The Liberty strike force's advance scout, Razor 1-1, radioed in loudly over the channel: "All units, all units this is priority one. Two battleships are heading for Planet Manhattan. One has a very large plasma bomb. The base is a diversion! Uploading waypoint. Hurry, we haven't got much time!"

Chief Constable Alistair Grey's face blanched as he heard that. The Bretonians were going to attack Manhattan. They were going to destroy Manhattan! This was insane! Capturing the Liberty capital to demoralize the Navy was one thing, but to destroy the planet itself, kill all those innocent civilians. This was a war crime.

He wasn't about to let it happen on his watch. He keyed his comm. "This is Bretonia Police Red 1-1. All Cavaliers form on me. We're breaking off to intercept the bomb."
The action was unnecessary. The entire strike force changed their heading and turned towards the two Bretonian battleships that Razor 1-1 had tagged. They were already starting to be silouhetted against the sun's corona. Their cruise engines already at full steam, they were catching up quickly.

The comm came alive.
"This is the Oregon . We've alerted Manhattan to the incoming weapon and they're pulling in fighters from Fort Bush as a last line of defense. When we reach the weapon, the Mt. McKinley and the St. Louis will concentrate primary fire on the battleship towing the fusion bomb. All fighters will handle the escorts. We'll cover the bomb and try to dispatch a heavy lifter to capture it. Under no circumstances must that weapon reach Manhattan!"
Edwards' voice came across the channel. "This is Liberty Police Blue 1-1. Oregon, has the president been evacuated?"
"Blue 1-1, the president was moved to Newark Station in case a speedy escape was needed. The Battleship Wisconsin will transport her to Fort Bush pending the outcome of this battle. Worse comes to worst, she'll be evacuated to Planet Denver."
"Acknowledged."

After a few more moments, they came within a few kilometers of the Bretonian strike force and dropped out of cruise speed. Grey hoped this wouldn't turn into a battle. He'd felt guilty enough attacking the BAF Crusaders back in Magellan. They were his countrymen after all.
Grey keyed his comm again. "Oregon , this is Bretonia Police Red 1-1. Request permission to hail the strike force. I might be able to talk them out of it."
There was a pause across the channel. "Make it quick, Red Leader. We can't dawdle for long."
"Understood," Grey replied. "Red Flight, form up on me." The dozen Cavaliers engaged thrusters, pushing ahead of the pursuing Liberty craft. They were about 1.5 kilometers away when Grey keyed his comm to all channels.

"Attention Bretonian strike force, this is Bretonia Police Red 1-1, Chief Constable Alistair Grey speaking. Gentlemen, I'm sure you've been given orders to launch this weapon against Planet Manhattan, but think about what you're about to do. You're going to destroy a planet, with millions of innocent civilians. There's a difference between taking the capital intact and obliterating it.
"Don't get me wrong, I answer to the crown too. I believe in Bretonia, even despite all the bloody insane policies Wessex put in place. But I swear, if you do this, the blood of millions of human beings will be on your hands, and it will never wash away. For God's sakes, can you live with yourselves if you kill all these people? They're civilians, not military. I'm begging you, don't do this... don't do the devil's work. Take this weapon out of Liberty, and the Navy will let you go freely. Please mates... don't commit mass murder in the name of glory. There's nothing glorious about it."

Grey waited uneasily as silence settled over the comm frequency, and the Liberty pursuers set into formation directly behind them.

Alistair Grey/Cavalier/New York System/Bretonia Police Authority

Grey knew he'd struck a nerve somewhere in the midst of the attack fleet. And thankfully, they were willing to help. He'd gotten through, even it if was just one Crusader that was now opening fire on the battleship. Nonetheless, he'd heeded the officer's words. He opened his comm to the Liberty fleet behind him.

"I made contact. One of the Crusaders is disabling the lead battleship's engine. He needs you blokes out of the system, so clear off to Alaska."
"This is the Oregon . Negative. We are not going to disengage pursuit. They're only about 25 kilometers away from Manhattan-"
Edwards' voice cut in. "Oregon , this is Blue 1-1. We'll watch over things. You and the other Navy boys get to Zone 21 and get through that gate. We'll advise you when the coast is clear."
Yet another pause across the channel. "This is the Oregon . All Navy fighters disengage and form up on us. Plot heading for Zone 21. The Mt. McKinley and the St. Louis will remain here and provice heavy fire support. Agreed?"
Neither Edwards, nor Grey had any qualms with that. They could probably use the heavy cannons to take out the battleship's engines in the long run.

Soon after, the Liberty battleship, two squadrons of Defenders and one more of Patriots disengaged, turned, and headed at cruise speed for Zone 21.
Grey keyed his comm to the Crusader that was now attacking. "We're not leaving you to fend off these bullocks by yourself, mate! Moving to attack!"
Edwards' voice came across the channel as well. "Blue Flight, overlap Red Flight's formation, provide suppressing fire. Cruiser wing, target your primary cannons on the lead battleship's engine. We have to disable it!"
A series of acknowledgements echoed across as a dozen Cavaliers and a dozen Patriots, trailed by two cruisers, entered into the foray, streaking for the battleship carrying the planet-killing bomb.
"Target locked!" someone cried out.
Grey didn't hesistate.
