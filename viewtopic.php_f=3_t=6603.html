Lev Arris

Sat Feb 22, 2003 11:36 pm by Lev Arris

It's not entirely true that you are being attacked all the time. When you use the tradelanes you are pretty much save. Sure, once in a while they disable a tradelane ring but you can escape them. You will have the most enemy encounters in debris fields or in sectors that are controlled entirely by a faction that doesn't like you. The main problem is: at the beginning of the game your reputation with the rogues is so bad, it couldn't be much worse (due to the first mission which is mandatory). The result: they attack you whenever possible. In the final game you will have the option to earn enough money to bribe some barekeeper who will improve your reputation with the rogues. This will hopefully result in less attacks.

Lev Arris

Sun Feb 23, 2003 7:00 am by Lev Arris

Yeah, check the patrol routes mode on the system map. you can see that rogues patrol all the debris fields in a very dense pattern. And some of those routes come very close to tradelanes. You can use the map to plot a secure course. They may take longer to fly because you most likely need to take a detour, but that's the best way to evade fights when you are not using the tradelanes.

A little hint for exploring nebulae or large areas of space in general: create yourself a search pattern with custom waypoints. This way you can make sure that you check every single part of the explored area without checking some parts multiple times.
