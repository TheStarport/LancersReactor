1. scaling ships along with their surs(hitboxs) is easy with flmodeltool,however stations can't be resized correctly yet as the moving parts do not resize or reposition, weapon effects also can't be scaled(yet)

#2 & 3 empathy.ini controls inter-faction relations,there are more tutes on this and random encounters.faction_props assigns ships to factions

as to #4, you should look at singeplayer capable mods like freeworlds or evolutions etc to figure that out - i don't think theres any specific tutorial to building toward SP compatibility

for #5 see my tutorial Changing MP Start Details, Adding New Pilots w/ Locations & Factions.
