A terrible accident happened earlier in the Tau-37 System, (20 Years Later Mod).

Escorts of the train mistook this accident for hostile action and began attacking the Cruiser. The Captain of the Cruiser had no other choice than to return fire. Action quickly escalated near the Terran System Jumphole as other IMG convoys joined the frey.
Then, all was quiet.

"Really, it was a terrible accident. We weren't paying attention when that train crossed our path. It is an unfortunate loss and we have sent a formal apology to the IMG Headquarters. As for the others, we had to defend ourselves.
That last Eagle just scratched our paint is all. But, it isn't the house ships that worry us. We just came from the Terran System where the nomads seem to be only second stringers.
