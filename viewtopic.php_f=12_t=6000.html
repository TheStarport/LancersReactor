Wed Feb 05, 2003 6:58 am by Bhurak Starkiller

There's a trailer you can download that will explain it.

Basically the Wachowski brothers got some of their favorite anime directors to do anime shorts based on The Matrix universe, filling in some of the history, exploring other stories, that kind of thing. Aside from anime directors, there's also a short being done by the remnants of Square Pictures. All of the shorts were either written by the Wachowski brothers, or the scripts were given their approval.

There's going to be a total of 9 Animatrix shorts, which will be released on a DVD in June. At least 4 of them will be released online, however.
