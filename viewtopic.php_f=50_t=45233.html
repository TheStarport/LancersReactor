I highly recommend that you look through the tutorials and start reading. I do not intend this to sound rude or anything, just there is a lot to modding. There is a ton of turorials that will get you off to a good start. You also need a good understanding of the ini file structure and other files and how they inter-relate with each other which the tutorials will also assist you with.

Along with the above, just search the forums. There is a great chance that what you are looking for is here already in a thread or 2.

For starters, use a program called Freelancer Explorer. Search the downloads for it and you will find it. This will allow you to place a ton of things into a system.
