My favorite trade route. Requires the NY ---> Magellan gate to be unlocked.

Stop 1: Beaumont Base, Texas
- Sell Cardamine
- Buy Engine Components

Take the Texas ---> NY Jump Hole
Take the NY ---> Magellan Jump Gate

Stop 2: Mactan Base, Magellan
- Sell Engine Components
- Buy Cardamine.

Take the Magellan ---> NY Jump Gate
Take the NY ---> Texas Jump Hole
Repeat
-----
I made about 200K per round trip with a fully loaded Dromedary. 5 runs and you can buy a fully loaded VHF. Only 2 jumps. Requires Lane Hackers and Junkers not to hate you. Recommended Outcasts and Rogues not hate you. Although, you can always hang around Rochestor killing junkers for their cargo and making about 100K per Junker.

Don't know where you're starting from, but here's my 2 credits:

1. Buy diamonds at Frietal Base in Omega-11, $198.
2. Sell diamonds on Planet New Tokyo, $1650. Buy luxury consumer goods, $810.
