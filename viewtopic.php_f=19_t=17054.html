The two hour disconnect will totally screw your MP gaming experience!

Best thing about MP is grouping or fighting with others but it'll be pointless if you're gonna be getting disconnected every 2 hours. You also restart at the last station you docked at so you'd end up docking at every station possible so not to loose to much game time and end up re-starting half way across the galaxy!

Won't be feasible to run a server at all because everyone will get disconnected mid-game when your ISP kicks you off, but you probably wouldn't want to off a dial-up anyway unless it's only for yourself and a friend - even a 56K connection is only really good enough for one player connecting to a server.

My advice would be to switch to a decent ISP with no disconnect time. Even some of the free UK isps are better than what you're using (BT Internet I assume?).

I use Demon Internet which are cool because they have no disconnect, surftime packages for "un-metered" dialup, ADSL and fixed IP (great for running servers) but any of the "big name" ISPs such as Pipex, Tiscali, NTL, FreeServe, Virgin etc will be fine if you just wanna connect to other peoples servers.
