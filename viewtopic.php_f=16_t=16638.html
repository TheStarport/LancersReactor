I just started a new MP character for an experiment and got to lvl12 without killing any ships except Xenos (about 275 ships I think) and without doing any missions (because finishing a mission improves your rep with the faction you got it from, so your rep goes down with their enemies)

Now everyone is neutral or friendly. Apart from Xenos. They don't like me much.

It's surprising how much your rep changes from doing missions. If you do a mission for the Libert Police, your rep goes down a lot against Rogues if you succeed, even if the mission is against Rogue enemies like Xenos.

Try just flying round killing your chosen faction's enemy ships, not doing missions, and your rep with get to neutral a lot quicker.
