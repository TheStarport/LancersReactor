Those are interface functions, most can be mapped to a key but not all of them work. You can enable vertical strafing, weapon grouping, and turning controls. The last two are very useful for people that use a joystick inaddition to the typical keyboard and mouse setup.

See the TNG Teaser (I think its called) or TNG itself in the downloads section for examples of their functionality. I'm not sure if there are any tutorials or other mods around. I'm playing with this stuff now to add a few things to my clan's mod.
