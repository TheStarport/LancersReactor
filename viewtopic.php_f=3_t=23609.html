NukeIt

Sun Sep 07, 2003 3:47 pm by NukeIt

Humpback is a ship to fly in turret mode, using goto to reach destinations by setting nav points. It's better off in the civilized systems, where your enemies aren't as likely to have high-level weaponry. If you're a pure trader, choose the humpback.

Dromedary is a better ship for the borderworlds, because it can "turn and burn" much faster than its fat cousin. Because of its larger hold, it is also better for carrying perishables. If you like to pick fights between trade runs, the dromedary is your ship.

Both are relatively equal opposites, and are pretty well balanced. The choice depends on piloting style and situation.
