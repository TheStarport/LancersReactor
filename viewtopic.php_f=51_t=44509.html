Puke Barwalker

Tue Oct 10, 2006 4:23 am by Puke Barwalker

A few basic hints to get you started:

1. You don't have to race back as soon as Juni calls you for a mission. She will wait, and you can do some exploring and making money. She won't go away.

2. Look for wrecks when you are flying. They will show on the map as a red x. You can make a lot of money and get good equipment.

3. Try mining, even it is only for scrap metal. What you make mining is free money!

4. Try to get the best shield you can, whenever you can.

Now have fun.
