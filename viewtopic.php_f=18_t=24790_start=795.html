Lethal Dosage

Sun Mar 27, 2005 2:18 am by Lethal Dosage

Grenade_Magnet - I havn't really looked at missions, so this is a complete guess, but have you checked the max level of missions in the game? For example, the maximum level of AI difficulty is 19, so there might not be a level 25 mission in the game - It could be that, something like 5 in the .ini's could turn out to be a level 20 mission in the game, etc.
Although, better someone who actually knows what they're talking about tries to work on your problem.

Now - Paw. You said you have removed some gates from New York - Have you also removed the recieving gates in the joined systems? Like If you removed the Cali gate, did you remove the NY gate in Cali too? I'm not sure If that would cause a crash unless you tried to jump between them, but you could always try.

Otherwise, run FlServer (with your mod running of course), and open the view toolbar, and open the console. Then start up FL, and try to log in/make a new character/launch from manhatten. Whenever the crash happens, quickly hit alt-tab and look at the console. Alot of the time an error message will appear in there. if It's not obvious, post it here. If theres nothing, then close FL and the server, and run FlSpit (download it from this site). Mot likely there will be errors in there (there always is, even for vanilla FL). Post anything peculiar in there here and then we can (try to) work out whats up.

Alternatively, you could add in mpnewcharacter.fl in your mod and change the starting location to somewhere else, and see if the crash still happens. If not, It's probably Li01.ini thats messed up (check the new jumpgate entry, etc.).

Also, you could just remove all the files from your mod, and add them back in one at a time until It crashes again, and then you'll know which file the problem is in.

If that doesnt help, then hell, your Fl is messed up or something lol.
