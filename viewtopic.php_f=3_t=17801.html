Best VHF weapon config (in general)

What do people find to be the best weapon config for a VHF? I'm not asking for specific guns, more strategies. The best setup I've found is:

Two level 10 guns
Two level 8 guns with 600m/s velocity and a higher rate of fire (to aid in tracking targets)
One pulse-type gun (to bring down enemy shields)
One unguided missile

This delivers a lot of firepower without draining energy too quickly. On a head-on pass against an enemy, their shields go down quickly. Fire the missile right before you pass each other and they can't avoid it--massive damage. I tried four level 10 guns and two missiles, but you can't control what type of missile you fire. And with only level 10 guns, you don't get the solid-spray-of-fire effect, since they all have a refire rate of 2.00.

What strategies do others use? More missiles? More lower-power-level guns? Only level 10 guns? What works for you?
