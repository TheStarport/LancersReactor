I know you posted in the spoiler forum, but trying not to spoil things too much for you (like telling you of higher-level cargo ships to wait for)...

Yeah, you'll want a ship with lots of cargo space. The Rhino is the only easy one to get in Liberty space; available on most of the planets including Los Angeles there in California.

To start trading, just buy a commodity somewhere and then go somewhere to sell it for more money. Simple really. A decent run to get you started from Los Angeles (without dipping into the world of smuggling and contraband) is to take Polymers from there to Planet Denver in Colorado for a profit of $160 per unit. Then buy H-Fuel at Denver and take it back to somewhere in the California system for a good profit as well.

Visit many bases and stations, get all their commodity prices into your neural net, and you'll be able to figure out good cargo runs yourself.
