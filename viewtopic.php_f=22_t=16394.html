NEW sever: Chicago 24-7 4 CPU, 4 Gig RAM, Datacenter Hosted.

Chicago 24 - 7 in now online. IP is 216.33.68.100 Server specs are:

Compaq 4 700 Mhz (1 Meg Cache) processor Xeon server.
Microsoft Advance Server 2000 OS
4 Gigs RAM.
Compaq Smartcache 32 SCSI controller with RAID 5
Hosted in a state-of-the-art Data Center.
Tested 9 mbs up/download.

All are welcome to email chicagofree247@yahoo.com for the password for playing. Please indicate your primary player name. Look for updates on the sever news DAILY. We hope to be able to remove the password requirment after the patch is released. Do not give away password to anyone. Currently, player limit set to 40. We might be able to raise the limit higher, but this depends upon what we see utilization wise. FL-Server does use multiprocessors, but how effcient remains to be seen. Please email chicagofree247@yahoo.com for password, cheaters and rule-breaker reports, comments, and ANY lag, which we do not expect unless cheaters are online or it's your connection. You can also post cheater reports and problems here.

Rules are:

No PvP in Liberty space, unless attacked, and not just accident or play.
No cheating or mods of any type.
No wreak camping or gate camping.
No sexual, racial, or foul language names.
Keep system chat free of foul language. Children may be playing, so be considerate.
Do not give or loan other players more than 10k per THEIR level in a 24 hour period. You have to earn your levels on this board.

Rules are subject to change, but probably not. Currenty, the server news does not have all the rules, but OBEY the above or have your account banned.

Rule breakers will be warned, have their account deleated, or out right banned depending on cirumstances except cheaters, who will always be banned.

Notes: We MAY be opening up all the gates and jumpholes soon, otherwise, probably no mods at this time, maybe mods latter. Server will be randomly restarted two too three times a week for respawning once the MP patch is released and applied. We are currently working on a backup soloution for accounts.

It is our hope that this server will be one of the fastest, most stable Freelancer server online for yours and our enjoyment. However, please remember that the admins for this server have personal lives and that real life (and I hope you have a life...) will sometimes keep us from responding quickly to issues. We will, however, try our best and most of the time, will address any issues within 24 hours if a lot sooner.
