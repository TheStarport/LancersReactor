Freelancer: The Undiscovered Nation

Section 1 Chapter 1 Part 1

March 17th 806 AS

Freeport 10 Tau 37 system 08:30h

“The Time is 8:30h, This is your Requested wake up time, The Time is 8:30h, This is your Requested wake up time”, Said the Alarm Clocks Voice. The time is.... “Oh Shut the **** up, I'm up” Replied Arron. As he forced him self out of bed. “What should I have for breakfast” Arron thought while he headed for the shower. The Quarters on space stations where small, A Bed, Dresser a few chairs, a small Kitchen which had a stove for cooking a sink, a small fridge and a small bathroom with a Toilet and Shower in i, Arron did not mind it... It was fine by him because it was better then living in Bertonia, and he also did not like the idea of “Living it big” so to speak.

Arron Sinclair was born on planet New London into a working class family. He had Black hair and Brown eyes and had a lanky posture. He would often dye his hair a different colour form time to time for fun. Both his parents where miners for BMM. He remembered his Mother and Father always talked about how corrupt the government and how cooperations had all the influence with in the government and the people had no voice, even though Bertonia was suppose to be a Democratic house, like the other houses. Which was a load Bull ****. Anyone would who would raise their voice against the corruption in government or the corporations would be disappeared and never heard from again. This was not just limited to Bertonia, All this houses were as corrupt as Bertonia. His parents were barely able to make ends meet because miners were worked long 12 hours a day and paid very little. When Arron was 14 his father was killed in a Mining accident on Stokes Station in Leeds. Arron was not stocked at this Miners very few miners live to die a natural death. When Arron was 16 he decided that there was nothing for him in Bertonia, His Future in Bertonia was to be an over worked and under paid worker, like his parents and his grandparents. The only people who had futures were the rich. So he decided he would leave Bertonia for the border worlds, With the hopes of becoming something. He remember learning about the Gas miners Guild in school, about there how they beat Rheinland during the 80 year war. So he Decided to do more Research them. He found out that Gas Miners were paid decent wages and being Gas Miner was a safe Job. But two things kept him always one was that the Sigma systems were very far away and it would cost him a fortune to get there. The second reason was that he remembered hearing in the news that Rheinland was building up their military along the Sigma systems and could “annex” the systems again. He did not want to end up being killed in a war over gas. He also thought of Joining the IMG. But IMG losses a lot of its miners to Pirates such as the Outcasts. His Mum told him that Outcasts make the Molly's and Gains look like Angels, She told him that the IMG Miners go nuts because of the Outcasts threat

One day Arron knew where he was going to go.. He was in a New London bar, A place were he should not have been in, and over a transport pilot talking about the factions called the Zoners. Which lead him to do some research. He found out that the Zoners were a neutral faction that traded with everyone Regardless of there legal Status. He also remember read that a lot of people become Zoners are sick of the life in the house systems and want something different in life. At this point he knew were he was going to go when he left Bertonia.


When he was 18 he left Bertonia for Freeport 6 in the Borders worlds and joined The Zoners. He learned being a Zoner was about accepting everyone who came onto the Station, Didn't not matter weather they where a Navy Hero or a Outcast Drug Runner who had killed hundreds of people in Cold Blood. He also found out that Zoners came from ever corner of the Sirius sector and every walk of life, Also a few of them where born Zoners and some of them had never set foot on a planet. What banded the most of the Zoners together was the corruption in the House system, they could not do anything about it., And did not want to hurt anyone if they tried to do something about it He started off as a worker in the Bio-Domes on the Station he also did some repairs here and there. He did not mind this Job, It was better then being a Miner like this mother and father and a lot safer and the pay was not that bad either. After a while he was able to get some lessons on how to fly a ship, It was ever Zoners Birth Right to be able to fly a ship.... After about 7 months of living on Freeport 6 one of the freighter pilots called in sick, and Arron was offered to be a temporary replacement for the Pilot, and Arron Jump at the chance to do something different. The Run was rather short and Safe(For Zoners), and did not require any escorts. It was running supply's to Freeport 10 in the Tau-37 systems. After the Run to FP 10, Arron sensors detected the wreck armored transport. It looked to be a Samura transport and was probably the handy work of the outcasts playing “Hide and go Seek” with Transports again, when he scanned it he was surprised to see that their was still some cargo in the ship. The Cargo was Diamonds, so Arron looted the wreck and took the Cargo back to FP6. He was able to get 200,000 Credits out of the deal, More then enough to buy his own freighter. After three years of running cargo between FP-6 and FP-10 and the odd run to Bertonia and Kusari, Arron decided to move to FP10. He thought it was the one of the most Beautiful systems in all of Sirius and it was very very far away from the house systems.

Which bring us to now




After he finished shower he got dressed in his usaly blackish clothing, and proceeded to the small kitchen in his Quarters. “I think I will have Synth Paste Supplement A7 today, with some coffee and an apple.” Arron Thought. As he was about to sit down to eat, his neural net Comm. Rang. He got up and proceed to the computer panel on the wall. It was flashing “Incoming Transmission”.. “Hmm, I never get phone calls, I wonder who it could be, the only person I get call from his Mum or it could be someone playing a joke on me again” He thought as he pressed the receive Transmission button. The “Incoming Transmission” Message was replaced by the helmet of an Outcast Pilot, He could make out the face of the pilot behind her helmet and it was Nora. “Hola, Arron, Guess what”, “Hey, Nora, What am I supposed me be guessing on this time” Arron Replayed. “Well I found something Interesting” Replayed Nora, “How Interesting” Arron said, “Lets say interesting enough for me not to talk about over an open channel, I will have to tell you about the Interesting thing when I get to Freeport 10, My Squadron will be there in about an Hour or so, So I will meet in you the bar when I get their, Okey?” Said Nora. “Yeah okey, I will meet you their, This must be quite interesting”.. “****ing is. C ya in an hour, Arron, Nora out”... “Hmmm That interesting” Arron thought... “Well I have an Hour to kill, till she gets here”
