Currently having a problem when creating new characters at my bases. I load up the mod and when I hover the mouse over, for example, my Mattheson Station Bartender it says "Jane Halcrow (correct), Fugatives (correct), Pirna Bartender (incorrect).
Anyone have any idea how this can be fixed?

Chips

Sun Nov 23, 2003 5:23 pm by Chips

yes - go into the mbases.ini file, and find your bartender. Next - punch in his ids name into the FLEDIDS and see what comes up, you will see:

Jane Halcrow/Fugatives/Pirna Bartender

NOW - YOU MUST FIND A FREE IDS NAME. I left in the newbies thread, the reference to which numbers weren't used by the game - so you can add it yourself.
Change that too:

Jane Halcrow/Fugatives/Losda del Carnagos bartender

The bars (you cannot really recreat them though) make it go onto the next line. Make sure you have your NEW ids name number inserted into the field, and then hit save as. Put your new ids name into the field, go find your bloke, and your sorted.
