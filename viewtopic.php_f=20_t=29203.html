Chips

Sat Mar 13, 2004 7:20 am by Chips

NO NO NO!!!!!!

Go to FLMM - then go to the tools option, now click on the "go to save game folder" and then once there delete all the save games!!

This is a very common problem, PLEASE do this and you will be working in seconds. Whilst all the other suggestions are good for problems involving missing files, or corrupt stuff, its just this that is causing the problem for you.

ALWAYS do this after using a mod to avoid crashes.

The reason is because you have a save game, or autosave that has YOU in a ship, or with weapons, that does not normally exist....hence the game has a fit and dies...
