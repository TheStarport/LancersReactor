Lightning Fanfic

I've never actually made a fanfic before, so if it is completly stupid please tell me and i'll try new ideas.

"we're here live at the Lightning Nebula as Santiago, Corsair expert pilot, is going to fly into the heart of the nebula and destroy the mysterious unknown faction that is stopping traders and other people going through the nebula."

Santiago knew that this was a suicide mission, but he had to try anyways. This is the only way he could get the promotion that could make him General. As he flew into the nebula he wondered: Should I do this? How much do I need this promotion?

The answer was simple: he needed it badly.

As Santiago lost sight of the base, he activated the secret Corsair anti-radiation sheilds that had newly been descovered. Since he knew no one was watching, it was safe to use these new sheilds. Soon he spotted unknown ships. Santiago used the stolen cloaking device to hide from them as they passed, and he immedietly recognised them. They were blue, and their ships were organic.

The aliens named Nomads.

He fired his Salamanca Mk2's at the aliens as he uncloaked, and the aliens died immediatly as a battleship uncloaked and began launching nomad fighters.

I don't KILL the House Factions. The House Factions just happan to fly into planets when they see me. Of course, nobody listens to me.

Santiago only just got out in time. He gasped to the dozens of reporters that the Nomads still had troops, and were massing in the nebula. Commander Yreal came, and gave him a promotion from Captain to Colonel. Santiago gasped his thanks and went to the bar to relax.

No such luck. People were swarming him with questions, and attempting to give him money for more information. Santiago pushed them away and just sat with his Sidewinder Fang. People were still swarming him with questions, but there were less people now. They don't know what it's like. They haven't been face to face with a Nomad fleet. They haden't even seen a Nomad before. Just people who wanted a person to look up to...

Santiago looked around as the base suddenly shook. He saw a glimpse of a Nomad fighter. Santiago ran to his ship, and attempted to launch. Sucsesful, he began shooting the Nomads to get them away from the Freeport. Almost dieing, he cloaked and ran to the jumphole due to the jumpgate being destroyed by Nomads. As he arrived and uncloaked, two (how could they!?!) Nomad battleships also uncloaked and blocked the entrance.
