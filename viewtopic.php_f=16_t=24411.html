:: WAYS TO MAKE QUICK AND EASY BUCKS! ::

At the beginning of the game when the hunt for Sean Ashcroft is on, proceed with orders from King and wait for "Ashcroft" at the mouth of the tradelane. Once you find a unidentified DSE carrying artefacts and start shooting at him, he'll start swearing about calling his lawyers and you just found out that he isn't Ashcroft. Once you got new waypoints to proceed on to Pueblo Station, kill the unidentified chatterbox and tractor his alien artefacts (x20) ( these can fetch 1,282 credits on Planet Manhattan or 270 credits on Battleship Missiouri )

The catch is that, once you leave the battleship and a naval officer scans your cargo, run like hell to manhattan and try to avoid contact or even sight with police or naval fellas to prevent them from hunting your a** and preventing you from docking in Manhattan. Once at Manhattan sell your artefacts and Kah Ching! Money Money Money very funny.
