Wash

Thu May 06, 2004 2:29 pm by Wash

While many of the characters seem like cliches, the writers have tried to give them more depth. For example, true-blue naval officer Walker ironically has to fight against the Liberty Navy for a higher cause. Trent, who doesn't believe in causes, is the opposite of Walker but also ends up giving his loyalty to The Order. Ozu comes and goes pretty fast in the story, but it is clear that he and Trent made a great team. Trent is somewhat in awe of Von Claussen, the famous ace. And Trent and Juni have this Tracy-Hepburn thing going on.

King, Hovis, Tobias, Sinclair, Quintaine, Hakkera, et al. are well played. Once I took too long to loot wrecks in Sigma-13 and Hakkera gave me mission failure, saying "you have dishonored yourself and The Order." I actually felt ashamed to let him down. The actors deserve a lot of credit.
