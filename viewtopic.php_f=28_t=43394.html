Just_For_Fun v3.0

I have submitted a new version of my Just_For_Fun mod and it should soon be available. Hwew is a summary of the changes:

VERSION 3.0 CHANGES:
Summary (see below for details):
a. Added new options and new choices in some existing ones.
b. Salvage is up, WAY UP.
c. 19 new weapons, and tweaks on others.
d. Ship tweaks. Also added 6 ship variants and one new ship.
e. New London now has 3 bases.
f. Some Dockable miners, both Gas and Asteroid
g. Permanent station Glorious.
h. You can now land on Sprague.
i. New dockable base in the Nomad Pink Unknown world.
j. The Firebase bases Sangley Point and Subic now have 2 bases.
k. Fixed a few bugs from version 2.0.
l. Benford, and Baxter stations now regenerate and are dockable.
m. Ithaca Research Station and Freeport 7 are now dockable
n. Space anomalies like the ones at Benford, and Baxter stations are now
hypergates.
o. Added a few wrecks.
p. Tripoli Shipyard and Thames Outpost now sell ships.
q. Did a bit of work on paths.

The response to the previous version has been good and people are still occasionally downloading it. I hope you also like my latest effort.
