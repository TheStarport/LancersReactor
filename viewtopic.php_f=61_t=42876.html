Not star spheres, but model components.
Either they've been deleted from Freelancer directory, renamed, or moved.
A fresh installation of Freelancer & FLMM may clear it up.
I got those kinds of errors after several dozens of runnings of large mods I've made.
For some reason, things get deleted, renamed, or moved, around after you've messed with a new mod a lot.

Watch your 6!
