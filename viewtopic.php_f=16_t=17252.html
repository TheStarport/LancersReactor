Mon May 19, 2003 4:23 am by Peachhead

I like taking 250 (a humpback) of artifacts from Leon on Omega-41 to Planet New Berlin, then picking up a load of light arms at The Ring (right outside planet New Berlin) back to Leon.

250 artifacts from Leon to New Berlin=206,000 profit/250 light arms from The Ring to Leon=245, 000 profit (451K profit per round trip)

Or if you don't mind extending it to Crete on Omicron Gamma it's:

250 Artifacts from Crete to New Berlin=212,500 profit/250 light arms from The Ring to Crete=262,500 profit. (475K profit per round trip)

But you have to go offroad with Artifacts in Rhineland so it's kind of a drag. But just come in from below Planet New Berlin and they never scan you. And you have to be friendly with the Corsairs for either route. It really helps to be friendly with the Red Hessians too.

(I go from Crete-> Omega 41->Omega 11->Dresden Jump Hole->New Berlin Jumphole->Planet New Berlin. Then I can use tradelanes in New Berlin/The Ring , Stuttgart and Omega 11 on the way back with light arms. With the added bonus that you can get a Titan on Leon or Crete after you're done.)
