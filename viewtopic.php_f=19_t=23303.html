this has several effects. First: it brings more fun and exitement to the game, both for us, as those high-levels who have nothing else to do than doing missions or keep running hte same trade-route over and over again. with 1-2 missions you will have earned even more money than you lost in the toll, and any decent pilot can do 2 "kill that guy" or "destroy that cargo" mission in like 4-5 minutes.
