Bejaymac

Sat Oct 01, 2005 12:27 pm by Bejaymac

A couple of pointers on trade lane traffic for you.

1} The factions in the tradelane need to be in a base on at least one end of the lane to appear, so if your bases are empty nobody will appear.

2} When adding factions to zones make sure that they have the ship class for that zone type or they will not appear. This is something that FLE has a problem with as you can add practically any faction to any encounter type with it.

If your still having problems mail me the "faction_props.ini","Li01.ini" and your florida system ini and I'll take a look at them
