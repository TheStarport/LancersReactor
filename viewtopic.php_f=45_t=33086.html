The Roughnecks

We are the Roughnecks. The clan has been around for several years in several different games but the RN FL division has been around since FL was released! We are a PvP clan.

We have a reputation to maintain and do our best to maintain it. Our player's skills are some of the best out there. This is in evidence insofar as the Boston SuperMod v1.0, currently installed on Boston, was envisioned, heavily programmed and thoroughly tested by all active RN members. Why? We crave a challenge!

RN has gone against many clans where in the end we matched or exceed their skills. We ally ourselves with some of the best clans out there which makes us a force to be reckoned with. We continue to train constantly and look for ways to improve comraderie and skills. We are ACTIVE!

Our application process is long and exhausting - for the members and applicants alike. We need to ensure clan loyalty is not just a couple of words in your vocabulary but an active, living attitude in everything you do in FL. We expect ruthlessness when needed and magnaminimity where required. We expect every member to excel in some skill, even if it isn't duelling. We are democratic in nature so all members have a voice and responsibility in RN's destiny so active participation is a MUST. We have a leader to enforce the clan's decisions and move operations quickly when needed. We have intestinal fortitude!

Currently we are only accepting applications for the most skilled players out there as RN FL membership is quite phat To find us come to our home server Boston Freelancer server.
