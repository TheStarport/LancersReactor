Good questions.

No this game will not break your wallet. And the system requirements can be found on the webpage. The main need is for a large graphic card that has 256Mb memory in order to play the game in all its glory.

It will run on a 1GHz system though with 512Mb RAM and a 128Mb graphic card. Just not in all its glory.

Also, a little self advertisment is in order I think

I also run a personal website, which can be found at www.ascending-pictures.com.

Why go there you ask yourself? Well, I am in the process of releasing the chapters of my first book, currently in the rewrite phase. I could use feedback from scifi addicts like yourselfs.

And any consistancies with stories running on "CURRENT" TV Series.. well, that is because this book was originally written like 10 years ago by a very young person.. me! This rewrite is actually due to something really cool that happened to me which obviously I do not want to mention until something solid comes out of it. Anyway, register yourself there (its free) and head to the article section. Currently 5 of the 22 chapters are online.

A note on the design of the book though. A book is a SEASON, a chapter is an EPISODE and an EPISODE is a closed short story. Each story is part of the Season and each season is part of the grand story.

2006 marks the release of Season 2
2007 marks the release of Season 3
2008 marks the release of Season 4
2009 marks the release of Season 5
2010 marks the release of Season 6
2011 marks the release of the final Season 7

Those dates will most likely shift though. I could write 2 seasons in one year, it all depends on the amount of time I get from work
