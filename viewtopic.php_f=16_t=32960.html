I think airport was asking about being piratical when starting from scratch in OpenSP. For me, that means replacing the Starpants you get at the start as soon as possible and not being red with the local criminal factions.

After looting the LPI storage containers a few times, I'd sell all my equipment (and I mean every single thing) at Detroit Muntions and buy firearms, sell them at Montezuma Base (Cortez), buy Cardamine, sell that at P. Manhatten. This leaves you neutral with the criminals and with enough money to buy a Dagger at Buffalo Base.

You're behaving like a criminal right from the start and are quickly in a position to get on with the hijacking, pillaging, looting, etc.
