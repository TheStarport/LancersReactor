I've been messing around in Freelancer using TSearch, to try and find the address that forces you to quit once you reach level 2. Here is what I have found; it appears that once you reach the desired wealth, right before the routine that would advance your level the process throws you to that demo ended screen, then you are booted once any input is given. Thus you never actually reach level 2, because tecnically the DMA that holds the players level information is never increased. (I don't know if that is correct, it's just a theory that I thought of by watching the addresses change.)

I'm no programmer, and don't no much about this stuff; so I'm thinking that it would be possible to block (nop?) the instruction that sends you to the demo ended screen, thus the game would continue as normal.

What do you all think, the only reason I am interested in this is my desire to fly the other 3 ships that you have to be above level 1 to use. So it's either stop the auto-logout or reduce the ship's levels.
