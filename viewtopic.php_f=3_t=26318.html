Docking Computers

Anyone remember playing Elite?

If you do, you'll remember the most heinous of all features, the one which made veterans sweat and the greens cower in fear. Was it the numerous pirates? Was it the off chance of getting sucked to another dimension and having to fight aliens, with no missiles and only mining lasers? No... it was the dreaded MANUAL DOCKING!!! It was because of this, that players saved for nothing other than a docking computer and the criminally extortionate rent-a-dock was a godsend.

My point?
