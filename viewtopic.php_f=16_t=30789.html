At the beginning, when they're coming for you head on, hit your afterburners while firing at your target. This gets you out of their direct line of fire and breaks up their formation right away. Concentrate on 1 target at a time. Make good use of your afterburners during combat. Don't be afraid to break if your shields get too low.
I've tried the storyline with every fighter in the game. I made it to mission 9 in a Patriot before getting killed. I've been playing since last May, so don't let that get you down. My point is that the Patriot is a very capable ship.
