RuneScape

OK, as some of you know, I have just recently disappeared for about three days. That is because I have been playing this game called RuneScape. It is a MMO(Massively Multiplayer Online for those of you who don't know) that runs from your internet browser. No downloading or software required. All you need is a mouse and arrow keys. The arrow keys control your camera(yes this game is in 3D) and the mouse controls everything else.

This game takes place in mid-evil times, but sort of like Freelancer, you can choose your own path. In freelancer, all you can basically do is choose to follow the story or not. But with RuneScape, everything is an option. The graphics are horrible, but this greatly reduces lag. You create your character from the beginning, and here is the best part about this game.


THERE IS NO SUBSCRIPTION REQUIRED!! You just create an account with a user name and password, and that's it. This game is incredibly addictive(any game that can keep me away from TLR for three days is a **** good game. especially for the internet.)and fun. It just never ends.
