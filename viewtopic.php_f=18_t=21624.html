Mon Jul 21, 2003 3:44 pm by Wanderer

To get missile weapons with fixed firing angles you need the following 3 items:
1: A fixed hardpoint preferably inside the ship pointing where you want the missiles to go.

2: Rename the missile DA archetype to this:
DA_archetype = equipment\models\turret\li_cruiser_gun01.cmp
material_library = equipment\models\li_turret.mat

3: Change the force_gun ori from false to this: force_gun_ori = true

That should do the trick.
