Thats the only thing I can think of that is moving fast enough to produce a high pitch squeal.

Try disabling your case fans one by one by unplugging them, see if it makes a difference. I know in the past I had some cheapo case fans and the bearings went, sounded like a cat was being tortured inside my case. replace the offending fans, total cost $16, all quiet again .

Hopefully you system isn't shutting down, ie not processor fan = badness.
