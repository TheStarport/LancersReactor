endor

Thu Feb 27, 2003 7:53 pm by endor

In one of the Tau systems, there's a rebel base owned by a guy named Krass or Krazz or something like that. I think he's associated with the Outlaws or some other rebel group. You run across him in the single-player storyline missions. Anyways, his base sells Cardamine for very cheap, something like $100-$250 per unit. You can resell it on Manhattan for $1500. I was at one of those "level up" points in the SP campaign, so I tried to make a drug run from Tau to Manhattan, going through the as-of-then unexplored "asian region". I made it to Manhattan while being attacked by the Liberty police/navy, but unfortunately I was hostile to the whole planet, so they refused to let me land. I had to fly to the Magellan sector and land at the Lane Hacker base, which was the next-best selling point, but at only $800 per unit. I still made a good amount of money, but nowhere near what I was expecting. It might be better in Multi-player, as long as you're neutral to the Liberty system.
