Sapper_Park

Thu Mar 20, 2003 4:19 am by Sapper_Park

I found that since they don't have shields, it doesn't matter what kind of weapon you use on them. The greater the hull damage, that's the only thing that counts. Plus, they're a really frisky type of flyer, so I would recommend something with a high velocity (699+).

It's possible to do with 600 and even 500, but those weapons also have a lower rate of fire so you hit them less frequently due to the rate of fire and their ability to dodge some projectiles.

It's all about balance. If you can hit them really hard in a couple shots, then that's all you need. If you can take on the Xenos in their LFs (in Colorado space) like they're nothing, then you'll be able to handle the nomads. Difference is that their guns will kill you.
