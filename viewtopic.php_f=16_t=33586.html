Trade routes

I'm putting this up as a general thread to collect all the good trade routes used by people on this forum, both as a matter of interest as to what other gamers are doing and as a resource for those who are having trouble finding good trade routes.

A good trade route is not just one which is profitable in one direction, there must be some sort of profitable trade to be made on the return route. Sometimes it might be necessary to sell at one planet and buy at another that is either near to the planet you just sold at, or on the route back to the other trading planet.

Sometimes trade routes will be triangular. e.g.
Planet A: Sell commodity 3, buy commodity 1
Planet B: Sell commodity 1, buy commodity 2
Planet C: Sell commodity 2, buy commodity 3

The number of nodes is not necessarily limited, I'd love to see 4, 5 or even 6 node trade routes, with a good profit to be made at each node.

Also, it would be good to include information as to which factions you need to be friendly with in order to dock at each planet/base and what pirate attacks (SP) you're likely to face. It shouldn't be necessary to give positions of jump holes used, as it will be up to each individual player to find those for themselves.

The idea of this thread is to find the most interesting, most profitable, shortest, least (and most) risky trade routes.

Anyone who is inclined to respond to a posted route with: "Everyone knows that one, what a n00b" please consider yourself ignored in advance.

This is a very long route, but still quite profitable(700,000+ per run). I also like doing it because I'm on good terms with most Liberty and Kusari factions.

Buy Engine Components from New Tokyo

Sell them at Freeport 4 in Magellan. Buy Food Rations.

On your way back to Kusari stop at Pueblo Station in Colorado, sell the Rations, and stock up on Gate/Lane Parts.

Sell the Parts at Chugoku Gate Construction Site in Hokkaido.

Stop at Sapporo Station on your way out of Hokkaido and buy H-fuel.

Go back to New Tokyo and sell the fuel.

Rinse and Repeat. The Food Rations to Pueblo and H-fuel to New Tokyo trips are there for a few extra bucks. Most of the profit comes out of the Engine Comp. to FP4 and G/L Parts to Chugoku Gate.
