Anyway the plot and the rules:

What if the turf war with the Junkers and Hogosha got out of hand?

One day the Hogosha send out 20 CSV to go and make a base in the Sigma 13 when they stumble on the base that the Junkers had made there. A might strugle occured and the Hogosha end up destroying the Junker Base. With the base destroyed the Junkers seeking revenge. Killed numerous Hogosha leaders.
And thus a war with the scrap collect factions has begun.
