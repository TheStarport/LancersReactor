Mon Oct 27, 2003 3:10 pm by NukeIt

Quick cash is readily available in several systems...it is very helpful to swing past the border worlds between missions- you can often pick up loot from the wrecks that can be sold for high prices- the codename class 10 weapons, in particular, net you 30k apiece when sold. My favorite trick is to stop by Omicron Alpha and loot that system's Viking wreck just as I enter Kusari space in the story missions, since it is a mere 3 jumps away from Kyushu(taking the Tau-31 route). Since I started doing that, I've never had cash problems later in the game.
