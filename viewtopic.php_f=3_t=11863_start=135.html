Just started posting here, this looks like a decent place to make an intro

34 going on 35, got started on an off-brand Pong console around '78 or so. Then some text-based games on the TRS-80. Had a brief stint with the Coleco ADAM computer, it caught itself on fire (!), went to the C64. I never played Elite, I was more into the Temple of Apshai type games back then.

Got into the PC gaming world in '92 or so, got hooked on space sims thanks to Wing Commander. Became a major Privateer junkie, but was disappointed with Priv 2... I had been anticipating the arrival of Freelancer for a LONG time, but I allowed it to fade to the back of my mind, and didn't realize it was finally out until a little while ago. So I'm a little behind the curve...

But I think this game is pretty great - it's not perfect, I'd like to be able to take cargo missions a la Priv, fly some perishables through a Xeno blockade or something like that. On-call missions like the ones from Priv 2 would also be nice, one thing I *did* like in that game.

I'm taking my sweet time with the game, concentrating on exploration and faction dynamics, I'll get to the uber weaps and Edge World ships later. Right now my Hammerhead is serving me well.

Ever get the feeling... you're standing on plastic?
