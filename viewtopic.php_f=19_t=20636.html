The easiest way i have found of backing up a Freelancer MP account, involves entering the registary, Start/run/type "regedit" go to HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Microsoft Games\Freelancer\1.0 highlight the install key then go to the menu File/export save it as a name you will remember. Then once you reinstall Freelancer before or even after making a new MP key, just double click this file you exported and all will be as it should be. REMEMBER TO ALWAYS BACK UP THE REGISTARY BEFORE EDITING IT IN ANY WAY.
Saving this file to a cd or floppy is an easy, sure fire way of getting and keeping your MP account correctly.
