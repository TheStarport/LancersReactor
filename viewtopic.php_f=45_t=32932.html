Kyrrum Röddum - ARGamers Faction

Welcome to the Kyrrum Röddum.

The first player run faction of the ARGamers Roleplaying server.

Here I shall lay out rules, regulations and some of the Kyrrum's work. The name means translated from Icelandic, Stilled Voices.

First the RULES

1) No infighting outside of practices and tournaments.

2) Respect newbies, and people above you in the Kyrrum

3) NO CARDAMINE

4) Protect those who hire us. Waste those who attack them.

5) Protect OUR territory.

6) You need to be on the "Pilgrimage"

ROLEPLAY Essentials

1) Always have in your cargo hold, 2 water, 2 oxygen and 1 food ration. Or you will be considered near death and escorted none too gently to the nearest base to procure these essentails.

2) ALL Cardamine will be considered for personal use. Any member found with it must destroy it immediatly or be destroyed.

3) Attend the "Pilgramage"

You are allowed the following classes of ship

Civilian
Pirate
Borderworld (NO TITANS)
Independant World

Any loadout is acceptable BUT No Nomad weaponry and No Class 10's should be used(Shields are ok )
