@ hoistimus
I've found that if you plan on editing the character, you need to play the character a few minutes and buy items for it. What I mean is that if you plan on changing weapons and shields (or what ever), you need to fill ALL of the slots in game prior to editing the character. Otherwise, those slots will not be available when you attempt to edit. I also discovered through trial and error that some engines and powerplants do not work on other ships and can even crash the game when you attempt to load the character. Or, you can load a character, and not be able to launch, fly or shoot weapons because the game dropped the incompatible item. If you plan on using the aromored transport, I suggest making sure the initial items like engine and powerplant are compatible- in fact just choose the items that should be assigned to it. I hope that helps...I also new to this, and this is what I have discovered so far. It's really a lot of fun figuring this out.
As to the original topic I posted for... The prometheus ship makes a rockin' admin/ server police ship. It is very unique and identifiable, and it doesn't really require a lot of editing... it kicks trash right out of the box.
