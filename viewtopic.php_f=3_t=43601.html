FL Movie Project

Hey Everyone,

I havn't posted on here for ages, but i still had my old account name and password saved.
Anyway, the reason i'm back on is to open up a new discussion about a FL Film i am currently producing for my University course. As it's a pretty basic Foundation course (which i'm only on cus i got rejected from the full Film BA Degree last year - which with any luck i should be on this year ) i've been limited to 10minutes in length.
For the storyline of the film, i chose to tackle some of the events that take place while Trent is onboard Freeport 7, including the Nomad attack and Trent's escape. I chose this part of the story, because its not really covered in the game (apart from exterior shots of the Nomad attack during the game's opening) and would allow me to basically come up with new plot elements.

I'd appreciate any feedback and comments from anyone.

Thanks a lot
AJ

Well, in the Neural Net Log on the game, i think it says that Trent is carrying Boron to sell to Lonnigan, but i changed this to Diamonds, mainly because figured that Diamonds might be a bit more interesting. I have given Trent a basic freighter (i have based the design loosely on the Freighter Trains), but at this point i havn't really put anything resembling armaments onto it, so i'll mayb have to give that a think.

Unfortunatly, much as i would like to include all of the details of what happened on Freeport 7, due to my time-limit, i've had to cut out all but the most basic. At the moment, i have just over a week left until my deadline, so things are almost in a state of completion. Hopefully when i'm done, i'll be able to upload a low(ish) res version of the finished piece for download, which would be cool.
