Fri Nov 14, 2003 12:28 am by Laserblast

Go to the mod folder and open equipment/market_ships and it will list all the bases/planets and ships sold at them (just do not edit it). Li is liberty, Ku is kusari, etc. This will give you a general idea where it is. If you are not certain what base is what, look up the original market_ships and you can determine what some of the planets are by what ships they sell. With this knowledge you can deduce the system at least.
Or, if you don't want to go through the process that some call boring, you can wait for someone to post an answer (datastorm might work also).


p.s. Btw, if I got the name of the folders wrong, it is because my computer has died and I have a hard time remembering names.
