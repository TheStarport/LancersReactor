The Dog Fight Mod Details

THE DOG FIGHT MOD STORY
It all started a little over a hundred years ago with the appearance of the alien race the Kon. The Kon have been major traders for aeons and have been trading with the Dom Kavosh for longer than man existed.
With the increase in the number of alien artefacts being activated, the Kon had detected their energy traces; they sent an envoy to find out what was causing this. Discovering that humans had repopulated the system they opened trade negotiations, supplying the locations of nearby systems and helping with the construction of the jump gates to enable us to get to their newly constructed supper trade system. As unlike them we don’t have worm drive engines and need “Primitive forms of assisted travel” as the Kon called the jump gates.
They guard the only access to the universe beyond systems and control every thing to and from this vast group of systems. Though there are rumours of its location to be brought and some say you can work out where it is from these. They also control what technologies get through to us and the prices.
There is apparently a good trade to be had in humans and human body parts to feed certain races. Is this why the prison population has dropped so dramatically in the last fifty years? There are places that pay exceptional amounts for both live and dead humans or even bits of them. Many a rich man claims to be that way because of knowing a prison warder, but it’s a dangerous trip and just as many don’t make it back.
There is a massive supply route in place supplying our goods and bringing theirs back, which of course has been a wind fall for the pirates and because of this you have to make sure your rep is right before even attempting to enter the Kon system as they kill on sight any who are slightly unfriendly. Because of this the pirates lay in wait in the outer systems hunting in packs. Only the ace pirates get to go there as the pickings are plentiful but so are the risks. The Liberty Navy insists that none of the pirates get past their check point and there is no other entrance to what has now become known as the death zone. One source even stated that the transports were smuggling the pirates and their ships in, but the large numbers found there make this implausible.
With the good always comes the bad. Another race has set up in the Kon system, also known as the Konflict system because of all the fighting there. The new race the Ad Min is even older than the Kon with technologies we can only dream of, to us they are indestructible. They take pilots and infect them with a parasite which gives them full control over the pilots, who are then sent out on patrol for the duration of their calculated decaying life span, never eating again and breathing a liquid made up of a small amount of added sustenance (acquired from those they kill) and the pilots own recycled body waste. These pilots are called the DEATH, steer clear as you will die no one other than time has killed one and their time is short and your body will extend their life.
It is said that they even consume their own unused limbs (their ships are controlled by thought) to survive longer in the ever vain hope of rescue, the parasites playing with their tortured minds.
The Ad Min have declared war on a small but universally hated clan called the CHEATERS and these are the preferred pilots to receive the control parasite. The Ad Min have the ability to sniff out the Cheaters and so called F1 users over several systems then the dreaded Blood Red Ad Min ships appear. Though the cheaters and F1 users run they can not hide and do not survive long never to be seen again. YOU HAVE BEEN WARNED THIS IS THE ONLY WARNING YOU WILL GET.
It is also said that the universe beyond, as it’s become known, is full of even more horrors of which we can not comprehend. The few pilots who have managed to return from the universe beyond ended their lives whimpering in corners of mental institutions, either from horror or sheer wonderment of what they saw, but never quite being believed.
The Fugitives are the only group to get any real sort of understanding with the Kon but true to all pirates they are unpredictable and can attack anyone with out warning, friend or foe.
There is another race starting to appear only a few sighting’s have been reported but it seems they are connected to the nomads some how, who them selves are being encountered more. The prophets of doom as well as the well known God Squad are warning of a return of the Dom Kavosh, will they return to reclaim their system.

So Says The Book Of Tank.

What’s in the Mod

SHIPS:
Seeing is believing and you still won’t 41 of Baene’s ships.
Visit Baene’s forums to see the ships he makes. www.gamer-forums.com/vision3d/index.php
WEPS:
Baene has worked hard on these as well they all fit in with the mod as it was intended. There are no mega-torps or missiles!! Pilot skill is required to fight it out with guns. If you want to have two second battles and make mega-money in no time then this mod is not for you. Well you can make mega-money, but ships are expensive and the weapons are a lot as well.
COMMODITIES:
At this time there are a limited number of new commodities as this is a major job on its own. What I plan to do is sort them out in an interim release prior to stage 2.
There are places where you can collect goods and lots of them if you're into trading, but don’t expect to have an easy time! You will need to be able to fight.
There are no transports etc but there are freighter fighters that can carry a good amount of cargo, so don’t expect traders to be an easy target.
NPC’s:
Npc's have regen shields, use nanos and batts. They also have better ships.
SYSTEMS:
A couple of standard systems have been slightly modified and there are four new ones. There are five factions that were in standard FL but either not used or only partially used, these are now fully functional, with their own bribes, ships, bases, missions, rep, npc’s etc.
There are four new factions all are fully functional, with their own bribes, ships, bases, missions, rep, npc’s etc. Except for the Admin which has its own fleet of npc’s to guard the Admin bases. As you would gather from the name these are for server admin only.
There are 24 fully functioning new bases, all with rumours (still being added to for stage two), bribes, missions, commodities, ships, weps, new characters (have a drink in the bars with well known FL community people). Two more new bases with ships, weps, commodities but no rumours or missions yet as this system ties in with stage two, when they will be added.
All new and edited systems are fully populated with their own factions and have Trade lanes, patrol routes, encounters etc.

Important Information...
The mod is supplied in an exe file format that will install the mod into the Freelancer Mod Manager.
By default the Mod Manager/mods folder is located at:
C:\Program Files\Freelancer Mod Manager/mods if yours is else where then browse to it. After installation you can start the Mod Manager and activate the -
Dog Fight Mod ver7 issue1

Tip of the day...When a Mod such as this one changes the Version number you may have problems connecting... It's your Firewall. You should be able to manually configure your Firewall software or another way is to remove freelancer from the firewalls program list, so when you restart freelancer it will ask premission to connect it. So if Freelancer freezes when you try to connect to Internet in Multiplayer you'll know what the problem is...

Dog Fight Mod Stage 2 Release

It being some 100 years since the Kon’s first contacted the humans; their advanced technology is slowly filtering through. First there was the expensive but O so worth it freighter fighters with the new alloy hull’s and improved engines, followed swiftly by the fighters. Now some of the old favourites have been rebuilt using the new technology.

But with the good there is always bad, the increased survive ability of trading with the large cargo holds of the freighter fighters, has seen an increase in the sale of prisoners. While this trade is not legal none will put a stop to it as it is resolving the overcrowding problems in prisons.

The law enforcement factions are not having it easy as every one has better equipped and more agile ships. This is why there is an increase in the amount of missions being offered to Freelancers, the increased pay out’s reflecting the increased danger.

As the routs to new systems slowly gets out to the general populous, there is increasing contact and conflict with new races. The effect of this being more and more groups forming their own factions. The most notable at the moment is the GS Clan. Being of a religiously fanatical nature, and some say they have mystical powers they are now a force to be reckoned with. Having developed their own ships and employed the best robot pilots for patrols and general security, they successfully took control of a system. For those who are able to pay the passage rates, using this system as a short cut saves time and expense for trading.
