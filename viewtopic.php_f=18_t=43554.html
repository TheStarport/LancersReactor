it sounds like an encounters reference is missing it's second parameter, although I've never seen the game spit that out!

It could be the headers:
[EncounterParameters
nickname = miningp_trade_transport
filename = missions\encounters\miningp_trade_transport.ini

Maybe no filename is specified?

OR - and my first thought, was
encounter = tradelane_trade_transport, 1, 0.170000
faction = co_hsp_grp, 0.170000

The second parameter would be the 1, the level of the npc to appear (the first being the encounter it is, the second their level, and the third their percentage chance).

You may be able to search for:
, ,

in the file to locate the error, but i'd just sift through the changes you've added and see if you can locate it that way.
