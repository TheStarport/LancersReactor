Mon Oct 20, 2003 12:57 pm by Ijuin

The key to using mines is to know when to launch them. If you just go dumping them out of your rear as you fight, you're not going to hit anything. There are three times when using mines pays:

1.) When shooting a capital ship, train, or other big, slow target, especially if you are flying a ship that does not carry torps.

2.) When an enemy is tailing you.

3.) When you make a close pass of an enemy, you can dump one right on top of him.
