I think you got your B (bytes) and b(bits) mixed up. The fastest is OC-48 at around 2.5 Giga-bits per second.

OC-48's theoretical bandwidth is 2.48Gbps, which makes it about 46000 Times Faster than 56k

Math

2.48Gbps *1024 Mbps/Gbps= 2539.52Mbps *1024 kbps/mbps=

2GBps (OC-48)= 2600468.48kbps
56kbps (dial up)= 56kbps

2600468.48kbps/56kbps= 46436.94

DSL opperating at lets say 786kbps is almost 15 times quicker than dial up.
Cable opperating at lets say 1.5 mbps, or 1536kbps, is about 28 times quicker.

You would have to go 2800kbps to be 50 times faster than dial up, 2.7mbps.
