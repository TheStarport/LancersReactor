I own the Logitec Extreme 3D USB version, and I have used it with Windows 2000 and later Windows XP. The drivers that came with it worked out of the box and it is very durable. I find that with a one gig athalon and 256 Mb RAM the response is excellent, and it is as responsive as my favorite all time stick, the standard CH Thrustmaster using the standard joystick port. It has six buttons, two axes of movement, a throttle and a hat switch. I find it great for my large hands. I tend to break weak sticks. and the Ex-3D has stood up to my rough handling.

* For the record, I am very hard on sticks. I would break that Saitec in no time. I tried it in the store, all plastic, all weak as crap.
