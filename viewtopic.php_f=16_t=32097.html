Arania

Sat Jun 12, 2004 10:45 pm by Arania

jump holes arent unstable, if anything, they are MORE stable than jump gates.

think about it: a Jump gate requires a MASSIVE amount of energy, channeled through a HUGE structure, to generate a SMALL and TEMPORARY route to another system relatively CLOSE to where the gate is.

Jump holes, on the other hand, are SMALL, require NO outside energy support, are PERMANENT, and have the potential to cover HUGE distances much easier than gates (look at the Omicron Theta to Omicron Gemma hole, one side of sirius to the other).
Plus, Holes dont have those annoying defensive turrets shooting at you when your waiting to dock, jump holes dont even have qeues, because they can be entered from any side, gates, on the other hand, can only by docked from ONE side.

plus the texas incident, that was a GATE, i personally dont use gates unless its the fastest route, usually holes present a much more appealing journey
