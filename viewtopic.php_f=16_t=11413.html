Yes. After the story is finished, the number shown where it states what is needed for the next level is how much more you have to get. Say you're at 100,000 credits. The next level box says '100,000'. Accumulate 50,000 and you'll see the box number shrinks to 50,000. Get another 50,000 (50,000 + 50,000 = 100,000 don't ya know) and you'll level up. A new figure will appear in the box, and the credits required will drop as you earn more.
