Earendil

Tue Feb 24, 2004 10:32 am by Earendil

If you don't mind someone actually being able to do ANYTHING to your server, you could enable M$ Remote Connection software. This is the easiest and fastest but less secure way to allow someone to remote admin your server. This allows them to do anything you could do sitting in front of the 'puter through the Internet from anywhere in the world. The only thing I heard you can't do is bring up Task Manager remotely. Requires you to open port 3389 in your firewall (there is a selection for the Remote Connection in WinXP's firewall) same as you would open the one port needed for flserver. You also need a account on the server. Would HEARTILY suggest you limit account amounts and password each one.

Otherwise only Fiik's FLAdmin program has flserver remote admin capabilities.
