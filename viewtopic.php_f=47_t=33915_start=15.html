Name: Pedro Villanueva
Ship: Titan
Faction: Corsairs
Location: Omega-41
Credits: 825,000

Pedro sat in his quarters aboard Leon Base, looking at a datapad he held in his hands.

"Hmm... I've got Salamanca Mk. I's on my Titan, and I can get........3 Mk. II's without overloading my reactor..."

He was startled by an announcement over the base's comm system.

"ALL CORSAIRS, REPORT TO THE HANGAR BAY. REPEAT, ALL CORSAIRS ARE TO REPEAT TO THE HANGAR BAY."

Pedro put down his datapad and rushed off to the hangar bay. As he arrived, he saw that it was packed with over 100 corsairs. The commander of the base was addressing them.

"Attention, Corsairs. We have signed a temporary treaty with the Outcasts-"

This caused an outburst of angry shouts from the crowd. The commander continued nonetheless.

"A deadly illness has been sweeping the House systems; their militaries and police have all been weakened. Both the Corsairs and the Outcasts see this as an opportunity to launch attacks, and perhaps gain more power in the Sirius systems. We will band together and develop joint weapons, joint ships, and we will perform joint attacks. We will start with a pincer attack on Bretonia; Outcasts will go through the Tau systems and we will go through the Omega systems. Their police and militaries are already spread so thin, we think we will be able to take the outer Bretonia systems with few casualties. The Corsairs operating in Bretonia will press towards New London, causing the Bretonians to divert their defenses there. Corsairs stationed in Omega-5 will move into Cambridge and take it; while Outcasts from Tau 23 will move into Leeds. This is a list of all Corsairs who will be immediately sent on sorties into Bretonia..."

The commander started to read a long list of names. Pedro contemplated. If they could pull this off, the Outcasts and Corsairs would have a large amount of territory...

"...Pedro Villanueva......"

Pedro sighed. Of course he would be sent on a sortie, he was one of the best pilots in Leon.

Twenty minutes later, he found himself in space along with a huge fleet of over 25 Titans and Centurions. They all moved to the Omega-11 jump hole.

Pedro was puzzled as to why they didn't go directly to Omega-5; he hailed the group commander. The response he got made him smile; he prepped for combat....

"With a fleet this size, we're a sizable force for even a base to defend against.... We're going to show to Hessians in Omega 5 how powerful the Corsairs can be."

With all ships through the jump hole, they set a course for Freital Base and prepared for combat...

Name: Serg Vorbarra
Ship: Valkrye
Credits: 105,000
Faction: Rheinland Military
"Yeee-ahh!" screamed Jurgen over the comm-link as the last Corsair was sent into a fiery death spin. They had been hunting the Corsairs that had began to trickle in from Omega 5. They must of finished off the Hessians in Omega 5 because Freital had been all but overrun. Serg's display showed no hostiles except some Hessians limping away but still he craned his neck around the cockpit. A massive fleet of Valkryes, supported by Gunboats floated outside in formation. "Okay boys," came the Commander's raspy voice over the comm-link. "Time to head home." A series of faint whoops and cheers flooded the comm-links as the fleet turned around and headed back to Stuttgart.

--One day later--

Serg and Jurgen strolled down Stuttgart's cobbled avenues glancing around the shop windows and street cafés. They clanked everytime they moved because as Rheinland was at war, normal military fatigues would not do. Futuristic german helmets sat atop their heads and they had Daumann Perforaters tucked in to their brown leather holsters. Utility belts and ammo pouches covered them and a Detroit Landblaster pulse assault rifle slung over their backs.

They walked out onto an alpine promenade and looked down into a gentle rolling valley. It used to be pretty, until Synth Foods came and covered it with their cellulose grey weed. Two pine trees swayed as a chilly mountain breeze swept through one of Stuttgart's many alpine towns. The capital city of Stuttgart was a gray/blue blur on the horizon. "You know that the Outcasts are hitting Bretonia pretty hard" said Jurgen suddenly. Serg looked up. "Don't worry, we have the most poweful Military in the Sirius...they can't get us...Although I would like to think the other houses could defend themselves against some inbred druggies." he finished off. Jurgen laughed. "Yeah...Damn Outcasts.." All of a sudden there was a rucus at a nearby bar. The publican, a portly man with a thick flowing blond moustache came running out wheezing. "Corsair spies!!! I overheard them talking and now they trash my bar!" Serg's eyes narrowed. No matter how strict the border guards were, some pirate spies always managed to slip in somehow with the rough traffic heading in from the Border worlds. As they kicked open the ornately carved wooden doors Serg spotted them. Bronzed skin and latino features. They both had Salamanca pulse pistols drawn and were helping themselves to a drink. One of them held a struggling Policeman in a headlock. Serg grabbed his Daumann Perforator and fired off several shots off. The pirate fell writhing to the ground. The other glanced at his fallen comrade and let go of the young policeman who fell heavily to the ground. The pirate took off towards the back door and towards the village's local landing pad. "Go after him!" yelled Serg and Jurgen sprinted after him. Serg glanced at the pirate who was moaning on the floor. This was no mere pirate informer. Judging by the condition and the scars on his body this was a for real Corsair. Serg hauled him up on the bench and went to help the policeman.

Meanwhile the other pirate was sprinting down the concourse to his most likely stolen Hawk. He pushed all the other passers-bys on to the ground and this made things only easier for Jurgen. He unslung his Detroit Landblaster and fired three times. Three purplish blue bolts burnt massive holes through the Corsair and he was carried by them off the cliff that the landing pad was perched on.

Back at the bar, Serg had just got the shaken policeman to his feet when the Corsair sprung. He had regained conciousness and swung down on Serg. Serg winced with pain but reacted by sending his elbow into the Corsair's nose and swinging around with a mother of a punch. The Corsair was immediatly out cold and fell back into the bar and knocked over the drink tubes sending them shattering and liquid spewed everywhere. Serg rubbed his back ruefully and the Policeman gratefully thanked him and said he could take things from here. Serg walked outside into cobbled streets again and Jurgen joined him. "We fight 'em in the air and we fight 'em on the ground." he said wearily. Serg chuckled. "Yeah but we sure gave those guys a physical beating they're not likely to forget." Jurgen thought for a moment. "Two less Corsairs in the Sirius we have to deal with...Heh, I'll make sure they get to Vierlande so there can be two more prisoner's *****es." Serg shook his head and laughed. "Sounds like an ideal punishment....Let's head back to the Freidrich." They took a personal shuttle back to the capital city on Stuttgart where a gunboat was waiting to take them and the other crew on leave back to the Freidrich, currently in orbit around Baden Baden

"Good Lord Horatio! I do believe that is an extra-terrestrial space craft!"
