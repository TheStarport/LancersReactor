Circumcisor

Thu May 29, 2003 11:58 pm by Circumcisor

To change cruise speed
Go to constants.ini and under [EngineEquipConsts add the line CRUISING_SPEED = xxx.
To change engine speed, find the name of the engine the ship is using and change the engine_equip.ini

eg
[Engine
nickname = ge_gf1_engine_01 (starflier engine)
ids_name = 263671
ids_info = 264671
volume = 0.000000
mass = 10
max_force = 48000
linear_drag = 599

Your speed = max force divided by linear drag.
Change linear drag to 480 for a speed of 100, but beware handling changes.
