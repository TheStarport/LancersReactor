Has anyone tried this possible turret mode tactic??

I've had a thought and a search of the boards does not seem to reveal this particular tactic.

We've all complained about how one cannot maneouver and use turret mode simultaneously. So what if you did this....


First make sure you're in a battle with an ally (a.i. or otherwise) or two. Target him and enter formation with him. While in formation with your buddy enter turret mode and blast away. This way your ship will still maneouver in the battle area - since your in formation with another ship in the battle - and you can use your turrets to your heart's content. Of course if your buddy gets toasted, you'd need to exit turret mode and find another one or change tactics.

Any reason why this wouldn't work???
