Ranpostgen wrote:



- Mollies have no allies



They may not have any allies listed on their inforcard information, but according to empathy.ini, killing Mollies will lower your rep with the Bundschuh, Unioners, Red Hessians, Liberty Rogues, Blood Dragons, Junkers, and the IMG.

Even worse, your reputation with almost all these factions will lower *much* faster than you will gain reputation with the Corsairs. For example, for every unit of rep gained with the Corsairs by killing Mollies, you lose *six* units of rep to the Unioners! The sole exception are the Rogues -- with them, you lose reputation at the same rate that you gain reputation with the Corsairs.

However, if you don't mind the ire of the other factions mentioned above, then it is true that killing Mollies will eventually get your rep up with the Corsairs.

To sum up:

Corsairs are totally indifferent to whether you kill Xenos or whether you take missions for them. This is called lack of empathy (neither sympathy nor antipathy).

The same goes for the following factions:

BMM*
Armed Forces*
Bretonia Police*
Corsairs*
Daumann*
GMG*
Gateway*
IMG
Kruger*
Naval Forces*
Kusari State Police*
Planetform
Rheinland Military*
Rheinland Police*

Those marked with an asterisk will still shoot Xenos on sight despite their lack of empathy. This is because Xenos are considered Enemies and the Enemy/Ally attribute is separate from the empathy settings.

Any faction not listed above will by delighted more or less if you kill Xenos and you will improve your standing with them throug this. The three exceptions are:

Synth Foods
Xenos (obviously)
Zoners

These three factions have sympathy for the Xenos, and thus if you kill Xenos your reputation with them will turn worse.
