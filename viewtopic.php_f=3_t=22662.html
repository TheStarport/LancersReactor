Sherlog

Thu Aug 07, 2003 6:15 pm by Sherlog

After most campaign missions you get a bit of free time for exploring, getting rich etc. pp. During the first three freelancing periods your movement is a bit restricted but starting with the fourth freelancing period you can go wherever you please.

In fact, in one of my campaigns I did precisely that: during the fourth freetime I did a tour of almost all of Sirius in my Dagger (excepting about half a dozen systems that were too hot), took about 100 missions, killed about 1500 enemy fighters (most of those off-duty) and earned $2 million during that time.

However, most ships/equipment/jobs have certain level requirements and during the SP campaign this means that you have to go back to Juni eventually and fly a mission for her, in order to advance your level. This has nothing to do with your chosen path - Juni does not care at all whether you are a lawful citizen or a pirate, a fighter or a trader.
