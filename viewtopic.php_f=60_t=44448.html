first, welcome...

if you are looking to MOD, you will need Freelancer Mod Manager... otherwise know as FLMM. This program allows you to try mods, and chang back to regular, or try other mods, without having to reinstall. If your looking for mods to try, it comes with some, or you can look for your favorite Scifi shows or many other goodies on the site.

Multiplayer is different depending upon the server you play on, some are roleplay, while others are geared towards player v. player contests. you can join a clan for protection, get involved in wars, or just go it alone, with all the fun and trouble that comes with. Some servers run mods that require you to have beofre you can play on them, others do not, some run just regular FL. Its all up to you.

Try different servers, see what you like, this site is a good place to look for servers. the main page has banners with links to various FL servers that are up and running.
