Cool! Thanks man! I've sent it to your email.

For those that need to know, you can change the cruise charge time in the engine_equip.ini file. From there, it's pretty blatantly obvious what to do.
Then download and unzip the file (after backing up your files) to \data\audio\sounds\engines.
The files being replaced are all the ones that say ...cruise_start.wav
Change your cruise charge time to 1.5 seconds, and they should match pretty perfectly to the sound files.

The one thing you'll notice with that mod is if you hit the cruise from a dead stop, then your engine animation will lag a bit. It works fine if you're already moving close to 80.
