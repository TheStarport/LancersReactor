Yes there is a jump hole in Omega-7 (the same place Freistadt Base is located) that will take you to Omega-11. The Van Rohe belt is a huge cluster of rocks in Omega-11 with many having diamonds inside. Freital Base which is owned by the Red Hessians is nearby. Make sure you are neutral or friendy with them as you won't be able to land there or even mine for diamonds peacefully if not. It's really fun mining there, very scenic area.
