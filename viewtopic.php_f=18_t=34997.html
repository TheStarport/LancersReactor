WELCOME TO THE LANCERS REATOR!!!
Now that your with us you will never get away!
You not much of a newbie, most newbies don't know that their are any other forums besdies the FL Discussion forum.

As for your questions:
o Using FL Explorer is good for creating systems because it is very visual, but it has limitation and you may need to edit the files it makes.
o Also, if you want to edit non-system stuff, download the FL SDK. It has all of the FL files unencoded and fixed up for easy modding. Check out the tutorial forum for (guess what..) tutorials!
o Editing dll's are harder and required a untility called FL ids.
o Try downloading other mods to see what they have done and get inspiration from their ideas.
o Have fun and good luck.
