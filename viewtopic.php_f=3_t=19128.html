Ether Dragon

Thu Jun 12, 2003 5:01 pm by Ether Dragon

I've found a decent counter to the light fighters that run circles around the heavy fighters. Come to a complete stop and keep turning to face the light fighter. At that point, the light fighter is faced with two options: run away, or get in close and keep circling to keep the VHF from getting a bead on you. One or two hits from a VHF will destroy a light fighter. By having to constantly circle at close range, the light fighter will have a tough time keeping the VHF targeted, as well.

Seeing as how I was the guy in the light fighter, I don't know why I'm spilling the beans on this tactic. Maybe some other erstwhile light fighter pilot can provide a counter-counter tactic. As it stands, I circled a Sabre for a several minutes, slowly wearing away his shield with my weaker guns, only to have him use shield bots when I finally got them down. I was trapped in circling him, since any attempt to disengage would give him the opportunity to lock onto my fleeing ship and really unload. Eventually he managed to get back-to-back hits on me and blow me out of the ether.
