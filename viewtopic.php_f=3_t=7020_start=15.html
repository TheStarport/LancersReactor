Tue Apr 15, 2003 1:34 pm by carl

It's all really simple but for one little fact.

first of all look at a faction's 'sheet' and see who their friend and enemies are, if you want to be friends with them don't attack them or their friends, and don't be as nice to their enemies.
if you wan't to be enemies with someone: open fire..

so to answer the 'how did that happen?' xenos will love you for killing anyone but them... since they are enemies with everyone...

about docking with white stations (neutral) this will only work if you aren't spotted by one of your enemies as you approach the station, if that enemy of yours is friends with the station. If that friend of the base does spot you everyone that's friends with him will turn on you...

tip: approach white pirate bases with no cargo, so that no pirates will try and rob you, in which case the whole base will turn on you; this makes smuggling cardamine to manhatten from a pirate base that you aren't friends with all the more fun!...

the little fact remains that story-line mission will screw up your reps with factions. otherwise the story wouldn't exactly work right.(although I must admit some changes are a little weird (like the outcasts after lvl 4)) so either don't do story-line missions or do all story-line missions and then play.

one more thing: killing xenos that come around naturally is good thing... killing xenos on a mission from another faction isn't nearly as effective: say you want to be buddies with the rogues then killing xenos while working for ageira won't help...

have fun!
