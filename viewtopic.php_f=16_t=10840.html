It can indeed be hard to get there. I died once getting from Omega-11 to Omega-41 and that was only because I went left after taking 11->41 Jump Hole, and ran in to the most lethal cloud I've ever seen in this game. However I'd recommend using a class 7-8 Graviton-based shield, and some GMG Skyblast A guns since Hessians use Positron-based shields. Once you get there, head south on the map and look for a large Asteroid. Should be Leon Base. Oh you need to be at least nuetral with the Corsairs, accomplished by killing Hessians...lots of Hessians. Do not run from the Hessians, they will use disruptors if you try to cruise, and boosters will not shake them, not to mention you'll waste your shield bateries by the time you turn around and attempt fighting =P Now that I'm in there, I've been doing $235k missions with ease. If you're going there with a Freighter, expect to die swiftly.
