Wed Mar 05, 2003 10:31 am by endor

A lot of the "red X's" are spaced out so that when you fly to one, another one will appear on your nav map, and when you fly to *that* one, another one will show up -- if you get what I mean. For example, if you see two on your map, one directly south of the other, just keep flying north or south and you might run into another wreck. If you see nothing, try the other direction. If you still see nothing, well, that's probably all the ships there are.
