I am the ONLY FreeLancer!

*IN Character*

Greetings,


I just want you all to know that apparently *I* am the ONLY "freelancer" in the Sirius System.

That's right, I have flown endless miles in space and have found NO other being which is not aligned with some group, government, or guild.

I am the last of my breed it seems.


Fly well,

Captain Dredd
Last FreeLancer

============================

*OUT Of Character*

Does it seem odd to anyone else that we, the player, are the ONLY person in the entire game that is not part of a bigger group?
We are the ONLY "freelancer" in the ENTIRE Sirius System?

Anyone that gets a bug up their bum can feel free to blow us to spacedust, with no worries of reprisals from ANYONE else in the system.
Yet, no matter whom we decide we wish to engage in battle we must always fear what their guildmates will do as revenge.

Does this seem odd?

I think that either;
A ) There should have been another "faction" added to the game. A faction of "freelancers", which would ALWAYS be neutral to the player unless the player has previously attacked that INDIVIDUAL member.
Yeah, a lot of programming, but would make more sense than it currently is, and would add a lot of flavour and even more options for the player.

or

B ) They should have actually allowed players to JOIN a faction and thereby gain the BENEFITS of being a member...like unwavering support in combat, hailing for "backup" in battles as they seem to do, better prices at faction planets/bases for selling/buying, and maybe even some faction only ships (Like make the best ship of each faction be sellable to members only.), etc.
This seemingly would have taken far less work than "Option A" above, but they seem to have been to stuck on the name being "FreeLancer" to tie it into the "StarLancer" game that they made some rather strange desicion, and also seem to use the title as an excuse for those decisions(Or at least the title seems to be the justification that many FANS give for the way many things are in the game).


Yeah, the game IS called "FreeLancer", I know.
(So please save the apparently standard "But it is called FREELANCER for a REASON!" comments for somebody whom hasn't read that 10,000 times, it is getting old, thanks. )
But, when the ONLY "freelancer" in the ENTIRE game is the PLAYER they may as well have just named it "OutCast"...because that is what the player TRULY seems to be, an outcast which is never truly accepted into any group no matter how well he proves his loyalty and intentions.

Any comments/theories are welcome.
But please, try to keep this a conversation about the concepts of the topic and game, and not allow it to become a "if you don't like it then don't play it" thread or a "flamewar", thanks.


Fly well,

Pyrate Dredd
