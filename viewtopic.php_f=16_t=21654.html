There are 3 ways to get on with Corsair. The easyest is to attack the Red Hessian base in Dresden, or you can fly missions against Hessians and LBW in Stutgart/Dresden/Berlin. Finally you can make your way to Freeport in Omega 41 and pay off that guy in the bar.

Same goes for Freeport in Omicron Theta, but it will set you back 140-150k, so the killjobs is better. And killing Hessian Sabres is no big deal.

This is how I have done this, in all sorts of ships. And it's great fun and great money.
