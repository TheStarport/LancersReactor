Megistos

Mon Jan 24, 2005 12:38 pm by Megistos

Doing missions for the Outcasts will very quickly make the majority of Police, Militaries and House Corporations hate you, especially in Bretonia and Kusari who obviously have an especial hatred for the Outcasts. After that, if you want to get your rep up with them again, you can do one of two things:

1) Kill Criminals and lots of them. Avoid the temptation to kill lots of Xenos to make everyone like you, as doing that will annoy the Zoners, and you do NOT want the Freeports to be hostile to you. Good choices are the Corsairs and Red Hessians.

2) Get a Bounty Hunter bribe. It doesn't matter that the Freeports are owned by the Zoners, because the missions are offered by the BHG. You'll just get this message saying "No missions available. Come back when you're friendlier with the factions on this base." Luckily for you, you can pick up BHG bribes in many places, including the majority of Freeports.
