1: Undo everthing you have done.

2: make sure you have your sound card drivers up to date (latest dates 3/19/03)

3: Disable your Midi.

4: Use Det 41.09 Not version 43

5: Turn Video acceleration all the way up. Having it turned down turns essentially turns your card into Virge DX, meaning nothing will run.

6: Ensure all acceleration features are enabeled.

Now try it.

You cannot transfer an installed game from one system to the next. Especially across different OS versions.
Search your machine for Kernel32.dll. It should be found in one of windows systems directories. Ensure that it is not corrupted or missing.
