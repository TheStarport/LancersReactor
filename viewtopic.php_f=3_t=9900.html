You don't need to do the story missions when Juni contacts you. You can still do some trading on the way to meet Juni (I recommend the Cargo Computer announced on this websites home page) or become a pirate for a while. You can also set your own waypoints for trading and once you reach the final destination, the story mission waypoints are automatically put back on the map. I had to do this a few times to ensure I had enough dosh for shield packs and nanobots, etc.
