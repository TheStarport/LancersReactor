Anubis or Barracuda

Since you can get either of these ships at around the same time... I was faced with the choice between getting the Anubis or keeping my Barracuda. Pros for getting the Anubis? I'd earn 49 grand, and it has better weapons support, 300 more armor, and an extra slot for a turret.

But I tested them both out and found that the Barracuda fared much better in battle because it could turn and maneuver so much more easily than the Anubis (especially against those pesky Nomad interceptors). But the general sentiment here, from what I'm gathering, seems to favor the Anubis (maybe just because it's a ship that you can't get in MP?).

What do you think?
