Primary

More RAM

Alternative Secondary Option

If your HDD is not one of the onboard hardware cached variety, I suggest that you upgrade to one of those. Any make you can afford that has an on board hardware cache of 8mb will significantly improve your PC's performance. You don't have to get the 10,000 rpm SATA monsters. A regular ATA HDD from WD, Seagate, Hitachi or Maxtor will do, whatever is cheapest for you.

I had to replace my non-cached 7200 rpm ATA HDD with one that has a 8mb cache and the performance difference was very pleasantly surprising.
