A release candidate (RC) is a disc of Half-Life 2 that Valve Software (Creators of HL2) considers the final version. Vivendi, being the publisher, will check the RC for any bugs, and if it finds any major ones, they'll send the disc back to Valve, with instructions on what bugs there are. The bug checking process takes about 72 hours to do, and after that, it will take about 2 weeks to publish.

A Vivendi spokesman has been heard saying that Valve has gone through a serious de-bugging of Half-Life 2, so the first RC SHOULD be accepted (which is in itself quite rare).
