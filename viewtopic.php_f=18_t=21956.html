Open up your market_ships.ini. Open up the Find box and type in Li01_01. Now, under that heading, choose one of the ships to replace with this line:

marketgood = oe_package, 0, -1, 1, 1, 0, 1, 1

Voila, Anubis is now sold on Planet Manhatten.
