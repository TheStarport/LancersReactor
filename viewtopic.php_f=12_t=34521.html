Mon Sep 06, 2004 10:25 am by Tawakalna

Darjeeling is a specific area of NE India, in the Himalayas. Darjeeling (at its best) has a light distinctly grapey flavour, which some say is similar to Moscatel. it's generally considered the champagne of teas, and the best darjeeling is very light and refreshing without any additives at all; however, there are a plethora of cheap nasty Darjeelings made from the poorer plantations and from rejected stock. Most supermarket darjeeling falls into the latter category.
