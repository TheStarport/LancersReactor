Fri Jul 16, 2004 5:48 pm by Wiz914

Hint: while in a fight you can't win, use your cruise engines and keep far enough from the center of the battlefield, running around in circles. Your allies often take out a few ships and then it's easier to rejoin the fight.

Also, when faced with ships/gunboats/battleships, take out ships first, then gunboats and lastly battleships. Rheinlanders use tachytron and plasma weapons. They also use molecular shields (not 100% sure). Plasma weapons do a lot of damage at close range, but can be easily avoided at longer ranges. So keep your distances.

Other hint: always buy the most expensive ship/shield you can afford! Usually, I go as follows: Defender/Crusader/Dragon/Anubis/Stiello. Heavy fighters are usually a good compromise between firepower and maneuvrability.

As for batteries, they only recharge a certain percentage of your shield. When you push G (or is it F?), you use as many batteries as necessary to fully recharge the shield. The stronger the shield, the more batteries are needed.

Other hint: Gunboats and battleships have a weak spot in their engines. So target them for maximal damage.

Sat Jul 17, 2004 12:16 am by Dev_Wood

Against battleships, consider using hard hitting missiles (or maybe torpedoes, not sure if you can get them that soon) on their weakspots, like engines. Rheinland gunboats move quite quickly, so your best bet to destroying them is to use your thrusters to catch up with them. Try to get directly behind their engines, because at that point, they can't fire at you. When fighting the regular fighters, use the tactic of tailing them and letting loose with your cannons. And lastly, always remember to tractor in shield batteries and nanobots from destroyed craft to supplement your decreasing amounts.
