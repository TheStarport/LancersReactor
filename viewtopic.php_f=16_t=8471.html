This is how I did it:

Place your ship at the Kruger Base and start shooting at the Kruger Ships. You will find out that the police joins the Kruger ships when you are to close to the spaceport. Fend them off and follow the police lines going north for just a few klicks. The Krugers are frequently using this line so you should run into some Krugers (commonly in Falcons) very soon. Shoot some of these until your reputation with the Hessians just reaches Neutral (it's still kind of pink then).

Then fly to Vogtland Base and buy the ship. In my case I was so barely neutral with the hessians that I was shot at by them while returning to the Trade lanes after purchase. I don't know what went wrong because I went in the base being tolerated, went off being shot at. Anyways, I have my Sabre now.

BTW, I could need a lvl 10 shield. Does anybody know if the corsairs sell some (I'm fed up with these Hessians, I'm from Westphalia anyways)?
