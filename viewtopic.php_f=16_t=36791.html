This will go to the spoiler forum, and I'll email you to let you know. You have to find mutual enemies of the faction you wish to become loyal to. Example: to get in good with the Corsairs, shoot a ton of Red Hessions. Check your reputation status til it turns white (or green if you've really kicked butt). Go to one of their bases, and bribe a bartender to get into the "green". Use the "info" button when docked and highlight a faction that you wish to become friendly with, it'll show you who their enemies are.
