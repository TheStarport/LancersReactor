Chips

Tue Jun 22, 2004 10:57 pm by Chips

X2 is a good game, its very different from FL - which is something people seem to have a hard time trying to grasp. Ergo it also attracts alot of "hatred" in comparison to FL. However, IF you have the time, and IF you like investing ALOT of time into characters and gameplay, its open endedness is very nice - stacked with features. However, IF your expecting a mere FL2 kinda thing - you will sadly be disappointed and angry. The fighting system is COMPLETELY different - which is the main reason people don't like it - for some reason they thought it was going to be like FL - well, its not.
The trading is different - but MUCH better - as is the "experience" of flying in space too.

X2 is a great space sim on its own, you just have to stop trying to comparing it to FL -cause they are COMPLETELY and UTTERLY different. Having said that - you may be disappointed that their voices actors for the storyline are WAY below the standard that FL has. So are the "cutscenes" too - and it is heavy on your graphics card, so ensure you have a decent one. To be honest, that is where you can draw comparisons with FL. You would have thought they could have done better on the acting bits - cause the emotion/expression of the voices is terrible.....................really makes you realise that FL did have some very very good voice actors working on it.
