Refresher Course on the Story....

Alright, first off, let me start by explaining that I am in no way new to this game. I have been playing Freelancer for going on three years, now, but that still does not excuse my absent-mindedness that I'm often prone to suffer from...

Alright, now, on to the good stuff.

I'd just like to start by saying that I love LancersReactor. I often come to this website on average of two times a week to see what's new. It's wonderful to see that a game that is several years old still holds such fascination with the general public.

Now that that's out of the way, I'd like to announce that I am currently in the process of writing a fanfic story based on Freelancer. The main character of this story is a character that I have been known to play in various Roleplaying Games; such as Star Trek, Stargate, et al. This character, Nathan Hunter, is the commanding officer of a squadron for The Order. It's set about five years after the events of Freelancer, allowing The Order ample time to change the public opinion from a criminal organization to its original intent of a peace-keeping force.

My posting here today is to request information on the game that I have seemed to have forgotten. I have beaten this game dozens of times, both in its original version and with dozens of other mods and addons for the game. What I'm having trouble remembering is the name of the species talked about on Sprague and Trent and Juni travel there to find Professor Quintaine. If anyone recalls, instead of finding Quintaine, you find his assistant (whose name currently escapes me, as well.) She is standing in the middle of an 'archaeological digsite' that was supposedly to have belonged to an ancient civilization that died out thousands of years before mankind even learned to walk.

Again, if anyone recalls, the Nomads were supposedly the gaurdians of this civilization before they became extinct. What I'd like to know is, what was the name of that civilization? I forgot what the good doctor called their species...
