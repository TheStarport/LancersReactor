Please pardon the necroposting...

CW requested how to add the cargopods...Here is what I have discovered.

In the Ships/loadouts_utility.ini file, it has the loadouts to the transport ships(trains).
here is your code:
In your ship's package in the goods.ini, Copy/paste this code.
equip = cargopod_red, hpcargo01
Delete the equip text and replace w/addon. it should now read
addon = cargopod_red, hpcargo01

This will make it show up in space, but because it isn't part of the ship cmp, those cargo pods disapear in the planet landing cinematics.

now in your ship arch.ini, make sure that this is added: (add or delete as ness.)
hp_type = hp_cargo_pod, HpCargo01, HpCargo02, HpCargo03, HpCargo04, HpCargo05, HpCargo06
