Technological Colony is in the need for ship designers.

This is an ad requestiong anyone who is able to create new and exotic ships. We are in the mids of starting a mod based on a book that I am writing, entitle Galactica's Chronicles. If you would like to see what I have, please goto the link on our website. As for the kind of ships that we need, we need ships that could fit the following descriptions:

- For the American Republic, we would like ships that look like different forms of the Battlestar Galactica: Colonial Raptor (http://www.shipschematics.net/bsg/image ... raptor.jpg). Anything along this line may work. As for colors, anything in the tradition red, white, and blue that looks fashionable.

- For the Fe-Zu Dynasty, we would like ships that look like the normal Freelancer Kusari ships, but a little more realistic, like it could enter the atmosphere of earth and not shatter into a thousand pieces.

- For the Grand Tribal Territorial, we would like ships that look like the Mercury Atlis (http://www.silentthundermodels.com/cust ... _atlas.JPG). Once again, an assorted line would be nice in desert colors.

- For the European Alliance, we would like ships that look along the lines of the kind of space shuttles we have now, such as Space Ship One (http://www.silentthundermodels.com/spec ... own_03.JPG), the Nasa Space Plane (http://static.howstuffworks.com/gif/space-plane-ch.jpg), or something in that line and color scheme. We once had a great moder who did a very, and I stress that, good job at the Space Ship One that made it look almost realistic, but he never finished it.

- For Colonial One, we would like ships that look to be of Atlantean origin. We would also like a few crafts that look like they could have been made from dissembled tanks, trucks, and other modern day vehicles. Sorry we can't give any pictures.

- For the Orbiters, we would like ships that look like they have been constructed from the spare parts of the other ships. Ships that basicly have no two same parts and are all different colored because of it. Also besides being multy color, we would like if they looked to be rusty, line with holes, the works, just to make it look like it has been scrapped and barely flies.

- Finally, we would like a craft that is human, but looks like it comes from 5000 years in the future. This I think in going to be the most entertaining.

If anyone could help with this, we appreciate it a lot, but are sorry that we couldn't be specific to show exactly what we would like. We want to be new and original, so are asking for you.

If you can help, please email me at alfa_astrix@hotmail.com. Thank you again for your help.

Everything is nothing, but nothing is not everything.
