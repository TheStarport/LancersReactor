How to make millions in seconds and increase your cargo hold

This is my best discovery yet...
follow this to make millions without leaving a station

1. open the equipment folder
2. open "goods"
3. with "goods" open remove "goods from the equipment folder to the desktop
4. save as: goods
(now you can edit this folder)
5. open ships folder
6. repeat this for the "ship arch" document
7. know your ship and its make(border world-bw, liberty-li,rheinland-rh, corsairs-co and so on)
8 search for your ship and edit "hold_size" to desired amount
9. land on a station and check what commodities are for sale
(the example is cardamine with a 3000 space cargo hold)
10. open "goods" and search for cardamine
11. set price for cardamine (or desired commodity) to 1, save but don't exit
12. run the game and fill your hold with the now cheap commodity
13. save game and exit
14. in "goods" folder set the price for cardamine (or whatever good your using)
to a desired amount and save
15. open freelancer and sell your now valueable hold for cash
