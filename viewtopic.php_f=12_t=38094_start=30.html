Tue Feb 21, 2006 7:44 pm by house atreides

ok in one of the other forum topics people were starting to dis EVE Online. So that I wouldn't totally hijack that thread ( and as this one is about other games).

EVE Online is one of the better MMO's out there. BUT the Evolutions mod for Freelancer has so much of the same content that I didn't see justifying the cost.
In it defence thou OTHER then it's jaw droping graphics, and huge and amazing soundtrack, I LOVE the UI in it. and the 3D starmap with the navagation interface was awsome. Mining was as fun as it is in Evolutions and the player comunity is extreamly friendly compared to many MMO's. The CS is amazing, and the Dev's made an amazing game to playin.
On the down side the "off-hand flying" didn't please me, targeting while simple could be difficult at times. once you were fireing on all targets it was just sit back and keep an eye on your sheilds and armor, that was a bit boring for me. The skill system while cool was pourposely flawed in that you could only train one caracter at a time, and you were allowed up to three caracters.

Some of the comments I have seen on other forums:
" travel takes forever" only in the begining, once you get better ships the speed of the ships improve.
" you have to pay to play" true it is a MMO.
" the player community is unfriendly" in my experence a majority were very friendly.
" the Dev's suck" they are better then many game devlopers out there.

all in all EVE Online is one of the better MMO's out there and there are on average around 16,000+ players online AT ALL TIMES. and while there is a bit of lag in the starter systems all the other 5,000 systems are lag free. the ships are a blast to play and once you destroy your first station your hooked lol. Despite tghe downsides it is defintly worth checking out, eve if just for the trial version. But it is very cheap to buy, I got mine for $7.

Blessed Be to all those that still dream of the flight to the stars.
