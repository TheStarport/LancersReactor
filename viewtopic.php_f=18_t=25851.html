Chips

Thu Nov 06, 2003 1:42 am by Chips

To swing in for the defence:

The 'Kids' and others that make mods like the simpsons, have usually only just started modding FL, or that is the first time in their direction (systems for example).

Sometimes (with uber ships), they create something they want to see, and like all of us, they like it, so they assume others will. Hence it is posted for download.

With systems and other mods, a level of creativity and thought is needed for the mods that are made. At this point, they make a mod, and showcase something they like - for the same reasons as above. However - this can have ONE other vital, and overlooked part to it too.

The comments that are posted, can often be about the creativeness of the mod. It is HELPFUL when someone posts - 'I like it, but if you did blah blah blah then it would be better'. You cannot get that kind of suggestions coming from playing with it yourself.

Also - negative comments such as 'your mod sucks' do nothing whatsoever. The persons mod you are belittling now MIGHT just be the foundations of one of the most incredible mods you ever play. Interesting eh? If you just try to pull them down with negative comments - well, who knows what you might be terminating in the coming weeks?

To sum it up - young, old, new and vet modders need ideas to mod. They need your help to develop their modding ideas and techniques. After all - if something was built on the vision of one, then it would be a monochromatic world we lived in - so give them a break eh?

Chips
