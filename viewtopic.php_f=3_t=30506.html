Fri Apr 16, 2004 9:04 pm by Ro9ue

yeah, try finding a bribe on a Freeport for them. Unfortunately, they do not have any enemies, but they do have pseudo-allies. Despite their infocard claiming they have no enemies or friends, the empathy file says they like a few factions to a small degree, like the Bounty Hunters and a few corporations.
For example, I have made it a habit (or maybe a hobbie ) to shoot any BH I encounter; it just is a consistant and reliable way to sweeten up my rep with any pirate or criminal faction without affecting any other faction (well except for the Zoners, and that's a very little amount). Since Zoners DO havea slight soft spot for BHs, my rep slowly fades from the green when I bribe them to a more and more nuetral white, and sometimes down into the almost-red as I kill BHs over a period of several days.
So be careful, because if you shoot the wrong guys, your rep will feel some pain.
