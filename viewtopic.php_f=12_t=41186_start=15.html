Michio Kaku, the author of Hyperspace is one of many who currently promote the concept of a multidimensional multiverse.

Also a theory sometimes thought to be convergent with String Theory if not being a component of the Theory itself.

The notion that parallel lines in our four known dimensions actually may intersect in other ones beyond the four is not impossible but, from our perspective in the four known dimensions and with our current needs for existence, relatively without meaning.

To say that parallel lines "in fact" intersect and is an accurate representation of what Kaku wrote is, at least, an overstatement and may very well be an "wrong" answer were a test given on the subject.
