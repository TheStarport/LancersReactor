Argh

Sun Nov 28, 2004 8:59 pm by Argh

If I were making a clan-oriented mod... here'd be the things I'd put in:

1. Balance all of the ships/weapons/shields/etc for PvP combat, not against the AI. That way clanners who acted like ****s might just get vaped, if a skilled n00b showed up and they tried to pick a fight. It'd make everybody a lot more polite In short, dump the crappy "level" system from FL, and make the game more about playing skillz than about camping the server 24/7. I keep saying this to mod designers... but I seem to be the only one who's actually doing it, thus far...

IOW, do the opposite of Evolutions. Evo is a wonderful SP mod- I actually enjoyed winning SP again with Evo 1.28. But Evo would've been a disaster for MP, just like vanilla FL is- it's terribly boring when everybody is either a n00b who stays away from PvP combat because they'll always die... or yet another boring clan player in an Eagle for the millionth time. Make the different fighter classes a lot closer than they currently are in terms of HP, weapon capacity and shields, and it'd make being in a clan a lot more exciting- and there might be a point to all of the ships besides the Eagle (or whatever super-"1337" VHF is at the top of your food chain). In short... try *not* to create a balance where top players all use the same ship.

2. Build custom Missions that involve giant capships or other very challenging targets, where players have to band together to win them. And make the money worth it.

3. If you're going to mess with trade/commodities... keep it subtle. Putting an ultra-cheap Cardamine factory right next to NY is not subtle

4. If clanners can have Systems, then they should have some sort of custom Bases and custom paintjobs for their spacecraft- different MAT files with 10 min. of Photoshop and editing work could get this done...
