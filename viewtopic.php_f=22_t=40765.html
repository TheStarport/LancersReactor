I had a problem that was pretty much the same thing and here is how I fixed it.

I tried doing all of the port forwarding that everyone mentions.
I checked (and was not running) tcp/ip6.
I removed my wireless NIC card.
I did everything short of pulling out my hair.

Here is what worked for me.

If went into the software for my router (192.168.1.1) and told my router config software to reset everything to factory defaults. My game runs great and I have no idea why doing this works. Every time I reset or reload the server I have to do this, but it DID give me a workaround that allows me to run my Freelancer server.

Try this and see if it works for you.
