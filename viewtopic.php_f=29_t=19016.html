Actually there are a couple of good tutorials that cover this topic to one degree or another. You can find the following ones in this forum:

1: "Goods.ini, market_ships.ini, engine_equip.ini, weapon_equip.ini" by Hairfarmer.

2: "Compilation of ALL IDS Names, Mod Info, Tutorials, INI Explanation" by Reynen. ( LOTS of useful info in this one )

Before you get started, you'll need to download an ini decompressor ( I recommend BINIQDU v.1.1 ) in order to decompress the .ini files and convert them into readable english. You can download BINIQDU from the utilities download page:

www.lancersreactor.com/t/editing/download/

Hope that helps. Good luck, and happy modding.
