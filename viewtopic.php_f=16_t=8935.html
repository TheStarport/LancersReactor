Jason

Sun Mar 09, 2003 10:52 pm by Jason

France was part of the Alliance.

It depends on which storyline you go with. Someone or other said that in one version, 5 of 8 ships made it out, the rest being destroyed. In that case, the destroyed people could sensibly be, I dunno, the French, the Italians, and the oddsorts.

However, there are French names in some of the other factions, so I'd assume they were in Liberty, Bretonia, Hispania, Rheinland or a combination thereof.

The French, South Africans, Australians, Canadians, Italians and a few others were all in the Alliance in Starlancer (the Coallition being Russian, East European, Chinese, and "Middle East", broadly interpreted I think) from what I've been able to sort through on the Starlancer part of this site. However, the fictional world has undergone a few changes - for example, the destruction of Sol has been cut. I definately hope for an expansion involving the Sol/Coallition, and probobly the Nomads too since they were only moved away in FL. Although fighting the Nomads was intensely annoying. If only they'd allowed a few more levels of shields and guns it could have been a straighter fight, with less terrified flying around in circles waiting for "shield restored."

The inclusion of nationalities in a space game was a clever, if not entirely uncontroversial move for DA. Adds a fun touch for people with affinities. At the moment I'm torn because I don't want to be enemies with all kinds of factions I have soft spots for because of history or the SP storyline.
