Yeah guys your totally misinformed about GTA, part of the reason why its so interesting is because you dont really have to do those things, you simply have the freedom to do them if you so desire. But its funny cause you can ALWAYS tell when someone has never (or played very little) GTA when they say its all about killing everything in your way. Oh and I also bought Manhunt, and while it is very gory, the story is extremely compelling, and its a very solid stealth game aswell. Besides your killing people like white supremacists and escaped-mentally-unstable-serial-killers and so forth. But San Andreas is in a whole other league from the older GTA's. Every mission is completely different (in one your shooting up a shopping mall, the second chasing a train while your brother is shooting guys off of it, and in the third your in a dance competition DDR-style) so its got way more variety. I was actually surprised how many new things there are, besides my liking of the other games, I really thought they were kinda shallow. But San Andreas is really deep actually, you character has stats like muscle,stamina, each weapon even has skills to develop now. Not to mention even things like Sex Appeal (which is how people react to you) and you can talk to people on the streets now, they say something and you can press a button on the D-Pad for a negative or positive response (their HILARIOUS too, sometimes even you get into fights cause of em)

You can also increase your driving,biking,cycling skills, got a lot of RPG flavor in it. But the biggest part of the attraction is all the things you can find around town. You can for example, restore health by buying soda from soda machines, or goto the local bar and play some pool (2 player also) even play the various Arcade machines, which actually have fairly interesting games in em. Or working on your relationship with your girlfriend (easier said than done, she never seems to know what she wants, but I found it hilarious that CJ's replies no matter what she says are like "yeah uh huh" or "this is really interesting, im just gonna listen" then theres territorial conquest, now areas of your map are shaded certain colors, by going in them and shooting up rival gang members. You can incite gang wars, in which you and your (now from start of the game) controllable gang survive three waves before the 'hood becomes yours. Basically guys I can go all day about all the things I have seen and found, but what im trying to say is with SA, GTA has evolved into a much deeper, more interesting game than it ever was.

Theres also the fact that its a whole country now, and no maps or screenshots do it justice, its absolutely MASSIVE, In all my time of playing I have only seen a bit of Los Santos (the first city) which is always a pleasure, to drive around and occasionally run into areas I have never seen before. Theres also the countryside which kicks ass, lots of little peaceful towns with cops that just cant wait to fill you full of lead the first mistake you make. The new weapons are great, yesterday I found one of those little hooks that people who watch animals carry. The one they use to pull people off stages what was it again?? its long and white with a hook at the end. Among that the knives (with the newly implemented and mind you very solid stealth, and stealth kills) and god knows how many more (you can even dual wield most lighter weapons when your skilled enough.

PS: The collectables are still there, there 100 hidden packages, but now in the form of 'Tags you have to graffiti your gang sign over, and theres a second one I dont know much about, which is going underwater (you can swim and dive now btw, also ditch cars that fall in) and finding "oysters" but i dunno what they do. Theres also flower bouqets.

PS2: Oh and I know it had multiplayer but it was a mod, hopefully this one will be official.
