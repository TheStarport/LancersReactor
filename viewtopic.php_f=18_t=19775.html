Sat Jun 21, 2003 2:31 am by Wanderer

You don't have to move the guns inside, just use this as the model type:
DA_archetype = equipment\models\turret\li_cruiser_gun01.cmp
material_library = equipment\models\li_turret.mat

Its the cruiser forward gun which is invisible since the "fake" gun is actually part of the model.
