@Zeds
Very cool ship designs. I really like the BOB fighter. I also like the X3. It would make a pretty intimidating capitol ship.
As for getting them into freelancer, check out the Drizzt's tutorial on making models in milkshape. It covers the creation of hardpoints. After that, all you gotta do is change the shiparch.ini file to point to your CMP and MAT file instead of one of the in game ships. As for getting it to hold all the right guns and stuff, you can find out how to do that in the editing tutorial area. It's really quite easy once you go through the tutorials.
