Get rich fast - mining

Anyone else like mining? I find the only place its really worthwhile is Graves Station in Dublin, mine 20 gold in 90 seconds or so which can be sold straight to graves for $340/unit. It's like a mission that gives $6800 and takes a max of 2 minutes and can be done in any ship with a gun. Graves is protected by a minefield, but there are no mines above the station so just go up and over them to get in and take the same way out.

When starting a new MP character I usually fly straight to dublin, stopping only to fix my rep by harrassing traders or vanadlising depots, then mine until I have amassed 35K, take a final load of 20 gold to new london and buy a drone and a load of luxury food, at that point the universe is my oyster. The other time I mine at graves is when I'm a trader but am completely broke from dying and losing my cargo - because all you need is a gun.

Obviously not as profitable as looting wrecks, but you cant rely on that in MP.
