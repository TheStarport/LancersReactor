Okay, I usually just talk rubbish in the OT so thwack me if I am wrong in what you are asking.

The FL ships move on a Vertical and Lateral axis, they do not roll in a clock wise manner however they do correct themselves automatically after a manouver when you hit the space bar (default) and come out of free flight. If you are trying simply to stop them from this act of rolling automatically just go into the game menu, options and click auto correct to "off".
