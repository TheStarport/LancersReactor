{hunter}Skyevallen

Sun Dec 19, 2004 10:47 pm by {hunter}Skyevallen

About all a server can do in the case is enforce a total RP rule, where the whole server is under RP, no player can claim not to be in this atmosphere. It eliminates a lot of other rules that would need to be placed into the server setup and everyone would eventually learn it as this way.

While although making a mod to conform to specific factions isn't really idealistic because of the constraints and the headaches, even the possibility that the game mechanics won't allow some things.......to achieve players playing factions isn't too difficult if they want to do it.

I know that on my server, most of the players have taken up the roles of NPC factions and they RP under their own tags, but clearly state that they are playing as whichever faction. We've also just created a mod where the ship flying mechanics have changed to more realistic (rather than this stop on a dime in space stuff)......and where clans (or very rich individuals) have the choice of adding to their claimed territories by "exploring" new systems, "upgrading" for better hardware for the NPC's and themselves (from weapons to ship experimentation), buying pretty much anything available in the game from JH's to Terraformed planets. It is a patch based system where we upgrade the map and systems as needed per patches once a month (if anyone gets anything done).

I know there was a post somewhere on here where players were looking for some of this, whilst players on our server were looking for the same things. We've decided to give it a try and see how things go.

So yea in a way there is a server out "there" that does practice what some of you are looking for.......but it may not live up to expectations because there is only so much you can mod into the game.
