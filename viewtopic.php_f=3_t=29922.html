Earendil

Thu Apr 01, 2004 12:31 pm by Earendil

All my responses are about MP because I feel SP was too short and should only be used as a learning process for MP.

- I like to see modding but only if they can be stable and well-supported. FL has no info from DA about how to mod.
- I do like the open-ended play as it leads to player interaction. That's where the real fun is.
- I, too, like the vastness of space as portaited in the game. Just enough to not be bored.
- I like the fact when you do something there are subtle and not so subtle interactions in the game. I.E. Take out neutral Xenos and they become instantly hostile yet your rep with other factions become a little more friendly.

While I am not trying to be negative I wouldn't say from the server side FL is fully stable. I also don't think the flying interface should be so limited to the mouse only.
