Read Before Posting

I'm not really in a position to make rules for this forum, but theres a couple of guidelines I would like to be followed.

1. Please look back in other threads to see if your idea has already been proposed before creating a new thread, asking for the exact same mod.

2. Please check the other threads to see what other mod teams are doing before starting to work on a similiar mod. For example, there are a lot of people working on putting pilotable capships into the game.

3. If you're interested in learning how to mod, your best bet is to download the INI decompressing tool and read through most of the older threads to see how others are doing it. Don't expect to be able to post a thread on "How do I mod?" and expect them to stop work to teach you how.

4. Share information! The more we learn, the faster mods can be produced, and the game made better.

Thanks
