yeah, it "looked pretty"...thats about it. It was a benchmark, thats all. None of the fun of comanche 3 in my opinion.

Comanche 3 wasn't *that* realistic at all, but it didn't feel arcade, you still felt like you were playing out real battles, it had just enough unrealism to make it fun. There was a hard mode where more realistic physics were used etc.

The missions were VERY well done, there was never a repetative or boring mission each new one was a totally new experiance. Also a good thing about not having it too realistic with too much going on inside your aircraft, you could concentrate on what was going on outside and you actually had to use a bit of strategy and tactical thinking. I really liked that you were vunerable and that you had to use your advantage of stealth and speed to overcome the odds.

My favourite mission was the one with A-10's, I think it was the last one, where there was a massive battlefied, and also the one with the train that you had to protect.
Comanche 3 is definantly the best (most fun) helicopter sim ever made, and I can say that because there are so few helicopter sims available.

btw Those fighters you refered to in the last thread kimk were SU-25 "frogfoot" close air support, I loved it when one of them appeared, you heard "we got a frogfoot!" and it really made you sit on the edge of your chair while waiting for it to appear.
