archkaine

Tue Mar 08, 2005 12:57 pm by archkaine

Step 1:
Install FLMM

Step 2:
Double Click the Mod file, let FLMM unzip and load it into its database.

Step 3:
When the mod has been added, FLMM will let you know that it's ready. You then just have to double-click on the name of the mod, or select the name of the mod and click the activate button.

Step 4:
Start up Freelancer. and have fun
