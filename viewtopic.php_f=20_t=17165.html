Found the problem at another site. This is what they told me to do

Click--Start->Run
then type in "cmd" (without the quotes) press enter
This will bring up a command prompt. From there type:

regsvr32 /u msxml4.dll (enter)

then

regsvr32 msxml3.dll (enter)

After that goto the folder where you installed Freelancer and doubleclick the msxml3.msi and go through the process to install msxml3 again. Hope this helps
