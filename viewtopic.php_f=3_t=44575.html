^ Meaning that you use jumpholes to get out of liberty space and the jumpgates are all usable in any other system. Now to get back into liberty you will have to use any of the jumpholes in any of the independent worlds that jump to liberty to get back in. If you leave after mission 1 you will have to use a jumphole to get back into New York. If you do not leave Liberty you will not get a better ship to fight in for mission 3 because the better ships are not in liberty. So it is best to get the Gunslinger, Dagger, or Cavalier. Have fun finding them.
