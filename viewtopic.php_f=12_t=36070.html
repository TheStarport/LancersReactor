Now to get to the point...

*Manages to stop self from posting violent rant*

As you may or may not be aware, HL2 will require product activation before it can be played. I was under the impression that this was only for the individuals who downloaded the "Steam" packages, but apparently the retail versions will also require activation. I despise product activation for various reasons, and I cannot sufficiently express my anger over this matter. I am especially annoyed because the store I pre-ordered HL2 from neglected to inform me of this (greedy bastards). If I had known, I would not have purchased the game, and would have waited for some cracks. Because of the fact that I was not informed of the activation, I may let HL2 sit on the shelf for a week or two until I acquire some cracks before I play it. I'm a patient man. At this stage though, I will try to keep an open mind and, depending upon exactly what is required, I will utilise this "activation" system. That being said however, I am confused as to what will happen the next time I want to play HL2. Will I need to activate the product again? What if I have upgraded my PC? Why the hell do I have to install "Steam"? These questions and more have not been answered to my satisfaction, and I am hoping that someone on TLR has the answers.
