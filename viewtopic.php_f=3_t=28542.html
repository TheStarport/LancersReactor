Midaus

Thu Feb 26, 2004 1:14 pm by Midaus

It all about the MODS. Some of the Mods for FL are just flat out awesome and very creative. There are some elements of the game that are Cheese, but some of the mods make great strides at reducing the monotany. The "Real Space" Mod give a whole new feel to the game and frankly is far more realistic with regard to light and shading. I just started testing out the "Evolutions" Mod in SP mode and I have to say I'm highly impressed with the changes they've made. It's been a blast so far. The combat seems much more realistic with hostiles actually using their shield batteries and nanobots, the pilots have more skill, there are more thrusters with varying top speeds and thruster drains, more realistic pricing for equipment and ships. One of the things I really like is that Liberty has some very heavy defenders which makes sense, because the way it was before if there was a war between Rhineland and Liberty the Rhinelanders would roll Liberty forces with ease since their ships "SUCKED" comparitively. GOOD Job guys!
