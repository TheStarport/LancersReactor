Freelancer Massive Addon v2 release info and wishlist

PROJECT SUSPENDED DUE THE LACK OF FREE TIME AND NEW MODS.

Since FMA 2 doesen't differ much from 1.1, I'll wait for more mods to compile into before release.

I'm preparing FMA 2 to the release.

FMA is a compilation of many different mods made by many authors. It allows you to play all this mods in one game. All authors sharing their work to FMA became co-authors of the whole compilation. I'm only the editor/compiler here.

Estaimated release date: not near future.

Features:

Over 100 new ships by various authors, including near all FL NPC ships, gunboats, battleships to buy.

New weapons: all FL NPC weapons like train or station turrets to buy, some new ones.

About 30 new systems, including

-Story Systems(6) -Now you can visit Planet Toledo and Nomad city
-Sol System (1) -Implementation of solar system
-Orion System (1) -Rich weapons storage
-Helios System (1) -Nice place to buy Scythe fighter and porton torpedoes
-Sam's Jump System (1) -Access to Story only systems
-New Stars systems (5) -Now called New Europea
-Earth system (5) -Beautyfull implementation of out Solar system, new equipment.
-Galapagus systems (4) -Check that Battleship!
-New Australia(1) -Look for kangaroos

This will work both in MP and SP, including the story missions.

Thanks to all authors who agreed (and liked) to share their work here.

Post your thoughts, wishes and ideas you want to share to this tread.

But remember, this is a compilation, not a mod by itself. As a rule, it will not change the originall game, it will only ADD new systems and new stuff there.
So please don't ask for destructable bases or battleship encounters or shortcuts from NY to Omicron's.
