At the moment we can't make our own working SUR's, the SUR exporter that we currently have can only give us a 50%-ish working SUR and that's only after a complete rebuild and reworking of the ship model.

The best bet for all current CMP's is to use a vanilla SUR and resize it to fit with FLModelTool (in the D/loads).
