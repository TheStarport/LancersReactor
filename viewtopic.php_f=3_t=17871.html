Chandrasekhar Limit

Mon May 26, 2003 10:13 pm by Chandrasekhar Limit

Where FL has made significant improvements over Elite and Privateer:

- Graphics (obviously)
- Large universe with many bases, jumpholes and wrecks (some hidden and you have to hunt for them).
- On-line multiplayer feature.
- Ability to implement user-made modifications to the game.

Where FL was "a step backwards" from Priv and Elite:

- non-dynamic econcomy.
- simplified flight and combat (for example, all ships have the same top speed)

Where things are subjective and you might or might not like depending on your tastes:

- storyline (some people loved it, some people think it's unoriginal, contrived and/or corny).
- Trent Edison (aka your protagonist character, which someone here aptly named him Mr. Eloquent for his drollness, heh heh).

My opinion: Yes, it's worth a go. Just don't expect it to be better than Priv or Elite in every way. Good luck!
