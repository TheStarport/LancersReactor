Just remove these two lines in loadouts.ini for every ship, EXECPT the player loadouts.

cargo = ge_s_battery_01, #
cargo = ge_s_repair_01, #

But first you have to turn off the mod, and you edit the MOD not the game.
Mods are located in C:\Program Files\Freelancer Mod Manager\mods
