Freeworlds RP Server News

Yes, the Freeworlds RP Server Forum is down. No, we don't know the exact reason why it has gone done and though some people have some speculation, there is no official word as to why it went done. And yes, ZaccaR has managed to back up most of the work from the server before it went down, so most of the posts should be saved. The Server itself is still running at this current time. Until further information is available, Jadean has constructed a temporary Freeworlds RP Server Forum at the following address:

http://www.evalve.org/boxed/index.php?mforum=&mforum=jadean

On a final note, to those wondering, yes, I have also returned in these last few days to Freeworlds development to help the team put the finishing touches to the mod (completing and fixing some THN errors mostly and pointing out glaring mistakes to the rest of the team). However, please do not ask me about anything else to do with Freelancer development, as I am not sure what my plans are in that regards once Freeworlds 1.66 is released.

Edit: On a final, editted note and to dispell rumours, no this is not because we are putting 1.66 up on the server yet. The 1.66 mod is not ready yet. Close, but not quite there yet, just some last bugs to iron out from the ships, systems and THN's mostly.
