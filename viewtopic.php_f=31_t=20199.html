IMPORTANT Regarding Freelancer: The Next Generation Expansio

Tomorrow evening at 8pm Eastern Time, Starfyre Studios, the developers of Freelancer: The Next Generation expansion pack will be holding a chat for people to come and ask some questions before the pack's release later this week.
Developers who will be there include Reynen, Duriel_LoP, Spawn, Bobthedog, and Squall Loire. Other possible attendees include Michael Dan, Giskard, Kurgan and IGx89.
We would like to extend an open invitation to anyone who has any questions or comments about the pack to come along to #FLNextGen on irc.gamesnet.net at 8pm EST on Thursday 26th June.
