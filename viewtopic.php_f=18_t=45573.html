I can't speak for questions two and three, but to make the story systems appear on map, open up universe.ini and find this:

[system
nickname = St01
file = systems\St01\St01.ini
pos = 14, 5
visit = 128
strid_name = 196655
ids_info = 66115
NavMapScale = 2

Change the bolded line to visit = 0 and, when you visit the system, it will appear on the sector map. Repeat for all of the storyline systems you want to appear.
