Asgard RPG v4.0



Asgard RPG v4.0

The year is 1200. Asgard has advanced throughout the years in many ways.
Once the dwelling place of the ruling titans, now a closley defended system of many systems.
Suns now being harvested in the attampt to preserve human life within the Asgard Sector.
Nomads procede in attacking our homes, a new nomad base has been revealed, but what to do about it.
All we can do is stand and fight, fight for what is ours

Asgard is an addictive RPG mod for the well known Freelancer Game by Microsoft.
The basic Freelancer story still stands but many new additions have been made,
Such as new Commodoites, Systems, Ships, Planets, Bases and Equipment
Many new factions have come to asgard with a interesting unique RP status.

Asgard is one of the longest running Freelancer servers with over 2 years under its belt.
It is ranked in the top 5 servers on the Freelancer Global Statistics site.
With a max player capacity of 51 players it can easily host many RP events

Version 4 is the most extensive change ever for us. There is more new code, adding more new role play opportunities, than we have ever had.
It is also a clean slate restart for everyone. All previous character files have been removed, so everyone starts over with this release.
All previous player bans have been removed so everyone has a fresh chance to play.
The mod code has been optimized for the most lag free operation possible, and the entire community is there to help new or seasoned players alike.
There has never been a better time to start playing Asgard than now.
