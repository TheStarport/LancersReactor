One failed solution after another (re: Connecting to Global

Ok.. First off. I know this topic is almost a year old now, and people are undoubtedly getting tired of it, but I was finally able to get a copy of the game now that I have a machine capable of running it. Listed below are the possible solutions that I have tried already (all of which were found on these forums):

*Uninstall and re-install of Freelancer (Even went as far as to remove any straggling registry entries and files)
*Re-installing DX9.0b (people said something about DirectPlay9, but DX9.0b installs DP8, so I don't know what's up with that)
*Rolling back DX to XP minimum and reinstalling DX9 from the Freelancer CD (There is a working utility at guru3d.com that safely removes[95/98 or rolls back[2000/XP DirectX)
*Made sure all my drivers and firmware were up to date (Just in case, 'cause you never know)
*Inputting a server address manually using flipdirect.hta
*Disabling the unused PCI Fax/Modem in Device Manager (Thinking maybe it was trying to use that instead of the network adapter attached to the cable modem)
*Opened ports 2300-2400 in my software firewall (No flame wars about software please, but yes I use Black Ice and haven't had any problems to date.)
*Installing The All Seeing Eye (Which hasn't worked for me yet.)

If I've missed anything, please let me know, or post a link to such a thread.
The system I have running Freelancer is (and again, please no flamewars, this isn't about who has better hardware or your opinion of such):

Dell 4600
P4 2.6Ghz w/HT
512MB DDR Ram
nVidia Geforce FX 5200 128MB
SoundBlaster Live! 5.1 (Dell Digital hybrid)

Also I can post a copy of the DXDiag log if anyone so wishes.

Thank you ahead of time for your patience and understanding concerning this matter.
