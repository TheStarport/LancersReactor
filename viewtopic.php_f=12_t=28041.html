Tue Feb 03, 2004 7:17 am by gromit

i have one question. how can i get it so that the music comes in with the fade of background or not? Cause, the WHOLE point of fade in background was that it would reveal everything as it played music.


that depends whether you want to be ethical or not! if you don't care then it can be done with javascript - there's free scripts out there - but if you do care then scrap the idea altogether or go with a "flash intro" sort of thing. The reason I say that is for a number of reasons -

1. some people have javascript turned off on their browsers, which means they'll never make it to your frontpage
2. search engines hate javascript
3. I looked at it on a 2Mb connection - if I was on a 56K connection I would have given up way before the home page loaded.
4. Old browsers won't cope with it
5. Neither will Macs (very well anyway)

Best practice is to always go straight into the homepage, leave the fancy stuff til later...get them looking at your homepage first so they actually have something to stay for.

And to answer your question about music - nowadays flash is the most efficient way of streaming audio on the cheap. I can give you a few pointers if you want so let me know.
