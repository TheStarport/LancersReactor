Cutting in line. This one is tricky, but it works.

Position your ship at the very entrance of the white lights. Make sure that the other ships entering are BETWEEN you and the light...as soon as you form on them, you will be sucked in. I have entered Jump gates instantly before by doing this. It takes practice, but it can be done.

If you are too close to the white light, it will make you circle around, so positioning is important. As long as you are in front of the ships trying to enter, you will enter first. It's an easy way to cut in line. If you are fast enough you can do this on the fly without even slowing down...go from cruise mode to hyperspace instantly.
