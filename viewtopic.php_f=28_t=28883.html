Freelancer 2 MOD (not any more)

Well this has been announced a while ago, but I thought I should start a new thread to make life easier. This is basically the general idea of what will be/is in the MOD:

FACTIONS:

FUP (Federation of United Planets): Dedicated to the promotion of peace

Zoners: Have now vanished, but some say they are still around

Kusari/GMG: Have joined forces and have become quite seperate from the rest of Sirius

NEW SYSTEMS:

Betas:
An area between kusari and rheinland which is contested by both because of the gas found there
Taus:
More Tau systems have been colonized by the Outcasts who have made these their homes
Thetas:
A new area, south of the main part of sirius, no-one is sure what goes on here...
Geneva:
A new system set up by the FUP to promote peace

NEW SHIPS:

NEW CLASS SYSTEM:

Super Battleship
- only 1; stationed in New York
- bigger battleship (2x as big as normal one)
- lots of firepower (2x as much)
- only Liberty has the resources to make it

Ground Assult Cruiser
- player can't get
- used to take planets

Cruiser
- a cruiser...

Carrier
- hopefully launches fighters (don't know if this is possible)

Prototype
- what it says...

Super Heavy Fighter
- think uber powerful
- slow as a sloth

Bomber
- used to kill capital ships
- lots of torpedos, few anti-fighter weapons

Raider
- used to raid convoys
- lots of cargo space
- can disable transports/trains fast, loot, then leave
- not meant to kill fighters or capital ships

Communication's Vessel
- for those times when the fleet needs to hear orders; but can't due to disturbance
ie: in a nebula
- not avaliable to players

Troop Transport
- what it says...

Anti-Fighter Cruiser
- good vs Fighters
- horrible vs anything else

Mine Layer
- used to create minefields
- lots of mine droppers

Scout ship
- very very very fast
- little firepower

This is only a taster, there is more to come...
