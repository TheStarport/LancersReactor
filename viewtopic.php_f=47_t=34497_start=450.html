On the subject of the Hermes Cannon, it is my pleasure to announce that construction of Station Styx in the Cortez System is complete, and that refittings on Pueblo are now at 66%, with inertial stabilizer equipped and construction of actual engines begun.

In the end, I will have you all know that regardless of the outcome of the coming projects, Ageira will not be joining either the Axis or the Alliance. We are a corporation, not a government, and our role in society is to ensure the proper operation of the five Houses. You can drive us from your systems and lose our trust and assistance, but no matter what we will remain neutral in this conflict, and no Ageira craft will open fire on any Alliance or Axis ship.
