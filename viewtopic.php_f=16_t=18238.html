How to get Adv. Scorpions quickly

Go to the Silverton asteroid field in Colorado and start cruising the area between Ouray base and the tradelane. Watch out for Startrackers that fly alone in cruise mode. If you find one, fly an intercept course that puts you behind the Startracker on its line of flight; then engine kill, shoot, tractor an Adv. Scorpion.

Before I tried this I fried several wings of Xenos patrols in that area but got only Scorpion drops, even though there were lots of Startrackers among them.

Sherlog

Wed Jun 04, 2003 12:02 am by Sherlog

Yogimus, the Xenos are normally hostile at the start of a game, both in singleplayer and multiplayer modes.

Some more observations: the Startrackers that do drop the Adv. Scorpions are ferrying cargo and they do not normally engage you. If they do engage (because they just came out of a jumphole, for example) then they will try to cruise away at first opportunity. If you attack them while they are about to dock they will not engage either, and if you have only Justice Mk I then they have a good chance to escape before you have hammered through their armour.

I have seen them flying between Ouray base and the New York jumphole or between the base and the tradelane. Once I saw one that was headed in the direction of the planet, and out of curiosity I followed it (did not have a cruise disruptor). I thought I could catch it when it tried to dock with something but no dice, it took a dive in the sun corona.

The jumphole and the base are good places for picking them off but if there are regular patrol wings in the area as well then it can be difficult to choose the right targets (because you have to scan them to see if they carry cargo).
