"No autopilot? Isn't goto enough for u?"

Obviously, you've never played Privateer.

Privateer featured waypoints similar to Freelancer, however, transport between those waypoints was much faster.

All you had to do was select ANY waypoint in a system, and if no enemies were around, you hit AUTO and you flew instantaneously to that waypoint.

Only enemies or asteroid fields could interrupt your autopilot travel, and once they were gone, you could hit the autopilot again.

But Freelancer is not like Privateer. Privateer had an empty and ugly universe. Freelancer's univerese is pretty, you don't want to miss it. So the tradelane is a compromise over Privateer's instantaneous autopilot.
