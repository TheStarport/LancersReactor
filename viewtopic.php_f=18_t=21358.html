Ok, download the save game decrypter from the Reactor, then decrypt the appropriate save game (probably autosave). Open it in notepad, then seach for co_shi_grp. You should find a number like -0.xxxxx. Take the - sign out and they should be exactly opposite of how they feel towards you the next time you open the game (ie, all red to all green). Hope this helps.
