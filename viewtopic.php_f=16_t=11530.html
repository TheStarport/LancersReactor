HeymlicH

Mon Mar 24, 2003 5:36 pm by HeymlicH

1. go to a base and visit the equipment dealer.
2. sell 2 weapons so you have 2 free class 10 slots
3. select the new (class 10) weapon
4. click that tiny "mount" icon at the left of your weapon.
5. the selected weapon is mounted into the empty class 10 slot.
6. don't worry, took me ages to find out how to mount a weapon without selling and buying it back from the dealer

the guns are only properly mounted when the little blue boxes by their picture are connected. If the boxes are separated the guns can be mounted, but are not currently mounted. If there is no little blue boxes but the text is blue then the gun is good but there is no room. If the text is gray the gun is too high level for you.

Clicking on the little blue boxes mounts and unmounts weapons.
