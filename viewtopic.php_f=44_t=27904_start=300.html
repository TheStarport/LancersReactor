harrier

Thu Jan 20, 2005 10:35 pm by harrier

Cupp@,

Hmmm.. One point... Did you rationalise your groups into as few as possible starting with the largest which you gave an unique name? i.e. MYShip (the ship name)?
Plus, the cmp exporter can be funny without at least one hardpoint: Hp/Fixed/HpMount , without it there can sometimes be problems, my suggestion would be to add it at least before you export, then do the balance in Hardcmp as you intended...

If all this fails it may be that the model needs bounding box info, (see... Antons FlModelTool), or the textures are not being recognised... did you do the textures by hand or use the .mat file exporter?

Harrier..
