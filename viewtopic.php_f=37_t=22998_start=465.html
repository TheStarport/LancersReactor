News about Cyclone.

It's almost finished will probably be out in the next few days. I've added some BIG guns coming out of the pods the sides. They are built into the mesh and guns will be hidden inside them to make it look like the guns on the mesh are actually firing. The new gun for the Cyclone is the Cypher. It's a new type of weapon, Electron. Here are its stats:

Name: CC-9F "Cypher"
Type: Electron
Level: 10
Refire Rate: 1
Hull Damage: 7500
Shield Damage: 500
Range: 1500
Speed: 1500
Energy Usage: 900
It is a fixed fire weapon. Which means you cant rotate the gun. If you want to have success you'll have to line your shot up. Also, if you're gonna shoot the Cypher don't miss as it uses a ton of energy. And make sure to bring with some guns to work the shields of the other ships down too. These will just ping a shield. Cost: $300000, pre-equipped with Cyclone purchase, only obtainable through purchase of Cyclone. Which means you'll have to get on the good side of the Outcasts and Lane Hackers
And remember...the AI will have these guns too
