Last Hope : FinalStand Thread

This mod is part 1 of 2. Half of the factions in this part will have their own faction equipment.

This newest version will have -

All bribes will be removed. This will pervent the game from being very easy by just paying some NPC and becoming friendly. Not going to work here.

New Faction Navy/Pirate Version of equipment will be sold by high ranking stores to people with a very good rep with that faction.

There will about 6 to 12 new ships in this new version. Most will be faction issued ships.

Up to 35 new/custom weapons will be made for this new releas and Gunboat, Cruiser, and BS weapons will be made for those ships to be able to add things like more missiles and torps and anti-fighter turrets, Ion Cannons.

Capital Planet Citys like New London, will have BS spawns and their Elite fighters around the planet.

Everything besides nanos and batts take cargo space and will be listed in this way.

Loose guns = 1
missiles 1 ec. = 1
Torps 1 = 3
Nukes = 5
Large Ammo Charges 1 = 0.100000
Med. Ammo Charges 1 = 0.010000
Small Ammo Charges 1 0.001000
Items = 1
Nano bots and Batts = 0

NPC are much harder, I am using AI Deathwish by Warzog.
All Cruisers,Gunboats, Battleship armor have been set higher so that it is hard to kill them. Should take a group of 3-4 good fighters to take a fleet out if they don't get support on them.

I will be resizing all BS models, they will be much bigger and I will keep them that way if no problems arise. I am aware that making them bigger may make the textures look bad/ causes problems with them spinning around like crazy. Boo hoo!

That is all the ideas I have right now for the release, I will be taking ideas for anyone who wants too. You can find the forum right here. There are also pictures from the older versions on the main site.
You have to sign up in order to see the rest of the forums, this will pervent the amount of troble someone can do.

You may also check older versions :

Last Hope 0.5 Alpha
Note also that older versions have a greater chance of crashing and newer ones have a less chance becuase in the newer ones I got to fixing the bugs.

Release Date is soon

Pictures on the main site has been updated

I am taking names for weapons,ships, limited items, system planets and system names. This will be ending soon due to the fact that this version is the second to last being released!

The Real Rep. Changes is that now if you kill 6 or 8 NPC of that faction, you will become all the way red with them, and it takes a lot of time to gain back their trust and to earn their faction weapons. So it's almost impossible to become an all friendly account. Still being worked out a little, but it's working pretty fine for me and is a very nice add on.


All Weapons from the older versions can be bought without having a friendly rep, most new weapons made will be faction weapons.
