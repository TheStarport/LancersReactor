Actually, the Crow nebula is the whole, big, lightblue thing in the northern part your "all of sirius"-navmap. The long white thing to the left is the Barrier, the green thing to the right is the Edge Nebula. I forgot the name of the orange thing in the south.

You'll find parts of the Crow Nebula in many of the sigma and Kusari systems, just like you can find parts of the Barrier in multiple systems (some of the Tau Systems, and in some Independent worlds (Magellan, for example).

So "The crow nebula in Sigma 19" is perfectly fine. That particular part of the Crow Nebula might have a separate name (just like many parts of the Crow Nebula in Kusari have different names).
