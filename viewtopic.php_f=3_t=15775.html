OddjobXL

Mon May 05, 2003 2:05 am by OddjobXL

Yeah, the mines thing happens anywhere there are mines or any hazard like that. Doesn't matter if the faction put them there themselves. I call it the bug-light zapper effect. It's really annoying and immersion breaking but, eh, just don't hang out where the mines are if it really annoys you.

About reps changing - I think I've figured this one out having had it happen to me before. A faction owns the tradelanes and a station. This faction might be hostile to you. If it starts shooting and that faction is 'more friendly' with passing combatant ships then, regardless of their affiliation with you, those combatant ships will attack you. As weird as it is this makes sense as certain areas are dominated by certain folks and it behooves the explorer to know how all the factions fit together as much as possible. A friendly freighter passing through space hostile to you might seem to become suddenly hostile itself - but it's just checking how friendly it is with everyone involved before picking sides and if it's 'more green' with the local dominant faction, or any passing ship that's hostile, it will fire on you.

This wierdness can play in your favor. After a colorful career with The Mollys and Corsairs running gold, small arms and artifacts I'd made some enemies at Bowex among other places. Later on I'd mostly reformed and was on good terms with law enforcement in Kusari and Rheinland. Well, I was running a load of diamonds to New Tokyo when I popped through a jumpgate to encounter a bunch of hostile Bowex transports with escorts. These were law abiding traders who were fine until they went 'red' on me and suddenly my now friendly Kusari police came in to wipe them out.

You can see them wondering the same thing you are "Hey, the Kusari police are neutral to me - why are they shooting if I attack this (ex-)Molly ship? Why is the spaceport shooting at me too? Why are Samuri transports suddenly hostile?" The answer is, because I've done the Police alot of favors and they're not allied to Bowex. Bowex ship went red on me, that made the Kusari police go red, and then the station and Samura freighters, allied with the Kusari police, went red on the Bowex ships too.
