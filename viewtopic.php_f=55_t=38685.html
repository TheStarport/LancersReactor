Hi, in responce to your problem, I am a PC tech at an Audiotronic outlet and have dealt with this prob before. You can play, { but better video would have been nice}, first lower your video refresh rate { to lighten load on GPU}, to 65 or 70 Mhz , bring your color settings down to 16bit again free up GPU and ease up on VRAM, and then right click on your freelancer shortcut , choose Properties and then choose Compatibility, Enable Compatibility mode for Win98/me.
then try game goodluck
