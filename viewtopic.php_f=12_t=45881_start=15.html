One post-Glastonbury shower later and... oh sweet jesus. That was the best music festival EVER! 2005 was amazing, but 2007 just blew it out of the water. Highlights for me included Bloc Party, Cold War Kids, a bit of comedy from the legendary Bill Bailey and, oh yeah, Arcade Fire. The Arcade Fire, who's set was so unbelievable, so massively immense, there were people next to me literally crying through it. No word of a lie. About 180,000 people attended the festival and there must have been at least 100,000 watching that set; as far as I could see they were ALL singing along at the tops of their voices. I just came away in a daze, it was...well...moving. Plus, y'know, I got in for free which really was the icing on the cake.
