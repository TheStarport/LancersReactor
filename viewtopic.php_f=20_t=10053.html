Fear Factor

Sat Mar 15, 2003 9:57 am by Fear Factor

No. Because inverting the mouse will screw up the flight-and-turret control systems. Inverting mouse will only work if the firing reticle is locked in the centre of the HUD, and guns fire co-axially with your ship (in other words, your ship's movement directs the guns' bearing) while in Freelancer, it's the other way around: the reticle is free-floating; your guns' bearing directs your ship's movement.

Click here to read a previous thread.

Anyway, welcome to the family, and I really hope you can get yourself used to this control method and squeeze maximum fun out of the game

Technically, none of that is true. It might happen that way if the reversal was done in a higher level of the programs, however, if the reversal is done at a lower level then the difficulties do not occur. There would be no way for the algorithms to know of the reversal.
The reversed mouse would only be needed when in freeflight mode. The switch to freeflight would need to switch the mouse as well.
This could probably have been done easily when the systems were originally being written, but the cost now may be prohibitive.
