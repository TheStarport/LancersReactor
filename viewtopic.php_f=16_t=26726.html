Hey, welcome, hope you stick around.

No more storyline for you I'm afraid.
Go out and take random missions, or
Trade your way to extreme wealth, or
Explore the universe (Higly reccommended! ) There is a lot you haven't seen yet. Note the little red crosses that sometimes show up on your navmap (There's one in Sigma 13 ) check them out for some surprises. There are lot of them.

And if you get bored, try out some mods (modifications). Some add new systems, or new ships, or new weapons and some add all of these into one big mod. Search through the download section and new mods are announced on the mainpage.

If finally Single Player (SP) starts to bore you, try out Multi Player (MP) and meat many other players online with or agains who you can fight, trade, explore.

Have fun out there!
