the Tetragrammaton (Greek derived from Hebrew) is the name given to the four lettered "name" of God in ancient Hebrew, YHVH, spoken as "Yahweh" but erroneously known in the West as "Jehovah" While you see it around in various places, the true significance of the word and the letters Yod, He, Waw and He) that form it are hidden deep in Jewish mysticism and kabbalism (not the rubbish Madonna's into, but the real thing)

YHVH (corresponds to IAUE or "ee-yow-u-way" is only one of the names of God. it corresponds to Heavenly Father I suppose. Other names are Adonai (Lord) Elah or El, Elohim, El-Shaddai and many more. YHVH is the revealed name of God and actually means "I am who I am" or "I am who I will be"
