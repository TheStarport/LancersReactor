fox Unit 01

Wed Apr 18, 2007 2:09 am by fox Unit 01

Well, I'm not going to say much, as it'd be tough for me to give a non-objective review of my own mod. =3

I would indeed recommend checking out the site merlinthewizard recommended. However, if you want to actually try it, I will say that you should try the latest 1.27 beta before anything else. The beta is pretty much done and in final testing phase (report bugs! =D), and there's a 24/7 beta server running the show. Also, if you play by yourself, you will be able to have the AI Companion assist you, a unique feature that allows you to hand-pick a squad of 4 player-like bot wingmen (screenshots in that link).

We don't have that big of the community. Flak is a unique mod and certainly not for everyone. However, people who get involved usually stay and we hope to gain some members when we start our advertising up again. If you do try Flak and like it, I'd highly encourage you to get involved in the forum discussions, as I think you'll find that everyone there is friendly and always happy to see a new face. =)
