That's really a question to ask yourself, when do you feel you have outlived the game? Depends on what you want out of it. You could try finding every wreck in the game, mapping out every anomaly in every sector. Trying to find the best trade route. Try taking the lightest ship you can as deep into enemy territory as you can.(try surviving in unkown with a defender or somesuch). Try some of the mods out there, not a mod person myself, but think that would give you some variety.

Just a few ideas, try setting a goal that you know would challenge yourself, then see how to pull it off.

Anyway, good luck out there.
