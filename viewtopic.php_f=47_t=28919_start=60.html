Captured Pt. 9: The El Signia

Lord Hakkura was holding one of the Rienland Crew members by the neck, and another by the hair. "Put down your guns or they die" Hakkura's voice thundered. By this time Trent had noticed a sniper about 50 yards away, aiming right at Hakkura, Trent knew that the sniper might kill the hostages,. Just as Juni was setting down her rifle, there was the noise of a sniper rifle firing its lasers. Hakkura went down imedatly, and when he was on the ground, everyone could clearly see two laser entry holes in the back of his head as the hostages crawled away. " 'cmon, we need to get to the Rienland and get out of here!" Juni screamed. As Juni, Trent, Orillion, and several others rushed to the Sleeper Ship, Lord Hakkura was getting up. Just as they entered the Rienland and sealed all doors, Hakkura jumped on the hull of the ship and started tearing holes in the Cromandigi-zoid (the hardest metal in the known universe) roof. The Rienland blasted off to space with Hakkura still holding on, and making his way through the hull on his way to the boules of the ship. " Everyone hang on, this could get a little rough." Orillion yelled over the loudspeaker. The ship started to spin in very tight circles, and Trent looked out the window and saw Hakkura lifting right off the hull of the ship. Finally he lost his grip and went hurtaling into the Red Super Giant sun. The Rienland hit its stardrive engiens and started flying away from Centauri. The scanner on the ships control pannel beeped. It was the lost Mexican sleeper ship, the El Signia, Orillion used the ships scanners to look for any signs of life, but there were none. All of the El Signia's passangers were dead. The controle pannel beeped again, this time it was a ship. " Nomads?" Trent asked. "No, it looks like a human craft, but not one ive ever seen before. The radio crackeled, and a voice said somthing in another language that no one could understand. Orillion had the ship translate into english, it was spanish. " You have entered a restricted area, cut your engiens and prepair for termination"

End of Captured Pt. 9

Captured Pt. 10: Meating the Survivors

"Cut your engiens and prepair for termination!" the voice over the radio said. Orillion picked up the microphone and asked the unknown craft for its identifaction. "You are in a restricted area, its you who need to identify your selvs." Again Orillion picked up the microphone. "This is the German sleeper ship Rienland. We need to get back to Sirius to stop a Nomad invasion." There was a gasp on the radio, then the voice said "Your tring to stop the Nomads? Fine, follow me and land on that planet." Everyone was puzzeled, the same ship that was going to kill everyone 15 seconds ago was now giving them an open inventation to land. The Rienland touched down on the frozen surface of the icy planet. A man came walking up to the people slowly making their way out of the Rienland, he said ¿ "Usted es los tring de parar los Nómadas, corrija?" No one could understand him, then he said "Si es hombres que usted necesita, toma tanto como usted quiere. Somos las únicas personas que sobrevivieron el choque de El Signia Hay sólo acerca de seis mil. Pero por favor, toma algo usted necesita, los Nómadas se deben parar." Orillion and Juni put on their language translators. "This is spanish," Orillion turned to the man and said "Repita por favor." The main said " Tome cualquier usted necesita, la raza humana necesita ser libre de los Nómadas." Orillion pulled out his translator and hit the TRANSLATE button. The translator beeped three times and then said "Take whatever you need, the human race needs to be free from the Nomads." Orillion then said "We need some men, a few of your best ships, and alot of guns. We have a plan that might take Sirius back from the Nomads." then he hit the TRANSLATE button again, and the translator said "Necesitamos a algunos hombres, algunos de sus mejores barcos, y de alot de fusiles. Tenemos un plan que quizás tome Sirius espalda de los Nómadas." Trent was compleatly lost, he had never heard of the Spanish language before, and now Juni and Orillion were having a converstation with him. Orillion handed the man a translator and showed him how to use it. He spoke into the translator and hit the button and the translator beeped, then said "We have some very fast ships, we can spair you about one hundred and fifty men. But this is the first I have heard about Sirius being taken over, we are the El Signia survivors, we crashed here about fifty years ago, and havent been able to contact any one but our own clan. Im comming with you, the Nomads distroyed our solar system, and we need to get even with them." "Fine, you can come, but you will have to do exactly what I say." Orillion told the man. As everyone was walking away, the man yelled his name out. "Enrique" Over the radio Trent could hear him ordering people around, and telling them to suit up. He could only understand this because Orillion had given him a translator. As Hundreds of ships launched to the sky, Juni walked over to trent, "Hey, you ready?" she said. "Yeh, I just wish King could be here to help out with this." Orillion yelled for Trent and Juni to hurry up. As they got on the Rienland, and blasted off, in plain site was a jumphole that led right to Sirius. Orillion had the ship run some tests and found out that the jumphole came out right where the Texas jumpgate used to be in the New York system. All of the ships went through, and came out in view of the ruiens of the Texas jumpgate, and on the radar screen, there were about 10 Nomad Battleships heading right for them.
