This is what i did when i was 15. I did not even understand english enough to write anything. I began to read some Forgotten realms novels (the avatar trilogy) and it's true that i did not understand much of the story but enough to follow what people were doing in it. The more novels i read, the easier it became to know the meaning of sentenses.
So with no particular method, no courses, i learnt english by reading novels (and watching Star trek ) I seldom used a dictionary; i used the probable meaning of the sentense to extrapolate on the meaning of a particular word. With time, when you see a word into enough sentenses, you acquire it's definition without dictionary!
Now i need to develop my vocabulary but there is a trick; every author has it's personal choice of words so if you read from many authors, you will understand more words.
