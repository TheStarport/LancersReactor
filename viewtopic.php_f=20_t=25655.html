It's neither your RAM or your router. I know that for two reasons:
- Your computer's probably been working perfectly up until now. If you had RAM that was bad, in the very BEST case, you'd have wierd things happening all over the place
- It's not your router, because when you play multiplayer with the client and server on the same machine, it stays locally and doesn't even go out to the network. As an aside, I've got a Linksys 8 port router and haven't had an issue.

I'd say one of your mods is conflicting. If you can't enter a couple systems but the rest work fine, this is a clear indication that there's a problem in the ini files now. Try deactivating the mods one at a time, restart FLServer, and join your server again and try it.

Good luck buddy
