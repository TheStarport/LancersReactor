NukeIt

Fri Jun 02, 2006 1:33 pm by NukeIt

They are quite worth the money...mines moreso than missiles, IMHO. Mines cannot be deflected by countermeasures and, properly used, are just as accurate and powerful. They also mount on every ship in the game, which is of use when flying something like a LF- which cannot mount the class 9 Cannonball missiles, but can mount the equally potent Ripper mine launcher.

Then there's the trade lane trick, the guaranteed getaway- drop a mine when you are just passing the trade lane ring on activation. The mine will disable the lane after you have already been whisked away, putting you at the other end of the lane by the time the opposition can cruise to the next ring out.

If only you could set them to remain where you drop them...that would be ever so sweet for bushwacking folks as they enter an area...
