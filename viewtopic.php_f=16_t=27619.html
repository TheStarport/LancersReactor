Switchblade

Fri Jan 16, 2004 5:21 am by Switchblade

I found it really easy, although, of course, the class of ship you ae in affects the handling, and therefore how easy you find it on the fast turns, but I won, first time, in a clydesdale.

take my advice, during the cut scene lead up to the race, hold the SHIFT key. whack "W" the moment the race starts, and you should find that your cruise engines start working at about the same time as Hovis' (being a machine, he'll have a sight edge on you at this point, but his charge time is the same)

the first quarter of teh race is a straight scramble, which Hovis should win through dint of getting his cruise engines going first.

the second and third quarters are the difficult parts, involving a few sharp turns. do it wrong and you'll spin out of the race and lose.

however, if you're in Mouse Flight, and you hug the inside of the turns, you should be able to overtake round here, as Hovis tends to swing wide on the turns.

the final quarter is a straight dive for the finish line. take my advice and ignore the missiles and firepower. you can survive them easily, and trying to evade them will just slow you down.

remember: always take the shortest line possible, and get your cruise engines going as quickly as possible, so that you're on Hovis' heels all the way.
