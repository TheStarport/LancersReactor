If you'll notice, the prison ship is right smack in the middle of the rings. There is a similar set of planetless rings in the Frankfurt system, around that system's prison ship. It must be debris from the prison ships sent into orbit around them- it is also a handy way of finding them. Whenever you see a set of rings with no planet, it's a good bet there's something else in the middle.
