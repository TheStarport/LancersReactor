A way to avoid "Meet Juni at X" mission map and message:

Mark a way point somewhere. Anywhere. It will override the mission waypoints, and get rid of Juni "nagging" at you to join her.

Sometimes, especially when you quit the game and restart, or reload a saved game, your player waypoint dissapears and Juni's mission waypoints show up. No problem, just mark another waypoint somewhere.

When you're ready to get back to the storyline, just open the nav map and "clear all waypoints". The storyline waypoints will show up and guide you back to Juni again (or Hovis, or whoever you need to meet).

Anytime you're given the opportunity to "take on some missions or trade or earn money" between the storyline missions, you can do whatever you want.

You can even go back to Liberty after Mission 3 and become neutral or friendly with Liberty Police, Navy, LSF, etc. Dodge and avoid all contacts (trade lanes are your friends) and go to Colorado. Find Xenos and kill a bunch of them. Eventually LPI, LSF, Navy, and even the Outcasts will become neutral, and the Bounty Hunters, too, if you didn't buy a new ship to throw them off the tracks.

It's a mixed bag, though, because after the next storyline mission they go back to hating you....
