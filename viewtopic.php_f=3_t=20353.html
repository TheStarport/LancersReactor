laughing plasma

Sat Jun 28, 2003 12:08 pm by laughing plasma

OK, here's my 2 cents:


I like using one pair with 3.03 refire rate, and one with 2.00 refire rate. Usually a pair of 'JADE's and a pair of 'DIAMONDBACK's. This is because of the way that shields work. If a shield has only 100 points of strength left, and you smack it with 4000 points of damage, it will only knock the shield down and won't carry through to damage the hull.

With weapons firing in a 'staggered' rhthym (like the config. above), you won't be hitting with all weapons at once, so less damage is wasted in knocking down the last shreds of shields.


I also usually add in a couple of Skyblast B's, to use on fast moving ships or on ships with only a tiny bit of hull-strength left - it saves on energy.
