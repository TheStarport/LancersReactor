Its a spoof hospital drama, that was "supposedly" written and made in the 1980's (obviuosly made recently). The story is that it was "banned" from being shown on TV, and only now is aloud to be seen. Its a complete piss take of 1980's TV perfectly to be honest.

Plot is around the 3 doctors and their manager, set in the hospital:

Main Guy (Garth Merengie)- kinda "action" doc.
Other doctor (male), kinda fake tan, gold earing, quiffed hair, voice of 80's perfection!!

Female doctor: Useless woman, with mindreading powers.

Hospital Boss - 100% wooden acting and lack of any acting skills.


Its of course all on purpose - and its funny........
