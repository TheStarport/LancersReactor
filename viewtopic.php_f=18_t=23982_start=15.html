cD_STRIKER

Thu Sep 18, 2003 2:53 pm by cD_STRIKER

It's not too hard to place hardpoints.....just time consuming. Pick a known location (like a light or other hardpoint) and keep adjusting it's position until you like where it is located.The position co-ordinates work like this:
x.xxxxxx = X co-ordinate....+ moves point towards right
y.yyyyyy = Y co-ordinate....+ moves point upwards
z.zzzzzz = Z co-ordinate....+ moves point towards rear of ship

hint: seems to make it easier for me if I start with a Y co-ordinate that I know is too high.....this makes the hardpoint float above the ship and is easy to see.
