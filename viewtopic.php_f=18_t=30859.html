Thu Apr 29, 2004 11:09 pm by firebase

right download the SDK and edit the Diff2Money.ini...
the first number is the what i call the level range in decimal, the second one is the reward money...

if you want to make it difficult... then you must edit all the system ini located under the Mission folder... mbase.ini to be exact.

look for the DestroyMission parameter

the first decimal is the lowest level range, the second one is the highest level range... refer to diff2money.ini for levels..., the last one is the percentage chance that the faction will give you a mission...

as you can see there are two destorMission parameter one is for NPC and the other one is for Faction... Make sure that they are both the same....

this is only an insight... do more experimenting... and you will have a difficult mission... and maybe create above level 20 mission.

BTW... make sure that the system's npc is in the correct level as you have made in mbase.ini and to be sure check out the npcshps.ini in the Mission folder, it holds the levels of the npc.
