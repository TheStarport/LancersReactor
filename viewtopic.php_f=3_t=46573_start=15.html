Sun Oct 21, 2007 4:43 am by w0dk4

>Or it really is completely serverside. Greatly appreciated if w0dk4 could clarify this.

It is completely server-side. It is stated in the developer notes, there is a timer for every player that has a cloak device but is not cloaked, to send every other player every 0.5 seconds the "uncloak" FL packet, which will make them visible.
Actually, the real breakthrough here is, that the client himself can see the own ship cloak and uncloak (which is not possible with ini-only modding), but since the server handles the cloaking behaviour, the client cannot cheat it.
You can always have a look at the server-side code to understand how it works, escpecially HkCloak and HkUnCloak functions.

And as I said, balancing the cloak is another topic.
Of course it is completely retarded to implement a cloak with infinite duration and no disadvantages.
