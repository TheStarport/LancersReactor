TSA is looking for allies/members

TSA is looking for allies/members
TSA, or The South Alliance is alliance between pirate clans of the 24/7 HomeWorld server, it's an alliance that was started over a year ago on NorwegianHeaven. At the moment it's an alliance between four clans. Blood-Adders, Raiders, TBD and FwF are proud clans that make up this pirate alliance. With the increase of law factions on the server we would like to invite other pirate clans to join us in kicking some butt, TSA should be the greatest alliance around. The only drawback is that clans wishing to ally with TSA would need to control one of the south systems, or systems in direct connection with southern ones, like Omicron Alpha and Omicron Theta. We are not inviting others to join because we are outnumbered, but we invite them to join us since the fun is on rise around here, and we do not want our fellow pirates to miss it. If this strikes you as being a cry for help, than we also invite law factions to come so we would have a lot more to shot down, since the current ones are falling apart. This is an invitation for all pirate clans to join us, but it’s also invitations for people to come and make our server even a better PvP place.
Also, if you are a lonely pirate without a clan, If you are a renegade and would like to join ranks of pirates, please come to the server and give a call to any of the TSA clans. We are looking for new members as much as we are looking for new allies, so anyone who wants to bring chaos to Sirius and its pathetic factions give us a call.

TSA Principles:

1. A Player that is hostile against members of TSA clans will show hostility against all clans in TSA. This includes players on KOS list of clans as well, they will be hunted down and killed by TSA as whole.
2. If a clan wishes to make an alliance with any of the TSA clans, that clan will have to accept alliance from all TSA clans. All TSA clans also have to accept the new clan as well.
3. The clans of TSA can tax players in the system of their allies. This can be done only if no members of that clan are online, or that clans member asks for assistance in Taxing someone. Since we are allies, we need to look for each others territory like its our own.
