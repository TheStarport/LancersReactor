Esquilax

Wed Apr 14, 2004 2:19 pm by Esquilax

A number of previews of nVidia's new "flagship" card have been posted today. There is one on THG, and one on ExtremeTech, as well as others elsewhere. From what I've read (since many of you will be too lazy to read the article ), I'll give you a quick overview. The peformance of the "6800 Ultra" appears to offer superlative performance, such as between 55 and 60 FPS in Halo at a resolution of 1600x1200, but there are some problems. Firstly, nV are STILL using a special bridge to allow for "AGP 16x", which means that the card does not offer PCI Express natively. This will be mistake in the long run in my opinion. Also, nV recommend a PSU of 480W, because you wil need the extra connectors as the 6800 uses two cables, and requires around 110W. Additionally, the graphical quality is not quite as good (at least in Far Cry).

Despite that though, and despite my grudges against nV, I would not be averse to picking up this card. Still, I will be very interested to see ATI new card when it comes out in a few weeks. nVidia is looking pretty good about now...
