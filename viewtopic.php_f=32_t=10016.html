IGx89

Sun Mar 16, 2003 12:01 am by IGx89

Use method "sectionreplace", and put the following tags under it:
[section
The section header, can be more than one line
[/section
[dest
The line(s) you want to change
[/dest
[source
The line(s) to change the dest lines to
[/source
Repeat dest and source for every non-continuous group of lines under that section (if you need to).

Look at the mod scripts included in the beta for examples on this.
