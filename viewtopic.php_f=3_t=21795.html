T-Hawk, minor quibble: the Anubis has the most firepower. 7 forward-firing weapons (6 guns slots + 1 forward-firing turret, all class 6) with a powerplant capacity of 4000. Plus torpedo launcher, of course.

The Drom has the same powerplant as the Drake (capacity 2900) so you have to place your shots carefully, but the Drom can utilize the energy much better than the Drake. Since it has so many weapon mounts it can easily spare two for shield busters without sacrificing too much of the punch against hull armour.

Gung_Ho

Fri Jul 25, 2003 2:42 am by Gung_Ho

These are my opinions:
VS: AI
Freighters are made for freighting, not fighting.
Every second spent fighting, causes profit margins to become slimmer. Time is money.
A skilled pilot will escape all AI enemies, wether he be in a Humpback, Drom, or Rhino. In this case the largest cargo hold wins. Therefor: Drom
A less skilled pilot needs more manueverability to escape. In this case the drom wins.
A completely unskilled pilot does not effectively use manueverability. Therefor the Humpback wins in this case.

VS: PK'ing fighters
All freighters are no match for a fighter of class 6 or higher.
Escape is the only option. Escape is easier with manueverability, therefore the Drom wins.

VS: Other freighters:
Open season. Pick a style and use it. Slow turning CAN be overcome with the proper strategy. I'd give the edge to the drom, but I don't believe it's completely an open and shut case.
