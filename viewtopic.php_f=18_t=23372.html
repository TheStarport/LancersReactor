Right, guys:

If you want to change the Main Menu Music, do this:

Go to Universe\Systems\Intro\Rooms

There you will see 3 INIs

Deccompile them with BINI

Look at them. You will see one thing referring to the .THN file and another referring to the music.

In the Data\music folder, there is another INI called music.ini

In that you'l' see the names of the music to choose from.

Replace the name of one of the music with another from the music.ini

It should then play that music in the main menu screen. Its useful to change all the music in ALL 3 of the INIs in Intro\rooms to make sure that the music is changed
