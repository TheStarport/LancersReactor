Killer Klown

Mon Mar 17, 2003 7:22 am by Killer Klown

As far as 'best' shields go, I find that's mostly a question of mathematics - basically, the 'best' shield is the one that is strongest against the weapons that do the most damage. For example, a shield that's strong against lasers eventually loses it's value because laser weapons, by themselves, do relatively little damage. I don't have the game infront of me, so I can't give you the exact breakdown of what shield does what, but something that is strong against Plasma or Tachyon weapons usually serves the best - it helps cut down on the ungodly damage those types of weapons do, and the loss of protection against lasers and the like doesn't hurt much.
