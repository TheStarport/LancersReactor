I'm losing patience here - STOP COPYING AND PASTING from other sites! make a compilation if you must, but if you took them from other sites, just link it to the original site instead of pasting the entire text. There is a whole bunch of legal and ethical issues involved here. For example, if your original source site charges for access, or displays banners/popups/online store for THEIR visitors, and TLR simply "steals" their visitors, because they would rather visit a banner/popup free site that TLR is. It is of course more convenient to the visitors, but it undermines our partner TLR sites - rather unfairly.

To sum up:
1. Remove all the pasted text.
2. REPLACE pasted text with links to their original source.
3. You are allowed to put your own text addition to the links, provided that YOU are the author of that text.
4. If the said source sites have gone offline/retired and cannot be found anywhere else, then you are allowed to paste the text.
5. Minimize the amount of posts you make (fit as much content possible into one post) so that the Mods don't suspect you of spamming.

Respect intellectual copyrights! I notice that you do credit your sources - that is well-intentioned but unfortunately not enough. Contents of a website are often meant to attract visitors, ie to make them shop at their online stores, or attract potential customers to their advertised clients (banners, links, etc). Some sites depend on these stuff, and some who don't might just like the intellectual gratification or the popularity or quantity of hits/popularity. We should not deny these to them.
