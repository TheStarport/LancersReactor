Thu Jun 05, 2003 5:24 pm by SpyderMurphy

Yes there is a way to copy a character, but its long winded and only works on servers you have full access to.

firstly it isn't easy to figure out where the character is actually stored
("my documents\my games\freelancer\saves\" or some thing similar, i am at work; can't check)

Characters are stored in folders with very unfriendly names.
eg. "8745-23923"
each of these folders relates to someones login on the server, files with in this folder relate to thier characters. To copy the character you need to know the correct folder to place them in on the server you wish to connect to.
You need to have logged onto both servers in order for your folders to have been generated by the game. both servers will have different names for your login (great!)

once you have figured out which files to copy where and have copied them you can log in and play with your favourite character!

Personally I would have thought that saving characters locally would have been better. Also I don't think you can move characters to and from single player.
