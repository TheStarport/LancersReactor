Liberty Guard: Hey fellas, where do you think you're going?

DA: We want to get on the Liberty.

Guard: Who are you?

DA: We're the Digital Anvil team. We make cool video games.

Guard: Sorry guys. Video games aren't on the list of critical colonization skills. I'm afraid you can't come. Now beat it, I've got to let this agronomy team onboard.
