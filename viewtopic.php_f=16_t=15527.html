Cham

Tue Apr 29, 2003 4:36 pm by Cham

Both the wrecks at the Omicron Alpha Unknown Jumphole and the Omicron Gamma Unknown Jumphole yield loot, though not all of them. A prominent item from Omicron Alpha is the Ripper Mine Launcher.

The Unknown System that can be reached from Omicron Gamma holds planets Gammu (Order robots) and Primus (Order apes), plus their sun, a Red Dwarf. It's a doomed system that's already been sucked into the effect tunnel of the black hole in the top right. The system wasn't finished so the black hole won't do any damage (it's a sun-type star, its corona poses no harm though). Ideally, the blackhole shouldn't shine any light (cf. "unfinished". There is a weird distance distortion when you fly toward the black hole.
