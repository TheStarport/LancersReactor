Yep you are right unless you are near their base where they seem to spawn and get all the goodies. You can tractor back what they can not hold. However they will turn hostile and go guns on you as they want it all (greedy bastards).

I posted this trick a while back but it is worth repeating. When asked to drop cargo "immediately!" hit cruise engine button (remember it takes a few seconds to charge) wait a second for the engines to "warm up" then hit the "Drop Cargo" button and hit "B" just as quick. You will beam back your cargo and be at cruise almost the same second. A word of caution do not use "Go To" as they will fire disruptors and ruin your day. Instead fly at an angle and juke like heck. I even drop a couple of CM's just to make sure if there are a bunch of them.

This trick has saved me money on more than one of my runs for profit and it is funny to hear them say stuff like "What the......it was just here!", Or "My Loot!", there are others but you get my point. Yes you will be red and hot at that base or with that group so if you are trying to change your rep just dump and run.

Also someone mentioned that they just dumped their "bulk" cargo. The screens I have seen are specific in the request of the item to be dropped so that isn't going to work from what I have seen at least in SP.

Just some thoughts from the edge of space.
