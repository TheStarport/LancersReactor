No, you don't have to restart. What you do have to do is add the relevant Hardpoints into the ship's listing in shiparch.ini, or you won't be able to mount things on those Hardpoints (although you could put them on AI ships through Loadouts.ini, which bypasses this information, which could be handy for modders wanting the AI to have a gun/extra shield/whatever that players couldn't have).
