The best trade route I've found is this one:

Diamonds New Berlin ( 396 ) to New Tokyo ( 1650 ); Optical Chips New Tokyo ( 88 ) to New Berlin ( 400 ). Using a 250-cargo Humpback and the Sigma 13 jump hole ( Sigma 13 5C <-> New Berlin 5E ), you can pull in a 393,000 credit profit in under 20 minutes. I've timed the round trip repeatedly, and every time I finish it in 18-20 minutes, including the occasional lane hack or jumpgate queue.
