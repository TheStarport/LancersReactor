Tau-37 (Where the crazy people at Falklands Base reside)

I've flown through this system so many times... but only three days ago, did I realise that there was ANOTHER STAR in the system! The twin-whites are actually NOT the only occupiers. The third sun is even on the nav-map, but I've been concentrating so much on skirting the twin-whites' corona that I've never paid much attention to it.

The yellow star is most easily found by leaving the Falklands base and heading to the LEFT of the twin-whites, and after emerging from the Niobium field, looking up a little, and maybe to the left a touch. A small little yellow star...

Of course, if the creators had looked up some reference material about stars, they'd have realised that things like the green stars are something purely out of science fiction, and that beasts like the red giants would have filled most of the system map and vaporised all of the planets through shedding their outer layers of burning gas.

Still, FL isn't a posterbook of accurate astronomical data, and we shall not get into that argument here.
