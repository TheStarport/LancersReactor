I had to be on something, you were right all, there are better ships than H. Defender in Bretonia. Nevertheless, yesterday, after I post the orig. post here I went for a last attempt to break free from Tau-37 and I made it . After I emerged in Kyushu (I took jumphole which also "magically" emerged there to save me ) there were something like 10+ Hawks, but apparently I had to develop some skills back in Tau systems, since I was able to last enough to finally got helped by local authorities.
Having been inspired by several posts here about light fighters you can guess, what I am flying now and I must admit it is indeed *a* difference. Riding Heavy Defender was like driving truck now I feel like having much closer touch with surrounding. Heavy Defender also used to loose its equipment very easily. Several times I made it through with bare hull only, with no wings, no CM, no Turret and no missile . For the L.F. I was at first afraid that changing from 5/1 configuration to 4/0 would be in fact loss, now I am happy I did it.
Since I was already carrying bunch of interesting weapons around (not being able to mount them in H.D) I used them now and they also bring some new experience to the fights. I noticed I prefer challenging way when doing my stuff so I mount two Pyros Type 2 (salvaged somewhere) and these two not even look graphically nice, but give some nice punch to enemies too . With those (and two lesser weapons) mounted (I ditched missiles for the preference of guns) the whole route back to Dublin system became quite easy. And when I finally arrived there I was told we are heading to "dangerous" Border World systems - ).
