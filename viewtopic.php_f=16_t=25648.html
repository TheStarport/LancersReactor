Wed Oct 29, 2003 4:46 am by [NN]Crossbow

As I fly for the Corsairs on Bobways (www.bobway.com), right now my "homes" would have to be on Cadiz and Manhattan. I usually fly back and forth between these two, as there is heavy Outcast traffic to be found in the independent worlds between them and hence frequent opportunities for popping cardamine haulers. My philosophy is simple: Why buy it when you can TAKE IT??!!

Right now, I am flying the Corsair Light Fighter with an assortment of high-end Corsair pulse and neutron weapons. Although I can more than afford even the Titan, I just like the Legionnaire because it handles better than most heavy fighters yet boasts the same heavy armour for which the Corsairs are so notorious.

Victor's 1.62 mod, available at Bobways, makes most fighters able to carry up to level nine weapons and also allows you to buy most tradeships and warships available in the game. Hence, you are able to fly the ship that you like rather than that which can just carry the best guns. You can even buy a CSV and make a living collecting scrap around Beaumont if you like. It's a dynamic universe out there.
