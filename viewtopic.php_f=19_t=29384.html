Wed Mar 24, 2004 10:22 pm by shaitanro

Mind not to make a confusion between your LAN speed and your Internet connection (or WAN) speed. Usually you have some router, modem or media convertor to connect to the Internet and the link between your computer and this device is 100 Mbps. The link from this device forward on is the one that matters. If unsure may it is always a good ideea to ask your ISP.

Anyway if your connection bandwidth is indeed 100 Mbps, you should divide it to 50 Kbps per player => 102400 / 50 = 2048 players, if you have the CPU and RAM for it
