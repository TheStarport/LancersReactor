Tue Apr 11, 2006 12:59 am by SWAT_OP-R8R

if the ship disappears at the edge on the screen then its because of a bounding box

if it disappears in the middle of the screen then its the LOD... and like it already has been said.... custom ships usually only use 2 values for the LOD
so if your ship accelerates the distance between it and the camera gets higher so your LOD (2nd value) is not high enough to show the ship anymore

other players wont see your ship either in mp or only if they are very close to it
