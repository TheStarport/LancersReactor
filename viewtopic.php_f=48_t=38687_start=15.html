Agreed with Louva above on that. I think you will find most of the people who know anything about THN's have already answered. Only two other people spring to mind who have gone deep into the THN's recently, Wasabe and myself. However Wasabe is rarely around these days as he is busy with real life from what I understand while myself I am too wrapped up with getting all the Freeworld's new landing, take off and room THN's replaced for 1.66's release to be off any use. I will probably write some tutorial up at some point about what I've learnt on THN's, but FW 1.66 is my main focus.

There are probably a few others out there with working knowledge of THN's, but you will be extremely lucky to get hold of them (most are either to engrossed in other projects or are not active these days). Most Freelancer coders are still at the ini stage, very few go onto deal with THN's. The best advice I can give is do what I did, go through and read what tutorials and threads are available on THN's here on this forum (using the Search feature) and then pick apart what THN's that exist in Freelancer comparing that information. Also, give yourself a crash course in Vector Mathematics, if you don't know it already, as it will be needed for orientating in the THN's.
