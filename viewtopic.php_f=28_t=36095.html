Freeworlds TC 1.65 (the "Star Wars" mod)

The Freeworlds Mod team would like to invite you to try out our latest and greatest version of the Freeworlds Total Conversion mod. This mod attempts to completely transforms Freelancer into a Star Wars universe. While there are limits to what can be done with the Freelancer engine, this mod does the best it can to immerse the player into the world of Star Wars.

This mod is MP only.

Features:

-Over 100 Star Wars ships available to purchase, all complete with infocards
-11 complete systems to explore, including Bastion, Corellia, Hoth, Y'Toub, Kessel, Kuat, Coruscant, Calamari, Bilbringi, Bespin, Tatoo
-62 Bases scattered across the galaxy, all complete with infocards
-All systems and bases complete with custom infocards, NPC's, missions, bribes, custom rumors, commodities, mining, wrecks, star wars music, etc
-28 custom Star Wars factions, all with complete reputation balances. Factions include the New Republic Navy, New Republic Security, Rogue Squadron, Jedi Knights, Sith Brotherhood, Imperial Navy, Imperial Elite, Imperial Research, Hutt Syndicate, Smugglers, Bounty Hunters, Black Sun, Disac Pirates, Human League, Peace Brigade, Corellian Sector Authority, Corellian Security Forces, Corellian Engineering Corp, Arakyd Industries, GeneTech Corp, Golan Arms Corp, Sienar Fleet Systems, Commerce Guild, Trade Federation, BlasTech Industries, Kuat Drive Yards, Independent Miners, Civilians
-New commodities and new economy, including illegal smuggling runs
-Star Wars weapons with custom graphic effects, like Turbo Lasers, Quad Lasers, Proton Torpedos, green lasers for Imperials, red for New Republic.
-Custom NPC's, like Wookies, Imperial TIE fighter pilots, Calamari, Chiss, Bounty Hunters with Mandalorian armor, etc
-Upgradable Sensors and Power Plants
-Players drop their cargo when killed (aka player loot)
-Server runs more efficiently and uses less memory than "vanilla" Freelancer

This mod is freely available to be used on any server. The most popular server is Burnout's 24/7 USA server, which is strictly Role Play. That means that if you want to fly one of the better ships, you will have to join a major faction. In order to join a faction, you must register on the USA forums. You can do that here: http://usa.assassinsden.com/

Freeworlds 1.65 can be downloaded right here on Lancer's reactor: http://www.lancersreactor.com/t/download/download.asp?id=1338

Or you can download it from the 24/7 USA server page.

Have fun!
