Intro:

This is a topic concerning my faction (like an informal clan), FU. This topic is mainly for the benefit of FU members, but all are welcome to read/post. Topics covered will be: Aim/goals, Faction News, Member List, and Faction Protocal.

Aim/Goal:

The purpose of FU is simply a group of people playing together. FU members help each other out, chat, and enjoy playing. If this intrests you, Give either me or LV_38Eagle a page on Sir Warwick's server.

Faction News:

Member List:

(note: This is for members on Sir Warwick's server, if you were a member on Elite, notify me) FU_Cid22011, LV38_Eagle, FUBlowfish, FUZeRo. (There are brakets before and after the FUs, but for some reason they do not show up here)

Faction Protical:

Under no circumstances will any FU member unprovokedly attack and destroy any clan member's ship (unless otherwise directed by either me or LV38_Eagle in a time of war), Failure to follow this will result in immeditate explusion. This is the only rock hard rule of FU. Members are recommended to be polite and considerate to other players (unless the person in question is RPing a pirate). It is considered a requirment to help other FU members, but there are no repurcusions for refusing to (except for the dismay of the rest of us). No FU member has the right to order another member around (me and LV38_Eagle may give strong suggestions in a time of war however). If a user is just starting out on Sir Warwick's server, (s)he is elligible for 8k to start out. I will give this out when possible, as will Eagle if he agrees.
