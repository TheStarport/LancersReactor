The price at the shipdealer is the cost of the hull plus all of the other pieces added in the package (goods.ini). The hull is set to 100,000 cr for the Corvette (the same for all of the other Star Wars mods by that author) but the remainder of the price is the sum of the other equipment in the package. You would have to change not only the hull price (to say, 1cr) but would have to go through and change all of the other prices as well.
