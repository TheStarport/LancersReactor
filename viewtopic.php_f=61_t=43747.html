Modders have to be real careful in New York. So much of the game is tied to that system that it will sometimes even crash if you change the ids_name or Ids_info o on something in that system.

All of those errors are in the MOD you are running. Run the CRC tool available on this site and you will easily be able to located the bad entries. Some of them listed are a bit cryptic and require much more digging to fix.

When you say you crash on level 3 , do you mean your ranking level or the mission 3?
