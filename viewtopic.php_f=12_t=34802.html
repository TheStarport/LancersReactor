Okay, to be more coherent this time.

There are 3 programs I use.

Sonic Foundry Sound Forge. Best wave editor. Ever. No competition, and i've tried the rest. Great for trimming the fat from recorded samples.

Sonic Foundry Acid PRO 4.0. Useful if you use .WAV based samples, not good if you're trying to make loops from scratch. Lack of LFO and formula controller plugins makes it an assembler for me.

Fruityloops FL Studio 4.5.
Loop creation, compisition and plugin heaven. Once learned, never forgotten, rarely beaten. Exellent intergration of effects, plus formula controllers and LFOs. The main program I use. Requires a basic knowlege of music theory to mater fully.

There are others, but I dont have full versions, and i dont want to resort to piracy, the ones i have suit me fine.
