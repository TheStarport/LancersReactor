jedierrant

Thu Jul 17, 2003 8:05 pm by jedierrant

To mine, go into a asteriod field. In the field move you cursor over one of the small spinnings rocks. Your crosshairs should start spinning. Fire one gun and one gun only (too many guns and you blow up what you want to mine). If you've got a commodity a white square should appear in front of you. Push "B" to tractor it in. Not every rock will have something. If you want to make mining easier, go to you menu and turn off "background dust". That will make the minable rocks more obvious.

Just bear in mind that it is in most cases faster and earier to buy the commodity you're mining at the nearest base. Mining is only a real option if you are bored, very poor, or very cheap. There's always some mining company offering niobium or H-fuel at very low prices.
