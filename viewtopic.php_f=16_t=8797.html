Zone 21 - NewYork - Alaska - Minefield Entrance Found

** I've found the entrance to the minefield in Zone 21 ***

(1) Get to the New-York System and get to the Battleship Missouri.

(2) Point your ship down the New-York-Texas TradeLane.

(3) About 16 K down from the TradeLane / Battleship (NB: Before jumping into the TradeLane, put a waypoint on the Battleship Missouri as a ref.) - Escape the Tradelane. (Press Esc key) - You should be roughly by a TradeLane ring construction.

(4) Pointing to your waypoint (towards the Battleship) turn your ship 90 Degrees to your right. You should now be pointing roughly at the NY -> Alaska Gate.

(5) Select the ALL option off your Contact list (bottom right), and select the gate in the list.

(6) If you're pointing in the right direction and at the right target, You should now have the focus/target set to the NY -> Alaska Gate in the distance in front of you.

(7) Goto your Nav/Map (F6) and clear All waypoints and set a New Waypoint on the NY -> Alaska gate. The gate should be approx 26+K away

(8) As long as you keep on course for this waypoint you shoud fly down the narrow passage in the minefield.

Note: You can use your CruiseEngine for most of the way there, but I'd suggest turning it off once you start seeing mines clearly - as you wouldn't want to slam into a wall of mines, as you'll probably need to fine tune your (up/down) in relation to the minefield entrance, the closer you get to it, as I had to do.

Once near the entrance (you can't miss it now!) you should be able to fly right on in.. The entrance is approx 100ft wide. Unfortunately the gate itself doesn't activate

Well, I hope this has helped... - Happy Flying
