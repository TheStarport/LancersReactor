@Poojah: The easiest way it to think of the mod and multi play as totally separate issues. If you like Freelancer for example and wanted to play against others you do not necessarily have to play a mod, you could play the original verion in multi player. If however you enjoy the game but wanted to try new aspects such as new systems, ships or weapons then you could modify the original game to suit your own imagination. Combine the two and you have what is most common, the Multi-player modified version.

Freelancer is I suppose rare in that the servers are run privately by fans of the game and rare too in the number of mods done while new release games are marketed by the game producer as a Multi-player and the "mods" are their expansions.

Otherwise...this game certainly looks like it is worth a look. Hopefully it will be playable though as the last time I gave a space sim other than FL a try it was X3 and I gave up on the flight controls after about an hour.
