BLEEP!
A outcast attack.
Gordon ran to the shelter, and watched the cctv footage of them.
The attack appeared normal at first, but Gordon noticed 1 of the ships was carrying a torpedo launcher.
It could do serious harm to the station!
SIZZLE!
Gordon felt frighted as the torpedo slowly moved towards the station.
The life pods!
He jumped into a life pod...
He tried opening his eyes but he was too weak...
It was too late.
