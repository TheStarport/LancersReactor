Mon Apr 14, 2003 3:41 am by pressgod

It depends upon what one wishes to be, (amazing just like the game promised). I like the Titan for exploring as the extra armor is handy if "jumped" by baddies or when in radiation zones. It also has some ok cargo space so you can pick up a few coins along the way.

The Sabre or Eagle if you choose to be a bounty hunter and just love those kill ship missions. There are those who can't live without the thrill of the hunt but also love the slaughter later! LOL

And if you want to be a cargo hauler, make nice and smile just right, then go get yourself a Dromedary for freight. The 285 cargo space is nice to make a buck with and it has a good armo, nanobot /shield batt. rating as well. It also isn't quite the pig the Humpback is when turning!

Just a quick note to those who thought the SP mission was too easy or simple to complete. You should try playing the entire thing again as a freighter pilot! I believe you will find the challenge more than enough to keep your blood pumping!

Just some observations from the edge of space.
