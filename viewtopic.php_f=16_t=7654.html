If you want to be completely legal.. a triangular route:

First, buy a Rhino freighter at planet Los Angeles, California. You may wish to get a level 3 shield from Willard Research station, just below the California Minor-San diego Border station tradelane. Now:

Planet Los Angeles (California): Buy Polymers, take to Planet Denver (Colarado)
($161 per unit x 80 = $12,880)

Planet Denver: Buy Optronics, take to Planet Huston (Texas)
($308 per unit x 80 = $24,640)

Planet Huston: Buy Engine Components, take back to Los Angeles.
($374 per unit x 80 = $29,920)

That's $67,440 per round trip. You will have to survive the odd ambush, but just go into cruise if you get into trouble, they don't use cruise disrupters in Liberty.

Although it takes a while to amaass any real profit, there is an easy trade route for beginners in New York.

Grab a Rhino on Manhattan, load up with Water and take it to Planet Pittsburgh. Then, at Pittsburgh, grab as much boron as possible (may take a while to get enough cash to buy a full load) and take it back to Manhattan. Quick, reasonably safe and totally legal.
