Copperhead

Fri Apr 04, 2003 4:24 am by Copperhead

Shooting Police, Bounty Hunters and especially the DSE facility in Hokkaido are quick & dirty ways to improve reps with ALL pirate factions.

A word of caution: Don't go overboard. The police and bounty hunters are almost everywhere. Shooting DSE sounds harmless enuff, but I learnt the lesson well.

Shoot DSE and the negative effects include:
Their allies - Liberty Police, Liberty Navy, Liberty Security Force, Liberty Corporations
Their competitors - Daumann Heavy Construction, Samura Heavy Industries, Bretonia Mining & Manufacturing

It seems that once their competitors get the word, they tip off their friends in high places.

Allies of Daumann Heavy Construction - Rheinland Police, Rheinland Military, Republican Shipping
Allies of Samura Heavy Industries - Kusari Police, Kusari Naval Forces
Allies of Bretonia Mining & Manufacturing - Border World Exports, Bretonia Police, Bretonia Armed Forces, Planetform

I was once overwhelmed by Kusari Police & Naval Forces after a "routine" rep fixing mission in Hokkaido. I fondly call that last mission "End Game".

Juz be aware of the butterfly effect.
