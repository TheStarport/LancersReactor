Ok here we go

sakke: this may be a problem outside of the ship model. it is probably an error with goods.ini, if you made an error with that file. it would crash the game when you click on the ship icon. if you post your script from goods.ini (for your ship) i can help out.

aquilus and frost beast: here is a website that has assorted tools for Gmax, the second link down is Md3 importer for Gmax... click here

slig: your problem would be with improper use of the .dll, it is a very tricky kink in modding, and freelancer is very picky about it. consult catscratchers tutorial on dll editing (it is for weapons but you should be able to use it for ships) click here the second post of the thread concerns .dll editing.

archon: freelancer inverts the ship in the game. so you have to flip your model upside down in your modeling program prior to exporting, you also have to invert your textures if you want them to show up the same way
