Lagging horrifically! Help!

I have a friend who plays freelancer.

He has a 1.3ghz machine with a decent ATI graphics card.

If you load an AUTOSAVE right before the fight, it will run nice and smoothly, but the moment he has been fighting for about 5 minutes, about 75% of the gametime is spent looking at a frozen screen. Thu Apr 24, 2003 8:27 am by Murgen

Microsoft has a decent FAQ about Freelancer for ATI's. I assume this is a Radeon? Go to Hardware acceleration for your friend's videocard and move it one notch from maximum. That solved the problems for my ATI Radeon 9500pro.

It will not harm anyone to check his amount of RAM and the way the swap-file is configured. Is it dynamic (enough space on the HD) or fixed (which is much faster).

And finally ... close all other applications. Especially monsters like Kazaa.
