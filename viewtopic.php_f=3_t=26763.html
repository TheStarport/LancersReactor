gromit

Thu Dec 11, 2003 3:33 pm by gromit

sorry nick, I got busy and totally forgot. Its only 49Kb by the way (aaaah they don't make em like that anymore). One thing to bare in mind though, its pretty slow going in comparison to games nowadays, so give it a chance. It takes some getting used to...but its worth it in the end for the trading alone. If you're not sure what to do, the F* Keys are pretty self explanatory...

Quick start guide...(keys are vague but you'll get the idea)

Buy something (F3 or F2?!), choose a system within range to you (F8 or F7) and has an opposite political frame (ie. agricultural/technological etc - avoid anarchy systems to start with), Launch using F1, "H" to hyperspace over there...aim for a planet, or if you can't see one turn your back to the sun, "J" to jump towards the planet....look for the orbiting space station, attempt to dock. Repeat until you get used to docking (frustrating at first). Ignore the basic crappy graphics and try to get a feel for the game itself. It took me almost 2 years of solid play to make elite, so bare that in mind if you think you're not getting anywhere!

Enjoy!
