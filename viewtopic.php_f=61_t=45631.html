E:\FL\Scratch\Source\Common\Systems.cpp(3071) : *** WARNING: get_base(ST04_02_Base) failed.
I think this one means your base isn't listed in Universe.ini, or mbases.ini - one or the other I guess.

E:\FL\Scratch\Source\Common\BaseData.cpp(339) : *** WARNING: base name ST04_02_Base is unknown in market data file ..\data\equipment\market_misc.ini

This one means your base doesn't have an entry in the market files. Even if you're not selling anything, you should still have an entry with nothing below it. So this would be enough:

[BaseGood <--add bracket
base = ST04_02_Base
