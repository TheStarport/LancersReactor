Ro9ue

Sat Mar 27, 2004 8:24 am by Ro9ue

Hello, rello. Good to see a new face; it's great for the FL community to grow some more.
This mod is (as you might hace guessed) one of the very best, in some people's opinion. It takes many ideas, features, ships, weapons, and other things (i.e. TIEs, X-wings, and Rebebl Blasters from Star Wars) from various different sources and combines them with other new and cool features.
The list of all that's added/changed is far too long to sit and read through (much less type!), but here are some brief and basic ones:
- New ships (close to 100), weapons, star systems, bases, factions, and equipment (like shield powercells [speed up shield regeneration rate, and your ship's powerplant & engines [buy very good powerplant= not so much drained weapon energy)
- Original FL ships have improved stats (increased weapon mounts, more cargo, higher tech levels)
- Battleships encounters, where patrols of capitalships, destroyers, and gunboats escorted by fighter wings pop up to maybe raise a little ruckus
- New Random Mission types and payments (a $440 thousand mission is kinda run-of-the-mill in the Omicron systems)
- Greatly stepped-up AI: they use Nanobots/Shield Batteries, and their weapon loadouts have been seriously overhauled; a nice challenge, and even better loot
- New Jumpholes link close systems (like Tau-37 and Chugoku) to permit much easier and faster travel across the Sirius sector
- More updates and additions to come (I think) from the designers

That's a little summary; the reason there wasn't too much detail about it is b/c it's an established mod that's been continually improved and enhanced for a long time now, and there have been so many changes that it would not be easy to list all of them.
Try it out to find more things that I might have forgotten-- believe me, you won't regret it!!
PS - also check out RebalanceMod.com , the new Rebalance v3.4 site, for a little more. Welcome to the larger dimension of FL, and happy hunting
