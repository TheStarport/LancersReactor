MegaStar

Tue Jun 24, 2003 8:42 pm by MegaStar

The hack listed in the other topic does not work (or at least I have not been able to get it to work) but this one will. It is definitely not the most elegant solution but I am guessing that you do not really care and just want to move on with the game and your life. Open Folder Options from the View or Tools menu bar (depending on your operating system) in Windows Explorer. Choose the View tab and uncheck the "Hide extensions for known file types" option. Decrypt m06.ini (located in \Freelancer\DATA\MISSIONS\M06 directory) to m06.ini.txt. Change line 775 of the file from "Act_ActTrig = com_0170" to "tm_dexterhumbled" (all without quotes). Load your game and after listening to Dexter yell at the weapons platform for the last time the game with register that you have beaten him and instruct you to fly back to the battleship hood.
