Chips

Wed Apr 27, 2005 1:09 pm by Chips

We always found that having "go kill this particular nomad named xxxx" was detracting from the fact that they have no individual names, no stations etc etc.

Seeing them occupy a shipyard, or any other normal station was pretty much a travesty! To that end, edit your Vignette Params.ini file in the random missions folder - so that you can have them ONLY being in "kill ships" missions.

One of these is:
[DataNode
node_id = 593

I haven't been bothered to go through the whole file and work out which one is which... I was far too lazy after I got through about 20 different 'pick the loot' types... and gave up.

Hope that info helps to those who don't want extraneous mission types against factions.
