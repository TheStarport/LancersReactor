Freelancer: What could have been?

The story seems to end awkwardly. It looks like my English exam, when I realize that I don't have enough time to complete the composition. Which's: Pick a good point that's not actually the ending and stop the story there.

Additionally the developers said in one of their interviews that the game was designed, so that if your rush through the SP story campaign, you'd have difficulties later towards the end. This seems to suggest that the story should not have ended when it did. Coz even if you rush through the campaign you'd still end up well.

You also notice how the ending is so incomplete that you actually need to read your log to realize the game was over?

Also did you realize that trent said that manhattan was not a bad home? But then he changed his mind? Something seems amiss there, not quite right you know.

The planets in the unknown system. They're clearly made for something else, something more... just by looking up the info of those two planets you could tell.

I believe there're other features that realy show some sign of incompletion. Discuss what could Freelancer have been in the original design, if M$ had not rushed the developers.
