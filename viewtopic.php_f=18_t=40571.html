The images in the intro/images folder must have exactly the same name as the originals to work unless you change the links in the freelancer.ini file. If after this then you need to try a different conversion package, I use photoshop and they work perfectly.

As for your info tags it's dificult to say what problem could be without seeing your files. Have you created a new .dll for your mod or are you using the originals. The only thing I can suggest is to make sure the references are correct and that the xml text is formatted correctly. Copy an original one and just change the text. Use FL IDds, it works perfectly well for me.
