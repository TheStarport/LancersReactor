Cland

Sun Mar 23, 2003 5:09 pm by Cland

The reason you have trouble countering disrupters (as opposed to missiles) is missile actually have to hit your ship. Disrupters have a blast radius (EMP) and it just has to be *close*.

Also, counters only last a few seconds then dissipate. If they're not far enough behind you (meaning you have to be thrusting at least to put some distance), the disrupter may indeed be countered and hit the flare, but the EMP still hits you.

That, coupled with the ridiculous speed of the disrupter and the relative snail's-pace in charging the cruise engines means you're probably going to have to thrust and then cut the engines and just coast.
