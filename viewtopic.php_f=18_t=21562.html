Truly Guided Missile

Does anyone remember Descent 3? There was a guided missile you could fire and then your view would change and you would directly pilot it to its target. It was difficult but really fun. If someone could get this in the game it could be used as an "armed" probe or a way to get around countermeasures. Give it a decent blast radius and explosive punch and you would have an excellent tool for long distance destruction of freighter (or capital ship) escorts. Maybe it could use the turret view for direction but with the viewpoint shifted to the missile instead your ship and the fire button the trigger to explode. Of course you would lose the normal turret view but if you don't like turrets or just don't mind, well, enemy go boom from long range anyone?

Oooo, pretty....what do we blow up first?
