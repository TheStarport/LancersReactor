*MOD* Freespace mod for Freelancer

The mod will be released in two parts. Part 1: the GTVA & Part 2: the Shivans

GTVA Mod features:

- New faction (GTVA) [DONE
- 5 to 7 GTVA systems (not enough room for all)
- Freespace GTVA fighters available to player.
- Freespace GTVA capships (perhaps even available to players )
- Beam weaponry
- Freespace GTVA arsenal

Description:

The GTVA part of the conversion adds the GTVA as a new faction to the Freelancer universe. You will be able to trade, loot, fight, etc like you would in any other system. However, because this is a new faction it will also have its own ships and weapons. The Freespace ships and weapons that is

Shivan Mod features:

- New faction (Shivans)
- Several Shivan systems
- Freespace Shivan fighters (Not available to player [perhaps 1 or 2 exceptions)
- Freespace Shivan capships (Not available to player)
- Beam weaponry
- Freespace Shivan arsenal

Description:

The Shivans are a very hostile faction. They kill. And that is all they do. But due to this nature, many scavengers go to their systems to claim the wrecks and claim their remains. This is a very profitable business, but also a very dangerous one. Many end up as wreckages themselves.

The Shivan systems are known to hold great treasures.
-----------------------------

This mod is not just for the Freespace fans but also adds a new dimension to the game for the non-Freespace players. New factions can't be bad We still have a lot of work ahead, but I'm sure we will make this an enjoyable mod for everyone.

Questions? Ask 'm here

- http://dynamic4.gamespy.com/~freespace/forums/ for the Freespace community who supports this.

*NOTE: Not a conversion. The two factions will be added to the existing FL universe
