Hi you can increase the speed of the trade lane by chaning a specific peice of equipment they use. (i've done it for te mod phase 2) under the ini file in the equipment directory called select_equip.ini find the line:
[Tradelane
nickname=basic_trade_lane_eq

This is the equipment thats makes the trade lane solar a workable tradelane. To increase the speed of the tradelane it's self (or decrease) during travel find the line:
spin_accel=15

Changing this number will change the speed of traving throu the tradelane. As with everything expermient but be carefull, i've increased mine to 20 witch works well as it's faster but not to fast to barely notice You can also use this to change the way tradlane effects and other various things works. Hope that helps Happy freelancing
