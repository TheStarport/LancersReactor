Thorkild,

Sorry to disappoint you, but it doesn't work that way.

When you get disrupted, and the pirates demand your cargo, you can hit cruise and get away. It's because the computer keeps the pirates at the ambush point until they succeed in ambushing someone.

If you're in open space, and you run into a neutral pirate group on patrol that demands your cargo, you can't run away on cruise. The pirates will match your speed and start to "form up" on you, until the timer runs out and they turn hostile.

If the pirates don't have cruise disruptors (Liberty space), then it's not a problem. But if the pirates have cruise disruptors, then they'll knock you out of cruise and engage.

If you're near a trade lane or jump gate/hole, then you can get away easy. Otherwise, you're in for a fight.
