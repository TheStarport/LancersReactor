Wash

Fri Jul 16, 2004 12:37 pm by Wash

If you haven't logged onto a server yet, pick one off the FL Global list or look around the forum here to see which one interests you. I recommend starting with a server that does not require a player-side mod, for example Boston or 3NET. Be aware that there are server-side mods that make the game different (often harder), for example regenerating shileds on NPCs.

Anyway, log in, create a character and start playing the game. Try to find other players to team up on missions with and trade with, and avoid the ones who are trying to rob and kill you.
