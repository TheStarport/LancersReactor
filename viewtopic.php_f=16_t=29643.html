NukeIt

Mon Mar 22, 2004 9:08 pm by NukeIt

They're not really anything special...great damage, but high energy cost, long refire time, and sloooow projectiles. Statistically speaking, the Skyblast B beats the top two Codename guns, as does the Kraken II(I think), in damage per second(the energy costs are also a bit lower for those two weapons, being class 9 instead of class 10). Some people like the Codenames because they're free, but it's almost a ripoff since even a VHF can evade the shots from them. Use the above mentioned class 9 weapons instead.

I don't recall the exact damage ratings, but DIAMONDBACK(2.00 refire) and CEREBUS(2.00 refire) are the top two, followed by the WILD FIRE(2.00 refire). The most energy efficient of the bunch is the JADE(3.03 refire). For all intents and purposes, I consider the JADE to be superior, since the refire and efficiency make it able to fire longer and faster than the other Codename weapons. After all, when you're slinging that much juice around, it doesn't really matter if you slice off a few hundred less HP- especially if you can do it faster than the other guy can.


(Note: some people like Nomad guns since they do not use energy, but I personally consider them legalized cheating. User preference, but be warned that they will make the game so unbelievably easy that your pet hamster could play it.)

Bounty Hunter Jackson

Fri Mar 26, 2004 2:30 am by Bounty Hunter Jackson

There are some class 9 guns that are actually better than some of the low-end class 10 ones. I think only Outcast and Corsair weapons, though. But they're freely available...

Class 10 weapons are a matter of fighting style. If you're a sniper-esque flier, then you'll want something slow-firing with a massive punch. The high-end class 10 weapons are about as good as you can get. If you prefer to dog-fight, then you'll avoid class 10 weapons like the plague and go for Bounty Hunter of Civilian lines. And then there are various inbetween alternatives. Nomad weapons have their place too! I find it challenging to take on someone in an Eagle armed with Nomads. They can just fire and fire and fire, not bothering to aim. Tests your flying skills. It's no fun to use them, I don't think, unless you want to take a nap with your hand on the mouse.
