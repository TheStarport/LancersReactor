If you're looking to get into a passworded server, look in the forums for the host that's advertising, and post or e-mail your request.

There's no right or wrong server, just think about what your playstyle is and select accordingly. Also, it helps to look in the multiplayer and server forums to see what others say about this server or that.

I would suggest my server for starters, but you can look at the Freelancer Heaven topic in this forum and the server one for details on what type of mods I run. That more than anything will determine what servers you can or can't play on.
