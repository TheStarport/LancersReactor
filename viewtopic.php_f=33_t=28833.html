Special request(s) for 3.4, etc.

Hello, I'm a great fan of the Rebalance Mod and have enjoyed playing it for some time now. However, there are two things that I would really REALLY like to see in the next version:

1.) I have always been hoping that someone would, in a mod, restore the Rogue base which is destroyed at the beginning of the SP game. I just think that a pirate base which you could dock at inside that air bubble in the particle field (inside the Pittsburg debris field) would be really cool. Though Rochester may be strategically located if you are preying on the tradelanes around Fort Bush and Manhattan, a Pirate base in that Pittsburg particle field would be extremely useful to pirate beginners as it would be near the almost entirely Boron freight line between Pittsburg and Baltimore, and would offer... hidden in that bubble in the particle field... a very real sense of being part of the pirate underground.

2.) The Mule is a funny ship for the Xenos to be flying... as they are former aegira employees and enemies of most Liberty pirate factions... so, how would they get it? Could you maybe give them the starblazer or some other enhanced but civil looking ship instead? Maybe a souped up Rhino could do the trick? Also, so as to not make it (the Rhino) too common, why not give the Junkers something like that freighter sold on Newark instead?.. or, possibly even a more rusty coloured heavy lifter? It doesn't make sense that a mostly scrap collecting organization like the Junkers would have shields on ~all~ their ships.

Thanks a bunch!

Eric

Actually, I think those ships models are pretty cool... well detailed compared to others I have seen. The problem in servers is that everyone wants to drive a ~hot rod~, which doesn't create the most realistic gameplay experience. Nobody flies these ships because they're not armed and armoured enough to be competent against anything sold outside of Liberty, and yet they can't carry enough to make long distance trading worthwhile. Even so, however, they're decent for they're price tags and I, amongst these, am particular fond of the freighter and light fighter (a.k.a., inter-gallactic egg beater) sold on Newark. I haven't personally bought any of the nifty homeworld ships on Trenton, yet... but they don't seem to be bad either.

Online, the problem is to find people who actually ~enjoy~ playing the mediocre, anonymous corporate cargo pusher/miner/escort pilot/scrap collecter. Yet, having a lot of players filling these roles would add alot to the gameplay experience. The problem is, almost everyone who plays MP wants to think of themselves as the supreme fighter ace of the galaxy... so it is no wonder that player controlled, mid-level ships are few in number.

You need not ~like~ me... you need only FEAR and RESPECT me!!!
