Gibbon

Okay, well one problem i can see is your shop_archetype icon and your equipment icon are the same.

Try this,

shop_archetype = equipment\models\hardware\li_fusion_reactor.3db

Remember one is for sale at the equipment dealer in the shop which the above refers to, then the icon you have for powerplants defined here

item_icon = equipment\models\commodities\nn_icons\EQUIPICON_powerplant.3db

shows up once you've bought the new piece of equipment and should be visible once it is on your ship
