Will Freelancer sell???

This is the question that has been in my mind since I played the demo last night, Do you think Freelancer will sell? We all have our opinions and likes/dislikes of the game right, but from what you can tell, do you think it will sell? I personally think there is a good chance Freelancer will not only score big in the niche market, but earn a decent stake in overall game market.

Mouse support means everyone can play, and it's not to difficult to do combat but still good to be fun and probably not to boring.

At first I was relucent with the graphics, the screen shots were beautiful but I wasn't sure how they would work in the game. After playing the demo, the graphics remind me a lot of the origniality of Black and White.

It's cool as hell to pick out a point and fly there, right now my ship is off the system map on the demo and I'm waiting to see how far I can go. After 2 hours it's only moved a little bit on the universe map.

And the gameplay has definetly meet my expectations plus some, this was something else I was reluctent with.

Like I said, I wouldn't be surprised if we started seeing Freelancer mania in mid, or late March.
