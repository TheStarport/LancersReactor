Cyaron

Fri Jul 29, 2005 10:36 am by Cyaron

Remember evasive maneuvers. Fly by the generators in runs. As in, fly toward them with guns firing constrantly, firing out missiles/torps at the same time (so you get the effect of guns and also the torps), then as you go by it, hit your afterburners and all, still flying away from it for a few seconds evading shots.

Then turn around quickly, like by hitting reverse thrust as you turn around, then as you face it, immediately think evasive again and let your weapons rip.

By evasive, I mean you can use your breaks/afterburners/strafing to be a more difficult target for the nomads to hit, even while you're firing on your target. But you probably know how to do that anyway if you've gotten that far into the game.
