Megistos

Sun Jun 26, 2005 12:24 am by Megistos

You must finish the Story missions before really choosing your own path. But once you have finished, you are literally free to do whatever you want. You can take missions from any faction you like (assuming that you're friendly - if not you'll have to either work a bit or be rich to get them friendly).

The first step to being a bad guy is once you finish the story missions, get a Junkers bribe if you're hostile to the Junkers. Then, by visiting Junkers bases throughout Sirius, you'll be able to pick up bribes for most criminal factions. You can use arbiter.de for navigation purposes and to find the Junker bases.

The other way to become a bad guy is, of course, to kill the criminal factions' enemies. That means Police, MIlitaries, and Corporations. A good starting place is the set of Storage Depots outside Manhattan. Killing Storage Depots does wonders for your rep.
