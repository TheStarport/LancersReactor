geronimo the crossfire mod is not SP storyline compatable, what I mean by this is that the mod has altered some of the story only systems to such an extent that the story halts when you arrive in them and can't be continued (Tohoku's the first one). Using a save game from one mod to another is a very bad idea as at best you'll get corrupt save games at worst you'll experience the dreaded CTD's ( Crash to Desktop).
