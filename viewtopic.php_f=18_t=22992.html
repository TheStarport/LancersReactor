Tue Aug 26, 2003 2:33 pm by Born To Be Wild

OK, i will help. First, you have to open constants.ini in the DATA folder. Under

[EngineEquipConsts
Add the line:
ANOM_MAX_VELOCITY = 100000000000000000000000

This will alow speeds up to that to work.

Under

[PhysSysConsts
Add the line:
CRUISING_SPEED = whatever you want (must be lower than MAX_VELOCITY value)

hope that helps,

born to be wild

P.S. If you get an error saying: cant launch without engine the only work-around i know is to get a new ship (it can be the same one)
