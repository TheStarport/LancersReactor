Your basic Starflier starts with two of them; they aren't that hard to locate if you just pull the trigger a few times.

They make excellent mining and wreck looting weapons, BTW- they have too little firepower to do any damage to your loot. If you intend to do a lot of mining or wreck raiding, set aside a hardpoint for one of these (especially since, unlike other weaponry, you'll never have the need to pay for the Justice I, since you get two of 'em for free to start off with).
