Mon Jul 21, 2003 6:53 pm by Sherlog

The level/rank of enemies varies signifcantly only during missions; for example, in one mission you may get 3 x lvl 17 Sabre followed by 4 x lvl 16 Sabre followed by 8 x Stiletto, but another mission of the same difficulty level may have just a single wing of 6 x lvl 17 Sabre or a swarm of 12..16 mixel-lvl Stiletto.

The level/rank of enemies in the wild is determined by the area. If you drive from Honshu to Omicron Alpha you will get low-level Stilettos (or even Daggers) in Honshu and most of Sigma-19 but inside the nebula with the Omicron Beta jumphole they get a bit tastier (up to lvl 14). On the Sigma-19 side of Omicron Beta you get mostly lvl 14 Stiletto mixed with an occasional lvl 15 Sabre and the rest of Omicron Beta has mostly lvl 16/17 Sabres, as does the area around the Omicron Beta jumphole in Omicron Alpha (but you may get the occasional lvl 18 Sabre wing there). Inside Omicron Alpha it is lvl 18/19 Sabres.

The number of enemies in each wing varies within certain bounds; regular patrol wings tend to be between 4 and 8, dito for reception committees at jumpholes/jumpgates. If you are extremely lucky you may get an incoming patrol in addition to a reception committee, for a total of 12 to 16 enemies. But this is very rare. The best way to ensure a good supply of enemies is to make the right factions hostile. Add BH for Omicron Alpha/Gamma, BH and GMG for Omicron Beta.
