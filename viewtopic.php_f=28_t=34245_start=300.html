Mace_166

Thu Jul 27, 2006 3:57 am by Mace_166

The Federal Elite and Imperial Special forces are initially hostile to everybody. The only way to get them friendly is to attack their rival, but be warned these guys are some of the most heavily armed in the game.
A feature of FS is the long range missions where you buy (or note down) a data file giving you the location of a destroyable base in hostile territory. Its up to you how you approach the target, but you blow it up tractor in the loot and return the mission complete cargo back to the base of origin to get full payment.
For the Imperial and Federal targets, they are guarded by the special forces.
If you are visiting Planet Capitol in Archenar or 70 Ophuichi watch out for the special forces there. Planet Capitol is the emperors planet and there are special forces outside occasionally. (*note, there is actually a bug in this version where imperial specs and imperial navy fight in this version, they will be allies. Meaning they will all turn red and attack you)
70 Ophuichi is well worth avoiding if you are hostile to both of the special forces. These also alien ships in there just to make things more interesting.
Oh, and there is also the Rheinland special forces too.
