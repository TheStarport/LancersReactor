Tue Jun 10, 2003 5:35 pm by NukeIt

A few handy tips that could help:

1. Turn your graphics settings ALL THE WAY DOWN. Yes, it looks ugly that way, but it will improve your framerate like you wouldn't believe. That alone will give you better control over your ship, and a higher probability of winning.

2. Do NOT use GoTo, as it turns off your cruise after each waypoint.

3. Sell any crago you might be carrying, IIRC it will make your turning rate improve a little.

4. Lead your turns. The heavier your ship, the longer it will take to change course.

5. If you really have problems, buy the Cavalier LF sold on the Hood, and do the race in that. There's a very good reason why it's sold there.
