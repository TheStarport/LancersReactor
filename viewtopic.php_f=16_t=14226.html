This is a very good list....my tip of the hat to you, Ultimate.

Here are a few more suggestions/corrections to make the list more complete/correct:

##### The 2 "Unknown Object" Jumpholes #####

Include the two "Unknown Object" jumpholes located in Leeds and Cambridge. Since they are not marked on the map, nor show up on any target listing unless selected, here are detailed directions on how to get to them:

1) Leeds->Cambridge jumphole

Zoom into the area of the Leeds->New London Jumphole, count 1 square south and 7 squares east, and point a waypoint in that square. Now fly to it, and it should show up right in front of you.

2) Cambridge->Omega-3 jumphole

Zoom into the area of the Cambridge->Leeds Jumphole, count 4 squares south, then 4 squares east. Plot a waypoint right on the line separating the 3rd and 4th squares. Now fly to it, and it should show up right in front of you. It may be a bit difficult to see as the jumphole is white, and the ice cloud surrounding it is quite foggy, so keep your eyes peeled.

Needless to say, both are SP only.

##### Minor corrections/suggestions #####

1) Re-arrange so that it is in alphabetical order by system name...makes looking up the list easier for us. Now, I realize this may be a lot of work, so if you can't be bothered doing it, then don't do it.

2) If you flagged one end of the jumphole as SP only, don't forget to flag the other end too. For example, you flagged the Magellan jumphole under Leeds System as SP only, but the corresponding Leeds jumphole under Magellan System isn't flagged.

3) "Cortez System" is incorrectly spelt as "Cortex System" -- dynamite, anyone? (Sorry, couldn't resist the joke)

4) Under the entry for Hokkaido System, Tohoku jumphole is incorrectly listed as "Thonko"

5) Under Magellan System, Manchester is incorrectly listed as "Manchster"

6) Under Sigma-13 System, Chugoku is incorrectly listed as "Chuguku"

7) Under Omega-5 System, Cambridge is incorrectly listed as "Cambrdige"

Keep up the good work!
