First- Mustang, the best and only worthwhile factory to start with is a Solar Power Plant in Kingdoms end, or lucky planets, or somewhere else I forget.

WLB- you need to direct them to a shipyard or dock. You can only buy, sell, and repair ships at a shipyard or dock. There is one in Argon Prime, and one in Argon Prime. There is also a pirate base in Ore Belt. To get it to the shipyard you have to select it from "my property" or press R I think, then access command console from the menu and you can do it from there.

25 MW sheilds are mainly found in Split space, in 25 MW sheild factories. (There are 5 main "house" races, Split, Paranid, Teladi, Argon and Boron) then Khaak, pirates, Xenos and UFO's.
But to get friendly with Split so you can dock with their stations you need to kill a few pirates in their space (yes a few is enough unlike FL)

To transfer between ships you need to have them both docked at the station, then select the other ship through "my property" (or R I think it is) then get to the main menu, it should be there.
OR you could by the cargo tranfer....thing, which lets you remotely transfer cargo from space...over short distances.

Dunno about the police licence, are you killing police in the same space (argon, boron, etc) that you bought it? I'll check it out.
