Metroid Mod

I'm planning to release a mod based on the Metroid video game series.

Here's what to expect when I release the mod:
5 New Ships: ALL of Samus' 3 ships (The First Ship from Metroid: Zero Mission, The Second Ship from Metroid 2 to Beginning of Metroid Fusion, and then finally the New Ship that Samus gets after the Beginning Scene from Metroid Fusion).
The Space Pirate Fighter and The Space Pirate Mothership (Both from Metroid: Zero Mission)

1 New System: accurately representing that star map found in Metroid Prime in the Observatory.

1 New Commodity: Metroids.

5 New Weapons representing SOME of Samus Arsenal from Metroid Prime: The Power Beam, The Wave Beam, The Ice Beam, The Plasma Beam, and The Phazon Beam (which will require Phazon to operate).

2 New Factions: The Space Pirates which I would like to make them Nomad-like (dosen't respond when you hail them, ALWAYS your enemy, dosen't show up in the Faction list, etc.) and The Galactic Federation.

the most interesting thing of them all (and how am I going to do this is beyond me.): Put Trent in Samus' Power Suit, and put the helmet on him too (coming in 4 different styles as seen in Metroid Prime: Power Suit, Varia Suit, Gravity Suit, and Phazon Suit), put King in the Metroid Fusion Suits and the helmet (coming in 4 different styles as seen in Metroid Fusion: Power Suit, Varia Suit, Gravity Suit, and The Orange Suit that you get while fighting the Omega Metroid ),and put Juni JUST IN BAILEY (Bailey = Bikini) .

I hope to have this done in 2-4 weeks (maybe more), and I am open to suggestions.
