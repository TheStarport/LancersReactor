Battleship kills are calculated only if you have the last shot in, the killing shot. Learnt this from experimentation and the hard way.

Basically I use the semi-lame method of killing battleships, I would find a safe place IN the battleship to hover over where when other fighters shot at me they would be shooting the battleship. Then I would just relax and hold down my fire key, no point wasting missles or torpedoes.

Well, was experimenting, this is the battleship near the last few missions, the one that is beside the Order's battleship. Basically I shot and I definitely got the most damage on the ship, 3/4 of the damage was mine. Then I stopped when there was a sliver of a bar left and left the Order battleship to destroy the enemy battleship. Check registered kills and yeap, the kill wasn't mine.

Did the same thing again but this time kept firing all the way till it exploded (Explosion from even that close to a battleship does not damage you in any way...cool.), got 1 battleship kill.
