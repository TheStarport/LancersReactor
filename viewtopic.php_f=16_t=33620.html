Favorite Piracy Spot?

There doesnt really seem to much about good piracy spots on the forums. I personally have no problems raiding Daumann Trains full of diamonds in at the Dresden jumpgate then hot footing it to New Tokyo. Piracy is superior to Trading because you are making 100% profit.

My favorite spot, which is a ripper because its against the Rogues (so your pirating the pirates!) is in the Cortez system. From the Cortez - California jumpgate fly south toward the California jumphole. Wait for any Rogue Mule's to show up. This is the easiest way to get Cardamine for free. Once you're full head to manhattan, avoid being scanned and let the crdits roll in. The beauty of doing this is you will also grab Usiels and Vassago's to add to your overall profit. However the best (and rarest....i've only ever found them twice) is when you scan a mule with Alien Organisms. Pop him off, tractor, go through the jumphole which you conveniently are already at, fight off any hackers/rogues on the other side. Set a course for California Minor and sell off the organisms that didnt die for 2000 a piece, too easy right? Rinse and repeat. You can mix it up by looting Gold, H-Fuel etc.. and just dump them at Curacao for decent Creds.

Once i jacked a train full of Niobium at the Dresden jumpgate. Head on down to Leipzig....pay day!

Anyone else into piracy like me and would like to share some hotspots they know of?
