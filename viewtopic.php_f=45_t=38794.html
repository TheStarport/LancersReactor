The 9th infantry division is looking for new pilots!

The 9th Infantry Division is a huge multi-gaming clan, of about 170 members. We have just recently come in to the Freelancer world, and we love it! It is not only a clan server though, as many freelancers are also part of the server. The server is here at: http://webpages.charter.net/olympus1/index.html

I am in charge of our Freelancer squad, and we need more pilots, noob or ace, to come and fight by our side! We supply all new members with 50 million to buy a corvette, or 60 million for a profiteer.

9th Infantry is not only a clan, but also a small community. We interact with each other, and become good friends. Please come visit us at www.9thinfantry.net and feel free to post in our forums, and fill out a join form if you are interested. We would love to have you as a part of our community. Some of the other games we play are: Counter Strike: Source, America’s Army, Battlefield 1942: Forgotten Hope MOD, EVE online, Freelancer, and Joint Operations: Escalation. We have a Team Speak server, as well as a CS:S server, an AA server, and a BF1942 server. Please contact me at evilsycofreek@hotmail.com for any info on servers, or just more info about us.

Hope to see you in space!
