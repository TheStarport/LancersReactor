@bob that tends not to work...if you step back and the click post you always get the error "message already posted", even though it hasn't.

@froboz this works for me:

1. click "reply"
2. type reply
3. copy message to clipboard
4. attempt to post message
5. on receipt of error, hit "back" twice on your browser
6. click "reply"
7. paste message
8. post message

works every time, and doesn't involve you shutting down your browser. As I mentioned in reply to Bob, if you hit "back" once instead of twice and simply try reposting, the system thinks you've already posted the message. Bizarre but true.

Hope that helps!
