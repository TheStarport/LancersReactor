Random Crash to Desktop - a solution

Hello forum from a sunny Cape Town!

I run Freelancer on a GeForce 4 card. Latest everything (Win98SE), plenty of hardware and a random crash to the desktop has been drving me nuts! I could (for instance) never even consider taking a trade route with more than 2 jumps. Would'nt get there. Will crash out to the desk!!!

Tried everything, even talking to MicroSquish. As usual, they are hopeless, thinking that all people with problems with their games are low foreheaded... Well, I am not - have also been messing around with PC's and games for a long time (IT proff for close on 30 years now).

I finaly solved my problem - this is how:

1. Disabled all SOUND acceleration in DirectX (Run DXDIAG, all sound tags - acceleration turned off);

2. Setup the game resolution to: 800 x 600 x 16 ("Options" button in Freelancer);

The solution is working for me and I am now able to go anywhere and play the game without crashing...

I thought that others here might just benefit from this.
