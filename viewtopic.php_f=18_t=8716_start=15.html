Vexios

Sun Mar 09, 2003 4:37 am by Vexios

You can put torpedoes on any ship by changing/adding this to their shiparch section:

hp_type = hp_torpedo_special_1, HpTurret01, HpWeapon01
hp_type = hp_torpedo_special_2, HpTurret01, HpWeapon01

What this does is tell HPWeapon01 (hard points can be mounted with anything they only name Weapon/Turret/Shield so its easier to identify) that it can use both cruise disrupters (hp_torpedo_special_1) and torpedoes (hp_torpedo_special_2)
