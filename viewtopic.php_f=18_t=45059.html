Zeus,
Did you check the "Base Template" in the FLExplorer?
You can still keep the basic "outside" look of the base, but it's what is listed for the inside that makes the difference. The base template is what allows for this change. If it doesn't have "ships" listed, it won't sell the ship. I happened to find this out too. It isn't the explorer, it's just the base templates.
What I mean here, is that a base must have a ship dealer so it can sell ships.
