What do YOU see? (Or: The Future of FreeLancer.)

Greetings All,


As you play this wonderful game what do you see in regards to it's future?

Do you even thinks it has a future?
Or, will it go the way of many games and be a "Was fun for a while, what's next?" type of game?

I, personally, can see it being around for quite a while.
(Even if DA/M$ choose not to release any "Expansion Packs", or even patch the few things that have been requested numerous times by many players.)

I can see a very good "Modding Community" developing already, and the game has been "officially" out only a week.
And, that usually means that a game will at least have a strong "cult" following which can make it last far beyond what it normally would...look at "HardWar" for example.
I can see this "modding community" taking this "base product" and making it something that even the "nay-sayers" can't complain about.


Anyone have any predictions on what will come in the future of this game we are all here to enjoy?
