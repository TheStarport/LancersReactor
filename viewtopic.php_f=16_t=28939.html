Personally I like guns with a high rate of fire but your choice of weapons may vary based on your style of fighting in that regard. If your aim isn't so good, then a higher rate of fire is what you want, generally speaking. Missiles are good, but be sure you know how to use them properly or you will spend lots of credits replacing missiles that struck nothing whatsoever.
