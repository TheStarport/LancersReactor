Balefire

Fri Mar 28, 2003 10:39 am by Balefire

I think you're asking how he amassed that much money before he finished the SP campaigns, right? It's quite possible, easy even. The missions appear as you reach X amount of money, but the trick is to ignore the requests to meet Juni, and just go ahead and do whatever else you want to build up your funds. The missions for the SP campaign will wait for you while you take other freelance jobs. You won't gain levels until you do the official missions, so your choice of ships/gear is limited, though. But once you finish the SP official campaigns, your level is determined by your worth, and if that's high enough, you can instantly gain multiple levels...max it out, in fact, if you're rich enough.

It's one approach, but it might lead--depending on your preferences and playing style--to missing out on some of the interesting intermediate ships and gear combinations before you get to where you can buy the (inevitable?) Eagle/Sabre/Titan.

It's better to burn out than to fade away
