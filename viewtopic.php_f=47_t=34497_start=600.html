Okay, here is basicly what the rogues have done since they basicly dissapeared.

At the beginning of the conflict, during the testing of the new jumpdrives and such, they detected a new jumphole forming on the far outskirts of the cortez system that lead to a system uncharted by anyone before them. After a raid on the liberty main computer that accidentally turned up a complete history of liberty's near forgotten parent nation of america, along with all it's culture, the rogues decided to take on a new destiny, and left for the new system, christened
New America by the newly born idealists in the organization, and minimized the presence on all of their stations in the known systems.

They found 3 planets, one barely habitable, and one that could possibly terriformed. Also, it had a reasonably large asteroid belt encircling the inner two planet's orbits, while a gas giant with several moons kept the outer section vacummed.

They cristened the habitable planet "New Philidelphia" and began construction on a docking ring for it and built themselves a fighter construction/ temporary headquarters facility out of the largest asteroid in the belt, which the cristened "Boston". It was there that they began research into the Alliance class VHFs, finally developing them at about the time of bretoina's withdrawal of military support from the axis of power. Also at this time, the docking rings for New philidelphia and the under terraforming planet "New Richmond" were completed, allowing for the rogues to finally strech out and begin to truely expand. They have been secretly recruiting once more since the docking ring completions among the houses to try and get the best of each house's potential, with pilots from all houses who are fed up with the war and want to see someone who is mostly neutral try to end it.

A few days after the completion of the docking rings, they began work on a capital ship shipyard, called "Seattle" so that they could match liberty's navy.
It was designed to have 10 ship bays, but they dropped it down to 8 to complete it in time.

They only completed it just a few days before the convoy raids began, so that they could study the new weapons in use by the houses and develop a unified fleet to counter these threats.

Currently they have:

500 mules (Many used to harvest resources from the asteroid feild for use on the new shipyard and now the captial ships. The rest are used to haul back missle loadouts from the convoys as well as replacement guns being shipped to the outer shipyards of the houses.)
450 bloodhounds (Many have been scrapped to supply resources for the new Alliance class fighters, but the remaining ones have been upgraded to handle class 6 equipment to keep enough manuverablility to be survivable, yet pack enough punch to accomplish it's job. New construction on the upgraded version of this class is now being considered.)
700 wolfhounds (These fighters have also been upgraded, putting more armor and faster engines on them. A step class to replace them due to the other houses faster, more powerful fighters is being tenatively called the Deerhound.)
300 Alliance class fighters (Compareable to the rapier class fighter in abilities, and now being equipped with cloaks salvaged from reinland escorts, this ship is dangerous and heavily armed with 5 class 10 guns and 4 class 10 turret mounts.
The ship is belived to be slightly more manuverable than the rapier, as well as faster, but it is hoped that this matchup does not come into play anytime soon.)

None of the houses or organizations know about the hole, as it is about 400 k out from the center of the cortez system. In fact, the pirates were chasing a navy fighter out there when they discovered it. However, it is expected that the rogues may contact the rogue LSF soon to ask that they move in with them and break off the alliance with the corsairs and outcasts.

They are now building the first battleships of a new class, designed to outmanuver and outclass the current battleships in survivablility.

It carries 75 fighters, and has 2 large forward only guns, partially designed off of liberty's cruiser cannon. What is unusal about this ship is the design. They designed it partially based off of the history of the americas before liberty.=, taking cues from various places, eventually coming up with a fearsome design from the past. WIth a large engineering hull containing the ship's main torpedo launchers and fighter bays, with two large (Currently used as weapon platorms until they can get their hands on intersystem jump technology) nacell type strutures connected to both it and the crew quarters and command center saucer structure, with a neck-like struture connecting the saucer to the engineering hull. The saucer carries 6 main guns, 3 lower-sode, 3 upper side, based partially off liberty designs, but changed slightly to allow for a higher firing rate at the loss of some stopping power. IT also carries one fighter bay where the in planning bombers will be stored. it also carries the 4th torpedo launcher of the ship, the 1&2 mounted forward on the engineering hull, the 3 on the rear of the ship. The rogues have recently developed a torpedo launcher capibile of firering any type of torpedo, vampire, sunslayer and any others in existance, though it is slightly bulky and take some time to change arment types. The engineering hull, where the main drive unit is stored, also has the new device that allows this ship to have it's soon to be unpresidented manuverability. When the rogues tried to find a way to build an intersystem drive, they accindentally discovered a process where space can be warped slightly to drop the apparent mass of an object. Thus, they can make their battleship manuver like a gunship. The engineering hull carries the aformentioned forward guns as well as torpedo launchers, and also carries 7 flak turrets, with an increased fire rate, and 5 secondary offensive turrets, which have a very high fire rate, compareable to some fighter's gun fire rates. However, it suffered a loss of power efficency and damage potential as well. The class also has an experimental battleship designed shield, which has been only semi-sucsessful in testing. The rogues hope to have the bugs worked out soon. The class's name? In honor of the once venitrable ship that served the once budding america's navy so well, as well as honoring the country now lost to the destructive liberty, they are calling this class the Consitutions.
