Rebalance Mod Initial thoughts

Ok, I've started single player with the Rebalance mod and I like some of the additional parts. The idea of having Battleship and cruiser groups patrolling makes sense especially.

I like the new ships, the Cylon Fighter although it maneuvers like a brick is pretty good early on, I'm now flying the Hercules Mk II from Freespace 2 and although its nice I find the Energy banks drain pretty quick.

The idea of tractoring in Pirates and selling them for bounty to Police bases is nice, this was one idea in Privateer 2 that was good in as you got a small bounty for each criminal ship killed no matter what your mission was at the time.

I've heard there's more mission types, like Battleship killing missions but haven't seen any yet, granted I'm still in Liberty space so maybe that isn't till later.

I also like the idea of extra components for your ship like the deep scanner and engine and powerplant upgrades.
