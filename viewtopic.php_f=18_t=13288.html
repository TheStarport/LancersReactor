Tractor Beam and Shield Generator - an unrequited love story

Why is it that my tractor beam is able to get cargo through my shields and into my hold while my shields fend off intense laser fire, yet jettisoning cargo forces my shields to go down?

My feeling is that - back in development - your shields would go offline under both circumstances. Then, the feature was scrapped when they fired the QA team and replaced them with monkies (and too many of the monkies died because they tried to tractor in loot during in a firefight - we all know how monkies can be sometimes!) Somehow, the jettison cargo feature was never discovered by the monkies, and therefore never "fixed".

It doesn't necessarily make no sense, though it looks like it at first glance.
The tractor "beam" may not be a beam so much as a tube that extends through the shields and brings the cargo in through it.
Since there is no "pressor" beam, the only way to get cargo out is to shut down the shields, open the door and kick it out.
E.E."Doc" Smith wrote about this kind of stuff in the 1930's, so it's not exactly a new concept.
