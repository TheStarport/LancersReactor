There's a money hack/cheat? Hell i cant even do uber,but money would help me alot.


Anyway i have a tip for noobs.


If you dont feel like doing missions or getting into any fights, trade water. From manhatten to pittsburg is a fast, good run. Buy water at manhatten for 15 credits. Sell it at pittsburg for 30. do this till you can afford a rhino. Then do the water run again a couple mmore times till you feel you can trade vast distances.

Oh also when you start out in multiplayer. Dont waste your money on better weapons. The key to staying alive is mostly energy useage. More energy you use, less you can shoot. So buy another justice mk1 if you feel the need for a 3rd gun. And upgrade your shields. to max if you can afford it. If you cant, ohwell just start running when rouges come if you dont feel like fighting. If you feel like fighting anything that comes in your way, stock up on as much nanos and shield batteries as possible. Preferribly more nanos than shields. Shields can regenerate. Hull cant.

And if you want to get neutral with everyone at least. Kill xenos, or bounty hunters. try not to go trading alone, try to hook up with a convoy. of course if one convoy is going from manhatten to colorado, you might wanna find a convoy going from ft bush to pittsburg after you get to bush.



Well there ya go, my little tip.
