TITANS FOR ALL!
uh *ahem*
You allready have a good Corsair rep, thats good. The Titan Ship is available at Leon Base, Omega 41, or Planet Crete, Omicron Gamma

To get to Omega 41
Take the jump gate to Omega 11 in Stuttgart (Rhienland).
-Or-
From Cambridge, follow the trade lanes to Omega 3 (you should have the system allready since you went there to get Sinclair), then in Omega 3, follow the Trade Lanes to Omega 7, and follow those Trade Lanes to the Stuttgart Jump Gate. You can take a right after that and go to the Omega 11 Jump hole, or go to Stuttgart and take the Omega 11 Jump Gate.
If you go to the Omega 11 Jump hole in Omega 7, then you will see Briesen Mining Facility. If you see that, good. Keep going, Keep going, there.

In Omega 11
You'll have to travel a loong way. On your right when you come out of the Jump gate, you see buoys. Follow them to "Solarius Station" (owend by Daumann Construction). Go there and repair (the radiation damage is heavy in Omega 41) and restock on Nanobots. After you leave Solarius, set a way-point for sector 5C, Madsack Fragment. In there is the jump hole to Omega 41

In Omega 41 (to get to Leon)
Leon is in 6D. USE CAUTION. The Neutron Star you see there is VERY Dangerous. Going close to it can damage your ship a lot. There are also Red Hessians in Sabre ships, so, use caution. I might also advise having an Adv. Countermeasure Dropper if you dont allready. Keep going to Sector 6D and Leon will be there.

In Omega 41 (to get to Omicron Gamma)
The jump hole to Omicron Gamma is in 5E. Use the same caution. Only this time, there's a stop. On the way to Omicron Gamma, Freeport 5 is there. Stop there and repair (you could not stop on Freeport 5 to Leon because they are on oppisite ends). After you take off from Freeport 5, go to the Jump Hole.

In Omicron Gamma (to Crete)
Crete is in 5E. Since you have a good rep with the Corsairs, there are no cautions you need to take here (unless you are enemies with Bounty Hunters).

Heh, this was all from memory. Sectors too.
