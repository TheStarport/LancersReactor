For the first patrol just use afterburner and hard turns. Make a few attack runs on your current target but remember those other ships are helping you shot down those other fighters. Have to break your wing commander habit your wingman can’t make a kill

At the arch cruise directly to the arch. Target a shield generator and fly right up to it. The arch's shield should protect you from most of the weapons fire. If not afterburner out and make a few fly by's. Remember to keep maneuvering like crazy.

Once the four generators are taken out fly really close to the arch and use the F3 key.
