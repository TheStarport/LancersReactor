i played around with the dyson sphere gate...it turned out to be extremely unstable in gameplay since the jump out point, when you return from the interior , is not well defined (remember in the SP you don't jump back).

When i tested it, it worked from the outside (was very funny because i used the jumhole effect to evade the script), but on the way back my ship sometimes popped out beneath the surface of the dyson sphere...and was lost forever.

My solution was to place a nomad jump gate just above the gate and use this instead...works great in my mod
