Source of my nightmares? WEDDINGS

Hehehe...All my life I've been a lucid dreamer...when I was about 14 I went away to Loughborough University so they could study my brain pattern while I sleep. That was odd. Lucid Dreams for those who don't know are (in the most extreme) dreams that you are "awake" inside...so its a dream but you can control the outcome of it 100%...and I mean 100%...I even managed to get it to the point as a kid where before I went to sleep I would decide where I'd like my dream to begin.

eg. before going to sleep I would decide on a beach. Sleep. "Wake up" inside the dream on a nice cosy beach...then I could wander around say "hang on we need a woman over there" and bosh there's your scantily clad woman. etc etc

Now why am I telling you this? Because as you get older lucid dreamers slowly lose control of their dreams....so nowadays I can only get control sometimes once a week or maybe even once a fortnight....but the downside is, you still get EXTREMELY vivid dreams. I honestly can't remember the last time I woke up without remembering what I dreamt about (Was thinking of starting a dream blog at some point)....which means that when you have nightmares....you reeeeaaaalllly have nightmares. I'm talking heart stopping, take your breath away sort of stuff.

So if anyones ever wondered why I'm permanently on the cardamine...now ya know....its great for destroying nightmares well saying that, even if it doesn't destroy them, they tend to turn into dreams about food and/or bunny rabbits after a few minutes so they're not as bad

I totally sympathise with nightmare sufferers....
