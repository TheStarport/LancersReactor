Since this thread is still in the "Discussion" forum, I feel the urge to warn everyone of the

spoilers ahead .



Very simple to find the new systems. Just follow the patrol routes, which will lead you almost everytime to previously undiscovered stations or jumpholes. There are jumpholes, too, to where no patrol routes lead to, but there are relatively large objects like planets close to them. In addition, take a good look at the navmap, since there's at least one jumphole which is missing it's description.

As for improving your current weapons loadout, you'll find better stuff in the outer systems (Omicron and Omega) and, if you're a most excellent pilot (relatively speaking *g*), in the unknown systems, too.
