Sherlog

Tue Jun 24, 2003 8:58 pm by Sherlog

If you equip guns of the so-called 'optimal weapon class' then it is guaranteed that you cannot utilize the energy generated by the powerplant efficiently, so it is quite a bit of a misnomer. The job of a gun is to turn the energy inside the powerplant into destruction at the target, as quickly and as accurately as possible.

Financial aspects aside, the optimal weapon class is always the maximum that can be equipped. A bigger gun can convert more energy in a shorter time than a smaller gun, and so you can do more damage in the small time window of opportunity and, consequently, destroy the target faster.
