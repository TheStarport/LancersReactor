Rangers are the same in any time zone
Though the film will not give you that impression, the book will.

Anyway, with the exception of the Xwing, Cyclon and now viper cmp and mat files sent to me by Kurgan, Hostile universe is entirely created by yours truely. I love scifi so im motived here

I have worked with teams before and to be honest, whilst ive had a lot of fun and made a lot of friends that way, teams work slower than i do on my own. You see each team member will have real life issues and those issues occure at different times making any sort of time table hard to achieve. Where as working alone, i work at my own pace and im not left waiting for work from others. Less stressful that way I think. My arrange me with Kurgan is different, He sends me models when he is ready and i add them when i am ready. I dont announce models or features unless i already have them in my possession and thus can add them to the game.

Edit: the model is now finished, i fixed the windows so they are transparent and im about to setup the ini so it flys like a starfury.
