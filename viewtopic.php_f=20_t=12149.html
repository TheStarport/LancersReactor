Wireless Net? Does this involve satellites? Find out, as if it does you may as well stick to the modems for gaming.

For those about to ask:

Because it takes 250ms for a signal to get to a geosynchronous sattelite and back to earth. Add that value to any ping time you may think you have.
Bandwidth of the connection does not matter with sattelites. the ping will always be at least 250 and probably closer to 350 (= unplayable ping)
