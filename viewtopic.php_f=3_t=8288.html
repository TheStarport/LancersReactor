In fact, the controls would actually be better suited for the Xbox. Setup the sticks like a FPS, inverted of course, the d-pad could cycle through the nav/info menus, black button for mines, white for countermeasures, X button for hostile targets, Y non hostile, A for afterburner, B for missles/torpedos.
