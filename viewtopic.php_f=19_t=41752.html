CrossFire 1.52 Server

Greetings all...

I want to encourage all of you to come check out the CrossFire 1.52 Mod and server.

I have played many mods and IMO this is the best one out there. With the addition of 20 + new systems, the resurrection of the ASF CSF war (the video in the beginning of FL), the addition of many coalition forces ships and the weapons to match, with realistic damages.

The introduction of the coalition space, with the lost sleeper ships, the CrossFire 1.52 mod has breathed new life into the FL community.

We have 3 clans and several associations, all of them participating in daily competitions, events and PVP. There is even a good puzzle that will lead you to the unchartered systems and new adventures.

I am the CEO of the *at Corporation (Arms Traders), come to the server and we will help you get started, give you a tour and fix you up with some weapons to start ( I do mean the best weapons too). From there you can request to join a clan, become a pirate, simple trader etc.

Find me Gunny*at / Gunny[tourguide*at and we will introduce you to CrossFire.

Members of the DC, BG & PiA clans are always willing to help you as well.

We have good role playing here and we want more players, who want to have fun, join our server.

There is too many things to list here and it will spoil the fun....so come on out.

See you in space!
