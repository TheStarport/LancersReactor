FriendlyFire

Fri Mar 04, 2005 11:39 am by FriendlyFire

type = exclusion vignettes will not do the exclusion at all!
The "type = X" is there to give Freelancer an idea of how the environnment should look, but this applies for Missions only. It wont change anything, it's only Freelancer that needs these for missions.

To do an exclusion zone, you need to make a zone (You can take your Mission Vignette Zone too) and insert this in your asteroids field/nebula file :

[Exclusion Zones
exclusion = X

where X is the nickname of the zone.

Hope this helps
