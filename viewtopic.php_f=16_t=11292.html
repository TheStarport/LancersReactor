HeymlicH

Mon Mar 24, 2003 4:56 pm by HeymlicH

If you are trying to fix your rep that way we are talking about hundreds of xenos.
Here my method to do it (I use it on bounty hunters)

1. get 1 mio credits as a merchant.
2. Find a base where you can dock and launch and always xenos are nearby.
3. get a heavy fighter
4. equip the HF with 6 missile launchers. (2 for shields, 4 for hull damage) group the weapons into 2 groups. g1 for shield damage, g2 are 4 launchers for hull damage. Don't fire all missiles at once, if 6 missiles of different types hit the target at once, the damage calculation doesn't work properly. 4 missiles of the same type do work. So you need 2 shots to kill a fighter.
5. it takes like a minute to take out the complete wave with all those missiles, and you don't have to worry about shortage of missiles since you have to dock after each wave. The money needed for new missiles is made quickliy if you know a good trade rout.
