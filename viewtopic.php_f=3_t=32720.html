Instant maneuverability!

More for the newbies to the game, but it might help some vets too. It's quite simple, and blindingly obvious once I found it. If this is common knowlege then flame away.

Set your in-game mouse sensitivity to it's highest level on the slider. It takes a little while to get used to, but it really DOES give your ship that little bit of extra responsiveness when you don't have to move the mouse as far to get to max turn rate.

Wed Jul 07, 2004 6:41 am by VroomZo

How about instant cruise .
Make sure you're not in "go to", docking, or formation. (cancel these with the escape button.)
Start cruise as normal, but hold down strafe (it can be left or right, just hold it down)
When you start cruising (while holding strafe) press your engine kill button.
surprise, your cruise engines will still be engaged but your forward speed is 0.
Releasing strafe will cancel this, so in order to make your ship stay in this mode, press enter, release strafe and press enter again.
Now make sure you haven't selected anything at all on the screen (ie. another player) and press F2 (go to). If you did it right, you should zoom to whatever your cruise speed is.
