Cavalier is good enough for the rhinelanders...I never took a bit of hull damage in my cavalier. I replaced that with a hawk(choosing it over the barracuda for the improved maneuverability).

The key is your thruster. Use your thruster every time something starts hitting you. If you don't have an advanced or heavy one, use it in short bursts. It works wonders for your survivability.
