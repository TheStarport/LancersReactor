For those of you whom are afraid that the SETI software may actually drain your system resources or perhaps comes with hidden spyware or adware then let me try to calm your nerves a little bit.

The standard SETI application can be set up in 2 different ways. The first way is specifically for fanatics or those wishing to show of the speed of their CPU's in number crunching leagues.

Using this set-up, SETI will attempt to run all day long, and depending on your system specs, this may cause slowdowns as SETI like all applications, requires CPU cycles and some ram.

The second way, may be the way that is likely to be more appealing to people here. You can set SETI up as a screensaver, when you leave your computer for X ammount of time, the SETI screensaver will appear and begin to carry out its process's, when you move your mouse, SETI will cancel all process's and let you continue with your work / play. The screensaver is a rather attractive one considering the non-attractive tasks that it is carrying out.

Adware / Spyware /

Naw SETI doesnt come with any of that type of stuff.
