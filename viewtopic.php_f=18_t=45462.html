The fact that the bartender appears tells me it's not a typo that's causing the problem, as you wouldn't see anybody if it was. You are going to have to compair the bases in the universe.ini with the bases in the mBases.ini, as you have a base entry in the mBase.ini that doesn't exist in the universe.ini. At some stage you've edited/deleted a base with FLE but it's left the mBases.ini entry untouched.
