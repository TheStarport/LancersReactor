Wing Commander Movie: Was it REALLY THAT BAD?

I saw it in the theaters with my cousin, I have it on home video, and I'm watching it on TV right now. Now, we all know it didn't meet the enormous potential it had from the moment Chris Roberts took the directorial helm, but was it REALLY THAT BAD?

Freddie Prinze, Jr. and Matthew Lillard were not the best choices for Blair and Maniac, respectively, and Safron Burrows was not the best choice for Angel. And let us not even touch the Paladin issue with a ten-foot pole. I'll just say that you can't substitute a short Frenchman for a large Scotsman and I'll leave it at that. Now, even with such bad choices for the cast, the movie still had potential.

I've made this post because I want to know what everyone (who has seen it) thinks ruined the movie.

Personally, I hope one day Chris Roberts can make a sequel. It is my hope that somone here might have some way to contact him and convey our thoughts on the issue. In addition, I am going to post a topic asking for everyone's suggestions for a sequel to the movie, including plot ideas, cast choices, and anything else you might be able to think of.

I would like to ask the moderators to check on these two topics from time to time and see if they are popular enough to warrant being stickied.

Thank you all for you help and I look forward to all your thoughts.
