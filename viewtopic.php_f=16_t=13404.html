Freelancer Commodity Mining Zones

For all you miners out there, here is a listing of all the zones that can produce various commodities.

The Probability column lists the chance of mining the particular commodity from the zone. Sorry for the formatting, but it wouldn't take the HTML code I had it in. Email me if you'd like a ".txt" version.

Recommend using only one gun to shoot your targets as too much firepower might also destroy what you are after. Use the Loot Filter and/or watch for the Loot Symbol in your lower right portion of your HUD.

Note: this data comes from the Freelancer Strategy Guide. Enjoy!
