Preacher0815

Mon May 19, 2003 9:07 am by Preacher0815

Fire up "Regedit" and goto "HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Microsoft Games\Freelancer\1.0". There, you´ll find 3 entries named
"InstallKey"="x"
"MPAccountName"="x"
"MPAccountNameSig"="x"

Simply delete these three entries (or back them up if needed later) and launch Freelancer. It will now generate new values for all three entries.
