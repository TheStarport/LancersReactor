Nog

Sun May 04, 2003 2:19 pm by Nog

Ports 4202 to 4300 not being routed to the server is your problem. (although routing just port 4202 to the server should do) NAT seems to work good for the clients, but does not seem to do good for the servers unless you tell the router exactly where to send the packets. Don't know why, but I had the same problem and that fixed it.
