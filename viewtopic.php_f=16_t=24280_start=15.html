Just some fun info for those of you interested in Dyson Sphere's. The Dyson sphere (or Dyson shell) was originally proposed in 1959 by the astronomer Freeman Dyson in "Search for Artificial Stellar Sources of Infrared Radiation" in Science as a way for an advanced civilisation to utilise all of the energy radiated by their sun.

The original proposal was actual that of several thousand individual "islands" orbiting one star. It wasn't until later that the concept was interpreted to be a single shell encasing a star. The idea is based on some very sound scientific research and calculations, although the undertaking of such a project is far beyond the capabilities of our civilization.

A similar concept is the Ringworld created by Larry Niven.

If you're interested, you can read more about the Dyson Sphere concept at this site:

http://www.nada.kth.se/~asa/dysonFAQ.html#WEB

It's incredible to think that such an idea was first realized almost 45 years ago, and a decade before man even made it to the moon.

Anyway, just an FYI for those of you who are interested.
