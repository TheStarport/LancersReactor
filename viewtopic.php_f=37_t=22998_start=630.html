harrier

Fri Oct 01, 2004 5:15 pm by harrier

To all,

Thanks for the support, lets just say that the depression index has gone the other way and a positive attitude (never absent for long) has returned...

We have done some recruiting and I am pleased to announce we have 2 new members.. Buck Danny has joined us with his extraordinary knowledge of how freelancer works. and is busily organising the whole mod into a cohesive structure, plus doing system work and finding and fixing all sorts of stuff. JustSomeGuy has joined us and is busily fleshing out the systems and bringing life to the new systems.

Plus Acid has agreed to help with bug hunting and has already shown his abilities, by identifying some errors that we had been unable to see.

Well to bring you all up to date... Phew... This will take some doing...

We have been working literally non stop on this since any of us last posted.. I could not detail the number of changes that have been made..

here's a sample:

All references to the expiring pilots have been removed.
Most if not all of the archetype errors have been found and fixed, there are still some warnings but we are gradually finding and updating them and as we do there are less errors found..
Faulty thrusters, Power supplies and other odd items have either been fixed or updated, incorrect gate loadouts have been fixed.
The Nansei problem has been fixed, the Hokkaido gate problem has been fixed
Some of the original Freelancer ships have had the file references changed. The way the loadouts were written in goods.ini were wrong i.e. several of the freelancer ships have HpHeadlight when it should be HpHeadLight (Capital 'L') this is in the .cmp. - oh and btw there are quite a few of these.
There was a problem with the Mantis found and fixed (.cmp again)

The Kandor crash was found and fixed, Battleship Batlor also had a large asteroid removed from the docking bay,
Nehida has now got planetary bases.. about 12 new ship buy places, Amdor has had Siren updated and a couple of new things added.. (a new surprise)

we are working on linking some of the new systems (though I think that will take a little time yet)

2 new ships have been added a fighter and a super freighter - (not size wize - capability) and I am working on 2 more..

A lot more stuff I cannot remember... new weapons etc., as well, mainly low level including a New "mini" Chaingun - Effects courtesy of Glock - tho I dunno if he thought they would be used that way - lol

Epsilon1.dll was faulty its been replaced and the ids are being transferred

I cant think of much more now but there is a lot of work being done.. I often put in 16 hour days on this but there is still an amount to do..we are all working hard.. We will want some beta testers soon, so volunteers are requested.. there will be lots of updates, however I have a secure ftp site (in fact we have 2 ftp sites) so fairly frequent downloads of approx 30 megs will be the norm.. I also have a private locked server for online games tho it will not be 24/7

Harrier
