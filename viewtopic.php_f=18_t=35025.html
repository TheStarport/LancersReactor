Can you give what loadouts you would like, so far what you have asked for would take about 5mins to mod. I can`t modify the hardpoints of the model, other than to use lights as weapon mounts.
Failing that you could try the tutorials section. There`s a whole forum just for newbies who haven`t done much modding a and want to give it a go.
To change the inital ship, download SDK (its all the encrypted freelancer txt files un-encrypted), then go into you get go into data/missions/M01a/m01a.ini

In it there`s a line saying :
Act_SetShipAndLoadout = ge_fighter, msn_playerloadout
ge_fighter is the starflier, you want it to be = bw_elite2, msn_playerloadout
for the loadout go into data/ships/loadouts.ini
(i`m hoping that you have done a bit of modding already and that you know the names of the weapons, like = gd_gm_gun01_mark04, which is the gmg skyblast B the best rapid weapon in the original game). Otherwise, if you just post what you would like and i`ll put the loadout up for you on my reply.
Hopefully this has been helpfull, c ya.
