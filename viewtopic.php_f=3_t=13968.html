Yes, the Turret view has one good use only:

The Humpback.

If you load up the Humpback with a bunch of Sunblast A cannons and turrets, you've got a formidable killing machine. You want weapons that are FAST, so it's either Photon or Laser-based weapons ( and Photon weapons are by far the better pick ).

Just force your enemies to trail you, hit the H key to go into turret view, then just bring the guns to bear as if you were attacking them normally. Your turret view will maneuver around similarly to the way you maneuver the ship while in regular combat.

Try going Xenos hunting in a Humpback, it's a kick
