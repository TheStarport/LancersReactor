(2 SFs, 2Gs, 2Os) - they EAT POWER in no time.

THATS WHY Nomad weapons are better - they DONT USE ENERGY AT ALL

Now take a look on their damage:

Nomad Energy Cannon: 847 x 3.03 = 2566 (!) damage per second
Nomad Energy Blaster: 645 x 4 = 2540 (!) damage per second...

Place a great shield-damage turret on your sabre - and you will be the best :

[ side note: yeah, I am not using missiles - even I have a pack of

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
When...
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
After the SP missions I've finished on level 22 or something, it was
a complete mystery where to go and what to do.

I wont tell you HOW to make money quick - since I stayed FIGHTER
not a merchant all the way... wandering around systems you can find
alot of ship wrecks and those weapons you pick up make you level pretty
fast ( not to say you may sell them and use money for some trade if you want )

So its obvious that Nomads drop their weapons mostly after level 36...
At 38 (max level) you will sure get them - just give it some time and kill
alot of them. I had the problem being level 38 and fighting alot of them -
with no result, but then weapons started to pop out of them.

So once you are level 36-38, have a nice level 10 ship packed with good
weapons - you are ready to go - you have to be ready since they attack
in fours (4)

Sometimes you get one Cannon or Blaster per 4 killed Nomads,
sometimes nothing - so keep killing them

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Where
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
You can find Nomads close to Unknown sectors...
There are two of them:

One is from the Omicron Gamma & other is from the Omicron Alpha.

1)

IF you are on a good terms with Corsairs ( and maybe Bounty Hunters
they fight with there ) you should take the jump hole from Omicron Gamma
to Unknown which is situated in the center of that stinky green cloud in
the upper right corner of the Omicron Gamma.
( there are alot of wrecked Corsair Fighters around )

The jump hole from the Omicron Gamma to Unknown leads to the
system with 2 planets - Monkey and Robot. Beware - they dont sell
nanobots or shield batteries and actually useless. So if you dont encounter
Nomads in Omicron Gamma near the jumphole to Unknown - jump in,
but stay close to the Jump hole to jump back and flee to planet Crete for
example. ( Corsairs base

2)

If you are cool with Outcasts ( and probably Lane Hackers they fight with )
you should go to Omicron Alpha. It has a jump hole to another Unknown
situated in the green cloud in the right part of the sector... there are alot
of wrecked Outcast fighters as well. This Unknown sector has nothing,
so you can jump in and fight nomads there or stay in Alpha near the jump hole.

Of course, you can always flee to Outcasts base - Planet Malta in O.Alpha.
