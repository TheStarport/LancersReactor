Own your own system for 400 Million Credits
You trade, make money anyway you can. Then one day ask an Admin for a system. Pay him, and then you e-mail or PM the modder and they will create your system per your instructions.

Own your own faction ship for 200 Million credits
Simple. If you a faction that has 5 members and each of you have at LEAST 10 hours playing time on your faction character, your faction can get a faction ship. There will be 10 premade faction ships, which will be the max ammount of faction ships allowed on this server.

Want your own base? 300 Million Credits
Pretty much the same as the system. Except, you choose what base, and where it goes. The name ect.

Want your own Special ship that has your name in the infocard and only you can access? 600 Million Credits for research.
If you a freelancer and you have enough credits, you can pay and admin and we will make your ship. You cant give us detailed designs, all besides what texture you want on it.

Basicly, you pay us one version, and then we make it for the next version (Which is each month.) Hope that helps.
