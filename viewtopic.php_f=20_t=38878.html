At 8Mb you are under the required specs, also the savage listed is the Savage4 ProSavage
If you had to add anything to the file to put yours in, then I would suggest that it really isn't compatible for the game. Below is a list of the features it cannot handle, and deleting the list doesn't mean it will handle them... it means it will cock it up instead.

Disable1600x1200
FL_BAD_WFOG
FL_BAD_DXTN
FL_BAD_MOD2X
NoParallelism
NoTriLinear

I think your best bet is that 32Mb card, but in honesty, you should try and stretch to a 64Mb card minimum - because you will struggle with many games otherwise. They are as cheap as chips these days... get em for about $30 or so.
