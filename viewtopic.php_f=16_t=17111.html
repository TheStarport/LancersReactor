Getting on the topic I do have a rather good route for starters in MP. Once you have earned yourself 15000 credits or so and a Rhino, buy luxury consumer goods at Manhatten and sell it at Ainu Depot in Hokkaido (GC base), then ship cardamine back. At the start everybody in Kusari is neutral so there is little risk short of a few hackers, xenos, police patrols and the occasional server bully in a VHF. You can't do much about the VHF but a basic Rhino with me at the helm have no trouble at all evading the other three problems. One round trip will get you enough cash to buy 3 Dromedary freighters.
