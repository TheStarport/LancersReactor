easymac

Sun Mar 16, 2003 1:15 am by easymac

My personal suggestion on acquiring the Sabre is to befriend the Outcasts, not the Hessians. I've actually managed to do this while still being on green with the Corsairs and Bounty Hunters (i killed xenos till I was neutral with the Outcasts and green with bounty hunters, killed red hessians and mollys till i was neutral with the Corsairs, then went to both of their homeworlds and bribed them to green). This really makes your trips through the border worlds less stressful for both sides, and gives you the option of trying out the titan and the sabre (if you got the cash). My only problem is that now that I've allied myself with the three strongest factions in the game, only the occaisonal hessian sabre wing and of course the nomads present any challenge to me. Kind of disappointing, but much less stressful, as i said.

The other reason to go for the Outcast's Sabre is that you find a lot of cool stuff along the way, assuming you take the time to explore the systems on your way to Omicron Alpha. Seeing those two unknown systems, finding that niobium rich field in tau-37, exploring those beautiful landscapes, reading all that cool backround information on those mysterious pirates... it's all worth it, more so than just staying in the center of Sirius and getting the Sabre with the Hessians. But then again, that's my explorer-side talking.
