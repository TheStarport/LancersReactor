FriendlyFire

Thu Jul 19, 2007 10:12 am by FriendlyFire

Going for a true scale would require individual systems (1 per planet), and you'd often have to make the planet be outside of the playable zone (otherwise it'd take up the whole system). This is especially true for planets such as Jupiter.

FLCE had to make the ships a lot smaller too. I think the biggest ship was 200m long, while the smallest was around a meter long (in-game). This made Earth around 500k of diameter, with the sun being a few billion meters away. (My numbers might be off, it's been a while I didn't look at the code)

We couldn't even fit Jupiter in a system, so we made the system revolve around Europa and you could see Jupiter in the background.
