Ship balancing: Opinions needed

Hello people,

I am balancing out all the standard issue ships, so that they all can use level 10 weapons, have increased hull and energy etc.
Everything is going fine, except that I do not have a lot of experience flying all of the available ships.
What my goal is now is that ships like the Drake, which handles incredibly fast, will get a lot less armor than a Dagger, which maneuvers much slower. This should balance out the fact that a Drake can outmaneuver about anything.

What I'd like to hear from you is your opinion about every ship in Freelancer.
On a scale of 1-10, how maneuvrable do you find the various ships in Freelancer?
1 is dead slow, 10 is very fast.

Dev

Fri Jun 16, 2006 12:57 am by Dev

In the vanilla game, a lot of ships have identical handling. Whoever was in charge of balancing the ships out was likely either forced to scramble to make a deadline or simply at a loss for ideas. Anyway, I will list identical ships in groups, and these groups in decreasing order of handling.

- Patriot
- Starflier, Drake (these could also arguably be more agile than the patriot)
- Falcon, Eagle, Barracuda, Hammerhead
- Hawk, Piranha, Cavalier, Banshee, Dagger, Legionairre, Bloodhound
- Defender
- Startracker
- Crusader, Dragon, Valkyrie, Stilleto, Sabre, Centurion, Titan, Wolfhound, Anubis
- Rhino, Clydesdale, Drone, Mule, Dromedary
- Humpback

In the interest of variety, you may want to first pursue some tinkering of ship handling first and then balance durability and firepower afterwards (it's quite easy; the hardest part is making decisions). But if you don't want to, those groupings above ought to be helpful.
