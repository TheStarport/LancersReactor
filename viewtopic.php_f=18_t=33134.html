Chips

Fri Jul 23, 2004 5:46 am by Chips

I don't think there is a tutorial on how to do it in 3d - you just make a system, then hike stuff up or down as you wish. Things to note - tradelanes CANNOT be angled - the lane rings MUST stay "verticle" otherwise they ONLY dock one way (The other sends you off down the tradelane BACKWARDS through space instead of towards the next tradelane) - so once that was out of the way - it was simply a case of making a normal tradelane, then adding the 3rd verticle positino value afterwards. 500 or so was a nice easy amount - with 1000 being the limit of "****iness" or belief. Slap it at 2000 and your rising so quick it looks bad.

now just move planets, populations etc around that area.

oh - theere was ONE thing i had to do. Tradlane populations -i had to work out the angle of the lane using some simple trig, and then angle the tradelane population to match it - so that NPC's can travel/join if disrupted etc
