There can be only one...five times

I was just checking some movie news, and I learned that a new Highlander movie is ready to start filming. Now, I've heard about this one about two years after Highlander: Endgame hit theaters. It's called Highlander: The Source. It stars Adrian Paul Jim Byrnes and Peter Wingfield(from the tv series and aforementioned Endgame). Now, for those of you who don't know what Highlander is, the whole movie series is about a group of Immortals struggling to become the last one on Earth. The only way they can die is if they have their heads lopped off. The victorious Immortal would then take the power of the dead one and become stronger. The original movie was awesome, because the story was original and fun. The Immortals would (supposedly) fight until only one remained, hence, "There can be only one." Unfortunately, they decided to start making sequels, which nullified that idea. Three sequels,and now a fourth one. I'll admit that while I enjoyed the sequels alot(I have all movies on DVD), they just didn't have the magic or imagination of the first movie. This new movie, as I said above, is called The Source. The storyline, thus far, is Duncan Macleod and a bunch of other Immortals going to try to find the source of their power and immortality. Now before anyone I've got mixed reactions about this new movie. I love Highlander. I have always been a fan, but I have to wonder, when will it stop?
