Hi all

A cmp importer is at the planning stage, but it may take a while to do. I have some other programs to finish first - time permitting etc.

I haven't had any problems with the last version of the CMP importer so far. The models such as li_dreadnought and br_battleship can be imported into Milkshape 1.7.0+, but the various (destructable) parts need rotating/moving into the correct positions manually. They also need regrouping to get the main parts of the ship at the top of the mesh list.
