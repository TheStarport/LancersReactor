Nightsilver

Mon Apr 14, 2003 5:08 am by Nightsilver

Don't just "kill your allies". There's basically two ways to do this.

First, look in your factions list. Click on the faction you want to be friends with and see who is listed under the Enemies section. Then, target and kill those ships until you go neutral with the faction you want.

Second, kill Xenos. This works for raising your rep with basically every faction out there. The only one I KNOW it hurts is Zoners (although not very much). Also, it might have a slight negative effect on the IMG. Killing Xenos doesn't raise every faction, though...some groups, most notably Bretonians, could care less about the Xenos.
