Porche''s Millenium Falcon

After downloading and using Porche's Millenium Falcon, I have to say i was a little dismayed. I found many inconsistancies, namely the lack of turrets that the infocard said it had (supposed to have 6 turrets, but it only had 2) This encouraged me to give the Falcon a complete overhaul, improving upon its systems and and visual appearance. Here is a list of the changes I made:

-removed 2 gun mounts, added 2 turret mounts. guns/turrets now 4/4
-turret placement is as follows: 1 top, 1 bottom, 1 rightside, 1 leftside, all providing 360 degree coverage
-guns are all forward facing
-removed "heavy fighter" shield capabilities and added freighter shields
-tweaked turn radius, turn angle, and ship physics for more realistic flight
-modified engine fire/glow for improved appearance
-modified front headlights for improved appearance
-added 4 docking lights: 2 top, 2 bottom, for improved appearance
-changed camera distance for improved appearance and gameplay
This baby is still the fastest ship in the fleet!

I could re-submit it, but I need Porche's permission first. But in the meantime, anyone have any comments, or suggestions for further improvements of this model?
