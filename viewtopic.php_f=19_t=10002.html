Deltrax

Sat Mar 15, 2003 12:57 am by Deltrax

Open your profile window (F9) and you will see your faction standings. if you click a faction it will display it's enemies and allies. Killing allies or members of the faction themselfs, you will get an worse standing. Killing an enemy of that faction will get you an better standing.

So if the police and/or bounty hunters like you, the criminals (Liberty Rouges, Outcasts, Blood Dragons, etc) will dislike you. It's all connected, you can only be neutral to everyone (difficult), not friendly. It is possible to get yourself disliked by everyone.
