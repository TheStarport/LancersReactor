Sariel Jones

Thu Apr 24, 2003 4:05 pm by Sariel Jones

Have a look at the flconfigdatabase.txt file in the freelancer\exe folder - it contains a list of 3d cards and drivers. I guess Freelancer looks up your card/driver in this database and displays a message if it isn't supported. New cards like the Radeon 9500 PRO won't be in the database, so you'll get the unrecognised 3d card message - just answer Yes and pray.
