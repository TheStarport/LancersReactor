Update:
The Gemini Sector map is done!!!

I still need to change the Mission ini's to:
1)Reflect the exclusive use of jumpholes throughout the Gemini Sector.
2)Change the jumpholes that are locked out during missions.
3)Change the jumpholes that are unlocked during, and after missions.

The Gemini Sector was arranged so that the missions still follow the same paths.
(They're just stretched, and contorted, a bit.)
You'll be forced to "Explore" more of Gemini than in the standard FL game.
Going from Kyoshu to New Tokyo to start Mission #8 requires going through a minimum of 6 systems.
There are 17 new systems with minable fields, patrols, random encounters.
Perry has a fully functioning base.
You'll have access to Freeport 7, Toledo, and all but the last mission's system.
(But you'll pay the price for going in solo!)
(I've added a bunch of Nomad Defensive patrols around the jumpholes.)

I still need to go through and make sure that names are correct, and that the jumpholes are at the right spots in the systems.
(Some missions require jumpholes off the beaten path, and I can't change that much.)

Also, there's only one path between the systems.
Not the 2 or 3 as in FL.
(Makes it more interesting, and more Privateer-like.)
