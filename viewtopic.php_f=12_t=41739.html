If you can't stand showing-off then you shouldn't read further

I had a dream vacation in early 1994 - seven weeks on Pacific Islands:

Starting in Hawai, Oahu, Honolulu - by coincidence there was a Depeche Mode-gig the second evening

Later Fidji - Blue Lagoon Cruise (it was delightful even without Brooke Shields)

Easter days on Samoa (at that time the island was ravaged by a taiphoon earlier in the year)

Tonga - Nukualofa and Fafà Island: each "hotel room" was a 'bure', a little house. From my hammock it was 15 m to the sea and 50 m to the bar - oh nice memories ...

10 days in Bora Bora

and on the way back to Europe I spent 6 days in California. In the Sierra Nevada watching the BIG trees (Sequioa NP) I was in the snow (End of April 94). Will never forget the change within 44 hours from 38 degrees Celsius and 90% humidity in Papeete to minus 12 degrees at the mountains.

All the trip did cost a fortune. But at that time I ran Radio stations.
