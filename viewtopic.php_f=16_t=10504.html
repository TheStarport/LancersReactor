Darkness2k

Tue Mar 18, 2003 1:28 am by Darkness2k

As far as I know, the only "cheat" is to change the PerfOptions.ini in My Documents\My Games\Freelancer.

Fine the area that says "DIFFICULTY_SCALE = 1.00"
Change it to 0.00 and you can't die... try smashing into planets, you'll bounce straight off again.... fly into the various stars... you'll bounce off....
and you won't take any damage from enemy ships (although they can still damage other NPC's).

NOTE: This (fortunately) has no effect on multiplayer games.

(I never tried this, but perhaps if you change DIFFICULTY_SCALE = 1.00 to something like "DIFFICULTY_SCALE = 2.00" you'll take twice as much damage from the enemy (makes for a harder game).)
