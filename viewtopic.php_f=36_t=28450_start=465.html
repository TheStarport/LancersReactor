Laserblast

Sun Sep 26, 2004 9:40 am by Laserblast

The ships above level 38 are set at that level for multiplayer purposes. In theory, they are set to this price because you can't get money that high without obtaining that level. In order to change it, go to Freelancer\Data\Equipment. Open the market_ships area and change the first number to 38 or lower, and it will be available in SP. The file can be opened with notepad...

Higher than lvl 38 is not normally atainable in SP. If you want to go higher, you should probably go to MP.

The Enterprise A is supposedly on Ganymede, but I have not checked it out. It could very well be the original enterprise misnamed as far as I know.
