Anyone ever tried multi shields?

Just for a bit of fun during spare, I decided to see what would happen (or if it was even possible) to turn an armored transport into a vessel worthy of battle. (Hey, if not, it would at least be an extremely powerful trader) So, first on the list was to see if it was possible to add more than one shield to such a ship. Turns out - you can! With a wee bit of tweaking, I yielded this:

http://i71.photobucket.com/albums/i145/ ... reen15.jpg

Seeing as it worked, I decided to see how it made out in a fight. The result was incredibly odd, but incredibly cool at the same time. See, you only had one shield health bar, but once it reached halfway, you heard the shield down message, and could observe something really neat. As it turns out, one shield will only guard one side of the ship, while the other guards the other side. The result is a ship significantly beefier, but with a strange weakness. If you smash say the port side shield enough, you can shoot through them to the hull, but if you shoot from starboard, it'll be blocked by the remaining shield. So cool. I could turn this into a perdy cool mod...

Well, tweaking wasn't that big of a deal. (It actually wasn't hard...) Basically, I added a couple lines to shiparch.ini under the armored transport (Actually, its in the Moo mod... I didn't even notice that I had it on until I went to run the game... sorry Bliggy I'll probably make a version out on vanilla to test it out...) just adding the shield hardpoint and link:

shield_link = bw_freighter_shield01, HpMount, HpEngine01
hp_type = hp_freighter_shield_special_10, HpEngine01
hp_type = hp_freighter_shield_special_9, HpEngine01
hp_type = hp_freighter_shield_special_8, HpEngine01
hp_type = hp_freighter_shield_special_7, HpEngine01
hp_type = hp_freighter_shield_special_6, HpEngine01
hp_type = hp_freighter_shield_special_5, HpEngine01
hp_type = hp_freighter_shield_special_4, HpEngine01
hp_type = hp_freighter_shield_special_3, HpEngine01
hp_type = hp_freighter_shield_special_2, HpEngine01
hp_type = hp_freighter_shield_special_1, HpEngine01

Then in Goods.ini, added this to the Armored transport package entry:

addon = shield03_mark10_fr, HpEngine01, 1

I used the armored transport, but it could probably just as easily be done with an actual vanilla ship. I'm thinking of making a quick little mod that makes all of the ships able to mount an extra shield... the tricky part would be to make the hud read it properly. Perhaps there is a way to make more shield indicators?
