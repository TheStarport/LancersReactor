Division-By-Zero

Wed Apr 09, 2003 7:51 am by Division-By-Zero

I think the freighters are pretty well balanced in this game. The good freighters all carry at least 100 units more than the VHF's, they can live long enough to run away from a fight (I've done this several times running in the edge worlds/border worlds in a humpy no less). And, they can carry enough firepower to take out lesser wings of fighters.

What it sounds to me is that people haven't had enough time to *learn* how to drive the freighters correctly. By correctly, I mean in a manner that will let you kick ass and still live. The best advice I've seen on this board and I've worked with in the game is to make good use of the autopilot. If I get jumped/lane hacked, I would set my autopilot to the next lane ring/waypoint, then go straight into turret mode. While in turret mode, I keep strafing, afterburning and braking. It's very effective at throwing enemy fire from your ship. It does take practice though.

Better yet, travel in a group with a couple of people on the server with you. that's the best way to go. They take care of trouble while you keep going forward on your business.

As for make a freighter with better shields, consider the transports in the this game. none of them have shields. just a lot of armor or something. In fact, you can selectively target cargo pods on the larger transports and steal goods without kiling the ship. Again, though, the main idea behind a transport is to carry crap, not duke it out with anything. that's the escorts job.
