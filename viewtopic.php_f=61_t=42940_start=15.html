Mace_166

Tue Mar 28, 2006 3:45 pm by Mace_166

Well, looking through the posts.. You do need to change the ids_name location to be in names_resources instead of the custom one.
The short name can be in a custom dll, but the ids_name must be in names_resources.
Another good little check is to back everything up and to use entries from a different faction in place of the ones you want. That way you know if the problem is with the faction or the ship+loadout. At least then you can narrow down the possible causes.
After that you could try using openSp to spawn using the different encounters. Even if you don't want OpenSp as a part of the mod you can still use it by modifying the m13 file.
When you say you can't get the encounters working, are you sure that the faction has been added completely? Its the kind of thing where one little mistake will cause the whole damn thing to crash.
