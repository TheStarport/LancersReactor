EB,

Actually, I got the idea from Jorg Neumann, in his Dev Diary at; http://www.gamespy.com/devdiary/february03/freelancer1/ .

He said:
A) Free Flight: When the player left-clicks and holds the mouse button down ("dragging" ), we're basically turning the mouse into a magical hand that moves an imaginary joystick . Moving the mouse in any direction acts just like moving a joystick and holding it in that direction.


So, I thought it would look pretty cool that when in cockpit view we actually saw the cockpit, and "our" arm holding the "stick" of the craft we are piloting and if it moved to the correct position to match the manuevers we are doing with our mouse.

Yeah, it would be only for "show", but it would really give the "immersion nuts", like myself, that extra bit of "I am there." that they/we crave...and of course could be turned off, essentially giving three chioces for cockpit view; True Cockpit/Struts Only/HUD Only.

Yup...I am a dreamer!
