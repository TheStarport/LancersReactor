warzog

Mon Jan 23, 2006 1:47 pm by warzog

The problem is being caused by the "fuses."
I discovered this while doing Wing Commander FL.
Open Data\FX\Fuses.ini
The first section calls for the destruction of groups.
(Under Death Comm)
Remove all of the [destroy_group sections to stop that error.
You may have to rework the other fuses (in fuses.ini) that are listed for that ship (in shiparch.ini and solararch.ini)
I had to rework all of them, and created a few new ones for the new ships.
Download Wing Commander FL v2.6 to see.


Watch your 6!
