That sounds like a texture issue. Had that my self in my mod early on.

Report the error as the Voyager texture size is not conforming to the 64x64, 128x128, 256x256, 512x512 limits. Once its resized you will see the texture but until it is it will remain white all over.

One word of warning though, because most mods use other peoples ships, the people who made those ships may not be willing to update them for other mods so the fix may never happen.

Mods that feature ships by ship makers who are still around and still supporting the game may be better or made by the mod team them selves may be better options for you.
