@jimmybob:
You must also define the weapon hardpoints in shiparch.ini in order to make them useable for the player.

@bjj6:
Try and buy it, and check how much cargo you can put in her. Unlike weapons, the stats displayed for a ship are not generated automatically, but also just infocards (which you can change with FLEd_ids). Thus, if you change e.g. the Rhino's cargo bay to 2000 units, the infocard will still say 80, although you can put in 2000.
