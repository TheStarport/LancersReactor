I'm kind of new to this forum...well..not really...I was on under a different name really...the same ...but different at the same time.
Anyway...
Some other good games are:
X² the threat -
Kind of hard in the beginning...but worth it. Dogfighting can be easy or hard...depends on the level of the AI you happen to be up against at the same time. Also there is trading in the game with a working economy...something that isn't always included in some games. Oh... and lots of eye candy...LOTS of it.
Like I said...it worth it. Excellent game.

Independence War II
-the edge of Chaos -
Another good game by Particle systems Whom I'm very familiar with.
The original (Independence war I & Defiance) may or may not work on WinXP.
It was designhed in the erra of the 3DFX...which as everyneknows is defunct now in place of OpenGL.
Its a good game though. The website also has mods to download. If you purchace this game, you might want to know that there is a very slight bug in the game.
One of the systems for a "side" mission was spelled wrong. There is a fix for this on the website though. The one in particular actually allows MODS and the like to be added without trouble. Ind of like the FLMM but for Independence War. Its called F14 patch I believe...can't remember really its been a while since I had to download the patch. But its a goodgame ll around. Also...before I forget. With the F14 patch, the game becomes open ended instead of ending after the last mission.
The flight dynamics are a little difficult at first, but after a few runs and pirating...you'll get the hang of it. Its a very vast universe...and in the beginning...you can't leave the Sector/system (Cluster??) you begin in, butafter you complete a few missions, you can eventually get money for a jumpdrive/engine and can leave. The missions are really good and the story line isn't bad either. The cutscenes are excellent as well. A game you might enjoy alot.

Battle Cruiser Millenium-
This is a game that takes a long time to play, but it plays in real time. The graphics look like someone hijacked them from the late 80's early 90's, butthe game works great either way. It runs in real-time and the damage a ship takes take a long time to repair.
This game is for someone that likes to take their time playing and exploring.
There is also a sequel for this game called Universal Combat. Basically the same game with better graphics and all the updates for it.
If you get BC Millenium, I strongly suggest going to the website and looking up the patches that might be there for it. They are very good at repairing what damage there was to the game when it was released earlier than it was supposed to be. Thus the reason for all the patches.
Even though it needs (or may need) patches to get straight, its a good game.

Someone has already mentioned Free Space and Tacyon...so I won't get into them.
Both are decent games though...and I have Tacyon...but not Free Space...can't seem to locate it anywhere LOL.
Thats all I can think of ATM.
I'm sure that there are ithers here that have heard of a few games that I haven't.
Good hunting though. Even thought there may be a game that isn't mentioned here that someone knows about doesn;t mean it doesn't exsist.
