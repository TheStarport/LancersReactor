4 Nomad cannons and 2 Tizona del Cid is my current loadout for my Eagle. I'm considering replacing a Tizona del Cid with a Cannonball Missile launcher but I'm still not sure - 2 Tizonas seems redundant but I like symmetrical weapons as well. I prefer the Nomad Energy Cannon for my Level 10s because they do more damage per second than ANY gun in the game, followed very closely by the Nomad Energy Blaster. Plus they use NO energy. Incidentally, the firing arc on the Eagle's turret does extend into the front hemisphere- if you go to the *extreme* right or left it will begin firing. It's not very useful, but I put a Skyblast B on there just for the hell of it. The Sunslayer Torpedo is loads of fun to fire but the launcher is difficult to get without ticking off a lot of people, and at 7000+ a pop, you'd beter have some serious creds to stay stocked. Otherwise, the Starkiller is a nice, economical way to add a little punch to your head-on runs.
