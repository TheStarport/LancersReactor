A Couple of Questions and a new System.

First off, let me indroudce myself. I'm a long-time Freelancer player and have been playing for quite a while. I have just become interested in modding because of all the cool things I have downloaded and tried out. Some really impressive things, guys.

Lets get the questions out of the way. First of all, can someone provide a link to DL Freelancer Explorer? This is for another project I'm am working on, not this new system I refrenced in the title. Another thing. I cannot get some of the mods to work in FLMM, namely the Cruise Speed mods. It says something like
"Error: problem activating Speed 600 mod. This was only the test run so no files were changed..." Anyway to fix this?

Ah, now onto the good stuff. I am currently creating a system named Tiberus-Prime. As some of you might know, Tiberus was the river Rome was built on, so this a very wet, moist, system. It curretly has a very large Gas Giant in the center, with 9 moons around it at different poistions, heights, sizes, appearences...
But what is special about this system is that all of the houses are allied with each other in this system. Liberty, Bretioan(sp?), Kusari, and Reinland control a perimeter around the main Gas Giant. The planet is consently under seige by multiple factions. It's really cool, seeing all of these Cruisers forming a perimeter around the planet, dogfighting and attacking the pirate forces. The 2 Suns are a Meduim Green and a Blue Dwarf(really cool). The Jump gate to New Berlin in inside the perimeter, so coming out of the Jump Gate you get this REALLY cool view of the Green Sun halfway around the planet. There are no stations as of yet, but any suggestions whould be helpful.
