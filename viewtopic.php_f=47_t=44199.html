hiya gents! Zeta1 here with an RPG i'll actually decide to do,lol. anyway, heres the deal. you dont control factions in this, only play with them. the thing that makes this rpg unique, is that you dont play as any of the houses, police, or militaries, or companies. Only criminals. The Outcasts, Lane Hackers, and Liberty Rouges have started a new group. The Tri Alliance. The Outcasts also have relations with anyone who wants Cardamine. Anyone who is a member of, The Maltarian Syndicate. Anyone who has alot of credits to join that is.
The Red Hessians have formed an uneasy alliance with the Corsairs, founding the Othatie Coalition, in response to the Tri Alliance. The Hogosha, who work artifacts directly with the corsairs, are mindful of the Hessians, wondering if it is wise to allign themselves with such uneasy friends.
The Molly's are still at odds with the Corsairs, and now the Hessians for the Dublin gold fields, but will they be able to stand the Othatie Coalition onslaught?
The Junkers really don't know where to stand in this, so they have assumed a stance somewhat similar to that of the zoners, trying to keep it neutral, but this is a very dangerous game, how long can they stay that way?
The Xenos, as usual are hostile with pretty much everyone. [What else is new?
The Hogosha are fully allied with the Farmer's alliance, forming Greater Kusari, seeing that it is for the greater good of Kusari.
The Gainains have formed an Alliance with the Molly's, under the condition they help with freeing Edinburg, and They will protect and try to free Dublin.
The Blood Dragons and Golden C are fully allied, forming the Free Kusari Union, but the Blood Dragons are mindful of the GC's addiction to Cardamine, if they go to war with the Outcasts, will the Alliance hold? if so, will the GC survive without the cardamine in their systems? The Free Kusari Union also is at War with the Hogosha, but secretly attempting to sway the Farmer's Alliance to join the Free Kusari Union, promsising bountiful Farm subsidies if needed, and kicking out Synth Foods once they retake Kusari from the courrupt Kieretsu controlled goverment. [Kieretsu are Kirshio and Samura, huge goverment like companies wielding massive political and economical power.
The LWB, Unioners, and Bunshuch have formed an uneasy alliance, the New Rheinland Republic. however, it's uneasy do to the LWB and Unioners after different Companies, and the Bunshuch's distast for common piracy and killing, perfering a more peaceful action of demonstration for reforms.
The LWB, Unioners, and Bunshuch would like to have better relations with the Hessians, but the relationship with them is only degrading since the New Rheinland Republic wants a better Rheinland, and Corsairs are enemies of Rheinland.

That covers all the Criminal factions, play as a member of any one of them you want, but you must work with your allied players. But how long before the entire Sirius underworld errupts in Pandemonium and Bloodshed? Can it be stopped? You will decide! have fun, and repect other players.
