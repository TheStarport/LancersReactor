Hi! One suggestion for hardpoints.
I add the hardpoints in hardcmp to new ships. Its good. Then i open the cmp in milkshape and in utf editor the same time. By moving the cursor in milkshape you will have very nice hp coordinates. So I edit the cmp by having the coordinates right in front of me.Then save it in utf editor with another name, since utf editor cannot overwrite. Close ms without saving anything.
Done. Same moves go for coorecting the hp coordinates. Easy way, fast and very accurate.

Same can be done for ships with existing or wrong hardpoints.
