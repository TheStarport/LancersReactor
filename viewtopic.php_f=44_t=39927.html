Mace_166

Thu Jul 21, 2005 5:46 am by Mace_166

Well if you have two thrusters then you will go faster when you use them but the power used will go faster accordingly. Your basic speed or cruise isn't affected in anyway by having more or less thruster mounts.

If your doing the mounts, i find it easiest to create a face and copy it around the model in milkshape rotating it where needed.
Then export and bring it into hardcmp to see where the mounts are. Its a lot easier modifying the mounts than creating them in hardcmp completely.

It might be worth doing a basic model first just to get the feel for it and learn the skills. Then go back and do the model that you really want. I guarrantee if you did the original model first, you would only want to go back and do it again anyway..
