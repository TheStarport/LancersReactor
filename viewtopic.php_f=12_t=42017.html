Babylon 5 - Multiplayer - Free Game

The hardcore-Babylon 5-addicts probably already knew about this game; but did YOU ?
A Moder-Community "Space Dream Factory" released back in 2003 a FREE game “Babylon 5: I’ve Found Her — Danger and Opportunity” .

The team made this game now even better: the "Skirmish Pack" offers


The long-anticipated mission simulator allows players to re-experience missions from the original campaign, or create and play their own missions! It also includes the so-called “Skirmish mode”. (...)
A major addition to the game is the LAN multiplayer with top number of 8 allowed players, with several types of scenarios: cooperative, free-for-all and team deathmatch. Challenge your friends in multiplayer battles, shooting, sliding, rolling and getting the large doses of adrenaline-surging action! Or join them in cooperative mode, helping each other to fight your way throught the well-known campaign missions from the single-player game!

Another important addition is the graphics. (...)The sharp detail textures provide the best look on ships and installations at close range, eliminating the blurry texture enlargement. The advanced particle system effects improve the quality of the special effects, such as explosions, fire, smoke and debris from destroyed ships.



Haven't tested it yet but I think the project could be of some interest for the FL-community, namely
