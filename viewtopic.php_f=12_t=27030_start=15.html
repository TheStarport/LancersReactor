Nickless V2

Tue Dec 23, 2003 1:26 am by Nickless V2

I found the insystem jump (target something with the T key and then press A ) to be very usfull, but I haven't been able to dogfight or jump to another system.
The game seems very basic and isn't completed into very much detail. But hey, It's only 164MB and it's free, so what do you expect.
I used arrows for steering, and it kinda works alright. But for the rest it's kinda crappy imho. Not realy a game for me.

Btw, Helpkey is shift+M .
Dunno why shift because M on itsself does nothing, but who cares. The help is extremely basic, but it gets you moving.

Quick overveiw of keymapping:

T = Target . Kinda obvious, but it's used for targeting everyting. Planets, bases, jumpholethingies and ships, wether they are friend or foe.

D = Docking . You need to be inside the white squares that apear near a base/planet to be able to use it, although being inside a 2D object in a 3 universe is kinda dificult, just type D a few times when manouvering, and you'll notice when you're close enough

A = Insystem Jump . Target an object with T and press A . You jump right in front of it.

A = Increade speed (not 100% sure)

Z = Decrease speed (Also not 100% sure)

\ = Increase speed to max.

Backspace = Full stop

Tab = Thruster , although I haven't noticed any increase in speed when you're already at full speed.

Space = Fire guns

Enter = Fire missiles.

To exit the game press esc and then Q . Press Esc instead of Q to abort exit.

Thats all I figured out in about one hour of gameplay. It doesn't realy hold my interrest.
