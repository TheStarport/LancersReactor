Nephilim

Tue Nov 04, 2003 11:15 pm by Nephilim

Unfortunately no. While stock FL ships all have multiple .sur files assigned to them (one collision frame for the entire mesh and "subsets" of that frame for each destructable component), at present there is no way to "extend" an existing hit detection box around a custom ship by sticking additional .sur's to specific parts of its mesh (and since the CMP exporter itself only supports one component, the issue is compounded further).

As for the jumpgate dilemma... let's just say your phantom_physics method proved MOST helpful once the initial mountpoint crisis was over.
