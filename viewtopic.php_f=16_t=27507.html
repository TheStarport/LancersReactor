Well, it may not be the best route, but I use it to get me started off on MP. Boron from Pittsburg to Ft. Bush, and Pharmaceuticals on the way back.

(The following is outside of the 4 Liberty Systems)
After awhile get a Rhino, and when you can afford, get a full load of Boron, class 2 graviton shield, and full nanos and batteries. Then bring the Boron (very carefully) to LD-14 in Leeds and make a small fortune. Then Optronics from Leeds to New London, and Lux food (Buy a Clydesdale) from New London to Manhattan (or a farther away place that buys for more). Makes about $220 000.
