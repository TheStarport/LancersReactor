On my Titan I have a pair of Tizona del Cid cannons on the outer (class 9) gun mounts. On the inner most gun mounts (best accuracy) I have a pair of Diamondbacks, and the remaining gun mounts have a pair of Guardians.

The Tizona del Cids take out any shield really fast (4.00 refire rate, 1242 shield damage). I use these by pressing 1 and 2, the remaining loadout (Guardians and Diamondbacks) are grouped together and I just use the mouse button for them.

I dont use missles, first they are expensive and I have not found them to be overly effective.

The most effective method ive found is to use the Tizonas del Cids to break through the shields, and then use my main loadout to destroy the hull. The Tizonas use very little energy, while my main loadout drains my power very fast.

Revenge

Sun Mar 23, 2003 6:46 am by Revenge

Well basically, when you are playing a human, they tend to hit the panic button as soon as they lose all shields and batteries.

Consider using a pulse weapon or 2 to knock down the their shields faster. Hopefully they wont realise what you are doing and will simply assume you are using really powerful weapons. Once they are down to only armour left, things change a lot.

Make sure you deselect your shield breakers so you can fire with your real guns for longer. Some players will focus on trying to run away at this point, so be prepared to either cruise disrupt them or engine-kill -> boost to chase them. The stronger minded individuals wont stop fighting, but atleast they'll be shieldless and you'll be armed with a longer amount of time to keep firing than before.
