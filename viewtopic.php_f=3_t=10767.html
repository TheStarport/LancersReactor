Being friends with the badguys is nice for ships. One good Light Fighter is the Legionnaire, available from the Junkers owned Trafalgar Base, so it's worth making friends.

You can also get some sweet deals on illegal commodities from some of these bases. Outcast owned planet Malta sells Cardamine for 300 and, if you can get there without being 'arrested', you can sell it for 1500 on Planet Manhattan.
