Actually, you don't need to fly back all the time, just check the faction alignment from player status. If it's under 5 'blocks' on the negative side, it should show as neutral. However, the value of your cargo may make them hostile (and demand your cargo) even if you are like 4 points on the positive side (but still neutral). So check on that faction bar once in a while and when you get to neutral, go empty your cargo hold at the nearest friendly base, then fly to Buffalo, or whatever outlaw faction base you want to get in to.
