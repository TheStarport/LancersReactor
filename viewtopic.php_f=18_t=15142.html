Hey man, whats the point of getting the Transport when you can just mod your Ship Database folder. I modded my M10 Titan to hold 30,000,000 cargo. Its not hard, just open the "C:\program files\microsoft games\freelancer\data\ships\shiparch.ini" file. If your not sure what your desired ships nickname is you can just look at the HP, anyway there is a phrase on the left that says Cargo Hold then a number, with the titan the number would be 70 and the Dromedary would be 275 for example because that is there default setting. Just add a whole bunch of zeros to the end of the number and you'll be satisfied.

If you don't feel like doing that, Drop me a line at toolsparabolix@yahoo.com, include which ship you would like modded and to what and i'll get the files to you.
