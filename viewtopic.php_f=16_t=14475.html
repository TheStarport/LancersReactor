No. You don't need to be neutral with them or whatever. You can use the bribe for the Hackers no matter what. The more hostile they are to you, the more it costs you to turn them friendly.

If the bribe option is not given at the bar on Freeport 4, I suggest you launch and simply dock again. If there, as you say, is a bribe for them on Freeport 4, you should be able to do it there.

If that doesn't help at all, Go after the Xenos. Kill as much of them as needed. The hackers will turn to neutral and in time, may even start to like you (depends how many Xeno ships you add to your killboard). This will take some time, but it's also an option.

Edit:
Come to think of it, Freeport 4 is not in the hands of the Zoners, but the IMG. Usually, the bartender is the one that has the bribes. I don't think that the IMG bartender has a bribe for the Hackers. It is more likely that the bribe for the Hackers is available by the Hacker that is on that base. But he won't talk to you, because you are still an enemy to them.

I have never used a bribe on Freeport 4, so I don't know who actually has the bribe for the Hackers there, but if the Hacker himself has the bribe for the faction, you can either use the kill Xenos way or look for another place for the bribe.
