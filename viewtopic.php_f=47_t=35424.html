Oh, and hear is a brief summary of the events that have taken place in the RP so far, in roughly chronological order:


Outcasts ally with Farmers' Alliance and seize the battleship Nagumo. Beginning of Outcast militantizing.

Rheinland moves ships into Kusari to counteract the Farmers' Alliance's union with the Outcasts. The Farmers' Alliance cuts formal ties, but remains friendly to the Outcasts. Rheinland pulls out of Kusari.

Outcasts seize control of most of the Tau systems. Corsairs become more active and seize the bulk of the Omega systems. A Corsair/Outcast war seems imminent.

The Bounty Hunters' Guild joins forces with Rheinland, forming the core of the Rheinland Axis.

The Liberty Security Force disappears overnight, every one of them siding with the Outcasts. The remainder of the Liberty government lends formal support to Rheinland.

Outcasts and Corsairs join and become House Hispania. Hispania takes over Leeds, Cambridge, Edinburgh and Dublin.

Bretonia enters a formal alliance with Rheinland and Liberty. The newly formed "Rheinland Axis" prepares a counterattack on Leeds.

The Battle of Leeds is a quick victory for the Rheinland Axis. Rheinlanders use a highly unstable bioweapon from orbit, killing all hostages and Hispanics on the surface, then razes the remainder of the Hispania presence in the system.

Bretonia, concentrating on rebuilding after the Battle of Leeds, pulls military support from the Rheinland Axis, but remains a member. The Axis is renamed the Axis of Powers.

The Bretonia Records are released by Hispanic agents, indicating Liberty and Rheinland sabotage of the AFS Hispania and AFS Bretonia during the final hours of the Solar Wars.

Bretonia forgives Liberty and Rheinland for events of the past, but does not provide military support.

Kusari and the GMG begin militarizing. Despite Rheinland/GMG pressures from the 80 Years' War, they are quick to join the Axis of Powers.

Ageira Technologies' Dr. William Larings, who lost his wife and children at Leeds, restarts the Promotheus Project and retreats into his lab aboard Marathon Station.

Axis forces sucessfully attack eastern Omega systems.

Most Recent: Junkers begin investigating a mysterious datapad (I can't help but feel that something's up with it)
