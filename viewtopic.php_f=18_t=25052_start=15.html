Victor

Sun Oct 12, 2003 5:40 pm by Victor

FL Explorer was designed for XP if I understood his page correctly

FLE is good for a few things, I use it while manually editing a system because it's a quick way to get a basic visual map of the system to make sure everything is falling into place where I want it. I also use it to place patrol paths since it's a lot easier than trying to figure out the dimensions for each leg on your own, the paths do require a bit of cleanup work afterwards but the time it saves me in placing, rotating and sizing the path zones is worth the few minutes of cleanup. It also has a nice little feature to preview the wireframe of many objects, I find that helpful when I can't remember exactly which station archetype I wanted to use.

When all is said and done however it's still good to know how to do most or all of that by hand. That knowledge is essential when you're debugging things and there are better ways to setup the zones that what FLE will do for you, especially when it comes to encounters.
