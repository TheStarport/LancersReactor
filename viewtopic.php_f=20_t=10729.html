Yea, I agree with the previous 2 posts. Some other things to consider:

Make sure the Direct X 9 is installed.
Try changing your resolution before going into the game (tweak down first, then increment up)
Make sure the "latest" video drivers means WHQL certified, NOT beta drivers.
Defrag the system (always wise)
Check on your virtual memory settings, make sure things are not out of range.
Check the refresh rate you've got the monitor set at. Don't go too high at the beginning.
Add some "Ad-aware" software and check out some of the bull-hockey that might be on your system. Search out, identify, and destroy.
Do a system virus scan (you never know).

Good luck, report back with any updates.

Thank you all guys..

I solved my problems

it was about my monitor's refresh rate is less than my graphics card.
The game starts 1024x768 with 70 or higher Hz and my monitor didn't support this rate i changed from Direct3D settings the refresh rate of the graphics card. Right now it works fine. I didn't need any uninstall or anything.

If someone has the same problem may be it help him or her a lot
