C. A cargo ship, for salvage and in-combat or pre/post- combat reloading and repair (such as moving Botts/Batts over to another ship)

7th. As for the way of the new system, the layout is not too specific. A border station, some patrols of the bases and jump gates, one or two cap ship patrols, and trade lanes connecting the jump gate with the main station/s would be great.

However, the stations should be very, very hard to kill. killing them should require an extremely large frontal assault including a huge amount of torps, capital ships, and missiles to blow up my stations. Have some good anti-fighter guns available, and missile turrets on stations for fighter defense.
11th. Please make capital ships available for purchase. A huge cost, certainly, but not too big a cost that it would be impossible to buy.
12th. These may seem like a lot to ask, but one more thing. Make a built-in server operator and curse guard. I want my server members to have an enjoyable time.
