Here's the deal...short version

Some time ago last summer a "cloaking mod" was created. It was great, it was wonderful, it was simple to use and everyone seemed to enjoy using it....until the darker side of MP playing took it's normal course and people were logging into C L E A N servers and screwing up the playing experience for everyone else on the server by player killing everyone and no one could see them. There was a group of players that made their rounds to all the bigger servers and simply wrecked havoc for everyone including the server admins in charge of keeping their servers enjoyable for everyone. It was like running in quicksand for the server admins. It took all their time to track these jokers down and ban 'em. Once that wave got banned the next one came and so on and so on. It was a frickin' nightmare for everyone involved except those that cloaked.

We here at the Lancers Reactor felt we had a hand in this nightmare and decided to completely BAN the use of, discussion of, distribution of this spawn from satan for the betterment of the Freelancer community.

It was the right thing to do.
