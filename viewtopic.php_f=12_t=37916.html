Well, at current, weve got five civilisations:

Arania - Youve all seen them, so i wont go into any firther detail
Aggraviel - Sun worshippers. live in a massive jump-capable station that eats stars. last i heard, its the size of a star system.
Drork - Genetic cross between Drow elves and orcs (We had a bit of unsightly images on how that couldve come about...). they steal technology. goa'uld, replicator, asgard, federation, ancient...
Elemental - I really dont know much about them, to be honest... im told they're like elementals from Chronicles of Riddick
Flood - I mean exactly what i say. basically halo flood with technology. just, alot more of them
Graviton - Much like the Aggraviel, but these live in a black hole station, graviton weaponry, and the like.

Basically, people create a civilisation, and simulate trade and battles with other civilisations. each civilisation has a defined border (I got a massive starmap for that, 50 parsecs in every direction), and has distinct technology. they can do pretty much anything, so long as it is possible with thier technology (Some restrictions, no military time travel for one). they can develop technology, trade resources, but everything major needs to go through me.

We had the first test of the Capital ship battle system on friday, we didnt get the chance to finish, but it was a sucess (one loss to us, a Flood 'Peanut')

two distinct alliances have already formed: Aggraviel, Flood and Graviton (Im helping them, to a degree. if i didnt my forces would be stuck with doing nothing), while the Drork and Elementals are allied (I think)
