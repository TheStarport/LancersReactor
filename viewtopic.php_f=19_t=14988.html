Wed Apr 23, 2003 11:56 pm by sandorski

I like to do a little of everything. It's not the fastest way to Level Up, but leveling up for the sake of leveling up seems kinda boring to me.

Anyway, I like to get a Defender as soon as possible, that way you can travel further with some assurance of survivability(throughout Liberty and to the edges of Kusari, Rheineland, and Bretonia). If you are on a server where New York is a no-PK zone, there are a few short trade routes you can do where the per unit/profit is low, but the short trip(one trade lane)makes it worth the effort worth it IMO. It's more fun to do Missions though. You could also do some mining, but the most money you'll get this way will be from the goodies you get from Rogues and other dead baddies.

Once I get a Defender I like to go to Planet Pittsburg and pirate from the Junkers who like to use the Trade Lane. Most of the time they'll carry junk like Water, Food, or some other low profit item, but sometimes they'll have Gold, Diamonds, Cardamine(avoid using Trade Lanes and the cops), or something of value.

After making a couple hundred K, I travel out of New York to buy a Barracuda. This isn't the fastest or best way to get started, but It's what I use.

1. Destroy atleast 1 whole storage depot in manhatten to make a lot of the pirates attack you less (lane hackers, rogues, outcast, Gaian mostly)

2. Trade

Manhatten, NY: Lux Cons Goods -> Cambridge, Cambridge / Cambridge, Cambridge: Lux Food -> Manyatten, NY is a populer route as you only have to deal with a few corsairs that you just run from, takes about 15-17 minutes one way, and you can get the rhino after the first round trip, than clyds on next visit to Cambridge. Also puts you in position to get the Dorm if you want.

Leeds, Leeds: Light arms -> Ronneburg, Omega 5 / Ronneburg, Omega 5: Diamonds -> Leeds, Leeds Is a good route if you are pirate friendly, and its pretty short.

Best in system trade that I have found is N. Berlin, N. Berlin: Engine componantes -> Krutsburg, N. Berlin / Krutsburg, N. Berlin: Caldimine -> N. Berlin, N. Berlin With a Dormy you can get 100k round trip and it takes about 5 minutes.

And the Staple trade run is Krutsburg, N. Berlin: Diamonds -> N. Tokyo, N. Tokyo / N. Tok, N. Tok: Nibonium -> The Ring, N. Berlin takes about 15 minutes I guess and with a Dormy gets you about 550k.
