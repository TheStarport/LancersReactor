Freelancer Super-Universe Development Concepts Introduction

Hi out there.

Well, I am sure you know I am new here. I am a long time mod builder for a wide range of games including NWN, Treadmarks, Doom, Quake & others.

Just got the game, it ROCKS! I have seen all these kick ass mods, and am now starting to work up for a new mod for FL. I am wondering though, does anyone know if increasing the size of the overall universe itself is possible? I am talking like adding jump-holes and jump-gates that will take you to an entirely new system grouping map. Hope you guys have some suggestions and other info.

Here is the beginning of my list of ideas and some of my plans for the Mod.

All current ships pilotable (Already done apparently, but not in ways I was impressed with)
New ship classes for Fighters, Freighters plus the addition of entirely new ship classes including bombers, construction ships(to re-build destroyed trade lanes) and repair ships(to allow 'in the field' vessel repairs)
Asteroids and other solars to all be destroyable(I know about the crashes when bases with players in them are destroyed, but there has to be a fix/work-around for this issue)
New resources of both legal & contraband types(also need to figure out how to make different resources considered contraband in different systems. ie Gun-Running in some systems should be illegal)
New weapons in all classes.
New thrusters in all classes.
New shields in all classes.
A wider range of performance envelopes for all ships, shields, thrusters, etc.

Anyhow, here is your introduction to my plans and ideas. Do give me feedback and suggestions. Thanks for reading this.

Sir Spectre

Mon Jun 16, 2003 8:15 pm by Sir Spectre

First off, don't get irritable.

I would also like know myself if the FL universe is a fized size or expandable. I think it is fixed, if I remember correctly. I don't think you can add in another sector either. But if I am correct, I think it is possible to add in a great number of systems into the Sirius sector.

Since you can't fly (based on game programming) from one system to another, you can only jump, it may be completely possible to have overlapping systems, because they aren't connected by anything other than a jump. At the moment, there is no need to overlap, there is plenty of room for several systems as already being made by other modders. I may be wrong about the overlapping and limitless systems idea.

As for some of your ideas, I like the idea of having more classes for ships, but they may not be able to used as you may like. Construction ships and repair vessels may not be possible for anything but adding traffic, because I don't recall the game actually being able to have ships do such specific tasks. One modder did think of adding a "med gun" which had a negative damage (adding back armor) but it doesn't work for shields. But applying it to a repair ship at the time that you need it, that would be hard.

More contraband is also something others are doing, and much needed in my mind. I personally would love to see 1000's of commodities, but that may never happen. But commodities only being contraband in certain systems doesn't sound possible.

Adding new weapons, engine & shield classes I don't know anything about. I would just offer the suggestion, beware of things that are hard coded, you may end up wasting your time for no result. Same thing with the other things, thrusters and such.

As for no previous responses to your post, lots of newbies post "great ideas" or so they think. I'm not discrediting yours, I'm just pointing out that constant posters get a little tired of it and it's very likely to be lost in the mix. If you want a greater reponse to what you write, I also suggest being very specific with your subject line. Yours is pretty good, albeit a little vague. And perhaps TOO big a concept. "Super Universe" this might say to expert modders "This person doesn't know how hard it is just to get a "somewhat neat universe."

You can agree or disagree.

Spectre
