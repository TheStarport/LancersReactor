Tis for NPC usage......you will see references to auto_turret in the pilots_populations.ini file;

<pre><font size=1 face=Courier>fire_style = multiple
auto_turret_interval_time = 0
auto_turret_burst_interval_time = 5
auto_turret_no_burst_interval_time = 0.1
auto_turret_burst_interval_variance_percent = 0.100000
gun_range_threshold_variance_percent = 0.300000
gun_fire_accuracy_power_npc = 6 </font></pre>

Setting it to false means that the NPC's cannot use it for firing at you from - like when they whoop you as they fly away from you.
