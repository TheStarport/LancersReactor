if you piss off bounty hunters, red hessians, and corsairs, ALL at the same time in one of the systems that you may see and fight against all three.
if fighting against 3 wings of nomads seem like no challenge, go to the system
(in the spoiler forum)...
http://www.lancersreactor.com/t/forum/t ... er&M=False

, after making sure hessians, bounty hunters and corsairs are your enemies, then don't kill any of their ships, just keep evading missiles, and weapons, once it looks like you're in a bee hive with dozens of angry bees, then start shooting. if you can make it you might just wish non-exist "level 39" was like that.
Needless to say that, in order to make it more challenging you should start the fight with no shields / nanobots since you would start picking them up once you start the killing spree.

but please do yourself a favor, do NOT try it with a level 1 ship, while it's possible to kill a few of the each enemy, then get the heck out there, if you wait / if your ship doesn't explode by that time, once they become more than 15-16 ships... that's one sure way to find out whether heaven/hell exist.
