Stinger

Sun May 04, 2003 9:42 pm by Stinger

Mods on elite are no really encouraged as a free-for-all right now. We are trying VERY limited experiments with VERY limited mods. We all know why, not all mods are created equal. Some mods are tweaked beyond normal and those will crash the server in a heartbeat. This is something we do not want to get into at all. But we are dabbling just to make sure the quality of play is not adversly affected by something that adds too much and breaks the server.

Bottom line, check with us first. Email the admin and get approval BEFORE you jump on with a mod (or you'll be banned no questions or discussions period). When you do ask, tell us what it is exactly your doing (what mod), what your nickmane is (so we can watch) and some explicit details on what the mods does would be vital information to share. Email addy is right there on the server news, please use it.

If you can't handle telling the owner of a server what you're doing, then your outta there pure an simple. We're flexibile, we just want to be informed...that's not asking too much. If a mod can be used (by informing, getting approval and testing) and it doesn't crash the server then chances are it can live in harmony on the server.

We do NOT want to start haivng to check for mods (we haven't been told about) and don't want to hear "so and so's mod is working, why can't I use my mod?" We don't want to have to wipe all the characters again and if this gets out of hand, that's what we'll have to do. Remember, the bottom line here is server stability for everyone.
