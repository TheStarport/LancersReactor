PATIENCE

Hi all,

Having followed this game for oh so many years, though only recently been posting here, I've decided to start this thread for those of us who HAVE NOT played the apparently prolific EB/GAMESTOP/WHEREVER version that's running around.

When it was just the PR release to a few, such as our mods, that was great. They limited the amount of spoilers and info getting out. The anticipation was on fire.

However, maybe it's just my humble opinion, but as more and more people get the "retail beta" or whatever you want to call it, the questions are getting more and more specific. It's annoyingly easy to find on the net now.

So the question is, are you going to give in to the "retail beta" or wait for the real thing?

I'll open the debate. Personally, when I start FL, I want the storyline, the planets and as much as possible to be fresh. I don't want to know wherte to go to get this or that. I'll know some from here and there and what I read, but my first sight of the opening cinematic will be the first time I suit up and launch to make my fortune.

So here's to the Release Date, and the first time I see FL in action.

What are your opinions?
