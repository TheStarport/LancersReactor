Can't continue the story? How odd....ummm...let me look around.


Ok, on the save editor, scrole down till you find

[MissionState

Change mission_accepted = 1 to mission_accepted = 0

Now, delete everything from [TriggerSave to [BStoryMissionDone. But do not delete [BStoryMissionDone.

Now change Mission = Mission_* (* being the number of the level you're on) to the next highest lvl.

Once thats done, save it. I would recomend changing the name a few numbers higher or lower.

Some numbers it dosn't accept, some it does. You'll have to play around with it.

Good luck!!
