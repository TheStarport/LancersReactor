Rankor

Mon Sep 24, 2007 6:15 am by Rankor

When I've set up "zone populations" such as in my Outworld battle zone,

I ignored the toughness setting of the zone itself. Instead, I set the toughness of the npcs encountered there.
In this case it works perfectly. Nomads set at 10 in one zone, then 19 in another zone. So, when I have them overlapping..the appearance of a mix of combatants shows up. As for the defending factions within those zones, they happen to be set at the same toughness level as the nomads.
Watch this happen and you'll see ships that normally cannot mount higher level weapons will actually have them. (Such as a Liberty Defender npc with class 7 guns)
