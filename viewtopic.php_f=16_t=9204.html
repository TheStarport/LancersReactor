Best trade routes thread

If there's already a continuous thread for this, I apologize, but I didn't see one already. I'm hoping this will grow.

I just finished the sp yesterday using heavy fighters all the way, so the whole freighter/turret-view deal is still fresh and new to me.

So far, here's the most economical (both time and $) trade route ive found is:

1) Pick up Boron in New York (Pittsburgh has it for 120, or if you're really lazy and want to shave off 5 waypoints get it for 155 at Manhattan)
2) Take the New-York to Magellan Jumphole (fight for a couple minutes, ally status depending)
3) Magellan to Leeds Jumphole
4) LD-14 commodities broker buys it for 960 or so
5) fill up there at LD-14 on ship parts ($80 I think)
6) Goto Kensington Station in New London system (adjacent system), sell the parts for 160 a pop then fill up Cargo with beryllium (290 I think).
7) Go back to Leeds then get in the jumphole--magellan. Then retrace your route back to New york.
Dock at Newark station and sell Berylium for $960 or so
9) Next door is Manhattan

This whole round trip only takes about 30 mins but pulls in like $500,000 (in a humpback). Its the first real "round trip" route I've found, but like I said just started on the whole trading aspect of the game.

Please post your favorite trade routes here! (or ignore if another thread is doing this)
