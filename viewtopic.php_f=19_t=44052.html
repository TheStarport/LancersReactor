A DOS window is the interface to use DOS so you can type in commands rather than using your mouse. DOS (Disk Operating System) is what the old times used before windows came along...

There are a couple of ways to open an MS-DOS Prompt window.
To do that, click on the "Start" menu (at the lower left of your screen) and select "Run...". Then type "command" (no quotation marks) into the box and click "OK".

You can also click on the "Start" (at the lower left of your screen) and hover your mouse over All Programs, then hover your mouse over Accessories, then click on Command Prompt.
