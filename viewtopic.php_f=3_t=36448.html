Boscoe

Thu Dec 02, 2004 8:47 am by Boscoe

Freelancer, like any other game, will eventually get to the point where you've "been there, done that" enough that the game gets stale. Fortunately, with the various roles, the hidden objects, the alliances and reputation changes, and differing ways to make money, the game can last quite a long time. This is why the storyline is in many ways the beginning of the game. But, again, even FL has its limits without modifications.
As people have mentioned, there are ways to refresh the game, multiplayer games, many great mods, clans to join, etc. If it weren't for the mods and helping test them, the game would be gathering dust on the shelf for a long time now. Try something new, maybe you'll enjoy the new challenge.
