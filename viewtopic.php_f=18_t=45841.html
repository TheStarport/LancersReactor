Dev

Tue May 22, 2007 3:42 am by Dev

When an NPC tries to scan your cargo, he will try to close to within 500 meters of your ship. As a guess, maybe the collision avoidance behavior of the NPCs is causing them to stay beyond that distance? If this is the case, and if you feel up for some easy hex editing, check out the scanning constants in Limit breaking 101, specifically this one:
500i in content.dll, 0x6C790 = max range at which NPCs will scan your cargo

Try pushing up the 500 to, say, 1000. Also, if you're using the patched version of Freelancer, be sure to use the 1.1 offsets (they are listed in an obvious fashion somewhere in the thread).
