Sun Nov 28, 2004 5:25 pm by Indy11

An Italian hero style sandwich, one of my all time favorites:

12" long fresh hero.
0.20# thinly sliced mortadella
0.20# thinly sliced sweet soppressata salami
0.20# thinly sliced provolone cheese
0.5 cup shredded lettuce
0.25 cup thin sliced or diced tomato
dash of salt
dash of black pepper
1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon red win vinegar

Open up the hero by halving it down its length.
Lay out one of the meats, then the cheese, then the other meat.
Evenly layer out the lettuce and tomato
Lightly season
Evenly sprinkle the oil and vinegar
Close hero

Enjoy.

You can add some chopped up pickled hot cherry peppers
as an additional flavor zinger too.
