The so-called "grey" satellite dishes are indeed illegal up here in Canada. Basically, "grey" dishes are ones that are not licensed by the Canadian government. In my area, BellVu and StarChoice are ones that are allowed so far.

As far as licensing is concerned, there are many reasons. One is that the Canadian government doesn't want the US market to flood Canada. Another probably is the CRTC Canadian content requirements. Taxes, as mentioned before, could be another factor.
