Hitman 2

I know its a far cry from FL but its a fairly satisfying (sp?) day or so of play. You get a rating after every level based on how stealthy/aggresive you were. Its easily completable within 10 hours on 'psycopath' or 'mass-murderer' but the real fun begins when you go back to the start and try and get 'Silent Assasin' on every level. Playing it like this means you are rarely allowed to kill anyone outside of your targets and very rarely allowed to fire a single shot. You've really got to think about what you're doing. Its very frustrating but eventually you find yourself in the bank (real world ) checking out the exits, guessing how many guards and trying to spot their patrol paths. Might sound crazy but it is sweet and even tho it gets a bit frustrating being rewarded with huge guns/rifles for not shooting a single bullet, all your hard work is repaid in the last level where you are allowed to run rampage with all the hardware you have accumulated
