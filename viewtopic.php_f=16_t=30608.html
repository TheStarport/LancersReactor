If you go hunting Xenos in the Silverton Asteroid Field you can increase your rep with all other criminal factions: the Liberty Rogues, Outcasts, Corsairs, and Lane Hackers are all enemies of the Xenos.

Another option is going to Rochester Base and taking on missions for the Junkers: the Bounty Hunters around Manhattan make tempting targets if you've well-equipped. Plus your neutrality on missions means that you can avoid getting into a scrap with the Liberty Rogues.
