a new strategy?

i duno if anyone already knows this or if you guys all consider this history or that this should belong in the spoiler forum, but here goes,

9 titans are chasing you, they want your guts, you're a noob in a valkaryie in sigma 19. what do you do ?
well this is what i did. i began to engage, just long enough to have them all bunched up. no i didn't fire a torp. then i make a run for the planet (i forgot the name, but thats irrelevant)
fly towards the planet, until the comp says you're entering the atmosphere. then sharply pull up. pull up and burn to the north/south pole of the planet. npcs, i don't think what ship they're flying matters, but they'll lose about half their comrades into the atmosphere.
repeat process. if they get smart, take them out the old fashioned way.

it's a relatively new trick for me but it saved me from using up more bots/bats than i'd have preferred to.

Switchblade

Mon Feb 02, 2004 2:35 am by Switchblade

in those situations, I tend to load up with some really good mines (Rippers, for preference)

if I wind up being chased by too much to handle, I just aim in a single direction, kill engines, hit the afterburners and then coast on out of there in turret mode, dropping the mines to discourage them from following, and flares to ward off the missiles.

I have done things your way though, and it did help me win a pretty tough fight near Malta, but I think it's too much of a risk to attempt on too regular a basis. you might also consider using solar coronas in a similar way, although its harder to tell which bit you need to avoid, and it takes longer for a ship to die in a solar corona thatn from hitting a planet.
