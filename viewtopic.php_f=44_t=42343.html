Cold_Void

I don't think you should tell anyone to go around and get a freeware model of the ship they're trying to make. What's the point? Eight Ball's ship is a very nice ship and as he said he is just starting, quite impressive for a beginning piece of work.

Eight Ball

I'm liking the model, grouping it may likely prove to be difficult due to the amount of curves and lack of flat faces. Only other recommendations I have would be to try some extrusions in the model. If you do not know how, this is what I mean. Select a face (or multiple) and click the extrude tool. You can extrude the face in towards the model slightly and it will create an indent. You then scale the new face slightly down so that you don't have duplicate faces (they "shimmer" or flash black and white in Milkshape). I can show you some examples if you want. But like I said, the ship is looking good. Hope it turns out well in game, Freelancer is sometimes picky about long ships, they look a bit weird in the flight angle.
