LukeSkyfocker

Fri Apr 25, 2003 3:06 pm by LukeSkyfocker

Is this game really that difficult? If you keep dying, then you are doing something wrong. Try changing your tactics, because if you're doing the same thing all you are going to get is the same result. Also, having the right ship/gun/shield combo does wonders. Another good tip is making sure your guns have matching range and speed because the gun sights get thrown off if you have guns with varying speeds and ranges. So instead of having 5-6 guns hitting your target, you only have 2. Also, get the best thruster you can. If the action is getting to hot, then just hit tab and try to take some evasive action. Flying slow and straight is the best way to die. Also, what method are you using to control your ship during combat? Always Always Always use mouse flight in combat (by hitting spacebar). This gives you better aim and maneuverability. This may sound stupid, but are you using nanobots and shield batteries? having a good supply of them really will help you out when the going gets tough.

It's a bit difficult to help you without knowing what ship you are in, what guns you're using, and what system you're flying in. The best ship in liberty is not the best ship in bretonia.
