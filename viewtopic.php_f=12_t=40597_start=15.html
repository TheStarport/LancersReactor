no its not just marketing. its really easy to use and presents global information in an immediately accessible format. no mucking about scrolling from map-to-map, its just *there* - 3d-terrain, 3d-buildings, roads, railways, borders, all sorts of public information, sort of like a super 3d world autoroute. i love it, i spend hours on it. im doing an overlay for all the main archaeological sites in the Middle East - Assyria, Babylonia, Persia, Hellenistic, Roman blah blah blah.
