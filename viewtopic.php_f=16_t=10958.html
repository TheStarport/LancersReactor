Well... it's true that FL uses a lot of highly S/F-typical stuff.
But I think those parallels are coincidental - or at least rather based in the use of the genre's 'general vocabulary'.

I mean how else would one get to a different star system if not with a jumpgate? The only other valid option would be a "jump engine" inside the ships, and then someone would go call it warp drive and say "hey FL is just like Star Trek".

Or take "glowing baddies". Well, let's say they don't glow. A meek, unglowing human would look terribly boring as an enemy, don't ya think? And afaik, the nomad-infested humans never use any real "powers" during the SP story. Basically all they do is glow, pretty lame compared to the stuff the right Goa'ould can offer you.
