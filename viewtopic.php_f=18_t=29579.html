black_lightning

Sat Mar 20, 2004 10:54 am by black_lightning

umm... you do realise that the "HpShield" command line in the ini's is refering to a hardpoint in the ships cmp.

Most freelancer ships which don't have a shield in game also tend to not have a hardpoint called "HpShield" in the mesh. What your doing is trying to add an item on a hardpoint which doesn't exsist on the ship.

So if you want to add shields to the hardpoint called "hpshield" your going to have to go manually make a new hardpoint with the utf editor for that ship. Or, what's easier and what I've done a number of times, just reuse another hardpoint. You can add more then one thing to a hardpoint, so forinstance I added contrials to the gunboats runninglight hardpoints. For a shield you could just slap it onto the HpEngine01 hardpoint. Just verify that when your adding something onto a hardpoint, that that harpoint exsists on the model.
