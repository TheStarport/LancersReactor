Rudger Macvanlen
Sheffield Station, Manchester
Personal log June 5th 801 A.S.

Well i've decided to make a new log, my old one was destoryed with my old ship. So here gos, im 30 years old, and from Bretonia. I was born on Cambridge, and when i was 18 i signed up to join the New Bretonian civil gaurd. That floped after 6 years. So when i was 24 i joined the Freelancers guild, since then i have been drifting around Bretonia and Rheinland. Takeing a year in each nation, then heading to the other.

Then last month my luck hit the fan. It started when i took a contract to take out a lead molly in bretonia. I was to back up a wing of bounty hunters. So we linked up and headed to the nav point where the molly was supposed to be...he was there all right, so were at least 20 mollys. So we engaged them, my wingmen went down in a fire ball, and then i took a hit to my thursters and life support cut out, i had two minites before i would run out of clean air. luckily 10 mollys were already down, and i had a clear shot to my target. So i took it. He went up in a beutiful flash of hellish flame and burt metel. and i pased out.

I woke up an hour later on a station near by, i was a hero for that kill. But my ship was gone, and i had to get a new one. So now im in a Crusader instead of a Cavalier.

So thats my first entry into my new log. Im gona hit the sack, its a new start tomorow.

Rudger Macvanlen
Unknown sector Nav offline, last known sector was system rim, Tau-23
Personal log June 10th 801 A.S.


Well i guess i will update what happend since june 6th. I picked up a long term contract to help out in the Tau-23 system against outcasts. So i left on June 6th and arrived on the 8th. Then for the last two days i have been running convoy escorts, and patrols and racking up kills against the outcasts. Untill 6 hours ago when i got word there was a high profile smuggler in system with a 1 mil bounty on his head. So i 7 bounty hunters and another freelancer headed out to intercept him and take him out.

Two of his 4 dagger escorts went down pretty quick but each killed a bounty hunter. Then my wing man took a few hits and was gone, and i took out another dagger, then the smuggler took out the other freelancer and 2 bounty hunters. He even managed to frag a 3ed hunter by himself before i took him out, and the last to hunters nailed his escort. So i got the full mil and they both got 200k for helping out.

They decided to call it a day and left, but i stuck around to salvage the wrecks of both the daggers and my fallen comrades. Witch ment i picked up 7 guns, 2 turrets, a CD and some cardamine from the smugglers cargo bay. Plus some black box containing data cards, some small cargo, and nanos/bats, plus a bit of extra ammo. Then my proximity alarm went off. I checked the scaner and here were 3 daggers comeing to see what was going on. So i engaged, took out 2 and the last nailed me good, but my missle got him back. So now we are drifting helplessly threw space, on the rim curseing at each other over short range com. So thats whats happening, i guess someone might be reading this in a month when they find our wrecks floating out here. Hell mabey a patrol will come out this far. If one dos it will be outcast though, so im pretty much dead.


Thats it for now.
