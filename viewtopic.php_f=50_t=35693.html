Sat Oct 30, 2004 8:33 am by Megistos

It is indeed possible:



Simply open misc_equip.ini, and find this:

[Tractor
nickname = ge_s_tractor_01
ids_name = 263744
ids_info = 264744
volume = 0.000000
mass = 10
max_length = 1500
reach_speed = 2000
color = 178.500000, 178.500000, 255.000000
operating_effect = tractor_beam
tractor_complete_snd = tractor_complete
lootable = false

Just alter the highlighted line. They're simple RGB values. The one I created up there was 255, 0, 0.
