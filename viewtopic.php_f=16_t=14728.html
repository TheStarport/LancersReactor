Balefire

Mon Apr 21, 2003 9:12 am by Balefire

If you have finished the SP campaign, as I suspect you have, then that denied jump hole was one of the story ones and is no longer accessible, I believe. If you haven't finished the storyline yet, it will be temporarily accessible during the latter part of the story. Several jump holes are like that, actually.

If the nomad ships are too much for you, it may be because of the "best Corsair weapons"...they're probably not good enough. I suggest that you search for some of the many wrecks that have excellent Class 10 guns, and get yourself the best shields you can find, and stock up on nanobots and batteries. The nomads attack in--usually--waves of four, and they're not that hard if you're sufficiently well equipped. Be sure to attempt to tractor everything dropped by nomad ships you destroy: the nomad weapons work very well against their makers, without using energy.

Also note that the nomads have no shields, but very strong hulls, so choose any less-than-Class-10 weapons accordingly.

It appears that there may be a minimum level (around 30+ ?) required for the nomads to drop weapons. I can't verify that myself, as I was level maxed by the time, post-storyline, that I went hunting nomads.

Good hunting!

Nightsilver

Wed Apr 23, 2003 1:45 am by Nightsilver

As people have been saying, speed is your best friend against nomads. Use mouse flight and move in erratic patterns. That circle tactic someone suggested is EXCELLENT against all computer fighters. Just move your mouse cursor to the top center of the screen and you'll start doing circles. You'll get hit, but very rarely - allow your shield to recharge, then attack again!

Remember, Nomads don't carry any sort of repair items, unlike some enemy ships. You can spin as long as you need to to recharge - Use this spin tacting INSTEAD of shield batteries - none of the planets in the Unknowns sell anything, as far as I know.

Also, the Dromedary is the only freighter bigger than the Humpback. The Humpback can carry better weapons and is stronger as far as power goes, but the Dromedarys hold is 275, whereas the Humpback's is 250. The Dromedary is on Mactan Base in the Magellan system.
