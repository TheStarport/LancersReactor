Dev

Tue Jun 19, 2007 3:46 am by Dev

This problem is easy. When Freelancer goes looking for engine hardpoints, it looks for "engine0", not "engine" as you would expect. Just rename all engine hardpoints over 10 appropriately (i.e., engine010, engine011, etc).
