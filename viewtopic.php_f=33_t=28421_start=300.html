Ro9ue

Mon Feb 07, 2005 7:44 pm by Ro9ue

Heh, well, there's the question, huh?
I am going to say that there is no "best" ship. It totally depends on your playing style: if you prefer big ships, big guns, heavy armor/shields and heavy maneuerving to match, there is no shortage of "uber" Heavy anf Very Heavy Fighters to be found - too many to list, so take a look around - If you are more into freight and trading, there are also many decent freighters to be found. And if you're a light fighter person (as I am), the best ship is probably the X-9 Ghost, available on Pueblo Station, CO. The ship is small, freakishly fast, has Hyper (super engine-kill faster than tradlanes), and mounts some pretty mean firepower. Give it a shot if you like LFs.

Heh, and weaps. There is also NO shoratge of dang uber weapons, from level 7 on up. Heck make that 3 - the Poison (light mass driver) is pretty crazy for a leve 3 weapon.
This one, my friend, is one that you will need to just try all the ones you come across to see what suits your tastes and you ship's equipment best. There ain't no special loadout to speak of -- all up to you and your style

I know most people would complain that this kind of post isn't any help at all - it just frustrates them even more because there are apparently some nice ships/weapons out there but they don't know what/where they are. But I see it more as a tip to just cruise around and try new things when you come across them.
Hope this helps
