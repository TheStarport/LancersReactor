Part of the purpose of giving you time to take on jobs in between missions in SP mode is help you get a better ship and more experience flying in combat so that you do not get whack so quickly on the scenario missions! Use the time to get the best weapons, ship, and battle experience so that your next mission with her will be successful. Enjoy
