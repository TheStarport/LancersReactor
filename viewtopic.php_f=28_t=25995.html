SWMM (Star Wars Music Mod)

Greetings. I have not submitted my mod to TLR as yet, because I wish to receive some feedback before it "goes live" as they say. SWMM Version 1.0 adds 21 tracks from the 1993 "Original Star Wars Anthology Soundtrack", which was conducted by John Williams and performed by the London Symphony Orechstra. All tracks have been adpated, edited, and converted by me. I wish to stress that the music has not just been ripped off from Jedi Knight, etc.

The mod contains tracks such as;
-Mos Eisley Cantina Music
-Imperial Theme
-Asteroid Chase Music
-Any many more! (I'd better stop before I become a shill )

You can find the mod at the below link:

http://opax.swin.edu.au/~222447/SWMM_v1.1.zip.flmod
Click here.


The mod is just under 10 meg. Sorry modem users (I am one myself). The mod contains A LOT of music and, if I reduce the quality anymore, then the tracks will sound awful. Let me know if you have any problems or (constructive) feedback.

Requests, anyone?
