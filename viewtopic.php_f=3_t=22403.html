Sat Aug 02, 2003 9:37 pm by Sherlog

Bribes are a quick (though expensive) way of adjusting one's reputation but the effects are pretty drastic. You can do minor adjustments easily by blowing up ships or depots belonging to enemies of the faction whose favours you desire, and quite often even the criminals have enemies that are criminals. So you can get factions like the Outcasts or the Corsairs to like you without getting on the bad side of the lawful factions.
