DarkHydra

Sat Aug 23, 2003 11:26 am by DarkHydra

So once again, a historical reference behind a faction. The Bundschuh in Freelancer had exactly the same problem: They helped a revolution only to see private capitalism change to state capitalism.

The whole Rheinland stuff is based on history:

First, the 80-year-war, which is their version of the first world war.
After that, Rheinland was in deep economical problems (Germany 1914-1939). People would be lucky to have a job in one of the factories, where working conditions were bad... Freelancer version: "where working condtions in the Daumann and Kruger factories were bad."
Then Hitler comes up. In Freelancer, the Nomads take over.
Germany annexes a few countries, and starts WWII -- and Rheinland basically takes over Sigma 13 and Omega 7, and then starts a war against Kusari.
