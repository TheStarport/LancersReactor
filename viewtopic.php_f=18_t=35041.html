Mephistopheles

Sun Sep 26, 2004 9:44 pm by Mephistopheles

You can't do this unless you add a CM02 hardpoint to the ship's cmp file (using Hardcmp). What you can do is add a countermeasure mount to the mine dropper hardpoint. If you dont remove the original mine mount entry, you end up able to mount both a CM and a mine on the mine mount, giving you freedom to mount 1 mine and 1 CM or 2 CMs...

Chips

Sun Sep 26, 2004 10:49 pm by Chips

As long as you have made those mounts on the CMP.

If you made two mounts called:

hubba
bubba

Then as long as they have position and orientation - it would be:

hp_type = hp_countermeasure_dropper, hubba
hp_type = hp_countermeasure_dropper, bubba

Hope that clears it up for you! They can be called whatever you wish - as long as the mount exists on the model!
