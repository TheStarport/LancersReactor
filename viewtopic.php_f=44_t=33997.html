A Call for Heroes (Help! - Relates to Ragnarok)

Basiclly it's a lack of means. I've practiclly finished the Ragnarok, I've tweaked it's stats, balanced it's weapons, set up all it's turrets and trajectories, everything is ready except for one final detail

The primary turret mount on it's underside, the model is done, it's textured and it works. However weapon mounts REQUIRE .DDS textures from what i understand, and DDS textures require Adobe Photoshop, since the only plugins to my knowledge exist for that package.

Unfortunatly I don't have Adobe Photoshop... no I'm not asking anyone to illegally mail it to me, rather HERE you'll find the gun's texture in .bmp format.

(Copy and paste the URL)
http://www.geocities.com/ryuujin_0/Sour ... cannon.bmp

If some kind soul could convert that into .DDS format for me you'd have my eternal gratitude/gratitude of everyone else here/credit in the model credits. Basiclly this one texture is the only thing stopping the Ragnarok being uploaded and made available for everyone to mess with to their hearts content.

I recommend you make two DDS files, one normal, and one flipped vertically. The Milkshape exporter tends to flip the texture co-ordinates vertically so skins usually have to be flipped over before export. So do it the right way up AND flipped just to be on the safe side.
