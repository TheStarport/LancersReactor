Well to be honest, I was going to slate it. But as I've just found out you're only in year 9 I honestly can't.

Lose the yellow though, trying going for a nice clean background with contrasting text.

Also, try to get away from using NetObjects Fusion to build your sites....it will do you no favours in the long run. Get a book on HTML/Javascript and a copy of a half decent paint package (preferably Photoshop but later versions of PaintShop Pro will do for now). If ya can afford it DreamWeaver will be your daddy when you move into larger sites.

Lose the image on the right hand side, it looks like an advert and is lost in translation because the section name is at the bottom (off screen a lot of the time) rather than the top. I noticed that its the same image as at the top of each page just rotated 90 degrees clockwise...therefore its not needed. First rule of web design, if its not needed and it doesn't add to the style...lose it.

Also, check your table sizes they jump around like crazy. Your page widths are all over the place.

I'm not slating you I promise, I think its a very good effort. But if ya want a full critique or a little help, just ask
