The spew file is located here:

By the way, you'll need to allow hidden files/folders to be viewed, or you may not be able to find it. If you play the game a lot, this file will be huge and could take a minute to open.

Might want to give FLSpit a try too, it'll reduce all the useless stuff out of it.
