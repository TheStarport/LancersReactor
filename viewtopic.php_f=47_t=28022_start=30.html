Time: Eight days after the Rheinland invasion
Location: Outside Leipzig, Dresden system

"This is where we get of, Sir."
"Thanks for the escort, Major."
"No problem." The group leader in Steir's squad said.
"This is Leipzig Station," the Daumann worker on Leipzig said, "where are you going?"
"Im going to Leipzig."
"Roger that, your request to land is granted, please proced to docking bay one."
"Thanks."
Dündock landed in bay one, and flew to one of the hangars, and landed. When he exited the ship, a man dressed as an rheinlander walked up to him. Behind him, four other men was walking.
"We heard that you should negotiate with the Corsairs, and happened to be in the area. We will give you help, if anything... unexpected happens."
"Very well. Where are we to meet the Corsairs?"
"An armed room in the west part of the station. The room got gun drones, so if they begin to mess..."
"Yeah, i got the point, now get me there." Steir replied.

It took Steir and the guards ten minutes to get to the west part. The one Stair has talked to, who had presented himself as Fisher, who once had helped Trent to get Sirius rid of some Nomads, opened the door to the armed room.
Inside, one Corsair was sitting. On his both sides, two other men where standing. One of them held a rifle, and a second one held a breifcase.
"Good morning, my gentlemen," the Corsair sitting down said.
"Thanks," Steir replied quick.
"I am the the Corsair ambassador you talked to yesterday," the man sitting sitting presented himself as, "this is Herr Von Claussen," he said and pointed at the man who held the breifcase, "and this is..."

The door was blown wide open, and in six Kusari men stepped.
"We are here from the Kusari Security. Herr Von Claussen and Steir Mündock, you are under arrest," their leader said.
"What? There is no Kusari Security! Even if there where, you don't have the power to arrest us. Im from Rheinland."
"You're talking with the wrong man, Von Claussen. Maybe you remember me?" A man behind their leader said.
"Ozu...!"

Location: Near Alaska Jump Gate, New York system

"Where are you, Juan?" I said in my radio. No answear. I had lost Juan and all the other Outcasts in a fight with some Rheinlanders, and ended up near the Alaska Jump Gate. Suddenly my radio began to make noises, and i could make out voices:
"...roger, Düsseldorf, sending Alaska Jump Gate codes..."
Silent.
"..18684927492749375946230576947597839638690..."
"...roger... that..."
I quickly wrote down the code on a paper, and headed to the Alaska Jump Gate. That was my only chance, since Liberty had managed to contact Agiera, and had blocked the Jump Gates in New York. Even the Jump Holes had been blocked, with some kind of drones.
I aproached the Alaska Jump Gate, and gave it the right codes.
Nothing.
