Right, thats screwed up backfaces you've got.

Triangles are only one sided, but by default Milkshape tries to pretend they're two sided. In the Milkshape 3D, perspective window, press right mouse button, and untab "Draw backfaces", and your ship will be full of holes as seen there.

Now, firstly try selecting everything and clicking "reverse vertex order", if you still have lots of holes you're really kinda in trouble, and it's tricky to fix. The best way to solve it is to like "hide" half the model, then "Select -> Face" each of the inverted triangles, and click "Reverse vertex order" to flip it the right way around. Then unhide and hide the otehr half the model and repeat.

Ultimatly the main solution for this is to always ensure draw backfaces it turned off , and to fix any inverted faces as they happen.
