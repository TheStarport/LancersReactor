Pirates

I just found out something rather interesting about pirates, while on a cargo run...I was hauling new shoes and lipstick to some drugged out chicks who thought they were all that. You know the drill--Luxury Goods:Roppongi Station/New Tokyo->Ainu Depot/Hokkaidu.

Started with little money (just bought my Humpie), so I bought as much luxury goods as I could afford, and then used up the change on Food Rations, just to haul something.

Then, just outside Ainu Depot, I got jumped by Blood Dragon pirates. And do you know what she wanted? Yep, that's right: THE 7 TONS OF FOOD RATIONS! I had over 50 tons of Luxury goods, and she wanted food? Here you go, honey, and have a nice day somewhere else...

So I got curious. I loaded out my ship with 1 ton of everything safe (didn't take Cardamine) I could get my hands on at Ainu & Rappongi, and flew the route. The only place pirates attacked, was right there at Ainu Depot by Blood Dragons, and they have very special needs:

* Food Rations
* Light Arms
* Consumer Goods
* Pharmaceuticals

So I stopped carrying just those items, and sure enough, no more pirates!

My guess? That band of washed up Sons of the Great Samuri Whoever are just there gathering basic supplies to fuel their Great Comeback Someday.

Anyone else know of specific bands of pirates, where they are, and what they want?

As soon as you pop into Hokkaido, fly 30K WNW, into C6. You have to be pretty close before it will pop up on the sensors, because of the Shiden Cloud.

Hauling Luxury Goods from Roppongi Station nets $630/hold for one jump. If your ok with Blood Dragons and Golden Chrysanthemums, it's about $150K profit per run in a humpie.

A longer stretch is from Colorado to Ainu Station, but you'll also have to deal with Xenos, Liberty Rogues, and Lane Hackers along the way. Luxury Goods from Planet Denver->Ainu Depot nets $1207/hold in four jumps.

BTW, DON'T befriend the Xenos! I spent 125k trying it, just to see what would happen. The police, most freighter convoys, and the Bounty Hunters all started attacking, not to mention a bunch of the bases became hostile. AND they were not red in my relationships list! The relationship list doesn't seem to be accurate, in my opinion.
