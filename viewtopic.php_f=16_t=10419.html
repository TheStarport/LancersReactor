Ether Dragon

Mon Mar 17, 2003 5:18 pm by Ether Dragon

When you say "jumps" I assume you mean trade gates and not jump gates. I've got a couple that I use when I want quick cash without too much hassle.
Planet Manhatten -> Planet Pittsburgh = Pharmaceuticals
Planet Pittsburgh -> Planet Manhatten = Boron
Using a Rhino, you make about 2000 credits each way. It's a good, safe, run when you're starting out.

Planet Curacao -> Kingstone Border Station = Water
Kingstone Border Station -> Planet Curacao = H-Fuel

The water just gives you a little return for the trip back, the real money comes from the H-Fuel. This requires a stronger ship than the Rhino, as Cruise-Disrupting Corsairs and Liberty Rogues attack the trade lane intermittently. My experience is that they'll attack in force the minute you put all of your money into a load of H-Fuel.

The beauty of the Curacao/Kingstone run is that it's one jumpgate, and one trade lane, a quick and easy run. If the trade lanes seem to risky, you can also run the route with cruise engines, as it's only about a 35m trip. Just make sure to steer clear of any enemy you spot along the way.

Note: I'm in no way endorsing these two routes as the best there is. They're simply two that I happened upon and made use of. They helped me in time of need, until I could afford a big boat for the longer, high profit runs.

The best run I have found is this. You can turn $5 into almost $1400 per cargo hold. Its a long trip and filled with lots of badguys .. but thats half of the fun!

Start in Britonia and find the cheapest water you can. I think Planet Leeds is a good source of $5 water. You then want to head towards Reinland space. When you hit Omega 3, ( or is it 7 .. the one with all the asteroid miners ) Look for the jump hole to Omega 7 ( or is 5 .. sigh! ). There you will find Sirius were you can sell your water for almost $200 a unit. Decent profit.. BUT you can buy diamonds for cheaper than you sold the water. Take those diamonds back to Leeds and sell it for almost $1400. So at the beginning of the run you fill your cargo hold up with $5 water and in the end you sell each hold worth for nearly $1400. Thats some tidy profit per cargo hold.

I never fight on this route. Its RUN RUN RUN. If you follow the trade lanes its roughly 25 waypoints, I find it less hazardous to only use the System Jump gates and then I fly across the sysems through open space where there is less chance for pirates. Trust me pirates will come after you with a shipload of diamonds. My ship always gets battered and Ive died a few times .. but thanks to the autosaves Ive never lost too much time in real life..

The best Ive found so far..
