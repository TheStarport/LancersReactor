Just got done playing the demo for about 3 hrs

Good:
- Mouse control is great!
- The info you can get on the fly through your map is very nice.
- Attention to detail...
ex1: Got a little to close to the sun and you start to get effected by the corona.
ex2: Police checking ships and comm chatter between ships.
ex3: Weapons fire looks fantastic!
- Nebulas/junkyards look nice and fun to navigate when going fast specially the badlands.
- Character animations look nice.. gotta love the female LT .
- Menus are layed out well and there are always descriptions on mouse overs

Neutral:
- I know its a demo but it damn sure costs a lot to fix that rep
- Would be nice to expand onto your holds... kinda like IWAR2.
- Some of the background gfx in the stations/bars look a little dated. I maxed out all the options on my 9700Pro and it didn't shape it up. (But i'm being picky)

Bad: (I know a lot of this is happening because of your rep)
- I love combat so I am not complaining. But for the people that don't love too... You get attacked an awful lot. I have to admit freelancer looks very nice and I just wanted to soak it in and explore a little. Those dame ROGUES!
- Some of the lines the NPC's use get a little old.
- I don't know if I was using it wrong or just never had the right ships or structures selected but... (p) Hailing. Didn't seem to work???

This demo is very nice and I don't plan to go home after work on the 4th until I have my copy. Guess my wife won't have a husband for a bit... she'll have a freelancer Yeah!

Oh and if you talk to the right people... they will give ya the location to a lost liberty patrol Little hint its in the badlands!

For those people who really know the game or are playing the gold cd's I have a few questions:

- Is there any devices you can purchase for the ship to bring down your radar signature?
- I know we can tractor in cargo and ship items. Can we tow or tractor in people as well?
- And will we find any places in the universe to explore where there isn't a lot of people shooting at you?

The "estimated" waiting time was about 40 mins...but it was more like 1hr 40mins. Not only that, but early in the download, my computer started acting all funny and I had to restart it...only to be put back in the queue again. It was gratifying when I finally got it.

The graphics look pretty good, but at first they didn't seem quite right. I think it's because the textures are "washed out" or something. (it was on 1024*768*32) I hope it's just like the UT2k3 demo where the the texture quality was reduced to minimise the demo size.

From what I've seen, it looks and feels like DA have done EVERYTHING to make the controls/interface as simple and user friendly as possible. When I first activated the "free flight" mode, it seemed a little difficult to handle, but I got used to it pretty quickly.

To summarize...it looks like FL really does meet most, if not all my expectations. I was going to put off buying it until I got my new computer...but it plays just fine on this one, even on highest settings. So now I'm thinking of pre-ordering it.

Finally, thanks DA for bringing us such a great, well made game.
