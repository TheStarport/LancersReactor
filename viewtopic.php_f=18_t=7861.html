Haven't used the trainer, but used GameHack 2.0 for the demo so that I could up my level (to ten only, didn't want to spoil the rest of the retail for myself). Found the cash, but didn't modify it.

Your problem, and the trainer's, is that during gameplay your cash is stored in two locations. The first is the 'real' value. This is stored in your save game and is used to calculate your worth. The second is the 'trade' value. This is a dupe of the first value... argh, I forget the details, I'm at work at the moment and don't have access to my notes. Basically, the trainer is only editing the trade value, not the real value.
