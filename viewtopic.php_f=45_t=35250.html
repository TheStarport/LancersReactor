<Sir> Clan Members Wanted for the DS9 Server

I need players to be over the age of 30, mature and sensible. You also need to do a minimum of 100 hours on our server. The reason why we ask for such a large amount of hours is so that we can get to know you and find out what you are like.

Upholding the DS9 server rules
Helping players, whether new or regular players
Informing DS9 members of problem players (backed up with screenshots)
Remaining calm when a player is verbally abusive
Prove that you are responsible with a powerful ship

<Sir>KingArthur will give full training to new Sir Members before allowing them to police the server by themselves.

If you are interested in joining, just turn up on our server, and let me know that you saw this recruitment post on here.
