Merchant Trader

Wed Apr 14, 2004 7:35 am by Merchant Trader

Solarius Station: Omega-11 system

The prices for diamonds at Solarius are lower than those of New Berlin ($219 at Solarius, $198 at Freital base [a Red Hessian facility). If you want to try mining in the system's asteroid belt, try to stay on good terms with both sides.

WARNING: Radiation is present throughout the entire system, courtesy of the local sun. Make sure that you are carrying a full load of nanobots before entering the system.

ALSO: Omega-11 isn't accessible via the standard jump gate. The jump hole is located just south of Briesen Mining Station (Omega-1: Edge Worlds).

TIP: If you want to cut down on your costs for repairs, unmount some of your weapons before entering the system. For mining I took off everything except my thruster, shield, countermeasure and mine launchers, and three Reaper mk II. If you're on good terms with everyone then don't expect any serious fighting.
