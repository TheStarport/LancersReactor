<Enemy destroyed>

<As far as we know>

<Hold on...Corsairs party headed this way. DAMN! We can't afford to fight these guys. Lambda 1 and 2, launch Countermeasures. We need to cloud our escape. GO!>

<Get these guys off me!>

<My shields are down, losing hull integrity!>

Once out of Galileo, it was a smooth ride through Liberty--a clash with some Rogues' bloodhounds, but they were easily dealt with. Finally they all vaulted out of the trade lane ring in Bering at Freeport 2. Erich sighed with relief.

Inside the station's bar, he met up with Herr Sega.

"I've just finished talking to the Order chap. the artifacts are transferred, we've all been paid..."

"How much did he offer?"

"10 million. per pilot."

Erich gasped "Who HAS that much money?"

Randy shrugged. "You said you had business to discuss."

Erich ordered a Darkhundt from the bartender. "Yes. We have...an informant, if you would put it that way, in Bretonia. Highly respected political essayist, spreading anti-Rhineland propaganda. We need to get some documents to him, concerning...well...corruption in the Rhineland government. And something about a second Nomad invasion. We need to ship them to him, we can't get them to him any other way. You're the only guys we think can ship it.

We're prepared to spend...a lot less than the Order, but a reasonable amount. 1 million per pilot. Plus any extra cost of shipping and equipment--nanobots, new guns, shield batteries. you name it." Erich took a deep sip of his beer.

"I'll have to consider it. But it sounds likely. You did save our arses back there, mate."
