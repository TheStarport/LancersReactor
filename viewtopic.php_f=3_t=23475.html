Also, there isn't a whole lot to discuss. The designers gave agility to the Eagle and an additional forward-firing hardpoint to the Sabre, but they forgot to give the Titan even the tiniest advantage whatsoever. The marginally stronger powerplant of the Titan is negated by the fact that the most powerful guns drain no energy whatsoever, and the marginally better armour and nano/batt capacity is negated by the pityful maneouvrability.

The question Eagle vs. Sabre can only be solved by duelling between equally-skilled players, but I think it is very difficult to find players that have the same skill level and whose skills are not biased towards the fighting styles associated with either the Eagle or the Sabre.
