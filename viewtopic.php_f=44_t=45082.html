Bejaymac

Wed Dec 13, 2006 12:59 pm by Bejaymac

The "no_fighter_gun.cmp" is the standard Nomad weapon model, it's a tiny almost invisible triangle with a HpConnect and HpFire. In your CMP you attach the HpWeapon hardpoints to the ends of the gun barrels, you then make a new weapon in the ini's and use the "no_fighter_gun.cmp" as the archetype for the gun. When this is mounted on your ship in game there is no weapon showing but the projectiles look like thier coming from your static weapons.

This is how most mods do it for ship models that come with inbuilt weapons, the X-wing as one example and most Star Trek ships as another, the X-wing would look stupid with a Justice Mk1 stuck on the end of it's built in gun barrel, visible or invisible.
