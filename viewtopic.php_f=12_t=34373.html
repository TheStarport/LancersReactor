dark_reaper

Tue Aug 31, 2004 11:56 pm by dark_reaper

I consider Warhammer to be role-playable as you want it to be. It could just be a army of painted figures, or you could name your army and characters, come up with a background story, fight narrative battles against your friends, develop a history for your army based on previous battles etc.

Or you could play some of the sub-games such as inquisitor or mordhiem which is more role playing than warhammer, these games use the warhammer core rules but involve a much smaller number of figures whose stats and abilities change after each battle, depending on the roll of the dice.
