Serious Server Admin Issues that Need to Be Addressed

1. When a player deletes their character/account on a server it should only be FLAGGED as deleted on the server, not actually deleted. The player name SHOULD still show up in the account list(flagged as deleted) so that players who grief can't just delete their accounts when they are busted and before they can be banned.

2. Logging - There should be a option to log of all player transactions and all leveling. This way admins could look through and see when a player goes from level 1 to level 38 immediately. The log should also show trades between players as someone could legally go from 1 to 38 by being given money from someone else.

3. The memory leak or whatever causes the 24 hour crash to occur.

4. An easier way to kick people from the server, like being able to right click in the chat with player window to ban, delete, or kick.
