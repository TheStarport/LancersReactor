The single most important tip EVER is to have at lease one Pulse weapon on your ship. Don't forget that EVERY weapon in the game (except Pulse) does half damage to shields. If you can rip down the enemy's shields quickly, your guns can do much more damage. Combine Pulse weapons with the Javelin series of missiles for maximum damage.

Miscellaneous other tips:

- ONLY the Javelin series of missiles is worth having. NO other missiles are AT ALL good value. The Stalker series just don't do enough damage, and the Eraser series is pointless in the extreme - just buy a Pulse weapon instead. Don't worry that the Javelin series has virtually no homing ability. Just only launch when the opponent is unshielded and heading towards you and you should hit every time.

- To get away from pursuing NPCs equipped with Cruise Disruptors, Engine Kill and use your thruster for a couple of seconds, in the direction you intend to go. Then release the thruster and turn around to face the enemies. You'll still be travelling at thruster speed, but since you're aiming at them, the NPCs will scatter. Wait until they disappear from your sensor display, turn around and engage cruise. Job done!

- The faster the refire rate on a weapon, the less efficient it is. This means that if you were to fire 6 Skyblast Bs constantly (refire 8.33) and 6 Firekiss 2s (refire 2.00), your energy would run out much more quickly with the Skyblasts. It's the tradeoff for being easier to hit with. That's why I stick with weapons around 4.00 or 3.03

- Don't trust the Go To autopilot for taking you through minefields or fields of explosive gas. Do it manually.

Tip for avoiding piracy by neutral AI pirates:

Carry around a few units of worthless (cheap) cargo in addition to your main payload. Scrap metal, Oxygen, and Water are the best three, since they cost next to nothing at all no matter where you land. If you happen upon a would-be pirate (AI), chances are they'll demand that you drop the cheap crap instead of your real payload. This way, you can give them what they want and skedaddle without worrying about losing any time or money.

Online tip for creating a new character on a Vanillia Server to get a 100% neutral Reputaion and Good ship in a few hours work

Method one if the New York Police Depots are there:

1. Your starting ship, sell enough Bots to buy a Patriot and counter measures.

a. Hit the first Police Depots for its cargo

b. Quickly enter cruise and fly around the back of Manhattan and dock at Trenton, this resets your temporary bad reputation, there is another way but considered unsporting by some. Then repeat for all the other police pods until you have enough to buy a Rhino making sure you buy a level 2 shield and bats and at least 3 weapons that aren&#8217;t too powerful. Finish off the police depots with the above method. This helps later on when you want to buy the Dromedary as the Hackers/Outcast/Rouge reputation is neutral by that time. Note at some point go to Rochester when you have 90k and get the Junker Bribe.

c. Hit Flint for its Cardamine, if you hit this then you can afford buy a Dromedary, then go to Mactan in Magellan while carrying H-fuel. After buying it take Cardamine back to New York but also hit the wreck in California for its Cardamine.

2.

a. Buy Luxury Goods at Manhattan and fly to the GC base in Hokkaido and sell the Goods there.

b. Next trade Luxury Goods and Cardamine between Roppongi and GC base in Hokkaido.

Repeat the above trading until you have enough cash for the ship you want, takes about 40 mins to get 1.5 million enough for an Eagle and shield and minor weapons.

3. Finally get the Zoner reputation hack, that fixes the remaining hostile reputations.

Method 2 if the New York Police Depots are Not there:

1.

a. Loot what wrecks you can in New York / California without killing anyone and do some minor trading so you have at least 90k in cash [buy a Patriot if you need to but makes getting that initial cash longer

b. Then dock at Rochester and get the Junker bribe

c. Buy a Rhino and buy Luxury Goods at Manhattan and fly to the GC base in Hokkaido and sell the Goods there.

d. Take the Cardamine to NY then buy H-fuel and go buy a Dromedary on Mactan in Magellan

e. Now trade until you have enough cash for a fighter of your choice, just remember to get the Zoner reputation hack, that fixes the remaining hostile reputations.
