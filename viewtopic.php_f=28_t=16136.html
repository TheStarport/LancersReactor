Frost-Works - Freelancer: Star Trek Total Conversion!

StarTrek : Total Conversion AKA ST:TC


The Goal of this mod is to completley convert the Freelancer Multiplayer universe into a fully playable Star Trek Universe.


the 4 playable races will be

Liberty >> Federation
Rhienland >> Klingon
Kusari >> Romulan
Britonians >> Cardassians
New Race BORG (top level race see below)
Ferengi Maruaders (pirates)
+ other pirate factions


Pirate Factions for each main faction, the names of which are still being decided up - be sure to post your ideas.



there will be other races in other systems including:


Ferengi Pirates - Similar to junkers and other pirates of the FL universe.

Borg Space (ultra high level area - you dont want to go in here alone!)

TTM (The True Marqui) a new group of the most elite Marqui, with new technology and a new sponsor (the dominion). They hate the federation.

RFR Romulan Freedom Rebels - A group of extreme romulans that do not like the idea of the romulans allying themselves with anyone!

KLR - Klingon Last Right - An assasin group of warriors that are a bit unatuhoradox. They kill anyone, and everyone, they are paid to kill.


-other changes-

All the systems will be renamed, all planets will be renamed, all jumpgates and existing sytems will be made use of.


Multiple Warp Drives included ranging from Warp 5 , Warp 7, Warp 9 and Warp 9.9 + NPC engine for 8472, to provide for exciting warp chases.

A multitude of weapons including numerous beam, Pulse, Missile (including photons and quantum) + others.

New special items such as cloacking devices and transwarp drives will be in game.

New, trek, comodoties will exist in game be sure to post your ideas here for trek comodoties.

All ships will be capeable of warp.

Tradelanes have been removed.

All UI sounds will be replaced.

A new UI using 0LCARS interface will be present.

Music in all areas of the universe will be replaced, but will only be included as an option extra due to the size addition in MB's.


THE BORG

The Borg may well be playable - however! This is not for the faint of heart.

First you will have to gain the top level in the game, this is no easy task! This will take a great deal of time (but should also be alot of fun!) Once you haev attained ultimate power in your race you must, to appease to borg, attack every major race (including your own) to gain sufficient standing with the Borg to be allowed to enter their space. Once you have done this, and are in good enough aligment you may submit yourself for assimilation at a borg station where your current ship will be assimilated and you will be able to purchase a borg ship of your choseing.

Being a borg mean EVERYONE in the universe will hate you - forever. However you will have acces to the most powerfull ships, unless you happen to drop by 8472 ofcourse but thats another story.

Phasers
photon torpdoes
quantum torpedoes
+ equivelent for all other factions.

We will also attempt to include as much of what was stated up as possible.

The models for the new ships are top of the range, crem de la crem, models created by the comunities best artists.
