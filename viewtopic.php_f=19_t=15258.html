Stinger

Sat Apr 26, 2003 2:05 pm by Stinger

Thanks for the feedback! As a follow-up, the random crashing isn't so random. It happens when you join with someone in a group. As your group member takes off in a direction, you form up with them. They stay the same speed but you "catch up" to them by going faster than is "possible." We've heard two people so far say this will blow them up for now apparent reason. The patch believes you are going too fast, and will blow you up as a result. OUCH!

Please log on to the Elite server, find someone to test this out and report all finding to the address in the server news. This is not required but it will go a long way in helping us fix what might be wrong. If you find any other "issues" try to duplicate the event, then report your findings to the address in the server news. Be clear what you were doing, where you were when you were doing it, what steps you did to duplicate the anomoly. We can't thank you enough for helping us out!! This will only help the greater good of the servers out there with your time and effort.
