Independant Systems

Not many people realize the independant systems as independant, especially the newer players to freelancer, so I just want to inform those that don't yet know. Cortez, Galileo, Kepler, and Bering are all independant systems. Galileo and Kepler are not part of Kusari, or Liberty. Cortez is not part of Bretonia or Liberty. And finally, Bering is NOT part of Rhineland (or Liberty). As I have stated before, this is just to inform those who don't know this, for many people that i have played with on multiplayer don't realize that these systems are independant.
