Ah, did you get the game freighter? If you need any help just give me a buzz!

It is quite hard to get the cash to do what you want, like to save up for a Destroyer (over 50 million) would take literally DAYS of playing non stop (real time), and thats with a factory and a few trade ships doing your work for you. Most people put SETA (time accelerator) on and go do something else for a while (even overnight for the hardcore people) while their trade network rakes in money. Thats what I like about the game!
And you have to think strategically too, for instance, it would be silly to place a solar power plant (SPP) in a system with many other SPP's, and no place to buy Crystals (required for the SPP to produce energy). So you have to search for a place where demand is high and the resource you need is nearby, for a reasonable price, so you can be making a profit. And its not just SPPs, there are Ore mines, shield factories, weapon factories, Crystal Fabs, research labs, computer factories, countless other things which require other resources to run.

Theres nothing more fun than setting up a massive trading network and parading round in your M2 Destroyer while it rakes in cash!

Another cool thing is, Battles. Large battles. Think you in a Destroyer, with five other destroyers, a carrier, corvettes, and countless small fighters, versus a mass of Alien (Khaak) clusters. And you control the ships. Its almost like a strategy game, advancing, withdrawing, flanking,...and get this....you LOSE!!! For once your can't just get one thing and PWN the world!
