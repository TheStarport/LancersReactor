Prologue

The 23rd Century. Mankind has been divided by a deadly civil war lasting for 100 terrible years. We do not know much, but we know this. Our ancestors divided into two camps, the Alliance, consisting of those from mostly western nations, and the Coalition, a power block headed by a Sino-Russian alliance with much of Asia and Africa behind them.

Despite promising early victories, the Alliance quickly realizes the overwhelming strength and technology of the Coalition, and begins suffering defeat after defeat. Soon, Earth is conquered by the Coalition. The western-friendly nations in Latin America are quickly overrun by their Coalition brethren and march towards the United States and Canada, while a Coalition army advances into Europe.

The Alliance launches into space, and begin to attempt counter-attacks, but quickly the inner planets are claimed by the Coalition. The Alliance fleet falls back to Neptune, losing the French and Italian fleets in the process.

They manage to reclaim the planets towards the asteroid belt, raising morale among the Alliance soldiers. Fate was not on their side, and they quickly realized that their victory was short, and the Coalition fleet punctures into the Alliance strongholds.

The Alliance realizes then they can not escape from certain destruction. In desperation, the Alliance draws up an escape plan- to send survivors of their nations far away from the Solar System to the Sirius Sector, which they had discovered some inhabitable planets as well as others with terraforming potential. It was from there they hoped that they could start anew from the issues that had divided man.

The remaining nations set to build sleeper ships in the Jupiter moons while they put up a suicidal defense to buy time. The United States and Canada created the Liberty, the United Kingdom and Ireland on the Bretonia, the Germans on the Rheinland, the Japanese on the Kusari, and the Spanish and Portuguese on the Hispania. The French, Italians, and Australians constructed their own as well, but as we know, not all the sleeper ships were lucky.

The Coalition finds out about the project, and launches an attack on the moons of Jupiter. The Alliance puts up a final defense to buy time as the sleeper ships are launched. The French, Italian, and Australian sleeper ships are destroyed, but the Liberty, Bretonia, Kusari, Rheinland, and the Hispania manage to escape. They jump away from the Solar System, leaving the cradle of mankind behind them. The Alliance pilots who were left to defend watch with mixed feelings of pride and grief, and fight their last hopeless battles against the Coalition.

The Liberty arrives first, claiming the richest area of the Sirius Sector first. The Rheinland follows, claiming the ore-rich Walker cloud in southern reach of Sirius Sector. The Kusari arrives in the gas rich areas of the Crow Nebula. The Bretonia arrives last due to engine complications, but managing to claim the mineral rich areas in the western reach. When the survivors made contact with each other, the Hispania was no where to be found. Over time, the Hispania was forgotten, and the sleeper ships began to form their own nations, quickly forgetting the Alliance they had with each other as they set out for dominance. No teams were ever set out to find the Hispania…

A sleeper ship, with the word "Hispania" inscribed on its hull is floating in an unknown system. A large explosion rocks the ship, and the emergency systems set in. The people in the cryogenic pods awaken as the emergency procedures are set into action. Some of them believe they have reached their destination and immediately rush to the window to see where they are. They are nowhere close to the paradise they thought they were in. All they see is the rolling darkness of space, and the small packets of color indicating the Sirius Sector.

The Elders are well respected for their knowledge of the events preceding the Exodus and the days of the Golden Age of Earth. All the Hispanians look to the Elders for guidance, and soon they laid out their first orders.

The Elders set down quotas of consumption of the supplies they reap from the little agriculture the land managed to sustain. Then the process of naming the planet came up; initially Castilla was considered, but a number of the populace rejected the name. When the Elders questioned these subjects, they revealed themselves to be Italian and French survivors, put on the Hispania as a part of the Alliance's backup plan to have small numbers of each nationality in the other ships in case their respective sleeper ships were destroyed. They argued that they should strive for something that could unite all of them under one banner, not just a Spaniard/ Portuguese one. The Elders decided on a Mediterranean feeling to their new civilization, and eventually the populace agreed upon the name of Crete, which Carlos had brought up, relating the island’s hilly nature to the valley they landed in. They also set down the establishment of the first city, Nova Madrid, and the architects are instructed to construct the buildings within the mountains, as to create a temporary relief from the heat.

The survivors also come across strange ruins dotting the landscape. When the Elders inspect them, they discover that what ever they were, they weren’t human, and instruct the people not to disturb them in anyway.

The Hispanians went from day to day, looking to the skies, hoping for an Alliance ship to come upon them and save them in some way from this hellhole. Days became months, months became years, and years became centuries. Cynicism took root among the populace, many of them rigid in their beliefs of a conspiracy by the other members of the Alliance, or the other Hispanians became widespread. The Hispanians looked inward, forming strong bonds with one another. They well believed that they would never see any trace of humanity again, and set out to make what little they could of that bleak planet.

Alvaro looks out the window and sees the asteroids and the neutron star. He can see a skirmish between bounty hunters and corsairs off in the distance. The intercom comes on.

"Attention crew, we are approaching the jump hole. We may be ambushed by Hessians on the other side, so gunners, stay sharp!"

The freighter goes through the jump hole and a few seconds later they arrive in Omega-5. Alvaro sees the mighty base of Cadiz in the distance.

"It's quite a site, isn't it?", said Alonso, "Only the Blood Dragon's home base rivals in its size and mightiness, or so I hear"
"Yea", said Alvaro, "It withstood many of the Hessians biggest attacks"

"We've payed a high price for that thou..."

Before Alonso could finish the sentence, the ship starts to rock. Alvaro is knocked off the table and he slams into the wall, his injured arm hitting the wall. His cast comes off and the blood spatters on the wall.

"AHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH!", he screamed.

Alvaro drags himself to the window, and sees a squadron of Stiletto's firing on the ship. Lasers from both the Corsair ship and the Hessians go off firing, blazing past the window. The intercom comes on blazing.

"WARNING!!! THE HESSIANS ARE ATTACKING!!!! ALL CREW MAN THE TURRETS! SHOOT THOSE BASTARDS OUT OF THE SKY!!!"

The ship continues to rock, and Alonso can see the ships closing in. Two get destroyed. One blows up out straight, but the other one goes on a crash course towards the transport. It crashes against the hull and it rocks the whole ship.

"CADIZ!! WHERE ARE YOU! WE'RE UNDER ATTACK!!!

A Stiletto launches a torpedo on the engine, and it gets destroyed.

"ENGINES HAVE BEEN DESTROYED!!!!!", said the Captain, "CADIZ WE NEED BACKUP!!!"

"****...", said Alonso, "The Hessians never try to go this close to Cadiz..."

Suddenly the Stilettos are pounded by heavy fire, and are destroyed out straight. Some titans come into view.

"Transport Barcelona, this is Lieutenant Octavio Gulliento, we're here to escort you to Cadiz"
The titans pull the damaged freighter to Cadiz. Soon they enter the base of Cadiz, and Alvaro can see the skyscrapers of the base. They pass through traffic of hover cars and transports, and pull down the freighter in front of a garrison. The pilots of the titan land behind them.

"Ok", said one Corsair, "All of you guys go into the garrison..."

Octavio notices Alvaro in the corner, in a pool of his blood.

"Alvaro!", he yelled as he ran over to him. The wound from the steel is very noticeable, and he sees the eye patch, "What the hell happened to you!"

"Oh, nothing..", says Alvaro with a smirk on his face. He falls unconscious. Octavio looks over at the other Corsairs.

"WHAT ARE YOU BASTARDS WAITING FOR!", he yelled, "HE'S LOSING BLOOD FAST, GET THE DOCTERS AND A STRETCHER!"

Alvaro can barely see his father, and he slowly begins to fall unconscious.

"Hang in there!", said Octavio, "Don't die, you're all I got left..."

End Ch 3.

Ch. 4

Alvaro looks around, and notices he’s back in the same locale as he was in earlier. He touches the sphere, but there is no effect. He floats back down to the ground and sees the aliens, who are speaking a language he can't understand. Alvaro stands in their way to get their attention, but they pass through him like a ghost. He can't understand their language; it's just a series of clicking and unrecognizable sounds. He continues to follow them trying to get their attention, and slowly the language becomes understandable. The aliens are chatting about useless everyday events. Suddenly a laser beam comes down from sky and hits the structure. Rubble onto the floor, and the aliens begin panicking.”

"We're under attack!!!!", yelled an alien.

"Who is it!” questioned the other, “Was it our enemies? They can’t seem to help that other races exist with them!”


"I don't believe it!” said the alien near a star chart, "It's the guardians! Our guardians!"

"We're receiving a transmission from the home planet...” said the alien, "They've said that the guardians have turned on us. They're telling us to initiate the plan now!"

Alvaro goes outside and sees a strange crystal like object in the sky. It's bombarding the surface of the planet, and the aliens are running to their houses and locking the doors. Alvaro goes back into the building, and sees that the aliens have opened up the sphere, and a hologram control panel is shown. The aliens push the buttons on the hologram, and the building begins to deploy into a massive cannon. It fires into the sky revealing a massive laser beam; it hits the mysterious ship, destroying it on impact. The ship goes on a crash course, and it burns up in the atmosphere.

Alvaro hears chatter from the inside of the structure.

"I never thought the guardians would turn on us...” said one alien.

"It doesn't matter...” said another, "It's happened all over the home sectors, but there has been no casualties, just damage to our structures"

"We have to go with the operation, our top warriors our going to trap the guardians before they deploy their full force", said the other alien.

The aliens begin to push another series of buttons, and a large barrier beam deploys. It goes in both directions and the aliens disappear.

Alvaro is left all alone by himself. He goes out of the building, and it seems that all the aliens are gone. Slowly, the world around him begins to fall apart, and he sees shadows in the distance, and lights above him.

"Alvaro....wake up.....Come on!!!....Don't die now!!"

"This is strange....His pulse has suddenly come back...he returned from the dead..."

Alvaro slowly awakens from his vision, and sees the room around him. He can see his dad standing above him, a doctor, and Alonso.

"He's awake!!!” said Alonso.

Octavio goes and hugs him and starts to cry.
"I thought that you were gone... You're the only one I got left...” said Octavio.

"Boy", said Alonso, "You are one stubborn kid. First you get shanghaied by those mysterious ships, captured by Bounty Hunters, escaping a Bounty Hunter base and receiving serious injuries, surviving in you space suit, and now this!"

"I got to say", said the doctor, "You just don't want to die, you have a tight grip on that ledge boy...”

"What happened to me...” said Alvaro, "I had a strange dream..."

"That's normal", said the doctor, "People typically get strange dreams when they're on the verge of death...”

Alvaro sees that he still has the eye patch, and his arm is in a much more reinforced cast.

"You're lucky we didn't have to amputate that arm", said the doctor, "Corsairs don't have good prosthetics"

"Is he free to go?” said Octavio, "He's been here for 2 days now...”

"Yes", said the doctor, "Just keep him in your quarters, he won't be able to pilot anything in that condition"

"Will do, sir!” said Octavio.

Octavio and Alonso help Alvaro into a wheelchair and roll him out of the hospital. The skyscrapers of Cadiz rise above them outside, and hover cars and freighters are going back-and-forth outside, high up in the asteroid. The three go into the hover car and go into the streets. The buildings blast past them as they speed down the street.

"I was going to visit you, son", said Octavio, "I was really proud when you passed the initiation, even with that attack. You've brought honor to our family; your mom and brother would've been very proud if you...”

"Yeah, kid", said Alonso, "At the rate you're going, you’ll rise the ranks faster than your dad!"
The hover car turns and goes to the launching complex, and Alvaro gets puzzled.

"Yes, thanks", said Alvaro, "Where are we going?"

"We have to drop of Alonso", said Octavio

"Where are you going?” said Alvaro.

"Ever since you destroyed that base, Bounty Hunters have increased their attacks on us", said Alonso, "The Elders are recalling some Corsairs back to Crete to go on Bounty Hunter raids. Those Bounty Hunters are becoming a real thorn on our side"

"Good Bye, old friend", said Octavio, "Thanks for looking after Alvaro!"

"No problem", said Alonso, "And keep up the good work, you might become a big shot like your dad"

They exchange farewells and Alonso boards his Titan. His ship takes off and exits through the docking port. The two got back onto the Hover car and continue on.

"Dad", said Alvaro, "Where are we going now?"

"I have a house here", said Octavio, "I’m on leave for one week so we can stay together, and it's a good thing, your injuries need tending. By the way, how did you receive them?"

Alvaro looks at the buildings for a while, and then starts his story.

"I was captured by the bounty hunters. Alonso and I got quite a reputation from the raids we did on the BMM. The Bounty Hunters ripped me out of the cockpit and took me to a bounty hunter base. It was filled with Hessians and Outcasts, but not a single Corsair, excluding myself"

"Sounds like quite a hellhole", said Octavio, "I'm amazed you got out"

"Yea, but it would've been impossible as a one man job. All the Hessians, Outcasts, and I came together. We would've never done that in space, but we all have a common enemy- the Bounty Hunters. I would never work with a damned Hessian, after what they've done to our family, but I needed them to be meat shields for my escape. An Outcast and I...." Octavio interrupts.

"You were able to convince an outcast to work with you!?!?!” said Octavio, "You really have guts, they typically put themselves above others!"

"Anyways, his name was Miguel if I recalled correctly. We were able to escape and we fought our way through the base with the others. We reached a barricade, with Bounty Hunters and gun emplacements. I used the vents to be able to drop the Plasma Grenade above them. I succeeded and their guts splattered all over the wall. Some of the debris hit my eye though, and it got blinded. Only my left eye has vision now. When I escaped, those damned Outcasts kicked me out of the ship and left me there. I had to hijack a Bounty Hunter escape freighter. I killed some of them with a grenade and locked myself in the cockpit. I got a suit as quick as I could, and got out. Just in time to... That place blew up as soon as I got out. Some metal pierced the cockpit and hit my arm. Passed right through and glued me to the cockpit. The rest you probably know", said Alvaro.

"You are really a Gulliento", said Octavio, "You can survive in the worst conditions. I'm glad you're alive. I lost Maria and Carlos to those damned Hessians, You're all I got left now", said Octavio.

The hover car lowers down to the alley and parks in one of the houses. Octavio helps Alvaro out and puts him in the bed and puts on the holo-net. The Corsair news comes on

"Recently... the war in the houses has ended.... Outcasts kill an innocent Corsair family..."

"Dad", said Alvaro, "Who are the Outcasts? They speak our tongue and have our culture...”

"I don't know too well myself. I only know what the Elders have told me. They say that along time ago, we had a nice country and green land to ourselves; on the Planet they call Earth. There was a war, and our side lost. Alongside the other nations of the group they called the Alliance; we built ships to carry our peoples to safety. There was the Liberty, Rheinland, Bretonia, Kusari, and our ship- the Hispania. When we arrived here, our engines were destroyed. The houses say that it was the work of a Coalition (The enemy of the Alliance) Saboteur. Some say an Alliance member may’ve done it, more than likely a Rheinlander, to get more land or blood purity, and others even suggest some of our own brothers may’ve done so!”

He pauses for a moment, almost sighing.

Our peoples split in two, since we disagreed whether or not to settle on a nearby planet or somewhere else. We already distrusted each other, thinking the other try to kill us by detonating the bomb. Some of us took the lifeboats and found Crete weeks later. You probably know what happened from there. The other group was able to use the secondary engines on the Hispania, and found their way to that planet. The ship didn't get there; only close enough to the planet to get a ship to go there. We were right, the planet was damned. They got addicted to the drug caradime, and they can't leave their planet without getting killed, so they carry caradime and wear breathers to survive out side of their planet.”

He starts punching things into his computer, and information about caradime appears in the hologram.

“That evil drug has changed them, and they believe they are no longer humans, they are something better. When we first encountered them, we sent an Elder to meet their leaders. They were glad to see we survived, but had no interest in making an alliance with us, saying that we were the reason that the ship was disabled. They said we were inferior, and told the Elder to leave. The Elder says that he was attacked by them on the way back, and some of our pilots were killed. From that day on, we became enemies…"
Alvaro sees the window from the bed and sees a fleet of titans going out of the city.

"Dad", said Alvaro, "What's going on?"

Octavio steps up to the window and sees the titan squad.

"The Commander of this base had got orders from the Council of the Elders; they presented a new plan to end this menace, and said to go on the defensive for now, to weaken those bastards. Those Hessians' advantages are in numbers, we have advantage in skills", said Octavio, "But recently, they haven't been attacking much...”

"When I get better", said Alvaro, "Will I fight with you?"

"Probably", said Octavio, "I'm giving you my old Centurion for your new ship. It's rigged with a great engine, power plant, and can mount some very powerful weapons. It can mount a powerful shield. You could probably take a Sabre out in that ship"

Alvaro smiles a bit, and looks at the picture of his family.

"Dad", said Alvaro, "What happened to Mom and Carlos?"

"I can't tell you that yet", said Octavio, "All I can tell you is that those damn Hessians are responsible for both of their deaths. But enough of that, let's watch some movies from Liberty!"

The two spend a week to their selves. They go to bars, watch the holonet, and practice their skills in a simulator. Alvaro is able to take off his cast, but it’s still healing. He has a massive scar where the wound was, and it still gives pain when too much stress is put into it. One day, the two drive back from a wrestling match.

"Damn, that Rodriguez guy was one hell of a wrestler, wasn't he?", said Alvaro.

"Yea, Caesar was good too, but Rodriguez was too good for him", said Octavio.

"Dad", said Alvaro, "Who was that man you were talking to? He didn’t' look like a Corsair…"

"He was a Freelancer from Bretonia", said Octavio, "He says that the war in the Colonies destroyed the economy, and Rheinland has been hit the hardest. He also says that there were three people, a Freelancer and two members of the LSF who got full pardons for some reason. They were the biggest enemies of Liberty, but they got excused, and got the Lone Star. Not too many people know what they did, and they disappeared afterwards"

"They must have done something big", said Alvaro, "Getting excused and getting a medal means they've probably fought something big"

"You know, you look like the Corsairs of old, in Earth. Pirates then had the stereotype that they had eye patches..."
Before Octavio could finish, a massive explosion rocks the city, and some debris falls from the ceiling.

"WHAT THE HELL!", yelled Octavio.

Alvaro turns on the Holonet in the car to the Alert frequency.

"Warning! Warning! The base is under attack from a massive Hessian fleet! All pilots, active and un-active, report to the Landing pad.. AHHHHHHHHHHHH!"

An explosion is heard through the holonet, and it turns off.

"DAMN!", said Alvaro, "What could have caused that explosion!"

Their hover car rushes to the landing pad, dodging falling rocks from the ceiling. People are screaming around them and they can clearly see the corsair pilots going towards the landing pad. Fleets of titans and centurions launch and exit in swarms. They arrive at the landing pad, and lots of ships are launching as they jump out of their hover car. They rush to the observation deck.

"Oh good Lord!..” said Octavio.

A massive battle is taking place. Lasers fly everywhere, and Sabres and Stiletto's fly through the space. Titans and Centurions rush out of Cadiz and engage them. Explosions are seen through the space, and an occasional explosion rocks Cadiz.

"What a battle...”, said Alvaro.

"That's not the main attraction kid", said a Corsair behind them. The corsair points off to the right and what he sees makes his eye widen. A fleet of Rheinland Battleships, Cruisers, and Gunboats are pounding Cadiz.

"This is going to be one hell of a battle...” said Octavio.

End Ch. 4
