John Bown

Thu Apr 03, 2003 2:29 am by John Bown

um....no
Personally I love the racing mission and load it up nearly every time I play, but thats just me (Speed demon at heart)

As the race stars, a message pops up explaining rules. Once you close this message immediatly hit cruise (shift+w, you can still pilot in cruise, use the mouse flight mode=space) and as long as you don't have a freighter you should be able to clear the gates fairly easily.
My advice is don't try to hard, especially by cutting to close to the corners. As long as you get a good start at the beggining you can cruise through the center of each race gate. The guy messes up near the end, or slows down (one of the two). If you are having trouble near the very end where the turns get tighter just cut your cruise and go full throttle with thrusters; going 200 instead of 300. Ignore the missles and don't open fire.
