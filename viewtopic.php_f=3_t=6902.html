Tue Feb 25, 2003 7:34 pm by DiscoJason

Well, if you bring up the screen that shows all your factions and their attitudes, if you click on a faction you want to ally with, it will show you who their enemies are. You then starting killing those people until you get your faction into the friendly zone.

Now, this is a little hard to do while playing the single player missions as you might make the story in general hard for you if you have the police always chasing you or the armed forces. So, you may have to wait somewhat in SP to do this or you can do it right off the bat in multiplayer or starting a LAN game on your system ( which is the multiplayer universe with only you playing).
