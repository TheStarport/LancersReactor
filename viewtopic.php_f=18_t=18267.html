Does sound like a sweet idea. I'm pretty crap at modding, but heres an idea for how to do it :

As it would only really apply to capital ships, stick an extra HP on them as near as possible to the engine. Set the default loadout so that HP is mounted with a new weapon - 'Engine Damage Turret' or something. The define that weapon to have a really high refire rate, no energy drain, a really low damage and a very limited range. Obviously it would have no sound effect or visible projectile.

Its pretty crude but well within current modding capabilities.
