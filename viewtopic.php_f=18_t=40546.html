Accushot

Thu Aug 25, 2005 2:41 am by Accushot

It sounds from your post like you're installing the SDK after your own mod. The SDK will overwrite your files with standard Freelancer ones - the order you should do this in is:

a) Install Freelancer
b) Install the SDK
c) Install your mod.

The best thing to do is change the properties of the SDK files from read only to archive and then copy your MOD files to the folder where you extracted the SDK files to...overwrite the necessary files... and then zip up your MOD with the SDK files included.

Accushot is right, it sounds like your overwriting your MOD files by installing the SDK files after you activate your MOD.
