Your sound card or sound settings could be going down hill, but I would advise reinstalling the sound card drivers, and then trying FL. After you uninstall your sound card drivers, use a program such as "Driver Cleaner" to remove all files, as uninstallations do not always remove all of the files. Therefore, these older driver files may be conflicting with the new drivers that you have.
