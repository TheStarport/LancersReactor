The preferred method for escaping bad guys when you're unsure whether or not you can make cruise without getting popped (or if the enemy has cruise disrupters): thruster, engine kill, and flip your ship around to face your pursuers. You can't equip any form of equipment with out being docked on a planet/station/base/thing, but If you mean while docked, press F7 or click on the Inventory HUD icon in the top right corner of your display, after you do that, find the weapon you want to replace, unequip it (click ont he equip/unquip square), go to the weapon you want to put in its place and click on the equip/unequip square. Presto! You have just changed out your weapon but remember you have to be docked to do this.
