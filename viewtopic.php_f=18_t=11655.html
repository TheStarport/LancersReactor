although it was posted elsewhere...

"Enable God Mode"

In your PerfOptions.ini, located by default at My Computer / My Games / Freelancer / PerfOptions.

Locate this line:
DIFFICULTY_SCALE = 1.00

Change it to:
DIFFICULTY_SCALE = 0.00

Now, nothing does any damage to you (the player) whatsoever.

If you think you're the uber freelancer, change it to 2.00, and you'll take 2x the damage, etc.
