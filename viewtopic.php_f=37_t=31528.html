To all modders interested in incorporating Epsilon files in

We have recently received a couple of emails asking to use parts of our mod in theirs. We have been fairly tolerant to these people as we do respect them for asking. It has become quite a hassle to need to email them back several times to learn more about the mod. Thus we are asking that if you request to use something of ours in your mod you do the following.

Please provide details on your mod and any other members you may have in your team.

Be very specific about what items you'd like to use, and what changes if any you have planned

If this applies to you, please leave a link to your mod website.

If for whatever reason you choose to ignore these rules, be advised there will be a much smaller chance you will receive a response from us.

Sorry to sound possessive but you must understand the amount of work we have been putting into this. We have spent the last 11 months working on this and it is aggravating to need to send 5 emails back to the same person. If you need to reach any of us our emails are:

Parabolix - toolsparabolix@yahoo.com
