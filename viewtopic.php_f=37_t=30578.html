Epsilon version 2.6 TC MOD ... MOD RELEASE

Well hello again,

Epsilon 2.6.9 TC MOD - Can be downloaded - Here

----Features List----

Changes in 2.6.9

-21 New Ships -15 Ships From Version 2.5-

-The Ceres, -The Gorgon, -The Gryffin, -The Mystere, -The Nightstalker, -The Spectre, -The Rapier SP -The Rapier ES, -The Shrike, -The Basilisk, -The Spider, -The Wraith Mk I, -The Wraith VHF -The Taipan, -The Nemesis

-6 New Ships Added-

-The Scimitar - Model and textures by Harrier - Custom performance, weapons and setup by Glock 36
-The Black Knight - Model and Textures by Dark Dragon - Assisted by Harrier
-The Warlord - Model and textures by Harrier - Dedicated by the team in memorium for Worth Whitcomb"Warlord Bob" Riley
-The Cyclone - Model and textures by Parabolix - Special effects for both ship and weapons by Parabolix
-The Mars Flyer - Original Model Design by D.Proctor (permission granted for its use) Conversion and texturing by Harrier Custom performance, setup, sound and visual effects by master modder Glock 36 - with performance modifications by Crabtree
-The Minerva - Model and Textures by Harrier - Wing flame design courtesy of Viator

-12 New Systems - by Dark Dragon

Zone 21, Century, Akuma, Europa, Arachnia, Mace, 38th Parallel, Dundraggen, Nehida, Amdor, Kandor, Niyad

-36 Bases - by Dark Dragon

Explore the systems to find bases, planets, ships, weapons, commodities.
Almost all factions are represented, some systems are relatively benign, some are extremely hostile

-4 Working Factions - by Dark Dragon

Spiders - An extremely nasty collection of very well armed powerful enemies - at war with everyone
Lizards - Another nasty collection who are at war with the Spiders and anyone else they feel like attacking
Liberty Rangers - The "best of the best" and who's motto is "Eternal Vigilance". An upgrade in both skill, ships and weaponry for Liberty - a powerful ally - an awesome foe.
Bretonian Invincibles - Veterens of years of conflict - a Bretonian evolution in new weapons, tactics and ships. Highly skilled, hand picked pilots have taken up duties in both home and New Epsilon Territories. Quick to answer any challenge.
Next faction to be added-
Exiles - The best of the worst - a collection of disparate, desperate individuals exiled from their native factions, but with ties of blood and duty. A very bad disposition in very powerful ships. They tend to shoot first, ask questions later.

-31 Guns -

Cypher, Tesla, White Lightning, Hellspawn, Equalizer, Riley MAG, Retribution Cannon, Venom, Silver Sword, Nightshade Mk I, Nightshade Mk II, Nightshade Mk III, Hammerblow, Green Terror, Steel Rain, Vulcan, Yuurei, Kiri, Liberator, Slicer. Radian, Wake Blade, Dragon Breath, Excalibur, Spider Bite, Death Adder, Mars Flyer Custom Loadout.
-5 Turrets-
Hammerblow, Liberator, Steel Rain, Golden Lance, Black Harpoon,
-7 Missiles-
Hellhound, Diablo, Sidewinder Mk I, Sidewinder Mk II, Twister, Sniper, Copperhead
-9 Mines-
Snapper, Shredder, Slasher, Splitter, Bruiser, Crusher, Nutcracker, Thrasher Mk I, Thrasher Mk II
-8 Shields-
Scimitar Shield, Warlord Shield, Nazo Shield, Exile Shield, Invincible Shield, Ranger Shield, Hussar Shield, Cyclone Shield
-22 Custom Beam Effects-, -7 New Jumpgate Effects- , -22 New Lights-, -8 Upgradable Tractor Beams-, -8 Upgradable Scanners-, -91Upgradable Powerplants-, -71 Original Powerplants-, -20 Custom Powerplants-, -Armor Upgrades-, -7 New Countermeasures-, -8 Engines-

-2 New Weapon .cmp Models - Riley MAG and Hellspawn - models and .cmp files by Harrier

The original 71 Powersplants have been added to the game
21 Custom Powersplants also added
20 Custom Ships all have been added to the NPCS
Players can progress to level 100
Custom Rumours
Regenerating Shields on both NPC and AI

So far it works in both SP amd MP.....
