Are you using the original factions? Or have you added new ones for the nomads and the order?
The nomads in the original game did not give out missions, it was partly a hardcoding thing and partyly that their ships had the FIGHTER ship class not the class_fighter that you need for random missions.
For the order (which i thought was also hardcoded not to give missions in original fl), try using a combination of vignette types and check through the initial world values for any other groups as mostly of them are not hostile to them.
So, whats actually in the system? Is it just Order and Nomads? I believe you need to have both factions in the same ambient pop zone for them to be mission eligable, which is why i normally do a low density level system wide pop zone.
For the missions in other systems thing. That is one of those would be nice things that haven't really be sorted.
Try making the path between systems legal and add a base close to the jump object in the other system. Then add the mission vignette things near to the jump hole in the system that you want and with a bit of luck they will show.
