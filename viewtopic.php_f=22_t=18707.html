@ 2 days after applying the patch; I've posted some of this elsewhere so I'll summarize and be brief:
- Dual CPU usage is down on both (compaq dl380 g2 2x1.4) and we've upped to 30 players (from 23), which was not possible before.
- Majority of common mods autokicked (nomad battleship, Uber shields, etc) some not (600/1200/3000 cruise speed, beam weapons) It only appears to kick you *once you have commited the crime* of acquiring the modded object. Simply modding with FLMM does not activate the autokick sequence upon entering the server.
- Stability has never been an issue in sixty (60) public days so I can't comment on lack of crashing.
- ID logging is great and the console log is easy to search. As perhaps one of the few remaining totally unmodded servers, I am very pleased with this patch.
