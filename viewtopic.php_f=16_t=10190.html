UnjustEnd

Sun Mar 16, 2003 7:37 pm by UnjustEnd

RaptorMC is right about his trade route, wrong about profit per unit. Its 375 basically profit per unit.

This is an excellent trade route. If you start a new mp game, you can do like 3 missions for someone go there, then start running the route, save up enough money from it to fill up your 20 cargo ship and buy a rhino, then save up enough to fill up a 80 cargo ship, (roughly 80,000) and a humpback (roughly 170,000).
Then make the run to Rhineland in your rhino and buy the humpback (its hard but its very possible) on your way back to los angels go ahead and buy engine components at Planet texas (junkers will probably ask you to drop them, but you can actually still manoveur your ship while the "Drop cargo" dialogue is up, and get into a trade lane, then hit cancle to the dialogue when you are like 9k away from them) Run it back to freeport 4, you make double your profit on the run but it takes much longer then its worth, luckly its on your way back. Then you quickly start filling up your humpback and once you get about 250,000 dollars all you make is profit in your Humpback and the actual profit per-run on that is about 93,000. Do that about 7 more times and you will have a million. This all takes about 2 and a half hours. The Run is 6 minutes round trip if you are good at "last minute speed docking". A lot of people have more profitable runs but they take a very long time to run. Plus nearly 100,000 k per run is not bad at all.

Usually after I get my million in 2 and a half hours, I find the best way to go about getting a titan, is to go to rhineland, buy the Valk from their Navy, then go to Sigma13 via Frankfurt, then Sigma13->Sigma17 Jump hole(roughly on the line of 5E and 5F towards the bottom of their shared line). Then Sigma17->Onicron Theta(upper right E6) dock with the freeport in Theta. Go to the bar and purchase friendship with corsairs. If you feel that you have fine piloting skills then go ahead and go to Omnicron 41 (jump whole to there from Theta is at C5) to go Leon base in Omnicron 41 (which I'm gonna say is near the bottom left of D5) you can buy your titan their and have very profitable runs (highest I've seen is 250,000). The beauty of runs from corsairs here is that you only fight red Hessians which I personally don't like at all. So no one really gets mad at you for it that you should care about and they drop TONS of good weapons and missles (a lot of which sell for over 1,000 a pop). You usually make double what the mission pays you because the profits from loot will run about the what they were paying for it. Needless to say you can make another millions in about 6 missions.
