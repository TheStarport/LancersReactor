Game improvment

I was thinking to myself about all the other Space games out there that work on a Ranking System. I know the game does already have a Ranking system of some sorts. Were you go up in numbers. 38 being the top rating! (Not very imaginative I must say).

But maybe they should have intruduced more than one Ranking System. One being a Military Rank and the other a Trading Rank. Were you could only get the two different types of better ships (Fighters and Traders) as these separate ranks increased. Meaning the Military Rank would not allow you to buy better Trading Ships, and vice versa!

Also they could have done the Missions were you started out as a Rookie Pilot, and on finishing all the Missions were only promoted up one level only. Leaving a very large Milirary Ranking system to work your way through. This would also make you feel like you was somebody, rather than just a number 38 as you worked your way up through the ranking systems. Obviously the Military and Trading ranking system would be much harder to achieve. Making it much harder also to buy better Fighters and Traders. You could have also included many more medals in the game. But these would only be avalable at certain ranks from certain missions in the game, and depending on how well you completed the mission dictated if you got them. Again helping to make you feel as though you really was working your way towards being a "4 Star General".

They could have made the Ranking system just as hard for example as the famous "ELITE" game back in the 80's. After all that was the main thing that made the famous ELITE game last so long. And had you coming back for more months later.

What do you think!
