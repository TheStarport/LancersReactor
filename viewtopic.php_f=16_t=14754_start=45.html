About the Coalition...

Why is it that everyone's all set to avenge the Alliance? After 800 years, there is no Alliance anymore. There are just four major feuding Houses with numerous smaller factions. No one in the game is interested in going back to Earth at all.

By the same token, there's probably no Coalition anymore. Odds are, whoever holds Earth has morphed into something else. They are probably just as faction ridden as the Sirius sector. They're also probably more advanced technologically because they didn't spend the past 800 years sleeping or building up from almost nothing.

That's why I liked to use the Tachyon background for what the post-Coalition Sol System looks like. It posits a weak central government with feuding frontier factions. Tachyon: the Fringe also takes place about 400 years prior to FL's present so we get to see how Tachyon turns out. It's a setting that's different from the Sirius sector, but similar enough to be compatable with FL.
