Meatboy Dogfood

Tue Apr 15, 2003 12:08 am by Meatboy Dogfood

thank you for the responce. Funny thing was that I was in the system were they were but just had not scoped out the system yet. For those that want to know, they can be found in Omicron Alpha in the nasty green nebula.

The system contains the outcasts home planet Malta. Among the many features is a unknown jump gate that I will assume leads to the Planet of the apes, and ringing the jump hole is a whole bunch out Outcast tomb ships. You will have to turn into a grave looter to get the ripper mine. Most ships have already been looted but I found four that contained a wealth of weapons plus the sought after mine launcher and a supply of mines also.

Beware! the nebula around the tombs is hot as hell and will melt your armor quickly. Also squads of Nomad Hornets act as the tombs caretakers and will not be happy that you are there at all.

I made the trip to Malta in a Dromedary so looting the graveyard was tough. A quick calulation is "Dromedary+Nomad squad=Dead camel jockey" Be prepared to flee unless you are in a very heavy fighter. Three hits from a Nomad Hornet will cheese a Dromedary and the Nomads shoot quick and are real nimble. The only good thing is that the Nomads have not figured out how to use cruise disruptors so you have a good chance to flee. Run like the stellar wind, then return to your looting once you lose them.
