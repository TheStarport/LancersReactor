Yep, you can trade plain money

Guns:

like if you have a level 4 hardpoint, you can only put level 1, 2, 3 and 4 weapons on it, but no level 5-10 weapons. (the same system is used for shields)
of course, the higher the number, the better the gun

Linx

Sat Feb 22, 2003 1:33 am by Linx

You can't buy anything you don't have a hardpoint for. I don't think you can purchase ammo you don't have a launcher for... though to be honest I've not tried it. You can trade ammo, and you can certainly pick up ammo from a slain foe. You cannot carry anymore ammo than what the weapon specifies so no extras in the hold that you load in after you run out of your initial. So if your launcher has a capacity of 50 rounds, then 50 rounds is it. If you use some then find some in a debri, you can add those to your existing inventory up to the 50 limit. If your friend has some and trades you, he can give you up to the 50 limit.

Does that answer it?
