AcidRock, did you remember to close the captions with the other [. This forum apparently eats it up. If you didn't close the caption then its very likely that that is the cause of your problems. As for Kru Noctum, try going to the same thread and looking at the similarites. They should help. Also the nickname of a ship in the shiparch usually corresponds to its counterpart file in the equipment folder. For ships, its usually goods. There the engine name corresponds to engine_equip, the weapons to weapon_equip and so forth. Remember modding isn't an exact science. Its all about dissassembling what you got to find out what makes it tick.
