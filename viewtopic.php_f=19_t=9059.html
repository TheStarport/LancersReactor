Hanzo

Mon Mar 10, 2003 7:19 pm by Hanzo

For the Corsairs, fastest way I found was to run missions in Frankfurt against the Red Hessians, and also in Dresden. In fact, if you take the jumphole in Frankfurt *to* Dresden, you'll fight off quite a few Red Hessians going to and from those systems. You'll pick up a lot of stuff from them and you'll improve your stance with the Corsairs. Once your neutral to the Corsairs, then you can get out there and jack up your stance with a hack (mine cost about 43k).
