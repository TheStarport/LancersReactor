Argh

Sat Nov 20, 2004 6:34 pm by Argh

If you look through the goods.ini file, you'll find the Loadout for the Titan. Check this file in the EVO mod, and the XML files... eventually you'll find it. That Loadout is what you're looking for, not the Loadouts.ini (which is for Wrecks and AI-controlled ships only). Once you've found it, you can find the powerplant entry.

Now... what are you wanting to do next? If your SP/MP character has been corrupted, then your only real hope is buy another ship. But chances are... what you really need to do is install a fresh copy of the FL SDK 1.3, which should get rid of any bad INI files that might've gotten left behind in your FL installation....
