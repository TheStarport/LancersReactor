Fri Feb 03, 2006 8:34 am by Moonhead

For my custom systems, I did modify some of the existing starspheres. If you just change the color, and maybe do some minor modifications to the original pcitures (like narrowing to Barrier so that it looks farther away), you can get nice results. For instance, I've darkened all the Liberty starspheres in combination with lower ambience values in the systems .ini files (my adaption of Louva-Deus "dark space" idea, ). I also made a kind of pink/purple starsphere based on the Li01 one, and made the edge tile smaller -with more black around it to keep the pic the same size) to make it look more to the south (as this system is north from Colorado).

I have no idea how to make totally new ones (although that must be possible).
