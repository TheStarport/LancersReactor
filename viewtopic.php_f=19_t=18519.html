CLANS
Clans are now permissible on this server, but must be licensed. Read below.

CLANS
To operate a clan, you must first recieve a license from the server. Clan licenses cost $1m per clan (maybe per clan participant), and can be contributed by any or all of the clan, as long as it comes to server in one lump sum!. Any group of 3 or more players operating without a clan license in unison against another player will be considered an unlicensed clan. Names will be recorded, and you will be required to pay the license fee.

Clan or clan members must not fight against players below L30, unless specifically found trespassing. Exception to this are pre-agreed duels.

Exceptions to the trespass rule, if a trespass rule is applied by the Clan. Players requiring sanctuary, that is player who consider themselves badly hurt, may enter the Clans property and claim sanctuary by using a system hail. Players who claim sanctuary must be allowed safe passage through system. Players must not dock with any object within the Clans property. Players who do so, will lose sanctuary rights, and considered a trespasser. Players claiming sanctuary may barter for shields or nanos with clan members.

Clans may develop their own rules for use within there own property, but these must be agreed with the server first.

Legal clans, their host bases/systems and basic trespass rule will be posted here.

Players reporting unlicensed clan activity will recieve $100,000 reward.
