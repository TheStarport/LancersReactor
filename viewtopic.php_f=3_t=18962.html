Mines can be useful, it all depends on your fighting style.

When approaching a group of NPCs on the first pass, when they get within 300-200m away, launch a few and they should slam into anyone you don't take out with your guns.

In another situation, if your ship has a turret, switch to that view and look aft. (This supposes you have a superior ship to your pursuers). When they come up behind you, launch your mines (especially the homing types). They should track into them and do whatever damage they are supposed to. It can be fun to watch them blow up

Some of the high end mines can also be useful with attacking a stationary target or large ship. Flying along close to your target and dropping mines as you go can supplement your guns/missles in taking out the target.
