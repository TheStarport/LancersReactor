DUBLIN - Right now Dublin is in chaos. Arranmore base and Battleship Essex have been destroyed, and everything is beyond help. "Wh just can't get enough of them, they're so lovely." a local commented. There are also rumours of at least one suspected rape of a Nomad by a BPA constaple.

HAMBURG - The Hispanian Fleet just won it's last battle in the Hamburg system. At 21:40 the last Rheinland Military forces where defeated in the Battle of Vogtland, where the Rheinland Military and the Rheinland Federal Police witnessed a humiliating loss at the hands of Hispanian forces. This means that the Texas, Bering and Hudson systems are totally isolated from Rheinland. But the tides may turn as it has been reported that about 3million Xenos joined the Rheinland Military at lunchtime. They've also given up Dawson Base to Rheinland.

VALENCIA - Hispanian sources tell us that Nomad activity has heavily decreased during this week. The Hispanian Fleet has worked hard to curb the Nomad activity within Valencia ever since the Malta Jumpgate was finished. Another reason for this sudden decrease can be the Nomads invasion of Dublin, where half of the current Nomad fleet is used. A few Nomad patrols can still be sighted here and there in the system, we recommend every traveller to stick to the military patrols or the jumpgate if they enter the system.

CHUGOKU - Deep Space Engineering has finally finished the construction of the Hokkaido->Chugoku Jumpgate, the Kusari Naval Forces is pouring in troops one after one into the Chugoku system. These troops will unlikely return though, as there is no gate from Chugoku back to Hokkaido. The Blood Dragons seem to have expected this ad in response all the elite fighters from Kyoto have worked together with the best painters found on Kyushu to paint all the rocks of the system to different colours. The thinking behind this is to confuse the arriving Kusari forces into believing that they're high. Then the blood Dragons will come in and laugh there arse of at the confused Naval Forces troops. This will force the pilots to commit suicide, as it is expected within the Kusari society that when one is humiliated then he/she is expected to commit suicide, according to tradition.
