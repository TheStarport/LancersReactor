CURRENT DEATHS: 3
_________________________________
Status
Location: Planet Manhattan
Credits: 7329
Ship: "Starflier" Civilian Ultra-Light Fighter
Level: 3
Faction: Freelancer

Today was a great day in my life - I bought a ship.

After years of living on Manhattan, I'm out to explore Sirius. After I bought my Starflier, I decided to do some Merchant running. I went to Ft. Bush and bought some Optronics and shipped them to Houston. I realized fast that this wouldn't make me enough money, and that I would be losing faster than gaining.

So, I decided to become a Freelance pilot. I have a Starflier (don't laugh), 2 Justice Mk IIs, a Drail, Class 2 sheil, and a Thruster. I did 2 assasaination missions and 1 kill ship mission today. I'm saving up to exchange my Starflier for a Patriot, then a Defender. After that, I'm gonna move to Bretonia.

Until then I will make the best of things in Liberty.
