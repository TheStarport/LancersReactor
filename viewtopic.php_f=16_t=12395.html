The $30 profit from selling H-Fuel between Fort Bush and Baltimore Shipyard is a good start.

If you don't mind doing a little more flying i.e. letting the Trade Rings do most of the work for you...

Boron = Pittsburgh (120) -> Fort Bush (144)
Pharmaceuticals = Fort Bush (111) -> Pittsburgh (144)

That's a round trip profit of $57. If you can fill up a Rhino Freighter you'll get 80 x $57 or $4560 per trip. Early on, it's not bad for a mining facility and police base seperated by one Trade Lane.
