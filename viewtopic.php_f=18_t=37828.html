Freelancer RTS

Greetings.

Have you ever wanted to have a massive battle between Liberty and Rhineland...just to see who would win?

Well, I have an idea. We can use Homeworld.


For those of you who haven’t heard, Homeworld (1/C/2) is a 3D RTS set in space. Think mammoth Battlecruisers shooting each other with earthshaking power while nimble fighters dogfight between, narrowly avoiding fire.

I tell no lies.


Now imagine this:

A fleet of Liberty Battleships and Cruisers escorted by Defenders is facing off against a Rhineland fleet of Battleships, escorted by Valkyries.

The Liberty Cruisers move into position and open fire on the enemy Battleships, signaling the beginning of the end.

The Defenders' engines light up, moving forward to engage the Valkyries, who come hurtling at them with all guns firing.


Now, this idea came to me when I was thinking about how there are no large-scale fleet battles in Freelancer, at least in multiplayer. And there are precious few in single player...

So. Who's interested?

Well. First of all, I was talking this over with my partner in crime and we have decided to lay this off for a while. We put it back on the shelf so to speak; the reason being is we have started on a TC for HW1.

I think there should be something awe inspiring when you watch your little Patriot fly along side your massive Battleship.
