Starlancer v. Freelancer..

..which isn't a simple question of "which is better?" - that would be a silly question, and one I wouldn't ask; rather,

why does FL get all the attention, and SL doesn't? For what reason, do you think, is there this perception of a yawning gulf between them when they are in fact, part of the same series?

I have my own opinions on this issue but I'd like to hear what other people's thoughts are.

I think SL is great and it's always been a source of disappointment to me that the hundred years or so between the end of SL and the intro to FL have never been addressed apart from a brief mention in said FL intro - not even in fan fiction afaik When SL was released I was hoping for another series like WC, which got five offical releases plus numerous expansions and add-ons, as well as Armada and the superlative Privateer. Instead we got one release then a long wait for FL, which although a great game, and I've played it to death, didn't really continue the SL story; rather it ended it and started up a whole new story. Why does this never seem to bother anyone? (except me)

I may be displaying a shocking level of ignorance, for which I apologise ina dvance but has there ever been an FL mod (finished) that covers the Alliance-Coalition War in any way, shape, or form? I believe that there was a Return to Sol mod once, but what's happened to it I don't know, and my memory may be ill-serving me.... again.
