Hey guys,

Any future patches will be provided by the original "coder" of the Mod. If you're having troubles with certain aspects of the mod, I'd suggest you wipe everything, including your save files, and your now nuked FL install, since Rebalance 3.50 leaves all kinds of stuff behind, even when it's deactivated by FLMM. Then reinstall Freelancer, apply SDK 1.3, and activate FLRebalance v3.50, then apply its patch.

This is all I have to say about this Mod, other than good luck avoiding crashes and other problems. It's early Alpha quality at best, and ridden with bugs. Ro9ue, Bejaymac and I at one time tried to rebuild the mod (unsuccessfully). It just has too many problems, and is too hacked-together to be easily or even with reasonable difficulty, rebuilt from scratch. The worst part is this used to be my favorite Mod, too. I'm disappointed to say the least.

To MD:
If you do want help fixing the Mod, please let me know, I'm more than happy to help. But you have to be willing to communicate and take advice.

In essence, use the mod at your own risk.
