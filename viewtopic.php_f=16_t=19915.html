This takes probably an hour to get an Eagle. I had an eagle with only 5 kills total on it. 2 fighters and 3 transports.

1== Fly starflyer to Honshu buy hawk/baraccuda etc. and upgrade weapons and shields.

3== Dock at Gas Mines station incase you die.
4== Kill GMG(Gas Mines Guild) until Corsairs are neutral. This made me neutral with Corsairs,Outcasts, and I think Red Hessians as well, while still being neutral with GMG.

Next you want Weapons and shields.

5== Fly to Gamma buy class10 shield from Crete. (Rep should be ok to buy it.)
6== Fix rep better with corsairs if you want weapons from Gamma. Try the station and planet bars to bribe. Or kill some bountyhunters in the area.
