For any class 10 VHFs,

2x Tizona Del Cid
2x Nomad Energy Cannon
2x Nomad Energy Blasters
Sunslayer Torpedo

The Nomad weapons have different refire rates, so you have a "machine gun" effect, which is really important in PvP since your opponent will be bouncing all over the sky unlike the NPCs. The Sunslayer torpedo actually moves at 260 m/s if you launch it while going at full afterburner speed (compared with 290 or 299 for the missiles, there is really not much difference between them in terms of respond time for your opponent), it has better turn rate than some missiles, doesn't take up a gun slot, not to mention doing massive amount of damage. I probably shouldn't give out my tactical secrets here but I can do one-pass-kill if my opponent is stupid enough to press on with a head to head gun pass on me.
