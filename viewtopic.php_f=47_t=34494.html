Big RPG!

Okay, I'm gonna start an RPG. Here goes...

This RPG will take place in the top half of the Sirius map.
Systems...
All Sigma
All Tau
Honshu
New Tokyo
Kyushu
Chugoku
Omicron Theta
Omicron Alpha
Omicron Beta

Story: The Outcasts claim the top portion of Sirius as their home. That is disputable. Many other factions want a part of that area for themselves. The Corsairs are coming up in bigger numbers from Omicron Theta to attempt to gain more systems, and to attack their ancient enemies. The GMG and other Kusari corparations want a piece of the vast amounts of mineral and gas deposits that the Outcasts claim in the Tau and Sigma systems. The Blood Dragons want to extend their reach to the Sigma Systems. Groups like the IMG and Zoners, who want to stay where they are, are caught in the crossfire.
Who will win? Will the Outcasts be able to keep hold of their home? Or will they be destroyed by the forces that oppose them? You will decide, in this brand new RPG!

Factions and Ships:
Outcasts- Any of the Borderwold line. (Dagger, Stilleto, etc.)
Corsairs- Any of the Corsair line. (Titan, Centurion, etc.)
GMG- Hawk, Falcon, Eagle.
IMG- Startracker, Hawk, Falcon, Eagle.
Zoners- Any Borderworld or Civilian Ship
Kusari Navy or Police- Drake, Dragon, Drone
Other Corp- Any Civilian ship.
Blood Dragons- Dragon, Hawk.
Freelancer- Any of the above ships. (If you choose to be a Freelancer, then you must be semi-aligned with at least one faction, and you rep must be adjusted accordingly!)

Other notes: No Order or Nomads! No Codename weapons! If you are a member of a faction, then you must use that faction's weapons!
Last, and most important rule... Have Fun!
PS- Thanks to Com. Lynch for coming up with the idea!
Edit- Don't forget to put Name/Faction/Base/System on the top of your posts!

Although most Outcasts and Corsairs captured deny all existence of project 'Dara Dan Doa', at least ten have given tidbits of information concerning all plans for 'Dara Dan Doa'." Nicholas was staring blankly into the TV. If project "Dara Dan Doa" was real, and not just a bunch of rumors, then Sirius could be in real danger. But for now, he ignored the news. He had just made one- and- a- half million credits with his "Triangle of Cash" trade route that he had discovered, and he planed to enjoy himself. He was wondering if he should make one last run before returning home, but he was not sure. He was lost in thought when the waiter came to his table, food in hand. He had ordered the most expensive meal on the menu.

After he had finished his dinner, he headed to the landing pad. If he did one last run, he would get home at about 3:30 A.M. SST (Standard Sirius Time). He thought for a moment, then climbed into his ship and headed for the Comididy dealer...
