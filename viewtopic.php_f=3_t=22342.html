If you accept a mission for, say, the Mollys, even if it's not against the Corsairs, their rep will still go down. As the Molly rep goes up, Corsair rep goes down.

Criminals is anyone who attacks the corperations or police forces. Obvious ones like Mollys, Corsairs, Outcasts and Rogues, but also the Farmer's Alliance (their hits on Synth Foods) and Gaians. There are quite a few other groups.
