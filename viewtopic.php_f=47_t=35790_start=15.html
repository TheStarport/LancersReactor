That should cover all of the math-y bits. Now, to the fun stuff!

I’ll start out with a general announcement...
Bretonia has long been a nation desirous of peace. Unfortunately, organized crime and piracy are on the rise. Every house has at least one unique criminal group that is a constant thorn in their side. Additionally, we all have to deal with the increasingly violent depredations of both the Corsairs and the Outcasts. In the interests of peace and prosperity I would ask that all houses put aside their petty differences and unite against these common foes. We must not wait for them to grow stronger. Until they have been crushed, the colonies will not know true prosperity.

And now, on to some specifics...
Bretonia has long been plagued by chronic H-Fuel shortages. The Bretonian government has therefore decided to enter into an agreement with Rhienland. We are willing to trade 10 Metals for 10 H-Fuel or 5 H-Fuel and 10 Credits per turn. Additionally the Bretonian government would like to announce that it plans to expand into the Tau-31 System. The H-Fuel, Water and Oxygen found therein will undoubtedly prove useful. Additionally this expansion will serve to further restrict the movements of various criminal organizations. Any factions wishing to aid us in this endeavor should make their intentions known. The Bretonian government will reward any actions we deem helpful.


That should cover everything.
