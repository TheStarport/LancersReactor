Metallica - Metallica is a great album too......flippin awesome in fact. So they became more commercial - but alot say 'pre black' because its 'cool'. Fact is that Metallica - Metallica ROCKS. After that things changed - but what do you expect? They were loaded, they no longer lived their hard up days etc.

As for the change in their writing and production - they grew up. They moved on in age - as Alice in Chains Guitarist Jerry Cantrell once said:
"Our music - its who we are, its what we do, its what we live, its where we're at, its who we are" - meaning their experiences in life, encounters and everything else influenced their songs. Like when the Gallagher brothers stopped doing coke, got off the streets by being rich - started living the 'Good life' - they could no longer write about how life was back in the 'cigarettes and alcohol' days.....because it wasn't what they were experiencing anymore.

Metallica moved on and matured. Yeah - i don't like the new stuff either....but i don't hate it/consider it a sell out - they progressed. There is only so much hate they can write about - hence why most bands 'fizzle' out. Good thing is that they still perform all the classics live
