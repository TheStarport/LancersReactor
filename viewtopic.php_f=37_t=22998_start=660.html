parabolix

Sun Jan 02, 2005 9:46 am by parabolix

Well I'm back to post after a bit of a hiatus from posting. And I've got some good news

It seems the mod is becoming much more stable. Even with the addition of new ships and weapons. Harrier and I have been busy ripping apart the ships and remaking them or making new ones in general with the help of Buck Danny pointing out errors and fixing them. 13 Ships have been added since the last version and multiple ships have been redone. The Howler, Mirage, Spectre, and Cyclone have all been redone, and all look relatively the same (except for the Spectre which Harrier re-texed and looks awesome now). Most texture changes however have been entirely in changing tga files to dds (which for those who dont know, are smaller, and have built in LODs). New weapons have been added, not to mention more equipment. Included in the new features are the multi-fire guns and Harrier's transparent Nomad ship (the Nightmare), among other things. Some of the systems have been redone by JustSomeGuy and are looking much better. Bases are being populated and encounters enhanced. The .dll errors are non-existant now and most spelling/grammar errors have been fished out.

I'm currently building a website (just a free geocities one) mainly to showcase screenshots and for general information on the mod. Until it's finished, I'll use Photobucket to host screenshots. You can see a screenshot of the Vixen in action with its custom guns here . I'll edit this post and add more screenshot links as I add more to Photobucket.

Btw, the mod will be released very soon, the mod has been sent off to bp and he just needs a couple updates and the readme files. It should be very soon now...

Parabolix
