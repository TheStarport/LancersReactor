Painkiller

You guys play the demo of this game??? I think you can get it off Filefront or something, its supposed to be coming out in march this game is absolutely awesome, Its like Serious Sam but in extremely twisted gloomy disturbing way. Your some dude who died in a car crash and even though you led a modest life you were not admitted into heaven so your stuck in purgatory attempting to stop a massive holy war. Somewhat of a fun storyline, anyway the game is excellent because, unlike almsot every game on the market, out of the 24 levels and 5 boss arenas there is no enemy or texture reusage, that means every level you play has its own enemies and textures. I only played the demo but nonetheless the game is absolutely excellent, in fact, I think I found my favorite weapon of all time, called the Stake Gun. Its like a crossbow that shoots really thick foot long wood stakes at high speeds. Its great, and it uses the Havok 2.0 engine from Max Payne 2 the physics are the best i have ever seen. You guys should give it a shot

PS: the truth here, in the demo, there was a undead guy about to throw one of his innards at me (they use their organs as projectiles, you can tell this game is rated M) he lifts his hand up, I fire a stake at it and attach it to a swinging hotel sign above him as the sign and his body slighlty rocks back and forth
