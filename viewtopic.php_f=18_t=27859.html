Download the Bini editor Bini install it to the mods folder and then copy it to any subfolder you need. Once there, click on and hold the file (shiparch) and drag onto the bini and it will convert it to a text file. I open this and select all and copy. Then open the shiparch file. Hilite it all and click paste. You will then have a workable shiparch file.
