Well, personally I'd like to see the time period just prior to the Kilrathi war. Maybe even the events leading up to the war. Heck, I'd also like to see the actual Tiger's Claw and a wing of hornets/raptors/rapiers capturing the Ralatha class cruiser (you know, the one Hobbes was in...er, that is you'd know if you were huge fans of the games--particularly the first one and the second expansion). And here's an idea: depict the movie in a sort of Starship Trooper-ish way. Even though ST sucked (flame me if you want...but I believe that there is such a thing as being too campy), the way they delivered the movie was pretty cool.

As for cast considerations........well, I haven't seen any movies recently in the theaters with actors who could play a blair/marshal/whoever. Keep in mind, if they made a movie taking place in the time period of the first game, the actors would have to be under 25. Looking back on the way Tolwyn was illustrated in WC2, I'd say he could be played by Tom Skerritt (hehe, the last movie I saw in the theater was Tears of the Sun, Skerritt played the commanding officer of an aircraft carrier). But this time, they better make Paladin scottish, by gum! Of course, this would be my idea of a good (at least better) Wing Commander movie. Anyways, there's my 2 cents. Cheers.
