Star Wars---Twilight

Hey guys it me...anyway I'm finishing the rules for the game Red_dude and myself were working on before he passed away. Basically the time period is this:
New Hope-Return of the Jedi and beyond. There are six major factions:
The Empire
The Alliance/Rebellion
The Hutts
The Bounty Hunters
New Jedi Order
New Sith Order

There are two smaller faction types, you don't have to play as these two, you have to play as one of the other ones:
Government of an individual Planet
Ship building corperation(one player factions for each one)

Factions are broken down into ranks, so many players can join the same faction.

It is a turn based game, but with much Role-play, little complicated math, big battles and all the other fun stuff that made CYOF a hit(thanks jacob for giving us the idea to work with)

Anyway the actual game will be held here:http://swtwilight.proboards41.com/
because it will be very big.

So sign up and I hope to see ya. I'm Emperor of the Empire.
