Okay, it goes like this.
Red = Hostile. Will attack you on sight.
White between -6 bars and -2 bars = Will not attack on sight, can dock and trade, will not offer missions, might try to jack your cargo, refusal can make this base but not the faction hostile, base will go back to white when you re-start the game.

White between -2 bars and +2 bars = Will not attack on sight, can dock and trade, WILL offer missions, might try to jack your cargo, refusal can make this base but not the faction hostile, base will go back to white when you re-start the game.

White between +2 bars and +6 bars = Will not attack on sight, can dock and trade, will offer missions, will NOT try to jack your cargo, will not aid you in a fight unless the enemy is common.

Green = all of the above but will aid you in combat if the enemy is not their ally.
