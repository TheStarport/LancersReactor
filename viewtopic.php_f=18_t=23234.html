Mon Aug 25, 2003 9:25 pm by Glitch

I know the cloaking and jamming stuff won't work, and I don't know about the engines and scanners but the cruise speeds can only be changed for all ships ie, you can't make 1 ships faster than all the others. Add Cruising_speed=x under [EngineEquipConsts, anom_limits_max_velocity = x under [PhySysConsts in constants.ini. Change the x to whatever you want, tho 3000 makes it kinda hard to go through asteroid fields. Try setting it up to 10000, and watch as you pass through things
