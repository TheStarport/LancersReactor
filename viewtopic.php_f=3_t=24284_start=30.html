NukeIt

Mon Nov 03, 2003 3:22 am by NukeIt

/start rant

My new opinion on cheating:

I thought about it for a while, then expanded my definition of cheating to include Nomad Weapons. There is no penalty for using them(i.e. energy usage or ammo) AND no cost for getting them...in other words, they're an ultimate weapon, and by definition a cheat. Energy/ammo management is a skill, and by taking that out of the equation you give yourself a tremendous advantage over anyone who doesn't have them. For comparison, all the other class 9 and 10 weapons that do that much Damage Per Second deplete even a Titan's reactor in a matter of seconds.

In other words, ANYTHING done for personal advantage over other players that has NO DISadvantage is a cheat, plain and simple. Even if anyone else can use it. I'd love to find a MP server where they're not allowed.

Now I'll probably get flamed into oblivion by all the Nomad proponents on these boards, but so be it...

/end rant
