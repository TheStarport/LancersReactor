Welcome to TLR etc. Don't start any "Best Ship", "Best Weapon" or "Where can I find Weapon/Ship "X" ? " topics and you should be fine. On the same topic, there are sticky's in the spoiler forums which deal with weapons/ships etc and so on. Okay, now my rant is over, onto the topic at hand ......

I'd try TNG (The Next Generation) mod,it has new ships/new accessories/new weapons etc. Just don't expect to fight in the nexus in the beginning of the storyline. Freelancer Evolutions is at the moment the best add-on mod available and I would reccomend you download it and try it, as Chips put quite alot of effort into it.

Before you ask what's the best multiplayer mod, it depends on what your taste's are. The Star Wars Total Conversion mod is quite a good one. REMEMBER that only use mods on servers that allow you to, otherwise you're giving yourself an unfair advantage a.k.a cheating.

Evolutions v1.27 is nicely improved - plot missions are about the same, sometimes a bit harder, sometimes easier (one of the new mods is increased firepower on most ships, including friendlies..as an example i died last night from friendly fire, a transport i was escorting caught me with a couple of shots and toasted me, but when i replayed the mission it all went perfectly ) but the biggest difference is the random fights - improved AI, they ALL have nanobots and shield batteries...makes for more involved dogfights...it just has some really nice touches, all enemy ships seem to have better weaponry, and know how to use 'em...you start with a better ship than in the normal game but that isn't a bad thing, and the selection of new ships available in Liberty is a very nice touch - as is the upgrades you WILL need to carry out ( i won't say anymore, you'll love it).
hope it won't take you to long to download, start with Freelancer Mod Manager from the TLR download centre...the one I use is FLMM 1.2 beta 9, you're not supposed to use it with v1.7 but i had crash issues with the FLMM 1.2 finished version (probably just me ). anyway, either should work fine with 1.27.
My favourite touch is the luxury liner, available from one of CHIPS's new systems...

Thanx again, CHIPS!
