When you export a CMP it exports the mesh, mesh names & mesh material assignment names and nothing more, so adding a specular setting won't do anything. It means even less when it comes to exporting your MAT as all of those settings are ignored by the MAT exporter, your plain TGA/DDS texture is all that's add to the MAT.
