Help Wanted on a StarFleet Command TC

Needed:

1. People with experience adding Hardpoints to Models
2. People with experience with Mission & Bribe and factions

The mod will port as much of Starfleet Command as possible (based of Starfleet Battles). This means weapons, ships, traditional alliances.
