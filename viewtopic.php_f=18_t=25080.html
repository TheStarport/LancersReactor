Holst/Wagner Music Mod... anybody interested?

I was bored one night, so I decided to play around with Freelancer's music files. Using the excellent tutorial by Chips (Thanks!) and the suggested software ("dbPowerAMP", I decided to add some custom music. I thought that Chips' use of Wagner's "Ride of the Valkyries" would provide an interesting juxtaposition to the action in FL, so I decided to replace some of the music with this track. In order to further add constrast, I also utilised Holst's "The Planet Mars" for battle music, and it works out quite well. I then created a .flmod file for practice, and thus I now have a mod. Now that I have finished my rather lengthy anecdote, I'll get to the point; is anybody interested in downloading this mod? I just wanted to see if there was any interest as I created it for fun, not for distribution, and whether or not it would be worth submitting to TLR.

Regards,

Oghma.

PS. Chips, I hope you don't mind my appropriation of your choice of music (ie. "Ride of the Valkyries" for use in this mod, but I thought that it would be amusing when fighting Nomads around Toledo, etc.
