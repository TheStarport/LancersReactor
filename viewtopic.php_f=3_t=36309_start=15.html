Thu Dec 02, 2004 6:50 am by NukeIt

No matter what game you are playing, and no matter what size guns you have, head to head is a tactic that only invites disaster. It doesn't matter how fast you are travelling when you make a head on pass; you aren't moving as the other guy sees you- you are just getting bigger. If they, by some off chance, have more firepower up their sleeve than you do, you WILL die. Do not approach any enemy head-on; weave and dodge, close your distance by thrusting and strafing; cross their flight path, do not go along it.

Vary your speed whenever you aren't shooting at something- A shot that is calculated for a ship going 80m/s is not going to hit a ship that is going 200m/s a quarter second after it is fired.
