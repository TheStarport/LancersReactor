it could have different reasons

1. the faction and the ships aligned to that faction could be wrong (the tags for them or the alignment itself)

2. the definition of the ships in the shiparch could be wrong

3. the cmps could be corrupted (if players see wrong ships but the info shows the correct ship) -> crc error or simply a duplicate hull name (which is a problem of almost every big mod that uses different ship packs)

without the code or the cmps its not possible to say more
