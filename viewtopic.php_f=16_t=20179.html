witchdoctor

Wed Jun 25, 2003 6:31 pm by witchdoctor

Lane Hackers tend to have graviton shields and to use tachyon weapons, at least in the usual places I have encountered them playing through the game. Some factions have different weapon types at higher difficulty levels. I haven't been to Sigma 19, so I don't know if they are carrying different weapon and shield types. You can always scan the ship and see the types of shields and weapons they are carrying.
For graviton shields, you will do most damage using particle weapons and least damage using tachyon and neutron weapons.
For defense against enemies using tachyon weapons, use graviton shields. Tachyon weapons do extra damage against positron shields.
Where exactly the sweetspot is in trading off between a higher grade shield vs one that is more vulnerable to a specific weapon is, I don't know. That is why I keep lots of extra weapons on the ship, so I can change them out if need be for specific star systems and enemies, to maximize damage and defense.

Sherlog

Wed Jun 25, 2003 8:15 pm by Sherlog

Slight correction: neutron/tachyon weapons are most effective against molecular shields and least effective against positron; laser/photon have the most effect on positrons and least on gravitons.

Some Outcasts and some Bounty Hunters carry particle weapons - Pyros/Kraken or Warrior/Buckshot respectively. These can bite pretty hard into a graviton shield and are more difficulat to evade than the slow plasma guns other folk are using.

The gravitons are the best choice overall, though, because they have the same capacity as the other shields one class higher and enemies with particle/plasma weapons aren't all that common anyway. Among shields of the same class the gravitons are best against all weapon types except plasma/particle, where they are the second best. And gravitons also have the best resistance against shield busters ...
