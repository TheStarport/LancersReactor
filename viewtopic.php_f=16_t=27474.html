Sherlog

Wed Jan 07, 2004 5:11 pm by Sherlog

Full Tarantula kit (2 x Tarantula, 2 x Adv. Tarantula) works well agains low-level enemies but it gets tedious against class 9/10 NPCs - especially masses of them - because the Drake does not have a strong powerplant. Alternative: Borroco plus Pyros kit (1 class 5, 2 class 6). Slightly slower projectiles but slightly increased range compared to the Tarantulas, much better energy utilization against ships with non-trivial shields (class 9 and up) but the Nomads won't care obviously. Both configs have the advantage that you do not have to dock with a base unless you want to sell loot or book a mission (i.e. no ammo required except for cruise disruptors for which you can loot the ammo if you use the Wasp). For Omicron Alpha/Gamma I'd recommend some medium mines like Drillers as life insurance.

Using one class 5 slot for a Catapult launcher gives the Drake a lot more bite but of course then you have to watch your ammo and replenish occasionally, which is though if you're in one of the easter egg systems and the next friendly base is 3 jumps away. The Outcasts Sabres drop Lancer missiles which fetch a nice buck but cannot be fired from a Catapult launcher.

Sherlog

Thu Jan 08, 2004 10:05 am by Sherlog

Yes, it's a bummer with the guns. But they hold quite a while, a lot longer than the wings. And the Drake can easily afford to lose the latter, unlike other ships where lost wings mean lost equipment (e.g. Barracuda).

Btw, I'd recommend Adv. Tarantulas instead of Dragoons or Stealthblades. The Tarantulas have the same projectile speed but much better energy efficiency and the highest damage output in their class. This more than offsets the slightly shorter range (550 m instead of 700 m), especially as the Drake has excellent manoeuvrability but a rather weak powerplant.

Sure, it is nice if you have guns with 700 m range so that you can sting the enemy from far away. With the Tarantulas you have to get a little closer, but the high damage output (best in class 5/6, together with Pyros kit) and the excellent efficiency mean that you can hit enemies hard and fry them in short order even with guns alone. This isn't possible with Dragoons or Stealthblades. The Dragoons are nice for mopping up the stuff left after the Catapults have done their work but the Stealthblades have nothing to recommend them except perhaps their colour. Class 5/6 guns are so cheap that it should always possible to buy some decent kit, and if money is a problem then one can always harvest Angelitos from the Corsairs or Dragoons from the Outcasts (depending on political expediency ). Also, the Liberty/Rheinland plasma guns aren't bad at all if you can handle the slow speed and ROF, and I'd rather use a Flamecurse or Magma Hammer than a Stealthblade. Downside: all incoming missiles have to be dodged or flared because it isn't practical to shoot them with the ponderously slow plasma guns.
