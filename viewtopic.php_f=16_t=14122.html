Mounting class 10 weapons has absolutely nothing to do with your reputation, or the fact that the information is classified. All the class ten weapons (excluding the Nomad Blasters and Cannons) say that their information is classified, but that does not hinder your ability to use it. You just have to make sure you have open class 10 weapon hardpoints.
