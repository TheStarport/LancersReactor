warzog

Mon Jan 03, 2005 10:15 pm by warzog

1) To change the tradelane meshes, you could edit the texture, or remove it altogether.
IE:
In SolarArch.ini you'll find an entry:
[Solar
nickname = Trade_Lane_Ring
DA_archetype = solar\dockable\TLR_lod.3db
material_library = solar\Solar_mat_TLR.mat

Change it to this:
[Solar
nickname = Trade_Lane_Ring
DA_archetype = solar\dockable\TLR_lod.3db
;material_library = solar\Solar_mat_TLR.mat

Problem is, you ouldn't see the first one, to dock with it.

The Tradelane effects are in the:
Effects_Types.ini
Effects.ini
Gate_Tunnel.ini
My mod doesn't use tradelanes, so I'm unsure how to do what you want.

2) Some of the aforementioned files affect how cruise looks.
Constants.ini affects it's ACTUAL speed.
And the Engine_Equip.ini affects it, too.

3) Changing the size of the starfield could be done with "Model Tool," but I don't think it would have the affect you want.
I believe that the FL engine is set to stop drawing objects beyond a certain distance.


4) To resize a planet, use "ModelTool."
(However, Freelancer Explorer can do it, too!)
To retexture one, use "Milkshape."

Watch your 6!
