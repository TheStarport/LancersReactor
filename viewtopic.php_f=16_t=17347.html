Bases that can''t be damaged

I'm intrigued by the fact that most bases are impossible to damage; they're a permanent part of the game. Fair enough with major installations, but you'd think it would be possible to damage some of the small bases built into asteroids. As far as I can make out, you can't. You can sit there forever throwing torps at it, and all you will get is attacked by more defenders.

So presumably as a result, it's impossible to eliminate any of the factions; you could never wipe out the Red Hessians for example. It's not part of the story of the game. This is disappointing because it means you can change your own rating/rep/whatever, but you can't really change the way things are. Just a limitation of the game, I guess.

Rob

Tue May 20, 2003 11:50 am by redeye

You can mod the game to make anything you like destructable. There are certainly a few 'off the shelf' mods kicking around to make dockable battleships dockable.

However the problem is that this would still have no effect on the game. Using the Hessians as an example, you could quite happily wipe out all their bases and stations. Trouble is they'd still have an infinite armada of ships which would appear as if from nowhere around the wrecks of their ex-bases. Its back to the subspace ship/clone factories again....

Like Spiro says tho, this is a very hard issue to address. If you allow it in MP it would quickly lead to the depopulation of the whole universe - check out the clan armageddon going on on ELITE at the mo, imagine those guys could blow up bases. There would be nothing left within about a day. That leaves you with having it in SP - which could have worked but to make it have any significance the news/other factions would really have to notice your genocide which would have presented a major programming chore.

So back to the mods
