Well, normally you just type in a number similar to the ones in SDK for equipment or shipnames/info etc ant it shows you whats at that location. So, you just find a spot that isn't aleady used and save the entry.

There are resources that are at TLR that have the ids_name numbers for just about everything and if you haven't got it yet, you will need the latest SDK.

You might want to get hold of the blank dll thats available for download too and use that for the entries as it has space for both names and infocards.

Some of the entries in names_resources are linked to other dlls, so its best not to add to that one unless you need to. There's plenty of space there, but if you want to add things like new factions and have them stated properly with random missions its best to save the names resources places for them.

Another option is to get hold of the Arghs toolkit. Its got a bunch of useful stuff and shows how to add items to the game without using FLEd_IDS. You can use XML to generate the name and info instead.
