Michael Dan

Tue Mar 09, 2004 9:34 pm by Michael Dan

v.3.40 is going well, I tested MP several days ago and seems to be stable, even in EFA. I bought Niobium and other stuff from Earth and i wasn't kicked. Did a lot of RM missions as well, no problems. There is a bat file that allows you to change the density of encounters in the EFa and Edge worlds(Omicron Alpha and Gamma).
I'll try to put a version togheter after testing the SP campaign again.
Major changes: enemy AI reacts faster at higher levels, faction weapons have further increase their damage, NPC loadouts are now much better. Remember those Firesprays that didn't even scratch you even if they were at level 17? That bug is fixed(they all had lvl 1 BH guns), same problem was with the Outcasts high level setup. Most of the VHF's use now 8 weapons and a deadly combination of missiles: a class 4 normal or homing missile assisted by a class 3 EMP missile.
