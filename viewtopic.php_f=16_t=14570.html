Thw wreck of the fifth sleeper ship Hispania is in Omicron Alpha. You can even fly around inside of it and tractor in some scrap metal and other stuff. Beware though, this is an Outcast system and they are ALL flying decked out Sabre's. Make sure your at least neutral with them if you enter the system.
