I started something very similar on Browclops. The Clan Council meetings were fun, but others resented not being able to attend. I think that others should at least be able to attend the council meetings, if just to witness it and maybe have some input too.

The arguement against the Grand Council being Leaders only, is that the meetings are fun for the leaders, but of little value to others. In addition, open meetings allow for more input and make the members feel more like an important part of something. This could help get your clan members to be more involved and devoted to the cause of the alliance.

A way that you could set up a mass meeting is to go to a little used system, such as Edinberg. You could have the meeting on the system chat. Or you could do the debates on a forum.

That is just one side of it. There are advantages to exlusive attendance policies too.
