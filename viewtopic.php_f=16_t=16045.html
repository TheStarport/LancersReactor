I've just made a character to work as a pirate with Rogues, Outcasts, Junkers, Hackers, Xenos etc. The best way I found was to hang around in Liberty near the Colorado gate and attack every single trade convoy and police patrol that comes through. It is really easy here even in the starflier you start with. Doing this gets you on good terms with the Rogues and Junkers who have bases in this area. Then you can do jobs against the Police, Bounty hunters and Navy to make the Outcasts and such like you more.
This does mean that the police really hate you as do most of the corporations, but who needs them with such a range of pirate bases availiable.
