Midaus

Mon Mar 15, 2004 5:30 am by Midaus

You'll really have to work on using engine-kill to be able to put distance between you and the mob. I don't know how you execute your assassination missions, but it may not be best to go right after the guy your looking for unless you can keep him from running once you get his shields down. The target almost always flees towards his buddies once you've given him a good whack or two. If you engage the target NPC first that means all his buds from both locations will be protecting him once he bugs out. That is of course if the guy was at the first location to begin with. Sometimes you don't see him until you've taken a few of his cronies first.

Still your best bet is to get him soon as you see him and flee using engine kill and thrusters, while dodging like the dickens. You may even have to charge the pack a couple times. What I mean is, if they are too close to your rear that you can't realisticly get away, engine kill / spin 180 and charge/ zig zag/ right through them. Keep engine kill engaged. By the time they turn around to chase you you've put some distance on em.
