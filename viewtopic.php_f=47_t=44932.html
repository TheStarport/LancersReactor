Hi everyone, i am going to start a fanfic of my own. It will be about a freelancer named Mark Zellerman, and so now we begin!
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mark Zellerman (Mark) sat in the bar on Freeport 1, just after he helped the Zoners destroy the Red Hessian ships, who, out of greed, decided to attack any kind of base they see, even the bases of their allies, the LWB. They died quickly because Bowex (BorderWorld Exports), the Bounty Hunters, and some Zoner ships fought back. Mark found some Natter Zwei guns from the wreckage of the Hessian ships. They seemed interesting. He had them mounted on his ship (that replaced his Zoner "Fury" cannons). He talked to a Bounty Hunter in the bar, and he offered him a mission against Corsairs in the system. Mark wanted to try his new cannons so he accepted the mission.

Mark launched from Freeport 1 and went to the coordinates the Hunter gave him. Someone said to him over the comm channel,"Hey! Good to see you here. Help us destroy these scumbags!" Mark began shooting the Hessian laser cannons at the pirates. They did quite a bit of damage. He was having such a high time of shooting away at the pirates that he didn't realize how much damage his Hawk ship was taking. The computer on his ship said, "Shield failed!"
Mark quickly used some shield batteries and continued to blast away the pirates.

One of the pirates launched something at Mark that would destroy a weapons platform- a SunSlayer torpedo. However the pirates managed to fit that onto a light fighter was unknown. Luckily for Mark, he noticed some flaming ball or something flying at him. He used his thrusters and the torpedo missed him. He responded to that by launching a torpedo of his own. The starkiller torpedo hit its target perfectly and took out its shield. Mark finished the Corsair off by ripping it apart with his guns. The last pirate said,"Well, see you around, scum!" He dropped a mine and activated his cruise engines. Everything was going well for the pirate until he was hit with a cruise disruptor. The Bounty Hunter that shot it at him took the pleasure of shooting him with missiles. The ship exploded. All of the pirates were destroyed, but then, Mark's onboard computer yelled,"Leave area quickly! Extreme radiation detected!" Mark did not know where the radiation was coming from, until one of the Bounty Hunters screamed,"Holy s**t! Look at that toxic gas coming out of the dead ship!" Mark looked at it and was terrified. Some green gas was coming out of a dead Corsair fighter. It was eating away at everyone's hulls so they activated their engines and flew back to Freeport 1.

On the bar of Freeport 1, Mark could hear all of the Bounty Hunters talking about the incident. Mark had a headache, and that was for 2 reasons- the price of fixing his ship after the radiation, and the radiation affecting him. Suddenly he didn't feel too good. He hit his head on the table, and then he was unconsious.

Mark's eyes opened, and he realized that he was still at the table that he fell unconsious on. He saw that there was no one in the bar, and most of the lights were off. He decided to go to the ship dealer to buy a new ship, as he was tired of his Hawk fighter. The radiation took out half its hull in 15 seconds. At the ship dealer, he saw something very good- a Rheinland "Feuer" Light Fighter for 96,000 credits. The dealer showed him the infocard for the ship:

RN-325A-Z "Feuer" Rheinland Light Fighter

This ship is part of the Rheinland effort to provide high-quality ships made from good materials for a decent price, with the only drawback being that the ships are made from recycled materials and then refurbished. ALG Waste Disposal has created a chemical that makes recycled-material ships good as new by making it hardy and resilient to some damage. It is an excellent choice.

Hull Capacity- 5100
Guns/Turrets- 4/1
Optimal Weapon Class- 5
Maximum Weapon Class- 7
Nanobots/Batteries- 36/36

Mark smiled, as this ship was a lot better than his Hawk. He decided to sell the Hawk and buy this ship. He transferred the weapons and ship over to the dealer, and paid only 70,000 credits for the Feuer. He bought some strange-looking weapons... Electron Cannons and Proton Blasters. He had never heard of these weapons, as he was only familiar with plasma, laser, and tachyon weapons. The electron weapons had a very low refire rate, but they had a very long range and high damage. The proton weapons had a very high refire rate, twice that of laser and photon weapons, but low damage per hit. He decided to try another mission to test the weapons and ship.

"Freelancer Beta 5-2, this is Freeport 1, everything's ready for launch. Good luck out there." Mark launched from Freeport 1 and looked at the waypoints the Bounty Hunter at the bar gave to him. He was going to kill some Red Hessians.

5 Minutes later, he got to the waypoint coordinates... but there were no ships in the area. Mark tried to communicate with the Bounty Hunter, but there was no response (by Bounty Hunter I mean the one who gave Mark the job, who is still at the bar). Suddenly, a red light flashed before his eyes and the Red Hessians came out of nowhere! There were 5 or 6 of them, but they were only using Daggers, and it seemed they were using some weak proton weapons, as they did barely any damage to Mark's ship but fired very quickly.

Mark attacked one of them with his weapons, and the proton weapons were used first. They ate through his shields, and then the electron weapons blew off his wings and made holes in his hull. There was a hull breach, and then the Dagger exploded, leaving behind some ammunition and weapons. Mark tractored them in. He launched a Lancer missile at one of the Daggers and took out its shield. Then, something very unexpected happened..

Mark had always dreamed of flying a Sabre or some other awesome ship. It was amazing to be finally flying one. He checked the weapons system and saw that it had 6 Red Hessian proton cannons and a proton turret. He set course for the Verrazano jumpgate.

Mark set a waypoint to the Omega-9 jumphole, which was 31 kilometers away. He sunk back into his seat and waited. The area was unusually quiet. After all, there was a Nomad base in the area.

He pressed the "flash thaw" button on one of the compartments, and a light "click" could be heard. He took the bowl of paste out of the refrigerator and set it on the table next to Mark.
