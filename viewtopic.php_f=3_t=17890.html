Yogimus

Tue May 27, 2003 2:25 pm by Yogimus

If you play for a while, you can generally predict when the enemy is going to rush directly at you. (they usually stop to turn) Fire the missile then. The optimal range for missiles is over 500 meters for targets NOT going straight. Boost to 200 before firing to give the missile more speed. Using this method, I have been able to get torpedo kills on fighters consistently. Also, make sure you are out of the blast radius of torpedos.

Tue May 27, 2003 5:09 pm by GNDN

To aim, I wait until I target the 'plus' reticule and only within a range of 500. I only keep one slot devoted to missiles and I only use homing missiles. I try to use my missiles once my targets shields have been stripped away (all hull damage).

Granted, it costs me bucks to maintain missiles. To keep me in my habit, I usually do one or two jobs to supply myself. I do not use them on the in-between missions but they come in handy on the story missions.
