6. The technology gap is largely irrelevant. The Order is the culmination of the colonies, while the Monkeys are the evolution offshoot and the Robots are not even related. The technological power these factions should be exceptional to the colonies as they are in war and new technologies are always invented in war, and thus creating the circumstances for them to create weapons of their destruction. Sirius has an economy to worry about and many other internal distractions (one of the many reasons the Robots and Monkeys disdain them) that distract them from progress.

On a simple game play level the technological gap isn't as large as it appears. Shields and armour have only increased by around 25%, (Comparision between Sabre and Anubis Mk II, Titan has 18% less armour) although the light fighters are far more advanced than anything the colonies could match in class. The Mark I or Type A weapons all match power levels with the best weapons the colonies pirates have to offer. I do not see a gap, instead I see a progression that survival has simply forced upon those factions. I don't think making the Order the weaker of the factions is fair.
