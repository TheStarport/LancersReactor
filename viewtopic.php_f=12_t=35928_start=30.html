In the olden days, ff, a fob was attached to a watch or to keys, usually. It was a decorative device that also provided a kind of "handle" to grab pull out your watch or keys. Picture a pocket watch with an ornamental piece attached to it with a short chain. It was once meant to hang out of your waistcoat pocket. It could be a medal, a piece of embossed leather, embroidered silk, anything, really. I had a small swiss army knife on my key chain. Being on my key chain and not being a key, it was a fob.
