This is one of the tougher missions in the game. Despite having played FL for over two years now I never have flown the barracuda, and had best luck with this mission in the hawk. Don't rely on torps too much as they can get you killed if you spend too much time launching and not enough evading. Don't worry about the battleship, its not neccessary to kill it. If you want to go for the kill, fly straight toward it in cruise, then engine kill about 15-17 k away from it and launch torps like mad... they'll cover the distance with the extra speed and destroy it. When going for the shield generators themselves use the big shield to block you from some of the turrets, don't hover in one place but rather thrust around a lot. Dock quickly and enjoy the cutscene. Also, ignore the advice about flying in through the bottom of the asteroid. It may be cool, but its pointless.
