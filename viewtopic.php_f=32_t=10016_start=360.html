Glitch

Tue Dec 30, 2003 8:14 pm by Glitch

If you're trying to use the xmldata macros then you have to read the instructions CAREFULLY! They're not particularly clear in saying that the macro data (the stuff that's being inserted) needs to go directly under the very top of your script, as in the description that appears in FLMM. You also don't need to put the tag <pre><font size=1 face=Courier><xmldata> </font></pre> at the top since it is implied in the <pre><font size=1 face=Courier><xmldata name=X> </font></pre> tag.
