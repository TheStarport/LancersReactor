Glazed Bacon and Eggs with Fried Pancakes.

First get atleast two pans ready
Then once pans are ready,cook the bacon 6 for 10-15 minutes.
Halfway through,glaze the bacon with your favorite syrup.
Boil or fry some eggs.
Once eggs are done,peel add salt mustard.
Cook pancake batter with LOTS of oil on the pan.
TaDa! My favorite morning dish!
