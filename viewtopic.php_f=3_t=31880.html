House Advantages

I'm not sure if this has been brought up before, but I felt it would be an interesting topic.

People may wonder why a group of Rheinlander ships doesn't just waltz in and wipe out all of Liberty because they have really weak fighters compared to the rest of the Sirius Sector, but I believe that Liberty's strength lies in its capital ships. Bretonia has slightly more powerful fighters, but its battleships aren't that great. Kusari has even stronger fighters, but their capital ships are even weaker than Bretonia's. Rheinland has the most powerful House fighters/freighters, but their capital ships are the worst compared to the other Houses.

So in theory, Liberty could be compared to the early Galactic Empire of Star Wars. It had extremely powerful capital ships, but its fighters were weak and mass-produced.

Thoughts? Comments?
