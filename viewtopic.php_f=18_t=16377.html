>what exactly does the loadouts do and like the sme **** is in goods.ini what do
>they both do they confuse me a lil

Sub:

The goods.ini defines what can by on any generic ship, the loadout specifies exactly what is in a particular ship.

Think of it this way, the goods.ini (and the other *_good.ini) describe what is for sale at a grocery store. The loadout.ini is what is in a particular dish you are preparing.

The same generic ship type can be loaded out several different ways. For a good example of this, take a look at the various MSN loadouts for various ships (in loadout.ini).
