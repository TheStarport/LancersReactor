of the three lvl ten ships, there are the titan, saber, and eagle.

the titan has the most armor and a better power system, holds more nanos and sheild batteries. However it is the slowest turning ship of the 3. I've seen many titans get a wing blown off resulting in lost weapons.

saber, handles well, power system is same as eagle, low frontal profile (hard to hit during head on strafing runs), i've never lost a weap due to high damage.

eagle, same power system as the saber, big wingspan, easy to hit when it's banking or from above/below. it pretty maneruable.

I personally fly the saber, i have it on 3 different servers, i play alot of PVP dogfighting and like it better than the other 2. (fixin to buy another saber on a fourth server tonight after my humpback gets me about another 500k)

defaultuser

Sun Apr 27, 2003 7:24 am by defaultuser

My experience has been that the Sabre handles nearly the same as the Titan, and FAQs I have seen tend to agree.

The Sabre's one advantage is the usable turret. Normally, it's an additional hog in an already stressed power system. But, you get your obligatory 4 Nomad weapons ( the use no power ), you have a power surplus. You can actually afford the power budget to max out your remaining class 9 weapon slots, and the turret.

The other two fighters will have a one-gun disadvantage.
