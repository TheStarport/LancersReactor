Tawakalna Qubt-ut Allah

Mon Aug 21, 2006 4:03 pm by Tawakalna Qubt-ut Allah

FS SCP is wonderful, and it's pretty stable now too. the graphics mods make the fan-made campaigns look superb. I play FS SCP almost every evening. You can even play original FS with the SCP, and it looks and plays great. I'm not too keen on the Inferno core-based campaigns though, too much of a change away from classic FS in storyline, concept, and ship design. And I have a lot of trouble with the bugginess of the Inferno mod and the difficulty of the missions (although to be fair the guys do warn you that Inferno missions are hard)

I highly recommend any of the FS SCP campaigns by Blaise Russell, especially Derelict with it's wonderful voice acting, as distinct from the generic computer voices that are used in most of the SCP mods.

still waiting patiently (sorta) for Wing Commander: Saga and Battlestar Galactica: Beyond the Red Line, the total FS conversions.

You might also like the open-source Privateer which is almost completely faithful to original Privateer, but with vastly superior graphics.
