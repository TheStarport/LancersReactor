Returning to the Hood with Hovis

In TNG 2.56, Reynen has basically crippled Hovis so the race is easy to win. With the increase in cruise speed, winning the race was nearly impossible unless he did this.
Anyway, because of this, you need to be quite patient when returning to the Hood. You'll need to stay with Hovis on the return, often going quite slowly, and it takes a few minutes. You can't dock with the Hood until Hovis says you can, so don't try to cruise to it and dock, you won't be allowed. When you both finally reach the Hood, Hovis will give you the OK to land, and the game will continue. This workaround will most likely go into the manual, otherwise Reynen would have to have Hovis go at full speed, and you won't beat him unless you're an extreme ace flyer (face it, you won't beat him at increased cruise speed, ok?)

Edit: This has been fixed and changed in version 2.6.5, see my post below.
