Lord Emperor

Mon Jun 12, 2006 5:45 am by Lord Emperor

There are several kinds of bases:

Small Station- Stations that have a population of less than 1000. Small Stations produce 10 credits per turn and mine resources.

Large Stations- Stations that have a population greater than or equal to 1000. Large Stations produce 20 credits per turn and mine resources.

Capital Ship Shipyards- All stations that are called “Shipyards” are considered capital ship shipyards. (Tripoli is an exception. It is a large station.)

Planets- All dockable planets are considered planets.

Large Gas/Metal Miners- All dockable Gas or Metal Miners. There are only two: Naha and Ogashawa.
