If you have torps heres the trick I used:

flying anti clockwise round the whole shield strafe right constantly, whilst pointing ship to the left. You should glide around the shield in a large circle. Arm you torp launcher PERMANATLY. Fire at first shiled gen, afterburn to next, slow down, fire again. Repeat for first lap, firing sets of torps at all gens.
Un-arm the torp launcher, and repeat using only guns.
I had a Dragon, 2x Wakisahi's, 2x Scramblers, and 2x Tantos.
It helps to take out the Kusari Battleship first too. Torp run, gun run, fly past droping mines over the hull, jet out to about 1200 metres, turn, repeat, dead.
