"its a fair cop guv"....its like saying "ok ok fair enough, you got me"....can be used in several different contexts, the most common being the image of the "good natured burglar" (you know the type - black and white striped shirt, black mask, big black bag with "swag" written on it) being caught red-handed in someone's living room - he puts his hands up and says "its a fair cop guv".

However nowadays its used regularly as something you say when you are caught playing a joke on someone etc.

Breaking it down...

"a fair cop" - an undeniable arrest
"guv" - governer, slang associated with someone who holds a position of power, usually the "Chief Inspector" or the "Mob Boss".
