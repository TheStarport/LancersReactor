What ship are you flying? Look it is not supposed to make you loose all of your hair and sanity ok?!

Try this trick, when he says go hit THRUSTERS not cruise engines toward the first gate as you approach hit kill engine (Z) ,then hit your cruise engines (~) you will be ahead of him at this point and your ship will maintain it's speed (remember no atmosphere = no friction to slow you down). I assume you make it to about the halfway point and then have trouble. You need to swing out a little on the gates before the end to get a better angle for the finish line as they tend to tighten up a little down there. Look at your system map in the enlarged view it actually shows the course and how it is laid out.

I also tended to stay a little higher up in the gates as it gave me a kind of run down hill to maintain control and beat him.

I suppose now would not be a good time to tell you I beat him in a freighter a Dromedary to be exact!

If you absolutely can not beat this level there is a mod available on the mod site to by pass the race. Hope it helps good luck!

Lev Arris

Fri May 23, 2003 4:04 pm by Lev Arris

The race actually is one of the easiest missions of the whole game (i won it with the first try). So if you already have trouble here, you're really gonna hate some of the later missions.

In the race all you have to do is pressing SHIFT+W to engage the Cruise Engines and fly through the curves by staying as close as possible at the inner side of these curves, preferably while strafing into the direction your are going. Doing this you will overtake Hovis about halfway through the course and when he starts to cheat and fires rockets at you, you only have to release a bunch of countermeasures and cross the finishing line. Piece of cake, and i really don't get why so many people do have so much trouble with the race.
