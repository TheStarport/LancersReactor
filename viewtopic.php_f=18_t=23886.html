Wanted: - Systems designs, Ship designs, Faction help, Beta

the Epsilon Mod Team - Nova Design is looking for the following:

New ship designs:

Anything from light fighters, medium fighters, heavy and very heavy fighters, freighters, transports - through to capships

so if you have a design and would like to see it in a mod..... We would prefer original designs but will look at anything within reason
We have about 40 designs of our own but are interested in others idea's

Systems designs:

Designs that would suit for example - Bretonia ( though we have two systems completed), the Border worlds, Rheinland, and Independent as in primarily Bounty Hunters and Zoners.

We would like descriptions of the system including number of stars, bases, features like asteroid fields and hazards. Plus access points, jumpholes etc.....
If you have made a system that you don’t know what to do with, or even some named and set out sketches please apply. We don’t want sketches or completed systems as yet these can be provided at a later date.

We are looking for 10 - 12 systems for the above areas, but if you have a system for any of the following: Liberty, Bretonia, Rheinland, Kusari, Independent worlds, Edge worlds, Border worlds, or the Corsair system - please apply

Factional helpers:

If you are good with factions - new factions - not rehashes of old we would welcome your assistance. We have the factions documented but would like some help with the actual work on encounters etc.....

Beta testers: some volunteers would be appreciated, those that have already indicated they would like to test should be getting some material soon

you may ask why we are doing this.. the answer is simply time.. sure we could do it all ourselves (well most of it anyway) but would like to complete the mod fairly soon.

All contributors will be fully acknowledged and credited - the list is long already and we have found that the general TLR community is only too willing to help and you wont be forgotten.

Thanx,

Harrier
