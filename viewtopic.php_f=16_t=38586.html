Sinder

Sat Apr 09, 2005 3:24 pm by Sinder

After the single player campaign is completed, the player info screen no longer displays the net worth needed to reach the next level, but instead lists the amount of money you must earn to reach it.

Planet Toledo is a campaign-only base that cannot be reached once the campaign has been completed. There's better equipment to be found than what the Order supplies anyway, you just need to find it.

There are no more "storyline" elements to be found after you complete the campaign, but there are a number of systems that you haven't discovered. There are a few intruiging secrets waiting for you, and finding them is half the fun!
