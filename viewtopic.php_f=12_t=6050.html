Freelancer is not like Allegiance!

I have been playing Allegiance for three years now ... (multiplayer only, good flight model, great teamplay, sufficient complexity, Joystick control etc... and it really is free now! can be downloaded at Alleg.Net or you will find Links at Allegiance HQ)

However it is so different to FL that you can't compare these games. It has no persistent universe, no trading, you don't "own" your ship (You fly what your team can afford, and depend on the resources your team gets from mining and on the tech-development that the commander (human player) will choose)

There is no single player option (except for training missions) and the games last between 30 Minutes and 4hours (depending of the quality of the participating teams/ pilots and very often on the skills of the commanders (who actually get to play the game as a realtime-strategie game ordering "real" pilots around, building bases researching tech etc.)

For me there is no competion between the two games - they are coplementary!
(And both definitely worth playing for any spacefreak )
1. FL - the nice story, trading, nice graphics and atmo, the exploring getting new ships, mousecontrol etc. - and independent of yot internetconection! We all know the other pros of FL

2. Allegiance - teamplay (up to more than 30 pilots per team!), tactics, great Dogfighting (with diffrent types of fighters/interceptors/scouts) using your joystick, multicrewing (bombers with 1 pilot 2 Turretgunners and capships with up to 4 turretgunners), "real" missions from your comander like scouting (searching unknown sectors putting probes out), repairing other ships and bases("naniting" with a special repairgun/tool in your scout), rescuing pilots of your team who have been shot down (i.e. picking up their life(eject)-pods), guarding your teams miners, hunting the enemys miners (best done in special stealth fighters if your team has them), escorting a bomberrun (often 1 Bomber 3-5 repairscouts and Fighter escorts), defending sectors/ bases etc. ...

@Whoopie
if you have only played the practice missions you really haven't seen Alleg yet! (You will need to download the newest version of Alleg though as the M$ servers are of)

I guess everyone who played WC, Starlancer, Privateer and is into space-sims can have a good time with Alleg while waiting for FL (and he can keep his joystick from gathering dust)
