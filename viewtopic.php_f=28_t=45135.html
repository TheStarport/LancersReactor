Requiem: A Total FL Conversion

I am creating a new post for our mod as we have had a slight name change.

Welcome to the new total conversion mod for Freelancer called:

Requiem: A Total FL Conversion

This mod is a total rebuild of the game Freelancer. All systems have been removed and all systems will be new and original. Some will be based on actual stars in the real universe, and others will based on your favorite sci-fi movies or shows.

All factions will be new. Some will be based on your favaorite sci-fi shows and movies, and others will be original.

All ships will be original as will all weapons and commodities. As we speak, our programmers are working auto connection program that will download any updated files prior to joining the server. This will allow us to have a total dynamic universe where bases can be destroyed and the market price of commodites can change. Ownership of a system or base can also change. This will make an excellent RP server as there will be many ways to gain power for your faction (clan).

All planets will be unique and original as will bases.

Also, all ships will be created by our ship wright(s). Here is an example of what some of them look like:



TO keep up to date on the progress of this mod, or if you would like assist with ideas, coding, ship bulding, please visit us at: http://requiem.moddb.com

Work is progressing on this mod and a lot of work is yet to be done.

It has been decided that Requiem: A Freelancer Total Conversion will start off small with a limited number of factions and systems. As we have integrated a number of custom factions along with a number of Sci-Fi species and races, we will be allowing a clan who wishes to take over a faction the chance to create it's own history and story. This will allow players to re-write a Sci-Fi races' story.

This also means, that when you start, you will be limited to where you can go and find. It will take time to locate new areas of space and to interact with other races and factions. This will depend on your clan or factions RP.

We are about ready to release our pre-alpha testing version for our mod team to test. During this test, we will also begin testing of our dynamic universe program. This program will allow us to have the universe around the players change on a daily basis based upon the activities of the server and forum.

So, please keep an visit our site for further news and development updates on our truly unique mod.
