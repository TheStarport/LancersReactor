JOLT freelancer Reloaded

After a bit of a wait, now presenting JOLT Freelancer’s FL-Reloaded MOD
This is a Public Beta Mod, and at this time should be considered a work in progress.

This MOD should be considered an “Elite” version of vanilla, and should be played by people looking for a challenge rather than having everything handed to them on a plate in the 1st hour.

The MOD includes,

· New ships
· New factions
· New weapons
· New commodities
· Rebalanced combat dynamics
· Rebalanced rep & bribe system
· Rep sensitive trading
· Dedicated event system
· Starting funds 100,000 credits

The MOD itself is our vision to add to the scope and difficulty of traditional vanilla Freelancer, and yet add new depth to the Multi-Player experience by addressing those little niggles in gameplay that shorten the length of this otherwise fine game. By adding House VHF’s and a range of competitive weapons we hope to make the Role-play aspect offered by the JOLT Freelancer server more immersive.

Please feel free to add feedback on this mod and aid in the development process.

A big thanks to all who helped in the development of the MOD to this stage, and to the JOLT Freelancer community as a whole for supporting our project.

We look forward to seeing you in game.
