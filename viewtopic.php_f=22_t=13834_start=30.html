Just to give you an idea

65msec of player lag = some lag for players, but mostly unnoticble
100msec of player lag = noticable lag for most players (yellow or red connection flashes)

1 Normal player of Rank 1 - 30 = .5 - 1.0 msec player lag
1 Normal player of Rank 31 - 36 = 1.0 - 1.5 msec player lag
1 Cheater = 4 - 10msec of player lag

So for every one cheater you loose about 4 - 10 real players. Also if we get 5 or more cheaters into the game, lag hits at about 30 - 40 players vs being smooth at the full 50.
