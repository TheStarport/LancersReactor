Ultrasound

I'm overjoyed right now and therefore I just felt like sharing this with you, so bear with me.....

Me and my wife had our first ultrasound yesterday Everything is fine and perfectly on schedule. 10 weeks and 3 days old, 33.4 mm large (shrimp size ) and growing....
We were looking at the screen and it was soooo beautiful to watch, we just couldn't say anything for a few minutes. After a while the lady (who performed the ultrasound) said "Look, he's moving ! Look at him go !" He he, the little muggle

It's such a beautiful and yet mysterious process the little one has to undertake. First the fight between a few hundred million sperm cells and after conception the beginning with just a few cells which multiply in an incredible fast way to thousands or even millions of cells. It's fair enough to say that we are actually all a bunch of winners after winning the race from a few hundred million competitors (talking about murdering competition ) Even more fascinating is that all those cells know exactly where to go....some form the heart, others form the kidneys, eyes, hands, feet, everything. As we speak, my child (well, foetus to be exact ) has already have all its organs, though only a little over 10 weeks old Right now its beginning to grow a consciousness (further development of the brains) and is refining the organs it already has.

Well, sorry about going on for this long, but it's just a great experience ! Bargib, Ug and some others here know exactly what I'm talking about.
