chantdeguerre

Sat Mar 27, 2004 3:31 pm by chantdeguerre

If you plan to make your mod multiplayer compatible I'd watch out for destructible solars. If you blow up a base and someone is on it (and possibly isn't even logged into the server at the moment, but had last saved there) it will crash the server. There have been some more indepth posts about this, and why it happens. I don't think there is a currently known way to make it either just kill the player(s) on the base and respawn them to another base (next closest, last saved etc), and from my own experience looking through the game files, I haven't seen anything that could help with this problem.

But if it's single player only it might work out (as long as you don't destroy a base that the story missions require).
