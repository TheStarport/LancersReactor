My new computer!

After months of planning and indecision, my new laptop has finally arrived... and oh is it nice. The specs:

HP Laptop... 15.4" widescreen

Intel Core 2 Duo @ 2 ghz
2 GB DDR2 RAM
120 GB Hard-drive
256 mb GeForce Go 7400 video card

And of course, integrated wireless, bluetooth etc...

And Windoze XP Pro... none of this Vista garbage.

Its quite fascinating to watch how well the workload is divided up between the cores, and multitasking seems to have no limit as of yet, save for the perilously small battery life (2 hours with wireless on). Contrary to Taw's predictions, it runs BF 1942, and other games, even Darkstar One without any problems. Granted DSO can't be cranked up to full power... but even most desktops can't handle that just yet. Nice this is though, DSO appears to run on both cores simultaneously, though I'm not sure if this gives a huge performance boost or not. Off to save the universe again!
