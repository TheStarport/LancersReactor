With this game, you must be enemies with someone.
The key is to pick who. I recommend go to Colorado
and kill Xenos to get Silver & good reps with just
about everyone EXCEPT the Zoners. Watch the Zoner
rep carefully. Stop when they are going negative.
Then go to Dublin and kill Mollys & get Gold.
Try to avoid Corsairs, you kill Mollys to get
them neutral. Then try being a Freighter Captain.
