You'r second question, the groups are so inter-related that eventually your rep carries over (kill republican shipping in Manhatten, and the Rheinland military will eventually want to have a little talk with you), so keep that in mind text time you're out for a little wonton destruction in one house and want to flee to another. And for your third question, you have the right idea but be careful. Police will help you mop up some criminals, but will then turn on you if you're still red with them, so you may be hated by both sides for a while (which equals no landing sites or saving). Easiest way is to find a bribe in a neutral base (zoners or junkers), but the most fun is killing xenos (hated by all). And look for groups that your favorite criminal element and house both hate (like rheinland and the Outcasts both hating the GMG), that way you get to kill a few ships, make a few credits, and make everybody happy (minus you victims, of coarse). --- VH16
