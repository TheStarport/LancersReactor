It sounds like a good idea, but it probably wouldn't work. People flame in reaction to what others say, so they flame right then and there. In addition, there would be a huge debate and flame war over whether something should go in the flame forum or not. Debates between two civilized users would probably be labeled as flaming and flamed for not putting it in the flame forum.

EDIT: Removed example

A flaming forum could work if the posters on this site were mature enough, but few are. If everyone worked constructively to help out, then the flaming could be essentially isolated to the flame forum.

In addition, I highly doubt that the admins would create something like that. It would appear that they were encouraging flaming. Flaming is one of the things that the admins are supposed to minimize, so making a home for it seems to conflict with their goals. But one could argue that it is impossible to eliminate, so why not regulate it by keeping it isolated.
