Use the F8 key. Checkout your status with each group. A group has to be 4 boxes and a little bit from the at most red to be neutral. (4 1/8 clear boxes on the left)

Click on the people in the bars to see if they can bribe each group to like you. Also avoid fighting anyone who is red if you can. I think in a Titan Junkers could shot at you all day and do nothing.

It is possible to get everyone neutral but this won't stop people from wanting your cargo.

Also in Multi play I noticed you have to land somewhere in that same system to make people your neutral or even on good terms with reset.
