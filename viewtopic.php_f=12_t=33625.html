Two things: Fog table emulation and gamma correction.
1. Gamma correction. Enter options in Freelancer and under video options there is a gamma correction slider. Usualy it's set to 0.30, try increasing it slowly, at about 0.4 the picture should return to normal
2. Fog table emulation. This is an old characteristic of the GF2 cards, it may be automatically enabled in the newest drivers (ForceWare), but try searching for it under display properties, advanced settings -> performance & quality settings.


Careful what you wish... You just might get it.
