Good Trading Route

If anyone's interested, I reckon I've found a good trading route. Trade Boron from Pittsburgh, New York to LD14, Leeds and then Berylium from Stoke Station, Leeds to Newark Station, New York. Takes quite a while, but if you've got a decent freighter (Clydesdale or bigger) you make upwards of 100,000 each way.

the routes i use are Engine Components from Honshu to Freeport 4 then Luxury Consumer Goods from Planet LA to Planet Baden Baden then Diamonds from Solarius Station (Omega-11) back to Planet New Tokyo via Sigma-13.
