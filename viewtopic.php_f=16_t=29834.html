Arania

Mon Mar 29, 2004 11:17 pm by Arania

Load yourself up a dragon with 4 blood dragon lasers, 2 missiles and a torp launcher, oh and a mine and countermeasure.

As soon as you have finished off the first attack patrol, fly to the bottom of the rock and fly up through the tube, target each of the crystal generators and loose four torpedoes at each of them. Target the one closest to the battleship and launch two extra torps, it sould have been destroyed by now, if not, launch a few missiles and blow it up.
The other three generators are relativley easy, launch 2 missile pairs, take an afterburner run at the gen, and fly around it, shooting it with your lasers as many times as possible, if the gen is offscreen when your circling then dump a mine to boost the damage on the generator.
Repeat for all three generators.
When the shield goes down, afterburn to the top of the arch as get a fix on the docking port, once you have it sighted, fly towards it and dock when ur about 400M away.


After you get off the arch, you come under attack by 8 Dragons and 2 Rhienland gunships, this bit is fairly easy, destroy the gunships by taking a mine run first, and then swinging around and pounding the @$$ out of them from behind with ur lasers, finish what fighters there are left (Juni and some blood dragons have turned up and will make mincemeat of the kusari forces pretty quickly) with whatever tactics suit you. Once the last fighter is dispatched, you can relax, just one hop back to chugoku and land on kyoto, no more fighting, for now.
