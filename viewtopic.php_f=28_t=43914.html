Minor Earth Major Anarchy

Greetings Freelancer Community,

Myself and a small team will soon be undergoing work, to try and achieve a Total Conversion for freelancer.
This, however, will not be a conversion to anything specifically, more like a complete change of everything from vanilla freelancer, into something completely different.
Anything from homeworld 2, Starcraft to Red Dwarf will in some way be included.
Current estimations point to around 200+ systems, 140+ ships, 200+ weapons + armor upgrades, power upgrades, shield upgrades, and buyable powerplants.
Completely new factions, complete with new story and even a new sector (we no longer play in Sirius )around 80+ factions, split into 8 categorys of faction, for easier choices later on in life (youll see when the mod is released
Factions can take over bases and systems from other players, through special wars.

Currently we are designing the mod and getting straight exaclty what we want to be added into the mod. We do have a forum for anyone who is interested in helping with the project: http://z13.invisionfree.com/Minor_Earth ... hp?act=idx

Not much on there, but more will be added very soon.

We are also on the look out for any texturers interested in working with us, as this is wha we are lacking at the moment. Please add the hotmail (or email): CaptainDelahue@msn.com, for more details on the mod and ideas, or if you just want to have a nice firendly chat

I also have one question for the community, if we were to put together an early demo beta release, with all ships in, but without textures, would this be ok for a beta demo? Or would everyone frown upon it? Obviously they would all be textured in the public release.

Thanks all and i hope this project keeps people interested
