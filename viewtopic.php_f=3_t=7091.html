Awesomeness

I know this is old news for most, but today I went in to my (one of many) local Gamestop to preorder Freelancer, and they gave me the marketing beta. I was kinda hoping they would, but didn't count on it since it had been so long since they did that. The guy told me they weren't supposed to be giving them out (so it wasn't just because I preordered), but he said that they had the original three they had gotten that had just been setting in a drawer because no one there wanted them (most of the Gamestops I've seen have been going console only). I was a little shocked, but happy none-the-less. Needless to say, I gave a hearty "Hell yeah!" Too bad it's only a week from release. At least I've got something to tide me over until then.
