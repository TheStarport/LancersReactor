Kendo,

An addition to what parabolix said.

The chaingun is designed to have a very high refire rate similar to a real chain or mingun - somewhere around 6,000 rounds per minute, but use ammo like crazy, so can only be fired using short bursts - that and the heat buildup are the limitations.

This version is very similar to that, but instead of ammo, which as parabolix says, would mean an increase in missile numbers, ours uses energy like crazy, so you cant just blast away and expect to hit summing.... the idea is - using your piloting skills, line up the target, and whoomp... goodbye......

There are also more 'special' weapons on the way, like the Chaingun - 'brand new' models created in doga and milkshape - and then added to the game as a new entry, not a rehash of an existing weapon model with upped damage ratios........

btw this is not a flame ..... I just wanted to add my 2 cents worth - lol
