Sat Aug 02, 2003 11:26 pm by Sherlog

In a rating like '3/5' the 3 is the so-called optimum weapon class for your ship and the 5 is the maximum weapon class. The latter is the highest class of weapons that can be equipped on your ship, though not necessarily on all hardpoints - some slots may have a lower rating.

The 'optimum weapon class' is probably supposed to be a crude measure of the strength of your powerplant but this can be pretty misleading, especially as some weapons do not drain power at all and even among the different energy weapons the efficiency varies by a factor of more than 2 : 1.

Have a look at the thread Truth about the Optimal Weapon Class for some information about this, including precise power ratings for all pilotable ships.
