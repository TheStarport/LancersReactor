AFAIK there's no such option. However, you could try to slim down your Freelancer installation by deleting the intros, the music and all the single player campaign stuff, then copy it onto a ramdrive (it's like a harddisk, but completely stored in your computers RAM) using NTFS as file system in combination with drive compression and with a size slightly larger than what is left of the Freelancer installation and run it from there.

There are free ramdrive tools. For example, check the Downloads > Rocketdivision Downloads / StarPort-StarWind > Starport section of this link.
