Depends a lot on what cargo ship you are using. I found the Drone agile enough to "dog" fight when I wanted to and I also used the turret view and just pounded anthing that came into range.

If you are using the Humpback...well that's a different story. That thing really does drive like a asteriod draging a ball and chain. Until I got top of line weapons for it I died constantly. The only thing you can do is go to turret view and concentrate on targets at you're rear who have the best shot at you. Also don't forget to strafe left/right. It enables you to avoid a lot of incomming fire when you can't actually manuver.

Ether Dragon

Thu Mar 20, 2003 11:59 pm by Ether Dragon

Don't engage, especially with a Humpback. Run away. Freighters aren't meant for combat, they're meant for hauling cargo. If you get ambushed, do your best to get back on the trade lane and escape. I usually tap "goto" after targeting the disrupted gate, then shoot at any enemy via turret mode. The moment the gate becomes operational again, I tap "dock."

If you're using any other freighter, you'll be maneuverable enough that you can try to dodge for the time it takes for the gate to come back online.
