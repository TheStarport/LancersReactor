Help save Thief 3

Seeing as this is a PC gamers' forum, I reckon most of the self-respecting gamers will at least know of, if not love the "Thief" series of games. Unfortunately the company making the new Thief 3 game, Looking Glass, has been sold to Ion Storm, the same people who took Deus Ex (another wonderful RPG cum sneak-em up cum FPS type game) and dumbed it down for the X-box market. Although Deus Ex: Invisble War is still a fairly good game, chief elements of the original were removed to make it easier to understand and simpler to control with only a few buttons for the console market. A lot of the depth that made Deus Ex such an incredible game has been removed. Ion Storm has now indicated their intent to make Thief 3 available on the X-box. If the progression of Deus Ex is anything to go by, this could be a disaster for all PC gamers who want a more cerebral sneaky type of game. If anything else, the Xbox gamers have MGS2 for their stealth gaming and will probably ignore Thief 3, unaware of how great the last 2 games were. Please sign this petition and try to help save Thief 3 from being another X-box game cheaply ported to the PC
