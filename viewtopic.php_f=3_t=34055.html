You'll get to it during the campaign, at which point it will be easy.

Afterwards, the easiest way is to keep watching the jump gate while going through the Norfolk -> Texas Gate lane. When you're directly in "front" of it, exit the lane and head towards it. Look around some and you'll see the opening.
