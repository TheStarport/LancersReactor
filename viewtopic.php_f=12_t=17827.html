1x read speed for a CD-ROM is 150 kB/s so:
7800 kB/s IS 52x
1050 kB/s = 7x for audio extraction
600 kB/s = 4x for CDFS (file register)
Read Method = 1 -- Reads data in streams of 25 blocks with 2 block synchronisation.
Overall verdict -- Mid-class quality, medium reliability.
