1) The Kraken Type 2 particle guns do significantly more damage than the Wyrm Type 2 tachyon guns, and they do it with better efficiency.

2) The Wyrm Type 2 and Kraken Type 2 have a projectile speed of 700m/s, while all c10 guns, including Nomad guns, have a projectile speed of 600m/s.

While you are matching refire rate with the Wyrm Type 2 and Nomad Cannons, you are not matching projectile speeds, and the targetting system will be thrown off. The effect of mismatched projectile speeds is that head-on shots will usually all hit the target, but angled shots (more to the side) will often result in the slower projectiles missing the target; and your slower projectiles, the c10 weapons, do the big damage! This is why Salamanca Mk II neutron guns, with a projectile speed of 600m/s, are a more effective choice of c9 guns to pair with c10 guns on a VHF, even though they don't pump out damage as fast as some of the others.
