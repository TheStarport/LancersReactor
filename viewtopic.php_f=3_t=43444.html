Chips

Mon May 01, 2006 12:53 pm by Chips

PLAY THE ORIGINAL GAME!!!

I never know why on earth people buy games and then try the mods out before finding out how awesome the game is, and how badly most mods will mangle it.

If you get bored of the original game after playing the storyline and trying out the multiplayer aspect of it as well, then by all means try some mods. But to dive right into mods will cripple the games enjoyment for you imo. The game is superb without mods, and doesn't need any to run correctly on 99% of systems.

Once bored of the original game, try some mods as it will add some variety to the game, some neat features and fun things - but it's a massive mistake to play a mod without enjoying the game. Mods, no matter how well balanced, aren't anywhere near as good as the original game, and more often stuff up most of the aspects in the game by trying to make "wow" factors.
