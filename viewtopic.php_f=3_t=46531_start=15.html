Rankor

Sat Oct 13, 2007 9:03 am by Rankor

Actually, the artifact itself is a "Crystaline" form. This ship is so
densely covered with it, that this technique of textuing is what gave
the ship it's glittering appearance.
Metallic textures in my way of thinking here would be too
"colonial" and therefore "primitive" for this one. So this is why
I developed the "crystal" texture for it.
I got the "glitter" appearance by mapping the texture onto the surface, then scaling it way off the chart. (like 1000:1)
