Freelancer/Privateer.

Privateer rewarded you with better ships through story driven missions. It got you hooked and I, when I was younger, played it literally for hundreds of hours.

Yet with Freelancer, I played through the story, levelled up to 38, have 10 million creds in the bank, a decked out Dromedary (cause, well, I like the cargo room and I'm not jiggy with the Rheinlanders' boat like turning of the Humpback) and have cruised the entire universe from end to end. Indeed, everybody DOES know my name in that universe.

Even MP is a little boring after some invested time because you're right back to being uber powerful with nothing to do.

Yes, I have tried the Sabre/Titan/Eagle...yes I have the level 10 shields and weapons in my hold collecting dust.

But why bother playing if there's no CHALLENGE.

The problem that Freelancer has is that it lacks challenge where Privateer made you sweat to wonder if you were going to be able to make it out of this mission alive (whether script or random).

Freelancer is the START of a great game...but needs more community driven content as well as developer driven content.
