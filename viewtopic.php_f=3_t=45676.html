flybyu

Sun Apr 08, 2007 5:23 pm by flybyu

If you get Freelancer Misc. Data Base.xls and look at the weapons in code you will see something like this

fc_c_turret02_mark01 that is a Corsair gun fc_c
gd_bh_gun02_mark05 that is a BountyHunter gun gd_bh

Now these guns will be sold most likely on corsair and Bountyhunter bases.

The exception is
co_turret02_mark02 that is a Civilian gun it is sold on almost every base in the game.

Just look at the code and you will find out which bases to look at to find these guns.
