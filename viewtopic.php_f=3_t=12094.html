The Defender's the best of the best of Liberty ships. You might be able to get a different one (such as a Rouge or Junker ship) by changing alliances, but that's a lot of trouble. It's also not necessary - the Defender's quite a ship.

Make sure you outfit her right. For example, you said you have two shield-only weapons, which in my experience (not criticizing by the way - just played around with this a bit so giving my two cents) is a waste. One gets shields down plenty fast, and you can actually get regular weapons in Liberty that make them pretty much useless (since they wear down shields very fast regularly). Also, I'm not sure what TYPE of shields your using, but that does make a difference. See what weapons the enemy is using and then get a shield that defends best against those type of weapons. Also make sure you bring along a full hold of shield energy and nanobot packs.

Good luck!

defaultuser

Sun Mar 30, 2003 7:31 pm by defaultuser

Avoid Lavablade weapons like the plague. They're really only useful against Libery Rouges, because they're the only folks in Liberty space dumb enough to fly slow, ungaingly craft.

The Xenos fly Starfliers, the Oucasts fly Daggers, etc. You need weapons that travel FAST, especially since you're not very experienced at tactics and understanding enemy maneuvers. The Lavablades are %33 slower than lasers, and you can feel it.

Also, avoid the shield-killer weapons. Even the crappiest weapons in the game have little problem whittling down shields, it's the armor that takes forever.

My suggestion: until you get good pirate weapons, like the Drail and the Scorpion, you have to live with multiple crummy weapons. Also, until you get out of Liberty Space, your best shields will be Class 2. Armor is going to be a last resort - I mean, come on folks, when was the last time having a few hundred more armor actually saved your hide? You're either dead meat, or your shields take most of the damage.

Make your target the Patriot Light Fighter. I know everyone hoots about the Defender, but honestly, you don't have the powerful guns available to make it worthwhile ( unless you pick up a few Drails or Scorpions, as mentioned above ).

The Patriot can handle 4 Adv Starbeam guns without straining the batteries ( they're more efficient than the Justice series ), and that's far more firepower than the Starflier you start out with. Save the Defender until you get those Drails and Scorpions.
