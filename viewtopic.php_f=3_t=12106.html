Altough I really like your ideas, I think a few of them are beyond the scope of a patch. What I think would be feasible though are the following:
I feel that TOO many of the advanced ships cannot be accessed until after the plot is over. While I feel the sabre, titan, eagle should be left for after the plot, you do run accross bases that sell the stiletto on more than one occassion in the main game itself and it is frustrating not to be able to buy it. I would like to see the requirement for purchasing the stiletto lowered to level 17 so that I can purchase it before the whole stretch of missions at the end of the game.
Next, I think it would be bad if you could destroy battleships that are dockable bases in the game you could end up with a multiplayer server with no bases...it would suck. I think those should remain invulnerable. However, I feel that some other battleships should be added that aren't dockable and that are around to find in random encounters and in missions...ie: just cruising around like they were in Privateer one. They should be undockable, but vulnerable to attack just like installations in the random missions: u can't dock to them but you can destroy them.
I think both of those ideas should be easily feasible as the models already exist and simply need to be edited slightly. (Random no**d battleships would be nice too)
Thirdly, escort and cargo random missions should be created, and unlike the current random missions, some should require that you leave the system.
Fourthly, in accordance with changing the level of the stiletto, in the single player campaign, I think the difficulty of all the systems should increase with your level. That way you could return to liberty in a sabre and still be challenged while a level 1 ship would not be.(This was implemented in Privateer 2)
Finally, if it is possible, the really unique and artistic stations seen in the E32001 trailer should be replaced. They were awesome. I understand this might not be possible, but at least the first two suggestions would make the game more fun.
Last but not least..THANKS TO THE DEVELOPERS FOR ALL THEIR HARD WORK>>YOU GIVEN US A GREAT GAME!
