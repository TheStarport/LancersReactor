In two of the wrecks around the "Unknown Jump Hole" in Omicron Alpha. This jumphole is near the Theta and Beta holes, and is easily found by looking for a rather large ring of shipwrecks. Pinger

Sat Mar 06, 2004 3:20 am by Pinger

ok the definative guide to get the wrecks in alpha

1. get to the beta jump hole @ F5 in alpha after getting the unknown jump hole @ F4 on your map and the wrecks around it.
2. enable the Station/Solar listing for the bottom left hud section
3. find the first wreck listed, then double click it
4. press your target next key, default is T until you find the one with the Cerberus, usually the 5th ship in the list, sometimes more depending which side of the hole your on.
5. fly to it and loot it while keeping it in ship scan.
6. then quickly press T until you find the next ship, fly to it and repeat for the rest, there are 4 in total not 2
7. on the 3rd wreck watch for nomads as they usually spawn about then but sometimes earlier.
