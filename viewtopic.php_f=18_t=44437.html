Thu Aug 17, 2006 7:09 pm by bakedpotato

The Marsflyer is a one of a kind ship, that's for sure.
It was made by some of the best in the business. Harrier, Glock, Crabtree and myself.
Part of it is scripted but it has some custom files such as the sound the ship makes, custom weapons, custom manoverability, and a few other things.
So if you really want to add the Marsflyer you would have to add the necessary files including the cmp, mat, sur, marfly.ini add the custom lines from the beam_effects.ini and effects.ini engines, weapons, and on and on.
In other words you have to know what you're doing and it would take a lot of time even for someone that does know what they are doing.

You would be better off just using the Marsflyer mod in normal freelancer without any other mods.
You could ask SWAT_OP8R if he could include the Marsflyer in the next version of the Crossfire mod but then SWAT_OP8R would have to get permission from Harrier because he made the Freelancer model of the ship. All the other stuff you really don't need permission to use because I asked Glock and Crabtree to do what they did and I'm sure they wouldn't mind anyone using their work because it was done because I asked them to and I don't mind at all.
