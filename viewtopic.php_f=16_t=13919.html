Actually, I beg to differ. You would die FASTER to a complete newbie ship if you were using that shield than if you were using Adv. Brigandine. Ever wonder why computer ships don't regenerate their shields? It's because they have a regen rate of 1. Basically, damage done to the shield is permanent, unless you use a battery. Take a newbie for example. They have two justice 1's doing about 10 damage per shot and firing 8.33 shots per second would knock out the Adv Champ in 66.02 seconds - a little over 1 minute. With the recharge rate of an Adv. Brigandie shield it is technically impossible for a single newbie Starflier to knock out your shield. You just regenerate too fast for them to keep up. Now, compare 66 seconds to knockout to INFINITE shield life....I think I'll stick with my regenerating shields, thank you very much.

If you were going against stronger enemies, though, then that shield might be useful. Against more powerful enemies, they can knock an Adv Brigandine shield in, say, 5 seconds. At that speed, the regen rate simply doesn't have enough time to make a difference. In that situation, the Adv. Champ would buy you about 1-2 seconds extra shield lifetime, but you're still SOL when it finally dies, which it WILL do. At least with an Adv Brig, you can run in circles or something so they don't hit you much while you wait for your shield to charge a bit.
