kaegogi

Wed Apr 14, 2004 2:31 pm by kaegogi

Yes. In your equipment folder there's an ini called misc_equip. This ini contains the power plants for your ships. Each power plant has capacity and charge rates that tell you how much power is available for weapons and for thrusters. You can find out what type power plant you have by looking at your ship's loadout in the ships folder. I believe the eagle is ge_elite2 or ge_fighter5 or 6. Can't remember which.
