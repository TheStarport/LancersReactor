I had this problem, too, and I've found the solution. It has something to do with your account files, located in My Documents\My Games\Freelancer. When I moved that directory to another location, the game started up just fine. I haven't yet tried to figure out which particular files in there cause the problem -- it may, for instance, be the autosave, if for instance the autosave happens in a mod and you then deactivate that mod, or it may be something else -- but it's in those files somewhere.
