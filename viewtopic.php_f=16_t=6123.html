a little FreeLancer Encyclopedia:
Cruise engine: Fast Drive system on your ship that allows speeds up to 3 times as fast as the normal engine, but no weapons use.
TradeLane: Innersystem "Highway" between planets, very fast, but confined to a specific route.
Jump Gate: Artificially built Gate to allow traffic to another system
Jump Hole: Natural phenomenon (usually somewhere in a nebula, gas cloud or asteriod field) that allows traffic to another system.
