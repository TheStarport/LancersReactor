Good Trade Route. But other problems.

Hi there,

I don't want to be a spoiler here but I want to share some of my experience and mebbe get some feedback from others. (no flames pls!)

I find the game universe to be really huge !!! I found the missions tough going & after a while I got bored and started to explore. I have visited places like new tokyo, honshu, shinjiku etc ... the ships for sale there are really cool looking and deadly but alas I couldn't buy them even when I had something like 200,000 credits because didn't progress well into the missions enough to advance to other levels.

In the beginning of the game, money was a big problem !! The missions available were tough going and the pay sucks. But as you advance to other systems pay gets better but missions get tougher and tougher. For example in the tau31 system the average pay for a mission would be abt 8-9000 credits but the cost of replacing expended missles/mines to complete the mission would be abt 7000 credits and sometimes you even make a loss.

I find that on other systems, having 4 guns on you ships is really inadequate to fight on missions as the enemy fighters are really agile, fast and really numerous (8-9 enemy ships). I find that a good agile ship would be the Pirana bounty hunter ship which can be picked up quite cheaply on Planet Leeds.

Loadout on Pirana
=================
3 different missle launchers (150 missles)
1 fast laser cannon (like the scorpion from fighting XENO)
Advance Chaff flares (from tau31 system)
1 drone seeker launcher(for escaping from hordes of enemy)
shield batt/nano


I started looking for profitable trading routes in the bretonia system and found 1 so far.

Pharmaceuticals and Water connection.
===============================
From the Glasglow outpost in the leeds to the holmer outpost in the tau31 system is just 2 jumps.
I found this to be very good trading route. I didn't get jumped by outcast very often mebbe only once every 4-6 trips. So save the game every time you go out.

I would get a chesdale freighter and load up 125 units Pharmaceuticals in glasgow @ $255/unit. Then jump to holmer outpost and unload @ $400/unit
Damage = $31875
Sale = $50,000
Profit = $18,125

Then from homer outpost I would buy water for $30/unit and sell at glasgow for $75/unit.
Damage = $3750
Sale = $9375
Profit = $5625

Total Profit for return trip via glasgow-homer-glasgow = $23,750

I did this for about 10-15 times.


But after that I went back to the missions and got stuck with Racing Hovis in the Dublin system on the Hood Battleship.

I need info or a patch or hack on how to bypass this mission. Anybody can help ??
