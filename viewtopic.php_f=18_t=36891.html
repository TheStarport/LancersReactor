@FlyBy
You need to look in Data/Solar/solararch.ini. Find the archetype of the station you want to change (e.g. largestation1 is the model for Newark etc.), and scroll down to the "docking_sphere = " lines.

The best way to do this would be to look through the Rebalance version of the file and see what they have changed - especially since I don't have any files with me at the moment - but I'm pretty sure you need to change "docking_sphere = moor" to "docking_sphere = jump". This will prevent you needing to enter the station to dock. Also, change the number after this to increase the distance you can dock from.-Accu
