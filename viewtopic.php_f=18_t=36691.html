You can make thrusters drain power and not regenerate it back buy just going to DATA\EQUIPMENT\misc_equip.ini and changing this on the powerplants (all of them, use notepads replace to make it quick):

thrust_charge_rate = 1000

to:
thrust_charge_rate = 0

You may also want to boost this up so that thrusters last longer:

thrust_capacity = 1000

maybe this:
thrust_capacity = 20000

When you dock at a base, this will regenerate, like your shields do.
