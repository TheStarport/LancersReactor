i think you will find that the gun mounts on all fighters go in this order

MONUTS 1 & 2: Outer most (usually wing tips)
MOUNTS 3 & 4: Middle points (mid wing)
MOUNTS 5 & 6: Inner most (cockpit sides)

at least that is how they are done for the Titan. of course an odd number of mounts usually has the last mount along the centerline of the ship.

hope that helps. would appreciate anyone telling me if i am wrong but i think is applies to all types of fighters (no idea about freighters i never fly them)
