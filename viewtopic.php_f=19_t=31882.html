A Warning to New & Old comming back to FL

I have found these over the last year Running/Admining a server, these issues can possibly get your Account Banned or Corrupt One of your characters, which Cannot be saved only Deleted.

1. Lag and Buying/Selling anything in FL online

When buying any multiple items online - use the slider to select the amount, don't as some do to replesnish bats/bots just click the single buy as this can sometimes give you Too many Hence the cheat Kick Ship Exceeds spec alot of the time.

-

If the server is lagging when selling weapons, then Wait for the audio clink of coins to sound before selling the next or you could be logged as selling an item Twice and so kicked. Or if after clicking Sell and nothing happens wait a second, if not you guessed it kicked.

2. F1 moving of Weapons/Cash etc

If in doubt don't use this method to move cash or lots of weapons between characters, get someone to hold them for you. If the F1 switch and item move is done repeatedly over a period of time and or quickly then Two things happen.

a. One of your Characters WILL be Corrupted.
b. The server will crash if and when you try to use that character again, only cure is deleting that corrupted character.
