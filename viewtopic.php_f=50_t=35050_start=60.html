parabolix

Fri Jan 27, 2006 6:15 pm by parabolix

Beanybaby,

First off, please don't use all caps. Second, one of the nice features of TLR is the search forums function. If you would've done a bit of searching, you could've found quite a number of tutorials documenting how to edit effects. Here is my old tutorial on how to make weapons and edit effects.

Now, you can't simply copy the Liberty Cruiser forward gun entry for your weapon. It is not in beam_effects.ini. However, if you follow the directions in the tutorial, when you get to the part when you need to edit weapons_ale.ini to add your effect, your entry should look like this:

[VisEffect
nickname = custom_effect
alchemy = fx\weapons\li_cruiser_maingun.ale
effect_crc = -24850011
textures = fx\sarma.txm

You can add as many textures lines as you want. Experiment with them for different looks. However, keep the alchemy and effect_crc as they are listed there.
