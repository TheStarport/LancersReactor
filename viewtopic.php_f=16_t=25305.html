welcome to the boards

all i can say is....ouch! you started a campaign mission with a freighter!?! I can honestly say that I've never tried to beat the race with a freighter so I can't advise on how it handles etc....but I will tell you how that its a relatively easy mission to complete otherwise. When you are lined up for the race, hover over SHIFT+W until he announces go and then hammer on the engines as fast as possible. If you did it quick enough you should be either fractionally ahead of him or at least level. Now all you have to do is stick tight to the corners and don't miss a single hoop - he always messes up, so you should have no trouble beating him. Restart if you fall behind at any point.

I don't know how your freighter will handle the corners though, your best bet might be to find an old auto/save and swap your ship before you go off to find him....you're gonna need to fight later on at some point anyway.

As a rule of thumb, if Juni calls you to do something, equip yourself heavy and get ready to fight for your life. You can always swap back to a freighter later on.

Hope that helps
