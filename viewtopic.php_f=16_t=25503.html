In MP: Destroy three cargo containers found on the two _Police_ Depots near Newark Station. (8 available)
Just buy a Lavablade, do one container at a time, then run like hell to another
location. If you do the square containers, you get 15 Polymers which sell @ $438 each
at Baltimore Shipyard. The round containers have Hydrocarbons, go to Detroit then
return to Newark to sell 20 units for $240 each. You only need three containers.

Rogues, Outcasts & Xenos get Neutral. You won't keep Xenos neutral for long,
go hunt them near Detroit your increase your rep with everyone BUT Zoners.

At this point, you can go to Buffalo or Mactan and bribe for outcasts, or if you can't adfford that,
you can act the Pirate and get Rogues and Hackers to like you, and Outcasts are their allies.

Outcasts are easy compared to Corsairs.
