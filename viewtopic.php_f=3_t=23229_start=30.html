If you like space sims, the ones I mentioned are great -- HARDWAR and the HOMEWORLD series. HARDWAR has been called "Elite on a planet" -- actually one of the mmons of Jupiter, which you are trying to get off of. HOMEWORLD was game of the year around 2000. There are several updates, plus a new one about to come out. There's also the Wing Commander series, which PRIVATEER is a part of. The Wing Commander series are linear games -- just follow the missions through to the end. But PRIVATEER is like an update of ELITE. You can find a link to download PRIVATEER on the off topic forum (I think). The game's 10 years old now! PRIVATEER II is also a fun one -- similar to FREELANCER. You can download it for free too.

Then there are the Egosoft games ... X-BEYOND THE FRONTIER and X-TENSION. Both are a few years old and rather slow play, but great graphics and an ELITE feel to them. A new version is about to come out ... called X-2 or something.
