RunOrDie

Sun Mar 16, 2003 3:20 pm by RunOrDie

The monkey and robot planets are located in the Unknown system off Omicron Gamma. The jumphole to the system is in the middle of the green cloud at the north-east of the map. Switch to "Minable Zones" and you'll see two zones in the cloud. The jumphole is about 10K directly to the north of those zones.

Although there's high radiation around the jumphole, once in the Unknown system there shouldn't be any, but you'll encounter 4 Nomads every few minutes.
