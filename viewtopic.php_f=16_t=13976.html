Nightsilver

Mon Apr 14, 2003 11:53 pm by Nightsilver

The passage is simple to find. Basically, stay vertically level with the trade lanes and jumphole (everything is at approx the same height - stay around this level). Now, sight on the jumpgate. You know how they're tapered at one end (the end that opens up when you jump through)? Position yourself on that end at the outside edge of the minefield. You should see the hole near you (it's pretty big).

Lets see if I can explain myself a little bettwe with a diagram:

XXXXXXXXXXX
XXXXXXXXXXX
XXXX =>---------O
XXXXXXXXXXX
XXXXXXXXXXX

X's are mines, the => it the jumpgate (with the pointy end being the one you need to line up with). The -'s are an imaginary line between you and the Gate, which needs to pass all the way through the gate, spearing the pointy end, with you being the O.

Once you find the passage, you'll smack yourself in the forehead - it's not hard to find, nor is it particularly long (only 1-3 klicks...the open area with the Gate is pretty huge).
