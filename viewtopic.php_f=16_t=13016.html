If your cruise engine is going off of it's own accord, then you're not flying manual.

Make sure you have NO WAYPOINTS, and you don't use GO-TO (F2) or DOCK (F3).

Don't select ANY waypoints. Just fly the sucker by eye, and engate the cruise manually ( SHIFT-W ). Don't stop until you win, and don't miss any rings.

That's all I can tell you, because truthfully, I beat that thing my first time out. I guess being an impatient bastard and flying manual cruise right up to the gates before I docked helped me out.

It's easy, if you've mastered the mouse control system. I honestly think flying that particular race with a joystick would be much harder, so if you're still making the transition, I can understand why it would be tough.

Zaratomb

Mon Apr 07, 2003 2:54 pm by Zaratomb

I have gone back a few times to retry the race with some differing techniques. By afterburning till my ship speed hit 80+, then hitting cruise I was out in front by ring 2. (this is both with hf and lf) would have to go back a bit more to try with a freighter. EIther way, when the weapon platforms go active, they do fire missiles (like almost every other weapon platform in the game) at you. However I have never dropped a counter measure and have never been cruise disrupted.

The most frequent problem most people have completing the race is cruise engines dropping off line, and from most of the previous posts this is caused (as stated a few times above) by selecting goto instead of being in free cruise. As soon as you get within about 500-800 of the intended waypoint your cruise engines will drop off line. What confuses most people is that you can be in goto and still control your ship with the mouse as if in free flight. But your cruise engines will still shut down when you are close to the waypoint, then waypoint shifts and the process is repeated. This can end up in catostrophic loss of mouse/keyboard. So for the sake of what hair you might have remaining and the structural integrity of your computer, please ensure you are really in free flight when you start this race.
