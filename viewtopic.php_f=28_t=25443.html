Samurai Studios - Alternate Universe Mod Info

Samurai Studios - Alternate Universe Mod Information & Update

This mod will incorporate lots of missing items from freelancer. It will also be highly player interactive. Each players efforts, action or lack there of will have an effect on thier faction. These are just a few of the things we will be doing for this mod:

1) Each Faction will have a full range of ships, including these along with the default ones: New Starter ship, Interceptors, Medium fighter, Light Bomber, Heavy Bomber.

2) Each Faction will have a full range of guns, missles, mines, and equipment ranging from class 1 to 10

3) Each faction will have 'Special' Class 10 weapons

4) Each faction will have capital ships in each of these classes: Gunboat, Corvette, Frigate, Destroyer, Light Cruiser, Heavy Cruiser, Battleship, Dreadnaught, and Carrier. As well each faction will have 3 faction specific frieghters from small to large.

5) 4 all new factions will be added. Each with thier own unique weapons, ships, and equiopment.

6) Tons of new commodities will be added. Both Legal & contraband. There will also be contraband items that will be illegal in some houses and not others.

7) an all new default New Character Ship to replace the Starflier.

Players will be allowed to construct bases, tradelanes, etc ...

9) Players will be allowed to colonize and settle new systems

10) Some of the standard upgrades will also be included like: Armour, Scanner, Deep Scanner, Engines, and more ...

11) The Universe will be destructable, so if your fleet invades a system you can wipe it clean!

12) We will be incorporating the Docking/Mooring fix by Wanderer/Chips/and Duriel_LOP

Plus alot more as the mod is developed ...

We have already started to post pics of some of the new ships that we have designed and will be using in the mod.

The development is well underway and we plan Alpha Testing to begin near the end of this month.

Visit our Site at: Samurai Studios

We welcome your thoughts, comments, & ideas.




Roland "Hobbes" Orr
Founder, Samurai Studios
