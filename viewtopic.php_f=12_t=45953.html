The Final Frontier Podcast

I've been a little hesitent about posting a thread like this, but I think that now would be the best time....just because >.>

I joined the LR community a couple years ago, but I was never very active. I posted here and there, but that was way before LR expanded to all the other games it covers now. Since it has grown, i've realized that I've missed out on something great...which is why I come to you now with my latest


The Final Frontier is a podcast that recently (as of April 1st) broke away from a formerlly Star Trek-gaming podcast called The Argus Array. Since then, we've expanded our content to include Science, Scifi, and the biggest of the three: Gaming.

Now, on a persona level, I found this site through Freelancer. What a great game, too. I loved the music. Anyway, being a new show that we are, one could say we're starved for content =P But not really. While our options are limited at the moment, we have found solid ground both with Star Trek: Legacy and a game that I saw has it's own place here- Infinity: The Quest For Earth.

TFF is very open to suggestions, proposals, ideas, and submissions. However, since we have only just begun to get our foot in the door, a place like this would give us a great opportunity to expand our programming. I should point out that we're very loose with what we accept on the show. Considering how broadly-topical we are, this is a given! So basically, almost anything goes.

If there's any game-related news that anyone would like to get as much publicity about as possible, then this might be a great way to do it! We (as a team) have established ourselves on many forums around the net, so we can reach a very diverse audience. Obviously, we can't please everyone, but we will still do our best to cover as much as we can, as often as we can.

You can email us your comments/questions/complaints/rants at our current email address:
final.frontier.podcast@gmail.com

Our current website is located here:
http://www.startrek-gamers.com/tff


Once again, we would be happy to do a segment, or two, or three....on any one of the games here. While I personally have not played all of the ones here, we will happily invite anyone onto the show if they have something to say about the games!

Thanks for reading, and once again, feel free to suggest whatever you'd like

-Magellanic, co-host of The Final Frontier
