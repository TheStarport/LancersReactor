Punctuation and some notions of proper writing style (intended to be objective style as opposed to personal) are found in style books. The US version that is fairly common is the "Elements of Style" by EB White. It is a small manageable paperback. A good reference for those who want to get really serious about writing. Don't know about the UK variant but I think Oxford or Cambridge (or both?) publish style books too.
