witchdoctor

Wed Jun 25, 2003 7:09 pm by witchdoctor

The only way I know to find the wrecks without the spoiler list is to fly close enough to them. They will then show up on your nav map as a red X. They will also show up on the all things filter on the HUD when you are close enough. The only other way to find them is to pay an npc in a bar for the location. I have been offered in some systems, but it is random and often costs more than you get for selling the weapons and cargo. They will also offer to sell the location of a wreck you have already found and plundered. If you accept, the wreck will show up as a red X on your nav map and you can set it as a way point. Better yet, save the game, then accept, so you know exactly where the wreck is, then reload and mark a waypoint where you saw the wreck and go there. You should be aware that player waypoints don't persist when you load a saved game. The easiest is to get the wreck locations from the posts. It should be stickied above in the spoilers forum. I usually still have to fly a search pattern and then set a way point when I locate the wreck.
