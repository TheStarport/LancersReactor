Wed Aug 29, 2007 12:00 am by dudeofthed

Honestly, it doesn't take that long to get the dagger and become neutral to the rogues.

Here is my method:
1) Complete the mission with Ashcroft to get to level 4.
2) Now get a heap of random mission with high pay. Do not worry when Juni calls. Or do trades.
3) When you have at least 60,000+ credits, go oustside and find some bounty hunters.
4) If your rep is really low, kill a heap of bounty hunters that will just reach near the mark of hostility to the bounty hunters.
5) Go to Rochester base and enter the bar.
6) Find a Bribe that is offering to edit the Rogues reputation. Accept it. You may become hostile with the bounty hunters but easy to ware off. You'll still be allies to Liberty so don't worry.
7) Go to Buffalo Base and buy the Dagger ship for 17 grand.
8) Buy other weapons from there too.
