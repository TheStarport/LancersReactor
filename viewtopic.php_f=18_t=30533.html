Pete, you really don't realize what your asking.
1. To do a new story line , means voices, scripts for NPC's setting the cut scenes, like Trents and Kings flyby in the early part, ect. A great deal of work.
2. Some folks have created new areas/systems, like the Nexus, ect. Factions can be renamed, but the cards, ect must be changed to reflect the new names, like Klingons instead of Corsares. Again, lots of work.
3. Never have delt with servers, so no comments on that.
4. There are already a lot of new ships out there. Getting togeather with those folks and import them into a different mod could be done, but again, more work needed.
5. Again, more work. The nexus has a shadow faction that is auwsome.
6. Like the above, in the nexus is where the Shodows are.

The best thing you could do is to learn. Learn to mod exzisting weps, ships ect, making them stronger, differnt ect. After you get that down, learn to import ships, weps from other mods, so you learn what all it takes to have a new ship in the game. There are several files involved to bring a ship in. I have learned to do this. I took one from a mod that would crash on me when I tried that ship. I imported it and deleted the set weps and got it to work for me. Now I can fly a Maquies rader, Bird of prey, ect in Sp games with out having a mod in use.

You can learn to do these things no matter your age. Take the time, going thru the tutorals and read. The experiment. Who knows, you may one day make your own mod. But again, it takes work and time.
