Dower

Mon Mar 31, 2003 7:27 pm by Dower

I've been flying a Humpback for a little while now, and doing the Diamond run from Berlin to New Tokyo. I go the back way around Sigma 13, I've also got a bad rap with ALL the pirate factions... So, the run is far from safe. Here are my tips for survival with a Humpback:

1. Get an Advanced Countermeasure Dropper, you will want this to deter cruise disruptors, and launch them as soon as you hear a missle warning. ( for a cool view, jump to a turrent behind view and you can see your countermeasures working)
2. Get improved thrusters, Advanced/Heavy (forgot which is better)
3. Get big guns for the humpback, at least class 5 turrets.
4. Don't stick around to fight if you are VERY outnumbered.
5. The humpback is the only ship (I think) that has 5 guns facing your enemies at every angle (thus the better turrets are nice). Don't try and manuever (you barely can anyway), make use of the turrets!
6. Buy the biggest shields she'll support. The combination of the massive shields and the ships armor makes you very hard to kill.

With all that, I've never been taken down making solo runs.

Hope this helps, Good luck...
BTW, I link the tip about the engine-kill (Z key), never dawned on me.
