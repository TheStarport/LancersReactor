Yours will probs be a planet - this just happened to be where BiniQ was at the time, you are after this part:


toughness = 6
density = 5
repop_time = 20
max_battle_size = 4
pop_type = nonlootable_ast_field
relief_time = 35

Toughness - make it high - very high(max level of AI)
Density - i think, not sure, that this is the amount of craft total allowed in area - pump it up there - like 40 odd will do.
Max battle size - don't go above about 28 or so, 24 is more than enough - but you only need this if you are flooding the place with extra NPC nasties etc
Repop time - you can lower this if you like, but it should be fine with the high numbers of the above!
Relief time - as giskard said - he thinks that this is the amount of time between badguys arriving - but i have not found this to be true, or false!
