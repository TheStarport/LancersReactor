zazie

Tue Jul 12, 2005 3:15 am by zazie

It should be more ticklin' than tacklin' ... anyway, sorry if I offended you.
In my experience Graviton shields are best against Nomad weapons THOUGH these weapons do not belong to any of the traditional Laser/Plasma/Photon/etc. class. The ship you've already changed (well done imo).
The other posters are right: you must know your equipment. But even in a Sabre with best shield a full frontal attack of four or even more Nomads can be dangerous. So change your way of fighting: change to free flight (space bar) and keep moving your mouse or use strafeing with A- and D-keys; best of course is a combination of strafeing AND additional moves.
You must be a moving target - and you'll avoid a lot of hits.
In Vanilla FL (i.e. unmodded game) after the first rush turn sharply (use X-key too) to get in the back of the Nomads. Thrust and hit one of them as hard as possible. As soon as one of your opponents get's in YOUR back turn sharply ... - repeat as many times as needed.
In a modded game you have to change tactics: Modded games usually have NPCs using nanobots (and regenerating shields off course - but Nomads don't have shields normally even in modded games). There you should NOT break your attack at one particular enemy but go after him to finish him. That's why fast moving during your attack is even more important to prevent being hit deadly by following ennemies. In such a moment it is not the baddest of all ideas to use a mine too.
