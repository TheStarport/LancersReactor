Crash-Drive

Tue Apr 01, 2003 7:40 pm by Crash-Drive

I don't know if it's a good idea to use just FL as a measure of the features and support in the 2 major competing gfx cards. Putting a souped-up gfx card in an underpowered system still makes you turn off many of those fancy quality options for the sake of framerate, thus negating their usefulness. There are numerous hardware sites with reviews that compare and contrast ATI vs. nVidia... and you're going to find staunch supporters in both camps. My suggestion is just find out the facts, not the hype, and choose the best features/performance you can afford.
