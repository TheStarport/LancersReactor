One thing you can use this to do is target vulnerable areas. In one of the SP missions, one of your friends tells you to "target the engines of the gunboats, they are the weakest area", or somesuch. You can set this to target the engines, and you don't necessarily have to be directly behind the gunboat and constantly adjusting your aim. The computer will do it for you. You can also knock turrets off of capital ships and bases.
