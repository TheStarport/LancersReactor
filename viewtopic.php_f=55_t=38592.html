Matti Kuokkanen

Sun Apr 10, 2005 10:46 am by Matti Kuokkanen

I bought the game few weeks ago and before I got chance to install it, HDD of my new PC messed up. I'll get it back tomorrow in working condition (I hope) and first game to play on it shall be Freelancer. I don't even want try the game with this outdated piece of junk I'm using right now.

I have heard about 2 Star Wars mods, which are mostly MMO type multiplayer: Freeworlds and Tides of War. I would like to know what other mods of that caliber exist. Name, description and what game modes are available: SP, MP and/or MMO. Feel free to tell shortly about other (smaller caliber) mods.

What I have heard about Freeworlds, it is MMO (Massive Multiplayer Online) type game. There is at least one server which is always available and stores details about players. So player can play, make small fortune, quit and return to continue where was left. There are quite number of different factions including civilians, New Republic, Empire, Black Sun and many others. Available ships range from TIE Fighter to small freighters (YT-1300) and light starships (Corellian Corvette and Dreadnaught). Mod staff has also made up some weird fighters including TIE Vampire and TIE Raptor. Heavy starships act as stationary bases. Scheduled events include Territorial Engagements where starsystems (or part of one) change hands. Available game modes are MP with few players and MMO type server. I have heard single player to be possible but there is needed some trick to get it work and there might be problems with it.

Tides of War sounds more promising. Heavy starships are available for players including Star Destroyers. Factions are limited only to 5 including civilians and gameplay weighs more towards war. MMO type server is available. I don't know about other game modes.

General opinion seems to be Tides of War is better than Freeworlds. But I guess it really depends what you want to play. If you want to be something like Han Solo, Freeworlds could be better choice. Tides of War is more (but not only) for war players.
