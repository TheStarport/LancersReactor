Lev Arris

@Sarpedon

There is most likely something wrong with the surface you are moving the mouse on. The consequence is that your mouse sometimes can't recognize wether it has been moved or how far it has been moved. Try a surface with some sort of texture to it. It shouldn't be completly smooth but a little bit "bumpy". I'm using a "ratpad", no trouble with it. See www.ratpadz.com for more info.
