The *only* way to avoid spoilers completely is to avoid all discussion. It's hard to have "certain" discussions in "certain" forums without suddenly needing to move the post to the spoiler forum. There will always be the danger that something you didn't want to know about will be revealed even by something that wouldn't even be considered a spoiler by most people. But things like "secret" systems, I will agree, are a little more obvious. However, they aren't really related to the SP story at all. It's a little harder to call them spoilers if they're part of the MP universe, easily found, and contain useful things. If you read the boards, you can't expect to have a totally unspoiled experience with the game, even if it's just a little bit.

If you're looking to see a movie/anime series/etc... NEVER, EVER, EVER read the online reviews for it, on sites like Amazon or Netflix. It's too easy to read right past the *SPOILER* tags and read something like "it's too bad Chracter B dies in the end." Ugh.

Thats why I say, if you absolutely can't stand spoilers, it's best not to read the boards until you beat the game. The game will be more fun that way. And you might as well avoid MP servers as well, because noone has problems with telling you the locations of all the best weapons/ships/systems in a few lines there.
