Ditch your missiles first thing. Use your afterburners as much as you can and don't be afraid to let the NPCs do the dirty work. Make sure all your guns have at least the same speed stats, otherwise your aiming will be all screwed up. If all else fails, run circles around the jumpgate hoping they follow you and let the AI pick them off.

edit: Ouch on the Rhino, definately not made for combat. Go for a patriot/dagger at least, I reccomend a Defender.
