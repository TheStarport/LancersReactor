Tue Dec 16, 2003 7:42 pm by gromit

@tom I too am an infrequent MP player...and I can understand your conerns. But you can still get a lot out of MP if you do it right. firstly you need to choose yourself a good server....the only way to do this is to take recommendations, check out a few and chat to the people online. I can recommend Alpha Centauri 2, they seem to be a decent bunch on there, but I'm sure other people can recommend good ones too (try the MP forum). Next, when you've found a server that interests you...put aside 2 or 3 hours when you get the chance and play on that server until you've got a decent ship/payload. After this you can log on any time that you want to whether it be for 20 minutes every month or whatever. If you've got yourself a ship that you enjoy flying then you'll find it much easier to jump into pvp battles. Its definitely worth that first couple of hours though. Also, if you're on there and see that members of a clan are on there (usually have strange letters before their name), talk to them and ask them to help you out. Usually clan members are obliged to help out server newbies...so they may be able to help you build up your ship a bit quicker by sharing mission cash etc.

I've got a half decent character on AC2 (flying a hammerhead at the moment), that only took me about 3/4 an hour to build up....plan your money-building strategy in SP before going onto the server, it will help you in the long run. I only log on about an hour or two a month, and I still get loads of fun out of it.

Hope that helps!
