Seems to be specific pilots, as I was talking to a friend when I found out (was going to a specific system to bribe. Arrived and was unable to talk to anybody), and he wasn't having any problem with it. All I did previously was log on and fly around a bit, no killing, was just completing the trip I started before I left for the night.

Both times this has happened to me, the only common thing between the two was that when I logged out I still had waypoints set. I kinda doubt it's related, but it's the only thing I can think of. The first time I was on a mission and got disconnected. The second time I was too tired to keep flying, so left for the night *L*. In both cases when I logged back in I could no longer talk to NPC's. W/ the disconnect, I re-logged immediately, with the "need sleep", I was gone for about 16 hours. I'm fairly sure I've logged w/ waypoints set on other occasions and not had problems, but I wouldn't swear to it...

I haven't had this happen on other servers (yet?), nor have I heard of it happening to anybody anywhere else, so it's seeming to be part of the alpha code.

As to re-logging to fix it, nope. Relog, reboot, change IP (when I had dialup). I didn't try the "new player" option, but I did log into another server to see if it was acting up. It was fine. Everybody talked to me. Logged back to Elite and they were all mimes still...
