Revenge

Sun Mar 23, 2003 7:32 am by Revenge

In your player status window, click on the name of a faction, and a short description of them will come up. You can then see who their allies and enemies are.

Kill a faction's enemies to increase your standing with them, or kill their allies to lower your standing with em.

Crash-Drive

Sun Mar 23, 2003 8:28 am by Crash-Drive

Within most of the factional ally/enemy relationships is a fundamental theme of the law vs. the outlaws. No surprise there; but it can have effects that are surprising if you don't pay attention. Killing any outlaw can lower your standing with other outlaw factions, even though the outlaw you are killing is not a direct ally or enemy of those other outlaws, because you are likely raising your standing with law, military, and bounty hunter groups, which ARE direct enemies of most outlaw factions.

As an example, I managed to lower my faction standing with the Corsairs from max friendly to neutral by killing Hogosha... which I didn't understand at first.
