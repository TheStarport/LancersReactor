I would not associate a "blue screen of death" with an mods issue. Blue Screens usually occur due to a hardware failure, and are a result of windows shutting down instantly to "prevent damage".

This is usually related to drivers mainly - so I would advise updating vid/sound drivers.
When you reboot - you should get generated error report - file this to MS - as it will "diagnose" the problem. Mine finally fixed my own issue, which was to do with my Soundcard and Windows XP (fix created by Soundlabs or whomever they are).

Mods will just tip you onto the desktop.
