It depends on a few factors: your maneuvrability skill, your opponents' aim, and the strength of his weapons (and of course a bit of luck). If he's got guns that can blow you away in just a few shots plus good aim, the longevity of your guns isn't going to be worth anything because he'll blow you up before he runs out of gun power. Only if you can keep him missing for awhile does your advantage actually come into play. I wouldn't consider it good for piracy, you want to move in, strike, and have your opponent dead before they can adequately respond.
