A little feed back.

I have watched some threads lately. New ideas and thoughts abound. New mods and stories. Fan fiction has taken off as have modding ideas. We now have the Ragnarok, which I love and Evolutions 1.28. New faces, metaphorical, to the forums. A new thread asking about TLR and getting some history about it. A lot of interesting things apparently happened before my time. But also some good folks making friends and helping each other out.

But, I have seen a bit lately, of a darker side. A point of view that would prefer a private forum, or club without new people. The problem with public forums, is people will want to join. Is that a bad thing? Everyone here was a “Newbie” at one time. Tring to find acceptance and a place to fit in. Not an easy thing to do. Everyone will not always agree on things and that is human nature and I don’t see it as a problem. People make mistakes sometimes and get called out on the carpet for it. Caps, smilies, flames, wrong forum postings, etc. And most learn where not to step after that. Some need a more direct approach and the matter ends.

Judging by some of the threads in off topic, there are a variety of ages here and interests as well. People from different locations on the map and opportunities to learn new ideas and cultures. To learn about people and make new friends. A place of new experiences.

I just hope, folks learn and don’t want to “go back to the way things use to be”. We have change everyday in life. It would be a shame to miss it here. Who knows, maybe a person comes in and creates the ultimate mod and people love it, or they write the best piece of fiction ever written, or simple make some great friends.

Thank you for the opportunity to give a little feed back
