Warning to all modders

I just wanted to send out a little heads-up to all modders. Don't use St02 as a name for a system. This will cause one or two issues to occur: a. nav map will not display properly b. jumpholes leading to the system could potentially be locked. I discovered this purely by accident as I was modding away, and it took me hours to figure out the cause of the bugs. The cause is that the game uses St02 as the name of the Omicron Major system in vanilla. Also, be careful to remember that coding in the .ini files is NOT case-sensitve.
