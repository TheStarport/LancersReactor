Wed Aug 18, 2004 10:07 am by Wasabe

Yep. I'm assuming you mean the reference to the background in the system ini files:

[Background
basic_stars = solar\starsphere\starsphere_stars_basic.cmp
complex_stars = solar\starsphere\starsphere_li01_stars.cmp
nebulae = solar\starsphere\starsphere_li01.cmp

The two for "complex_stars" and "nebulae" must be compatible otherwise you'll get the strange looking triangle stuff. In otherwords, if you use the li01 starsphere, you need the li01 stars. You can always just take out the complex stars if you want. Or, if you want a system with no background, just black with stars, then take out the nebulae.
