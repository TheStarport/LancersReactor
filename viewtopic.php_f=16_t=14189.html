Nightsilver

Thu Apr 17, 2003 6:48 am by Nightsilver

For Raw defensive power, Titan wins. For sheer destructive potential, Sabre wins. For everything else, I think Eagle is the best.

Titan has, simply put, the highest hull rating in the game. Also, it can hold 8 more nanos and batteries than the next closest contenders. All other factors ignored, it has the most defense.

Sabre has identical weapons setup as the other two, except for one thing: its turret fires forward. The turrets on both the Titan and Eagle are incapable of firing forward. Because of this, the Sabre can spew more damage than the other two can in the same amount of time.

Eagle is far better for maneuverability than the other two. Also, it's a smaller target by quite a bit. Because of this, I would give it the best overall award. Its turning ability means the other two VHF's are dead if they get into PVP with an eagle. Also, its smaller cross-section means it's harder to hit, giving it better survivability than the Sabre/Titan.

A further advantage of the Eagle is its purchase location. Freeport 9, a Zoner base, will probably ALWAYS be neutral with you. Malta and Vogtland (Sabre purchase locations) and Crete and Leon (Titan locations) will probably start out hostile, requiring you to destroy a decent amount of something to get the base's pirate owners neutral (Vogtland is Hessian, Malta is Outcast, Crete/Leon are Corsair).
