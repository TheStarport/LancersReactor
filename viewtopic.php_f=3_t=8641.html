Yes. First you have to land somewhere. Then you go to your weapons inventory and if you have a close look at the weapon icons you will eventually see a little gray square that looks like a hatch. Click on a closed "hatch" to unmount a weapon/equipment piece and click on the open "hatches" to mount the particular equpiment. Mounting, of course, works only on hardpoints that have a better level or the same level as the piece of equipment you want to mount.
