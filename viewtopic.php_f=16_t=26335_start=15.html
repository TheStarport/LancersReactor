I didn't have a problem with it, and I did the race in a Bretonian Heavy Fighter. Won the first time.

Just start out at cruise speed, stay relaxed, and wait for him to screw up near the end (he always will). Then you're home free - his guns can't hurt you unless you're shieldless or something. The trick, I think, is to start turning slightly BEFORE you go past each ring, like you're slaloming between them. Your ship will probably take some time to turn, so it's nice to have a head start.
