Mace_166

Mon Mar 13, 2006 4:32 am by Mace_166

Patrols can be a bit of a pain. Are you using Freelancer Explorer? Hopefully you are as it makes everything so much easier.

Just add the patrol points as you would normally, but remember that in order for patrol to start at a base there needs to be a person from that faction in the mbase for that base. This seems more picky with trade.

Its best to just use attack patrols for most of the patrols and keep the chances down to a very low 0.05 type amount. Try not to try too much, there are options to add "attack this base" and things like that but if they are hostile, then they will anyway.

If you add these and have trouble with crashes, then decode the systems ini and check that the patrol labels are all unique. Some of the older versions of FLE had a problem with the labels, but i believe that they are all fixed now.
