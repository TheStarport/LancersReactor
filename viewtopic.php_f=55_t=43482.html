J Dawg

Sun May 07, 2006 4:34 pm by J Dawg

I don't think this is a glitch, perhaps it is the mechanism that destroys the Mollies so quickly....

Anyway, if you have to get around (not through) a minefield, you can take advantage of the 2D layout of most FL systems, by flying up, and over a minefield rather than through it. Also use this to navigate hostile systems as AI ships don't spawn when you fly high enough above the plane of the system (usually 10-15k I think).

It's not just this minefield. The NPCs seem to fly around in minefields oblivious to the fact that their shields are down. And even the strongest ship with the heaviest armour will not survive a single mine hit. I've watched Mollies, Corsairs, Red Hessians and others get blown up in mine fields. The Corsairs are great ones for getting blown up by gas pocket explosions too.

Best thing with the Mollie's minefield is to avoid it as much as possible until after the story game has ended. Then go back when you have a ship that carries plenty of shield bats! Same advice holds for almost any mine field.
