A Good Idea or what?

I Have always luved the potential social atmosphere of FreeLancer having multiple systems with dockible planets . However when it comes to the bars on the planets I feel with a simple mod it could be enhanced so much more. For example in multiplayer in addition to having the standard scripted charactors located in the bars also add low/res models to represent each player currently in the room. Also the ability to sit at a table/or the bar and invite players over to your table for a Group chat with the option to trade goods with out having to launch into space. I dont know if this would even be possible or not or if anyone would even be interested for that matter, but I've played 2-D chess games b-4 with a similar type chat feature creating indivdual rooms or tables as they called them. I just thought it may be a welcome chat feature to someones mod if its possible to implement. What say you ?
