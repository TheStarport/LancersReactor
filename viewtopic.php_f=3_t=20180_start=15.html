Fri Jul 04, 2003 7:00 am by MasterPooBaa

All BH weapons suffer significant "efficiency" losses due to their slight increase in range and/or projectile speed.

And efficiency (damage done/ energy used) is important.
Here is a list of some high and low efficiency weapons.
HIGH:
Most Plasma guns due to slow speed and small range
Most Neutron guns, especially the ones with a fire rate of 3.03 (LBA & FA).
Tachyons with a fire rate of 3.03
Particle cannons, especially the Xeno's & outcast ones.
GMG & Gian photon blasters.

LOW
BH lasers and particle cannons.
Rheinland tachyons due to their high fire rate
Civillian lasers are average.

So i allways go for the guns with HIGH VELOCITY and high efficiency. refire rate and range are less important.
