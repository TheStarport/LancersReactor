chips hit it on the head. I have to do this a lot - line up photoshop with a new file the size of your resolution and then "printscreen" - "paste" - "printscreen" - "paste" - repeat as necessary. You'll end up with a photoshop file with sequential layers of screen shots.

And here's the handy bit. If you DON'T move your browser when doing this, each layer will look exactly the same but the contents of the browser will be different....so marquee the browser area of one of the layers and crop. This will leave you with a perfectly cut out sequential photoshop file.
