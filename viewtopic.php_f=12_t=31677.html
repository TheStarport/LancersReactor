I've never been a big fan of the more extreme breeds. Too much interbreeding takes place and the poor animals get congenital defects. Toy dogs tend to get a lot of joint problems especially in their front legs, mostly because they have to move around relatively mountainous terrain inside the house all their lives. Also don't forget no matter how cute it is, it's still a dog, it wants to sniff butts, chase smaller animals and play tug-o-war, too many people forget that with toy breeds.
