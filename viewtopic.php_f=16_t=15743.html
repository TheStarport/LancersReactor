Crash-Drive

Fri May 02, 2003 2:44 pm by Crash-Drive

The Kraken particle guns have a projectile speed of 700m/s, while the Nomad Blasters and TDC have a speed of 600m/s. You'd probably be better off with a Salamanca Mk II which has a speed of 600m/s. Also, the Blasters, like all c10 guns, have a range of 699m, which is 99m more than the TDC or the Sal II, so it's up to you to remember to bring the target within 600m, because the targetting indicator will show up at 699m.
