I personally prefer the Sabre as well.....2 Tizona del Cid/2 Nomad Blaster/2 Nomad Cannon, plus sunslayer torps and driller mines.

It's true that it's not the easiest ship to maneuver, but once you know what you're doing? It's unbeatable.

In SP mode, I can take out 4 inbound bogies before they ever reach me....strafing with guns, then a torpedo or two.

In MP, I use different strategies depending on who I'm fighting *assuming a PvP deul here*. What usually works is diving straight towards my opponent in cruise, then killing the engines. Throw torpedo, then mine....if that doesn't kill them, I've spun and attacked with guns as they attempt to evade, before they can go on the offensive. Practice with engine kill/thruster only mode is a MUST tho!

It doesn't always work, but I've won MUCH more than I've lost...
