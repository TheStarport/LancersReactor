That is kind of strange. My first thought was that you needed to do a mission, but since it's giving you a credit total as the next level requirement, that's obviously not the case. I have absolutely NO CLUE what is going on there, but I'll offer a few suggestions anyways, who knows?

Did you ever have Juni contact you and ask you to meet her at such-and-such planet or ship? If so, maybe the game just glitched and won't give you that next level until you've actually met with her. It SHOULD NOT do that, but as a tech support agent, I know how computers and software can be too;O)

At any time did you skip or abort a mission and keep "doing your own thing"? Because you're not really forced to do all of the missions to keep playing the game, but you cannot level up if you do quit them.

If you get to this level and get stuck again, then it's almost certainly a corrupted program code somewhere. If you get back to this level and progress the way you should? Then don't ask me, it was the gremlins;o)
