Chips

Wed Jun 02, 2004 9:39 am by Chips

You will have to find out who made the models, and ask if you can use them. This includes contacting the mods you have taken them out of, and asking whom made them originally. You can email Porsche - check out his downloads in the downloads section that lets you know where to get hold of him. For others, its semi detective work if the credits aren't up to scratch - but just because they were lazy in making terrible credits, it doesn't mean that YOU can blame them and not bother to find out.

Also - tell the mods that do use other ships that aren't theres, that they should update the readme file. Basically - if you cannot tell whom made the ships, then they have got it wrong. Ships credits are a VERY large part of my readme file - as i tell you where to contact etc etc.

If the models are theirs, you have no choice but to ask permission. You cannot use and put credits IF they made them - you HAVE to get permission. We have had enough trouble in this area recently, so please avoid any further complications by getting correct permssions

Thanks for asking about the issue rather than just plowing ahead, very wise
