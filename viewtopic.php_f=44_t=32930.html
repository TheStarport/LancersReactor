there are some tut's somewhere on the subject which I don't feel like trying to locate in the archives of TLR. For the most part I think the current ship builders just decide to leave destructable components on the side cause it's too much a hassle for something not much worth it. But if you really feel like making destructable components, it is doable and the information is somewhere here, just hunt for the info lol. I think the newest cmp exporter was supposed to have some features regarding destructable components, read up on it's readme, might have something.
