Neutral units/bases will convert to hostile if there are any hostile units in the area that are allied/neutral with the neutral faction.

In other words:

If you kill a Hessian in front of a Bundschuh(or however the frick that's spelled), the Bundschuh will become hostile as well. Likewise if you kill a Hogosha in front of the Kusari navy, a Daumann in front of a Rhineland cop, and so on and so forth.

Even if the hostile just shows up but you don't kill it, their friends(if they aren't already your friends) will turn hostile as well, until you leave the system and try again.
