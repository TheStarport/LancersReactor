Eerazor

Mon Mar 06, 2006 5:47 am by Eerazor

WOW, now that is a lot to ask of little ole me. I suppose I could split the answers into a number of parts, rather than spending the next hour thinking up answers when I should actually be continuing my work ^^


Anyway, here it goes:

1. Is that in battle? Ok, I am just going to assume it is. We have a number of races. Each race has its own specific ship design and its own specific tactical array of abilities. Some ships will concentrate on using turrets, others on missiles. Their flight patterns depend on what general psychology is behind a race. Races also have ACES or BOSSES as we call them. They also include a series of special abilities that they can throw at the player. Suffice to say, when fighting in Darkstar One you will be spoilt for choice in the number of ways you can die ^^

2. Paint jobs? What is wrong with the colour it has now? ^^

3. Well stations are large enough to have fighters dock with them. We are not going for the type of scale as in X-3. More in the direction of Freelancer. So big but not gigantic.

4. We try to watch out for scale were we can. For gameplay purposes that is not always a good idea. If we were to do a simulation, then scale would play a large role. In DSO, an action orientated game scale is not always an advantageous thing. Long story short, there is attention to it, but it is not always realistic.

5. No. We try to keep things as easy as possible when it comes to player orientation inside the systems. That means you will always appear from a Jumpgate and you can always jump out of a system at any time. The galactic map on the other hand is a little harder to orientate yourself around and that is intentional. There are plenty of systems to visit depending on the range of your hyperdrive and the further along the road you get, the more planets will be available to you on the map.

6. That depends on the outline of the missions we provide. Some missions play better without the use of a map so therefore we can disable it (which we have done). Systems in DSO are mainly discovered by either the Terran or alien races so, unlike the X-Series, you dont need to map out every system. Targets, stations etc are displayed on your Target list, dependant on the range of your radar system. The further the range, the more you can see.

7. Asked many times and answered many times. We are aiming for an exceptional single player experience. We currently have no plans for Multiplayer before the official release of the game. Please note, the decision concerning MP is final for the release version, it is not final for future decisions.

8. Yes. There will be plenty of horrifically expensive stuff you can buy ^^

9. Something inbetween. Again we want action and not simulation ^^

10. 5.1 support

11. That would be the swap file. Depending on RAM etc. The more the better. Not to mention savegames (ok granted they are 1Mb a piece but we have known some players to be savegame fanatics ^^)

12. Playership + Planet = pain

13. Jumpgates act as a point of origin into a system. The hyperdrive targets a destination system through it's gate. Escaping a system does not require a jumpgate, just a lot of afterburners and a certain distance from those evil cops that thought I was being bad when I tested my nuke on a civilian transport.... i was testing a nuke... i am allowed to test my nuke... ok, ranting...

14. Uhm no. As pilot of the DSO you make you own way in the galaxy outside of the plot line

15. You can play freely from minute 1 without doing the story (although it will still be activated).

16. More than a few and less than too many

17. Plasma Weapon (Spell Weapon) has a number of really really cool uses. EMP for instance. First a sweet effect (i like nice effects) and the ship is dead in the water. You can pick it off or you can concentrate on a more imminent threat. The Plasma Weapon can also be used to boost your own shields or weapons for a limited amount of time (based on the level of that technology). You can also do a number of extremly powerful offensive moves (that can litterally disable half a star-system for a limited amount of time) as well as some other nice and nasty things...

18. Might be.... ^^

19. The game has not come out and you ask me something like that? ^^ If there was one thing I could change in the game then it would be to have it finished yesterday so I can play already ^^
