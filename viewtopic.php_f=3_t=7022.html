I'm a lefty. I can play with both hands but I like to use the arrow key setup.

I use the arrows for increase/decrease speed and strafe left and right. DEL-missles, END-mines, INSERT-countermesures, PAGEDOWN-thrusters, NUMPAD1-enginekill, NUMPAD0-reverse.

Those are the main ones I have used that I can remember now and I like it so much better than the wasd setup. I dont have to look at the keyboard at all to make sre where my fingers are. I use the other NUMPAD keys and the right side SHIFT, CTRL, ?, ",\ for some of the other keys like targeting and so forth. I just keep the defaults for everyhting else though like hail and others. Mainly I just have the combat keys at an optimal place and the non combat thingsd default since a few seconds delay wont get you killed.

Hope this helps.

By the way. I find that the insert, home, Pup, DEL, END, Pdown keys are good for things like missles and thrusters since I can reach then and not affect my movement controls and strafing.
