The Republic Mod Beta: Competition for Beta Testing!

This is a competition for anyone who would like to Beta Test the Republic Mod in roughly 12-14 days. The FULL Beta will be released to EVERYONE but this competition is for people who would like to test the Beta.
Winners will get roughly 3-4 systems, 10 weapons, 12 ships, and 6 factions to test, but this will probably change over time.
Please give it a go: we may not be as famous as other teams, indeed the only active people on the team are myself, a ship modeller, a storyline editor, and an admin pack builder. We have lost one or two other people to Real Life problems, but we try our best and this is intended to prove to everyone: The Republic Mod can do it!
Here are your questions folks:
1) In what month was the TRM team formed?

2) Which of these is not a ship to be featured in TRM: X-wing, YT-1300, Delta Flyer, Coralskipper?

3) Which of these is the correct type of mod TRM is: Star Wars, Star Trek, Battlestar Galactica, Star Trek & Freelancer, Star Wars & Freelancer, Lost in Space?

4) The Beta release date was orignally scheduled for which day in September: 5th, 8th, 11th, 23rd?

5) Who is the owner of Cloud City in Empire Strikes Back?

6) What is the name of the Emperor in Return of the Jedi?

7) Why should you be picked to be a Beta tester?

E-mail your answers to trm.zack@gmail.com Closing date is 21st October, so get your entries in now! If you have any questions feel free to mail me
