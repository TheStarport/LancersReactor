A Thread very simmilar to this was started last week, you might want to run a search on it on the forum, since i initated the thread, you could run it on my username to find all the postings

But i'll answer as best i could. To get a Liberty heavy fighter it would take about and hour or 2 of playing. To get the Titan (The ultimate ubber ship in the game) it can take 10hr's to an experianced player (meaning a player that knows what missions to take, where and when) So i'm guessing a new player to the game will take double the time?

There are 31 pilotable ships, in 4 categories
Light fighters, heavy fighters, very heavy fighters and freighters.
There are only 4 or 5 very heavy fighters in the game: Titan, Sabre, Hammerhead, Eagle and Anubis. Check out the ship database on this site, it has stats and pics for most of the ships in the game. To follow the stats (and the comments made by the beta team) best ship is the Titan. The most armor, shield/nano capacity. Closely followed by the Sabre.

Your concern about everyone getting the ubber ships is shared by me
From what i gather those ships are a MUST once you get into the more lawless system of the borderworlds, where lesser ships wont cut it. As for MP? since it looks like it will be based on co-op missions rather the individual H2H, or co-op H2H if you will, i think that balancing teams will be crucial. Sort of like in Mw where there's a maximum tonnage limit you can wield. So i think a mix of fighters of all classes will be very likely.

Since the game isn't out yet, and we are only begining to form the basis for this community it's all open to debate, and the good will of people putting up dedicated or home servers.

Time will tell.
