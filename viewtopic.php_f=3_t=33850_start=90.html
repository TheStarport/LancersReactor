Appears I'm a little late to the party, but...

You know what I remember most? The debates and disccusions about the most irrelevant things EVER! For example, the original cut of the intro had Mr. Rockford surviving the sun exploding on the other side of Pluto. It occured to me that he was a general, and had a perfectly good ship and he was hiding on the other side of the planet! I argued that he was a coward and was hiding from the fight. Of course, that doesn't even begin to compare to the neuronet threads. Yeah, back then the neuronet would steer for us! I'm pretty sure several comparisons of joysticks to unflattering phallic proportions were thrown around.

That was a long time ago, though. Oh yeah, I remember the agonizing periods that could last for entire seasons where we didn't get a single word of any news from DA or MS. And then the magic moment when you load the front page and theres a new screenshot or some new preview with new information.

Then there was the off-topic forum. All sorts of great stuff happened there. EB, Mad Dog and I would work a jab into every post about Stinger and Boscoe's age. Steamed carrots anyone? Oh yeah. And any discussion I ever participated in with Rheinland Destroyer (where is he)? I swear, we could never agree on anything at all. Ditto for MinisterPhobia and Stinger. In fact, their inability to disagree eventually lead MP to leave the site, only to come back to the sweet embrace of our collective arms. I guess he left again though Wait, is Geek Orgy still around? I guess he was already mentioned, but I have to say resisting Zeratural's communist propaganda was both difficult and entertaining.

I remember being confused when I can't find Denmarkian on Babelfish. I can't remember how this injoke originated, but I think it's pretty easy to figure out... Hmm. What else? Oh yeah. I used to fly around with Stinger, EB, and Mad Dog (and sometimes others) in Freelancer. Havn't done it in a while, obviously. See, I'm a lazy bastard at heart, and it was terribly convenient to just click Formation on Stinger and turn and watch TV, only to come back and occasionally kill a flight of Outcasts or look at the new planet we found or something. Specifically, though, I remember the habit of our guide blindly trusting the autopilot to steer us around planets, only to send everybody trailing him to a fiery death barrelling into the planet at 300 kilometers a second, while goes on his merry way, and I remember *****ing about it a lot afterwards. That ruined FL for me. Even in SP I always use manual fight, even when I'm supposed to be in formation with a transport or something

I also remember my habit of making bigass posts. Oh yeah, hi

Stupid real life, why can't it just go away?
