No no.. You have it wrong. They don't kill to prove they are the best. They kill because they like to shoot things. (any NEO feel free to correct me. I don't think I'm really out on a limb here, though..). It's nothing personal. Your freighter fit the criteria of 'thing' and so it was shot at.

Now I will admit that flying a freighter over a long distance trading run only to be shot before you can drop off your cargo is quite frustrating. However, so is being humbled in a duel, killed by the AI, flying into a planet while alt-tabbed and reading the forum, forgetting to click the 'buy' button for your cargo before making the trip, and a host of other occurances. That's just the way it goes. You can learn from all these mistakes though, and avoid them in the future. In NEO's case, it is as easy as watching the Player group/location list and not flying your freighter right past a pirate-oriented clan member. This minor (and I do mean minor - it's pretty easy) challenge makes the game a little more interesting. Without a challenge, the game quickly becomes quite stale.

To bored players like myself, it is worth flying a freighter through NEO territory just for the rush. I give them full props for making the game a more exciting place to be.

On the other hand, if you want to learn a little something about combat, go engage a NEO pilot in combat (with an eagle or whatever), and figure out why they kill people so well. Pride is the first barrier to improvement. Or if you are a great pilot, go kill one of them and pat yourself on the back. Either way, its sure to be fun.

They like to shoot things. I like to shoot things. You like to shoot things. We all like to shoot things. What's gonna be wrong with that?

EDIT: The 'you go bai' thing was meant as tongue-in-cheek. It's not like i'd expect them to say that, I've just found they're usually pretty direct about things. If you are around them, and you aren't already friendly with them, you can expect to be shot at, or taxed, or whatever based on mood and whim.

If, on the other hand, you are friendly with them, you can probably expect to be shot at based on mood and whim. But at least in this case you can have a good laugh.
