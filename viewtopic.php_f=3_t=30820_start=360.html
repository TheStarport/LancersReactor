Akaiho

Sat Sep 02, 2006 6:26 am by Akaiho

Hi!

I’ve finally read the 19 pages of this topic! Uff... Now I can post my ideas for a Freelacer sequel, if there is going to be one.

About the Universe:

The planets are in fact very small in Freelancer, I think they should be bigger. Also, the distances between planets are very small. These two things are what I’ve been reading in the last 18 pages. I also agree that planets and that stuff should have more accurate sizes. However, if planets have accurate sizes, you will take ages to go from one planet to another. This happens in EVE! The sizes in EVE are accurate, but most of the space between the stations is simply useless! If the sizes are accurate, the systems wont get bigger, because the real activity of those systems is concentrated on small areas where stations are built.

About the docking rings:

I’ve seen people saying that docking rings are stupid and we should be able to land manually passing by the atmosphere, etc. Please remember that the actual NASA space shuttle takes more than 10 minutes to leave Earth! We suppose that in Freelancer those 10 minutes would be obviously reduced. Then what? Even if it takes only half of the time: 5 minutes to go to space! Even if it takes 1/5 of the time: 2 minutes to go to space, when in Freelancer you take 0 seconds if you press the Escape key while the ship is automatically launching. So I still agree with the docking rings.

Customizing stuff:

This idea has been given a few times already, I’m just going to develop it.

The ships:

When we buy a new ship, it comes in its standard default form and colours. While buying the ship, the price should change depending on your reputation, on you persuasive skills to the dealer and on the eventual ups and downs of prices, inflation, etc. The persuasion should be a matter of probability. After buying it, we should be able to paint it, write our own text on it being free to choose colours, effects and everything. Then we should be able to change everything since type of engines, number of engines (we can have 2 engines of one type and other 2 engines of another type), position of engines and wings (this would change the behaviour of the ship), the width and weight of the ship which would change the cargo hold size, position of weapons, and also be able to manage the amount of power that the engines provide to the ship’s weapons, shield and the engines power themselves. We should also be able to add things to the ship like wings to increase stability. After modelling the ship, we should be able to customizing it even more! Isn’t this and RPG? We should be able to choose the materials of the ship. The materials would be cheaper and more expensive depending on the station where you are and depending on your reputation. Different materials would increase or decrease ship’s heaviness and its behaviour in space. Even more than this, we should be able to mix materials on the ships armour and have more than one layer of armour with different materials. While fighting, we should be able to damage other ship’s engines, and even blow other ships with a single shot right on the middle of an engine. I think I have nothing more to add to the ships, the rest is up to Microsoft of whoever makes a Freelancer sequel.

The weapons:

The weapon’s price should depend on the same things I explained on the ship prices. We should be able to modify our weapons, over clocking them for example. If we over clocked them, they would consume more energy per second but would also take much more damage per second. However, we would take the risk that the weapon could overheat too much and explode. This would result in a loss of a weapon and the damage of the ship’s hull. We should be able to choose our weapon’s materials and that stuff..

---------------------------------
Ok at least for now I’m done with ideas. Sorry for my terrible English mistakes at grammar and sentences construction… everything!

Reading nineteen pages! Thats alot, I was thinking about it but I only did about three pages.

What I want is, as mentioned before, a more interesting life as a miner. That is that you can get many of the resources you need by mining. That way you could make your own utilities and ships. This means that you could save game in space, if not including MP...

Being a miner shouldnt be the simpliest thing either, many harsh enviornments demands you to specialize your vehichle and utilities for the exact locatioon and cause. Things like total mass, mass per volume, shape, ect. could be modified ( a bit like in the racing PC game Need for speed U2) giving advantages to the miner in his mining enviornment.

Utilities shouldnt just be a laser gun, an advanced HP system would be inteteresting, allowing utilities like drills and other things that works differently on different surfaces and smelters and the like to be equipped at the ship.

Some people want more up-gradeability at their ships. I think it is important that certain dis-advantages comes with expensive "better" stuff, like they get easily destroyed in harsh enviornment and are expensive to repair to make the game more about strategy, not what you have accomplished before.

I think it is important with realistic physics, especially gravity gets important. Land vehichles should be possible, but making a game too advanced make it boring for new players just searching for adventure. I have myself removed the "linear_drag" from all ships and engines in my FL game to make ships float endlessly in space. Linear drag determines how much the ships get slowed down over time and their maximum speed, making flying very easy for an average FL space sim. Linear drag isnt realistic in open space, but it is realistic in planet atmosphere so the games with realism should start there.

I like the thought of a partly strategic game a bit like Age of Empires (everyone know what I am talking about right?). Just that you are only one person which could do different things like flying small space-ships, foot-travel on planets and also in Astronaut suits in space, and give orders to your company or battleship or just suggest if the situation is democratic or something. You might just start as an "average-Joe" living in a poor society with their inhabitants mining with simple small vehichles in Ice-Asteroid fields which require advanced maneuvering to be money-efficient and then you get your money to do bigger the business.

I hope you liked my ideas
