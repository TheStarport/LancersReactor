Argh

Sat Mar 12, 2005 11:01 am by Argh

I'm using a 6600GT, and I'm not having any problems. That said, everything on this machine is up-to-date- DirectX, Windows XP, and I'm using the current Beta Drivers from nVidia (which also fixed my problems with MS3D crashing when attempting to initialize OpenGL, but that's another story).

Sooooo.... it's not the card, really. Check that your resolution/refresh rates are set to something logical, and look through your perfoptions.ini file, which you can find at:

"Name_of_Your_Windows_Identity" \My Documents\My Games\Freelancer

...or just do a Search of your comp's HD. It has settings for the video there. My settings:

[PerfOptions
-------------------> other stuff above this
general_graphics = 1.00
advanced_graphics = 0.00
default_graphics = 1.00

[Display
size= 1280, 960
color_bpp= 32
depth_bpp= 24
