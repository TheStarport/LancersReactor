Puke Barwalker

Wed Jul 23, 2003 5:16 am by Puke Barwalker

No more game missions after level 18. But you have a total of over 45 systems to discover and explore, 100+ jump holes to find, lots of wrecks to salvage, more powerful ships to buy (when your level is high enough) and even a few surprises to come. To reach the Border World system (including the Outcast and Corsair system, plus some real nasties), you have to go through jump holes. There are no jump gates to the "wild" parts of Sirius.

From now on, you are on your own. Explore, make friends with factions, do whatever you want. Then when you get tired of that, go to mods or MP.
