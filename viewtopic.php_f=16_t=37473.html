A lot of asteroid fields are duds, don't forget. Toggle the Mining filter on your Nav Map and it'll highlight any locations in the system that you've surveyed that you can mine.

Bit of a further spoiler here...

Base your operation on Kyushu. From planet Kyushu, take the jump hole to Tau-23, from there the jump hole to Tau-37. Just park right by the jump hole and pelt Niobium rocks as they float by. That field has a very high yield rate (most rocks will give up Niobium), and the route is a fast one. Just beware the Outcast fighters that thick the area.
