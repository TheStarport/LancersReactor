He has a five o'clock shadow and a calm look in his eye. He´s also pretty muscular. He has a tattoo on his right shoulder and a few small scars scattered around the upper part of his body.

Personal History:

Connor Almasy was born on Denver from Lynn Marcella Almasy-Brenner, an interior decorator who died a few minutes after Connor's birth because of complications and Thomas Reginald Almasy, Executive Vice-President of Ageira Technologies, which has a head office situated on Denver. When Conner was 15 years old, Thomas R. Almasy finally died from an incurable disease he had been struggling with for years. Soon after the funeral, Conner went to live on Cambridge with his uncle, who became Conner´s guardian, because he was still a minor. His uncle, William (Bill) S. He taught Connor everything about the technology of all kinds of spacecrafts (freighters, fighters etc.), including building them. His uncle strangely disappeared a few years later. Not a soul in known space knows what happened to him or the reason for his disappearance. In the meanwhile, Conner had to make it on his own and therefore went into martial arts training to learn the art of hand to hand combat and flight simulation. After months of intensive training, he decided to work for himself and become a Freelancer. He ´acquired´ an Anubis fighter on a remote station in the Border Worldsk, modified it extensively and took on some missions. He quickly learned how Freelancers deal with certain situations " kill or be killed ! ". As his skills grew so did his reputation and kills. Because of that, he was soon known as ´Eraser´, because he mercilessly "erases" his enemies from existence...
