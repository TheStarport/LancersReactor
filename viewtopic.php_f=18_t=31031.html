Sun May 09, 2004 9:02 am by platman

Thanks a lot for the advices and your help, but I finally found what the problem was and had anything to do with my code.
I post here the solution in order to help if someone else has this.
I erase some old bases from universe.ini that exist there, but never used. By doing this, there was an entry in mbases.ini about one of these bases that created the problem.
So take extra care when you decide to change something in your mod.

Thanks again
