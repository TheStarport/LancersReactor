You say you have a router, is it a gateway/firewall/DHCP server AND firewall? A gateway could be accessed through a browser and and settings could be set. A router has no such option as it is a simple switch. If you have a gateway then you can switch off the firewall only if you knowingly enabled something called DMZ, was that done? If you have WinXP it's internal firewall could be on, is that also disabled? It's highly improbable but possible your ISP is blocking certain ports (this happens all the time on college campus' but not with an ISP).

Those ports for DirectPlay (part of DirectX) may not be used until FL connections to a remote server. Hence with the bocking or non-functioning of those ports you would never connect. What am I driving at? Basically update and test to the latest version of DirectX, currently v9.0b.
