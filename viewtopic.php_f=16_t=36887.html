Megistos

Mon Dec 27, 2004 4:08 am by Megistos

Ahh, the classic Loadout question.

Unfortunately for you, the Corsairs are hostile. You should work on getting them friendly again, because one of their weapons, the Tizona Del Cid, is quite possibly one of the most useful weapons in the game. Anyway, some staple gun configurations: (assume all have Ripper Mines, Adv. Countermeasures and either the Hornet or Sunslayer, that's down to personal preference)

1x TDC
1x Cannonball Missile/Salamanca Mk II
4x Nomad Blaster

Straight down the middle, no nonsense firepower. Use the Salamanca if you don't have the funds to finance a Cannonball habit. However, if you don't want to use Class 10s:

2x TDC
4x Wyrm 2/Kraken 2/Salamanca Mk II/Skyblast B (your choice)

This one's a little lower down the Class ladder, but it's easy to use:

2x TDC
4x Sunfrenzy 2

But, as with most aspects of Freelancer, the key is experimentation. Explore, find out which weapons suit your style. For example, do you like the heavy hitting Plasma weapons, the fast Photon weapons, or the tachyon and neutron weapons somewhere in the middle? Just experiment.

Megistos

Mon Dec 27, 2004 7:05 am by Megistos

The Hawaii is in Sigma 19, not 17.

Corsair bribes can be found all over the place. Try Freeport 5 or 9, in Omega 41 and Omicron Theta respectively. Outcast bribes...I'm not sure, I've never taken one. Try Freeport 10 in Tau-37, that's near their territory.

The Hispania is at the centre of 6D in Omicron Alpha. You can't miss it. The Hawaii is orbiting Planet Hiran in sector 6E. Equally, you can't miss it.

For Nomad Blasters and Cannons, you have to be level 34 and kill Nomads. You can go and find them in sector 4F, Omicron Alpha and 2F, Omicron Gamma. There you'll find jump holes which take you to Nomad systems. The reason I recommended the Blaster is that it's easier to use. The Cannon does slightly more damage (30 per second, or thereabouts), but the Blaster has a better refire rate, and they both use zero energy
