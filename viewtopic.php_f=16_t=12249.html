Sun Mar 30, 2003 1:45 am by daddyo

I recommend that you cheat until the missions are over. Then you can go back to normal play. Edit you "PerfOptions.ini" file. Change DIFFICULTY_SCALE = 1.00 to DIFFICULTY_SCALE = 0.00. Voila! No more shield bats or nanos to worry about. You are bullet proof except for Planets. If you run into a planet without a docking ring you are toast as this cheat doesn't make you "completely" invulnerable.
Hope this helps.
