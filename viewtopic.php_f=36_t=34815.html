Hey guys its my first post and the first thing I want to say is that this mod is excellent in scope and dimension(many others have tried, few have succeeded)and I thank the author(s) for all their wonderful content and the time they put in so we may enjoi the fruits of their labor(Thanks guys Now down to business, I will make this short.

1. Proteus Tome
If you are having trouble with the gunboats go into Program files/Microsoft Games/Freelancer/Data/constants.ini and edit the following line:
CRUISE_SPEED = 600
To edit and save this file you will need to go into properties and UN-check READ ONLY.
Now change CRUISE_SPEED to 100.
Now click save.
Done.
This will work on any mod without having to uninstall and re-install(I'm lazy)
After that encounter YOU WILL want to go back into the file and change the CRUISE_SPEED back to 600.

2.(for the pros)
The factions you need to be the coolest with in this mod are the Bounty hunters guild, zoners, and any middle faction(s)i.e. blood dragons, bundschuh, Independent miners guild. What this does is makes you as neutral AS YOUR GONNA GET with the military/police factions so they will bother you as little as possible(depending on the system)although the outcasts still remain somewhat of a mystery for me. I have not gone through every part of the mod yet, but if I find any inconsistencies I will post them and "hopefully" solutions that the layman can understand and implement.
