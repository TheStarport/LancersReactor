Apocalypse89

Sun Aug 08, 2004 3:39 am by Apocalypse89

Welcome to TLR. Hope you enjoy yourself here.(and you will as long as you behave)

Anyways, I recommend the "Evolutions 1.28" mod. I only played a bit of it, but from what I can gather it's almost exactly like original FL except:

1. Completely new ship customization system. Change your ship's power generator, scanners, tractor beam, and much more.

2. All original FL ships have been rebalanced. Light Fighters are now actually faster, VHFs slower, ultra-powerful weapons are removed or extremely hard to get, etc.

3. AI is harder. Their shields will now recharge, and they'll use shield batts as well as nanos. Add the rebalanced ship stats and you've got one difficult AI to fight.

4. Tons of new ships. Some of them are totally new, while others (such as new house ships) use the same concept and designs as original FL ships.

5. EVERY ship in the game is now playable. Transports, Gunboats, Battleships, etc. You name it, this mod has it. But unlike other mods of this kind, the big boys are incredibly expensive.

6. A bunch of new factions and systems, but I'm not sure what they are.

7. Lots of new commodities, the most noticeable are humans(ranging from ejected enemy pilots to tourists) which you take from one destination to another.
