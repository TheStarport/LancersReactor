The French and Italian Fleets were not completely destroyed at the signing of the Balma Treaty.

In the Game Manual Captain Robert Foster of the ANS Reliant,
states that the remnants of the French and Italian Fleets will rendezvous
with their American, British, German, Spanish and Japanese Allies in orbit around
Neptune's Moon Triton to regroup, establish a new command structure and asses the best strategy.

Also in-game you get to fly or read about the following Alliance Squadrons:

205 France -Marauders
503 Italy -Stingers
559 Italy -Hornets

It is also mentioned that Hornets operate from the ANS Roma.

Notice that even though you don't get to see or fly with Spanish
Squadrons, Foster clearly states Spain is part of the Alliance, so
there could be room for other nations to be part of the Western Alliance.

This reinforces the possibility of having at least one Sleeper Ship for
France and other for Italy, which makes perfect sense with Freelancer's rumor about 8 rather than 4 Sleeper Ships trying to reach Sirius. (At the beginning of Freelancer the other 4 Houses are not aware that Outcasts & Corsairs are the children of the Hispania).

So this leaves us with the following Sleeper Ships:

1) Liberty - US - Arrived
2) Bretonia - UK - Arrived
3) Rheinland - Germany - Arrived
4) Hispania - Spain -Thought to be missing - (See note below)
5) Kusari - Japan - Arrived
6) Name? - France - Destoyed
7) Name? - Italy - Destroyed
Name? - ? - Destroyed

The Hispania barely made it to Sirius, due to sabotage....
There was another rumor where it was stated that it wasn't the Coalition,
but rather an agent from Kusari (Japan) who sabotaged the Hispania in order to give the Kusari Sleeper Ship and its population a better chance of finding a habitable and resource rich sector in Sirius to colonize. Ironically this event would mark the beginning of the competition within the Houses of Sirius and effectively terminate the Old Western Alliance.

The 8th sleeper ship still remains a mistery, since no evidence has been found to link it to any specific nation or ethnic group, if we speculate, it could have carried a mixture of citizens from all Western Alliance Nations even if they didn't have a significant military force.

What we do know is that it certainly didn't belong to any of the Eastern Coalition Nations given the fact they ended up victorious over the Western Alliance:

Eastern Republic - Soviet Union + (Warsaw Pact: Hungary, Poland, Romania, etc)
East Asian - China & Korea
Tigris Confederation - Arab + ?
