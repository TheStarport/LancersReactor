Depends on your piloting style- if running around blasting every last AI in the game in 1-3 hits is your idea of fun, go for one of the Class 10 ships. Of those, the Eagle has the maneuverability, the Sabre the firepower, and the Titan the armor.

Maneuverability: Eagle>Sabre>Titan
Firepower(placement of rear turret): Sabre>Eagle>Titan
Armor: Titan>Sabre>Eagle

If it isn't your cup of tea to Pwnz0r everything in your path with little to no effort, I'd suggest one of the mid-level HF's or LF's, such as the Drake, Banshee, Siletto, or Falcon. If you're really the adventurous type, you could always go back and use something like a Starflier or Patriot- every kill is harder, but more rewarding at the end.

Or, if killing just isn't for you, go trade.
