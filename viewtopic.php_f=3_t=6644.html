1. Will you be able to hire people yourself like you want some help on a mission and you hire people to back you up.

No, the mission itself dictates if you will have back-up or not.

2. Will it be posible to when you are just exploring you suddenly hear a destress call and someone is asking for help and when you help him you will get credits/better reputation?

No, it was a neat feature in I War 2 though

3. Will there be a mission editor so people can create there own missions etc.

No, at least not an official one.

4. how can I take screenschots in the demo? (I just got this new ship and it looks very cool in cruising mode it has this HUGE exaust)

Use the print screen button on your keyboard and then use the paste function in MS Paint or another imaging program.

5. Will there be big ships in the full version (with a huge amount of shield power but slow for example will it be like 4 times the size of a normal ship?)

The biggest ships that the player can pilot are frieghters. But there are some very powerful fighters, check out here for a full list of pilotable ships.

6. Will some planets have more then 1 docking point?

No, it doesn't take that long to fly around a planet using cruise engines though.
