TLR web site

I have been using the TLR website for only a couple of weeks, I wish to offer both my thanks and congratulations to all those involved in the maintenance of it, as it is a really good site.

However, could I offer a few suggestions to make it a great site:
(I have searched the forums and can't see a match for the following).

(1) Would it be possible to include a spellchecker on the post topic page?
(2) Reverse the colour scheme of the whole site to a white-ish background with
black or dark blue text? The existing scheme looks trendy but is difficult to read, especially in the small hours.
(3) Increase the page width of forum messages?
(4) Increase the page width of the download pages?
(5) Increase the information carried on the Personal Profile, to include other things like: time zone (GMT+1 or 2 or 4 or 5), town & city.
(6) Increase the font size of the Search Forum page.
(7) Change the Home or Front page to only show the index and not include comments, as this is what new gamers see on entering the site. I suggest this is where simple instructions on how to use the site are posted in plain english, without having to click to a page to read it.
(8) Change the radio button 'Post New Topic' to 'Send'.
(9) On the downloads page, number the different downloads, from 1 being the oldest to 999 being the newest using some sort of automatic number system. This modification would allow us, the users, easier access for when we have to re-enter the site time and time again, only having to remember the mod number, not how far down the page and the title, which changes on a daily basis.
(10) Have a box showing how many gamers/users on the site, to allow users to judge when the best time to download mods etc.

These few suggestions are offered in the hope of easier reading and greater enjoyment of an already good website.

Kind Regards,
Corporal.
