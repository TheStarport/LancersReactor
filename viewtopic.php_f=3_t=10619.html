I was wondering, I've finished the game, explored all there is to explore, bought everything that can be bought and made friends from enemy's and enemy's from friends... twice and vice versa :p
But about the missions/rumors in the bar, is it just all blabla or do they actually have a meaning. Sometimes they say something specific in the bar or on the news like some protected alien artifact site in mahatten, or some ransom that have to be payed/left behind in some debri field.
