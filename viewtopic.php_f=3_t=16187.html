Mining Diamonds is an excercise in futility compared to Niobium. Up in Tau-37 right near the Tau-23 jump hole, you can mine niobium, and expect at least a 50% drop rate, though usually it is 100%. Then, going over the top of Sirius, its a small 20-30 minute trip down to Dresden to sell it for 1600 a pop. Diamonds sell for 1650 at their highest rate, and the length of the run to get to New Tokyo to sell it like that, isnt worth the 20% max drop rate you'll get in Van Rohe. Then, since I'm going over the top area, which takes me through kusari, I buy cheap diamonds when i get to Dresden and sell them in New Tokyo to maximize my profits.
