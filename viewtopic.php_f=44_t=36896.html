why on earth would you want to put it into gmax when he has lightwave? ...lol

Just get miklshape and import the lightwave .lwo and open it up into milkshape, from there you can export it to freelancer cmp. And also conduct all your hardpointing using milkshape. But a word of caution, if you made it in lightwave... did you use uv maps? Or planar mapping.... Cause it will need uv maps for use in other programs.
