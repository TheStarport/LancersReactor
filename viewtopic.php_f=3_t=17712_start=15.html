Looking over the stats again, there is a trade route that has to be the shortest in the whole game. In the New York system, the battleship Missouri is moored right next to the Norfolk Shipyards. Get H3 at the Missouri for $336 and hop right over to Norfolk and sell for $360 then get toxic waste for $10 at Norfolk and goto the Missou and sell for $12! Unbelievable, you stay under the the safety
of the Norfolk's and Missou's guns and no need to worry about a trade lane disruption while you shuttle back & forth between them making profits!
When your done, you can go buy the fighter Defender at the Missou before going on to the next mission. This is a great way to start the game and build quick cash.
