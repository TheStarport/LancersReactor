You could've used the "search forums" option... But I'll answer you anyway...
No, there is nothing else but the strange looking sun in Unknown system off the Omicron Alpha. Except Nomad Interceptors, of course...
In sp campaign, Nomads have weaker weapons, and they drop nanobots & shield batteries, after sp campaign, they drop Blasterc and Cannons (but not the lasers), and you have to be at least lvl 33 to make them drop the goodies...
Nomad weapons do the same amount of damage to all of the shield types, and so do the codename weaps... As for the other weapons, there is a 20% difference -- more efficient against a shield type means 20% more damage the shield takes and vice-versa. But... Because positron shields have more than 20% higher capacity than the other shields of the same class (I even think that the positron shields have the max capacity as the other shields of one level higher), so you should stick to the positron shields.
Good luck hunting for Nomad weapons!
