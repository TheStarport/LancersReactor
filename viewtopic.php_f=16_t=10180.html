It can take many, many tries to get a Rogue bribe in a place like Manhattan. Rochester is a more reliable option but in SP you can simply destroy the police depots near Manhattan. If the 8 pods of the police depots are not enough then continue with the DSE depots. One is right there beside the police depots and there are others near Baltimore Shipyard. If the Rogues are fully hostile then you need 8 police pods plus 5 DSE ones, if I remember correctly.

Nukenin and tai-pan, I have to disagree.

There are fresh newbies every day - which is good since it keeps the community alive - and many of them do not read the FAQ or the stickies even though they could find many answers there. And so they post the same questions again, and again, and again, waiting hours for the answers to trundle in when a quick search of the forums or a glance at the topic list for the last ten days or a look into the sticky posts at the top of some forums would have gotten them plenty of answers in mere seconds. And so it is necessary to gently remind newcomers of these alternative sources, both because these sources can give better and more comprehensive answers quicker, and because it helps to reduce the noise level in the forums.

Another aspect is that the quality of threads about a certain topic is bound to decrease significantly the more often someone feels they have to make a new thread for it. The people who know the answers probably have participated in a dozen threads of the same topic already and are not very likely to chime in, so the newer threads tend to get more input from newbies who don't know much. Thus the posts tend to be of poorer value overall or even downright incorrect, and the information contained in the newer threads tends to be much less comprehensive than in earlier threads.

I also post reminders or even rebukes on occasion (although the ratio to other posts is probably 1:50) but I challenge you to take back the 'lousy pilot' unless you can prove in some way that you aren't.
