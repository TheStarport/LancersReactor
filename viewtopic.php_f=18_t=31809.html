Good for you! First thing to do then, is to read up in the Tutorial section of the site, which should give you a good idea of where to start out modding. Next, if it's ships you aim to design, grab the demo if Milkshape 3d...It's an inexpensive 3d modeling program that is the only one currently able to export to Freelancer's format. It's a 30 day trial, but to purchase the program it's only $25 US i believe. There are also tutorials here on the site for help getting started modeling in Milkshape...Just do a search for Drizzt and you should be able to find his Milkshape tutorial, which is very simple to follow.

Grab Milkshape Here.

Good luck
