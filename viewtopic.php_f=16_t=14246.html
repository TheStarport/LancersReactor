Nightsilver

Wed Apr 16, 2003 11:59 pm by Nightsilver

There's two kinda of Unknown Holes.

The first are the two which are truly Unknown Jumpholes. They are normal Junpholes which lead to to the two "Uknown" systems. Around these, you have a chance of seeing Nomad ships, which can shred anything less than a VHF.

The second kind are jumpholes which are considered Unknown objects. An example is the New Berlin jumphole in Sigma 13, which is only usable during the SP campaign. In MP games, it's labeled as an "Unknown Object" and is not a functioning jumphole (nor does it appear on your targetting scanners or navmap). These hold absolutely no inherent danger, except that you might be happened upon by a wandering partol.
