I have a pretty good one, most likely nothing will be hostile for the majority of the trading. It involves having atleast neutral (better to be friendly) repuation with GC, which most people are.

Planet New London with Luxury Food -> Planet Manhatten, NY
Planet Manhatten with Luxury Goods -> Ainu Depot, Hokkaido (GC)
Ainu Depot, Hokkaido with Cardamine -> Roppongi Station, Tokyo
Roppongi Station with Luxury Goods -> Ainu Depot, Hokkiado
Repeat steps 3 and 4 aslong as you like, its a pretty fast and safe trip

horsewhisperer

Sat May 17, 2003 1:49 pm by horsewhisperer

A good one is engine components from planet honshu ($150) to planet malta ($1350) in the Omicron Alpha system. That's 3 system jumps with no one firing at you if you've bribed to get friendly with the outcasts (you can find that in the bar at freeport 6 in tau-29).
Unfortunately there is no return trade that is profitable, but my friend does a more lenghty trade route that pulls in a reasonable amount fairly quickly.
It goes as follows:

*Honshu: Engine Components ($150) -> Malta: ($1350) -- Profit ($1200/4 gate jumps)

*Malta: Oxygen ($30) -> Ruiz Base: ($60) -- Profit ($30/1 gate jump)

*Ruiz Base: Alien Organisms ($100) -> Shinjuku Station: ($1100) -- Profit ($1000/9 gate jumps minus $100 times 6 to 8 organisms that perish)

*Run form shinjuku to New tokyo or Roppongi station empty (1 gate jump).
Buy Luxury Consumer goods ($810) -> Honshu ($990) -- Profit ($180/5 gate jumps)

That's $2410 profit per unit and a total of 20 gate jumps across 4 systems.
Sounds like a lot, but it isn't really, and your most profitable runs are on the longer routes.

I'm sure there are better trade routes around, but this one netted my friend about 4 mill in 2 hours which is not to be sneezed at (It took that long because I was badgering him to look around a bit).

Anyway, Happy Hunting
