Mustang, For the teasing comment I only ment that the game just wet's my appetite for the full version. Plus I have seen in other posts that some of the PR demo's do not have cockpit struts.. The version I have does.. Flying the Liberty Defender and the struts are right in front of me. I do like them.

As far as things missing, well I'm not sure exactly what DA decided on taking out of the game, but I do wish that there was a Left, Right look button. There is a look behind button by using the " v " key, thats O.K. But I like to look at things as I pass them but from inside the cockpit. You can use the Turret view to Pan around your ship which is cool, but I have not found a zoom in and out feature for that. A zoom feature IMO would help a lot in turret look around mode not the Turret combat mode which is fine. Oh and yes there is a " Storyline" to follow, but after certain points you are free to Freelance to your hearts content.

Of cousrse there may be those features stated above in the full version, I'm not sure. But for all intensive purposes I'm very happy with the game. Hope I have answered your question.
