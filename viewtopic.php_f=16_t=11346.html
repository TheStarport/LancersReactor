Alcatraz Depot is a Rogue base, not a prison, as the namesake implies. It's in an ice field at F5 in the California system. Be careful if the Rogues are hostile towards you, there are lots of them around there.

BTW, there are many things to discover by exploring off the beaten track, meaning off the trade lanes. Most systems have a central star or stars, and it's a good idea when exploring to make a circuit around the system to see what's out there... although there are things inside the various fields/clouds that won't be revealed until you fly around inside them: no risk, no reward.

Zem

Sun Mar 23, 2003 4:43 pm by Zem

When searching for bases, put your scanner on "Solars". This will show all dockable locations that you've found. The base will show up there when you're close enough. Also, use the Nav map (F6). Select "Patrol Path Map". If you know you're looking for a neutral base, select "Neutral" in the upper right corner of the system map. This will show you the patrol paths of neutral ships. A base will have several patrol paths originating from the same spot. If you're close enough, you'll see a bunch of patrol paths converge on the same spot. Fly there and you'll find either a base or a jump hole.

If you're looking for wrecks, put the scanner on "All". Check the nav map periodically to be sure you haven't missed it. Wrecks will show up as red "X"s.
