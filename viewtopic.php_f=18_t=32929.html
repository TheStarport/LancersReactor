As for the stuff in the market files:

1 defines it as a sellable buyable good? I'm not sure
2 is the nickname given by the Goods.ini file
3 Level required to purchace the item
4 reputaion level required to purchace item (on a scale of -0.9 to 0.9)
5 still testing
6 still testing
7 Basically determines if the base sells the item (0) or just buys it (1)
8 is the cost multiplier

For 5, 6, and 7, I've done some testing and found some really really cool stuff. 5 and 6 basically determine how much of the item is available for sale at once (though it seems to not actually restrict you in-game), and in some experiments I've done, I've found out some really cool stuff. Item number 7 seems to determine if there are price reductions when you sell the item (like when you sell a gun, you only get 1/3 the value, etc). I'm still fiddling with the 5, 6, and 7 values, but it's low priority so I haven't done any experimenting in about 3 months (back when I was a modding noob =)
