M-A-C-E_166,

From you description of a mass of hard points "they all appear in one lump in the middle and none of the textures show"..... it could be a problem of scale.. if all your weapons etc are in a small bunch floating (or partly buried) then it usually means that the ship is too small.

Try this.. Import the pilot.. then scale your ship TO HIM in milkshape, delete the pilot.. and scale the model by a factor of 100 in milkshape.. scale should be close then.
