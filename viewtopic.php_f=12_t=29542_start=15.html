From what I have seen ....

"Lee" is the western spelling. "Li" is the official spelling, using the roman alphabet, as determined by the Government of the P. R. China

Other examples: "Chin" Dynasty is now the "Qin" Dynasty, "Hsia" Dynasty is now "Xia" Dynasty.

Both spellings are intended to represent the same sounds and pronuciation but one is the official spelling method of the Beijing Govt. The other is unofficial "western" spelling.
