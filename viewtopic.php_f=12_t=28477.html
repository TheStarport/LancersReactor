It's a naff problem gromit and you should be spanked severly for asking it.
Mathematical trickery at it's best and a right bastard to answer but i'll give it a go.

You cannot add what the bellhop took, you can only subtract it. So dividing up the 30 dollars:

The hotel has $25, ($30 - $25 = $5)
The 3 men have one dollar each, or $3 total ($5 - $3 = $2)
and the bellhop has $2 ($2 - $2 = $0)

So there is no missing dollar.
