It's been my experience that groups that are friendly to you will assist provided the group attacking you is not a stated ally. Thus, getting attacked by Hogosha with Blood Dragons as allies will get you one or two Blood Dragons joining the fray against the Hogosha. However, exact opposite is true with attacking a group that your neutral with but they are friendly with your opponent. I've had GC's turn up, attack with neutral BD's in the background, after killing a few GC's, the BD's turned against me. It does make sense if you think about it. The enemy of my enemy is my ally. Heck, the Lane Hackers even said that during the SP mission when they come to your aid after fleeing Liberty.
