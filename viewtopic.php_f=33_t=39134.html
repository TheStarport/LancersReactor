Thu May 26, 2005 7:40 am by Ro9ue

No, you cannot reattach engines once you unmount them. FL only recognizes a ship as needing one engine, not more than one. So, yes, the only way is to buy the ship again.
But there is not a limit to the number of engines you carry. You can carry 5 different engines in your hold if you want, them mount/unmount them (one at a time) at will.
