A general note:

Most of the fields in the list is self-explanatory, but some require clarification

In the Loot field, if the name of the weapon is followed by an asterisk, that
means that only the ammunition for that weapon type is available on the
wreck, and the actual launcher is not available. The most common example
are the Paralyzer missiles you will find in a lot of the Bounty Hunter wrecks.
Conversely, if there is no asterisk, you may assume that the launcher comes
with the ammo you are picking up.

I only include the WARNING field if *immediate* hazards are present. For
example, if the wreck is located in the middle of a mine field, that I'll note
that there are MINES!! present. However, if the wreck itself is in the clear,
but you have to go through a mine field to get to it, then I won't be mentioning
the mine field! In other words, I'll give you a heads up as to immediate
hazards...but getting there is totally up to you.

Also, notice that I did not mention any potentially hostile forces flying
around the wrecks. That is because most of the wrecks lie close to
patrol paths of various factions, so unless you are friendly with all the
locals in a given system, expect them to take pot-shots at you while you
are trying to loot the wreck.

Finally, if I did not mention a system in the list, then that system has
no wrecks.
