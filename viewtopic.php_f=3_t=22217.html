Nukenin

Wed Jul 30, 2003 12:52 pm by Nukenin

Guns are easier, but if you put your mind to it, missiles can be quite effective--you just need to know where to place the missile you're using.

On a lark the last time I went through SP, in my Starflier I mounted a Javelin Launcher on the 3rd Starflier hardpoint, and learned how to best use the dumbfire missile. First, even the "dumbfire" missiles have homing ability, they just don't turn as well as the seekers. Second, if you hit a ship square on with a missile, it disintegrates--the missiles seem to do up to three times their rated damage if you score direct hits.

My best success with the dumbfires was firing them ahead of an enemy who was turning to face me. If I'd already dropped his shields with guns, the missile was usually a guaranteed kill.

Alas, in the later stages of the game, the expenditure for the more powerful missiles you'd want to use really can add up, so most folks would just slap guns on those hardpoints.

They really should have put missile launcher only hardpoints on the various ships, or make the cruise disruptor/torpedo hardpoints also capable of mounting normal missile launchers.

Mines can be very effective--even if all they do is cause your opponent to jink to avoid. Still, the expenditure at the high end ($7K+ per mine) means you probably don't want to turn every mission area into a "minefield".
