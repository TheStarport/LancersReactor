If you can't beat Hovis the first 2 times you race, check your ship. If it is a "Very Heavy Fighter" or "Frieghter" class ship, (or something of the sort) load your autosave when you loose to Hovis for the second time, go to the ship dealer on the Hood and buy the ship he has. Outfit it as you see fit, you won't have time to re-equip the weapons. Race Hovis again, you should win. I hope this helps
