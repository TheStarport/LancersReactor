Wilde

Mon Jul 11, 2005 2:49 pm by Wilde

I already explained this to Konn...and hoped that he would pass on the message to you.

This isn't an ego ploy by me to make Damien into a messiah. The point of the book was that without it Damien would feel his sacrifice useless and pointless, because he would have died to save a race that in his opinion in its present state barely deserved to be saved. He would have saved the shells of existence, but the true problem from his perspective lay not in threat to physical health and life, but rather in that humanity had confounded itself in an ocean of suffering. Creating the book wasn't his attempt to bloat his name in history, he couldn't care less about that. It was an act of compassion to pass on the forgotten wisdom of previous sages to humanity.
