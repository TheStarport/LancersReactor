I've never heard of an ATI Radeon x800 but with 256 MB I assume it's been out within the last few years.

Sometimes the newest drivers, especially video drivers, do not cut it with games old and new. Try going to the next older drivers, the ones released before the latest were released. I had to do this once because the latest video drivers were not WHQL certified but the next earlier version were - they worked.

If that doesn't work try installing/reinstalling DirectX. Or ratching down some of the features of the video card like hardware acceleration. Any of those little tweaking actions that may bring it on line when you least expect it to. Good luck.
