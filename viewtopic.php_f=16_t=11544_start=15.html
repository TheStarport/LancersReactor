Personally I like using missles, and after tweaking them I like them even more. Now I don't have to save them for a close pass in order to score a hit.

Jav (and similar) missiles move at 120 m/s (was 90)and turn at 4.355(was 2.903)
Stalker (and similar) missiles move at 140 m/s (was 99) and turn at 6.5325 (was 4.355)
EMP missles move at 125 m/s (was 90) and turns at 4.355 (was 2.905)

Also changes the hull points on the missles, now I don't need to worry about 1 missle destroying the other missiles I fire.

Not sure if I'm going to alter them further, I haven't play-tested these settings enough yet.

Oh, and due to the way the ammo is managed for the missile launchers, I wouldn't reccomand carrying more than 2 of the same type.
