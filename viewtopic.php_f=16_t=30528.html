Freelancer Equipment Calculator 1.0 **''MASSIVE SPOILER**

Just finished my Freelancer Equipment Calculator's first version. I downloaded Weapon List Team's Freelancer Weapon List and it was cool, but it missed some things. So I made a similar Excel calculator thingy with more advanced features, some of them are listed here:

-Get a list of all ships, guns, turrets, missiles and shields and information about them
-Pick up a ship, weapons and shield you like, calculator does the rest
-See if all the equipment are mountable or not
-Check how long you can shoot with the weapons before all energy is drained
-Hull/Shield Damage / second / volley / shooting time
-Total destruction / second / shooting time
-Destruction in 3, 5, 10 and 15 seconds
-Average fire rate
-Automated shield recommendations
-Weapon speed matching
-Summing all up in a form of rating to see how good your ship, weapons and shield are
-And more

Download:
http://koti.mbnet.fi/mika_j/equipmentcalculator.zip

Read the readme.txt file to use it right.


----FOUND BUGS-----
-Valkyrie has only 3 class8 hardpoints, not 4, will be fixed in the next version
