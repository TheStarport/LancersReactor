Browclops server shutting down August 1

I know two weeks is not alot of time to prepare, but I will be turning off the Browclops server August 1, 2003. I need the server for real company work during the second half of the year. I will be turning off new account creation tonight during the reset. I can email your character folders to you if you wish to move to another server. I have thoroughly enjoyed playing with all of you these past 4 months and regret that I am unable to provide this outlet for the rest of the year. I have tried to find an alternative to simply shutting down but cannot. There is a great deal more I would like to say but if don't press the submit button now I may never, and I must. I am indeed sorry.

Browclops
