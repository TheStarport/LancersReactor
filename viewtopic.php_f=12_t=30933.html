Google make the majority of their money from selling "top ranking positions". Speaking as someone who has to try to improve rankings on a day to day basis, I can tell you that its pretty near damn impossible to get into the top ranking of a search unless you fork out some money to the search engine themselves. Now, I pay them about £1500-3000 a year to get my sites noticed...and I know at least 9 other people who do the same, so from 10 people alone they make £15,000-£30,000 a year. Now bear in mind that there are something close to 50,000 "professional" web designers in the uk alone. Are you beginning to understand how they're making their money yet? Thats a figure of £75million-£150million a year from UK web designers alone.

Then of course there are the unbelievable amount of sites that offer google searches through their own websites - Yahoo! being the biggest reseller. So taking all of that into account, and the fact that they are one of the oldest and probably THE most used search engine on the net, I'm just completely surprised that its not more money than that.
