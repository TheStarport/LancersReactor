Titanite

Thu Mar 27, 2003 5:21 pm by Titanite

Strange, I prefer to go for the gunboats first... at max speed they just about move away from you, so you can conveniently tap afterburner every other few seconds just to keep up -- this will, incidentally, throw off the aim of your pursuers. After whacking the gunboats, the bombers appear, they're pretty easy as they're usually aiming at something else. Usually.

Finally, when they're done, wipe up the fighters. Yes they hurt, but I noticed don't panic straight away when you get that "shields down" warning -- your armor, strangely, holds up much better. When you take one down, afterburn away, run around the field a bit to let your shields go up some points, before engaging the next target.
