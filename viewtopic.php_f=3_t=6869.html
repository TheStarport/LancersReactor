Unlock your FP demo - 2MB download

Hey, all, I figured most of you playing the demo wouldn't want to have to connect to Fileplanet everytime you want to play. So since I downloaded both versions of the demo, I had a chance to compare them. And I quickly found a way to change the FP version to the unlocked version. This should work on WIndows XP; if someone else is running a different version and knows where the files discussed below are stored please update.

1. Go to C:\Documents and Settings\User\Local Settings\Temp\, replacing "User" with whatever username was active when you installed Freelancer.
2. Open the tempinstall folder.
3. Copy the 021803_release_1050_Idemo_ver20 folder to your desktop.
4. Download this file: http://67valiant.com/freelancer/Freela_1.exe. Save it on your desktop.
5. Open the 021803_release_1050_Idemo_ver20 folder (on your desktop)
6. Open the game folder.
7. Open the Exe folder.
8. Overwrite the existing Freela_1.exe file with the one you just downloaded.
9. Remove Freelancer and EZPad.
10. Reinstall Freelancer from the setup inside the 021803_release_1050_Idemo_ver20 folder on your desktop.

Congratulations! You're done!
