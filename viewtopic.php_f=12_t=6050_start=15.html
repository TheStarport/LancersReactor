@Whopee
You should try it!

As I pointed out it is totally different to FL. But it has its own merrits - and especially Dogfighting and Teamplay + complexity make for a long-lasting game-experience.

I know the install and getting it to run for the first time is a bit tedious... but its worth it.
That is due to the massive upgrades Patches and new factions the Game got over the years. The community is very active (creating new factions, Patches etc.).

1. Download the allready patched version (important for it inludes the connection to the new Auto-Update and Lobby) - after that the Auto-Update will update to the newest Version.
2. Download and install the little SOV-Login program and create an account with your Pilots callsign/ID (this was developped after M$ shut down their official servers - the program serves to keep people from spamming/flaming/constantly changing names etc.)
3. start SOV-Login it will ask for your password and automaticly launch Allegiance, connecting you to the lobby (there you can chose a server)

The community will be helpfull to you - tell them that you just started - they will give advice!

The links:

1. http://www.allegiancehq.org/portal/portal_content.asp
- there you will find all kinds of infos further links and downloads + the "official" forum and guides of all kind

2. http://www.alleg.net/
there you will find the download and help getting started!

3. http://www.german-borg.de/
the homepage of my squad, you will find usefull infos, reviews, guides, a forum where people will and can help you getting started (the page is in Englisch, the forum Englisch and German the squad (clan) is international by now (Euro/US/Canadian).

@the unfriendly remarkers
please feel free to verify you opinions! And please dont give devastating judgement on something you have not - or not recently experienced!

We are all FL fans (and eagerly waiting for FL for years! - I was actually waiting for FL when Allegiance came up three years ago - as were many of the other Allegiance players)
