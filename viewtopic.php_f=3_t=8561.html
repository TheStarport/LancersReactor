guptasa1

Sat Mar 08, 2003 3:17 am by guptasa1

I'll admit I'm playing Devil's Advocate a bit here. I have no problem with customizable controls - in fact, I'm a big fan of them and a big fan of joystick support as well.

BUT, my theory is the developers limited customization of the mouse and eliminated joystick support entirely because they wanted people to try their control scheme. The reason? Because for this game and its style, I think they realized they had a much BETTER control scheme.

I would imagine that had the game shipped with joystick support, even though the developers recommended mouse and keyboard, people would immediately configure their joystick for it before even trying their scheme (I know - I would have too), and then there would be a lot of people complaining about how bad the interface is and how hard it was to juggle a joystick and mouse at the same time. I can just see the "What were they thinking?" posts now.

Yeah, it might have been nice if the mouse was a bit more customizeable, but I think the default scheme for it works very well, and I believe the keyboard is completely customizable. I would imagine (just my opinion) it'll become almost natural once you're accustomed to it.
