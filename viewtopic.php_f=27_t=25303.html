Tygrys

If you have mod installed and some equipment from it and then you uninstalled the mod, that means you are in a big trouble. Missing engine is a light case here. Avoid messing with mods on MP at all cost, unless the server uses the same mod, in which case you have to install it too.

BTW, please use more informative topic for your question.
"Need help" or "Can you answer" will not direct anyone interested in particular mod to that thread. Good topic here is :"Missing engine after uninstaling the mod" or "... Massive Addon mod"

Have fun,
Tygrys
