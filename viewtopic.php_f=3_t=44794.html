I'm at work at the moment, so it makes it hard to search, but there is a wingman mod out, som 3-4 months that will let you hire wingmen.

Edit - Found it! Advanced NPC Wingmen Movie submitted by ww2jacob

Created as a cooperative project between the Phoenix and 88 Flak mod teams, the Advanced NPC Wingmen Movie is an incredible concept, and is one of the most fun developments ever made for Freelancer. The way it works is that if the player buys a piece of equipment or a commodity called the "Wingman Ticket", and is in the right place, a patrol of four NPC's will follow the player. They will follow steadily with cruise speeds of up to 650, and will thrust when the player thrusts. They will assist the player in combat, and will also fight enemies that they see on their own.

However, they will always return to the player after combat is completed. They even go through tradelanes with the player! However, they will not follow through jumpgates and jumpholes, and will dismiss themselves if the player docks or logs off.



Multiplayer only at this point.
