core for a future Crusade mod

As I am still unsuccesfully trying to convert ships from FreeSpace: The Great War to FL, I present at least the core universe of the mod. Already giving some trivial things like fleets guarding border gates, new ships (multitorpedo bombers, some enhanced fighters and also majority of capital ships, can be found on shipyards) and also a little surprises in Alaska, Tohoku and near Freeport 9. It's a SFX, but my site is enough protected . Enjoy!

http://imperium.nazory.cz/if/THE MOD.exe


Update: it seems that mod will include Apollo, Valkyrie, Medusa, Hercules, Ulysses and Ursa of terran, Manticore and Nephilim of shivan and Anubis of vasudan ships. Also expect FS guns and missiles, as well as bombs. I'm not sure if I'll place their flagship to some of my own systems, worse is that she won't be Galatea...yet.
