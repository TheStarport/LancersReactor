Here goes:

Eagle - lvl 10 shield

Loadout:

2 Tiz (tizona del cids for newbs )
2 cannonball launchers
2 nomad blasters (fire rate matches the tiz - as does shot velocity!)
1 sunslayer torp
no mine
adv countermeasure ( a must)
adv thruster

Okay - firstly - i fly with total engine kill and thrusters. Why? With no engine heat, and a couple of counter measures - no other torps, missiles etc will hit you . It also happens to be best for moving.

Second. Two blasters - matching fire rates and projectile speed with tiz = maximum damage PER shot on impact. Without matching fire rates you can get two shots firing after others - and missing if not continually in the line of fire. Therefore - this maximises damage. Also - your recticule won't be shot to hell by having different shot velocities! Also - i have these guns as it means after all those missiles and torps have been used - i can still fight on and HAVE won!


Third. The missiles and torps usually mean no-one survives the first pass against me. If they do - the second kills them!
I strafe on approach under thrusters - shooting guns - missiles are firelinked. I start about 1k away with the fire, as soon as near and their shields are damaged enough - break and thrust away. The remaining shots flying towards them kill sheilds and then the cannonballs blow them to hell and back.

I have fought and won against anywhere up to 9 opponents at once. I have taken down anywhere up to 16 players in a fight before i karked it. I fought them all in eagles/ sabres with the same loadout, or maximum guns loadouts too - and caned them. To put it mildly - i cannot remember the last time i lost a duel. I can remember fighting so many people that the screen was filling up with 'a player has died'.
There IS a tactic of WHEN to launch things for maximum effectiveness - and it DOES take time and practice.

I can use guns, and often win in gun duels with that loadout too. I have nothing against gun only loadouts, but honestly - they cannot take out large volumes of player opposition in eagles/sabres unless they have torps and missiles.

I don't claim to be the best pilot, and i am not the best pilot. But i can say that when in a fight, i often level the playing field quickly and easily. I am good at fighting - especially when the odds are stupidly against me, and in duels i just don't lose..............lol

Navigation_Boy

Thu Jan 29, 2004 12:40 pm by Navigation_Boy

Yeah. Here's the thing. The CD travels fast. It hits the enemy ship and creates a nice explosion with a nice big fat radius. Any munitions *live* within that radius are detonated. Therefore, when you hear "incoming missile", and fire a CD right away, it zings to the enemy ship at up to 800 m/s and detonates, catching any emerging missiles/torps/mines IF it was fast enough to get there before those munitions left the blast radius. Typically, If you are within 350 meters of the enemy or so, and have good reaction speed, you can detonate any single munition he fires, by shooting your CD. On the other hand, if he's smart, he's spamming you with CD's too, and you can't tell the cballs from the CD's.

Never feared a torpedo. Ever. Actually, I'm happy when people are packing sunslayers, because I make them eat them on their own shields. If they were firing missiles, they could use cruise disrupters to spam out the missile warning so I don't know when to counter with a CD. But with a torpedo, they don't have that luxury. Torps will thin out the newbs really fast, but a good pilot with a CD will eat the torp users for breakfast.

Besides. Cballs have all the hull killing power of a sunslayer (insta-kill), turn 5 times as fast, and allow you to still mount a CD.
