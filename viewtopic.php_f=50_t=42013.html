Yes, you can make new Cityscapes. They're just CMPs like ships are, really. The trick is getting the scripts written, for things like landing sequences. These are THN scripts. Please see the THN scripts included with my XML Toolkit Mod series, the previous versions of Freeworlds... or check out the Freeworlds development Forum, where they talk about the new landing scripts they've been writing.

Argh

Mon Dec 05, 2005 10:24 pm by Argh

DA built them in parts for two reasons:

1. It made it possible for them to do animations. Kind've hard to have animated trains in New London if they aren't seperate objects. Yes, they did certain types of animation within the models themselves, but for the most part they did it with scripts.

2. It made it possible for certain types of tricky camera things to be done, without building entire cities "in the round". If you look at the way they handled a bunch of things like landing sequences... they often did some fairly fancy things with the relative positions of objects to play tricks with viewers.

Basically, though, you're entirely correct. I've built new Rooms, and they work just fine. No reason why Cityscapes couldn't also work very easily.
