Wilde

Sat Dec 11, 2004 11:47 am by Wilde

@ Dragonborn: Codenames were outlawed in the first one, so I don't know if we'll be able to incorporate them this time around. The idea's not bad tho...I suppose the GMG would be rolling in them--Sigma 13 has about 6 different codenames. Omega 11 has 1, Omicron Alpha has a few...Capital ships tho can't mount codenames, only fighters can.

There are a bunch of rules on the cyof site (Click here) about how to steal cap ships. Basically you send say 15 LF wings to a small station guarded by 10 LF wings and 1 gunboat. If all defending units are destroyed except for the cap ship you're trying to steal, you get a random number roll at pirating it--10-7 failure, 6-1 success. Afterwards you have the design for that capital ship and can build it as long as you have a cap shipyard.
