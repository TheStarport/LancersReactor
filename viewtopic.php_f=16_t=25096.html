Liko

Thu Oct 09, 2003 8:41 pm by Liko

First and foremost, make sure you are above lvl 35. They will not drop weapons period if you're under 32, and the probability of a drop increases with each level Do some missions, make some money, and boost yourself up the ladder. Another tidbit I've heard is that the Unknown system out of Omicron Gamma is better for getting weapons, and it's a more hospitable environment to boot. I've only hunted there, and I can usually collect enough for whatever I'm doing (changing loadout, selling weaps) in a couple hours.

Secondly, how many account holders on the server have nomad weaps? I've heard that there is a soft limit on the number of salvaged nomad wepons available in the game, such that once the limit is reached the drop probability goes way down. It's not impossible to get them in a populated server, but it's much easier on smaller servers.

Third, once you have a couple of nomad guns loaded, killing nomads can be done faster. The less time you spend firing at a target (any ship in the game, really), the more chance you have for an equipment drop. Remember, EVERY piece of equipment that isn't ammo or nanos/batts gets fixed to the outside of the ship. Fire directly at a piece of equipment and you damage it. Damaged pieces stand a far reduced chance of surviving the destruction of the ship. So in summary, kill them as quickly as possible.

Fourth, make sure you're not firing at the explosion. When a nomad is almost dead, fire a shot at a time and make sure it has hit or missed. If you fire through the explosion, chances are really good you'll destroy a pickup, and nomads only drop one item per ship.

Lastly, it will take time. From each group of four, I get an item maybe a quarter of the time (the math works out to about a 1 in 16 chance a particular nomad will drop a gun). They seldom drop two items per group, and I've only gotten three once. If you just can't get them to drop and you're starting to think it's not worth the time, it probably isn't.
