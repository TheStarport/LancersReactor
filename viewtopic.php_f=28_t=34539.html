Level2 1.44 Full Server side Release

Sunday 5th of September 2004

Hello ALL!
The Mod has come to an end, but a good one! Theres a sequal in the works that will showcase lots of new things, as well as elements from New Universe (our other premier mod) it will prequel this mod and will be called Level 2: Prelude to War - it's been developed by us her at Samurai Studios.

There is one final release of Level 2 though - version 1.44 FULL - full becuase it finally includes the server side files. Please make sure your server adheres to the rules set forth on this website, and that you do not modify the mod in any way. Also the mod may not be used as part of, or basis of any other mod. Please read the readme!


v 1.44

New Updates

- Includes the Player loot Drop code: THanks a bunch to Wasabe & LordfJord, you guys are awesome and true freinds.

-Server side now included

-New Redone Alaska System

-Redone Merc Weapons, given new effects and completely redone

-Several changes made to new fighters from 1.43, new ships have been rebalanced

-3 New Ships have been added to the game:

"Olympus" Corsair VHF (Titan Mk 2)

"Condor" Civilian VHF (Eagle Mk 2)

"Raven" Civilian LF - Dedicated to Raven

-New "EMP" Mines that damage shields. (suggestion from forums)

-Wreckages through out Sirius have been found with this strange new emp mine technology...

-New Outcast Base, undisclosed location.

-Scythe has been moved to new Outcast base. (the one mentioned above)

-Two new Intro Animations replacing 2 old ones (its not possible to create NEW ones, you can only replace the old ones. The volcano, and gas miner animations were replaced by a water planet intro that comes with the game but is unused, and a new one made by Darkblade.)
-Mercenary Empathies redone, they no longer turn hostile after the destruction of a few ships.

-Bribes for redone United Mercenaries Guild now available on bases in system with Outreach, + new rumors.

-Redone ship infocards, including descriptions, and they now match the actual ship specs.



You can download the mod here and dont forget to check out the readme & rules.

THE ioncross FILES can be found here: http://www.samuraistudios.net/upl/gamedata.zip

Roland "Hobbes" Orr
Founder, Samurai Studios
