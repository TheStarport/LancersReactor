Firewing

Sun Feb 15, 2004 4:14 pm by Firewing

I suggest using Lithium Unwrap - the best program for texturing out there...

It is very simple and supports practicly every model format out there(3ds, lum, obj, ms3d...etc...)

You basicly make a render of your ship looking at it from the top, left/right, botom, fron and back.(NOTE: if the ship is symetrical, then you need only half of it, since you can mirror the Uv mapping). Edit that Render in Photoshop and add detail..

Then the program will devide your model into views from sides(depending on which poly is best percived from which side, but will retain the ship's shape!)so you can easily just drag&drop(allso stretch and rotate..to fit) your model top view to your texture part showin the fighter from the top...etc...
