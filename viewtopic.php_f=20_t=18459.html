You really should try to lok at the myriad older threads on exactly the same topic, to see if they apply, also state what you've tried to date, to save others wasting their time repeating what you already know.

The 44.03 openly state that they are tuned to improve appearance and not performance. Which they have.

I had same problem (all posted at LR). In my case i beleive i had duff (unbranded) RAM, i purchased and swapped my RAM for 512 of Twinmos (only £10 more on Ebay), and put the unbranded stuff in bank 2. I also upped the voltage in Bios by .2v to 2.7.

These two had the biggest improvements and i've gone from freezing every 10 mins or so, to nothing in about 2 weeks.
