Cold_Void

Fri Sep 07, 2007 6:25 am by Cold_Void

all textures including tgas must be 64X64, 128X128, 256X256 etc - make sure to select 24 bit uncompressed for your targa format. also, my 2 cents: don't use targas - they suck-suck-suck. use DDS, and the headaches are reduced greatly (unless you're making transparencies, which is harder with DDS's not-so-well supported format)

failed to get start location sounds like an openSP error, unrelated to the texture problem other than it's the last thing the program can say before it crashes completely due to a bad format texture.

MIP0-1-2-3 etc is for TGAs
MIPS is for DDSs

use the mat exporter - its much simpler
