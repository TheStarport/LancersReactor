Caesar

Tue Apr 06, 2004 10:04 am by Caesar

There's a sudden drop in the amount of Outcast and Corsair activity in the border worlds, people regard it as good news... for a while...
One Bounty Hunter patrol pursues a Corsair through the Omicron Theta jump hole and discover that more has been happening around the border worlds than previously thought - Overrun with the tendrils of a Nomad fleet coming through the jump holes known only to freelancers and these pirates, systems are falling to the might of this alien strength.

With the Outcasts abandoning Omicron Alpha, the cardamine supplies in the rest of Sirius are dwindling. Criminals groups are becoming more of a threat as the addicted populations of the major systems join up with these pirates for the chance of capturing a shipment of the drug.
With crime on the rise, and the Nomads beginning to advance again, the military powers of Sirius unveil their latest developments. Heavy Fighters, Crusiers, Dreadnoughts, joint ventures by the Houses in the event that the arch-enemy would return, are put into service.

In the Omega systems, ships have been spotted, long range vessels, not of Nomad design, more... human... but too advanced in their nature to have been constructed by anyone in Sirius...

And in the distant system of Omicron Minor, the Order are amassing on Planet Toledo, ready to contact one of their most couragous pilots that fought alongside them the last time. Edison Trent.
