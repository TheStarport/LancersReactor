Infinity Shipyards___MOD

Okay I just wanted to make an offical Infinity shipyards thread. (Gibby or EB could you make this a sticky for me..pleeeaase.)

First of I want to thank everyone who has contacted me so far, its good to get as much feed back as possible.

This mod is designed simply to put new custom ships into freelancer. All the ships are going to be original ones, not from any films or tv series. Straight from the minds of sci-fi and gaming fanatics. The mod will place either new shipyards into the existing systems or to create a whole new system with several shipyards. In those shipyards you will be able to spend your hard earned credits on some of the finest ships ever created. we have some great moddellers on the team.
We also plan to add in a few more secrets and other mods in with it as we go. So thats the plan. and to buisness

we are also in the process of creating the website and I would like to ask a few people to beta test out our online ship viewer. To use the ship viewer you will need to download a small file from here
3d viewer you need to download the file called MS3DViwereOCXSetup1001.exe . you also need direct X9 or newer so if you need that download that from microsoft.com. however this should have come with your retail version of freelancer.

once you have installed these files click on the link below and please give me your feed back.
