parabolix

Mon Jan 09, 2006 1:02 pm by parabolix

Sorry about the delay...Those of you who know me know I've been dealing with some real life issues...

First off, thanks to everyone who took the time to comment on the mod. We really do read these and appreciate the comments you leave.

@ Hop Scotch:

Hermes...We might be able to fix bases to add mooring points for large ships to dock at. As of now, that's the only solution I see.

You mentioned the cold fusion generator...A couple of the powerplants in our mod have very high thruster regen rates, meaning you can thrust endlessly. This topic has come up alot recently and we are working on rebalancing the powerplants and thrusters to resolve this issue.

Drops have been increased a fair bit already, but in order to get the best weapons you can get, you need to be at a high level. The higher your level, the better the drops. I won't give you specifics, but I've come out of fights with a ship full of level 8 and 9 weapons that sell for $40,000 and up.

Equipment placements will be adjusted in upcoming releases. The previous version was rushed and so placements of equipment aren't as good as can be. Rest assured, they will be revamped later.

Enigma's shield...The Enigma isn't the only ship with this issue. This comes from making ships before we had many shields. We have added more shields now, and will add even more in the future, so we can fix issues like that.


@ Techdaire,

Good to see you solved your problem. You should always move your saves (or delete if you don't need them) before playing a new mod.

Prof Quasar,

It's been a while. Hope all is well. The positive comments are definitely appreciated and quite humbling.

We have been talking for quite some time about implementing the SP Campaign into the mod, players have also expressed approval of that idea. We could rescript this mod around the SP campaign, but writing a new storyline would be incredibly difficult and take years to complete. Maximum player level can also be raised as you mentioned.

As the mod expands and new systems open up, there will of course be much more room for indepth additions to the mod such as huge missions with massive payoffs. Bribes will of course be expanded, along with rumors and base populations. Traps could be done, but only to the extent of killing the player. You couldn't make the player lose their weapons/equipment. The way FL works, if you die, you instantly get your equipment back on respawn. In MP however, you would lose all your spare equipment that wasn't directly attached, so traps might be a road we pursue further.

@ Sabatino,

When you launch from Crete in a Nightmare the game crashes? I haven't heard of this before, but haven't tested this yet, I will look into the issue and see if I can find a culprit.
