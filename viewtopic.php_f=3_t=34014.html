When you bring up the Nav map in the top right of the pop up box there are icons. Hover the mouse point over them and the will tell you what the lines mean. The ovals are either political areas or mineable areas. The lines indicate patrol paths. Red are hostile paths, green is allied paths, and white is neutral paths.
