As both FriendlyFire and myself have pointed out in your other thread on this subject, the "startupscreen.tga" needs to be 800 x 600 in size and saved as a 32 bit RGB uncompressed Targa (TGA).

A bad or missing TGA will not cause a crash, a crash when you should be seeing the startup TGA is INI based, bad market, equipment, effect or faction files are just some of the possible reasons your crashing there.
