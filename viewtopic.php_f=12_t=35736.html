Privateer Remake Beta released

I just thought that I should mention that the "Wing Commander Universe - Privateer Remake" Beta has just been released to the public. Previously, it was only available by emailing the creators, but it is now publicly available (you can find the site here (86 MB )). The remake uses the "VegaStrike" engine which is included in the remake (click here if you are interested in the original game), and looks promising, especially considering the excellent graphical quality of the VS engine. The remake also features the music and voicework from the original game as well as the plot, so it should be just like the real thing (more or less). I have just downloaded it, and am about to give it a try. If you decide to download it, please share your impressions.
