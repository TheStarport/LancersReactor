PsYkO

Thu Apr 17, 2003 8:53 pm by PsYkO

After beating SP, I made(and still make) my money running Cardamine from Yanagi Outpost to New Berlin and diamonds on the return trip to Yanagi. This route is 1 jump hole and 0 trade lanes. If your hostile with Red Hessians you'll get attacked coming out of the jump hole. High speed docking is the way to go. Make a run at the docking ring w/cruise engines on then hit F3 when you're a few hundred meters from it. Even if you get scanned by police you'll be docking before they can do anything. The only hassle is I'm attacked by Corsairs every time I leave the outpost. Depending on my mood I either dust them or run for the jump hole. I clear @ 90-100k and you can make the round trip in 5 minutes. Oh, to speed up the return trip, set a waypoint on the sigma-13 jump hole and take the trade lane. Press escape key when you're near the hole.
