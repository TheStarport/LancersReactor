Puke Barwalker

Fri Nov 14, 2003 3:54 am by Puke Barwalker

Some jump holes work in SP, but not MP. But I suspect what you are probably talking about is the earlier levels of SP. Remember that at first you haven't got a ship capable of using even jump gates. Then Juni gives you the codes for jump gates in a few systems only. Until that happens, you can't use any jump holes either -- which doesn't make much sense, since they are supposed to be a "natural anomaly". Later you may try to use jump holes and get that "access denied" message. All I can figure is that the jump hole is meant to be usuable only on a certain mission, and after that the programmers closed it down. There is a mod that opens all jump holes, but I wouldn't use it until you have finished the SP game first. Might spoilt some surprises.
