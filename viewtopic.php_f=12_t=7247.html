As you post you move up. For every so many posts you move up. But the higher rank you attain, the number of posts to attain a higher rank becomes greater. For example, from recruit to 2nd Lt. only requires 4 posts. But to attain 1st Lt., you have to post 10 or 15 posts more. It requires alot of posting to attain a really high rank. Be warned though, when you post be thoughtful with something real to say. Posting trash or emoticons just to attain rank gains you no more respect.
