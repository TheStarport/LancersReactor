Norfleet

Mon Mar 03, 2003 7:58 pm by Norfleet

Anyone who tells you that he has bought a Class 10 Graviton or Molecular shield is either lying outright, or has modded his game. I've decompressed and grepped through the market_misc.ini file which controls the vending of equipment: Which items are sold where. Nowhere in the game is a molecular shield of greater than Class 9, or a graviton shield of greater than class 7, sold.

5 places in the game sell the L10 positron, however, and two places sell the L9 molecular. 6 places sell the L7 Graviton. Zero places sell anything better. Furthermore, I have never had a shield drop from a destroyed ship, nor have I ever popped one off a wreck I found in space, so it's pretty safe to conclude that, either due to a bug or oversight, these items do not exist in the game in a form that can be used by players.

Nobody has yet confirmed that these items can be found in a reproducible manner.
