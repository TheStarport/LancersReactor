Most fun route with a ton of profit for advanced people:

-Start at Manhattan, full load of Luxury Goods.
-Stop at Honshu, sell. Load up on Optronics.
-Stop at Planet Malta, load up on *gasp* Cardamine.
-Take whatever route you want back to Manhattan, sell.

Round trip? Million bucks, give or take some depending on your freighter.

The last leg is incredible fun if you aren't friendly with people or get scanned. I spent 40 minutes trying to sneak into Manhattan cause Cryer hated me, and kept gettting everyone hostile. I also got scanned in Kusari on the way back. That was intense. Took out 4 or 5 Navy Dragons with my Dromedary.

EDIT: you can knock off a leg or two if you want. Optronics from Honshu to Malta is absurd, tons of profit, short distance. Or you can play it safe and do Manhattan to Honshu.
