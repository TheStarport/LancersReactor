Nomad

Does anyone remember a space sim called Nomad? I got it free when I registered my first pc game back around 1995. I don't remember the company that made it.
It had a lot of similarities to Privateer and Freelancer. You had to trade, explore and get along with a lot of different races in order to fully equip your ship and finish the game. One race (the Arnen, I think) had the best shields and looked and sounded sort of like Wookies), another race had the best missiles, and there was a race of marauders who would attack you if you had some good cargo.

I wonder if the Nomads in FL are a sort of tribute to that game. Anybody have any info on this?
