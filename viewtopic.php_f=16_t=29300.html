Wed Mar 10, 2004 4:42 pm by Chips

Basically it depends (don't let this be a best ships thread people ), if you go for things like the legionare, it can mount higher class weapons than its counter part light fighters, and better shields etc. However, the drake is more manoverable, but offers lower class stuff. Its a tradeoff. As a new player, the more power is probabily better for you, but as your skills are refined, manouverability will far outstretch mere firepower alone, as the skill in avoiding fire, and such will be more important than flat out gunning them.

However - its up to your skills. If your not that great - take the high end light fighters as they can take a severe beating, and trying to control something as flighty as a drake might be too hard........

Otherwise, use more skill, and take a smaller faster ship - like the drake or something - as they can really move and run rings around some ships.

The ships selection is to cater for everyone. From non-manovourable bulldozers (titan) right down to the drake/hawk/patroit for the conossuers.....those that like a challenge, and enjoy flying. Heavier ships will allow for you to concentrate on just shooting the opposition, which the titan does amply, whilst the lighter ones concentrate on tactics and skill. The range is there so that veteran players will still have ships to choose from, and offer opportunities to be challenged in, whilst STILL being capable of doing anything - all the way to the newbie who needs a tank to flatten stuff without having to steer at the same time
