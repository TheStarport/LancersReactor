Schizm

Mon Mar 15, 2004 9:00 am by Schizm

drizz, in your next tutorial version, might i suggest an explanation of what a sur file does aside from allowing destructible ship parts?

it also makes sure that a beam weapon can actually hit the ship you create. if you don't have a sur file of about the right size, beam weapons will literally pass straight through your ship. missiles will still hit, because they don't just do point damage, they have an area of effect. you can still ram into things.

the best bet with a sur file right now is to find an already existing freelancer ship that has very nearly the same shape that your ship uses, make a copy of the file, then rename it to "yourship.sur" and place it in the same directory as you place your cmp.
