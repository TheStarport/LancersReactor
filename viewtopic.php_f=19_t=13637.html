New challenges for bored vets

After creating two level 38 charactes in two different servers, each with millions on their bank account, I get bored. Bored enough to switch between Eagle-Sabre-Corsair like changing clothes everyday. Been there, done this, done that, a million times.

Now my gig is to start new characters, then try to get rarely used ships early. Sabres and Titans are dime a dozen, but it still does catch people's attention if you are flying a Bloodhound, a Mule, or Wolfhound. Daggers are neat too, as an early plane, and so are Legionaires. As you go up the scale, my ambition is to get a Stilleto and then a Centurion. Once in a while, maybe a Dromedary. People see this ship that looks like a Titan, but it ain't a Titan and see how low your level is. Kind of a reverse snobbery (using a Toyota Prius in front of a Mercedes/BMW crowd.
