Lane hack somewhere where there aren't higher-level defenders. I did that with my humpback while I was trying to get a dromedary. once you get neutral with liberty rogues you can head to hudson and take level 15-30-something missions against the xenos in that system from the rogues base there. i've been doing this recently in a barracuda and have made a fair stack of cash.

the missions are all around $15-$50k, plus you salvage the weapons from the xeno ships (the tarantulas they use are actually pretty decent on my barracuda at the moment) and if you do it for long enough (as i have) it drags your rep with liberty rogues, outcasts and the lane hackers all up into the green.

i've only just maxed out all 3 factions and am planning on making the detroit munitions->montezuma base->planet manhattan run about a bajillion times in a dromedary to celebrate

oh, one word of warning though - if you are, like me, planning on doing this in a barracuda or other like-powered craft then make sure you have the best shield on that particular rogues base that you'll be launching from, as it's listed as being the shield that is best against the weapons that the xeno use. (i *think* it's molecular, but i can't remember)
