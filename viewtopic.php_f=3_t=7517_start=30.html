What a great thread!

Privateer 1...wow, thats all I have to say about that game! I also remember when I first bought it. Heck, I still read the manual sometime because I love the story and the background on the planets. I got started on Wing Commander with WC2:Vengence of the Kilrathi. After that, I just had to get the first WC and the expansions. And then Privateer came out...

Does everyone remember that feeling you got when you first landed on New Detroit and how shady it was? Wow...and the sound effects that went with the planet just made you think "Yes, I am a criminal and I love it!"

I am sorry to say, though, that Privateer 2 could never compare to the first one. I was very disappointed with it. I couldn't quite place my disappointment until I realized that it was not set in the Wing Commander universe. The Kilrahi were gone as well as jump points! And the new universe that it was set in was so bland and not very entertaining. Thanks a bunch Erin Roberts! =)

Thankfully, I like the universe that the Lancer series is in. I am looking forward to having that same feeling I had so many years ago when I landed on New Detroit...and I think FL will deliver!
