Switch, every time you deactivate a different mod in your current game, it can change the savegame file. Check your saved ones and use the view at the to to change to details and check the dat/time setting to find the newest one move back to the main saves page. When you activate a new mod, it creates a new folder listing that mod and will keep the saves from that point in it if you deactivate that mod.
