Velocity matters, too. Those long range guns won't help you out much unless you're fighting battleships or star bases. They're slow velocity insures that whatever you shoot at will be long gone by the time your shot gets there.

That said, if you really want some firepower with a super-sized cannon, go with the cruiser turrets found on the White Star Cruiser. They have a range of 3.0k and a high velocity (can't recall the exact number.)

Really, if you're going to use gunship weapons, there's only one kind to use: missile turrets. They have a range of 1500, a velocity of 200 (+ your ship speed,) are homing, and the ammo never needs to be replaced or purchased. I have at least one on every ship I own, frequently more. The only problem is that it completly takes the fun out of any dogfight. You might as well have god-mode on.
