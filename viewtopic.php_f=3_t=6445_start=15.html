Lance Cutter

Sat Feb 22, 2003 2:22 am by Lance Cutter

Hello everyone,

I hope you do not mind me getting in on this one. For my first post.

A small tidbit of myown history. I have followed Chris and Erin Roberts work from their very early days. Pre-Origin. One thing they have proven is that they provide very high quality entertainment.

P1 was IMHO one of the best games ever for space sim fans. I did like P2 and agree that it should have been named differently. After all it was a UK based game and not really linked with Chris or Erin. Although it held some excellent gameplay and I too loved how you had to discover the story.

P1 was the best of the best at the time, WC1-4 were also great titles and set some of the begining graphics standards that we have come to expect from games of today. The stories are what had kept me coming back. The first time I saw a Kilrathi rip the heart out of a human really infuriated me. I thought that discovery was really cool, that a game story could evoke an emotional response out of me like a good book or a movie.

Freespace and FS2 were very righteous games in their own right within the genre of space flight sim. Great games. FS2 does still have good GFx.

But Privateer really hooked me and showed me what computer gaming was capable of. Graphics are only 1/5 of what it takes to become a killer game. Game play, Sound, Story and FUN. The latter being very hard to quantify as it is different for all of us but no less important. Privateer 1&2 were great stories and the delivery mechanism was excellent. I think game play was better with P1 but P2 had better graphics they both were freeform games and existed on my hard drives till I couldn't play them anymore. They were both FUN.

My hope is that FL will fill this void I have had for so many years. I am looking forward to the MP side of this game. I have been watching this game develop from its begining and waiting am patiently. I was not fortunate enough to beta test it. Maybe next time. BTW I think the ideal PC game length of play time is 30-40 hours. But the press has a hard time waiting 5 years or so on a big project, not to mention the Corporations overall cost to produce such an animal in todays give it to me now world.

I say give em time to make it like games used to be. Something to remember.

Lance
