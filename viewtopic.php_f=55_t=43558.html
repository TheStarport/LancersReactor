Fred the Dead

Wed May 17, 2006 7:23 am by Fred the Dead

This can't be said enough...
Finish the Storyline First- you will be missing something noteworthy if you don't.

Then, take the time to explore all the places that the storyline doesn't take you in the vanilla game. Do a little trading- a good way to build up a nice chunk of change - and find the best routes you can. Get a VHF and wander around the Borderworlds for a while, because you'll never know what you can find if you take the time to look.Do the same thing in the inner systems for the same reason.Try different ways with different ships- what you can get depends on who likes you and who doesn't, so take a little time before you decide that you're bored and wanna go whack Rouges for a bit.... you might miss a nice little ship going at it the 'easy' way.

Then try one of the many good Vanilla servers out there, 'cause while you might be a god on Your copy, others online may have different ideas...

Then would be a time to try and work through all of the mods available- and with the FLMM, turning 'em on and off is dead easy.

Have fun- the game is just begining....

And, let Juni hang out for a bit in the storyline- just remember where you need to meet here when You decide it's time. You'll find she's good about that.
