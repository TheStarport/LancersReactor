Thu Feb 27, 2003 1:16 pm by Petru

When thinking of Sabre vs Titan, it becomes a very clear choice when you start getting into the high end weaponry.

I've currently got a Sabre, packing 2x Diamondbacks and 2x Onyx, these are big guns that use a LOT of power. Most I can manage for the last 2 gun slots are Class 8 weapons. I can get off about 10 salvos before the energy is gone.

Titan has 8 weapon slots. Given the above those extra gun slots really aren't much use, since it will merely drain your battery all the more if you are using Class 10 weapons. Best you could do is mount some missiles, which aren't overly effective against good pilots, too easily avoided.

The only useful factor the Titan has is that +2k armour over the Sabre.

It's slower, bigger, and those wings, the wings are just begging to be shot off.

Sabre forever.
