Free Piece Of Advice kimk :

In homeworld 2, retire ALL your fleet at the end of a mission, and the opposition will be next to none in the next mission ! This is because it works on a "dynamic difficulty" setting where it counts up your remaining units and a) Makes a elite counter fleet to combat your unit AND b) Doubles the size of their counter-fleet.
If you use my technique, you don't get any ships next mission, and you have the resources to make a super-powerful fleet of your own !

(Your fleet size times two = the enemy units you face in the next mission)
0 * 2 = 0

I don't suggest this for the last mission, I won't tell you why, because I don't want to spoil the game for you. Good Luck !!
