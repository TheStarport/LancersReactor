NukeIt

Thu Mar 18, 2004 6:28 am by NukeIt

The Anubis is, in fact, a VHF. The term VHF refers to the number of weapons it has, not the weapon class- a VHF has 7 guns/turrets, a HF has 6 guns/turrets, a Freighter has 8 guns/turrets, and an LF has 3-5 guns/turrets. There are several possible combinations of guns/turets for each type, except for the VHF type, which only has one combination- 6 guns, 1 turret.

The Anubis, having 6 guns and 1 turret, is a VHF.
