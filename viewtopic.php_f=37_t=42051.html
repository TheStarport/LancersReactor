Nova Design - Epsilon updates (Screenshots included)

Hey all...

Made this thread to stop the main Epsilon thread from getting too crowded with images. From now on, any new screenshots of the mod and works in progress of ships going into the mod will go here. I'll kick it off with some screenshots from my new system Gemini II, and a picture of the updated Cyclone. On the Cyclone I removed those clunky fixed guns and retextured it to look more suited to a combat group or mercenaries...I also added a trim to the cockpit area, recessed the canopy, added some torpedo pods into the big energy pods on the side, and slimmed it down a bit. It is also alot fewer poly's now.

Sat Dec 10, 2005 4:20 am by parabolix

Some piccys of Harrier's newest work in progress, the Praetorian. This ship is truly a monster. It is larger than the Liberty dreadnought (I have included a scale of the two ships in the screenshots) and will carry a loadout that tops any battleship native to the game. It will be dockable as a fixed base, though I don't recommend approaching it if it shows up red.







The model is still in progress as you can see. Also, the Liberty Dreadnought is missing some parts of the model there, that's simply because Milkshape has a Poly Limit and the fewer polys in the model the more efficiently it runs, so parts of the Dreadnought that weren't needed for scale were removed.
