Bob McDob

Sat Feb 07, 2004 12:34 am by Bob McDob

olddog - At the moment, Reckoning is mostly a ship mod. Eventually I'd like to expand outward to make it into a full universe, with new systems and the like, but I'm afraid what you're saying simply isn't possible at the moment. Will try to get other Privateer features in.

warzog: what's this about you being "outta here"? Do you have some sort of outside commitments? Are you quitting the team? Is this too intense or something? I know that, back when I was modding in the middle of last year, I went through many of the same things you did - couldn't sleep, could't eat, couldn't concentrate on anything other than the mod. I was working almost solo then, and it was hell. I'm not going through that again.

That's why I don't want you to leave the team. I really want to make this into the best mod for FL ever ... not just a glorified shippack (like I said the last one was), but a living, breathing, universe. A total conversion, or at least as total as can be made. Not just a good WIng Commander mod, but a good mod, period. And for that we need people. People who know their stuff.

This might seem ambitious, but I don't think it's any more insane than the Free Worlds mod ... as you might be able to tell, I've been playing it a bit and I really admire the professionalism put into it. The mod itself is bits and pieces, but the most important ones are there, and despite its unfinished state it's compelling as ever. I want The Reckoning to be the Free Worlds mod, only in Privateer. I don't want it to be just a slapdash mishmash of ships and places ... I want it to at least feel the tiniest bit authentic.

This mod needs everyone it can get - turnover has been pretty bad ever since it died the first time. About the only people there from more or less the start are Tolwyn and I (even if you look at the people in the threads, they're different - I think only sp00n and Sir Spectre have been around mostly all the way). If we're going to make this into a great mod, we need to attract attention. And the best way to do that is to keep making progress. Slow, perhaps, but progress nonetheless. Tolywn and I can't do it all ourselves - Tolwyn, for all his talent as an organizer, has his hands full working on at least two other mods, and me ... well, you saw what happens when I work alone <G> The point is, we need everyone who'll help. Even two modders are still better than one, and if we get Acid back, and Stannum to finish his meshes, and the other people from back then ... who knows?

Anyway, I don't know if I misinterpreted you, but I felt I needed to get that off my chest. And I want everyone to know that I am dead serious to finish this mod.

bob

The easiest way for putting 4 docking rings around a planet (in my limited editing experienced opinion) would be to quadruple the entries for planets. Like Li_01a, Li_01b, Li_01c, & Li_01d. The problem with this is, I don't know which dock you'd fly too if you can't see the rings and you just dock with the planet.

The more direct approach would be to somehow link all 4 rings (or however many) to the same entry. And this is what I don't know to be possible or not. Because whoever did put 4 rings on a planet did have separate entries, because he had put the major 4 factions all with their own bases on the planet.
