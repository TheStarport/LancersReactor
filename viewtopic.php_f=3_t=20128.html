detonate, have you beaten the game already? If so, then the advice of Fear Factor is correct; after the end of the campaign the game switches the display to how much more you need to level up.

If you are still within the campaign then you have been hit by the 'stuck level' bug. Have you applied the patch perchance? Try buying a hold full of the most expensive commodity that you can find, then sell again. If that doesn't help, fill your hold with a grossly overpriced commodity and start buying equipment upgrades until your net worth drops under the threshold. Should be easy as you are not very far over the limit. Then sell the overpriced commodity again to level up.
