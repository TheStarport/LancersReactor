need help finding a mod that fits me

First off, thx to anyone taking the time to read through this post.

I have been trying different mods and add-ons, but so far I havent found one who suits me.

What I want is the following :

I want to play the single player story campain.

The mod should :

Add a collection of ships and new weapons, new planets or systems is also ok.
However what I do not want is for the mods to unbalance the game. I have tired some mods that adds all of the above, but they introduce overpowered weapons that i can fit on my first vessel, ships that I can buy at lvl 4 for 300.000 credits that far outrank the best original ship in the game. Besides, the loot I get from the first missions are also so overprized that I gain money so quickly I could do all the story quest after each other without having to spend the normal time trading and taking on mission.

What I want is a mod that doesnt alter the game to much, preferably it should prolong the time inbetween story mission and i want to fight and think to earn my cash. In other words i want it as close to the original game as possible while it still adds the odd new ship, weapon, planet etc without ruining the game balance.

If anyone knows an add-on or mod that perhaps fits this I would be forever thankful

Incase anyone else is looking for the same thing as me....

After searching through most of the mods I have found the one that most closly fits my needs.

Freelancer Reloaded and silvers weapon pack.

By installing these two I still retain the balance in the single story campain. All the lvl req for ships and equipment have been removed so this beasically increases the playing time in the game. This basically means that even though I cant level up i can still buy every kind of ship if I play long enough and gather enough money.
