Hp/Fixed/HpThruster01 Hp/Revolute/HpWeapon01 Weapons should be revolute hard points. Make sure you use the straight up and down line and not slash or back slash.
In order to make a new addition to your shiparch.ini just copy one from your current shiparch.ini to notepad. Try to get one that is as close as possible to the ship you have built as far as addons etc. go. Make the changes nescessary in new notepad .txt. Once you've got your shiparch addition ready make a backup of your original shiparch.ini then add your ship.
Warning. I'm pretty new at this myself but if you're desperate enough to post the same topic twice in three days and to bump a 4 hour old post I guess you'll take what you can get.
