To get houses & corps happy is not diff but can take a wee spot of time. Kill Mollys, Reds, Rogues, Hackers, Junks; basically all the non-house factions. Here is another trick I used in SP: When they are least bit neutral {even if still way left of center} get in form {F4} with a patroling group. Then when they fight you do so with them. Then re-form after fight & continue patrol with them. When they get close to base very off & join another. This will help speed the process along and provide lots of loot to help with credits. Even if no enemies encountered just hitting NAV points with them makes them feel all warm & mushy inside; like: "HEY! This **** might not be such a ****."
Hope this provides a bit of help.
