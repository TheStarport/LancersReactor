Here's the general way to do it: (for WInXP)

1.) Press CTRL + ALT + DEL
2.) Press "fr" without the quotes
3.) Press ALT + E
4.) Press Y

Short and sweet: It brings up the task manager, selects freelancer.exe, presses End Process, and confirms it by pressing Yes.

You're doing that all blindly so make sure you follow the steps correctly.
