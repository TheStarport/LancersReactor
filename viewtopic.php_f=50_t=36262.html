Chips

Mon Nov 22, 2004 11:38 pm by Chips

Its taken me around 18 months of modding to know what I know.........and there is no way I can impart it all to you at all. We cannot just teach you all you want to know in even a month, as it would take that long out of our lives to teach you! Would you be willing to sacrifice all that time for someone you have never met?

However, if you read a little - check the tutorials forum, check the threads stickied at the top of this forum - which were designed to help you locate 'how to' do most things, with explanations/examples - then you can learn. You will find that people have spent their time writing answers to questions, explaining how to do stuff for you already. They have invested so much time in trying to help others already, that this place has enough information to make large mods in just a few months of modding. I would suggest you start reading, and all the best - because you cannot expect those people who have donated all that time already, to just start doing it all over again for every person who asks that question. After all, you can still read what they said last time.

So - please read the stickies, use the tutorials forum to find out what you want to know, and use the search button to try and pull up relevent topics/how to's.

Once you have got started, then maybe you will have specific questions to ask - that can be answered. As it is - that isn't a question - you just made a statement!
