Crash-Drive

Sun May 04, 2003 4:08 pm by Crash-Drive

Regen rate is a constant for all player shields at all class levels, it is always 1/45th of capacity per time unit, whatever that time unit may be. Graviton shields have 1/3rd more capacity than Positron or Molecular shields at a given class level. Why do they have more capacity? Well, this is my theory: when you first start out in both SP and MP, the only shields immediately available to you are low-level Graviton shields, so maybe the devs decided they needed a boost to keep rookie players from getting frustrated by deaths. This boost was applied across the board to all class levels of Graviton shields, and because shield capacities increase exponentially with class level, the capacity became too large at high levels; so they just capped the Graviton shields you could acquire at class-8. But it's just a theory, only the devs know for sure.
