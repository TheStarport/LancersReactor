Ok, now that this is in the Spoiler section, I can answer you.

You can become friends with the Outcasts or the Corsairs by two ways.

The harder is kill lots of there enemies, the other is bribing. I prefer the latter, as it is much quicker.

You can get bribes for the Corsairs on:

Freeport 1
Freeport 5
Freeport 9
Islay Base
Cadiz Base
Luxuary Liner Hawaii
Leon Base
Trafalger Base

You can get bribes for the Outcasts on:

Freeport 10
Freeport 6
Freeport 4
Cali Base
Rochester Base
Buffalo Base
LPI Sugerland
Mactan Base

They can also be found on other bases, but it's best for you to find out for yourself. Good luck!
