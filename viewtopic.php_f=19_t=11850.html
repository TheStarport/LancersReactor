Hey folks, I'm putting in a plug for a great 24/7 server called "Zampolit". Its an RPG based server that's being modded as things develop in the Zampolit community and with modders successes. The ping is on average very good. The server is passworded and locked down to ensure that hackers and cheaters are mostly eliminated. How do ya get in? By going to : home.kr.rr.com/zampolit and applying for registration on the server. We run an advanced gaming experience and we want serious folks who really want to roleplay to the hilt and have a blast. Not everyone uses a Titan...many ships are faction limited. We run battles with battleships and swarms of fighters...and good luck if you think the Nomads are easy pickings for farming their weapons, many things have been changed to provide a challanging experience. So get on over to the Zampolit's website at http://home.kc.rr.com/zampolit/ and fill out the application so we can get you up and running with a great group of people asap!.
