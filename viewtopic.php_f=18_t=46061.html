All you need is decompile the THN file using FLED-Thorn, the a lot of patience and willpower ^^
THN editing is long and difficult, but you can do awesome things with it! (Look at the "THN Menu Screen Showcase" thread, by Ruppetthemuppet)

You can also find a very good tut at Universal Modding School Site, written by Ruppetthemuppet.

Good luck in your THN scripting, you'll need some 8D
