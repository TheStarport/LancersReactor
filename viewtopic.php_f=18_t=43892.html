Opinions needed with balancing out vanilla ships

(Sorry about starting a new topic, the forum wouldn't allow me to post in my older topic.)

What do you all think? I am overhauling the current turning rates of the ships;
Purpose: Making a clear difference between heavy, light and very heavy fighters. Right now it's a freaking mess. Basically everyone ends up flying an eagle.

All ships support level 9/10 weapons, have around the same armor and energy as their class counterparts.
LF run out of energy sooner than other ships. Also have about half the armour of HF, but are very agile.
HF are the midclass, good handling, good weaponry, armor etc etc.
VHF should be the slowest of them all, but packing a big punch and have the most armour (besides FR).
Liberty, Kusari have better handling ships with less armor/energy vs Corsairs, Rheinland, who have slower/tougher ships. The rest is somewhere in between. The differences are not severe though.

I agree in general to what you write.
Right now it's a freaking mess
and to that in particular

I haven't really made my mind up about if and how the ships should be changed, but I think it would a nice idea to customize the cruise_charge_time per ship (in engine_equip.ini, and if I remember correctly you'd also have to lower the CRUISE_STEADY_TIME in constants.ini if you wanna make it faster than 2 seconds). So, a Starflier would be able to go much faster into cruise than a Titan, which on its turn would do that much faster than a Rheinland Battleship. That way there would be a real advantage, in some respects, in flying a Starflier (or another light fighter).


EDIT: because it's not possible (at least I didn't find out how, please prove me wrong!) to add class to engines, engines should be created in such a way that it's not really convenient to put a light fighter engine on a capship, or vice versa (though the player should have the choice - at least that's what I think)
