I'm dead far from being a modding god, but at least by now I have gained some insight into the engine workings of FL. And the only way to change hull color seems to be to modify the surface texture of the ship itself - but that'd apply to _all_ ships of that type galaxywide, then, meaning every "Startracker" out there would be black with a red rim from then on. So no individual "identifying" effect with that.

Besides, even this wouldn't work in MP since only YOU would have that modified texture and see it in the game, but everyone else would still see that standard grey hull instead. You'd have to make every client copy that new texture into his FL installation make them see it.

*erm* a word to the gods:
Did someone even figure out how to import textures into the shipfiles yet? Correct me, but I think not... =/
