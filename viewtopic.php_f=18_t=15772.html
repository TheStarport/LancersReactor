Don't know about reseting the missions to run again, but here is a thought. Set your initial ship that Juni gives you to something else. This is assigned in:

\DATA\MISSIONS\M01A\m01a.ini

Act_SetShipAndLoadout = ge_fighter, msn_playerloadout

If you set ge_fighter to what you want, you should have that as your initial ship. Again, I haven't tried this yet myself, but would be interested in the results. Happy Hunting.
