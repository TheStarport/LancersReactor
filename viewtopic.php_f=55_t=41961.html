After installing a Mod and increasing my money worth, weapons, etc., when I uninstall a Mod and then try another one, will I retain my current money, weapons, status, etc


Not realistically, I am affraid not. Many mods add new items to themselves, and certainly don't necessarily share the same items between them.

Everything in FL is created with a unique nickname - so ships are unique on their nickname. Your save file simply uses the ships nickname to identify the ship you are flying... but if that nickname (in one mod) doesn't exist in another mod (highly unlikely) - then it will crash the game (as your ship doesn't actually exist).

The same goes for stations, systems, weapons, commodities and much much more. Simply put, the save games will most likely be completely incompatible.
This is also why the Autosave will cause crashes if not deleted when trying normal Freelancer after using mods too.

As for which other mods to use, I will let others answer that - as I may be biased
