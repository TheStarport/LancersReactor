Fri Sep 03, 2004 8:38 am by zlo

Well, it's a space sim (joystick-controlled). The action is set around 2160s. Most of the Sol system is colonized, there's tension between the Alliance (formed of the US, European countries, and Japan) and the Coalition (formed of (of course) Russia, some Islamic countries, and some countries of the East (sth like China, Northern Korea, etc.). The're planning negotiations, but the COalition's head, admiral Kulov, makes an ambush and destrys ca.1/2 of the Alliance fleet and bases. Here the action begins, as the Alliance starts to retaliate. Due to the lack of experienced pilots, volunteers are recruited, and youo become one of them, flying with the 45th volunteer squadron. A good game, really worth playing. I enjoyed it immensely. There's a demo version in the Downloads section featuring two missions not included in the original game and making a kind of a prequel to the game. Hope I answered your question.
