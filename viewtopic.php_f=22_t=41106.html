Wed Nov 09, 2005 11:25 am by Earendil

I have had this problem (a long time ago) and had to open my LAN wide open before I found the specific problem. My software firewall wasn't set correctly and DirectPlay (part of DirectX) changes the ports it uses every time it starts over.

While this may not be your issue, it is definately an issue with your FL server ports getting read by your gaming 'puter through your LAN. Most likely culprits are your firewall(s).
