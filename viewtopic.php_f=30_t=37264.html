problably the same thing I tried doing for one of the exelcia releases a long long long time ago, I had kept talking to the creator of both the tobini and the sebini (the program which locks a comressed ini so it cant be decompressed.... well actually you can uncompress it still cause he realesed a program which can uncompress the secured binis, it's on lancers, just no one dls it =p)

But back to the original problem, the thing is you can't use tobini on files which are too large... it loses data... I talked to the creator about it and he gave me a reason why it wouldn't work on large files, but I forget what it was.... anyways what I did to get around this was to just break up larger files into multiple smaller ones. You can easily cut an ini's data in half, put the second half in a new ini, and just add the appropirate refrerence in the freelancer ini right after the original ini's entry in it. Thus your files are small enough for tobini to work, and then you can lock them with sebini or whatever program or utiillity your using to lock it from bini from being able to uncompress it, or from datastorm from being able to read it.

Thing of it tho is... it was a lot of effort... and i found a much much much easier and faster way of blocking programs like datastorm from reading any of the files in freelancer... just screw up the freelancer.ini in a way that freelancer can still read it, and datastorm can't
