black_lightning

Mon Jul 19, 2004 7:52 am by black_lightning

well dds are perfectly fine for me, and I still prefer them over targa's in regards to how much space you save, you just need to set their values right when saving cause there are quite a few options. The main things to remember is that you need mips mapping, but you can still then chose to only have 1 mips map, this will prevent having multiple smaller lod mip maps made. So for larger ships where the camera view is further away it will still use the original resolution picture and not a lower one. Also just having 1 mips map makes the file size of the mat smaller anyways. So much better then targa's.
