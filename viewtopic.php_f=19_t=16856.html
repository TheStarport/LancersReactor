Sapient6

It's possible that he could cut his engines from cruise and fire at you and still catch up IF he wasn't directly behind you in the first place and the angle of your flight path and his flight path placed him slightly closer to the point where your paths intersect.

HOWEVER

While it's hard to really come to a verdict on whether someone was cheating in a case like this without witnessing it yourself, the dialog you describe suggests that he said to you that he would enter cruise, cut engines and fire, enter cruise, cut engines and fire, etc etc. If he actually suggested that he entered cruise more than once (which he would have to do if while chasing you, you changed directions sharply enough since it's not possible to change directions with engines cut), entering cruise instantly cuts your speed down to 80 for five seconds. He was probably cheating.
