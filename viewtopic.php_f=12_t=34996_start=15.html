I don't think that the global warming phenomenon is purely man made. I think it is happening to us regardless of what we do. What we do may accelerate the process but I don't know that it is something that we can control all that easily.

The cycles for it are in the historical record. The question has been whether the next change is going to be just another "minor" warming cycle as happened before the year 1100AD or whether what now is happening is a precursor to a very big Ice Age like the one that ended some 10,000 years ago.

No one has the answer to that one. That the world is getting warmer, by the records being taken and the fact that the glaciers are melting is a known fact.

I don't see why people still dispute that it is happening.

The question really should be "What can we do about it, if anything? And, when it will take a turn for the worse"
