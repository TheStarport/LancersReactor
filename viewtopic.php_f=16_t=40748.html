Fri Sep 09, 2005 1:21 am by ajduk

If you've done mission 2 you should already have it on the Navmap for Colorado...

But anyway.. From the New Your jumpgate, take the tradelane to Pueblo Station in the colarado system. Now look at your navmap, turning on patrol paths (Try all of Neutral, hostile and friendly); on one of these views you will see a lot of paths conmverging to the left of the trade lane you have just used. Set a waypoint at this point and cruise towards it, when you get near, select 'solars' in your scanner and Ouray base should show up.
