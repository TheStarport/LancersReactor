Drake vs. Banshee

I don't think this one's as clear cut as people make. The game description says the Banshee is the best light fighter, but it also says the 'Titan' is quite simply, the one (ROFL).

I've tried both for a while (although I must admit I have used the Drake more). The drake is far more agile - test this by counting the number of complete spins you can do in 1 min - for the Drake, it comes out to over 16 wheras the Banshee manages just 12.5, putting it on a par with the Eagle (!) for agility. This means that the Drake turns a full circle about a second faster than the Banshee - this is a substantial advantage.

The point I will concede is the shielding - the Banshee can equip class 7, making its shields about 30% better than the Drakes. However, vs. the AI, I've noticed they don't really tend to be able to hit the Drake.

Finally, weapons. Assuming that you go for 1 shield killer + 3 hull killers, there is actually surprisingly little in it. I prefer to match speed on all my guns, meaning I would equip the Drake with a Borocco + Pyros weapons. For the Banshee, I'd be tempted by a Debilitator + 3 sunblasts (2 A's + 1 B ). The Banshee with this load out gets about 25% more damage over time, but with the Drakes extra agility you can probably keep its gun on the target much better.

Sorry for the long post and all, but I really don't like the way that everyone says the Banshee is the best LF almost as a mantra. I myself have on one of the servers I play on traded down from an Eagle with optimised loadout to the Drake outfitted as above, and I enjoy it. With the Drake, I can easily take on Level 32-34 missions, possibly higher if they are of the assassinate or destroy base (without having to kill all enemies) types.
