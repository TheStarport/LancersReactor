blackhole

Sat Dec 02, 2006 8:57 pm by blackhole

The star sphere files are normal .cmp objects but they are loaded by the engine and displayed as a skysphere. To make them spin would require a graphical engine hack, which naturally is impossible. The only way to achieve that would be to make a "fake" sphere thats over 1000000 in radius (or something equally as insanely huge) and then make it so that it can be seen from that far away. Then you could get it to spin. Unfortunatly a persistant player could also bump up against it, and the sphere would move slightly as you zipped across a large enough system.

So, i would suggest just resizing the .cmp file to an egregious size, loading it into the system as an object and giving it spin. I have no idea what will happen.

\Freelancer\DATA\SOLAR\STARSPHERE

Thats where the cmps are located.
