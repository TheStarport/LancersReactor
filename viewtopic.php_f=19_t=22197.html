@666Heretic

Firstly, download mIRC from www.mirc.com (don't worry, it's free).

Install mIRC.

Go into "options" and select the "connect" category. There will be a pull down menu with a list of servers.

Click the "add" button and add the following information:

Code:
Description: Gamesnet
IRC Server: irc.gamesnet.net
Ports: 6667


Next, click "add".

Now select "Gamesnet" from the pull down list (normally it will be at the bottom) and click "Connect to IRC server"

Now you're all connected and ready to go. To enter the channel just type:
Code:
/join #gamertalk

Or we could create a #USLF channel, I'll have to talk to Gingercat. He's an IRC Operator. Also, a good friend. This shouldn't be a problem at all

Or if you already have mIRC installed:
Copy this line into the adressbar of your browser and press enter.
irc://irc.gamesnet.net/gamertalk
It'll open your client and connect to #gamertalk. I'm the co-owner there.
