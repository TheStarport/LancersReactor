Problem with Resolution

Hi

I have a problem with the screen resolution of Freelancer. As I installed a Geforce FX 5200, I cannot play FL anymore. I guess it has to do with the screen resolution, as my actual monitor (Highscreen LE 38 P) just supports a max resolution @ 800x600. I played FL before on a S3 Virge where that was no prob. Is there any possibility to change the resolution externally, or do I have to wait until my new monitor will be there?

Greetings

Odeesi

Rogueone

Fri Jan 06, 2006 7:26 pm by Rogueone

You need to edit the 'Display' section of your PerfOptions.ini file (should be in My Documents\My Games\Freelancer).
Just change the 'size =' line to 800, 600 and you should be good to go.

regards,

RogueOne
