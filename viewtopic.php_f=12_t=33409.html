Kylen Selena and the Tellus survivors are traded with the Chigs but the deal goes sour, Nathan West rescues her and the others from the damaged transport but Vansen, Wang and Damphousse(?) have to stay behind and the last you see of them is the transport falling into atmosphere. I think actually Vansen and Phouse were in the lifter cockpit and Wang was in the cargo pod which had got separated (I seem to recall it got blown up maybe?)

Only West and Coop' the Tank are left at the end. Colonel McQueen was really badly injured in the bomb blast when the Chig ambassador tried to kill the Earth commanders, lost his legs iirc. The admirals and generals got cut up badly too but Commodore Ross was ok. And that's it; that was the end, they didn't make anymore.

the best episode was "Butts;" I fookin' loved Colonel Raymond C. "Kick" Butts. However Vansen's lips scared me, she got more botoxed every episode.
