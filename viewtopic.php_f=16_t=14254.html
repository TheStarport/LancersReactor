PLEASE NOTE: Original Poster --> LukeSkyfocker

First: Get to Cadiz Base in Omega 5. It's a Corsair base. Buy Artifacts for $135. Leave Cadiz and go to Planet New London (Omega 5 -> Cambridge Jump Hole -> Planet Cambridge Trade Lane -> New London Jump Gate. Then You go to Planet New London). Sell the Artifacts for $1,147, which is a profit of $1,012 per unit.

Then Buy Small Arms from Planet New London for $350. Go back to Cadiz the same way you got to New London. Sell the Small Arms at Cadiz for $840 which is a profit of $490 per unit. What is great about this route is that its really short. It takes two systems and there are only 2 Trade Lanes and about a 50K run between the Jump hole and Planet Cambridge. It takes 6 minutes give or take a few seconds to make it ONE WAY. So its 12 minutes total. If you have a Dromesdary, you can make some pretty quick cash. It holds 275 units of cargo. If you do the math, you make a profit of $413,050 round trip. Not so bad for only 12 minutes. If you break it down, you make $34,416.667 per minute of space flight. The only people that give crap are Molly's, but just fly past them.
