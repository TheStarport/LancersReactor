katoc

Thu Mar 25, 2004 11:50 am by katoc

I'm on my second thumb operated trackball mouse(currently a Microsoft Optical
Trackball) and its so much easier to use and so much better for dogfighting
maneuverability in Freelancer that I would personally never go back to a joystick.
It's much smoother just spinning the trackball, and quicker to change direction.
Also , I've had carpal tunnel - I tried various ergonomic mice to no avail until
I tried a thumb trackball. I think the difference is that you don't need to grip
the mouse. Also, the trackball doesn't need a large mousepad because the ball
is on the top.

There's actually two types one with the thumb controlled trackball and the other is more offset to the right for control with you other fingers. Both are really great. Heck...with optical, you don't have those darned rollers to clean.

Makes control (especially in Freelancer) VERY reponsive, very light and crisp. I'd say the precision stands up to the best joysticks. I certainly reccomend it over a regular mouse and pad. The wheel is nice for thrust control.

The trackball is also nice when ya don't have a lot of room on your keyboard tray.
