Well, comparing XML to plain text is like comparing loose paper collections to a library.

With plain text you do not have any kind of structure. And please don't tell me now that giving headlines to sections can be called a structure )))

SInce the information stored belongs to something XML makes it really easy to modify the data, rearrange it, restructure it, build relational databases with it etc.

XML has various enormous positive side effects and is in no sense a waste of space when it comes to storing structured data in a human readable form.
