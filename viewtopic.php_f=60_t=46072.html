Hello! Take me to your leader!

I completed FL's storyline a couple years ago; it was one of my most satisfying gaming experiences, right up there with Warhammer 40,000: Dawn of War, and more compelling in some respects than EVE Online. After solving the storyline, I remember landing on some planet in Kusari space, deciding to retire for a while; I knew the game hadn't really ended (and I managed to hold on to my save-game data until just several months ago). In recent days the game has proven to be replayable.

I used to lurk around these and other forums quite a bit, and when I unshelved FL and its strategy guide the other day, I wondered if there was still any sort of online community. It seems there is still a lot of life in FL, and the old multiplayer issues have been sorted out, as well as a wealth of mods, content and an impressive unofficial patch (thanks EOA). So I just wanted to say hi to you people, and welcome any recommendations for mods, FL-related websites, etc.
