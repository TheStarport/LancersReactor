Like FlakAttack said cheats and mods will really play hell with a server. Also I have heard of people dialing-up slowing a system way down. Are your network up and down traffic that small? Others have theorized that player limits on faster CPUs is due to upload speeds being the limiting factor (for those who don't know most broadband, ADSL and cable, upload is far less than download speed).

I used to have flserver on my main 'puter and had a few issues with lag, don't know why. Now flserver is on another 'puter on the home network with pings of 0-3 msec but occasionally I get bad lag too. Not sure why.

As for maximum players, I have restricted my server to 21. The most I've ever had was 20 players and the CPU (Athlon 1800+) was maxxed out not to mention flserver was loaded. I also run ZoneAlarm and by itself it had 25% utilization! I eventually plan on making flserver a service plus some other tweaks that could justify a higher player cap.
