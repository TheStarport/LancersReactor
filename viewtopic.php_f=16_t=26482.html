With a level 10 shield and a full complement of bots you should be able to stay in in the Unknown systems long enough to kill a number of nomad ships before heading back through the jump hole. The southern system is the easier system. Stay near Primus and dock directly after fighting. When you exit again they'll be another set waiting for you. You should be able to kill off at least three or four sets of six nomads each before your Hull bots run out.
