Hi,
Can't really say exactly what 's wrong without seeing files but here's a few pointers:
Try Shipscripter by Crabtree if you havn't already. It will let u sort out your info card stuff easily and lets u set up a ship quickly and easily. You can always modified the produced script to suit yourself afterwards if necessary.
Doubler check all your file paths are correct and files are in right places.
Make sure the texture displays okay on the ship in something like HardCMP by Louva-Deus & Shsan. also checking your ship addons(equipment) are ok with the hardpoints available.
Scan yor files with FLscan by acushot which will pick up most ini file errors once you've activated your mod before starting freelancer.

Doing the above you have a good chance of finding your problem. The progs are all available through Lancers just use search function.
