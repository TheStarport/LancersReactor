Thanks for the kind words! Be very worried outside of Liberty though! I would like to make it much harder than it currently is. But that will be implemented slowly, over time. Time I have as I don't currently have any intention of shutting the server down. Go Boston!

If I remember correctly yellow lag shows up on my LAN (ping of 0!) at about 200 to 300 msec load. Red lag shows up at about 400 to 500 msec load. Since ping is nothing for me my total latency is just the load. On your 'puter your total latency, which manifests itself as the yellow or red lag symbol, is the addition of the ping and server load. Usually when the server is in the teens load can be 70 to 150 msec (the average range). So that would seem about right you getting a red lag indication. Of course all this goes out the window when a modder comes on. Just one modder with 13 players can double the server load. Once with at least three modders and a full server (21 players at one point) I had a load of over 8000 msecs! So with my low ping if I get lag playing, then I know something is wrong. Anyway, just some ramblings.
