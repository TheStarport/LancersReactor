Because of how the pirates seem to hate my guts (despite hunting the Xenos around Pueblo Station), I actually started using an Anubis as a merchant vessel.

It's got more cargo room than comparable fighters, is well-equipped (I left the armament/shielding on board from the end of the storyline campaign), and it's cheap (only $1,100 on board the Osiris). With a single cargo run to Ainu Depot I doubled the amount of money I had. Besides, who can beat flexibility? I took on 2 or 3 missions with the Blood Dragons once I made it to Ainu, then took on a cargo and beat feet out of Kusari space. All without selling/buying ships.

If you want to start talking about a Humpback, it depends on where you'll be using it. Certain weapons are attuned for maximum damage against certain shields. Yet the same isn't true of mines. (Be sure to get one that generates a significant amount of hull damage.) You might also consider mounting a missile launcher on one of the gun mounts. I did that, and it generated one heck of a surprise every time a pirate tried to come up along my flank and pound me at point-blank range.
