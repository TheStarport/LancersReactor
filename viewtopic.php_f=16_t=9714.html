After finishing a mission level your next level will usually be based on finances. After you hit this mark Juni will contact you and ask you to meet her at some area. I have noticed if already on a mission I will respond with something about being obligated right now, then she says get there as soon as I can.
Even then I have performed many missions before actually going to meet her, and she has always been there.
Also after she contacts you you should get mission waypoints that show you the route to where she is.
