Sapient6

Thu May 15, 2003 5:40 pm by Sapient6

No, you're not the only. We get at least one post a week of this. There are a few available solutions you could pick from when this happens.

Multiplayer or Singleplayer:
- Select the base from your list and dock with it. Of course in this case this isn't an option since the base is hostile with you. In the future do NOT attempt to dock with hostile bases, and NEVER attempt to dock manually.
- Wait. If you're patient a ship might try to dock, or exit, the base via the docking tube you're in.

Multiplayer:
- Commit suicide and respawn
- Convince someone else on the same server who isn't hostile with the base to come and dock with it
- If the base belongs to a faction that ISN'T hostile to you, and is only hostile in this shot because another nearby faction is hostile, then you could try quiting and rejoining. There's a chance you'll rejoin the server right where you are instead of losing some of your progress, and if no other players are in the same system then the NPC ships might reset (possibly resulting in no hostiles nearby allowing you to dock, or resulting in the base going hostile and having to launch ships to fight you, which might set you free).

Singleplayer:
- load your last save game
