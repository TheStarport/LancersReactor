Wow, work here is proceeding farther and faster than I'd expected. I feel a bit bad, since I can only really do stats, but I did another hack of the ships tonight. Download http://www.geocities.com/GTVConFlt/shiplist.txt, and rename it to shiplist.ini (GS doesn't allow .inis) and place it in your Freelancer\Data\Ships folder to test. I have't really had the time myself to test it yet. Here's the stats.

*Shield levels are calculated from an overly complicated formula which involves references at http://www.ugcs.caltech.edu/~hedgehog/, http://www.crius.net/zone/showthread.php? and s=e20370fee8b1bb1630f64f8abbb6d504&threadid=4067&highlight=privateer. Armor is just adding up all the sides of a ship's armor and adding two zeros at the end (except the Orion; that thing has insane armor)

*Most ships cargo are meant to be the same as their repair bots, unless they have a lot of cargo or only a little cargo, in which case I just made stuff up.
