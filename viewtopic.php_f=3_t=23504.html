Thu Aug 28, 2003 4:00 pm by Frobozz

No, but you can minimize its' effects by careful planning. In SP I bought a whole
lot of cheap Order equipment and mounted that stuff before entering rad areas.
This cut down on the repair costs. Also, plan your routes carefully, both in and
out. Shortest distance/time results in lower repairs. Not all rad fields are the
same strength, you can fly in Tau-23 forever and get no damage, despite rad alert.
