Becket Velar

Mon Mar 03, 2003 9:58 pm by Becket Velar

I prefer the term "vaped" to "Aced" or Iced". A couple of terms that are interesting from my old Star Wars days are terms like "Vac head" for someone who is in deep space a lot. Common Pilot jargon works too as in "12 oclock high" or "he is on my six". A nice euphamism for he died is "He sucked vacuum" or "the final jump" as in " Yeah he was outgunned six to one. 5 sucked vacuum before he made the final jump". Bogie, Bandit, and Indian(please guys take no offense this was actually a moniker given to German planes during the Battle of Britain) are terms for opponents ie. "He should have armed himself abit better before heading to Indian Country". Heavy...the term for a big ship but not a man'o war. Cap Ship abreviated capital ship typically in reference to a Man'war. Pig- This comes from many places and Mech Warrior in particular...Any vehicle that is slow and ponderous...most freighters,trains, armored transports. Anybody got any others?
