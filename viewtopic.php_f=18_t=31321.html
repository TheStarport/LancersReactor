Freelancer Explorer.

Add a base.
Add 'other object' to base, and check 'use nearest base as parent' then right click on the base, and click edit base layout. 3d little previewer to place the meshses of the addons and bases whever you want. Not always 100%, every blue moon I get an extension looking different ingame then ti does in FLE, but its very rare.
