Edit: Bundschuh should be fine. Just be aware of the fact that they only have one base and, in this game, your income is tied directly to how many bases you have.

To get back to shasla's original question, the game is a Turn Based Strategy game set in the Freelancer universe. A turn last one full day (24 hours) and begins at 00:00 TLR time. Each turn the players collect resources, build stations, fighters and capital ships, place units, fight and conduct diplomatic and trade negotiations. There are rules governing all of these things and more. Jacob, the game's self-appointed Rulemaster, will post a complete (and very detailed) ruleset either later tonight or some time tomorrow.

That's the Cliffs-Notes version of the game.
