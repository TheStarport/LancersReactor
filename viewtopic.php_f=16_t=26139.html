In the standard game, there are no wrecks that hold Adv. Scorpion guns. Around Ouray base, there are Xeno *Startracker* ships that do carry them, and they can drop as lootable. Xeno *Starfliers* don't carry them. Both types of ships fly around Ouray.

AFAIK, there is no networth threshold to get them to drop; this is true for all equipment class-3 and below. Often when flying an MP character's first mission against Liberty Rogues, you can get class-3 Drail gun drops.
