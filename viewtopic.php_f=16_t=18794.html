Neat trick - get Dromedary in SP early

After Mission 5 in the SP campaign, you're level 10 and can buy the Dromedary, sold at Mactan. The problem is that Outcasts hang around Mactan, and have an alliance with the Hackers, so if you're hostile with the Outcasts (which the game forces after mission 4), you can't land on Mactan.

You can shoot up Bounty Hunters for a couple hours to get the Outcasts neutral... or, there's a better way.

Go to Freeport 4 in Magellan, which for some reason is not run by the Zoners, but by the IMG. That fact is very important, because Zoner bases don't offer missions. Accept a mission at Freeport 4 - doesn't matter what or where, as long as it's not too close to Mactan. Because you're on a mission, the hostile Outcast faction won't attack you - and you can go land on Mactan

Buy the Dromedary, and then you can either go complete the mission or just abort it.

Do make sure you have the credits to buy the ship, or you'll be looking very silly. Also take along some H-Fuel from either Leeds or Freeport 4 for some extra cash once you get to Mactan.
