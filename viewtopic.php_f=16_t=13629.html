I've been most unlucky in t his mission. Can you folks outline the tactics you used? I've got an Anubis loaded with Death's hands, max torps and this and that. But I could go back to Osiris and refit.

Would cannonball missiles be better than torps?

I like the rapid fire Death's Hand weapons for ship to ship -- but they are obviously not putting much of a dent in the static targets. I hate to use weapons of different projectile speed, recharge rate and energy efficiency at the same time. I am getting great results ship to ship using a uniform selection: either slow firing high-damage weapons or rapid fire weapons, but not both.

Up to now, when any fighter you can name comes at me, I give him a missile, dodge his fire and then swoop after him to dispatch him with rapid fire guns.

Something else: in a large cloud of fighters I won't use missiles at all, because when there is too much firepower out there, I do NOT usually like to select a particular ship using the R key. That seems to tell the program where I am and key the fighters and other weapon points to shoot me. I merely pick a ship and use the small cross in front of him to blast him with guns.

I wish to hell I could use a joystick.

Incidentally, I verified for my own satisfaction that flying a fighter aircraft such as a WW II Hurricane or Spitfire using a joystick is exactly like flying a real aircraft of the era. I went up in a WW II trainer -- the pilot gave me the stick and I executed some of the maneuvers I had learned on the computer without a hitch -- even did a wingover. He wouldn't let me do the barrel rolls, he did those. What a great thing to do -- put your life at risk flying an airplane made more than 60 years ago!! Try it sometime.
