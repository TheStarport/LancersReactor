The mines around the stations are designed to go off in proximity of any other metal object. I am pretty certain of this fact as you don't need to be really close and all of the sudden BOOM you get hit by one. This causes you to **** the mouse control away from the offending mine as we all know it doesn't really blow up and will return to reek havoc on you if you don't move! It seems however that by doing this you find the next one to be closer than you thought and well we all know where we go from here. Out of nano's and Sheild Batts? How can that be? I was full before I left the base just a few min. ago!

Also I love to drop a few just as I go into cruise mode and retrieve my cargo I was forced to drop.
