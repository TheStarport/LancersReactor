AI fault?

I don't know if anyone else has noticed, or whether it might even be my imagination, but I think I've found a way to kill AI more easily. The secret lies in not attacking the one that you've got targeted. Normally, I like to attack the one in my reticle, but I found that the one in your reticle knows it and wiggles a lot. On the other hand, the other ones like to fly out further in order to get a longer strafing run. I like to shoot them as the go out and wait for them to come in and "joust" with them a little. They always lose (unless there are a ton of enemy) and usually make little or no dents in your shields. I would think that the second a ship was attacked, it would break off its strafing run, but these AI are STUPID with a capital DUMB.
