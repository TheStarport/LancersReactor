Firewing,

1st: Check your LODRanges , best setting ffor custom ships is : 0, 99999
2nd: Check the spelling in your mat fiole, make sure they are correctly spelled with what you have in the milkshape file, so if you have a material named: cockpit_glass in your ms3d file, in your mat file it should be: under material tree: cockpit_glass , and under your texture tree: cockpit_glass .TGA.

3rd: make sure the scalling is right. you can easily check this by going into freelancer and buy your ship then mount weapons on it.
