Missions and NPC Spacecraft

I have a couple of questions, one of which was sort of answered in the excellent FAQ, but I want to be more sure of, and another that is probably dumb but I would like to find out anyway.

The first is concerning the random mission generator. According to the FAQ, there seems to be one, and it relies on your ship type etc, but I'm wondering if this is similar to the Privateer random mission generator which seemed (to me at the time anyway) to have limitless random missions to run. Does the Freelancer random mission generator have limitless missions to give out? Welcome Sarpedon!

There are several thousand random missions, not infinite, but certainly plenty. Now, the missions are relatively simple, and are based on four concepts. There are no merchant runs in the missions, but you certainly can run merchandise as much as you wish, it's just in the "freeform" part of the game. You have to go to bars to get missions, and you can only land at planets or stations which are at least neutral. To get missions, you can't be hostile or neutral-hostile with the faction which runs the base.

You can't wipe out the entire Sirius Sector. You can certainly try, and it's a lot of fun doing that, but there will always be plenty of NPC's around. The universe created in the game is just much too big to be able to get everyone.
