Rep hacks will not necessarily destroy your rep with other factions, but it will take it down a little bit...if you are friendly with outcasts and then take a hack for corsairs, you will find a few bars missing from your outcast rep. It may or may not still be friendly, though.

You CAN accept more than one bribe for a faction, and while one bribe won't top off your rep with them, two certainly will. But then the opposing faction's rep will be somewhere around midline neutral.
