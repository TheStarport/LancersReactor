bakedpotato

Mon Mar 15, 2004 3:14 am by bakedpotato

If you are using Team Speak while playing the game ((bind a key such as the Ctrl key)) to hold down to talk. That way you don't need to minimize Freelancer to access Team Speak.

How do I do that?
At the top of the Team Speak screen click on Settings
then under Sound Input/Output Settings
Voice send method
put a . in Push to talk
Set your key..... I like to use the Ctrl key because you have to hold the key down while your talking....
