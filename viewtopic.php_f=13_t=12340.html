Important Announcement

In short: we will switch to Freelancer.
As much as it breaks my heart to do this, I doubt that we would be able to finish the mod with starlancer. It seems more and more unlikely that the missioneditor will come out, and Freelancer on the other hand is much easier to mod.
We will probably at first just port the campaign i've written for SL (with a few minor modifications), and maybe (if there's interest, and we have the capacity to do that) then create a universe for open-ended gameplay (and who knows, maybe another campaign after that, but first we'll have to finish this one).
I very much hoped we could do the mod for StarLancer, as i very much like the flight-engine, and i'm sorry if you are dissapointed.
This step wasn't done lightly, rest assured. I was struggling with this issue for the last few months.
btw, this wasn't the announcement i was talking a few weeks ago...

RubberEagle - Project Lead

p.s.: this shouldn't affect my work on the SL HOG creator

On the surface, Babylon 5 seems like an odd choice for a Freelancer mod. B5 was one of the very few popular science fiction worlds that at least tried to depict some realistic space flight and combat. The characters in B5 were mainly military personnel who were required to follow the chain of command. Missions served a military goal. There wasn't a whole lot of wandering about trading commodities and upgrading Starfuries with the latest PPGs and missiles smuggled in from a Narn outpost.

So, with all that in mind, what kind of B5 mod are you going to make? Is this just going to plunk B5 ship meshes into the Freelancer story line, or are you trying to remake the game around the themes of the B5 world?
