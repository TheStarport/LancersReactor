I recently took 2 eagles, a saber, and a titan apart with my drake. All fair duels, although obviously they were a little green as pilots.

To me that is fun. Taking a weaker ship and ripping apart a stronger ship. Modding is too easy to be challenging, and although you can get a good challenge eagle vs eagle or whatever, you might have time finding a good opponent at the right time. So I keep a Drake nearby for when i get bored, and no suitable challenges exist. Love dueling the class10 ships. They get so surprised that a class6 ship can rip their shields off in about 3 seconds..

Using this loadout:
2x Borrocco (shield killers)
2x Adv. Tarantula
Hornet Cruise Disrupter
Ripper Mine Launcher

The tarantula's, although great guns for their class, don't do enough hull damage to finish a Titan very quickly, so the ripper is really an essential part of the loadout. Or any mine that instakills shieldless targets, i guess, like the screamer. Also, the Hornet is sometimes essential for stopping the big ships from getting away. 1/2 of the time, once they realize they are going to die to a Drake, they cut to cruise mode to avoid the shame.
