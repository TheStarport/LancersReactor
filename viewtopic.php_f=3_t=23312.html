The way to get Liberty Rogues to like you is by shooting the police, not by shooting Xenos. Alternatively you can shoot bounty hunters, which will help your reputation with them more than shooting Xenos will. Sure, the police will hate you if you shoot them, but they will be friendly with you again as soon as you get contacted by Juni for the next mission.
