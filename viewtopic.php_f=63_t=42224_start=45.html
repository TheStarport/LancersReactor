Eerazor

Wed Feb 15, 2006 12:55 am by Eerazor

@flybyu

Many Answers

1. In my opinion the mouse controls are better than Freelancer (My fingers killed me after too much FS playing. DSO is a lot nicer to the hands)

2. Hull upgrades and add-ons are done as you will see in (hopefully) this weeks trailer

3. Yes, power plants are upgradable

4. Ah a little more complicated. The three main upgrade groups (Hull, Engine, Wings) can be upgraded from lvl 1-10. Same goes for generators, lasers, missiles etc.

5. MAX money in DSO is defined by the long intreger... 2.2+ billion go over that and negative numbers start cropping up.

6. Tricky question. We found it very easy to mod the game (with specialized tools). How we are going to support the modding community (if we officially do this, as this has not fully been decided yet either) then certain things will need to be made available. Discussion on if this is going to happen are also in progress.

7. She handles pretty clunky at the beginning of the game. Pretty fine towards the end of it so yes, you can upgrade that too.

8. 20Tons can be stored on DarkStar One. Then you can use cargo containers (50t, 100t, 200t). With upgrades you can take a maximum capacity of 620t.

9. Yep they can, watch for the trailer....

10. Yes they can. You can also purchase the weapons from the other 6 races and upgrade those too during gameplay.

11. Q2 2006 or "When it is done..."

12. Cargo ships can and will drop their cargo on occasion. There are plenty of surprises to be found in them. There is also the occasional container floating about in a system...

13. Ascaron values its customers. Being in Customer Support for 3 years prior to joining the Darkstar One Team I can assure maximum customer support from our side. The team will continue patching the game until it feels it needs no longer be patched so upon release those quirks etc. should be posted in the then created report section of the official DSO forum (Which will then be passed on from myself directly to the team to work on). So Game and Customer Support are big "YES'"

14. Kinda but not directly. You could inter-change ships (if you have Maya Export Filters), change ship stats etc. How far the modding is going to do is going to depend on how far we can try and make it mod-friendly in the time we have. An alternative plan, and one we will most likely follow, is look at a more concentrated mod support after the game has been released.

Almost missed your post there Neder.

1. There are not only aliens with one head, two arms and two legs. Some have more, some have none...

2. To be honest there is not really a viable reason to spend months developing over 300 planet surface structures just so the player can do the same things he can do on a station. Remember a space game is a "SPACE" game, designed to be in space. Stations fill the role that planets did in Freelancer. Sure it looks nice at first but you skip the sequences after a few days of playing and you ignore them completely after a few weeks of playing until you do not notice them. Then comes a new game and you suddenly remember how cool a feature seemed that you only noticed for a short while in a game

Besides, what we don't do on planets surfaces means that we have plenty of things we can do in space. Planet-side missions will be available though, so you can fly on a planets surface in certain missions. Not just canyons but tunnels as well...
