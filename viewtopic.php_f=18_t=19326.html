Thu Jul 03, 2003 8:56 am by Jackel

Actually, the design for the ISD is pretty simple. I've done a few that use the textures for the detail. The actual hull of the ship is smooth and it only requires a total of 32 objects. Most of which are either 3D triangles, spheres, or rectangles which were slightly bent and shaped. The trick is to do the 3D with your textures and that will keep your poly count almoast non-existant.
