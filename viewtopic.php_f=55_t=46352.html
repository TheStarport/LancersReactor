1. Strafing left-right, sometimes it helps.
2. Don't know.
3. When you have to destroy Rheinland fleet in the Tau systems, you can hide from the gunboats behind an asteroid and wait. Invincible Juni and Kress will destroy the enemies. I don't know about the latest version, but in Discovery 4.79 it was the only possible way to complete the mission, because the Rheinlanders were too tough. Also, when I returned from Tekagi's base, I had to hide somewhere for the same reason, but I don't remember where.
4. I always use mouse flight.
