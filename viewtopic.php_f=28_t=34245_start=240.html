Mace_166

Tue Jul 04, 2006 8:15 am by Mace_166

When it comes to the next version, i would like to release two versions. The complete full download and an update patch. As quite a few problems have been found i want the next release to be a big step forward rather than something to make do in the meantime.
I have found that quite a few of the errors posted on here i could not reproduce and they seem to be docking with bases and their base population. I'm hoping that once the other errors are removed it will be more stable. I'm assuming that in their case the base might have the ini entry too many type problem which might just be causing the game engine to say, "sod it, i'll just dump them on the desktop".
I've been given a few ideas for the next version and quite a few of them aren't going to be included. Its a fine balance between multiplayer and single player and if you gear too much towards one it causes problems with the other.
Version 3, (most likely v1.74), will be aimed at stablitity and removing mistakes. There will be very few additions to this version. There will be, however, an "events" system and the Eastern Coalition have unique repscreen entries.
I've got to admit that i have had less interest in working on this project since its release. It will be supported, but i'm not planning on spending hours of my time working on it right now. August sounds like a reasonable target seeing as i have so many (damn) errors to fix.
Cheers for you interest.
