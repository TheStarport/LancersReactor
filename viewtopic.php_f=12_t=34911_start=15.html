Hmmmm. This website actually appears to be derived from some studies in the past that have produced consistent results as far as an individual's broad and general pyschological make up based upon each person's subjective affinity to colors. But the studies never really got that close being able to predict personality to the level this site presents.

Those older studies provided only basic color influences such as:

Favorite color is green = person seeks security, safety
Environmental color is red = more alert and awake state is promoted
Oddly, most often those with psychological disorders chose yellow as their favorite color.

Even so, I thought that the test has me "summarized" in generalities fairly well but I agree with Grom, if you read every horoscope in the paper today, you will find something under each sign that echoes with you to some degree.
