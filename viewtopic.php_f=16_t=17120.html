Sat May 17, 2003 5:27 pm by |707th|Selerox

You cab bribe anyone, no matter how much they hate you. But the more they hate you, the more money you'll have to shell out.

You also have to bear in mind that if you bribe factions, you become less freindly with all their enemies. So bribing a faction like the Corsairs or Outcasts will annoy a lot of people, and bribing the Hessians will make you seriously unpopular with the Crosairs and most of Rheinland. On the plus side, bribing house police/military will boost your reputation with the whole house.
