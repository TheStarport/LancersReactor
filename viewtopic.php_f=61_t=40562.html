Hi Buck,

ERROR: cannot create_instance(). Archetype (0xbcc74b87) has no file assigned
this is the entry in the Engine_equip ini and engine_good ini for ge_be_engine_01

The other one is not a native FL one, it will be one of your modded entries.

I think the entries you get on your screen (last part of your post) are caused on the console when the server pc is also being used as a player. i.e. both a player and the server are being used on the same pc. This doesnt happen when the pc is only used by the server. Hope that makes sense.

It may be I have to start locking some so ppl get the message..

There are two types of archetype errors.. one where you change something on a standard Freelancer item and it goes wonky... these are easy to identify.. as the hashcodes are already known
The second type is one where you create a new item and stuff up somewhere and it goes wonky..it could be a scanner, engine, ship, weapon etc.. Then there are two ways to find it.. create your own database.. and use that.. or.. WATCH the console.. most times even not knowing what the item is as freelancer spawns ships etc.. the error code will appear on the screen.. now it still can take some detective work to find.. or you can run accushot' s FLScan which can also help..
