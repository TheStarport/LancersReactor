Changing of the Guard

Well, it's been a fairly low-key thing, but an epoch in the history of this mod has passed. Warzog, lead programmer, lead designer, and general everyman for The Reckoning, has stepped down from his de facto role as project manager after almost exactly one year at the reins. Thanks, man, and good luck. It's been a hell of a ride; we couldn't have done it without you.

Which leaves me, I suppose, back in charge of things. What we essentially have is a mature product, fine-tuned and painstakingly engineered over the course of many months, and I don't want to make any drastic changes to it - at least, not yet. First, we need to regroup and reassess.

If you think you can contribute to the mod, or have worked on it in the past, drop me a line at bobmcdob@gmail.com, or at the team mail of reckoning@solsector.net (they both go to the same place), or catch me on AIM (my screen name's mrmcdob). I've also set up a LiveJournal community here. This is not intended to replace either of our message boards, but rather act as a more organized form of the feedback threads where most of the action has occured in the past. It's still being set up, and is intended primarily as an interim stage until we get a website proper.

No matter what happens in the future, I rest assured of one thing: things will be interesting. Thanks for your interest in this little project, and best wishes to you all.

warzog

Sun Feb 13, 2005 3:53 am by warzog

Bob,
Did you added them to:
Commodities_per_faction.ini
Goods.ini
Market_Misc.ini
LootProps.ini
RMLootInfo.ini
Faction_Prop.ini
And depending on how you modified them, ShipArch.ini?

As to the no Error/Spew messages, that means that you have a very basic setup error.
Or, in plain English, you've asked Freelancer to do something it can't, under any circumstances, do.
Error/Spew messages are only generated when you do something the wrong way.
This has been one of the more time consuming problems of modding.
You'll need to undo the changes, then make one change at a time until you locate the one causing the crashes, then try and figure out why it's crashing, and then fix it, or remove the change altogether, before adding the next one.
(Loadsa FUN!)
