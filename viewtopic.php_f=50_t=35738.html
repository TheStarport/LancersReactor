If the contrail hardpoints are in the cmp file then they need to be in goods.ini
If you did not put these hardpoints in your cmp then take them out but this is not causing your crash.

if you cannot launch, check your cmp file to see if you have HpMount, HpTractor_Source, and HpEngine01 in your cmp file.
