All your answers lie in the costumes.ini file if all you want to do is change clothes, heads, or accessories. When changing accessories simply copy an accessory line from another character and paste it into Trent's section and change what accessory you want.

A word of warning: The heads have different mouth and jaw positions so only certain heads will work with certain characters, otherwise their mouths will look as though they're speaking with their lips sewn shut!

Personally, I finally settled on Trent wearing the 'rh_wilham_body' and Juni on the 'br_kaitlyn_body', both being much better than their original costumes and besides, with this costume change Juni looks like she's wearing her underwear on the outside...
