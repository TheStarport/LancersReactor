Blood-Adders are recruiting new members

Hi everybody. This is BA General Leonhart and I have an urgent message for all that are interested.
Blood-Adders are recruiting new members. We are looking for skilled pilots and good jokers (to cheer us up in hard times), or just if you wish to fly as a pirate you are welcomed as well (no matter what are your skills). If you are interested please post and I will get back to you. Now lets see what you get from becoming a member and also lets go over the rules and regulations:

Rules, Regulations, and Your Rights

Free protection from everybody. No matter who attacks you the rule is that all Adders on the server MUST assist you at once. Also note that as we are pirates therefore we will be attacking other players. This does not mean we can go and bully everybody around , therefore there are certain rules about attacking other players, these rules will be addressed once you become a member. Help from other members. This is a new rule that is in affect immediately. The older members are REQUIRED to help new members get on their feet. This may include giving credits, giving equipment and helping in missions. Also members note that you HAVE to help a new member in any way you can. If you are unable to give credits or equipment the least you can do is help them on the missions. Also note that failing to follow this rule or any other rules you may be severally punished.

One of the most important things is FUN. As BA will be REAL pirates now there will be a lot of PvP against other palyers/clans. This will make game experience more fun and will make our server little more exiting place. And I am looking forward to it, so I truly hope that some of you are willing to join us and share our experience!!


I hope to see you all as my wingman,

Sincerely,
Leonhart




Many other clan rules will be found in the Blood-Adders section on servers web site.
All other rules you can find in RULES section of our website.
