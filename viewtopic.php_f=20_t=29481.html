What kind of firewall? Are you referring to Windows XP's built-in "firewall"? I'll assume that that is what you are referring to. To disable what MS laughingly term a "firewall", go to "Network Connections", right-click on your connection; eg. LAN, Cable, Modem, etc, and select "Properties". Go to the "Advanced" tab, and turn off the firewall.
