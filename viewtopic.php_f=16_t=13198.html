There are TWO black holes in Freelancer. None were fully completed by Digital Anvil.

THE FIRST BLACK HOLE: The first black hole is more of an Event horizon. As most of you might know, it is accesed by going to Omicron Gamma and going to sector 2F where there is an Unknown Jump Hole. There you will find Planet Primus (Home of the monkeys) and Planet Gammu (Home of the robots; are they evil robots?). Plus a infinite assortment of the good old Nomads. Note: you cant buy, go on missions, retrive information, or anything from the Monkeys and the Gammu Robots (evil robots?). Personally it is an easter-egg, not a mission bag.

The big black hole with the red swirls (shown at E3). Ready.......It is in an unfinished system known as OMEGA-13. I did not belive it either.
