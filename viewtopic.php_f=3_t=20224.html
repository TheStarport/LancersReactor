Thu Jun 26, 2003 1:07 pm by Gung_Ho

It is area specific to an extent on many ships. These are the ships with seperate hp values for various components in the shiparch.txt files. For most fighters, this includes irrelavent damage, like wings, tailfins and such. It also includes weapons themselves, shield generators, thrusters and components of that nature. Any component which displays visibly on the ship is vulnerable. Shield gens, weapons, mines, countermeasures etc are vulnerable. Beware if you have one of these shot off: You will lose the weapon if you dock - the best option is often suicide to keep your items.

Of course, you will likely only have the chance to blow off one or two of these components before the hull of the ship caves in. However, a really nasty tactic when a person is afk is to blow off that class 10 weapon they have equipped without killing them, and wait for them to dock for repairs. I've never done this myself (i'd feel too bad even if i didn't like the person), but the possibility is there.
