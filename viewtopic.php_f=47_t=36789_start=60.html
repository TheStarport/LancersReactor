I see that you have elected to ally yourself with the Hessians. Despite what you may have heard, I am not an unreasonable man. In fact, I do not hold this act against you in any way. Your current situation is a difficult one, of that there is no doubt. You currently have but one base and your financial situation is highly unattractive. Your income is a low 35 credits per turn and you have no access to some of the basic materials necessary for you to survive and thrive. Clearly you need an ally. I maintain that the Hessians are not the ally you want, however.

If you truly wish to change the funamental nature of Rheinland's society why not do it from the inside? Join me and take control of one of our great corporations. As Chancellor I can install whomsoever I please into the position of CEO, should the situation require it. Instead of working to destroy the foundations of our great society, help me re-make it. Help me fix the problems that my predecessors caused. All of Rheinland will be ever grateful.

The benefits to you are quite palpable as well. Should you elect to take control of Daumann, our largest corporation you will have a total of 6 bases (one of which is a shipyard) and one planet under your control. Your revised income would be 130 Credits per turn and you will recieve 30 Metal, 6 Water, 16 Oxygen, 1 Food and 1 H-Fuel per turn.* This is clearly a better deal for you.
