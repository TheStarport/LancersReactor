Ro9ue

Wed Mar 17, 2004 11:32 am by Ro9ue

This Rebalance Mod is the best mod for Freelancer, in my opinion.
The things that it adds and changes is far too long to tell you in a single post (check for more complete Details at RebalanceMod.com).
It adds around 100 ships; around 50 (maybe even more ) new weapons and tweaks/changes others; adds new star systems with new bases and factions; and increases the skill level of AI ships -- all NPCs use Nanobots and Shield Batteries, along with drastically improving their loadouts. You can also take on missions in which you destroy Battleships and Gunboats, which are among the most fun experiences for a freelancer.
Like I said, this is just an extremely brief summary of the changes made by th e mod, all of which make the story and open-ended play (both Single- and MultiPlayer) incredibly fun.
