I don't even have a freelancer.cfg.

I do have these two instead:

C:\Documents and Settings\<username>\My Documents\My Games\Freelancer\perfoptions.ini

C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer\EXE\freelancer.ini

Both contains screen resolution settings, but it seems that the first takes precedence - which makes a lot of sense since two users may prefer different resolution settings.

So I guess you should edit your perfoptions.ini.
