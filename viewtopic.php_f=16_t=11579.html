Paul MJH

Tue Mar 25, 2003 6:04 am by Paul MJH

So far I have found that the weapons used by a faction do not vary from one to the other unless they are flying different classes of ship. That is an outcast flying a dagger will have a different weapons layout than an outcast flying a saber. Which is fairly obvious. Those numbers indicate a 40% swing so choosing wisely will save your sit down. The scanner (wire frame) in your ship will allow you to identify all the components of a targeted ship so make use of it. The various defensive and offensive systems is one of the better aspects of the game. Any defense can be countered. We call it an "arms race". What you do with your knowledge is called strategy. There are no dangerous weapons only dangerous people.
