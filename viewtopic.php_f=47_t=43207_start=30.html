- In the calculator, Smugglers get +5C foundation bonus. This is not listed in the rules.

- The Starting Fleet listing in the calculator is completely wrong.

- The cell calculating Total Money This Turn is missing Medium Faction's Foundatione (e.g. +C8), Corporation Wealth bonus (+C14), Smuggler bonus (if it should be there, +C16) and uses number of in-game Battleships (C24) instead of Total Money Earned (C25)

- Tech Modifiers are not included in the calculator.

- Small Transport is called Freighter in the calculator.

- All Transports get -1C cost for Corporations in the calculator.

- Houses get -5M for capships price according to the calc, but in the rules it's -10M.

- Salvagers don't get the -1M cost for all ships bonus in the claculator.

- Effect of the Improved Navigation research is not included in the calculator.

- Cost/resource differencies between the calculator and rules:
Tech Research - 30C in the calculator, 25C in the rules
Exploration Party - 30C in calculator, 50C/5M/2G in the rules
Omega-7 - 0O in the calc, 3O in the rules
Galileo - 0M/5O in the calc, 5M/0O in the rules

I'm not finished yet, but most of the issues you mentioned have been fixed. I made a change to the system used to determine starting fleets as well, However I'm not going to actually make the changes to the calculator until everyone has a chance to comment on the change.
