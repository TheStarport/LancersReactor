Star Wars, the dark side

Yes, it really happened.

That's the answer to the question most people ask, when the topic of the Star Wars Holiday Special comes up. Many people actually did see it in its first and only airing on November 17, 1978. Because of, well, pretty much everything about the show, you're justified if you doubt your own memory. It's not really what you'd expect would have come from George Lucas.

For those not in the loop, for those who might have heard whispers of this show, from strangers lurking in corners, here's the deal:

The Star Wars Holiday Special is a two-hour Christmas special in the tradition of holiday cartoons and shows from the 1970s and 1980s, with everyone from Yogi Bear to Alvin and the Chipmunks, all with hijinks ensuing around a holiday theme. Christmas is a big time for the kids, and cartoon people would be nuts not to capitalize on it. Well, the Star Wars gang did the same thing.
