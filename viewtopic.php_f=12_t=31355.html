why the hell are you fiddling about in the registry? please don't unless you REALLY have to and even then just do exactly what you're told to do. This is the real heart of Windoze, it's (very crudely) a huge database that contains all the info regarding your hardware/software, all your apps and settings and drivers that Windoze needs to refer to in order to run.
