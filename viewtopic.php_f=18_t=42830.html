MarketGood= explained?

Hi,

I am still searching for a definate documentation of the MarketGood line for bases buying/selling commodities. Don't get me wrong, I know most pieces now after lots of hours for searching and thinking, there's even wrong docs outthere

I also did a search on the forums with "MarketGood" w/o any relevant information.

MarketGood = <good>, <level required>, <reputation required>, <unk0>, <unk1>, <unk2>, <price factor>

<good> - The nickname taken from goods.ini.
<level required> - Level required to buy this item (usually vessels have it >0).
<reputation required> - Minimum reputation required to buy this item (-1.00000 to 1.00000).
<unk0> and <unk1> - Still unknown afaik. "Rumours say", the values are 150 and 500 if the base sells this item and not only buys it for a special price below or above the default price. But who wants to rely upon rumours?
<unk2> - Still unkown afaik. Looks like a boolean switch (0/1 value).
<price factor> - factor of the price from goods.ini

So... unk0 is 150 and unk1 is 500 if the base sells this item. What do these values mean?
