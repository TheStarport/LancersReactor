BACKSTORY
---------

The year is 2681. Eleven years has passed since the destruction of the Steltek drone and the death of the Retro movement. Shortly after, the Empire of Kilrah surrendered to the Terran Confederation. But out on the frontiers, in the Border Worlds where policing is scarce, little has changed. Piracy is more widespread than ever, joined by ex-Kilrathi warriors turned raiders. The economy is slowly recovering after three decades of total war. But out on the frontiers, there are a surpluses of brave pilots, and an economic boom that brings dangerous work. Anyone with the right ship and enough guts can make a killing ... or die trying.

Confed
Drastic cutbacks in military spending has forced the once-mighty Confederation Armed Forces to curtail their operations in Gemini. Despite the threat of Kilrathi attack being higher than it has ever been, the only active base in the sector is Perry Naval Station.

Pirates
Years of lax patrolling and focus on the war has stimulated the rise of massive pirate cartels. Despite the best efforts of the militia, the groups are far too well entrenched. The best that can be managed is containment, and with pirate links to military contractors a commonly whispered rumor, their task is only more complicated.

Militia
After the war, the RIF forced many of the Confed's best pilots into civillian life. As celebration turned to confusion, many found solace in the bottle. Others joined sector militias, doing what they do best. These are mostly skilled veterans, older than many of the proper military types, and just as deadly despite their older equipment.

Kilrathi
Once a proud, honorable race, the destruction of their homeworld and death of their empire have relegated the Kilrathi to a race without a home. In the chaos of postwar, prominent clans turned against each other in a bitter fight for superiority. Those lesser hrai who did not have the resources to compete turned to piracy in the border worlds. These Kilrathi have a grudging respect for the humans who bested them, and their mutual hatred of the Confederation has united them in an uneasy alliance with the pirate cartels.
