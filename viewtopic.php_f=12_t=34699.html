Redundant Acronym Syndrome

NewScientist's Feedback pointed this out a while back, where you might encounter signs or people which/who say words and then add acronyms either through habit or simply to sound intelligent. I will give you an example:-
Please enter you Personal Identification PIN Number

Silly person; this, of course, means "Please enter you Personal Identification Personal Identification Number Number".

Perhaps a more common one here:-
The TLR

The The Lancers' Reactor

N.B. When I said more common I meant as in compared to entering PIN codes

Can anyone else think of an example? I'm collecting them (though not entirely sure why).
