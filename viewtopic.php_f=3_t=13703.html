pressgod

Sat Apr 12, 2003 5:34 am by pressgod

I always save 1 hard point for missles as they can do a great deal of damage when used properly.
These are not the type of missles where you can fire and forget. You must first weaken the enemy ship and get the angle on him. Do NOT try to shoot one up his tail pipe as they usually turn very quickly and they will miss, unless it is a very slow or very large ship. Then you probably want to use Torps to take them down anyway.
I like to use a combo of Torps, Missles and Mines to take out a group of targets quickly. But the best use is head to head or a side angle shot at a close range with their sheilds weakend by canon fire or a shield weakening weapon. Usually one missle is all you need using this tactic.
You can also straffe them with your mines as you go by but be careful as you can also damage your own ship it you run into them later in the battle. If they didn't find a target yet it could just be you who becomes the prey of those toys.
Just some observatons from the edge of space.

LancedBoil

Sat Apr 12, 2003 6:08 pm by LancedBoil

I gave up on missiles about 1/2 way through the plot missions, I figured the large number of gunpoints on the heavy and very heavy fighters more than made up for a lack of a missile launcher. 6 or 7 gun points shooting at once does more damage than a single missile, and you don't have to pay to reload the guns. But at the beginning of the game, when you don't have access to the better ships, I think the missiles are a great asset. A good missile launcher gives even the lightest light fighter a real heavy punch. I think that's why they are put in there. When you first start, you only really have 4 or 5 gun points, and the guns you can put there aren't that lethal. Having a heavy hitting missile launcher really helps when you get jumped by 4 or more baddies.

I know having a missile launcher saved my ass plenty of times when I was stuck flying a Startracker. But I don't understand the need for multiple missile launchers, but to each his own.
