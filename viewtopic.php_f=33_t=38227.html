Heyas,

The speed is only the listed maximum potential speed of the engine. It's affected by the mass of your ship, the less massive your ship, the faster it will go. Do NOT try to decrease your ship's mass to 0, it will cause strange things to happen. I normally don't set a ship's mass to lower than 100, unless it's a really light fighter, like the Ghost X-9.
