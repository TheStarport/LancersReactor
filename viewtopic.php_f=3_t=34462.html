VroomZo

Fri Sep 03, 2004 12:04 pm by VroomZo

Target view is an advanced sensor option. You can use it to target specific parts of a ship so that your guns will be focused on a part of a ship instead of the general body of the ship. You can also use target view to check if a wreck is looted from long range. This is essential for getting Ripper mines easily. Also, target view will allow you to see what ship your target has even if they're not within sensor range.
