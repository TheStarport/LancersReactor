We didn't do much except pretend to play instruments and sign contracts while some people in a studio hundreds of miles away recorded the actual songs.

Anyway, after a bit the project collapsed and me and my friend (I was penniless, he had about $100) wandered the streets for a bit, after being very rude and drunken to the manager and being kicked out.

After an hour or two, we went into a Wild West style bar/restaurant. When we got inside, we noticed it was long, far longer than it was wide, like a train. The seats were very odd, with metal bars sticking out at all angles, forcing people to sit facing away from the people next to them.

After a bit the train/bar/restaurant started to accelerate at very high speed, the other people apparently not noticing, and it became very hard to stay in my poorly designed seat. Everyone seemed to be shouting and some were swearing, but not in a violent kind of way. I stood up, and suddenly started shaking violently (I think I did it in rl, too). At that point, I realised it was a dream, but decided to stick atound for a bit just to see what would happen. However, everything faded away and there I was in my bed.
