You need a mod to get back into the SP-only systems such as Nomad homeworld, Nomad lair, Omicron Minor, Tohoku, and Alaska...there are several such mods on the TLR downloads page, most of which only take effect if you start a new game.

The Omicron Alpha unknown jumphole leads to an empty system, populated only by nomads. There's not much to see there.

The other unknown jumphole is in Omicron Gamma, Corsair space. It contains the two easter egg planets, and is also populated by Nomads.
