Tue Mar 25, 2003 8:24 am by taikau

Well done wonderer but i'm sorry to say that your mooring isn't fix in your way. Let me help you in that. For the mooring to fix this is what you should do.

1.open shiparch.ini
change the "can_use_berths " to "can_use_med_moor "

**It is better to use medium moor than large moor cause using large moor sometimes will cause hang is you didn't press esc when the ship stop in cinematic but using medium moor your ship will always hit the hull and bounce back and try it again. So if you forget to press esc you can try again when the ship move back into mooring.****

2.open solararch.ini
find dock_ring then change the docking sphree from ring to jump
example:
change
docking_sphere = ring, HpDockMountA, 40, Sc_open dock a
docking_sphere = ring, HpDockMountB, 40, Sc_open dock b
to
docking_sphere = jump, HpDockMountA, 40, Sc_open dock a
docking_sphere = jump, HpDockMountB, 40, Sc_open dock b

**because the ring can't use medium moor, i have change it to jump so that all type of ship will be able to enter planet without problem. Even your battle ship**

That's all. I'm sure it is much more easy. I'll have to say thanks to wonderer and some other guys who is have forgotton his nickname cause without their guide i won't be able to understand all this.
