SuPaFrEaK

Sat Mar 15, 2003 7:23 pm by SuPaFrEaK

In order to view the ini files, you need to uncompress them. There are a few programs floating around the forums, but the most used one is called Bini. You can find it in the Utilities section of this site. It.s called "FreeLancer INI uncompressor (command line, v1.1)". After getting this program, place it in the directory where the ini's are (recommened), then drag the ini over bini.exe. What this does is it decompresses the ini and it pops out as "whatever.ini.txt". This is a plain text file. In order to get the game to read your uncompressed ini, you need to rename it from "whatever.ini.txt" to just "whatever.ini". There is no need to recompress it.

NOTE: ALWAYS BACK UP YOUR ORIGINAL INI's FIRST! I suggest you do this by creating a new folder called "BACKUP", then create subfolders for all the INI's (e.g. EQUIPMENT, SHIPS, etc.), then copy all the original INI's into their respective folders.

As for editing them, I will not go into detail about how to do that. I suggest you spend a day reading throught the forums and reading through the INI's. I learned how to mod Freelancer in one day by asking Q's on the forum and reading through everything.

And a suggestion to all you newbie modders/editors out there: DO NOT ask a question about how to do something withough reading through the ENTIRE forum. If you cant see the older topics, go up to the top of the page and where you see "Show topics from last 30 days (or whatever it says), change it to "Show topics from last year". That way, you can see EVERY topic anyone has ever posted.
