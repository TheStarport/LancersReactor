You were going in the right direction heh...

Systems_Shortest.ini = Shortest route using both jump gates and holes

Systems_Illegal.ini = Shortest route via jump holes

Systems_legal.ini = Shortest route via jump gates

Using FLExplorer, you can actually add your custom systems to the mix correctly, including paths from the originals and updates reflecting gates and holes between custom and origianl systems.

If you did your systems by hand and not with FLEx., you'll have to recreate the gates and holes in each of the custom systems. If you started out with FLEx., just create an "unnatural" link, prefereably from a custom system to an original that you don't intend to be there. After creating the gates and holes, remove both, including the zones that are left after deletion. The path files should then be updated, adding your custom system to the list and adding paths from the originals. This can alter the paths to and from other systems, making use of the shorter routes you may have created. Also, you may have to do this for each of the custom systems as sometimes it covers them all at once, or just the one at a time.
