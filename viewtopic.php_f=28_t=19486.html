Wed Jun 18, 2003 2:30 pm by Zytec

In the shiparch.ini file (you need to open this with BiniQDU or something that will read ini files) on each ship there is a part that says cargo. You can change this number to whatever you wat - the higher the number, the more the ship holds. To find the ship, you need to use the ship ID's. On this link is a list of them (paste the link into your address bar!):

http://www.lancersreactor.com/t/forum/t ... diting&M=F

Just search for the id in shiparch.ini and then change the cargo amount. Thats it! Hope that helps.

Zytec

Tue Jun 24, 2003 9:19 am by Zytec

It should let you change ini files, but they might be read-only. To solve this, right click on the file you want to change and at the bottom tick off of the box that says read only. That is hopefully the problem.

As for Heretic, check the link I put at the top of this thread - ship ID's are in that. I did have a look at the shiparch.ini, and I found these are the ID's for the titan:
ids_name = 237021
ids_info = 66542
ids_info1 = 66543
ids_info2 = 66608
ids_info3 = 66544
Just search for one of the numbers in the ini file, and then check that it is the right one by making sure its hit points is at 12600. Also, its nickname is co_elite2, so you could also search for that (infact that would be quicker as long as you check it is the right ship!).

Hope that helps you both...

Zytec
