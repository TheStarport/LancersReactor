Chips

Thu Oct 20, 2005 12:14 pm by Chips

Have you tried the idea of looking for the references?

Take a peek at the cloak and the engine entries - you will see the effects that they use. Then search the files for those names instead.

They didn't just stick the effects in the effects.ini for the game engine to decide to use... each entry in the files (weapon_equip and engine_equip) actually have lines stating which effects they use.

The whole game works on the same principles, everything is referenced to something
