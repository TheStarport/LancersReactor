Fri Jun 06, 2003 3:05 pm by richard_s

Yes you can change Trent’s outfit at anytime during the game. It is simply a matter of renaming the body files found in the Freelancer\Data\Characters\Bodies directory.

First make a back up copy of the whole Bodies directory in case you make a mistake or want to restore the originals easily.

From the Freelancer\Data\Characters\Bodies directory choose the body file you want Trent to have .

His default body is: pl_trent_body.dfm.

Copy the body file you require to another directory , change its name to the above and copy it back to replace the default.

You can do this at at any point in the game and as often as you like.
