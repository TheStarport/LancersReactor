Um... since you're working in Max, you *should* be able to mesh it, and then use Select by Facet to create Groups, which you can then name appropriately. Rhino's a little easier to use for this stuff than many other programs, because with the 3.0+Bonus Tools, you can take NURBs surfaces, perform a bunch of Booleans to create your geometry, and then mesh it and explode the result, and the exploded mesh will then consist of every area with coplanar faces, which means that you don't have to Group things tri by tri.

But... you can create Groups directly in UVMapper or even in MS3D. It's just a matter of making sure that before you create the uv-maps, you've sorted your geometry out carefully, is all.
