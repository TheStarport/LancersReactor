Cool bug

I don't know if this is a MOD bug, but I suspect it is not. I'm only using the Slave 1 mod currently. I suspect this has happened to others though - please reply if so!

I was harassing a pirate base somewhere between Bretonian and Rheinland space this morning when I noticed I was flying backwards through the clouds. More specifically, my ship was either moving forward at 999km/h, or gradually slipping backwards at about 186km/h. It made for some very interesting dogfights - it was like being stuck on a spring! I could line up & kill the bad guys at the ends of the arcs, but their missiles and shots missed me completely. When I hit afterburner, the ship would to 299 or 300 km/h forward. When I hit the cruise drive, all normal control resumed.

Has this happened to anyone else?

I don't think this is a bug, it happens when you kill you engines. You can press "z" (default key) and you will continue forward with your momentum.
