Tseng

Thu Feb 20, 2003 3:39 pm by Tseng

Basically it's just a game of patience, keeping the trade lane down until you die or get bored of pirating. If you have quick eyes, you can see ships zooming towards/by you in a lane but by that time it's pretty much too late to take the lane down if it's still up because of the immense speed they travel at.

If you have a large amount of people, you could easily organize a few different groups to each hit a popular trade lane (really, you only need two people for one ring -- one to make sure the lane stays down and one to pick off the computer's ships. Both of you together to take out players); or if you're feeling mean, camp all the trade lanes around an important trade spot like Manhatten and pillage everyone.
