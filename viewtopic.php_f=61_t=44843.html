Bejaymac

Wed Oct 18, 2006 3:42 am by Bejaymac

Twice in a week, this problem is spreading.

Your CMP has material assignments coded in to it, they have to match with the material names in the MAT, if they don't match you get an invisible ship. If you built it in a program other than MS3D then the importing into MS3D might have screwed up you material assignments, check in milkshapes 3D window that your model is actually textured before you export to CMP & MAT.

*EDIT*
It could also be a case of bad textures, if you have Hardcmp then open your CMP with it, if your MAT is in the same folder or 1 up from the CMP then Hardcmp will find it and apply it. If you see your model with it's textures then you know your textures are bad (hardcmp isn't as fussy as FL), however if you see a dark brown almost black object in the shape of your ship then it's the material assignments that are screwed.
