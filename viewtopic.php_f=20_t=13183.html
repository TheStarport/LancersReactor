Well, I seem to have solved my crash problem - to a certain extent at least. The trick seems to have been to reduce the load on my Geforce card. Here's what I did:
- Reduce the hardware acceleration to the minimum FL will run with.
- Reduce the FL graphic details a notch or two
- Reduce to nothing, the Background Stars slider under Detail *** this seems to have made the biggest impact
- Reduce Space Detail to about 50%
- Turn off the Dynamic Comms window
- Turn off Background Dust
- Turn off 3D sound

Now I only seem to crash when I try to go to the Commodity Trader on Manhatten and other similar planets. FL crashes when it tries to start the intro sequence for the Trader. On stations and outside traders on planets, the intro does not cause a crash.

Thus far I've played continuously for quite a few hours without an in-space crash - although I have died a few times :
