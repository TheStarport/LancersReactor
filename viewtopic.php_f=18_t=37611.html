Argh

Sat Feb 12, 2005 12:40 am by Argh

Regroup your groups in MS3D, if at ALL possible.

Or tell Meshconverter that your model consists of 5 sets of parts... and then define each part hierarchically. The only problem being that MS3D's assignments as to what is the "top" listing in the Groups is what the CMP Exporter uses to assign the various VMeshrefs... so if you weren't super-careful about what stuff is in what order... you're going to have a bumpy road getting everything to work like it should.
