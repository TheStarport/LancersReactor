Megistos

Fri Apr 23, 2004 8:24 am by Megistos

It resists Laser, Photon and Pulse weapons. With the exception of the high level GMG and BH weapons, those types are mostly low level (shield strength has little bearing on Pulse weapons anyway). In outer systems, you're much more likely to run across Tachyon and Neutron weapons. These give Positron shields an extra 20% protection which brings them up to the same level. Particle weapons aren't too rare either, so the same can hold for Molecular shields
