Wed Mar 19, 2003 5:11 pm by RunOrDie

I suggest you forget going to the Border World systems in that junk heap. You'll get eaten alive while still dazed from the jump if the local pirates don't like you. They fly Sabres, Titans, Centurions, and other powerful ships, by the way.

In any case, some people are suicidal, so here is how to get to Omicron Theta (JH = jumphole, JG = jumpgate). Local pirate factions and some hazards are in brackets.

From Bretonia [through Tau systems:
First go to Tau-31, then
JG/JH to Tau-23 (Outasts) (low radiation damage) (minefield)
JH to Tau-37 (Outcasts)
JH to Omicron Alpha (Outcast homeworld) (moderate radiation damage)
JH to Omicron Theta (Corsairs/Outcasts).

From Bretonia (through Omega systems):
First go to Omega-7 (or Stuttgart for a JG), then
JH to Omega-11 (Red Hessians) (moderate radiation damage)
JH to Omega-41 (Corsairs/Red Hessians) (severe radiation damage) (minefield)
JH to Omicron Theta (Corsairs/Outcasts).


I think that's it. I don't have a map so I might be wrong about the Omega path.
