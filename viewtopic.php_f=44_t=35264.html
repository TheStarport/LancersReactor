Wasabe

Tue Oct 19, 2004 10:16 am by Wasabe

Yes, it seems to work beautifully. I resized the dagger to 20 times its original to go with a stardestroyer model (great triangular shape =) and it works flawlessly. I do have some problems with changing all the components to be root, but I just don't do it and it works fine. Some ships are better for sur resizing than others. The dagger seems to work great. The bretonia freighter is also a very very good one for those roundish models (you can stretch it to be round or oval shaped, flatten it, flip it on it's side, whatever). Basically those ships with fewer components work the best, because you get more predictable results.
