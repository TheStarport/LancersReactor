5) One thing that you need to also take into account is the refire rate... the larger it is, the more .... particles of harmful energy (?) that your weapons emit over the chosen period which is rounds per minute (sorry to get slightly technical...must be from working at NASA).

The higher the rounds per minute (rpm) the lower the overall damage for each round will be...but the per second damage should be about that same as any similar weapons. Just really depends on whether you want to pack a big punch but not have as good of a chance to hitting things (lower rpm's) vs. having a greater chance of hitting the target but trading off the amount of damage that you will do for each round hitting their ship.

But that's up to you...if you're a fairly good shot then you might want to go with something that has a lower refire rate....that's just something that you will have to flesh out for yourself....
