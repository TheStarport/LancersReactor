Cruise Disruptor or Torpedo?

Should I mount a Cruise Disruptor or a Sunslayer Torpedo? My weapon loadout (on a Sabre) is 2*Wyrm Type 2, 4*Nomad Energy Blaster, 1*Adv. Debilitator Turret.

Sunslayer Torpedo can:
1. Kill any unshielded ship in one hit, and it's easy to hit if fired correctly.

Hornet Cruise Disruptor can:
2. Stop an enemy from cruising away (like in an NPC assassination mission, when the target runs towards reinforcements).
3. Easily kill a ship that has no shields and only a few hundred hull points left.
4. Be fired before real missiles in PVP as a counter-countermeasure.
5. Be used to detonate an enemy's torpedo in his face in PVP.

I'm leaning towards the Sunslayer Torpedo, because if I kill the enemy fast with a torpedo he won't get to run away (as in #2), and I don't really PVP.
