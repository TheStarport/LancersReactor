Ok, once you're in a break from the storyline missions, attack the good guys (Such as police) when they're near Jumpgates or on normal patrols. After that, you'll get neutral with the bad guys and you can buy their stuff.

To find criminal bases, switch to the Patrol Path map in your Navigational Map (Nav Map, F6) and where a heap of white lines intersect, that is mostly a base to got near that area and you'll find a base.

Good Luck!

I remember at some point along the story line, you'll end up at Bruchsal Base in the Frankfurt System. (that's the base where the Sunslayer Torpedo and Borderword Stilleto is sold, but only available at a higher levels)
However, you'll still be able to buy the weapons that will fit your ship there.
Those weapons are some of the most powerful available although they will also eat up energy. I've used the Lugars from that base with better results than Order weapons.
So, if you monitor you energy usage..they're very effective and can carry you through the remainder of the story.
