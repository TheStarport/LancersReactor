Lucky Jack

Mon Oct 18, 2004 4:46 pm by Lucky Jack

1. Once you have completed the single player game, one should be able to enter 1 of 2 different multiplayer environments: one for dialup and one for broadband -- mixing dialup with broadband introduces unacceptable lag to MP servers.

2. eliminate all "timing" weapons like the Nomad Cruise Disruptor in the Top Freelancers mod -- cut down on lag this way.

3. the multiplayer environment should become a better income producer for Microsoft so that we can look forward to more FL development without waiting 2 or 3 years between editions. You do this by:
a. free access to basic level multiplayer servers running FL 2 MP ver. 1
b. pay a fee or subscription and you get to download successive MODs made by MS FL staff adding story lines, changing ships, equipment, missions, etc.
c. there will be, of course, an independent group of MP servers that spring up around "amateur" mods -- which can be bought and paid for by Microsoft and incorporated into their Pay-Mod service, thus encouraging and benefiting the modding community while getting the bugs out professionally.

I suggest the income producing way for Microsoft as a realistic way to give MS an incentive to maintain their investment and interest in the game and to commit to continuing to expand its horizons and support the FL community.
