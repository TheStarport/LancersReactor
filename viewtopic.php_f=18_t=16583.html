Mon May 12, 2003 1:22 am by giskard

Hello.

The problem sometimes occures when the cockpit and turret views are wrong too. Sometimes a wrong angle in the shiparch.ini or a bad viewing angle in the ships cockpit file can cause those problems.

If you set it too

0,999999

Then sort out the cockpit view and shiparch ini view before finally checking at watch distance the ship turns into a square and only the engines are visable and noting that down. You should get your ships lob range.

To do that you need to add your ship to an AI so they can fly it. See ships/loads, missions/ncpships.ini and then add it to missions/faction_prop.ini in the desired faction.

Loadouts requires your shiparch.ini & goods.ini and should explain why some ships dont have all the data required for players to fly them but are still listed.

Giskard

Think I found it. Your HpMount was inside the ship. It needs to be below the ship. Placing and hardpoints inside the ship is generally a bad idea. Placing them passing through a surface is not a good idea either. Other than that you just needed to align the hardpoints on the surface of the ship. I fixed it and tried in the game after making the HPMount outside the hull and it was ok.
