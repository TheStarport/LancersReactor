I think the other Aussies have it covered, livestock, meat and weat are probably our most renowned exports. Unfortunately there isn't a wide range of farming land within Australia, most farms are situated in the coastal regions or at least within a 500 km radius of the coast or a major river artery.

Unfortuantely most of Australia is inhospitable due to the lack of rain.
To give you some idea, we only have a population of approx. 18 million in a land mass the size of the USA. At the moment the counrties water supply is under a heavy strain whereby the govt. has enforced water restrictions on most states. This generally means you can only water your garden within designated times, wash your car with a spray gun etc.
