Shield effects on custom-made ships

I have serious trouble producing a good shield effect on a self-made ship.
From the front, the model resembles this. though the wings are not that steep, but rather in a 45°.
From the front it looks like a big X, with HpMount just a bit below the lower wing ends to make it float above the floor on bases.
Now I see HpMount is also used by the shield_link. I found changing HpMount there to HpShield01, for example, change th position of the shield effect itself.

The Problem is I can't get the shields to be larg enough. With the default HpMount in the shield_link I get a shield for most of the lower half of the ship, but even the cockpit is not entirely covered with shield.

In the game this has the effect as long as the shields are up most of the upper half of the ship is unhittable, the NPCs might shoot from above but the shoots go straight through the hull until they hit the shield. Also, when shields are down they don't really try to hit the ship, but rather shoot between the two bottom wings.
And even when I clearly see they should hit ym hull, often the shots go through.

I tried FlAddRadius, which didn't help, btw.

Any suggestions?

One other thing,

Look at the size of your ship and compare it to one of the ships in game, if the ship is large, use one of the larger .sur files

If light fighter sized, use a light fighter .sur, if transport/freighter sized, use either the Rhino or Humpback..

Also the HpShield Hardpoint should be as close to the true center of the ship as you can get it.

Hey meph its me

well anyway , i use this basic rule for scaling ships.

1) import a ship as big is you want your model to be.
2) scale down your ship so it is as big as the imported ship.
3) delete the imported ship and scale your ship up to 40 - 50 times (i tough it was somewhere near this value last time checked)
