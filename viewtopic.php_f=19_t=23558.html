Doctor_Death

Sat Aug 30, 2003 3:34 pm by Doctor_Death

Check you freelancer.ini file. It's in x:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer\EXE where x: is the drive you have Freelancer installed on. There should be a line in there labeled Port=xxxx where xxxx is a 4 digit number. Make sure this is 2300. I tried putting it on 2303 and got the same problem. Changing it back listed a whole boat-load of servers. And as always, make sure Freelancer isn't running when editing the INI file.
