J Dawg

Sat Apr 24, 2004 8:18 pm by J Dawg

Kill Red Hessians. Kill them by the boatful. They are found redily in most Rheinland Systems and all Omega Systems ( I think). The Hessian Stilletos can be easily outmatched. Even if you don't get money for it, camping outside a Hessian base and killing them is the fastest way to get neutral with the corsairs. At that point land on one of their bases and bribe them and take lots of missions. The Red Hessians are one of those factions with few allies and lots and lots of enemies. Killing them will put you in good taste with many factions, but don't carry it too far. A high rep with the Corsairs also has its drawbacks. Just don't plan to show your face in Bretonia.
