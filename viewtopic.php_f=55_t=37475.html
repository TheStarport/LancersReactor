Thu Feb 03, 2005 2:25 pm by Yomahn

Hey man! I'd just like to say, Welcome to the Universe! anywayz...A good strategy for finding these wrecks (if you don't want to be told where to look) is to fly a pattern. wrecks are found in high danger areas, e.g. asteroid clouds and nebulea w/ reduced sensor efficiancy, radiation damage, and lots of dangerous criminal factions. Bring lots of bats and bots.

You know how to set waypoints, right? just set a zigzaging path through any danger zone you encounter. keep your eyes on the nav map, and if you com near a wreck it shoul pop up w/ a bright red cross.

Also, ONLY FIRE ONE GUN at a time when you see the wreck, so that you don't destroy anything on accident. its a good idea to have an empty hold, too.
