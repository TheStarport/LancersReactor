PantherX

Mon Dec 22, 2003 9:09 am by PantherX

Just highlight the face and go to FACE-->reverse vertex order.

Usually what happens is that when you mirror objects in other programs you reverse the order of the faces. Milkshape interpets the faces created clockwise as outward and anti-clockwise as facing inward. So when you import the file the mirrored objects are facing inward and appear black hence the hole.

What you want to do is go to the groups tab and select the group that's black click select then revese the vertex order. That will save a lot of time.
