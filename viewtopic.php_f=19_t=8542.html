It's actually easier to get back on the "good guys" good side. Either you hunt down and decimate Hessians, Corsairs, Outcasts etc. Or if youre able to land on a "good guy" planet/base and bribe one of the companies. Usually you choose what faction you wanna be in on and you just wail on their enemies. And if the faction you choose have allies everytime you gain a point with that faction then all their allies warm up to you too. Like someone said b4 though it's either one or the other you cant have it both ways. I have a server that I run and admin, it's an everthing goes server so PKing/pirating is allowed.
