@Esq, why did you take Bex beer that route? You could take other more profitable items from Bex to Hephaesteus only two jumps away. That was my hexagonal trade route. Bex to Hephaesteus to Karatakus to Janus to Hades to Crius to Anhur back to Bex with nothing in the hold, hopefully I would be able to get a mission near the planet to make the return trip more worth while. Like I said, Bex is horrible for selling commodities.

I never did find the stations or small planets too profitable. I wish I they were more useful. If there was one thing FL excelled at it was broadening the universe that it was full of potential. But again I cite that it was unfortunate that their commodities weren't more limited and prices weren't more dynamic.

However, Privateer 1's trading system for what Bases bought as opposed to sold, and vice versa, was the best. I often bought commodities for two bases in one load, because there weren't enough to fill a hold for one base and there was nothing else profitable to take to that base. The only bad thing was that the profits were too low to do this very effectively. Whereas FL has much too high profits for singular runs.

I will always think Priv 2 has the best trading system, even though I essentially only stuck to the big planets for my routes. The news items that actually affected prices was a really fun feature.
