Lev has it right, anyone who just charges thru the main plot is used to SP "railroad" type games and does not realise that a game like this requires a bit more creativity.

Travel to different bases, different systems and get to know the local economies ... fly in some needed commodities not because they will bring you a profit (they will which is nice) but do it because the people treated you kindly and you want to help them out .

Go for a "Drive" for a couple of hours and check out the scenery, mabe you will stumble across somthing unique.

Fancy yourself a Bounty Hunter and go looking for only the "Bring this fugitive to Justice" type jobs.

It's not all about getting "the best" of everything (although that's always fun too), name your ship ... try different loadouts to see how bad@$$ you can make your old faithfull friend.

The possibilities are almost limitless (as far as I can see, just from the demo).
