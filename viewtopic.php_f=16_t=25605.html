NukeIt

Tue Jan 27, 2004 5:12 pm by NukeIt

DO:
*Use the Battleship as cover. The battleship has a massive blind spot over its entire upper hull. Pulling in close to it can give you a much-needed break.
*Strafe when firing on generators. The more often you reverse your strafing direction, the less often you'll get hit.
*Wait as long as possible before using shield batteries. If you think you can pull back and let it recharge on its own, do so.
*Use nanos, and ONLY nanos, as soon as you take damage. Unlike shield batteries, they won't be gone after 2-3 heals.
*Kill any fighters that get above the battleship. Those are the ones that might be problematic.

DO NOT:
*Follow the recommendation to approach from below the base. By going through the large tunnel, you will actually take a bit of radiation damage, which is totally unnecessary.
*Spend a lot of time dogfighting. The faster you get into the arch, the better.
