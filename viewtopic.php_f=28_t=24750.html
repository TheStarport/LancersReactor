WC Mod Beta Test

Over the last six months, the Wing Commander mod for Freelancer, Privateer The Reckoning, has ran into a number of stalls and bumps in the road, but probably the most debilitating one has been a lack of input and beta testers. To be sure, there has been some C&C, but for the most part this mod team of one has been virtually in the dark, and work - not to mention morale - has plummeted. Meanwhile, the few willing testers have found numerous bugs - too many to submit to LancersReactor

This is the last chance. I cannot hope to do any kind of thorough testing on my own, and so I am releasing the mod to the general community. All I ask for this is a quick review (posted to this thread, preferbly), suggestions to improve the mod, and detailing of any bugs found.

http://blacklance.org/TC/Privateer_The_ ... .zip.flmod

Here are some screenshots.

http://www.crius.net/zone/showthread.php?t=7378

Have fun, and "remember our bargain".

Bob McDob
Lead Designer/Producer/Modeler/etc.

rfc812

Thu Oct 02, 2003 8:12 am by rfc812

I have downloaded the Privateer mod (being a WC Privateer fan myself) and have been playing it for the last few days. It is a really good mod for not being 100% complete. This is what I myself have discovered so far that could be fixed in the mod:

- The game crashed on me when I was in the Badlands area of Sol heading for the Aggressor wreck there.
- Mission cards still show faction names from original FL game, not the modded one.
- When flying toward a mission waypoint, your ships engine sound decreases to nothing until you come out of cruise mode.
- Shield batteries and nanobots still show up as dropped loot.
- The "booklet" that comes with your mod seems to be the ONLY way to find out the power level capability of your ship, since this info does not show up in the info cards.
- ALL Power Plant cards read the same. The price changes, but all the Power Plants read as a level 0 power plant.

Thats all I noted for now. Will continue playing the mod and provide any further details as I come across them. All in all, a really great mod!
