All right, if you choose to do a hit and fade attack. It isn't a battle to the death. Its a battle for x number of turns. This would be done to slowly wear down a base instead of overrunning it.

For example before you say:

I attack with x number of LF Fighters Freeport 4

In this you say I attack with x number of LF Fighter Freeport 4 for 4 combat turns

You don't take the station but you kill ships. It lets people have a chance to survive. I won't lie, this degrades the dominance of the house in the game. But it lets pirates actually act like pirates and corporations to act like corporations. In other words its an actual roleplay instead of a statistics game.

*spoiler*














Let me give you this analogy to why this is better, Freelancer you fight the Nomads and Rheinland gradually with your band of heros. Well wouldn't from a military sense, it be better for them to throw everything they had at the Osiris in one battle and wipe the Order out. But that would be a pretty dumb game. Its the same way in this. House leaders should play their role as house leaders not all wanna be Napoleons out for galactic domination. Corporations and Pirates should not be mini-houses, but allowed to do what they do, trade and steal. The military juggernaut at the moment is the only angle people can play right now. The way I propose you can actually ROLE play.
