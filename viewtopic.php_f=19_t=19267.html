666 Squadron - all newbies pay attention!

The newbie clan that has been mentioned over the past couple of weeks has finally been set up under the name "666 Squadron"! We have administrators and a few semi-experienced players as squadron-leaders, and a website at:

http://free.hostdepartment.com/6/666squadronhq

Anyone new to the Freelancer multiplayer element and wishing to join, contact us at:

squadron_666@yahoo.com

Membership is non-exclusive, but we only have a limited number of places at the moment (as I have to ratify all members by hand!). We should even have a forum running by next week, as our very own DeaconCrude has agreed to make one for us over the weekend! We focus heavily on co-operative play and multiplayer techniques, which will hopefully make for a close-knit clan of professionals by the year 2130...we all stay in touch via MSN messenger, so if you don't have it and you want to join, it might be an idea to download it. We don't refuse entry to anyone as long as they're new to the game, and we aim to make things fairer for the less experienced pilots out there!

(Any more experienced people out there, just have a look at the website and tell us what you think - you can never too much feedback from anyone!)
