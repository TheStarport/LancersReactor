I don't know. Have you checked the forum of the folks who put out FLE? Go to the EOA forum and they have a pinned section dedicated to FLE. I'm sure someone else has probably asked this question before. If not you can ask your question of Louva-Deus there.

Edit = Ok, I went in and looked at the navmap, systems and solar files. If you look at your DATA/UNIVERSE/SYSTEMS/BR01 you'll see Br01.ini. Open this and find Br01_01. It has an archetype = planet_earthgrncld_4000.
Go into DATA/SOLAR and find your solararch.ini. Now find that solararch (planet_earthgrncld_4000). You'll notice that the last line in here is "shape_name = NNM_SM_MEDIUM_FOREST_MOON" This line is what I think you have to change to get a new icon.
So now to find your choices go to DATA/INTERFACE/NEURONET/NAVMAP/NEWNAVMAP/SPACEOBJECTS. This is what I think the shape_name is refering to. In here you can see all your choices. I've never tried this so I have no clue if it will work but good luck.

Edit#2 = Oh yeah, for finding something in 20 minutes you couldn't find in 2 days I expect to have a base named after me in your mod.
