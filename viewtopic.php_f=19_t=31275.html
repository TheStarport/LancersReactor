Freelancer Heaven can be a fun server for you. It frequently changes how a new player starts. one day you can be an outcast ship at buffalo base, another might find you in new london fending off bretonia's enemies. It's a very simple way to keep the server a little dynamic, but it's fun. my favorite character started at the lane hacker base with an anubis ship. I made quite a bit of creds raiding those transports and tractoring in engine parts.

Most servers give a credit handout or change your faction settings to neutral. Freelancer Heaven actually starts you out as an outcast, or corsair, or police, or even the mollys, gaians or xenos. I heard that when the host gets his cable connection, the server will support 25 players or more.
