Fallout

Thu Mar 27, 2003 4:27 am by Fallout

Scan your enemies (press O) and see what weapons they use. Then equip the shields that will counter their weapons best. It also depends on your profession. If you're a pirate in Bretonia I think you'd get a shield that is better at countering Tachyon weapons (if I remember correctly this is what the Bretonian police/navy use). If you're one of the good guys you'd need something different for the weapons the mollys and corsairs use in that region.
