The max the game is programmed for is 128 players for starters. that would be the absolute most you would ever see. Secondly the absolute best servers have the best hardware, T3 lines, etc. and they can't support more than 40 tops. The reason is that the game moves way too fast and the graphics are way too good. Especially when you get into an asteroid or debris field or a cloud of some sort. All these things tax the server systems. Third, the connection speed of the players can lag the whole universe. For instance if 15 people log on with 56k it will cause lag even to DSL or broadband players. Every server i think pretty much started at 128 but shaved it down some as they realized they couldn't support it. It would be nice to play with 128, but I'd rather play somewhere stable with less players
