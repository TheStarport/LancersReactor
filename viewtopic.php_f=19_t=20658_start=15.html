I did my own tests with FLServer 1.0 and found that even a simple money cheat caused an extra 4ms lag to all players on a server. Cloaking mods add about 8msec as the system doesn't know how to calculate it right and display it. In same cases it can even take down the server. So much for Harmless ehh.

Weapons that shoot farther than the game expects cause lag in multiple ways. 1) the game has to now render all ships and units in the area the gun fires through. 2) Data has to be sent to an increased number of players that a shot went by at a certain trajectory. 3) Larger packet sizes are sent that may be the cause of some of the memory leaks as the game was designed for packets of a certain size. In worst case scenarios these can cause buffer overruns that can take down the whole server, not just that app.
