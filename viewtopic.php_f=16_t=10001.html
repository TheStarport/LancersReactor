Becoming a Crosair *kinda spoiler*

Well i recently completed the single player missions and due to an unfortuante situation with my appt complex can not play on the internet yet. So i decided to become the baddest mo fo in SP i could become. The first thing i did was collect a lot of cash. I did this by getting the japanese cargo ship and did a few circles around sirius(mainly japan to us to britonia and back to japan), very proftiable. When i had about 600000 creds i went to new berlin and bought a falcon. From there i headed to omega 11. The neat thing about omega 11 is there are only red hessians and the daumann. Well i was not in very good terms with the hessians at this point so to change that i attacked the daumann, the only problem here is that once you do this you can land on the daumann base and you can land on the hessian's base for a while. And at the very begginng you will have to deal with both factions. But after you kill enough daumann's and no hessians you can finally go land on their base. From there i earned some cash killing bounty hunters. Don't stick with the hessians too long or the crosairs wont like you. I stayed here untill the corsairs were about -.4 and then jumped to omega 41. there is a freeport station here which is nice. Use the freeport station to stay healthy while you kill bounty hunters. Don't pay to get into good standing with the freeport station to get missions, it will just tick the corsairs off. So just kill bounty hunters for a while and then you can go to the corsair base in omega 41 and then pay about 100,000 creds and you are in! The great thing about the corsairs is they have the titan the biggest and badest fighter in the whole game. Currently i am lvl 29, so i need to get to 32 before i can buy it but the missions here are 75k plus so it won't be too long.

I hope this helps anyone is interested in becoming the best pirate they can be. Good Luck, i will tell you all how great the titan is when i get one. hehehe
