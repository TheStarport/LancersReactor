Earendil

Tue May 11, 2004 11:48 am by Earendil

In my experience (I've had a little ) flserver needs half a GB memory to run just fine. Since this doesn't include whatever else is running a GB total memory is fine overall (it's what I run).

Pipe is a big thing though and is contrary to most mods out there unless they are well balanced in terms of traffic. The immediate compensatation at the sysadmin level is to lower the maximum player limit but that is sometimes unpalatable as well.

In the end it will be a bit of a give and take. If your u/l speed at home is the same or less than your d/l speed then your u/l is the bandwidth limiter, not the CPU. If you can't get more memory for your work 'puter then regardless of your pipe, the server load will be more significant because of potentially lots of memory page swaps to and from your hard drive.
