Command & Conquer: Red Alert 2
Playing on the Internet a long time ago, well last summer, I downloaded this map off of somebody, and played it with him and two other guys. We were all on broadband, and if somebody joined who was on anything less, we booted them lol. So anyway, us 4 broadband kids on this mega-map. I aligned myself with this guy, and the other two aligned.

Now this map was phenomenal! It was absolutely massive, and everybody started on a raised section of land, with only one way in or out of the base. Much much later on, as in about 4 hours later, I had set up about 50 or so Prism Towers near the entrance to my base, but had forgot about Patriot Missiles for ground-to-air defence.

So I decided to start building up my army! This was the fun part! I built up an army of loads of Prism Tanks, and Chrono Legionnaires, but put them in them Mobile things that changed when you put people in.

So anyway lol, after on numerous occasions using Spies to steal all of my enemies resources (there are advantages to being British!) and taking out anybody that came within a few hundred (cyber) yards of my base, I built up my force.

I attacked my first enemy with everything I had, losing about 75% of my army, but completely and utterly obliterating him! I called in for reinforcements (so to speak) from my Ally, who sent in Tesla Troopers, Nuclear Lorries, etc. and using his Nuclear Lorries, took out most of the infantry before I got there.

During this time, my second enemy had assembled a massive force not too far from my base, but too far for either of us to see each other. He had sent in spies a couple of times and got me back, so he could see my base still. So while my ally and I were obliterating our second enemies base, he turned his attention to my base! which I was NOT happy about.

So I pulled back, leaving my Ally to be destroyed. My base was under attack, as the computer had told me numerous times lol, and I tried my best to defend it, but he split his forces into two groups, one of Apocalypse Tanks, and loads of other stuff, to attack my base from a small distance. My Snipers were useless against these, and my Prism Towers were slowly but surely being destroyed.

As my force that had pulled back was destroyed, I made a load of them Chrono Legionnaires and started phasing out some of the tanks. He wasn't happy, and turned his whole force on my 10-15 Chrono Legionnaires, which were made quick work of.

During this time, I repaired my Prism Towers, and built about 10 more, so I had maybe 45 - 50 Prism Towers just standing there. Once he'd took out my Chrono forces, he turned his attention to my base once again.

But this time there was no stopping me! I had my Barracks, about 10 - 15 Power Plants, my Ore Mine thing, an Ore Refinery, and best of all my War Factory. I could only hence build basic units, no mega ones like the Prism or Mirage tanks. His mega force of Apocalypse Tanks and Yuris took no time at all in obliterating or converting my remaining forces.

As he approached my base, the rhythmic sound of Prism Towers powering up was amazing, and with one shot each, his Apocalypse tanks were taken out one by one. I was amazed!

My ally, who had in the process of all this had repaired and rearmed, was on his way over, when I told him to STOP! I wanted the glory for this last stand! I took out his forces until he retreated! Oh it was sheer bliss!
