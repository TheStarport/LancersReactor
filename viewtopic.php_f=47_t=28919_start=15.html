About sound not traveling in space ... true, but explosions and debris can still cause concussive or impacting effects which will translate into sound that could be heard because you are on a ship or planet that has air.

For instance, if you're in a ship, and a ship next to you explodes, you won't hear the explosion itself really, but you WILL hear the effects of that explosion acting on your ship. So in a way, you will still hear it.


I was more bothered by basic words such as "episode" and "coming" being spelled wrong. There are ways to check those things.
