Sherlog

Fri Jul 25, 2003 7:37 am by Sherlog

Top speeds are the same for all the ships but acceleration is different (this includes turning).

Only the price of commodities varies between bases, the prices for ships/equipment are the same everywhere. Different kinds of equipment can have different ratios between buy price and sell price but the sell price is fixed (does not change over time).

The refire rate of a weapon is less important than how much damage it does (multiply damage per shot with refire rate to get damage per second for comparing weapon damage), what the projectile speed is (faster projectiles make it easier to hit a target) and how energy-efficient it is. All other things being equal, a weapon with a high refire rate tends to give better results than one with a low refire rate - it is better for 'flak' shooting (e.g. against missiles, torpedos) and it delivers the damage in a higher number of smaller packets. The latter can have a noticable effect sometimes since a collapsing shield can absorb arbitrarily large amounts of energy.
