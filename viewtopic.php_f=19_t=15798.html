My groupings usually last for quite a while, but I havent been able to make them by hitting assign weapon group. What I do is I have it select weapon group one, and it will be empty, so i turn on the weapons in that group. Then i go to weapon group 3 (2 wont work for some reason), and it will have the same guns i selected for the first group, but i unclick those and turn on the others i want. Voila, weapon groups that last even after youve logged off until you manually click or unclick weapons.
