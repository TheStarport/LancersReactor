Ok ... need your help now with video problem

I have searched through the tech support forum and was not able to find my particular problem (of course, half the posts are titled "HELP ME NOW" or something as equally worthless).

The easiest way to describe it: It looks like the contrast on the screen is all the way up.

Windows 2000 Pro SP4

It's a gamma correction issue with the GF cards... Set the gamma to 0.40 or higher, and everything should go back to normal.
If this doesn't help, try installing WHQL GF drivers. (they also have the gamma correction problem, but at a lower level, say .75 in windows, and .15 in game)


Careful what you wish... You might just get it.
