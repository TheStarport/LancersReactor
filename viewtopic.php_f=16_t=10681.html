I found two GUARDIANS in the Omicron Gamma system, on a wreck called Vengeance that was right next to the jump hole from Omega 41 (close enough that it was on my scanners as soon as I came out). I also found two JADES and two DIAMONDBACKS on wrecks that were just called something like Old Rhineland Imperial Fighters in the Simga 13 system. I was surprised to find such weapons there, because you pass through that system quite early on, and presumably you can get those weapons when you first go through, although I haven't tried. And of course, they would be useless to you at that stage There could well be more of those ships in that system, as it's supposed to be the graveyard of a huge battle.
