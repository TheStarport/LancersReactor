H.Q. Victor

Fri Jan 16, 2004 5:50 pm by H.Q. Victor

If you have a problem with the ship reacting too fast, in SP you can edit your ship in this area.

steering_torque = 18000.000000, 18000.000000, 58000.000000
angular_drag = 15000.000000, 15000.000000, 35000.000000
rotation_inertia = 2800.000000, 2800.000000, 1000.000000

To slow down the "oversteering" effect and make it easyer to stay locked on the enemy ship..
