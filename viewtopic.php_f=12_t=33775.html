What''s so good about the Olympics anyway?

As you should be aware (hopefully), the Olympics are about to commence. There has been so much excitement of late, and I honestly can't understand why. To me, the Olympics seems to boil down to one simple phrase; "my country is better than your country". It seems as if the event is nothing more than an exercise in national pride, but in an arrogant and self-serving way. If you think about it, you have a bunch of people who are being paid millions in endorsements to play sport. This is not to demean their preparation and training, but a number of these people are put up on pedestals and are venerated despite rumours of drug-taking and general improprieties. Added to this, we have money-poor countries sponsoring teams instead of fixing social problems. Well, I'm stepping into forbidden territory, but to reiterate, I do not see the Olympics as a glamorous and honourable competition. Instead, instead I see it an excuse for countries to spend millions in an attempt to upstage all of the other countries. Well, I could go on, but I would be interested in hearing your thoughts on my provocative assertions .
