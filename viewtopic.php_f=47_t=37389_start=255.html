Ok here's mine then:

Name: Stefan Quist (Yes, he is a Swede! A minority)
Callsign: Vater (to those who work with him)
Age: 58
Faction: Officially he's a member of the Zoners movement
Appearence: Heavy built, slightly sunburnt, Lech Walesa Mustasche, Blue Eyes (blue as the Atlantic ocean ), mostly happily smiling.
Occupation: Gathering Information about various things using his optronic and inbuilt Neural Net (it's in his bain in form of several microchips, yes he's a cyborg).
Lives: In a camoflagued (spelling?) residence in a forest close to Heiligenstadt on Planet New Berlin.
Ship: Some junk he made out of various scrap metals.

Bio: Stefan was captured 26 years old by the LSF and put in a secret reasearch base hidden somewhere in the dephts of Alaska. There Mason financed a project to try to develop a supersoldier through genetic and mechanic modifications. Stefan was part of the genetic modification experiment focusing on strength of the soldiers. This yielded in giving him an unusual strength in his arms. If he hits something long enough it's reduced to dust or something else depending on what it's made of. (He can't break through walls or concrete or metal though).

The project was cancelled by Liberty when several subjects began showing various symptoms such as horrid sicknesses and mutations. The station was destroyed. Stefan was one of those lucky to escape by stealing one of the stealing a ship during the chaos that was caused by many of the test subjects escpaing from their cells. He went to Freeport 4 in Magellan (he was 42 years by this time) to meet up with an old friend of his and told him everything he could remember. His friend realized that Mason would probably be looking after him sooner or later and took Stefan to Mactan base. He lived there for a few years. He got most of his implants their. These implants expanded his memory capacity. Making it possible for him to take in more amounts of information than an luxury Kusari optronic.

Now he lives in Thüringer Wald near Heiligenstadt on Planet New Berlin were he has gathered a small group of friends and other people who has experienced similiar things. He has contacts with most of the factions in Sirius. His companions have even more. He is supportive towards the Bundschuh and the IMG and sympathizes with the Blood Dragons. He has a rather good reputaion among the Outcasts.

That's all. If you think any of this should be changed just tell me.

He also likes Jazz, Soul and Blues.

The strengh is only triggered by a bersek-like frenzy wich happens very rarely. But it happens.
