Fun little gameplay quirks

Post whatever you've found that helps you out in combat or is just kinda fun.

I discovered while using Starkillers to take out capital ships in ReBalance, that a well timed afterburner boost can get a torpedo travelling faster than your average missle. Made it a little more interesting than seeing one giant line of torps. about to sail into one capital ship, haha.

Also, it's possible to get inside the front O of the Liberty cruiser (the one with the giant foward gun) and sit there moving with it. It's pretty entertaning if you screw up and bounce around it like a pingpong ball too.
