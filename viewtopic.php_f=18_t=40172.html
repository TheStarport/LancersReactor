Black Eagle

Sat Jul 30, 2005 4:53 am by Black Eagle

Hi FriendlyFire!

The News images can be found in Data/Interface/Neuronet/Newsvendor/newsvendor.txm. You can easily add new news images or replace old ones. I already did this in The Monkeys Mod and it worked perfectly. Just open the txm with UTF-Editor and add a new node under Texture library (where all the other news are).
One of my entries for example is 'news_battleship_osiris'. Then under this node add another node named MIP0 and import your picture in this node (remember that it has to be in tga format).

For the patrol and trade paths I don't know an answer, as I left all of mine visible, sorry.

Hope this helps!

Black Eagle

Patrol paths, like any map item are written into you character file, once you've seen it making it invisible afterwards will make no difference, its already written to your character.
