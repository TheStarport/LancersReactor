Jevryn Markes

Sun Aug 03, 2003 9:53 pm by Jevryn Markes

You need four charges laid to blast the station.

The trick is to kill all the enemy fighters in the area before you attack the defense turrets. Ignore whatever the commanders say (they're wrong many times) and ignore the laser turrets until you kill all Coalition fighters. This way when the Rippers finally arrive, you only have to worry about the new Coalition fighters that jump into the fight. Those newcomers all come in close together, which means you can attack several at once and focus on the ones heading towards the Mammoth cargo ship that jumped in.

I reccommend using the Shroud fighter at first. Two advantages with that one: it's very fast, allowing to get from are to area faster; and it has a cloaking device: enagage it when targeted by the ion cannon to break target-lock. And when the new enemy fighters come in to attack the Rippers, you can cloak, afterburn to the enemy fighters, snuggle up behind them undetected, de-cloak and send a load of Screamer missles into the tails of several of them.

I usually stay close to the Mammoth and protect the Rippers that are lined up close to it. Thus you avoid wasting your time chasing distant fighters (they'll come in close anyway) and don't need to bother about the ion cannon, since you're out of range. BTW, I've heard that the DC version of SL requires 6 rather than 4 charges (on PC) to complete the mission, so it may depend on which version you're playing.
