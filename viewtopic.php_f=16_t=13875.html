Frobozz

Mon Apr 14, 2003 6:54 pm by Frobozz

Hovis race is the same old ring course you see in Tachyon, etc. etc.
I won first shot, but he did get an early lead. I just hit Cruise, maybe he
used afterburners THEN cruise, I don't know.
How I did it was like all ring courses:

Plan at least one ring ahead of the one your flying thru, try to cut the
distance you travel by going thru ring on side closest to next ring,
NOT dead center. This works best on ordinary curves, sometimes on really
sharp curves you gotta hug the outside of the ring. Look at car races.

Hope this helps,
Frobozz

There is a scripted event in the race. I think a little after the 3/4 mark, he'll miss a gate. At that time, he cuts his cruise engines and circles around on normal engines (not burn) until he clears the gate - then, he fires up his cruise again. If you pass him in this section, he'll unleash the weapons platforms on you. If the two of you finish roughly neck-and-neck, he will not turn on the platforms (I guess for fear of hitting himself).
