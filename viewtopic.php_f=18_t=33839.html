Phoenix_IRA

Mon Aug 16, 2004 4:13 am by Phoenix_IRA

ok go to the Mbases.ini file

the entry should look something like this:

[MBase
nickname = Li01_01_Base
local_faction = li_p_grp
diff = 1
msg_id_prefix = gcs_refer_base_Li01_01_Base

[MVendor
num_offers = 8, 12

[BaseFaction
faction = li_n_grp
weight = 13
offers_missions = true
mission_type = DestroyMission, 0.000000, 0.112387, 30 <-------make sure this is correct
npc = li0101_lnavy_001_f
npc = li0101_lnavy_002_m
npc = li0101_lnavy_003_f

If that is correct - then we go to step 2

With your vigenette zones.

1. are they open, field, or exclusion?
2. do they have a patrol or a zone population either, inside or running through them.

Basically with the vigenettes:
Field - goes in asteroid or nebula fields
Open - can go in normal space
Exclusion - these are for exclusion zones

another thing to make sure is that the zones or patrols running through the vigenette should be enemies to the base faction that you want missions for. If you dont have this...then u wont get missions. Also if you want your missions to have good re-enforcements then you must have a patrol or zone with the base faction running through it

Other than that...ask chips....lol
