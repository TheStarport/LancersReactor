Timeline SL to FL

okay im having trouble with the timeline from SL to FL there are some odd inconsistencies.

Okay it took thousands of years for humanity to get where it had gotten in SL but it only takes 800 years to rebuild in a brand new galaxy! theres something wrong there. 46 systems populated in only 800 years? come on!

oh and here we have a set of events leading up to FL.

23rd century earth sol system
Alliance - Coalition war. result coalition wins and alliance leaves in 5 sleeper ships. (edited intro plus rumor, 8 sleepers and nomads nuke earth sol system)

0 AL (after landing)
4 sleeper ships arrive in sirius intact and land in their respective systems, the liberty in new york (the only system not needing the New prefixative as it already had it), bretonia in New London, Kusari in New Tokyo, Rheinland in new berlin. the fifth sleeper ship, the hispania, was "Lost" in omicron gamma.

0 - 100 AL
the newly formed governments slowly lost contact with each other as they expanded their borders and grew within themselves.

101 - 200 AL
first renewal of contact between liberty and bretonia. trade pacts start and use of jump gates and lanes increase. Economy skyrockets.

201 - 300 AL
contact between liberty and Rheinland restarts thus opening trade between the three and opening communications between rheinland and bretonia. Expansion into the border worlds begins. no contact has been made with kusari in ages.

301 - 400 AL
Kusari is reached by a liberty probe group out of galileo. It is learned that kusari is a great ship building population of quality not quantity ships. Liberty is granted exclusive trade with Kusari. Tension mounts between liberty and bretonia/rheinland over the trade exlusion.

401 - 500 AL
Trade is opened from Kusari to Rheinland and Bretonia to release tensions between liberty and the two. resentment still exists to Present Day.

501 - 600 AL
Rheinland Expedition into the border worlds produces astonishing results when the party heading into omicron gamma is slaughtered by a rogue pirate group named the Corsairs and their expedition ship, the schiller, is taken. This Provokes rheinland to heavily militarize, rheinland produces heavy strong ships.

601 - 700 AL
Outcasts are discovered on the Edge of kusari space and any attempts to contact them are met with death.

701 - 800 AL
80 years wars between the GMG and Rheinland Military begins. 10 years pass with no end in site, popular revolution in rheinland begins, it is promptly crushed. 10 more and The glorious revolution begins in Kusari with no real end. Battle for sigma 13-begins, longest lasting battle in war, lasts 60 years, ends with the end of 80 years war and defeat of the rheinland military at the hands of the GMG. rewind 10 years within decade, Edison Trent, Junko Zane, King, Walker and most concurrent characters are born. 35 years later the current leaders of the four houses come to power. The war between the houses begins with the nomads pulling the strings. the Nomads are finally pushed out of sirius and stopped but unfotuantely not for good.
