Chips

Fri May 19, 2006 9:13 am by Chips

AFAIK, the paths are determined by the "systems_shortest_path" and other related ini files, and if it cannot find a path, then it will simply say "no best path".

If it's missions related, it could be attempting to target a certain objects nickname, like a jumpgate in a system as a nav point. If it doesn't exist, it would crash.

I dunno what you've done, and it could take months of forum posts to try and ascertain the issue. If it only has issues with tons of jumpgates, then I'd just stick to Giskards claim that more than a certain amount = crashola (it could have been Reynen who found this out, or Michael Dan - its just that I heard it from Giskard). Other than that, try running FLScan on your mod when it's activated, and see what it pulls up.
