Hey Diangelis, welcome to TLR. Let me try to help you

Before I begin, these are all spoilers. I request this topic to be moved to the spoiler section.

1) Yes, and no. After you complete the first storyline mission you only have access to New York. When you complete the second storyline mission, you get access to the other Liberty systems (Texas, Colorado, California). After the third mission, or the fourth, you should be able to go anywhere you want.

2) Yes, there certainly is. The easiest way is to shoot up the good guys. Attack some police and navy ships, or tradeconvoys (see below on how to do that). Killing Bounty Hunters is the most effective I guess. Shooting up Cargo Containers is also very good. There should be a few near Manhattan. After you get a neutral rep, take a few missions and it should go a lot quicker.

3) Check their bases for ships, guns and commodities. There are only two illegal commodities in the game though.

4) Try using a cruise disruptor gun to stop them. Or mount bigger guns, cause once you shoot them enough they go red (everything will btw) and they will stay around to fight. Missiles are also effective.

I hope I was of any help
