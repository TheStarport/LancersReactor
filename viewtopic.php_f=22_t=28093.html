here is the default folder it is in:
C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer\EXE
the file is names freelancer.ini

in the [startup section you can put a ; infront of the movie_blah lines, and it will stop showing the intro movies.

go down to [server and look for :

death_penalty = 100

change the 100 to whatever % of cargo you want players to lose on death.
