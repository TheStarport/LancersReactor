Informal Episode III poll

After seeing Episode III, it was difficult to decide which (of the following 2) characters was my favorite. This is not to say that I don't like other characters, I just want to gather opinions on who you think is cooler:

Yoda
R2-D2

After much thought.... i have to say R2D2. Between all the movies, especially ep3, R2 saves the main characters more times than anyone can count. Now that he gets his rocket engines and can take on battle droids with more gadgets than ever, he has topped my list. Yoda is a close competitior however... in ep3, in the final showdown with Palpatine, it was entertaining to see the little guy talk big, and drop the guards without even touching them. There's something scary about a midget with a lightsaber.
