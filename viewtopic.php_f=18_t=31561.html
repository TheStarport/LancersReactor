Fri May 21, 2004 10:47 am by Heavy B

if you have your icon ready , you can place it "equipment\models\commodities\nn_icons\YOURICON.3db
" in your mod folder

then in the entry you created in the "goods.ini"

[Good
nickname = YOURSHIP_hull
category = shiphull
ship = YOURSHIP
price = 10400
ids_name = 12003
item_icon = Equipment\models\commodities\nn_icons\YOURICON.3db <--refers to the icon you placed
