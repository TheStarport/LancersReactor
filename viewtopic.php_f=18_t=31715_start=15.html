To make items invisible on the nav map:

Actually, it has to do with their archetype. So for example, if you used archetype:

archetype = depot

Then go into your solararch.ini file in your Solar directory. The setting for "shape_name" determines if it shows on the map or not. For the solararch for "depot", the shape_name is:
shape_name = NAV_depot

Change that to:

shape_name = NNM_SM_COMMUNICATIONS

and it will no longer show up on your nav map! Yeah!

Well, of course you don't want to change that for depot because that is used all over the place. So I would suggest making a new entry in solararch like "depotmoonhead" or something, and making it exactly the same as for "depot", except to change the shape name.

Hope that helped!
-Wasabe
