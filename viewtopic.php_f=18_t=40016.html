Argh

Sat Jul 23, 2005 3:45 am by Argh

If you're going for a "purist" approach... erm... you could just de-compress the INI with BINI, just like the old days. I don't think, in the case of weapon_equip.ini, that that would result in a significant advantage in terms of accuracy or lack of bugs, however. As I've probably done more extensive modding of that file than anybody else has, and have re-written almost every entry, I can assure you that that file is actually not a significant cause of instability, bad references, or other problems. Mbases.ini and many of the Systems files in the SDK do appear to have problems, but these are ultimately fixable, I believe. I only wish that the extensive modifications I've been making to Mbases and to Rooms entries could be integrated with the SDK- now that I've gotten them done, it's rather a shame that they're going to be a "proprietary standard" when they add features that should be available for any modder imho...
