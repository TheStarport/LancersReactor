Rankor

Sun Jul 01, 2007 3:33 pm by Rankor

From the looks of it, You've already met Tobias in the Equipment room.
You MUST earn enough credits to make the next level requirement for Juni to contact you.
Your "CURRENT WORTH" does not always reflect that you have that much "cash in your hands" .
In other words, you may be "worth" TwoHundredThousand and something credits,
but all that worth means nothing unless you have the money on hand (in your pocket) to buy something.
IF you go to the equipment room and click on some piece of equipment to buy, you'll see what amount of credits you actually have on hand. THAT is the amount you have to increase.
