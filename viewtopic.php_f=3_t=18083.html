a fuzzy monkey

Thu May 29, 2003 8:26 pm by a fuzzy monkey

I've gotten it to work perfectly in sp after the main story.

Just copy the 3 whitestar.* files into your DATA\Ships\babylon\ directory.

make sure they are in that folder exactly as it is spelled.

place r_cruiser1.ini into \DATA\COCKPITS\rheinland (or all of them if you want purchase it elsewhere) and extra.dll into \EXE
Here's the most important part that most people forget:

go into the install_readme.txt in the whitestar folder and copy the codes into the *.ini files that are named above each segment.

It should all work when you launch the rebalance mod.

I wouldn't recommend playing the story campaigns with this ship, though.

It's HUGE and very difficult to maneuver, and would be impossible to pilot in the latter missions in the asteroid fields, not to mention being unable to dock with essential objects.
