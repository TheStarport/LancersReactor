Easiest way to get leveled up after the transport mission:

1. Launch from Kyoto
2. Find the Hokkaido Jump Hole
3. Go through the Hokkaido Jump Hole
4. Locate the three wrecks around the Hole, and loot them(should produce 2 guns each, total of 6).
6. Return to Kyoto, and sell/equip guns.

If it just plain won't give you missions(it did this to me once), check for any modded files in your FL directory- if you just uninstalled a mod, there may either be some files there that aren't supposed to be, or you may be missing a few files that you need.
