Argh

Wed Jun 29, 2005 8:41 pm by Argh

1. Dc, Oc and Ec values can be found in the CMP using UTF edit. For details, please consult my Tutorial, called, "How to Skin" that discusses the proper values, etc.

2. The short answer is, "yes". The long answer is, "read my tutorial".

2B. Yes, there's a CMP Importer for MS3D. Search for it in the Downloads section here... I garantee that it exists, and I've even used it a few times. Be warned, though- some FL models will NOT IMPORT CORRECTLY. And that's just tough. If you want to clone/change those models, you'll have to use FLModel Cloner and then do a bunch of work.
