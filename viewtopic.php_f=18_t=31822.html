Shadow51689

Mon May 31, 2004 6:52 pm by Shadow51689

I have no idea about 1 or 2 since I've never tried it..

We're gonna need alot more detail on 3 if you want us to solve it, but if you mean the game crashes to desktop upon entrance to the system, it could be a few things... the most common thing that happens to me when using FLE is that FLE stupidly makes an asteriod field listed as a nebula or a nebula listed as an asteriod field.

To fix this look at your system.ini , and look for the lines [asteroid and [nebula. Make sure they are properly linked (solars/nebula/li01badlandnebula.ini for example) and make sure they have the right tag.

Also when editing fields and nebulas, sometimes FLE creates new ones instead of editing the original. Make sure you dont have 'ghost' entries. Go to your ini and look at the [nebula and [asteriod entries again, and make sure each of them is linked to a zone, and that the ini actully has a [zone entry, if it doesnt, then its probably an old one delted by fle, and it causes a crash when you go into the system.
