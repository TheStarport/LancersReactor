Main issues with MP vs SP:

PvP balancing: Sure the lower levels don't have much chance against ppl with big ships and hardware but levelling up and getting such stuff is easy (if anything it's TOO easy) so most people who PvP are in flying with lvl10 gear and Nomads anyway so duels are generally pretty evenly matched.

PK: Most good servers have a no PvP rule in the NY or all of Liberty space and most good admins enforce this by banning ppl who break this rule or camp outside bases picking off lower level players. If you're on a server which doesn't then MOVE - there are plenty of Dedicated 24/7 servers to choose from!

LAG: Down to server spec and network latency. Again, if you're on one which is crap for you (doesn't necessarily means it's crap for everyone else!) move to a better server!

CRASHING: Known problems with FLServer cause it to bomb out with a lot of users or become unstable when players are running client-side mods/cheats. Avoid servers with a player limit over 30 until the patch is released and only use servers which explicitly prohibit cheats - it's no guarantee but at least it means the admin is probably proactively monitoring for cheaters and banning them.
