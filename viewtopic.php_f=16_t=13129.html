noelho

Mon Apr 07, 2003 5:34 pm by noelho

I disagree with the logic that non-Nomad Level 10s are novelty. I used to think that because the stats would prove that to be true. Technically, the nomad guns are the best in the game. But since the difference is slight, what type of level 10 guns you pick would really depend on the kind of player you are... and the type of situations you get yourself into.

Config A
if you like to fight great odds or are just trigger happy or a lousy shot, go with 2 TDC and 4 NB/NC .... endless stream of firing.

Config B
If you are not nuts or trigger happy, but still like to face greater odds with some finesse and accuracy, 2 TDC, 2 NC/NB, 2 WF

Config C
If you are the kind who fights only one on one and likes to shoot only when he can see the whites of his enemy's eyes (metaphoric!)...take 2 TDC, 4 DB/C/WF (go with WF if your shot aint that good )

The differences in damage are slight, but you will find the different energy drains very drastic! From unlimited shots to very limited! Of course, there are a lot of in between configs (2TDC,2NB,2DB / 2TDC,2WF,2DB / etc)

For those who choose to use non-nomad level 10 weapons like myself, a tip to lengthen firing time is to De-Activate the TDC once the enemy's shields are wasted! If they recharge, re-activate those TDC

simple yet effective ...

FYI, the config i use - 2 TCD, 2WF, 2DB

Legend:
TDC - Tizona Del Cid
NC - Nomad Cannon
NB - Nomad Blaster
DB - Diamond Back
C - Cerebus
WF - WildFire
