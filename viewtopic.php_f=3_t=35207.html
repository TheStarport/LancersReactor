Think of it this way. Where you start it's easy so a new player has a better chance at surviving when his FL skills are low. As you move farther from the NY system it gets more challenging. As Puke said the Border worlds, the worlds farthest from NY, are the most challenging.

Once you conquer the AI try Multi-Player. Real people as players are much more unpredictable and challenging.
