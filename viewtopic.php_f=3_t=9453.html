Easy Rhino

Wed Mar 12, 2003 11:27 am by Easy Rhino

A DirectX9 COMPLIANT card can do all the fancy pixel and vertex shading stuff. This is pretty much only Radeon 9000's and Geforce FX's.

A DirectX9 COMPATIBLE card just means that it works okay with DX9... doesn't need to support all the features. This includes all sorts of old stuff like TNT2's and Voodoo5's.
