There was a thread a week or so ago regarding the actual bandwidth usage FL players take up. I thought I had it bookmarked but I don't, although you could do some searching.

The bottom line was the lag and stability issues in MP FL have little to do with bandwidth. The player traffic doesn't take up that much. It's the server requirements that scale up tremendously depending on if you have over 25 players.
