Sounds like you want to have fun with friends, good idea. there's a lot of mods and servers out there, and yes mods need to be both server and client side. anyway here's a few possibilities for you.

1. Lan party,, only you and your friends this way you can set the tone of the game and have fun in an RPG environment, without having to worry about clans spoiling your fun. ( not all clans are good or bad by the way) simply find a mod or two that may interest you have your friends and thier PC's over and away u go.

note you will need a few tools , I suggest Ioncross Freelancer server (FLSO V )operator, Freelancer Mod manager (FLMM 1.3) the SDK 1.3 and Server patch 1.1

2.Start your own Internet Server if your friends can't bring PC's over same suggestions as above.

3. Find a ready to go server that is open to all and meet your friends online ( a lot of clans started that way u know ), some admins will even give u start up cash. there are many out there like Jolt, Epsilon 2.75 open rpg, Bobways Asylum51,and many TNG servers, ect there are hundreds out there.. most have websites that tell you rules and what mods u need .. some are listed with FLstat or post their rankings online so u can watch how your friends and others are doing, Some even host weekly events like races, hunts, combat, and Clan Wars

I hope some of this helps you and that u have a blast with your Lancer Experience
