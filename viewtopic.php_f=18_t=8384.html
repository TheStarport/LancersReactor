Fri Mar 07, 2003 2:32 am by SirCarcass

Here's what I do. First, drag and drop the ini on the uncompressor. It'll create xxxx.ini.txt. Next, copy the ini, then paste it. This will create "Copy of xxxx.ini", which is useful as a backup. Next, delete the ini. Then rename the "xxxx.ini.txt" to "xxxx.ini". It'll warn you that changing the filetype can make it unstable or whatever. Just click yes. Now you can edit the ini file, and Freelancer will still read it.
