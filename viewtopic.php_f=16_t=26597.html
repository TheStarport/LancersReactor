Campaign jumps availability in MP

Several of the recent posts advising trade runs here have made reliance to varying degrees on the jumps available in the single-player campaign. Some of these "shortcut" jumps really skew the balance and desirability of certain trade routes, particularly the New York - Magellan and New Berlin - Sigma-13 jumps.

I thought I'd post a list of the "special" jumps used in the campaign, and indicate their availability in multiplayer. (This of course doesn't consider any mods.)

New York - Magellan jumpgate: Locked
Magellan - Leeds jumphole: Unavailable (Doesn't exist / not present on nav map)
Omega-5 - Cambridge jumphole: Open
Cambridge - Leeds jumphole: Open but you can't see the Leeds side on the nav map
Leeds - Dublin jumphole: Locked
Tau-31 - Tau-23 jumpgate: Open (Tobias claims it's not functioning yet)
Tau-23 - Tau-29 jumphole: Open
Tau-29 - Tau-23 jumphole: Open
Tau-23 - Kyushu jumphole: Open (I didn't know this until recently)
New Tokyo - Honshu jumphole: Open
Honshu - Chugoku jumphole: Open
Chugoku - Tohoku jumphole: Locked (of course)
Chugoku - Sigma-13 jumphole: ??? (I haven't tested, as it's not very useful in MP anyway)
Sigma-13 - New Berlin jumphole: Locked
Frankfurt - Hamburg jumphole: Unavailable (doesn't exist), but there is a second Frankfurt-Hamburg hole that is available
Texas - New York jumphole: Open
New York - New York jumphole: ??? (I haven't tested. I think it's not on nav map?)
New York - Alaska: Locked (of course)
All campaign Omicron systems are inaccessible (of course)
