are you saying that even if you complete a job and get paid, your money will drop instead of go up? that would be really weird...(if this is the case, do not read the rest of the post)

If you started with 1,000,000,000 and did not finish the single player missions then you will see a requirement to gain the next level. I edited the game to give myself 20,000,000 after first mission and in order for me to go to the next level I had to go up to 20,011,965(not sure of exact figure). If you did not modify the game in any way and earned the money by trading or doing jobs then you will eventually hit a point where you have to complete a mission in order to get a level. After you finish the mission, you will only get one level. In order to get to the next level, you will need to meet the new requirement.

For example, if you got 1,000,000,000 credit by trading or doing jobs but did not finish mission 1 then after you finish mission 1 you will be at level 1. But in order for you to reach level 2, you will need to get the new requirement of 1,000,011,965. The game adjusts itself to how much money you have after each mission and creates a new requirement for you.
