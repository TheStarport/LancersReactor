When you are really desperate, try this:

Afterburn to max speed
Cut your engines (default key is Z)
Turn 180 degrees to face them

The AI will think you be turning back, so they will slow down to engage you. Found that out while running away from Mollys in Dublin but watch out for the mines!

Tue May 06, 2003 11:29 am by Pinger

The engine kill works but one thing to note, dont fire at them. Especially the higher level Lane Hackers etc as if you hit them they will continue firing just when you bats/bots will be low enough to make it a kill.

But counter measures are best dropped as soon as you hear the audio missle incoming. Then start to dodge to throw the missles tracking off. But you don't stay in straight line flight ie auto pilot, if your not in free flight then your not going to make it out 80% of the time.

So when trading I find its best to stay in free flight all the time and manual target gates etc and fast gate ie get to the gate in cruise or engine kill and then dock dont use the auto pilot as that wastes valuable seconds when you are vunerable.
