Sat Mar 13, 2004 11:09 am by Chips

random missions?

First off - does your faction respond to other NPC's? Does it attack those that are enemies? Does it defend itself from pirates?

If the answer is no, then there is a reason you cannot take missions against them, and why not from them. Notice that lib navy never gives missions against the bounty hunters? They only hand out to kill factions that are hostile, if yours is not hostile to anyone = no random missions for you. Also - the best way to work this out is making your npc's interact with the normal NPC's in the game.

The other half of the reason is this:
Vignetterparams.ini - this is where the details of WHICH type of random mission is given out. Where-ever you see factions names in here, is basically a type of random mission (don't get excited there are about 10 kill ships ones here, all of which are fly and kill the ships........its just that there are 10 different texts telling you to do the same thing ). Each one you add your faction to will ensure it gives out missions of that type (kill shoot, tractor loot, assassinate etc).
