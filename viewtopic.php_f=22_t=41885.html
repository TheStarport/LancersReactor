Freeworlds 24/7 Vanilla Roleplay Server

Okay ladies and gentlemen (and JJ Dutch), I've come up with the clever(ish) idea of running a Vanilla Roleplay server, in the same RP vein as Freeworlds (the Star Wars mod)

But, at this point, I'm expecting you all to say "Um, won't everybody just fly Sabres/Eagles/Titans?"

That's where the Freelancer Balance Mod 1.01 comes in.

It's a mod I'm developing which balances out the ENTIRE GAME. So, now a Starflier has a chance against a Titan (although a very minute one). Four Drakes can take on two Sabres and win. A Patriot can beat a Banshee. Everything is balanced.

No more do you start in New York. You start in a special system designed by me that contains some eye candy, and jump to your system. Make sure you jump to the right place, because there's no going back.

So, anyone interested? We still need a high-capacity server, but we've fixed up nearly everything else.
