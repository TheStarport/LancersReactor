Sabre
Class 10
Affiliation: Border World
Sold at: Malta(Omicron Alpha)
Sold By: Outcasts
Advantages: Good balance between Armour and Manuverability, most firepower of the 'top 3'
Disadvantages: little, apart from being a very radical design, DONT let a nomad fighter fly into the assembly, it will get stuck

Eagle
Class 10
Affiliation: Civillian
Sold at: Freeport 9 (Omicron Theta)
Sold By: Zoners
Advantages: High Manuverablility
Disadvantages: Large Target Silhouette, wing mounted weapons vulnerable to sub-target attack, lowest armour of 'Top 3'

Titan:
Class 10
Afilliation: Corsairs
Sold at: Crete(Omicron Gamma), Leon (Omega-41)
Sold by: Corsairs
Advantages: High Armour
Disadvantages: Handles like a flying tank, and i would know, ive flown a tank, only the humpy is worse. Very large and radical design, Weapons on extremities vulnerable to sub-target attack

Thos are the 'top 3' in-game ships without having to MOD, and these are also the ONLY ships that can LEGALLY mount Class 10 guns.
