Michael Dan

Sun Sep 14, 2003 1:59 pm by Michael Dan

sry guys i was away for a while.
the problems with these custom craft is that their surface defintions file, the .sur files were not created specifically for them but imported from existing original FL ships. and the auto-lock feature from FL only makes it worse. if it lock to an area of the ship that is not "solid", u'll miss all the time. Soluion is to use missiles or guns with area damage(btw area damage means these guns have explosive projectiles just like missiles). the quark cannon has 400 hull damage and 400 explosive area damage. the damage is real, since the game extracts the damage info directly from the weapons definition file(weapons_equip.ini) and not from some infocard.
