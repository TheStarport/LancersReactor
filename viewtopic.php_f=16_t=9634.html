God Mode - works in single player only (thank god).

Open PerfOptions.ini file located in "My Documents\My Games\Freelancer" folder and change the following line

DIFFICULTY_SCALE = 1.00

to

DIFFICULTY_SCALE = 0.00

Edit: The only things that can kill you are running into a planet or sun. You can even fly through radiation, minefields, gas pockets, atmosphere, and the sun's corona, etc...only actually crashing into a planet or sun will kill you.
