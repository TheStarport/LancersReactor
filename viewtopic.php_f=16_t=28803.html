NukeIt

Wed Feb 25, 2004 3:26 pm by NukeIt

The level requirement isn't decreasing- you're getting closer to it. The requirement is the amount of cash you have left to earn before you will level up. It's figured like this:

Level Requirement: 100,000
Your worth: 75,000
100,000 - 75,000 = 25,000
So the requirement would read 25,000, since you already have most of the cash you need.
