@ChampsZeWonder

If you want to make you model available, there is a post for this here.
You will need to find some place to host your files then create a link to them.
I use turbosquid.com. It's free and I've actually managed to make a few bucks on a couple models I have for sale there.

@Abdhyius

When you post you message there is a small link calle "Forum Codes" on the upper left of where you put your text in. This will pop up a window explaining how to show your image. You will have to get a free account at imageshack or photobucket to have a place to house your image file. Please do not put huge pictures here. It makes all the words run off the side of the browser and also makes the page take a longer time to load. Most of the free places to put your pictures automatically generate a thumnail. Use the thumnail as an image and the full size photo as the url link.
Here is an example.


Pirate Logo

This is the code. Replace the the greater than and less than characters with open and close brackets.
<url=http://photobucket.com/albums/a342/jsncalif11/Textures/Emblems/Pirate01.gif>
<img=http://photobucket.com/albums/a342/jsncalif11/Textures/Emblems/th_Pirate01.gif>
Pirate Logo</url>
