Puke Barwalker

Sun Mar 11, 2007 7:51 am by Puke Barwalker

In the old game ELITE, which is the grandfather of FL, you could buy an escape capsule. You had to use it in time, though. Wait too long to eject and you'd get blown up along with your ship. The escape pod would lock onto the nearest station and fly your there. You would keep your credits, but would lose your cargo.

It is a bit odd in FL that the "bad guys" you hunt have escape capsules, but you can't buy one yourself. It might make an interesting addition to a mod. If you used your capsule, you would be limited to whatever ship and equipment the station you docked with offered. Thus you might lose your high level weapons, shields etc and have to continue with a much lower level ship. That would be the price of ejecting. You might even find you had docked with a hostile faction, which could be rather nasty!

This wouldn't have to chance the bats or bots in the game. It would just be another option.
