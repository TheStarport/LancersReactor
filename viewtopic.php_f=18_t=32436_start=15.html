Regarding moveable powerplants.
I am working on a modification to Chip's excellent Evolutions 1.28 (strictly for use amongst our local gaming group). I wanted to add an alternate upgradeable power system which can be added on to ships for an exorbitant cost. Kind of like adding a turbo charger or intercooler system to a car. Anyway Some of the upgrades are intended to be useable on fighters and others on freighters etc. To do this I gave the powerplants a volume greater than zero, this has been done many times in a number of mods but I made mine highly restrictive. The basic light fighter in this mod has 10 cargo, hf/vhf have 15 or so and supremes have 18 cargo.
So my basic upgrade takes ten cargo, the next takes 18 and from there on up they are far too large for the fighters.
So basically the player has the option to mount a power upgrade above and beyond those which are intrinsic to most of the ships and thus lose most or all of his cargo space, or he/she can do without and still be able to pick up loot.

This somewhat solves the problem as it makes it impossible to mount a larger powerplant on a smaller vessel however it is very restricive and rather granular as doing more than a few such powerplants would require redsigning the cargo settings extensively.

Hope this helps at least a little

Crymson
