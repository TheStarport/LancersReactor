A crafty trick

A nice trick I've come across that can be very useful.

Start you cruise engines and when you're speed hit's 300 turn off your engines. You are not in cruise mode but are freefalling through space at 300 and slowing down 1 M every 8-12 secs.

Now how can this be taken advantage off? Well first of all cruise disrupion missiles don't work on you as you're not cruising so you can outrun enemy ship.

You can also turn the ship around 180 degrees and face the enemies chaing you USING CRUISE SPEED.

Now for the ace bit. As the enemy ships are cruising at a constant 300 they slowly catch you up BUT you are not using cruise so....OPEN FIRE!!!

They can't return fire while cruising. Yay!

In fact thinking about it, you could hit them with a cruise disruption missile yourself and watch them fall back.

Cutting the throttle and sliding is not a bug. The bug is that NPCs get confused by it when they shouldn't. If you're behind someone that does this, they are effectively motionless with respect to you while they swivel... which is bad for them.
