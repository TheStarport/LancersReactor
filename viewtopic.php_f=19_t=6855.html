WingC3

Tue Feb 25, 2003 9:50 pm by WingC3

You're right, it is very hard...However the xenos should become your bit**es. They have no allies and will improve your rep with EVERYONE else. My recommendation is do police missions until you can afford a freighter...then to cargo runs for a while. Once you can buy a decent fighter go to hudson system and nail the xenos there..The higher the level they are the more your rep will change per kill ( thus killing falcons will do more than starfliers) Continue doing this for as long as you can afford to. The more they like you the less it costs to bribe them. Once they are friendly enough that you can afford their bribe, land on their base and bribe them. Then you're all set to go. I recommend bribing the liberty rogues as it will make you neutral with the outcasts, blood dragons, lane hackers, and the golden C. all at once. good luck
