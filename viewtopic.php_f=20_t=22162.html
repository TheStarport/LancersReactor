The problem with the G-Force 4 card was addressed by Microsuck on their windows update page. I had a simular problem, but not with freelancer, but with unreal II. In my nVidia settings in the dispay property's, I have the image slider set to Quality. Antialias set at 4x and the antitropic thing set at 8x. These settings are recommended by Computer Gaming Weekly for the G-Farce series cards for gaming. I hope this helps
