buckaroobanzai

Fri Jun 13, 2003 7:38 pm by buckaroobanzai

you get errors because the files are "read only". That means you can't write over; for reading only; no writing.

goto freelancer\exe and turn off all file attributes

1. select all files: ctrl A
2. mouse right click, then choose Properties from the menu
3. click on the "read only" attribute to turn off
