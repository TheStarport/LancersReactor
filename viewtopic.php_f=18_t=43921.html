Just use FLMM, it has the ability to add 2000 or so ids and infocards. Someone with a very worn out keyboard and sore fingers reached that limit one time, can't remember who.
You add this to the ini files themselves or the entries for the ini's in the scripts. It's very helpful.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------
ids_name = 0;GENERATESTRRES("Your Id's name"(quotation mark then a closing parentheses)
ids_info = 0;GENERATEXMLRES("<xml>Your Id's Infocard</xml>"(quotation mark then a closing parentheses)(stupid faces!)
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

FLMM adds it automatically when activating the mod it's in.
