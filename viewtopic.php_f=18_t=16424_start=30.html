Yep.. I make all my models in Milkshape...I base them on models other people have made hence why I ive them credit.

Most of the models for 3Dmax are way to lare for milkshape to import and reducing them down usually gives me weird defects in the mesh.

I found it easy just to create them myself, this also gives me the power to skin them the way I want because I can control the grouping format.

Same for textures, If the model they have done includes textures, I base mine off of theres and then add alot of my own things.

Without there models it would take weeks to do a model, because I would have no road map to follow..so big props to all those guys who came before me..
