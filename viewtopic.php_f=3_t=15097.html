defaultuser

Fri Apr 25, 2003 5:22 am by defaultuser

Look mikedan, this is an attempt to make the universe seem larger and more immersive. By FORCING you to see it all rather than skip it, you get a feel for how large it is.

Take Privateer. The systems were large, but they felt tiny because you flitted from waypoint to waypoint. You still had to hit the autopilot button between waypoints, there were simply less of them.

Frrelancer tries to make the systems much larger and deeper, yet reaches a compromise where immense games like 'X' suffered - they make the systems huge, but they make it possible to traverse quickly if you use the trade lanes.

Of you want to fly it straight between jump holes ala Privateer, you're welcome to kick in the cruise, select the next jump and hit go-to. But you can't skip the scenery or the time.
