Chapter 5 – Flashback


The flight back to Sprague through Bretonia was a safe one.

Omaelia had boarded the Transport with the Alien vessel and ordered an immediate quarantine zone around the ship until they reached their destination of Sprague.

“What do you think it is?” said Von Claussen over the private comms to Trent.

The confusion in Trents voice was clearly apparent.

“I dunno. That writing…Its…”

Trent was cut off by a group communication by Aldershot Station.

“Transport Convoy, this is Aldershot Station, please proceed through the Jump gate, Sprague is aware of your imminent arrival”

“How rude!” said Trent.

The Transport convoy made its way to the Dom Kavash docking ring. It was large enough for the transport to make its descent down to the planet.

Trent, Juni, King, Von Claussen and Parkin followed shortly after, along with the rest of the convoy ships.

Waiting on the landing pad was Ganesh, his two advisors, Orillion, Jacobi and Queen Carina.

Everyone made their way out of their ships and waited for the alien vessel to be pulled out of the transport.

Omaelia, Quintaine and Sinclair had already left the transport and had gathered around the ship with everyone else.

Trent, Juni, King and Von Claussen went to stand with Orillion and Jacobi. Parkin went over to stand by Queen Carina

There was much apprehension on the ground as the alien ship drifted out of the large belly of the transport.

“Well, its erm, doesn’t appear very alien does it” said Jacobi.

The ship appeared to be very crude, made out of materials not common to Sirius.

“Doctor, can you decipher this writing” said Omaelia to Quintaine while pointing at the writing on the side of the ship

anr koanuuu

“No, its like nothing I’ve ever seen” replied Quintaine as he rubbed the palm of his hand over the writing.

As he brushed his over the writing, the cargo hold sprung open startling everyone.

“What about the life sign?” said Trent.

It appeared everyone had forgotten Trent’s scan in awe of the ship.

“Take a look” said Omaelia looking at Trent.

“Be careful” said Juni.

Trent walked slowly over to the cargo hold and peered inside. Pushed up against the back wall was a rectangular object with what appeared to be a raised curved glass pane over the top.

It had gathered a layer of dust.

As Trent went to brush it away, he stopped. His mind had cast back to his first visit on board the Dom Kavash Mothership. His current situation had reminded him of what happened the last time he brushed dust of a container.

“Trent….Trent….Hey Trent!” Shouted Juni as she placed her hand through the cargo hold and on to Trent’s shoulder instantly snapping him out of his daydream.

“Huh, oh sorry” replied Trent. His hand was still hovering inches above the dust covered container.

“Are you ok?” asked Omaelia.

“Yeah, I’m fine, just a bit of deja vue that’s all”

“Well what’s inside” she replied.

Trent placed his hand firmly on the container and slowly brushed away the a small segment of the dust at the top of the container.


His eyes opened wide and he slowly turned round to look at Omaelia and Juni, along with everyone else who had moved their way over to the hold.

“Its human…..”
