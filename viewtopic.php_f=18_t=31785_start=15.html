I understood what you were talking about. What I'm saying is that if the wreck is a ship then you can find what it's carrying in the loadouts file in your Ships folder. If the wreck is anything else what it's carrying will be in the loadouts file in the Solar folder.
I only threw the part about system inis in in case someone who ran a search for this sort of thing could find all the info in one place.

Edit = Sorry I was typing while you posted that edit. Look at the loadouts and it will become obvious to you:
[Loadout
nickname = SECRET_ou_bw_fighter_li02
archetype = bw_fighter
equip = fc_ou_gun02_mark02, HpWeapon01 <---put the weapon you want here
equip = fc_ou_gun02_mark02, HpWeapon02 <--or here etc. just do your wrecks like these
equip = fc_ou_turret02_mark01, HpTurret01
cargo = commodity_cardamine, 20

To make sure the stuff drops take a look at the fuse entries in the solararch.ini
