Try this.
<ul>Delete or move any previous saved games... move the saved games to a new folder or just delete all the files in your saved games folders.
Saved games are located in:
C:\Documents and Settings\your name\My Documents\My Games\Freelancer\Accts
Both your Singleplayer and Multiplayer folder are in the Accts folder.

Don't just un-install and re-install Freelancer.
Delete your Freelancer folder, don't worry this will not cause any problems.
By default your Freelancer game files will be installed and can be located at
C:\Program Files\Microsoft Games\Freelancer

Install the QuickFix version 1.0c
