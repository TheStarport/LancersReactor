Sherlog

Thu Jul 24, 2003 9:54 pm by Sherlog

There are two other fighters besides the Sabre that have forward-firing turret slots. One of them is very easy to get during the campaign ( ) and both are really excellent ships that stand out among other fighters of the same class.

The main difference between guns and turrets is that guns can fire only in a restricted arc (the firing cone) while turrets can fire pretty much everywhere except when ship or shield are between them and the target. Also, it is not possible to obtain missile turrets for fighters or freighters and so you can mount only the normal missile launchers that go into weapon slots (not turret slots).

As a rule, turrets tend to have higher range than equivalent guns and different projectile speed; sometimes the latter can make it difficult to plan a good weapon loadout with matched projectile speed (but there are also cases where things work out perfectly - think Outcast particle turret).
