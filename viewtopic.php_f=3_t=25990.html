Laserblast

Mon Nov 10, 2003 5:34 pm by Laserblast

First of all, you will want to get Freelancer Mod Manager (FLMM). Otherwise, you will have to reinstall Freelancer everytime you want to try out a new mod. Many of the mods here feature the ability to work with it.

The FreeWorld mod is one of the more popular mods due to the drastic changes. It makes the game more realistic by making system crossing take forever and multiple ship encounters always hurt. This mod is a pure Star Wars mod.

The Next Generation mod (TNG) and Rebalance mod both add a hug amount of ships to the game. TNG replaces all buyable guns with their own new guns and rebalance adds more guns to the old guns. Rebalance swaps NPC ships to new ones and battleship/cruiser fleets can be found in major systems. TNG has capital ship encounters in their own new system, the Nexus.

TNG always has a server with many people on it, but Rebalance does not (Rebalance has had past issues with crashing servers and causing CTDs). Freeworlds mod seems to have waves of people who come and leave. The missions are hard with your first ship and mining is the only feasible way to make money. However, this mod only features 4 systems and no uber weapons or shields.

In the end, the choice is yours however. If I have said anything that is completely off, feel free send death threats, bombs, anthrax, spam mail, flames, assassins and anything else to my email.
