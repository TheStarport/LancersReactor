Without taking contraband into account, the trade route I usually run is diamonds from New Berlin to New Tokyo, and niobium from New Tokyo to The Ring (in orbit of New Berlin).

This route goes through Sigma 13, so you can get jumped by Corsairs, Hessians, etc in short order and it can get real ugly. However, with a Humpback (250 holds) I can make over $500,000 round trip. That covers the cost of shield batteries nicely.
