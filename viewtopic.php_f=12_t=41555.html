Heathens! Ginger ALE and Ginger BEER are *completely* different beverages! Ginger Ale is a beverage made from carbonated water, food acid, flavours, and colours. It is primarily used as a mixer. Although cheaper GB is the same, proper GB is made from yeast, sugar, water, and ginger. It is a *brewed* product like beer, but it is not always alcoholic. Most GB is non-alcoholic, but if the fermentation process is altered slightly, then it can become alcoholic. The Rabbit-God's choice is Bundaberg Ginger Beer. Damn fine (non-alcoholic) stuff.
