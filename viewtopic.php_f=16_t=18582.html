Fri Jun 06, 2003 10:20 pm by NukeIt

Corsairs are no worry if you want to get to Omicron Alpha; that system is populated only by Outcasts, Bounty Hunters, and Nomads. You can even pick a nice, roundabout route to get you there- just go in the back way, through Tau-23 to Tau-37 to Omicron Alpha to Omicron Theta(which has the added bonus of skipping all that annoying radiation, and passing several nice wrecks). After you are in Omicron Theta, you can turbo/Engine kill to Freeport 9 without much trouble(a 4-pack of Titans may be camping the jumphole, but if you make a run for it immediately, you won't get beaten on).

Omicrons Gamma will be different a story; That has Corsairs in Titans. Check the rep hack list for a few good places to find Corsair and/or Outcast bribes.

Rockets: $100,000
