Revenge

Fri Mar 28, 2003 4:00 am by Revenge

Ok, well try this:

Before you start (but after you have launched), select that battleship you came from.
Hit spacebar

When the race starts, hit F2. It should not only kick in the cruise mode for ya, but it will also turn on that auto-dodge the AI uses when following waypoints. It isn't guarunteed that it will ALWAYS avoid objects, because there is only so much strafing you can do before you steer into something. There will also be a problem in that if you aim head on towards the edge of a ring, then there's a chance the auto-dodge will try to make you pass outside it, which can slow you down as you have to turn up a wee bit to fight against it.

However normally you wont have these problems. You can also do this in dog fights in asteroid fields to help you avoid the rocks during combat. (Once again, it is normally really useful but will very occasionally annoy you when it tries to dodge the wrong way...)
