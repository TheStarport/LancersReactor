Cleric

Wed Mar 05, 2003 7:30 am by Cleric

Hmm... Let's see.

I don't want to explore a whole world, just the bases and there would have to be at least 2-3 bases on each planet. Definately want to walk around in 3D and talk to the NPC's. And give me a side-arm, so that I could fight off assasination attempts that would probably occur at the bases/outposts.

A huge universe with many different systems, planets, bases, etc. and some "Hidden" and "Unexplored" places.

How about being able to "sign on" for "mini-campaigns" with the different game factions? Maybe 3-5 missions a shot. Of course, you'd have the option to pick which groups you wanted to work for.

Occaisional "clean up" missions from the main storyline even after completing the primary SP campaign.

Variety of missions, both main and random. Help a ship in distress, help knock out a bandit base, help protect a convoy of cargo ships, test experimental ships and equiptment, gather intelligence, deliver top seceret plans/orders/items, go to remote regions of the galaxay to observe strange phenomena and take readings, fly a freighter for a corporation, plus the basic assasination, bring in criminal, clear out bandit type missions.

Allow you to rent a hanger so you can own more than one ship.

A realistic, dynamic economy, where some places run low on stuff, causing prices to rise and other's get overstocked, causing prices to fall. Changes should be gradual though, no drastic overnight ups or downs.

There should be alternating "Hot Spots" of Bandit activity and when you "Clean Out" an area, it shoud take a little time for them to build their numbers up in that area again (except near the bandit bases).

Unique weapons, ships, etc. that are hard to get; maybe you could "tractor" in certain derilec ships and have them repaired, or be allowed to buy some of the weapons/ships you tested.

The ability to "call for help" if you're outgunned and outnumbered in a battle, or to hire a wingman for really risky missions.

A radar to show where other ships/objects are in the nearby area.

Differing ship speeds, stats, upgrades. Ex; Light Fighter->fastest ship but can only use light->medium weapons, (which have the fastest fire rate). Can buy slight hull upgrade, but has only limited cargo area and weakest shields due to space constraints. Basically, need to give enough plusses to each ship class, to make it viable to prefer that type of ship depending on your playing style, or to keep one of each in your "hanger" for use in specific types of missions.

Make it possible to "break the law" without getting caught by giving a player 1-2 minutes to eliminate all ships that may have seen the transgression. Maybe make some "storage depot's" only lightly guarded, so that you might be able to raid them without getting caught (if you were lucky and had your timing down).

Let players "name" their ships and buy decals for them. Maybe even allow players to build their own "custom order" ship from a shipyard.

Give several "non-combat" things for players to spend their money on. Ex: an apartment/private hanger/wingman/custom ship/casino, etc.

Well, it's late and that's about all I can think of for right now, but my "Ideal" game would have all of that and probably more.
