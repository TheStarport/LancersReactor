Sherlog

Thu Jul 10, 2003 11:05 pm by Sherlog

Buying guns can help since everything is counted at resale value for calculating net worth. 30% of purchase price for equipment (including guns), 80% for ship hulls and 100% for ammo. Commodities are counted at a fixed 'nominal price' that can be lower or higher than the actual price the commodity fetches at your current location. You can see the nominal price in-game - it is the price a commodity fetches at a base that does not trade this commodity (this can be higher than the price at a nearby base that does trade this commodity!).

From what I've seen the 'stuck-level' bug has only hit people who applied the server patch to their game, and there seems to be no reliable way to get the game unstuck until the patch has been removed by doing a clean install (remember to back up all your savegames and the multiplayer id). After reinstalling load the latest savegame and do a trade transaction or fly a mission.
