Zack Lyons

Thu Jul 29, 2004 3:50 am by Zack Lyons

The Republic Mod fixes this problem. There will be almost as many criminal systems in the Republic as there are lawkeeping ones. Example: if there are 8 lawkeeping systems in the Republic, there will be 6 or 7 criminal ones as well. When you enter the game, you can either go off to the Republic straight away as a lawkeeping individual, then carry on with "life": doing missions, trading, whatever.
OR you can go off and find a criminal bribe, enter the Republic, and do exactly the same as if you were lawkeeping.
