the way a server behaves is relative to the hardware it is build on and the amount of players there are playing plus of course the bandwidth it has available

If for example a server has a Full T1 connection and 30 players are on it every player only has the theoretical equivalent of a 33.6k modem if it would update all at the same time . your connection speed has not much to do with at this point

My best advice is to try a few servers that are in a low ping range (50-100) and see which work out best for you
