Mon Feb 27, 2006 10:17 pm by Puke Barwalker

Click on "Home". That will take you to the main page, where you can find maps, lists of wrecks, lists of jump holes, informations on the systems etc.

The German page you quoted also is wrong about 48 systems. There are more than that.

In the game, their are the house systems: Bretonia, Rheinland, Kusari and Liberty. Beyond them you get into the border world systems, where there are lots of bad guys. And beyond them are the edge world systems, where your enemies are flying really tough ships. Keep looking for jump holes and try them to see where they take you.

p.s. If you already have an Eagle, it sounds like you must have been into the edge worlds already. I made the mistake of flying into them once in a Crusader -- and didn't last long against the bad guys!
