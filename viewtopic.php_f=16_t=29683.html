Story Line Quirks

FL story line quirks.

Anybody else scratching his/her head over some of the story that doesn’t quite make sense? Here are some of my favorite quirks.

As you fly to Leeds to meet Tobias, in the time it takes you to reach the Leeds Docking Ring Juni somehow manages to fly to Manchester, New London and Cambridge and dig up information.

The Dom Kavash were a highly advanced civilization that recorded important information on parchment scrolls. Guess they didn’t trust computers.

Escaping from the dig site on Planet Sprague, Dr. Sinclair makes it out first but you never see her take off.

The two Rheinland deserters destroy the Battleship Wotan by firing only four torpedoes (maybe they were Sunslayers).

When Trent, Juni, Sinclair and Quintaine fly directly from Leeds to Stokes Mining Station and the Tau 31 jump gate, Tobias somehow has enough time to contact his old wingmen and catch up with the action.

In the corridor inside Tekagi’s Arch, Ozu fires two shots but three guards go down.

Botzler, the head of the Bundschuh rebels, blows up seven Nomad battleships in a suicide attack but we never find out how he did it.

When the Nomads are defeated, you can clearly see Von Claussen’s Valkyrie flying alongside you but the mission log says he was missing after the battle.
