Depends on what the turret is on. Freighters need the biggest ones you can mount,
but they all must match in projectile speed.

On the ships with turrets that can fire forward, match close to your main guns.
(sabre,bloodhound,wolfhound,stiletto,dagger,anubis)

On ships with turrets that can't fire forward, stick on the largest Pulse gun that fits.
Use it to knock down a tailgater's shield before you drop that Screamer mine.

DL section of TLR has spreadsheets with stat/location info.
