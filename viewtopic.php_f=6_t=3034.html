Before the mission, in the loadup select the missiles that auto-target enemy targets - can't remember what they are called since it's been a year or 2 since I played. However, if you hover "above" the Yamato and every time a salvo of torps come in, fire off half a dozen of those missiles and keep pressing cycling through the target select to find the nearest torps. They usually come in batches from opposite sides of the Yamato, so you can turn round, fire off your own salvo then watch the fireworks!! Took me ages to figure this out for myself - many a frustrated evening watching that carrier blow up!
