Wooo I LOVED Allegiance! I got it first in I think it was PCGAMER on that free CD but lost it a WHILE ago (talking couple years) man this is a great link, thanks for reuniting me with one of my favorite games hehehe . By the way guys the tutorials are boring as **** but you need them, the game has got a helluva a learning curve, its not exactly a simulation but very hard nonetheless. Although even after so long i just skipped them and went to the practice arena......... still got it . The Voice Chat (or QuickComm) options still amaze me to this day, you basically hit a quickhat key, touch a button under a certain category and your pilot says over the comm channel whatever he says, theres various voices but theres TONS of these. Which I absolutely love, instead of typing I just hit which category I need and hit the command/taunt/order/request whatever. My favorite has to be the taunt where your guy says "oh whoopty ****ing-do" (really) or the one where you can say "you havent heard the latest news? we just got our asses kicked man!".

Ah well, its a great game, too bad it bombed, I wonder if they'll ever make Allegiance 2........ I can dream........ Its like part RTS, part space shooter.... very tactical and interesting. And that game Savage: Battle For Newerth is saying they were first to incorporate hundreds of players MMORPG style with RTS...... yeah right, Allegiance can hold up to 200 players, lots of bases, upgrade options, the whole RTS layer the game is DEEP.
