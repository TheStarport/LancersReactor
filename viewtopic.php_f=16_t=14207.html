The most important thing to remember is that you dont have to fight. If a trade lane gets disrupted simply select the next link in the trade route and avoid the incoming fire. Once it comes back online make a run for the trade lane by flying as close to the center of it as possible before pressing dock. If you fly into the right place you will instantly dock with the trade lane without the need for the autopilot to guide you and you wont take incomign fire whilst docking.

I do this on every server im new to with a simple cheap rhino freighter and i find a good trade route to earn 250k... Once I have that I buy a humpback or dromedary and run this trade route mentioned in this thread--->

2 Hours of trading is all it takes usually to get to level 38.
