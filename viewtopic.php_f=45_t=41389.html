Freelancer Nation: The Nation Wants You!

Hello, I go by the callsign Her0. I am one of three Commander in Chiefs of Freelancer Nation. The Nation calls upon you to join in its ranks to acheave victory. Will you accept?



We are a very large group of Freelancer players, based mainly on the Freelancer Universe server, though we are expanding to the Void2015 server.

Many have said that we are one of the most organised groups on Freelancer. I tend to agree. Our ranking, and tag structure may seem a tad bit overboard at first, but once you get used to it, it clearly shows its uses. My tags for instance are [FN-G-Zero-[CIC, [FN-G-ZeroCool-[CIC, and [FN-G-Her0-[CIC. Lets break it down, shall we? =)

We have six active devisions, and one on hold until the next server expansion. There is [FN-C; is the Civilian devision, which has only one rank: Recruit. (RCT) [FN-T; trade devision. This devision hauls cargo around, or does missions for moeny. [FN-M; this is the millitary might of Freelancer Nation, as well as the lions share of the ranks. [FN-SO; The special operations devision of Freelancer Nation, the rest is classified. [FN-GS; Government Staff devision, The distinguished government staff are experts in their own areas. Currently we have the Department of the Interior, Department of State, Department of the Treasury, and the Department of Defense. [FN-G; Government, also called the Executive Branch. We control the general direction of Freelancer Nation, and make the final call if our Government Staff haven't come to a conclusion. [FN-N; The navy, this devision is currently on hold, but if Freelancer Nation ever happens to expand to a server with a battleship mod, then it shall be reactivated. Finaly, our ranks go at the end, for example, if you were a Private (PVT) it would be -[PVT. This lets people know exactly who to ask for orders, and who to report to in case of emergency. Let it be known that you may hold any rank, in any devision, and still keep a trading character, also, you will be selected for Government, Government Staff, and Special Operations. You will first be placed in a week long recruitment period, in which you will be trained, tested, and shown the ropes. You will then graduate, and become a fully fledged millitary ranked member.

In closing; we are strong, we are organised, and we are many. Almost everyone who applies will be accepted, and trained through duels, wars, and personal tutoring.

If you wish to join, click here to apply, log into your new account, then click here to apply. I hope to see you in space soon!

Also, if you choose against joinin Freelancer Nation, I implore you to try out for our sister clans. The Great Legionnares, The Lord Clan, or Delta Core. Either way, you will be in good hands.

Happy flying, and always remember. If you are ever in need of assistance, you can call on a Freelancer Nation pilot. There should be no descrimination of power amongst men, help should be no different.
