ok can you buy the ship?
if you cannot buy the ship... as in it crashes your game... then you need to rebuild your .mat
if you can buy it... and fly it... then it is either your LOD or it is the scale of your ship
try changing the LOD to 0, 5000 if you can see your ship lower it down to 0, 3000
if you still can't then you need to scale your ship up bigger...
in the top, side, or front view of ms3d zoom all the way out... litterally till you cannot zoom any-more...
then scale your ship slightly bigger than that for a light fighter, should only be able to see the center of the ship on a heavy or very heavy fighter or transport, and you should only be able to see a small portion of the ship on a capital class...
