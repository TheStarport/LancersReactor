Wed Mar 29, 2006 7:19 pm by parabolix

Howard,

Any custom ship jumps off the screen during cutscenes...Anton developed a tool that can fix this problem. You can download the tool here. To fix the handling issue you need to edit shiparch.ini. Make sure the mass is relatively low (75-100 is good for smaller fighters, 100-150 for larger fighters...generally). Also, check the handling specs on it, that is the section that looks like this

steering_torque = 26000.000000, 26000.000000, 58000.000000
angular_drag = 12000.000000, 12000.000000, 32000.000000
rotation_inertia = 2400.000000, 2400.000000, 1000.000000


Of course the numbers will be different depending on the ship...Try changing the numbers to the numbers of a ship that you would like yours to handle similarly to, that may also fix the "jittering" of the ship. You can adjust them from then on also, it's always good to experiment with them.
