Your best change of meeting TE's is to come to the ALPHA CENTAURY 2 server. Have a chat with LTC-WIlka or MAJ-2Stoned or COL-Soultaker if they are on. You can also chat with a TE with a lower rank but the high ranks can tell you a bit more of the "rules" of The Elite. Thats entirely up to you though.

Or you can email the Marshall via the email link on the TE website and ask him what you need to know.

My suggestion to you is that you should complete the Single Player campaign before starting Multiplayer. I've come across alot of people that started MP and barely knew how to get to the various systems. It's weird to have a lvl50 player in an Eagle asking people how to jettison cargo.


But the most important thing being on someone elses server is that you obey the server rules. If it says "NO PK IN NEW YORK" then that's what it means, don't shoot anyone in New York. Also, most servers frown upon player that use mods (cloak, cruise, weapon mod) Yuu'll most likely get banned from that server.
Should you ever join The Elite know this; Do not EVER use a mod while you're in uniform. Never. That'll get you into huge problems and quite possibly you get kicked from The Elite. (In Uniform mean that you are playing with your TE-rankgoeshere-Yourname character)
