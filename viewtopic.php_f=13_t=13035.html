Not quite ^^

Babylon 5 is one of the best Sci-Fi series out there because of many, many different elements. One of the major reasons still being the fact, that it has a 5-year storyline, which it sticks too. The lack of the "big-red-reset-button" which Star Trek uses all to often also contributes to its success

B5 has a semi-realistic space combat, the best looking (will not say coolest...) ships and plenty of actual combat. There is a lot more, but since this a space-sim mod, I figure these are the most important bits for us.
