In winxp
goto mydocuments/freelancer/perfoptions or perfdata.ini (find this file and open with notepad or word)
open find "DIFFICULTY SCALE 1.0" and change the 1.0 value to 0.0
this will give you god mode but will totally take the fun out of the single player game b/c the game is very easy in SP mode. None of the missions should take more than 2 tries at the most, if they do then you simply need to upgrade your weapons/shields etc.
