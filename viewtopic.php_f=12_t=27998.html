EVE take ages to actually get anything done, and i mean DAYS. Its cause you have to "train" your guy to be able to do stuff, so you spend your first three days just going back and forth mining. It looked very very nice, and apart from the fact that the fighting was crap, i almost started to like it more than FL (*gasp*), but that was probs just cause it was different. However, i had to download 2 updates before i could play, and then i downloaded another 2 within three days. Servers are down once a day for an hour, and i couldn't swap to another one either - so i was "stuck" (*as in i didn't manage to find where i could select another). Add the fact that your first month will consist of trying to get anywhere with your character, and its bloody fustrating.

The fighting method sucks

By the way - if they haven't updated the edition they are selling with the updates, you might have about 100meg of updates to download before playing . It was good, but not worth the money, especially since you couldn't really do much (subspace flight is so bad that you get no-where, and its kinda fustrating). The thing about EVE is that it was probs designed with some form of realism in mind, and due to this it takes bloody ages to do ANYTHING. If i hadn't had to pay for it, then i would probabily still be playing. As it was, i cancelled the payment within 7 days of getting the game, and got refused a refund from the shop (on their 10 days no questions asked money back garuntee - they even refuted the fact that i was not informed about having to dl 30 meg of patches before i could play the game when i only have a 56 k modem (which crashed halfway through).
