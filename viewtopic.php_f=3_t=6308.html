Due to the fact the bulk of freelancer takes place in space, it is unlikely that people eat/drink/sleep. However, they may, and since they are in a ship you will only be able to see it if they stop and you fly close enough to look inside. There is no clock time in the game, so you won't go into a bar at 1 a.m. cause time does not exist in game. NPCs are always there, all the time.Planets are mainly to repair, buy/sell stuff, get a job and go. Cutscenes for missions also take place on planets, but other than the cutscenes, you won't see yourself walking around getting shot at or anything like that.
