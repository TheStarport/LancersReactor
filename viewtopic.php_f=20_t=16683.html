Ok, I've found it

The only app still running was my firewall. I didn't block netaccess though but it still wrote to a log file every second, got it.

I disabled the firewall and all works fine now.

But it still leaves the issue. When a disk access occurs during game, the screen freezes until the disks r/w operation has ended.

I did a search on some forums about that issue and they give a solution. The card simply needs more power. You can do this buy modifying the mb bios, setting agp voltage to 1.7v (instead of the usual 1.5v).

But the agp's voltage is unmodifiable on my bios ...

So, I need to sort out a way to give more power to the gfx card. And I've got a 350 watt powersuply already.
