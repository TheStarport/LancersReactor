You may want to go for a slower P4 if you can find one than a 2.x Celeron. It will work, but they're really not gaming processors. Better yet, look for one of the new AMD Semprons, which are priced comparably with Celerons but are nearly as fast as a full-out Barton AMD chip in tests.

If you want a 9200, get yourself a 9250. They've just been launched and are DirectX-9 compatible, unlike the 9200s. They're slightly slower (and cheaper!), but should deliver better quality. They're ATI's new "Budget Card".

And lots of RAM is good. Get yourself 512MB and you'll be sitting pretty for any game that PC can handle.
