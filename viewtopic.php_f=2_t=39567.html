Sun Jun 26, 2005 3:17 am by AkulaSharpe

Generally it's a good idea to clear out most of the stations turrets before the Rippers come in. Once they do, stick close to them and pounce on the fighters chasing them as soon as they show up.

At some point after the Rippers have placed their charges, they will fly off and you will find the charges attatched to the "hole" in the station as being targetable. Shooting any one of them will set off the chain and blow up the station.

However, as was already mentioned, if you are not quick and diligent in securing the Rippers from enemy fighters they will not plant enough charges and you will fail the mission.
