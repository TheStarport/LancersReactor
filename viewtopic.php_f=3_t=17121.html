Hey, always nice to see people are still buying FL.

1. I'm on cable so i'm not to sure about dial up but just select multiplayer from your main menu and pick a server. You will find plenty of info on servers with IP's etc in the multiplayer forum.

2. When playing MP you can 'group' with other players. This means when you accept a mission it is given to all members of the group and when it is completed (by anyone) everyone in the group recieves an equal share of the reward. You can also trade cash/goods/weapons and equipment with other players. Each side puts up what they offer and both sides must agree before the trade is made. This allows you to give gifts to other players and/or even out the looted stuff after a group mission.

3. Not really sure what you mean here, do you have the demo and wnt to know ow much the full version costs? Depends on where you stay but amazon are selling it for around $30 at the moment.
