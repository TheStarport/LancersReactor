Your ISP is probably throttling the download automatically after a certain amount of bandwidth has been "consumed" by your connection. It's a survival technique on their part. (Note in your TOS it likely says you can't expect any certain bitrate.)

One good option would be a download manager. I'm not recommending a product, but Gozilla is on of these programs. They allow to you download big items in chunks which you can resume later. Another solution is a different browser. I'm almost positive that the Mozilla browser has "download manager" functionality by default.

-Wylfing
