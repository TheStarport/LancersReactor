hit_pts = 1000 How much damage it can take to be destroyed
mass = 2 Weight, when you tractor it in, how much of your hold it takes up the higher, the more area.
lootable = true That it can be picked up after destroying a ship

The price can be changed in the goods ini and depending on the damage to it, is worth more or less There are different thrusters too, each with different values and can be modded.
