Balefire

Wed Apr 23, 2003 7:48 am by Balefire

Considering mostly looks and putting aside the considerations of power and handling, I like the Dragon, the Anubis, and the Sabre among the fighters, and the Dromedary--which appears to be an effort to blend capacity with some aerodynamism--among the freighters. The Eagle has its points, but it just looks too conventional to me.

Handling and firepower are a different story, of course. I like the Eagle's handling but the unconventional-looking Sabre's forward-firing turret, but the Titan's extra armor doesn't make up, to me, for its ugly outriggers and sluggish handling. Among the freighters I think the Dromedary is the best, but it's hard to survive in firefights or radiation.

If the firepower were similar, I'd probably still be flying a Dragon or Anubis instead of an Eagle or Sabre. But when I feel like a merchant, I only have eyes for the Dromedary.
