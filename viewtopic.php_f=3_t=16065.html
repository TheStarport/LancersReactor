I need help with mounting weapons!!

Ummm, Hello... Ive been playing Freelancer for a couple of days and I like it, but there is something bugging me...
So, i destroy an enemy ship or find a secret wreck and tractor in the goods. Sometimes is get weapons such as level 6 ones which are better than my own. I want to mount them on my ship, But I dont know how! The only button i can see is to sell the weapon. And yes, i do have an available Hard Point and cleared the space. However, i dont know how to switch guns from my cargo hold to the hull, and back. So, can someone give me the step-by-step procedure how to mount and replace guns or missiles or Countermeasures or mines or Torpedos?????

You can only mount and dismount weapons while at an equipment dealer.If you are of a sufficient level to mount a class 6 weapon(for instance),and you have a free hardpoint which must also be capable of mounting a class 6 weapon, then there will be a small symbol on the icon of the weapon in question. Click on this symbol to mount the weapon and also to dismount the weapon. You may have noticed these symbols on ALL of the weapons you already have mounted on your ship! This is clearly explained in the manual also.
