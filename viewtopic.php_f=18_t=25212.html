I think it is in this,DATA/EQUIPMENT/weapon_equip.ini
then on your selected weapon,

flash_particle_name = li_laser_03_flash:
flash_radius = 15 flash radius:
light_anim = l_gun01_flash:
projectile_archetype = li_gun01_mark04_ammo:
separation_explosion = sever_debris
auto_turret = false
turn_rate = 90
lootable = true: this makes it lootable
LODranges = 0, 20, 40, 80, 100
You can also do this on other weapons, like No_Cloaking_Device ect.

LordFjord

Mon Oct 13, 2003 6:18 pm by LordFjord

rmlootinfo.ini just defines the lootdrop in random missions (hence the name rm...) but NOT in encounters.
try taking a mission of the faction that u changed, the effect should be quite noticable then.
Take a look at loadouts.ini to define stuff that NPCs may drop.
u can either equip things (so NPCs actually use them with the
equip = < name >
line or if u just want to have uneffected NPC behaviour but more loot then assign them the stuff as cargo.
cargo = < name >

I hpoe this helped.
