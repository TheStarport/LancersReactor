Because I Got High and Instant Warp Speed MOD''s

My first MOD's which i am proud of but now they are nothing special.

Because I Got High: This mod increases the amount of debris particles by a lot in the Detroit Debris Field and makes Cardamine (9) come out when shot upon. The chances of something coming out are extremely high. But watch out for the police searching for contraband! Enjoy and don't get addicted!

and...

Instant Warp Speed: This mod increases top cruise speed to 10,000 km/s and has no charge time or acceleration time. Good for getting across systems and quick getaways! Enjoy!

Because I Got High is stupid but can easily be modified to have whatever oyu want ome out so i guess mayeb it's for beginners who want to learn from an example.

Instant Warp Speed is one I am sure many have done on their own but some of those speed MOD's lacked which is why i simply improved them.


I hope to post here more with more complex and original MOD's but we'll see!
