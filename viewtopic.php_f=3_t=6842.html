Reviews who needs em?

Well I spent most of my evening playing the demo.

IMO it made me want the game more. Let me break it down bit by bit. Both pro and con.

Pro

1. The SP story will be good even though maybe a little short. I really like how it starts.
2. The game has good replay value. Especially for MP.
3. Graphics are good. Very nicely done.
4. The game plays very smooth even on a minimum requirement machine. This is a big one for the masses and nice to see. I tried it on 2 machines a minimum and a new machine built for gaming.
5. It has a good solid easy to understand interface. Flight/combat takes some getting used to but good overall.
6. Combat is as good as any space sim. I just haven't decided on 3rd person or First person. They both have their good points.
7. Cut scenes are very nice, clear and fluid at full screen even on a minimum machine.
8. Navigation is a breeze!

Cons

1. The we don't own this place but know people who do gets real old real fast. But it is just an annoyance, nothing more.
2. Sometimes it is difficult to stay on target and you need to use the throttle a lot.
3. When your rep is bad you get NO rest unless docked.
4. It is very expensive to fix your rep. Early in the game this could be a problem.
5. I wouldn't mind walking around a bit on stations and planets. Hey I had the same wish in PI&2. Having instant access to resources is ok though.
6. The economy is static. I know I'm beating a dead horse. But a static economy has good points as well. With a bit of exploring you can figure out what the good trade routes are.
7. The sound is sometimes a bit choppy on a minimum machine. Not bad though.

Overall I would give it a 8.8. For a Privateer fan it will do nicely. It is sure to satisfy the MP gamer for this genre as it will be a good addition to the collection.

The bottom line....I will still buy it.

Look at it this way. It beats the heck out of E&B.

Lance

The demo does raise a few Qs.

1. In the begining you do get to choose who you are. Correct?
2. Is the starting scenario the same for each character?

Sometimes it is hard to judge a game based solely on a demo as many elements are just not there.

Like I said in the begining. Definitely a good game and like all genres not for everyone. For example I do not care for the side scrolling fighting games. But hey that is just me.
