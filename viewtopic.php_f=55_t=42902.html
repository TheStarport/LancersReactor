Cold_Void

Wed Mar 08, 2006 6:40 pm by Cold_Void

a big welcome to TLR - just a few suggestions;
1. most people detest begging, but if they see a nooby they will gladly drop loot or gift money seemingly at random - esp if they see you struggling with entry level missions.
2. join your prefered server's forum and get active in it - you can get lots of help and quickly figure out the terrain clanwise.
3. ditch the "vanilla" 1.0/1.1 version of FL and get a mod; my personal favorite is evolutions - quality mods like this have more ships and new systems and even factions, so they are generally where you find lots of veterans who are uninterested in vanilla FL. not that there's anything wrong with stock FL besides being a little too easy/short and being restricted to fighters/freighters

Cold_Void

Thu Mar 09, 2006 12:38 pm by Cold_Void

don't forget the Search forums and the front-page search(searches actual website)- that is,if you really want a spoiler like a complete map of the FL universe w/ locations of jumpholes noted by grid references - yes the map has grid markers. there is a spoiler forum, that is the first place i would search for trade-tips(besides just asking a trader) - the common 'legit' route most people do is diamonds to new tokyo & niobium back(to new berlin), but if you are friendly/neutral with pirates you can buy illegal goods like artifacts and cardamine and smuggle them into new york.

as to Evo's hardpointing;it isn't expanded for capships, instead capships only use 1 class(capital) guns.this is really one of the things that bugs me(because a BS can equip 21 flak or missiles etc) and i'm adressing it in my own mod which is based off Argh's XML toolkit but unlocks additional capships/transports and more importantly reclasses turrets to free up 5 hardpoints/classes for cap guns;antifighter,guided(flak or missile),defense,primary, & gunboat gun/ship-to-ship torpedo. but evo has a huge community, whereas my mod is not yet fully stable & has a supporting community of about 11 people
