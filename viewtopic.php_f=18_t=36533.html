You may be able to sort of do it; create a new faction with a high chance of scanning nearby ships and then give them scanners that have a scan range of 0. They should continue to follow whichever ship they attempt to scan first (preferably yours, but it's a first-come first-serve sort of thing). It's kind of goofy and it probably won't work too well, but it's likely to be about the best you're going to get short of some serious effort.
