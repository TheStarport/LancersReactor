Sun Aug 01, 2004 10:01 pm by Solar Pilot

Nathan Edwards/Patriot/Cortez Jump Hole, Magellan System/Liberty Police

Neither Edwards nor Grey could blame the pilots of the Orbital Spa and Cruise ships for being skeptical at first when they hailed the convoy, offering to escort them to Freeport 4. Perhaps it was only after they noticed the two police vessels from each House weren't trying to blow each other to bits did they finally agree to trust them. Altogether, their hodgepodge convoy consisted of the 20 Armored Transports, relatively well armed, eight Startrackers, and two Hawks. Edwards hoped it would be enough to fend off any potential pirate attacks.

He reached down, keying his comm to a private channel the convoy was using. "Orbital WIng, this is Liberty Police Blue 1-1. Assume formation. Orbital fighters, take covering positions. Bretonia Police Red 1-1, take rear position."
The Armored transports moved into position: two columns of five ships each, stacked upon another set of two colums of five ships each. A long obelisk of Armored Transports to the far-off eye. The Orbital Hawks and Startrackers formed a spherical formation outside the transports (the Hawks closest to the front of the convoy), providing cover should an attack come. Constable Grey's Cavalier took the rearmost position, directly behind the transports, closing off the sphere. Exactly opposite to the Cavalier, at the front of the formation saw Edwards' Patriot.

"This is Orbital Spa and Cruise Sigma 1-1. All transports report ready status."
"This is Orbital Spa and Cruise Delta 1-1. Portside fighters in position."
"This is Orbital Spa and Cruise Delta 2-1. Starboard fighters in position."
"Bretonia Police Red 1-1 here. Rear checks out. Ready when you are, Nathan."

Edwards nodded with satisfaction. "All craft, set course for Freeport 4 and engage cruise engines on my mark." He gave each of the pilots a moment to reach for their cruise switches. "Three... two... one... mark!" Like a carefully orchestrated symphony, a Patriot, a Cavalier, eight Startrackers, two Hawks, and 20 Armored transports engaged their cruise engines in tandem, speeding in a curving path away from the Cortez Jump Hole, bearing in mind to keep a certain distance from Mactan Base in the process.

He only hoped no one noticed the high-profile convoy enough to consider attacking it. But as experience had tought the captain, you could always expect the unexpected.

Solar Pilot

Mon Aug 02, 2004 7:10 am by Solar Pilot

Nathan Edwards/Patriot/Magellan System/Liberty Police

Edwards figured this would happen. A convoy this large couldn't go undetected, even two sectors away from Mactan. However, he hadn't ancitipated they'd bring this large of a fleet to intercept them, much less mostly Liberty Rogue vessels. Things had officially gone to hell.

He took a moment to reassess the situation. All of Orbital Spa and Cruise's fighter escorts were demolished in nearly a single phalanx of missile fire. The Hawks and Armored Transports' turrets had managed to take out about six of the 20 Bloodhounds and one of the Daggers before the Hawks were destroyed. Another Dagger among their raiding party suddenly had turned on them, now transmitting a Bretonia Armed Forces ID tag after destroying two of the Rhinos. And he'd just transmitted a distress call on an open channel. No doubt a BAF task force would be here soon. And as if that weren't odd enough, a Bounty Hunter Barracuda was now engaging in the foray. He didn't mind the extra firepower. As for the convoy itself, Edwards counted ten of the 20 Armored Transports had been nailed by cruise disruptors and were struggling to restart their cruise engines in the midst of defending themselves. As for the other ten, they sped away unhindered for the last leg of their journey towards Freeport 4.

Dodging another stream of laser fire, Edwards keyed his comm. "Al, what's your status?" He targeted another Bloodhound as he waited for a reply.
"Taken a few hits, nothing the shields can't handle," Grey replied, the sound of his Skyrails pounding away at another Bloodhound evident in the background. "Apparently there's a BAF operative that came among the raiding party."
"Yeah, I noticed. Any chance he radioed for help?"
Grey gave a small cheer across the channel as he vaped the Bloodhound he was attacking. "BPA and BAF channels don't run in tandem, but they're close in frequency. I'm pretty sure I heard enough bits and pieces of his transmission to know he contacted a battleship for assistance."
"Wonderful," Edwards thought grimly. Not that he wouldn't mind the help dealing with the pirate attackers, but he hadn't hoped this thing would turn out primarily in Bretonia's favor. The last thing Magellan needed right now was a capital ship from either side breaching the Independent Systems line once more.
"I wouldn't worry too much, Nate," Grey said as he pressed the attack on another Bloodhound, easily ravaging it to spare parts. "I'm sure the big guns would-" Grey was cut off as an alarm sounded in his cockpit over the channel. "BLOODY HELL!"

Edwards turned his fighter just in time to see a pair of Javelin Missiles streak past Grey's Cavalier. Grey had dropped enough countermeasure flares to throw off the missiles, but he was disoriented, trying to figure out who fired the missiles at him. Edwards was equally confused and looked up, checking his scopes as well. Then he saw it. A flight of three Liberty Navy Defenders. And they were bearing down on Grey. Just great. Angrily, he keyed his comm to an open Liberty frequency.

"Attention Liberty Navy Defenders! This is Liberty Police Blue 1-1, Captain Nathan Edwards speaking. Disengage your attack on the Bretonian Cavalier! He is an ally, I repeat, he's helping defend the convoy. If you want to help us out, feel free to attack the Rogue raiding party but do not attack that Cavalier!" He keyed his comm back to the convoy channel. "Al, break off your attack and give yourself some distance between the Defenders before you reengage. I've told them to hold off, but I'm not taking any chances with you."
"Understood." Grey's Cavalier immediately turned about and sped towards a farther target above the remaining transports.
"Orbital convoy, this is Liberty Police Blue 1-1. Keep firing your turrets! Engage cruise engines as soon as you can!" The Armored Transports complied, sending pulses of energy out from various points on the hull. The raiders had gotten a bit more selective though as they now began to target individual turrets on the transports, or attempted to blow open the underside cargo hatches and tractor in whatever loot might have come out. Fortunately, the transports had enough clear time to enable six more of the ten remaining to engage their cruise engines and streak away from the foray.

Nathan Edwards/Patriot/Magellan System/Liberty Police

Edwards cursed as he saw the Bretonian task force come through the Cortez Jump Hole, along with the fact that one of the transports was in fact destroyed. Still, the Rogues were getting a little more careful with their shots. Rather than trying to blast the transports to smithereens, they were now only aiming for their cargo doors, tractoring in the loot that spilled out. Now, the three remaining transports broken and empty, the remaining Rogues.... and one Outcast, Edwards noticed, began to turn tail, speeding off towards the direction of Mactan Base.

There wasn't much more left to do now. Edwards was mildly satisfied at the fact that sixteen of the twenty transports escaped, cargo... and especially passengers intact. He was irked that the Rogues were careless enough to blow up one of the transports, but then again, he wasn't all too surprised.

The thought was fleeting though as he saw the Liberty Navy Defenders open fire once again, this time on the Dagger with the Bretonia Armed Forces ID tag. Idiots! he thought. He keyed his comm. "Al, I'm bugging out of here. Go back with the task force. Good work today. I'll keep in touch."

He didn't bother to wait for Grey to reply as he switched to the Liberty frequency again. "Liberty Defenders, this is Liberty Police Blue 1-1! Disengage your attack at once! The BAF and BPA forces will watch the rest of the convoy. Come about and alter course for Freeport 4! NOW!"
