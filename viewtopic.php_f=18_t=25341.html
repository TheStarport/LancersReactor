Fri Oct 17, 2003 10:56 pm by Chips

Well - good luck with the systems - its not the amount, its the playability that counts with systems.

For ships - if you can model them - then go for it, if you cannot, then best off asking permission to use peoples models that you like. It is easy then to put these into your mod - a case of copy and paste, then also do your info into the dll tables

For the names - you will need a UTF editor and a quick visit to the newbie 2 thread. In there i have listed empty ids numbers in several dlls for use. Also - for how to acutally do it, see the tutorials forum where i have recently answered that question on giving factions new names.

I am just one man, but i have nearly finished mine
it doesn't have 20 systems though only 4 - but lots of other things

However, the reason for these forums is not just for friendly banter with a faceless chum, but to get help if you need it. Just ask - but prefereably ina way that outlines what you are doing - ie, not 'how can i put ships into the game' but more along the lines of, 'okay, i have a ship with its mat files, CMP etc etc and other stuff, how do i make that into a flyable ship - it is apparently FL ready, it just needs putting into the game'.

That sort of question will get you an easier response as people will know exactly what you are after. Saves them answering what they thought you asked!

Just make sure you back your mod up after every successful addition - as you never know when it will go t**s up!)
