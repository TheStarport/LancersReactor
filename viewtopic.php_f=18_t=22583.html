FYI ... of GLASSES of CANOPY...

I have found out that when i used the configuration of another mat file, ie. bw_ships.mat i copied the glass data.

material library
I-bw_glass
I-Dc (floating array)
I-Oc (floating array)
I-Type (String)

of course the Dc, Oc, and Type data was the only ones i copied.
and walla i have now a transparent glass canopy which is my HpPilot is now visible. I never really tested my ship long enough, but it works as far as i'm concerned.

Note: please delete the glass entry in your texture library, and back-up your mat file just in case something goes wrong.
