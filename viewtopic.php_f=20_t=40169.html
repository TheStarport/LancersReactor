Hi Trev,

It seems to me that the issue is linked with the MP id, and you cant get rid of it.
If you reinstall Freelancer, it will keep your exisiting MPid anyway.

What you need to do is uninstall FL, now on the options to uninstall it has boxes like "remove saved games" and stuff, you need to make sure you tick these as default is not to delete.

Then reboot your pc, then reinstall FL.
You will know if you have done it correctly when you go to MP, it will stop and give you a new MPID when you go online. If it doesnt, you have not fully uninstalled the game (remember the tick boxes you have to click)

Good luck
