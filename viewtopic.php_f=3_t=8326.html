This technic will boost your profits in no time but you need to travel to Planet Tokyo first and some money to buy the Drone. (New Tokyo is in the North direction.) Once you arrive at Planet Tokyo you buy the Drone which costs about $60,000. Immediately you buy Engine Components and head for Planet Denver where you can sell them at a high price. Then you buy the Luxury Consumer Goods and head back to Planet Tokyo where you can sell them at very high price too!! The profit on Engine Components is $600. The profit on Luxury Consumer Goods is $577. The distance between Colorado and New Tokyo is only 3 jump holes so it should be an easy task.

In only one trip forth and back I earned impressively $ 206,000! Of course you need the money for Drone which can be a problem, but once you get hold of it you will become the richest man or woman in the world i no time.

Why the Drone? - Because it can take impressively 175 cargo units at a time, has a descent armor and many firearms. Of course you can do the runs with another ships, even with smaller ships but I can highly recommend the Drone due to the cargo it is able to carry.

If you know better trade routes plz let me know.

For Cargo simply buy an good cargo ship that you can at least decently equip (shields, weapons, etc), and stay in the areas that you believe that you can make it from trade run end point to end point. Once you've got enough to move on to a larger frieghter, then move up....

From least to greatest cargo capacity:

Liberty - Rhino 80 Cargo Units
Pirate - Mule 90 Cargo Units
Bretonia - Clydesdale 125 Cargo Units
Kusari - Drone 175 Cargo Units
Rhineland - Humpback 250 Cargo Units

The most that a fighter can carry is 70 Units....

with the Humpback you can make some nice cargo runs that make you in excess of 450k, the top end mission that you can get is probably about 250k...

So, the way to make some good $$$ is to find a great trade route load up the frieghter of your choice and go for it.... Good Hunting!!
