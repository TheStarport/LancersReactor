Can the player data from the demo be used on the full FL? Can you export your player once you get the full game, or will i need to re-do the whole first 2 missions over?

I think you can copy the content from under "My Documents/My Games/Freelancer Trial/" to "My Documents/My Games/Freelancer/", but the change on the leveling up might confuse the game, so I would advise to start over.

My other question, can you land on planets and bases of factions that hate your guts? (Like to get ships only they have)? Or do you have to bribe someone for it?
Or fly a mission for that faction to improve your rep with them?

Your rep must be at least Neutral for you to land on a base or planet. The lower thrid of the scale is "Hostile", the middle third "Neutral" and the upper "Friendly".
