Cold_Void

Fri Jul 01, 2005 12:18 pm by Cold_Void

in your engines stats change this:

cruise_power_usage = 0
to something greater like this:
cruise_power_usage = 500

increase the power use until you begin losing power in cruise,when you run out of power the cruise engine will continue running but your average speed will be lower(and much less steady:/ well there's always a tradeoff)
