Actually, there is one exception to the "jumpgate swallowing NPC craft" situation:

If you "Join" with an NPC convoy (which is a fast way to skip the queue into a jumpgate), and enter the jumpgate with the convoy, the convoy will reappear on the other side.

Personally, I think it's really cool that NPC radio traffic is not purely random. If someone reports that he's hauling water to Baltimore Shipyards, it's true: he really is hauling water (just scan him and see for yourself), and if you follow him, he'll go to Baltimore and dock there.
