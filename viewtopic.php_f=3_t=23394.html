Levels

Having just finished the campaign for the first time, I have this to say.... I'm disappointed that the campaign seemed to use only about half of the game. Here I am, struggling around in all of these storyline battles, only to get up to level 17 and have the Anubis (Lv16 ship) as the best fighter available to me, when there are much better, more powerful ships available to players with higher levels - levels that you simply cannot get to while in the campaign. After having finished the campaign, of course, I'm suddenly level 32... so why are there levels between 17 and 32, if you go straight from one to the other?

During the story, I would have enjoyed the challenge I just went through of finally attaining a Sabre, a ship that would have come in very, very handy towards the final few missions.

P.S. - after having killed many, many nomad fighters, I find that I still haven't found any of their weapons, which I want. With the story now being over, is there still a place I can find them?

P.P.S. - where can I find some Xenos to kill? What with everybody hating them, I figure they'll make it an easy job of improving my reputation overall. I just don't know where to find them...
