Hahukum Konn

Sun Sep 25, 2005 9:03 am by Hahukum Konn

Right-click My Computer on the desktop or in the Start Menu. Hit Properties, Hardware, Device Manager.

Display adapter, expand, click the first one if there's two (My Radeon 9600 has the "secondary adapter" listed separately), right-click after it turns blue, Properties, Details, Device Instance Id. Write down the stuff you see, then type it in here and we'll go from there.

The above all assumes you're using Windows XP.

Last resort: Put your Freelancer CD in, go to Add/Remove Programs, hit Freelancer, change/remove, Click "Reinstall Freelancer", and it'll overwrite all your files.

Then go snag the FL 1.1 patch off Microsoft's site (not strictly necessary for client-side, but might cure one or two server incompatibilities), and then edit the freelancer config file like we outlined above, and THEN put your mod, and only your mod, on.
