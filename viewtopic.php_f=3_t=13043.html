maswastage|BG

Sun Apr 06, 2003 4:08 am by maswastage|BG

#1 Turrets are controled manualy; there is no way to switch them to automatic. However, they are not just like regular guns in that they can aim to the sides and/or behind your ship.Go into turret view (default key H) and you'll see what I mean. Turrets are only really useful on freighter, however.

#2 The thrusters are what power your afterburners. To activate your afterburners, press and hold tab (default key). You can only use the Ab for a short time, after which it must recharge. Upgrading your thrusters won't make your afterburners faster, it will make them more efficient.

#3 there is nothing you're missing; mining is essentialy flying to a minable zone, sitting there and blowing the chunks of rock up, tractoring in whatever comes out, and then selling it. If you really want to, you can go ahead and mine; but it is not the most effective way to get money...

#4 In order to get more jobs from Juni (it's Juni, not Judy) you must attain a certain set amount of money. After you have reached that amount, you gt called in for another job. Therefore, it is really YOU who controls how much time there is between missions. If you want more time, just don't get money as fast. And make sure to upgrade your ship; it's usualy a good Idea to upgrade your ship before and after a story mission.
