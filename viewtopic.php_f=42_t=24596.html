Privateer the Reckoning & WC Conversion Mod is BACK ON!!

EDIT: This is an old post that alerted some to the fact that there was a Privateer mod suffering from lack of attention. Though long after I posted it, it did alert Warzog to the possibility of taking up the mod where it was left. And that is to the benefit of all of us.

I was visiting Crius.net boards from WCNews.com and saw that Bob McDob, who does post here from time to time, has stopped working on his Freelancer to Wing Commander/Privateer conversion mod. These are some reasons he cites:

Well, for one thing, I completed everything I set out to do, with some notable exceptions. Almost all the ships I wanted converted have, along with weapons, and I'm satisified with their stats ...

For another thing, enthusiasm has dropped off considerably. This may be due to the fact that I have been unable to get my mod submitted to LancersReactor ...


What's this unability to get the mod submitted to TLR about?

Also, I ran out of ideas. Quite frankly, when "working on the mod" means picking mp3s that nobody else will listen to due to the swelling size of the file and questionable legality, it's time to stop deluding yourself.

Therefore, this mod is dead until further notice. I won't be handing this off to other people a la Fleet Action (although as per the Open-Open Source philosophy, anyone who downloads the mod is free to edit it and upload their changes, provided they credit both me and themself). Nor am I willing to abandon hope entirely ...


It would be nice for users to be able to edit it if only we could download it from TLR. I know the problem lays somewhere with being able to zip it up for FLMM or something, but I think this is a problem best solved quickly because it would be a shame to lose this MOD to an uploading/downloading problem. Please, lets help him get it uploaded.

I wish I knew more specifically about what McDob was doing, has anybody had any discussions with him?
