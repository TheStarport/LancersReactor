You have to think about what is being simulated on the server before deciding to try a mod or not. So far it seems the server only simulates positions and damage (hit or no hit) with any type of authority. After that, as long as it can render what your pc says is happening, then it happens. So you can MODIFY tons of stats, like weapon refire, damage, speed, range, ship speed, shields, armor. You just can't ADD things that the server doesn't know about ie... ANY mod that says "add such and such piece of crap to shiparc.ini". Those mods don't work. They are for people who want to play glorified MS flight simulator with themselves and look at their beautiful model. If you want your mods to work in MP, make sure you have no textures the server doesn't have and make sure you have no classes of goods the server does not have. You can modify ships in shiparc so you can fly them but you can't go creating shiptypes and weapontypes that don't exist. So basically, don't try adding your own personal star system and expect to emerge from safety into the buzz and flurry of Planet Manhatten...
