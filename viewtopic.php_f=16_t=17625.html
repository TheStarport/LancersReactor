Yep, the depot pods around bases will release cargo when you destroy them. However, it goes without saying that pirating cargo ships and cargo depots will seriously annoy the factions that own them.

Like maswastage said, make sure you're in decent level before you start nailing cargo ships. If you do want to take on cargo ships, try lane hacking them rather than taking them out near bases, they'll be far easier targets without support.

Ok, I have been raiding cargo ships since lev. 3. No big deal.

SCAN the trains. Do not attack Rhinos, Depots, or armored transports. ONLY ATTACK TRAINS. Preferrably ones from other houses.

Scan them first, find one that carries something OTHER than water or oxygen. Something like niobium, pharmecuticals, boron, beryllium, weapons... ya know... EXPENSIVE stuff.

Destroy the cargo container if you can, the whole transport if you are in a hurry. Tractor in the cargo, and HAUL ASS into a shipping lane, without attacking anyone else. once you get 2 or 3 rings down, everyone forgives you.
