Holliday

Sat May 17, 2003 3:37 am by Holliday

I wondered about this question from the first day I bought the game. I always assumed the Titan would be the best. I finally got myself one of each in single player and I have a save game for each one. The best test for me was a Corsair mission in each one. The only time I ever hear Bitchin’ Betty say “Shield Failure” is in the Titan or Sabre. I attribute this to my flying style in each one. The Sabre and the Titan just don’t turn very well, so it is easier for the enemy to get on your tail, stay on it, and soak you until your shields drop. In the Eagle nothing much can hurt me because it moves so well. A skilled pilot in any of the three would have little trouble against the NPC’s even with overwhelming odds. In the end I decided the Eagle was the way to go for me. I have Nomad cannons and blasters, two of each, mounted in the level ten slots. And a del Cid of course. Anything else is just decoration really. I never use missiles, mines, torps, or counter measures for that matter.
