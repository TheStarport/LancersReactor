b4pro

Tue Mar 25, 2003 8:12 am by b4pro

If you want everything handed to you why are you playing video games?

Even if the Hackers are your biggest enemy, you can get a Dromedary in less than an hour. Here's how. Go to the Magellan system and become a pirate (kill a bunch of transports). In this system, transports are only guarded by Starfliers (the ship you started the game with). Once you load up with goods, sell them at Freeport 4 and repeat the process. Kill any Navy patrols you encounter in the Magellan system as well. Before you know it, you will be neutral to the hackers. In fact, others like the Corsairs and Rogues will probably be neutral to you as well. The only enemies you will make are the trade companies and the Magellan Navy. Most of your other ratings won't slide below neutral. Now you can buy your one humped camel (Dromedary). Remember, you only need to be neutral in the eyes of the Hackers to buy the ship (It kicks the Humpback's ass).

With all due respect to the person who started this thread, don't diss a part of a game you haven't even attempted. I found this subquest to be one of the most enjoyable in the entire game. It is quests like these that give Freelancer life after the single player game ends. If you don't try these little subquests, you'll end up with a ship that costs almost three times as much and is only half as good. On paper, the Dromedary doesn't look like much (well, 275 cargo), but it is the best transport available in the game.

It's really easy to make money once you have the space.
