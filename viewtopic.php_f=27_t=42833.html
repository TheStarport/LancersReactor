Killer I think your running without the official 1.1 patch where as MD has done Rebalance with it, but he's forgotten to add the common.dll from the patch to the mods EXE folder (amongst other things). You have two choices, get the patch and use it to update your Freelancer or if you don't want to patch your game as it could cause problems with other mods, then if have winzip then right click on the patch's exe and select open with winzip from the winzip options that appear and extract the common.dll into the mods EXE folder in FLMM's mod directory, Just make sure to uncheck the box that says "use folder names".
