Hello,

I am a proud owner of a Kusari Drake light fighter. I won't even bother to tell you what the milage is. One good owner (me).

Anyway, I decided to go play about with it when I completed the game using said drake.

I found I could easily hold my own against EVERYTHING.

Everything, exept VHFs.

Hell, even in Omega-5, I would wander about the minefields on the Corsair (my buddies) side of town, waiting for 8 or so Stilletos to ambush me, so I could wipe them all out single handedly.

Earendil

Mon Sep 13, 2004 5:39 am by Earendil

If you are just talking a Drake built just for taking down player VHF's, load it with the best missles and the Hornet. Shields should be the best but you are right, a good shot and you are toast and at level 6 the positron, gravitron and molecular differences are a wash. Guns are useless in battles with player VHF's unless you want to spend a long time taking down anything. The only downside to this layout is they require another character to maintain as all those missles cost and are too expensive to use to do missions.

If you are talking about fighting NPC VHF's the loadouts mentioned above work just fine as they are rounded out for all around types of fighting. Still expect it to take a while.

Sounds like you want the maneuverability without a ton of firepower, flitting like a Drake but not bulldozing through them like a Titan. Try the highest level LF in the game, the Banshee. Not as maneuverable as the Drake but it's level 7 and thus it can carry the Lancer missle and Debilitators (EMC guns). Might be what you are looking for.
