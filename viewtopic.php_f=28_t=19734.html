I hear what you're saying about sticking to the original designs but (theres always a but isnt there?) there is no sense in being too strict.

For example any of the elite ships should be limited to one gun (IIRC) but in FL that would be ridiculous. The cobra was a formidable opponent in elite but even with a nomad gun fights would be tediously long. I also fully expect anyone that uses these ships to have them for NPC's rather than flying them themselves. Maybe the cobra is flyable but i think they are much better used as NPC ships. Having said that i suppose an NPC with one big gun and a few missiles early on might be o.k. Maybe i'll change the cobra... I am using an updated one at the mo, more like the one version in Rebalance. I got a bit excited and made it far too 'uber'. Do you think 3 guns would be acceptable?

The krait has 3 weapon mounts which i would envision having 2 missile launches and a gun for NPCs. This would be strictly in keeping with the original cause they always fired 2 missiles then started shooting.

I have finished the ferdelance now and just need to do the infocards and make a few decent textures, then i will stick that up. At the moment it has 6 guns.... Its slow with 100ish cargo hold and can take 2 class 5 guns and 4 class 7 turrets. Shield is class 7. I might remove 2 of the guns and take the shield down to a 5 and the guns/turrets to 4/6.

I am more than open to suggestions/demands for ship specs as i always have trouble deciding what to put on stuff.
