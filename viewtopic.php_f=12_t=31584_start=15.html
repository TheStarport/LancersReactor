I usually do my steaks on the BBQ, once the pan is scalding throw your piece of meat on. Wait until the blood rises from the meat, add a dash of soy sauce, flip it over and wait until the blood rises from the other side and you should have a perfect medium steak. The most important thing is to turn it only once, additional turns will render the meat tough.
