Cool stuff. I'd like to know whats going on with it too.

I'm not sure about that CMS or forum, but Media Wiki can share a database with phpBB (including most flavors of *nuke). Its mainly used for syncing user accounts but I think wiki links and article quotes can be used in the forum via special BBcode. Porting threads from forum to wiki is also possible. I know my suggestions for adding a wiki are more of an annoyance than a serious consideration but its something to keep in mind. If anyone is interested in that stuff I can take a look around for forum/wiki add-ons (its something I've been meaning to do anyway for Openlancer).

EDIT: Did some research on DNN and wiki's. MediaWiki won't work, the extension is based on the phpBB user account system and only works with CMS's that use that system. DNN does have its own wiki module but its not as well developed as MediaWiki. I saw a file management module that has revision control, could function as a sort of CVS. I haven't work with ASP based CMS packages before, didn't even know DNN existed before seeing this thread.

Interesting stuff, didn't know there was much of an open source community using IIS. I'll stick with LAMP based stuff myself.
