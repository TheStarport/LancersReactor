Feasiblility Study: Blockading New York

I had a discussion a while back with some pirates friends of mine (on the Jolt server, pirates can tax etc) about whether it would be do-able to blockade New York in an online game. By blockade I mean station ships at every jump hole and jump gate (not on the NY side) and destroy all player traffic through them. The idea then would be to hold the system to ransom, with the demand of 10 million credits to all the pirates involved.

The way I figure it, we'd need 12 players. This will station 2 players at each exit, the exits being:

Colorado -> New York Jump Gate
Colorado -> New York Jump Hole
Texas -> New York Jump Gate
Texas -> New York Jump Hole
California -> New York Jump Gate
Magellan -> New York Jump Gate

Being as I am no longer a pirate, I figured I might discuss this with other players.

Do you think something like this is reasonable, or is it a 'bad thing' to do? Perhaps a less traumatic idea would be to blockade Liberty?
