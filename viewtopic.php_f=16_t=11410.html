Shen

Sun Mar 23, 2003 9:38 pm by Shen

Took me a while to get past that mission. Try not to use your nanos to repair your shields or hull until when you are ready to dock.

After finished destroying those four generators, go straight down into the tunnel below and wait until your shields and hull is repaired (f and g) before docking with the arch. Go cruise and fly straight towards the arch until you are right outside the entrance and than press dock.

Keep an eye out on your shields and hull.
