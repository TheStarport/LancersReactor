Greetings and thoughts

First I would like to say hello to all. I have been lurking the boards here for quite some time. Getting my "fix" as it were anxiously awaiting FL. I have played Privateer 1 and 2 and any Star Wars Space Sim I could get my hands on. I never played Elite or any of the other space sims that have been mentioned in here as I had a bit of a sabattical from PCs for about 7 years. I did play some on the Playstation but those really do not count much IMO.

First I would like to say that your site is very well done. There is a horde of information which has been leaving me salivating since I first found it. The more I see the more I cannot wait for the game.

Second Your forums are equally informative. And for the most part quite friendly. Disagreements do occur and of course there is the random troll. But these are handled quickly and professionally by the MODs.

Thirdly and most important the demo. I was casually surfing the web last weekend and found that the demo was being made available through file planet. I practically tripped overmyself getting to File Planet and got in the short line for downloads as I am far to cheap to pay for FIle Planets service. 3 1/2 hours later I received the demo....I started playing and was I hooked. Scratch that...I was landed cleaned and filleted. The graphics are very nice but more than that I was amazed that there wasnt any slow down on my machine. I am not saying that my machine is a slouch but it is certainly not Uber l33t. The story line mission was good, the controls were fantastic (and I am an avid joystick user after a couple of years of true flight sims and Mech Warrior 4). The mouse however was such a fluid choice. The true test came after the story mission where the demo basically says "Ok that was the story now go play and have fun". And boy have I. I purchased a Rhino loaded it to the gills with guns and have been careening aimlessly around the System. I have tried a couple of the missions which granted are a touch limited but frankly I find the lack of "railroad tracks" refreshing. What I mean is you do not have to go from point a to point b to point c. If you wish to kill a lot of bad guys you can... assasinations, killing groups and locating objects not bad. So there arent any milkruns( transporting item 1 to point A). I can live with that. Perhaps there will be some in the story missions...if not I can live with it. All in all I cannot wait for the full version to come out (already bought and paid for). I just have to survive the 1 week to go.
Well thank you for your time and allowing me to become a member of this forum.

Becket
