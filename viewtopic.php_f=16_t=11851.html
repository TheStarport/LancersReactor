Lev Arris

Thu Mar 27, 2003 12:52 am by Lev Arris

Don't under any circumstances use the "Goto" command (or the "Dock with" command for that matter) to engage the Cruise Engine during the race. It means that you are in Autopilot mode which makes your ship evade obstacles or even stops it if the obstacle is too big. Use the combination SHIFT+W instead. This enagages the Cruise Engine while staying in FREE FLIGHT mode, which is absolutely crucial to win the race.
