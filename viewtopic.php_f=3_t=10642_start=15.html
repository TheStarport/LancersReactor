Seeing I had a headset already and didn't fancy filling the coffers of Mr Gates I was on the look out for free software that did voice commands. Due to gameclubcentral (thanks again Stone1295) I came across a program called Shoot. It wasn't the easiest thing to install and configure but after a while I got it working with Freelancer.

I have to say it's better than I expected. 'Nav Map', 'Cruise', 'Dock', etc are all pretty instantaneous and the voice detection is nearly 100%. Overall it is more productive than using the keyboard. The only thing that I didn't map was the 'fire missile' etc as I thought I'd better cut down on the possibility of friendly fire!
