Stinger

Fri Feb 14, 2003 8:29 pm by Stinger

Verifed.

Ships have certain weapon level capabilities. This means you must have a ship that can handle some of the higher level upgrades. If you are flying a ship with say level 4 capabilites, you won't be able to buy or mount a level 8 gun. The highest level in this category is level 10.

You can increase another "level" that is based solely on your net worth. 38 is the highest level obtainable seen so far.
