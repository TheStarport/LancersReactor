Oooh, that's a tough one... I never really thought about 'money is no object' situation. It didn't seem right, but I'll answer anyway.
First off, 24 months is a very long period in IT industry, especially nowadays. (I'm into computers since I was 7, which means 11 years of expirience, and trust me, IT's going exponential. Very.)
