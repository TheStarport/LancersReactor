Demo - Too easy?

I downloaded and played the demo for about 2 hours, and I love the interface, the graphics and everything, but....

Does anyone else think the combat is too easy? I mean, to be fair, I am flying the weakest ship in the game, with the weakest shields, weapons, etc., and I get jumped by pirates. Four against one, and I dust them all without my shields going below 50%?! I was guessing that I'd be using nanobots and shield batteries repeatedly to stay alive, but it seemed like they wouldn't engage me unless I was firing at them.

I hope this is just the AI being merciful to a newbie, but I'm having my doubts. Don't get me wrong, I hate getting blown to bits over and over again, but I would like to be challenged, also. Anyone else worried about the difficulty?
