Fri May 02, 2003 10:13 am by DarkGUNMAN

Thanks for the encouragement so far, as Vichama has stated we don't want to upset the game balance.. DA had spent 3 years working on it, so why fix it when its not broken The aim is to open up the FL universe in it's entirity, and make it a more immersive experience. I also plan to make better use of the existing music.

My personal take on this mod came to realisation after i had a chat with P81.. even by taking on an RPG role such as the Outcasts and trying to limit my ship types, it only took me 8 hrs to get to Level 34. Killing low numbers of a certain faction allowed me to get in to good books of way too many factions and get what i wanted way too easily, and by that time the enemy ships no longer posed a threat to me, even in a freighter.

So what i want to do with this mod, is add new challenge to the game and increase it's longetivity.. to REALLY make you work to build up that character instead of getting to where you want to be in a matter of hours.. because after that, whats left?
