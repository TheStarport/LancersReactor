Don't know what you're flying, but here's what I did to get a Titan one time.

**Minor Spoiler Alert**
I was faily close to neutral with the Corsairs and really hated by the Red Hessians (the enemy of the Corsairs). I flew to the Omega 11 System and worked out of the Solarius Station. Just Kill every Red Hessian you come across and check your status with the Corsairs frequently. When you get slightly positive (and you have the cash) take the Jump Hole to Omega 41 and fight your way through much tougher Red Hessians to the Corsair base on Leon. You can buy a Titan there.
