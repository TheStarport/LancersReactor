Frank the Rabbit is a messenger from the future, to guide Donnie through the things he needs to do to repair the alternate future that's been created. Ok he's also Donnie's sister's boyfriend, but Donnie shoots him after drunk human Frank runs over Gretchen and kills her. From the moment the jet engine lands on Donnies house to the moment it land again (and kills him) nothing is "real" its an alternate reality that Donnie has to put right, but in the process he gets to change a few things. His conversations with Gretchen tell you this.

God's channel. meaning the "right" path, the "correct" timeline. see above.

"human suit" - Donnie actually is a superhero! he isn't just shining up Gretchen, he's given abilities by whatever "powers" sent Rabbit Frank from the future. That's how DD is able to wrench the jet engine into the correct time "tunnel" and put time right. That's also how he knows Frank isn't really a rabbit.

I appreciate it can be a difficult film to grasp, it's not your normal teen superhero adventure by any means. I recommend you try out the user comments board for Donnie Darko at IMDB

if you fancy a brain-curdling totally frustrating hour, try out the Donnie Darko website.
