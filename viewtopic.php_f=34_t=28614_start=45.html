The mod is substantially bigger - its approx 22Mb (without splash screen or extra sounds). However, if i make new sounds and so on, I might try and make a second option available to poeple. Multiple downloads that can be "assembled" at your end....or in effect, it builds itself at your end instead. For example, you could have the main download of the mod - which means it will 'work'. It would still be around 15 or so megabytes in size, maybe 18 meg or so (ships files). After that, the splash screen and basic sound files - which could be a seperate 10meg for splash screen, and 2 meg for sound files. Then the version number - which is around 2meg or so as well........this way to play the mod in SP you will really only need ONE download - the main one. However, to get the full effect, you can download the main part and the version change to play online, then the next day download the sound and splash screen too.........

Once we have it made, i will look more into this option for people
