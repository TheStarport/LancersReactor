Earendil

Thu Sep 23, 2004 5:46 am by Earendil

Are you talking about all this as RPG rules or to be built into a mod? Some stuff can be placed into a mod like buying certain equipment only if you are real friendly with the faction that sells it but even that is no garauntee. Or can you keep faction reps fixed in the game? It would be something applying to everyone I believe. Other things could really only be enforced by rules which may be difficult especially if the rules are profuse and complicated.

One thing that make it real difficult is when high level ships encounter low level ships a faction is "forced" to fly. So an Outcast or Corsair in their level 10 ships taking on the best the Liberty Navy has to offer (level 4 Defenders) is no fight at all. One way to deal with that is no level 10 ships are allowed to any RPGers.

On Boston we have an opt-in system of RPG. Place the RPG tag in your name otherwise the RPG rules do not apply to you and you become a Freelancer. We have only six basic classes basically because of the same issue {MN} Admiral Nazbit raised so we tried to balance things among them to make them appealing to all. There still are more Mercenaries than Escorters but the more challenging server-side mod is helping recitify that somewhat. We do insist on specific ships for each RPG class and allow peer pressure to ensure everyone conforms. Every RPG class (Pirate, Mercenary, Military, Police, Trader and Escorter) has as it's best a level 7 to level 9 ship they can fly, no level 10 ships allowed. This is important for game balance. Freelancers of course can have what they want in ships and who to attack and are only bound by the server rules.

The RPG rules are the hardest to make up and there is a wide, shadowy line between too much and too little, too simple and too complicated. Our RPG rules are the result of much community involvement. It's important it's not too much or too complicated because it really should be intuitive. I also haven't forgot the name of the game IS called Freelancer and you should have the option to be what you want to be.
