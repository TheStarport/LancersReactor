Puke Barwalker

Mon Apr 17, 2006 3:51 am by Puke Barwalker

Yep, level 38 is the max in the unmodded game. Fastest way to get one's level up to 38 is by wreck hunting, not doing missions. Those level 10 guns are a big help. But what really does it is the paralyzer missiles. In the unnmodded game, you can't find a launcher for them, so just sell them when you find them on wrecks.

p.s. The best wrecks are in dangerous country -- out where the real tough guys will attack you. Be warned!
