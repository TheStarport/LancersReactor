Cpt.Kazo

Tue May 13, 2003 11:52 am by Cpt.Kazo

...all weapons do hull damage and some have additional shield damage.
Reason:
in /DATA/constants.ini is set how many percent of the hull damage do apply to shields in general (set to 0.5 = 50%).
But to be able to have some shield killing weapons there is an additional setting in the weapons_equip.ini which adds extra shield damage to some weapons (like ElCid Corsair gun).
