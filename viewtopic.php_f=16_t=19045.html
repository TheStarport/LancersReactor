Tired of getting jumped/lane hacked?

I have only tried this in SP

When your trade lane is disrupted, try this :

1. Hit the thruster and head towards the next usable trade lane ring.
2. When you reach top speed, hit your engine kill key.
3. Now turn and face the pursuing enemy.
4. Once the hostiles have dropped out of range, resume your course!

The enemy does not appear to chase you if you are facing them and are travelling AWAY from them. I have tried this several times, and it seems fairly reliable. This is great if you're flying a freighter and can't keep up in a firefight.

Wed Jun 11, 2003 1:15 am by Setsuna

Troubleshooting time!
--Engine kill is a toggle, so don't hold the button, just press it once to "kill" the engine(s). The second time you hit the key, the engine(s) "wakes up".
--Don't hold the forward key (default w) when you attempt an engine kill - it wont work. Holding the thruster key wont interfere with the engine kill.
--You cannot perform an engine kill while in "go to" or "dock" mode. Also you cannot engine kill while in a formation. You must be in the "free flight" mode.
--Dont try to strafe while the engine is off, strafing will bring it online and slow you down.
Have fun and good luck!

Oh, one more thing...

DON'T TRY THIS IN AN ASTEROID / DEBRIS FIELD!

You'll quite possibly end up backing into an object at 200k, going wildly off course and having the hostiles catch up to you in no time.

It's also worth targeting your destination or waypoint as you accelerate before killing your engine. This saves a few seconds when it comes time to get back on course - when you've put enough distance between you and the hostiles, just hit go-to or dock, and let the ship do the rest.
