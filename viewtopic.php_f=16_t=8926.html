RevRaz

Sun Mar 09, 2003 10:05 pm by RevRaz

I just finished the last mission and didnt use 1 starkiller, used all lasers. When the mission starts, Cruise to the left side towards the base. Head right for one of the generators. As you close in, make sure you are in freeflight mode and when you hit about 1000m from the generator, exit cruise and fly right at the generator. Keep circling around the generator, moving at 79, staying right on it and keep firing. You will destroy it and the outer turrets wont hit you. Worked for me.
