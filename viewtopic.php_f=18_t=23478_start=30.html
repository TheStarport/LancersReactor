Xinos, if you want to place missiles inside the booster, just place the hardpoints inside them and use this as the archetype:

DA_archetype = equipment\models\turret\li_cruiser_gun01.cmp
material_library = equipment\models\li_turret.mat

And set the force_gun_ori to true. You won't have a model for the launcher so it'll seem like the missiles are coming out of the boosters. If you have different ideas of what it is supposed to be, email me and i will see what i can do to help.
