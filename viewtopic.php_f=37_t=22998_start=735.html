Yeah, large ships do have problems docking with most bases, it can be sort of a hassle sometimes, but even moreso in the vanilla systems where the planets generally belong to lawful factions. That's one of the reasons we included planets as bases for both sides of the law. Harrier has started work on another monster of a ship and, while it's just in its beginning stages right now, I have to tell you, you'll be impressed with it when its all finished up. I have also gathered up the new Cyclone files (I retextured it and removed those wonky fixed guns) and it will be included in the next version of Epsilon (bar another computer crash!!! ). Due to a couple reasons, Harrier and I have decided to try and release the next version of Epsilon (we will call it 3.12 for now) in shorter time than we released 3.11. There will be new ships, better systems, hundreds of new weapons, and a more complete feeling to the mod than ever. As work progresses, you'll see and hear more about it.

Also, I'd just like to personally thank Richard Sabatino and OdinsEpsilonServer for using their own time and resources to put up Epsilon mod servers for everyone to play on. Thanks alot guys, from the team and the Epsilon community.

One more thing, the download count on 3.11 has reached 2370! Thanks to everyone who has given their time to try out our mod. Just a request though, As you may or may not know, the mod has been patched. Servers should be running this patch by now as it solves a fatal error. The problem is, the patch has only about 350 downloads. That means there are 2000 people out there running an unpatched game. Even if you do not play on MP this patch will improve the stability of the mod and we recommend you download it and install it on top of Epsilon 3.11 (ignoring FLMM's activation errors) so you eliminate the risk of your game crashing.
