Welcome to TLR Brett. Well done on X2 and X3, I threw 'em in the bin 'cause I could not control the craft so I will bow to your skills on that one.

Your own style and interest will determine whether you invest in a freighter or fighter so I would not say it is up to me either way. Personally I enjoyed the combat and I certainly think you have a better chance of survival in an average fighter early on. If you start again and follow the game story iirc you can stay on the right side of the Rogues long enough to get a Dagger. ***(you already know about it but this may be spoiler territory now, there are still new FL arrivals like yourself regularly)*** The Dagger rocks. Totally! Looks, moves and fights exactly as it should. Last time I played SP I used it right pretty much to the end of the story.

Sell the spare weps and make some creds. Get a Patriot if you want, it is enough to get through the early game missions and better ships will make themselves available as needs.

Use energy weps if you want to save creds on missiles as those level 1 and 2 ones are not worth the effort anyway.

In Dru's humble opinion of course!

EDIT: Oh yeh, forgot, why not a Liberty Light Fighter? They are available early on are they not?
