Hi everybody! I've been reading the forums for a while, but felt it would be good to throw in my two cents on this one.

I like to do commodity runs for a while, at least until I have around 2 million in cash (at least -- the last time I rode it up to around 5). During this period, I run from every engagement and therefore need only upgrade shields and thrusters. I prefer the Drone, it's more maneuverable than the Humpback. The Dromedary is nice, but you have to waste time getting on the Hackers good side.

Then find yourself a Falcon (they're all over Rhineland) and slap some decent weapons on it. I usually stop over at Gas Miner Naha (Sigma 13) to pick up Skyblast A's for the Falcon. From there, it's a piece of cake to get to Omicron Theta (two jumpholes, I believe).
