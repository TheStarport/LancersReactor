Earendil

Tue Oct 05, 2004 11:18 am by Earendil

Always meant to post to this thread and forgot.

I had a GeForce Ti 3 that ran great but it was getting a bit long in the tooth. So I got a GeForce FX 5600. Had no issues with my older motherboard. When I upgraded to an nForce2 Biostar m/b had all kinds of FL issues. Thought it was the video card but I DO have a 20% CPU overclock so maybe it was that. Went from 1.675 vdc to 1.8 vdc CPU voltage, single to dual channel on the memory bus and used Nvidia WHQL drivers only (not the latest drivers). Now I have no issues. Not sure why all three of those changes had to be in effect for FL to run smoothly but after many changes, reboots and retrys, it turned out this was the only way that worked.

Basically my GeForce FX 5600 works fine, now.
