Epsilon Mod, Story and Specs

Hello,

I'm working on a mod with three others, as the title implies, it's called the epsilon mod....the name epsilon comes from our five new epsilon factions, (epsilon being the 5th greek letter) that are the biggest threats to the sirius sector, larger than the nomads...it will also consist of 5 new counter-epsilon factions, 1 from each house, and 1 from the pirate systems (hispania)...the counter-epsilon are factions like the order, who were formed to protect sirius from its new invaders...here are some specifications on the mod...keep in mind, everything in this mod is original, so you wont see any hercs or tie-fighters...

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
30+ Ships are already built, we are in the process of cleaning them and making MATs

12+ Systems...2 for each house, 4 independent / pirate systems...possibly home systems for the epsilon factions

Weapons, no number yet, but they are fast...we could easily pull 100+

New sounds

Complete Infocards

Possibly new news entries...it is easy, but we dont know yet
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

There is no release date for the MOD...but we are working about 16 hours a day on it...we are trying to get this thing off the ground...we have a website in progress, i will post a link when we get more pictures on it...

THE TEAM
Alex (Me) - Systems, Ship Models
Dave - Ship Models, MATs
Steph - Weapons, Sounds
Pete - Testing, Debugging, .exe compiling
