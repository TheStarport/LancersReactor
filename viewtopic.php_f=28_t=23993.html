Your best bet is to have a read through the top few posts on the editing tutorial forum. Software requirements are BINI.exe for ini file editing, FLids editor for the text captions and FL mod manager for the conversion of yor edited files into a mod. All of the above are available in the editing utilities forum.

The best bet then is just to have a play, start with a new jumphole and work from there.

If you have any specific enquires after a bit of messing around, post again, someone will always help.

Happy Editing.
