Well.. yes, at least for a while. In Freeport 1 (Omega 3 if I´m not wrong), you will find a guy (usually the bartender), and he'll make you a little fix in your rep. For a "small" (100000) contribution. He can put you in good terms with both sides, all you have to do is talk to him, then pay him. And then talk to him again, he will offer you the same but with the opposite faction (If he doesn´t, get out and try again). It depens on what you do next, how your rep changes again.
Allow me to give you this advice:
Work for both sides, get a job from every one of them (alternate), in this way you can get (at least for a while), a balance. Also if you want to be in good terms with the good guys, work for the BH too, that should be enough.

Freeports are usually good places for this kind of operations.
