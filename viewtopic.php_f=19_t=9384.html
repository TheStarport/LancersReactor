Sad to say, but chances are it's not you. I mean, sure you have some hardware or circumstances different than most people, but if you can get a ton of other online games work on your setup, this is probably Freelancer's fault.

So if you've done your homework and tried everything, I think in this case you truly have "tried everything", and the only thing you can do is wait for a patch to address the connection issues.
