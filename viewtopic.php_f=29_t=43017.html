1.you can't.not easily anway - your commodity would need several voice clips, one for each unique pilot voice, or you could just use one.either way it would not sound natural,and it would involve a good deal of INI editing(adding the msg's to each voice) and UTF editing(adding the sounds to the UTF files)

2.don't make mooring rings dockable, you will always undock at the first object from top to bottom in the system ini for that base.or move the mooring rings so they're below the docking rings, in the INI lines. the usual easy fix for the docking-ring problem is to "ghost" the rings by adding phantom_physics to its solar.

3.dunno how, but it is possible (see mephistophles' Sol mod)
