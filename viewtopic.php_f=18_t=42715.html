Bejaymac

Sat Feb 18, 2006 8:40 am by Bejaymac

Para you are correct in that you can't jetison an equiped item, but an unequiped item is a different story.

There are several mods that allow you to buy and equip a new engine. You start by creating a goods entrys for every engine in Engine_Good.ini, this allows the engines to appear in your inventory. Add the goods entrys to the base of your choice and go and see the Equipment Dealer to buy a new engine. Unequip the old one and equip the new one and then launch, you can now jetison the old unequiped engine and give your self some target practice.

You can only sell engines if your running with a somewhat illegal EXE hack, but theres no point in doing any of this, unless you've done a lot of changes to the engines to allow different speeds.
