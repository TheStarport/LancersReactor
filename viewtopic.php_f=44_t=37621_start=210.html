@Trem0r:

That's a nice re-interpretation of the drawing, but the skin... well... basically I think that you've done a pretty good job technically, aside from a few areas, like the tops of the "engine bulbs" that are obviously stretched/distorted... but I don't care for the clash of colors / styles between the main body and the wings. Now, this doesn't mean you suck- just that I would've made different choices. But, if I were doing this... I'd give it a more uniform color scheme, and then use subtle details to enhance things, along with using a glowmap to put some tiny lights and other glows onto the skin, to really give it a "gigantic" feeling without adding any polygons or making the skin ridiculously huge.

Anyhow... for a second try, that's pretty darn nice, so I shouldn't quibble about it- don't take it the wrong way... it's a quibble about the artistic choices, not about your technical execution.
