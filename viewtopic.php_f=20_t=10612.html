MP DX9 Problem Fixed

Well, I finally figured out why DX9 was getting corrupt. After much trial and error, I discovered that my Virus-Check was corrupting the install of DX9, particularly the DirectPlay component. This is the first time it has ever corrupted an install, but I guess there's a first time for everything.

This has resolved my freeze at the MP Server List screen, and I am able to play MP!

Don't be an idiot like me. Disable your virus software before installing DirectX 9!!

FYI, discovery of this fact only occurred after multiple restores and reinstalls of DX9. From what I can tell, once you have a corrupt DX9 install, there is no reliable way to fix it. The only way I was able to fix it was to use the Restore Point prior to installing DX9, confirming that my DX8.1 DirectPlay tested OK, disabling the Virus checker, and then installing DX9.

Even then, it was hit or miss. The only 100% sure way to fix this problem is to do a clean install of Windows.
