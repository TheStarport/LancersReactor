You can move saved games from the beta to the retail, here's how:

Find the saved games. If you deleted them when you uninstalled the beta and installed the retail, you're out of luck.

Open the saved game file you want to make accessible in a word processor, since the files are large for text it is probably best to open them in Word. The text file will have "FLSG" and then a bunch of garbage afterwards. Alter that to read "FLS1". Do NOT change anything else in the file. Save and close. You can now open that save in the retail version.
