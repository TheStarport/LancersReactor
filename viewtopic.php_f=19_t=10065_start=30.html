Just a few observations since that time.

I do this mainly because it encourages other players to participate more than just hauling cargo forever. Certainly, it's like dipping you in the fire, but it gets people pissed off and vengeful, and that's a great way to get caught up in the game.

I'd like to add one new suggestion: blind hunting, especially of groups, is a LOT of fun. Randomly select a mid/high level group many systems away, and hunt them down. Don't compromise though, the whole point of the blind hunt is that you do not break off under any circumstances. You kill your targets, or they kill you.
