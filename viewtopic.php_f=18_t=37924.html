devineman

Fri Feb 25, 2005 5:57 pm by devineman

Its sounds like a script problem. Theres a script in your mod which has something along the lines of this in it:

<data file="data\ships\loadouts.ini" method="sectionappend">
<source>
equip = fc_or_gun01_mark01, HpWeapon01

It is probably one of the ship files, if you have a look around for this, you can then find the problem and add the relevant lines that are missing to loadouts or edit them as you need.
Hope this helps
