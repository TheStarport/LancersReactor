Argh

Sun Sep 25, 2005 10:49 pm by Argh

The easiest way to have banking that I know of is to have invulnerable pieces of Equipment that have a set worth (both buying and selling - they need to be available as Commodities) that one can buy multiples of and mount on hidden Hardpoints. That's a very solid method- if they cannot be looted, cannot be destroyed, and are worth what you paid for them, then you can easily have more than a billion credits on your ship. I don't think anybody has actually done it that way, but it's the easiest method I can think of.

Another method is to have special "characters" for clans, etc., which are in invulnerable ships, that serve as "banks". This is pretty crude, but it's the method most servers use.
