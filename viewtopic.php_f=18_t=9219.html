To all you modders, an idea

Before you say "make it yourself" I would kindly point out that between work, family and building various linux distros for isp's, work and home I am glad I just get to play

Anyhow here is my idea and I am sure some of you in the mod community would like this or maybe are already considering it. Unfortunately the ship classes and names will have to be changed to protect the copyright.

I would like to see some mods based off of the various battles found in the Honor Harrington book series by David Webber. Excluding the last book, the rest of the series had some very detailed an immense ship battles that I think could be duplicated with this engine.

Heck theres plenty other scifi books with grand battles that could be done as well, i just mention this one since im re-reading the series again.

Another idea (same author with Steve White) would take place in the universe in which the Shiva Option was written. Another set of great space battle material for any modders looking for ideas.
