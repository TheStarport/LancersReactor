Lunaphobe

Fri Mar 14, 2003 6:06 am by Lunaphobe

The transport ships are probably very impractical for conversion to pilotable ships. Have you noticed that the cargo containers underneath them can be destroyed individually, and that they change with the type of cargo being carried? How could this work if a variety of commodities were being carried?

The "armored transport" ship is probably the best candidate for conversion imo. You wouldn't have to mess with mooring at all. It carries about half as much as a transport, but that's still thousands of holds. To balance that, it has no shields and is not very maneuverable (requires escort). An armored transport pilot would have to travel with protection to have any safety at all.

This brings me to another balance point: neutrality. If you are truly neutral with every faction in a group's space (which is very possible), you have basically won the game, because you can trade freely with no opposition. I think the effects of your actions on reputation should be doubled. If I destroy three navy patrols, I expect them to be hostile to me next time I meet them (and I expect the rogues to at least let me dock at their station). It should not be possible to be neutral with everyone at once.

Also consider bribe costs.

I think you all should look at this mod as an opportunity to choose your ship as an expressive device. When all ships are nearly equal in gameplay, you can feel secure in flying a ship that you think looks cool.
