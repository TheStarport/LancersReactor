You have got the wrong idea here.......
HpWeapon01 - means the hardpoint for weapon 1
hpWeapon02 - hardpoint for weapon 2 (note - NOTHING about levels)
HpWepaon03 - hardpoint for ewapon 3
So, you have given yourself 10 hardpoints (um - doubt it without some CMP editing) and the hp_gun_specialX part is the level of the mount......
so hp_gun_special01 means this is a class 1 gun classification. You then add the hardpoints to be able to take that classification instead, you will want all six i guess, so
hp_type = hp_gun_special_1, HpWeapon01, HpWeapon02, HpWeapon03, HpWeapon04
means that gun mounts 1,2,3 and 4 are class 1
hp_type = hp_gun_special_3, HpWeapon01, HpWeapon02, HpWeapon03, HpWeapon04
means gunmounts 1,2,3 and 4 are class 3

hp_type = hp_gun_special_3, HpWeapon01, HpWeapon02, HpWeapon03, HpWeapon04
hp_type = hp_gun_special_2, HpWeapon01, HpWeapon02, HpWeapon03, HpWeapon04
hp_type = hp_gun_special_1, HpWeapon01, HpWeapon02, HpWeapon03, HpWeapon04
This means that gunmounts 1,2,3 and 4 can take classes 1,2 and 3 inclusive
If you didn't include all the hp_gun_special_1 part, those mounts would take class 2 and 3 only, no class 1 capability.
Now then, from that work out what you should have put, otherwise you won't learn anything!!
Its better to teach you right, and let you do it for yourself, than giving it to you, and never knowing your errors or learnong stuff!!! Work it out from there - post back if you have any probs though
