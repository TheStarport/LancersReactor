New Australia Released!

Beta 1 has been released. It contains the complete Northern Territory. The jump gate to get there is behind Planet New London, at the end of the new Trade lane.

I am doing a Mod for the Australian players of the game. It's a new house, of which the people are Australian.

Here's a list of all the things I want to add:
- 7 new systems, New South Wales, Western Australia, Southern Australia, New Queensland, New Victoria, Northern Territory and Tasmania
- 3 new ships, a Light Fighter, a Heavy Fighter and a Freighter
- 3 new factions, New Australian Police, New Australian Armed Forces and Namatjirans (pirates)
- Patrols
- Trade Lanes, Jump Gates and Jump Holes
- Wrecks AND loots

NOTE: The New Australian Police and Armed Forces will have Bretonian voices, and the Namatjirans will have Liberty Rogue voices.

At the minute I am a one-man team, so if you want to help, by all means tell me.

Many thanks to shsan, who without his exeptional Freelancer Explorer this would have taken me months to do, possibly years.
