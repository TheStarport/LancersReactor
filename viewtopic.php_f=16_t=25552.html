Most profitable route

Hi all. Been a while since I posted. How you all doing?

I think I've found a very good trade route. My figures are based on having a Dromedary.

Right then, start at Freiburg Station in Stuttgart. Buy as many Engine components as you can and go to Omega-7 (1 jump away). Sell at Freistadt Base and then buy Silver and return to Freiburg.

The total profit is $162,250 per run with a full load. The time it takes to do a complete run is between 4 and 4.5 minutes. So, even though this is not a very big numbers run, as it take so little time you make more money per minute of travel time, it works out better than any other run in the game I've found so far. In 30 minutes I can make 1 million. The actual money per minute made is between $36K and $40.5K. Other runs that take more time tend to earn you between $21K and $30K at most.

Hope this helps some people.

If anyone thinks they have a better route please post it here as I am curious as to the "best" route in the game.

Thanks

Made a few new characters last night, so making the cash in a short time is required.

So stage one:

Sell bats/bots and buy a Patriot. Then hit the police depots to help get your outcast/lane hacker rep neutral - requirement for later. Hit some of the wrecks in NY, no-one had hit Flint so I got the cardamine.

Stage two:

Buy a Rhino and out fit it with the turrets from Flint, there just for show, as your Lane hacker rep should be neutral so they wont bother you. Buy lux Goods at Manhattan, sell at Anui in Hokkiado. There buy cardamine and return to New York. Now on your way you should have enough to buy a Drone, do so and then raid the wrecks in Shioko for there cardamine and weapons - dont sell them.

Stage Three:

Once your back to New York, sell your cardamine and buy Engine components from Norfok Shipyard and go sell on Mactan base in Magellan for the Dromedary, outfit with the looted wreck weapons. Next buy as much Cardamine as possible and return to New York. Then do as many NY - Anui runs as necessary for the cash you need.

Total time to make 1.3 mil little over 2 hours

Here's one of my faves - probably not the most profitable but not bad:

Buy boron on Pittsburgh for $120
Sell it on LD-14 for $960 - $840 profit per box.
For the return, buy ship hull panels at Stokes Mining and sell them at Baltimore Shipyard, just a hop away from Pittsburgh.

Now if you do it the "boring" way you'll do 40 waypoints each way taking you from NY through California, Cortez, Manchester, New London and into Leeds. There's a short cut - take the NY-Magellan jump gate then run across to the Leeds jump hole, and you come out just a short way from Planet Leeds and just a couple of minutes from LD-14. Quicker, and with the rogues and outcasts it keeps your blood pumping too.

Most of the "shortcut" jumps, including New York-Magellan, Magellan-Leeds, and New Berlin-Sigma-13, are only available in single player, and that after the campaign has ended. Of course the shortcut jumps let you do the higher-profit trade routes in less time. For sheer profit per minute, nothing comes close to the New Berlin-Yanagi Depot engine components/cardamine run. The bases are supposed to be two systems plus an offlane flight apart, and give profits commensurate with that, but that jumphole really short-circuits the balance. Cardamine from Mactan to Manhattan would also be abusive of the shortcut if there were anything worth taking on the return trip, and the various New York-Leeds trade routes heavily rely on that as well.
