I like the Drake light fighter very much. It can mount decent guns and dodge a lot of fire. But if you want the most powerful guns, a light fighter probably won't handle them. And if you want to do something like fly into the Outcasts' system when they are hostile -- which is almost always -- you'd better have the top guns to knock out the baddies who swarm all over you.

In the SP game, I stayed with my Drake long after I could have bought a heavy fighter. But eventually I switched over for the fire power. I could defeat almost anything in the Drake, but I got tired of taking so long to do so. Also, I got tired of having my shields knocked down by only one or two hits and having to duck and dodge until they came back.


Heavy fighter or light fighter ... depends a lot on your style of combat. Bob and weaver or go in swinging? Up to you.
