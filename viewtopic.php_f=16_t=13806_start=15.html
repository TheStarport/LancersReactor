Well..if you're in a dromedary, there's a couple trade runs you should be able to do to get the cash you need...

Try the Diamond/Niobium trade from New Berlin to New Tokyo (Buy Diamonds at New Berlin, sell at Tokyo. Buy Niobium at Tokyo, sell at The Ring, which is right next to Berlin), or the Engine Comps/Some Other Commodity route from Berlin to Yangai (Buy Engine Comps at New Berlin, sell to Yangai Depot in Sig13. Buy whatever thingy sells well at Berlin, sell at Berlin).

Both routes offer really quick cash.

cavok7

Mon Apr 14, 2003 4:42 pm by cavok7

Once you get your Titan, there are many places to explore and earn some serious cash (plus build a rep/kick the crap out of people.)

Try the Dublin System. In the lower left portion of the system is a Molly's Base - Arranmore Base. It sits in the best GOLD Field in the entire sector. Fly out there and slaughter every Molly ship you encounter. They drop weapons and sometimes cargos. You can make a good profit selling their stuff back on the Battleship Essex near the Jump Gate. You can also mine GOLD in between waves of Mollys. 70 pieces (your cargo capacity) can fetch a nice sum at the right base. Killing Mollys will put you in good with many factions (consult the faction relationship chart in the Downloads portion of this forum.)

Another super place (and my personal favorite so far) is in the Dresden System. Work out of the Daumann location: Leipzig Station. About 25K away is the Jump Hole to Omega 11 (upper left portion of the system.). Head towards the jump hole and you will encounter wave after wave of Red Hessians flying Stilettos. They come in groups of between 2 to 8 at a time. Virtually all of them are hauling goods. Each wave has something different, but it's not unusual to score Diamonds, Cardamine, Artifacts, and most importantly: Niobium. Leipzig Station pays 1600 for each piece of Niobium! A cargo hold full of those beauties will net you 112,000. And that's on top of the huge sum you'll make from all the level 6-8 weapons you will tractor in after sending the ships to that great scrap yard in the sky. Level 8 weapons usually sell for something around 16,000 a piece. Have a dozen or more of those on board, plus some level 7 (@ 7,000 a piece) and level 6 (around 2,500 a piece) weapons (not to mention the missiles, mines, thrusters, etc.) and you can see the possibilities.

30-40 minutes of smearing Red Hessians all over your cockpit and a quick run back to Leipzig should net you 350-500,000. Repeat this process as much as you like and your bank account will certainly grow in a short amount of time. You will also gain combat experience and become REALLY good friends of the Corsairs in the process.

There are many other great ambush places - have fun finding them!

The Nomads aren't so tough, their ships drop like flies. And my ship of choice is the Hawk, a medium-toughness light fighter. If you can maintain evasive manoeuvres while engaging a bandit, you'll survive longer than a heavy fighter.

I have two games going right now, one as an honest trader/mission taker, and one as a pirate. I base myself at Buffalo base as a pirate, in New York where the pickings are easy. And there's those containers near Fort Bush filled with H-Fuel that nice repair ships come and fix. And the cordamine run from Buffalo to Rochester.
