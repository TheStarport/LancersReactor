But it's not entirely as Fd says... Hayden did make it now to Return of the Jedi, but not when Luke takes the mask off, but only as the 'ghost jedi' appearance next to Yoda and Obi-Wan. Why? Because Lucas felt that he should show the 'young' Anakin because that was the last time he was still 'good' so to say, so as a 'ghost jedi', he goes back to that point. Next thing Lucas changed was the other actor who played the holographic emperor in TESB, he replaced him with Ian McDiarmid and he also changed all the spoken lines from Boba Fett into the voice of the guy who played Jango Fett, as Boba is a clone of him, he should sound as Jango as well is what Lucas said about that. The Han Solo with Jabba the Hutt sequence in ANH has yet again been redone, trying to get it more 'real' this time and the Han vs Greedo scene has been tweaked as well (they almost shoot simultaneously now). Also some lightsabers have been tweaked (some have a bit more color now) and they've added Naboo in the end (party) sequence in ROTJ. That's the changes I can remember, but there are probably some more....
