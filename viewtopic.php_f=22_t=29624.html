The Mercenary Navy Opens A Second Server. . .

Launch Of Second Mercenary Navy Server:
The Mercenary Navy has upgraded its original server and is now launching a secondary server for its players. The server will operate late in to the night in the UK, but will provide a great opportunity for players from the USA to become a bigger part of the mercenary navy community.
The servers will both enforce the same rules and will have the same amount of starting money (100,000 credits and a defender). The opening times and specifications of both servers can be found on the mercenary navy website in the server section.
There is also a new dedicated forum for both servers where players can meet up, talk about their server operations, discuss missions, arrange to meet up online or feedback on the server operator.
At this moment in times, tournaments and official clan missions will only take place on the primary server, however they may soon run clan missions on the secondary server and gradually increase this as the server gains players and becomes more popular.
Those players who have been active on the server {MN} Mercenary Navy (High Speed) will find that the server has been re-named and that all of your player files are now on the server called:
{MN} Mercenary Navy (Primary)
While the new server will operate under the name:
{MN} Mercenary Navy (Secondary)
With both servers having different opening times the Mercenary Navy can now offer near 24 hour coverage on its Freelancer servers. And they look forward to seeing you on both of them at url=http://www.mercenarynavy.co.uk www.mercenarynavy.co.uk .
