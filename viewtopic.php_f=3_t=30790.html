the anubis is not the best thing. Heres some better ships:
Falcon- better hardpoints and maneuvers better
Eagle- maneuvers the same as anubis, handles c9 and c10 weps and shields and is the weakest of all VHF's.
Sabre- In order to get htis, you must go to malta and be friendly with outcasts. Also has 6 guns of c9 and c10 and has a c10 forward turret.(7 forward pointing powerull weps.) It maneuvers a little less than the eagle but has better armor.
Titan- is owned by corsairs in planet crete. Has the same stats as sabre, except the turret points backwards and has better armor, but maneuvers like a battleship.(not really, but close.)
if you download the victor's mod 4.4 at www.bobway.com, you can get all ships
