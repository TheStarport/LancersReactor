Hey people

Just to let you know, I've been testing the Pre-release Final, and I gotta tell you it's mind-blowing.

The Osiris II is available, and it's a nice ship, though I'm thinking of having Ro9ue tone down its maneuverability issues. It is definitely THE toughest battleship out there now though, custom shields, heavy armor... You'll see

Anyway, regarding the Damocles, I personally like it becaise it's not as flat-out UGLY as the Balistner, and with the right loadout I can take out anything else, including a heavily loaded Balistner. But that's just my own thing.

There are some new goodies for Rebal 3.5 Final... Enhanced Order Shield Powercells among them. Also there are new Order weapons that Ro9ue did a really nice job on. The Wrath starts out with a pretty nice loadout, but you can only get it in one place... Battleship Osiris. So for those who are having trouble finding the thing, keep looking Also, The Wrath (actually ShadowWrath, in game), starts out with its own custom powerplant. Suggestion, don't sell it and think of upgrading to the Upgrade Powerplant MK V, you'll wish you hadn't. Also, none of the "Uber" powerplants, like the Balistner powerplant, are usable on fighter class ships. So consider yourself warned, things are going to be a lot more energy tight in this version. I think it's a good thing, lots more challenge if you have to watch your energy levels.

I'll tell ya though, with the right mix of components and the right ship, you can still put out a lot of nastiness to the enemy, so don't fret if your ship isn't able to fire a continuous stream of Star-trek death for an hour at a time

There's so much more in this version that I've barely scratched the surface, so bear with us, and we'll get it out there soon

Well, hope this has helped to let you all know that we're doing everything we can to release asap

Have fun,
ArchKaine
