What ship are you flying in? Humpback? You deserve to die if you do...

Obviously you should have got the anubis by now. Fit it with the 8.33 fire rate weapons (because you'll be doing dog fighting) Chuck out any missiles, and put the best mines you can get.

On the approach to the sattellite, a group of fighters will appear. Stay away, let the NPC take care of it and take the brunt of their shots. From here onwards you can choose to directly assault the sattelite, or finish up the fighters first, then the weapons platforms..

Also, get a proper shield againts rheinland fighters.
