The Bounty Hunters are a great nuisance if they are hostile, both because they are virtually everywhere (even in the homeworlds of their arch enemies), and because the loot they drop - if any - is not adequate for the work involved. The Mollies or Red Hessians or Corsairs are different: if they bother you then you swat them and you get compensated handsomely with loot.

The reason is probably that the Bounty Hunter weapons are so extremely cr*ppy that the game designers had to put weapons of much higher weapon class on the Bounty Hunter ships than for the other factions, and a class 9 gun does not drop for you unless you are stinking rich already.

Also, not only do the BH have the potential to be a nuisance, the game makes sure that they realize this potential at least twice during the singleplayer campaign.
