Mercenary

Sun Apr 20, 2003 11:14 pm by Mercenary

For aiming:
You may notice a small red cross that appears when you highlight an enemy. Always move your cursor to highlight this, rather than highlight the body of their craft only. You have to account for the delay from laser fire to actually travel the distance and hit the target. That's why you should aim at the small red X that appears infront of their travel path, as this will put your laser fire into their oncoming path.
Also move your cursor as quickly as possible to their red arrow if they attempt a fly-by.

Lock onto their craft properly:
Take the time to hit space bar and autopilot. Quickly highlight their craft in the bottom left of the HUD and keep clicking to highlight the part of their ship you wanna target. This helps focus your attack. Then switch back to free-fly mode and chase them down!

Use Afterburn:
Use the Afterburn (shift key) to catch them up and keep up with them. Use it sparingly because you'll need the gas to evade other fighters trying to blow you to hell. It's a valuble tool to help you maneuvre. Look closely at their movements. They speed away, and fly towards you, speed away and fly towards you. Use the afterburner to your advantage.


Get outta there!
Finished the job? Taken out your target? Why hang about? Hit the spacebar key and go into autopilot for a second. choose the "go to" icon for you next waypoint. This disables your weapons but makes you move at speed to get out of a tight situation. Hit the space bar again to assume control (while staying in the fast speed mode). Keep strafing left, right, up and down and get clear of any danger. They'll find it hard to catch you up.

Hope that helps.

Mon Apr 21, 2003 4:32 am by pressgod

I love fighting in the asteroid fields especially those with the explosive gas clouds those who have been there know why! LOL

I use the kill engine slide and gas and go manuvers. Offence, Defence.

First come at them with your afterburners on full then highlight the ships and fire missles and guns at them. As they pass hit the z key to kill engines and quickly turn around (Slide in reverse) and let them have another blast of cannon fire and missles up the pipe. This usually takes a bunch of them out and weakens the rest for easy clean up. Hit your engines and persue and mop up.

For defensive moves the gas and go method works for me. I gas the afterburners and head straight for the thickest part of an asteroid field. (when available, it works just as well in space use whatever is around trade rings jump gates, space staitons, etc.)

Use the strafe keys and mouse moving your ship back and forth to throw off their aim, and when you reach a large rock, push straight up to loop back around as the AI usually hit them head on, and bounce off. You can get a great shot at them while they try and regain control.

This method also helps keep his buddies from locking onto you as the rocks keep most of their shots from a direct lock on hit.

The other part is to drop some mines around a couple of the asteroids as you go along as this creates a pocket of them in the small space they followed you into.

Don't forget to hit your space bar once in a while as it gives you a sort of false barrel roll manuver to keep them off balance.
