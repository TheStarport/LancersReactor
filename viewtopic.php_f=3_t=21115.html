T-hawk

Mon Jul 14, 2003 5:40 am by T-hawk

Actually, I'm working on doing exactly that - hacking the game .INI files in the Missions directories to remove all the cutscenes.

It isn't quite as easy as it sounds, since there are several ways to go wrong and crash the game. But you can get some REALLY interesting things going on...



For the Freeport-7 intro, I got the game to be in SPACE-FLIGHT mode instead of cut-scene mode! I can fly around and look at FP7, scan the ships flying around, watch the bombers destroy it (captured in that screenshot). Unfortunately, FP7 won't let me dock. And if I shoot anything (to where it goes hostile) the game crashes.

All the ground-based cutscenes are pretty easy to snip out. Talk to Juni in the bar and the accept/decline box immediately pops up, and hit accept and you immediately have control back at the base.

But I think I might have to not tamper with the space-based cutscenes. For mission 2 when you launch from Manhattan, there's a long space cutscene with Trent and King yakking back and forth ("goodbye million credits". If I try to dock somewhere during that, boom, game crashes.

More to come...
