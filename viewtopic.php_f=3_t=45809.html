New Storyline Mod?

I was wondering how many love the storyline and still play it?

Have you ever wanted more in the storyline then what vanilla has to offer?

If so and you want a storyline done the way you like it please post here your ideas of what you would like to see in a single player storyline mod. I am very interested in finding out what us SP players like and do not like.

If I get many replies/requests I will make a storyline with the most requested and liked add-on's. So here is your chance Storyline players to get a mod just the way you think it should be.

For Starters

Here are some things that could be done, but not limited to these. This is just more of an idea starter. I don't want to get away from vanilla but use what the game has to offer.

1. Higher paying missions both random and storyline
2. Harder missions in the House systems and Independent Worlds.
3. Up to 12 missions for all bases/planets
4. 3 types of scanners and tractor beams
5. Upgrades: Armor, Power Plants, and Engines
6. Cruise speed 350-450 Remember the Hovis race when thinking about the cruise speed
7. More NPC’s
8. Battleship encounters with real tuff battle fleets
9. Add lootable pilots to all factions and a place to be sold at.
10. Add Mancers Freelancer ship pack
12. Rebalance all weapons
13. Raise commodity prices
14. Add commodities to other bases that should have them
15. Open Jumpholes after mission 1
16. Where is the LSF? We can add the LSF to Liberty Space
17. Where did the Order go? We can add the Order to the Unknown 1 & 2 to fight the Nomads there

There are many more options we could go with but this will help you get some idea of what can be done.
