Manhattan_Guy

Mon Apr 17, 2006 12:57 am by Manhattan_Guy

Most ships don't have the turret view definitions in their cockpit.ini anymore and therefore use some sort of standard setting, but at least one ship still used it. You are able to use those entries in any new ship of yours.

Ah, found it in k_elite.ini.

Here it is:
<pre><font size=1 face=Courier>[TurretCamera
tether = 0.000000, 6.709000, 29.226000
yaw_rotate_speed = 2.000000
pitch_rotate_speed = 1.500000
accel_speed = 5 </font></pre>

Accushot

Tue Apr 18, 2006 12:52 pm by Accushot

Not on a per-ship basis. You'll have to decompress the room *.thn file with Fled-Thorn, repair it with FLScan, then find the entries that correspond to the camera position and move them further from the ship marker. This has an effect every time you enter the room no matter what the ship, and can cause visual artifacts if you move too far through the scenery, so it's probably best to just live with it...
