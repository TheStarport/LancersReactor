BZ

Sun Aug 17, 2003 4:24 am by BZ

Upper right hand corner of Omicron Alpha and Gamma. Each has a jumphole with wrecks scattered around it. Wait around and nomads will come, or go through the jumphole if you are brave. You have to be at least lvl 34 for weapons to drop, and they drop more often if you are higher. Nomads always appear in 4's and are tough to beat. Don't try to take them without a Eagle/Sabre/Titan. You'll need one to mount the guns anyway.

You can find a ripper mine launcher in one or two of the many wrecks around the Alpha hole. Only place to get it in the game, I think.
