Oracle , Beserk , everyone else :

Alright, since this seems to be a tough nut to crack for many people, here's the basic rundown (should have given one sooner ): forget legality, and concern yourself with initialworld.ini ONLY. Staging a set piece battle is accomplished primarily through that file... it's The Matrix Of Relations for FL's many factions. Each group has a bunch of rep entries that link it to every other group in a massive web of empathy. Pretty self-explanatory really:

[Group
nickname = li_n_grp
ids_name = 196846
ids_info = 66200
ids_short_name = 196895
rep = 0.65, ku_n_grp

[Group
nickname = ku_n_grp
ids_name = 196851
ids_info = 66198
ids_short_name = 196900
rep = 0.65, li_n_grp

Guess what happens when you sneakily insert a minus sign in front of 0.65, ku_n_grp and 0.65, li_n_grp ? Hint: "shoot first, ask questions later"

Doppel: I think you accidentally strengthened your character's relations with Bretonia... you didn't forget to place a minus symbol by any chance?

Snake: this is the code that's giving you a hard time, right?

encounter = capitalships_Escorts, 19, 0.50000
faction = li_n_grp, 1.000000
encounter = capitalships_Kusari, 19, 0.700000
faction = ku_n_grp, 1.000000
