Cmp files in Freelancer are mostly 3D mesh files for objects.

What OP-R8R means is get the utf-edit prog from the downloads and open the starshere.cmp with it, this will allow you to see what format the texture is in (.tga or .dds).

These 2 formats are all that Freelancer uses.
