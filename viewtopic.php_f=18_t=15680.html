Thu May 01, 2003 3:21 am by Leonhart

You can make stronger weapons by your self.Open weapon_equip.ini using biniQDU.This file is located in DATA\EQUIPMENT of your freelancer directory.Now find weapon you want to edit (here is an example)

[Munition
nickname = or_battleship_turret01_ammo
hp_type = hp_gun
requires_ammo = false
hit_pts = 2
hull_damage = 10000.500000<---increase this for damage done
energy_damage = 0
one_shot_sound = fire_capship_forward_gun
munition_hit_effect = li_capgun_01_impact
const_effect = li_cruiser_maingun
lifetime = 3.500000
force_gun_ori = false
mass = 1
volume = 0.000100

Now by increasing hull_damage you will increase damage done by that weapon.(be careful enemis will also use edited weappons).If you increase hull damage to mush your weapon might do 0 damage(this is what happened to me before so be carefull)

Sorry can not help you with cruise disruptor .
