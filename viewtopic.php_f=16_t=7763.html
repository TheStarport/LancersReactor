I just finished the SP campaign and a little hacking befriended me with the Corsairs. A lot of flying around looking for jumpholes and I'm now the proud owner of a Titan, Planet Crete registration.

Oh and the Titan has a Turret slot too.....so I went to Cadiz and bought....yep you guessed it, the salamanca turret! Hehe. Adv Brigandine shields means she can take a pummeling whilst killing almost anything in one or two passes. Highly recommended.
