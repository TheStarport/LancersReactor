I am looking for XXXX what ini... Look no further it''s here

Data\equipment\st_equip.ini
Shields and thrusters under lying stats like how strong they are or how fast the make you go or how much energy they use

Data\equipment\st_good.ini
the above stated as they appear in markets

Data\equipment\misc_equip.ini
Scanners, Power plants, Nanos and batteries

data\equipment\misc_good.ini
the above stated they appear in markets

data\equipment\weapon_equip.ini
Any weapon is here as well as cloaking devices (don't ask how to get one to work I don't know i am a n00b with under a week's worth of experience and generally people don't like cloaking devices and such)

data\equipment\weapon_good.ini
the above stated as they appear in markets

data\equipment\engine_equip.ini
Ship engines their under lying game stats like energy usage and how fast they make you go and how fast the cruise mode makes you go (I think)

data\equipment\engine_good.ini
empty but the above stated as they would appear in markets

data\equipment\light_equip.ini
The cool little lights on the various things like your docking lights

data\equipment\good.ini
Commodities and weird other things like random mission objects and ship hulls/packages ussally obtainable in the market and other stuff mostly commodities and mission commodities

data\equipment\select_equip.ini
contains the actual good.ini data for game stats this also contains armor scales and shield objects as well as cargo, and loot pods and some other stuff like interalFX and attachedFX

data\equipment\weaponmoddb
Weapon type dmg Vs. Shield type in terms of effectiveness

data\equipment\prop_equip.ini
I don't know about this one anyone willing to take a stab at it?

data\equipment\commodities_per_faction.ini
Contains what the various npc faction ships can have in their cargo holds like sumggler's drugs or other contraband

data\equipment\market_commodities.ini
Where various commodities will be sold in the game like the H-fuel on manhattan or the cost modifier of the item at the given station/dock

data\equipment\marktet_ships.ini
Where various ships will be sold in the game like the patriot in manhattan and there level requirements friendlyness requirements and cost modifiers

data\equipment\market_misc.ini
where various other things like guns will be sold in the game like the liberty justice laser cannon in manhattan and the level and friendlyness requirements and the cost modifiers


That's it for the equipment folder if I forgot something or other wise need feed back just post or come find me on msn messenger (Kaclean@yahoo.com) I'll post other stuff soon as I learn what everything else is.
