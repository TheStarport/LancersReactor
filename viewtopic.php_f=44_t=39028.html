Um, I wasn't involved in the above crediting, lol... I just directed Aluminum to the above proggies... I certainly didn't write them

Anyhow... I dunno why anybody would get defensive about Milkshape. It does what it does pretty darn well. It's not a supurb modeler, but that's not what it's really made for. It's an import/export program with lots of tools to help modelers go from Point A (a more powerful modeler) to Point B (working game data).

For $25, it's the best deal in modeling, period. It doesn't need to defend itself- its record with helping modders realize their dreams is very long, and we should all just keep hoping that none of the big companies ever offer the programmer enough money to quit making new versions
