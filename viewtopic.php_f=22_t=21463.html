To make it really really simple for anyone else unsure.
Press ENTER button to open up the chat box.
Automatically set to system, but if you press the up arrow (or down) you can scroll through everyone one the server- which includes the console (green box - white writing). Now type in your message and hit enter to send it. It will come up WHITE on your screen instead of the usual blue - and NO - no-one else will seee the console message you sent. That clear enough for anyone to understand?
