The game is out of proportion in order to compress it in space/time, and my only quibble with it is that they made trade lane travel speed so slow (apparently this was done so that you can admire the scenery, ho-hum). And that you cannot skip the cutscenes/scripts in SP, hehe.

Just look at how long it takes you in real life to go from one big city to another on the same bloody planet, or even from one spot in a big city to another. If history is anything to go by then space travel won't be any faster, only slower. So, if they had used realistically sized planets/star systems and realistic travel times then players would probably get bored out of their minds. Although I did run across a thread where somebody seriously suggested that traveling from one system to another should take at least 10 to 20 minutes ...

Just to get the thing about realism in proper perspective: the light from Sol does take about 8 minutes to reach Earth, and compared to other planets Earth is still comparatively close to the sun.
