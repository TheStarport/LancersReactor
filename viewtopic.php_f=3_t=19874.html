Sherlog

Sat Jun 21, 2003 6:50 pm by Sherlog

Beating the game in a Patriot is even easier than using the Dagger (just try it, I know this because I did both) but the Starflier is a tad more difficult. I think it should be possible since the Starflier is only a little bit worse than the Patriot and moves much better than the Dagger. The worst thing is when there are lots of missiles flying about because if the Starflier gets hit by a missile better than a Slingshot when the shield is down then it is toast. Enemies that use their rear turrets are also a PITA, because normally you expect your shield to regenerate when you are at the six of your target and the shield of the Starflier cannot absorb much fire.

The Patriot seems only a little better than the other ships when you look at the stats, but it actually flies/feels much different. In a Patriot you are the hunter, in a Starflier/Dagger you are the prey ...

P.S.: forgot the weapon loadout. I used Xeno guns on Starflier/Patriot and Liberty plasma guns on the Dagger (but Xeno guns would have been better); on all ships I used the cheapest homing mines except for the last couple of missions where I used the best mines that were available, for bombarding slow-moving or stationary targets. I considered using missiles but after doing the math I stuck to guns; missile storage capacity is limited and so the missiles would last only for a small portion of a mission.
