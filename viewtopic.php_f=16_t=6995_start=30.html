1 Million credits (level 20 - 37 in 45 minutes)

Engine parts from new berlin thru the warphole near new berlin to Sigma 13, sell at that little depot (I think yanagi station or something, its like 4-5k from the warphole inside of Sigma 13) Buy Cardamine there and return it to new berlin. The run is VERY short, but you WILL die quite often coming back thru the warphole returning with the cardamine to new berlin cause often there will be 10+ hessians there waiting for you and not even god himself could escape.

Your best tactic to get away is to drop countermeasures as fast as you can press the button and activate cruise engines (works about 70 percent of the time) -- If you die it will reload you right as you came out of the warp hole to new berlin just before you died, and there are almost NEVER any hessians when it reloads its always bounty hunters. I've never once seen hessians after getting killed and reloading.

Berlin police scan you on every trip, on the OUTBOUND leg toward sigma (when you have engine parts) -- but they have NEVER ONCE scanned me coming with the cardamine.

This is the trade run I did for a long time to build up my funds. It is incredibly long so I don't know if it's the best but it does make alot of credit.
Diamonds from new berlin to New Tokyo.

Engine parts from New Tokyo (planet honshu if you have the patience) to Freeport 4 in magellan

Take what ever from Freeport 4 to Kensingto shipping platform for a minor profit

Then take Beryllium from Kensingto to The Ring above Planet New Berlin. That puts you back at New Berlin to get diamonds and do it all over again. The sigma and omega systems help make this shorter but it's still quite long. But it does make insane profits and as long as you haven't pissed too many factions off...the pirates aren't very threatening.....merely annoying.
