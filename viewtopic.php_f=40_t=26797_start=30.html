Killer Bee

Fri Aug 03, 2007 10:45 pm by Killer Bee

I'm sold on Free Worlds 1.65, which I think is a great mod altogether. A friend of mine was ready to delete it because when he starts off in open singleplayer, all he has is a Z95 with two weak guns. Here's what I told him to get a better ship and to make some quick credits:

1. The minute he gets out of the Multiplayer Nexus in the Kuat System, go to the ice field (if you select the Corellian Hyperbuoy your autopilot will take you right through it). Shoot some ice crystals and tractor them into your hold until you are full, then proceed to the jump hole leading to Corellia.

2. Once in Corellia space, select the Tattoo jump hole, and head for Tattooine, where you can sell your free water for 1,440 creds each! This beats going all the way to Bespin and paying for water. In no time you'll be able to upgrade your ship (or buy a new one with all the bells and whistles) and take on a few bad guys. It took me about a day before I could upgrade to TIE Bomber, Civilian (which has a 100 unit cargo hold) and about two more days before I got my dream ship, Dash Rendar's Outrider.

Well, time to smoke some Black Sun pirates, have fun people!
