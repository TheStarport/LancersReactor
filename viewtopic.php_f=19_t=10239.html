Nog

Wed Apr 02, 2003 6:22 pm by Nog

When you first Join a game you actually have no affiliation what-so-ever. But you need money so almost always the first thing you do is a Navy Mission.

In the beginning you can avoid this by leaving Manhatton space and doing a mission for someone that is not Liberty Navy or Police. From there, the best way to change affiliation is destroy the enemy of the faction that you wish to join, and then Do missions/favors for them untill they become top of your list on the green scale. Favors being things like supplying needed goods to the base even if you take a loss, or rescuing faction members in a fight In the most extreme cases you can get other rewards too when doing these "favors".
