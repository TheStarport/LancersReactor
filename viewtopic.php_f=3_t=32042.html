How about the vitals on the ship? This info comes directly from the loadouts.ini file.

I think it has about 5 or 6 hardpoints for guns and one for a turret. It's cargo space is actually 50 and it holds unlimited nanos and batteries. It carries the standard engine and powerplant for the more common defender ship, so I wouldn't bother using class 6 weapons unless you change out the powerplant.

Wanna fool someone into thinking you're below rank 10? just buy as many bats and nanos as you got credits for. With enough cash, you can pretty much outlast most fighters in combat.

I wouldn't try to use the side thrusters though, as drains down your thrusters fast. This is an inherent problem with the hull, much like the legionaire allowing you to thrust past a cruise speed of 300 from time to time.
