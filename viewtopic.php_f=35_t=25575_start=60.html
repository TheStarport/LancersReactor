I for one love this mod. I consider it more of an enhancement than a modification. I can't wait for the next version.

For those who are slightly miffed about RS not being compatible with the super-packs, I personally use the following in conjunction with RS.

- The missing Starblazer fighter
- Sliver_Fire's various weapon addons (the pack doesn't work, but the stand alone weapons work just fine)
- The Colonial Viper (BG freak, I'll admit)
- APS Shippack V2.1 (top notch original ships IMHO)

To those who are complaining about how tough the baddies are now, all you have to do is pick up a starpulse gun and watch how they shrivel up in front of you. Personally it makes the game delightfully challenging. I think the Mules still use the bats/bots every now and then, so they're a delicous meal that fights back once you peel them open.

But then again if you have Sliver_Fire's Mass Drivers, you can own the solar skies even with a patriot, all you have to have is a Starpulse, a Mass Driver, and any two weapons of your choice. Many things will wither and die as you touch them.

So yank off that Drail and shell out the credits to pop on an Adv. Starpule, and quit yer *****in'.
