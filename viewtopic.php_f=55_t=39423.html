Sun Jun 19, 2005 4:30 pm by Onyx_Assassin

I had the same problem with my comp crashing at the same point. The worst part was I reinstalled my entire system about 3-4 times (I was getting crashes in a few other games as well). Seemed like no one could figure out what was wrong. Turned out I was missing drivers for my Nvidia chipset on the board. I had the latest drivers for my card but the chipset had its own set of drivers. Now no more crashes in Freelancer or any other game. So my advice is update absolutely every device driver you have if your comp is crashing.
