Ok, the best route that i have found so far for single player is

Planet New Tokyo ----> Planet cambridge (engine comps) making $1000/unit
Planet Cambridge ----> Planet New London (Diamonds) making $177/unit
Planet New London ----> Planet Denver (lux food) making $1120/unit
Planet Denver ----> Planet New tokyo (lux consumer) making $577/unit

This route should get you about $700k profit in a humpback, and about 50k more in a dromedary. Remember that lux food is perishable, so u will lose 8-10 units from london to denver, but the profit on the rest makes up for it.

Tanks to those who have posted up the routes included in this route. I found this is the quickest way of making monel legally in SP. If u fancy the risk though, there is always the cardomine/artifacts/diamonds runs to be done.... which is no biggie if ur factions are right and your in a big ship
