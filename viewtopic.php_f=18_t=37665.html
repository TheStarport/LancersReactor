Heh, well, I could be mistaken here, but I do not think it is possible to make Class 11 anything. What you'll have to do is
A.) reduce all guns by one level and all ships by one level. Leave the cap stuff for capships (which will be the only ship(s) in te game that can mount Lvl10 stuff.
or
B.) reduce just the level 10 weapons and the VHFs that can use them. This way you're only chaging a few things instead of every dang ship and weapon :p

Believe me, I just finished up a week's worth of editing doing part A -- it is more balanced, but ugh, is it so NOT-fun when you're actaully doing it.

But again, don't think classes other than the ones already in game are possible ... lazy Microsoft
