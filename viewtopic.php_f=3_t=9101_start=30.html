pressgod

Fri Jun 06, 2003 12:48 am by pressgod

Much debate all saying the same thing, it depends upon what you like! LOL, the only suggestion this "old timer" has is to try and match particle speed with range and you will rarely miss what you are shooting at.

Try to stay at around 50 ms difference or less and about the same for the range. I also recommend you fly out in safe and neutral space and turn off all of your weapons then fire each one, all by itself to see where it is placed on the weapon mounts. Go into the H view turret mode but don't get it too far out of shape or they won't fire. This way you can see the location of each actual mounted weapon and how it "points" in a dogfight on your ship.

I did this and once I started putting weapon Combos together by how they were mounted on my ship it made a difference in how often I hit the target and the damage that was done. Now I hardly miss in close combat situations and a lot of times you will hear them say we need to bug out it would be suicide to attack right now. No duhhh!

For example If you mount a shield draining weapon above a heavy hitting hull weapon on the same side of your ship they should reach the target one behind the other as they have (again 50ms difference in speed) , the same range. Try it you will see what I am getting at.

I personally like the better grade Missles as they can do a lot of damage in a short period of time. I mean if you can do 3000 damage or more in one shot why would you not use them?

I think most people just don't know how to use them properly as they are used to fire and forget missles from Jet games. These missles in this game are tatical weapons so you need to be fireing down on the enemy with them, or from an angle that will bring the enemy towards the missles projected flight path. If you try to shoot them up their pipe (unless it is a very slow target) you will always miss as they can turn away or use counter measures against them. I have even seen NPC's have their buddies shoot them down for them.

Head to head use missles, mines and guns then use the kill engine slide and kick them in the rear. If you are persuing someone then get an angle on them by turning away then back towards them on a diagonal intercept and fire the missle from that angle they almost always hit as they can't dodge what they don't see coming.

Good Flying All

Just some thoughts from the edge of space!
