RunOrDie

Wed Mar 19, 2003 3:14 pm by RunOrDie

You could try bribing them. Zoner (Freeport) bases in proximity of pirate bases usually allow you to bribe that pirate faction to improve your rep. Check in the bar. If you can't bribe anyone, try undocking from the base and docking back, it usually cycles the bar attendees.

Or you could try killing their enemies. Killing Bounty Hunters works, but I would recommend you go to the Dresden system and kill a dozen Red Hessians (try the orange cloud at the north-west corner of the map). Bounty Hunters are all over the place, in every corner of Sirius, and it would be annoying if they attacked you on sight.
