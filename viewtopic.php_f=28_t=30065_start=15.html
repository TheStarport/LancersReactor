u know how release dates are ... they change a lot, get delayed, ...

maybe a few more notes on the mod:
we give certain things more depth gameplay wise. things will get a bit more complicated, ppl will have to watch out more.
but this also opens new possibilites and makes the game more interesting.

examples for this are the gun and thruster energy balance.
gun energy is also used by shields, both capacity and recharge affect it.
also when you cruise ur power will rebuild slower.
thruster energy is used by thrusters (doh) but also by sliding.

people will find new tactics to get used to that. standard FL is pretty straightforward in many aspects (which isnt nessessarily bad). we try to use various aspects to make the arcade-game style more interesting for experianced players who are looking for new challenges and opportunities.

You may be an expert pilot on standard FL and it will surely help u also on excelcia, but to learn all the nice lil tweaks and opportunities we create here is the true challenge for an ace
