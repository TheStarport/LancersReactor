Nightsilver

Thu Apr 17, 2003 12:06 am by Nightsilver

TDC (Tizona del Cid) are the best gun for dealing shield damage. It does 1280 shield damage at a 4.00 refire rate (speed 600m/s). It's advisable to have at least one of these at all times (I would carry 2 if you're going to PVP, since player shields can recharge pretty quickly).

The problem with carrying a TDC is the weapon speed. If you use a combination of weapons with different speeds, you generally miss a lot more. This is because the reticle is placed based on your fastest moving weapon's travel speed, so, generally, only your fastest weapon will hit. You'll only really notice this problem when the enemy is running at an angle to your path. If the guy is moving along the same line as you are (either running away or charging you), you'll probably hit with everything.

As such, the best gun I know of to pair with TDC's is the Salamanca Mk II. It's the only class 9 with 600 weapon speed (I think). It does somewhere around 480 hull damage at a 4.00 refire rate.

Other options are Wyrm 2, Buckshot, Kraken 2, and Skyblast B, none of which do I really know anything about. I never ran class 9 weapons other than the TDC.
