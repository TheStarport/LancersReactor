Well since I know there are 4 wrecks in the Texas systsem (all in those corrosive black clouds - 2 each.) i'll look for those.
I uncompressed the Li02-04 systems to find Texas, which is Li04.

From Li04.ini

[Object
nickname=Li04_suprise_transport
ids_name=261712
pos=-35705, 1000, 59671
rotate=-6, -20, 15
archetype=suprise_transport
visit=16
ids_info=66463
loadout=SECRET_transport_Li04

If i recall correctly, this is a wrecked transport carrying Gate parts.

[Object
nickname=Li04_depot_superconductors_surprise
ids_name=261713
pos=-44985, -39, 40579
rotate=-30, 0, 0
archetype=surprise_superconductors
visit=16
ids_info=66464
behavior=NOTHING

This is the wreck of a station and has Superconductor parts

[Object
nickname=Li04_suprise_bh_fighter_1
ids_name=261714
pos=-40071, -500, -32779
rotate=-27, 45, 9
archetype=suprise_bh_fighter
visit=16
ids_info=66462
loadout=SECRET_bh_bh_fighter_li04

Wreck of a Bounty Hunter ship, has some decent weapons on it (class6 Winchesters)

[Object
nickname=Li04_suprise_pi_fighter_1
ids_name=261715
pos=-32268, -500, -45867
archetype=suprise_pi_fighter
visit=16
ids_info=66465
loadout=SECRET_lr_pi_fighter_li04

This is a pirate ship that was smuggling Diamonds, and gives a very nice boost to cash flow early on.

You should be able to find any of the wrecks by scanning the files for "surprise".
