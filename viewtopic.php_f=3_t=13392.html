Wed Apr 09, 2003 10:11 pm by bobk

Another thing that you may want to consider that may be even more important than your gun selection is your defense. Let's face it, at any one time, you can only be shooting at one ship. But, at this same point in time, you may have five or six enemies with you in their reticle. So, it's imperative that your ship hold together long enough for you to get out of the jam that you're in and your shields are the key to keeping you in the game.

There are three different types of shields, Graviton, Molecular, and Positron. Graviton shields work best against laser, photon, and pulse weapons. Molecular shields provide the best protection from plasma and particle weapons. Positron shields protect best against tachyon and neutron weapons. Within each shield type, there are three additional types, Light Fighter, Heavy Fighter, and Freighter.

As you have probably figured out, by now, there are seven different weapons types which are used by the various factions. Each faction will use only one or two types of weapons, but what weapons they use will determine what shield you should be using. You should buy a shield, of each type. Then, when you are looking for missions, take notice of the enemies that you will encounter and equip your ship with the appropriate shield before you launch.

Below, I've listed some of the factions and the weapons that each faction uses:

Blood Dragons Laser
Corsairs Neutron, Pulse
Gaians Photon
Golden Chrysanthemums Photon
Lane Hackers Tachyon
Liberty Rogues Laser
Mollies Neutron
Outcasts Tachyon, Particle
Red Hessians Plasma, Laser
Rheinland Plasma, Tachyon
Unioners Neutron
Xenos Particle
Zoners Photon

Remember, "the best offense, is a good defense."

I have found that the most important factor in choosing a weapon setup is to be sure all weapons are the same projectile speed. If they aren't, the aiming cross is set for your fastest proj. wep, so all the rest will miss, and this can be a big mess. Having the right shield is important too, but be sure that the ones you use are all the best you can get, you don't wanna switch from a class 6 grav to a class 4 molecular.
