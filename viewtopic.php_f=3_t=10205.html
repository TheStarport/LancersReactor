aerundel

Sun Mar 16, 2003 6:52 am by aerundel

Ok, I just made my own groups, since you reminded me of them, so here's how I did it.

For me, I used the numpad, but you're going to just use the number row over the letters by the looks of it (I should probably do that too). Anyway, I assume you've already cleared the hotkey assignments for at least 1-6. Now put 1-6 as the hotkeys for the ACTIVATE weapon group hotkeys. Now, press one of your hotkeys - let's start with 1. Then use your mouse to activate the weapons you want to use on your weapon menu (lower right of screen). Press 2. Activate the weapons you want for that key. Continue until you're done making groups. Now every time you press 1-6, the group you made will be activated, and you can shoot it with your right mouse button.

The ASSIGN hotkeys seem to be kind of redundant, because the ACTIVATE ones seem to assign things automatically when you click a weapon. I don't have either the ASSIGN or SELECT weapons groups hotkeys assigned, but I was able to make these groups easily. I don't know exactly what you want with the "hardpoint" stuff, since that's dependent on where you equip the weapon. But there you go.
