Found a prob with a few released ships

While playtesting my mod, i've found that some ship packs have a few problems with them. Mostly, they are:
APS-Pegasus, Griffen and Hydra-They share the same archetype names so the first spawn dictates what the others look like.
The Predator series- Raptor, Predator and Pheonix. The Raptor and Predator share the same archetype names again (I think the pheonix might be ok).
The Blackbird- Had some backfacing edges that look wrong and its HpMount is inside the ship.
Whats the rule on this? Do I request to the shipwrights for permission or can I just re-import them using milkshape? (I would keep everything the same, just change the archetype names).
Edit
Before you all jump on me, the idea of re-importing the model is only so that the hard work of the ship modellers is not wasted by something silly like clashing archetype names. If I was to re-import, all credit still goes to the creator of the original models.

Argh

Tue Oct 18, 2005 11:51 pm by Argh

I'd suggest fixing the ones that don't have mesh problems by using FL Model Cloner to fix the bad names... easier to do than to import them, re-export them, and then re-hardpoint them or re-import the VMESHDATA and VMESHREFs.

As for the issue of whether or not you're screwing up their work... my suggestion would be to fix 'em, email the creators, and if they don't write back, credit them in your mod. Me being me, I'd probably also do a quick-and-easy ship addon mod via FLMM scripts and put it out seperately- if you're already adding the new ships to your mod that way, then putting them into a seperate ship pack would be a piece of cake, unless you're doing something really weird with these ships and their hardpoints, etc. That way, everybody can use these ships without having problems. At any rate, good catch- there have to be a *lot* of ships out there with these basic problems...
