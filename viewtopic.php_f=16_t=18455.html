Wed Jun 04, 2003 9:36 pm by T-hawk

There's another way to do it, if you're good enough with the Junkers to fly missions for them (no more than one box into the negative.) Every random mission you successfully complete improves your rep with the Junkers by one box, and also by about one-fifth of a box with the Corsairs which are friendly with the Junkers.

Beaumont in Texas offers missions that you can fly for the Junkers to kill Xenos. Against weak Xeno ships, you can knock off missions in 3-4 minutes. An hour of that gets the Corsairs up from fully hostile into neutral, and another hour can get the Corsairs high enough so that you can go fly missions for them, and that's an easy way to get them up to fully friendly.

Takes longer, but doesn't have the bribe side-effect that you piss off everyone who hates the Corsairs, which is half the galaxy...
