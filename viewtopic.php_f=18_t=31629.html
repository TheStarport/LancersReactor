"How to" > submitting files large and small to

Caution! some reading involved

Submitting News...
MOD's, Tutorials, Tools/Utilities, Server Announcements, Pilot News, Clan News, Websites, Your Birthday and other goodies of community interest.

On Lancers Reactor click on Submit News for duh! News.

For submitting files click on Submit files and your email program should pop up ready to send.

You can send file attachments up to 250 megs by email to Lancers Reactor or if the file is larger than your provider allows try MSN Messenger or a free file sending service such at SendThisFile.
You can get a Free Account to send MOD's and other large files!
SendThisFile doesn't overload the email system. You won't have problems with fire walls, proxy servers and routers. And with no additional software to install, sending large files couldn't be easier.
Check it out at http://www.sendthisfile.com/

Send your larger emails to (copy and paste versions)
bp@lancersreactor.com
and/or
eraser@lancersreactor.com

Gmail accounts can also send and receive very large attachments.

b.bakedpotato@gmail.com

You can add bakedpotato and/or Eraser to your contact list on MSN Messenger and transfer the files that way.
Use these email addresses to add us to your contact list on MSN Messenger.

and/or
get_erased@hotmail.com

Sorry it happens... Please send it again.

Enjoy! yourself and the characters you will meet while you're here.
