Calling All PKers

Just a shout-out to all the people out there who take the time to add a little spice to the game.

Now, I know a lot of you folks don't like the concept. The only reason I do this is because I know Freelancer so very well, and have come to the conclusion that the NPCs offer zero challenge to an even half-capable player.

Players who have finished SP are more than a match for any NPCs, that's the PKer's whole purpose. As a PKer, I strive to be that unplanned assaliant, that Trade Lane pirate you can't just fly away from. I live to pick a player at random and hunt him down over multiple systems. I love to stop a player in mistride and challenge him to a duel.

I love flying around in ships that aren't Level 10, just for the challenge. I only use guns, because missiles feel so impersonal and indirect. I engage players even when I'm outclassed...to be selective and run would ruin the fun.

I love it when I win. I love it when I lose. I don't use batts or nanos, just fight by my wits. A PKer has nothing to gain, nothing to lose. The battle is foremost.

Yes, I'm a PKer, proud to be. And I'm sure I'm not the only one.
