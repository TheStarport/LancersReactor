Of the mods available Evolutions 1.30 adds a lot without disrupting the storyline or unbalancing game play. Its been called “what Freelancer should have been”. Most other mods are targeted at multiplayer and some don't support single player.

EDIT: I don't know of any final/public release mods that actually expand on the storyline. Some people have tried to do this but its generally seen as not being worth the trouble since 99% of FL is multiplayer at this point. Difference being singleplayer supports scripting, multiplayer doesn't.
