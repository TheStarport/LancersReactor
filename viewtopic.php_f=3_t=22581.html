Gung_Ho

Fri Aug 08, 2003 6:49 am by Gung_Ho

I prefer a graduated list of features. not three, but four sections, with the first covering basic features, the second covering greater detail, the third covering techy detail, and the fourth covering a list of changes from the last patch in a somewhat generalized notation (added this ship, moved this jumphole to 'somewhere' else (not specified) etc.)

In my experience, people will read from top to bottom until they are out of their depth, so scaling the list from top to bottom does the trick.
