To quote Chips in a sticky above,

IF you find your game crashing (and you haven't been using mods at ALL), then please go to your options in the game menu - and disable 3D sound.

This includes:
If your computer restarts at random
Your Computer restarts when going through jumpgates
Game crashes to desktop when going through jumpgates
Game crashes to desktop whilst playing normally.

If you ever have the option to "Send report to Microsoft" after your computer "recovers from a serious failure" - then take it. It may diagnose your problem (I had one for months, finally they updated the page accordingly, as SBLive had created a NEW driver. The old one caused crashes with Windows XP!!) - since then, no blue screens of death.... so give it a try
