Havent posted here in months....Just wanted to come back and see how the game is going. WELL i didnt think the game was boring, for about 1 1/2 months, but after that, i lost interest. Doing trade runs to get one of the top 3 best ships (the vhf's) got a bit boring, and constantly killing pirates for more money than i could ever want or use, also got a bit boring, so i went to mp. You did the same, just with some player interaction. It got fun again when i went pirating convoys in liberty with a group of people, but even that lost interest. Then i went on trade runs with a group, and then i also lost some interest in that too. Yes, there are 60+ systems, and yes there are some cool things to find (wrecks, jumpholes, hidden bases, etc.) but there is little to do after that. I had problems with most of the mods, and if they worked, i probably would have gotten a bit more enjoyment out of this game. The modding community is good. However since it started so soon after the game was released, it would appear that the game lacked so much content that the PLAYERS, who are here to PLAY the game had to add on more and more so that they wouldnt lose interest. I bought this game to play and have some fun, not to modify it over and over.
(BTW, i am playing EVE for almost 3 months steady now, and i am still enjoying it. Gonna get flamed for that...Maybe EVE is more my type of game)
