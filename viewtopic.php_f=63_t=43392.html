Now taking beta test applications for DARKSTAR ONE

We are now selecting beta testers for the English version of Darkstar One, Ascaron&#8217;s new space action game!

To become a beta tester, you must satisfy the following criteria:
- Be prepared to invest your time testing Darkstar One
- Be prepared to document bugs and submit them to Ascaron
- Be skilled in English grammar
- Possess a fast internet connection without a download capacity limit (could you download several Gigabytes of data without problems?)
- Be an avid science fiction fan, and especially enjoy shooter games like Freelancer, Wing Commander, X, etc.
- Live within a country of the European Union
Note: Only people from these countries can take part!
- You must be 18 years or older

If you meet these criteria then you could be the right person for us. With our beta test application you can sign up to become part of the team.

The application period begins now.
The betatest itself starts at the beginning of May and will run for approximately two weeks.

Please email betatest_dso@ascaron.com, with the following information:

*Salutation:
*First Name:
*Family Name:
*Email Address:
*House Number & Street Name:
*Postcode:
*Town:
*Country:
*Date of Birth:
*Nickname:
*Telephone Number:
Mobile Phone Number:
*CPU Speed and Manufacturer:
*System RAM:
*Motherboard Model (or &#8220;Unknown&#8221:
*Graphics Card Model:
*Graphics Card RAM:
*Sound Card:
*Operating System:
- Do you use an SLI system (2 Graphic Cards simultaneously)?
- Speaker type (stereo, 5.1, 7.1, etc.)?
- Available controllers (mouse, gamepad, joystick)?
- Briefly state why you think you&#8217;re the right person for the beta test.

* = Mandatory field

Notes:
1. Applications where the mandatory fields are not completed will be rejected.
2. We cannot guarantee that every application will be accepted since there are usually more applicants than available positions. You will be informed of your acceptance via email).

Apply now if you want to be involved!

The Ascaron team wishes you good luck in your application!
