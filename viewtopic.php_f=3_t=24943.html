Erm - there is a patch, and nearly everyone i know of has no problems. If you are playing online and are suffering disconnections - i would look towards other things before just blaming FL, which has proven to be extremely stable.

a) Check YOUR connection to the internet. If you are on a modem especially - noise in the line can cause disconnections for brief seconds
b) Server connection might be lousy - this is less likely though because you say it happens on every server you go on.
c) You have a mod running, and the patch detects your mod and boots you instead - hence your random 'disconnections'

Seriuosly - due to the vast amounts of players who have never complained or experienced these problems - i suggest you look to your equipment and software before blaming others.

Chips

Sun Oct 05, 2003 8:26 pm by Chips

However, having knowledge about mods, it is more than likely the rebalance mod. If you know about mods, FLMM will not remove all the files, any systems files, ships files and other files will still be in your game. It removes alot of files, but it won't always deactivate properly either, although it won't pull a message up about this. I would suggest that you delete the FL folder, then reinstall the game(notice delete, not uninstall - and reinstall without the delete will still leave files behind) and then have a go.

If you still have problems when you have done that, then i would look towards connection (bt aren't great (fellow brit ), and possibly your connection to the servers - where are the servers located, are they known as stable (servers sometimes do just crash and dump people - or if there is bad lag you might get disconnected too)

After that - it might be the game - but i still doubt it.
