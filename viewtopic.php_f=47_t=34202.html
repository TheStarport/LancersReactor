RPG - Sigma Systems

Okay okay, I know this might look a little too much like Outcasts, but I think this is a legitimate RPG to post up, only because the Sigma Systems fascinated me so much.

So.... rules and whatnot:

RULES:
1. Your posts MUST, I repeat, MUST be in complete sentences, and in the English language. If we get the occassional misspelling or missed capitalization, I'm not going to throw a conniption, but please be reasonable here folks. Complete English sentences, with capitalization and punctuation. Otherwise, I might just tweak your post myself. Also, if everyone wrote in the third-person, that'd make things a heck of alot easier too.

2. There is not NO LIMIT to posts you can make per day. So... post away.

3. If you're first starting off, and I'm sure many of us are at this point, you can only start off in Sigma-13, Sigma-17, or Sigma-19. You are allowed to travel to the following neighboring systems ONLY: Frankfurt, New Berlin, Honshu, Chugoku. No others, unless I advise otherwise. (I will allow you to start in any of the above neighboring systems if you feel so compelled to be a part of the Rheinland or Kusari Police or Military Forces; also if you want to be a Blood Dragon or Bundschuh. But please, no Kusari intrusions into Rheinland or vice-versa.)

4. NO Very Heavy Fighters unless requested first. And you have to work your way up to a VHF. I will allow you to start off with Light or Heavy Fighters, or a freighter.

5. For the sake of making things interesting, I will allow Freelancers in this RPG. If you're not a Freelancer, you must be part of the following factions:
*Orbital Spa and Cruise
*Kishiro Technologies
*ALG Waste Disposal
*Gas Miners Guild
*Junkers
*Cryer Pharmaceuticals
*Samura Industries

6. Please try to keep the members of each faction relatively equal. It makes things much more diverse.

7. Make sure to include your character's name, their ship, their base/system at the time of the post, and what faction they belong to.

And being the nice guy I am, I'll allow someone else to make the first post.

So let the games begin. ^_^

Name: Johnathan Faires
Ship: Humpback
Credits: 527,687
Location: Planet New Berlin
Faction: Freelancer

"Sidewinder fang." John said to the bartender. After he got his drink, he sat down at the table and watched the TV.
"In other news today, several Outcasts have been arrested in involvement with project 'Dara Dan Doah.' The operation to over through the Rheinland government.
The operation has been successfully eliminated and for the first time in the history of Rheinland, the Outcast problem is under control. After the commercial break... President Jacobi's proposal to lift the artifact ban in Liberty. More on this when we return."

By this time, he was already drunk. He had downed three fangs already, and was walking to the restroom.

After being in the restroom for almost an hour, and vomiting six times, he finally made his way back to the dining area. He spied a couple in the corner eating an expensive meal. And so he walked over to them and sat down next to the woman.
"So, how much this meal cost?" John said in a slurred voice, barley able to keep his head off the table.
"Who are you and what do you want, you stupid drunk?" the man said.
"Please, just go away. I just want to eat my meal in peace." said the woman.
John stared at her strangely, then without giving it a second thought, grabbed a shrimp- like creature off of the man's plate and ate it. He spat it right back out.
"Yuck, you actually like that stuff? Tastes like a gardienia's diarrhea. What's in that stuff anyway?" he said in his usual drunken voice. The bouncer stepped over to the table.
"This man bothering you Ms. Lilian Jean?" the woman just nodded.
Before John had a chance to react, the bouncer picked him up and carried him to the door. When they got there, he threw him into a pile of trash in the alleyway behind the bar. John gave the man the "middle finger" sign.

When he got to his ship, he thought for a moment. Should I just go home tonight, or make one last run to New Tokyo before calling it a night? Those diamonds would fetch a Good price. He climbed into his ship and turned on the engines, and headed to the commodity dealer.
