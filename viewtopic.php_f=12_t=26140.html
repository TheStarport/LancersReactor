no those are the system device controllers, not the actual physical slots on yer mobo. for god's sake don't fiddle with those in device manager!

when u open up ur pc, u will find a row of white slots about 4 inches long by 1/2 wide, those are ur PCI slots, next to them and slightly to the right and back may be a similar sized brown slot, thats yer AGP (u might have some long black ones over to the left, theyre ISA, forget about those. if u cant find the AGP slot then u will have to get a PCI graphics card that has an AGP chip on it, they do exist for those who aint got an AGP port. u'll have to tell ur BIOS to boot from PCI not AGP though if thats the case. this is a pre-assembled machine yeah? if its got a make and model number, let me know and i'll tell u what the score is, no problem i do this a lot!
