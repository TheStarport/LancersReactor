The Catalyst version 3.9 drivers may work with your graphics card , however ATI never supports IGP cards since those are made specifically for one notebook so it is a bit unfair to say ATI does not support your card , for support you will need to go to the manufacturer of your notebook and see if they have an upgrade

However you probably have a better chance to get freelancer running by
a. install directx 9b
b. see if there are any BIOS or MB driver upgrades for your notebook

If all else fails you could try installing a generic VESA driver (you will get penalized severly in preformance for that)
