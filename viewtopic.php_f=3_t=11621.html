Tue Mar 25, 2003 8:25 am by b4pro

More specifically, when at a parts dealer, look for the puzzle pieces in the upper left corner of each weapon you currently have equipped on your ship. The pieces will be put together if a weapon is equipped. If the pieces are separate and you have a free hardpoint, click on the puzzle pieces and the piece will equip itself. Don't feel bad, my friend had to call and ask me how to do it.
