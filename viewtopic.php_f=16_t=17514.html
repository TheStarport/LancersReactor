How to transfer Cash/cargo between your own characters ONLIN

Have Both characters go in to space outside a Safe station, away from moving traffic, in near enough the same space.

eg launch Character1 then press F1 to choose the change character, repeat the launch for Character2

Best I've found is under a station where no ship goes.

Then jettison the cargo etc from one of the characters, choose to switch characters while STILL in space and then tractor in the new character. Test with a single tonne first to make sure it works.

Found if you do it in front of docking rings etc then the cargo goes missing.

edit: ps only really an issue if you dont have someone willing to hold the cargo for you, but I dont trust anyone when good weapons are concerned
