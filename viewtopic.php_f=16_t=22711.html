Sherlog

Fri Aug 08, 2003 6:11 pm by Sherlog

The lootable wrecks show as red crosses on the nav map and they also appear on the solars list. If you target them in space they show a health bar and have a name (like 'Corsair Fighter' , 'Patrol 27' or 'Othori'). There are other wrecks and fragments that are simply there for scenery, and these don't have the aformentioned properties. In one of the eastern New London asteroid fields there is a spot with three or more of these relatively close together. Also, during mission 2 you pass at least one scenery fragment in the Silverton Asteroid Field in Colorado.

In some places there are clusters of lootable wrecks where most are duds and only a few yield loot (Omega-5, Omega-11, Omicron Alpha, Omicron Gamma).

Some of the wrecks can be destroyed completely but most are indestructible. Due to a game bug the wrecks 'forget' the damage you do to them and show a full health bar the next time you visit. A few wrecks even yield fresh loot every time ... (Omicron Beta) The reason is that the universe is created anew every time you launch or jump into a system and the game fails to track certain things in its state data (savegame).
