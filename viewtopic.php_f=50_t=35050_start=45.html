Er... well, yes, most questions that aren't obvious do get answered. If they're asked in the right place. Start a new thread in either this section (if you're pretty sure this is a beginner question) or in General Modding (if you think it might not be a common problem).

We don't get paid or anything to help you newbies, and there's precious little glory... so if you're "not impressed"... I'm sorry, but what part of "free help" do you not understand

I help because:

A. I can.
