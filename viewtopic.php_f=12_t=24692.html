King Kupalamalupa

Years ago in the Hawaiian lived King Kupalamalupa. He was the leader of a small village on the northern edge of the island. His village was like any other Hawaiian village, with all of the structures being grass huts. King Kupalamalupa's hut was no different in that sense, but it was different in that due to his stature and rank, his was a two-story hut. This was ironic, as though he was the only person in the village with a second story to his home, he was acrophobic, and would not spend much time upstairs, so he slept on the main floor of his home. King Kupalamalupa was also unique in his hobby - he collected thrones. In every trade dealing he had with a neighbouring village, he would ask that a throne be incuded as part of the deal; subsequently, he collected a large number of thrones. Since he used the main floor of his hut as his living quarters, he stored all of the thrones up on the second floor, which was fine for many years, until one night, when the aging floor finally collapsed under the weight of those many stone thrones, and they came crashing down, killing poor King Kupalamalupa in his sleep.

There's only two types of people who play Freelancer:
-Those who think the Sabre is the best ship;
-Those who are wrong.
