I'm very pleased that your problem sorted itself out. SL is a great game, in some ways better than FL imho, but it can be a little buggy at times, I had lots of ctd until I figured out the correct config for the game.

#1... Your saying That basically we have DX9 AND DX 7 or 8 installed at the same time?


it's very possible, although it isn't really a matter of two installs at the same time, it's an earlier version overwriting parts of a later version, and it happens quite a lot. Many installs include the DirectX redist and if this is an earlier version, it can automatically install itself and b*gger up your current version. Even I've inadvertently done it a few times, but it depends on the install routine.
