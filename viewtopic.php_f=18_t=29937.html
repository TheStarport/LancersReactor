that's all scripted data, and you have to manually "remake" everything in terms of writing the .thn scripts. You designate a ship hull (.cmp) then you have to addon engines, lights, and anything else you want on it in the specific .thn file. Then you have to make a new START_PATH_ANIMATION for the ship in the .thn. Though oyu might be able to just get away with changing the ships template_name="yourship.cmp," but you wlill have to go through and change the engines which are attached to it else the flames will be for that of another ship.
