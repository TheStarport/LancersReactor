republic gunship (model pics/previews)

so, ive moded for X Wing Alliance, and i decided, what the heck. why dont i do it for this game too? anyways, ive made a republic gunship model. right now there are no textures, it doens't work in game, etc. i made it in rhino using tri faces (i know what im doing, dont worry about the importing). i plan to get it into milkshape soon enough. however, i cant work on it again till next week. so, here are the links to the pics:

http://www.boomspeed.com/jango_sky/preview1.jpg
http://www.boomspeed.com/jango_sky/preview2.jpg
http://www.boomspeed.com/jango_sky/preview3.jpg

comments, suggestions, all welcome.

and by the way, i know i dont need to include guns into the model, but i did just to give you some idea of what it will look like. plus i might just edit some guns and shove em inside the ones you see. i plan to edit the stealthblade mkI in that case to be a beam weapon.

which brings up another thing. i removed two of the beam weapons on purpose, simply because i dont want the game to be totally unfair. im thinking of making the beams that i do have eat energy ridiculously fast. also, where in hell do i put engines on this thing? i cant infd any based on looking at other models, at the movie, or in Clone Wars for Xbox.

and yet another "one last thing." i am probably not including an inside to this to be more movie precise. it is too weird in space, because if i dont have glass over it, the lasers go through, and if i do, the lasers hit the glass, which remains intact, so its kinda weird. the poly count right now is 1900, which im pretty sure is ok.

so, comments, suggestions, tips, rantings, whatever. its all welcome
