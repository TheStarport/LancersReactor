Alrightie, last time we suffered from too many choices, too many options, and we got so focused into the semantics of it that we got no where. This is going to be a zombie story, but not in the traditional sense. I don't want the full bio when you start i want you to gradually show us your persona as the story folds out, like a book.

The story :
Humanity was on it's way to a new stage when the zombies first started. The world was stabilizing, and the environment was being treated with more respect then ever.
Then they came. Starting in china the virus quickly spread in a chain of refugees who were either uninformed or didn't care.
Soon the entire world was experiencing class 3 outbreaks on multipul fronts.
During this chaos however a change was slowly spreading as well.
People were walking along there normal days when they would find themselves having intiamate conversations with the plants around them, or picking up their coffe with their minds.
People were fearful of the changes however,
and people who had the ability were shunned. Slowly they began to realize that they were not welcome in society,
and there hide outs from the zombies became hidouts from "normal people"

With the world over run you are in one of the last remaining sanctuaries,
in a place where evolved and unevolved bicker over the food, where bandits roam and prey on the weak, where zombies mill about the world, where you are last, and only hope.

For the evolved:
Your person will start out in a refugee camp in northern canada first learning about their powers and how to control them.
Over time you will learn how to harness your strength and gain strength that can decimate entire armies, or you will fall prey to the menace and become humanities greatest fear.

For the unevolved:
Your person will start out in a refugee camp in northern canada learning how to deal with the life that you have been given.
You will learn how to fire a rifle, how to defeat a zombie in hand to hand combat, how to rally your allies, and how to lead an army. You will then be able to either push back the zombies
or you will fall prey to their onslaught and become one of the dead.
For both:
You must learn to cooperate or you will fall, the humans do not posess the firepower left to defeat the zombies and the evolved are the only thing that can hold a candle to previous technology,
however on the same token evolved numbers are small and they need the backing of the masses to recoup from using their might and protect them while performing time consuming powers.

The Facts
These are solanum zombies. Headshots will kill em but body wounds are no good.

Using your powers will drain your stamina.

I'm only going to allow 5 evolved right now at a first come first serve basis. Futher transformations will be done later in the storyline as i permit. you can however evolve midway through the game.

etc.
