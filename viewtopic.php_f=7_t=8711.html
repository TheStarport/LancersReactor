Ship I made

I wanted to contribute something to the comunity as I am a newcomer but I am not a good modler by any standard, the best ship I have is a kitbash, it's not that good but I like it. I've tried to make it look a bit better by upgrading the textured but it didn't look that good with mine, so if anybody wants to even it out a bit be my guest. Sorry, it's not that good but I felt I should have a go anyway.

It is meant to be a German medium fighter, aproximate to the Tempest or Patriot. It has 5 hardpoints, 2 colapser cannons and 2 messon guns (I was thinking of swaping them for medium lasers), it also has a rear firing turret. Although it has high firepower, it's energy is quicky depleated and it isn't very manuverable, some of the stats are up on the below site but I am still balancing it.

Please feel free to post comments and suggestions on both the ship and it's stats. I will try to improve it and if it is good enough at the end I will submit it - but this probibly won't happen because it's only a kitbash, I'm not skilled enough to make my own models.
