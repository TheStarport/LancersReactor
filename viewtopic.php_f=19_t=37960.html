IONCROSS Freelancer: Total War

We'll soon be announcing that the IONCROSS Freelancer: Total War mod is no longer BETA and "released" for public use.

Soon, we'll be doing one last player wipe, setting a "winning" score, and leaving the server alone for everyone to enjoy without having to fear endless crashes (though they are still possible) and the frequent player wipes that were required during the testing phase.

We set out to create the most advanced, challenging (and fun!) Freelancer mod in the world, and we are very pleased with the results.

If you haven't yet tried TW, please give it a shot. If you find regular Freelancer boring or too easy, this is the mod for you. If you don't like challenge, teamwork or only play games to become god-like in under an hour, stay away.

If you've tried TW but got sick of player wipes or lag, please, try it again after our release. The server is moving to the US (currently in Canada), which should provide better pings for everyone worldwide, and the hardware will also be upgraded to Intel's latest and fastest components.

The server is cheat-free, updates are automatic, and the economy is dynamic based on the progress of the war. IONCROSS Freelancer also has the most comprehensive player and house stats pages in the world. We have properly working ship upgrades, we were the first to implement player death loot drops, and the Total War death penalty adds a level of realism to the game that no other server can offer.

I want Total War to be the last mod you ever enjoy, and we plan to be around for a long time. I want the server to be full 24/7, and I've given the last two years to the Freelancer community in an effort to realize that dream.

Let's make it happen! When TW releases from beta, please, come help determine the outcome of the war

I hope to see you there in IONCROSS Freelancer: TOTAL WAR! (and if you're on the opposing team maybe you can kill me while you're at it)
