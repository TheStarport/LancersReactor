Argh

Mon Sep 19, 2005 4:24 am by Argh

1. Run FLSpew to see what errors are coming up when you run your mod. When zero errors come up, you're done (heh).

2. Run FLScan with your mod installed, and see what errors come up.

And... when all else fails, go back and hand-edit your INI. FLE is not perfect, and does not always do what you expect it to. It's a great program, but it's not a substitute for knowing how to hand-code INIs.
