Basically, when you start off in multiplayer, you're on Manhattan with a Starflier and $2000 (although these values can vary from server to server). There's no story, you go straight into freelancing. Missions are generally harder and pay more than in singleplayer, reflecting the fact that you can team up with other people to do them. You can trade items and money freely with other players, and obviously you can chat to other players. Those are the main differences, other than that it's mostly the same, ie there aren't extra systems or ships or anything like that
