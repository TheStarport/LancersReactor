Thu Jul 10, 2003 4:04 pm by Sherlog

The game data does not the turn rate of the ships since this is defined indirectly via values named torque, drag, and inertia. We don't know what the underlying mathematical model is and so it is difficult to get hard data ...

Turn rate is only one aspect of manoeuvrability, though. Inertia is also important. For example, Starflier and Drake have slightly better turning speed than the Patriot but noticably higher inertia. That is why the Patriot reacts much more snappily, which makes it very hard to hit and enables it to track enemies better.
