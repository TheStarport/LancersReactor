Fleeing combat game glitch...

OK, this is my first post and I'm not even certain what forum it should be in... oh well, here it is anyway.

I found what I think is a glitch that allowes you to run away from any combat with computer ships, which is pretty handy for freighters and some fights.

Basically, if you hit your afterburners and then cut your engines and swing around to face the enemies, you get away. I don't understand why, but they never catch you. Even though you are slowing down slowly and they should be going faster than you.

Now, I hypothesis that it could have something to do with the computer locking onto engine emissions, or losing touch with you and announcing the 'area is clear' or some such logic, but it seems like a glitch to me.

Has anyone else found this ? And would anyone on Elite be interested in helping me test this out with player controlled ships ?
