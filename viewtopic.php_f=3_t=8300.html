Lev Arris

Thu Mar 06, 2003 3:20 pm by Lev Arris

No, it just means that your cargo room is full and/or you are carrying the maximum amount of nanobots/shield batteries. Make some room, then you can tractor them again. One more thing: check how many units a floating cargo container holds because you need to have that many free cargo units in your ship to tractor the container.

Lev Arris

Thu Mar 06, 2003 8:33 pm by Lev Arris

That's true. There is a little trashbin icon on the upper right of the cargo window when you select a commodity. After clicking on it you can select how many units of this particular stuff you wanna jettison. It's not recommended to do this in the middle of a combat situation because the ship lowers the shields in order to jettison cargo. And no shields = bad news for your hull, wings and eventually weapons.
