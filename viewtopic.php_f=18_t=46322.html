The way to do it I think (haven't tried yet)

is to go into weapon_equip.ini and look for the weapon nicknames that are equipped on your ship. Once you locate them all paste this in each block of code that contributes to the weapon function:

phantom_physics = true

I think that should work but I haven't tried and it is unconfirmed whether it will be successful. For shields, equipment that are not in weapon_equip.ini then you just go into the ini's that store the information of the equipment that is on your ship and paste the line in the block of code associating with equipment that is on your ship.

I guess strafing on the ship you are using is not fast enough to work with the crusing speed that you have got from speed mod

I'll bet that if you unmount your shield, you'll be able to pass through everything. Just be sure that you have phantom_physics enabled for everything mounted on the ship - thrusters especially. Try unmounting the rest of it (ie, guns) so you don't have to worry about it.

Scanners, tractors and engines shouldn't need phantom_physics. But your ID cards probably will if they're coded to use shield hardpoints.
