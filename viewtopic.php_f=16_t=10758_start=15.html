Well, a "safe" route depends on who likes you.

There's a couple short routes I really like for a bit of quick cash.

Buy Alien Organisms at Ruiz base, Omicron Beta for 100
Take to Atka research station, sigma 17 and sell for 700
Buy Phameceuticals for 415 and return to Ruiz base
Sell Pharmeceuticals for 800.
Repeat

It takes just under 5 minutes travel each direction, and between Ruiz and Atka you only lose 2 units of Alien Organisms to decay.

So you end up with profit of approximately 985*cargo space in 10 minutes, and you can turn around and do it again if you want to.

Another good one is back and forth between Planet Malta and Freeport 9, cardamine one way and light arms the other. That takes about 4 minutes each direction, so its 8 minutes round trip for 800*cargo space for profit.

I've never timed the Diamond/Niobium run, so I don't know how much money per time that produces, but with a Humpback on that cardamine/light arms run, you average 25,000 credits per minute. Do 5 laps(40 minutes) and you've got a million credits in profits.
