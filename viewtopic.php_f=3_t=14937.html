Ah the question of the ages which ship is better?

Well lets see the first question to ask is what are you doing with it? Are you bounty hunting, Exploring, Trading, Pirating, working for the Police, Are you playing in SP or MP. All those things make a difference.

First if I was just after the thrill of the hunt and kill a Sabre fair handling, good power rating and yes the foreward facing turret does help.

Exploration then the Titan wins hands down as it has better shield/nanobots/hull strength for the radiation zones. The plus is she is a true VHF class it is slower on turns than the other VHF's but if you get jumped your chances of survival are better due to it's sheer strength.

Trading get the dromedary some would say the Humpback but she turns like a pig and kind of looks like one too. LOL Seriously the Dromedary has 285 cargo (unmoded) and turns pretty well for a freighter. Remember freighters are not fighters!

Pirate and Police work I would take the Eagle great manuverability and while the shield/nano hull deal is a little weak think about what you are attacking. You need to hit them hard get what you came for and escape with the loot.

Everyone has an opinion these are mine for what they are worth.

Just some observations from the edge of space!
