Fred the Dead

Wed Mar 01, 2006 9:12 am by Fred the Dead

It's not PC platform, but- an old Sega 32X game called 'Shadow Squadron'- six levels, but a fighter based storyline- you even get to choose your ship, a choice between a light but resuppliable ship, or a heavier ship that was far more durable. The enemy had it all- Carriers, battleships, destroyers, etc, and your job is to take 'em out. 3D universe, just not much to see..

The best part was the ship library- images of all the ships you see in the game including the ones in the 'cutscenes' and the stations, too. For the time, a good space sim and one I still play to this day when the mood hits. If you will, a 'short story' type of game
