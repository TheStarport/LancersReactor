1. Do not cheat. It is disgusting in this game. A first person shooter AFTER you have beaten it, maybe. I SAID MAYBE.

5. Hey, if I can beat it with out a cheat, any one can. I suck at these space flight sim type games.
6. Buy a new mouse. When I upgraded to an optical mouse, my game play got much better. I swear by the Logitech brand, but find one that suits you.
7. As was said before, you will feel a sense of accomplishment when you beat it without cheats.

Keep your chin up and keep trying.

The race really isn't that hard, once you get the hang of it. But you have to hit the cruise engines IMMEDIATELY or Hovis will have won the race before the first ring. If he gets a lead on you, it's all over. It's easier to beat Hovis on the second round, when that instruction panel doesn't pop up in front of you and slow you down by having to click it off. Also, try to position yourself so that you are almost aiming at the next ring by the time you enter the one in front of you. You don't have to go through dead centre.
