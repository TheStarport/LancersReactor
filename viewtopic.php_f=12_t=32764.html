A freind of mine runs Freelancer on a PCI TNT2 32MB card with the latest drivers. No joke.

Anyways, install SP1 for XP, update your video AND sound drivers. That 800Mhz P3 isn't helping, but it IS adequate - I've played on a 16-player server that was running a 600Mhz Celeron without too much diffculty.

Take apart your case and have a look inside. Clean it out with a can of air duster and a vacuum cleaner (take the job to someone else if you've never seen the inside of a PC before) if it looks dirty. Check your CPU heatsink and fan, make sure they're on tigtly and the fan is spinning freely. Give the fan/heatsink a few short blast with the air duster if it's dirty - you won't hut anything.
