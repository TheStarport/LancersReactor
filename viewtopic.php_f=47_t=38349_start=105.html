The Colony News!

The Kusari Union? : In the last few days, a series of pacts and agreements have been signed by most of the Kusari factions. Now, much of Kusari is under the Samura-led Ecomomic Union, and those in other houses are wondering about this. Shogun Bret and CEO Codename both met earlier today and agreed to another ecomonic union. That means that the current union in Kusari includes the house itself, Samura, the Hogosha, and Kishiro. What can come of this? More later about the Kusari Ecomonics.

IMG Raising Prices? : Today, Guildmaster Jacob of the IMG released a notice to all factions that he was establishing a price for all resource trading. This is obviously a response to the dozens of trade offers he has recieved, since the IMG is an eagle among pidgeons in that they are incredibly rich in mineral resources, and are the only faction with such vast stockpiles. The Guildmaster commented that the notice was to, "Make the other factions aware that we are not to be taken advantage of for our resources. They will either pay us our fair price or find another dealer, and there is no one else with our amounts of resources."

Sigma Troubles : The rumors have been proved true. In a stunning move last week, the Corsairs advanced into Sigmas 13 and 19 and took over the systems. They have now turned control over to Rheinland, and the IMG and Zoners have been given charters to construct bases in the system.

Liberty Struggling With Pirates? : Liberty has imposed a new policy on its piracy troubles, and it hasn't been very successful. Elusive groups like the Xenos, Hackers, Rogues, and Outcasts still are very active in both Liberty and the Independant Worlds, and the other houses have been jumping on Liberty to fix the problem. President Creator has started massive hunts for pirates and pirate bases, but it might be causing Liberty to fall behind both in income and military. As of yet, the organized crime elements in Liberty have been successful, but that all could change easily. At this point no one, be they pirates or policemen, sleeps well in Liberty.

That's all for this addition of the Colony News!
