Megistos

Tue Sep 21, 2004 8:21 am by Megistos

You want to learn as much as possible about Freelancer? Explore. If you see a base, go dock there. Talk to people in the bar, read what they have to say and take it all on board. Read info (F9) on various things you see while flying around. There's a ton of back story to the game that you can find. People will offer you information about secret bases and jumpholes. If you have the money, you may as well take them up on their offer, if you haven't already found it. Look in the Spoiler forum for many threads concerning events in FL's story. Good examples are the "8 sleeper ships thread" at the top and the Dallas Incident thread. If you want to know about anything particular, ask about it! There are a lot of good people on this site who'll be only to happy to help.
