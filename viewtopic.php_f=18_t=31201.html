Cloak(I know its a sore Subject but an intriguing Idea)

Like i said i dont mean to create trouble. I along with other modders know its secrets and how to create it, like i said i know its sore, so i will not reveal any information regarding it. also if this idea has been dicussed i didnt know about it.

My idea:

When it came out, we had rogue modders using the cloak without regard to other players in mind. We had several updates to it and then the Cloak detectors came out. I know getting it to work was a Feat in itself, but i had an Idea. Cloaks i think should be used in the game, BUT not without a counterpart. Sure having them shoot weps with no damage is nice, But how about be able to buy a cloak Detector. Now i realize that if you have the cloak available u can already see it, but what if we could change that all around and make it work Right.

So modders could purchase a cloak and a cloak detector. Now i think that would make the game fair. This is probably way outlandish seeing as it took us months to get the original cloak out, but i think its time again that we attempt to get it right.

Anyone who feels they want to join in the effort just say so, i'm willing to work with only a few people and that being the more knowledgable one thats have been around a while, that being to keep it as secretive as possible, and not letting it get into the wrong hands as it once did before. Also as added precaution if it were to be released it would be with that of an encryption, so it couldnt be opened and misused.
