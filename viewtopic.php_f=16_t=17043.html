Mon May 19, 2003 7:01 pm by Peachhead

With a Humpback (250 units):

Artifacts at Crete in Omicron Gamma to New Berlin=212,000 profit, Light Arms from The Ring back to Crete=262,500 profit (475K profit round trip). I don't know how long it takes, but it's only about 4 or 5 jumpholes/jumpgates either way.

If you're carrying Light Arms you can use tradelanes in New Berlin and Stuttgart, then jumpholes in Omega 11 and Omega 41. With Artifacts use jumpholes, go through Dresden instead of Stuttgart, and come in below Planet New Berlin. I've never gotten scanned until after I drop off my cargo in New Berlin.

For this route you have to friendly or neutral with the Corsairs, and it's nearly impossible without being friendly or neutral with the Red Hessians. And after you make a run or two you trade in the Humpback at Crete for a Titan.
