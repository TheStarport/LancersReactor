Actually, I think Galileo is pretty nice-looking when you're not inside any of the clouds (though there wouldn't be a point in being in that system if it weren't for the hideous greyish brown clouds).

I think the only Omega system I find appealing is Omega 3, and in Rheinland, Frankfurt and New Berlin have beautiful markings in their nebulae.

And I can't believe I forgot about the upper Unknown system, that one's gorgeous, and the other Unknown system is gorgeous, as well, but I'm always scared of it for some reason -- maybe because of that black hole... Or maybe because Planet Primus is run by damn dirty apes!

Omicron Gamma is another system rich in gorgeous bright colors, and one of the best-looking suns in Sirius.

I just love traveling in the Omicron systems, because they arouse a sense of freedom and yet also a pleasing melancholy and loneliness that the other systems don't come close to.
