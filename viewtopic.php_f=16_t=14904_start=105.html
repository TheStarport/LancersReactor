OK, I've sorted out my sheet now. Basically you have 7 cells. 6 guns, 1 turret.
If you want to use less you just leave some blank (And this won't work for Freighters Im afraid)
Each cell has a drop down list, and you pick your gun.
The sheet then gives you a summary:
Total energy output (Pershot that is)
Energy used per sec
Total Shield Damage per sec (Assuming 100% accuracy)
Total Hull Damage per sec (Assuming 100% accuracy)
And wether or not all speed of projectiles match.
Should come in handy as it allows you to see flaws in your weapon set up design. I'm emailing it onto Nickless to upload if he would be ever so kind, replacing the current one.
