Freelancer Hakiu/poetry project

First things to do is give credit where credit is due. "Free Trader Grendal" posted in the "best Freighter" thread. In his post he wrote the following Hakiu.

"See my Clydesdale go,
New York Lux goods to Tokyo.
Now I'm paid-- hookers please!"

"Gee, my Clydesdale looks
awesome. A shell for a hull
and twin engines too."

I added my two cents to the thread and stuck in my own Hakiu. Dealing with my freebooting Dromeadary.

"Dark tradelane lurker
My camel drinks deeply
Dying transports my Oasis."

All fine and good but I am willing to bet that the rest of the fourms have a Hakiu or an Ode rattling about their insides trying to get out. So here's your chance.
