Wed May 03, 2006 10:53 am by Cold_Void

the UTF editor(see the stickied utilities thread) - you'll also need nvidia DDS plugin to generate your new DDS files, its designed for photoshop but if its installed correctly(directory name differs from PS' "plug-ins" to PSP's "plugins" folders)it works with paintshop too.one more basic, DDS files are named .tga in the texture library, so the way to distinguish them is that true targas are always in a MIP0/1/etc node whereas DDS is in a MIPS node because it contains all its own mipmaps. also, making trents hair physically longer isn't possible, although you could make a helmet like wig for him - but, you probably meant lengthening its texture
