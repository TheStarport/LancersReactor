My favorite trading loop is one that runs from the outer edge of Kusari to the lower reach of Liberty. It is a loop, so you can jump into it from almost anyplace you are in Sirus:

Planet Manhattan > Luxury Consumer Goods > Planet Honshu
Planet Honshu > Engine Components > Freeport 4 Magellan
Freeport 4 Magellan > H-fuel > San Diego Border
San Diego Border > deadhead empty > Planet Los Angeles
Planet Los Angeles > Gold > Manhattan

Here is the sweet part. That loop will earn you 2,121 credits profit per unit of cargo. Flying a Rino - you will make almost $170,000 per trip. In a Dromedary - can you use more than $583K?

It goes through fairly safe country, and doesn't really take all that long to fly.
