I actually spent big bucks bribing the pirate groups so I could by a Titan and a Sabre, and came up with the following comparisons.

Eagle has lightest armor, good maneuverability, can only fire its turret forward at extreme angles.

Sabre has better armor, best offense (turret points forward), but is a bit sluggish in turns.

Titan has worst offense, since its turret faces backwards, best armor and repair capacity. It's armored like a tank, and flies about as well as one too. TURN! TURN!

So, in SP, I have an Eagle w/4 Nomad cannons (they do a little more damage than the blasters - 1%) cannonball and firestalker launcher, level 10 shield, adv. thruster, etc. etc., and I eventually got bored and started MP.
