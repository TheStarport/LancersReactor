njhockeyboy

Thu Jun 30, 2005 12:19 am by njhockeyboy

To find minable areas, check your Navmap with the "minable zones" filter on. Any areas with a brown background are minable. These are usually asteroid or debris fields.

To actually mine, go to a minable area and shoot the small rocks that are drifting around. Your crosshairs will turn white and start spinning whenever you point at one of these rocks. You won't get something everytime you shoot a rock though, so you can spend a lot of time for very little profit. Much better to just do missions for various groups.

Hope this helps.

Thoroughly explore particle fields, especially large ones inhabited by crimnals. When you encounter an apparently random radiation area, it's generally a wreck. Additionally, following your cruise, scan your navmap for Red X's, which represent wrecks.
