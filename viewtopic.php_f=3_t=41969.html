To make friends with any factions, become hostile with their enemies. So who does that mean for the Corsairs? Well, The Mollies (easy to beat), the Bounty Hounters (tougher and you'll have to fight them everywhere you go) and the Red Hessians (tough until you get a decent ship).

If you want to fight the Corsairs' enemies, you'll probably need a powerful ship such as the Valkyrie, Hammerhead, Eagle or Sabre. And if you've got one of them, who really cares a fruitcake about getting a Titan anyway?
