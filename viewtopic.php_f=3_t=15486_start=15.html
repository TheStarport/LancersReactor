After the SP campaign, I was flying an Anubis. I embarked on few high risk combat missions to get my cash reserves up and save up for a Humpback. After I bought it, I did few trade runs inside the Rheinland and Bretonia systems. After getting some more money for good CL. 6 and 8 turrets and Cl.7 shields I went to Freeport 9 in Omicron Theta and bought a full load of Alien Organisms for $400 a tonne. After that I just hauled my ass through the two Omega systems (Omega-41 and -11) to Dresden system, and from there it was a piece of cake to get to California Minor to sell the cargo for $2000 a tonne. Nice profit of around $380,000. Of course Alien Organisms is a delicate cargo so as soon as I entered radiation areas of Omega-41 they started dying. By going full throttle I lost maybe 10 organisms throughout the whole trip. Sure, it's almost $20,000, but it doesn't seem like a lot of money when your bank account increases this much. Now, you know the advantages of this trip, so you might ask, "what are the cons?" Well, wild hordes of Corsairs and Hessians stand between you and your destination. A remedy? ****load of saving and lots of patience. One time they might kill you, but the other you might escape. It's all up to you.

Fri Jun 20, 2003 8:50 am by gus

Ok, here's *the* way to make money:

After you've completed the SP campaign, go to Rochester. Kill any rogue ships, and then start camping outside the docking ports.

Every once a while, a CSV will pop out. It will be carrying 79999 of a commodity.

So, if it's something valuable, such as Cardamine, Artifacts, Diamonds, Gold, kill it and pick it up, and go to Manhattan to sell it. A full Dromedary load of Cardamine will net you 412500 credits.
