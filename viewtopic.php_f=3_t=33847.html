Best looking Freighter contest.

To my surprise, the "Best looking ship contest" seems to be going rather well. Now, my idea is to do the same basic thing, except this time, we narrow the field to freighters alone.

Remember, this is based on LOOKS ALONE, not how agile it is, or how powerful its weapons are, or even the size of the cargo hold. If it were, we'd be fighting over Humpbacks and Dromedaries all day. If you like, you CAN factor in the position of the turrets/guns on the hull and their felds of fire, as this is directly related to hull form.

Before I go on, if mods feel this is redundant, feel free to lock it. I won't object.

Now, I'm going to do this slightly differently than the other thread. Everyone gets ONE post to vote. No coming back to vote for something else. I don't condone "best ship/freighter" threads, but if you would like to discuss them otherwise, I won't object. Keep it civil and I hope the mods won't either.
