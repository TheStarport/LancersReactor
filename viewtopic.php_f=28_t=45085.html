Known Space Mod

I have an idea for a mod (which I have begun work on). I have a universe which I have made which I like to call Known Space, based around a group of alien species, which I created. I have always thought it would be cool to have a mod which would allow me to fly one of their ships, or land on their planets, or whatever.

Recently I acquired a CG program which I have realized is perfectly capable of exporting the 3D files in such a format that Milkshape can import them. I have begun work on the ships, and I hope to possibly publish this as a working mod at some point. I don't know what I would call it, but I do know that I would like to create this as a total conversion to Known Space, seeing as it's a big setting and that it might not be possible to add enough systems to make it realistic along with the original systems.

Unfortunately, I have no scripting skill whatsoever for making of systems or planets or whatever. I can make the ships, but in order for this to be a reality I would like someone who could make all fifty-sixty something systems, or at least even just a reasonable amount of those, plus the governments and weapons. I would provide a map (shown her) and descriptions of the planets, stations and likewise, where they go, what kinds of weapons the governments use, and anything else anyone might need for this.

Of course, I could also just make this a ship pack, but that wouldn't be any fun, would it?

There are some things that nanobots can't repair. For everything else, there's Mastercard!
