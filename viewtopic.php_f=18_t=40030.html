Thu Jul 21, 2005 5:38 am by Mace_166

In your windows folder there is a program called sndrec32.exe, if you open it it is a very basic sound recorder and quality encoder.

Pop ur file in there, if its cd quality its most likely 44100, stereo, 192 kbs or something similar. They seem to class cd as being between 160 - 192 kbs..
Anyway, Go to file> properties> convertnow and change the format to mpeg layer 3.
Thats the format that freelancer uses, then pick the quality.
I use 24000, stereo, 56k kbs. I think normal freelancer is 22050 kbs, stereo, 56 kbs.

Its amazing how small the file becomes and the quality isn't reaaly affected too badly. It is certainly playable and during the game you wouldn't notice at all.

A normal CD quality wav is approx 40 meg, using the mpeg layer 3 its more like 1.5 meg. Making your cd player thing a possibility..
