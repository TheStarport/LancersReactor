zlo

Sat Sep 04, 2004 6:55 am by zlo

That's easy - ignore the orders and deal with the fighters/kurges first. Second, go to each of the power cores and fire at them. You won't destroy them, but they'll need only a few shots later. Then go to the research facility and deal with the turrets. Next, locate the Gamma as soon as they jump in and run to them on your afterburners. you'll see a Kurgen jumping in. Destroy it ASAP (screamers are best IMO). Even if it manages to destroy one of the Gamma, the mission is still go. After that, afterburn to the warp gate, wait till the shileds are down (i.e. the research facility is destroyed) and finish off the power cores. You'll have a clas one Coalition carrier toasted for dessert. Good luck
