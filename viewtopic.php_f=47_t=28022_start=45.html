Thu Feb 12, 2004 10:37 am by Orillion.

Time: Nine days after the Rheinland invasion
Location: Alaska system

The ride to Alaska had taken two hours, under that time i had got some rest, and inspected the weapons and missiles on the ship. I had jumped out pretty close to two stations, where one was under construction. So, Liberty where working on something here. But what?
One of the stations where looking like pretty much like a big rock, and a second one was built with one big platform, where you could dock, and some kind of gas that was pouring out of holes on a ring that went round and round. I tried to hail the rock-like one, wich was under construction. Nothing. Then i tried to hail the second one. From there i got an encrypted message:
"SOS! SOS! Failing. Oxygen emptyj.5mg,dkdjdjxkxja.fe5,fkdf"
Something was very wrong, and i flew as fast as i could to the station. Fourtnatley, the docking computers where online, so i could get in. I landed my Dagger in one of the hangers, and got out. It was creepy outside the ship, and the whole station smelled... death. To be secure, i took a oxygen tube form the ship, and a very heavy laser rifle. Then i stepped over to the Traders section. Nothing. When i got to the Ship Dealer, i saw a very nice Sabre.
"Neat!" i said, and smiled. I first looked around the ship, after bombs and such devices. Nothing. I started the ship, wich was fully functionall, and got back to my own ship, and loaded everything on it onto a hover transport. Then i loeded it back into the new ship, wich was much more comfortable, and had three seats. It even had a combined sleeping room and toilet and a very small kitchen, with some food. It also had two rifles in the cockpit. Could be usefull. I took the heaviest one of the two rifles, and got to the Bar of the station. In there, several people was hanging from the roof, dead. I saw a young man, and a women, and even a little girl, that not oculd be more than six or seven years. Who the hell did something like this? I was about to walk out of there, when i rememered the two adults hanging dead. Trent and Juni. I couldn't be true. Trent had retired after the Nomad Incident. He had retired to Manhattan, where he had a recidence. And the girl looked a little like both of them. I walked around them, and saw a projector in the back of them. They where just holograms... But who wanted to project pictures of Trent and Juni, hanging n the roof of a station, in a locked and unknown system?
