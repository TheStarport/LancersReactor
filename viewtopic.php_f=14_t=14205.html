I do not believe a Freelancer Clan section is something we should have on here for two reasons...

1. Clans would be server specific (by design).
2. Clan members come and go like the wind.

1 good point for this would be:

1. One place for clan memebers to check in with each other (although, in my experience, this invites some language that is not acceptable on this website)

I think this site is becoming enough of a mess to read with the numbers of forums we have already. As long as it's a link to another website I don't see this idea adding to the clutter. But another forum just for clans is just going to end up being a section that just gets in the way of the other 2731723598715 forums in place today.

Don't get me wrong, clans are fun and they provide a vital player base for games. With the talent that is out there, can't we just link user created clan web site and call it a day?
