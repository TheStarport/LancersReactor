the PK killing vote is not absolute. It is easily changed from one to the other. We'll listen to what people have to say as it goes along, so relax a little. This vote is only to determine a starting point. Where we go from there is guided by the community.

We probably will mix things up a bit from time to time anyway, (variety is the spice of life you know) but we have to determine where we begin. I will tell you this much, my personal TLR server I will most likely do this in the beginning:

Turn off PK during the weekend allowing players to help each other build characters up, ask questions, spread their own wings and generally get the feel of MP. During the week I'll turn it on to let people try out their strengths against each other to again, get the feel for what life might be like in the big server. Then maybe switch it from time to time. I actually envision my server to be a sort of training grounds for people so they can go into the big Elite server with some confidence in what they're doing. They'll be better prepared to go into the deep water and enjoy the wide open spaces with others without necessarily having to be burdened with the need to ask about ever little detail. Seeing a server with or without PK turned on might become a big issue.

Pipe dream I'm sure.
