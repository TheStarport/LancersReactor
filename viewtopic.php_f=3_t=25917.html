cardam|ne

Thu Nov 06, 2003 12:50 pm by cardam|ne

On a planet/base go to the equipment dealer, and scroll down till you find the weapon(s) you want to mounbt. You must have a free weapon slot before proceeding past this point.

Once you have a free weapon slot, look at the icon on the left of the gun. If the weapon is unmounted, there will be two bits from a jigsaw that are not joined. The jigsaw pieces will be blue if you have a free weapon slot. Click the jigsaw pieces and you will hear a noise, nothing major, its just hard to describe lol. The weapon will then be mounted.

I had the same problem, and I let my m8 borrow the game and he discovered it. It saves selling a Class 10 Weapon, for example CEREBUS, for 36k, then buying it back for 120k.

Hope this helps,

Puke Barwalker

Thu Nov 06, 2003 3:16 pm by Puke Barwalker

Just make sure your ship can handle the guns you pick up. Sooner or later, you'll be tractoring in some guns which are too powerful for your ship. Also look at the gun mounts. They may be of different levels ... say two mounts of maximum Level 2 and two of Level 3.

If you get some really good higher level guns, hang onto them for later in the game when you'll be able to buy more powerful ships to use them. When you sell a gun, you only get 1/3d -- I think -- of the price it sells for.
