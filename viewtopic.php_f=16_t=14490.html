Hmm, that's strange. I found Valkyrie to be one of the best HF next to the falcon (obviously due to its weapon load out).
Also, I thought the Bruschal Base only sold the Stiletto? The

Well, the quickest way to get the rep fixed for Sabre/Stilleto is get to CA > Magellan gate and start blasting away at Liberty Police until your rep with them returns to about a nuetral state. (This will naturally bring your rep w/ Lane Hackers and Outcast from hostile to border-line non-hostile).

Go to Lane Hacker's base in Magellan and start trafficing the Cardamine drug w/ Dromedary using the Magellan > New York jump hole until you got enough money (Lane Hacker's base > Planet Manhattan: 217250 for single jump trade means you'd end up with a million credit in an hour or less)

After you've acquired enough sums of money to get yourself a Sabre or Stiletto, (in this case, most likely Sabre first), head to Rogue's Buffalo base in New York and purchase yourself a dagger, and head to Omicron Alpha through Magellan > Leeds > route ( You could purchase Sabre in Hessian base, but the considering Omicron Alpha allows you to acquire 4 lvl 10 weapons, and possibly Nomad weapons in the same area, it would be much faster to just go to Omicron if you're looking quickly outfit the Sabre.)

After reaching Omicron Alpha, buy the Sabre, kill some Bounty Hunters in those 100k+ missions, pick up the goodies in a radiation field and you've gotten yourself a fully stocked Sabre.
