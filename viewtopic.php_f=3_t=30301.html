Campaign w/ Freighters--oddities?

I'm currently playing through the campaign while limiting myself to only freighters. At the moment I've got a Rhino, though I hope to soon have a Mule.

Here's my problem. I've noticed a few things that seem to be operating differently than when I fly as a fighter in the campaign. Has anyone else noticed these?

1) I level up slower. A LOT slower. I'm at level 4, and I've flown perhaps a dozen missions and haven't gotten to level five yet. I don't remember it taking this long with a fighter.

2) I can't befriend the Junkers or the Rogues. I remember that the best way to do this is to fly missions against Xenos--at least when I was flying a fighter. But now the Junkers and Rogues progressively hate me more and more with each mission I fly, even if those missions are against the Xenos.

Has anyone else noticed this? Is it my imagination, or does your net value and your alliances develop differently when you're flying as a freighter?
