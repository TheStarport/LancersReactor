I have been running a Merchant Guild in MP Freelancer for about 4 months now, on my third server (the other two shut down).

If you are really interested in running as a Merchant, want to learn how to avoid pirates (not the NPC kind, the PvP kind), wanna fly wing on a convoy, or just plain are interested in having some good old fashioned fun, then:

Click here for the Allied Exports Forum (separate from our Server's Forum).

Click Here for the Excelcia Role-play server where we play.

Click here to find where you need to go to get the latest mod.

After you're done clicking, PM me on either forum (Excelcia Forum / Allied Exports Forum) and ask for more information.

We currently have three "jobs" available inside Allied Exports, and here is a quick listing:

Convoy Merchant : We current have two long term, exclusive contracts to deliver Luxury Foods to the Luxury Linere Hawaii (Sigma 19) and raw materials & components (Berylium, Optronics, & Niobium) to The Ring (New Berlin). The Convoy Merchant is KEY to the success in this role. Pirates are everywhere, from the Red Hessians, Unioners and Bundschuh in Rhienland, to the Outcasts and Corsairs in the Sigmas, and the Yamato Damashii Yakuza "YDY", Liberty Rogues "/-LR-/", Fringe Raiders "Raider_", and/or United Marauders of Sirius "UMoS". They don't matter. All that matters is getting that cargo safely to the destination and reaping the benefits of doing so.

Convoy Escort Pilot : While convoys are essential to the successful fulfillment of our contracts, the Convoy Escort Pilot is both the shield and sword of the Convoy. Your duty is the protection of the freighter, at any cost, even your life. The cargo must get through, and you are there to ensure that it does. Although you are the muscle here, you are not expected to fly for free. You are entitled to 25% of the profit from each Freighter Captain you successfully escort alon the way. Bring your determination and martial spirit, this job demands it.

Mercenary : Allied Exports has a wing of Pilots without scruples, who are elite, excel at the hunt, pay attention to detail, and know the value of maintaining discretion. The Mercenary doesn't care about anything else other than completing the assignment and collecting the pay for doing so. With pirates hitting freighters, political intrigues, and blood fueds abounding, there is no shortage of work available for the dedicated Mercenary. In times of war, the Mercenary is a gun for hire, capable and willing to engage the enemy at any price. Think you have what it takes? Step up to the plate and find out.

Wanna learn more? Come visit the server, contact me (AE-GP_BLACK_PEARL) or anyone in Allied Exports (AE) for more information. Make sure that you ask about our generous sign-on bonus.

Victory, a true victory is not merely crushing your enemy, pounding it into submission. No, my friend, true victory comes after the battle, when your enemy is convinced that they were wrong to oppose you in the first place.
