Puke Barwalker

Mon Apr 17, 2006 4:29 am by Puke Barwalker

I mentioned on TLR long ago one of my favourites -- HardWar. It came out about 6 years or so ago and is a lot like Freelancer, only it takes place on Titan, one of the moons of Jupiter (the one astronomers have found has water, ice etc on it).

It has an alien mystery and you start out in a basic ship, which you have to make money to upgrade. You get given missions periodically, which help you solve the mystery. The colonists on Titan have been cut off from earth for centuries, and have divided into rival factions.

It's a steal on eBay these days. Comes on 2 CDs. The main game loads on to your HD, whilst the CDs in the drive contain the live actors who provide you with missions, tips etc.

Sadly, like FL, there was no sequel to HardWar, which was meant to have some. Seems most folks don't go for this type of game. I remember when FL came out and a poster complained bitterly when he finished the SP game and discovered nobody told him exactly what to do anymore. He said he was returning the game and getting his money back!
