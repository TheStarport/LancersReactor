Simplicity itself:

Simply open up freelancer.ini and look for this section:
[Startup
movie_file = movies\MGS_Logo_Final.wmv
movie_file = movies\DA_Logo_Final.wmv
movie_file = movies\FL_Intro.wmv

input wahtever movies you want to play there (I assume they have to be in wmv format) or stop them from playing by putting a ; infront of them.

Changing the planets in the menu screen involves editing a system, and is covered at the link you posted.

We have been playing with this some ourselves and what mykrul999 said about the movies is right on. Once you have edited the ini for them make sure and place the movies named as you put in the ini in your DATA\MOVIES folder. The pic problem sounds similar to a mistake we made when first trying this which is. The size has to stay the same and the new file has to be saved as the same bit depth as the origional or else it will not work.
