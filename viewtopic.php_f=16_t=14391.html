Haenlomal

Thu Apr 17, 2003 10:00 pm by Haenlomal

Champion and Adv. Champion Shields are lvl 9 and 10 Graviton shields respectively. They are defined in the game .ini files.

However, no shop sells them, and ships never drop shields (because they're really using some other shield type..but that's another matter altogether). So bottom line is that there is no way to get Adv. Champion Shields in the game unless you mod your files.

FYI, the maximum level Graviton Shield you can get in the game is the Lvl 8 Adv. Protector Shield. However, you cannot buy it in any store...I'll leave it up to you to discover how you can get one.

For Molecular Shields, it's Lvl 9 (Bulwark Shield, I think), and for Positron Shields, lvl 10 (mentioned above). These latter ones you can buy in shops...if you can find the right shops.

-- The Haen.
