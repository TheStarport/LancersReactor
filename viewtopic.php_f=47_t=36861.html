Aces of Siruis RPG Signups

Aces of Siruis is an RPG about squadrons and individual cap ships. Instead of controlling and individual member to a whole government, you control an army unit. You develop specific characters and you defend you region of space Eventually all the different units will merge into one storyline as told by different units.

Anyways the combat system will be standard rp based. You describe your attacks, the enemy posts their damage and counters. You can attack NPC's also within reason or request the GM me or assitant GM who will be appointed later, to RP your enemy.

If your a member of a house you can choose the following, they can also choose to serve at their own unique base or at an existing in game base:

Battleship Commander:
1 Battleship, 4 Heavy Fighter Wings (4 fighters each)

Cruiser Group Commander:
3 Cruisers, 8 Light Fighter Wings

Gunboat Squadron Commander:
6 Gunboats
2 Heavy Fighter Wings
4 Light Fighter Wings

If you are a Pirate, Zoner, or BHG Commander you control:

Fighter Squadron Commander:
3 VHF Wings
or 6 HF Wings
or 12 LF Wings

Smuggler's can opt to replace any number of wings with CSV's

If you are a merchant you control:

3 Trains
2 Armoured Transports
4 LF Wings

All groups will recieve equal chances to make an impact on the story line. We need all groups, merchants can be heros and if they do well. Rewards for missions will be awarded on merit and some can be very nice.

What you need:

A character profiles and what type of unit your commanding and for who. Here is an example of what is needed, this will be mine. Each profile can be unique, but a description of your unit and commander would be nice.


Unit Name: Corsairs '32nd Squadron: Black Jackals'
Symbol: An anubis head symbol painted onto the side wings of the ships
Description: The Black Jackals model themselves after Anubis they decide who is just to live and who is not. At least that is their belief. They are an incredibly superstitious squadron. They do not pirate convoys carrying what the deem soft goods. Rather they hit those carrying hard goods, this includes niobuim, gold, gems, and light arms. They like the ancient egyptian god envision themselves holding a scale and measuring what souls should be devoured and what should be allowed to live.
Unit Base: Alexandria Base (located in Magellan)
Unit Force: 3 VHF Titan Wings
Commander Name: Triyun Kun
Description: The commander of the squadron is a man who has served for nearly 20 years in the service of the Corsairs and is coming to the end of his piloting days. His unit is currently in a pretty cushy spot between Liberty and Bretonia taking their booty. His units main competition is the Lane Hackers, but he has yet to discover the location of their base. Most of his supplies he has to steal because of their position so far from the main Corsair supply bases.
