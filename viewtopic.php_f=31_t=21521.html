Sat Jul 26, 2003 2:54 am by reynen

What type of computer are you using. I run a very fast computer and FLMM took about 1 1/2 minutes to install it the first time.

Once you download the file with .zip.flmod, double click and it will install to FLMM, IF you are on a slow computer it can take up to 10 min. Uncompressed the expansion is about 90 meg and it takes awhile.

Once it finished a little thing will pop-up and say "such and such mod installed sucessfully" After that FLMM will open and you will see the expansion and then click on it and click activate.

It sounds like you never let it finish installing to FLMM and got impatient. Delete the current one in FLMM and reinstall it to FLMM and BE PATIENT.
