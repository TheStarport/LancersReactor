Though I haven't had to personally deal with cheaters since about 4 months ago now, I do know how bad it can be for more servers out there, especially unmodded ones.

If you really want to control your cheating problem, then go to a password and offer the information of that password on a forum that you control through a zip or txt file. Or if you feel you have enough players on your server, disable new accounts and start filtering out the bad weeds that way.

All of these anti-cheat files and programs do work to an extent, but they also have been known to auto-ban people that weren't cheating at all. Every program has a flaw, and eventually someone who has nothing better to do that find a way to cheat on your server, will figure it out and cheat anyways......
