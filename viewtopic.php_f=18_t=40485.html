Even if someone did have a copy of the beta, it's no use just having a copy of the interface file. The ini files in Freelancer are, at best, simple configuration files that provide information to the main executable - that's what does all the work. A copy of the beta executable would be more use, but that would still require dissassembling and would probably still not reveal how to do it.

Unfortunately it's not just a case of adding a few magic lines to an ini file, otherwise (As SWAT said) we would have done it by now.
