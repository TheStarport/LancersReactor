nuce

Sun Feb 23, 2003 1:14 am by nuce

Hi everyone!

I was having missing sounds in pr-beta build 1131 and now that I got the
demo I found out that majority of the sounds were missing again.

After tweaking the codecs, installing patches and things like that,
I found someting unusual.

So, if you have missing sounds, check this

Control Panel->Sounds And Multimedia
Select Hardware-sheet
Select from the list Audio Codecs and click Properties (Double click)
Select Properties-sheet
Doubleclick Fraunhofer IIS Mpeg Layer-3 Codec
Check that "Use this audio codec" is checked!

For reasons unknow it was "Do not use this audio codec".

After that all sounds are working!
