Taking the little ones to their fullest!

I've seen a few examples of real VHF-killer loadouts on low-end ships, like Adv. Stunpulses and high-end mines on a Patriot and similar things. What are some of the loadouts you like to use on the really low-power ships in the game, IE Starflier, Patriot, Cavalier?

Right now I'm using a Patriot on a few MP servers with three Barrager 1s (amazing deal, as it's a class-2 that outclasses a class-3 Drail) and an Advanced Stunpulse. The agility lets it work quite well in all the House systems, though I have yet to venture into the more irradiated areas of space with it.

I never use mies or missiles, as my fighting style gives the mine a roughly 50-50 chance of hitting me instead of an enemy, and missiles are just too expensive and easy to evade.

When I'm in a Dagger the loadout is quite similar - one Stupulse Turret, two Barrager 1s and two Barrager 2s. Those Junkers may be scum, but they make some nice low-class weapons.
