Wed Jul 21, 2004 9:53 am by OutKast

Generally this is a problem with the 1.1 patch, especially the mid campaign issue. The way to solve this is to make a seperate Freelancer directory without the 1.1 patch and play from that one for singleplayer. For those of you without sufficient diskspace for two FLs, your best bet is to reinstall every time you want to play singleplayer. Just make sure that patch is activated when you play on a server becuae without it, you cause massive lag for the other players.
