Meet the factions
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The New Europe Navy (NEN)
--------------------------------
The NEN main task is protect the New Europe sytems from nomad attacks, secondairy tasks are to secure NE borders, combat the Trix (a new unlawfull faction) & give assitence when needed.

The New Europe Police (NEP)
----------------------------------
The NEp is responsibale for the safty of all NE citizens, they mainly fight of the Trix & other criminal factions that recently snuck into the NE systems.

New Europe Space Authority ( NESA)
-------------------------------------------
NESA owns all tradelanes, all jumpgates & allmost every civilian base within NE.
NESA is neutral to all factions besides the Trix & nomads & will only respond in force against those who threathen its infrastructure.

The Industrial Steel & Mineral Reforming (IS&MR)
------------------------------------------------------------
The IS&MR is a commercial faction that operates within the NE systems, they have succesfully reengineered nomad technology & are working on several applications.
One of wich is the capability to produce high quality metals from scrap.
They allso work on weapons & thrusters systems.
The IS&MR has recently allied itself with the Bounty Hunters Guild in order to protect there shipments from their bases.

They recently started operations on the planet Denver.

The Trix (Trix)
-------------------
Allmost nothing is known about them, there based in the New Italy system, have no specific goals & are only neutraal to a handfull of unlawfull factions.
