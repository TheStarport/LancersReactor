Liberty is useless!

Hey everyone, this is one of my first posts on the forum. I've had FL since it's been released, and I have completed the SP game and am now exploring around the universe. However (sadly), I find little to no motivation to return to Liberty. I play my character as a fighter, not a trader, so there is no use returning to the Liberty system (or even Bretonia, for that matter) because of the extreme obselete equipment, ships, and missions they have there. I know that DA made an idea to make the systems "stepping stones," with one system upgrading in tech and difficulty from the next, but I think it really ruins the game after you get done the campaign.

Are there any mods out there to "update" the Liberty and even Bretonia systems to be on par with the rest of the universe? The Defender is still one of my favorite ships - but of course I can't fly it if I intend on visiting the Border Worlds.
