Mission types:
-Transport escort missions for corporations
-Hijacking missions for criminal groups
-Cardamine and Artifact smuggling for outcasts and Corsairs
-Transport missions for corporations (they pay you to haul stuff)
-Border raids (E.G. Liberty navy sends you to rheinland to kill a cap. ship)
-Bounty Hunters board with a list of most wanted criminals, stipulations to capture(alive or dead) the bounty posted, and where to take them to collect

Creating Factions:
Let players pay to have a station built (say 50-60 mil.). Start a faction there that players only can join. All weapons and commodities must be brought there by faction members (Piracy of course). Of course the faction would set the prices on everything. Players bringing items to the station get half of the price of the goods when they sell them. Imagine a place to buy black market CODENAME: weapons! Or also equipment requiring a rep with criminals. Imagine a base in NY where noobs could buy Drails, Scorpions, or barragers or outcast pyros for defenders immediately into the game. Of course this base, unlike other "permanent" bases would be vulnerable to torpedoes, to open the possibility for clan warfare.

And of course any Expansion should have about 30 more ships, and about 30-40 hours of single player story missions
