Captain Flint

Tue May 20, 2003 6:51 pm by Captain Flint

Check in your shiparch.ini that you have the LOD (level of detail) ranges set correctly. The stock freelancer ships seem to have about three levels of detail depending on your distance from them. Most of the custom ships have only a single level of detail and so the range in shiparch.ini should be set to 0, 999999.

Best of luck.
