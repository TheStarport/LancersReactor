NukeIt

Thu Jul 22, 2004 10:36 am by NukeIt

Make sure you have a heavy, Order, or advanced thruster mounted. Get used to tapping it and jinking whenever you aren't shooting something...and once you've gotten that down, work on doing so even when you are shooting. If you can possibly get away and let your shields come back on their own, do so to save on shield batteries. Staying on the move is the single best defense against enemy fire; don't rely on shields or armor alone to save you.

Stalker missiles are quite effective at taking out Nomads due to their high maneuverability and the Nomads' lack of shields. I'm not sure if the launchers are available on any Order bases, but if they are consider carrying one to help thin out the herd(but be aware that the ammo is limited).

Mines can also be useful; try laying 5 or so in a big swarm, most if not all of them will hit something.
