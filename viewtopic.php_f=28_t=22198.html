UltraLeon Works prerelease (DarkFighter)

Developed by: UltraLeon Works
Team leaders:
Leonhart
Ultra_64


This mod adds DarkFighter to Planet Manhattan. Ships weapons and weakest DarkWorld equipment are included too. Click here to download.

This mod is part of huge mod that is currently still under development. Any information about this mod is still confidential.

DarkFighter comes with:

DarkFighter -DarkWorld VHF
Handles like eagle
4 lv10 gun/missile mounts(lv10 only)
Hull=20k

Dragoon Shield -DW lv9 shield
capacity 15k
recharge 2k.

Dark Thruster
normal thruster with infinite power

Own powerplant (sorry, no infocard yet)
designed to be usable with 4 DarkStars

DarkStar -Sold by Manhattan Weapon Dealer
Hull=3000
Shield=4000
Energy usage=800
Refire=2

Any bug reports or opinions please post it.
