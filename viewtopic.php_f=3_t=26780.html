Sir Spectre

Fri Dec 12, 2003 2:03 am by Sir Spectre

Get FL Mod Manager and try them all. It's very easy to try each one without having to do much work. Flmod is a program where you can choose from a number of mods that you have downloaded to try at that moment. It manages your savegames so they don't get confused with each other.

And that goes for your current game as well, I doubt any mod will allow your original FL savegame to be used for each mod. In other words you'll have to start over and create new savegames for each mod, because they save different information between them.

BTW you might consider changing the Arcon part of your user name, there is someone called Arcon on this site and he's a bit touchy and likes to rant. So to avoid that abuse you can easily change it via the "Your Profile" link at the top.

Or don't, if you can handle someone being short with you then keep it. This is just a warning of what may happen.
