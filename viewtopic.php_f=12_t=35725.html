gromit

Mon Nov 01, 2004 3:45 am by gromit

I saw the 4 hour pilot a couple of weeks ago and absolutely loved it. So far the series has been ok, but I think sometimes they try to cram too much storyline into a one hour episode...it would have worked better as a purely continuous storyline, almost serial-ish, rather than the typical Stargate SG-1 type different-story-every-week-along-the-same-theme....but saying that, I'm really enjoying it. The characters are strong and the whole thing seems well directed....I even managed to get my wife to sit through 30 mins of an episode without taking the p***, so it must have a semi-universal feel.

I think the one thing that makes it stand out in the sci-fi field, is that its like watching a drama with an unusual setting, rather than an explosive sci-fi fest with minimal storyline. They definitely put some thought into it.

And I liked the way they introduced the "old technology" (ie. the same technology as the original series) by showing that "new technology" is easily destroyed by the cylons....I thought that was a very clever way of making it look like the original series without it all looking too dated.
