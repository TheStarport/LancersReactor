gromit

Tue Oct 28, 2003 11:40 am by gromit

the quickest way to become a pirate is to shoot the hell out of the good guys, raid their trade routes, attack their stationary objects and generally give them a hard time everytime you see them

Be warned though, make sure you've got a half decent ship before you start doing that, because by attacking one sect you will find that you lose favour with their allies too. Which sometimes can mean blasting your way through several systems before docking again.

As for the 56K thing - it is possible, I did it for a while - BUT! Its irritating when it lags because you're on a server where lots of people die and are respawned. My advice with that would be, get as high as humanly possible with the SP before you try out the MP option. That way, people will respect you more on the servers and you'll be able to handle the lag skips when they happen mid-combat.

Hope that helps!

Shooting Bounty Hunters is one of the best ways to get points with the criminals (especially Outcasts and Corsairs) but it also becomes a pain in the ass. Bounty Hunters are in nearly every system in the game. If you piss 'em off, you are going to need to be a pretty good pilot. If you aren't, you soon will be

Pirating is a lot of fun though. Escaping massive police groups around capital planets for example. Sometimes just shooting trade lanes in Magellen does the trick. Get yourself one buddy in an eagle, and a few guys in droms, and watch the money roll in.
