Hey gang,

Here is the bottem line, if since you are asking...

Trade, and dont waste a trip; When I joined my server, i had $3k and the basic fighter.. did some local trades and after some local trips I was able to buy the RHINO; Just keep in mind that if you get killed with yor cargo, the cargo is lost and you start back on the place you last docked with.

Start small, and before you know it, you will be making $550k+ a trip...

KEYS TO TRADING AS A NEW MERCHANT:
1- Dock with everything; get the bases on your list, and you discover alot of items and costs for everything, and befoer you know it, you find the good routes.
2- Buy GREEN - even if you only buy one item, buy the green ones when you start.. you never know, that $2 oxygen could make $80 at a local station not far away = easy trade route. (thats a big markup on a cheep item.. not big profit, but big %)
3 - When you travel, go with friends: you can form [F4- key with other merchants, and thier escorts to aid you. They drive and you use your turrets [H-key and not worry about where you are going... and with that note...
4- HAIL other merchants - They tell you who they are, what they have and where they are taking it... they make money in those things... you can SCAN them too [ o -key
5- Have a beer - In the bars there are merchants, talk to them... they give you some good info on routes as well... and who hates whom..etc. You can also refresh the bar by going to the equipment place and going back to the bar = new people show up...
6- Upgrade your shields- As a merchant, get your shields as high as you can, then worry about your weapons.. save your @ss before you think about going against some nimble fighters in your pregnant watter buffalo freighter...with the shields, try and save or get your hands on countermeasures; missiles suck on the receiving end...
and Lastly....
7- Save some cash- It REALLY sucks when you get killed and only have $150 left and have to do a mission with your frieghter: see #6...

I hope this helps....
Good luck fellow Trader, and know that all the fighter jocks were merchants at one time...we just chose to mack some more creds....
