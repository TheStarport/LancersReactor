just pull up your navmap (where your waypoints are) and hover your mouse over the small icons, one of them will be to show mining zones (i think it's the pickaxe one). then head to that area and shoot the little rocks/things that actually move around, not the big stationary ones.

before long you'll get to a place where there is a debris field called "silverton" and a "copperton", guess what you can get there

as far as the new york system goes, expect to get lots of scrap metal from the debris fields in the area.
