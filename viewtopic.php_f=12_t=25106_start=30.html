@Tawakalna, I was what we call regionally a gopher, but they don't call anything otherwise they would have had to pay me and credit me in the movie I worked on. In my area of USA we say someone is a gopher if they generally do a little of everything. I go for this, I go for that. I gopher.

The film I worked on was Joe Somebody with Tim Allen, Julie Bowen & Jim Belushi. I wasn't paid a salary until the last few days of shooting, because I was just a Locations Assistant. I was actually only suppose to work a few days, but I kept showing up regardless if they wanted me, because they always ended up needing me anyway.

Through dilligence and hard work, the raised me to Production Assistant. I was the only person on the set to be elevated. Films are too short of time frames for promotions so to get one is extraordinary. But I wasn't just a PA, I was everything. I helped out every department and asked a reasonable number of questions, so as to get advice but not to annoy them. I had a good time. Learned a lot.
