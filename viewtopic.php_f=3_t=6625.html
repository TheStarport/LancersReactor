Something about the PR (and maybe final) version that is rea

OK, I was lucky enough to know someone at a Gamestop who has let me play the PR version and I ran into a bug that seems so obvious to me that it makes me wonder how they missed it and what other bugs might be present if they missed this one.

So, here is the deal. When you start the game you have access to your ship's scanners and tractor beam. So, you can use these from the beginning. However, in the beginning of the second main mission, the character you are taking orders from tells you that they are going to upgrade your ships with scanners and a tractor beam. Doesn't she realize I have had them from the beginning? I am trying to figure out how they let this one get past them.

Now, I know that the PR versions are not final, but I had a friend play the demo and he says you start the game with scanner and tractor beam. I have also talked to some people who have the released "not-so-legal" version of the game (which is supposed to be final) and it occurs there too.

Anyone else who has played this run into this and think it was odd?
