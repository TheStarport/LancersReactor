Captain Tylor

Tue May 11, 2004 10:08 am by Captain Tylor

the advanced champion shield is just another thing left out of the singleplayer experience by the programmers. There's also coding for freighter shields over class 7 as well, but obviously they aren't usable without further mods to ships.

Uber shields are just that. made to stand against anything. It's probably a shield the programmers made for testing purposes, and didn't think of taking it out before public release of this game.

Other inconsistent things to note is that wrecks blow up or remove some of the loot they're supposed to carry, right at startup. This is why paralyzer launchers can't be found anywhere unless modded in. You'll probably find more items that "should" have made it into the SP game but havent, or even MP.

Many servers out there (mine included) allow the use of these items by fixing wrecks that destroy them when server starts or by allowing them to drop from killed enemies. If it's something you want to persue, try out one of these servers and see.
