Civilian StarBlazer LF, Kusari Blood Dragon HF, (more)

Would like to apologize in advance for any regurgitation of info on this, but I didn't see a good listing of the ships that *should* be pilotable, but were left out for whatever reason.

I want to assume these ships are relegated to NPC's, and were left out of MP for one reason or another..

Civillian StarBlazer - IMO this ship looks miles better than the flier/tracker. The differences are almost subtle, but after seeing this ship all the other star* series look like chopped up versions (likely the case). This is the ship that I believe should have been ge_fighter3, and judging by the gap in the nicknames, was initially intended to be pilotable. As of this point the only reference I've found to it is in rtc_shiparch as a civilian freighter, though its class is still fighter.. at any rate, it was missing entirely from the shiparch.ini, so I did a really quick'n'dirty graft onto the star tracker entry to give it the unused model.

If anyone's interested in seeing it first hand, go into shiparch.ini, to the ge_fighter2 entry, by DA_Archtype change "tracker" to "blazer" for both the folder name and the file name.

Kusari Blood Dragon - apparently a class 9 ship that was either pulled from MP, or is simply an NPC vehicle. I have yet to mod this one into a base, but I'm really interested in seeing what it looks like. I feel like i've seen it before, but I'm not sure. At any rate it's a shame that Kusari doesn't have a decent advanced fighter.

Corsair Freighter - From what I recall, this one is a low-level NPC freighter. Haven't seen it yet, but I think the power rating was like 4/2. If it looks tough, perhaps it'd make a nice new heavy freighter. Why the Corsairs were left without their freighter, I refuse to understand.. suppose they just hitched up trailers to their Titans instead. (hrmm..)

Liberty Elite HF/VHF - just stumbled on this one, didn't notice it when I was looking up the others. WIll edit when I know more.
:edit: looks like Juni's Defender. Almost the same, but has a vertical fin on the back, gives the ship a real attitude.

Mubata

Thu Mar 13, 2003 4:27 pm by Mubata

Actually Juni's ship is the equivalent to the Liberty Very Heavy. As you have probably noticed, it has a different vertical fin that the normal Defender lacks. It's mentioned in a few places in the ini's but was not made available for purchase.

If you do want to make it available for purchase, you need to edit it in. I did this with the defender in a mod I created for the demo. You need to create a loadout package, link to the cocpit mesh for the li_elite ship, as well as make it available for sale at one of the bases. The downside is that the content dll for the game, will still call it a defender when you go to buy it from the base that you added it to. So be sure to look at the stats to know which one you're purchasing.

If your curious how I did it, the mod'd ini's that I used are in the weapshipmodv3.zip, that is available for download here at LR.

Hope it helps...
